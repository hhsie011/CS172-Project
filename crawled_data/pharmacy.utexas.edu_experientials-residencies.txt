The experiences increase in duration and complexity as the student gains knowledge and skills, culminating with the final year of rotation experience. Visit Experiential Programs Practice Residencies and Clinical Science Fellowships (Post Pharm.D.) The College of Pharmacy in conjunction with clinical facilities throughout the State provides a variety of outstanding Practice Residencies and Clinical Science Fellowships for pharmacy graduates who wish to pursue advanced clinical practice or research training. In addition, the College offers advanced training in combination with a graduate degree (M.S. or Ph.D.). Visit Residencies & Fellowships Our mission is to provide exemplary education, training, research, and professional development in the pharmaceutical sciences; to advance discovery, innovation, and patient care; and to provide service to the university, professional and scientific communities, and society.

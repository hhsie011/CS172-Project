Brown embraces diverse perspectives, experiences, and backgrounds. Diversity among our faculty, staff, and students creates a greater number of role models, broadens perspectives, and makes us better partners to the communities we serve. Programs and Initiatives Our diversity initiatives and programs are grounded in the University's mission of education and the advancement of knowledge.

Applications will only be accepted from practicing physicians and healthcare professionals who have extensive training and experience performing circumcisions. All applications must be accompanied by a letter of recommendation from a referring rabbi who is a member of the Rabbinical Assembly. Apply here Participants must commit to the entire in-person conference at JTS in New York City, beginning Sunday, October 23, 2022, at 2:00 p.m. through Thursday, October 27, 2022, at 4:00 p.m. ET. Participation Fee: $4,800 (includes four breakfasts, four lunches, and dinner Sunday evening) Housing and other dinners must be arranged on your own. Please reach out to Ilana Garber at igarber@rabbinicalassembly.org with questions.

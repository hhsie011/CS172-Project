Cachexia, a muscle and adipose wasting syndrome, significantly decreases both quality and length of life in patients suffering from advanced cancers. Currently, my focus is to elucidate mechanisms of cardiac muscle dysfunction in cachexia and how decreased cardiac performance may contribute to cachexia pathogenesis. I work with an animal model of cachexia and employ techniques in physiology, molecular biology, and biochemistry to measure heart function and identify changes in protein and gene expression that contribute to functional changes. I am particularly interested in the role of altered calcium handling and metabolism in the progression of cardiac dysfunction in cachexia.

As the only Ivy League business-management program to focus on hospitality, we actively prepare students to be leaders in a dynamic global industry. The School of Hotel Administration is one of the three collaborative schools that comprise the SC Johnson College of Business within Cornell University, giving it the ability to adjust its curriculum and services to keep pace with changes in the hospitality industry. Our students reap the benefits of this flexible, intellectually-rich, and creative environment.

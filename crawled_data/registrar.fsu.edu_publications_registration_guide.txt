Registration Guides are available in PDF and HTML formats at http://registrar.fsu.edu/registration_guide/. The PDF version is easily searchable and allows the reader to print any or all pages of the document. Email questions regarding Registration Guides to ar-regpub@fsu.edu. Please type Registration Guide in the email's subject line. The following information is intended for those individuals involved in reviewing content for the Registration Guide. To update your area's reviewer contact-information, please email ar-regpub@fsu.edu and specify the name and contact information for the person who should receive specific Registration Guide files.

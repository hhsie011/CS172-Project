Branded Materials If you are interested in obtaining a Columbia Business School branded tablecloth for your club events or if you would like to request branded gifts for speakers or event hosts, please contact Alexandra Kennedy ([email protected]); please note that gift availability is dependent on Alumni Relations stock, event suitability, and number of requested gifts. Alumni Club Leader WhatsApp Group Columbia Business School alumni club leaders can opt into this conversation by reaching out to Alexandra Kennedy ([email protected]). This conversation allows club leaders to discuss events and programs, questions you may have, pain points, and share best practices. Social Media The School is always on the lookout for new social media content. If you have some good photos and would like to have your club’s event featured on the School’s official social media channels, let us know! If you are promoting your event on your own personal or club-related social media, we suggest using any of the School’s hashtags: #CBSAtTheCenter, #CBSAlum, or #WhyCBS.

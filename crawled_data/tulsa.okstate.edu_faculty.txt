services and other references to help them better perform their duties. OSU-Tulsa faculty are at their best in the classroom and in the research laboratory. They share their knowledge, encourage students to think and take a genuine interest in their students and their goals. OSU-Tulsa faculty members also are leaders in their fields. They lecture and make presentations at national and international conferences; conduct research and publish books; and are recognized as the best teachers and leaders by national professional organizations. The primary goal of our administrators and staff is to provide a nurturing and friendly environment for our students, while being responsible stewards of the funds provided by the state's taxpayers and through gifts from individuals, foundations and corporations. For those seeking employment with the university, Oklahoma State University has a job database available through Human Resources. Job postings are updated regularly, so check back often.

Here are some things to know that will assist student worker managers with tools and forms for hiring, rehiring, separations and any changes. Guided Learning and the Journeys Tile in Ignite are also available tools for student worker hiring. Preparing for Summer Hiring Check the Reports For Current Assignment Review: Ignite Homepage > Tools > HCM Management Data Portal > Current Student Assignment For Requested Changes/Rehires: Ignite Homepage > Tools > HCM Management Data Portal > Future Student Assignment Allow a 2 week processing period from the offer creation date or hire request to the start date I-9s must be completed prior to the student starting work Federal Regulation Students starting prior to the I-9 means no Ignite access - which creates further complications to get timecards submitted on time If hiring new students, use the Journeys Tile in Ignite for their hiring process Summer Reminders Students who are NOT taking Summer courses at Baylor are eligible to work up to 40 hours per week: FICA deductions will apply when a student is working, but not currently taking courses Timecards must be approved each bi-weekly pay period when students work over the regular 20 hours/week during the summer Review this timecard approval training for a refresher If you have any questions regarding the Summer Student Worker Hiring Process, please email Student_Employment@baylor.edu.

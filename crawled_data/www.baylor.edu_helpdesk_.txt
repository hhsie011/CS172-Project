Learn More Always Use Multi-Factor Authentication Using a multi-factor authentication method to log in to online accounts is a great way to protect your personal information. In addition to usernames and passwords, the Duo app serves as a second step in authenticating user access. Learn More Use Strong Passwords to Protect Your Accounts Passwords are an important piece of protection for your personal information. Make sure that your passwords contain at least 12 characters, are unique and kept in a secure location. Learn More Beware of Phishing and Spearing Messages Be cautious of unusual emails and messages. Clicking on a fraudulent hyperlink can put your information at risk. If you are uncertain about a message, verify the source, check the URL and refrain from submitting personal information. Learn More Be Cautious When Using Public Wi-Fi Connecting to public Wi-Fi causes information stored on your device to be at risk of being hacked. Help keep your data safe by using a virtual private network (VPN) and prevent your devices from automatically connecting to public Wi-Fi networks. Adjusting your social media settings from public to private and rejecting follow requests from unknown accounts can help safeguard your information.

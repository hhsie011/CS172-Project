Our Office of Diversity and Multicultural Affairs (ODMA) directly supports our efforts to recruit and develop a diverse student body. The office advises students as they advance through our medical school curriculum and provides guidance concerning future career choices. ODMA hosts a number of pipeline programs that provide opportunities for middle school students to recent college graduates and supports a number of multicultural student organizations. These groups provide a forum for student participation in campus affairs, student recruitment, social events, and service initiatives in the local community. VP&S Anti-Racism Task Force The Vagelos College of Physicians & Surgeons (VP&S) Anti-Racism Task Force (ARTF) was commissioned by Deans Anil Rustgi, Jonathan Amiel, and Lisa Mellman as part of a Columbia University Irving Medical Center (CUIMC)-wide initiative to confront the issues of structural racism and implement durable antiracist solutions.

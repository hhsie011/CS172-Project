A brick inscribed with your name or others’ names will remind future generations of the path that brought you or a loved one to veterinary medicine at Oklahoma State University. You can reserve a place on any or all three of the Walk of Honor Courts for $100 each (or 3 for $275). Your gift to the Walk of Honor builds the Veterinary Medicine Scholarship fund helping new students on the same path to achieve their veterinary medicine degree. The Commemorative Courtyards Offer Meaningful Ways to: Recognize a graduation, birth, or anniversary Express appreciation for a family member, a faculty or staff member, a client or a student Remember a beloved pet Celebrate the achievement of a pet-lover or pet Honor a veterinarian The Walk of Honor Location The walkway of inscribed paver bricks are on Farm Road in three locations: Onyx Courtyard – located at the Veterinary Medical Hospital Military Veterinarian Honor Court – surrounding the flagpole at the northeast corner of McElroy Hall The Walk of Honor – the north side of McElroy Hall How to Purchase Giving opportunities exist to provide individually inscribed paver bricks to these areas for a minimum donation of $100 per paver.

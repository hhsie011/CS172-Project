Co-sponsored by CAPS and SAS, and co-led by Erin Lane-Aaronian, Psy.D. (she/her/hers) and Desirae Mix, M.Ed. (she/her/hers) Meets Tuesdays, 1-1:50, over Zoom, starting 4/5/22. Contact [email protected] to access the Zoom link. DBT Group This is a skills training group with focus on fostering mindfulness, emotion regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal effectiveness skills. Group will start on 2/17/22, and will meet on Thursdays for 9 weeks from 2:30-4:00 at the Health & Wellness Building (450 Brook Street, room 197B). Co-led by Chonlada Jarukitisakul, Ph.D. (she/her/hers) and Diane Farber, Psy.D. (she/her/hers). For information and to register please email: [email protected] and/or [email protected]. Trans and Gender Diverse Support Group This group is designed to be a safe and confidential space to talk, explore, support, challenge and just be present in your gender identity without having to explain/educate/fear judgment. Group is led by Jayden Thai, Ph.D. (he/him/his; CAPS) and Caitlin O’Neill (they/she; LGBTQ Center). Will meet in person Thursdays, 12:00-1:00, at the Sarah Doyle Center for Women and Gender starting 1/27/22. Contact [email protected] or [email protected]) for more information. Processing to Thrive Processing to Thrive is a community space hosted by the BCSC and CAPS, aimed at providing students of color an opportunity to discuss the joys and challenges they experience within the college environment. Processing to Thrive is open to all undergraduate, graduate and medical students. Processing to Thrive follows all current COVID-19 protocols. Group is co-led by Corey Martin Fitzgerald, M.Ed., LMHC (he/him) and Lehidy Frias (they/them). Group will meet in-person at the BCSC, from 12:00-1:00 pm on the following dates: 2/15/22, 3/1/22, 3/15/22, 3/22/22, 4/5/22, and 4/19/22. For more information please email the facilitators.

The Double Discovery Center is located on the third floor of Alfred Lerner Hall in Room W-306. The main entrance to Alfred Lerner Hall is located within the Columbia Campus, accessible through the first black gate at 115th Street and Broadway. Visitors must stop at the Lerner Hall information desk and receive a pass to enter. Visitors are kindly asked to have a photo ID to enter Lerner Hall.

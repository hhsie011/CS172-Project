With the help of a dedicated professional and student staff, Andrew organizes international student orientations, advises student organizations, and builds intentional community spaces here at Brown University. If you would like to meet with Andrew to talk about any shared goals, get your voice heard, or just to chat, he welcomes you to make an appointment here. Current Community Program Coordinators Yohan Mutta '23 Luke Prestwich '23 Cultural Programming Coordinators The cultural programming team organizes programming to facilitate cultural exchange among the diverse student community, create spaces for intercultural dialogue, and foster conversations around intersectionality for both international and domestic students. The team puts together International Education Week and International Festival, Brown’s largest cross-cultural extravaganzas! Current Cultural Program Coordinators Sally Zhang '23 Francesca Mustain '23 Social Media and Outreach Coordinator The Social Media and Outreach Coordinator manages general communications and student group outreach throughout the academic year, which inludes taking photos at events, managing the center’s website and social media presence, managing the weekly newsletter, creating posters and other graphics as needed! Current Social Media and Outreach Coordinator Marley Thompson '22 International Mentoring Program (IMP) Coordinators The International Mentoring Program is a year-long program that facilitates the transition of international students to life at Brown and in the United States. Led by four student coordinators and a team of over 20 mentors, IMP organizes international undergraduate orientation for around 300 students and events throughout the year such as the IMP Gala, gingerbread making, karaoke, and study breaks. You can email [email protected] or click here to learn more!

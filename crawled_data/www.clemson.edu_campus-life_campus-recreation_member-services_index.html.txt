Campus Recreation members enjoy activities such as group fitness, and equipment rentals from our Outdoor Recreation group. As a student member you have exclusive use of Douthit Hills Fitness Center and all of its programs. Your membership allows you discover all the ways Campus Recreation can help you feel better and enjoy being active. Our Welcome Center staff at Fike Recreation Center can assist you with your membership, schedules, programs, and facility hours. Our Welcome Center staff at Douthit Hills Fitness Center and the staff at the Rental desk at The Andy Quattlebaum Outdoor Education Center can help inform you about our facilities, schedules, programs, and services provided by Campus Recreation. Visit our Welcome Center located at the entrance of all of our facilities for assistance anytime during your visit. For additional assistance you can contact one of our member services professionals below.

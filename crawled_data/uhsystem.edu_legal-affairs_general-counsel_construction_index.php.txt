These legal services include: drafting and negotiation of contracts for construction and for professional design services; development of standard and model contracts; advice and counsel regarding construction disputes and other construction matters; monitoring and coordination of construction litigation referred to the Texas Attorney General; dispute resolution procedure representation; and advice and counsel regarding new and innovative methods of project delivery.

The University of Louisville (UofL) has been accredited by SACSCOC since 1915. The university’s accreditation was last reaffirmed in December 2018. The reaffirmation reports linked below were submitted during UofL's last accreditation process. The university's accreditation is linked to its commitment to achieving preeminence as a nationally recognized metropolitan research university. Many thanks go to the university’s trustees, faculty, staff, and students for their contributions in support of the university’s successful reaffirmation of accreditation. Go Cards!

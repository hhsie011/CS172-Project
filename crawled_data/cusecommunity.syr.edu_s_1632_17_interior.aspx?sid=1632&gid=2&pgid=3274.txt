Emeritus William P. Tolley, the Melvin A. Eggers Senior Alumni Award “was created out of my deep respect and admiration for Chancellor Eggers and to honor senior alumni whose leadership and service to society has been joined with loyalty and service to Syracuse University.” The award is presented to those alumni who have graduated more than 50 years ago and have demonstrated loyalty and service to Syracuse University.

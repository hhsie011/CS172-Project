Skip Navigation California State University, Fullerton Site Menu Administration and Finance Student Business Services Main Navigation Services Forms Faculty & Staff About Titan One-Stop Shop Tuition Installment Payment Plan (TIPP) The TIPP gives students the opportunity to break up their Tuition Fees (State fees) or Non-Resident Tuition Fee (Out-of-State fees) in monthly installments. For California residents, this payment plan will divide tuition, campus fees, and professional program fees (if applicable). The payment plan does not include the Student Involvement Representation Fee (SIRF) and online distance fees, which have a separate payment deadline. See the list of all student fees for more information. Financial Aid Recipients: You may be responsible for an out-of-pocket payment if your financial aid is insufficient to pay the amount due or not disbursed before the payment deadline. Check the Student Homepage to see if the Anticipated Financial Aid award is sufficient to cover the due charges. Summer 2022 Payment Plan: Last day to enroll in the summer payment plan is May 2nd. Enroll online through your Student Homepage. Enrolling in the installment payment plan (Completed each semester) Online Enrollment Sign-up for the payment plan online through CSUF Portal. Student Homepage > Financial Account > Account Services > Enroll in Payment Plan If Student Homepage enrollment is unavailable: Complete the online form on our Forms Page during the payment plan enrollment period. For students under the age of 18, a parent or guardian will be required to co-sign the document. Requirements for Self-Enrollment on the Student Homepage -Student is at least 18 years of age -The student has already enrolled in courses for the term -There are no past due balances for a prior term. -The student is currently admitted for the specified term. -Tuition fees (resident or non-resident) for current term have not yet been paid. -Financial Aid is not awarded. Extension and International Programs (EIP) For more information on enrolling in EIP payment plans for Open University, Self-Support Degrees, and selected Extension Programs, please visit the EIP Payment Plan Page. During the Prepayment period Online enrollment is disabled during Prepay. Complete a payment plan application (Available on Forms Page) during the payment plan enrollment period to submit the form electronically for approval. Payment Plan for California Residents State Tuition and Campus-based fees divided into three installments (excluding summer and winter terms): A non-refundable $33.00 Payment Plan service fee is added to the first TIPP payment deadline after successful enrollment in the Tuition Installment Payment Plan. Payment Plan for Non-California Residents Non-Resident Tuition (NRT) divided into three installments (excluding summer and winter terms): A non-refundable service charge of 9% of total Non-Resident Tuition Fees is applied and divided into the three payments. Plan does not include Resident Tuition (State Fees).

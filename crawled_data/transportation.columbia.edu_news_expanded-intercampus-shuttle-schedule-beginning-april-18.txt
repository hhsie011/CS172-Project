Beginning Monday, April 18, the following changes will take effect. April 12, 2022 New stops at 137th Street and 106th Street All three lines will now run until 12:30 a.m. Saturday service is extended to 12:30 a.m. Buses will run more frequently during evening hours Click here to view the PDF schedule. Once the changes take effect on April 18, you can find the updated timetables on the Intercampus Shuttle page and in the Passio Go! app, where you can track the buses in real time. Thank you for your cooperation as we continue to make improvements to the shuttle service.

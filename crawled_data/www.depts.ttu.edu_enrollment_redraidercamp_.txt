Red Raider Camp is an exciting next step for new students attending Texas Tech University. RRC is designed to prepare students for success by providing an opportunity to have fun, make friends, experience traditions, and learn more about their new home, Texas Tech University! RRC is a three-day adventure in Brownwood, TX led by current Red Raiders who are prepared to share their personal experiences and keys to success for being at Texas Tech and Lubbock. Please note: Red Raider Camp does not take the place of Red Raider Orientation. Red Raider Orientation is REQUIRED for all incoming students. Connect with us!

the University or visit https://nds.registrar.ncsu.edu. All Undergraduate Studies (UGS) students are charged at the undergraduate rate for all courses. Post-Baccalaureate Studies (PBS) students who enroll in undergraduate level courses ONLY are charged at the undergraduate rate. PBS students who enroll in any graduate level course are charged at the graduate rate for ALL courses for which they are registered.

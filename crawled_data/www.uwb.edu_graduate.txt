The university's graduate programs have been widely recognized as among the best in the nation. Because many graduate students work while pursuing their degrees, our program offer flexible learning options including hybrid programming and part-time/full time scheduling. Our graduate classes are small providing the opportunity to work closely with your professors. Our graduate faculty will connect you to cutting-edge research opportunities and help you navigate the Puget Sound’s local and global networks of professionals, many of whom want to hire from our pool of experienced graduates. Whether you’re setting out on a course of academic study that will define your professional life, or seeking to build advanced skills to pursue your passion, the University of Washington Bothell’s graduate programs offer a range of options to match your needs.

Global Dialogues Events Past Event Teacher Training & BNCC | Columbia Global Dialogues Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo March 13, 2018 2:00 PM - 7:00 PM FGV Cultural Center (Praia de Botafogo, 186 - Botafogo, Rio de Janeiro) Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro, FGV EBAPE / CEIPE, the Lemann Foundation, and Columbia University’s Teachers College will host the event TEACHER TRAINING AND BNCC (Brazil's National Common Core) on Tuesday, March 13, to discuss the challenges faced in implementing a new common core and teacher training program in Brazil. The event will feature Susan Fuhrman, President of Columbia’s Teachers College, Claudia Costin, Director of CEIPE (the Center for Excellence and Innovation of Education Policies), as well as many other key figures in the field of education in Brazil and around the world. Moderated by the Brazilian journalist Fábio Takahashi (Folha de São Paulo) and Camila Pereira, Director of the Lemann Foundation, the event’s panels will cover topics related to initial and continuing education, BNCC's approval as a revolutionary movement, and the development of socio-emotional skills in teachers. The event also celebrates Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro's 5th anniversary and launches the first Columbia Global Dialogues, a series of events dedicated to promoting the exchange of knowledge on issues of extreme relevance for Brazil in a global context. RSVP PROGRAM* 14:00 - Opening and Welcome Remarks Claudia Costin Director, CEIPE (Center for Excellence and Innovation on Educational Policies) Thomas Trebat Director, Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro 14:20 - Teacher Training for curricula implementation worldwide Claudia Costin interviews Susan Fuhrman, President of Teachers College, Columbia University 15:00 - Panel 1: Pre-Service Teacher Education and the implementation of BNCC (Brazil’s National Common Core) Moderator: Fábio Takahashi, Columbia Journalism School alum and journalist from Folha de São Paulo Susan Fuhrman President of Teachers College, Columbia University Maria Helena Guimarães de Castro Executive Secretary, Ministry of Education Paula Louzano Universidad Diego Portales - via video Miguel Thompson Executive Director, Instituto Singularidades 16:00 - Coffee-break 16:20 - Panel 2: Professional Development for in-service teachers and the implementation of BNCC (Brazil’s National Common Core) Moderator: Camila Pereira, Director at Lemann Foundation Claudia Costin Director, CEIPE Tonia Casarin Teachers College alumna; socio-emotional skills and abilities expert José Edmilson da Silva, Former Director at Ginásio Experimental Olímpico 17:20 - Q&A 18:20 - Teachers College and Lemann Foundation partnership announcement 18:20 - Closing remarks and Conclusion Claudia Costin Director, CEIPE Thomas Trebat Director, Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro 19:00 - Celebratory cocktail *Program is subject to change.

By creating and managing an ever-evolving assortment of activities and services—such as hundreds of regional and virtual Brown Club events, class-led meet-ups, Reunion Weekend, Campus Dance, travel and learning opportunities, affinity-based mentoring, and more—we foster a vibrant Brown community and support alumni throughout their lives. Our Team It is our pleasure to help Brown alumni “discharge the offices of life with usefulness and reputation” as embodied in the University’s mission. Reach out to any member of the team to learn more about ways to get involved or how to connect with activities or resources available to you.

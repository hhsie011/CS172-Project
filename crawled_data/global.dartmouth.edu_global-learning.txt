Undergraduate programs around the world include Language Study Abroad, Foreign Study Programs, exchange programs, and transfer credit from other institutions. Opportunities for global exchanges, clinical training, and project-based experiential learning are critical components within our graduate and professional schools. Polar Science graduate students Simone Whitecloud and Laura Levy undertake field work in Greenland. Sam Streeter runs the trails in his host community in rural Peru during a 10-week global health fellowship. Sarah-Marie Hopf works a treadle pump in Myanmar while conducting field research for her thesis on human-centered design.

PREEMINENT STATE RESEARCH UNIVERSITY DESIGNATION Enacted by the Florida Legislature in 2013, the Preeminent State Research University Designation recognizes excellence in the state’s top universities. To maintain this designation, FSU is required to meet a minimum of 11 out of 12 performance benchmarks. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS The Florida Board of Governors has selected several Key Performance Indicators from its 2012-2025 System Strategic Plan and from accountability metrics identified by the Florida Legislature. U.S. NEWS & WORLD REPORT BEST COLLEGES RANKINGS U.S. News & World Report ranks National Universities in its annual edition of “Best Colleges.” These metrics represent areas where FSU will continue to focus in its efforts to climb in the rankings of public national universities.

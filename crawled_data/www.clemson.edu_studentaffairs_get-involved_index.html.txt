Student Affairs Current: Get Involved Fostering Connections for Students Campus Recreation Clemson students are introduced to a variety of programs and services during their time on campus, including fitness and wellness initiatives, as well as participation in Intramural and Club Sports and adventure activities Clemson Outdoor Recreation Education (CORE). Clemson boasts state-of-the-art facilities through the Swann Fitness Center at Fike, Douthit Hills Fitness Center, Snow Family Outdoor Fitness and Wellness Complex and Andy's. Campus Recreation Student Leadership and Engagement The vision of the Center for Student Leadership & Engagement is to create a uniquely Clemson Experience that prepares every student to be a leader in their profession and community. The center strives to prepare students to lead with honesty, integrity, and respect. Through the center, students begin their Clemson Experience successfully, belong and connect to campus and become Clemson leaders. Center for Student Leadership and Engagement Career and Professional Development Through the Center for Career and Professional Development, the UPIC program manages and facilitates our extensive on-campus internship and cooperative education program. Additionally, the Michelin Career Center offers many unique services, including large-scale job fairs, career counseling, resume and cover letter workshops, on-campus interviewing, experiential education opportunities (co-op program and internship programs) and much more. Center for Career and Professional Development Fraternity and Sorority Life Fraternities and sororities are student-led organizations that enhance the Clemson Experience inside and outside of the classroom. Fraternity and Sorority Life provides excellent leadership, scholarship, service and social opportunities and prepares members to make significant contributions beyond the undergraduate experience. Fraternities and sororities are governed by four councils: College Panhellenic Association, Interfraternity Council, Multicultural Greek Council and the National Pan-Hellenic Council.

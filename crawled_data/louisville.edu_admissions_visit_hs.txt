Your campus visit will include the following: Information session: a 30-minute information session with a UofL admissions representative. A student-guided tour of campus: the tour will include academic buildings, a residence hall, the Student Activities Center (SAC), and more. Optional academic visit: If you schedule a visit on Monday-Friday at 10:30 am, you may also be able to schedule an academic meeting to learn more about a specific major. Academic meetings are available on a limited schedule, and often fill early. Check out the standing departmental visits schedule. Optional FREE lunch voucher for students for any UofL dining location. Parking is FREE, and once you arrive on campus our staff will direct you to your visit location. Parking and directions will be included in your confirmation/reminder emails after registration. Other Ways to Visit UofL: If you are unable to attend our regularly-scheduled campus visit program, there are other ways to see campus.

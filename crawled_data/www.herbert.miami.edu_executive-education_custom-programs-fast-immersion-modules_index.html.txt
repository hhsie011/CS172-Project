Home Executive Education Fast Immersion Modules More in this section Executive Education Online Spanish & Portuguese Programs Our Approach Fast Immersion Modules Partnerships Results Certificates & Boot Camps Certificates Boot Camps Contact Us Whether delivered online, in-person, or as a hybrid format, Miami Herbert’s Executive Education Team works with you to co-create high-caliber custom program modules to address and help you overcome critical organizational deficiencies. In addition to fully customized integrated programs, we also offer Fast Immersion Modules which are condensed microlearning sessions that offer a snapshot on a particular topic/content area, with general customization. Fast Immersion Modules™ Delivered Online Pick from 90 Fast Immersion Modules™ that address a skills gap in your organization. We deliver these two- to four-hour FIMs, with computer-based self-assessments and team projects, fully or partially online, as best suits the organization. Fast Immersion Modules™ Delivered In-Person Faculty present the selected FIMs over two- or three-day seminars or in sequences throughout several weeks or months. Renowned professors offer personable instruction and coaching, addressing many of the main challenges that global corporations face today, such as in developing their leaders, managing organizational change, and navigating digital disruptions. For specialized discussions, we draw on the full range of scholars at the University of Miami, as well as many of South Florida’s entrepreneurs, experienced practitioners, and former CEOs. Joining our in-person programs brings you to vibrant Miami, a multicultural gateway to Latin America and other regions of the world. Network, learn, and strengthen your skills while enjoying the tropical setting and an energetic town. Connect With Us Rony Shir is the Assistant Dean overseeing Executive Education at Miami Herbert Business School. In her role, she focuses on leading the strategic and operational priorities of the School’s Executive Education Department. Rony works closely with faculty and staff to develop and deliver a portfolio of non-degree programs and facilitate collaboration and sustainable partnerships with clients. She oversees program design and implementation focused on the financial and operational aspects, with keen attention to the success in both content delivery and program execution. She graduated valedictorian and Summa Cum Laude from the University of Miami’s Professional MBA Program.

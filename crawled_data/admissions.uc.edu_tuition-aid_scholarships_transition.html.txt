Transition students are those that are moving from one University of Cincinnati college to another. For students who meet specific criteria, various scholarship opportunities exist to support them in their pursuit of a bachelor's degree. Arts and Sciences (A&S) Transition Scholarships There are scholarships available to UCBA and UCCC students applying to the McMicken College of Arts & Sciences for the fall semester. The deadline to complete the Change in College Application for the fall semester is July 1. Contact LaDreka Karikari at LaDreka.karikari@UC.edu or call 513-556-5210 for additional information. CECH Forever Bearcat Scholarship The College of Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services and Information Technology (CECH) is pleased to offer the CECH Forever Bearcat Scholarship for students transitioning from UC Blue Ash and UC Clermont. Eligible students must meet certain criteria and submit the Change of College form and scholarship application by the stated deadline in order to be considered. Fall Deadline: June 15th Spring Deadline: October 15th Summer Deadline: February 15th UC Blue Ash College and UC Clermont College Scholarships Up to twelve $2,100 annual scholarship will be awarded to students who have graduated with an associate degree from University of Cincinnati Blue Ash (UCBA) or University of Cincinnati Clermont College (UCCC) within the last two years and who have a 3.50 or higher cumulative grade point average and who have transitioned to a University of Cincinnati four year college.

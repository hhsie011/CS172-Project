Gathering in Beijing Events Past Event Teachers College Students and Alumni Gathering in Beijing Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo November 7, 2020 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM Parkview Place, 3F, Building 1, Zone A, 2 East 4th Ring Rd, Chaoyang Time: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM (Beijing Time) Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Teachers College China Society (TCCS) cordially invite you to a TC offline gathering at the Columbia-designated study space in Beijing! Here you will have a chance to play games and make in-person connections with your fellows, as well as TC alumni, learn about their academic and professional experiences within and outside TC. Looking forward to meeting you all. Alumni Speakers Siyu Nancy Shi ’16, Master of International Educational Development, Head of College Counseling Center at Moonshot Academy Jieqiong Joan Li ’19, Master of Instructional Technology and Media, Bilingual Computer Instructor at Haidian Kaiwen Secondary School in Beijing Liang Su ’20, Master of Neuroscience and Education, Management Trainee in TAL Group Yaqi Lu ’20, Master of Economics and Education, Education Business Strategic Analyst Xiaomo Yan ’20, Master of International Educational Development, Program Coordinator at Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Agenda 2:00 p.m. - 2:30 p.m. Registration & Free talk 2:30 p.m. - 3:30 p.m. Ice-breaker games 3:30 p.m. - 4:30 p.m. Alumni Sharing 4:30 p.m. - 5:00 p.m. Group discussion Reminders This event opens only to student and alumni of Teachers College. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time.

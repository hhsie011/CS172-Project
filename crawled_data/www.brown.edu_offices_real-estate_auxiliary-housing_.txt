Whether you are relocating to Rhode Island, are visiting the University on a short-term basis, or live in the area and are looking for resources to support a housing search, this web site is a good starting point. Short-term Accommodations (Visiting Scholar Housing) Whether you are new faculty/staff relocating to Rhode Island or a university guest, the Office of Auxiliary Housing can provide you with short-term housing conveniently located on campus. Please view the Visiting Scholar Housing information to the left for details and reservation information. The Assistant Director of Auxiliary Housing is also available to assist incoming faculty and staff by identifying resources available to them through the University. Brown Students Moving Off-Campus Graduate students and undergraduates with off-campus permission seeking housing in the neighborhoods surrounding Brown will find these sections of our web site useful: Brown-owned Housing Information Guide to Off-Campus Living Off-Campus Housing Service managed by Off Campus Partners Graduate Student Housing (graduate students are offered a limited number of efficiency, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments located on campus).

Page (opens a new window) Facebook this Page (opens a new window) Tweet this Page (opens a new window) Return to: College of Communications (21 units) The minor in Public Relations offers students the opportunity to engage in a systematic program of study in the field of public relations to complement their major field of study. Required Courses COMM 101 - Writing for Mass Media (3) COMM 233 - Mass Communication in Modern Society (3) COMM 361 - Principles and Ethics of Public Relations (3) COMM 362 - Public Relations Writing I (3) COMM 464 - Capstone - Public Relations Management (3) Electives (6 units) COMM 370 - Principles and History of American Mass Communication (3) COMM 407 - Communications Law (3) COMM 410 - Principles of Communication Research (3) COMM 465 - Entertainment Public Relations (3) COMM 468 - Corporate and Nonprofit Public Relations (3) COMM 469 - Crisis Communications (3) COMM 470 - International Public Relations (3) COMM 480 - Persuasive Communications (3) COMM 497T - Event Planning and Management (3) Additional Information Students majoring in communications cannot minor in communications.

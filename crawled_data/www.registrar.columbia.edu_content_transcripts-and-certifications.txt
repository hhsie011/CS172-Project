Request for Transcript - Application for Replacement of Diploma - Request to Withhold or Reverse Withholding of Directory Information - Name Change Affidavit Home > Transcripts and Certifications Transcripts and Certifications Current and former students who attended after 2001 must request transcripts online using SSOL. If you know your UNI, but have forgotten your password, you must reset your password. Current students should request academic certifications online using SSOL. The Student Service Center is unable to print transcripts and certifications at the counter during the first three weeks of each semester in order to focus on the provision of quality service to all students and their families. The Office of the University Registrar does not release transcripts to other educational institutions or third parties without authorization from the student.

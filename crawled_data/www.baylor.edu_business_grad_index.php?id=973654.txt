Hinnerichs COL Teresa Silvernail Hinnerichs, PhD Program Director & Assistant Professor Colonel, United States Army Email Teresa_Hinnerichs@baylor.edu Phone 210-221-6740 High Res Photo CV Download CV Professional Summary: Dr. Teresa Hinnerichs is the Program Director and Assistant Professor in the Army-Baylor University Graduate Program in Health and Business Administration. She holds a PhD in Business Administration with a specialty in International Management from the University of Hawai’i at Manoa. Dr. Hinnerichs is also a Colonel in the United States Army, serving in the Medical Service Corps since 2001. Dr. Hinnerichs is a Fellow in the American College of Healthcare Executives. She is a member of the Academy of Management, Academy of International Business, and Medical Group Management Association. Dr. Hinnerichs also serves as a CAHME Accreditation Fellow since 2021. Education: 2022 – Master of Strategic Studies, Army War College, Carlisle, PA (expected) 2020 – PhD, Business Administration, University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa, Honolulu, HI 2010 – MHA/MBA, Baylor University, Waco, TX 2005 – MS, Environmental Management, University of Maryland, University College 2001 – BA, Business Administration and BA Environmental Studies, University of Washington, Seattle, WA Courses: MECO 5330 – Principles of Macroeconomics (2020-current) MINB 5450 – International Business (2020) HCA 5V92 – Comparative Healthcare Systems (elective) Research Interests: Government Spillovers Multinational Enterprises Foreign Direct Investments Selected Publications: Ito, K. & Hinnerichs, T.

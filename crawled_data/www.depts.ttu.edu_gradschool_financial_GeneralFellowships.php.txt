Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Graduate School Menu Search TTU Graduate School Financial Support General Fellowships General Fellowships for New and Continuing Graduate Students Graduate students can apply to their academic department and the Graduate School for fellowship opportunities at Texas Tech University. The Graduate School provides a number of fellowship opportunities to new and continuing students. The full list of Graduate School General Fellowships is available here. Students may self-apply with typical awards ranging from $3,000–$5,000/yr. Deadlines for the 2022-2023 academic year Online application opens: November 15 Deadline for applications: January 15 Awards announced: mid-March Acceptance of award for student deadline: April 15 The application cycle for the 2022-2023 academic year is now closed Students are encouraged to check with their academic department for additional fellowship and funding opportunities. For More Information, click here... By submitting the Graduate School Fellowship Application, your application will also be considered for the following TTU college and department graduate scholarships and fellowships that they disburse: Education, Engineering, Human Sciences, Media and Communication, Visual and Performing Arts and other departmental graduate scholarships (including, but not limited to): Classical and Modern Languages and Literature, Economics, English, Environmental Toxicology, Kinesiology and Sport Management, Physics, and Public Administration FAQ's about Graduate School General Fellowships Applicants do not have to be officially admitted to a TTU graduate program when applying for our fellowships. Additionally, applying to the Graduate School does not automatically qualify applicants for our fellowships. Please note: All full-time students receiving fellowship support from the graduate school will be required to a) submit one (1) application or external fellowship/grant support and b) attend three (3) Graduate School-sanctioned professional/career development activities each year they receive support.

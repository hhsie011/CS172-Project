Follow Us Online Facebook Home Department of World Languages and Literatures Events Events Language Fair Each year more than 1,000 high school students demonstrate their knowledge of world languages and cultures at the Annual Language Fair at the University of Memphis. The students compete in a variety of contests, including poetry recitation, writing, costumes, folk dance, drama, music, projects, crafts, and a culture bowl. Competitive events are conducted in Arabic, Chinese, French, German, Japanese, Latin, and Spanish. Film Festivals The various language sections in the department regularly offer film festivals to complement the academic programs we offer. Information about the festivals will be published in the Gazette, the weekly departmental newsletter. Upcoming Lectures and Other Events Information on lectures sponsored by the department or of interest to department members will be posted on our homepage and included in the departmental newsletter.

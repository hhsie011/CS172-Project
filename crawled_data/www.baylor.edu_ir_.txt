Our office also administers the course evaluations for Baylor University. Explore Data Delivery Sources The IR Office provides data to University partners and external constituents in a variety of ways. We are continuing to expand interactive reporting and data analytics with the use of Microsoft Power BI. We also provide more tabular institutional reports.

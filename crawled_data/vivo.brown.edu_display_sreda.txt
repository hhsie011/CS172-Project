He joined the Computer Engineering Group at Brown in 2006 after receiving his PhD in Computer Science & Engineering from UCSD. His SCALE lab research interests are in the area of computing systems, with focus on energy-efficient computing, embedded systems, design automation, computer architecture, and combinatorial optimization. Prof. Reda has co-authored or edited two books and has over 130 articles in leading conferences and journals (Google scholar profile). Professor Reda received several best paper nominations and awards. He has been a PI or co-PI on more than $21.1M of funded projects from federal agencies and industry corporations. Prof. Reda is a recipient of NSF CAREER award. He served as a technical program committee member for many IEEE/ACM conferences in his research area. He is an associate editor for ElSevier Integration and IEEE Transactions on CAD. He is a senior member of IEEE.

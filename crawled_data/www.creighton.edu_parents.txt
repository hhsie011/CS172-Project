We exist for students. We care about how they are doing in all aspects of their education—academic, social and more. And we understand that you, as a parent, have an important role in your child’s education and well-being. The Office of Student and Family Support offers you and your Creighton student the resources to help navigate the college journey. She has been a leader in the Omaha community as a professional volunteer, fundraiser, and project and event manager for numerous Omaha organizations. She has worked with the Aksarben Foundation, Children’s Hospital and Medical Center Foundation, Creighton Prep and Duchesne Catholic high schools, and St. Vincent de Paul Catholic church and school. She currently serves as president of Creighton’s Nursing Alumni Advisory Board, and was a founding executive member for the Leaders for Life event, which annually celebrates female leaders and helps support women’s athletics at Creighton. Judy and her husband, Randy, BS’83, MD’87, have three children: Christopher, Class of 2022, Erin and Michael. Strive to Thrive: Support Your Student If you’re looking for a way to support your child’s transition to college, please encourage him or her to reach out to the Office of Student and Family Support with an email to thrive@creighton.edu. The office connects students to services across campus for their academic, emotional, social, spiritual and financial needs.

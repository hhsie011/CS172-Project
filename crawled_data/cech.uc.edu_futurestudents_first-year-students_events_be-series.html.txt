We are committed to #BeTheChange and make a difference. Choose CECH! At the College of Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services and Information Technology (CECH), you can go way beyond just pursuing your degree here at UC! We realize the impact our students can have in our community and the world, which is why our college, its' students, faculty, centers and partners all share one goal in mind: making a positive impact in their larger community, whether that is through education, service, or advocacy. Learn How You Too Can Make a Difference! Our students are ready and eager to #BeTheChange the world needs and CECH is committed to encouraging our students to follow their passions and become the future leaders of tomorrow. Start your journey to CECH by designing a virtual experience from the various events below: BE YOU BE INVOLVED BE EXPERIENCED BE BOLD BE INVESTED BE EMPOWERED BE THE CHANGE CECH has created a series of virtual events that will give you a glimps into the life of our students. They are designed to highlight unique aspects of our programs, like experential learning experiences, campus life, internships, and study abroad opportunties, that will prepare you to succeed even before graduation. Each week we will feature topics of interest for all majors, so tune in to for a learning experience that is catered to you. BE YOU Instead of asking yourself what you want to be when you grow up, ask yourself how you can be YOU in all that you do. Come explore this major and see how we can help you fulfill your goals and pursue your passions. Never compromise your goals and remember to always BE YOU!

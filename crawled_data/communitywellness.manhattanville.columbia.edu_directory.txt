Williams Nohemy Aguirre Noelia Jimenez Noelia serves as the Outreach and Wellness Nurse at the Columbia Community Wellness Center, where she, alongside Nohemy is responsible for community outreach, program development, health education, and screenings. She received her Bachelors of Science degree in Psychology from Fordham University in 2014 and a Bachelors of Science in Nursing from New York University in 2017. She joins us after having worked as a staff nurse at NYU Langone where her experience as a bedside nurse have laid the foundation for her passion in public health and preventative care. Having spent most of her life in Manhattanville, she is deeply committed to helping bridge health equity gaps and improving overall health and wellness in this community.

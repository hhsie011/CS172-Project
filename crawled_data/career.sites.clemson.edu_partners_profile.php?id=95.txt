When you work at Bosch, you are part of a leading technology and services company. We operate in over 60 countries around the globe. In fiscal 2014, our approximately 360,000 associates generated sales of 49 billion Euros. Bosch is a privately held company. That means we can act with financial independence, plan for the long term and invest in the future. To give just one example of how we do that, we spend over 5 billion Euros each year on research and development. The result: 4,600 new patents filed annually around the world.

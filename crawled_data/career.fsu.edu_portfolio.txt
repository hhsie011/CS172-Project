Development Publications Presentations Contact Career Portfolio Welcome to the FSU Career Portfolio Information site! The FSU Career Portfolio prepares students for the world of work through planning, reflection, skill development, and portfolio documentation. Building your Career Portfolio helps you get organized, showcase your skills and accomplishments, and stand out from the crowd. The Career Portfolio is also beneficial for interview preparation, enabling you to more effectively articulate your qualifications, skills, and abilities to employers and graduate schools.

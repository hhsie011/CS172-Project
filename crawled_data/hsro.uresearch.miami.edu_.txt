Virtual Office Hours HSRO Human Subject Research Office Welcome to the HSRO The Human Subject Research Office (HSRO)provides administrative support to UM’s Institutional Review Boards (IRBs). The HSRO supports the University IRB process by: Maintaining files on all human research at UM and JHS Maintaining a database for tracking all research proposals Serving as a resource on general regulatory information Conducting educational workshops Publishing UM’s Standard Operating Procedures for human research If you are a participant in any research being conducted by UM, and if you have any questions about your rights as a participant, call the Human Subject Research Office at 305-243-3195 or send an email to hsro@miami.edu Login to eProst Click here to login to eProst, the IRB's electronic submission & review system How to Submit to the IRB Information to assist with study submissions Forms & Templates Find the latest forms and templates to be used with IRB submissions Contact Us Contact the HSRO IRB 10 Upgrade Find information about the expansion of IBISResearch to include an upgraded IRB system University of Miami Coral Gables, FL 33124 305-284-2211 University of Miami Coral Gables, FL 33124 305-284-2211 305-284-2211 Resources About UM myUM CaneLink Academic Calendar Blackboard UM Network Alumni Association Medical Center Hurricane Sports Employment Opportunities Visit Campus Map Parking & Transportation Connect social-facebook social-twitter social-youtube social-instagram Copyright: 2022 University of Miami.

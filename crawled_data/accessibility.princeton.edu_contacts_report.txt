I have questions about being admitted to or affording Princeton I need help with a login or a password to access a resource I am looking for accessible parking, routes and transit I have encountered a disability-related website accessibility issue Help for Students & Prospective Students The Office of Disability Services should be the first point of contact for: Academic accommodations. Environmental adjustments, including housing, dining and transportation. Service animal residency requests. Provision of auxiliary aids and services. Modifications to University policies, rules, and regulations. The McGraw Center for Teaching and Learning provides tutoring and consultations for specific courses and for broad learning and academic strategies. The Office of Admission provides support for information about accessing Princeton as an international or financial aid student. Faculty & Staff Consult the Office of Disability Faculty FAQs regarding student accommodation requests. Employee accommodation requests should begin with a request to the employee's supervisor. Information Technology Report an issue with a University website or application on this page. Requests are monitored during normal business hours Monday through Friday. Getting to and Around Campus For detailed information on accessible parking, services, and paths and entrances, please visit the Transportation and Parking Services Campus Accessibility webpage.

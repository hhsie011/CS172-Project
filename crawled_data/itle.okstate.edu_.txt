Teaching, learning, and support are at the heart of our commitment to cultivate an extraordinary academic environment at OSU. ITLE supports excellence in teaching across the OSU campus. We provide resources addressing high impact pedagogy for faculty, instructors, and graduate teaching assistants, effective instruction through quality video production, multimedia creation, and state-of-the-art instructional technology for OSU and its students. Services Technology Services ITLE provides technology support and maintenance of technology in General Use and Teaching Studios across campus. We also support instructional technology that enriches the teaching experience and promotes meaningful student learning.

OFFICE OF THE UNIVERSITY REGISTRAR Office of the University Registrar The Office of the University Registrar, a unit within Enrollment Management, supports teaching and learning at Tulane by maintaining the integrity of academic policies and the student information system. We are the stewards of Tulane’s student records from application to degree conferral in perpetuity.

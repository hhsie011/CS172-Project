Greenlee In the News COVID-19 Updates Ethos magazine Ethos Magazine Ethos magazine is an award-winning publication serving the students of Iowa State. It is a general-interest magazine that covers the culture of Iowa State — anything from cool clubs to social justice issues. It is the only magazine for Iowa State students that publishes three times a year. It has a circulation of 1,200. Ethos provides an extremely valuable learning experience by giving students a chance to contribute to a widely circulated publication, both in print and online. Ethos is produced by Iowa State students, and Iowa State students only.

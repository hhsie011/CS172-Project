When volunteers give of their time and their talent, they demonstrate that the best of Yale can be the best for Yale. We honor and recognize those individuals and alumni groups whose service stands out, including faculty engaged in alumni programs, volunteer leaders, and outstanding alumni organizations. The Yale Medal Yale's highest honor for individual service to Yale YAA Board of Governors Excellence Award For outstanding alumni organizations and groups Lamar Faculty Award For individual service by faculty members to the alumni community YAA Leadership Award Individual award selected by YAA staff Yale-Jefferson Award for Public Service For sustained public service that is inspiring, innovative and impactful Yale College Class Awards For classmates who have dedicated time, energy, and enthusiasm to the Class Yale Club Recognition Awards For outstanding leadership and dedication to Yale through local volunteer activities Past Winners Looking for a particular award winner?

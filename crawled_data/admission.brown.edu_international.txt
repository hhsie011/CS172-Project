In fact, about 15 percent of our students come from abroad. The International Students Resources website provides links and information to support services and offers a view of what you can expect as an international student at Brown. There is not a separate application for international applicants. Please complete the application appropriate to your status as an incoming first-year or transfer student. You should indicate your citizenship according to the passport with which you plan to enter the United States.

Frank Barton School of Business Graduate Studies in Business Wichita State’s online business fundamentals graduate certificate is ideal for professionals with managerial responsibilities, but who lack a formal undergraduate business education. The certificate provides business knowledge essential to handling day-to-day business operations, managing workplace issues and developing positive business relationships. Contact coordinator at gretchen.holthaus@wichita.edu Curriculum This online 12-credit-hour certificate consists of the following courses: Fundamentals of Accounting Fundamentals of Finance Marketing Basics Management Basics Analysis of Macro-Economic Theory Analysis of Micro-Economic Theory Organizational Behavior OR Management Information Systems View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission. International students may enroll in the certificate program but have to make sure it complies with their visa requirements.

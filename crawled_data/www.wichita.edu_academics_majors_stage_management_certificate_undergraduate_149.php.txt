You'll learn a variety of artistic skills and valuable management tools essential for successful careers in touring productions, dance, opera, event management and more. Learn how stage management is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum To complete the 15-credit-hour certificate, you’ll take the following courses: Practicum: Management Stage Management Directed Projects – Stage Management Practicum: Management Directed Study – Stage Management Principles of Management Social Interaction View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor."

This program allows for additional time with your academic college, extended opportunities for peer interactions, and a more manageable pace of information. Start time: 9 a.m. Location: Student Union Credit hours: All hours Thursday, May 26 - Friday, May 27 Tuesday, May 31 - Wednesday, June 1 Thursday, June 2 - Friday, June 3 Monday, June 6 - Tuesday, June 7 Thursday, June 9 - Friday, June 10 Monday, June 13 - Tuesday, June 14 Thursday, June 16 - Friday, June 17 Thursday, June 23 - Friday, June 24 Monday, June 27 - Tuesday, June 28 Thursday, June 30 - Friday, July 1 Thursday, July 7 - Friday, July 8 Monday, July 11 - Tuesday, July 12 One-Day, In-Person Orientation This one-day orientation experience is a quicker pace of information that includes advising and enrollment, time with peers and opportunities to learn more about the OSU community. This program type is not advisable for those unfamiliar with OSU, are unsure of their major selection, or typically need additional time for decision-making. Start time: 9 a.m. Location: Student Union Credit hours: All hours Wednesday, May 25 Wednesday, June 8 Wednesday, June 15 Wednesday, June 22 Wednesday, June 29 Wednesday, July 6 Wednesday, July 13 Thursday, July 14 Friday, July 15 One-Day, Virtual Orientation This limited program for students who cannot attend in person is focused specifically on the enrollment process and requires students to prepare ahead of time through recorded sessions. This program is limited to those that live more than 500 miles from Stillwater and cannot make the trip, are in military training during program options, financial hardship prevents attendance, or will be out of the country during the entirety of program options.

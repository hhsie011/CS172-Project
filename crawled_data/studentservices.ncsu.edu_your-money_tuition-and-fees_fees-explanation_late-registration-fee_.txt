Registration Fee Tuition Surcharge Residency Status Previous Term Tuition and Fee Rates Tuition Rates Reference Guide Late Registration Fee Additional fees are required for students who register during the late registration period, whether or not they are registered by a department. Based on their late registration circumstance, students must adhere to these payment fees and deadline regulations: Student who register during the late registration period Bill due: the same day as registration Fee: $100 non-refundable fee for spring and fall semesters; $35 for summer sessions Students who register after the Census Date Bill due: prior to registration Fee: $250 non-refundable fee for spring and fall semesters; $100 for summer sessions Students who registered during regular registration whose schedules were cancelled for non-payment Bill due: must pre-pay prior to re-registering Fee: subject to the applicable late registration fee (including NDS students) Note: All new undergraduate and graduate students, NDS (Post-Baccalaureate Studies and Undergraduate Studies) students, Inter-Institutional students and those in Co-Op programs are exempt from the $100 late registration fee (unless cancelled for non-payment); however, they are subject to the $250 fee ($100 Summer).

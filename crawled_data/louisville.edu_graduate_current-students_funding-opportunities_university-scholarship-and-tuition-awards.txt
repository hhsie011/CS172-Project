Scholarship Awards University Scholarship Awards Scholarships provided by the Graduate School will cover stipend, full tuition, and health insurance. Tuition awards provided by the Graduate School will cover a maximum of 9 hours of Regular Graduate Tuition per term. The Graduate School does not offer tuition awards or scholarships to students enrolled in professional degree programs. The Graduate School has a limited number of tuition match awards. Students or graduate programs will be responsible for additional tuition charges beyond 9 hours, or for online courses or courses in Urban Planning and Public Administration. Preference will be given to doctoral students funded by grants and contracts. A small number of awards will be available for doctoral and master’s students funded by program revenues, gifts and endowments.

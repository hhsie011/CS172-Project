It is important for all citizens to take a proactive role in crime prevention. To this end, the CUPD provides numerous educational programs to the campus community. These programs include: Campus Safety (Crime at Clemson) Common Sense Self-Defense DUI awareness Rape Aggression Defense (RAD) Violence in the Workplace We can tailor programming to address your group's needs. If you have a need or interest in crime prevention programming contact Sergeant Michelle Young at 864-656-5256. or e-mail myoung@clemson.edu.

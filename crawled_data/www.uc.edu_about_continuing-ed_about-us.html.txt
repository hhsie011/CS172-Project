Victory Parkway Campus Map Download Parking Map (PDF) Parking Policies Visitors may park in the North and Cypress Lots between 8 am - 5 pm M-F. Anyone parking for more than 72 hours, must contact Parking Services at least 72 hours in advance of the visit. A visitor pass will be provided by the department you are visiting. The pass must be fully displayed on the driver’s side of the dashboard. Parking for Osher Lifelong Learning Institute (OLLI) is in the East Lot. Participants will receive a hangtag during the first week of classes each term. Parking for the Office of Professional & Continuing Educaiton (OPCE) and Communiversity is in the West Lot in the evenings only while attending classes.

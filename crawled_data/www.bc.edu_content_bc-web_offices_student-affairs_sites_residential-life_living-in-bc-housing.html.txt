traditional style rooms on either Newton Campus or Upper Campus. First-Year Housing Information Sophomore Year As sophomores, students participate in the room selection process. Students can select roommates and live in apartment style or traditional rooms based on their randomly assigned "pick time." Housing Assignments & Room Selection Junior Year The majority of incoming students receive three years of guaranteed housing. Students with three years of housing must live in an off-campus apartment junior year. Off-Campus Housing Senior Year Students are not required to live on campus senior year, however more than 90 percent of BC students choose to do so. Seniors are the first to choose housing as part of the room selection process. Housing Assignments & Room Selection The majority of Boston College students receive three years of guaranteed housing. Sophomore transfer students receive one year of guaranteed housing, while nursing students, Presidential Scholars, and certain student-athletes receive four years of guaranteed housing.

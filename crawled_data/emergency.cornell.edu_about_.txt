You are here: Home / About About The Office of Emergency Management is responsible for the Cornell University Emergency Management Program within Cornell University Compliance and Risk Services. For further information on the program please visit the Emergency Management Program web page. To contact Office of Emergency Management staff please call (607)255-8200 or e-mail oem@cornell.edu. Emergencies should always be reported to the appropriate emergency dispatch centers. For emergencies on the Ithaca campus contact Cornell Police by calling 911 from an on-campus phone, by calling (607)255-1111 from a cellular phone, or by using an on-campus emergency or blue light telephone. For emergencies off the Cornell campus call 911 from any telephone. Visit our Frequently Asked Questions page for more information.

Requests Facilities maintenance at OSU-Tulsa is handled by OSU in Tulsa Facilities Management, which serves both OSU-Tulsa and OSU Center for Health Sciences. Services include maintenance, housekeeping, construction, renovation, pest control, heating and air conditioning, carpentry and waste management. To submit a Facilities Management request, please utilize the Work Order portal at https://workorder.okstate.edu/. You can download instructions for submitting a work order here. Submit a Facilities Management Request Emergencies and high priority issues can be reported via the Facilities Management phone number at 918-561-8227.

Undergraduate and Graduate Degree Admissions Because Harvard Extension School is an open-enrollment institution, prioritizing access, equity, and transparency, admission to its degree programs strongly aligns with these values. You become eligible for admission based largely on your performance in up to three requisite Harvard Extension degree courses, depending on your field, that you must complete with distinction.

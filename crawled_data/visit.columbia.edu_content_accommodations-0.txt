View Current Hotel Rates The preferred hotels are located in Upper Manhattan and offer guests a variety of amenities. To make a reservation at a Columbia preferred hotel, please visit the Columbia University Travel Portal. New users must click on "create an account." Existing users may sign in to access Columbia-negotiated rates or to change/cancel an existing reservation. The rates negotiated by the university can be obtained only by booking through the direct link provided above or contacting the hotel directly by telephone and requesting the Columbia rate. There is no guarantee that you will receive the CU negotiated rate through the hotel website. In addition to single room rates, many of the preferred hotels offers group packages. To obtain group rates, please contact the hotel directly.

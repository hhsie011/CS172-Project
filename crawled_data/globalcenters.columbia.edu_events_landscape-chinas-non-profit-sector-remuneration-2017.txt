Sector Remuneration 2017 Events Past Event The Landscape of China's Non-Profit Sector Remuneration 2017 Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo September 16, 2017 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Event Intro On Sept. 16, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and A Better Community (ABC) will jointly hold a launch event of "The 2017 Non-profit Sector Remuneration & Talent Development Practice Report." At this event, key findings of the report on the current landscape of China non-profit sector remuneration and talent retention will be presented, and a panel of industry experts will jointly discuss challenges managing human resources in the field. Panelists and Presenters: Mr. Yonge Qian, Founder and CEO of A Better Community, a social enterprise in China providing professional consulting services to social sector organizations by skill-based volunteers. Mr. Yueguang Chen, Executive Director and Secretary General, Dunhe Foundation Ze Tao, President, China Foundation Center Mr. Yuhan Liu, Senior Consultant, Deloitte Consulting; Director, ABC Human Resources Consulting Service Line (Volunteer); Project Manager, Non-profit Sector Remuneration & Talent Development Practice Survey (Volunteer) Ms. Mei Wen, Senior Editor, China Philanthropy Times Ms. Min Shi, ShareONE Consulting, Director Ms. Tingting Guo, Talent Development Expert; Partner, ABC Human Resource Consulting Service Line (Volunteer) Agenda: 14:00 - 14:30 Registration 14:30 - 15:00 Welcome speeches 15:00 – 15:30 Keynote Presentation 15:30 – 16:00 Panel discussion 16:00 – 16:30 Questions and Answers Upcoming Events Columbia Global CentersAmman / Beijing / Istanbul / Mumbai / Nairobi / Paris / Rio / Santiago / Tunis Follow Us Facebook Twitter Instagram Youtube Columbia University ©2022 Columbia University Accessibility Nondiscrimination Careers Built using Columbia Sites Back to Top Close

Patients/clients look at their space number and inform the Information Desk at Redfern Health Center. If there are no Redfern parking spaces open, patients/clients can receive a parking pass at the Redfern Information Desk to park in any vacant employee space (except for handicap, service vehicles and carpool spaces). Patients/clients may also choose to park in any vacant metered space (no pass required; must follow meter rules).

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Career Center Menu Search TTU University Career Center Faculty & Staff Frequently Asked Questions Where is University Career Center located? We are located on the southeast corner of the Wiggins Complex. Wiggins is located West of the Chitwood Weymouth dorms and East of the Law School. You can view the campus map. Select Career Services, University to locate Wiggins. Do students have to schedule an appointment to utilize UCC services? Yes, students must schedule an appointment with a career counselor in order to use our services. To schedule an appointment, students will need to login to Hire Red Raiders using their eRaider information and make an appointment online. If you have any questions navigating the site, please call our office at (806) 742-2210. Can a UCC professional make a presentation to my class or student organization about career development? Yes, the UCC staff is available for presentations. To learn more, view our Presentation Request page. My students are required to provide proof of participating in a mock interview. Is a review of the student's performance provided? Any of our staff can provide a written critique through our standardized rubric. Please note that not all students who use our services are required to have a written review of their critique, so please inform your students to let us know they need the rubric. Login to your Hire Red Raiders account and schedule an appointment online. If you have any questions navigating the site, please call our office at (806) 742-2210. Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. View Map Meet the Staff The University Career Center encourages persons with disabilities to participate in our programs and activities.

Top-Notch Program, Tight-Knit Community The Canfield Business Honors Program challenges students with a specialized curriculum modeled after case-based MBA programs. The Program Application Process Student Recruiters Information Sessions Alumni Canfield BHP News Learn and Connect with Fellow Longhorns We challenge students to think outside of the box and make connections in the process. In small classes of 40 to 45 students, you’ll focus on group discussions, presentations, and analytic exercises. We also provide programming so students from all graduating classes can form strong relationships with one another. Our program is more than an education—we’re a community. Almost all of our students choose to study additional subjects. Most students will decide to double major within McCombs. Some students gravitate towards other fields or pursue minors and certificates. Students interested in technology careers might consider applying for Texas Honors Computer Science and Business. Our program also has a strong partnership with Plan II Honors.

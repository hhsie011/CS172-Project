Office of Education The Office of Education is the center of the student experience, overseeing all learning and support services through seven offices. We develop and implement the School's doctoral, master's, non-degree, and executive education programs to the highest standard of academic rigor. Admissions & Financial Aid The Office of Admissions and Financial Aid is here to guide you, from the beginning of your interest in our programs, through the application process for admissions and financial aid. Careers & Practice The Office of Careers and Practice provides resources and supports to students from enrollment through the completion of their academic program. Digital Learning Studio The Digital Learning Studio partners with faculty to create equitable and effective online learning experiences. Diversity, Culture & Inclusion The Office of Diversity, Culture, and Inclusion partners with School faculty, staff, and students to create an inclusive and equitable campus community dedicated to the development of public health practice and scholarship. Student Affairs The Office of Student Affairs provides individual support to students ensuring they receive robust and well-rounded training on the path to public health careers. Additional services include volunteer opportunities, social and alumni networking, and personal advising. Teaching & Learning The Office of Teaching and Learning provides resources for all aspects of education at Mailman, from initial course inception through instructional design, teaching practice, and student assessment. Educational Programs The Office of Educational Programs is a point of contact for the MPH shared curriculum and Certificate programming as well as other degree programs.

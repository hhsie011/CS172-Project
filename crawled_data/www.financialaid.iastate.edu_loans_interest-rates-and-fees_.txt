Success Interest Rates and Origination Fees Interest rates are amounts charged to a borrower for the privilege of using the lender’s money. The interest is calculated as a percentage of the principal balance of the loan. Origination fees are fees assessed by lenders to help offset the cost of processing the loan. The origination fee is assessed on each loan disbursement.

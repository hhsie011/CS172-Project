Accessibility Committee Parking & Transportation Library Assistance Wheelchair Stairlift The Accessibility Committee convenes each year to consult with Campus Facilities on campus accessibility. The Committee discusses barriers affecting the accessibility of areas around campus. The Office of Disability Services will take appropriate action when contacted about problems with accessibility of campus facilities. The Office of Disability Services will contact the Physical Plant, Customer Service Desk, if a problem requires a repair. For problems which are broader in scope, ODS will bring them to the attention of the Vice Provost for Administration and/or the Accessibility Committee to be addressed. Members of this committee include: Vice Provost for Faculty Affairs and University of Miami Administration Vice Provost for Undergraduate Affairs Assistant to the Vice President, Division of Student Affairs Assistant Vice President, Student Affairs Assistant Vice President, Director of Student Life and University Center Assistant General Counsel Assistant Dean of Students, Director of Disability Services, School of Law Director, Physical Plant Director, Student Health Service Director of the Camner Center for Academic Resources Director of Equality Administration Assistant Director, Disability Services President of Student Government Graduate Student Representative Undergraduate Student Representative University of Miami Coral Gables, FL 33124 305-284-2211 Camner Center for Academic Resources 1307 Stanford Drive Student Services Building, Second Floor Coral Gables, FL 33146 305-284-2800 305-284-2800 Resources About UM myUM CaneLink Academic Calendar Blackboard UM Network Alumni Association Medical Center Hurricane Sports Employment Opportunities Visit Campus Map Parking & Transportation Connect social-facebook social-twitter social-youtube social-instagram Copyright: 2022 University of Miami.

Held in-person for the first time in two years, the event was one of several commemorating National Public Health Week, April 4-10, 2022. Over 70 public health posters, presented and discussed by authors, were joined by representatives from local public health organizations who shared information on research, internship, and postgraduate job opportunities at the first SPH Community Engagement Fair. https://www.youtube.com/embed/U-NNehApJhA Communicating for Impact Brown public health students discuss the significance of their research to the community — and to their own educational experience.

We look forward to sharing about Clemson's campus, student life and what makes the Clemson Family unique! Clemson University continues to be under a modified operation plan in response to COVID-19. Visitors to campus must adhere to all guidelines during your time in the community. For the latest COVID updates, please visit our COVID-19 website. We are excited to offer limited capacity in-person visit opportunities. All guests must adhere to the safety guidelines while on campus. The statement of expectations included in the registration will be upheld for your entire visit to campus. We continue to also offer virtual visit opportunities weekly. Please confirm the visit category prior to registration. All guests must have reservations. No walk-in visitors will be allowed to participate in guided tours. Guided tour capacity is limited, there is no waitlist. COVID protocols are subject to change. Additional registration opportunities will be added as available. The registered student is limited to three total registered guests, who reside in the same household on the guided tour. During your visit, student tour guides will give you an overview of the University as well as the ins and outs of campus life. The walking tour will be limited to the outside of buildings only. There will be no access to residence halls, gyms, academic buildings, or dining facilities during this tour or during your time on campus. If you have any accessibility needs, including closed captioning for the virtual sessions, please contact visit@admission.clemson.edu at least 5 days prior to your scheduled event. Select a date on the calendar to see the visits available! Please confirm the visit category prior to registration.

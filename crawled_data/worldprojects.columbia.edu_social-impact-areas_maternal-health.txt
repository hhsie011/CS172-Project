Learn About the Project Postpartum Assessment of Women Survey Collects Data on Health of New Mothers The Postpartum Assessment of Women Survey (PAWS), the first large-scale data collection effort on the health of U.S. women and their families in the year after birth, is currently surveying women in six states and New York City. The survey has alreaded reach thousands of women around the country. Go to the PAWS Website Professor Jamie Daw on the Multidisciplinary Postpartum Assessment of Women Survey (PAWS) The survey is part of Columbia World Projects’ Leveraging Policy to Improve Maternal Health in the Fourth Trimester project.

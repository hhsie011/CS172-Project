In addition, we offer leadership development and support to several multicultural student organizations in order to enhance their overall growth as agents of change. We believe that diversity and excellence are interdependent pillars in delivering successful co-curricular programming. Our vision is to foster an environment where members of our community feel valued, respected, and are unobstructed in their pursuit of excellence.

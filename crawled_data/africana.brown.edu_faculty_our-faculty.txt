Breadcrumb Home Faculty Our Faculty The Department’s rigorous undergraduate and graduate programs are designed and driven by one of the leading faculties in the discipline. As mentors, instructors, and creatives, the Department’s faculty convene students for critical studies of the intersections of class, gender, nation, race, and sexuality informed by a broad range of theoretical and methodological perspectives.

Book Reading & Talk with TC alum Rongchan Lin Let’s go for a taxi ride with Rongchan! Join Rongchan Lin '18TC as she discusses her recent collection of Chinese short stories, “Where are you going?” 《请问你要去哪里？》. Her short story depicts the life of a taxi driver and is a tribute to her dad who was a taxi driver for more than 30 years. It has subsequently been adapted into a short film starring local actor Peter Yu, and into English by students from Hwa Chong Institution (College) have also translated her short story into English. We look forward to you joining us on this journey! About the Author Born and raised in Singapore, Lin Rongchan (林容婵) has benefited from Singapore’s bilingual education and is particularly passionate about language education. As a lecturer, she conducts assessment-related courses. Outside of work, she engages in creative writing and has won awards for her Chinese poetry. She also conducts creative writing courses for students.

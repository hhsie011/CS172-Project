Call 844-302-3886 Email Us Business Information and Technology The Department of Business Information and Technology (BIT) at the University of Memphis is one of six departments within the Fogelman College of Business & Economics which is the only business college in the Memphis area fully accredited by the prestigious AACSB. The Fogelman BIT degree program is highly regarded through: An emphasis on both the technology of the business world and related management issues, i.e., the organizational and behavioral impact of technology. Continuous program improvement, in which courses at all levels are regularly monitored to ensure content is in-line with the evolving business environment and changing student needs. Fogelman College's integrated programs that help students transition into successful business professionals or continue their studies at the graduate level. The BBA degree with a major in BIT requires a minimum of 120 hours to graduate. You may complete this degree entirely online through UofM Global or in combination with previously completed college level learning from other sources.

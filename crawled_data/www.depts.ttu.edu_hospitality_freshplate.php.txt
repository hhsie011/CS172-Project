The Fresh Plate All-You-Care to Eat & Snack Bar @ Bledsoe | Gordon The Fresh Plate is a state-of-the-art food emporium located in the Bledsoe/Gordon/Sneed resident complex! The Fresh Plate serves as an All-You-Care to Eat during traditional meal times during the week and for brunch on the weekends. New this year is the Snack Bar at The Fresh Plate featuring retail items for late-night snacks! When traditional dining plan holders dine at The Fresh Plate All-You-Care to Eat (excluding Scarlet, Commuter, and F/S Dining Plans) they eat at a reduced meal rate and also receive a 30% discount for purchases from the Snack Bar during late night. Some of the tasty choices available at the All-You-Care to Eat include handcrafted hamburgers, pizza, Mexican entrées, hot sandwiches, Asian items, comfort foods, a salad bar, a fruit bar, and desserts. The Snack Bar features chicken strips, pizza by the slice, chips, snacks, fountain drinks, and more! You can always find something to excite your taste buds at the Fresh Plate!

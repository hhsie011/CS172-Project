Individuals who have information regarding students who may not be meeting these standards are encouraged to fill out our easy online incident reporting form. To officially file a complaint, fill out the "report an incident" form to provide information for use by the Director of Student Conduct and Community Standards for the University of Cincinnati. Report an Incident If you have further questions or need more information, please contact the Office of Student Conduct and Community Standards at (513) 556-6814 or stop by Steger Student Life Center Suite 745. Faculty members should gain additional information about the academic misconduct incident reporting process by viewing our faculty academic misconduct page.

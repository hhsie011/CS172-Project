Jewish clergy across the globe, of all denominations, are welcome. What We Offer Free webinars to support and inspire holiday sermons and teaching Live online courses with JTS faculty designed especially for rabbis, new every semester An asynchronous training course on sexuality issues for Jewish clergy—learn on your own schedule Who It’s For Our online programs are open to rabbis of all denominations ordained by any recognized rabbinical program. Cantors are welcome at select programs where specified. Fees and Registration Stand-alone webinars are free. Pricing for online courses varies. Rabbis within five years of ordination and cantors within five years of investiture are eligible for a 50 percent discount on all JTS continuing education programs for clergy, thanks to the generosity of Michelle Kravtin Gary and Marc Gary, executive vice chancellor and chief operating officer of JTS. Active duty military rabbis are eligible for a 50 percent discount on all JTS continuing education programs for clergy, thanks to the generosity of Michelle Kravtin Gary and Marc Gary, executive vice chancellor and chief operating officer of JTS. For active duty military rabbis who are members of the Rabbinical Assembly, the RA will subsidize the remaining 50 percent of the costs for all online rabbinic learning programs offered by JTS.

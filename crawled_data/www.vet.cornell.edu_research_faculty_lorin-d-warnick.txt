Warnick Department of Population Medicine and Diagnostic Sciences Dean of Veterinary Medicine COLLEGE OF VETERINARY MEDICINE Department of Population Medicine and Diagnostic Sciences Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine Ithaca, NY 14853 Office: 607.253.3771 Fax: 607.253.3701 Email: ldw3@cornell.edu Research Interest My current research is focused on the epidemiology of Salmonella infections and antimicrobial resistance of enteric bacteria of importance in domestic animals and humans. I teach herd health, population medicine and health economics in pre-clinical courses. I also serve as Dean of the College of Veterinary Medicine.

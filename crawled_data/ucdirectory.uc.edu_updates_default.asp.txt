To update your listing: 1. Click Update Your Directory Listing. 2. Login and authenticate with your university Central Login Service username and password. If you are listed in the Directory and are unable to log in, contact the UCIT Help Desk at 556-4357. 3. Update your form. 4. Click the Submit Form button, which is located at the bottom of the form. 5. All updates to a directory listing will be placed on hold for review within 24 hours of receipt by Directory Services staff prior to releasing the information to the Directory Services website. Update Your Directory Listing Paper forms are also accepted. Print a paper form and send the completed form to Directory Services at ML 0107. If you have any questions or problems regarding this process, please contact Directory Services at: Directory.Services@UC.Edu, or at (513)556-1917.

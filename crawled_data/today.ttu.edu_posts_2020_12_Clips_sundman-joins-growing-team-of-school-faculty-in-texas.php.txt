Sundman began her duties as an assistant professor of medicine on Tuesday, Dec. 1. She joins a growing and vibrant team of faculty and staff at the School of Veterinary Medicine. Additional team members will continue to be added over the next few months as the school prepares to welcome its inaugural class in the fall of 2021.

The Law Firm Challenge is an annual fundraising competition where North Texas-based law firms vie for the highest percentage of alumni giving to SMU Dedman School of Law. Your gifts provide critical resources and immediate support to scholarships, clinics, and student organizations. Gifts to the SMU Fund for Dedman Law especially help the law school meet pressing needs. Plus, your generosity shows our students and faculty that they can count on alumni for support. Any gift to Dedman School of Law by an alum at a participating firm during an annual challenge year counts toward the Law Firm Challenge. Firms that reach 100% alumni participation will be featured in law school communications and on a plaque in Carr Collins Hall. Your gift to SMU Dedman School of Law enables us to develop the next century of world changers and leaders in the fields of law and business.

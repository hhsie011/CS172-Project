This plan provides an opportunity for families and enrolled students to pay University-billed expenses in regular monthly installment payments. Authorized user (parents) may set up the payment plan on behalf of the student. First payment due at time of plan enrollment $25 enrollment application fee (non-refundable) No finance charges as long as installment payments are current No enrollment/academic holds as long as installment payments are current Plan Enrollment Deadlines Fall: September 15th Spring: February 15th Summer: June 15th Please remember you still receive monthly billing statement email notifications while on the Payment Plan for informational purposes. The email notifications indicate a zero balance due. Log in to view the installment information. The $25 enrollment application fee is non-refundable and charged each semester enrolled in the payment plan. The student logs into Student Self-Service to enroll in the plan and may also authorize payers. Log in with the authorized user/designated payer access.

CSWS provides undergraduate students an opportunity to work part-time to help pay for the cost of their education and is based on financial need as determined by the FAFSA. To apply or verify your eligibility contact Student Financial Services. Job Listings Students may view the Community Service Work-Study job listings under the True Blue Neighbors Department heading on the Student Financial Services internal website. After an initial screening with our staff, students will interview with the organization to determine placement. Organizations interested in hosting a community-service work-study position may submit a job description here or contact our office for further information. 800 S.

COVID-19 Resources for Students Accommodation Form Request Interpreter/CART/Captioning Request Form Please complete the fields below in order to request an interpreter, CART, or captioning for an event that is outside of regularly scheduled classes. Events can include teacher/student meetings, group meetings, organizational meetings, on-campus events, etc. The interpreter coordinator will send a confirmation email to you that an interpreter has been secured. ** As of January 1, 2022: One time, "ad hoc" requests will be filled within five business days from the time of the request. We will do our best to fill all requests, however, requests received at the last minute will be less likely to be filled than what we have experienced in the past.

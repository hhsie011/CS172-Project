Expeditions with Patrick McMillan is a Public Service program of Clemson University Cooperative Extension, produced in partnership with South Carolina Educational Television. This program is offered through American Public Television and airs on PBS affiliates throughout the United States. It is also offered through APT Worldwide. Check listings in your area.

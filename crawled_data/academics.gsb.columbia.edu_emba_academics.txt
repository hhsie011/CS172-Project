You want to grow in your career, but you have a full slate of responsibilities. The Columbia Executive MBA understands your realities. It prepares you to accelerate your career potential, bring game-changing ideas forward, and prepare for a shifting global economy — all while staying in your current job. Here’s how: Classes are held on weekends or in block-week formats. Select from options that suit your lifestyle. Our comprehensive core curriculum builds a foundation for success in any business. Electives allow you to specialize or explore new arenas. Renowned full-time professors inspire you through academic excellence. Impressive adjuncts bridge theory with real-time business practice. A community awaits you — both in class and among the 40,000-plus supportive alumni who will serve as a network for years to come. The Executive MBA is the same MBA that full-time students earn. Everyone follows the exact same curriculum and spends an equal number of hours in class. If not now, when? The Executive MBA was created for high-achievers like you. Don’t defer your dream. Go forward.

Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more Job Road counselors work alongside students to develop their career goals through job readiness workshops and one-on-one counseling appointments. Our counselors teach students how to create and tailor their resumes and cover letters to specific job opportunities, conduct themselves in interviews, and search the internet for job opportunities. Job Road counselors at Community Impact are committed to not only assisting you with the practical aspects of career development, but are invested in your long-term success.

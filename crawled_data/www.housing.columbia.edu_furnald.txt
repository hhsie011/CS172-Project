It houses mostly First-Year students and Sophomores in corridor-style singles and doubles. Key Features: Close proximity to Ferris Booth Commons, Lerner Hall, and Butler Library Beautiful views of campus Large 10th floor rooms with high ceilings Provides opportunity for First-Years to live with Sophomore students Building Details: Housing Rates: Either First-Year or Upperclass Rates depending on class of student Entrance location: Inside campus near Broadway and 115th Street Number of residential floors: 10 Single/Double rooms within suites: 190 singles 28 doubles Flooring: Hardwood Air Conditioning: Yes Building Amenities: Bathroom Style: Shared, gendered men's and women's bathrooms on floors 2-10 Single-use, gender-inclusive bathrooms on first floor Laundry: Free laundry facility in basement Kitchen: A shared kitchen is available on each floor Lounge: A building lounge is available on the first floor A kitchen lounge equipped with cable TV service is available on each floor Bike Storage: Available in the basement Computer Lab & Print Station: Available on the 1st floor Cleaning Responsibility & Schedule: Bathrooms: Daily, Monday - Friday, by Facilities Kitchens: Weekly by Facilities Trash & Recycling: Residents are responsible for taking their trash and recycling to designated building area Additional Photos: Image Carousel with 2 slides A carousel is a rotating set of images.

Payment for such space will be in accordance with then-current University protocols. Columbia University space is generally awarded on a first-come, first-served basis, with priority given to Columbia and student activities, followed by local community activities. Eligibility Local community-based organizations, local artists and cultural organizations not affiliated with Columbia University may be eligible for access to the University's indoor and outdoor spaces for programming needs.

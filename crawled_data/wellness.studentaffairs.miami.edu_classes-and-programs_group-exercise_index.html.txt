HOW-TO: Register for classes on the Wellness Center portal HOW-TO: Register for classes on the UM Wellness App COVID-19 Guidelines The Herbert Wellness Center is currently offering physically distanced indoor group exercise classes and virtual group exercise classes. In accordance with University guidelines, all group exercise classes are limited to 20 participants until further notice. For everyone's safety, participants must: Wear a mask at all times; no exceptions Pre-register for the class Wipe down equipment after class and place it in the cleaning area Stay within their zone during the class To make the most of your experience: Bring a water bottle and a towel Arrive at least 10 minutes before class to get your equipment and to keep your spot Important Reminders: Maximum of 20-30 people per class (Studio Cycling is limited to 15 bikes) Standbys are allowed (5 minutes before scheduled start time) Only 3 participants are allowed in the equipment closet at one time No entry once the class begins Please note, membership must be current at the time of registration to enroll in classes during the registration period and to receive member rates. Registration for classes is only available during published registration dates. Questions? Stop by the Sales Office, Room 224 Call 305-284-5433 E-mail wellnesscenter@miami.edu Class Policies Only current, fees-paid University of Miami students or Herbert Wellness Center members and/or their official guests are eligible to participate in free group exercise classes. Patrons must be at least 13 years of age to participate in any classes offered at the Center. Participants should line up outside the designated entry door. Under no circumstances should members enter the aerobics room until instructed by the door monitor or instructor. Class sessions are limited to a maximum of 20 participants per class (Studio Cycling is limited to 15 bikes). Entry is on a first come, first served basis. After 20 participants have entered, the class will be closed. For safety reasons, participants may only enter an open class until 10 minutes after class start time. After 10 minutes the class is closed.

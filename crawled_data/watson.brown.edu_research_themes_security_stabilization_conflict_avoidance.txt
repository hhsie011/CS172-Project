Current research includes studies on multilateral peacekeeping and security stabilization efforts in Colombia, Liberia and Cote d’Ivoire; multilateral policing and drug control; multilateral nuclear non-proliferation regimes; the management of cyber threats; and new frameworks for understanding the costs of war.

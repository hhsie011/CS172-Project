A photo is the first thing we see on a page. It can draw us in and fuel our imagination. Guidelines Put the subject in a relevant context and environment Capture moments of real emotion: tenacity, spirit, challenge and achievement Capture action and energy but keep the photo casual in attitude Avoid posing subjects. Let them move around, perform their job and get comfortable.

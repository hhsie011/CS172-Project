Alumni of the University of Tulsa are allowed individual access to the building Monday - Friday 8 a.m. - 5 p.m., provided the library is open. University Alumni Page Maps & Directions Library Hours FAQ Library Associate Memberships Alumni are welcome to physically visit McFarlin Library and utilize our collection in the library free of charge. Circulation privileges are available to Alumni for a discounted rate of $60 a year. Paid circulation privileges for TU alumni include: The ability check out up to 10 items at one time. See our FAQ for information regarding due dates, renewing books and fines. If you would prefer to have your items pulled and held for you at the desk, then requests to checkout library materials can be made online at https://libraries.utulsa.edu/BookRetrieval. Paid circulation privileges for TU alumni do not include: Media items Interlibrary loan Access to the databases To apply for the TU alumni circulation privileges, please email terri-wickliffe@utulsa.edu.

ACL operates state-of-the-art facilities for this purpose, including a compact range and multiple free-space focused-beam systems. ACL has a history of pioneering electromagnetic measurement techniques and supplying the nation’s labs with innovative characterization tools. ACL is a trusted source of measurements in support of customer R&D and QA. In addition, ACL researchers regularly design, deliver, and provide training for custom measurement fixtures for government and contractor laboratories. Have a question?

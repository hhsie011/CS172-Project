Home / News Article Imported Celebrating the Class of 2022 May 23, 2022 - 11:00am | Tulane Today today@tulane.edu On Saturday, May 21, Tulane University celebrated the Class of 2022 at its Unified Commencement ceremony in Yulman Stadium. On Friday before the ceremony, Wave Goodbye took over the Berger Family Lawn. The annual party is for graduating students and their guests and features local music and food.

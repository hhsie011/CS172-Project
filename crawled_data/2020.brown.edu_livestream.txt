Class of 2020 Livestream Watch Brown's 254th Commencement live, May 28 and 29. Saturday, May 28 Back to Top The Class of 2020 Commencement Ceremony Livestream begins 9:45 a.m. EDT Members of the undergraduate Class of 2020 will assemble at the First Baptist Church in America for a unique ceremony complete with time-honored traditions of Commencement. President Christina H. Paxson will preside over the ceremony, including orations given by members of the Class of 2020, student performances and more. Watch the Class of 2020 Commencement Ceremony The Graduate School Ceremony Livestream begins 9:45 a.m. EDT Graduate students who completed in October 2019, February 2020 and May 2020 are welcomed back to campus for a ceremony that will include many of the time-honored traditions of Commencement. A procession will precede the ceremony, which will be presided over by leadership from the Graduate School and School of Professional Studies. Watch the Graduate School Ceremony Sunday, May 29 Back to Top The Warren Alpert Medical School Ceremony Livestream begins 10:15 a.m. EDT Members of the Warren Alpert Medical School Class of 2022 join members of the Class of 2020, returning to campus to celebrate their Commencement, in a combined ceremony at First Unitarian Church. The program is led by Dean of Medicine and Biological Sciences Mukesh K. Jain and includes an invocation, welcome, student speakers, music, the reading of the Physician’s Oath, the awarding of degrees and a benediction.

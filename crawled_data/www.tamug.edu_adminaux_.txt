In addition to Human Resources, Campus Police and the Bookstore, the division also manages the outsourced contracts for exclusive beverage and snack vending, campus dining, building maintenance, groundskeeping and custodial services. The campus master plan provides guidance for campus facility and infrastructure development and serves as a road map as the campus addresses new academic and administrative space needs. The last master plan was completed in 2009 and was recently updated in 2013. The updated master plan provides insightful direction as the campus moves to grow to 3000 students and beyond.

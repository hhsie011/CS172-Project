My experience and area of expertise include marine geology, paleoclimate research, isotope geochemistry and molecular analysis of organic matter. Fields of Interest My scientific interests are in the field of global carbon cycling, climate-carbon couplings and paleoceanography, with specific focus on the Arctic region.

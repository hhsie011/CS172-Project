Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index State Employee Charitable Campaign (SECC) Menu Search TTU SECC State Employee Charitable Campaign The State Employee Charitable Campaign (SECC) is a statewide annual giving campaign that Texas Tech, Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center and the Texas Tech University System participate in each year. Overall, the Texas Tech University System raised more than $789,000 that helped to change many lives, shape the future and make a difference in our community. A huge thank you goes out to all of the coordinators that made this possible! DONATE NOW Quick Facts New employees can make donations or pledge through payroll deduction during the first 30 days of hire. Send an e-mail to secc@ttu.edu to find out how to donate. Questions Regarding SECC and FLSA Find answers to your questions about how payroll deduction will be affected with the new FLSA guidelines by visiting our FAQ page.

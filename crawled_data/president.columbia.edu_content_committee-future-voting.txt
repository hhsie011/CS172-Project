On September 6, 2018, the Committee released its findings in a report called Securing the Vote: Protecting American Democracy. Bollinger on "Securing the Vote: Protecting American Democracy" Read the full report, "Securing the Vote" “We need to take collective action to strengthen our voting systems and safeguard our democracy. In addition, the nation’s leaders need to speak candidly and apolitically about threats to election systems. The American people must have confidence that their leaders place the larger interests of democracy above all else.” Lee C.

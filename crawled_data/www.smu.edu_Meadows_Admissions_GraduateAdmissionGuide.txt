Graduate Scholarships & Financial Aid Meadows School of the Arts provides a limited number of tuition scholarships and/or graduate assistantships to new graduate students who have been reviewed (through audition, portfolio review and/or interview) by the Meadows faculty and the administration and demonstrate the highest level of achievement in their academic and artistic careers. Scholarships & Financial Aid Graduate Q&A Do I Have To Apply To SMU and Meadows? No. At this time, the graduate application to the University is only through Meadows.

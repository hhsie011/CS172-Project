The Brown Center in Bologna, is located in the heart of city. After an intensive one-month orientation, students attend the University of Bologna (UniBo), the oldest in Europe, whose famous alumni include Dante, Petrarch, Erasmus, and Copernicus. Students live in single rooms in Brown-managed apartments, with peers from the program and Italian students. All apartments are located in beautiful and convenient areas of town. The program includes guided tours of the city as well as excursions to other historic cities of the Emilia-Romagna region.

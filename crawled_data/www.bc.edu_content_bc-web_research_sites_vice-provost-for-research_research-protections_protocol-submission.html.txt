Contact Postdoctoral Affairs ResearchMatch Protocol Submission IRB applications, including initial applications, amendments and continuing reviews, are accepted electronically via CyberIRB. CyberIRB is an online IRB submission system that researchers can access from any web browser and log-in with their BC credentials. CyberIRB How do I get help and information on the status of my application? You can sign into CyberIRB to check the status of your IRB application. You can also call ORP at 617-552-4778 or email us. ORP staff will be happy to help you navigate your way through the IRB process. If you are experiencing technical difficutlies, please contact the Help Desk for assistance with CyberIRB.

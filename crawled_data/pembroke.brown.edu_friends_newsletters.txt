The Friends of the Pembroke Center stay in touch via the Friends of the Pembroke Center newsletter, published three times a year, and a monthly e-newsletter. These publications trace the impact of the Friends in articles about Pembroke Center research, scholarship and programming; archival development; pathbreaking student research; and features on the work and experiences of Brown alumnae/i, parents and friends. Read the Friends of the Pembroke Center Newsletter Published in the Fall/Winter, Spring, and Summer, the Friends of the Pembroke Center newsletter dives into Pembroke Center activities, research, teaching, and news.

Requirements Related to Racism and Sexism Trainings Provost's Office Communications Spring 2022 Message for Instructors Index Office of the Associate Provost for Academic Programs Mission The Office of the Associate Provost for Academic Programs serves to promote Iowa State University’s land-grant mission by supporting equitable student participation in transformative educational experiences that deepen students’ ability to think critically, communicate effectively, and make positive contributions to society. We accomplish this by collaborating with internal and external partners to empower our students to grow and thrive in an increasing technological, complex, and global society. Priorities AY21-22 Undergraduate Programs Through partnerships with colleges, central units, and industry leaders identify and help implement contemporary undergraduate programs that will enhance student learning and attract new student audiences. Expand academic program delivery to include a selected list of online credentialing opportunities. Academic Enhancement Across all demographic groups, promote and support a culture of active student engagement in high-impact educational practices (HIPs). Encourage faculty/staff to collect, review, and use direct assessment data of student learning to enhance the student experience. Integrity of Academic Programs Support department, program, and college reviews of academic programs with a focus on continuous improvement and promoting achievement of learning outcomes across all levels of a students’ academic experience.

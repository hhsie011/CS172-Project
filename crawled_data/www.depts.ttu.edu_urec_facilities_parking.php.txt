Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Recreation Menu Search TTU University Recreation BasketBall Gameday Parking We all want to cheer on our Red Raider men and women as they get on the court for the 2021-22 Basketball season. But with the United Supermarkets Arena being right next door, parking for University Recreation will be a little tighter on gamedays. Below are the current parking plans for Men's Basketball gamedays and Women's basketball gamedays. Parking Information The drop down menu below gives the parking information for both Men's and Women's gamedays. MEN'S BB PARKINGWOMEN'S BB PARKING Men's Basketball Parking University Parking will start to control parking lots around the United Supermarkets Arena starting approximately 3.5 hours before tip-off of each game. Here are some tips for parking during men's games: The 2 hour Rec Center parking lot will not be available for anyone that does not have a parking credential. The parking lot control will be from 3.5 hours before tip-off until 30 minutes after tip-off. Below is a picture of the parking lot designated for University Recreation parking. After the game: Games typically last for 2 hours and after the game ends traffic flow (egress) will be both sides of Main street all headed east towards Student Health/Flint Avenue. University Recreation parking during Men's basketball Access to the lot will be from Flint Avenue. Please be prepared to talk with the lot attendant to let them know that you are working out at the Rec or attending a program in our facility. Women's Basketball Parking The 2 hour Rec Center parking lot will be used for Rec Center usage during Women's games.. The parking lot control will be from 3.5 hours before tip-off until 30 minutes after tip-off for all other lots. Need Help? For any inquiries regrading parking during basketball gamedays, please call University Recreation at, 806.742.3351 for more information.

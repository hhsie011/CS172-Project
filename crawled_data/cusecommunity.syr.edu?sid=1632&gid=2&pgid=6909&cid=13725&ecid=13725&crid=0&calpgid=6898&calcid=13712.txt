parents, students and friends of Syracuse University. We offer opportunities to connect and network with other Orange faithful and are looking for volunteers to assist us in our efforts. This club encompasses a vast area and we try to hold events in FFl, Miami and WPB. Email Steven Wallace L’99 if you are willing to volunteer as a contact in FLL or Miami, have suggestions for upcoming activities or events, or if you’re interested in joining the club’s leadership team. Also be sure to connect with us on Facebook. GO ORANGE!

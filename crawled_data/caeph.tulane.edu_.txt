CENTER FOR APPLIED ENVIRONMENTAL PUBLIC HEALTH (CAEPH) Center for Applied Environmental Public Health The Center for Applied Environmental Public Health (CAEPH) translates applied research to practice through education and training for Environmental Health and Public Health professionals using distance education. CAEPH has pioneered quality distance learning since the first class was delivered in January, 1994. Since then, our distance learning programs have grown with new technology, enhanced degree offerings in the occupational health and safety areas and disaster managers, added graduate certificates and been a leader in high quality distance education.

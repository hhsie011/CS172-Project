Corlette Staff Sergeant Linnea F. Corlette is an Administrative Specialist, Air Education and Training Command, University of Cincinnati AFROTC Detachment 665, Cincinnati, Ohio. In this capacity Sergeant Corlette is responsible for managing cadet pay, government purchasing, safety, and the accountability and security of computer equipment. Sergeant Corlette enlisted in the United States Air Force in 2015 as an Administrator. Sergeant Corlette has performed duty at both Squadron, Wing, and Numbered Air Force level assignments to include the Command Support Staff of the 355th Wing, and 55th Rescue Squadron. Sergeant Corlette also acted as the Command Executive Support for Headquarters 12th Air Force. Before her current assignment, she served within the Executive Support Division for the 12th Air Force, Davis-Monthan AFB, AZ.

To promote an emotionally and psychologically healthy workplace, everyone needs to recognize signs a colleague is struggling. It’s also important to know how to start a conversation to show concern, and be able to guide someone to support, counseling or immediate intervention. This webinar will help you learn to recognize signs of distress and possible suicidality, and will teach you steps to guiding someone to professional help, including assistance from the EAP (you can check out the online resources and/or call 888-673-1153 to speak with a representative).

Central credit evaluation requires an official transcript to be sent to the University for review. If your credits were not earned at a regionally accredited instituion, please check with your home college to see if the credits will be accepted for transfer through another type of advanced standing credit. Regional Accreditation Commissions Central credit evaluation requires an official transcript to be sent to the University of Cincinnati from a regionally accredited institution.

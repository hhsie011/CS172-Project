Student Employment Teaching and graduate research assistantships provide valuable experience and financial support for graduate students. But employment in a non-academic role at the university or in the community is also an option for many graduate students. Read More Graduate Fellowships The Graduate School facilitates a number of university-affiliated fellowships, in addition to coordinating select extramural fellowships. Read More Financial Aid For students who need additional financial support beyond fellowships and student employment, financial aid is available. Graduate students may apply for federal, state and institutional financial aid programs. Read More Awards and Other Funds In addition to funding designed to support students in paying the university’s cost of attendance, the Graduate School offers a number of awards to support prospective and current students, faculty and staff.

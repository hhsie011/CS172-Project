The purpose of this site is to provide faculty members, staff members, and students of Texas Tech University with information about our institution's Core and Multicultural Curriculum. Texas Tech University offers its undergraduate students a Core Curriculum in line with requirements set forth by the Texas Higher Education Coordinating Board.Texas Tech University also expects its students to complete certain requirements that augment the Core Curriculum. Those are the Multicultural requirement (three credit hours) and the Lab Science requirement (two credit hours). You can find out more about the Lab Science requirement here. To learn more about our institution's Core and Multicultural Curriculum, please click on the Core and Multicultural Curriculum tab on the top navigation menu. Clicking on the Core and Multicultural Curriculum tab will direct you to a set of informational pages related to Core and Multicultural Curriculum courses. Questions about the Core and Multicultural Curriculum should be directed to Interim Vice Provost for Academic Affairs Dr. Darryl James.

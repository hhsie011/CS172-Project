Frost Musician GET STARTED 1 Request Info 2 Visit 3 Apply GET STARTED 1 Request Info 2 Visit 3 Apply Frost students and alumni are ready to bring live music back to your life. Live or virtually. Gigging is their lifeblood, and due to COVID-19, the interruption of live in-person performances has caused financial hardship. Let’s keep the music playing and support these talented artists. You can hire Frost talent to lead in: Performances (Give the gift of music for birthdays, anniversaries, or just because) Production/Arrangement/Composition/Songwriting Private Lessons Clinics (for public and private school music programs) If you’d like to bring music to your home, or as a gift to someone you love, please fill out the gig form below. Live or Virtual Gigs Form Please Note: The Frost School of Music accepts no responsibility for the service provided as a result of this request. All arrangements are between the client and the student/alumnus who responds to the request. If you need additional assistance, please contact the Frost Gig Book manager at frostgigs@miami.edu.

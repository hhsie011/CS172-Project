Columbia Global Centers cordially invite all graduates of 2021 to come to the Beijing Center and Columbia-designated WeWork space in Shanghai, Shenzhen, and Hong Kong to celebrate your accomplishments and share your memories at Columbia with your fellows. During the event, there will be professional portrait photographer and various graduation kits available to you, including gowns and caps, Columbia mascots (Roar Lion), graduation booth props and balloons. We can't wait to see you in Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen, and Hong Kong, and to walk you through the end of academic year and cheer to a new start for your future life. Agenda The event will be broken up into two one-hour sessions to ensure appropriate health and safety distancing requirements. Students can register and attend any one of the two sessions. Session 1: 3:00 - 4:00 PM Session 2: 4:00 - 5:00 PM Reminders Please select one time slot you would like to come when you register. Due to limited capacity, graduates are permitted to take only two non-Columbia student guests.

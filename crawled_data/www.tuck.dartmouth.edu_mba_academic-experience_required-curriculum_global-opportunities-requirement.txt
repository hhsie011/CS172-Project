To help our students develop these crucial capabilities, Tuck offers immersive, experiential courses that take place around the world. During the MBA program, each student must take at least one TuckGO course in a country that is new to them. In past years students have studied throughout the world in countries from Ghana and Vietnam to Australia, Germany, and Chile. Funded by a sustaining gift from the American family foundation of Michaela and Zdenek Bakala T’89, the Bakala TuckGO Program provides valuable support to the entire TuckGO portfolio of courses to help ensure students develop the necessary skills, knowledge, and global mindset to lead across cultures.

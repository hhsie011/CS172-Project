Reunion Giving Celebrated every five years after graduation, reunions are more than just an opportunity to reconnect with old classmates. They are an opportunity to commemorate your Brown experience with your Reunion Class Gift through the Annual Fund. Each reunion class sets ambitious dollar and participation goals to send a powerful message that your class is committed to the ongoing vibrancy of Brown. Find Your Reunion Class In a reunion year? Find out more about reunion giving by contacting your class’s Brown Annual Fund staff liaison.

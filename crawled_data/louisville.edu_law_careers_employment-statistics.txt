Reports Employment information about Brandeis graduates is available in the annual American Bar Association (ABA) Employment Summary Reports. Select a year below to access the ABA information relating to a particular graduating class. Each report contains information for all December, May and August graduates from that year whom the school was able to locate and gather placement information. Employment Summary for 2021 Graduates (most recent data) Information on employment outcomes for the Class of 2021 may not reflect a particular law school’s typical results in this area. Please reference the 3 years of employment outcome data posted on the ABA Required Disclosures webpage of each ABA-Approved Law School or at www.abarequireddisclosures.org.

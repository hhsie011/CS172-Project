The UGC-HM prepares students to navigate the health care system and provides an introduction to health management. Learn how Health Management is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The certificate consists of 12 credit hours, including two required courses — Introduction to Health Administration and Policy, and Health Care Organization — and two additional courses chosen from the areas of leadership, quality improvement, health finance, health economics, human resource management, strategic management, culture and communication. Credits earned as part of this certificate can also be applied to the Bachelor of Science (BS) in health management. View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click on "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

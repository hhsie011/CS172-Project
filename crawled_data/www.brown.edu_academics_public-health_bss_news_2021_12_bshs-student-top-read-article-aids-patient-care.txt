William's article "COVID-19, HIV, and Migrant Workers: The Double Burden of the Two Viruses" was published with co-author Salome Kuchukhidze. The article highlights the risk that COVID-19 poses to HIV prevention and treatment efforts and grapples with the double burden of HIV and COVID-19 on migrant workers, specifically highlighting ways to use the existing HIV infrastructure to provide an effective coronavirus response. In recognition of World AIDS Day, William's paper was highlighted in a selection of top-read papers accessible with free access through December 31, 2021.

Events Past Event Economic Policy and a Green Recovery in Latin America post-COVID: Is a New Consensus Emerging? Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo September 30, 2020 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM Online Event REGISTER HERE Webinar in partnership with CEBRI (Centro Brasileiro de Relações Internacionais). >>> Time: 5pm (NY) | 6pm (Brazil) SPEAKERS: José Antonio Ocampo - Professor of Professional Practice in International and Public Affairs, director of the Economic and Political Development Concentration in the School of International and Public Affairs, Member of the Committee on Global Thought and co-President of the Initiative for Policy Dialogue at Columbia University. He is also the Chair of the Committee for Development Policy, an expert committee of the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) Rogério Studart - Senior fellow of Brazil’s Center of International Relations – CEBRI and a distinguished fellow at the Global Federation of Competitiveness Councils. Previously he was an executive director to the World Bank Group, representing Brazil and eight other nations. , Senior Fellow of the World Resources Institute, currently working on the new climate economy, an initiative to promote policies guided by evidence-based analysis of costs, benefits, and opportunities of the much-needed transitions to low-carbon growth.

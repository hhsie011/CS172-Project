Want to Bring Your Ideas to Life? You have big ideas. Ideas that can change the world. With our Start Something Network at Iowa State, you’ll find support and resources designed to help you nurture those ideas and bring out your inner innovator and entrepreneur. Here’s where you’ll get academic opportunities including project-oriented courses, student incubators, product development assistance, internships with startups, and (so much) more across six of our colleges. From starting your own business to marketing your own product to leading a group of innovative thinkers, Start Something is how you can, well, start anything you want. Opportunities for everyone, no matter the major. Start Something spans six Iowa State colleges so you can tailor your education experience to meet your unique needs and interests.

Home / Support Support Newcomb-Tulane College guides every student through their academic journey - advising you on classes, job seeking skills and best practices for success, providing tutoring and connecting with resources. We ask each student to consider what you want to achieve and help build a plan to achieve those goals. Advising Advising maximizes your potential by sharing resources that empower you to make informed decisions about creating academic plans. Career Services Career Services teaches you how to explore career options and achieve goals that fit with you values, interests, personality, and skills. Success Coaching Success Coaches assist you by providing questions, observations, feedback, encouragement, support, and accountability. Academic Equity CAE strives to provide an equitable academic environment on campus by fostering social equality and culturally-conscious learning.

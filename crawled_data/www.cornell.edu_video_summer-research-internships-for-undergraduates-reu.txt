Cornell's Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) program is designed to introduce eligible undergraduates to leading scholars at the nation's top research universities. Participating undergraduates produce graduate level research with faculty guidance over an eight- to- ten week period in the summer. Funding is often provided.

We believe that diversity fuels excellence, and that the best ideas are discovered when people—from different backgrounds, who have lived different realities, who have overcome different challenges—work together to expand their authentic perspectives. Our Mission 2022 Diversity and Inclusion Summit: Cultivating a Culture of Inclusion and Belonging Wednesday, May 25, 2022, 9:00 am - 4:30 pm, Gasson Hall The Seventh Annual Diversity and Inclusion Summit, Cultivating a Culture of Inclusion and Belonging, invites faculty and staff to participate in formative opportunities enhancing the quality of human interaction. The summit programs will equip you with the knowledge and skill sets necessary for cultivating a culture of inclusion and belonging, moving us toward inclusive excellence.

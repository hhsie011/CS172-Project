Fellows’ research projects and academic interests reflect the areas toward which many of the world’s major decision-makers have gravitated, as well as those that have the potential to open new channels of debate. Learn More Yale Visiting International Students Program The Yale Visiting International Student Program (Y-VISP) invites elite students from Y-VISP partner institutions to study at Yale University. Y-VISP students live in the residential colleges with Yale College students, take classes alongside Yale students, and participate in a variety of extracurricular programs and events that are open to the Yale community.

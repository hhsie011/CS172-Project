This is expressed in a design program that is idealistic and pragmatic, academic and practical, theoretical and actual. U-SoA trains visionary practitioners who pursue architecture as a combination of civic art and building science. Philosophy & Mission To prepare students for professional leadership and lifelong learning in architecture, urbanism, and related fields. History of the School U-SoA first opened its doors in 1983. Community Outreach U-SoA's outreach program is the cornerstone of U-SoA’s curriculum with days dedicated to service.

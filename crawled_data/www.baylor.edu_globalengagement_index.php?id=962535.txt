As the newest member of the team, he works closely with our F1 international students from all over the world advising students on policies and procedures to keep them informed on current regulations to keep them in status here at Baylor. He is coming up on 5 years of experience in international education. He uses his love of sports, comics, movies and games to connect with students to help them feel welcomed at Baylor.

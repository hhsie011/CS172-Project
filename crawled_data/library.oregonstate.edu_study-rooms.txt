When they reopen June 20, no resevations will be required. For summer term, the doors will be unlocked and 5th floor rooms will be available first come, first serve. An ONID account is required to reserve a study room. Rooms can be reserved for a maximum of 4 hours, based on availability. Rooms are not renewable. Patrons must check in at the Circulation Desk to get a key card for their reserved room and are responsible for returning the key card to the Circulation Desk at the end of their reserved time. Advance reservations are limited to one per person per day, and may be made up to 30 days in advance. Advance reservations are not required, but are strongly recommended. If the key card for a reserved room is not picked up within 15 minutes of the reservation’s start time, the reservation will be canceled. Immediate-use reservations can be made at the Circulation Desk if rooms are available. Immediate-use reservations do not count against the daily limit for advance reservations. See more on our study room and reservation policies. Room Attributes All rooms have white boards, power outlets, wifi access, tables and chairs.

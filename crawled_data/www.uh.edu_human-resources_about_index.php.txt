Our dedicated faculty and staff make the University of Houston a great place to work, where our research and our people’s diverse cultural backgrounds provide a unique experience for our students, employees, and our community. Our office, Human Resources, is committed to offer services to all our faculty and staff, with an ultimate goal in mind, enhancing our students’ success. I invite you to check our employment opportunities, our employee benefits, and our employee resources. We in Human Resources firmly believe our contribution to the mission and vision of the University of Houston is essential to achieve The University of Houston’s goals. As a result, we take seriously our institution’s strategic goals and always strive for excellence in delivering quality human resources services.

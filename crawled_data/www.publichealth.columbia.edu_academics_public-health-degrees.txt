Public Health Degrees Master of Public Health The Mailman School of Public Health's breakthrough MPH curriculum set a new standard for public health education. Master of Science The MS curriculum focuses on specialized knowledge with an emphasis on interdisciplinary education. Doctoral Programs Advancing scientific research and practice alongside renowned researchers and public health thought leaders. Master of Health Administration The MHA integrates coursework in organizational leadership and management, health policy, and public health and health systems. Executive and Professional Education Realize all the benefits of a full degree program while limiting time away from work. Dual Degrees Prepare for a multidisciplinary public health career collaborating with social workers, doctors, nurses, and more.

Performance Evaluation Performance Evaluation Period The performance evaluation period for employees follows the university’s calendar year from Jan 1 to Dec 31 each year. The performance evaluations are due to HR by March 15. *All regular staff, administrators and faculty follow the same performance evaluation timeline. Performance Evaluation Training Register for upcoming performance evaluation training sessions through the Employee Success Center or watch a previous recording below. The Professional Development Department will be providing training to help managers and employees: Understand how the performance evaluation works Understand their role in the evaluation Understand how to perform while working remotely Understand how to evaluate remote work Learn how to coach and be coached/evaluated How to work towards a common goal of team success Know the guidelines/criteria of performance evaluation If you are unable to attend the trainings, you can watch a previous training below: Ongoing Evaluation It is very important that managers and supervisors hold informal meetings about three times year (e.g. April, August, December) with employees to provide ongoing feedback on what they are doing well and where they may need development. Building those conversations in as a standard part of the management relationship will help ensure no surprises each year and should lead to stronger employee performance throughout the year.

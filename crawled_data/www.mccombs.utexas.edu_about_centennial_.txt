WE BOAST A LEGACY AS BIG AS TEXAS. For 100 years, we’ve built up leaders as a premier business school at a world-class public research university. McCombs is home to a diverse community of learners, scholars, and trailblazers at the forefront of transformation in business. Our pioneering faculty, students and over 100,000 alumni continue to shape industries and reimagine the world. YOU HOLD A CHERISHED PLACE AT MCCOMBS. The core purpose of the university is to transform lives for the benefit of society. We are a leading business school because of each of you. As treasured members of the McCombs community, you are invited to join us in celebrating our shared journey. Step into the next century of connection, innovation, and impact by taking part in our year-long celebration.

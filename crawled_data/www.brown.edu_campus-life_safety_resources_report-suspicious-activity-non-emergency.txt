An officer will subsequently be dispatched to the area to investigate the matter. When reporting suspicious activity at Brown, we encourage you to focus on the behavior of a person that appears to be suspicious. The behavior should demonstrate acts that raise your suspicion and cause concern for your safety, or the safety of others. Always be sure to head to a safe location before contacting DPS with your report. In case of an active emergency, e.g. witnessing an assault in progress, ensure that you are safe then call the Brown Emergency number: 401-863-4111.

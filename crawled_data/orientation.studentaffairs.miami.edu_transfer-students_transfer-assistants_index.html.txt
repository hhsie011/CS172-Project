ASSISTANT Transfer Assistants (TAs) are dedicated student leaders who serve as mentors and a resource to incoming transfer students. Transfer Assistants work with the Department of Orientation and Commuter Student Involvement to plan, promote, and implement initiatives specifically focused on transfer students. The purpose of Transfer Assistant initiatives are to aid incoming transfers in their transition to the University of Miami.

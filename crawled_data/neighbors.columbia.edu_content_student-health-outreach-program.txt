Volunteers serve in community-based organizations, St Mary's Church on W126th St, BCI on Broadway and 114th St. SHOUT partners with two active non-profits, SeedCo and Children’s Defense Fund for training and enrollment activities. Volunteer commitment of 3 hours/week, various days, off-campus.

Contact Sharon Worrell for assistance at sworrel@okstate.edu or 405-744-5630. Harbor Career Services Harbor is a member network that helps veterinary professionals and students take ownership of their careers and well-being. Students are invited to create a free profile, connect with classmates and colleagues, engage with mentors, explore career opportunities (including externships and internships) and utilize groups! Connect with Harbor Post a DVM Position We are excited about your interest in hiring our students. Our skilled students will impress you from day one thanks to an education balanced with theory, research and hands-on training with all the major animal groups. No matter your specialty, you will find the perfect candidate here at the College of Veterinary Medicine for your position. Post your job for free with OSU CVM Career Services (sworrel@okstate.edu) or HireOSUGrads to find OSU College of Veterinary Medicine students. For a broader reach, you can also post your open position on the Oklahoma Veterinary Medical Association's Career Center for Employers or the American Veterinary Medical Association's Career Center for Employers. You can also post a job announcement to be displayed during the OSU CVM Fall Conference or Summer Seminar. Please note, there is a fee to post through the OVMA and AVMA Career Centers or to submit an ad for Fall Conference/Summer Seminar.

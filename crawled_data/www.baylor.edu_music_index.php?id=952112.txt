Choirs Fall 2022 Choir Audition Information: Incoming Freshmen / New to Baylor Choirs (Concert Choir, Men's Choir, Women's Choir): Fall 2022 auditions will take place in person at Campus Connections on Day One of Orientation sessions in June and July (3:15-4:05 in the Foster Business Building). Additional auditions will take place during Welcome Week prior to the start of the Fall semester or by appointment throughout the summer. The audition will consist of: Vocalization/Range check Tonal Memory/Sightreading Sing an unaccompanied hymn or patriotic song - one verse or verse/chorus. (Possible examples are Amazing Grace, Be Thou My Vision, My Country ‘Tis of Thee, or America the Beautiful) If you have any questions about the audition process or if you would like to schedule an audition, please contact Erin Schmidt (Erin_Schmidt1@baylor.edu) A Cappella Choir, Chamber Singers (sophomores → graduate students, including transfers): 2022-2023 Auditions for A Cappella Choir and Chamber Singers have already taken place. If you have interest in these ensembles, please directly contact individual faculty directors. Bella Voce, VirtuOSO (sophomores → graduate students, including transfers): Fall 2022 Auditions for Bella Voce and VirtuOSO have already taken place. Additional auditions may take place during Welcome Week, just prior to the start of the fall semester. Please contact individual directors for questions regarding these ensembles. Early Music The Early Music Ensembles are open to all members of the student body by audition. No previous experience in early repertoire is required. Contact Dr. Jann Cosart for details Handbells No audition required. Jazz Jazz Audition Information and Excerpts* * updated July 26, 2021 New Music N/A Golden Wave Marching Band Details on the Golden Wave Marching Band site Opera Must enroll in Opera Workshop (MUEN 1104) to be considered for casting Those who wish to volunteer to work backstage are not required to register for the class, but must be available during the scheduled class time and rehearsals. Those who want to sing in the opera chorus do not need to audition; just register for the course. Without an audition you will not be considered for named roles. this option only applies when there is a chorus in the upcoming opera. Non-graduating students must audition in the spring for the following year’s productions. Auditions for spring performances are held at the end of fall semester for anyone who has not auditioned previously. Incoming Graduate Students: Your graduate audition can count for your opera audition as well, so long as the audition happens prior to casting. OPERA AUDITIONS: Singers will present two selections: one aria or art song in a foreign language one in English (The English selection can be an aria, art song or musical theater) A pianist will be provided at the audition the student must provide clean, 3-hole punched, copies of their music in a 3 ring binder for the pianist There is always a potential for callbacks Contact the Director of Opera if you have any questions. Orchestra and Band Contact Russell Wiley if you have any questions. Instrumental Audition Information and Excerpts* * updated July 21, 2021 Baylor Percussion Group Contact Dr. Todd Meehan.

Payment in full or enrollment in a Summer Payment Plan is needed on or before the due date. Payment due dates and payment plan installment due dates are listed below for each term within the 2022-23 academic year, beginning with the Fall 2022 semester. Beginning June 1, 2022, billing dates will be on the first business day of each month and the due dates will always be on the last day of the month. Please refer to due dates specific to your classification or program as indicated in the E-Bill System or in notifications from Student Accounts. If the payment amount needed is not submitted each month by the last day of the month, a late payment fee of $25 will be added to your bill.

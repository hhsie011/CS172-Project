Allison Society Dean's Council In This Section You are here Home Alumni Give Back Giving Programs We recognize our donors by offering membership in the following groups. Our gratitude to our many donors is matched only by the tremendous impact they are having on our mission. Littauer Society The Littauer Society recognizes generous individuals who give $1,000 or more to the HKS Fund each fiscal year (July 1 to June 30). Explore the Littauer Society Loyalty Society The Loyalty Society recognizes individuals who contribute to the HKS Fund through a gift of any amount each fiscal year, beginning with their second annual consecutive gifts. Explore the Loyalty Society Graduating Class Campaign The Graduating Class Campaign serves to inspire students to support Harvard Kennedy School and the next generation of students. Explore Your Class Gift Graham T. Allison Society The Graham T. Allison Society recognizes individuals who contribute to Harvard Kennedy School through planned gifts.

About the STEAM Outreach Program The role and impact of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics) on our lives is a matter of growing importance. In keeping with Dartmouth’s mission, Outreach and Engagement serves as a point of contact for partners outside our institution whose interests include STEAM awareness, STEAM education and STEAM literacy.

Calendar Fall 2022 Enrollment Access Enrollment for the Fall 2022 term begins on Monday, March 28. Enrollment start date and time: Students can log into MyPack Portal to see when they will be able to start enrolling for the next term, and to make sure that they have no “Holds,” which prevent enrollment. Students should arrange to meet with their advisor prior to their enrollment start date.

The 3-hour ‘Web-based Advanced COVID-19 Clinical Management Course’ would help the doctors at any corners of the country with the training they need before attending a Covid-19 patient. His leadership and unwavering dedication to improving the lives of disadvantaged and displaced in Bangladesh through health and education is an inspiration.

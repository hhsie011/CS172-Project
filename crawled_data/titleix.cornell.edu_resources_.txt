Individuals who believe that they have experienced gender-based harassment, sexual harassment, sexual assault, domestic and dating violence, stalking, sexual exploitation, or other forms of sexual misconduct should seek immediate assistance. Resources designated as “confidential” will not report any information to the Office of the Title IX Coordinator. These conversations are kept strictly confidential and, except in extreme, rare circumstances, will not be shared without explicit permission. Cornell is a caring community. For resources and information on Cornell’s Sexual Harassment and Assault – Response and Education (“SHARE”), please see the SHARE website.

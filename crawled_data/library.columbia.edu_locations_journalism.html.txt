The Journalism Library's on-site print holdings feature a small collection of reference books, circulating books, and current periodicals, as well as the most recent five years of Master's projects completed by graduates of the School. Many additional holdings part of the Journalism Library’s collection are located at an offsite facility, which allows for rapid requests and delivery. For access to the Journalism Library for non-journalism affiliates, please go to the Journalism Admissions office, Room 203 in the lobby of Pulitzer Hall from 10-5, M-F.

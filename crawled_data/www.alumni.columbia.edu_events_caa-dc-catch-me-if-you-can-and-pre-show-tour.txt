See how charming, young con man Frank Abagnale Jr. posed as an airline pilot, a lawyer, and a doctor—and then escaped police custody, all before he turned 22. Arena Stage will present a never-before-seen version of this classic that includes two songs not heard in the work's 2011 Broadway run. The tour will include a talk about the history and architecture of the gorgeous Mead Center for American Theater – the largest performing arts complex in DC after the Kennedy Center. For the play, we have secured a block of discounted seats in rows C through E. Sales will end at 12 noon on March 15 so reserve your tickets today!

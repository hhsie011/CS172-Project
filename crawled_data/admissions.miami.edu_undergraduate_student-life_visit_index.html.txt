Apply Connect with us Join us on campus for an information session delivered by an admission counselor who will review application deadlines, financial aid, student life, and all the opportunities that come with studying in Miami. After the presentation, a current student will lead you on a guided tour of our Coral Gables campus. Have your questions ready! Campus Visit Virtual Visit Our virtual visit options give you a variety of ways to get to know the U, get answers to your questions, and conveniently connect with us from wherever you are. Self-Guided Walking Tour If you want to explore on your own, stop by the Office of Undergraduate Admission to pick up our campus map and follow our suggested path. Getting Here Find your way around! Our Coral Gables campus offers a sense of community with the added benefit of being located in a major metropolitan city. Meet Your Guides Meet the members of the President's 100 organization (affectionately known as 'P100s'), who lead our campus tours, host events, and more! P100s A city as multifaceted as you Think young, high energy, diverse, and multilingual. From art to nature, from Coral Gables to Little Havana, Miami is full of opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Pandemic Security Project Kickoff The Global Health Initiative kicks off their 18-month project seeking answers to some of the chronic issues of global helath care brought to light by COVID. Faculty, students, and partners seek to analyze the pandemic by understanding the challenges in developing and providing access to quality health care and vaccines as part of the global response.

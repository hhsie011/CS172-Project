Students Assistant Dean for Recruiting and Diversity Jane Seibel Guarini Diversity Commitment and Plan Share Guarini Diversity Initiatives Guarini Diversity Initiatives The Guarini School of Graduate and Advanced Studies supports a broad range of diversity-related progams and initatives, many of which are accessible through this website. You may navigate links on the DIVERSITY tab above, or you may view a comprehensive but not exhaustive list in PDF format HERE. Guarini Diversity Committee Video of Guarini Diversity Intiatives -working Read and keep up with the current Guarini Diversity Goals in our slide deck. We will be updating this periodically online and invite those interested to participate in the Guarini Diversity Committee meetings, details of the times and dates are below. The Diversity Committee is open to all members of the Guarini School community. DIVERSITY COMMITTEE MEETING SCHEDULE All meetings are at 4pm unless otherwise stated. The Committee is meeting virtually until furtheer notice and a zoom link will be sent out to all registrants before the meeting. To register, please email Jane Seibel, assistant dean for recruitment and diversity. May 5, 2022 is postponed. Please check back for updates. Black Hair Care Guarini works with OPAL and other campus partners to bring a stylist specializing in curly, wavy, and coily hair types, to a local salon. Guarini Diversity Award The Guarini Diversity Award is designed to recognize outstanding leadership and determined efforts by graduate students in addressing and raising awareness about issues of equity, diversity, and inclusion across campus and in our community. Learn more HERE. Guarini Diversity Fellowship The Guarini Diversity Fellowship provides the opportunity to work in partnership with the Assistant Dean, Recruitment & Diversity to fulfill Guarini's commitment to building a supportive and inclusive community where diverse students are supported in their professional, social, and intellectual goals and pursuits. ASURE Dartmouth's Academic Summer Undergraduate Research Experience provides summer research experience to undergraduates not enrolled at Dartmouth. If you are interested in applying, or know someone who does, please visit our ASURE program page.

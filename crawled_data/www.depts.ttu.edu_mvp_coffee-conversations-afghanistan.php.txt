Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Military & Veterans Programs Menu Search TTU Military & Veterans Programs Coffee & Conversations: Afghanistan When: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Tuesdays 8/31, 9/7, 9/14, 9/21, and 9/28 Face-to-Face: TTU Museum - Memorial Room | 3301 4th St. | Lubbock, TX 79415 Virtual: https://ttuhsc.zoom.us/j/99139314392 The Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center Veterans Resource Center (VRC) and Texas Tech University Military and Veterans Programs (MVP) will be hosting a series of conversations on the evolving situation in Afghanistan and how it may affect veterans of all eras and their families. Feel free to bring your lunch; coffee and bottled water will be available.

Engagement and Outreach Research ready1 Online Learning Virginia Tech offers a variety of online or blended programs (graduate degrees and graduate certificates) to help you achieve your educational goals. With online learning, you can advance your career through education, enjoy flexible scheduling and smaller classes taught by faculty experts, and learn in a setting that utilizes the latest technology to provide you with the support and resources to be successful. Virginia Tech has participated in the National Council for State Authorization Reciprocity Agreements since January 2015. The university affirms its commitment to ensure that courses and programs offered through this initiative provide high-quality opportunities that can help students reach their goals. The university also welcomes students of the Southern Regional Education Board's Electronic Campus.

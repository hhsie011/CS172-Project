APPLY GET INVOLVED Search this Website Search Student Ambassador Program The Student Ambassador program provides an important leadership opportunity for our creative Longhorns to connect with prospective and admitted students by sharing important information about the College of Fine Arts (COFA) and the student experience. By leading in-person tours, facilitating virtual info. sessions and supporting the Admissions team at COFA recruitment events, our Student Ambassadors provide prospective students and their families a friendly, organized, and insightful visit experience.

Office of Admissions The Office of Admissions is responsible for determining student eligibility for admission to the university. Career Center The Career Center prepares Titans for future career success, providing resources and guidance to help with professional growth and transformation. Center for Internships & Community Engagement The Center for Internships & Community Engagement (CICE) creates cooperative linkages between higher education, community organizations and professional offices, striving to create high quality, community-based experiences that benefit students, partner sites, and communities. Educational Partnerships Educational Partnerships provides opportunities aimed at helping students progress onto college and be successful throughout the higher education pipeline. Office of Financial Aid The Office of Financial Aid handles all matters related to monetary disbursement, in order to help students pay for their educational expenses. Orientation Orientation prepares and supports students during the transition from high school and/or another college or university to Cal State Fullerton. Outreach and Recruitment Outreach and Recruitment serves prospective and incoming students, and any other interested parties in the information seeking and application process, as it relates to the California State University system and Cal State Fullerton specifically.

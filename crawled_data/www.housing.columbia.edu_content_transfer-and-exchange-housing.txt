Rising sophomores: six consecutive terms of housing Rising juniors: four consecutive terms of housing Students must apply and accept an assignment in their first term of attendance and continue occupancy through graduation to maintain guaranteed housing. Learn more about eligibility requirements. Assignments Students are housed with their class in upperclass residence halls. Assignment is based on seniority and building preference, with applications processed on a rolling basis. Please note that building preferences are considered during the assignment process but are not guaranteed. The majority of transfer and exchange students are assigned to doubles. Fall Assignments Transfer Student Housing Applications are available from a student's date of admission through July 15. Students should expect to receive their assignments in late July/early August. Spring Assignments Transfer Student Housing Applications are available from a student's date of admission through December 20. Students entering in the spring will receive confirmation of housing in December and their assignments in early January.

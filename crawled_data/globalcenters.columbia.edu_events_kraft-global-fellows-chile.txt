Past Event Kraft Global Fellows in Chile Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo March 17, 2019 - March 22, 2019 12:00 AM - 11:59 PM The Office of the University Chaplain’s Kraft Global Fellows Program is an initiative of the Kraft Family Fund for Intercultural and Interfaith Awareness led by University Chaplain and Associate Provost, Jewelnel Davis. The mission of the program is to promote interfaith and cross-cultural experience and to provide an opportunity for Columbia University students to add a global perspective to their academic experience. A group of six students, led by Chaplain Davis, will travel to Chile March 17-22 in the context of this program and, among many activities, will meet with human rights activists, immigration experts and religious leaders from different faiths.

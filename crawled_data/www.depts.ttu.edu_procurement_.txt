Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Procurement Services Menu Search TTU Procurement Services welcome to procurement services anticipating institutional needs through collaboration, consultation, and financial stewardship. The Procurement Services department includes purchasing and contracting, payment services, travel, procurement card, vendor services, and property surplus. Procurement Services' mission is to provide excellent customer service while adhering to the highest ethical values in the purchase, receipt, and payment of goods and services in support of the University's mission and goals; to maintain resourceful practices that follow the laws of State of Texas and to maintain and expand vendor relations that enable competitive and diversified vendor sources that will provide the best value to the University.

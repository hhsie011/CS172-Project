Programs support YSM For more than 125 years, YSM has been educating outstanding musicians while offering public performances that enhance the cultural life of our community. give now Close Ways to give From the beginning, Yale’s musical activities have been supported by generous donations of all sizes. Gifts are responsible for every element that makes the Yale School of Music what it is today. Alumni Fund give now Toggle Annual gifts to the School of Music Alumni Fund provide vital support for the educational and artistic endeavors at the Yale School of Music. Year after year, support from alumni and friends allows the School of Music to maintain its high standards and continue bringing together the most talented faculty and students. No matter the gift amount, your participation is greatly appreciated. Norfolk Chamber Music Festival Give Now Toggle Your gift makes a difference.

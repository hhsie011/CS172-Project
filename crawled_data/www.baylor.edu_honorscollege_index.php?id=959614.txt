The Honors College brings leading figures from all areas of specialty onto campus in order to engage with students and the public, such as in the annual Drumwright Family Lecture Series. Information about such past and future events can be found through the menu. Furthermore, we provide our students with the means necessary to excel in their profession, whether they pursue private careers or contribute to the ongoing scholarship of their field. In addition to close guidance from our faculty and staff, the fruit of which can be seen in our publication highlights, students can publish their research in The Pulse, Baylor’s interdisciplinary undergraduate journal. We encourage our students to take advantage of these resources!

News Give now Search Breadcrumb Penn Libraries About Social Media at the Penn Libraries Social Media at the Penn Libraries Main content Since 1750, the Penn Libraries has served the teaching, research and learning needs of scholars. The Libraries' collections was founded by Benjamin Franklin and consists of over seven million physical volumes housed in 14 libraries, plus a digital library of millions more. The Penn Libraries regularly shares news, resources, services, and event information through these platforms: Blog Facebook Instagram Newsletter Twitter This page lists only those libraries, centers, programs, and projects within the Penn Libraries which maintain separate social media channels. Biotech Commons The services and collections of the Biotech Commons facilitate research, education, and patient care decisions for the University of Pennsylvania health sciences community. Blog Canvas Canvas is the University-wide educational platform managed by the Libraries’ Courseware Services Team. Blog Twitter Common Press The Common Press — a collaboration between Penn Libraries, the Weitzman School of Design, and Kelly Writers House — is Penn’s letterpress and book arts studio. Blog Facebook Instagram Newsletter Community Engagement Penn Libraries Community Engagement works towards access and equity for learners of all ages through public school library advocacy, community partnerships, and literacy engagement initiatives. Facebook Instagram Fisher Fine Arts Library The collections of the Fisher Fine Arts Library cover architecture, city planning, historic preservation, history of art, landscape architecture, the studio arts, and urban design. Facebook Instagram Judaica Digital Humanities Judaica Digital Humanities at the Penn Libraries is a robust program of projects and tools for experimental digital scholarship with Judaica collections. Instagram Twitter Kislak Center for Special Collections, Rare Books and Manuscripts The Kislak Center fosters innovative approaches to integrating material and digital research and advocating for open data. Blog Facebook Flickr Instagram Newsletter Levy Dental Medicine Library The Dental School Library's collection covers all aspects of dental medicine and the related basic sciences. Twitter Lippincott Library of the Wharton School The Lippincott Library is the University of Pennsylvania's business library, serving the research needs of Wharton and the Penn community. Blog Twitter Manuscripts of the Muslim World Project Manuscripts of the Muslim World will include digital editions of more than 500 manuscripts and 827 paintings from the Islamicate world broadly construed. Twitter Penn Museum Library The Museum Library assists research, study, and teaching in anthropology and archaeology, as well as the mission of the Penn Museum of Archaeology and Anthropology. Facebook Instagram Twitter Schoenberg Institute for Manuscript Studies The Schoenberg Institute for Manuscript Studies (SIMS), located within the Kislak Center, supports an array of manuscript-related digital projects.

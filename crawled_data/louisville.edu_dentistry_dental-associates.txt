Oral Surgery Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery provides care for a complete spectrum of surgical problems from impacted wisdom teeth and dental implants to sophisticated facial reconstruction. Care is available under general anesthesia both in the practice facility and in the hospital and outpatient surgery centers. Comprehensive Care We are the largest practice in the region that offers the full spectrum of dental services in one location. This ensures comprehensive and continuity of care for their patients. Oral Pathology Our Oral and Maxillofacial Pathology practice offers prompt and accurate diagnosis of surgical pathology specimens through our laboratory’s biopsy service and evaluation of patients suffering from conditions of the oral and maxillofacial region through our clinical consultation service.

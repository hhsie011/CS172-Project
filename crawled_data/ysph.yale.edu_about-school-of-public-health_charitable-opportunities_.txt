Globalization, urbanization, climate change and the global pandemic all present unparalleled opportunities for the scholars and students at the Yale School of Public Health to impact the lives and health of millions. At the same time, they provide a unique platform for visionary philanthropists to help make this work possible. Yale University and the Yale School of Public Health are embarking on an ambitious campaign: for Humanity. We aim to partner with passionate donors who want to support our efforts, invest in our talents, and help us transform the school to protect and improve lives around the world… for the health of humanity. Amazing things happen when our public health passion and purpose work together. Together we value the high return on investment that public health offers to the world.

The University's Transit Program offers incentives and discounts to full-time students, faculty and staff using the Metrorail, Tri-Rail, Metrobus, and Miami-Dade “Park and Ride” express buses. For the transit schedule click here. Full-time students receive 50% off the monthly Metrorail pass. To obtain your discount College Easy Pass follow the steps below. Passes may be purchased effective the 20th of each month and available for use on the 1st of the following month. Final date to purchase the College Easy Pass is on the 10 of the month in which the pass will be used. Download the Go Miami mobile application at Google Play or the Apple Store. Register with Go Miami using your miami.edu credentials. Purchase a monthly pass before the 10th of each month. Tap and Go using your mobile app for Metrorail and Metro-bus entry. For assistance contact Miami-Dade County Transit at 786-469-5151, select option 1 and then option 5. Faculty and Staff with full-time status receive a Metro-Rail and Tri-Rail pass at a discounted rate based on annual salary (see chart below for monthly cost structure). To join the program complete this form by the 14th of the month. Earn up to 250 Well 'Canes Points. Click here to learn if you qualify and complete the form based on your respective campus: Coral Gables/RSMAS Gables One Tower Miller School of Medicine. To cancel your Metrorail pass complete this Cancellation Form. Miller School of Medicine employees visit this link.

PHYSICS Rooms BARUS & HOLLEY To view the availability of the following rooms click on the link: BH Lobby BH 330 BH 410 BH 555 BH 723 BH 724 PHYSICS Rooms BARUS BUILDING To view the availability of the following rooms click on the link: Common Room First Floor Physics Forum Second Floor (212) HOW TO BOOK A PHYSICS ROOM The following rooms in Barus & Holley (184 Hope St.), BH Lobby, BH 330, BH 410, BH 555, BH 723*, and BH 724* are controlled by the Physics Department and can be booked using Google Calendar. The following rooms in Barus Building (340 Brook St.), Common Room First Floor, Physics Forum Second Floor (212) are also controlled by the Physics Department and can be booked using Google Calendar. If you are a member of the Physics Department follow these instructions in order to book one of these rooms: Create an event in Google Calendar. Click on "Add rooms." Scroll down to "Barus & Holley," or "Barus Building." Select the room you want to book. Fill in the name of your event and click "Save." *Rooms 723 and 724 adjoin. If you want to reserve both spaces you must make a separate room request for each. Further instructions on how to book rooms using Google Calendar are available here. If you are not a member of the Physics Department or if you have trouble booking a room, contact Jesse Tessier <[email protected]>, 401.863.2641.

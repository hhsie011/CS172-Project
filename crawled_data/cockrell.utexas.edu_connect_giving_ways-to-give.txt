Virtual Visit Contact Us Ways to Give There are many ways to give to the Cockrell School of Engineering. Choose from the options below to make your tax-deductible contribution: Make your gift online Mail a check made payable to The University of Texas at Austin at the address listed below. Note your gift's designation within the Cockrell School in the memo line. Maximize your gift through your employer's matching gift program. Most matches are dollar-for-dollar (1:1), effectively doubling the value of your gift. Some companies match the gifts of retirees and spouses, as well as those of active employees. Make a planned gift through your will. Learn about structuring a gift that will help you honor your values and meet your personal, financial, and estate planning goals. Make a gift of appreciated stock. To ensure proper processing, please call the Cockrell School at 512-471-3395 to request transfer instructions. Send your contribution via wire transfer. To ensure proper credit of the gift, donors should contact the Cockrell School at 512-471-3395 to request transfer instructions. University employees may give through payroll deduction. All checks and documentation in support of the Cockrell School of Engineering should be mailed to: Cockrell School of Engineering The University of Texas at Austin 301 E. Dean Keeton, C2104 Austin, TX 78712-0287 512-471-3955 (fax) If you need assistance related to any of these gift options or have more questions about how to make a gift, please contact the Cockrell School Development Office at 512-471-3395.

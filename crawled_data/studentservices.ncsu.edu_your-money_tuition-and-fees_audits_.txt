Registration Fee Tuition Surcharge Residency Status Previous Term Tuition and Fee Rates Tuition Rates Reference Guide Auditing a Course Auditing allows a student to take a course without earning a grade or credit hours. The following tuition and fee guidelines apply: Undergraduate Degree students – The fee to audit a course is the same as if the courses were taken for credit. Graduate Degree students – One on-campus audit course is available at no charge when graduate students are registering during the fall or spring semester and are paying for other on-campus coursework. Any additional audited courses will have the same rates as credit courses. The following rules/exceptions apply: Distance Education courses do not qualify as “other on-campus course work” and are not eligible for the free audit. Courses changed to Audit after the 10th day of a fall or spring semester are not eligible for the free audit. New audit courses added after the 10th day are not eligible for the free audit. There are no free audits in the summer sessions.

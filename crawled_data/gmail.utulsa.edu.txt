Current category: All All Knowledge Base Service Catalog Search Sign In Show Applications Menu Client Portal Sign In Search Home Services Knowledge Base More Applications Skip to Knowledge Base content Search Articles Knowledge Base Email Gmail Gmail Gmail If you enrolled at TU before 08/01/2018, you will be on the Gmail platform. We have moved to Office 365 for student email and we no longer configure Gmail for new students. If you have a TU Gmail account, it will continue to be active. Login You can access your TU Gmail directly by visiting https://mail.google.com/a/utulsa.edu. Google will prompt you for your email address. You will need to enter your TuNetID followed by @utulsa.edu, for example abc123@utulsa.edu Login to TU Gmail Reset Password In most cases, your TUNetID and password have been synced with Google’s authentication system. If you have not changed your password recently, you may need to manually reset your password before you can access your Gmail inbox. If you still have trouble getting into your Gmail account, please email help@utulsa.edu and identify yourself as an Alumni when requesting a Gmail password reset. Two-step verification After you have changed your password, you will be required to enable 2-step verification for your TU Gmail account by visiting https://www.google.com/landing/2step/ If you have any questions email help@utulsa.edu or call the IT help desk at 918-631-3500.

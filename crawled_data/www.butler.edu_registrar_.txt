Need to obtain your transcript or grades? Apply to graduate? Schedule courses or classrooms? Students, faculty, staff, and alumni receive support in those areas and more from Registration and Records. We work with students and their advisors to facilitate registration and to ensure that transfer, AP/IB credit, or study abroad credit is appropriately reflected on the student’s Butler transcript. We also assist with grading; graduation certification and distribution of diplomas; NCAA evaluation and certification; enrollment verification; publication of academic policies and degree requirements; data collection and analysis for internal and external reporting; and general enrollment and academic information.

To accomplish this, we rely on employees committed to providing stellar customer service and security. Yale Security officers patrol the campus 24 hours per day and perform a variety of services, including walking escorts and lockout assistance. Security officers cover the entire campus on marked patrol SUVs, electric cars, and Segways. Security officers are non-sworn and have direct communication with dispatch centers and Yale Police to provide improved efficiencies and information sharing. All Security officers receive extensive training through new hire and continuous in-service training. Security Officer Operations Management and supervision of a security force assigned to fixed posts in buildings and parking facilities, as well as vehicle, foot, and bicycle patrols of the Yale campus. Security officers perform a variety of services, such as providing safe escorts, conducting bike patrols, and helping with lockouts. Cultural Properties Alarm Monitoring Station Provides 24-hour monitoring and dispatch of the University’s integrated security system; burglar, fire, duress alarms, and video for all sites housing the University’s cultural property.

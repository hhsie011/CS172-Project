English Language Proficiency Applicants whose native language is not English are expected to meet or exceed the program minimum language requirements. Submission of test scores is not required as part of the application process. Please review the expected language levels below. Students interested in the Advanced Painting Intensive must submit a portfolio of 10 images of their work for review. You must also include a Statement of Intent that is up to 500 words with your educational background, including degrees and independent courses, and your reasons for applying to the program. Please select the "Advanced Painting Intensive" option within the Arts in the Summer application.

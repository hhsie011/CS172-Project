We are excited to be creating a legacy of leadership with the official launch of the OSU Hargis Leadership Institute. Learn more Introducing the Hargis Leadership Institute Empowering outstanding leaders The Hargis Leadership Institute encompasses passionate individuals who desire to consistently exhibit exceptional leadership. We value cultivating leaders on the basis of three fundamental pillars—identity, relationships, and leadership skills. As an institute we partner with other university departments, private industry, and community groups to create leadership development opportunities. The Hargis Leadership Institute aligns with Oklahoma State University’s land-grant mission by investing in the leaders of today and tomorrow. Through their life-giving community leadership, this network of leaders will be equipped to create better communities for all. About Us #LeadOrange Lead the change. #LeadOrange redefines the way that we perceive and celebrate America’s brightest leaders at Oklahoma State University. Through storytelling, this campus-wide recognition of leadership excellence highlights students, faculty, and staff. These stories worth spreading are featured through a virtual leadership-centered campaign called the President’s Celebration of Leadership, as well as an annual Lead Orange awards ceremony. Learn more Leadership Institute Endowed Chair Burns Hargis Burns Hargis’ passion for both outstanding leadership and the state of Oklahoma is evident through his unrelenting dedication to service. He was an active leader throughout his career, as the former president of the Oklahoma Bar Foundation and serving as a Fellow for the American Bar Foundation. As president for Oklahoma State University, Burns brought to life a vision that built upon the legacy of Oklahoma State and firmly established the university as a modern land-grant institution. His leadership has been marked by record-setting fundraising, enrollment growth, new and enhanced facilities, and a growing national reputation for academic and research advances. Learn more about Burns Hargis Leadership Opportunities The Hargis Leadership Institute enriches the student leadership experience through a variety of programs and services President's Leadership Council The PLC is a competitive scholarship and leadership program for outstanding freshmen that teaches the importance of leadership and serving throughout life. SUMMIT The SUMMIT Experience is a leadership conference that is accessible to all students and teaches the key sources that help students reach their leadership peak. McKnight Scholars The McKnight Scholars Leadership Program identifies, equips and empowers young scholars to become life-giving leaders.

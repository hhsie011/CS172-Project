Home Home Meetings Council Membership Archives Resources Info Meeting Schedule Find your Representative Sponsors Contact Us About The Council of Principal Investigators (CPI) is composed of Principal Investigators (PIs) that represent PIs from members of the Texas A&M research community. The CPI is committed to the continued improvement of the research environment for students, faculty, and staff. Specifically, the CPI and the PIs it represents endeavor to work with administration to provide an effective and efficient research organization to achieve the goal of being nationally recognized and competitive in research and teaching.

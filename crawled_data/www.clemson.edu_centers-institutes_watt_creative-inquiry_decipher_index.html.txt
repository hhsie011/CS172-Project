Each year, more than 4,000 Creative Inquiry students investigate topics ranging from technology that addresses food insecurity to human factors forensics to using artificial intelligence to sex chicken eggs. Creative Inquiry provides students with an outlet for their curiosity and allows them to take a closer look at the problems facing our University, our community and the world beyond.

recreation and well-being, USU has it all. Recreation and Wellness Logan and its surrounding areas include ski resorts, lakes, rivers, and mountains to make USU one of the finest recreational environments in the nation. Plus students have access to several fitness facilities on campus. Ski Resorts within 60 miles 4 Minutes to Bear Lake 45 Minutes to Logan Canyon 5 National Parks within 5 Hours 8 Campus Recreation Campus recreation creates opportunities for students to enhance the college experience through play, wellness and adventure. The Aggie Recreation Center, HPER Building, and Fieldhouse are multipurpose facilities that offer countless ways to stay active and healthy. Learn More Campus Recreation Aggie Recreation Center Outdoor Programs Through programmed trips, access to outdoor equipment, and courses in outdoor skills and leadership, the outdoor programs connect students to their local and regional environement while learning how to recreate responsibly on public lands. Learn More Outdoor Programs Outdoor Leadership Cache Valley and Utah Club Sports and Intramural Sports With 23 TEAMS, the Club Sport Program compliments the University’s intercollegiate athletic programs, Intramural activities, and physical education course offerings. The Intramural Sports Program offers the opportunity for individuals to play organized sports in a recreational but competitive atmosphere within their campus community.

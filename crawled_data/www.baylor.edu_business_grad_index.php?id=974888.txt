Solenberg, PhD MHA Deputy Director & Assistant Professor Lieutenant Colonel, United States Air Force Email Allen_Solenberg@baylor.edu High Res Photo CV Download CV Professional Summary: Dr. Allen Solenberg is the Deputy Program Director and an Assistant Professor at the MHA/MBA Army Baylor Graduate Program since 2019. He holds a BBA in Healthcare Management and a MBA and earned his PhD in Health Policy and Management from the University of Kansas, School of Medicine. Dr. Solenberg is also a Lt Col in the United States Air Force serving in leadership roles since 2009. Dr. Solenberg’s dissertation research was on disparities in colorectal cancer screening for people with spinal cord injuries and/or disabilities. Dr. Solenberg is interested in research on healthcare disparities, disabilities, and military healthcare. Dr. Solenberg is a CAHME Fellow and a HFMA Certified Healthcare Financial Professional and currently working on advancing his research for journal publication Education: 2019 – PhD Health Policy and Management, University of Kansas Medical Center at Kansas City, Kansas 2009 – MBA, Aspen University, Denver Colorado 2007 – BBA Health/Health Care Administration/Management, American InterContinental University, Schaumburg, Illinois Courses: · HCA 5213 – Health Policy (2020-present) HCA 5V92 – Military Health Systems Management (2021) HCA 5218 – Finance III: Financial Applications (2021) MMGT 5425 – Healthcare Strategic Management (2020) MBL 5310 – Selected Topics in Business Law (2019-2020) HCA 5V92 – Seminar in Post Graduate Studies (2020) MMKT 5371 – Marketing Management (2019-2020 Research Interests: Health Policy Disability Disparities Preventive Screenings Selected Papers: Solenberg Jr, A.

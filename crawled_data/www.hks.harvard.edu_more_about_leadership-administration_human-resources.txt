About Leadership & Administration Human Resources The Harvard Kennedy School Human Resources department strives to be a strategic partner in the leadership and management of Harvard Kennedy School through specific contributions in employee development and support, clear and effective employee communications, and accurate and timely processes. If you are interested in working as a staff member at HKS, we invite you to explore university's human resources site, and find job openings on Harvard Careers, the university's recruitment management system, where both internal and external applicants can: View all job opportunities, search on specific criteria to find jobs that interest you, or set up a search agent that will notify you when positions are posted that match your qualifications. Find the step-by-step process to apply for jobs online and receive email updates about the status of your candidacy throughout the hiring process. Learn more about benefits, resources and compensation. If you are interested in faculty appointments at HKS, please review the recruitment procedure and policies through the Academic Dean's Office.

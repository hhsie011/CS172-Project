This album was released in October 2021 which presents all hues of contemporary Big Bang Sound. Michael Shults is the lead tenor saxophonist with Memphis Jazz Orchestra in their thirty year-long standing residency at Alfred's on Beale. Michael Shults is the competition committee chair for the North American Saxophone Alliance Collegiate Jazz Competition. 2020 Jack Cooper received the Pearl Wales Professor of Music.This professorship provides recognition to faculty that have presented outstanding teaching, research and service to the profession and achieved a high level of national and international recognition. Jack Cooper received the University of Memphis College of Communication and Fine Arts Dean’s Creative Achievement Award. 2019 Jack Cooper recorded two new works that were commissioned by Bernie Dresel’s BBB Big Band. It premiered at Vibrato in Los Angeles.

Cornell Employee Lunchtime Lawn Games Wednesday, July 24, 2019 at 11:30am to 1:00pm Arts Quad Central Campus Take a break and enjoy some fun and games with your Cornell colleagues! We're taking over the Arts Quad on Wednesday, July 24, from 11:30am - 1:00pm, to play all your favorites: Corn Hole, Badminton, Spike Ball, Pop 'n Catch, Frisknock, Kubb, Lawn Darts, Ladder Ball, Croquet, and Horsehoes. Lunch will be available for purchase from Cornell Dining (hot dog cart & ice cream), or bring your own picnic! Please bring your own cup or mug for ice water, which will be provided. Sponsored by Cornell Recreation Connection and the Employee Assembly.

Parking On-Campus Street Parking All street-level parking on campus requires a university permit at all times. Check signs for other restrictions. Off-Campus Metered Street Parking Metered street parking is available at the edge of campus along Dean Keeton St, between San Jacinto Blvd and Red River St. Meters are enforced Monday–Saturday, 8am–6pm and accept credit cards in addition to coins. Garage Parking Hourly-rate paid parking is available in two garages near the Doty Fine Arts Building: San Jacinto Garage Manor Garage $6 Click-and-Pay Event Parking A short-term Click-and-Pay parking permit can be purchased in advance for some events. For more information about parking on campus, please visit Parking and Transportation Services. Public Transportation The public transportation system in Austin is run by Capital Metro. For precise times and stops, visit the Capital Metro website or call (512) 474–1200. The buildings of the college are served by regular bus routes 7, 10, 20, and 37, as well as the campus loop route 640. Contact Capital Metro for other routes with stops within walking distance or with questions about accessibility. The above public transportation information is approximate and is subject to change. Please verify all routes and schedules on the Capital Metro website or by calling (512) 474–1200.

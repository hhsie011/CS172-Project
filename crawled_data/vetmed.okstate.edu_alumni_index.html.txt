In addition, membership gives you the opportunity, via the connections you gain, to share your opinions and potentially influence the future of the university. Your membership also keeps you connected to the CVM and activities related to the Veterinary College of Medicine as well as to Oklahoma State University as a whole. Membership benefits: Connect with other OSU alumni and friends Share opinions and potentially influence OSU’s future Connect with the Veterinary College Be advised of College of Veterinary Medicine activities Be in the know about OSU activities Discounts locally and nationally and more View a full list of OSU Alumni Association Membership Benefits. By joining the OSU Alumni Association portions of your membership fees rebate to CVM annually. These rebates help fund events held for all CVM alumni. Giving When you make a gift to the College of Veterinary Medicine at Oklahoma State University, you are helping to provide state-of-the-art veterinary medical resources that serve the citizens of Oklahoma, the nation and the world. The Alumni Affairs office supports our most valued resource, our people. Contributions directly assist with alumni activities, hosting of alumni internal and external events and occasionally provide extra support for unique veterinary class projects.

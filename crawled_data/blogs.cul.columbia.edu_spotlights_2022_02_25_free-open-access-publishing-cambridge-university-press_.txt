To begin the process of publishing open access with a CUP journal, register for an account via their online submission system with a Columbia email address in order to waive APCs. Have questions about publishing open access with a CUP journal? Columbia librarians are available via email at schlolarlycommunication@library.columbia.edu to help guide researchers through the application and publishing process.

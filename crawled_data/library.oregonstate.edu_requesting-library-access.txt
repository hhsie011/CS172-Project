Requesting access Request Form If you are an ONID user with a need for online library services, please fill out this form. Please provide a justification for access, such as continuing an OSU research project, finishing up incompletes, being a visiting scholar, or teaching an OSU course. The Acquisitions Unit staff in the library will review your request and either grant or deny it.

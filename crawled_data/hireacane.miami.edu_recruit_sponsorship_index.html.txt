Put your organization in the spotlight while supporting programming and new initiatives for our students! Providing financial resources allows your organization to support activities that benefit UM students. Your organization will make an impact, and as a result, will enjoy an increased level of visibility and branding on campus. Contact Ali Rodriguez by email or at 305-284-1819 for more information or to become a Toppel Partner or Career Expo/Fair Sponsor today! *Note: All partnership levels are accepted on a rolling basis and include a one-year term. If you have an interest in specific career fairs, we also offer event sponsorships. Career Expo/Fair Sponsorships This one-time event funding gives organizations the opportunity to make a direct impact on our students. If you would like to get in front of your target market and impress students in our university community, sponsor a Career Expo or Fair (Architecture or Accounting). Career Expo or Fair Green Premier Sponsor - $2,500 Targeted emails to students featuring career opportunities at your organization Scheduled classroom or organization visit (based on availability) Free HireACane Academy registration (Toppel’s annual recruiter conference) Organization logo included in Career Expo emails to students Promotion of your company’s recruiting efforts on social media Posts leading up to Expo on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram and spotlight video on stories Logo featured on Sponsors tab at HireACane.com Complimentary registration for Career Expo/Fair Career Expo or Fair Orange Priority Sponsor - $1,500 Organization logo included in Career Expo emails to students Promotion of your company’s recruiting efforts on social media One post before Career Expo on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram Logo featured on Sponsors tab at HireACane.com Complimentary registration for Career Expo/Fair Still not sure? As you can see, Toppel offers multiple ways for employers to offer sponsorship via our signature Partner Program with three levels of annual giving and our Career Expo/Fair Sponsorships. Both options provide significant opportunities to make meaningful contact with our students, faculty, and staff. Funds raised through the Toppel Partner Program goes to the Toppel Professional Development Fund, which supports students’ career readiness and professional development.

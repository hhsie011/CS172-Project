Home People Enquye Negash Enquye Negash Title: Postdoctoral Scientist Email: [email protected] Enquye Negash is a postdoctoral researcher interested in understanding the paleoenvironmental context of human evolution. Her research focuses on studying vegetation structure in modern African ecosystems using geochemical, paleobotanical and quantitative methods. The aim of her research is to apply the knowledge garnered from modern ecosystems to the fossil record to make accurate paleoenvironmental reconstructions.

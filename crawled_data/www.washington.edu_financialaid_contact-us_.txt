Student Financial Aid Contact us You can contact our financial aid counseling team by using the form below or by calling us at 206-543-6101 during our phone service hours. Prior to sending your message, please take a moment to review our FAQ page where you may be able to find the answer to your question. Your financial aid status can also be viewed on your MyUW-Financial Aid Status. If you are inquiring on the behalf of a student, they must provide their consent before we can release information protected under FERPA regulations. Students may authorize the release of their financial aid information on MyUW. Thank you for your patience during this time.

Admissions Contact Have questions? Whether you want to know more about admissions, financial aid, campus visits, or any other topic, Syracuse University’s Office of Admissions is ready to help! Sherlyn Sychua Regional Manager for Advancement and External Affairs/Admission G30 Blk13 Dubai Knowledge Park Dubai 500752 UAE (Hours: Sunday-Thursday, 9 a.m.-6 p.m.) Phone: +97144336012 Fax: +97144438935 ssychua@syr.edu Sherlyn Sychua is based in Syracuse University’s Dubai office to give support to all the students and school counselors from the Middle East, North Africa and Turkey. She is there to guide students from the start of the application process to a successful transition to Syracuse University.

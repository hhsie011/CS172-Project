Physical Sciences Building (PSB), Clark Atrium DOWNLOAD PROGRAM BOOKLET VIEW RECORDED SESSIONS 2019 DOWNLOAD RESOURCE FAIR MAP The workshops and exhibits at Staff Development Day give Cornell employees opportunities to invest in their personal and professional growth and well-being. The day's events will center on various aspects of education exploration, professional development, wellness and community in the workplace. Staff and supervisors are encouraged to attend this day dedicated to your success! Bring a copy of your resume and have it critiqued by a career specialist. A Continental Breakfast will be available at 8:30 a.m. compliments of your Employee Assembly! Keynote Speech by Peter Paradise, Associate Dean of Finance and Administration, CALS Dean’s Office, at 9 a.m. in PSB Seminar Room 120 The Resource Fair will be held 9 a.m. – 3 p.m. in the Clark Atrium. Over 40 workshops will be held from 10:30 a.m. – 4 p.m. Supervisors are invited to participate! Please encourage your staff to attend and provide flexible work schedules, release time, rotating staff office coverage, etc. to allow your staff to explore the diverse offerings. Off-Campus Offices – Although you may not be able to attend the Ithaca based events – there’s no reason not to get in on the fun! Please visit the Training website to learn about all our professional development offerings, sign up for a web-based training that interests you, and plan to spend targeted time on your development as well!

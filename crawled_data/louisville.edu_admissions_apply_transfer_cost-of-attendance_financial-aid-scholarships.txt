Residency Housing & Residence Life Dining Services Home / Cost & Aid / Financial Aid Financial Aid File FAFSA: Visit the FAFSA website to file your Free Application for Federal Student Aid. You must complete the FAFSA every academic year. If you previously filled this out for another institution, please update your school code to 001999 and inform your home institution you will not be enrolled there. If you have filed, log in to ULink to monitor your "Financial Aid To Do List." View scholarship opportunities How much does it cost to attend UofL?

The 2022 Baylor/Waco Piano Competition will take place on Saturday, February 5 in the Glennis McCrary Music Building and the Waco Hall Complex on the Baylor University campus. The contest will begin at 8:00 AM, with winners announced at the afternoon Awards Ceremony at approximately 2:00 PM. Please see below for contest rules and guidelines and the application form. We look forward to seeing you on the Baylor campus in February! Saturday, February 5, 2022 Piano Concerto Competition Junior and senior high school students in Texas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, Arkansas, and New Mexico are eligible. Division I, grades 10-12, below the age of 19 on contest day Division II, grades 7-9, below the age of 16 on contest day For each division, a movement from the standard concerto literature is required. Concerto-like works and student concertos (Division II) are acceptable. Piano Solo Competition Rules Junior and senior high school students in Texas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, Arkansas, and New Mexico are eligible. Division I, grades 11-12, below the age of 19 on contest day Division II, grades 9-10, below the age of 17 on contest day Division III, grades 7-8, below the age of 15 on contest day For each division, the first or last movement of a sonata by Haydn, Mozart, Beethoven, or Schubert must be prepared. A contrasting piece by a master composer from any other style period is also required. Both selections will be performed and must be performed from memory. Awards Concerto Division I Awards: $250, 1st place; $150, 2nd place Concerto Division II Awards: $150, 1st place; $100, 2nd place Solo Division I Awards: $250, 1st place; $150, 2nd place Solo Division II Awards: $150, 1st place; $100, 2nd place Solo Division III Awards: $150, 1st place; $100, 2nd place Entry forms should be post marked on or before January 10, 2022. Make checks payable to "Baylor University." Brochure and Entry Form Send the entry form and an application fee of $35 to the following address: Baylor/Waco Piano Competition c/o Keyboard Division Baylor University School of Music One Bear Place #97408 Waco, Texas 76798-7408 Acceptance will be limited to fifteen students in each division. Contestants must be in Waco and prepared to perform by 8:00 a.m. on the day of the competition.

This ERG will support CUIMC’s efforts to diversify staff. The LGBTQ+ ERG will serve as a visible, accessible resource for LGBTQ-identified employees and their allies and will provide LGBTQ-focused networking, educational and social opportunities for the CUIMC community. The LGBTQ+ ERG is open to all members of the CUIMC community – Join us!

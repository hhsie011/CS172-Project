Families play an important role in the success of your student, and we welcome you with open arms at OSU. Our staff and faculty have worked hard to ensure that you and your students have the best online orientation experience. Students entering in the spring will receive an email with links to their Oklahoma State student portal three days before their online orientation program. The portal contains orientation resources and instructions regarding peer mentor connections and academic advising. 2022 Spring Family Webinar Series Partnering With You for Student Success Our campus is divided into leadership areas that will impact your student’s success You are already familiar with Enrollment and Brand Management, as it includes Undergraduate Admissions, Scholarships and Financial Aid, and First Year Success. Academic Affairs oversees the faculty and advisors who will guide your student’s academic pursuits, while Student Affairs guides learning and activities outside the classroom to enhance the student experience. Institutional Diversity maintains a university system that is socially, culturally and globally competent, and embraces and promotes a more inclusive world. Academic Preparation The education of your student is our top priority. Find resources to support the academic needs of your student, such as LASSO Center, Writing Center and the many libraries OSU offers.

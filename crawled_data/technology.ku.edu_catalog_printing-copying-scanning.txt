Visitors Description — Multi-function devices (MFD) in campus computer labs and in many KU Libraries locations provide the ability for students, faculty and staff to scan, copy and print documents. In addition, most campus departments/units have multi-function devices for faculty and staff to scan, copy and print documents. See the following pages for more information: Student Printing Faculty/Staff Printing Visitor Printing Show MoreMore description Cost — Scanning is free. Printing or copying costs $.08 per page for black and white and $0.48 for color. Students receive $8 of free printing each semester. See the following pages for more information: Student Printing Faculty/Staff Printing Visitor Printing Access — Students Printing, scanning and copying services are available to students in many KU Libraries locations. Students must log in to the multi-function device (MFD) using their KU Online ID and password. Faculty & Staff Printing, scanning and copying services are available to students in many KU Libraries locations. Students must log in to the multi-function device (MFD) using their KU Online ID and password. Campus Visitors Scanning and copying services are available in Watson Library and Anschutz Library on the Lawrence campus. Printing service for campus visitors is available only in Watson Library. Visit the KU Libraries website for information. Show MoreMore description Support — Support is available through the KU Information Technology Customer Service Center at itcsc@ku.edu or 785-864-8080.

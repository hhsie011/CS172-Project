Teaching is a key component of the commitment to interdisciplinary knowledge at the heart of a Penn education. Penn’s Penn Integrates Knowledge professors are leaders in cross-disciplinary fields who hold joint appointments between two schools and actively engage the campus in cutting-edge interdisciplinary knowledge.

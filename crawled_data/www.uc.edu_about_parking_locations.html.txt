We also manage parking for 1819 Innovation Hub, UC Reading, Victory Parkway, UC Blue Ash College and UC Clermont College. During semesters where parking on Uptown campuses sells out, remote parking options will be available. Compare your options below and select the location that best meets your needs as a visitor, student, faculty or staff. Note which locations have visitor parking, or are permit only.

The Caruth Institute for Entrepreneurship in the Cox School of Business has been a longtime leader in entrepreneurial education and provides students with hands-on experiences and amazing connections. Learning to be entrepreneurial and innovative will benefit all facets of your life. The Specialization in Entrepreneurship consists of four courses that complement any business major: Identifying Entrepreneurial Opportunities (CISB 2379) Managing the Entrepreneurial Business (CISB 4398) Law for Entrepreneurs (BL 4340) Entrepreneurship-Starting a Business (CISB 5397) Students with an interest in entrepreneurship are also encouraged to get involved in the Entrepreneurship Cluband SMU Big iDeas.

Search schedule » Print Home  ›  Student Success  ›  Tuition & Financial Aid  ›  Tuition Comparison Tuition Comparison Oregon State University Ecampus students are never charged at nonresident rates, meaning your tuition is the same if you live in the state of Oregon or anywhere else in the world. All Ecampus tuition and fee information is available on Oregon State's Finance and Administration website – you can use the official tuition and fees tables to compare OSU Ecampus and OSU Portland tuition to the on-campus rates.

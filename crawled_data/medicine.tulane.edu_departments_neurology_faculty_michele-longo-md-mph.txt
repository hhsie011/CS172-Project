Home / Michele Longo, MD, MPH Michele Longo, MD, MPH Assistant Professor of Neurology School of Medicine Biography Return to Tulane Doctors Dr. Michele Longo is a clinical neurologist at the Tulane Center for Clinical Neurosciences at Tulane School of Medicine. A native New Orleanian, Dr. Longo earned her undergraduate degree, medical degree, and masters of public health degree at Tulane University. Her internship and residency were both completed at Tulane. She is board certified by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology. She has interests in all areas of adult general neurology including dementia and cognitive disorders, headaches, movement disorder, stroke and vascular diseases, peripheral nerve diseases, neuromuscular disorders, neck and back pain, seizures and sleep disorders.

And we've been there before. We connect you to campus resources that will help you along your journey to the classroom and life outside of it. In Transition & Engagement, we work all year to help students succeed quickly at Texas Tech University and to successfully integrate into the academic, cultural, and social experiences available across campus. Whether it's your first year college or your first year on this campus, if you need to find a major or need help finding a resource, our programs are here to help!

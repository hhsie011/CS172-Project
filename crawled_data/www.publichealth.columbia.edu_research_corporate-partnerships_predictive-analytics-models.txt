With access to one of the fastest computer clusters in the world, the C2B2 supercomputer, our researchers can process upwards of 200 trillion calculations per second. Those analyses can then be incorporated into predictive models that provide unique insights on an organization’s potential risk, exposure, return on investment, or cost-benefit analysis for decisions that affect your business.

The Gender and Sexuality Studies Departmental Undergraduate Group (DUG) encourages all current and prospective concentrators to participate in its events and gatherings. The DUG hosts an annual fall concentration open house and sponsors lectures, study breaks, and other activities throughout the year that bring together students with common intellectual interests.

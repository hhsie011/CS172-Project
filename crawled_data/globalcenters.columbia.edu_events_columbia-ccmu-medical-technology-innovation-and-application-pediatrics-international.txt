Calendar: Google Yahoo January 9, 2020 1:30 PM - 5:00 PM 2nd Floor, Academic Exchange Center, Capital Medical University, Xitoutiao No. 10, Youan Menwai Street, Fengtai, Beijing On January 9, 2020, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to the Columbia-CCMU Medical Technology Innovation and Application in Pediatrics International Conference. As a part of the academic exchange series between Columbia University and Capital Medical University, this conference focuses on pediatrics, in the theme of achievement transformation in clinical and medical research. Aiming to explore possible international cooperation in clinical treatment, medical research, application of the medical technology, capacity building, and etc., this conference will lay a foundation for mutual learning and understanding on innovative medical teaching and practices, and possible collaborations. Through the Center's partnership with Capital Medical University Biomedical Valley, a sustainable and effective dialogue mechanism between domestic and international medical experts has been established to promote the interactive exchange between China and international partners in health and medicine. Agenda Notice The Conference provides simultaneous interpretation between Chinese and English. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code confirmation. Please present your QR code at the entrance. Registration begins 30 minutes prior to the program. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

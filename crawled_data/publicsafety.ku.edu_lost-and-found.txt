Lost items are stored for 90 days. After that period, they are disposed of or donated to charity. Retrieving Lost Items To contact us about a lost item, please call 785-864-5911 or complete the Lost & Found form. You must provide the following information when inquiring about a lost item: Your name. Description of the item. Location where the item may have been lost. Your phone number. Your email address. Pick up times: 8:00 -11:30 a.m. and 1:30 - 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday Pick up location:1501 Crestline Drive, Lawrence, Kansas Other Lost & Found Locations The Lawrence Police Department has a Lost and Found for items that may have been lost off campus in Lawrence and can be reached by calling 785-832-7552.

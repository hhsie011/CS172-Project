Since public health conditions remain fluid, our protocols might need to change quickly. Please check this page frequently for updates. Updated: December 24 2021 Spring 2022 Update For the spring 2022 semester, we will welcome students back to campus and to the residence hall, with classes online until January 28. We expect in-person classes to resume the following week. Covid-19 vaccination is mandatory for all students living on or utilizing the JTS campus, and a Covid booster shot is mandatory for all eligible prior to the start of the spring semester or as soon as they are eligible. Students entering campus must have signed the Covid Community Compact and abide by all health and safety protocols. The Library is open to those authorized to enter JTS.

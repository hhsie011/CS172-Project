University Communications has designed a suite of posters, signage, and floor decals for this purpose, which BC Facilities will be installing in building entry lobbies, elevator lobbies and elevators, bathrooms, and other common areas. Department representatives may view available designs in various categories and size variations, as well as download and print selections, at the link below. The folder also includes customizable blank templates, for departments that have specific messaging requirements.

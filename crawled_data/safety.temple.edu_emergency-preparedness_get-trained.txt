TUready Training In our effort to promote everyday readiness, we provide community training on our primary emergency procedures: evaluation, shelter in place and lockdown. Learn more. Psychological First Aid (PFA) Learn how to provide crisis support during and after an emergency. In this training, you will discover the core actions of PFA and gain the necessary skills to support yourself and others! Learn more. Active Assailant Training Run, hide, fight! Get trained and learn about actions you can take in the event you are confronted with an active assailant. Provided in collaboration with Temple Police. Learn more.

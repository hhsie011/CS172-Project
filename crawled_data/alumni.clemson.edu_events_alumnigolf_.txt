Sponsor opportunities are available, so if you are interested, please complete the form below! Here are the deetails of past tournaments for example of what could be included: 18 Holes of Golf Beverage Cart Contests Post-Tournament Reception Giveaways from sponsors and the Alumni Association Awards and Prizes Tournament Costs (pricing subject to change): Team Registration: $800 per four-some Sponsor Opportunities: Presenting Sponsor: $5,000 Logo on promotional materials, website and social media, sign at the event, table at registration for one-on-one interaction with participants and the opportunity to distribute information and giveaways to all tournament participants. Reception Sponsor: $2,000 Provides food and drinks to the tournament participants, table at the reception to give out information and giveaways, and opportunity to speak at the event. Cart Sponsor: $1,500 Sponsor sign on beverage cart throughout the tournament. Sponsor Table: $500 Table on-site at registration for one-on-one interaction with participants and the opportunity to distribute information and giveaways. Tee Sponsor: $300 Sponsor sign at tee box of a hole and opportunity to include giveaway item in registration gift bag that all tournament participants receive. Hole Sponsor: $300 Sponsor sign at green of a hole and opportunity to include giveaway item in registration gift bag that all tournament participants receive. If you would like to provide giveaways to include in the registration gift packet that all tournament participants receive, please contact Randy Boatwright for more information at 864-656-5671 or at brandol@clemson.edu. All proceeds will benefit the Clemson Alumni Association. For more information and to register, contact Randy Boatwright at brandol@clemson.edu.

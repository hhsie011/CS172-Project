Congratulations new graduates! Welcome to the Harvard Dental Alumni Association (HDAA). You’re joining a dynamic community of over 2,500 alumni around the world who are advancing our profession through academics, research, and clinical practice. The HSDM Office of Development and Alumni Relations and the HDAA work together to keep alumni connected with one another, the School, and the University. Together, we will provide opportunities for you to remain engaged in the life of the School and stay in touch with classmates, friends, faculty, and staff in the years after graduation. We welcome your input on ways you’d like to expand upon your relationship with HSDM and look forward to connecting with you. Remember, the HSDM alumni community is your community! I wish you the best as you embark on new adventures and look forward to meeting you at alumni events in the future.

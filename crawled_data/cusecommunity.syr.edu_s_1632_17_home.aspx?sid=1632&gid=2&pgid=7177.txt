Library Partnerships and Discounts Home Advance Partnerships and Discounts Partnerships and Discounts Partnerships HireTalent and HireSessions HireTalent offers multi-school, alumni only career fairs held in various cities and regions around the country. HireSessions is a VIRTUAL event hiring solution that connects alumni job seekers and employers with the power of technology and embrace innovation in order to accomplish their professional goals without limits! Encore Finished with your midlife career but not sure what’s next? Looking for a way to transition to work for the greater good? Check out Encore Fellowships, a midlife transition program that matches skilled, seasoned professionals with social-sector organizations in high-impact, paid assignments for 6-12 months. Apply today! Benefits and Discounts My Software Tutor My Software Tutor (founded by Steve Bradbury '80), offers real-time, online courses in Microsoft Excel from a live instructor. Classes are offered across three levels: Fundamentals, Basics, and Intermediate. Syracuse alumni can take advantage of a 25-percent discount on courses by entering the promo code ORANGE25 at checkout. For more information, including how to register, visit https://mysoftwaretutor.com/orange.

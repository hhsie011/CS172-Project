Temple is a university rich in history. Here are the most important things you need to know about Temple during your college search process and some fun facts. Essentials Founded by Russell H. Conwell in 1884, Temple’s official motto is Perseverantia Vincit, or Perseverance Conquers. Temple University is Philadelphia’s public university, and has more than 340,000 alumni worldwide.

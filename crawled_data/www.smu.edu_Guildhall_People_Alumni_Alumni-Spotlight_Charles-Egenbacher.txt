Alumni Spotlight Alumni Success Students International Students Apply Now Charles Egenbacher Producer/Technical Project Manager at Epic Games Cohort: 17 Current position: Producer/Technical Project Manager of Fortnite Audio, VR/AR on the Unreal Engine — Epic Games Games shipped: Fortnite, League of Legends — including websites, backend services, in-game content, and CG cinematics Q&A with Charles What is the most interesting or enjoyable thing that you have gotten to do in your career? I got to work on League of Legends' first lore driven event, Bilgewater: Burning Tides, while at Riot Games. That was an amazing experience. Why did you choose to get your education from SMU Guildhall? Guildhall had a hands on approach to making games, which I really liked as opposed to studying theoretical concepts. How did SMU Guildhall prepare you for your career in gaming, and what impact has it had on your life? Guildhall taught me perseverance. It's given me friends that I'll have for a lifetime. Huge impact on my life.

Resource Guide Contact Us LGBTQIA+ at University of Miami Here at the University of Miami we understand that 'Canes Care for 'Canes. We're here to support our LGBTQIA+ 'Canes in all their needs. Welcome from Dr. Vega The Center is the H.E.A.R.T. of our LGBTQ Community here at the U! We provide a Holistic approach to Educating and creating Awareness around issues of gender, sexuality, and identity using Restorative practices that Transform the lives of our students and the greater Miami community. Whether you are looking for a place to get involved, feel connected, or just hang out, come visit us at the center. Everyone is welcomed! Dr. Gisela P.

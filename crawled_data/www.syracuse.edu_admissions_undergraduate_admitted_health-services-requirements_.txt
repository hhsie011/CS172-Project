As the hub for student wellness, the Barnes Center at The Arch features programs, services and offerings that promote holistic health and well-being, all in one accessible, centralized space on campus. Connect with Health and Wellness Resources Health History and Immunization Forms Requirement Registering for classes and confirming housing assignments cannot be done until health history and immunization records are submitted. After you submit your Admission Acceptance Form, you will be able to log into the patient portal at suhsportal.syr.edu , submit your health history and upload your immunization/physical form prior to arriving on campus. Visit the First-Year and Transfer Programs Required Tasks webpage for a complete list and instructions. For additional information or questions, please contact the Medical Records Office at health@syr.edu or by calling 315.443.2667. Community Wellness Requirements Community Wellness Requirements are centered on sexual and relationship and violence prevention, alcohol and other drugs and diversity, equity and inclusion. Prior to arriving on campus, students will receive emails containing next step instructions for completing mandatory presentations and mandatory EVERFI Online Training Modules. These requirements will be due prior to arriving and throughout the first few weeks on campus. Please note that failure to complete these requirements will result in a Conduct Hold. Mandatory Health and Wellness Fee A $391 per semester health and wellness fee is mandatory for all full-time students, and is included with billing from the bursar’s office. Part-time students are eligible to use Barnes Center at The Arch Health Care on a fee-for-service basis. The health and wellness fee does not cover all expenses such as pharmacy, laboratory, and referrals to specialty service providers. Please bring a copy of your insurance card when you visit Barnes Center at The Arch Health Care. Student Health Insurance Enroll/Waive Form Requirement Each year, all full-time, matriculated undergraduate, graduate and law students must enroll in or waive out of the Student Health Insurance Plan through their MySlice account. Online students are not eligible to enroll in or required to waive the Student Health Insurance Plan. Prior to arriving to campus, students will receive emails containing next step instructions for enrolling into or waiving out of the Student Health Insurance Plan. Visit the First-Year and Transfer Programs Required Tasks webpage for a complete list and instructions. Students within traditionally online degree programs are not eligible to enroll in or waive the Student Health Insurance Plan. For additional information, contact the Student Health Insurance Office at 315.443.9019, healthinsurance@syr.edu or visit the Student Health Insurance webpage . Health Insurance Coverage All incoming full-time students and returning international students are required to carry health insurance that is compliant with the Affordable Care Act (ACA) and provides coverage for services received in the Syracuse area. You may already be covered by an ACA-compliant insurance plan, and will therefore be able to waive participation in the University-sponsored plan. If you are not already covered, you’ll be required to participate in the University-sponsored student health insurance plan. Review the 2021-22 rates and coverage dates for student health insurance. Rates for 2022-23 will be posted in Spring 2022. Barnes Center at The Arch Counseling Available to students, counseling surrounds comprehensive/holistic mental health, substance abuse and sexual assault/relationship violence related services. Barnes Center at The Arch Health Care The health and wellness team includes physicians, physician assistants, nurse practitioners, nurses and other health care professionals who provide students a variety of confidential general health care services. Prescription Refills To refill prescription(s) online through the Barnes Center at The Arch Pharmacy, simply complete the Refill Request form on the Patient Portal.

Veteran Students Propel your career within a high-demand industry. Learn how to assess and control organizational cyber risks with a curriculum designed and delivered by industry-experienced faculty. Choose from two specialization tracks that each feature built-in exam preparation modules for professional designations: CISA (IT Auditing) or CISSP (Cyber-Security). Admissions Criteria Candidates for the IT Auditing specialization track within the 4+1 program must meet the following guidelines for admission: Meet the general 4+1 program admissions criteria.

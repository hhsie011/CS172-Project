The Doctoral Hooding ceremony will take place at 5:00pm on Thursday, May 12 at the Brooks Center on Clemson's main campus. Graduation ceremonies for Master and Bachelor candidates are scheduled for May 12 and 13 in Clemson's Littlejohn Coliseum. Tickets will not be issued for guests attending the ceremonies. However, it is recommended that each graduate limits guests to a maximum of six. See additional Graduation Details. Graduation Ceremonies Thursday, May 12 9:00 a.m. Undergraduate Ceremony College of Architecture, Arts & Humanities College of Education College of Science 1:00 p.m. Masters Ceremony All Majors 5:00 p.m. Doctoral Hooding All Disciplines Friday, May 13 9:00 a.m. Undergraduate Ceremony College of Business 1:00 p.m. Undergraduate Ceremony College of Agriculture, Forestry & Life Science College of Behavioral, Social and Health Sciences 5:00 p.m. Undergraduate Ceremony College of Engineering, Computing & Applied Sciences Graduates should wear their Graduation Regalia to their ceremony. For any questions regarding your cap/gown or other regalia, please contact the Clemson University Bookstore.

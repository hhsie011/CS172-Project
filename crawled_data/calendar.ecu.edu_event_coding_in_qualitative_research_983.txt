The first hour will cover an overview of common types of codes and approaches (e.g., concept driven, data-driven) and attendees will practice coding qualitative data. The second hour will include using codes to develop themes, and other topics such as inter-rater reliability. Attendees are encouraged to bring their own qualitative work to practice coding or may use an example transcript provided by the instructor. Recommended resources on coding and qualitative analysis will be provided. Please register below!

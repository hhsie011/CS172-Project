School of Accounting and Computer Information Systems . Degree Requirements Business Core Requirements Major Requirements Additional Requirements The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a major in computer information systems provides a basic understanding of the significant role of information technologies in the business world. Students develop the skills necessary to employ information technology resources with the objective of enhancing organization and user performance. The major qualifies students for positions as information systems professionals in all sectors of the economy. CIS students may also be interested in the Cyber Security Minor . Computer Information Systems, B.S.B.A., Degree Requirements Tulsa Core Curriculum (20 hours) ENGL 1033 Exposition and Argumentation MATH 1143 Mathematical Functions MATH 1243 Applied Calculus BUS 4003 Effective Writing for Business Language (8 hours) Tulsa General Curriculum (25 hours) Block I, Aesthetic Inquiry and Creative Experience (6 hours) Block II, Historical and Social Interpretation (12 hours) ECON 2013 Principles of Economics I: Macroeconomics ECON 2023 Principles of Economics II: Microeconomics Other Block II electives (6 hours) Block III, Scientific Investigation (7 hours, including one hour of laboratory or field experience) General Education Electives (9 hours) Only courses from outside of the Collins College of Business may be used for general education electives. Business Core (39 hours) Freshman Core (5 hours) BUS 1243 Minding Your Business CIS 1001 Introduction to Spreadsheets FYE 1001 First Year Experience Sophomore Core (19 hours) ACCT 2113 Concepts in Accounting Information I ACCT 2123 Concepts in Accounting Information II BL 2013 Legal Environment of Business BUS 2001 Essential Business Skill Development ECON 2013 Principles of Economics I: Macroeconomics ECON 2023 Principles of Economics II: Microeconomics CIS 2003 Introduction to Computer Information Systems QM 2013 Statistics I QM 2023 Statistics II Junior/Senior Core (15 hours) FIN 3003 Business Finance MGT 3003 Principles of Management MGT 4073 Strategic Management MKTG 3003 Principles of Marketing QM 3023 Operations Management Computer Information Systems Major Requirements (24 hours) CIS 2013 Business Programming Concepts I CIS 3023 Business Programming Concepts II CIS 3043 Telecommunications CIS 4033 Business Programming Concepts III CIS 4043 Database Design and Applications CIS 4053 Systems Analysis and Design CIS 4093 Systems Development Project CIS electives (3000- or 4000- level) (3 hours) Free Electives (6 hours) Any non-required undergraduate course offered by the institution. Total = 123 hours Computer Information Systems, B.S.B.A., Additional Requirements CIS majors must complete ENGL 1033 , MATH 1143 , MATH 1243 (or higher level math courses), and BUS 4003 with a grade of C or higher. CIS majors must complete or show proficiency through the first year of a modern foreign language. CIS majors must complete each of the Business Core courses with a grade of C or higher, except for courses designated Pass/Fail. ECON 2013 and ECON 2023 are required courses in the Business Core which are also used to fulfill part of the Tulsa Curriculum Block II requirement.

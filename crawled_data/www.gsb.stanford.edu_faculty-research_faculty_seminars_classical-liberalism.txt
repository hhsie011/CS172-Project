Seminars Classical Liberalism Classical Liberalism Seminars The Classical Liberalism seminar series is open to faculty of different backgrounds and perspectives, spanning the arts, sciences, and humanities who are willing to debate ideas and policy issues with rigor, even when doing so may challenge orthodoxy. Classical liberalism seminars are held bi-weekly on Thursday, unless otherwise indicated.

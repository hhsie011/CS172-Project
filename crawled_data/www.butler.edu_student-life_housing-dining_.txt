And at Butler, you’ll share the experience with new roommates and neighbors, all part of a campus community that make residence halls feel more like homes. Some of your best relationships will begin right here. Nearly all incoming first-year students, and most sophomores and juniors, live on campus. You’re never far from anything you need—your classes, the library, the HRC, and late-night snacks are always within walking distance. The housing application and dining selection process will be available beginning March 15, 2022 for incoming first-year students who have their $500 enrollment deposit paid and processed. More information will be sent via email. In the meantime, explore our available housing options below. Office of Residence Life The Office of Residence Life is responsible for promoting and maintaining an environment on campus where students are free to develop academically and socially. Developing a sense of personal and communal responsibility and respecting the rights of others also is an important part of this program. Residence Life staff members oversee the management of Butler’s three residence halls and two apartment-style options. Campus Housing Options Irvington House Our newest state-of-the-art residence hall, which opened in fall 2018, features 647-bed, suite-style rooms. Explore Irvington House Residential College (ResCo) Housing primarily first-year students, Residential College (ResCo) is a coeducational residence hall community. ResCo offers a wide range of experiences to its students, staff, and four full-time, live-in faculty members. Explore ResCo Fairview House Home to primarily sophomore students, this 633-bed, state-of-the-art residence hall opened in 2016. Explore Fairview House University Apartments As a junior or senior at Butler University, students are eligible to live in the University Apartments! Students now have the option to live in Apartment Village, South Campus Apartments, Butler Terrace, and University Terrace. Explore University Apartments Butler Dining Butler Dining is run by Bon Appetit Management Company who specializes in scratch-made, simple menu items that represent a balanced plate for the student. Butler Dining offers many locations, from convenient stores and coffee shops to all-you-care-to-eat cafés. All students living in a Butler University residence hall are enrolled in a Resident Dining Plan.

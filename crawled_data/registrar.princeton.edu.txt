The Office of the Registrar is located at: Helm Building 330 Alexander Street, 4th floor Virtual Office Hours Please visit our Virtual Office Hours hosted Monday through Friday from 1:00 pm EST to 3:00 pm EST, excluding University holidays. You may also contact us at any time via email: registrar@princeton.edu. We will respond during normal business hours. Supporting Princeton's diverse academic community The Office of the Registrar is responsible for the maintenance, security, and release of student academic records. Additionally, we coordinate services in the areas of course information, course selection, grading, concentration declaration, degree progress, classroom assignment, certification of enrollment and degrees awarded, administration of final examinations. We provide robust University reporting and research functions in support of academic planning and advising. Many student and faculty academic services are available online via secure web resources which may be accessed from this site. Students and faculty may also come to our office in the Helm Building for information and for individual services.

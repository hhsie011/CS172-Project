Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index TTU Chess Menu Search TTU TTU Chess Welcome The Chess Program brings outstanding students from all over the world to Texas Tech University. Offering chess scholarships to qualified applicants at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. Competing at all division levels, the program is able to provide opportunities for all ratings from International Grandmasters to club players. Director and Head Coach Alex Onischuk is an internationally renowned Grandmaster and trainer who has trained successfully with a wide range of skill levels, from beginners to world champions.

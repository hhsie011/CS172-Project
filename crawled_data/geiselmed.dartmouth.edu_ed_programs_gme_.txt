Faculty Positions at Geisel News Geisel NewsCenter Dartmouth Medicine Magazine Geisel in the News Geisel Insider For the Media Campaign Affiliated Graduate Medical Education Graduate Medical Education at Dartmouth-Hitchcock Medical Center (DHMC) puts you in the midst of one of the top academic medical centers in the country, a health care system recognized nationally for its leadership and for the quality of its patient care. With more than 30 ACGME-accredited residency and fellowship programs, we offer a full range of graduate medical training in a region that leads the nation in providing quality health care. The clinicians with whom you will do your GME training occupy the top ranks in their fields and hold faculty appointments at the Geisel School of Medicine. The Dartmouth-Hitchcock system includes Mary Hitchcock Memorial Hospital, the Dartmouth-Hitchcock Clinic and the Veterans Affairs Medical Center. We provide comprehensive medical care for 1.8 million people in New Hampshire, Vermont and Massachusetts.

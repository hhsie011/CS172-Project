Use the previous and next buttons to change the displayed slide Slide 1: Featured art Slide 2: Featured art Previous Next April 29, 2016 - April 30, 2016 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM The International Studio & Curatorial Program (ISCP) Spring Open Studios is a two-day exhibition of international contemporary art. The 38 artists and three curators from 19 countries currently in residence will present work in their studios. Reception and Open Studio Hours will take place at the International Studio & Curatorial Program, 1040 Metropolitan Avenue, Brooklyn Opening Reception: Friday, April 29, 6 – 9pm Guest speaker: Tom Finkelpearl, Commissioner of the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs, will make opening remarks on Friday, April 29, at 6pm. Open Studio Hours: Saturday, April 30, 1 – 8pm The Premio New York brings promising younger Italian artists to New York for a residency at the International Studio and Curatorial Program (ISCP) in Brooklyn, with the support of the Italian Academy, the Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Italian Cultural Institute in New York.

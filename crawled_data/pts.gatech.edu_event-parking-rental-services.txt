Phone: (404) 894-0061 Email: support@pts.gatech.edu Online support form Parking Validations Non-refundable validations can be personalized to fit specific time requirements and preferred parking locations, and can be purchased for small or large groups for up to several days. There is a minimum order of 10 validations. Georgia Tech departments are required to pay for validations using a PeopleSoft account number, a 10-digit document ID number, and an account code. Off-campus organizations and individuals can pay with credit card or money order. Please do not include credit card numbers; a representative will contact you with further instructions.

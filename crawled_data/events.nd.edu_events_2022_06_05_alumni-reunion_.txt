Skip To Content Skip To Navigation Skip To Search University of Notre Dame Office of Public Affairs and Communications Notre Dame Events Menu Search Search Home > Events > Alumni Reunion Alumni Reunion Time: Sun Jun 5, 2022, All Day Location: campus-wide Reunion 2022 is scheduled to return in full to Notre Dame’s campus from June 2 through June 5, 2022. This celebration will be the largest, welcoming back classes that end in 2s and 7s, the Classes of 1995, 1996, 2015, and 2016, Hispanic alumni, and all Notre Dame alumnae to mark 50 years of coeducation. Additional information — tentative agendas, accommodations, registration — has been shared on the Reunion website, within class and group communities on myNotreDame, and via email in the spring of 2022. Please contact the Notre Dame Alumni Association’s Reunion team at reunion@nd.edu with any questions.

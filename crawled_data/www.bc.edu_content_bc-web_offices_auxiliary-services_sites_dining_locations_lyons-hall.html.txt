Lyons is also open for Late Night on Sunday-Wednesday from 7:00 p.m.–12:00 a.m. Our late night menu features student favorites such as mozzarella sticks, chicken tenders, cheese fries, meatball subs, mac and cheese, vegetarian chili, and more. What's Open? Hours of Operation Monday – Friday 8:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. sunday – wednesday 7:00 p.m. – 12:00 a.m. Schedules subject to change. See What's Open Now.

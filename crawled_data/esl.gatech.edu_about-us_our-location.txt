The Georgia Institute of Technology, also known as Georgia Tech, is a top-ranked public college and one of the leading research universities in the USA. Georgia Tech provides a technologically focused education to more than 25,000 undergraduate and graduate students in fields ranging from engineering, computing, and sciences, to business, design, and liberal arts. It is an international city where many students from all over the world come to study. With a population of 5 million in the metropolitan area, Atlanta is the largest city in the state and the 9th largest metropolitan area in the US. Atlanta has a moderate climate which enables students to enjoy many outdoor activities such as walking, biking, and exploring local parks. Atlanta About Atlanta Why Choose Atlanta?

(Hand)shake On It Good things start with Handshake—our online job portal open to all Butler students and alums. Schedule advising appointments, search job openings and look up job fairs on this resource, used by many of the top institutions in the state (and nationwide). Our talented Career Advising and Exploration team is here to help make that happen for you. Meet Our Team For Employers Great Hires Start Here There’s a reason why more than 2,000 companies a year look to Butler students to fill jobs and internships: Our liberal arts-based education produces graduates able to think critically, communicate effectively, and solve problems readily. Turn to the Midwest’s #1 ranked university to fill jobs at campus career fairs, through online postings and via Butler BlueWork, our micro-internship portal. Connect with Students For Alumni Lifetime Career Support You’re never really done building your career: As long as you’re at work, you’ll be strengthening your skills. Wherever you are on your professional journey, Career and Professional Success will be by your side. Whether you’re looking to advance in your chosen field or considering something entirely different, you can turn to us for free, for life. Explore Your Options Jobs Happen Here Every year, Butler welcomes more than 500 employers to campus for job fairs or interviews. Whether you’re looking for a job or internship, or a qualified candidate, let your search begin and end on campus.

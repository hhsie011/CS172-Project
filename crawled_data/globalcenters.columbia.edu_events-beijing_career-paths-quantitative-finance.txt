Events Past Event Career Paths in Quantitative Finance Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo March 25, 2021 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM Online Time: 8:00 - 9:00 PM (Beijing) | 8:00 - 9:00 AM (New York) Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, Career Placement Office of Columbia Business School, and Graduate Career Development of Columbia University Graduate School of Arts and Sciences will jointly host a panel discussion to provide Columbia students with an opportunity to connect with alumni and industry leaders, and learn from their career paths. Points to be covered: How the panelists' university education is relevant to their particular positions; The realities and trends of the sectors they are working in; Strategies and tips for job hunting; Opportunities within their fields; Resources for connecting with professionals. Panelists: Amber XU, Director of Overseas Department at Zhaopin.com Xu graduated from University of California, Davis with more than 7 years’ experience of studying and working abroad. She once hosted positions in financial, cultural and entertainments start-up companies. Xu joined Zhaopin.com since 2015 and was devoted herself in exploring and developing overseas business. She leads many marketing activities in Zhaopin.com such as “Elite Club”, Overseas Intelligent Alliance, Overseas Universities Campus Tour etc., winning unanimous recognition from clients. She works with many well-known employers including TenCent, SF, Vanke, China Resources, FAW-Volkswagen, BAIC, Ping An, China Mobile Suzhou Research Institute, Country Garden, Foshan Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, State Administration Foreign Experts Affairs, Vivo, Oppo, Zijin Mining etc. Dr. Bo HUANG SEAS '14, Executive Director & Portfolio Manager at Jasper Capital Dr. Huang oversees technical signal research and production, which includes analyzing low/high frequency market data, generating high quality alpha factors using statistical modeling and machine learning, applying machine learning models on dynamic factor integration. He is also responsible for developing mid/high frequency strategies. Before joining Jasper Capital, he gained crucial financial expertise as a portfolio manager at Infore Capital Management. Dr. Huang started his career at Bank of America Merrill Lynch (Arxis Capital LLC split-off) in the U.S. as a quantitative strategist. He holds a Ph.D. in industrial engineering and operation research from Columbia University. Zhuxi CAI GSAS '14, Data Scientist at Harvest Fun Cai is a Data Scientist at Harvest Fund. He specializes in risk modeling and machine learning. Prior to joining Harvest Fund, Cai previously worked as data scientist at JD and DiDi. Reminders: Event language: Chinese This event is open to Columbia University students and alumni only.

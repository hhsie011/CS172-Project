May 7, 2022 - June 7, 2022 All day Recurring Event Add To My Calendar Buzzin Back to School Backpack Drive This year, GTHR has partnered again with SchoolKitz.com to offer pre-packaged supply kits. This will streamline the donation process and make sure all backpacks are filled uniformly. Each school kit purchased sponsors one child. If you would like to sponsor a child(ren), the deadline to order supplies from SchoolKitz is Tuesday, June 7. If you would like to request a backpack(s), the deadline is Friday, June 10.

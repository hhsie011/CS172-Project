Contact the agencies and organizations administering your specific benefits to update your address with them directly. See below for more information. Update Your Business Information Access the Change of Business Information Form to update office/business location and/or phone number. Please allow 3-4 business days for the change to appear in the online phonebook. Update Your Legal Name Faculty, Staff and Student Workers Faculty and staff can use one of the following options to update their legal name on file with the Office of Human Resources: Visit the Administrative Services Building during regular business hours, and be sure to bring your Social Security Card reflecting the new name. In the orange drop box outside the Administrative Services Building, drop a copy of your updated Social Security Card along with a note that references the current last name on file and requests to update your name. Mail a copy of your updated Social Security Card to the Office of Human Resources. Along with the updated Social Security Card, provide a note which includes the currently listed last name and requests to update your name. Attn: Data Center Clemson University Office of Human Resources 108 Perimeter Road Clemson, SC 29634 Do not email a copy of your Social Security Card or Social Security Number. Students Students must follow the legal name change procedure provided by Records and Registration. Update Your Preferred Name Faculty, staff and students can manage their preferred name through my.clemson.edu: Log into my.Clemson using your Clemson credentials Click your profile picture and select “My Profile” Select “Name” Select “Manage Preferred Name” Select “Edit” Review the information, select “I Agree,” enter your preferred name and select “Save” Update Your Information with Your Benefits Vendors In addition to updating your address on file with the University, be sure to contact the agencies and/or organizations administering your specific benefits to update your address with them directly. Insurance Programs State Insurance Package: Log into your PEBA MyBenefits profile* Central Bank Health Savings Account: Log into your Central Bank Account Retirement Programs SCRS Members: Log into your PEBA Member Access profile* State ORP Members: Log into your PEBA Member Access profile* AND contact your ORP vendor directly 401k, 403b and 457b supplemental retirement plan account holders: Contact your elected plan vendor *Name changes cannot be made online through MyBenefits or Member Access. To update your name on file with PEBA for state insurance and/or retirement, complete and submit the Name/Address Change form to PEBA.

Your contribution as a member helps support The Library’s services. As a member, you may request books from off-site for free, check books out, and enjoy on-site access to all Library resources. Annual membership in The Library is $360. (Alumni membership is $100.) You can join online or by mailing a check to: The Library of The Jewish Theological Seminary The Jewish Theological Seminary 3080 Broadway New York, NY 10027-4649 Please contact a librarian at library@jtsa.edu if you would like to become a member but are not able to make the full annual contribution.

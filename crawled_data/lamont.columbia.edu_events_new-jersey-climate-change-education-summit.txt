February 4, 2022 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM America/New_York Online New Jersey Climate Change Education Summit Let us come together to discuss sustainability and justice as key components of New Jersey's Climate Education policy. The Center for Sustainable Development at the Earth Institute, Columbia University will be hosting this event. This will be the 2nd annual Climate Change Education Summit to discuss policy and practice to further Climate Change Education in New Jersey. Partners to the event include: New Jersey Audubon Biotrail New York UN Sustainable Solutions Network SDGs Today UN Sustainable Solutions Network USA The Summit will include free workshops led by youth activists, educators, education and sustainability policy experts help to further participants' skills on climate storytelling, community organizing and thinking about ways to integrate learning and action toward sustainability and justice in schools and communities.

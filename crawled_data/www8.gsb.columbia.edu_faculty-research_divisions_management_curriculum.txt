Academic Divisions » Management » Management Curriculum Management Curriculum Management Faculty Curriculum MBA EMBA Doctoral Electives by Topic Seminars Doctoral The faculty of the Management Division is responsible for staffing both required and elective courses in the MBA, EMBA, and PhD programs. The core courses in the MBA and EMBA programs are staffed primarily by tenured and tenure-track professors. Elective courses in the two Masters programs are staffed by regular and adjunct faculty. Doctoral level courses are taught by tenured and tenure-track faculty.

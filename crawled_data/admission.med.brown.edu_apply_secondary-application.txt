Once your application is verified by AMCAS, we will send you electronic mail instructions for the online secondary application. No secondary application received after December 1 will be considered, and incomplete applications are not reviewed by the Admissions Committee. The secondary application contains Brown-specific information and requires a nonrefundable fee of $110. AMCAS fee waivers are accepted. If you are applying to the Combined MD-ScM program, you will select that option on your secondary application. See our FAQ page for more information about the MD-ScM application and admission process. Candidates selected for interview are notified by email. All candidates should receive a notice of interview status by early March. Only those candidates who are interviewed will be considered for admission.

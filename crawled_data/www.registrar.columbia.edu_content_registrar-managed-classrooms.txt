Request for Transcript - Application for Replacement of Diploma - Request to Withhold or Reverse Withholding of Directory Information - Name Change Affidavit Home > Registrar Classrooms > Registrar-Managed Classrooms Registrar-Managed Classrooms The Office of the University Registrar manages the selection and use of the 142 classrooms listed below. For your convenience, the list shows classrooms by building, with the number of seats and images of the room included. Classrooms supported by Columbia University Information Technology (CUIT) can be clicked for additional information regarding multimedia and equipment availability. During the first three weeks of each fall and spring semester, the Office of the University Registrar does not fulfill requests for non-course events. Because this period is always a critical time of shifting enrollments for courses, we must prioritize the needs of our teaching Faculty for classrooms for course-related instruction. Beginning the fourth week of the term, all requests for non-course events in Registrar-managed classrooms will be coordinated through University Event Management (UEM). Requests can be made conveniently online using Virtual EMS (no account required).

about Butler is to actually see Butler. Start with a virtual tour—then, when you’re ready for more, we’ll be waiting for you in Indianapolis. Ways to Visit Butler Explore visit options tailored to your student type below. High School Student Visits Whether you’ve just started thinking about college or you’re beginning the application process, we offer a range of virtual and in-person visits tailored to every stage of the search process. These visits are available for high school students of any age. See Visits for High School Students Admitted Student Visits Recently admitted to Butler? We’re off-the-leash excited to have you! Come to one of our Admitted Student Visits to get in that #ButlerBound frame of mind. See Visits for Admitted Students Transfer Visits You’re looking for a good fit and a smooth transition. Choose from a range of on-campus and virtual visits, or meet 1-on-1 with an admission counselor to discuss applying and transferring credits. See Visits for Transfer Students International Visits You’re coming a long way—but you’re not alone: Butler students come from more than 22 countries. Many of those students start with a virtual visit and online conversation with one of our admission counselors. See Visits for International Students Sponsored Groups Sometimes, seeing college is the spark it takes to get to college. We offer info sessions and campus tours for school groups and educational organizations. See Sponsored Group Visits High School Visits and College Fairs Can’t get here? Our admission counselors visit dozens of high schools and college fairs every year in the Midwest and beyond (and virtually!). We talk about all things Butler: academics, athletics, campus life, you name it. Meet Butler at Fairs Near You Can’t be here in person? Take a Virtual Tour Want to get a feel for Butler, but can’t make it in-person? Our virtual tour will let you digitally experience all the sights and sounds.

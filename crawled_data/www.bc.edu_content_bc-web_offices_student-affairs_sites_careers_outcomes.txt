Ninety-six percent of graduates from Boston College’s Class of 2021 were employed, enrolled in graduate school, or involved in a fellowship or volunteer experience. The findings, underscore the value of a BC education and showcase the impressive accomplishments of the students who earned their diplomas during an unprecedented, challenging year.

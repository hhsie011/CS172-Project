It’s a challenging but rewarding journey, and we’ve found that the best results are achieved when everyone—students, parents and guardians, counselors, and admissions staff—prioritize finding the right fit community for each student. In order to best understand how this process works, and whether Columbia is the right fit for your child, we encourage you to review information we’ve posted about the first-year application, our admissions process, and our approach to financial aid. These pages will help orient you in the process and tell you what you and your child need to know to make informed decisions about the best course of action for your family. We’ve also organized links to the information that parents most often request for easy reference.

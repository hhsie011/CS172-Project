NEWCOMB-TULANE COLLEGE The College Dean Lee Skinner has a vision for Newcomb-Tulane College. She believes her job is to help the rest of the college do their jobs better. Collectively, we ensure the academic rigor and integrity of the undergraduate curriculum, empower students to seek out intellectual challenges and to build academic pathways that create meaning for them and educate students to live their lives fully.

Admission requirements Inside the Program Older adults benefit from student-led exercise classes through Wichita State Highly trained graduate, practicum and undergraduate internship students are using their skills to lead exercise classes for older adults in the community. The program is called WellREP and is an evidence-based, progressive exercise class specially designed for older adults. More than 150 older adults are served at 10 locations in the Wichita area. Curriculum The minor in aging studies consists of 15 credit hours.

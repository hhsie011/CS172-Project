Class Day Details Date: Tuesday, May 17, 2022 Time: 8:00 a.m. Location: South Lawn, Morningside Campus Speaker: Tom Kitt, Award-winning composer and arranger Diploma: Diplomas will be mailed to students following graduation. Based on the degree conferral date for all graduates, diplomas will be sent to the "Diploma (DP) Address" that is on file. Graduates may update their “DP Address" on SSOL when they apply for their degree.

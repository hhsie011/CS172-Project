Get the Right Tools Teams Phone Teams phone enables you and your team to make and receive calls from your office phone from anywhere. Desk phone services are also available for on-campus users. Learn more about Voice and Telephone Services. Learn more about Teams Phone Chat and Collaboration Microsoft Teams is the only centrally supported university-wide chat and collaboration tool. Learn more about Microsoft Teams Calendaring Calendaring is available through Outlook in Microsoft 365. Individual calendaring options include creating and accepting meeting invitations with colleagues. Group, or resource, calendaring is available for coordinating vacation schedules among a team. Learn more about Outlook calendaring Duo Duo is required to sign in for many services on campus, including Workday and the UT Directory. Learn more about Duo VPN If you're rarely on campus connecting to the VPN once a week will keep your workstation secure. Learn more about VPN Cloud Storage UT offers two cloud storage solutions, UT Box and Microsoft One Drive. Using one of these two services ensures that your work is securely saved for you to work from anywhere.

Interested in forming friendships with peers who have similar interests? If so, you will find an abundance of opportunities at HBS, from student government to clubs and everything in between. With more than 95 clubs and over 200 leadership positions in the Student Association, there are many ways for students to refine their leadership and organizational management experience, explore interests, and make friendships that will last a lifetime. 2018 Class Day Student Speaker Serrena Iyer Video 2018 Class Day Student Speaker Serrena Iyer The Student Association The Student Association (SA) is the main interface between the MBA student body and the faculty and administration. Its goal is to represent the student body in making the HBS experience as rewarding and enjoyable as possible. All MBA students are considered members of the SA. → More about the Student Association Other Activities Club Conferences Associations New Venture Competition Clubs Student Clubs are a vital part of the HBS Community experience that support the school's mission to educate leaders who make a difference in the world.

Anchored by the College of Veterinary Medicine, North Carolina State University’s 250-acre Centennial Biomedical Campus (CBC) is an extension of NC State’s Centennial Campus. In partnership with corporate and government entities, the focus of this campus is on biomedical research, both to animals and humans.

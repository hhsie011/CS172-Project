Our Services The Internal Communications team supports departments within Operations at Yale University with the following services: Recognition and Event Planning We provide recognition program guidance, support, and resources including town halls, recognition events, and social events for Operations staff. Writing and Editorial Support In collaboration with subject matter experts, we offer writing and editorial support for multiple publications, messages, and website content. Strategic Communications We provide consultation and collaborate with Operations colleagues to develop and implement a variety of strategic communications plans. Web Development We offer support and develop new features for websites, including It’s Your Yale, Emergency Management, Facilities, Workday at Yale, and more. Marketing Based on your goals, we partner with you to develop strategy, identify channels, and create materials to promote events, campaigns, and services. Multimedia We design graphics, capture photos, and develop videos, in support of Operational initiatives.

Our collective future is bright, and I am honored to help us transition to a new moment in our history." - UofL Interim President Lori Stewart Gonzalez In fall 2018, former University of Louisville President Neeli Bendapudi announced that the university would begin the process of developing a strategic plan to guide its actions in becoming a great place to learn, to work and in which to invest. This process will be inclusive, open and transparent, incorporating input from campus constituencies and from the community UofL serves. The resulting plan will be shared with the campus community in fall 2019.

Accounting Services & Financial Reporting also serves the campus community by processing a variety of financial transactions including expenditure transfer requests, chargebacks (recharges), invoicing, (cost recovery/reimbursed activities) and trust accounting. We prepare the campus Financial Statements and provide accounting data for internal and external entities and ensure regulatory compliance for the general accounting and reporting function of the University.

Center for Career Education With experienced staff who are dedicated to helping you develop and reach your goals in every stage of your career development, Columbia’s Center for Career Education is an invaluable resource to both undergraduates and alumni. Working with you to develop key competencies, generate ideas and opportunities, and establish connections with employers and organizations, CCE supports the pursuit of your greatest personal and professional objectives, both during your time on campus and throughout your career. student: When I was in the application process for my internship with the Governor of Arkansas, I used the Center for Career Education to review my resume and cover letter so I could put my best foot forward.

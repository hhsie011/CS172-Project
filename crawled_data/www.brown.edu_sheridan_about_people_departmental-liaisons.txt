Data Science Course Design Institute Data Science Fellows News & Events Upcoming Events Past Events Newsletter Sheridan Center Departmental Liaisons The Sheridan Center maintains a network of Faculty and Graduate Student Liaisons representing each academic department or program on campus. These liaisons communicate departmental teaching needs to the Center and keep departmental colleagues apprised of Center events, programs and services. In recent years, the liaisons' work has focused on these topics: 2016-17: Sheridan Center strategic planning 2017-18: Graduate student advising and mentoring (in collaboration with the Graduate School) 2018-19: Academic integrity (in collaboration with the Academic Integrity Working Group) 2019-20: GTA professional development (in collaboration with the Graduate School) 2020-21: Relationship-Rich Education (a topic suggested by the College) Spring 2019 Faculty and Graduate Student Liaison Meeting Faculty and Graduate Student Liaisons for the current academic year

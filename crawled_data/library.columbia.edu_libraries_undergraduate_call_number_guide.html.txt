Check the Milstein shelving guide to find the specific reading room where this call number item is located. Circulation policies Milstein books may be borrowed for semester loan. For more information see borrowing privileges. Items may be checked out at either the Butler Circulation Desk (3rd floor). They may be renewed online. Please return or renew items promptly to avoid fines and fees. Milstein books may be returned to the Circulation Desk (3rd floor). When the library is closed, please return books to the outdoor book bins. What to do if a book is not on the shelf If a book you are searching for is not on its expected location on the shelf, and is not checked out, you may request a search for the book at the Circulation Desk (3rd floor). Please be sure to have the book's author, title, and call number. If all copies of the book are checked out, you may request a copy through Borrow Direct.

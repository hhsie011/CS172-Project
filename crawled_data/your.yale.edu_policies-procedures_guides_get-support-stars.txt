Create an online personal account to apply for jobs. Receive email notifications regarding your job application status. Create, update, and store multiple versions of your resume and/or cover letter using the online STARS Resume Manager feature. Next Steps A Human Resources recruiter will review your resume and forward your application/resume to the hiring manager, if you meet the minimum requirements for the job.

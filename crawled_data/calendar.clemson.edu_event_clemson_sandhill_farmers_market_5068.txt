Sign Up Log In Search Search Clemson Events › Event Details Clemson Sandhill Farmers Market The market is home to farmers from around the region. Items available include fresh local produce, meat, fish, eggs, butter, milk, cheese, honey, plants, flowers, shrubs, and baked goods. Many products are organic. Also present are several skilled crafters and artisans with a variety of offerings.

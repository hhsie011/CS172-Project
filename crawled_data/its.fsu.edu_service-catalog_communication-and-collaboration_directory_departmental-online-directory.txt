The FSU directory makes it easy to find department addresses and phone numbers accessible online or via our touch tone phone system. Searchable | utilize the webpage search bar or phone keypad to find exactly what you are looking for Multiple formats | access the directory online, in print or via phone with Interactive Voice Response (IVR) Dial in Access | access the Interactive Voice Response system, you may dial "0" from a 644 or 645 campus telephone line or (850) 644-2525 Different views | search for departments using the alphabetical list or organizational view Cost There is no charge for this service.

Effective August 10, 2020, the Registrar’s Office will be open for business in Room 104, Tech Tower Submitted by David Barbero on Friday, August 7, 2020 Published August 7, 2020 Effective August 10, 2020, the Registrar’s Office will be open for business in Room 104, Tech Tower. In order to provide the best customer service to students, we are offering our services in a variety of virtual, online formats. Drop-Off/Pick-Up Service Drop-off or pick-up in-person service for documents that need Registrar’s Office processing or review may be conducted Monday-Friday in Room 104 Tech Tower between the hours of 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. All other services are provided during standard office hours Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM., in a variety of virtual formats. See more information below. Email Use the following email addresses to contact us depending on the nature of your question. General questions: comments@registrar.gatech.edu Transfer credit, test credit, and degree-related questions: dc@registrar.gatech.edu Diploma address updates and inquiries: diplomas@registrar.gatech.edu Telephone Calls Particularly at certain times of the year, our telephone lines are very busy. You will receive better and faster service if you contact us via email. Especially if you have a complex question, we do not recommend that you call our main number. Use one of the email addresses listed above, let us research your question and reach back out to you so that the right person can assist you. Meeting with a Staff Member If you need to meet with a staff member, please use email to contact us so that we can set up a video conference on Teams, Bluejeans, or another medium. Safe Interaction with Staff Anyone who interacts with Registrar’s Office staff must follow all safety requirements as stated by the University System of Georgia, Georgia Tech, and the CDC. Remain mindful of the entrances and exits that are separate, the floor markers, the stanchions, and other instructions for the elevator and the restrooms.

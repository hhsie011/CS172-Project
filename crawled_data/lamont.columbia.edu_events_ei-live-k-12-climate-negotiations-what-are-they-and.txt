Events Past Event [EI LIVE K-12] Climate Negotiations – What Are They and... Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo October 25, 2021 11:00 AM - 11:45 AM America/New_York Online K-12 Education Climate Negotiations – What Are They and What to Expect? We are approaching the 26th session of annual climate negotiations (in November 2021), and yet we are still seeing a worsening climate crisis. In this session we will discuss how climate negotiations work, the progress they’ve achieved so far, and what to expect in the future. Presenter: Melody Braun, Senior Staff Associate, International Research Institute for Climate and Society Target Audience: Grades 6-12 This event is free but registration is required.

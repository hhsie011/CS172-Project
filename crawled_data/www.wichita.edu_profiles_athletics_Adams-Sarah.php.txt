Sarah Adams joined the staff at Wichita State in June 2021 as Senior Associate Athletic Director/Senior Women's Administrator. Adams came to WSU from Eastern Washington University where she served as Senior Associate Athletic Director and Senior Woman Administrator from October 2018 until November of 2019. She was promoted to Deputy Athletic Director in December of 2019 and served in that role until June of 2021.

The Loft is almost entirely student run and was opened in collaboration with BC Dining and the students of Real Food BC. Order now, pick up at Addie's. What's Open? Hours of Operation Sunday – Thursday (Dinner) 5:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m. Sunday – Thursday (GET pick-up) 5:00 p.m. – 8:30 p.m. Schedules subject to change. See What's Open Now.

B6801 - Operations Management B6101- Business Analytics B6014 - Managerial Statistics frameborder=&amp;amp;quot;0&amp;amp;quot; allowfullscreen&amp;amp;gt; DRO Flex Core Courses The Decision, Risk, and Operations Division offers one flex core course under the "Performance" menu. B5201 - Operations Strategy Electives The Decision, Risk, and Operations Division offers two types of electives: courses focusing on quantitative methods for analyzing business problems, and courses specific to the operations functional area within a corporation. These electives generally emphasize the use of analytic tools and quantitative techniques, drawing on the Statistics, Decision Models, and Operations Management core courses. The courses are particularly well suited for students interested in pursuing a career in consulting, operations management, or any career that requires substantial quantitative skills.

Flyers outlining various UofM benefits are available online for both faculty and staff. A full-time regular work week is Monday through Friday, 7.5 hours per day for a total of 37.5 hours per week. This is subject to change depending upon the job and department for which you are applying. The hiring department will be able to explain any exceptions.

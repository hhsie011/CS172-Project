Experience Now Student Experience Adventure Leadership The Student Experience Adventure Leadership Semester (SEALs) was created for students to pursue outdoor leadership and education opportunities & learn the skills to teach outdoor adventures to others. Explore Wilderness Medicine If you plan to spend time outside or in the wilderness, you need to be prepared. This two-day course is fast-paced and hands-on. Get Certified Outdoor Exercise Research and our own experience show that exercising in the outdoors, especially on or around water helps with mental and physical health.

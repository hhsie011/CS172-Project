Contact Coordinator Curriculum This 15-credit-hour certificate is comprised the following courses: Business Software - Word Business Software - Excel Business Software - PowerPoint and Access Cyber Risk Management Secure Web Development Systems Analysis and Design Management of IS Function View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor."

Planning Strategy: Just south of Penn Park is Penn's River Fields, including the recently-refurbished trio of facilities that serve as the home for Penn Soccer, Field Hockey, and Track & Field. Program: Rhodes Field, home field for both women's and men's soccer, included elevated stands for 650 spectators, a 180-degree rotating scoreboard, and the Rapaport Family Suite for alumni gatherings. Ellen Vagelos C'90 Field was designed for women's field hockey with Astroturf. It shares a rotating scoreboard with Rhodes Field. Irving "Moon" Mondschein Throwing Complex is a new facility for javelin, shot put, discus, and hammer.

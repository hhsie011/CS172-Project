Pages All Degree Programs Marketing Research and Analytics Admissions Curriculum Faculty Financing Your Degree Request Information Apply Now Accelerate Your Career in Marketing Research Enhance your marketing career with an in-depth understanding of the latest techniques and tools used to collect and analyze data in marketing research. The Master of Science in Marketing Research and Analytics at Temple University’s Fox School of Business will equip you with in-demand skills in quantitative and analytical market techniques so you can competitively position yourself in a fast-growing industry. In an online- and part-time format, working professionals will gain the support and knowledge to advance their careers with our market-driven curriculum. Fast Facts About Our Master’s in Marketing Research and Analytics 100% online with live classes 30 credits, 20-month completion pathway Hands-on experience with a variety of software tools Experiential capstone in developing a strategic marketing plan for a live client Built-in opportunity to earn industry certifications in Google Analytics and SAS Drive Effective Decisions with In-Demand Skills The Master of Science in Marketing Research and Analytics can support your career potential with experiential learning focused on developing your skills in areas that matter most for marketing researchers today, including: Strong and diverse statistical analysis Data visualization Integrating multiple data sources (such as big data and machine learning) Online quantitative and innovative research methods Advanced survey research modalities (including text analytics and social listening) Real-time virtual engagement with classmates and faculty will give you the opportunity to expand your network, while the program’s advisory council will provide real-world connections to the marketing industry. Through the Master of Science in Marketing Research and Analytics, you’ll graduate competitively positioned for a variety of career paths including but not limited to marketing research, consumer insights, product development, research methods and consumer experience and analytics. Admissions Fall enrollment period. Curriculum Keep up with industry trends with our market-driven curriculum focused on the most in-demand skills for market researchers.

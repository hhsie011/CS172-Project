Through campus visits, district visits, and briefings at the State Capitol and on Capitol Hill in Washington, D.C., each day we share knowledge and support, and showcase Georgia Tech’s tradition of excellence. Vice President Bert Reeves Bert Reeves serves as the vice president for Institute Relations, and oversees university relations with federal, state, and local government officials. On campus, he reports to Georgia Tech’s president and works closely with members of the president’s cabinet and other campus leaders to preserve and enhance external support for the Institute. A.

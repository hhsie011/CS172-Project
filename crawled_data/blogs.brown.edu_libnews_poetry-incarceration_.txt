It is an open-ended examination of the challenges and potential for communication across the socio-economic divide engendered by mass incarceration. Online exhibit: Poetry from the Age of Mass Incarceration Dates: September 21, 2015 – January 4, 2016 Time: Open to the public 10 a.m. – 5 p.m. Monday through Friday Location: Willis Reading Room Gallery, John Hay Library, 20 Prospect Street, Providence This entry was posted in Uncategorized by Jennifer Braga.

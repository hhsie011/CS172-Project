You will have access to University Recreation facilities for the semester that you are currently enrolled in. Distance Learning If all of the classes you are currently enrolled in are online, you have not been charged a University Recreation fee through your tuition and fees. To access University Recreation facilities, you will need to pay the fee. Pay the fee at University Recreation Monday through Friday from 8:00am-6:00pm. Use the Elect-to-Pay online option that adds the charge back to your tuition and fees. Teaching/Research Assistants If you are a graduate student with a Teaching Assistantship or Research Assistantship, your University Recreation fee will not be applied. To access University Recreation facilities, you will need to pay the fee one of the two following ways. Pay the fee at University Recreation Monday through Friday from 8:00am-6:00pm. Use the Elect-to-Pay online option that adds the charge back to your tuition and fees. Purchasing an annual membership saves you $60 over the monthly price as a Campus Affiliate. Summer Memberships Interested in a membership only for the summer months? You can purchase multiple months of the monthly membership at one time. Perfect for cooling off at the Leisure Pool! Can I get a membership for my spouse or depandants? Yes, Campus members can purchase memberships for their spouse and dependents (17+ years old) at the Campus membership rate. To purchase a spouse membership, the spouse will need to bring their driver's license and proof of marriage (marriage license, shared bank statement, etc.). Dependants 4 - 16 years old are eligible for a dependant membership. Dependants 3 and under do not require a membership to enter University Recreation facilities.

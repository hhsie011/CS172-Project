Archives Programs and Initiatives Our diversity programs promote inclusive excellence. NC State builds strength from the variety of perspectives and experiences of our community. The Office for Institutional Equity and Diversity advocates for equity, diversity and inclusiveness as critical components in our work to transform both local and global communities. We strive to be a champion of diversity and inclusion efforts so that all members of the community feel welcomed and supported, and the dignity of all people is respected and valued. Explore our programs and initiatives to learn how you can get involved. Engage with the stories of faculty, staff and students and the diverse communities we serve. Learn about how OIED supports diversity initiatives for faculty, staff and students. Diversity Programs and Initiatives Inclusive Excellence Certificate The IE Certificate focuses on providing the foundational components of diversity, equity and inclusion through four two-hour workshops. In this thoughtful and empowering program, participants examine several aspects of diversity and inclusion; Learn more The Credential In The Credential, our certificate program for faculty and staff, participants have the opportunity to learn strategies for understanding, utilizing and promoting cultural competence in their respective roles. Learn more Recognizing Excellence in Diversity The annual Recognizing Excellence in Diversity (RED) Event honors faculty, staff, colleges, students and student organizations' contributions in the areas of equity, diversity and inclusion. Learn more Inclusive Excellence Summer Intensive - July 28, 2022 The Inclusive Excellence Summer Intensive program (a shortened version of our Inclusive Excellence Certification program) is a chance to refresh and strengthen your foundational diversity, equity, and inclusion skills with other NC State employees. Learn More Building Future Faculty Program The NC State Building Future Faculty (BFF) Program is a two-and-half day all-expenses paid workshop for doctoral students and post-doctoral scholars who are interested in pursuing academic careers and who are committed to promoting diversity in higher education. Learn more Intercultural Development Inventory The Intercultural Development Inventory® (IDI®) is the premier cross-cultural assessment of intercultural competence that is used by thousands of individuals and organizations to build intercultural competence to achieve international and domestic diversity and inclusion goals and outcomes. Learn more Faculty Fellows Program OIED’s Faculty Fellows program serves to recognize and cultivate the instrumental relationships that faculty members have with students and the university community, a relationship that has the ability to further foster a sense of belonging and cultivate inclusive environments at NC State University.

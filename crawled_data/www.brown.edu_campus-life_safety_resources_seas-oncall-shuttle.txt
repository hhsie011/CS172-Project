SEAS onCall riders must register with the Student and Employee Accessibility Services office and provide documentation of their disability and need for the service. Potential riders can call 401-863-9588 or email [email protected] to obtain registration forms. Riders can also get registration materials from the SEAS Shuttle driver the first time that they ride. Registered riders complete a schedule form if they will need rides on a regular basis. Riders can request same day and unplanned rides too.

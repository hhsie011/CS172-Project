Home / Campus Life / Student Services / Academic Support Resources Academic Support Resources Academic Learning and Tutoring Center One on one meetings with trained tutors, Supplemental Instruction for traditionally difficult courses, academic writing coaching and The Math Center which is designed for students enrolled in difficult calculus and statistic courses. ALTC Success Coaching Success coaches are available to help ensure your academic success through conversations about your goals, time management and more. Our coaches also hold workshops for exam prep, test taking tips and community building. Success Coaching Case Management Services If you experience a crisis or have an extenuating circumstance that prevents your academic success, let us know. Case Management Campus Health CAPS Counseling Services Prioritize your mental health and well being. Work with Campus Health to establish any necessary mental health support. CAPS NTC Center for Academic Equity If you self identify as a minority, LGBTQ+ or any underrepresented student population, connect with CAE to explore resources tailored to you and your needs. CAE Goldman Center for Student Accessibility The Goldman Center seeks to eliminate barriers, advocate for inclusion, and ensure access for every member of our community If you have accomodations for testing or classes in the past and believe having these may help you be more successful in the college classroom. Goldman Center The Lavin-Bernick Center for University Life & The Commons The LBC is the hub for campus life that houses meeting space, campus programming, dining options, the Bookstore, student involvement opportunities, and fun, free student programming. LBC Reily Center for Student Recreation Cultivate an active lifestyle, participate in fitness programs, aquatics, club or intramural sports, and outdoor adventures with the Campus Recreation team. Riley does operate on a summer schedule with limited hours.

Why fill out a FAFSA? <!-- intentionally non-empty iframe; for xsl --> Scholarships High school students are assured a scholarship if you are in the top 25% of your high school class and achieve at least a 27 ACT (composite) or 1200 SAT (critical reading and math). These scholarships are awarded for students admitted before April 15. Merit Transfer Scholarships are available when you meet the required GPA and number of credit hours earned. Pay Your Application Fee We require a non-refundable application fee of $75 or a fee waiver to complete your application. If you have paid or plan to pay the fee on your ApplyTexas application, there is no need to pay again through this link. If paying with personal check or cashier's check, please include applicants full name and date of birth on check. Student Business Services How much will it cost? When will I be billed? Where and when do I pay? All the answers to these question and more can be found by contacting or visiting the website for Student Business Services. Their staff are ready to answer your questions by calling 806.742.3272 or by email at sbs@ttu.edu.

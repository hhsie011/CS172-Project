In this workshop for faculty we’ll demonstrate the instructor and student interface. We’ll cover the grading and dynamic rubric-building workflow that improves student learning and saves you time. You will learn how to create and grade assignments and exams using Gradescope, and link them to your course in Canvas. This 60 minute online workshop is for SJSU faculty and staff.

Admission requirements Inside the Program Chemistry professor provides experience opportunities for students Chemistry students at Wichita State University have opportunities to gain experience both inside the classroom and out. Assistant Chemistry Professor Katie Mitchell-Koch is providing valuable opportunities and resources for undergraduate and graduate students helping with her research project. Her project focuses on the movement of solvent molecules around enzymes, the proteins that catalyze chemical reactions.

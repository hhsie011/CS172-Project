Veterans of other national militaries should not use this application, but are welcome to apply via our other application options. Applicants wishing to transfer from U.S. military academies are encouraged to apply as transfer students using the Common App. Individuals who have previously earned a bachelor’s degree are not eligible to apply. Start the Veterans Application Create an account and start the Veterans Application here. The application is available in October for prospective entrance in the fall of the following year. The application deadline is March 1.

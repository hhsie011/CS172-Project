Reals Gallery of Art at the University of Kansas School of Medicine-Wichita has had ongoing exhibits since 1985. The facility is named for William J. Reals, M.D., who served as dean from 1980 to 1990. The gallery was originally opened to showcase the photography of a medical student. The purpose and scope of the gallery have grown to include enhancement of the medical school and community outreach. The gallery is free and open to the public from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. Monday through Friday. Gallery Information Hours: 9 a.m. - 4 p.m., Monday-Friday Location: 1010 N.

The Barnard Library collections are located on floors 2, 3, and 4, including the Barnard Zine Library on the 2nd floor and Barnard Archives & Special Collections are on the 4th floor. The interdisciplinary centers are located on floors LL, 1, and 5. Visit the Barnard Library website for more information.

Employment Reports Contact Us For on-campus interviews, recruiters should check in at the CMC reception desk on the 7th floor of Geffen Hall 30 minutes before their first interview. Guests will first need to get a badge from the security desk in the lobby and must provide their name to the CMC at least 24-hours before arriving. The Career Management Center staff will distribute a copy of final schedules and the current version of each candidate’s résumé. Columbia provides complimentary breakfast and lunch to recruiters and interviewers. Bagels, muffins, coffee, tea, snacks, and refreshments are available throughout the day in our Recruiters’ Lounge.

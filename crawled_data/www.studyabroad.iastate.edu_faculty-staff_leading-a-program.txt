If you are not part of an academic college, please contact the Study Abroad Center. Read the guidelines for preparing a proposal and meeting deadlines for submission as well as policies and other valuable information on the Education Abroad Committee’s website. Consider having Project Assist help with the logistics of your program, freeing you to focus your attention on academics and student preparation; contact the Study Abroad Center at studyabroad@iastate.edu for more information. Participate in the Program Director Training. If your program has been approved to run, please complete the Travel Registration, which also includes Program Director Training.

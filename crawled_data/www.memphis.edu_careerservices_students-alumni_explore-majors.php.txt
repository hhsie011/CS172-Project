Career Specialists can connect you to tools and resources that provide insight that can make choosing a major and career easier! Making “Major” Decisions? Determining a major is key when considering your next steps. Meet with a Career Specialist A one-on-one appointment with a Career Specialist for major and career exploration/planning gives you the opportunity to discover your individual path and clarify your goals. Request an appointment with your Career Specialist through TigerLink powered by Handshake (click Career Center > Appointments). PathwayU PathwayU offers a variety of career planning tools including free online career assessments focusing on interests, values, personality, workplace preferences, and more.

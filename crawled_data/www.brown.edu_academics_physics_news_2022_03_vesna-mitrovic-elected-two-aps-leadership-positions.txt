Mitrovic will serve as Executive Committee Vice-Chair of the Group on Instrument and Measurement Science (GIMS) and as a Member at Large on the Executive Committee of the APS Division of Condensed Matter Physics (DCMP), the largest division of APS. GIMS was formed in 1984 “to advance the development of new instrumentation and measurement science” and provides “professional recognition to those who advance instrument and measurement science in all fields of physics.” DCMP’s mission is to advance knowledge of the physics of condensed matter.

Whether teaching in-person, in a hybrid setting, or online, this site provides simple steps, recommendations, and templates for building an engaging learning environment that meets your instructional objectives. Learning Together provides instructional and assessment strategies, tools for enhancing the online or hybrid experience, and strategies to help you bring the best practices to life.

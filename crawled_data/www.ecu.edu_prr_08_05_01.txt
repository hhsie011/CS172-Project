Introduction 1.1 Purpose of Regulation To establish guidelines governing the use and connection of computing devices on the University's Data Communication Network. This Regulation applies to all University desktop, laptop, mobile and server computing systems. 1.2 Person(s) with Primary Responsibility Director of IT Security 2. Regulation All desktop, laptop, mobile and server computing systems (that have ITCS supported anti-virus software where applicable) connected to the East Carolina University computer network (herein referred to as "the network") or networked resources shall have ITCS-supported antivirus software (preferably the most current version) correctly installed, configured, activated, and updated with the latest version of virus definitions before or immediately upon connecting to the network.

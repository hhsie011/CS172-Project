One of several miniature manuscripts made by Charlotte and Branw Houghton Library Houghton’s collections also include the papers of several 20th-century British and Irish writers and artist, including W.H. Auden, Max Beerbohm, James Joyce, John Masefield, George Moore, Dorothy Sayers, George Bernard Shaw, Dylan Thomas, and William Butler Yeats. Books in parts, broadsides (particularly of poetry and ballads), and important association copies are also in the collection. In all cases, collections of papers are complimented by in-depth collections of these authors’ printed works. For some authors, such as Charles Dickens, emphasis has been placed on their printed books. Online Exhibitions Such a Curious Dream!

Teacher Education Events Past Event Connecting Theory and Practice in Teacher Education Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo May 3, 2018 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM Columbia Global Centers l Paris, 4 rue de Chevreuse, 75006 To celebrate her last year at the College, and to give thanks for all things TC, President Susan Fuhrman (Ph.D. ’77) is heading out on the TC2U: Thanks a Million Tour. She invites alumni to join her in Paris for a special presentation "Connecting Theory and Practice in Teacher Ed-A Conversation Between President Susan Fuhrman and Doug Ready, Associate Professor of Education and Public Policy." Join us for this opportunity to learn more from President Fuhrman and Professor Ready about how Teachers College helped to create a school of education at the University of Warsaw. President Fuhrman will also be sharing her final initiatives for the remainder of her tenure at Teachers College. A reception will follow the speaking program - which will provide an excellent opportunity to get to know more members of the local TC and CU community as well as local educators.

Online business courses and programs are an alternative way to meet your educational goals, whether you’re a working professional, a company looking to help employees enhance their education or a student trying to work around a scheduling conflict. From lectures to discussions to submitting assignments, all course interactions can be completed entirely online; although other course delivery formats are also available. All instructors teaching online classes are the same quality faculty who teach in OSU’s campus classrooms. The Michael and Anne Greenwood Center for Online Excellence staff and faculty are here to assist students succeed in their desired program.

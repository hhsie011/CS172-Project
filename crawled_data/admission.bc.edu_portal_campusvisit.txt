Led by an admission counselor, these two-hour visits include an information session and a campus tour. The information session covers the admission process and financial aid and features an authentic panel discussion with current students. Please note: Registrants and their guest must provide proof of vaccination against COVID-19 upon arrival to campus. Negative PCR test results will not be accepted. Advance registration is required. We will not be able to accommodate walk-in visitors. If you cannot find a time that is available, please note that we have a self-guided audio tour that can be taken at any time. Please review our visitor policies and guidelines in advance of scheduling your visit to campus. If you are a community-based organization leader scheduling a visit for ten or more students in their sophomore, junior, or senior year of high school, please register your group for a visit here. Spring 2022 campus visits will be offered on weekdays beginning Monday, February 14, through Friday, April 29. Please note that no sessions will be offered on the following dates: March 7 - 11 April 14 - 15 April 18 Summer 2022 campus visits will be offered on weekdays beginning Wednesday, June 15, through Friday, August 19.

5:00 PM America/New_York Lerner Hall, 2920 Broadway, New York, NY 10027 Show Map No-cost Flu Vaccinations at Columbia  Columbia Health is hosting a walk-in flu vaccine event from October 4—October 29, 2021 at Lerner Hall auditorium. Eligible affiliates only need their CUID. No appointments are needed. Columbia University makes every effort to accommodate individuals with disabilities. If you require disability accommodations to attend an event at Columbia University, please contact Disability Services at 212-854-2388 at least 10 days in advance of the event. Mondays: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM Tuesdays – Thursdays: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM Fridays: 7:00 AM – 3:00 PM These no out-of-pocket cost flu vaccines are available for students, faculty, and staff from: Morningside Campus Manhattanville Campus Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory Nevis Laboratory Jewish Theological Seminary Teachers College Union Theological Seminary Barnard and CUIMC affiliates should contact their respective campuses for flu vaccine opportunities.  For those over 65 years of age, Columbia Health has a limited supply of high-dose vaccines. Please speak to the nurse administering your vaccine if you may be eligible for the high-dose flu vaccine. If you require disability accommodations, contact Disability Services at (212) 854-2388 at least 10 days in advance for support.

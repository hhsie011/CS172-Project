The 96th Street stop location has been added to better meet the needs of our riders across the shuttle network. All departure times have been adjusted accordingly. Please remember the safety measures in place due to COVID-19 and always wear a face covering while aboard the bus. Intercampus Shuttle: Addition of 96th Street stop location Departure times have shifted throughout the schedule Saturday service will continue along the Red Line; no service on Sundays Additional service to Metro-North in the evening for Morningside and Manhattanville riders Additional run to Medical Center Campus between 5:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. View schedule Manhattanville Loop Shuttle: The Manhattanville Loop Shuttle has been discontinued due to low ridership and will not be in service this Academic Year. Hoboken Shuttle : The Hoboken Shuttle pilot will continue for Fall Term, using the Intercampus Shuttle’s Red Line bus. This term, only Columbia-bound A.M. service will be offered; no evening Hoboken-bound service is available.

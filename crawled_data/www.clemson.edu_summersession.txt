Studying as a Visiting Student Payment Deadline, Tuition and Fees Summer Financial Aid Options Summer Start Program Every summer, Clemson University students get a head start on classes, the completion of their degree and, eventually, the start of their dream job. We hope you will utilize this website to find the information you need for a successful and productive summer. Registration opens on April 11 for Summer Session at Clemson. For more than five years, our Summer Session has offered more than half the available courses digitally. This summer, our professors have everything planned out to continue to offer engaging online classes and some face-to-face classes. No matter your major, Summer Session has a class (or classes) that will give you a head start and meet your academic needs as you enter the Fall semester. More than ever, Summer Session offers the chance to take specialized courses tailored to your academic interests and needs. (Note: Summer courses, fees and housing choices will be updated for 2022 as they become available.)

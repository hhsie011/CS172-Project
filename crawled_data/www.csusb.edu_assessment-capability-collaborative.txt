Summer ACLI 2021 provides an opportunity for faculty and staff to develop leadership in the design and implementation of program, department or unit outcomes assessment to support sustainable and meaningful inquiry of program effectiveness and student, faculty and staff success. CSUSB’s ACLI is unique in that it draws together faculty and staff from all divisions of the university to learn together and to build cross-campus connections for a broad foundation for assessment. Read more about the Assessment Capability Leadership Institute 2021. Assessment Capability Fellows 2021-2022 The Assessment Capability Fellows program offers a professional enrichment opportunity for faculty and staff to build upon their assessment knowledge and leadership through engagement in novel projects that develop core structures for assessment at CSUSB. Fellows participate in ACLI during the summer and begin their yearlong projects in the fall. Read more about the Assessment Capability Fellows 2021-2022.

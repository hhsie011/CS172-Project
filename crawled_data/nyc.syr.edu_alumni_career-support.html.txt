No matter if you are a student, a Generation Orange alum, or have been in the workplace for many years… you are welcome to receive support from our career services team. #HireOrange gives our Syracuse University network access to career opportunities only available to Syracuse University alumni. Whether you post a job for alumni or you search for a new position, #HireOrange is there for you! We also offer the Handshake platform for our students where they can search for jobs and internships. We have resources available just for you including career counseling, career-oriented events and webinars. Don’t miss out on these these virtual programs and workshops to help you gain new skills. If you are navigating a career transition, looking for new career opportunities, seeking growth in your current role or organization, or hoping to hone your leadership skills, you may consider engaging with a career or leadership coach.

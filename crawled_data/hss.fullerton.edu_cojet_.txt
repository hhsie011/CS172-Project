Resources Black Lives Movement Resources IBII Contact Us Herstory Vision and Mission Core Values, Assumptions, and Beliefs About CoJET The Collective for Justice, Equity & Transformation (CoJET) consists of a team of H&SS faculty whose expertise addresses issues of equity, diversity, justice, community and organizational transformation. CoJET aims to transform higher education structures, policies, and practices by focusing on college-based initiatives, with implications for CSUF, our communities, and more broadly, higher education and society.

Admissions Decisions While the Office of Admissions makes decisions on a rolling basis and attempts to make decisions within six to eight weeks of receiving a complete application, please note that the School of General Studies begins releasing admissions decisions in March for the fall term, and in October for the spring term. All applications received after the final deadlines will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Fall Admission Applications completed by January 15 will receive a decision by March 1 Applications completed by March 1 will receive a decision by May 1 Applications completed by May 15 will receive a decision by July 7 Spring Admission Applications completed by September 1 will receive a decision by October 1 Applications completed by October 1 will receive a decision by November 15 Applications completed by November 1 will receive a decision by December 20 Deadline Reminders If you would like to receive emails regarding upcoming admissions deadlines, please join our mailing list, if you have not done so already.

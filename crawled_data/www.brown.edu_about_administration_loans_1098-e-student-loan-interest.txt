If you paid $600 or more of interest on a qualified student loan during the calendar year, you should receive a Form 1098-E, Student Loan Interest Statement (PDF) from each loan servicer. 1098-E information for interest paid on Federal Perkins Loans or any Brown University intitutional loan programs will be included in the January billing statement for borrowers who paid at least $600 of student loan interest in the previous calendar year. Borrowers may also view this information online at https://heartland.ecsi.net Interest paid on Federal Direct Subsidized, Unsubsidized, or PLUS Loans will be provided by the borrower's Direct Loan servicer. For more information about the student loan interest deduction, refer to Publication 970, Tax Benefits for Education (PDF).

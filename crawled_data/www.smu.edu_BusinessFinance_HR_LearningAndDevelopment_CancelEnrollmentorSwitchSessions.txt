A Training Summary will list all workshops completed, enrolled in, or cancelled out of (at the employee's request). Please note our cancellation policy for all workshops. Cancellation Policy for HR Workshops If you have enrolled in a course and are unable to attend, we ask that you notify Organizational Effectiveness at least 48 hours prior to the class. Providing as much advance notification as possible will allow us to contact other staff and faculty members who are on the wait list and hoping to attend. Failure to comply with this policy more than once in an academic year, may impact future enrollments in workshops. By requesting enrollment in a course, you are acknowledging and accepting this cancellation policy. Cancel Enrollment in an HR Workshop To cancel your enrollment in an HR Workshop (Professional Development, Quick Take Session, Manager Orientation) send us a cancellation email using the email link at the bottom of this page with the following information: your name and SMU ID# workshop title date of the workshop session you are currently enrolled in Please allow us 24 hours to change your status from "Enrolled" to "Cancel". Switch Sessions of an HR Workshop To switch sessions of an HR Workshop (Professional Development, Quick Take Session, Manager Orientation) send us a cancellation email using the email link at the bottom of this page with the following information: your name and SMU ID# workshop title date of the workshop session you are currently enrolled in date of the session you would like to attend Please allow us 24 hours to switch your enrollment to the new session. If the session is full, we will place you on the waitlist and notify you of your status. If an opening becomes available, we will email you to determine your availability before changing your status to enrolled. We do recommend that you block your schedule for the date and time as we often access the waitlist to fill the class. You may check the status of your enrollment by viewing your training summary in My.SMU.

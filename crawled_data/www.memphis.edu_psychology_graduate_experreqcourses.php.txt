In the first year PSYC 7301/8301 Research Design and Methodology PSYC 7302/8302 Advanced Statistics in Psychology I May substitute PSYC 7303/8303, Advanced Statistics in Psychology II, for PSYC 7302/8302 Additional Required Coursework for Experimental Students Two additional elective Advanced Statistics Courses (Partial list below) PSYC 7303/8303, 7304/8304, 7305/8305, 7306/8306, 7307/8307, 7308/8308, 7310/8310, 7311/8311, 7312/8312. For students NOT in the Cognitive area: Comprehensive Education Elective Courses (one from each category below) Biological Bases of Behavior: PSYC 7441/8441, 7701/8701, or 7705/8705. Cognitive-Affective Bases of Behavior: PSYC 7208/8208, 7211/8211, 7222/8222, or 7407/8407. Social Bases of Behavior: 7215/8215, 7217/8217, 7219/8219, or 7220/8220. Individual Behavior: PSYC 7207/8207, 7219/8219, 7412/8412, 7416/8416, 7420/8420, or 7516/8516. For students in the Cognitive area, the following four courses are required: PSYC 7530/8530 - Psychology of Language , PSYC 7222/8222 - Psychology Human Memory and Learning, PSYV 7213/8213 - Cognitive Science , PSYC 7313/8313 - Computational Models Cog Sci.

As the leading research university in South Texas, UT Health San Antonio has been awarded $10.9 million to advance the fight against cancer as it recruits new scientists to join its world-class faculty. Discovering new cancer treatments Academics Research Patient Care Community Training Future Generations With five schools offering 65 degree specialties, we're training South Texas' next generation of healthcare professionals. Leading Breakthroughs We're moving research forward and developing new methods for combatting disease, from diabetes to cancer to Alzheimer's. Advancing Patient Care Our network of 800 specialists are informed by the latest medical discoveries and can tailor a treatment plan for you. The University of Texas Health Science Center at San Antonio is proud to be ranked within multiple U.S. News & World Report Best Grad Schools categories. Our five schools offer 65 professional, graduate and undergraduate degree and certificate programs, across campuses in San Antonio and Laredo.

Our research involves the science and technology of terahertz radiation. We use lasers as a versatile tool for generating and detecting terahertz radiation, so our expertise includes terahertz technologies, laser science, and ultrafast optics. See the side bar to learn more about our research interests, publications, and the current and former group members. For more information, please contact Professor Daniel Mittleman, by email.

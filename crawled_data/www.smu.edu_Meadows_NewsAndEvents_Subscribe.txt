The newsletter is published weekly during the spring and fall semesters of the SMU school year. Meadows events that are publicized by the Meadows Marketing and Communications department are included in the newsletter automatically. All other events hosted by Meadows faculty, students, and staff are eligible to be included in the newsletter. To ensure your event is included, please fill out our event submission form. Members of the on-campus community receive the newsletter through their SMU email account. All other interested parties can subscribe to the newsletter here.

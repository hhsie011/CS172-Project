This program is specifically for students who wish to earn an Associate in Arts degree at TCC and then transfer to neighboring FSU to complete a four-year degree. Aspire TCC2FSU students benefit from individualized advising provided by TCC and FSU advisors. Right from the start, students are given an academic plan to stay on track to achieve an Associate in Arts degree and admission to FSU for a bachelor's degree. The Aspire TCC2FSU Program is also highly affordable as students pay TCC tuition rates while earning an A.A. degree. Students can also apply for special Aspire TCC2FSU scholarships. Learn more about Aspire TCC2FSU benefits and details at www.tcc.fl.edu/tcc2fsu.

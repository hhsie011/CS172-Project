Who We Are Music is essential to the human experience. Take control of your future as you prepare for a career as a professional musician, educator, or industry leader. Our music students thrive in small class sizes and 1:1 studio courses with faculty across a variety of instrument areas and musical styles. From outstanding teachers in public schools, colleges, and universities, to professional careers as musicians and performers on some of the biggest stages, our students find their calling through a long tradition of excellence at Texas Tech. undergraduate graduate minors Latest School of Music News School of Music to Perform End of Year Concert The University Choirs and Symphony Orchestra will be in concert at the Buddy Holly Hall.Read More Read More Associate professor and trumpet player fuses research, teaching and artistry to make his mark on Texas Tech.

To achieve those goals, we build partnerships and work collaboratively with partners across the campus community. Whether a project involves publicizing new research, unveiling a new initiative, highlighting student and faculty accomplishments, or collecting information to respond to a media inquiry, our colleagues across campus inform our work and play a critical role in partnering for a successful outcome.

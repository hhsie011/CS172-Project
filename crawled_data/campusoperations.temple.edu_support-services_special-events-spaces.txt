Work requests for these services must be received by Facilities Management at least five working days prior to the required setup date. Changes to work requests for setups must be made by calling the Work Control Center at ext. 1-8439. The change must be confirmed in writing, referencing the original work request requisition number. For special event setups within Temple University Hospital, contact Hospital Administration at ext. 2-8007 to reserve space, and Environmental Services at ext. 2-3110 to obtain tables and chairs. See Conferences and Meetings to learn more about special events, including how to schedule an event at a given campus and catering services.

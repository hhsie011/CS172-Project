Join us for dinner and drinks with Columbia Engineering alum Zhen Li, a Data Scientist at Twitter. Zhen has experience in various data science roles across different tech environments (startup, big tech) and working cultures (Shanghai, NYC, Singapore). Her work involves applications such as natural language processing, causal inference and machine learning to build measurement systems for ads delivery and network/infrastructure optimisation.

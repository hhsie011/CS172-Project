LLC/TC Home / Living / Living-Learning & Themed Communities 2022-2023 Living-Learning & Themed Communities A Community for Everyone There truly is a community for everyone here at UofL, and it all begins with our living-learning (LLC) and themed (TC) communities! LLCs and TCs are located throughout our eleven residence halls here on Belknap Campus. Students live together while accessing academic and staff support, leadership opportunities, and other resources tailor-made to their interests. In addition, LLC members participate in courses together. The Fall 2022-Spring 2023 LLC/TC Application will launch in the Housing Portal on our homepage on November 1, 2021. View frequently asked questions! Community options exist for both first-year Cards and upper-level students! Browse Fall 2022-Spring 2023 upper-level options on our Upper-Level Communities page.

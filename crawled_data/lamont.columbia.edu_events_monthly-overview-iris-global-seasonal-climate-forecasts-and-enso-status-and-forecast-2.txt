Events Past Event Monthly overview of IRI's Global Seasonal Climate Forecasts and ENSO status and forecast. Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo October 21, 2021 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM America/New_York Online The International Research Institute for Climate and Society (part of the Earth Institute) presents its monthly climate forecast briefing. The IRI's seasonal climate forecasts and ENSO (El Niño Southern Oscillation) forecasts are discussed in this briefing by IRI's chief forecaster, Tony Barnston. In addition, the IRI climate group will present results of its experimental sub seasonal forecasts.

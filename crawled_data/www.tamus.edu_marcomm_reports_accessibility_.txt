As such, we are committed to making our web pages accessible according to the standards established by: Information and Communication Technology – Revised 508 Standards and 255 Guidelines Texas Department of Information Resources (DIR) Statewide Electronic and Information Resources (EIR) Accessibility guidelines. Our goal is that anyone should be able to access our web page effectively the same whether or not they have a disability. If you use assistive technology and the format of any material on our web sites interferes with your ability to access the information, please contact the manager of microcomputers and network support at tamus-webmaster@tamus.edu. Additional information about accessibility programs in Texas is available from the Governor’s Committee on People with Disabilities.

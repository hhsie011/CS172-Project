For additional information about any of our classes, programs or services, please call the sales office at 305-284-5433. Group Exercise Whether you are just starting an exercise routine or you are an avid exerciser looking for a challenge, the Herbert Wellness Center offers a wide variety of group exercise classes to keep you engaged and help you meet your wellness needs. More Info Reformer Pilates Reformer Pilates classes are taught using Peak Piltes MVe reformer and tower systems. These classes focus on building core strength and edurance, increasing flexibility, improving muscle tone, and enhancing concentration. More Info Aquatics Knowing how to swim is an important life skill for both children and adults to have, especially in South Florida. We offer a variety of aquatics classes for participants of all skill levels. More Info Tennis Whether you are new to the sport or are looking to improve and refine your game, we have the class to fit your needs. More Info Martial Arts Learn self-defense techniques from around the world in our martial arts classes. These classes are fun, educational, and provide full-body workouts. More Info Adventure Programs Outdoor Adventures offers a variety of programs and educational opportunities for experiencing the outdoors. From instructional clinics to a climbing wall and outdoor exercise opportunities, there are options for everyone. More Info Weightlifting 101 This is a beginner-friendly ultimate guide to weight lifting. In this course, you will learn the basics of the barbell and major lifts including deadlift, bench press, and squat variations. More Info 65+ Fitness Program Living Independently Through Fitness and Exercise (L.I.F.E.) is a program specially designed for individuals sixty-five years of age or older. With improved strength and flexibility, activities of daily living are more easily accomplished.

Mobile Orders Beaver Dam Study Grounds Instagram Beaver Dam Located in Obsidian Hall The Beaver Dam offers flavors from around the world, and uses local, organic and sustainable products as much as possible. You’ll also find plenty of fresh fruits, vegetables and whole grains. We are proud to provide vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and Halal dining choices. In addition, our university dietitian can meet with you regarding any special dietary needs or answer any questions you might have. Learn more Study Grounds Located in Obsidian Hall Study Grounds is a coffee shop in Obsidian Hall proudly serving local Backporch Coffee Roasters. It offers vegan, vegetarian and gluten-friendly options. Study Grounds also serves non-coffee beverage options like power juices, vegan smoothies and Metolius tea products. It's a great place for Bend Beavs to study and visit with friends.

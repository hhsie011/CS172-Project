If you are a Raider Restart participant, or need complete any other Office of Student Conduct sanction facilitated by the RISE Office, we are available to facilitate those requirements in person and virtually. Please contact RaiderRestart@ttu.edu for details or be responsive to your TTU email for instructions. Need some extra support or guidance about campus resources and services? We offer drop-in appointments with RISE graduate students to help you find the campus resources and support services are available to help you succeed!

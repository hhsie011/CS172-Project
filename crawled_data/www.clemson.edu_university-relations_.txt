Mission Statement We are committed to being a valued-added University resource that provides strategic solutions and results-oriented ideas that tell the Clemson story in creative, engaging and innovative ways in support of the University’s goals and consistent with its core values. What We Do University Relations is the umbrella administrative unit that includes: Marketing and Brand Strategy Presidential Communications Strategic Communications Administrative Services The Vice President for University Relations also serves as Clemson University’s primary spokesperson.

Students will be asked to register an email address with the Commencement Office during summer semester to receive information after graduation. Fall 2022 Commencement Fall 2022 Commencement is scheduled to take place in December. Students who registered an email address over the summer will receive an email in September with RSVP dates, ticket allotments, and confirmed ceremony times. Related Resources Summer Graduate FAQs Summer Discontinuation Policy Office of the Registrar Spring Participation Spring 2022 Participation Summer 2022 graduates with two classes or less remaining and in good academic standing will have the option to apply for an early walk in Spring 2022. Read the official policy How to Apply Students may request an early walk by completing the spring participation request form by March 18. Late submissions will not be accepted. Applicants will be notified by March 25 if they are cleared to participate and receive an RSVP link at that time. Summer students allowed to walk early will receive an extension on their RSVP period. What to Expect Summer students participating in the spring ceremony should expect the following changes: Students will not be able to RSVP prior to approval. Summer students will not be listed in the spring program. All summer graduates will be listed in the fall program. Summer students will not receive honor cords for the spring ceremony. Summer students will have their name called as they walk across stage.

Whether you are looking for a new laptop, tablet or desktop computer, if you are in the market for new gaming or audio equipment, or if all you need is a cable or charger, OrangeTech has that and everything else in between. At OrangeTech, Oklahoma State University students, faculty and staff always get the best, lowest pricing on their technology needs thanks to our educational pricing partnerships. Plus, we offer incoming OSU students deals on specially constructed, deeply discounted laptop bundles individually customizable based on your particular field of study. OrangeTech also partners with several OSU colleges and academic departments to offer technology specifically chosen for students majoring in certain areas. We believe technology is not just about the product, it is about the experience. Because of that, the OrangeTech team is highly dedicated to exceptional customer service. We won't just sell you a computer, we will also help you set it up, make sure you know how to use it and be there to answer any questions you have along the way. OrangeTech has everything, including low-interest financing options available in partnership with the University & Community Federal Credit Union to assist customers with their large technology purchases, a certified service & repair center, and a trade-it-in program. OrangeTech is owned and operated by Oklahoma State University, and every purchase made at OrangeTech directly benefits the university.

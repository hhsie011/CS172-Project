It assures that full tuition and standard fees will be covered by grant or scholarship support for eligible students. You will not have to pay back these grants and scholarships, and if tuition increases, your grants will increase as well. The Husky Promise ensures that the UW remains affordable and accessible for Washington residents. Learn more about how the Husky Promise works.

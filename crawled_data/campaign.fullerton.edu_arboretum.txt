The 26-acre botanical garden serves students, faculty and the broader community as an urban oasis, an outdoor laboratory for research and environmental education, and a steward of worldwide plant conservation and regional agricultural heritage. Fullerton Arboretum seeks your support to enhance its collection work; increase opportunities for research, experiential learning and community education; and establish leadership in sustainable practices like composting and efficient water use. Together, we can amplify the important work of this botanical treasure and ensure its enjoyment by generations to come.

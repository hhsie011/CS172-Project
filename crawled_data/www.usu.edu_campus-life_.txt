USU is the oldest residential campus in Utah offering a variety of year-round activities and countless ways to connect with other members of the university community. Club Sport Teams (2021-2022) 23 Housing Communities 5 Logan Campus Eateries 17 On-campus job opportunities 1100+ LIVING ON CAMPUS There is no better way to experience all USU offers than by making it your home. RECREATION When it comes to recreation and well-being, USU has it all. HEALTH AND WELLNESS Healthy students do better. USU offers support for the eight dimensions of wellness. GET INVOLVED USU students can find many opportunities to connect academic study with public service. CAMPUS SAFETY USU works hard to keep the campus safe for students, faculty, staff, and visitors. DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION USU is committed to inclusion and cultivating diversity of culture and thought. On-Campus Events The Logan campus hosts several major events every year. The Luminary Processional, which welcomes incoming students, has become a USU tradition. The Howl is USU’s annual Halloween party and is the largest in the state. The End of Year Bash is a concert to culminate the academic year each April.

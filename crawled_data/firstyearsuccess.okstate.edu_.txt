Application Portal First Year Resources The Office of First Year Success helps you be your personal best during your first year. We’re available to brainstorm ideas that work for your unique personality, needs, and situation. You’ll leave our office with achievable, sustainable plans to help you with the personal and financial challenges of college life. Once you are enrolled as an OSU student, you can schedule an appointment with our team using the Star System. After logging in, select the blue "get advising" button, select First Year Success and select your financial planning counselor or campus connection coach.

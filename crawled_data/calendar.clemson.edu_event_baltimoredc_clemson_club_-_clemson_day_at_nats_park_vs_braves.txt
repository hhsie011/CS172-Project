Join Clemson alumni, students, parents and fans as the orange and purple take over the ballpark for the afternoon! With the purchase of a special ticket, fans will receive a discounted game ticket and an official limited edition co-branded Clemson/Nationals cap. The following ticket options are available: Scoreboard Pavilion: $33 Outfield Reserved: $45 Infield Box: $81 Game time is 1:35pm. Head over to Mission after the game for a post-game meet up and drink! Purchase your tickets at www.mlb.com/nationals/tickets/specia...ollege-day-series/clemson-university.

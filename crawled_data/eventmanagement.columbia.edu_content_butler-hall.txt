Pelham. It is located on the corner of 119 Street and Morningside Drive, overlooking beautiful Morningside Park. In addition to office space, the building is home to Butler Atrium, which is located on the top floor. The recently renovated event space features floor to ceiling windows that showcase breathtaking views of the Upper Manhattan skyline.

Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Peace by PEACE Peace by PEACE is a conflict resolution skill development program for elementary school students. Volunteer teachers offer a year-long curriculum, fostering ways of resolving differences by communicating, understanding different points of view, and learning together. Weekly one-hour sessions take place on weekday afternoons at partner schools. Partner Organizations P.S. 125 P.S. 129 Interested in volunteering?

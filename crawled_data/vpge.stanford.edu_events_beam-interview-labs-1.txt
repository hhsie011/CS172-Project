Grad Grow Overview Find more resources BEAM: Interview Labs Wednesday, June 1, 2022 - 3:30pm Add to Calendar iCalendar Outlook Google Yahoo Interview Labs The Interview Lab is an interactive meetup for you to learn about and practice interviewing techniques for jobs outside of academia. Learn what employers are looking for in an interview and how to handle a variety of behavioral questions. Discussions include topics on how to market the many skills and experiences you’ve gained during your graduate studies or postdoc as well as working with peers to develop the questions you want to ask.

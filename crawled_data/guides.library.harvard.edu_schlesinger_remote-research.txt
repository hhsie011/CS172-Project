Although the Schlesinger Library has reopened to researchers, you can still access an ever-increasing number of our collections online. Please contact us if you want to discuss your research plan, want advice about resources, would like to plan a visit, or have any other questions. For more information about our services please visit the Ask a Schlesinger Librarian page. In addition to email we can also offer to meet with you via Zoom. This guide also includes information about online resources from all of Harvard's libraries that might be useful for your research.

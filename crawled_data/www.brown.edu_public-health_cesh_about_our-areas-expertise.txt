We collaborate with diverse groups of scholars and professional societies worldwide, in health and other disciplines. These collaborations have led to numerous publications and publicly-available tools for evidence synthesis. The Center’s work is funded by, among others, the federal Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) and the Patient Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI).

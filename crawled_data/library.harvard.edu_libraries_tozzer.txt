Seminar Room The Seminar Room on the first floor can be reserved for small courses or meetings. The table sits 8-12 people. Covered drinks and small snacks are okay, but messy or smelly food is not allowed. Reserve here Study Rooms There are two study rooms located in the basement, which sit 4-6 people. Groups may reserve up to three hours at a time. Covered drinks and small snacks are okay, but messy or smelly food is not allowed. Reserve here Study Carrels There are 26 study carrels located in the basement, each of which can be reserved for the academic year and/or summer term.

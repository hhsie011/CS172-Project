Learn how the Additive Manufacturing certificate is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 12-credit-hour certificate focuses on key materials, technologies and benefits, and include topics on design considerations, post processing, secondary operations, and important quality and safety factors. Required courses Rapid Prototyping and Product Developments Selection of Materials for Design and Manufacturing Computer Integrated Manufacturing Elective courses (candidates will complete one) Advanced Laser Applications in Manufacturing; Biodegradable Materials; Multiple Scale Modeling and Micromechanics; Independent Study on Additive Manufacturing Topics. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

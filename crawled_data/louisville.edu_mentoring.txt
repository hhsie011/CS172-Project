Mentoring is the larger context for learning techniques and other professional and personal skills and insights needed for professional growth. Articulating the roles, process and outcomes/goals of the mentoring process are critical to success of the relationship. The University of Louisville’s Office of the Vice Provost for Faculty Affairs is committed to supporting faculty mentors, mentees, and mentoring environment.

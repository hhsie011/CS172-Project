Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Student Housing Menu Search TTU Housing Sign Up for Housing Demo How to Sign Up for Housing Students are encouraged to apply for housing as soon as they are accepted to Texas Tech and receive their eRaider account information. Students entering the residence halls for only the spring semester may choose up to three residence halls. Room assignments for spring applicants will be based on availability. Assignments will be emailed to the student's Texas Tech email address. Ready to sign up for housing? Click here!

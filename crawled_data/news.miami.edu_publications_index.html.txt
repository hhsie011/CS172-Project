University of Miami one of the nation’s most ambitious and respected institutions of higher education. Each issue ignites the inherent connection among ’Canes, tapping into the expertise of our scientists, scholars, artists, and pioneers to help us better understand each other and the world around us. Veritas Veritas is an electronic newsletter that provides faculty and staff a brief roundup of campus news, information, announcements, and events. It is distributed by University Communications to all University employees every Monday morning, except on holidays and during the winter break. Arts & Sciences The College of Arts and Sciences publishes Arts & Sciences, a magazine covering the news, course spotlights, books, profiles and philanthropy at the College. heartbeat heartbeat magazine is the official alumni publication of the School of Nursing and Health Studies. It is published bi-annually to highlight the achievements of our faculty, alumni and students. Miami Law doesn't just keep pace. It sets it.

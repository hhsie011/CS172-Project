The Syracuse University Alumni Club of Boston is one place in the region where Orange spirit really rings strong. Visit the events page for more information about what’s coming up, then follow the club on Twitter and Facebook for the latest news and updates. The club is always looking for more volunteers too! We welcome any and all who want to join the board—whether in a leadership position or as an at-large member. Contact us at suinboston@gmail.com to learn about our committee and board needs, coming events, and other ways to get involved with Syracuse University in the Boston area. We hope to see you soon! 2019-20 Alumni Club Board of Directors Your Club officers are ready to help! At its recent meeting, the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Boston elected a new slate of officers for this year, and they encourage all area alumni to stay in touch. Have ideas for a new event? Want to help serve on one of the club committees? Or are you simply looking for more information about the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Boston? Contact your officers anytime!

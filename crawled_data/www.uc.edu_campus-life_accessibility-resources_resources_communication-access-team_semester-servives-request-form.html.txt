COVID-19 Resources for Students Accommodation Form Request Semester Services Request Form Students registered with the Accessibility Resources Office intending to utilize CAT services are responsible for completing the form below prior to the start of a new semester. In order to complete the form, students must provide their name, UC ID, and course information along with services requested (ASL/English interpreter(s), Supernotes, and/or Realtime). Additionally, the form can be utilized to cancel services. For example, if a student requests services for a chemistry lecture, attends the first week of class and then determines he no longer needs services, the student will then be required to utilized the form to cancel services for that particular class. Students must utilize the form and not inform the service provider assigned to the course. Furthermore, if students want to add services to previously unrequested courses after the start of a semester, they must do so using the form below. Requests for ongoing classes and other weekly, ongoing meetings or activities will continue to require up to two weeks of notice. Requests which are not made by the set deadline for the approaching semester are considered untimely and may result in a delay of services. Requests for services or events outside of regularly scheduled classes can be made utilizing our Interpreter/CART/Captioning Request Form. If you have questions completing the form, please contact the Supervisor of CART and Interpreting Services, Katherine Vance, katherine.vance@uc.edu.

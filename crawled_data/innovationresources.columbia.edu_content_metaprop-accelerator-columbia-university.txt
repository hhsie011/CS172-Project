Each year, between 6 and 8 of the best technology-driven real estate industry ideas are selected to participate in intensive education, mentorship, and growth hacking programs, culminating in exclusive Graduation Defense panels, Roadshows, and Demo Days for partners, investors, VCs, and media. Founded in 2015, the 22-week accelerator program, now in a hybrid format, boasts 150+ mentors, 10 renowned corporate partners, and widespread media coverage from the Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Forbes, TechCrunch, Inman, EG, Nikkei, and more.

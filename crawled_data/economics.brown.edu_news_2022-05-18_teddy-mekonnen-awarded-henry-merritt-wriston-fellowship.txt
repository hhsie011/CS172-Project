Congratulations to Teddy Mekonnen, Orlando Bravo Assistant Professor of Economics, for being selected to receive the Henry Merritt Wriston Fellowship! According to The College's website, "The Henry Merritt Wriston Fellowship is awarded each year to regular untenured members of the faculty (assistant professors and lecturers) who have achieved a record of excellence in teaching and scholarship during their first years at Brown."

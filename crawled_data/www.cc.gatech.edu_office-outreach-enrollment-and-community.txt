The office works towards this goal through the areas of emphasis found in our name: outreach, enrollment, and community. From career fairs to mentorship programs, from student organizations to community building, the OEC will help you find your home and your path in the College of Computing. Pay a visit to the OEC and you’ll see the GT Computing family in action.

Programs of Distinction ASPIRE Internship Opportunities Graduate Studies Departments and Programs Faculty Awards and Fellowships Cooper Fellows The University of Miami’s College of Arts & Sciences (A&S) is a liberal arts college housed within a top research university in one of America’s most global cities. As an A&S student, you will participate in a proud tradition of inquiry, engagement, and creativity. You will learn from faculty who are leading scholars in diverse fields such as neuroscience, Latin-American literature, and tropical ecology. And you will choose from 40 majors and 46 minors that cover the full breadth of the humanities, social sciences, STEM, and the arts. Liberal Arts at the University of Miami A liberal arts education teaches you how to think critically, forge connections across diverse disciplines, and communicate clearly and effectively. What does the liberal arts mean at the University of Miami? It means intellectual and cultural engagement. It means working closely with a renowned mathematics professor on original research, participating in a service-learning class that builds a children’s library for Haitian immigrants, or winning a Beyond the Book scholarship to interview Zimbabwean refugees in South Africa. Whatever your major, your studies will be enlarged and energized by internship, research, and volunteer opportunities in our remarkable city of Miami.

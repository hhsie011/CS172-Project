Education”: U.S.-China Education Forum Events Past Event “Cross-Fertilization of Culture and Education”: U.S.-China Education Forum Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo December 19, 2020 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Time: 8:30 AM - 12:30 PM, December 19, 2020 (CST) | 7:30 - 11:30 PM, December 18, 2020 (EST) Join us in-person or online for a U.S.-China education forum, as we celebrate the 20th anniversary of the Center on Chinese Education (CoCE) at Columbia University Teachers College and the outstanding work of its founding director Professor Mun C. Tsang. As a special part of the ceremony, the Mun C. Tsang Chinese Education Endowment Fund will be launched in honor of Professor Tsang’s contribution to the promotion of the U.S.-China educational exchange and collaboration. In partnership with Center on Chinese Education at Columbia University Teachers College, Peking University School of Education, Peking University Education Foundation (North America), Beijing Normal University School of International and Comparative Education, EduChina Group, and Puxin Education and Technology Group. Agenda Center on Chinese Education 20th Anniversary Celebration Opening Remarks 8:30 - 8:45 AM Moderators: Ni Zhang TC '06, Founder of Children's Museum Research Center at Beijing Normal University Helena Xiao, Associate Director of Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Henan Cheng, Deputy Director of the CoCE Speakers: Thomas Bailey, President of Teachers College, Columbia University (virtual) Mun Tsang, Founding Director of CoCE (virtual) Fengqiao Yan, Dean of School of Education, Peking University Xudong Zhu, Dean of Faculty of Education, Beijing Normal University Launch of “Mun C. Tsang Chinese Education Fund” 8:45 - 9:00 AM Moderator: Ni Zhang TC '06, Founder of Children's Museum Research Center at Beijing Normal University Wei Wang, Chairman of EduChina Group Yunlong Sha, CEO of Puxin Education and Technology Group Keynote Speeches and Panel Discussion “Cross-Fertilization of Culture and Education”: International Understanding and Educational Collaborations in an Era of Chang 9:00 - 10:10 AM Moderator: Henan Cheng, Deputy Director of the CoCE Speakers: Mingyuan Gu, Professor at Beijing Normal University Weifang Min, Professor at Peking University Henry Levin, William Heard Kilpatrick Professor of Economics and Education at Teachers College, Columbia University (virtual) Alex Eble, Assistant Professor of Economics and Education at Teachers College, Columbia University (virtual) Coffee/Tea Break 10:10 - 10:20 AM Roundtable Discussion: Educational Challenges and Opportunities in China and the U.S. during the Pandemic and Beyond 10:20 - 11:20 AM Moderator: Jun Teng, Associate Professor Institute of International and Comparative Education at Beijing Normal University Panelists: Yongxin Zhu, Tao Xingzhi Institute Hongyu Zhou, Professor at Central China Normal University Dongping Yang, 21st Century Education Research Institute K.C. Pang, Beijing International Bilingual Academy Sheng Lu, Yuanbo Education Group Rong Zhou, Puxin Education Group Reflections by TC Alumni and Visiting Scholars Center on Chinese Education: A Ribbon Connecting Chinese and American Education 11:20 - 12:00 PM Moderator: Dakai Li Panelists: Dongmao Wen, Professor at Peking University Yu Zhang, Professor at Tsinghua University Feng Zhou, Professor at Guangdong University of Education Lin Qiu, Yunnan Provincial Department of Education Reminders Event Language: Chinese and English. The in-person session opens only to Columbia students and alumni.

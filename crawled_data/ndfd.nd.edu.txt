Our Mission The University of Notre Dame Fire Department is dedicated to the preservation of life and property and to the promotion of fire safety through public education, inspections and fire protection system maintenance. More than just a mission statement, NDFD is committed to achieving the above goals through rigorous training, state-of-the-art technology and a focus on excellence in all that we do. These efforts produce many positive results, including a less than 2.2 minute average response time and a reputation of skilled and compassionate service. An important part of the Notre Dame Community since 1879, NDFD has a proud history and a reputation of professionalism today.

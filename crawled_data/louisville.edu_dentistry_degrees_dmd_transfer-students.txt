Under exceptional circumstances, highly qualified students who are currently enrolled in an accredited dental school may be considered for transfer. The candidate must: be in good standing with their current dental school rank in the upper 30% of their class with a minimum GPA of 3.0 come from a curriculum that matches the ULSD curriculum The student should also have sound reasons for relocating to Louisville. The transfer application is a multi-step process. If a student meets all of these criteria, they should contact the Assistant Dean of Student Affairs to discuss the transfer application process at dmdadms@louisville.edu or 502-852-5081.

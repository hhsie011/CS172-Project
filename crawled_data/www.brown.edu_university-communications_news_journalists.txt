Our team of writers and media officers proactively shares Brown’s news with journalists and fields inbound inquiries on a daily basis. We respond to media inquiries, share official comment and media statements, and speak on behalf of Brown University. We also assist journalists who wish to visit our campus, track down faculty experts at Brown to serve as interview sources, coordinate photo shoots and broadcast opportunities, and much more.

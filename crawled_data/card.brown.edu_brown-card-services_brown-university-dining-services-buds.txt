Using your Brown Card at Brown University Dining Services (BUDS) The Brown Card provides access to your meal plan, which includes meal credits and flex points. Flex points are available to be used in place of cash at any BUDS operation on campus. All Brown cardholders who maintain funds in their Bear Bucks Account can make purchases with the card at any of the BUDS locations on campus. For information on dining services contact Brown University Dining Services at 401-863-3876.

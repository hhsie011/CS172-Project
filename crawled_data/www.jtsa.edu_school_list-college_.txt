Each List College student pursues a comprehensive core curriculum in Jewish Studies, and chooses a major field in which to specialize. Majors can range from Bible to Modern Jewish Studies, and from Jewish Ethics to Midrash. Majors Sample First-Year Schedule Beyond the Classroom At List College, learning extends far beyond the classroom—our students take part in dynamic internships and social justice work, hold leadership positions on both of their campuses, study abroad all over the world, and forge a powerful, unique, and diverse Jewish community during their undergraduate years. Internships Campus Life Study Abroad High-Level Learning and Personal Mentoring List College students learn with the world-renowned faculty of JTS, which boasts the most extensive Jewish studies program in North America. Our distinguished faculty take a special interest in List College students, and each student receives individual, personal advising from one of our deans.

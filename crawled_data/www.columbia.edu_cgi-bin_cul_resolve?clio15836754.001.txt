The Wall Street Journal (WSJ.com) Columbia University students, faculty, and staff have full access to WSJ.com through Columbia University Libraries. FAQ Who has access to WSJ.com Passes? For how long? Access is limited to current students, faculty, and staff. Faculty and staff need to validate their memberships once a year on our CUL Registration Page. Student accounts should stay active until graduation. If your graduation year changes, follow up directly with the WSJ to extend access.

We mail diplomas to the mailing address on file. You can view and update your mailing address through the Profile tile on your Student Homepage. WHEN DO I GET MY DIPLOMA? Most undergraduates receive their diplomas 4-6 weeks after the official graduation date for the term and most graduates receive their diplomas 8-10 weeks after the official graduation date. WHAT IS ON MY DIPLOMA? The full Student Name (first, middle, last) listed on your unofficial transcript will be printed on your diploma. Please review the name on your transcript. If you need to make any changes, e.g. correct a misspelling or add a middle name, submit a name change request with documentation here: http://records.fullerton.edu/services/securedocs.php Your diploma will include your name, degree(s), major(s), minor(s) and honors. We do NOT print concentrations or certificates on diplomas. Those will be noted on your transcript. Undergraduate Students who complete multiple concurrent degrees (BA and BS) and/or majors will receive one diploma with all degrees and majors listed. HOW DO I ORDER A REPLACEMENT DIPLOMA? You can order a replacement diploma or eDiploma here: http://records.fullerton.edu/services/diplomas.php MY DIPLOMA IS ON HOLD, WHAT NOW? Contact the department that placed the hold to resolve the issue. After the hold is removed, contact the graduation unit gradunit@fullerton.edu with your name, CWID and current mailing address and your diploma will be released with the next diploma order.

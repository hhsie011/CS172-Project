Library Express Delivery Request materials be delivered to your departmental offices. LEDS delivery is available for current FSU faculty members, staff, post-docs, and graduate assistants with appointments (i.e., graduate assistants, research assistants and teaching assistants). Available items are delivered to department offices within 48 hours. Scanned articles and book chapters are delivered by email. Interlibrary Loan Request materials, including books and articles, that FSU may not have access to. Electronic materials are usually available within several business days. Materials Purchase Request Suggest materials you would like to see purchased for FSU Libraries collections. This service is available for current FSU faculty members. Requests go to your departmental library liaisons for review.

The Brown Department of Public Safety Investigations Unit offers free fingerprinting services for current students who need to be printed for employment, teacher certification or travel abroad. We do not do immigration or concealed weapons permit prints. Fingerprinting hours by appointment only. Please complete the Fingerprinting Request Form and a detective will contact you to make an appointment.

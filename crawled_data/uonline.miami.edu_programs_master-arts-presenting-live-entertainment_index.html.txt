All courses are taught by the same high-caliber University of Miami Frost School of Music faculty who teach on campus. Meet the Faculty Leadership, project management, operations, personnel, finances, and for-profit and non-profit arts business models serve as the foundation of curricular learning, research, and activity. The program encourages you to begin your career in production, promotion, or management of live entertainment. You will gain hands-on experience producing events, marketing and promoting concerts, and working with venues on all aspects of a live entertainment performance.

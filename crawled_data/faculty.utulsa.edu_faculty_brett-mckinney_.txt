Warren, Jr., Professor of Bioinformatics College of Engineering & Natural Sciences Computer Science Mathematics brett-mckinney@utulsa.edu 918-631-3444 Website Rayzor Hall Room 2145 Education PhD – University of Oklahoma MS – University of Oklahoma BS – The University of Tulsa Bio Brett McKinney has a joint appointment in the Department of Mathematics and Tandy School of Computer Science. He is a native Tulsan who did his undergraduate work summa cum laude at The University of Tulsa in mathematics and physics. He did his graduate work at the University of Oklahoma, obtaining a Ph.D. in theoretical physics, followed by a postdoctoral fellowship in biomathematics and computational biology at the Vanderbilt University Medical Center. He was an assistant professor in the Department of Genetics at the University of Alabama Birmingham School of Medicine before joining TU in 2009.

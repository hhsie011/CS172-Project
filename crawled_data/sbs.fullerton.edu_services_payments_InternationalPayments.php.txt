Easily and securely make education payments in your own currency, using local payment methods from the safety and convenience of your home. How to make a payment Online with Flywire Log into your CSUF Portal to access your Student Homepage . Click on the Financial Account tile and Make a Payment to begin the payment process. Click on Pay Now to open a new window to our online payment portal. At checkout, click on the International Payment - Flywire payment option. Select your country and enter the amount due. Follow the payment instructions to send your funds through Flywire. You will receive real-time notifications of your completed payment by email or text (optional). Track your payment with the tracking link provided in your email, or create a Flywire user account to view your payment record by logging in. Flywire Support Email: support@flywire.com Web: help.flywire.com How to Pay Video Payments to India with Flywire It takes 3-5 business days to deliver payments to Cal State Fullerton. Payments must be received by the deadline to be considered on time.

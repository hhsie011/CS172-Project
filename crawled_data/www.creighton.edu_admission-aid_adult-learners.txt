It’s easy to get started. Simply follow the pre-admission checklist. If you have any questions or concerns along the way, just give us a call at 402.280.2703 or email us. Contact an enrollment specialist or schedule a phone call. Apply Now Speak to an Enrollment Specialist Find a Program Complete the FREE online application. Submit Supporting Documents: Active duty military and veterans: if you have a JST, CCAF or DLA transcript, please submit those as part of your application. Ask your high school or GED-granting institution to send your official transcripts to the College of Professional Studies. If you’ve attended college before, ask to have your official transcripts sent to the College of Professional Studies. *Due to the current developments of the coronavirus (COVID-19), Creighton is highly encouraging transcripts be requested and submitted electronically. Official transcript(s) requested electronically should be sent to: enrollmentservices@creighton.edu If a physical copy is requested, please mail it to the following address: Enrollment Services Creighton University 2500 California Plaza Omaha, NE 68178 Submit Required Personal Statement: Specific requirements are provided in the application process. Submit Recommendation Letter: One letter from persons other than friends or family members [academic or professional reference]. Include a current resume. Complete Financial Aid Application: If you’re planning to apply for financial aid, complete the FAFSA. Affordable Scholarships Apply for scholarships, such as the Dean’s Merit Scholarship and the Osher Reentry Scholarship.

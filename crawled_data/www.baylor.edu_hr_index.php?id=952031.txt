Baylor is a nurturing and rewarding place to work. Our campus is filled with energetic, talented, creative people inspired by the University’s mission. Helping each person fulfill that mission is what we’re all about. HR is a dedicated, responsive team working to serve the needs of every Baylor employee—from current faculty and staff, to part-time employees and retirees, to job seekers and aspiring candidates—and ultimately, our student body and the entire Baylor community. Our Mission We foster a caring community for Baylor employees to educate and transform men and women for worldwide leadership and service. It’s about striving to attract, develop, support, and retain the very best—then working to create an open, collaborative environment where every employee has the opportunity to maximize their talents to excel. Our Commitment Our commitment to each person’s success is at the heart of everything we do. It’s why we continually work to deliver creative solutions rooted in professional excellence, confidentiality, and Christian values. So whether you’re a candidate looking for employment, a current faculty or staff member looking for resources, a soon-to-be retiring employee looking for guidance, or an employee with a question, problem, or an issue—YOU—are our priority.

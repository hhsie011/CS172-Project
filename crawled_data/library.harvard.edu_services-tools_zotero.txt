Overview Zotero is a research management software program that enables you to save and organize your references, then output them as in-text citations, footnotes, and bibliographies through a word-processing software plugin. Zotero is: great for undergraduates as well as seasoned researchers especially good for saving non-traditional, web-based references aimed at humanities and social science research easy to learn cite How To Download Zotero Follow these step-by-step instructions for installing Zotero and getting access to unlimited cloud storage via your Harvard email address.

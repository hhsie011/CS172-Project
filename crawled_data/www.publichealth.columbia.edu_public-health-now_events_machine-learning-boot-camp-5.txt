Event Kind: Training Organizer: Department of Environmental Health Sciences SHARP Invitation Limited to: Open to the Public Website: Learn More Learn More Join us for the next live-stream Machine Learning Boot Camp on June 16-17, 2022. The Machine Learning Boot Camp is a two-day intensive boot camp of seminars combined with hands-on R labs and data applications to provide an overview of statistical concepts, techniques, and data analysis methods with applications in biomedical research. Investigators at all career stages are welcome to attend, and we particularly encourage trainees and early-stage investigators to participate.

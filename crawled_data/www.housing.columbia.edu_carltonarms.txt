Suites are comprised of singles and doubles. Key Features: Large rooms with beautiful views of the Hudson Private kitchens and study lounges are available in each suite Convenient access to Riverside Park Fitness equipment available on the top floor An opportunity for Sophomores to live almost exclusively with their class Building Details: Housing Rate: Upperclass Rate Entrance Location: 362 Riverside Drive Number of Residential Floors: 10 Single/Double Rooms within Suites: 25 singles / 53 doubles Air Conditioning: No Bathroom Style: Two shared bathrooms in each suite Cleaning Responsibility & Schedule: Bathrooms: Cleaned by Facilities. Note: Facilities will not move personal belongings to clean. Kitchens: Cleaned by Facilities. Note: Facilities staff will not wash dishes. Trash & Recycling: Kitchen and bathroom trash and recycling is collected daily by Facilities. Residents are responsible for taking their bedroom trash and recycling to designated building area. Flooring: Hardwood Furnishings: All rooms are furnished Building Amenities: Bike Storage: Available in basement Computer Lab & Print Station: Print station is available in the lobby. Computer lab is not available. See the list of computer labs and print stations in our residence halls. Fitness Room: Available on top floor and equipped with cable TV service Kitchen: Private kitchen in each suite Laundry: Free laundry facility in basement Lounge: Private lounge in each suite Take a Video Tour: Additional Photos: Image Carousel with 2 slides A carousel is a rotating set of images.

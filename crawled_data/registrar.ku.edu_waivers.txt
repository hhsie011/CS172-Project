Forms Tuition and Fee Waiver Forms The State of Kansas and Kansas Board of Regents allows the University of Kansas to provide a number of tuition and fee waivers. These waivers provide reduced tuition and fee rates for eligible students. Submitting a waiver form to the Office of the University Registrar does not guarantee acceptance. Eligibility for each is based on information on the waiver and is considered when received.

Training Tutorials & Documentation FAQ ERA Login Meet Our Staff Home › Research Administration › Pre Award Management Pre Award Management The Pre Award Management team is responsible for assisting faculty in identifying external funding sources. Pre Award Management is also responsible for submitting all proposals for grants and contracts for research and other scholarly activities to public agencies (federal, state or local government), private non-profit research organizations (such as the American Heart Association) and industry (such as testing agreements and contracts). The Pre Award Management staff assists investigators in preparation and submission of all proposals. Principal investigators or program directors may not apply for nor receive funding directly from sponsoring organizations without receiving review by the Pre Award Management team for University approval.

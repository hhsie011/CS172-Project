Parents & Families Get to know the Brown community through the resources below. Brown’s Office of College Admission is here to support students along with those who support them. While we recognize that the college search process centers around the student, we understand that parents and families may wish to assist in this meaningful and exciting journey, or get to know Brown on their own. Families of prospective Brunonians are encouraged to explore the content below for information on the admission and financial aid processes and other frequently asked questions about preparing for and applying to Brown. To receive updates throughout the year, please join our contact list.

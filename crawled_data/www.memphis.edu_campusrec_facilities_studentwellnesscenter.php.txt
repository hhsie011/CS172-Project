Brad Martin Student Wellness Center R. Brad Martin Student Wellness Center Our new Student Wellness Center at 3735 Southern Ave. is now open! R. Brad Martin Student Wellness Center provides a wide variety of fitness opportunities, classes, a teaching/demonstration kitchen and many more exciting features. With over 78,000 square feet of fitness fun, our new amenities include: A wide array of cardio and weight equipment and machines Indoor and outdoor basketball courts Tennis courts Wellness patio Tiger Cafe smoothie bar Tiger Food Lab teaching kitchen Four fitness studios for a variety of classes ...and much more! The former Student Recreation and Sports Complex next door remains in operation to provide programming for basketball, volleyball, badminton, racquetball and outdoor playing fields.

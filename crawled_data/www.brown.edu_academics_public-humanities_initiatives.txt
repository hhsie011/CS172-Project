Carter Brown Architecture Lectures Performances Workshops Blog Initiatives Working with local, national and sometimes international partners, the Center for Public Humanities incubates a range of initiatives that spark new approaches, methods or collaborations in the field of public humanities. These initiatives include conferences, exhibitions, publications, tours, performances, events and digital media projects that shape our understanding of public history, museum practice and curation, digital humanities, preservation and cultural heritage, and the arts.

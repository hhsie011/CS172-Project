Enter the Executive MBA program and learn important business fundamentals while developing the professional acumen to transform the way you do business. At Columbia, you learn from full-time professors who are engaged in ongoing research that is changing how business is conducted worldwide. You benefit from the industry leaders who step into our classrooms to share their experiences running successful enterprises. You’ll also find that our faculty encourages the peer-to-peer learning that emerges as you and your fellow students bring your own unique experiences to the classroom. A flexible, dynamic curriculum.

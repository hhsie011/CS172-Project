Texas Tech University System will not discriminate in its employment practices because of an applicant's race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, status as a protected veteran, or any other legally protected category, class or characteristic. The Office of Equal Opportunity upholds each institution's anti-discrimination and anti-retaliation policies and ensures that employees and applicants of the Texas Tech University System are treated fairly, equally, and respectfully. For additional information, please contact the Office of Equal Opportunity by calling 806-742-3627 or visiting the Office of Equal Opportunity website at www.texastech.edu/offices/equal-employment/.

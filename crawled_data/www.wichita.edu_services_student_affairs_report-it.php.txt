Reporting Forms COVID-19 COVID-19 This form should be used by students to self-report positive COVID-19 test results, quarantine/isolation requirements, or to ask general COVID-19 questions. Care Team Care Form The Care Team accepts referrals and incident reports from any person who is concerned about the health or safety of a WSU student, employee, visitor, or the campus community at large. Learn more about Care Team. Institutional Equity and Compliance Title IX / Discrimination WSU is committed to providing an educational and working environment free from discrimination on the basis of sex and all forms of protected characteristic discrimination. The Institutional Equity and Compliance Form gives you the opportunity to report any instances of this discrimination. Learn more about Title IX. CutH8 Bias Incident Reporting Form Wichita State University strives to create a community where all individuals can participate in the offerings of the University in a space free from bias. Student Advocate Student Advocate Reporting Form The Student Advocate is an executive, non-partisan member of the Student Government Association who works toward resolutions based on principles of fairness and equity, without favoritism toward any group or individual. Learn more about the Student Advocate. Student Conduct Conduct Incident Response Form This form should be used to report behavior that is disruptive to the campus community, infringes on any person’s ability to successfully pursue their academic progress, violates the Student Code of Conduct, or puts others or themselves in danger. Learn more about the student conduct process. Academic Integrity Academic Integrity This form is to be used by any individual wishing to report a violation of the WSU Policies and Procedures Manual Section 2.17 / Student Academic Integrity. Learn more about academic integrity. Clery Act Clery Report This form is to be utilized by individuals who have been designated by Wichita State University as a Campus Security Authority (CSA) under the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act (Clery Act).

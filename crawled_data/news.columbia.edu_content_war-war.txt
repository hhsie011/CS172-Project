There were many reasons why the nation ultimately broke apart, but enslaved black people repeatedly risking their lives to flee their masters in the South in search of freedom in the North proved that the “united” states was a lie. By awakening northerners to the true nature of slavery, and enraging southerners who demanded the return of their human “property,” fugitive slaves forced the nation to confront the truth about itself.

Tajikistan Tanzania Uganda Ukraine United States Zambia Zimbabwe Ukraine Recent unrest in Ukraine has threatened to undermine progress in rolling out antiretroviral therapy (ART) for people living with HIV, necessitating a strong, decentralized approach to providing comprehensive HIV care and treatment services. ICAP is working with the Ministry of Health to improve the quality of service delivery and increase patient retention in care, with particular focus on people who inject drugs and their sex partners, who are affected most by the HIV epidemic.

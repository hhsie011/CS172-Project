We engage students in the study of Education through undergraduate majors and minors, post-baccalaureate teacher certification programs, graduate degree programs, and professional development programs for practicing educators. Educational Studies students benefit from innovative faculty, supportive staff, and learning opportunities that are both interdisciplinary and experiential. As a school, we strive to produce critically engaged educators and citizens who promote equity and social justice across educational and community contexts and serve as leaders and change agents in their respective fields. Explore our School Apply Graduate Undergraduate Professional Development for Educators Advising Make a Gift Announcements Secondary & Middle Level Teacher Certification Admissions Pause The School of Educational Studies has approved a temporary suspension of admissions to the Secondary & Middle Level Teacher Certification Post-Baccalaureate. The program will be paused for two years (2022-2023; 2023-2024). Currently-enrolled students are not affected. We are reevaluating a program delivery model that will best serve our students, local school districts, and community. Please check back for further updates as they become available.

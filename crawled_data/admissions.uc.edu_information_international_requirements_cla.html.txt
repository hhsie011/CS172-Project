Conditional admission enrollment is a two-step process. All application materials must be submitted before the deadline date in order for an application to be reviewed. While UC International Admissions will work with applications submitted after the deadline, review for specific programs and start terms is not guaranteed. Deadlines Fall Semester: March 1 Spring Semester: August 1 Summer Semester: October 1 Before You Apply: Program Offerings Conditional Letters of Admission (CLAs) are limited to certain programs and terms. Before applying, be sure that your program is available. In the following chart, "x" indicates programs that offer conditional admission. How to Apply Step 1: Apply Online for Conditional Admission First-Year right arrow down arrow Attending university for the first time Online Application All undergraduate applicants must apply online. This includes the $100 application fee. Payments may be done by credit card (Visa, Master Card, or Discover), check or money order made payable to the University of Cincinnati. Transcripts We must receive official transcripts showing the current year’s subjects and the previous 3 years’ subjects. Transcripts should include a certified English translation, if needed. Send all documents using a tracking number, so you can follow the delivery of your materials: University of Cincinnati International Admissions 2618 McMicken Circle PO Box 210123 Cincinnati, OH 45221-0091 Transfer right arrow down arrow Attended a university outside of the USA Online Application All undergraduate applicants must apply online. This includes the $100 application fee. Payments may be done by credit card (Visa, Master Card, or Discover), check, or money order made payable to the University of Cincinnati. Transcripts Transferring from a university OUTSIDE the USA Course-by-course evaluations of all university transcripts by a member of the National Association of Credit Evaluators, such as World Education Services are required. Please have the evaluator send UC both the course-by-course evaluation and the original, official university transcripts. Transferring from a university INSIDE the USA Request your transcripts from your current university. Mail the sealed transcript to UC. Students with less than 30 semester credit hours (45 quarter credit hours) Original high school transcripts and proof of graduation (i.e. diploma or graduation certificate) are required in addition to university credentials. Send all documents using a tracking number, so you can follow the delivery of your materials: UC International Admissions 47 West Corry Boulevard Edwards One Building, Suite 5150 Cincinnati, OH 45221-0123 USA Step 2: Apply to ELS, UC’s English Language Partner For over 50 years ELS Language Centers (ELS) has helped hundreds of thousands of students from over 140 countries around the world learn English. The Cincinnati ELS Language Center is located directly on the University of Cincinnati’s Main Campus. Students receive weekly visits from UC Admissions to help students transition from ELS to UC. To enroll, visit the Cincinnati ELS Language Center Website or call +1 (513) 556-4034.

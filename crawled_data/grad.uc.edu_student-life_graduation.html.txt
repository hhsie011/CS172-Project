Watch the Spring 2022 Commencement Ceremonies Purchasing Regalia Doctoral Regalia Students participating in an in-person Doctoral Hooding Ceremony must have official UC regalia. Any graduating student may purchase regalia, regardless of their ceremony participation. Only officially licensed UC regalia is permitted to be worn for participation in the ceremony. Order Doctoral Standard Regalia from May 20 through June 30 on the Jostens UC doctoral webpage. The cost is $201.50 + tax and the Regalia will ship directly to you. If you have questions regarding the Doctoral Regalia, please call the UC Bookstore at 513-556-1700, then press 3 or email them at uc@bkstr.com. To inquire about Fine Quality Doctoral Regalia, please contact Phillip Shepherd at the UC Bookstore at 513-556-1287. Orders must be placed by June 5 to arrive in time for the August 5 ceremony. Master's Regalia Graduating master's students can stop by the UC Bookstore in Tangeman University Center (TUC) to purchase caps and gowns. Regalia may also be ordered online through the UC Bookstore website. Master's students have from May 16 to July 26 to order regalia. After Graduation Order your final transcript Where is my diploma?

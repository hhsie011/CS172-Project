collections meal plan management campus ID card We also administer non-student billing and accounts receivable and manage the cashiering function. We offer tools to students and their parents, like D-Pay, the campus billing and payment system and GET, the financial system associated with the campus card, to make it easier to manage student finances. We are here to help.

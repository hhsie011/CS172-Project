Investment in endowment will lay a foundation upon which future generations will grow in their knowledge and pursuit of their life's calling. Growth in endowment supports efforts to attract and retain exceptional faculty, sustain world-class research, and ensure deserving students from across the country and around the world have access to a transformational Baylor education. When alumni and friends give to endowed funds, their investments generate annual income to support faculty, students, research, and other priorities of the University today, while protecting and growing their original investment in perpetuity. Among the greatest needs at Baylor, endowed funds are a priority of Give Light in areas such as faculty chairs and professorships, endowed scholarships, and endowed funds for growth priorities. Faculty Chairs and Professorships To equip and sustain programs and experiential learning opportunities and allow Baylor to pursue renowned faculty who believe in the mission of the University. Endowed Scholarships To support undergraduate and graduate students as they prepare to excel as the next generation of leaders. Endowed Funds for Growth Priorities To support the construction and maintenance of facilities, innovative programs, and research across the University. Endowment Impact The Baylor Family's support to endowment continues to reap incredible blessings for the University through scholarship, through program and research funds and through faculty support, creating the rich Baylor Experience that allows our students to thrive.

Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more Best Buddies, HEAL, and SHOUT are CI's health related programs. These CI programs are involved in outreach into the community to assist local residents in signing up for health insurance, food stamps and other social services. The programs set up information tables at hospitals, food pantries, soup kitchens and our own adult classes. More information is found on the pages listed in the right hand column. There are many ways for you to get involved!

The College of Communications excels at producing the diverse, highly skilled and globally-minded communications professionals needed in today’s society. Students pursue a robust curriculum taught by world-class faculty with links to local industry; learning is enhanced with hands-on opportunities like the Daily Titan news, forensics or Al Día, and real-world experiences through the PRactical ADvantage agency, study abroad or the speech and hearing clinic. Now the college seeks to establish a home for Titan communicators — a building with cutting-edge media facilities and spaces to promote community-building, networking and international collaboration. With your help, we can continue to raise the bar in communications excellence for the 21st century.

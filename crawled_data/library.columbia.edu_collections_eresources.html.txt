Articles+ Search across full-text journals, e-books, and other materials. Citation Finder Use Citation Finder to find a specific article. Databases Periodical indexes & abstracts, directories, encyclopedias, dictionaries, other reference works. E-Books Links to collections of full-text books like Books24x7, Early English Books Online, NetLibrary. E-Journals Electronic journals and e-journal collections, such as JSTOR, Project Muse, and ScienceDirect. E-Music Sound recording collections, such as Classical Music Library, Naxos Music Library, and Smithsonian Global Sound. Locally-Developed E-Content Academic Commons: Online access to Columbia dissertations, working papers, proceedings, etc. CU Numerical Data Collection: Numeric data suitable for those interested in working with statistical software. Digital Collections & Exhibitions: A gateway to Columbia's digital collections and online exhibitions. E-Images: Image databases like the Columbia Image Bank and ARTstor. GeoData@Columbia: A portal for geospatial data at Columbia. See Off-Campus Access to E-Resources for more detailed information about accessing E-Resources, particularly from outside Columbia.

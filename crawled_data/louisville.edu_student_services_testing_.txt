Exams include: Math & Reading Accuplacer placement Tests, CLEP, DSST, Millers Analogy Test (MAT), and national testing such as the SAT and ACT. We are a full service center proctoring tests for career certiﬁcation through various vendors; PSI, Castle/Scantron, PROV, Kryterion and PAN, I-9 veriﬁcation for employment and administering tests for other schools, on-line courses and make-up exams. Office hours are Monday - Thursday 7 am - 5 pm Welcome to Testing Services at the University of Louisville. You can find us in Room 310 of Davidson Hall, behind the North Information Center booth off Cardinal Blvd & 1st Street. We administer academic, placement, certification and accreditation exams to students and the community. UofL students can take makeup exams, and Distance Education students can schedule their online exams in our office. See Parking and Directions for directions and Google Maps links to our office.

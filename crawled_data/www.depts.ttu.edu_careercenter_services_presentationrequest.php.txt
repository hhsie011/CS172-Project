Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Career Center Menu Search TTU University Career Center University Career Center Services Request a Presentation Are you looking for a guest presenter to provide marketable skills training and professional development for your class or student organization? Bring in a University Career Center staff member to speak with your students about their future! Topics include, but are not limited to, the following: UCC Services Developing a Strong Resume Preparing for an Interview Preparing for the Job Search Graduate School Application Prep Dress for Success Salary Negotiation Developing Your Digital Presence Translating Skills from College to Career First or Second-Year Career Exploration StrengthsQuest Strong Interest Inventory Focus 2 Career MBTI We also have prerecorded webinars and presentations available on the UCC Youtube Channel. Feel free to incorporate them into your class or let us know if you need a custom recording through the presentation request form. To request a UCC presentation, fill out the request form. If you have any questions, please call our office at (806) 742-2210. Login to your Hire Red Raiders account and schedule an appointment online. If you have any questions navigating the site, please call our office at (806) 742-2210. Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. View Map Meet the Staff The University Career Center encourages persons with disabilities to participate in our programs and activities.

His colleagues say he has a level of excitement for the writing process that boarders on the infectious. Nick is a great strategic planner that can envision and implement the big picture while being equally attentive to details. The department considers him a visionary that inspires others to achieve greatness. Nick's colleagues describe him as someone who demonstrates integrity and commitment to living out his faith which is evident in his work. One colleague said in their nomination, "Dr. Werse approaches his role with grace, generosity, and an ambitious pursuit of excellence and progress." Thank you, Nick, for being an outstanding staff member!

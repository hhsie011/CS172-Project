Apply Own Your Education Prepare for today's interconnected, global world with an education that's broad, deep, and uniquely your own. At the University of Miami, you will be able to individualize your academic experience with programs that cater to your strengths, goals, and curiosities. So whether you've known since Career Day in elementary school or are still unsure what you'd like to study, UM's wide range of majors and programs makes it possible to explore the topics that interest you most. BROWSE OUR MAJORS & PROGRAMS An Education That's Anything But General Education works best when you have a stake in your learning. Here, you’ll customize your curriculum through the Cognates Program of General Education. Mix and match disciplines to cultivate your curiosities, unlock your potential, and achieve your goals while fulfilling your general education requirements. EXPLORE COGNATES 12:1 Student-to-faculty ratio 97% Full-time faculty with a doctorate or highest degree in their field 50% Undergraduate classes with 16 or fewer students Real-World Experience Put theory into practice with hands-on courses and small class sizes, where our distinguished professors double as compassionate mentors. Experiential Learning Preview College Life Calling all high school sophomores and juniors! The Summer Scholars Program is your opportunity to explore your academic passions and preview college life. Earn college credit, interact with world-class professors, and develop as an independent thinker. Summer Scholars Program Study Abroad Programs Go further and dig deeper into another culture by studying abroad. Explore our 80+ programs in 40+ countries, including 8 U Programs designed and taught by Miami faculty. Explore Your Options Dual-Degree Programs Qualifying students have the opportunity to combine two separate degree programs which will allow you to accelerate your academic and career options. Honors &amp; Scholars Programs As one of the nation's most active research universities, we offer multiple honors programs for academically accomplished and motivated incoming students. Academic Resources From help with homework or finding a job, your ’Canes community is there to ensure you get what you need.

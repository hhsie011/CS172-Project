All alumni, friends and fans are encouraged to join the FSU Alumni Association as a way to stay connected to Florida State. Additional perks of membership include: Access to the FSU Alumni Reward Zone, where new members receive an exclusive FSU Alumni Association gift and all members have the opportunity to purchase exclusive alumni items including the exclusive FSU Alumni Association spirit flag featuring our Osceola and Renegade graphic VIRES magazine Five priority points toward FSU athletics season tickets Discount on the purchase of a commemorative brick at Westcott Plaza Discounts for many major entertainment attractions, rental cars and hotels across the United States Eligibility to serve on the Florida State University Alumni Association National Board of Directors For more information, contact the FSU Alumni Association at alumni@fsu.edu or 850.644.2761. Lifetime members also receive an autographed replica of the FSU Fight Song and a Life Member vehicle license plate frame. Which membership is right for you? FSU SAA member benefits differ from those for FSU Alumni Association members. Add a Second Member to your Membership Every Annual, 5-year and Life membership purchased after July 1, 2013, includes the option to add a second member within the same household at no additional cost. Both members should be living at the same address (not required for active military members); all membership information, including membership cards, will be sent to the single address associated with the membership. If you purchased a 5-year or Lifetime membership prior to July 1, 2013, you may add a second member at a greatly reduced price from joint membership. Contact the FSU Alumni Association at 850.644.2761 or FSUalum@alumni.fsu.edu for more information. Graduation from Florida State University is not a prerequisite to become a member of the FSU Alumni Association. Many members are parents and spouses of alumni, and fans of FSU.

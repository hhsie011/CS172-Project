Her background includes training in both social and health sciences. She obtained her Ph.D. from the Departments of Psychiatry and Family Medicine at McGill University. Her doctoral dissertation was a qualitative study about patient participation in palliative care decisions. She then completed a postdoctoral fellowship at the University of Montreal Public Health Research Institute (IRSPUM) in social epidemiology of aging. Dr. Belanger's research program involves mixed-methods designs and addresses the assessment and management of patient-reported symptoms among older adults, the delivery of end-of-life care in a variety of settings, as well as decision-making processes about palliative options of care.

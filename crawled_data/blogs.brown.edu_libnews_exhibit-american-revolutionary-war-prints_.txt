American Revolutionary War Prints London: Hogg, 1790 Brown University Library, Anne S.K. Brown Military Collection Explore Independence Day from a British point of view. “Engraved for Barnard’s New Complete & Authentic History of England,” this collection of 4 copper-engraved plates after William Hamilton, 1751- 1801 (artist) feature significant milestones from the American Revolutionary War (Apr 19, 1775 – Sep 3, 1783).

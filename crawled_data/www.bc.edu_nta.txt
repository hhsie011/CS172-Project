Every year NTA abstracts approximately 1,500 articles chosen from more than 500 periodicals in numerous languages. Hundreds of books are also summarized annually. In May 2017 NTA was featured in the BC Chronicle. Editorial correspondence may be sent to: New Testament Abstracts Boston College School of Theology and Ministry 140 Commonwealth Avenue Chestnut Hill, MA 02467-3800 NTAbstracts@bc.edu Print Subscriptions NTA is published three times each year. Subscription, payable in advance: $43 for individuals and $100 for institutions; back issues are $20 for individuals and $35 for institutions. A separate fee for international mailing will apply. Box 19966 Baltimore, MD 21211-0966 Phone: 800-548-1784 or 410-516-6987 FAX: 410-516-3866 jrnlcirc@press.jhu.edu www.cuapress.org New Testament Abstracts Online Over 50,000 abstracts and book notices from NTA are available to institutions in a searchable online format from EBSCO Publishing. The current version includes all abstracts and book notices published from 1985 through 2019 (NTA 29-63). Now in its seventh decade, NTA, a publication of STM has become the definitive source on current literature devoted to research of the New Testament and the times in which it was formed, and an invaluable resource around the world.

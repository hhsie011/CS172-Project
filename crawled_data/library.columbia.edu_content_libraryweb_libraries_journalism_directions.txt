Pulitzer Hall is located between Lerner Hall and Miller Theatre between W. 115th St. and W. 116th St. Pulitzer Hall is accessible via the Columbia University quadrangle; the entrance faces south. Journalism is situated inside the Stabile Student Center, located to the left of the main entrance to Pulitzer Hall, past the elevators and through the glass doors. Study space for journalism students is available just outside the library. Light snacks and refreshments are available for purchase in the cafe next door.

Read details about the 2022 program here! Read More Research & Scholarship 2022 Program Details Check out what's happening during this year's Summer Research Institute! Read More Distinctive Collaborations Between Students & Faculty The Summer Research Institute (SRI) provides summer funding, subsidized campus housing for qualifying students, and supportive programming for students conducting STEM research under the guidance of a faculty mentor. SRI provides a common experience for students, providing training and opportunities to build skills, along with faculty lectures, alumnae panel discussions, social events, and more. The summer concludes with a capstone Poster Session, where students present the progress of their research. Questions can be sent to sri@barnard.edu Learn About SRI Funding Committed to the principle of funding students who can successfully find a research mentor. Past Programs SRI has a rich history of growth, change, and success.

We will continue to update the community regularly to keep you informed of additional measures. Harvard School of Dental Medicine community guidance Return-to-Campus Planning The HSDM workforce began returning to campus in July, 2021. HSDM convened a Return-to-Campus Task Force to: Review and develop a safe workforce re-entry plan for our currently remote faculty and staff that meets the needs of the various business and academic units, while taking into account the unique needs of individuals Provide managers with HR guidance, tools, and support to facilitate planning, with the goal of each unit creating a plan to share with HR  Set schedule/timeline for re-entry milestones  Ensure safety and compliance with all University and State guidance, policies, and procedures Communicate points of the re-entry process in a cohesive and transparent way through email messages, online FAQ, and Town Hall meeting Support the transition back to work and foster a renewed sense of community About HSDM Overview Administrative Offices History Coronavirus Response Messages to the HSDM Community Return-to-Campus FAQ Contact Us Board Leadership Harvard Dental Center Cambridge 114 Mt.

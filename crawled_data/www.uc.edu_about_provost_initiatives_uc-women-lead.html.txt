UC Women Lead is a signature program of the Office of the Provost, and is vital to fostering and developing our greatest institutional asset: our people. UC is one of over 100 institutions of higher education that have pledged to advance women into senior leadership positions in higher education. The UC Women Lead program is designed to create an affirming culture for women on campus, help women develop their strengths and prepare them to be leaders.

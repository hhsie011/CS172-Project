Lee presents transformable robot at Jeff Bezos’ MARS Conference Dr. Kiju Lee attended the 5th annual MARS Conference to present her adaptive Wheel-and-Leg Transformable Robot that can efficiently traverse over varying surfaces using wheels or legs depending on its immediate need — with or without human intervention. New method for thermoplastic production offers potential to replace metals Dr. Amir Asadi has been awarded a Faculty Career Development Award by the National Science Foundation. The award funded the development of a thermoplastic composites manufacturing method that uses hybrid nanomaterials and is complete within minutes.

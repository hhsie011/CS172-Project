Experience Creighton The best way to find out if Creighton is right for you is to see for yourself! We build your visit around you—your academic and extracurricular interests, your wishes and needs. Interested in the health sciences? We can set up a virtual meeting with a faculty member who conducts research with undergraduate students. Interested in the performing arts? We’ll get you in touch with a student who is active in theatre. Schedule your visit and see why you belong at Creighton. Take a Virtual Tour Launch Experience Life in Omaha In-Person Visit Options Based on CDC and University guidelines, we are currently operating with the following practice: All visitors, regardless of vaccination status, may determine whether or not they choose to wear a face covering during their visit to campus. All visitors, regardless of vaccination status, are required to wear a face covering while inside a medical building or school. If you have questions, please call us at 402.280.2703. Visit Type Recommended For Daily Visits High school juniors and seniors Our personalized campus visit is a great way to learn more about Creighton, explore your academic and extracurricular interests, and tour the campus. A typical in-person campus visit, which includes a presentation highlighting Creighton's opportunities, a campus tour with a current Creighton student, and a one-on-one meeting with an admissions counselor, will last about 2 hours. Additional in-person appointments with departments/programs are offered during the fall and spring semesters and are based on availability. We are also happy to arrange these additional meetings virtually. If you have requested any additional appointments during your visit, please allow an additional 30-60 minutes per appointment. School of Pharmacy and Health Professions will offer small group information sessions for students interested in pre-pharmacy, pre-physical therapy, and pre-occupational therapy on select Mondays at 12:15 pm and Fridays at 10:45 am only. This requires an additional 60 minutes. College of Nursing simulation lab tours are available on Mondays at 10:45 a.m. and Wednesdays at 12:15 p.m. only. This requires an additional 60 minutes and is separate from your campus tour.

Some of the technology and tools are provided specifically for you as a student to use, such as Starfish scheduling, My Graduation Plan, and the degree audit tool. Other tools and resources are designed for your advisor to help them provide you with the best advising. In addition to tools and resources, we deliver training and professional development for advisors and leadership support for advising directors, so our advising staff can continue to offer high-quality advising. We also coordinate assessments and surveys to determine the advising needs of our students to ensure advising expectations are met. We also provide direct leadership to the Credit Evaluation Center, New Student Orientation and specialized advising offices, including College Credit Plus for high school students taking undergraduate level courses at UC; Transfer & Transition Advising Center for incoming transfer students and current UC students changing their major; and Pre-Professional Advising for students interested in pursuing health profession schools and law school. Advising mission and vision Mission The UC Advising community engages and supports students through teaching and collaboration to achieve their academic, personal, and professional goals. Vision The UC Advising community will advance a personalized educational environment in which: All students achieve their goals through exploration, reflection, and engagement. We are committed to inclusion, teaching, and lifelong learning. We are global leaders in advising practice, scholarship, and innovation.

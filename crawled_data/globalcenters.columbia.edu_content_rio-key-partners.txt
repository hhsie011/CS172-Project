Key Partners Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro serves as a hub for Columbia programs and initiatives throughout Brazil working closely with local partners and the University to tackle global challenges. Its mission is to improve the understanding of global challenges through a cross-disciplinary, transcultural and applied perspective constantly expanding our network. If you're interested in partnering with us, please contact our Program Manager, Teresa Borges ([email protected]).

Keep your eye on the Memphis Flyer for weekly listings of performing and fine arts events in the area. Two great local blogs to follow for great things to do in Memphis are the I Love Memphis Blog and Choose901. For a comprehensive listing of community arts organizations, see the following: Arts Memphis Crosstown Arts Performing Arts The heart of the performing arts season extends from September through early June. Local professional organizations regularly schedule nationally/internationally known guest performers, conductors and choreographers. Dance Ballet Memphis New Ballet Ensemble Music Rudi E. Sheidt School of Music Memphis Symphony Orchestra Opera Memphis Theater University of Memphis Department of Theater & Dance Playhouse on the Square Theatre Memphis The Orpheum Hattiloo Theater Theaterworks Memphis Circuit Playhouse Buckman Performing & Fine Arts Center Germantown Performing Arts Center Fine Arts The fine arts in Memphis are fueled by three university programs and an independent art college. All four institutions present gallery shows throughout the year. In addition, Memphis has two fine art museums and a plethora of commercial and experimental galleries, with a variety of museums dedicated to our long history of rock n' roll and rich musical heritage. The South Main Arts District lies approximately a mile south of Memphis Law.

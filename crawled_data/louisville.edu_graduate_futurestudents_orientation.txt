We are excited you have decided to join us at UofL for your graduate education! One of the best ways to get started in your graduate school experience, is to learn more about your new home and new academic experience. The University of Louisville Graduate School is the umbrella school for most graduate programs at UofL. If you're attending a graduate program at UofL, not only will you be a part of an academic program, you'll also be a part of a particular "college" or "school", as well likely a part of the "Graduate School". Here in the UofL Graduate School, we're determined to help you be successful! The first step in this journey to success is to attend the New Graduate Student Orientation.

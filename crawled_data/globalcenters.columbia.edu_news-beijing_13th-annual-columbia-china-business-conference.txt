Business Conference Beijing 13th Annual Columbia China Business Conference April 15, 2021 Columbia Global Centers | Beijing was proud to sponsor the 13th Annual Columbia China Business Conference organized by the Greater China Society of Columbia Business School (GCS). This conference is one of the most influential China-related business conferences in North America. During this four-day event, more than 15,000 participants joined live on Beijing Center's bilibili streaming platform. The conference focused on discussing the challenges and solutions regarding the current state of China and the rest of the world to explore the uncharted waters of different industries.

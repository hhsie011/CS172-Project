Learning and Research Center The Clemson School of Nursing Clinical Learning Research Center (CLRC) is an innovative, educational and research based center housed at both the Clemson and the Greenville sites of instruction. The CLRC has a Clinical Learning Laboratory (CLL) instructional space on the second floor in Edwards Hall on Clemson’s main campus and on the third floor in the Clemson University Nursing building located in Greenville. The CLRC also has a Learning Resource Center (LRC) in Edwards Hall. Clinical Learning and Research Center Clinical Learning Laboratory Simulation Education Clemson Home Centers and Institutes Current: Clinical Learning and Research Center I would like to welcome you to the Clemson School of Nursing Clinical Learning Research Center (CLRC). I, along with our competent faculty and staff, are here to help facilitate your learning by providing you with a safe environment to practice the nursing knowledge, skills, and attitudes you will use in clinical situations with patients, families, and communities. The CLRC provides you with the latest technology to practice nursing care delivery using models, task trainers, and mid- and high-fidelity manikins. This practice, in a realistic healthcare environment, prepares you for patient encounters and helps build self-confidence. Enjoy your time in the lab.

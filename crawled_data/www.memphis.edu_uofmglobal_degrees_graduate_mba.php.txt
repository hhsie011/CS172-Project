Call 844-302-3886 Email Us Master of Business Administration The Online Master of Business Administration degree at the University of Memphis is offered within the Fogelman College of Business & Economics which is the only business college in the Memphis area fully accredited by the prestigious AACSB. The Fogelman online MBA program has been designated as a "Top 25 Online MBA" by the Princeton Review in its 2016 and 2017 lists and is designed for ambitious professionals whose demanding schedule and/or geographic location makes it difficult to attend classes in-person at the Fogelman College of Business & Economics. Our top-flight, Online MBA faculty members provide you with synchronous and asynchronous instruction to permit a 24/7 learning environment wherever and whenever you connect to the Internet.

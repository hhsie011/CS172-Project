Skip To Main Content Iowa State University Online Distance Learning Search the website Open Search Box Open Mobile Menu Search the website Open Search Box Online Courses Finding Online and Distance Learning Courses An up-to-date listing of available online courses can be viewed using the Online Schedule of Classes. Follow the three steps below: Go to Advanced Search. Choose the desired academic term from the drop down menu. The current term is the default value. Go to the Course Type drop down box, and choose Distance Education. Choose Submit. The list you view will include the latest updated Distance Education offerings. Here you will find basic information about the course, including delivery fees, textbook information (if available), the instructor’s name (if available), and links to other information.

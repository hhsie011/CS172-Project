Online Permit Request (OPR) Faculty and Staff can request daily permits for guests and visitors through the Online Permit Request (OPR) System! All paid permit requests will be processed upon receipt when "faculty/staff" is the selected parking location. Visit Online Permit Request (OPR) FAQs page for more information. Questions? Contact the Permit Coordinator at mdelgadillo@fullerton.edu or (657) 278-3082. Purchase Daily Parking Permit Campus guests can purchase a Daily Parking Permit from one of the on-campus permit dispensers located in parking lots A, D, and G, and on levels 2 and above in the Eastside South Parking Structure, the Nutwood Parking Structure, and the Statre College Parking Structure.

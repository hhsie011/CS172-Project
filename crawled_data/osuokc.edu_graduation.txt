The online application for Fall 2022 graduation is active – make sure to get your application in today! 1. Log in to my.okstate.edu, and click on the student self-service portal. 2. In the student tab, click Student records. 3. Click Apply to Graduate. Once you’ve completed the survey, you’ll be re-directed to the graduation application. If you have any questions, send an email to okc.graduation@okstate.edu. Lost or missing diploma? Order a copy of your diploma online. Re-prints cost $25, and are payable online with credit card or check.

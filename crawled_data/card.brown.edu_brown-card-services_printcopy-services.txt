Print/Copy services are available in Brown libraries and computer clusters. Card readers are installed on printing equipment to enable payment for print/copy services. Swipe the Brown Card at any machine reader. The card's current Bear Bucks value will appear. The cardholder makes their selection. The cost of the print transaction will be deducted from the Bear Bucks Account. The print release stations will show you a combined balance of Paw Prints and Bear Bucks.

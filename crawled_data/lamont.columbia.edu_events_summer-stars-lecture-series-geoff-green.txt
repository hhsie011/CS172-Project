5:00 PM America/New_York Online Event Join LDEO Director Maureen Raymo for the second virtual event of the 2021 Summer Stars Lecture Series: Count to 10 - A Partnership Vision for the Decade of Impact with Geoff Green, founder and president of the Students on Ice Foundation, an internationally renowned program that has taken more than 3,000 youth, educators, elders, experts, scientists, leaders, and artists from around the world on educational journeys to the Arctic and Antarctic. Geoff serves as the Chair of the Board of Directors for the Ottawa Riverkeeper. The Lamont Summer Stars Lecture Series, hosted by Columbia Climate School co-founding dean and Lamont director Maureen Raymo, brings inspiring speakers to the Columbia community whose work focuses squarely on the intersection of our humanity, nature, sustainability, and the Earth. This is a virtual event. Registration is required.

Marine & Oceanographic Academy – TEAM A Citizen Scientist The Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science Outreach Program works in tandem with faculty to bridge the University to the general public and all of the communities of South Florida. Outreach at the Rosenstiel School aids faculty with science proposals and with synergetic partnerships with science museums and South Florida school districts. The Rosenstiel School of Marine & Atmospheric Science’s stellar faculty, cutting edge research, and state-of-the-art marine science laboratories make RSMAS a top-notch marine science research institution.

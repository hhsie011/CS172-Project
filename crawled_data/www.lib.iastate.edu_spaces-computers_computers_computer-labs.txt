Computers and Computer Labs Computers are located throughout the University Library and other library locations. For a complete list of installed software applications, see Software Available in the Library. Most workstations require log-in with ISU NetID; quick-use computers at stand-up desks, no log-in is required. All computers have USB ports and either CD/ROM or DVD/ROM drives. Other special features or intended uses are highlighted below. Computer labs and clusters in the Parks Library are intended for open use, except when scheduled for library instructional activities. Labs and clusters cannot be scheduled for non-library instruction. Please see Guidelines for the Use of Library Computers.

Campbell Director of Athletics Patrick Kraft has accepted an offer to become the vice president for intercollegiate athletics at Pennsylvania State University. Kraft became director of athletics at Boston College in 2020, and he served as director of intercollegiate athletics at Temple University from 2015-2020. University President William P. Leahy, S.J., said that a search for a new AD will begin immediately, and that Senior Associate Athletics Director J.M. Caparro will serve as interim director of athletics. “I thank Pat for his time at BC and wish him and his family the best in his new endeavor,” said Fr. Leahy.

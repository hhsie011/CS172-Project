The Oklahoma State University online MBA program moves up to 26th in the nation in the 2016-17 Top 50 Online MBA Super Rankings after coming in at 29th a year ago. This is the third consecutive year that OSU has been listed in the top 50. “OSU has been a leader in online education programs for a long time,” said Ramesh Sharda, vice dean of the Watson Graduate School of Management. “Our MBA program serves both military and professionals in other areas looking to enhance their management knowledge and skills to gain an edge for their career growth. Our MBA program is steadily climbing in various rankings, and we are really pleased that we are in the top 30 programs, given the proliferation of programs and the competitive nature.” The TopManagementDegrees’ review of the OSU program reads as follows: OSU’s distinguished Spears School of Business not only offers highly acclaimed on-campus programs, but online programs as well. The online MBA focuses on teamwork and helping students develop the skills needed to advance into management roles in the business world. Based on students’ professional goals, they can choose one of the following concentrations to personalize their degree: Accounting, Business Sustainability, Data Science, Economics, Energy Business, Entrepreneurship, Global Marketing, Human Resource Management, Information Assurance, Marketing Analytics, Non-profit Management, Risk Management, or a General MBA. TopManagementDegrees.com provides prospective management students with information on the top-ranked institutions in the United States from the most affordable to the best online programs.

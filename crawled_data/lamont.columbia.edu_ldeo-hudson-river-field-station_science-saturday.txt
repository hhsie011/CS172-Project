Science Saturdays are family-friendly organized programs that are aimed to engage and educate about our beautiful and unique environment. Science Saturday is held seasonally from July through September (Labor Day) from 11:00 AM-3:00 PM so come join us! Some programmed activities include: Seining and fish identification Rod and reel fishing clinics Water quality testing Plankton pumping and microbiology identification And more! Science Saturday program themes and activities are outlined in the Upcoming Education Events section on our landing page. These events vary from week to week and are completely free-of-charge so come out with us and explore the outdoors!

When you make a gift to the University of Washington through your will, trust or retirement plan, you’re seeding an orchard that will nourish generations of students to come. Planned gifts offer creative, flexible strategies for your estate and charitable planning. Some even provide you with income. Many can reduce your taxes. The greatest benefit? Knowing you’re supporting the world-changing work of the UW.

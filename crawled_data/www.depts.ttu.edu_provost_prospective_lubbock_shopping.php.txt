From one-of-a-kind boutiques, western wear or the latest fashion styles, there are plenty of options. You can always find a place to eat in Lubbock. With more than 1,000 restaurants, there is something for every taste. Down-home country cooking, we have that. Fine dining, we have that, too, and everything in between. Lubbock has a full complement of popular chain and fast-food restaurants. Want something unique? Lubbock's own microbrewery features a selection of fresh beers, and Lubbock's five wineries offer tastings of internationally award-winning wines. Want something unique? Try Skyviews, a full-service restaurant that is actually a Texas Tech classroom offering a hospitality laboratory experience for 150 to 200 students each week.

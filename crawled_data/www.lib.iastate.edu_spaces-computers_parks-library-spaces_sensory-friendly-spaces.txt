Accessible and Sensory Friendly Spaces The following categories of "Accessible and Sensory Friendly Spaces" have been identified in Parks Library: ADA Compliaint and Gender Inclusive Restrooms Accessible Elevators Natural lighting spaces Lower Distraction/Semi-private Quiet/Uncrowded spaces Brody chairs Use the StackMap Explore tool to help locate sensory friendly spaces in Parks Library. Hint: After opening StackMap Explore, click on the "Places" tab in the upper left corner of the Explore home page under the Search Bar, and then select from the list the type of place you're looking for.

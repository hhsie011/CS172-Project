Any Brown faculty member who has served as graduate advisor, trainer, or dissertation chair, unofficial or official mentor is eligible for nomination. The recipient is honored at the annual University Awards Ceremony. Nominations should be made through UFunds, found under the Graduate Student and Alumni Awards button.

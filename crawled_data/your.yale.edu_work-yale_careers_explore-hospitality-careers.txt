Committed to excellence Join Yale hospitality and be welcomed by a network of people dedicated to taking the University’s dining experience to new heights. You’ll have the opportunity to share your passion for food and welcoming personality with Yale community and visitors. You’ll be a part of a diverse culture of food, learning and service. “Our mission is to nourish a culture in which the interwoven pleasures of growing, cooking and sharing food become an integral part of each student’s campus experience and relationships,” says Rafi Taherian, Associate Vice President of Yale Hospitality.

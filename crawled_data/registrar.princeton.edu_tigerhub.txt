Contact finance@princeton.edu with questions. The system is available daily from 6:00 a.m. EST to 5:00 a.m. EST, except on Wednesdays when it is available from 8:00 a.m. EST to 5:00 a.m. EST. Prior to logging in, we strongly encourage you to delete browser cache and cookies, close all tabs and windows of your browser, and reopen the browser. TigerHub Login The information in your student record is protected by the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). See Rights, Rules and Responsibilities for a full explanation.

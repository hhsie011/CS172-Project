The certificate allows you to build skills and knowledge with respect to sport nutrition and the prescription of exercise, preparing you for a career in a school setting and beyond. Learn how physical education weight training is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 12-credit-hour certificate consists of four courses chosen from the following: Weight Training Exercise and Sport Nutrition Concepts in the Prescription of Exercise Seminar in Strength and Conditioning Motivation Independent Study View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click on "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

The Division of Human Resources supports the University's commitment to a diverse and inclusive workplace via an ongoing process to address sexual harassment. This process integrates efforts by the offices of the Department of Inclusion and Workforce Diversity (DIWD; 607-255-3976; owdi@cornell.edu), Workforce Policy and Labor Relations (WPLR; 607-255-6866; HRpolicy@cornell.edu ), Title IX Office (607-255-2242; TitleIX@cornell.edu), and Organizational Development and Talent Management (ODTM; 607-255-7867; org_dev@cornell.edu). The diagram below illustrates our process and lists resources for each stage.

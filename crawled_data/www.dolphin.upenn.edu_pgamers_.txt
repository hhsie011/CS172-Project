Penn Gamers Club Penn Gamers Club About Us The Penn Gamers Club is a group of undergrad students, graduate students, faculty and staff members, alumni, and community guests who gather to play games. We meet each Wednesday. For the most recent information about the Penn Gamers Club, please visit our Facebook page or our Google Groups page. If you would like to be placed on the Penn Gamers mailing list, you can subscribe online at our Google Groups page.

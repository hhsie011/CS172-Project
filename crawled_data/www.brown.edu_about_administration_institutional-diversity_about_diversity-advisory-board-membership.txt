The goals of the Committee shall include: Advising the Vice President for Institutional Equity and Diversity on issues occurring in the community as it pertains to diversity and inclusion and suggest ways to promote diversity and develop a welcoming and inclusive campus climate. Serving as a sounding board for the Office of Institutional Equity and Diversity on new or ongoing initiatives. Membership The membership shall consist of the Vice President for Institutional Equity and Diversity, the Vice President for Campus Life and Student Services, faculty members, staff members, two undergraduate students, two graduate students and two medical students. Members shall serve staggered two-year terms. Chair and Staffing The Vice President for Institutional Equity and Diversity serves as the Chair. The Council shall be staffed by the Office of Institutional Equity and Diversity. Procedures The Diversity Advisory Board shall meet 3-4 times each academic year (no meetings shall occur in the summer months). Appointment of Members Faculty members will be appointed by the Vice President for Institutional Equity and Diversity with advice from the Faculty Executive Committee (FEC). Student members will be appointed by the Vice President of Institutional Equity and Diversity with advice from the Undergraduate Council of Students (UCS), the Graduate Student Council (GSC) and the Medical Student Senate. Staff members will be appointed by the Vice President of Institutional Equity and Diversity with advice from the Staff Advisory Council (SAC).

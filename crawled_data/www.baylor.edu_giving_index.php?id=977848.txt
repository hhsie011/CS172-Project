Alumni and friends of Baylor University have a great opportunity to enhance their giving through corporate supported matching gift programs. Your support will provide opportunities for students and enhance university programs, and all this can be done effortlessly by simply filling out a matching gifts form for your employer. Just think of the impact your gift will have on Baylor University students when multiplied. What do I need to do to get started? Matching a gift to Baylor only takes three easy steps: STEP 1: Contact your employee's human resources department to see if they offer a matching gift program, or use our search tool: STEP 2: Obtain a matching gift form from your human resources department. STEP 3: Complete the form and send it in with your next gift to Baylor. It's that easy! Not only can you match often, but you also can maximize your gift potential. How to submit your gift Mail your completed matching gift form along with your gift to: Baylor University-Gift Processing Office One Bear Place #97050 Waco, TX 76798 For any questions relating to matching gifts contact our University Advancement Gift Processing staff at 254-710-2561 or gift_request@baylor.edu.

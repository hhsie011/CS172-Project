Barnard Music Barnard Music provides the vocal program for the university: the Barnard-Columbia Chorus and Chamber Choir, solo studio lessons, voice classes, and opera. Students may follow a course sequence for a major or minor in music or a major in ethnomusicology. Gail Archer is the director of the program.

Other Agreements for the Support of Research Other Agreements for the Support of Research The complex and unique agreements assigned to this office support research that combines multiple aspects of stand-alone agreements. Examples include: Non-Funded Research Agreements (NFRAs) Unfunded Cooperative Research and Development Agreements (CRADAs) Unfunded Research Collaboration Agreements with all types of institutions including the federal and state government Research Services Agreements Memoranda of understanding outside of the formulaic MOUs, including unfunded MOUs which contemplate future research collaboration Unique facilities and equipment use agreements, for use of hazardous materials or research equipment at Brown by external parties, in partnership with the departments which control the facilities Equipment loan agreements associated with broader research projects and initiatives Unusual purchases that include aspects of research collaboration or the sharing of research data and intellectual property ownership Unfunded Research consortia agreements Research collaborations where Brown is the sponsor with its own funding Contracts through which a third party provides in-kind goods or services in support of Brown-conducted research Need additional help?

Students Travel Registration Withdrawal Policy Exchange students from our partner universities may study abroad at University of Miami for one or two semesters. University of Miami’s Office of Study Abroad administers exchange programs with over 80 universities worldwide. To see if your university is a partner, please click on List of Exchange Partners. To the left, you will find links on how to apply, how to prepare for arrival and frequently asked questions (FAQs). If you have questions about applying to University of Miami, please check with your home university’s international office.

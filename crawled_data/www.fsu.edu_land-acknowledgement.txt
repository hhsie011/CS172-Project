We pay respect to the resiliency of their tribal members, past and present, and to all Indigenous peoples. The University honors its unique and collaborative friendship with the Seminole Tribe of Florida, together paying tribute to the Tribe’s great history and rich culture. We encourage all to learn about the significance of Indigenous peoples in this region and throughout the nation.

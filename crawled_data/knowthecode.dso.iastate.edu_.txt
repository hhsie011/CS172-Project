Using an educational approach, the office works to create opportunities for students to learn from their choices and experiences, while taking responsibility for their actions. Through the student disciplinary process, the Office of Student Conduct works to protect student rights, and to foster a healthy and safe educational environment that promotes mutual respect and fosters ethical standards. Individuals who believe that a violation of the Student Code of Conduct has occurred may contact the Office of Student Conduct to discuss options available for reporting incidents to the appropriate office/s on campus.

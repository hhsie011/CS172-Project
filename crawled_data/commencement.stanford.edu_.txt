I am pleased to invite you to Stanford University’s Commencement Weekend, June 9 – 12, 2022. This year, we will celebrate the university’s 131st Commencement for the Class of 2022 on Sunday and hold a separate in-person Commencement ceremony for the Class of 2020 on Saturday. Graduates from the Class of 2021, some of whom were not able to join us for last year’s Commencement, are also welcome to attend the Saturday celebration.

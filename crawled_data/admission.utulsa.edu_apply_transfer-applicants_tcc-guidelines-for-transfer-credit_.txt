Close Search Link to site home page Office of Admission > Apply > Transfer Applicants > TCC Guidelines for Transfer Credit TCC Guidelines for Transfer Credit TRANSFER ADMISSION Transfer Application Requirements Transfer Scholarships and Financial Aid Transfer from Tulsa Community College (TCC) Transfer Admission Counselors Transfer Frequently Asked Questions Request Information Transfer Admission Guide As a member of the Tulsa Transfer Collaborative, The University of Tulsa partners with Tulsa Community College with a focus on student success to ensure a smooth transition from TCC to TU. TU welcomes all TCC students -whether in Tulsa Achieves or as an adult learner. We have a team of transfer admission counselors, transfer credit experts, success coaches, academic advisors, career coaches and faculty ready to assist you in making TU a reality. We have transfer credit planning. Utilize our TCC Transfer Guides and our Articulation Agreements to plan your transfer credit. We can help you make a financial plan. TU has specific scholarships for TCC students. Be aware of scholarships for which you may qualify so you can start to get an idea of your educational investment. We also have need-based funding, based on the FAFSA. Your admission counselor is here to help you navigate the process. We can aid the reverse transfer process. We understand that your educational path may lead to transferring before completing a full associate degree. We can help you transfer your TU credits back to TCC to earn your associate’s degree. It’s never too early to start planning your transfer to TU!

Brown prepares its students to forge their own paths. We invite parents and families to journey alongside them. Parents and Family Information Parents and families are an important part of the Brown community. We invite you to get involved and learn about the unique academic community that is Brown. Medical School Parents and Family Information As members of the Brown Medical Community, parents and families have direct access to the Medical School’s parents and alumni relations team who can help answer your questions and connect you to resources. Parent & Family Giving As part of our vibrant and proud extended family, you sustain and strengthen Brown's distinctive brand of academic excellence and enhance educational opportunity for all students through your generosity.

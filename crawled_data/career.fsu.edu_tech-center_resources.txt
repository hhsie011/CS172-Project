Training Resources Presentations Technical Reports Service Delivery Handouts Resources To request special permission for the adaptation of FSU Career Center resources, print or digital materials, please complete this Permission Request Form. Upon submission of a completed form, your request will be reviewed and we will contact you within 5 business days with instructions and information regarding a decision and appropriate documentation or acknowledgements to add to any resource that is in use or being modified. The resources below are open access and available for you to review and utilize. CIP Introduction Includes an introductory document explaining essential elements of cognitive information processing (CIP) theory, research, and practice. Bibliographies and Research Includes a CIP Bibliography, CTI Research, CIP Evidence-Based Practice Research, and ICT Bibliography which provide a compiled list of research related to career theory, research, practice, and the use of information and communication technology in career interventions. Course Resources These resources are used for career development and career counseling related courses at the graduate and undergraduate level. Presentations These presentation materials are from various conferences and discuss topics related career theory, research, and practice. Service Delivery Handouts and Resources These handouts and resources are used in interventions with individuals receiving self-help, brief staff-assisted, and individual case-managed services. Technical Reports These technical reports review different topics related to the use of career theory, research and practice. Training Resources These training resources are used for practitioners delivering career resources and services.

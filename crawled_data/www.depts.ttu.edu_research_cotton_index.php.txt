The Excellence in Cotton & Natural Fibers Research Network brings together stakeholders from Texas Tech University, its academic and research partners, government, and industry to secure funding for rapid advances of technologies for improving the production, commercialization, and use of cotton and natural fibers in the State of Texas and beyond.

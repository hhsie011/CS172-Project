East Austin Stories: East Austin Stories Contact East Austin Stories Andrew Garrison 512-475-6297 Join our mailing list East Austin Stories: East Austin Stories Since 2000, as the forces of gentrification and development make profound changes in neighborhoods of East Austin, University of Texas RTF students have made short documentary films about the communities, families, individuals, institutions, and businesses east of I-35. East Austin Stories is the name of the ongoing class in documentary filmmaking; the annual, free, public screening of new films in neighborhood venues; and this collection of all of the short films.

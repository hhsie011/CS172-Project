OFFICE OF RESEARCH PROTECTIONS The Office of Research Protections partners with researchers to ensure that all Villanova scientific research involving human participants, vertebrate animals and biohazards is safe, ethically sound and compliant with all applicable federal, state and local regulations and University policies. It also offers guidance to researchers and oversees the review process related to financial conflicts of interest in research. Read More OFFICE OF GRANTS AND CONTRACTS The Office of Grants and Contracts (OG&C) works with investigators in all aspects of research administration of sponsored research projects by: supporting the development and submission of complete and competitive proposals, negotiating and executing various types of research agreements, providing education for the Villanova community, and ensuring proper stewardship of sponsored research funds awarded to the University.

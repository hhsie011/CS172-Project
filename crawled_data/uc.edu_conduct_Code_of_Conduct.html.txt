The Student Code of Conduct was approved by the Board of Trustees on August 25, 2020 and is published by the Division of Student Affairs Reference University Rule 3361:40-5-05, located in the Langsam Library, Board of Trustees Office, Office of the Vice President for Student Affairs, and college offices.

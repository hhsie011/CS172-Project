He received a B.S. degree in geology from the University of California, Davis, in 2019. Huy's research concentrates on applying electromagnetic (EM) geophysical methods for imaging subsurface structures in different geological settings. His current work is imaging offshore groundwater systems in the U.S. Atlantic margin and Bangladesh. Fields of Interest Geophysics, Structural Geology, Tectonics, Geodynamics Education Bachelor of Sciences in Geology, University of California, Davis, 2019 Honors & Awards Citation for Outstanding Performance 2019 from Earth & Planetary Sciences Department, UC Davis.

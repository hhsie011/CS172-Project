Other useful databases for scholarly articles in the: Humanities and Social Sciences, try Ebscohost Research Databases or ProQuest Sciences, try Web of Science or Scopus 18th and 19th centuries, try American Periodicals or British Periodicals To find articles in a specific newspaper or published in a specific city: For US newspapers from the 1990s on: ProQuest For US newspapers before 1990s: ProQuest Historial Newspapers, America's Historical Newspapers, and Access Newspaper Archive For international newspapers: Factiva Use Databases to browse all of our databases by subject.

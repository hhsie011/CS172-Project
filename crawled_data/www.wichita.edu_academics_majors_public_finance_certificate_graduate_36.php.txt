The certificate can also be completed as part of WSU's Master of Public Administration program and is offered in a fully online format. Learn how Public Finance is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum To complete the 12-credit-hour graduate certificate, you’ll take the following courses: Public Sector Economics Public and Nonprofit Financial Management State and Local Government Budgeting Fundraising and Financial Management in Nonprofit Organizations View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

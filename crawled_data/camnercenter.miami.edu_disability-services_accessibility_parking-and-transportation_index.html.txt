Accessibility Committee Parking & Transportation Library Assistance Wheelchair Stairlift Accessible parking spaces are located throughout each campus of the University of Miami. Both a University of Miami parking permit (including daily passes or other temporary permits) and a state disabled parking permit are required to park in a disabled space on campus. The University of Miami permit should be displayed in front of the disabled permit. All vehicles of the HurryCanes campus shuttle service are wheelchair accessible and are available free of charge for all University of Miami students, faculty, staff and visitors. Difficulties with HurryCanes shuttles may be reported to Parking and Transportation Services at (305) 284-3096.

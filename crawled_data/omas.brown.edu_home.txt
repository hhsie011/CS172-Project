Office of Military-Affiliated Students Brown is committed to building a community that actively supports veterans who are beginning, returning to, or advancing their pursuit of higher education. The Office of Military-Affiliated Students (OMAS) assists student veterans, ROTC cadets, and other military-affiliated students in making successful transitions into and out of the Brown community. Considering Applying to Brown? Although the transition from the military to college life can be daunting and challenging, Brown is committed to building a community that actively supports veterans who are beginning, returning to, or advancing their pursuit of higher education. Prospective students should contact our Office for more information.

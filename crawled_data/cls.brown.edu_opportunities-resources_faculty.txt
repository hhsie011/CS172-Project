Faculty find support, encouragement, expertise and camaraderie through programming and professional development offered through CLS. Come visit us on the sixth floor of the Sciences Library. Professional Development Reading Groups Join colleagues for a round table discussion of current trends in language pedagogy. Visit Page Open details for Reading Groups CLS Pedagogy and Technology Workshops CLS offers regular lectures, workshops and reading groups for faculty and graduate student professional development. By promoting research on language pedagogy and offering programming relating to the teaching and learning of languages, CLS helps to facilitate communication among language faculty at Brown and to encourage students to pursue the innovative language-learning opportunities Brown makes available. Visit Page Open details for CLS Pedagogy and Technology Workshops Consortium for Language Teaching and Learning As an institutional member of the Consortium for Language Teaching and Learning (CLTL), Brown's faculty meet regularly with other language faculty from the University of Chicago, Columbia University, Cornell University, Harvard University, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Princeton University and Yale University.

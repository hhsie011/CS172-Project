Vice Provost of Advising & Academic Services The Office of Advising & Academic Services team provides critical support to other university offices and students by centrally designing and delivering student-centered technology, quality academic services, university-level coordination, training and professional development for advisors, and leadership support. Our team is dedicated to promoting high quality advising for UC students with the goal that every one of them will graduate and achieve their goals. I am proud that our team is comprised of committed people with deep content expertise, fresh perspectives and creative problem solving. You are welcome to contact anyone on our team with inquiries.

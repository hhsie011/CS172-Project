CareerLab Website What Our Students are Doing After graduation, Public Policy students have found themselves creating change worldwide in the public, private, and nonprofit sectors. Since Public Policy exposes students to skills that serve them in many fields, alumni are employed in government, medical, legal, educational, and finance fields. Alumni hold positions such as Massachusetts state representative, director at US Department of Health and Human Services, and CEO of Evidon, Inc.

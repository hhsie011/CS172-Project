Perez, an ardent advocate for increasing the number of women in STEM disciplines, is a fossil preparator at the Smithsonian. In 2019, she was one of 125 women innovators across the country who were selected to be an AAAS IF/THEN ambassador. Perez was part of the SMU team that developed and installed the Sea Monsters Unearthed: Life in Angola’s Ancient Seas exhibit currently on display at the Smithsonian.

Alumni Events Dunlap is part of a local team that’s pioneering a solution for both the municipal and aerospace spheres — by turning trash into jet fuel. “ “With so many graduates who are friends, family, and co-workers, Clemson has always been and will continue to be a big part of my life, personally and professionally. ” Billy Dunlap '90 Fulcrum Bioenergy Alumni Awards The College of Engineering, Computing and Applied Sciences recognize Alumni who have made major contributions to their professions or have brought significant distinction to the world around them. Awards & Recognition Shaping the Future Expanding Industry Engineers and scientists have always risen to the challenges of their time, from putting a man on the moon to providing clean water here on Earth. As technology advances and the world becomes more interconnected, society’s grand challenges grow increasingly complex. It’s our job in the College of Engineering, Computing, and Applied Sciences to ensure that students are ready to meet those demands. Alumni contributions provide support for student engagement, faculty advancement, and academic opportunities that shape tomorrow’s leaders. These contributions are used to attract, retain, and award top talent, provide exemplary engagement experiences and support our students in need. IDEAS Magazine Lasting Impacts Alumni play a critical role in supporting the college’s drive to become one of the nation’s top-20 public colleges in engineering. Helping CECAS students receive a first-class educational experience that includes Creative Inquiry, collaborative learning, study abroad, and career options; Enabling Clemson faculty, staff, and students to address real-world problems facing communities, families, and industry; Increasing the value of a Clemson degree for all alumni; and Fostering creativity, critical thinking, and economic development. Ways to Give Get Involved We firmly understand that any college is only as good as its people. Identifying top talent and scholars and presenting them with impactful opportunities promotes academic success, cutting-edge research, and a direct impact on the future.

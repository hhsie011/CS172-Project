for: Dean’s Advisory Committee Dean’s Advisory Committeebrackman2021-07-27T12:55:21-04:00 The School of Communication Dean’s Advisory Committee consists of a group of communication professionals, alumni, and UM trustees who meet regularly to review and advise the School’s leadership regarding its strategic goals and program accomplishments. In particular, the committee is dedicated to: Providing the Dean with ongoing input and feedback on strategic plans. Helping the Dean and the School obtain the necessary resources for the success of its strategic plan. Providing the Board of Trustees and the Academic Affairs Committee with regular updates on the progress of the School.

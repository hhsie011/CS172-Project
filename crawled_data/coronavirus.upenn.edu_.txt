The following public health measures remain our primary tools to minimize virus transmission: getting vaccinated and boosted masking while in healthcare spaces and on Penn Transit (see the latest guidance) hand washing staying home when feeling sick completing daily PennOpen Pass symptom checker and exposure reporting PennOpen Pass PennOpen Pass is a daily symptom tracker and exposure reporting system designed to reduce the risk of COVID-19 spreading within the Penn community. For information on how it works, answers to frequently asked questions, the privacy policy, and other resources, visit the PennOpen Pass website.

For more information regarding the university’s response to COVID-19 and general updates, please visit: https://protect.utexas.edu Why come to the Staff Ombuds? Our office was founded by the Office of the President to provide you with a free and confidential resource for dealing with work related issues. We operate off-the-record and have assisted more than 600 new and long-term UT employees, including supervisors and postdocs.

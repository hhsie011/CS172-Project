Delphain Demosthenes Counselor Contact information Office Location Office Hours or Schedule a Meeting [email protected] 401.863.2344 (OCRL Office) Office Hours held in Page-Robinson Hall, Room 414 Wednesdays, 1 - 6 pm, or reach out to connect via email. Reverend Delphain Demosthenes is a licensed certified social worker and ordained minister in the American Baptist Churches of USA. He received a BA in Biblical Studies from Nyack College, a Master of Divinity from The Divinity School at Yale University, and a Master of Social Work from New York University. Reverend Demosthenes has counseled a wide range of clients including children, adolescents, adults, couples and families and is currently senior pastor at Memorial Baptist Church in Seekonk, Massachusetts.

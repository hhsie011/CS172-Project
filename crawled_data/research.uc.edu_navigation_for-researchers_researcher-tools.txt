Visit Dimensions The Office of Research has invested in an online tool that will help keep track of information on publications, research funding, patents, and clinical trials for the University of Cincinnati research community. Visit SPIN The University of Cincinnati offers SPIN to provide a platform to search for Funding Opportunities. It features customizable queries for funding opportunities that match exactly what you are eligible to receive. To access this tool go to spin.infoedglobal.com while on a UC network. In order to create custom searches you must create an account using the instructions found here.

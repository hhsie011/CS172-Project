Apply for your UM parking permit and CLICK HERE to learn about the importance of parking in your assigned color zone. All vehicles parked on campus are required to have a valid UM parking permit and must park head-in only. Parking permits are issued with an assignment to a specific color zone. Park in your assigned color zone where the lots and/or garage entrance sign includes the color matching your parking permit. In order to avoid fines, towing, and possible disciplinary action, all vehicles parked on the Coral Gables campus should adhere to these policies. Familiarity with, and attention to, the University of Miami's Parking Policies and Procedures is the most effective way of avoiding parking citations. Campus parking is strictly enforced Monday through Sunday 8a.m. - 11p.m. EV Charging Stations are available throughout campus. Be sure to familiarize yourself with the EV Charging Station parking policy. For detailed information on the rules and regulations pertaining to parking on campus, refer to our Parking Policies and Procedures (rev. June 2021). The cost of the 2021-22 permits is listed below: Regular Color Zone (Red, Brown, White, Yellow, Gray, Burgundy, & Pink) $553 Discount Permit (Mahoney Pearson Garage - Upper two floors) $276 Fall Only Commuter Permit $276 Resident Student (1st Year freshmen are ineligible for a permit) $596 Fall Only Resident Permit $301 University Village Resident $276 Nights and Weekend Permit (Valid after 4pm weekdays and any time at the weekend) $270 All Students may apply for a parking permit via the following link Apply for UM Parking Permit during our annual sign-up process. An e-mail will be sent to all with this link and the dates on which each group will be eligible to apply. Once in the university's parking system, we will advise you which color zones are available for purchase. IMPORTANT: Students with an unpaid parking citation balance may not purchase a parking permit until such balance is paid. MasterCard and/or Visa payments are accepted at the Parking & Transportation office by calling 305-284-3096, option 2. Cash or check payment for fine(s) are accepted at the University Cashiers in the Ashe Building. Parking During Winter and Summer Breaks (Resident Students Only) at Mahoney-Pearson Garage Vehicles may be stored during these breaks with no in/out access. Winter break storage is available the week of Fall Commencement through the opening day of student housing in January. Summer break storage is available the week of Spring commencement through the opening day of student housing in August. Commuter students are not permitted to store their vehicles on campus. For further information, please contact Parking & Transportation at 305-284-3096, option 2, or e-mail.

& Seminars Association Meetings Summits JSG Graduation Menu Home Education Undergraduate TNT 2021 TNT 2021 The Jackson School of Geosciences is proud to announce The NeoGeo Trip 2021 on August 23-24th Registration Deadline: August 6, 2021 Congratulations and welcome to The University of Texas at Austin, Jackson School of Geosciences! We are excited to kick off the 2021-22 academic year with the return of our freshmen field trip, The NeoGeo Trip (TNT2021). This invitation-only event is for new Jackson School geological sciences majors admitted fall 2021 and is scheduled the two days before classes start, August 23-24th. TNT2021 consists of a 2-day tour of the Texas hill country where attendees will enjoy seeing Texas through a geologist’s eyes as well as hiking, playing games and swimming. Attendees will spend the night at the Thunderbird Lodge on Lake Buchanan before returning to Austin for UT’s Gone to Texas celebration. This trip is a great way to meet your future classmates as well as members of the geosciences community including JSG faculty, staff and upper-classmen. An online registration form will be emailed to all eligible participants in early July. The checklist below can help students gather the information and documents required to complete their online registration and officially reserve a seat for TNT2021. Seats are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. Submit your registration paperwork before the August 6th deadline to reserve your seat. Hook ’em!

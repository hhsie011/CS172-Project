Hill College serves more than 4,000 students annually between its Hill County Campus, Johnson County Campus and Burleson Center. Hill College is also a partner to business, government and industry, providing customized training and work force development. By partnering with Texas Tech University, Hill College offers you the opportunity to join this active community in Cleburne while you take your upper-level coursework.

FAQ Racquetball REC Kids Camp Parent/Guardian Guide Team Building Pickleball Racquetball The CRC has 3 courts available: 2 racquetball courts and 1 squash court! Come in and try them all. Reserve your court today! Call the Member Services Desk an hour prior to the time needed at 513-556-0604. Book via our online member reservation system. Reservations can be made no more than 24 hours in advance The maximum reservation time per party is one hour. One person cannot make more than one reservation per day. Consecutive time slot reservations are not accepted. Play may continue if a court is available. Court reservations begin and end on the hour regardless of when play actually starts.

The services are available to SMU faculty, graduate students, and undergraduate students who are performing original research. Research Data Manage your data from idea to long-term storage. GIS@SMU Discover new insights using maps and spatial thinking to understand the world and solve problems. SMU Scholar Showcase your research in SMU’s digital repository and increase the discoverability and impact of your work. Digital Scholarship Explore digital tools and computational methods for your research. Scholarly Impact Measure and extend the impact of your scholarly work.

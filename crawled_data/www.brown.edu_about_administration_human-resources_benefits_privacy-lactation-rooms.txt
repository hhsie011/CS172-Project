Brown is committed to providing employees and their supervisors with the tools that they need to support breastfeeding parents while at work. Lactation (Privacy) Room Locations The following campus-based (red) or hospital (green) locations are accessible to Brown faculty, staff, students or visitors during Brown University business hours or as otherwise indicated. Nursing mothers must provide their own pump, adapter, and accessories as well as a small cooler or insulated bag. To determine the distance of the nearest Lactation (Privacy) room: click the full-page view, click on the drop down menu or icon on the map, then click on directions and type in your location.

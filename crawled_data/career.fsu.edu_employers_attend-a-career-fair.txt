The Career Center has multiple opportunities for you to meet with targeted groups of students and fill your hiring needs. Career Fairs and Events Part-Time Job Fair august 31 and september 1, 2022 Looking for part-time or seasonal help? Recruit FSU students actively seeking part-time jobs! The Part-Time Job Fair is typically held in the fall and spring semester. STEM CAREER & INTERNSHIP FAIR september 20, 2022 Held during the fall and spring, this event connects you with students seeking full-time and internship opportunities in engineering, computer science, and other technology-related industries. diversity & inclusion networking event (D.I.N.E.) september 21, 2022 The Diversity and Inclusion Networking Event (D.I.N.E.) gives students the opportunity to share their story, highlight their skills, and kickstart a successful future by networking with employer representatives from a variety of fields and backgrounds. Seminole futures all-majors career & internship fair september 22, 2022 Our largest career fair, Seminole Futures brings students from a variety of industries and backgrounds seeking full-time and internship opportunities. This event is held in the fall and spring. Virtual Career Fair October 5, 2022 Open to all FSU students, this career fair brings students from a variety of industries and backgrounds seeking full-time and internship opportunities. Graduate and Law School Fair october 19, 2022 Represent your institution at our Graduate & Law School Fair, and speak with students about the application process, requirements, and why your program is the right fit for them.. Health Professions Career Fair TBD Spring 2023 This event gives medical centers, hospitals, and health care facilities the opportunity to discuss career and internship opportunities with students and alumni majoring in nursing and other health-related areas. government & social services fair tbd spring 2023 Government agencies and non-profit organizations are invited to connect with students interested in these fields. Education and Library Career Fair tbd spring 2023 School administrators, superintendents, and other employers from the education field can meet with candidates looking for full-time and internship positions. Statewide Job Fair Recruit over 1,000 students and alumni from each of Florida's state universities at one event. This career fair takes place each spring. SEC & ACC Virtual Career Fair tbd spring 2023 Reach students from Florida State University and 27 other institutions in the SEC and the ACC in this virtual fair to fill your hiring needs. Additional Events ALL MAJORS RESUMÉ CAFÉ September 15 & 19, 2022 Connect with students from all majors and provide them with resume and career advice! Free event! STEM RESUMÉ CAFÉ (spring 2022 in-person) September 13, 2022 Featuring students from the STEM majors, employers will connect and provide them with resume and career advice! Free event! Questions? Not sure which fair to attend to meet your recruitment goals? Contact our Employer Relations team to discuss which fair may best met your needs.

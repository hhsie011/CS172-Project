and Equity Clemson University Commissions Current: Commission on the Black Experience The Purpose of the Commission The purpose of the Commission is to advise the Division of Inclusion and Equity and the University about the Black experience at Clemson University and advocate for specific actions to enhance its quality and the environment. During the fall and spring semesters, meetings are held monthly. For information, contact Karon Donald, Council on Diversity and Inclusion, Program Manager at (864) 656-3496 or karond@clemson.edu.

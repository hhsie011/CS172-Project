On-Campus Housing Off-Campus Housing EOP Academic Counseling The Instructional Center The Kelly Ethnic Cultural Center UW Counseling Center In addition to these resources, we encourage undocumented students to contact and/or visit the Leadership Without Borders (LWB) Center. This center is fully dedicated to supporting undocumented students and connecting them to campus and community resources. At LWB, you can learn about other resources, such as study abroad programs, how to connect with allies on campus, scholarships, career planning, leadership opportunities and more.

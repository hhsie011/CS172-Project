Yale Public Safety has a variety of uniformed and civilian career opportunities. Whether you’re considering a career as a police officer, security guard, or within public safety systems and services, a range of rewarding and fulfilling career opportunities are available. A career with Public Safety offers many rewards: competitive salaries and excellent benefits; opportunities to make a significant impact on people in the community; an amazing variety of work challenges; and pride in being part of a nationally recognized campus public safety department. We are searching for talented individuals of unquestionable integrity who can demonstrate sound judgment and decision-making skills under all conditions.

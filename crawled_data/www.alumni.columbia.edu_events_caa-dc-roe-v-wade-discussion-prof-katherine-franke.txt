Wade Discussion with Prof. Katherine Franke Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo May 11, 2022 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM America/New_York Online A near 50-year precedence of a US law protecting women's fundamental right to choice is seemingly about to expire, thanks to the designed conservative transformation of the Supreme Court. Please join us as Professor Franke, James L. Dohr Professor of Law at Columbia Law School reviews the history of Roe V. Wade and discusses how it has affected American welfare throughout the decades, how do the leaked arguments posed by the SCOTUS majority stand to legal scrutiny and Constitution, and finally what ramification should be expected on its possible repeal. Franke's remarks will be followed by Q&A.

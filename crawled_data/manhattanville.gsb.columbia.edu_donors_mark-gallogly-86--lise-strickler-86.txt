Elizabeth B. Strickler ’86 & Mark T. Gallogly ’86 Student Lounge and Screening Room Henry R. Kravis Hall The Elizabeth B. Strickler '86 & Mark T. Gallogly '86 Student Lounge and Screening Room, will be a lively hub of student life – a place where students can enjoy some down time, socialize with classmates, work on a pitch for a business plan competition, and brainstorm ideas for club activities. With views of the central plaza, the airy space will enhance Columbia Business School’s spirit of community.

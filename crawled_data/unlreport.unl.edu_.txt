You have accessed the University of Nebraska-Lincoln’s incident reporting, or UNL Report. UNL Report expands the methods by which any member of the university community can share matters they feel need to be elevated or addressed. While anonymous reporting is an option, please be aware that should you wish to remain anonymous we cannot provide you a follow-up. Therefore, some form of contact information is beneficial. If this is an emergency or incident in progress now, STOP and immediately call the Police at 911 or University Police at 402-472-2222 (2-2222 on campus).

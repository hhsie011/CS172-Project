With support from annual giving, Brown students learn public health by doing public health and communities near and far are able to thrive. Support public health today. With your help, we can tackle the pressing problems of today and train the public health leaders of tomorrow. MAKE A GIFT The Public Health Annual Fund supports: Advancing the School's work in high-impact public health arenas, including opioid addiction, climate change and health, and mindfulness. Financial assistance (including fellowship aid, research stipends, emergency gap funds, and travel expenses) to allow our students to commit themselves fully to their education and experiential learning. Bringing accomplished researchers and educators to Brown to further the School's research efforts and teach the next generation of public health professionals. Pilot research grants that help position the School to compete for large government grants. Improve human health for all. Be part of the School of Public Health's mission to enhance population health and well-being on a large scale.

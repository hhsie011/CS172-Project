The following is information for College of Education students completing their Master's, Ed. D., Multiple Subject, Single Subject, Special Education, or Preliminary Administrative Services credential in the Fall 2021, Spring 2022 or Summer 2022. More information can be found on the CSUF Commencement website. Date: Monday, May 23 Ceremony Time: 5:00 PM Location: Titan Stadium Line up time: Students must be lined up by 4:00pm Line up location: Students must line up in Parking Lot A South . Ceremony Speaker and vocalist We want to thank all of the students who took the time and shared their talents by auditioning for Student Speaker and/or Student Vocalist. Student Speaker: Brandy Garcia Student Vocalist(s): AJ Stevens Distinguished Faculty Marshal: Dr. Natalie Tran, Ph.D. COMMENCEMENT registration (for students) IS REQUIRED Registration is now closed. Please send an email to COEcommencement@fullerton.edu if you did not register by the deadline but would still like to attend. COMMENCEMENT TICKETS (for guests) Each student gets 8 guest tickets. You can access your guest tickets once you have registered for commencement. For more information about tickets please visit the CSUF Commencement website. COMMENCEMENT RECEPTIONS Your academic department will communicate with you if they plan to host a reception. COMMENCEMENT regalia requirements Caps and gowns are mandatory for the commencement ceremony and are available for purchase at the Titan Bookstore. Master's and Ed.D. candidates will be hooded during the ceremony. Comfortable shoes are highly recommended as there will be considerable walking and the graduate's chairs will be on a lawn. Regalia Attire Credential: CSUF's *NEW* bachelor's gown and mortarboard Master's: CSUF's Master's gown and mortarboard Ed.D.: CSUF's Doctoral gown (with velvet) and tam Tassel Color Credential: none Education: light blue Education Technology: light blue Instructional Design and Technology: purple Tassel Position Credential: none Master's or Ed. D.: left side of mortarboard PARKING Parking permits are not needed during the week of Commencement. Your ceremony location will determine the best parking lot or structure for you to park in, see campus map. ACCOMMODATIONS FOR PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES Disability Access Requests can be submitted until Friday, May 6th via the Commencement Accessibility page.

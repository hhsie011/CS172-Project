This committee is comprised of well-known research and physician scientists distinguished in all fields of science. Each year, this committee selects the lecturers for the Dean’s Distinguished Lecture Series in the Basic Sciences, Clinical Sciences, Humanities, Cartwright, and Samuel Rudin Distinguished Visiting Professorship. In addition, they choose the VP&S Faculty Commencement Awardees. Dean's Distinguished Lecture Series The Dean’s Distinguished Lecture Series at the College of Physicians and Surgeons is intended to focus on the collaborative nature of our scientific endeavors and to celebrate scholarly exchange among all Columbia University. Read more about the Dean's Distinguished Lecture Series. Faculty Commencement Awards The Faculty Commencement Awards seek to honor those who have served the College with the greatest distinction.

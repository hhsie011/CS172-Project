Contact Us Meeting Minutes and Agendas Meet the Senators Contact Us University Staff Senate The University Staff Senate works to foster communication and collegiality among Staff of the University, and to: provide representation for Staff and liaison to the University administration and others regarding issues of concern; initiate and review policies and procedures relating to Staff; be a cultural driver for Staff that reflects the values of Wichita State University’s Strategic Plan. Learn more Staff Senate Merger In December of 2020, the Unclassified Professional Staff and University Support Staff voted in support of merging the UP and USS Senates and adopting the constitution of the University Staff Senate. Beginning July 1, 2021, the two Senates will officially merge to become the University Staff Senate. For more information about the merger and to view the new constitution, visit wichita.edu/senatemerger.

To build an understanding of healthcare access among OAIs from multiple perspectives we will collect data from four data sources: 1) interviews with first-generation Chinese and Vietnamese older adults; 2) interviews with support persons for first-generation Chinese and Vietnamese older adults; 3) focus groups with health care providers working with the Asian American community, and 4) focus groups with health care staff working with the Asian American community. Additional information is below. Focus Group Details Approximately 1 hour Hybrid (occurring virtually through MS Teams and in-person) Medical providers (M.D.s (including residents), NPs, PAs): 5/24/22 at 1pm Clinical staff (medical assistants, intake specialists): 5/31/22 at 1pm Topics to be discussed include: Obstacles to healthcare access for OAIs, Observed supports or assistance, and Perspectives and recommendations regarding a potential Peer Health Wellness Storefront · All participants receive a $25 giftcard RSVP to receive the link to attend the MS Teams meeting!

Any Clemson faculty member, staff or student is invited to attend and/or participate during the "Open Commentary" section at the start of the monthly meetings. Join us for May's meeting on Thursday, May 12, at 2:30 p.m. at the University Union Senate Chambers (Zoom link available on the Staff Senate website). Please register if you'd like to speak during the open commentary period. Staff Senate exists to support and represent Clemson University staff employees by giving voice to and advocating for a safe and equitable workplace, work-life balance, and professional development by promoting resources and recognition staff members need and deserve to flourish in their positions and at the University.

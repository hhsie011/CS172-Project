Reservations are accepted on a first come, first serve basis, and any requests that do not contain accurate information will lose priority. In order to avoid this, please include the exact information (see Requesting Rooms instructions below) and double check the room’s availability (see Checking Availability instructions below). All emails should go to rooms@arch.columbia.edu. For additional chairs, tables, or blackboards AND to report any problems with a room (blown out lightbulbs, missing equipment, missing chairs, etc) please contact Mark Taylor. REQUESTING ROOMS Please email rooms@arch.columbia.edu IN THE BODY OF THE MESSAGE INDICATE: Date: Room: Time: 2nd Choice: 3rd Choice: Number of students: Name of Professor: Name of TA: Telephone no.: YOU WILL RECEIVE A CONFIRMATION EMAIL WITH: Date: Room: Time: ATTENTION: Please be specific about your reservation’s start and end times and please leave the room in the same shape that you found it. CHECK AVAILABILITY PLEASE FOLLOW THIS SEQUENCE TO FIND ROOMS: Go to: https://ems.cuit.columbia.edu/EmsWebApp/ Browse by Locations (left hand side of screen) Select Add/Remove Locations Select Views Check GSAPP and update locations Search by date You will now see a list of all GSAPP rooms and their availability. Please note that 301 Fayerweather may only be reserved by the Historic Preservation program. TENTED OUTDOOR SPACE Select tented outdoor spaces may be reserved by Columbia schools and departments during the day for a fee. These spaces will also be available to the Columbia community outside of reservable hours. Event applications can be submitted online through the EMS Web App. To submit an event request: Log in to the EMS Web App using your UNI and password Select ‘Tented Outdoor Space’ Template Complete all fields, including second and third preferences for alternate dates and spaces Please visit the Event Management website or email Event Management for more information on reserving an outdoor space.

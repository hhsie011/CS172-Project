Knowing how to fund your education is essential. This section will help you understand Harvard Divinity School's costs, financial aid resources, and other funding opportunities so you can make an informed decision. 90% of MTS and MDiv students receive institutional scholarships. The HDS Office of Financial Aid is operating on a hybrid schedule with both in-person and remote work. For the quickest response to questions or concerns, or if you would like to set up an appointment, please e-mail us at financial_aid@hds.harvard.edu. We make every effort to respond to messages within the business day if possible.

Haidian District, Beijing Time: 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM (Beijing) üìç Location: Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Hello, are you excited about coming to New York City? Congratulations on your acceptance to Teachers College at Columbia University, and welcome to our community! Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Teachers College China Society (TCCS) invite all newly admitted TC students to a welcome reception at the Beijing Center. At this reception, you will get to know your peers from icebreaker games. You will learn more about your academic experience at TC and life in New York City from our guest speakers. You will also have the opportunity to grab a drink and chat with current TC students, who are willing to answer all of your questions. We look forward to seeing you in Beijing soon!

Regulatory Assistance The Clinical Trials Office provides central regulatory support for University researchers through its various resources: The IND/IDE Assistance Program for University Faculty members who hold an Investigational New Drug (IND) or Investigational Device Exemption (IDE) Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC) Training Program for clinical research staff Clinical Trials Monitoring Assistance Program (CTMAP) for University Faculty members who serve as Sponsor-Investigators (S-Is) holding an IND or IDE ClinicalTrials.gov assistance for researchers throughout the Columbia University community Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, all monitoring activities must be conducted remotely.

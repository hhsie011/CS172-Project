Earth Day Generations Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo April 22, 2022 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM America/New_York Online Sustain What? Earth Day Generations Join host Andy Revkin in a Columbia Climate School solutions jam with an Earth Day founder, a rights-focused folk singer and emerging young sustainability leaders. The challenges facing humanity right now require crosstalk between generations and societal sectors. Here we build a solutions conversation with lessons learned from Earth Days past to foster progress every day on the sustainability and climate journey ahead.

Created with your future in mind, the Graduate Programs at Florida State University’s College of Business offer a wealth of innovative wisdom interwoven with best business practices. This distinct blend of knowledge and know-how prepares our graduates to thrive in whatever industry they choose. Designed to meet industry demand, the college’s strong slate of academic programs includes six master’s degrees delivered in flexible formats. You can complete your degree two classes at a time while working full time. Or, you can choose to jump-start your future earnings potential by completing your master’s degree at an accelerated pace. The college also provides a Ph.D. program with seven majors taught by a world-renowned research faculty and two combined pathways that allow top students to streamline their undergraduate and graduate studies. Choose an FSU degree infused with value and move your future forward today.

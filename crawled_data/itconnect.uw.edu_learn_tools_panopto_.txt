Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Panopto Lecture Capture UW-IT Service Catalog Lecture Capture Available for Available for : Instructors Students Staff Log in to UW Panopto ABOUT UW PANOPTO Panopto is the UW-IT supported lecture capture solution. Panopto allows for easy recording and reviewing of videos from courses, lectures, and presentations. As a cloud-enabled service, Panopto is designed to simplify the lecture capture process. Recordings in Panopto are viewable on most Web browsers and mobile devices. No service fee for eligible users. Review policies (FERPA Considerations) for using Panopto at the UW. Panopto helps you to: Record lectures and presentations Share recordings Share recordings in Canvas Schedule recordings in classrooms Upload recordings Add captions to recordings View recordings Usage information Service metrics: see adoption data Data retention policy 2019 UW Panopto Survey of Instructors and Students, 2016 UW Panopto Survey Panopto Support Online Help Center: Panopto Overview Log into Panopto Add a Panopto recording to Canvas Schedule a recording Help Desk: Email and phone Support staff are available during Learning Technology’s Help Desk Hours. Training: Free workshops: view schedule & register. UW Learning Technologies multimedia consultants are available to help faculty and staff effectively use video as a tool for teaching and learning. To set up a consulting session, email help@uw.edu with ‘Learning Technologies Consult Appointment Request’ in the subject line and a brief description of your goals. Upgrades, Known Issues, System Status, and Updates: 12/30/21 Issue with the Zoom integration: If you set up your recordings to automatically import into Panopto before December 30th, the recordings will not be moved into Panopto. Zoom is working on fixing this issue. Panopto will require all creators to upgrade recorders by the third Thursday of each July. At the minimum, you will need to be on the version that was current as of March 31st. Issues with Panopto quizzes Panopto now supports newer Macs with the M1 chips. Creators may need to download the latest recorder. Note: contact help@uw.edu if you need to capture computer audio. Course copy issue Third-party cookies need to be enabled.

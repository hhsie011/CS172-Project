Section Health & Wellness Overview Diversity Housing & Dining Health & Wellness Student Organizations Sports & Recreation Arts & Culture In the Jesuit tradition of cura personalis—care for the whole person—our comprehensive health, wellness, and safety services can help you with your physical and emotional needs. University Counseling Services University Counseling Services addresses the mental health needs of the Boston College community with counseling, consultation and psychiatric resources. The staff is experienced in assisting with a broad range of issues, including depression, anxiety and relationship concerns. About University Counseling Services University Health Services UHS is open 24/7 for health emergencies, and provides health screenings nutritional services, orthopedic appointments, routine medical exams and more. Our Primary Care Center includes both inpatient and outpatient units. More About University Health Services The Office of Health Promotion The Office of Health Promotion offers individual and group programs to help you develop strategies to maintain health and well being. Health educators, health coaches, and student volunteers work collaboratively to advance healthy living on campus. Services include health and wellness coaching, anonymous free screenings and assessments.

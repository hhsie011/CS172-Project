For additional information, please see the Procedure for Penn Accessible Transit (Handi-Van and Parking Accommodation). Requesting PAT Services for Disabilities The Office of Affirmative Action and Equal Opportunity Programs authorizes the use of PAT by faculty, staff and visitors to the campus. Faculty and Staff must complete the Penn Accessible Transit Request Form (PDF) and may be required to provide medical documentation to support the request Penn Accessible Transit Medical Authorization Form (PDF). Requests may be e-mailed (oaaeop@pobox.upenn.edu) or faxed (215) 746-7088 to the office. If request for the Penn Accessible Transit is granted, please follow these guidelines (PDF).

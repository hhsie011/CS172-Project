University & Student Business Services (USBS) is open in Wilder Tower. We encourage students to make use of the various online options first, reserving face-to-face appointments for those that do not have electronic access or have issues that cannot be resolved online. E-mailing us to set up appointments, coupled with UofM staff rotation, ensures safe distancing practices. To request an appointment from USBS, email usbs@memphis.edu.

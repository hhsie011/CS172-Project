full-time students and employees. Tri-Rail may be an appropriate commute for full-time students, faculty, and staff living as far north as Palm Beach County at discounted fares. In an effort to create seamless, regional travel in South Florida, Tri-Rail partnered with Miami-Dade Transit (MDT) to use the EASY Card as the preferred way to pay your transit fare. Your EASY Card is good for use on the Tri-Rail and Miami-Dade Transit (MDT) including Metrorail, Metrobus, and Miami-Dade “Park and Ride” express buses. Full-time students receive 50% off the regular price. To obtain your Easy Card visit the University Center ticket window or visit Miami Dade Transit website. Faculty and Staff with full-time status receive discounted Miami-Dade Metro pass and Tri-Rail passes based on annual salary. Contact Parking & Transportation to learn how to enroll or visit UM Public Transportation Program. Faculty and Staff earn up to 250 Well 'Cane Points.

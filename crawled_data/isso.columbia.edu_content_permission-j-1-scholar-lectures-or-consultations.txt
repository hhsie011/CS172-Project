Consultations J-1 occasional lectures or short-term consultations outside Columbia refer to single engagements rather than ongoing activities. Such activities must be incidental to your primary program and may not delay its completion. How to Apply Gather your documents ✓ Document Invitation letter Description The letter must describe the terms and conditions of the proposed lecture or consultation including the duration number of hours field of study amount of compensation a description of the activity for which you are being hired ✓ Document Recommendation letter from your department Description The letter must: recommend the activity you have been offered explain how the activity will enhance your program objectives ✓ Document Most recent I-94 Arrival Record Description Click here to access your I-94. Email the documents to your scholar advisor prior to taking part in the activity. It depends on your campus. (ISSO Morningside or ISSO-CUIMC). Your advisor is listed in section 7 of your DS-2019. We will contact you Allow up to 3 business days for us to review and process your application. We will notify you by email when your letter is ready.

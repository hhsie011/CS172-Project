Brown Residency Program in Obstetrics and Gynecology A four-year program offering outstanding clinical experiences with exposure to the entire depth and breadth of obstetrics and gynecology. Welcome to the Residency Program in Obstetrics and Gynecology at the Warren Alpert Medical School of Brown University and Women & Infants Hospital of Rhode Island. We are fully accredited by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education. Our four-year program offers outstanding clinical experiences with exposure to the entire depth and breadth of Obstetrics and Gynecology, exceptional educational support, and multiple and varied research opportunities for our residents in a supportive, warm environment in the wonderful city of Providence. Our Mission We are a diverse community of compassionate, intellectually curious ob/gyns who care for and support one another. We are united by our commitment to service and providing equitable, evidence-based care. We prepare our graduates to become agents of change, active community partners, and leaders in reproductive and gynecologic medicine.

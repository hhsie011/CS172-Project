Lerner '83CC and Stephen Reis invite the Columbia University alumni, student, and ally communities in Southern California to Columbia Pride's first-ever LGBTQ+ Social at their home in Beverly Hills. (Exact address will be provided after registration.) Guests will have the opportunity to mix and mingle in indoor and outdoor spaces. Beverages and light hors d'oeuvres will be served. Free street parking will be available. Space is limited. Please RSVP by Thursday, May 5. Attendees must be 21+ and are allowed to register only themselves and one guest. Our hosts request that all attendees show proof of COVID-19 vaccination. We look forward to seeing you on Sunday, May 15, from 3 to 5 p.m. We are beyond thrilled to be able to meet you in person!

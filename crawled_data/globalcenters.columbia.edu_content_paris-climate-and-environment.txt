Climate and Environment With Paris as the author of the Climate Agreement, it is at the forefront of green urban planning by developing sustainable environmental policies, ensuring biodiversity in the city and its suburbs, and reducing pollution and urban heat. It follows that our Center strives to address the most pertinent questions, pressing challenges, global regulations, and leading innovations regarding climate and the environment.

Trombone Audition Requirements Tenor Trombone Undergraduate (BM, Minor, Scholarship) Applicant should be prepared to play any major scale two octaves Etude of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bordogni, Blazevich, Tyrell, Voxman) Solo of the applicant’s choice (examples: Guillmant: Morceau Symphonique, Galliard: Sonata, Barat: Andante et Allegro, Bernstein: Elegy for Mippy, Saint-Saens: Cavatine, Sulek: Sonata, or similar solo) 3 contrasting orchestral excerpts (examples: Mozart: Tuba Mirum, Berlioz: Hungarian March, Wagner: Ride of the Valkyries, Ravel: Bolero) Sight Reading Tenor Trombone Graduate (MM, AD) Applicant should be prepared to play any major, minor, or chromatic scale two octaves Etude of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bordogni, Blazevich, Tyrell, Bitsch, Bozza, Boutry) Two contrasting solos of the applicant’s choice (examples: Grondahl: Concerto, Persichetti: Parable, Hindemith: Sonata, Sulek: Sonata, David: Concertino or similar solo) Five contrasting orchestral excerpts of the applicant's choice (examples: Mozart: Tuba Mirum, Berlioz: Hungarian March, Wagner: Ride of the Valkyries, Ravel: Bolero, Mahler: Symphony 3, Hindemith: Symphonic Metamorphasis, Rossini: La Gazza Ladra, Schumann: Symphony 3, Wagner: Tannhauser) Sight Reading Tenor Trombone Graduate (DMA) Applicant should be prepared to play any major, minor, or chromatic scale two octaves Two etudes of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bordogni, Blazevich, Tyrell, Bitsch, Bozza, Boutry) Three solos of the applicant’s choice from the standard repertoire (examples: Casterede: Sonata, Rota: Concerto, Tomasi: Concerto, Persichetti: Parable, Albrechtsberger: Alto Trombone Concerto, Bozza: Ballade, or similar solo) Seven contrasting orchestral excerpts of the applicant's choice (examples: Mozart: Tuba Mirum, Berlioz: Hungarian March, Wagner: Ride of the Valkyries, Ravel: Bolero, Mahler: Symphony 3, Hindemith: Symphonic Metamorphasis, Rossini: La Gazza Ladra, Schumann: Symphony 3, Wagner: Tannhauser) Sight Reading Bass Trombone Undergraduate (BM, Minor, Scholarship) Applicant should be prepared to play any major scale two octaves Etude of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bordogni, Blazevich, Kopprasch) Solo of the applicant’s choice (examples: Koetsier: Allegro Maestoso, Lebedev: Concerto in One Movement Sachse: Konzert, Jacob: Cameos, or similar solo) 3 contrasting orchestral excerpts (examples: Berlioz: Hungarian March; Haydn: Creation; Schumann: Symphony No. 3; Wagner: Ride of the Valkyries) Sight Reading Bass Trombone Graduate (MM, AD) Applicant should be prepared to play any major, minor, or chromatic scale two octaves Etude of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bordogni, Blazevich, Tyrell, Gallay, Bitsch) Two contrasting solos of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bozza: New Orleans, Ewazen: Concerto, Spillman: Concerto, Vaughn Williams: Tuba Concerto, McCarty Sonata, or similar solo) Five contrasting orchestral excerpts of the applicant's choice (examples: Berlioz: Hungarian March; Haydn: Creation; Schumann: Symphony No. 3; Wagner: Das Rheingold; Kodaly: Hary Janos; Wagner: Ride of the Valkyries) Sight Reading Bass Trombone Graduate (DMA) Applicant should be prepared to play any major, minor, or chromatic scale two octaves Two etudes of the applicant’s choice (examples: Bordogni, Blazevich, Tyrell, Gallay, Bitsch) Three solos of the applicant’s choice from the standard repertoire (examples: Gillingham: Sonata, Brubeck: Bass Trombone Concerto, Nelhybel: Bass Trombone Concerto, Vaughn Williams: Tuba Concerto, Bach: Cello Suite, Wilder: Sonata for Bass Trombone, or similar solo) Seven contrasting orchestral excerpts of the applicant's choice (examples: Berlioz: Hungarian March; Haydn: Creation; Schumann: Symphony No. 3; Wagner: Das Rheingold; Kodaly: Hary Janos; Wagner: Ride of the Valkyries, Schumann: Symphony No. 3, Strauss: Ein Heldenleben, Rossini: William Tell Overture, Respighi: Fountains of Rome) Sight

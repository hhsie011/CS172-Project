Antigen test kits are available to individuals who are not experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 at any of Cornell’s test sites. Cornell urges students, faculty and staff to use supplemental testing or antigen test kits before and after travel. Symptomatic test kits are available for anyone experiencing mild to moderate symptoms suggestive of COVID-19. Testing requirements may change based on emerging public health data and guidance. Summer Testing Locations & Hours (beginning May 31) All testing sites are closed on Saturdays and Sundays during summer.

In it we share stories, resources, special updates and events to help the Longwood Campus community get the most out of their library. Keep an eye out for this newsletter on the first and third Thursday of each month! Subscribe If you are an HMS/HSDM affiliate (student, staff or faculty member), you are automatically subscribed to this newsletter. If you work or study outside of HMS/HSDM and would like to subscribe to the newsletter, please visit countway.info/subscribe. Your Input is Welcome What do you think of our newsletter? Let us know!

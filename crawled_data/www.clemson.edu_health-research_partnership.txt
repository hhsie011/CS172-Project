One of CUSHR’s important strategic partners is Prisma Health, formerly called Greenville Health System, which named Clemson its primary health care research partner in 2013. Prisma Health and other partners are key to CUSHR’s efforts to affect positive change in health care, understanding that our efforts are better together.

Mentors and mentees meet on a schedule of their choosing to discuss relevant topics, get to know each other, share challenges and brainstorm solutions. The primary goal of the network is to increase access to talent on campus for members of industry and to provide students with an opportunity to get an inside look at real-world situations in their field of study.

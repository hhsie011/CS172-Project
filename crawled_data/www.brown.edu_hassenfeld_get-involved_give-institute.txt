Get Involved Give to the Institute Hassenfeld Child Health Innovation Awards Give to the Institute What your gift does! If you want to improve the health of children and make Rhode Island--and every community--among the world’s healthiest places for children and their families, you can help achieve this goal by donating to the Hassenfeld Child Health Innovation Institute. Gifts in any amount, when combined with funds from other supporters, have the power to make a real difference. Your gift can be designated to any initiative. If you make your gift unrestricted, it will drive the following critical initiatives: $1,000 - Strengthens the core Prospective Birth Cohort Study—Your gift allows Hassenfeld researchers to find out more from study participants today, multiplying the potential of the study to be the basis for new discoveries that make children healthier in years to come. $500 - Helps drive the development of mobile health apps—Your gift helps Hassenfeld researchers harness the power of technology to reach children and families near and far in new ways, reducing risk and promoting healthy living. $250 - Sponsors a Community of Learners Program participant—Your gift supports the education and mentoring of tomorrow’s child health leaders while they help address the needs of Rhode Island children today. $100 - Fosters family engagement—Your gift will help the Hassenfeld Institute provide families, in Rhode Island and nationally, with real-time information through its website, so they can find doctors, facilities, and support to optimize their children’s health. Your gift of $100,000 or more can make an even greater impact. Please contact Patrick Schaefer to learn about opportunities to support the Hassenfeld Institute with a major gift.

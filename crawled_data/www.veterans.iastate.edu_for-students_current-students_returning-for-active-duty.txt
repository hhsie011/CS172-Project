We are excited to welcome you back. Contact the Iowa State Veterans Center Our staff can help guide you through the re-entry process. Office: 3540 Memorial Union Email: veteranscenter@iastate.edu Register for Classes Get your RAN (registration access number) from your adviser for your initial registration each semester. A new RAN will be assigned each semester. The RAN is needed to add a class any time you have NO other courses on that semester's schedule.

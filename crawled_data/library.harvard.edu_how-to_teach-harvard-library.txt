Teach with Harvard Library Get support to help your students navigate the library and find the materials they need. Course Planning During the Semester Student Support Before the Semester We work with faculty across the university to tailor your students’ research experiences to your course goals. Whether you’re looking to select course materials, test-drive an assignment prompt, design an in-class activity, or facilitate a deep exploration of unique primary sources, we partner with you to make it happen. The best place to start? Reach out to Ask a Librarian and let us know what you're working on. We'll connect you with the liaison for your course. Service Course Reserves We work with faculty and instructors to help provide students with access to course materials.

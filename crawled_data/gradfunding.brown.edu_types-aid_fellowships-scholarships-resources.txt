Receiving outside scholarships and tuition benefits is an excellent way to finance your Brown education. Notify the Office of Financial Aid when you receive an outside scholarship; our office is required to reevaluate your financial aid package and account for the additional resource. This could result in a reduction of federal or private loan funding depending on the amount of outside funding received.

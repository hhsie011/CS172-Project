Notification Funding Resources Seminar Development Workshops Creating and Ordering Honors Seminars Review and Notification Proposals are evaluated by a subcommittee of the University Honors Council, comprised of faculty and students from across colleges. Criteria for selection will focus on how well the course meets the guidelines described. The number of proposals accepted is based on budget. We can typically fund around 20 courses. We typically receive 35-40 proposals. Faculty and department heads will learn the status of proposals via email in late-October.

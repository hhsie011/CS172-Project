Consider any of these convenient ways to give based on what is right for you. Give Online Find your giving opportunity. Give by Check Give via check made payable to Trustees of Dartmouth College with the intended purpose included in the memo section or accompanying correspondence. Checks should be mailed to: Gift Recording Office 6066 Development Office Hanover, NH 03755-4400 Give by Wire Transfer To give via a wire transfer, please complete this form. If you have any questions or need additional support, please contact the Gift Recording Office at gift.recording.office@dartmouth.edu or by calling (603) 646–0098. Securities Gifts of securities held by your bank or brokerage or by you offer tax advantages for donors and are easy to make. Your brokerage firm may wire gifts of stock directly into one of Dartmouth’s brokerage accounts. Complete the Investment Office gift form to provide qualifying information about your stock transfer, or contact them directly at security.gift.processing@dartmouth.edu or (603) 646–3201. Gifts from UK Residents The Dartmouth College Trust (charity #1054144) offers a tax-efficient vehicle for alumni, parents and friends who are UK residents. For donors who pay taxes in the UK at the basic rate, the Trust will recover the basic tax paid on your donation, thus stretching your gift to Dartmouth by 25 percent. There is no minimum or maximum gift necessary to qualify for Gift Aid. All gifts should be made through wire transfer, in British Pounds. Please email Dee Dee Simpson for or wire transfer instructions and your Gift Aid form. IRA Charitable Rollover Consider utilizing your required minimum distribution (RMD) to support Dartmouth. If you are 70.5 years or older, you can make a tax-free distribution from your IRA to Dartmouth. Any qualified contribution up to $100,000 counts toward your required minimum distribution. Your spouse may also make a qualified charitable distribution up to $100,000. To make a gift from your IRA, contact your IRA custodian (Fidelity, Merrill Lynch, Schwab, Vanguard, etc.) and ask the representative to send a check in the amount you desire (up to $100,000) payable to Trustees of Dartmouth College. Checks should be mailed to: Gift Recording Office 6066 Development Office Hanover, NH 03755-4400 Call or email the Gift Recording Office at (603) 646-0098 or gift.recording.office@dartmouth.edu and provide the following information: your name and class year the amount of your check the name of the IRA custodian (Fidelity, Merrill Lynch, Schwab, Vanguard, etc.) if the gift is to the Dartmouth College Fund, a restricted gift (if restricted, please describe the details), or a combination of the two.

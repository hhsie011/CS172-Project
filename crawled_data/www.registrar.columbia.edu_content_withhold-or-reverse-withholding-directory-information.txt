Request for Transcript - Application for Replacement of Diploma - Request to Withhold or Reverse Withholding of Directory Information - Name Change Affidavit Home > Personal Information > Withhold or Reverse Withholding of Directory Information Withhold or Reverse Withholding of Directory Information Students and former students may choose to request that the Office of the Registrar not release directory information about them without their prior written consent. Directory information includes: name local and permanent address email UNI phone number date and place of birth major field of study participation in officially recognized activities and sports weight and height of members in athletic teams dates of attendance at Columbia and school, department, or institute attended degrees conferred, awards received, and their dates other educational institutions attended A directory release hold may be reversed at any time.

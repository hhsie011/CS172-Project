China-Link Scholarship January 10, 2022 In partnership with the China Scholarship Council (CSC), the Scholarship supports full-time students, faculty, and academic staff who are citizens of countries other than the People's Republic of China to undertake a period of non-degree purpose study or scientific research. The rewarded students will receive up to full tuition, housing, living expenses, and medical insurance during their proposed stay while studying and conducting research at the host institutions in China. The Scholarship aims to restore two-way mobility between scholars and students and reinforce long-term research collaborations between China and the United States. Scholarship opportunities will be available for talented students and academic staff from foreign universities to undertake a period of non-degree purpose study or scientific research at their Chinese partner universities. The academic visit could be conducted by an individual or as a group of people with a mixture of both students and faculties, and started at any time throughout the academic year 2022/23. Expand all Collapse all Eligibility Citizen of a country other than the People’s Republic of China; Register as a full-time student or employed as full-time academic staff at the home university by the time of application and throughout the proposed stay in China; Meet relevant academic and language requirements of the Chinese host university. Study/Research Fields All disciplines other than Chinese language study. Duration One to twelve months Funding Successful applicant will receive a full scholarship from CSC during their stay in China, including: Tuition fee (if applied) Free on-campus accommodation Monthly stipend of CNY 3,000 Comprehensive medical insurance Checklist for Application Mandatory: Valid passport bio page Pre-admission letter/invitation issued by the Chinese host university Highest education diploma and transcripts Study plan or research Proposal Foreigners Physical Examination Form (sample form provided by CSC) Optional: Language qualification certificate (HSK certificates, etc.) List of publications within 3 years Working Language The working language of the proposed academic visit in China should be clarified in the pre-admission letter/invitation issued by the Chinese host universities.

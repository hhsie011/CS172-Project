Teaching Climate Change Education in Schools of Education One path to boosting students’ capacity to understand climate change and climate choices emerges through improving climate change education in schools of education. Join Andy Revkin of the Columbia Climate School in a brainstorm with two professors testing fresh approaches, along with some of their students. His guests are Jing Lin, Harold R. W. Benjamin Professor of International Education, University of Maryland College of Education, and Oren Pizmony-Levy, Associate Professor of Education, Teachers College, Columbia University, director of the Teachers College Initiative for Sustainable Futures, and Co-Director of the New York City Partnership for Sustainability Education.

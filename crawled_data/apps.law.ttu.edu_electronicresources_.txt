Texas Tech Law Library Electronic Resources: Databases, Journals, and Sites The Law Library maintains a number of subscriptions to both legally and non-legally related databases, e-journals, and news sites. Currently, you can browse through the collection through an alphabetical list or by subject area. Most legal electronic research resources can be accessed only by currently enrolled law students, faculty and staff. Many other databases and journals are available to all TTU students, faculty and staff at the University Library. See this page for more information on access restrictions and options for electronic databases and journals.

InfoEd Go to InfoEd Brown University has chosen InfoEd as its new electronic platform for faculty Conflict of Interest (COI) and Laboratory Animals (LA) compliance modules. InfoEd provides a fully-integrated system that manages the "administrative work" involved in research. Along with the implementation of InfoEd to replace Coeus and legacy compliance systems; participants in this project will look for opportunities to streamline and improve current business processes in Brown's research community.

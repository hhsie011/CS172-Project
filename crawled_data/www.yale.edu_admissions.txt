Or simply hoping to broaden your educational horizons? You can earn undergraduate degrees at Yale College, master’s or doctoral degrees at the Graduate School of Arts & Sciences, and specialized degrees at our 12 professional schools. There are also many non-degree programs that allow individuals to study or pursue research without matriculating. Apply directly to the school or program where you wish to enroll. Yale is committed to ensuring that qualified students are admitted without regard to their financial circumstances, and that they graduate without excessive debt.

Now Refer a Game Changer SMU Guildhall is committed to nurturing the talent and skill of the next generation of game developers and launching them into successful careers. Do you know a student or professional who might be interested in learning more about unlocking their potential as an SMU Guildhall Game Changer? Use the forms below to submit your information, followed by their information. We will contact your referral to let them know that we have received their information from you, and invite them to add their name to our mailing list so that we can continue to communicate with them. Should you experience any technical difficulties with your submission, please contact reneja@smu.edu.

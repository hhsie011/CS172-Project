Event 2018 Beijing Energy Week Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo June 1, 2018 - June 3, 2018 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing From June 1 to June 3, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing will host the 2018 Beijing Energy Week.

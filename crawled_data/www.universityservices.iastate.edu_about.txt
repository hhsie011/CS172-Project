The Division of Operations & Finance is dedicated to supporting the land-grant mission of Iowa State University by providing leadership and services to facilitate and enhance financial and strategic operations for thousands of University students, faculty, and staff. The Division is made up of seven mission-critical units working collaboratively to provide safety and accountability, customer-focused services, stewardship of physical and financial resources, offer aesthetic experiences that enrich the university and its constituents, keep fiscal and regulatory compliance, and advance and build campus and community partnerships. Our team is continuously working to develop strategies to achieve university priorities while upholding our values and team standards.

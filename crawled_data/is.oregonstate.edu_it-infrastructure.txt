This includes both University-wide access rights as well as services to allow colleges, departments and groups to easily and securely create privileges. Shared Infrastructure Group The Shared Infrastructure Group (SIG) is responsible for design and management of the hardware infrastructure located in Information Services' data centers. SIG's primary focus is on providing Infrastructure as a Service in the form of consolidated computing and storage, application delivery (load balancing and content switching), and backup/disaster recovery resources.

We've returned to 1104 Jordan Hall after a major renovation of the NRL. Visit our brand-new, light-filled study spaces. The Natural Resources Library provides research and instructional support for the College of Natural Resources and the department of Marine, Earth and Atmospheric Sciences at NC State. The library collection includes materials on forestry, natural resources, environmental sciences, sport management, parks and recreation, tourism, paper science and engineering, wood products, biomaterials and bioenergy, and marine, earth, and atmospheric sciences.

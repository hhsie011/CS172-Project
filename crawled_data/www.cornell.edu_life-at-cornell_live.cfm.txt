Consistently rated as a top area to live, work and study, Ithaca is a great place to call home. Housing We strive to create places where students can live, learn and grow together. Living on campus provides a built-in support system of faculty, staff and student leaders, as well as easy access to dining and recreational facilities. Beyond campus, whether you want to live in downtown Ithaca or in the country, the region offers the best of both worlds.

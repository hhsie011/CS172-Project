Submit an Event UH School of Art Annual Student Exhibition Friday, May 20, 2022 10:00 am - 5:00 pm Every spring this popular exhibition introduces the University of Houston campus and Houston audiences to the work of School of Art undergraduate seniors and first- and second-year graduate students. The show features works selected by School of Art faculty in consultation with student artists. More than 100 UH artists will present works during this exhibition. The exhibition has long served as a way to introduce rising artists to local patrons and provides students with the experience of presenting works in a professional museum. Visit this exhibition at the Blaffer May 7—22, 2022, admission is always free!

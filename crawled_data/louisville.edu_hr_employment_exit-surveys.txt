Design Workgroup UofL Event Calendar Employee ResourcesEmployee Resources Dropdown Toggle Benefits Design Workgroup UofL Event Calendar Workday Home / Employment / Exit Surveys Exit Surveys The following surveys were developed to provide you an opportunity to share your experience while employed at UofL and to provide the university with a better understanding of your reasons for leaving. You may complete either the on-line survey or a paper version. Select the survey that applies to your employment status. The faculty on-line exit survey is located at Electronic Form Exit Survey Faculty (PDF) The staff on-line survey is located at Electronic Form Exit Survey Staff (PDF) If you experience problems accessing the surveys, call 502-852-6169 or send an email to irsurvey@louisville.edu. To access the paper version, click on the appropriate PDF, open and print. Complete the survey with a blue or black pen. The survey should be returned to University of Louisville either by fax at 502-852-2344, email at irsurvey@louisville.edu or by regular mail - Office of Institutional Research, 305 MITC, Louisville, KY 40292.

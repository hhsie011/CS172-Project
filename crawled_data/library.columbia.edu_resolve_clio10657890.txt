Barney Rosset and China Home Barney Rosset: Early Life & Career Landscapes People War (1944-1945) Retreat (May 1945-Sept. 1945) Surrender (Sept. 1945) Return to China (1996) The images in this exhibition come from the Barney Rosset Papers held by the Rare Book and Manuscript Library at Columbia University. Kunming Municipal Museum in Kunming City, Yunan, China, mounted an exhibition of these images "Publisher-hero as Combat Photographer in China," which opened on February 20, 2014. Image selection and captioning was done by exhibit co-curators Bob Bergin, Karla Nielsen, and Astrid Rosset. The text and arrangement of the online exhibit was created by Margarita Castroman, Jessica Hallock, and Karla Nielsen.

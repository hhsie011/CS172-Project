America/New_York YAANW Presents: ‘Private Equity as a Structurally Superior Asset Class’ with David Royal ’93, ’97 JD David Royal ’93, JD ’97, Thrivent Executive Vice President and Chief Investment Officer, will speak on Private Equity at a YAANW happy hour at 4pm on June 1, 2022 at the Minneapolis Club. David will focus on the former Yale University CIO David Swensen’s principle of “private equity as a structurally superior asset class.” David will provide his thoughts on private equity and how to democratize this investment vehicle to make it accessible to more households.

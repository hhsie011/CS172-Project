Building Community (GT Login) Building Community Home Sustainable Practices Who to Call News & Notes Professional Development Helpful Policies About BM-COP Open Search Search Search Welcome to SBO Sustainability and Building Operations (SBO) is part of Infrastructure and Sustainability. SBO was created in July 2020 at the intersection of building operations and sustainable planning and practice. Core Functions Campus Sustainability Coda Building Community Support Services Team Living Building Challenge certified Kendeda Building Sustainable Building Operations focus area Purpose Strategic Sustainability and Partnerships To partner with Institute and I&S leaders at multiple levels to help craft sustainability goals, develop and track targets, implement programs, and act as trusted advisors around sustainability and building operations Operational Planning To work with and curate communities of practice on implementation of sustainable building practices, reducing operational barriers, and leveraging shared knowledge between groups Direct Services To provide project services on Institute-wide initiatives, as well as direct building and administrative services on a portfolio of properties including: historical buildings, research spaces, leased properties and cutting edge sustainably designed buildings Kendeda Building The Kendeda Building for Innovative Sustainable Design recently launched their YouTube page. View an overview video of the project and some bite-sized learning modules. You can also view a virtual tour with Shan Arora, director. Kendeda YouTube Strategic Plan for Sustainable Practice In 2016-2018, the Georgia Tech community came together to draft guiding principles and a framework for operational sustainability in a strategic plan for sustainable practices, which will be reviewed and aligned with the recently-released Institute and Division strategic plans. See the plan Sustainable Building Operations Georgia Tech has a vibrant community of facility service providers and facility managers. Working together, we can develop a strong community of practice with sustainable processes and programs. SBO facility managers also support 445k GSF in 8 key campus buildings. Sustainable Building Ops Coda CSS Team The Coda CSS Team supports the students, faculty and staff within the Coda Building and works to ensure smooth operations for Georgia Tech spaces in the building. Coda CSS Team Covid-19 asymptomatic surveillance testing One of Sustainability and Building Operations’ first big initiatives as a team was to organize Covid-19 asymptomatic surveillance testing collections on campus for the Fall 2020 semester, including site logistics and contact tracing. This initiative leveraged the partnerships that SBO strives to build with functional experts, service providers, and building managers across campus to deliver a fully operational testing collections program and support the related contact tracing and engagement initiatives.

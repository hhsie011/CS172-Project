This opportunity gives students a chance to enjoy a meal and share their own stories regarding their experiences here at NC State while learning more about the chancellor’s role and the university. Please note that the purpose of these lunches is not for Chancellor Woodson to intervene in individual issues best handled by established campus procedures. The Chancellor’s Office will contact you to confirm your lunch date. Space is limited.

Norton Associate Dean for Enrollment Management The Office of Admissions and Financial Aid is here to assist you from the beginning of your interest in our programs to the point of admission. Our staff is available by appointment or for walk-ins to answer your inquiries one-on-one. Whether it's discussing any specific area of your admissions application, walking through the different types of financial aid you may be eligible to receive, or getting assistance in creating a budget for school and living expenses, our staff is here to help. Do you have questions on what departments look for in a candidate? Are you unsure of which program would be the best fit for you? Are you interested in learning more about upcoming information sessions or open house events? Do you have concerns on financial aid options? Throughout the application process, our office is here to assist. CONTACT US UPDATE: Due to the current COVID-19 pandemic and social distancing guidelines, all Admissions and Financial Aid staff are working remotely with no access to telephones. If you would like to schedule an appointment with Admissions or Financial Aid, please click on this link. Stop by the Office of Admissions and Financial Aid on the north end of the lobby level of the Allan Rosenfield Building (722 West 168th Street, 10th Floor), or reach Admissions at ph-admit@columbia.edu and Financial Aid at sphfinaid@columbia.edu. Our office is open Monday-Friday from 8am-5pm. For more information on the application process and degree offerings, please see Become a Student.

If you have any questions about Hire Red Raiders, please call the University Career Center at (806) 742-2210 or email at careercenter@ttu.edu. STUDENTS/ALUMNI Current students, please use your eRaider username and password to login to Hire Red Raiders. If you receive an error message when logging in, please call our office at (806)742-2210. Schedule an Appointment To schedule an appointment, login to your Hire Red Raiders account. From there, click on the Appointments button on the left-hand sidebar. You can filter by appointment type, location, or staff member. After you've scheduled an appointment, you can log back in to edit or cancel your appointment. If you sign up for a video appointment, you will be able to access your Zoom link through the appointment confirmation in Hire Red Raiders. Register for Events To register for an event, login to your Hire Red Raiders account. From there, click on the Events button on the left-hand sidebar. Select from a wide variety of events, from career fairs to networking events to employer infosessions. Search for Jobs To search for jobs, login to your Hire Red Raiders account. From there, click on the OCI & Job Listings button on the left-hand sidebar. Use the filters to narrow your results and save searches to get alerts when new jobs are posted. Access the Resource Library To access the resource library, login to your Hire Red Raiders account. From there, click on the Resource Library button on the left-hand sidebar. View our guidebooks on resumes, interviewing, and more! Set Up Your Alumni Account To set up your Hire Red Raiders alumni account: During business hours, please call 806-742-2210 to set up your alumni account on Hire Red Raiders. Our office business hours are Monday-Friday from 8:00AM-5:00PM. After you call, please login here. If you already have an account, log in here. Share Your Career Plans! Log in to share your career plans today! EMPLOYERS Employers must use their company email address to set up an account. Review the Recruiting Guidelines & Policies before setting up an account. Set Up Your Account To set up your account, go to Hire Red Raiders (https://ttu.12twenty.com/hire). Click on Sign up for an account below the login button. Employers must use their company email address to set up an account. Review our Recruiting Guidelines & Policies before setting up your account! If you already have an account, login here. Post a Job Review our Recruiting Guidelines & Policies before posting your positions! To post a job, login to your Hire Red Raiders account (https://ttu.12twenty.com/hire). From there, click on the Post a Job button on the landing page. You can also use the left-hand side bar and click on OCI and Job Postings. View our Post a Job Step-by-Step Tutorial Register for a Career Fair To register for a career fair, login to your Hire Red Raiders account (https://ttu.12twenty.com/hire). From there, click on the Attend a Career Fair button on the landing page. You can also use the left-hand side bar and click on Events. View our Event Registration Step-by-Step Tutorial Host an Event To host an event, login to your Hire Red Raiders account (https://ttu.12twenty.com/hire). From there, click on the Host an Event button on the landing page. Login to your Hire Red Raiders account and schedule an appointment online. If you have any questions navigating the site, please call our office at (806) 742-2210.

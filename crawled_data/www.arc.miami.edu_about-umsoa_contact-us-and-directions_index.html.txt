Regalado Assistant Dean and Director of Academic Services (305) 284-3730 anaregalado@miami.edu Directions to U-SoA Driving Directions to the School of Architecture and Jorge M. Perez Architecture Center: Take U.S. Highway One (US1) or Ponce de Leon Blvd. into Coral Gables to the intersection of Stanford Drive. Turn onto Stanford Drive and proceed to the Information Booth. Ask for directions to the Pavia Garage on Pavia Street and Levante Avenue where free parking is available after 4 p.m. Walk from Pavia Garage along Merrick Street and Dickinson Drive to the School of Architecture at 1223 Dickinson Drive. MetroRail Station Directions to the School of Architecture and Jorge M. Perez Architecture Center: The Coral Gables Campus is located directly north of the University Metrorail Station. When leaving the University Station, cross Ponce de Leon Blvd and turn right. Turn left onto Merrick Street. Continue straight until you reach Dickinson Drive, turn left. You will see the Perez Architecture Center about 200 yards in front of you.

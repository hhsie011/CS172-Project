maintain maps in support of daily campus operations, such as maps of campus bike racks, buildings, trees and more. We also produce maps indicating the progress of roadway and sidewalk snow removal in the case of snowy weather. Maps are available through our online GIS dashboard and do not require special software to access. Who is Eligible to Use This Service? Faculty Staff Students Campus Visitors (without a NetID) Go to the GIS dashboard Additional Info Some maps are produced for internal use, these can be accessed by visiting the UW Facilities Applications section of the GIS portal and logging in with your UW Net ID.

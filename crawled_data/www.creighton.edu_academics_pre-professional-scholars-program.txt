The Center for Advising Resources and Support in Student Success offers a premier pre-professional advising program to all students that’s unlike any other university. The Creighton Pre-Professional Scholars Program (CPPSP) provides qualifying students the opportunity to be a part of our extraordinary guarantee program. Each CPPSP program requires an application with specific requirements. Questions? admissions@creighton.edu 402.280.2703 1.800.282.5835 Program Offerings Dentistry Law Medicine Occupational Therapy Pharmacy Physical Therapy Benefits Comparison The checklist below includes benefits that every Creighton student receives through the Success Center and Creighton Pre-Professional Scholars Program (CPPSP). The Success Center benefits are available to any Creighton student who is interested in pre-professional studies. CPPSP requires eligible students to apply and be accepted to CPPSP to take advantage of the benefits.

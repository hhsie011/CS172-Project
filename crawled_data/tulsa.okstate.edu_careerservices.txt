103 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. Monday-Friday Contact OSU-Tulsa Career Services tulsa.careerservices@okstate.edu 918-594-8404 Employer Career Fair Registration OSU-Tulsa Career Services OSU-Tulsa has a college career consultant available to assist students and alumni with every aspect of their job search. Some of the services Career Services provides are: Career Assessment Resume and Cover Letter Development Job Search Strategies Mock Interviews If you haven't visited the Career Services office in Tulsa, stop by! We have computers and a laser printer designated for job search purposes, job search related books, tip sheets and more. We also have free resume paper and thank you cards as well as leather portfolios for purchase. Schedule an Appointment Give the career services office a call at 918-594-8404 if you have any questions. Virtual Appointments OSU-Tulsa Career Services appointments are being offered virtually via Zoom. OSU-Tulsa students and alumni can schedule an appointment online at hireosugrads.com. Schedule Appointment For Employers OSU Career Services offers a diverse range of services, which are designed to reach the student or graduate profile that the organization is seeking. All services, except Career Fairs and Employer Information Sessions, are at no charge to employers. For more information about recruiting at OSU, visit at HireOSUGrads.com or contact us at careers@okstate.edu or 405-744-5253. Career Fairs OSU-Tulsa hosts a career fair every fall and spring semester, hosting companies interested in all majors and degrees. OSU-Stillwater hosts many other career fairs that we encourage you to attend as well. For a list of upcoming career fairs and the employers visiting campus go to HireOSUGrads.com.

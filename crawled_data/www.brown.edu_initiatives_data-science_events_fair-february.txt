Our goal is to bring together researchers from different areas of expertise to discuss issues with computation and society. Talks will all be virtual, with the opportunity to watch them together in DSI's open seminar space, 164 Angell, 3rd floor. Coffee and snacks will be served on Wednesdays at 1:30 PM and Thursdays at 10:00 AM. Lunch will be served on Thursdays at 12:00 PM.

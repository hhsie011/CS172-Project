School of Medicine News Releases UofL News City of Louisville, Kentucky Kentucky's largest metro area with a population of 1 million+ located on the Ohio River Home of the Kentucky Derby & Kentucky Derby Festival, one of the country's most extensive park systems and a world-renowned arts scene Climate - mild four-season climate with average temperatures of 79 in July and 35 in January Cost of living is consistently below the national average, with housing costs 76% of national averages. The city is also a top choice for solo renters as reported in a recent study by SmartAsset. Louisville, KY is within a day's drive of more than 50% of the U.S. population.

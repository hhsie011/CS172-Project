Namesake Schuhmacher Hall is named after Henry C. Schuhmacher of Houston, Texas, and was constructed in 1964. Mr. Schuhmacher is head of a wholesale grocery business bearing his name. He became a member of the Board of Director of Texas A&M University in 1925 and died in 2002. Mr. Schuhmacher also served a member of the Board Committees on Auditing and Finances and as a member of the executive committee. 2021 – 2022 Rates Balcony Hall $2,520semester Double Room $3,583semester Single Room $3,916semester Apartment Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Suite Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same multi-room suite are the same gender.

Breadcrumb Home Corporate and Foundation Partnerships Twitter Facebook LinkedIn Ways to Give Corporate and Foundation Partnerships Columbia University highly values partnerships with foundations and corporations, offering countless opportunities for engagement and collaboration. With over 200 research institutes and centers, a wide range of world-class laboratories, and a high caliber of faculty and students, Columbia University is a powerhouse of research, scholarship, and innovation. Partnership opportunities may include: • Funding research or education programs • Becoming a center or institute partner • Supporting fellowships, scholarships, and professorships • Licensing and investing in new technology • Sponsoring events, awards, and competitions • Promoting entrepreneurship The Office of Corporate & Foundation Relations in the University's Office of Alumni and Development works with foundations and corporations to develop partnerships that advance Columbia’s research, scholarship, and strategic initiatives, and that align with our partners’ philanthropic missions.

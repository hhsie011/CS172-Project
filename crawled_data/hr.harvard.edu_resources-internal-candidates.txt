LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT / CAREER DEVELOPMENT / Resources for Internal Candidates Career Connections across Harvard 2022 The Center for Workplace Development is excited to announce that after a two-year hiatus Career Connection across Harvard is back for 2022! Open to benefits-eligible staff with a Harvard Key, this all-virtual event will be held on Thursday, June 9, 12 pm to 4 pm. The agenda will feature career programming, networking opportunities, and the always-popular resume review and critique. As an event attendee, you'll be able to: Connect directly with representatives of Harvard schools and units across the University Explore future opportunities as you listen to inspiring career stories Sharpen your knowledge of career development tools and resources Resources for Internal Candidates Dynamic learning environment, innovation-driven culture, enviable faculty and staff. Amid our culture of achievement, it’s no surprise that many employees progress in their careers by taking on new roles within Harvard. The following classes and tools are available to current staff to help you get ready for your next position, put your best foot forward in the application process, gain an understanding of Harvard's career landscape, and just as importantly, to excel in your current role. Career Courses and Tools How to Prepare as an Internal Candidate This online course is available through the Harvard training portal anytime. The course is comprehensive and provides important thought questions and practical examples of resumes, cover letters and interviews. All internal candidates are encouraged to review the course content.

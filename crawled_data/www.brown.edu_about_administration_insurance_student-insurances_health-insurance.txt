Participation in SHIP is required of all Brown University students who do not have coverage through a comparable health insurance plan. SHIP is underwritten by UnitedHealthcare Student Resources, and includes: · A national provider network through UnitedHealthcare Choice Plus PPO that gives you access to in-network providers throughout the country; · Coverage at 100% for in-network providers & 70% for out-of-network providers; · Deductibles: $300 per person per policy year (combined in- and out-of-network); · $15 copay for Physician's Office visits; $25 copay for Urgent Care; $100 copay for Emergency Room per visit, $100 for inpatient hospital stay; · Prescription drug coverage with low copays; · In-Network Preventive care services with no deductibles, copays or coinsurance; · Worldwide Coverage including Medical Evacuation, Repatriation & Travel Assistance Services; · Wellness discounts including Vision and Dental; The information provided above is a general summary of benefits. To learn more details about SHIP, Brown’s insurance requirements and how to waive go to www.gallagherstudent.com/Brown.

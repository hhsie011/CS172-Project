CALENDAR / Career Connections across Harvard! Date: Thursday, June 9, 2022, 12:00pm to 4:00pm Location: Online The Center for Workplace Development is excited to announce that after a two-year hiatus Career Connection across Harvard is back for 2022! Open to benefits-eligible staff with a Harvard Key, this all-virtual event will be held on Thursday, June 9, 12 pm to 4 pm. The agenda will feature career programming, networking opportunities, and the always-popular resume review and critique.

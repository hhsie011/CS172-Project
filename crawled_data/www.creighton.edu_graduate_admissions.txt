If you’re still considering your options you can explore all of our Graduate School offerings or narrow your focus by discovering the options that fit your current path in life. The more you learn about graduate studies at Creighton, the better you’ll understand that this is not just a place to earn a degree. What you learn at the Graduate School can change your life and have a positive impact on the lives of those you reach. Creighton University is a rich community of students who, like you, want to better themselves and the world. You can catch a glimpse of this community by learning about the Jesuit principles on which our student experience is built, discovering more about the school’s rich history that dates back to 1878 and reading some of the success stories of Creighton alumni.

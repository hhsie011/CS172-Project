Joint Service Transcripts Transcripts can be ordered for Army, Navy, Marine Corps and Coast Guard veterans by logging on to their website or using your CAC card. Students must request that the transcripts be sent to the Third Street address for prompt delivery of your military transcripts. Your transcripts will be delivered to the university electronically. Community College of the Air Force Transcripts can be ordered for active duty members and veterans of the Air Force. Paper copies of your official transcript must be mailed directly to the university from the Community College of the Air Force: Office of Admissions Department AO University of Louisville Louisville, KY 40292 DANTES Exam Transcripts A transcript is an official score report for DANTES funded exams. Service members should request copies of score reports at the time of testing, and always designate an institution to receive a free official score report. Most scores can be reviewed online at the testing agencies website, within 2-4 weeks after test administration. Requesting scores and transcripts may be different for each of the DANTES-funded exams. For more information visit the DANTES website.

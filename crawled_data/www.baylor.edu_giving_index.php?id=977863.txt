Our faculty, staff and retirees serve the university both in the classroom and through their selfless generosity to provide assistance for students in need. Baylor University also is dedicated to strengthening the Waco community and our Central Texas home through partnerships with groups like the Mayborn Museum, Baylor/Waco Foundation and other community-based organizations. Through special giving opportunities like these, there are many avenues for the Baylor family to invest in the future of the university. Baylor in Washington Excellence Fund Help provide opportunities for students, along with Baylor leadership, faculty and alumni, to play a critical role in shaping solutions to some of the nation's most critical challenges. Baylor/Waco Foundation For more than 50 years, Baylor/Waco Foundation has served the Waco community. We annually partner with local businesses and individuals to enrich Central Texas. Museums Museums enrich Baylor University and Central Texas by bringing vibrant and educational exhibitions to our community. Explore giving options and current needs. Senior Class Gift For over a century, the graduating senior class has bestowed upon the University a gift to mark their time and contributions to Baylor University.

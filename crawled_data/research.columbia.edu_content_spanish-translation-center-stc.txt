(STC) Spanish Translation Center (STC) The Spanish Translation Center was established in 1996 by the Clinical Trials Office with the goals of enlarging the pool of potential study participants by reaching out to the Hispanic community. Their services include: Initial translations into Spanish; Modifications or renewals of pre-existing STC translations; and Reviews of translations done by external translators for all research-related documents (e.g, informed consent forms, HIPAA forms, brochures, newsletters, etc.). Please email all applicable items are required for the translation to the Director. These items may include: For translation, IRB-approved English documents for initial translations or the PDF version of the IRB-approved English documents. For review, the Word version of the Spanish documents.

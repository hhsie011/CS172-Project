A dynamic podcast focusing on Moving Forward through Research. The Research Podcast explores research stories and research-intersections between the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) and the Georgia Tech Research Institute (GTRI). The podcast episodes explore the human-side behind the researchers at GTRI and Georgia Tech, highlight unique research projects, and looks closely at research that benefits the State of Georgia. Make sure to subscribe to the Georgia Tech Research Podcast on Spotify, Google Podcasts, and Apple Podcasts for the latest episodes.

Merit and competitive-based scholarships are awarded to exceptional new and continuing students each year. The Office of Student Financial Aid & Scholarships also provides need-based financial assistance to qualified families. We support families in identifying all opportunities to take the pressure off the pocketbook. Athletic, band, choral, fine arts and departmental awards are also available through academic colleges and departments. Check with your department for more information. If you are experiencing financial hardships within the last year, click here to learn how Texas Tech can assist you further.

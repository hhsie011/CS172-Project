For over one-third of Butler students, building lasting relationships, developing skills as leaders, and organizing all-campus fundraisers through Greek Life is college at its best. Learn About the Recruitment Process Frequently Asked Questions Greek Life Menu Interfraternity Council Chapters Panhellenic Sororities National Pan-Hellenic Council Order of Omega Frequently Asked Questions Recruitment For Parents & Families Create lasting memories. With the potential to explore new experiences and activities, our chapters, consisting of five fraternities and nine sororities, plan formals and social events, service projects, and stay connected with alumni mentors while maintaining GPAs that are, on average, above 3.4. Additionally in any given year, members of Butler’s Greek organizations contribute more than 20,000 hours in service to over 200 Indianapolis area nonprofits. Greek Organizations at Butler Interfraternity Council Chapters Joining a fraternity is an opportunity for you to grow as a student, as a leader, and most importantly, as an individual. Explore Fraternities Panhellenic Sororities The primary focus of Panhel is to foster unity and encourage high standards among all sorority members and to work for the good of Butler University and its students. Explore Sororities National Pan-Hellenic Council In joining an NPHC organization, you become more than a member of one chapter—you also join a self-supporting organization that will guide you throughout the remainder of your years at Butler, and for life. Learn More Order of Omega This leadership honor society recognizes individuals within the Greek community for their service to the fraternity system.

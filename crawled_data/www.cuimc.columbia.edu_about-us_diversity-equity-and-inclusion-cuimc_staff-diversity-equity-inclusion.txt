We support this effort by ensuring our faculty and staff have opportunities to voice their perspective and by providing resources that can teach us how to make an impact, now and in the future. Staff Diversity Council The CUIMC Staff Diversity Council develops, implements and evaluates initiatives that leads to greater diversity and inclusion throughout the CUIMC community. The council meets quarterly and is comprised of the lead Human Resources professional from the respective schools. CUIMC Employee Resource Groups Employee Resource Groups, or ERGs, are voluntary groups for staff with shared characteristics or life experiences.

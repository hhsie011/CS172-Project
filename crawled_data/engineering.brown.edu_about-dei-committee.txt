Goal The goal of the DEI committee is to advise the School of Engineering dean on DEI matters in strategy and programming, to advocate for all members in the SOE community, and to actively implement initiatives to achieve the long-term goals defined in the DEI strategic plan. Contact Us For questions or concerns about diversity, equity, and inclusion within the School of Engineering, please contact a member of the committee at DEI_Engineering@brown.edu.

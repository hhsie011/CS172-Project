Return it to Undergraduate Admissions: Bring to the Visitors Center located in West Hall Fax to 806-742-0062 Mail to: Undergraduate Admissions, Texas Tech University, Box 45005, Lubbock, TX 79409 Important: If students would like to take access away from the person listed on the FERPA form, visit West Hall Room 103 to rescind education information access. FERPA, the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act of 1974, is a federal law that pertains to the release of and access to educational records. The law, also known as the Buckley Amendment, applies to all schools that receive funds under an applicable program of the US Department of Education. Click here to learn more.

As we each seek to continue to center equity and inclusion for each of our constituents, the following webinars may be useful to inform your thinking and decision making moving forward. Ongoing Webinars “Race &” in America Series This panel discussion series curated by Brown’s Center for the Study of Race and Ethnicity in America in partnership with the Office of the Provost, draws on the expertise of Brown scholars to investigate the origins, history, and enduring contemporary effects of racism in America from a range of fields and scholarly perspectives. Sessions will take place monthly on Wednesdays 12:00 to 1:30 pm over the course of the 2020-21 Academic Year.

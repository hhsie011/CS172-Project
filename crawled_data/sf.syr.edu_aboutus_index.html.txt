When you consider the number of Syracuse University students who live in the San Francisco Bay Area, the number of graduates who have found success here, and the number of alumni who support Syracuse University both financially and with their time and energy, it’s clear that the City by the Bay bleeds Orange. What is it that’s ingrained Syracuse University into the very fabric of San Francisco? A vast network of Syracuse University alumni. Over 5,000 Bay Area alumni open doors for Syracuse University grads in the worlds of engineering, technology, the arts and sciences, and many others. A large home base of more than 200 Syracuse University students from the Bay Area. A wide range of events. Alumni networking events, industry panel discussions, new student send-offs—all these and more give alumni and students the opportunity to engage with Syracuse University in the Bay Area, creating a lively exchange of ideas. See upcoming events. A strong presence of Bay Area alumni, parents, and friends who volunteer in Syracuse University leadership positions. Six are members of school and college advisory boards, and 15 serve on the San Francisco Regional Council.

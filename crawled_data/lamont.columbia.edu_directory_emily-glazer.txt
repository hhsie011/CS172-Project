In 2021, she began a PhD in Earth and Environmental Sciences at Columbia University and joined the Polar Geophysics Group at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, advised by Indrani Das. Her research interests include mass balance of Antarctic ice sheets, ice-ocean interaction, remote sensing, and glacial history. She is currently a first-year graduate student.

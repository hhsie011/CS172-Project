In addition to coordinating the campus-wide routing of proposals and awards, the office is also responsible for the OSU export control compliance program, central administration of electronic research administration systems, and coordination of OSU limited submission competitions. This unit is part of the Division of the Vice President for Research. Grants & Contracts Financial Administration (GCFA) serves the OSU research community by providing guidance, fund set-up, post-award services, audit and financial compliance support, and general assistance for externally-funded/sponsored agreements and contracts. This unit is part of the office of Vice President for Administration and Finance.

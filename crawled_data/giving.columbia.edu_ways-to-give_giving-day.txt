Every October, Columbians around the world—alumni, students, parents, friends, neighbors, faculty, and staff—come together for Giving Day, a 24-hour online fundraising event. Donors support their favorite schools, programs, and causes in challenge funds fostering research, art, athletics, and a transformative Columbia education. On Giving Day 2021, Columbians made 21,359 gifts to raise $27,913,242, changing lives that change the world.

Directions Campus Parking On-campus parking is available for all of our guests. When attending a campus tour or event our office will provide parking validation to all admission guests who park in a campus parking garage. Before you arrive, please take a look at these resources to locate garages, parking availability, and where to walk once parked. Parking garage listing with addresses and Google maps Real-time garage space availability Printable campus maps and location coordinates Note: The Stratford Garage and University Avenue Garage (except ADA parking) aren't available to non-permit holders due to space availability.

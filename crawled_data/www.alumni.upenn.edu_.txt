M. Elizabeth Magill nominated to become President of the University of Pennsylvania Scott Bok, Chair of the University of Pennsylvania Board of Trustees, today announced that M. Elizabeth Magill, who currently serves as Executive Vice President and Provost of the University of Virginia, has been nominated by the Executive Committee of the Trustees to serve as the ninth President of the University. Magill will assume the Penn Presidency on July 1, 2022. All three went to Penn on NROTC scholarships, completing their commissioning ceremony on May 14. A Weekend We Soon Won't Forget From May 13-16, members of the Penn community were invited to the first in-person Alumni Weekend since 2019. Campus was buzzing as Penn alumni came out in record-breaking numbers to enjoy nearly 100 events and activities hosted throughout the weekend, including Franklin Fest, campus tours, the Parade of Classes, and 20 reunion parties.

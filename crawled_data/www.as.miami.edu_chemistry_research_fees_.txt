Courses Careers Give News Welcome to the Chemistry Department The Department of Chemistry collectively strives to expand the frontiers of chemical knowledge through innovative research and teaching. Understood as a community of scholars, the department houses multiple educational and scholarly activities by faculty, postdoctoral researchers, graduate and undergraduate students, all contributing to the advancement of the chemical sciences. Chemistry is often described as the Central Science, highlighting its importance to numerous scientific disciplines.

He enlisted into the Active Army in May 2000. He attended Basic Combat Training (BCT) and Advanced Individual Training (AIT)) at Fort Sill, OK. Upon completion of training, he was awarded the Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) 13B, Cannon Crew Member. ASSIGNMENTS: Sergeant First Class Schooff’s assignments and duty positions include, CGS Operator, 103rd MI BN, 3ID Fort Stewart, Ga; Squad leader (CGS Platoon), 319th MI BN, XVIII ABN Corp, Fort Bragg, NC; M109A6 Paladin Crew Member, 2-5 FA BN Fort Sill, OK; M119A2 Section Chief, 2-320th FAR 101st ABN Fort Campbell, Ky; Gunnery Sergeant, 2-320th FAR 101st ABN Fort Campbell, Ky; Master Resiliency Trainer, Leader Training Brigade, Fort Jackson, SC; Platoon Sergeant, 4-319th AFAR, 173rd IBCT(A) Grafenwohr, Germany; Operations Sergeant, 4-319th AFAR 173rd IBCT(A). EDUCATION: Sergeant First Class Schooff graduated from Fort Campbell High School and has an Associates in General Studies from American Military University. His military education includes, Army Senior Leaders Course, Advanced Leaders Course, Primary Leadership Development Course, Battle Staff Course, Pathfinder Course, Jumpmaster School, Air Assault School, Airborne School, Anti-Terrorism Training Course Levels I & II, SHARP Course, Master Resiliency Trainer Course Level I,II. AWARDS: Sergeant First Class Schooff’s awards include the Meritorious Service Medal, Army Commendation Medal with 4 Oak Leaf Cluster, Army Achievement Medal with 5 Oak Leaf Clusters, Army Good Conduct Medal, National Defense Service Medal, Armed Forces Expeditionary Medal, Afghanistan Campaign Medal, Iraq Campaign medal, Global War on Terrorism Service Medal, NCO Development Ribbon with numeral 3, Army Service Ribbon, Army Overseas Service Ribbon and NATO Medal.

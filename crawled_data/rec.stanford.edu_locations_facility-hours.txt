View 2021-2022 Annual Hours, Holidays, and Closures Access Access to Stanford’s Recreational Facilities is available at no cost to Stanford’s Students, Faculty and Staff with valid SUID Cards. Please see detailed outline below on additional individual Rec Facility access privileges or visit your nearest Rec Center for assistance. More Details Lockers Day use lockers are available in both the AOERC and ACSR. These lockers require you to bring your own lock. Locks can be purchased at the front desk for $5 or rented for single day use. Annual and quarterly lockers can be rented in the locker rooms at the AOERC. There are no rentable lockers at the ACSR. More Details Equipment Rental Recreation Equipment may be checked out from both the Arrillaga Center for Sports & Recreation (ACSR) and the Arrillaga Outdoor Education and Recreation Center (AOERC). If you need to rent our equipment for a group event or multiple day use; or want information on payment for rental and deposit, please contact the Associate Director of Recreation Services.

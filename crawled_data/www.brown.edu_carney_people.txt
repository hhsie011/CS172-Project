View Institute Team Affiliated Faculty Carney Institute-affiliated faculty are leaders in their respective areas of research into the brain. They are experimentalists, theorists, engineers, and clinicians who come together to produce fascinating new insights into how we think, feel and behave. View Affiliated Faculty Center Directors The Carney Institute is home to seven research centers, which are directed by Carney-affiliated faculty members. Read More Executive Committee The Carney Institute Executive Committee meets monthly to review Carney research priorities and programs, discuss funding opportunities, and provide input to the institute director and leadership team. The committee includes representatives of 11 departments and academic units, as well as Carney Institute initiatives and centers. View Executive Committee Scientific Advisory Board The Scientific Advisory Board advises the director of the Carney Institute for Brain Science on the institute's scientific priorities.

Temporary Employment Temporary employment agencies are an important resource for applicants looking for temporary work and to expand their work experiences. The University of Louisville contracts with several temporary agencies to help meet department's temporary staffing needs. Working at the University of Louisville through a temporary agency will allow you to gain valuable job skills, create a network of contacts and acquire experience that can be extremely valuable. The following is a current list of temporary agencies on contract with the University of Louisville. Applicants interested in temporary placement at the university should contact one or more of these agencies.

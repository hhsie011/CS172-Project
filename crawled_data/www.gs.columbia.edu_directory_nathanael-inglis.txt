here: Home Staff Directory Nathanael Inglis Nathanael Inglis Dean Inglis joined GS in 2019 with a strong background in teaching and academic advising. From 2015 to 2019, he worked as an Assistant Professor of Theological Studies and as an academic advisor to nontraditional students at Bethany Theological Seminary. His teaching focused on the intersection of theology, social justice, ecology, and environmental ethics. In addition to his role at Bethany, he was a teaching fellow at Fordham University, where he completed his Ph.D. in theology, before spending two years as a human rights worker in Guatemala. In addition to academic advising, Dean Inglis provides academic support to students participating in the Joint Program with List College of the Jewish Theological Seminary.

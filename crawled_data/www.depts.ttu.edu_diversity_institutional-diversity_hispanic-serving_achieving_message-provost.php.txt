This accomplishment reflects the commitment of the university's leadership and the concerted efforts of so many people at the institution to increase the diversity of our student body and to ensure that the citizens of Texas have access to higher education. Reaching the enrollment benchmark for HSI status will provide the faculty and staff of Texas Tech with new opportunities to better serve and educate all our students.

Location North Hall 116 Monday - Friday: 6 a.m. - 10 p.m. Saturday: 7 a.m. - 3 p.m. Sunday: CLOSED Contact 918-594-8126 tulsa.wellness@okstate.edu Wellness Center The OSU-Tulsa Wellness Center's mission is to enhance the quality of life of students, faculty and staff through improved physical and mental development that leads to a healthy and rewarding life. The Wellness Center provides a healthy lifestyle environment with programs, services and facilities that meet or exceeds our members' expectations. Participants are motivated and encouraged to feel a sense of progress and accomplishment while using the Wellness Center. The primary measure of success is member satisfaction. Wellness Center Hours The Wellness Center will be open Monday - Friday, 6 a.m.-10 p.m. and Saturday, 7 a.m.-3 p.m. The Wellness Center will be closed on Sundays. Activate your I.D. for Wellness Center Access Membership to the Wellness Center is free to current OSU-Tulsa students, faculty and staff. In order to gain access to the Wellness Center, members are required to activate their student ID through the online enrollment form.

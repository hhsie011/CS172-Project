GEMN The Graduate Enrollment Management Network holds lunch and learns once a semester, covering topics such as student health insurance subsidies and Slate training updates. You can learn more about GEMN from Tosha Hendrickson (Tosha_Hendrickson@baylor.edu). Graduate Admissions Handbook for Graduate Program Directors The Graduate Admissions Handbook is intended to help departments navigate the Graduate School’s recruitment and admissions processes. We hope this handbook will be valuable to your work in recruiting and admitting students.

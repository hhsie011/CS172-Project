The College’s innovative programs of study immerse students in Tech’s world-leading technological ecosystem, providing exceptional interdisciplinary learning that integrates humanities and social sciences with engineering, science, and computing. Our graduates thrive in the global technical and technological arena Hire Ivan Allen College of Liberal Arts graduates and you gain team members who think critically and holistically to bridge the technology gap and create a whole new world of possibilities. Both undergraduate and graduate students engage in research and projects working with real problems and challenges of organizations in government, business, and the non-profit world.

The Georgia Tech Georgia Tech Global Learning Center (GLC) is a one-stop shop dedicated to professional learning environments and next-level event services. Meetings? Trainings? Conferences? We do them all. Let us find you the perfect space, manage your audio-visual needs, and fuel your group. Our experienced, knowledgeable, and friendly staff carefully plan and coordinate each and every event from initial booking through completion. We have proactively updated and enhanced many of our cleaning and safety protocols and will continue to take appropriate action as needed.

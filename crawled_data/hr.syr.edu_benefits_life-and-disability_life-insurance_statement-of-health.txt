yourself and up to $20,000 for your spouse or same-sex domestic partner without providing a Statement of Health form. Increases in coverage during Open Enrollment or due to a qualifying family status change require a Statement of Health, however, this form is not required when electing spouse or same-sex domestic partner coverage levels of $10,000 or $20,000. *Part-time faculty eligible for Modified Benefits will need to complete a Statement of Health form for any amount elected over $20,000.

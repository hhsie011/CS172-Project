The CSUF IT TAC efforts will allow the IT infrastructure to provide the most effective support of the changing needs of the institution, enabling it to remain agile and competitive in carrying out its mission. The committee will consider the architectural aspects of both IT efforts involving campus wide integration and significant IT efforts within the division, which present opportunities for common architecture or service elements.

We aim to help students thrive in, and contribute to, a vibrant, diverse and inclusive community at Cornell and beyond. Through participation in these programs, students can: Expand life skills, including interpersonal, human relations, communications, and counseling skills Learn and enhance abilities in decision making, appreciating differences, crisis prevention and values clarification Volunteer in service and community building Grow as leaders, trainers and mentors Make lifelong connections with other students and staff Enhance campus climate Have fun! Our staff is committed to supporting and guiding students, providing a helping hand when needed, and celebrating their successes and contributions.

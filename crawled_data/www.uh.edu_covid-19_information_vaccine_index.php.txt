UH Vaccine Efforts The Student Health Center in partnership with UH Human Resources worked with partner organizations to provide COVID-19 vaccines and COVID booster shots to more than 4,000 UH faculty, staff and students in multiple clinics from March to November 2021. UH continues to encourage the vaccine for our community. COVID-19 vaccines and boosters are widely available through pharmacies and health care providers. For other vaccine appointments immediately available, please refer to the vaccine locator tools to find an appointment near you. Vaccine Locator Tools Vaccine Finder Texting Line Vaccines.gov now offers a texting line to find a vaccine near you. Text your zip code to GETVAX (438829) in English, or VACUNA (822862) in Spanish, to get the contact information of three nearby locations with available vaccines, complete with transportation opportunities.  Frequently Asked Questions For Frequently Asked Questions about the COVID-19 vaccine for UH employees and students, please visit the UH COVID-19 Vaccine FAQ.

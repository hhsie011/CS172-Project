Contact Assistant Director Justin Richardson. Ask a question NC State offers a variety of pre-college programs for middle and high school students. The Office of Undergraduate Admissions offers three different on-campus summer programs for high school students to provide a strong sense of community while also preparing high school students for the college application process and university experience. Each program offers you the opportunity to: Interact with NC State admissions representatives, faculty, staff and alumni Participate in college preparation workshops featuring how to approach the college search process, navigating financial aid and scholarships and application essay writing tips Engage in sessions pertaining to your cultural community including knowing your worth and growing your value on a college campus “The most impactful lesson I learned was that you can find a community to support you in any place.” – Banessa S., 2021 participant 800+ participants prepared for college throughout program history 125+ participants are current NC State students Programs CAMINOS: Your Journey to College CAMINOS is a summer program for rising high school juniors and seniors who identify at Latinx/Hispanic or who have an interest in Latinx/Hispanic culture. Read more Emerging Scholars Academy Emerging Scholars Academy (ESA) is a summer program for rising high school juniors and seniors who identify as Black/African American or who have an interest in Black/African American culture. Read more Native Education Forum Native Education Forum (NEF) is a summer program for rising high school juniors and seniors who identify as American Indian/Native American or who have an interest in American Indian/Native American culture. Read more CAMINOS: Your Journey to College During the on-campus program, students will participate in workshops to learn about university student life, engage in topics as they relate to the Latinx/Hispanic community and higher education, and receive assistance with the college application and financial aid process. The 2022 CAMINOS program will be held June 19-23. CAMINOS: Your Journey to College is for you if you: Are a rising 11th or 12th grade student that identifies as Hispanic/Latinx or have an interest in Hispanic/Latinx Culture Have a cumulative unweighted GPA of a 3.4 or higher or are in the top 15% of your class Are interested in pursuing higher education Are a leader in your school, family and/or community Application Deadline The deadline to apply for this program was April 1, but we encourage you to consider other summer opportunities at NC State here.

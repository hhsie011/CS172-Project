Program requirements for admission into the MAT Program: Must have a 2.75 GPA in an undergraduate program from a reputable institution. Recommendation letters are not required. Exams are not required for admittance to the MAT Program. **Please note: Praxis II Content exams are required for admission into the Teacher Education program (TEP).** You will receive more information about TEP admissions once you are admitted to the MAT program.

This will NOT be an actual emergency. During the test, no action will be necessary and there will be no need to seek shelter. Testing of the BrownSiren and BrownAlert systems is part of the University’s ongoing emergency preparedness efforts. Further information is available at http://www.brown.edu/Administration/EHS/emergency_preparedness/. Please note that during an actual emergency, information will appear on http://emergency.brown.edu/.

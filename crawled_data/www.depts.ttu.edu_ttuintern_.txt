Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Government & Public Service Internship Programs Menu Search Government & Public Service Internship Programs The Government & Public Service Internship Program provide students with an invaluable, first-hand experience in government on a federal or state level. Learn more about the internship opportunities offered in Washington, D.C., Austin and Lubbock, Texas. The program's objective is to provide students who have an interest in government and public policy the opportunity to participate in public service by observing and contributing to the legislative process. The internship experience helps students understand the challenges and rewards of public life and helps develop their leadership skills.

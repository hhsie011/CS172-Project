Application and Enrollment Information GEM program courses provide airmen (active duty Air Force personnel, Air Force Reserve and Air National Guard) the opportunity to complete CCAF’s 15 semester hours of general education requirements online. Typically, the 15 general education credits students needed for their AAS degree include coursework in: communication, humanities, math, social sciences, and writing. If you want to take GEM-approved general education coursework at Oklahoma State University you must apply to OSU as a non-degree seeking student. To apply, simply complete an application and pay the application fee. Active duty military or honorably discharged military veterans may receive an application fee waiver from the Office of Undergraduate Admissions. To receive this fee waiver, students should submit a copy of their active duty orders or DD214 with their application materials by email at admissions@okstate.edu, by fax (405) 744-7092, or through the mail to Undergraduate Admissions, 219 Student Union, Stillwater, OK 74078. If you require more information about GEM, please contact your education center or OSU at osuonline@okstate.edu or 405-744-2161. Apply Now Courses OSU has a variety of online courses available to meet the general education requirements of the CCAF Associate of Applied Arts degree. Courses may be 16, 8 or 4 weeks in duration and scheduled for Fall, Spring and Summer semesters. Tuition & Fees The Princeton Review named OSU as one of only 66 public universities that give students the “best bang for their tuition buck.” OSU offers scholarships for active duty military, reservists, National Guardsmen, veterans and their dependents.

Texas A&M has taken many steps to improve campus safety & preparedness, but ultimately your safety and preparedness are a personal matter. In addition to online resources, the Office of Safety & Security, along with the University Police Department and Environmental Health & Safety, can provide assistance to you and your organization to prepare for emergency situations. We provide: Emergency Preparedness Training upon request, such as for Severe Weather or Personal Safety. Facility manager/building proctor training Assistance with Continuity of Operations Planning (Business Continuity) Templates for Building Emergency Plans University Police - Crime Prevention National Incident Management System (NIMS) Training Conducting a Tabletop Exercise Conducting an After Action Review Campus Safety Awareness Series Events - Spring and Fall Semesters To request training, contact the Office of Safety & Security.

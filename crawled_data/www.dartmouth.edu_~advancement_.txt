Dartmouth's Advancement Division—made up of the offices of Alumni Relations and Development—presents the College's strengths to the world and continually seeks meaningful ways for alumni, parents, and friends to help Dartmouth fulfill its mission through their expertise, life experience, and philanthropy. Alumni Relations To deeply understand what constitutes meaningful engagement with alumni and to offer programs and services that bring value to their lives while enabling them to serve their alma mater. Visit the Alumni Relations website. Development To maximize gifts in support of the College by matching donors’ passions with institutional needs and priorities. Visit the Development website. Conferences and Events Conferences and Events collaborates with the Dartmouth community and public to facilitate and coordinate high quality support for events and programs.

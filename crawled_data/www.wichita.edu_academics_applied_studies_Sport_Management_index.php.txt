Wichita State University's Sport Management program prepares undergraduate and graduate students for careers in the management of sport entertainment, sport participation and sporting goods organizations. At WSU, you'll go beyond the classroom, honing your skills and developing contacts in real-world settings throughout the city and across the nation. Learn more about Sport Management at WSU Beyond the Books Internships Internships provide students with the opportunity to utilize knowledge gained through their classes, to enhance their portfolios, to network with sport managers, and to take an important step in establishing themselves as practicing sport managers. Study Abroad Studying abroad gives you the chance to meet peers and mentors in your field from all over the world. Not only will you form valuable connections for the future, but you will form friendships which last long beyond the return home. Careers A degree in sport management is designed to prepare students to compete for jobs in the sport industry including intercollegiate athletics, major league and minor league professional sports, fitness, recreation, and specialized sport business. Accredited Program WSU is among a select number of universities in the world with multiple programs accredited by the Commission on Sport Management Accreditation (COSMA).

If participation in the field trip is voluntary: Students can arrange their own transportation (Consider RIPTA, which is free when you show your Brown ID) Students should sign a Release and Waiver/Assumption of Risk form (available from the Office of the General Counsel). In either case: Can Zip Car be used? Can Academy Bus be used? It is possible to rent a vehicle or 7 passenger van? safeDrive guidelines must be followed If an employee's personal vehicle is used, the vehicle owner’s personal auto insurance is primary in the event of an accident.

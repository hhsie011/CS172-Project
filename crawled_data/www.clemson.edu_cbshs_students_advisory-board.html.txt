Board The College of Behavioral, Social and Health Sciences Student Advisory Board provides support to the College in meeting its strategic goals. Board members are each assigned to one of four committees: Communication, Development, Diversity and Inclusion, and Professional Development. Each committee works to assist the College in providing an outstanding educational experience for all students. Selection Process Each Spring a call for applications is sent to all undergraduate and graduate students in the College in order to allow for selection of new board members. Once selected to serve on the board, upon successful completion of board membership students are given the opportunity to remain on the board throughout their academic career at Clemson. Therefore, the number of board vacancies will vary yearly. The board strives to provide representation across majors, class rank, and a variety of demographic factors to allow for diverse representation of students within the College. Advisory Board Committees Communication and Social Media Committee Work with College media relations staff on story ideas Social media take over once/semester Serve as “roving reporters” to take photos and create bullet points to be used for copy at events, presentation or during class activities Provide feedback to media staff on content of social media feeds Development and Fundraising Committee Assist with donor visits on campus Participate in week of gratitude activities Assist with phone-a-thon as needed Meet with interns to gain greater understanding of development goals of College Serve as student hosts as needed in President’s Box (football), etc. Diversity and Inclusion Committee Provide support for the College’s Strategic Plan for Inclusive Excellence Provide student representative on College Diversity and Community Committee Provide assistance at the Men of Color Summit Support recruitment efforts of diverse populations Professional Development Committee Conduct

House BUY PERMIT PAY CITATION APPEAL CITATION STUDENTS EMPLOYEES VISITORS SPECIAL EVENTS Home / Commute Green / EV Charging EV Charging EV Charging Station Locations 6 EV Charging Stations are located on the 1st floor of the Floyd Street Garage. Charging Station Types: 2- GE EVWSWBC LEVEL 2 EV CHARGER GE WATTSTATION & 4- GE EVWRN3 LEVEL 2 EV Charging Station Access to charging stations is limited to University permit holders between 7:30am-4:00pm (Monday-Friday). Stations are open to the public (hourly parking rates apply) between 4:00pm-7:30am (Monday-Friday) and on weekends. Regulations and Policies Parking at a recharging station will be limited to four (4) hours. This time limit assists in creating turnover for the spaces in order to maximize their availability and use by EV vehicle owners. A Warning citation will be issued to users that violate the policy. Parking for vehicles that cannot be properly coupled to the EV charger plug and charged is strictly prohibited (e.g., gas powered vehicles, standard hybrid vehicles such as the Prius, electric GEM vehicles, etc.).

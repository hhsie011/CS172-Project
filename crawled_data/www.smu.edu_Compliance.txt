Skip to main content SMU SMU Ignited Menu Search SMU Compliance Compliance Compliance Southern Methodist University (SMU) is committed to the highest standards of compliance, ethics, and conduct. All individuals affiliated with SMU are required to adhere to policies, procedures, laws, rules, and regulations as determined by University, legal, or government entities. SMU’s Compliance Grid is a community resource that lists the University contacts for specific compliance areas. The Compliance Grid is intended for general guidance and identifies the accountable senior officers, departments, individuals, partners, laws, regulations, and requirements of each compliance area within the University. The attached Compliance Grid was last updated on September 22, 2021. Updates to the Compliance Grid should be directed to vpbf@smu.edu.

Health Promotion Counseling and Psychological Services Disability Services Gay Health Advocacy Project (GHAP) Insurance Office Medical Services Sexual Violence Response Columbia Health You are here: Home International Students Workshop Events Past Event International Students Workshop Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo February 15, 2019 - March 1, 2019 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM Lerner Hall, 2920 Broadway, New York, NY 10027 8th Floor, CPS Conference Room Show Map Are you an international student new to Columbia University and America? It takes time and effort to feel at home and get the most out of life at Columbia. We offer two workshops to address your experience at Columbia. We will discuss: • Academics (on 2/15) - How to maximize your experience in class and with professors. How and when to get to know professors, expectations, participating in class, collaborating with other students, asking for help and more. • Social Life (on 3/1) - How to balance new friends and romantic relationships. How to navigate forming new relationships when living in a language and a culture that are not your own. Getting comfortable. Learning new social norms. Managing expectations. Feeling lonely.

& Inclusion Community & Engagement Office of the President Strategic Direction Give to Butler Mascot Blue IV Careers Maps & Directions Located on more than 300 acres on the north side of Indianapolis, Butler is home to both historic buildings and park-like settings. Whether you’re here for an admission visit, a performing arts or athletic event, or returning as an alum, we hope you enjoy your time exploring our beautiful campus. Campus Address 4600 Sunset Avenue Indianapolis, IN 46208 Campus Map Parking Directions to Campus Hotel Partners Admission Visitors Traveling to campus for an Admission visit? We can’t wait to see you! Find the appropriate set of directions to campus below, depending on your hometown. Most Admission visits begin in Robertson Hall, the only red brick building on campus with a steeple. Follow the campus signage directing you to the Office of Admission. Admission visitor parking is available in the Sunset Avenue Parking Garage or in front of Robertson Hall. A printable map is also available. North Split Closure The Indiana Department of Transportation will close the North Split interchange of I-65/70 the weekend of May 14, 2021. This project is expected to last 18 months and results in the significant rerouting of traffic for Indianapolis commuters and those visiting Butler’s campus. Please consult GPS apps such as Waze or Google Maps to determine the best route for your visit to Butler. Heavy Truck Traffic Heavy truck operators seeking access to Clowes Memorial Hall and the Schrott Center for the Arts and other Butler facilities must contact the Butler University Police Department (BUPD) at 317-940-9396 prior to arrival for assistance in navigating intersections and construction zones. BUPD must temporarily block traffic in order to ensure safe navigation to campus by heavy truck traffic. Access Boulevard Place, northbound, from 38th Street Turn left (westbound) on 49th Street Continue onto Sunset Ave. (south) and turn right into the parking lot entrance just north of 46th Street and north of the Clowes and Schrott facilities. In order to ensure safe navigation, heavy trucks must travel south in the northbound lane of Sunset Ave. Trucks must contact BUPD 317-940-9396 to arrange for a temporary lane closure. Loading docks are accessible via the lot immediately behind the Clowes and Schrott facilities.

Maguire Center for Ethics and Public Responsibility Resources Research Research We have collected some useful online resources for anyone who wants to do some background reading before diving deeply into a paper topic or research issue. IIT's Center for the Study of Ethics in the Professions' Resource Guides (engineering, business, marketing, academic, biomedical, and scientific ethics) Sharon Stoerger's Business Ethics website (collection of cases, codes, and on-line resources). Stoerger maintains similar pages on other topics, though most appear not to have been updated since 2006 or earlier, e.g.: anthropology, art, bioethics, history, journalism, research, social science, and sociology.

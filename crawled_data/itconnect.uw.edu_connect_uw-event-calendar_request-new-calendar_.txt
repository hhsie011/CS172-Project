Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Request a New Calendar Trumba Overview Video Learn how Trumba Campus Event Calendars work at the UW by watching our Trumba Overview video. How do I request a new calendar? Contact help@uw.edu. For a quicker response, enter “UW Event Calendar” in the subject line. In your message, include your UW NetID and provide answers to the following questions: What is your organizational unit (college, school, department, or center)? If possible, please give the name of the parent organization as well. What is the URL of the web site for your organizational unit? Do you plan to show any events on the Campus event calendar or any other calendars? Will your calendar events occur in multiple time zones? Do you want to allow the public to submit events to your calendar? The Calendar team will be in touch about next steps.

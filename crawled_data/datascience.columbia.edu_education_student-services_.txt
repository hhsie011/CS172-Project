Columbia Whether you are looking for an internship, job, networking in a new field, or brushing up on technical skills, we are committed to providing the necessary support to ensure your success. One-on-One Support Career counseling sessions are focused on job search coaching, resume and cover letter preparation, mock interview sessions, and salary negotiation. Data Science-Specific Career Events We host employer panels, career fairs, internship fairs, employer information sessions, hackathons, site visits, and alumni networking sessions. Data Science Opportunities Listserv Columbia data science students receive an average of 30+ jobs or internships per month directly in their inboxes. Research Opportunities Faculty throughout the University and Medical Center rely on DSI student talent for support with their research projects. Our Campus Connections program has connected students to 70+ projects in the last year. SEAS and CCE Events Access to SEAS Graduate Student Affairs’ professional development programming and Columbia’s Center for Career Education (M.S. only).

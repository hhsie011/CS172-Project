Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 12-credit-hour certificate comprises the following courses: Computer Networks Mathematical Foundations for Computer Networking Wireless Networking Six hours of electives chosen from: Routing and Switching Foundations of Network Security Multi-service over IP View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

It’s all of those things but, of course, much more. At its heart, it’s more than 26,000 students and over 200,000 living alumni who can all point to a collection of transformative Nebraska experiences that enrich the state, nation and world. You may be one of those people. You may be someone who has seen the positive effects of Nebraska in those around you. And we hope you’re visiting this website because you want to help us create new opportunities for the students of today and tomorrow. Measure your life by the difference you’ve made in someone else’s life. The University of Nebraska Foundation is available to help connect your passions to the university causes that mean most to you. We invite you to visit the foundation online to learn about opportunities to support Nebraska now, or you may request to visit with a UNL development professional for information about establishing your legacy on campus. Thank you!

Columbia has a number of bicycle enclosures and bike rooms that provide an added level of security and weather protection for your bicycle. A number of the bike enclosures house tire pumps and basic repair tools for your convenience. Some of the bike rooms and enclosures require bicycle registration by Columbia’s Public Safety office. The bicycle rooms and enclosures are not for long-term bike storage and are reserved for day-to-day use by bicycle commuters. See where bike parking rooms and enclosures are located.

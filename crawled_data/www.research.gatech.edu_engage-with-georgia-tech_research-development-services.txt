GT Community Inquiries Contact: Office of EVPR Team All Other Inquiries Contact: Georgia Parmelee, Director of Research Communications Breadcrumb Home Research & Development Services Research & Development Services Georgia Tech conducts research for federal government agencies, companies, private organizations, the state of Georgia and other organizations. Among top federal research sponsors are the Department of Defense agencies, the National Science Foundation, the National Institutes of Health, the Department of Energy, NASA, the Department of Commerce and the Department of Health and Human Services. Both basic and applied research are conducted by experts throughout Institute, in in our six colleges serving engineering, the sciences, computing, design, the liberal arts and business, in our 10 interdisciplinary research institutes, and within the Georgia Tech Research Institute (GTRI), our applied research organization. Our world-class researchers are eager to put their expertise to work for you. The way we see it, your challenges fuel our creativity. By focusing our energies on working together, transform discoveries into the innovations to advance your organization. If you’re part of a company wishing to explore opportunities to forge a broad-based corporate or relationship with Georgia Tech, our Corporate Engagement team stands ready to help develop an engagement model tailored to your objectives.

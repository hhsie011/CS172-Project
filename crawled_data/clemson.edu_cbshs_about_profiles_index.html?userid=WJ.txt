Johnson-Stokes Director of Student Media Office: Hendrix 302C Phone: 864-656-8933 Email: WJ@clemson.edu Educational Background BA Speech/Drama Charleston Southern University 1983 Courses Taught Broadcast News Productions Public Speaking Communication Law Profile Wanda Johnson is the director of student media. Previous positions held at Clemson aredirector of communications for Inclusion & Equity; strategic communications for University Relations; and, director of broadcasting for Public Affairs. Prior to joining the Clemson family, Wanda worked as the public information manager for the City of Greenville, South Carolina where she also oversaw the city's public access channel. Wanda spent more than 20 years working in television as an executive news producer, interim news director, news producer, reporter, and public affairs manager.

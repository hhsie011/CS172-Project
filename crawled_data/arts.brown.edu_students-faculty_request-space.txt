Space requests will open June 1 for the following academic year. The Granoff Center offers dynamic opportunities for a wide range of events—from performances, installations and experimental art forms to conferences, workshops and panel discussions. Spaces in the Granoff Center are available to Brown faculty, students and staff members. Priority is given to requests that align with the BAI's mission for work that reflects the experimental, collaborative and engaged and/or require the unique resources at Granoff Center. Space requests are reviewed on a rolling basis. Please note, space requests must be submitted at least four weeks in advance of the proposed event.

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index School of Law Menu Search TTU School of Law Admissions Recruiting Events and Virtual Resources At Texas Tech Law, we are proud to welcome students from all over Texas and throughout the United States. To build our diverse community, we attend in-person events across the country and participate in virtual events year-round. If you are interested in earning your law degree from our Best Value law school, we want to meet you! We are excited to announce the following list of in-person and virtual recruiting events for the entering class of fall 2022. We will also continue providing recordings with information about the application process, tips for creating competitive applications, and information sessions featuring Texas Tech Law students and faculty. Events will be added below as we schedule them, so be sure to check back for updates. If you would like to invite a Texas Tech Law admissions representative to visit your campus or schedule a virtual meeting, please email us at admissions.law@ttu.edu. Use this link to schedule an in-person tour. For a one-on-one virtual meeting with an admissions recruiter, use this link to schedule Zoom call.

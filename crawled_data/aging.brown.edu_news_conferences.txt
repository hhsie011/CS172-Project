The Brown Center on the Biology of Aging was well-represented at the Mechanisms of Aging meeting at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratories from Sept 26-30, 2016. The meeting centered on the exploration of the function and regulation of genes and processes that are known or suspected to control cellular and/or organismic aging. Several members presented their research: Marco De Cecco (poster), Takahiro Ito (selected talk), Brian Jones (selected talk), Sun Kim (poster), Jill Kreiling (poster), Andrew Leith (poster), Louis Lapierre (selected talk), Nicola Neretti (selected talk), Anna Petrashen (poster), Marc Tatar (poster), Ashley Webb (selected talk), and Jason Wood (poster).

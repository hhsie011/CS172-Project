By Kelly Moffitt May 10, 2022 The Forum was pleased to welcome Council Member Shaun Abreu and Congressman Adriano Espaillat for a Mother's Day celebration honoring District 7 moms. On May 6, 2022 Council Member Shaun Abreu (CC'14) and Congressman Adriano Espaillat hosted a Mother's Day celebration honoring District 7 moms at The Forum on the Manhattanville campus. District 7 covers a series of neighborhoods in upper Manhattan, including Morningside Heights, Manhattanville, Hamilton Heights, and parts of Washington Heights and the Upper West Side. Located on the corner of 125th Street and Broadway, The Forum is a unique gathering place that is open to the local New York City community, with a state-of-the-art auditorium, meeting and event spaces, and communal work areas. The Atrium, where the Mothers Day event took place, recently reopened to the public in March, after being closed during the pandemic.

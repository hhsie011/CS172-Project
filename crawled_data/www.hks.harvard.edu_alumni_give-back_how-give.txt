Magazine Contact Us Search Search How to Give Give Now In This Section Alumni Give Back How to Give In This Section You are here Home Alumni Give Back How to Give There are many ways to contribute to the success of our students and the ongoing excellence of our programs. We thank you for your donation. Online Make a credit card gift now through our secure online giving form. Mail Enclose a check payable to: “President and Fellows of Harvard College” (the legal name of Harvard University). In the check's memo section, please allocate your gift to the Harvard Kennedy School Fund. Checks should be mailed to: Harvard Kennedy School PO Box 419209 Boston, MA 02241-9209 Phone Call 617-495-1750 to give by phone. Gifts of Stock You may make a gift of stock through our online form or by contacting the Alumni and Development Services-Trust Administration: Alumni and Development Services 124 Mount Auburn Street Cambridge, MA 02138 Attn: Trust Administration Toll-free: 866-845-6596 Fax number: 866-457-6598 Email Donor Advised Funds If you have an account with Fidelity Charitable, Schwab Charitable, or BNY Mellon, you can use the DAF link at the very bottom of this page. You will be taken directly to your donor-advised fund provider's website to complete your gift. If your provider is not listed, contact them directly with a request to make a gift to Harvard Kennedy School on your behalf. Payment should be made to “President and Fellows of Harvard College” (the legal name of Harvard University) and include a note indicating you would like your gift allocated to the Harvard Kennedy School Fund, along with your full name, address, and phone number. If paying by check, the check should be mailed to: Harvard Kennedy School PO Box 419209 Boston, MA 02241-9209 Harvard's tax identification number is 04-2103580 Bank Wire If paying by bank wire, transmit to: Bank Name: Bank of America Bank Address: 100 Federal Street, Boston, MA 02110 Beneficiary Legal Account Name: President and Fellows of Harvard College, RSO account Account #: 009429263621 ACH ABA: 011000138 Wire ABA: 026009593 Swift Code: BOFAUS3N Mutual Funds or Other Securities To transfer mutual funds, foreign stocks, physical certificates, or any other gifts that may require special instructions, please contact the Alumni and Development Services-Trust Administration at 866-845-6596 or donorsecuritygifts@harvard.edu. Matching Gifts Find out if your employer will match your gift using our Matching Gift Program Search.

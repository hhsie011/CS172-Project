Where We Are Just because you find yourself in New York City doesn't mean you're away from Syracuse University. Thanks to the generosity of members of the Orange family, the Joseph I. Lubin House and Fisher Center proudly represent Syracuse University in New York City. With Syracuse University's commitment to the New York City area, it's not uncommon to see visiting professors or staff in the area! Both Lubin House and Fisher Center provide office spaces and rooms for faculty and staff to conduct business or host events while in the area.

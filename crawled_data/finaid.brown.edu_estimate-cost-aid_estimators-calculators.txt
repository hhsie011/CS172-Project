MyinTuition Quick Cost Estimator This estimator will give you an early cost estimate using basic financial questions. For U.S. Citizens, permanent residents, DACA students, and undocumented students. Brown’s Net Price Calculator This calculator requires more detailed financial information and provides a more narrow estimate of cost. For U.S. Citizens, permanent residents, DACA students, and undocumented students. Loan Simulator Loan Simulator is a new tool to help you make decisions about your student loans. Use it to find a repayment plan that meets your needs and goals or to decide whether to consolidate. Estimating Your Bill The Brown University Bill Estimator provides an estimate of the annual billed charges for first year students.

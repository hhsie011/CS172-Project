Home / About Us / For Faculty For Faculty Newcomb-Tulane College Summer School will offer both online and in-person courses for Summer 2022 To submit a proposal to teach a summer course, please submit the NTC Summer School Course Proposal. NTC Summer School is offering three sessions: Two 2-week sessions, and one 5-week session. The University will be closed on Monday, May 30th in observance of the Memorial Day holiday and Monday, July 4th in observance of Independence Day. Some students use summer school to catch up or get ahead and may need to take two classes during the Early or Late Summer sessions. To accommodate this schedule please use the following class meeting times. Please note that virtual classes will still be expected to abide by the below class meeting times. 9:00 - 10:30 11:00 - 12:30 1:00 -2:30 (no lab allowed) 3:00 - 4:30 (no lab allowed) Summer Course Proposals Please submit class offerings according to the Registrar's Office timeline and continue to use CourseLeaf CLSS. The process of approval is as follows: Faculty submits course Frist approval: Department Chair Second approval: School Dean Third approval: NTC Summer School Before issuing approval, the NTC Summer School staff will take the following into consideration: Past enrollment numbers Waitlist numbers for previous fall and spring semesters Cost analysis of instruction Other relevant information Instructional contracts for summer teaching are not guaranteed and they are contingent upon a course enrollment minimum of 8 officially enrolled students. A course must have a minimum of 8 students for the instructor to receive their full summer salary. The student minimum will be enforced on a course-by-course basis and not calculated on a departmental average. Graders and Lab Assistants Graders and Lab Assistants are not to be listed on the Course Proposal Form, and only classes with 20 or more students enrolled will be eligible to have graders. Courses will have the opportunity to request a Grader(s) or Lab Assistant(s) after they have been approved. If you would like to request a grader or lab assistant, please submit the below form by April 5th, 2021. Request for Student Assistance If you have any questions about your NTC Summer School course, please contact us at summerschool@tulane.edu or kruckstuhl@tulane.edu.

Pastoral Care at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital The Pauline A. Hartford Memorial Chapel at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital is non-denominational and open 24/7. Inside the chapel is a Muslim prayer room located on the right-hand side of the altar. The chapel is accessible to faculty, staff, and students through the hospital's garden on Fort Washington Avenue and down the stairwell on the right. The Washington Heights community may access the chapel at the entrance on 622 West 168th Street. For more information, visit: Pastoral Care and Education Department Staff by Site Chapel Services Information Pastoral Care at Columbia University University Chaplain Jewelnel Davis collaborates with schools across Columbia University to promote interfaith and intercultural awareness. Faculty, staff, and students may access pastoral care services through her office based at the Morningside campus. For more information, visit: University Chaplain Religious Life Advisers Weekly Meetings and Services CUIMC Prayer Space - Hammer Health Science Building Room LL2-221 For 24/7 access, students may submit a request to cumc-IDoffice@columbia.edu.

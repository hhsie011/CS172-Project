Faculty and Staff Parents and Families Peers and Students Emergency Contacts Faculty and Staff Resources for Faculty and Staff Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) - Confidential resource to consult regarding student situations Student Support Services (SSS) - Nonconfidential resource to consult regarding student situations Faculty/Staff Assistance Program - Counseling options for faculty and staff Sheridan Center For Teaching and Learning - Inclusive teaching resources and long-term professional development on supporting students in the classroom How to Take Care of Yourself Dealing with students in distress can be a stressful and taxing experience. Be sure to take care of yourself, too. Seek support from colleagues and supervisors. It may also be helpful to talk with a counselor. Counseling services are available free of charge for faculty and staff members through the Faculty/Staff Assistance Program (FSAP). If you're interested in counseling options for yourself or a colleague, please contact the Faculty/Staff Assistance Program (FSAP). How Can I Help?

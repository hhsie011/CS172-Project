With innovative, student-centered learning experiences, our professional education programs are designed to break down silos and integrate a diversity of perspectives to bring new ideas forward. Our admission process challenges us to discover how each applicant would contribute to — and benefit from — Brown's diverse and collaborative culture. Taking a holistic look at each application, we will consider your unique talents, accomplishments, energy, curiosity, perspective and identity. If you are drawn to our program, we strongly encourage you to apply. We look forward to getting to know you.

AFTL Members, Eligibility & Benefits Journal for Research & Practice in College Teaching Faculty Enrichment Center Programming and Resources Faculty Enrichment Center About Developed as part of the University of Cincinnati’s (UC) strategic direction, Next Lives Here, the Faculty Enrichment Center (FEC) is one of the only centers of its kind in the nation. We are an Intellectual and social commons for faculty. Distinctly different from a traditional teaching and learning center, we strive to drive professional development and career advancement, foster interdisciplinary collaborations, promote a culture of inclusion and enhance social and personal well-being of faculty at UC. Our Vision and Mission Vision The FEC provides support and advocacy to develop and retain a diverse faculty who feel professionally and personally enriched and satisfied, and who contribute innovatively to the mission of the university throughout their careers. Mission Engage and empower faculty for professional growth, personal well-being, and professional success in innovative ways throughout their careers. Tech and Space To meet the needs of our evolving faculty, the FEC provides a technologically enhanced space that operates seamlessly with the university’s systems and tools. Our space and technology offerings include:  4K high-definition conference room with fast and reliable teleconferencing Large conference rooms equipped with computers, 55+ inch monitors, and web-conferencing/video recording capabilities Small conference and project rooms for meetings, collaborations, research work, and more Two lounge areas, a kitchenette, fresh coffee, and daily lockers Meet the FEC Team Rita Kumar, PhD Executive Director, Faculty Enrichment Center Langsam Library 540 rita.kumar@uc.edu Keisha Love, PhD Vice Provost for Faculty Advancement and Academic Inclusion, Office of the Provost, Faculty Enrichment Center Van Wormer Hall lovekm@ucmail.uc.edu Jodie Mader, PhD Manager Special Projects & Programs, Faculty Enrichment Center Langsam Library 540 maderjn@ucmail.uc.edu Leyda Martinez de la Mata Program Coordinator, Faculty Enrichment Center Langsam Library 540 marti3ld@ucmail.uc.edu Aja Bettencourt-McCarthy Manager Special Projects and Programs, Faculty Enrichment Center Langsam Library 540 bettenar@ucmail.uc.edu NOTE TO UC FACULTY AND STAFF: More details about events, programs, and facilities can be found on our Faculty Enrichment Center Bearcats Landing intranet site (UC login required).

College of Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services and Information Technology (CECH), we understand that you are wanting more than just a great education. You are looking for opportunities to gain professional experience and explore the career of your choice. College scholarships do more than simply cover the costs of tuition and fees. They offer support in many areas, freedom of choice and time to focus on studies, and have advantages that will last a lifetime. In CECH, we offer the following scholarship opportunities to students who are seeking financial support. Each non-renewable scholarship is awarded based on available funds and are subject to fulfilling the application requirements and meeting satisfactory academic progress. If you have questions about any CECH scholarship, please contact us via email or call us directly at (513) 556-6308. The following programs are not eligible for the CECH Scholarships: Associate of Applied Science: Early Childhood Care and Education (online) Bachelor of Science: Early Childhood Education | Birth - Age 5 (online) Criminal Justice (online) Cybersecurity (Online) Information Technology (online) Law & Paralegal Studies (online) Substance Abuse Counseling (online) First Responders Scholarship We recognize and appreciate the contribution made by the First Responders of our communities. We are pleased to offer this scholarship as part of our commitment to support the men and women who serve our communities. Eligible students must meet the criteria below, and submit the appropriate undergraduate application for admissions, by the stated deadline in order to be considered.

Undergraduate Speaking/ Listening Sequence ESL1071 Basic Oral Communication ESL1072 Foundations of Academic Oral Communication ESL1073 Success in Academic Oral Communication ESL1074 Oral Presentation Skill ESL1075 Improving Pronunciation Other ESL Courses for Undergraduate Students ESL1011 (1 credit) Introduction to U. S. College Culture: This course introduces essential aspects of the U.S. college culture and is aimed at preparing first year international undergraduate students for a successful transition into an American University. ESL1076 (3 credit) American Culture and Communication: This course focuses on the cultural aspects of verbal and non-verbal behaviors, values, and communication styles. The goal of this course is to familiarize students with their host culture, while developing strategies to succeed in their new academic and social environment. ESL1077 (3 credit) Strategies for Academic Success: This course is aimed at raising and developing ESL students’ pragmatic awareness and competence in academic contexts through awareness raising activities and communicative practice. This course also discusses important aspects of the American college culture. The table below demonstrates the students- English proficiency level and corresponding courses.

If you are a student and would like to request an academic accommodation, please contact the Justin Dart, Jr. Student Accessibility Center at 713-743-5400. Workplace Accommodations for Employees In accordance with the University of Houston System (UHS) Reasonable Workplace Accommodations for Employees with Disabilities Policy, the ADA Coordinator in the Office for Equal Opportunity Services is responsible for coordinating reasonable accommodation request(s) for job applicants, faculty, staff, and student employees with disabilities. EOS works with employees and their supervisor(s) to identify appropriate and reasonable accommodations that enable employees with disabilities to perform the essential functions of their job. To request a workplace accommodation, please contact the ADA Coordinator at 713-743-8835 or via e-mail at eos@uh.edu.

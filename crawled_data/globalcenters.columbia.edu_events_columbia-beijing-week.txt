Event Columbia Beijing Week Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo June 14, 2016 - June 19, 2016 7:30 PM - 6:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing You are cordially invited to “Columbia Beijing Week” from June 14th to June 19th, organized by Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. Events include Guqin performance, film and documentary panels, art exhibition, as well as conversation with Chinese entrepreneurs. Columbia deans, professors, well-known alumni, and local scholars will join the activities respectively.

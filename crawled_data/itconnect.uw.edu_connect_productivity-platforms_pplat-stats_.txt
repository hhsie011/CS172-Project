Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Productivity Platforms Stats Overview Productivity Platforms Stats (aka PPlat Stats) stores information about the use of UW Google and UW Office 365 by UW employees and Shared UW NetIDs. UW employees can submit the form below to request access to PPlat Stats. Access to PPlat Stats is restricted to UW employees only and data should not be shared with anyone other than a UW employee needing it for their job responsibilities. Request access to PPlat Stats Click the link below to submit a request for access to PPlat Stats. Once you submit a request, you will receive an email from the UW-IT ticketing system with a link to PPlat Stats. By submitting this form, you are confirming that you need access to PPlat Stats for activities associated with your job responsibilities as a UW employee. You also agree that you will not use the data from PPlat Stats for activities that are not part of your job responsibilities as a UW employee, and that you will not share this data outside of the UW. Please note that access may take up to 4 hours to process. If you are unable to access PPlat Stats after submitting a request for access, wait 4 hours then try again.

Hall is named after L. J. Hart and was constructed in 1931. Mr. Hart served as Vice President of the Texas A&M Board of Directors from 1917 to 1919. Thanks to his able service, he was elected President of the Board and served from 1921 to 1925. 2021 – 2022 Rates Ramp Hall $2,100semester Double Room $3,163semester Single Room (Single rooms are extremely limited.) Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Suite Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same multi-room suite are the same gender. Only residents of the same gender share a restroom. Close Gallery Get a feel for all that Hart Hall has to offer by scrolling through the photo gallery. If you prefer, check it for yourself and join us for a tour.

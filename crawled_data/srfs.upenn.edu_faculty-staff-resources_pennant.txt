Faculty & Staff Resources Pennant Pennant is Penn’s suite of next-generation student information systems. The Pennant suite includes several components in the areas of Accounts, Financial Aid, and Records. The launch of the Pennant suite of applications provides students with an upgraded user interface and enhanced search capabilities. Faculty and advisors gain increased collaboration tools, improved visibility and reporting tools, and a more intuitive system for degree auditing. The project is moving all three areas of student services away from green screens to a more modern interface and improving workflows which reduces the number of manual steps needed to complete processes.

Communication Studies requires 42 units in the major, including 21 units of core requirements, 9 units of breadth experiences and 12 elective units not taken for breadth or core requirements, including at least one 400-level course that is not an internship. Core Requirements (21 units) HCOM 102 - Public Speaking (3) HCOM 235 - Essentials of Argumentation (3) HCOM 300 - Introduction to Research in Speech Communication (3) HCOM 304 - Introduction to Communication Theory (3) HCOM 307 - Introduction to Communication and Critical/Cultural Studies (3) HCOM 308 - Quantitative Research Methods (3) HCOM 420 - Communication Theory (3) Breadth Experience (9 units) Select three courses, each from a different area. HCOM 305 may be substituted in any area.

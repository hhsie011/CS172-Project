With a robust set of features, and a modern design, MyCBS serves as a one-stop-shop for alumni who want to stay involved with their community, further their career, or simply connect with an old classmate. MyCBS provies alumni with access to: A searchable directory of CBS alumni Links to job postings Invitations to events in your area News and updates from the School Discussion forums A live feed to post information that may be of interest to the CBS community A customizable email digest with news and information from regional clubs, affinity groups, centers, and programs Log in to MyCBS Class Notes Discover what your classmates are sharing and tell your own story by visiting class notes.

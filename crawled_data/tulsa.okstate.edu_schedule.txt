Information OSU-Tulsa's class schedules are available online through the Student Self Service system. Watch the video above for a quick tutorial on how to use the system. For information on enrollment dates and policies, visit Enrollment Services. Undergraduate students must meet with an advisor prior to enrolling. Students should make advising appointments as early as possible to avoid long lines during walk-in advising hours. To schedule an appointment, visit the Advisement Services website for information. Graduate students can visit Graduate Student Services.

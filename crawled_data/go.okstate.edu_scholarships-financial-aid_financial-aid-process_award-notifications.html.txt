Awards are made on an on-going basis throughout the year so we encourage you to check your portal often. Your award represents the best offer of assistance based on the FAFSA and the cost to attend OSU. Awards cannot be negotiated and we do not match offers from other schools. If you or your parents have experienced a change of circumstances (divorce, death, job loss, etc) please contact our office. How will I be notified? You will receive an award notice by email to your official OSU email address. If you are a new undergraduate student: your email will give you a comprehensive list of your financial aid awards. you will also receive an award notification by mail. We recommend you review your awards on your OSU Self Service portal at my.okstate.edu.

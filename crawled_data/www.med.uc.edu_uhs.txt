We are an outpatient facility offering comprehensive clinical services to the student population. All students enrolled at the university are eligible to use UHS. The board-certified physicians, nurse practitioners, counselors and professional staff at UHS provide the highest quality care. Our services include primary care, gender and sexual health, mental health, dermatology, immunizations and screenings, and travel health. Students must make an appointment to be treated at UHS. We do not take walk-ins, so please call to schedule an appointment in advance. Same day appointments are offered based on availability. We are open for in-person visits for both well and sick, as well as telehealth visits with a provider.

Health Studies The Graduate School Student Affairs News Home For the Media AAA For the Media The Office of Media Relations is available to respond to inquiries from the news media, including student reporters, and can also help put you in direct contact with a wide variety of experts in numerous specialties. Please call 305-284-5500 on weekdays, 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m., Eastern Time. On weekends or after hours, news media on deadline may contact the office by calling 305-546-9110 or 727-831-1131. SUBMIT MEDIA REQUEST SEARCH FACULTY EXPERTS Media with inquiries for the Miller School of Medicine, including requests for medical experts, should reach out to the Office of Communications at the Miller School of Medicine, 305-243-3249. For information on UM Athletics, contact Athletics Communications at 305-284-3822. As the University of Miami is a private university, arrangements must be made in advance to take photographs or shoot video footage. A member of the Media Relations staff must accompany reporters and cameras on campus.

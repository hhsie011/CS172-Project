Welcome, Orange family! The Syracuse University Alumni Club of Atlanta is one place in the region where Orange spirit really rings strong. The Alumni Club Board members thank all Syracuse University alumni and friends in the Atlanta area for your continued support. We’re committed to building a stronger Syracuse University community in the Southeast and will continue to represent and support the University’s initiatives. We encourage you to stay connected with Syracuse University and the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Atlanta by following us via social media and checking this website to keep up to date with the myriad amount of social, academic, athletic, and overall fun events taking place throughout the year. We always welcome any questions, comments, or ideas to explore new initiatives! Don’t hesitate to reach out to us on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, or email us at cuseinatl@gmail.com. On behalf of our entire board, we look forward to meeting you or seeing you soon! Go Orange! Allison D.

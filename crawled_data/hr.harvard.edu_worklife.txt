Harvard’s Employee Assistance Program (EAP) can provide information on day care options, including tailoring a list of providers according to your desired location, preferred type of care, etc. Current faculty and staff members may find EAP contact information on HARVie. In addition, there are six child care centers on Harvard’s campuses, serving children from age three months to five years. Harvard also provides child care scholarships, which can be used toward the costs for in-home, family or group child care; scholarships are awarded via an application process. You can learn more about our resources for finding child care here.

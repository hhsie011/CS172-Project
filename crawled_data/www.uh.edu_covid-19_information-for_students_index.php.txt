Students Students The University transitioned to traditional face-to-face classes in fall 2021 along with the regular complement of hybrid and online classes historically offered during a fall semester. Our core mission is to transform lives, and we do so by providing an immersive learning experience to students. In consultation with medical experts, UH continues to monitor current conditions.

The CCE administrative office is located in Roberts Hall on the Cornell University Campus in Ithaca, NY. We can be reached between 8:30 a.m. and 4:30 p.m. at 607-255-2237 or by email at cce-contact [at] cornell.edu. For localized information, please contact your local CCE office. CCE Leadership Director, Cornell Cooperative Extension  Christopher Watkins As Cornell Cooperative Extension director, Chris Watkins oversees the diverse portfolio of CCE programs reaching nearly 2 million NYS residents. Previously a CCE Associate Director, Watkins also is Herman M. Cohn Professor of Postharvest Science in the Department of Horticulture. Watkins joined the Cornell faculty in 1994 as an associate professor of horticulture after more than a decade as a scientist in the Postharvest Science Group of The Horticulture and Food Research Institute in his native New Zealand.

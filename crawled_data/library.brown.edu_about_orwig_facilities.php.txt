The collection supports the curriculum of the Music Department and provides material for general use as well. The Orwig Music Library includes a reference/information center with computer access to the internet and two spacious reading rooms which provide seating for 80 users. The Music Library has six stations equipped with video and audio playback equipment. The Library also has a piano room on the lower level which contains a fine Steinway upright.

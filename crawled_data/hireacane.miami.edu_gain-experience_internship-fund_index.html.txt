Through the generosity of members of the University of Miami Parents Council, we are able to offer competitive awards of $500 up to $3000 for undergraduate students who accept unpaid and paid internship opportunities. Eligibility UM undergraduate students who are currently enrolled full-time Applicants cannot be under any judicial sanctions Applicants must not be graduating in Spring 2022 Internship Qualifications Internship must be during Summer 2022 Internship can be either paid or unpaid Minimal stipend for housing, transportation, and/or meals from your employer does not qualify as payment Internship must be supervised by a full-time employee of the organization Unpaid internships that are required as part of a program curriculum are not eligible Organization may not be owned/run by a member of the studentâ€™s immediate family Application Timeline Application Deadline: April 29, 2022 Award Decisions made by: May 13, 2022 Application & Supplemental Documents The Toppel Internship Fund Application will collect your contact information and will require you to provide the following: Specific internship information, including organization and supervisor Answers to essay questions Budget Expenses Updated Resume (PDF Format) Internship Offer Letter An Internship Verification Form will be sent to your supervisor upon submission of your application to confirm that the position meets internship qualifications. Selection Process A committee will review each application and select the award recipients Each application will be considered on a case-by-case basis and evaluated against the application and supplemental documents received If selected for an award, you must register for the Toppel Internship Program All correspondence regarding your application will be sent to your UM email account You may be disqualified if: Application is submitted past the deadline Application is incomplete There are judicial violations that occur after the funding has been awarded The internship does not meet the qualifications described above Submit your application by April 29, 2022 For questions, contact us at toppel-internships@miami.edu or 305-284-5451.

All student clubs and organizations must be registered with updated information on CL in order to be recognized within the university as a student group. A new registration period will be open to all student organizations at the start of each academic year where students will be required to update their organization profiles. All club communication will be through Tiger Zone so ensure that you are checking the events, announcements, and opportunities posted daily! Tiger Zone > What can you do with TigerZone?

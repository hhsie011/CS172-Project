Maps Interactive campus map Look up buildings Find parking lots View accessibility features Parking Parking division Parking lots Visitor parking CyRide The CyRide bus system serves the Iowa State campus and city of Ames. ISU students and children 5 and under ride free. Regular fare is $1.25, with reduced fare (60 cents) for seniors, K-12 students and those with disabilities. SafeRide ISU Students, visitors and others concerned about walking on campus (6 p.m. to 6 a.m.) can summon rides by calling 294-4444. A ride-requesting app is available for students and staff. Help Van Need a jump-start? Tools to change a flat? A ride to a service station for a can of gas? Call the Help Van at 294-4444.

The SC Johnson College of Business brings together the excellence and breadth of Cornell’s leading undergraduate, graduate, and professional business programs to create a collaborative and comprehensive business management program, while preserving the individual excellence of the three schools. Students gain exposure to a wide array of faculty and learning opportunities across disciplines, through engagement at the Cornell Tech campus and in partnerships with institutions around the world. In an increasingly competitive global landscape, the SC Johnson College of Business establishes a world-class center of teaching and research for business management.

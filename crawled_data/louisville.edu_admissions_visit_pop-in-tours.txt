Pop-in Tours are available Monday, Wednesday and Friday for admitted students. Need one last look at campus before you make your final decision? Want to meet one-on-one to discuss your financial aid offer sheet? Would you like to take a peek at your future home with Campus Housing? 45-minute student-led tours leave promptly on the hours of 10, 1 and 3 EST from the Office of Admissions in the Houchens Building. We recommend arriving at the Office of Admissions 15 minutes before the hour of the scheduled tour. While you're on campus... Meet with an Admissions Counselor at the Office of Admissions (no appointment necessary) Receive one-on-one financial aid advising in the Office of Financial Aid (no appointment necessary) Check out Campus Housing (please request a housing tour in advance of your visit) Show your Cardinal Pride with 20% off one item from the Campus Store Convenient parking is available on the visitor side of the Floyd Street Parking Garage at 2126 S. Floyd Street. Proceed to the Houchens Building and check in with the Office of Admissions to start your visit! View directions and parking information. Questions?

Nim Kidd serves as the Chief of the Texas Division of Emergency Management and is the Vice Chancellor for Disaster and Emergency Services. In this capacity, he is responsible for the state’s emergency preparedness, response, recovery and mitigation activities. He is a Certified Emergency Manager, as well as a Texas Master Firefighter and holds Hazardous Materials and Emergency Medical Technician certifications.

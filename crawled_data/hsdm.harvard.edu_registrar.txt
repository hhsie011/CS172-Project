For assistance, please email registrar_services@hsdm.harvard.edu The Registrar serves as custodian for the official academic records of all students, from matriculation through degree conferral. The office maintains the HSDM academic calendar, provides information about courses, registers students for classes, and administers the academic policies of the School. Located within the Office of Dental Education, we also maintain contact information for students, provide academic transcripts, verify enrollment status and degree conferral, and process requests for educational loan deferments. In addition, we monitor compliance with degree requirements, bill student tuition and provide appropriate data to facilitate the educational programs of the School and the reporting requirements of both the School and the University.

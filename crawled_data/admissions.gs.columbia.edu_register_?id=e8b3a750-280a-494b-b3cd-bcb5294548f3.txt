About GS The School of General Studies (GS) is the undergraduate college at Columbia University specifically dedicated to students taking a nontraditional path to a traditional Ivy League education. GS students include persons who have interrupted their educations since high school for at least one academic year, persons earning a second bachelor's degree, and individuals who have compelling personal or professional reasons to attend college on a part-time basis As a reminder, all visitors to the Columbia University campus are required to be fully vaccinated and to refrain from entering campus if they have exhibited symptoms of COVID-19 within the previous 24 hours.

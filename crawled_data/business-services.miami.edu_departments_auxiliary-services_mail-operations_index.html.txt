Contact Auxiliary Services RELATED LINKS United States Postal Service USPS Postal Kiosk USPS Postal Kiosk offers services equivalent to that of a U.S. Post Office and is conveniently located in our Campus Store at the Whitten University Center. Mail Operations The University's Mail Operations provides incoming, outgoing and inter-office mail services for University departments at the Coral Gables campus.

College of Behavioral, Social and Health Sciences is committed to a culture of inclusion and equity. This commitment informs our teaching, research, and outreach both within the state of South Carolina and beyond as our students, staff and faculty strive to enhance opportunities for all as we work to build people and communities. Among our efforts in inclusive excellence have been our Inclusive Excellence Lecture Series (see below) and our Inclusive Excellence Learning Plans, which provide training opportunities for faculty and staff.

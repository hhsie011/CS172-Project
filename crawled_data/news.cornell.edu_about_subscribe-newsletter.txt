The new daily news email captures the top headlines and media hits from each day in a quick-read format shared Monday through Thursday around 4 p.m. Subscribers also receive Friday’s weekly highlights newsletter, capturing seven days of headlines and top stories from throughout Cornell. The long-running Chronicle weekly highlights newsletter is distributed Friday mornings and features top stories on research, education and outreach from throughout the week, as well as news from the administration and information about life on campus, upcoming events, athletics news, Cornellians in the media and more. Both the daily and weekly emails are sent to all faculty, staff and students on the Ithaca, New York City and Qatar campuses and are available to all alumni, parents, journalists and other friends of the university who want to follow Cornell’s official news source.

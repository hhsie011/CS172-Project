As society continues to evolve, the need for well-trained, highly-educated criminal justice professionals will be in high demand. Through the University of Cincinnati’s online BS in Criminal Justice, you will have the additional advantage of earning your degree from our School of Criminal Justice, which is consistently ranked among the top 15 Best Criminal Justice Schools by U.S. News & World Report. Is a degree in Criminal Justice right for me? Whether you are seeking an entry-level position or want to advance your current career in law enforcement, loss prevention, special investigations, social advocacy or private security, earning a Bachelor’s Degree in Criminal Justice can help you achieve your goals. Our online baccalaureate program combines professional skills training with relevant curriculum designed to help you develop a solid foundation of criminal justice knowledge. Ultimately, this online program prepares graduates to become leaders in criminal justice settings, policy reform and law enforcement. Students may enroll in the spring, summer or fall semester. It typically takes students four years to complete the degree. Get started with program details Academic Calendar Get start dates, deadlines and more.

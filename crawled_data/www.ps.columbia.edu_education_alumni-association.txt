About Us The VP&S Alumni Association, established in 1859, is an organization of MD graduates dedicated to the past, present, and future of our medical school and the quality of its alumni and student life. About Us Alumni Events VP&S Alumni Association holds a reunion each May, open to all alumni from any year, as well as networking, educational, and social events across the country throughout the year. Alumni Events Alumni Stories VP&S alumni are healing, teaching, and discovering across the globe. Read about them in profiles and interviews produced by the Alumni Office.

Specialized training in City and Regional Planning provides anthropologists with additional skills in planning the physical development of communities, as it intersects with the social, economic, cultural and environmental well-being of communities. This dual degree path can be completed in 6 semesters. The ability to apply certain credits to both degrees makes it easy. See below for credit hour requirements and a sample degree trajectory.

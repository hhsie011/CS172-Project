COVID-19 Vaccine Explore the Campus History Community Commitments Campus Construction and Opportunities Columbia | Neighbors You are here: Home The Columbia University Facilities and Operations High School Summer Internship Program The Columbia University Facilities and Operations High School Summer Internship Program The Columbia University Facilities and Operations (CUFO) High School Summer Internship Program is a structured, six week initiative that provides students with practical work experience before graduation. The program was started in 2011 and is run by the Columbia University Department of Facilities and Operations for high schoolers that live in the 17 local zip code area. Local refers to those students whose primary residence is located within one of the following 17 zip codes: 10025, 10026, 10027, 10029, 10030, 10031, 10032, 10033, 10034, 10035, 10037, 10039, 10040, 10455, 10451, 10454, 10474. This program is part of Columbia's Youth Internships for Local High School Students commitment.

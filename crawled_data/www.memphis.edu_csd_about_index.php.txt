This results in a highly qualified and diverse group of students across the several academic degree programs offered. The Memphis area contains one of the largest medical centers in the country and students are able to have diverse clinical experiences in a variety of settings. Clinical Service Delivery With the assistance of the Memphis Speech and Hearing Center's Board of Directors, service delivery is provided at two locations in the city of Memphis. Since its creation in 1947, an extensive variety of state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment services have been provided to thousands of children and adults in Memphis and the Mid-South region. Individuals, often with severe communication disorders resulting in dramatically restricted educational, vocational, and social opportunities continue to achieve vital and long-lasting help. Research The School has a long history of research productivity in the form of national and international presentations, peer-reviewed professional publications, book chapters, books, and external grants and contracts.

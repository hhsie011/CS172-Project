Financial aid information for parents OSU Parents Association When your student enrolls at Oklahoma State University, you become part of the Cowboy Family, too! We encourage you to join Cowboy Parents, a support organization for families of OSU students. Cowboy Parents sponsors activities both on campus and in home communities, including Family Weekends, Dad's Day events, Mom's Day events, scholarships, newsletters and more.

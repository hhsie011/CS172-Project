Sign Up Log In Search Search Clemson Events › Event Details Better Process Control School The Better Process Control School is a 2-4 day school designed for manufacturers of shelf stable, acidified and low acid foods. FDA regulations in 21 CFR 108, 113, and 114 require that acidified and low acid food manufacturers operate with a certified supervisor on hand at all times during production. This BPCS also meets U.S. Department of Agriculture’s (USDA) Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) regulations 9 CFR 318.300 and 381.300 for shelf stable, thermally processed meat and poultry products. Includes lunches and course materials.

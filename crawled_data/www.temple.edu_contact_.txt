Undergraduate Admissions and Campus Tours Visit the Undergraduate admissions website, call 215-204-7200 / 888-340-2222 or email askanowl@temple.edu. If you are an international student and have questions, email tuadmint@temple.edu or visit the admissions website. Graduate Admissions For general inquiries, visit the Graduate School website or email grad@temple.edu. For specific inquiries about the status of a submitted application, directly contact the department to which the application was submitted. You may access departmental contact information by visiting the Graduate Bulletin and selecting the appropriate program link. Transcripts Temple has authorized Parchment to provide online transcript ordering to our students and alumni. To request a transcript, currently enrolled students should access Parchment through TUportal. Alumni must log into Temple University's Parchment website. The requestor will be prompted to create an account the first time they access the website. Order progress may then be tracked through Parchment and email notifications. For more information, visit the transcript services page on the University Registrar website for more information. Continuing Education For more information about continuing education at Temple, visit the Academic Resource Center website, call 215-204-2500 or email cstudies@temple.edu. Posting Jobs for Temple Students or Alumni To post positions, visit Temple's Career Center website and create an account. Once you have opened the link, click "Employers" and follow the steps to create your account. If you have any problems or need assistance, contact the Career Center directly at 215-204-7981 or careercenter@temple.edu. Media Inquiries If you have a media request, visit the Temple University Communications site, or call 215-204-8561. Safety To contact the university about safety enhancements, please email safety@temple.edu. All Other Inquiries For all other inquiries, contact the Temple University operator at 215-204-7000, or visit Temple's Knowledge Base. (You do not need to log in to ask a question.)

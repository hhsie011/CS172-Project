Building Market Resiliency in Iowa Through Advanced Grain Marketing Courses for Women As farmers work to manage price risk in uncertain times, more Iowa women are taking on the challenge of improving grain and oil seed prices. The farm management team previously taught 22 Women Marketing Grain courses reaching 356 women. “We’ve had several ladies asking for the next step and they want to know more about options,” said Tim Christensen, Iowa State Univeristy Extension and Outreach Farm Management Specialist.

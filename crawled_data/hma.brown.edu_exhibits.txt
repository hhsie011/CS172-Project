The Haffenreffer Museum's public gallery presents a range of exhibitions showcasing the Museum's collections and the work of its staff, Brown University students, and faculty. While exhibits have included a wide variety of topics – including African textiles, Indigenous land sovereignty, cross-cultural brewing traditions, and archaeology of modern migration – they all aim to inspire creative and critical thinking about culture through the material world.

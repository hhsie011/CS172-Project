Fellowships News May 19, 2021 50 GS Students Named as 2020-21 Fellows 50 School of General Studies students were recently named as 2020-21 fellows -- three of these fellowship winners are the first GS students to receive their respective awards. Tags School NewsUndergraduateFellowships April 15, 2021 Columbia GS Junior Jarrell Daniels Named 2021 Truman Scholar Since 1977, this prestigious honor has been awarded in recognition of students’ exemplary public service work and leadership and offers students like Daniels support for graduate or professional school in preparation for a career in public service. Daniels, who enrolled at GS in the fall of 2019, following his own incarceration, has become a prominent advocate for criminal justice reform through his work with the Columbia University Center for Justice, Columbia University School of Social Work, and beyond. Tags School NewsUndergraduateFellowships December 24, 2019 Three GS Alumni Selected as 2021 Schwarzman Scholars Earlier this month, three GS alumni were selected to join the highly-selective Schwarzman Scholars Class of 2021. Only around three percent of this year’s applicants, which include students from 41 countries and 108 universities, were accepted to the graduate fellowship program.

Each year more than 100 staff, students, and volunteers participated in the Ashkelon Excavations (The Leon Levy Expedition), led by Museum Director and Dorot Professor of the Archaeology of Israel Lawrence E. Stager. Below you will find videos taken from the field by Harvard Museum of the Ancient Near East Chief Curator, Dr. Adam Aja. It is one of the finest and best preserved whole vessels from this period that have been found at the site. The krater has bright red and black decoration, a hallmark of the "Philistine Bichrome" class of pottery.

On This Page We offer two 10-12 month online programs that will build your credibility as a highly skilled and well-connected professional. Financing and Deploying Clean Energy This online certificate program — developed by the Yale Center for Business and the Environment — trains and connects rising leaders to catalyze the transition to a clean economy, aiming to accelerate the uptake of clean energy by building capacity in the workforce in the areas of policy, finance, and technology. Financing and Deploying Clean Energy Certificate Website Tropical Forest Landscapes: Conservation, Restoration & Sustainable Use In this yearlong program — developed by the Environmental Leadership Training Initiative — you will learn to design, implement and monitor effective conservation and restoration initiatives. Initiatives can be projects, policies, business plans and programs at any scale.

Visit the College Current: Recruitment Ambassadors Wilbur O. and Ann Powers College of Business Recruitment Ambassadors The Wilbur O. and Ann Powers College of Business Recruitment Ambassadors are a group of student volunteers who have been selected and trained by the college to lead one-on-one information sessions with prospective families and represent the college across all University functions. These students invest their time in the Wilbur O. and Ann Powers College of Business because they want to give back to a program that has given so much to them.

Executive Education Meet your professional goals with flexible, high-impact and innovative non-degree programs for individuals and organizations. 70% of U.S. workers are not engaged at work, and 95% of managers report needing additional training. A rapidly-changing workplace requires new leadership skills to keep your organization and yourself competitive. Programs led by world-class Brown faculty prepare individuals to lead the workplace of the future. Maximize results using leadership skills drawn from neuroscience, psychology and business research Collaborate effectively by cultivating strong relationships up, down, and across Communicate their ideas powerfully and persuasively Programs range from four weeks to six months and provide a mix of online and in-person learning as well as live and asynchronous options. This flexibility ensures you will find a program that fits you and your organization. In addition, we can customize programming to meet your needs.

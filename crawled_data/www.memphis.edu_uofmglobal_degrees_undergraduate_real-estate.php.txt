Call 844-302-3886 Email Us Real Estate The Department of Finance, Insurance and Real Estate (FIR) at the University of Memphis is one of six departments within the Fogelman College of Business & Economics which is the only business college in the Memphis area fully accredited by the prestigious AACSB. The Department of Finance offers the BBA degree with a major in Finance and concentration in Real Estate. The primary objective of this degree program is to provide the basic concepts, principles and analytical tools essential for business decisions in the fields of finance and is designed to provide a broad background for effective functioning within the discipline. The Real Estate Concentration is designed for students who desire careers in real estate and related industries including mortgage banking, banking, real estate development, real estate investment, real estate valuation and assessment, government, as well as real estate sales and property management. The courses in this online program may also be used to obtain a Minor in Real Estate or toward the Professional Real Estate Program . The BBA degree with a concentration in Finance, Insurance and Real Estate requires a minimum of 120 hours to graduate. You may complete this degree entirely online through UofM Global or in combination with previously completed college level learning from other sources.

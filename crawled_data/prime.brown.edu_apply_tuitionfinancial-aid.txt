Breadcrumb Home Apply Sub Navigation In This Section Tuition/Financial Aid FAQs Tuition for PRIME's 2021-2022 Academic Year is $68,072. Students are billed per course each term. PRIME students are required to complete 8 courses. For information regarding academic year costs please visit the Graduate School's Tuition & Fees website Financing your Education Teaching & Research Assistant Roles PRIME students are encouraged to explore work opportunities on the Brown campus. Two large undergraduate classes where many PRIME students TA are ENGN0020 Transforming Society, Technology & Choices for the Future and ENGN0090 Management of Industrial & Non-profit Organizations Financial Aid To supplement the other forms of support that may be received from Brown, students may also be eligible for federal direct student loans and other alternative loans. Student loans for graduate students are administered through the Office of Financial Aid.

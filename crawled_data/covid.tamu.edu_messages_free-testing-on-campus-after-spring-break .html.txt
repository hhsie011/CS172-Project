Tested Positive Exposed Search this site search Free Testing on Campus After Spring Break March 21, 2022, 8:30 a.m. CDT Welcome back, Aggies! We hope that you all enjoyed the Spring Break holiday. As we prepare to finish out the semester, we encourage all students, faculty and staff to continue routine COVID-19 testing. The university continues to provide free testing opportunities across campus. At-home test kits ready for pickup Visit a Protect Aggieland table to pick up a test kit without completing the online form during the following dates and times while supplies last: Weekly, Monday through Friday from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. - Wehner Building outside room 113 Tuesday, March 22 from 2 to 4 p.m. - MSC 12th Man Hall Wednesday, March 23 from 10 a.m. to noon - Evans Library Lobby PCR Tests Multiple locations on campus offer PCR tests. Results may take up to 72 hours. Visit the COVID-19 Testing Sites page to schedule an appointment. Please check the COVID-19 guidance website for updates. Remember to follow the Texas A&M COVID-19 Protocols if you are symptomatic, test positive, or are exposed to someone who has tested positive.

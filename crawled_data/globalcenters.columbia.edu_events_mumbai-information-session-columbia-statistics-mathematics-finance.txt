November 4, 2020 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM Online Are you interested in pursuing graduate studies in data science and analysis? The M.A. Statistics and M.A. Mathematics of Finance programs provide students and professionals the unique opportunity to learn about data analysis, machine learning, programming, and mathematical, quantitative finance and more from world-renowned faculty to accelerate your career in fields such as tech, computing, insurance, banking, capital markets and investments, risk management, healthcare, media, academia and beyond. Join us for an interactive Information Session for the opportunity to: ● Learn more about the various academic offerings from faculty ● Understand the admissions process ● Access resources for professional growth and career development ● Hear from alumni about their experiences and life after graduation ● Get your questions answered by an admissions official in a dedicated Q&A segment Speakers include: ● Professor Demissie Alemayehu, Director of Graduate Studies for the M.A. in Statistics Program ● Professor Lars Tyge Nielsen, Director of the Mathematics of Finance M.A. Program We want to hear from you! Do you have any specific queries? Want to connect with program officials? Are you unable to attend but still want your doubts answered? Please submit your questions in advance here.

Imperative to the success of our mission is our priority to continuously and intentionally foster and evolve an inclusive and intentional environment that prioritizes anti-racist and anti-oppressive practices; reflects the rich diversity and plurality of our community; and truly allows for a sense of belonging in which students can be their full selves. The vision for our work: collaborate, advocate, and support.

The good news is there are ways to cope and learn to heal, even when it seems the grieving process will never end. Coping with Grief and Loss is a webinar designed to help understand loss, and manage accompanying emotions. This webinar is audio only; video will be off for participants. If you or someone you know is finding it difficult to carry out the usual tasks of everyday life, we hope you will join us. Register Now Following this webinar, participants will be better able to: Identify and embrace normal grief reactions Find out when and how to get help managing grief and loss Support loved ones coping with grief and loss Go to Columbia's Well-being website to learn about the EAP and other resources.

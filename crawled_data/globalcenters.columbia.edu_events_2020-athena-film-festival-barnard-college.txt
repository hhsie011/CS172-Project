College Events Past Event 2020 Athena Film Festival at Barnard College Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo February 27, 2020 - March 1, 2020 5:30 PM - 6:00 PM Barnard College, 3009 Broadway, New York, NY 10027 Columbia Global Centers | Beijing is pleased to be partnering with the Athena Film Festival, which starts on February 27 for its tenth anniversary year of celebrating women and leadership! The Athena Film Festival at Barnard College in New York City is a weekend of inspiring films that tell the extraordinary stories of fierce and fearless women leaders. Over the last ten years, the festival has welcomed more than 35,000 people from all over the world to 200+ screenings of narratives, documentaries, and shorts that feature diverse stories of ambition, courage, and resilience. The festival will open with Unjoo Moon’s I Am Woman and close with Rocks, written by Theresa Ikoko with Claire Wilson and directed by Sarah Gavron. Additional films include Lost Girls, Military Wives, The Perfect Candidate, Clemency, Little Women, Frozen 2, Toni Morrison: The Pieces I Am, and much more. We are thrilled to be able to offer you a 15% discount on tickets! To get your discount, visit www.athenafilmfestival.com and use the code 20AFFPartner at checkout.

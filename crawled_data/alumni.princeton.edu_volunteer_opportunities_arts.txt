Arts Alumni Princeton University Art Museum Volunteer Opportunities in the Arts Lewis Arts Alumni If you are a Princeton graduate who works in, has interest in, or supports the creative or performing arts, consider joining this informal network of alumni who share your passion. Our goal is to network, share, learn, and open doors for Princeton students and alumni in their creative endeavors and careers. You can also volunteer your time as a panelist, a mentor, or host of networking events. Alumni who are interested in being involved with the Lewis Center for the Arts should contact Angel Gardner, associate director, external affairs. Learn More Princeton in Hollywood Princeton in Hollywood is a Princeton Club of Southern California committee that explores issues of interest and concern in the entertainment industry, fosters the continued growth of Princeton alumni in entertainment, and supports and/or organizes interesting programs and exciting events promoting dialogue, networking and collaboration among Princeton and other Ivy League alumni working in the entertainment industry. Learn More Princeton Arts Alumni Princeton Arts Alumni is an independent, volunteer-run organization that depends on collaboration from alumni to enhance the resources available to our community. Share your time and expertise with fellow alumni to support a sustainable arts landscape after graduation. Learn More Princeton University Art Museum Volunteer as a museum docent or ambassador and become an integral part of the Princeton University Art Museum’s educational initiatives and its front-line team.

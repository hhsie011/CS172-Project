For more information on the types of funds and how to apply, current students can log in through UFunds. Loan repayment must occur within 60 days or by the semester's end, whichever occurs sooner. Funding is generally not available during the Summer. Questions can be directed to Egap-Funds@brown.edu. You can also begin the process by contacting a Graduate School Dean at Graduate_Dean@brown.edu.

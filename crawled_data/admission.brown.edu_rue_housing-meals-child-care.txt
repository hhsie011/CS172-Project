Breadcrumb Home Resumed Undergraduate Education (RUE) Applicants Sub Navigation In This Section Eligibility Application Requirements Application Process Financial Aid Housing, Meals & Child Care Housing, Meals and Child Care for RUE Applicants There are a variety of housing, meal and child care options for applicants to the Resumed Undergraduate Education (RUE) Program. Housing RUE students generally live off campus, but they may apply to the Office of Residential Life if they would like on-campus housing. The Auxiliary Housing Office maintains off-campus listings for students seeking convenient off-campus housing. Dining Brown Dining Services offers a wide range of flexible meal plans available to students who live on or off campus. Child Care The Brown/Fox Point Early Childhood Education Center is located at the southern edge of campus and accommodates children from age three through pre-K. Tuition is on a sliding scale according to income. Eligible RUE students with dependents between the ages of zero to six may apply to the Office of Financial Aid for a child care allowance of up to $4,000 a year per household. Start Your Application RUE applicants complete the RUE application, not the Common Application. The application is available October 1 for prospective entrance in the fall of the following year. The application deadline is March 1.

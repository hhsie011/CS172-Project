In 2020, we also were ranked No. 1 in the nation for public colleges that provide the greatest return on investment for students. Want to see what undergraduate degrees UW Bothell offers? On this page you can browse through all our undergraduate majors and minors listed alphabetically, or you can see which majors and minors each school offers. Along with a link to more information about the degree, you'll find a Degree Map for each degree. A Degree Map (or "major" map) is a convenient tool for students to use that suggests courses you need to complete each quarter in order to be “on track” for graduation and your admission to a major.

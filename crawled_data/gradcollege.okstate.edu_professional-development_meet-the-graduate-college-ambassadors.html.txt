Ambassadors represent the Graduate College at a variety of campus events and outreach programs. These graduate students are passionate about OSU and excited about their graduate student experience. They are eager to positively and professionally share their insight with future graduate students and other diverse audiences. Ambassadors are all in good academic standing and are actively working towards their degree. Ambassadors are selected in part to represent many disciplines, degrees and academic colleges.

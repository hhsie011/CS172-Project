FAQ Contact Application Timeline Entering Fall 2023 Application Timeline The below timeline information refers to regular admission to the UofL School of Medicine M.D. program. Visit the early decision information page to learn more about deadlines for early decision admission. May 31, 2022 - AMCAS application open for submission Please Note: Application open for editing on May 3, 2022. Applications are reviewed on a rolling admissions basis and students are encouraged to complete and submit their application as early as possible. August 2022 - Interviews for admission begin October 15, 2022 - Admission decision notifications begin November 1, 2022 - AMCAS Application Deadline Transcripts for all institutions attended or where awarded credit must be submitted to the AMCAS application service no later than November 15, 2022. December 1, 2022 - Deadline for Secondary Application Complete secondary application includes the following Applicant Gateway requirements: Secondary Application $75 Application Fee or Fee Waiver Prerequisite course checklist January 1, 2023 - Deadline for Letters of Recommendation Letters of Recommendation must be submitted to AMCAS to be considered for the entering class. Remaining 2022 Admissions Cycle Timeline May 2, 2022 - Multiple Acceptances Deadline Applicants holding more than one offer of admission should narrow their choice to one institution, per AAMC policy recommendation. July 1, 2022 - Transcript Deadline Deadline to submit final transcript(s) from all institutions where credit was awarded. (Note: AMCAS does not forward transcripts to medical schools) Final transcript(s) must show degree posted where applicable Transcripts may be sent electronically to medadm@louisville.edu or mailed to the ULSOM Office of Admissions. July 5, 2022: AMCAS Choose Your Medical School Deadline Matriculated students must select "Commit to Enroll" to remain on roster.

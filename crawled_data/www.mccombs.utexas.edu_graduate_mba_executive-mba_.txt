You've made connections, made waves, and made a name for yourself. Now it's time to make a change. Welcome to the Texas McCombs Executive MBA program, where you'll enjoy the benefits of an internationally-ranked business school without losing any of the career momentum you've already built. It's time to shake things up and shape the world. Executive MBA Executive MBA Your Journey Starts Here. Earn your Executive MBA from the McCombs School of Business in 20 months. The program begins in August, and you'll meet for in-person classes just one weekend each month, along with three EMBA immersions in major U.S. markets outside of Austin and one week-long Global immersion. The cohort-based, executive-focused curriculum places an emphasis on leadership, strategy, and analytics. No matter your pursuits, our dedicated leadership and career coaches will be here to provide support as you continue to make your impact on the world.

SCHEDULE A CAMPUS TOUR Oklahoma State University Esports The OSU Esports Arena is located at the heart of campus in the historic Oklahoma State University Student Union. Oklahoma State University students, staff, faculty, alumni and guests are welcome to engage in the wide-variety of gaming options available. The 1690 sq. ft. space features 46 top-of-the-line Alienware computers, gaming gear, SecretLab Titan gaming chairs, and a live-stream broadcasting station. Read the Full State Magazine Article!

Faculty & Staff Students Degrees Galleries Partnerships News Welcome The School of Art, Design and Creative Industries (ADCI) is an academic hub for contemporary art, innovative design, fresh thinking and creative risk-taking at Wichita State University. ADCI offers four undergraduate majors: art education, art history, graphic design and studio art. Within studio art, we offer concentrations in applied drawing, ceramics media, community and social practice, electronic media, painting media, photo media, print media and sculpture media. An interdisciplinary Master of Fine Arts degree is also offered in studio art.

Guided by PC200, PC’s strategic plan, our team collaborates with faculty and staff to develop, submit, secure, and steward gifts and grants. Faculty and staff giving Gifts from Providence College faculty and staff affirm the commitment of our community to the College’s mission. The role you play on campus is critical to the lives of our students and the mission of the College, and your support is a way for you to further invest in the Friar experience. Reunion giving During a reunion year, alumni often increase their annual giving contributions and bond together to create a meaningful legacy as a class. For each memory, each moment, and each way that PC has played a role in your life, you can impact Providence College to be an even stronger, better institution. Senior gift The participation of the graduating classes in the senior giving campaign ensures a great experience for future generations of students. When you make your first gift to PC, you join a family of donors that spans the entire country. Every dollar counts and every gift makes a difference. Parent giving Parents are essential members of the PC community and our Friar family. Their gifts to The Fund for Providence College provide funds that can be used immediately and flexibly to respond to the needs of today’s students. <br> FOLD (Friars of the Last Decade) Giving Contributions from young alumni support the experiences that make Friartown so special. Learn More The St. Dominic Society is the leadership giving society at Providence College. With more than 2,000 donors, the St. Dominic Society provides generous support to help PC attract a more diverse student population by ensuring greater access to financial aid, enhanced academic programs, improved academic and athletics facilities, and increased financial stability.

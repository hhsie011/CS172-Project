APPLY GET INVOLVED Search this Website Search Graduate Study The UT College of Fine Arts graduate programs are among the finest in the world, offering exceptional masters and doctoral studies in a variety of disciplines. Graduate alumni are renowned artists, arts leaders and awarded scholars; with 500 graduate students enrolled, Fine Arts graduate students are engaged at the highest levels in their fields and represent the future of the creative disciplines and arts scholarship. Holly Williams, COFA Senior Associate Dean for Graduate Programs All Graduate Admissions are through the Graduate School. For more information about specific programs, please visit that program’s site.

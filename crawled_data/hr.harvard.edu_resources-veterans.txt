JOBS / TALENT ACQUISITION AND DIVERSITY / Recruitment Resources for Veterans, Service Members, and Military Spouses A Long History Harvard is pleased to welcome veterans, service members, and military spouses to our community. Throughout the long history of the University, our students, faculty and staff have served in all branches of the military and served in all conflicts. Veterans’ leadership skills and capabilities are well suited to Harvard’s leadership-oriented culture. Talent Acquisition & Diversity Programs seeks to support university-wide recruitment of and outreach to veterans, active service members, and military spouses. Our job offerings encompass a wide variety of career areas. No matter where your military career has led you, there may be work at Harvard that matches your experience. Whether your expertise is in IT, operations, program management, security, finance or development, we encourage you to search our job listings and apply! Outreach We post all of our open jobs on the Massachusetts state JobQuest site, so that our jobs are visible to veterans, and the Veterans Employment Officers at the state’s One-Stop Career Centers. Harvard’s employment recruiters often attend career fairs for veterans, active service members, and military spouses, such as the Hiring Our Heroes and RecruitMilitary events. For More Assistance Learn more about the university's Military Leave Absence policy. Harvard’s Affirmative Action Plan for Covered Veterans is available to employees and job applicants upon request for review. Please contact hhr_affirmative_action@harvard.edu to schedule an appointment.

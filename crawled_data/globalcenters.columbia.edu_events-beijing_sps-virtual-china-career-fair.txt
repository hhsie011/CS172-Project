(Beijing) | Sept. 14, 8:30 - 11:30 PM (New York) Join Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Columbia University School of Professional Studies' Career Design Lab for the virtual career fair exclusive for Columbia SPS students and alumni. Event Highlights For students who are graduating in December: Are you planning to start a full-time professional career upon graduation? Are you feeling anxious about finding suitable opportunities and making valuable connections? Are you worried that you may not get as much information about working in China as those who live there? Whatever concerns you may have, Virtual China Career Fair is here to help you get the answer and best prepare you for the recruiting season. Whether you are interested in finance, marketing, consulting, or technology, you can always find the right person to speak with here at Virtual China Career Fair. For new students who are interested in exploring working in China: Virtual China Career Fair is a great opportunity for you to understand a company’s culture and expectations, job functions, industry trends, and etc. Finding a full-time position might seem a little far away in time, but getting a headstart in gathering information for your ideal career is absolutely worthwhile! At the career fair, you will hear from senior business leaders, Columbia fellows, and CDL staff who are here to help you prosper! List of Participating Employers Amber Group Tencent PwC Willis Towers Watson Shein JD.com Uniskin P&G Guandata Manulife This event is open to Columbia SPS students and alumni only.

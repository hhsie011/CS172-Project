Library Partnerships and Discounts Home Advance Orange Network Orange Network ’Cuse Community The ’Cuse Community is our one-stop site where alumni can register for events, join clubs, submit class notes, share job opportunities, search for other alumni, and make a gift. Update your profile to stay up-to-date on all things Orange! LinkedIn Join the various Syracuse University affiliated LinkedIn groups to connect with the Orange network. Also, take advantage of the Alumni Search tool where you can search fellow alumni, students and the great SU community based on location, company, skills, etc. For an example on how to best utilize this search tool, check out the LinkedIn & Networking virtual workshop on-demand. ’CuseConnect Syracuse University Alumni Network List of School, College, Unit, Affinity Groups Join an alumni club There are more than 60 alumni clubs and groups across the country, so chances are good that there’s a Syracuse University alumni club within driving distance. Various events bring alumni together to watch a ’Cuse game, exchange career advice, or participate in a service project. Check out all the clubs virtual opportunities, too. Regional Offices Through our regional events, regional offices, and alumni clubs, we give you opportunities to network with other alumni, serve as mentors to students and young alums, and connect with SU’s “community of experts,” so you can continue to stay connected and experience the breadth and depth of Syracuse University. To stay up to date on activities where you live—or plan to visit—check out our regional websites: Atlanta Boston Chicago Los Angeles New York City San Francisco Washington, D.C.

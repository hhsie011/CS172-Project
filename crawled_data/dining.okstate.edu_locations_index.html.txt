University Dining Services is home to nearly 30 self-operated dining concepts in several locations across the OSU campus. With a mix of self-branded concepts and national franchises, we are proud to offer homestyle favorites, international cuisine, grab-and-go meals, convenience store items, vegetarian and vegan choices, allergen-friendly fares and more.

Platforms: Windows Mac iOS Android Linux Description — KU IT provides Wi-Fi (wireless) service for anyone needing internet access while on campus. You can connect to KU Wi-Fi networks by selecting the appropriate wireless network on your computer or mobile device. KU Students, Faculty and Staff JAYHAWK secure wireless network is available to all KU faculty, staff and students with a valid KU Online ID. Campus Visitors KUGUEST wireless network is available to all campus visitors. eduroam Campus visitors from eduroam member institutions can connect to eduroam at KU and enjoy the same security and speed of the Jayhawk network used by faculty, staff and students. Visit the eduroam service page to learn more. Housing WiFi KU partners with Apogee (ResNet) to provide advanced network services and cable TV to campus residence halls, scholarship halls and on-campus apartments. Visit the Housing WiFi service page to learn more. Show MoreMore description Access — Locate and select the appropriate campus wireless network (JAYHAWK, KUGUEST or eduroam) in your device settings.

If you'd like more information on future classes, please contact Samantha Houston at housto7@clemson.edu. Canning is a fun and essential method of food preservation when practiced properly. Safe canning processes involve placing foods in jars and heating them to a temperature that destroys organisms that could cause the food to spoil. or even be a health hazard. Participants will be engaged in hands-on activities using current USDA recommended techniques for preserving foods for home use and will receive a jar of the product preserved in class. Persons with or without experience are encouraged to attend the workshop.

The program can be completed while pursuing your graduate degree or as a stand-alone certificate. Learn how applied behavioral analysis is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum This 18-credit-hour certificate program meets the Behavior Analyst Certification Board (BACB)-required 270 classroom hours of graduate-level instruction. BACB supervision hours, required for certification, are arranged and completed by the student separately from the coursework. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission. All enrollees are encouraged to confer with BACB regarding specific licensure requirements.

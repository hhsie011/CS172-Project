Enterprise Architecture Learn how UW-IT plans the foundation and future of technology at the UW from Enterprise Architecture. Enterprise Architecture’s focus is on the UW’s broad, long-term interests. At the direction of the VP for UW-IT and CIO of UW, EA helps stakeholders maximize the architectural value of their services, solutions, data, processes, or organizations on behalf of the UW in an environment of constant change. Enterprise Data Services Enterprise Data Services helps you get the data you need to make informed, data-driven decisions. Learn about the tools and services available to help your work with UW administrative data that is secure, accessible, and consistent. Enterprise Information, Integration & Analytics The Enterprise Information, Integration & Analytics website provides a single point of access to information for users and developers of Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) data and tools. It addresses questions about access, database connections, report writing and deployment, query writing, available data, and more. UW-IT Design Guide The UW-IT Design Guide is a subset of the larger UW Facilities design guide, and provides detailed documentation and specifications for various technologies infrastructures, including voice, data, and wireless requirements for projects that will connect to the University-wide network.

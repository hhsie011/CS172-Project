For Postdocs For Undergraduates For Departments Assessment The Sheridan Center’s assessment and evaluation work is predicated on the notion that we are continuously improving as teachers and learners. Read More Certificates The Sheridan Center offers intensive certificate programs in which participants develop and reflect on their teaching practice in order to support diverse learners. Read More Consultations The Sheridan Center offers a variety of free, confidential consulting services for members of the Brown teaching community. Read More Departmental Support The Sheridan Center offers a range of services and support for departments, including customized workshops and consultations on departmental learning outcomes and assessment. Read More English Language Support To support students who speak English as an additional language, an array of services are available on campus. Read More Fellowships & Awards The Sheridan Center offers annual fellowship opportunities for junior faculty to engage in a teaching-focused peer learning community. Read More Grant Support The Sheridan Center provides pedagogical expertise and feedback for the educational components of grant proposals. Read More Institutes & Retreats The Sheridan Center offers intensive, immersive opportunities for faculty and graduate students across the disciplines to (re)design courses, or devote time to their writing. Read More Orientations to Teaching at Brown The Center offers orientations to teaching at Brown for all new instructors. Faculty and postdocs are welcome to attend the Center’s orientation for new faculty. Graduate students should attend the workshop series for New TAs. Read More Tutoring Academic Tutoring offers peer-led small group tutoring for undergraduates seeking to develop a deeper understanding of introductory STEM course content. Read More Workshops & Roundtables The Sheridan Center offers a variety of workshops throughout the academic year for faculty, graduate students, and postdocs. Read More Writing The Sheridan Center offers writing support for undergraduates, graduate students, and faculty.

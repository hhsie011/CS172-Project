Kenan, Jr. Charitable Trust will endow curatorship, fund fellowships at University Libraries Part of the gift is a $1.5 million endowment that will create the position of Sarah Graham Kenan Curator of the Southern Historical Collection. The Southern Historical Collection is today considered one of the nation’s leading repositories for materials about the American South. Arts Everywhere returns this week April 8 marks a return to an on-campus celebration of Arts Everywhere Day, with performances, installations and activities across campus. If these blocks could talk Our University Libraries recently acquired its 9 millionth volume: a collection of approximately 900 woodcut printing blocks from the Propaganda Fide press. The collection contains blocks in many languages, with a heavy concentration in East Asian and Middle Eastern languages, Slavic, Greek and other global languages.

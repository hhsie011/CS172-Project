a major investment for many families, and our goal is to make it as easy as possible to interpret your bill and make your payments. Within SRFS, the Office of the University Bursar is responsible for collecting your charges and generating your term bill, and also manages Penn.Pay, the University’s online payment software. SRFS also offers several options for financing and budgeting, including the Penn Payment Plan and guidance on federal and private loans. How can we help you?

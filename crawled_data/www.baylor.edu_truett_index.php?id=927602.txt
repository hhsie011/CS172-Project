Click Here "Encourage one another, and build each other up." (1 Thessalonians 5:11) At Baylor University’s Truett Seminary, we cherish our growing number of alumni and the many, varied ways in which they are ministering and serving around the world. The Seminary seeks to offer Truett Alumni opportunities both to engage—through resources and connection—and to give back—through recruitment and financial support. Learn more about opportunities available to Truett Alumni: Continuing Education Truett offers a substantial amount of continuing education programming. Learn more about Truett Church Network offerings, opportunities to earn CEUs, conferences and lectures, and more. Tools & Benefits As graduates of Baylor University, Truett Alumni have access to a number of useful tools and benefits. This includes alumni email accounts, ATLA access, and more. Employment The Office of Ministry Connections exists to help Truett Alumni and others in the midst of ministry transition. Visit the Ministry Connections site to view job listings, submit opportunities, and more. Events Calendar From the Friends of Truett Dinner to the National Preaching Conference to regional events across the country, find out more about upcoming events relevant to Truett Alumni. Student Recruitment Truett Alumni are often our best recruiters. We welcome our alumni to refer students to us, participate in recruitment events, and represent Truett both near and far. Learn more about how you can partner with Truett in student recruitment. Giving to Truett Over the years, thousands of Truett students have received and benefited from generous scholarship funds. Truett Alumni have a unique opportunity to give back to current students by contributing to the Truett Alumni Excellence Fund.

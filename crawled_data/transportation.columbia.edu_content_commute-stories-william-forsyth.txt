William Forsyth, a second-year student in the Masters of Public Health (MPH) program at the Medical Center campus, takes the ferry with his bike from Red Hook, Brooklyn to Wall Street and then rides from Wall Street to CUIMC. William was nominated for a Commute Story by his friend, who called him a "true environmental warrior." His commute is 15 miles total each way, 30 miles total, and encompasses multiple modes of sustainable travel. He loves to ride his bike and is passionate about reducing his impact on the environment in any way he can. Want to share your commute story?

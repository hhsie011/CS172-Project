H. Hill Jr. Library Floor: Second Floor The Academic Success Center (ASC) facilitates degree completion by providing a comprehensive variety of free programs and resources that promote academic skill development and independent learning within the university environment. The ASC is a central location to study, meet others, and find many academic resources in one place. Book an appointment & view services Services include: Academic peer mentoring — support for good study habits Undergraduate and graduate writing support One-on-one and group tutoring by appointment Tutoring, academic advising, career counseling, and wellness coaching What's inside? Furniture Work table and office chairs Whiteboard Walls you can write on Large tables Lighting Warm lighting Sound Level Conversation and chatter How do I get there? Enter the Hill Library from the Hillsborough St. entrance. Go straight through the gates and turn left. Continue down the hallway until you reach the stairs and elevator. Take the stairs or elevator to the first floor (1U). Continue straight across the lobby. Turn left before entering the Learning Commons. Continue straight past the right side of Ask Us and around the brick wall to the elevators. Take the elevators up to the second floor. Turn left out of the elevator bay Continue straight toward the South Tower. The Academic Success Center service desk is on the left before you reach the stairs. Who do I contact?

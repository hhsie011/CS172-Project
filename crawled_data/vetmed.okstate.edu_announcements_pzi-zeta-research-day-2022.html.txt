Congratulations to the Class of 2023 Initiates: Jacqueline Ball, Abigail Babbidge, Jessica Brown , Carly Clower, Ryan Mims, Abby Morse, Kirsten Ridley, Jacie Leigh Ross, Julia Sauer, Amanda Stamm and Erin L. Willis. Congratulations to the Class of 2022 Initiates: Mindi Bracy, Courtney Longhouse Cox, Cara Croft, Kelsey Gibbs, Sarah Gruber, John Hajiny, Michelle Ientile, Abby Kneitz, Gayle Long, Logan Morton, Kaylee Nejtek, Elizabeth Platts, Mackenzie Skinner, Tiffany Skinner, Isabella Steiner, Rylee Spangler, Morgan Stanley, Grace Thompson, Stephanie Vives and Jessica Williamson. Following the keynote address by Dr. Jason K. Sicklick, UC San Diego School of Medicine, veterinary students, house officers, graduate students and post-doctoral fellows presented their research. Awardees received a generous monetary award along with a certificate thanks to this year’s sponsors: Elanco, Institute for Translational and Emerging Research in Advanced Comparative Therapy, Oklahoma Veterinary Medical Association, OSU College of Veterinary Medicine Dean’s Office, Office of Research and Graduate Education and Department of Veterinary Clinical Sciences, Purina, Virbac and Zoetis. Congratulations to the award recipients: John King Award (Best Overall Veterinary Student Presentation) Titus Patton Intern/Veterinary Student Clinical Case 1st - Grace Chung 2nd – Auri Silverstone 3rd – Felicia Brennan Resident Clinical Case 1st – Kelsey Jurek 2nd – Elizabeth Soler 3rd – Evan Crisman Veterinary Student/MS Research 1st – Kaushalya Jayathilake 2nd – Kristin McClung 3rd - Nishani Wijesekera Intern/Resident Research 1st – Grace Chung 2nd – Marina Liles 3rd – Elizabeth Soler PhD/Post-Doctoral Research 1st - Kathryn Duncan 2nd - Megan Lineberr Poster Presentation 1st – Lindsey King 2nd – Bo Barillo This year marked the 25th anniversary of Oklahoma State’s Nu Chapter. A cake was enjoyed to celebrate. A special thanks to the Phi Zeta Team of Drs. Mike Schoonover, Lara Sypniewski, and Erik Clary, and Cindy Martens in the office of Research and Graduate Education for their work in making this event a success.

This site outlines some of the key ways that Iowans can access information as well as provide input to state officials. Public records requests Iowa State University is subject to the Iowa Public Records Law, Chapter 22 of the Iowa Code. This Q&A explains the law and how to request information. All hearings throughout the state will be video recorded and posted on the regents hearings website. The site also contains public hearing details and schedules through 2014.

Alumni Connection Clemson Home CAFLS CAFLS Student Services Prospective Students Current: Visit us Come see us! The College of Agriculture, Forestry & Life Sciences offers a number of opportunities for future students to come to campus to learn more about our majors and programs! You can also request more information about any of our majors and programs. To learn more about the admissions process and tour the campus, register with the Clemson Visitors Center. Visiting CAFLS Virtually Virtual Resources We are excited to offer you the opportunity to explore Clemson any time that is convenient to you through virtual tours, meetings and experiences. November 19th, 3pm - 5pm CAFLS Fall Open House Registration CAFLS Football Tailgate The CAFLS Tailgate is a tradition that must be seen as thousands of our closest friends and family gather to celebrate before a Tiger football game. CAFLS celebrates the accomplishments of our students, faculty and alumni. Look for CAFLS at these Fall events: SC State Fair in Columbia, SC National FFA Convention in Indianapolis, IN Sunbelt Ag Expo in Moultrie, GA Visiting in the Spring CAFLS Spring Information Fair Join faculty, staff and current students to learn more about each of the academic majors within the College. This is the perfect format for future applicants and admitted students to get answers to all their major-specific questions. Spring 2022 Dates Coming Soon! Little North American Livestock Show Held the first weekend in April annually, this is a long-standing tradition for students within the Animal & Veterinary Sciences students. This is a great opportunity to see our students and animals in action and get a glimpse of the hands-on experiences available within this major. Join us on April 2nd, 2022 at T.Ed Garrison Arena. Equine Center Open House Each April the Clemson Equine Center opens the farm to visitors to experience a full day of activities for all ages! Join us on April 30th, 2022, or for more information visit the Equine Center website.

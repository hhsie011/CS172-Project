Welcome Reception Events Past Event Columbia Undergraduate Class of 2024 Welcome Reception Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 15, 2020 3:00 PM - 6:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Please join us to meet and mingle with your fellow students and to learn more about the University and the Beijing Global Center! Representatives of current students and alumni will also be in attendance to share ideas on how to make the best use of your time at Columbia and in the New York City. We will also have a special session to discuss plans for international students in the fall semester, and we would love to hear you opinions. Look forward to seeing you and welcome to join the Columbia community. This event is co-hosted by Columbia Global China Connection (GCC) and Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. Agenda Registration 2:30 p.m. - 3:00 p.m. Students and alumni sharing 3:00 p.m. - 4:30 p.m. Discussion: Remote learning and student activity for the fall term 4:30 p.m. - 5:15 p.m. "Break the ice" and reception 5:15 p.m. - 6:30 p.m. Reminders Please register for this event before 11:59 p.m. in Beijing Time.

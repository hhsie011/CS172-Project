Learn More About Clubs & OrganizationsThings To Do Near Campus By Students, For Students Associated Students, a student-run organization, plans events and programming like GreenFest; manages the Aztec Recreation Center and the Conrad Prebys Aztec Student Union, among other facilities; and works to ensure that your Aztec experience will be unforgettable. Since its inception, Aztecs Rock Hunger has raised millions pounds of food for the Jacobs and Cushman San Diego Food Bank to fight food insecurity in our community.

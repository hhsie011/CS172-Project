Professor Stephen Penman Events Past Event Beijing Alumni Reception with Professor Stephen Penman Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo June 30, 2018 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Event Brief On June 30, Columbia Business School’s Office of Alumni Relations, in partnership with the Alumni Club of Beijing and the Columbia Alumni Association of Beijing, will hold an alumni reception with Professor Stephen Penman at Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. The reception invites all alumni and guests to a dialogue with Professor Stephen Penman, followed by a networking session. Professor Penman is the George O. May Professor of Financial Accounting and chair of Accounting Division. In this reception, he will share the latest news of the school, as well as present on the rules of accounting and the role of accounting both in business and in the society. Speaker Stephen Penman is the George O. May Professor in the Graduate School of Business, Columbia University. He is also co-director of the Center for Excellence in Accounting and Security Analysis at Columbia Business School. Special Notice The event will be conducted in English. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code of confirmation. Please present the QR code at the entrance. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

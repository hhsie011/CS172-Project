Gordon offers regular office hours to engage in open dialogue and to solicit feedback from the campus community. His office hours are: Thursdays* Noon—1:30 p.m. 434 Crouse Hinds Hall Employees are welcome to stop by his office; no appointments are required. *No office hours on July 7 or 14. HR Shared Services is your first point of contact for all types of employee questions, including benefits, compensation, and supervisor/manager transactions. HR Shared Services office hours are: Monday through Friday 8:30 a.m. — 5 p.m. 101 Skytop Office Building The Senior HR Business Partners office hours vary weekly, so please contact them to schedule an appointment. © Syracuse University.

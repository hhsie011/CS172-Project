Protect Your Bike on Campus Parking Services asks that you register your bike for use on each campus. Remember to always lock your bicycle to a designated rack with a secure lock (we recommend a "U" lock). While riding on campus, be diligent about avoiding pedestrians and obey all traffic laws. Sign Up for University Alerts We recommend that all students, faculty and staff sign up for the UofL Alert System for receiving text or email messages in case of an emergency on campus. Our text messaging service sends the most up-to-date information direct to your mobile device, including all weather cancellations. Download our Rave Guardian App Rave Guardian is the nation's leading smart phone app for personal safety. Download this free app, customized for use by the University of Louisville, on your Apple or Android device to enhance safety on campus through interactive features and a virtual network of friends, family and Campus Police.

It is an ode to our thriving and a reaffirmation that URMs belong here. Rebecca Raymond-Kolker: The Curbsiders Primary care pediatrician, Professor of Pediatrics at Brown Medicine, and Gender and Sexuality expert Dr. Michelle Forcier MD, MPH introduces us to practicing a patient-centered consent-based model of gender affirming care in the primary care setting. Angela Y. Zhang - Not Built For Us Audio essays on the intersections of medicine, structure, & the marginalized. This is not a podcast about health disparities, or even about unconscious bias.

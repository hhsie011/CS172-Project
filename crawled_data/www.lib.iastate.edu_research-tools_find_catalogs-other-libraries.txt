Catalogs of Other Libraries A catalog is the record of the holdings of an individual library (such as Quick Search on the Library homepage) or multiple libraries. "One-stop" searching WorldCat is a single, user-friendly database that includes catalog records from more than 33,000 libraries worldwide. It also offers a direct link to interlibrary loan (ILL) service. When you identify an item you would like to borrow, you can initiate a loan request by clicking the "ILL" button at the top of the page. Other Research Libraries Center for Research Libraries is a specialized library in Chicago with an extensive collection of rare research materials. Greater Western Libraries Alliance provides links to the websites and catalogs of 32 research libraries located in the Midwestern & Western U.S. Other Iowa Libraries Ames Public Library provides access to a wide variety of popular materials that may not be held by the ISU Library, such as car repair manuals and travel guides. Iowa Library Catalogs via SILO (State of Iowa Libraries Online) provides links to 600+ libraries in Iowa. University of Iowa Online Catalog (InfoHawk) University of Northern Iowa Need more help?

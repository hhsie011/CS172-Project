Events, both fun and educational, will be planned throughout the entire week. Follow @TexasTechSAB on Instagram for event updates! Monday, March 21 Kick Off 12 - 2 PM SUB North Plaza (Rain Out: Courtyard) Join us to kick off Diversity Week with a multicultural student organization fair, various activities, and learn about various language programs on campus. Free t-shirt with TTU student ID while supplies last. Mental Health Panel featuring Dr. Amanda Wheeler and Dr. Megan Thoen 12 - 1 PM SUB Red Raider Lounge Hosted by President's Gender Equity Council's Climate Committee Drag 101 6:30-8:30 PM SUB Playa Room Hosted by RHA View this post on Instagram A post shared by TTU Residence Halls Assoc. (@tturha) Tuesday, March 22 Faculty Panel: Navigating Religious Diversity in the Classroom 2 - 3 PM TLPDC 151 (Library 1st Floor) Hosted by TLPDC Wednesday, March 23 RISE tabling with SILC 11 AM - 1 PM SUB West Plaza Hosted by RISE & SILC Inclusive Leadership on Campus Seminar 2-3:30 PM SUB Traditions Room Hosted by Raider Education Classroom to Career Series: Networking Reception 5:30 - 8:30 PM SUB Ballroom Hosted by Mentor Tech and University Career Center Rawls Diversity Symposium 5:30 - 9 PM Rawls Room 105 Hosted by Rawls College of Business Administration 5:30-6:00 PM: Welcoming remarks 6:00-7:00 PM: Keynote speech with Q&A 7:00-8:00 PM: Student panel discussion 8:00-9:00 PM: Reception Thursday, March 24 Aussie Art 11 AM - 2 PM SUB North Plaza (Rain out: Courtyard) Hosted by SAB Come paint your very own boomerang and learn the history of Australia's aboriginal art! We will also be giving out free Diversity Week t-shirts with TTU Student ID while supplies last. Register at https://ttu.elementlms.com/all-events/ Celebration of Recovery 7 PM CCRC Building Celebration of Recovery is an all-recovery support meeting focused on celebrating individuals and their time in recovery within the Texas Tech and Lubbock communities. At this meeting, participants receive chips and share them experience, strength, and hope. Friday, March 25 sujatha baliga speaks on the value of restorative justice for Latinx and other BIPOC/immigrant communities at the Harris Institute for Hispanic and Intercultural Communication 9 - 9:50 AM MCOM, Room 156 + live streaming option Register at https://ttu.elementlms.com/all-events/ “Fill the Pantry” Food Drive 11 AM - 1 PM SUB North Plaza Hosted by College of Arts & Sciences and Red Raider Food Pantry Bring a dry good or canned food item for Red Raider Food Pantry and get a free Diversity Week t-shirt while supplies last! TIP: Students with dining plans may use their Dining Bucks at Sam's Places on campus to purchase pre-packaged food to donate to the pantry!

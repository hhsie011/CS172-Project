Credit Cards Money Management Workshop Request Covering Remaining Costs One Stop always encourages students to plan ahead. Asking questions, completing tasks as soon as possible and making a plan will make your money management experience as seamless as possible. Options for Covering Remaining Cost on your Student Bill Utilize this Remaining Cost Worksheet Tool to plan for how you will cover all costs, outside of your tuition and fees MyCollegeMoneyPlan.org is a free online course to help students and their families learn how to plan for the costs of education or training after high school and gain money management skills that last a lifetime. Consider a tuition payment plan. Find and apply for scholarships. Find a student job. Consider a private parent loan or a Federal Parent PLUS Loan. Consider the financial impact of where you live.

Build & showcase skills Access to thousands of online micro-courses through LinkedIn Learning Develop relationships One-year gift membership to the UW Alumni Association Access to Bothell Husky Landing: our mentorship and mentoring alumni engagement platform LinkedIn University of Washington Bothell Alumni group Find jobs Lifetime access to Handshake, home to thousands of job postings for UW students and alumni Get support Free recent graduate career advising appointments for 3 years after graduation Veterans lifelong career advising appointment support Unlimited access to a wide range of virtual career resources and events Recent graduate career advising appointments Many quality resources and services are available to address the career development and job search needs of UW Bothell alumni. Recent graduates who have received a degree from UW Bothell in the past three years (36 months) are eligible to schedule appointments with our Alumni & Graduate Career Coach for no fee. To schedule an appointment email career@uw.edu. If you are a veteran and also an alumni of UW Bothell and you obtained your UW degree longer than 36 months ago, please contact Career Services at 425.352.3706 orcareer@uw.edu to schedule an appointment with our Alumni & Graduate Career Coach. Handshake Handshake is a free recruiting platform used by the University of Washington that enable students and alumni to connect with employers, both domestic and international, for their next internship or employment opportunity. Take advantage of the thousands of job and internship opportunities listed in Handshake. LinkedIn LinkedIn helps you connect with potential employers and network with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out your LinkedIn profile today – and list "University of Washington Bothell" as your university! Join the UW Bothell Alumni & Friends Group on LinkedIn to make connections within the Husky alumni community. International students join the UW Bothell International Student and Alumni Group Visit University of Washington Bothell on LinkedIn Jobs: https://www.linkedin.com/jobs Mentorship and mentoring Bothell Husky Landing (formerly, The W) is an engagement platform that gives UW Bothell alumni like you the opportunity to become mentors to students or fellow alumni, seek out mentoring for yourself, search for alumni in your fields of interest, receive exclusive offers and discounts, stream exclusive content featuring alumni and faculty, connect to podcasts and streams hosted by alumni like you, and find alumni near you. Workshops, events & seminars Alumni are invited to attend any of the career workshops and events being held through the year. Check the events calendar for listings. Professional & continuing education Advance your career today. UW Bothell offers a variety of certificate programs to help you gain new skills. UW Bothell also offers courses through Professional and Continuing Education at the University of Washington Bothell and Seattle campuses.

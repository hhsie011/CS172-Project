Services for UCF Faculty From course design to technical support, CDL offers a variety of services and resources to UCF faculty. Some of these services are exclusive to faculty who teach fully online and have completed the award-winning professional development course, IDL6543. A full listing of services can be found below: Instructional Design Services Instructional Development Services Multimedia Services Professional Development Services Research Support Services Technical Support Services What is the Center for Distributed Learning? CDL serves as the central agent for online learning at UCF, providing leadership in distance learning policies, strategies and practices. CDL supports and expands student access to education through the advanced application of instructional technology, data analysis, policy and strategic planning for distributed learning programs and courses for the university.

Graduate Students The Career Center is committed to providing quality career services and resources to support Florida State University graduate students. Graduate students may have unique needs related to career planning while in graduate school, as well as questions about how to achieve post-graduate career goals both inside and outside the academy. Career Center staff are prepared to provide career guidance and support to graduate students as they pursue their career goals. The resources below provide some tips and strategies to help students think about their post-graduate options. The Career Center encourages students to also reach out to faculty and professionals in their desired fields for additional specialized information related to their disciplines. Advising Appointment? Log into your NoleNetwork account at https://www.career.fsu.edu/nolenetwork. Select Appointments ⇒ Request a New Appointment ⇒ Graduate Student Advising Appointments => View the dates and times available. Complete the Appointment Request form to submit your request. Select a medium type (Video, Phone, In-Person) Please note that all appointments will be listed under Dunlap Success Center 1st floor, if you are seeking a virtual appointment you can note under the medium type.

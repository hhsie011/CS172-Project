Our faculty has included: members of the National Academy of Sciences, the National Academy of Engineering and the American Academy of Arts & Sciences; Pulitzer Prize winners; Nobel Laureates; Oscar, Tony Award and Emmy winners; and Guggenheim, Fulbright and National Endowment for the Humanities fellows. FSU’s research expenditures totaled $328,604,766 for FY 2021. Florida State consistently ranks in the top 10 universities nationally in physical sciences grants awarded by the National Science Foundation, and FSU receives more in NSF funding than any other university in Florida. Our more than 45,000 students, including more than 11,000 graduate and professional students, come from across the nation and around the world.

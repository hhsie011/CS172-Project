Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Recreation Menu Search TTU University Recreation About West Rec Turf Fields Turf Complex is 278,000 square feet and opened February 20th, 2015. The complex is lined with four flag football fields, two full size soccer fields, one Men's Lacrosse field, and one Women's Lacrosse field with plenty of room for open rec. On site, we offer equipment checkout which includes footballs, soccer balls, cones, and a variety of other sports equipment for users. The facility also houses a picnic area and has both men's and women's restrooms that are open during open hours. Rules & Regulations The drop down menu below gives the policies while at the turf fields. Policies RULES & REGULATIONS The West Rec Complex is available for use by fee paying TTU students, faculty, and staff. TTU issued ID cards are required for use. University Recreation reminds all participants that their participation is entirely voluntary and that due to inherent risk of accident or injury, University Recreation assumes no responsibility for those injuries. Equipment may not be moved without permission of the University Recreation staff. Fields may only be used when supervised by University Recreation staff. If gates are locked, the complex is closed; unauthorized access may be considered trespassing. The following items are not allowed in the West Rec Complex: Pets Alcoholic Beverages Tobacco Products Glass Containers Metal Cleats In order to better protect the Turf, the following items are prohibited from the Turf playing area: Drinks (water is allowed) Food, including sunflower seeds and gum Bicycles, please use bike racks and do not lock to poles, fences, or trees Chairs of any kind Spring 2022 Hours Post Spring Break Hours 2022 Sun - Thur: 6:00pm - 10:00pm Fridays: 4:00pm - 8:00pm Saturdays: CLOSE Weather Permitting Policies University Recreation staff have the authority to remove patrons who fail to abide by these rules.

Experts in the Office of Financial Aid are here to provide the resources and information needed to finance a Brown education. To speak with an individual financial aid counselor, please select a 30-minute phone appointment slot from our Counselor Calendar. Please include the names of participants for the phone call, student name, phone number, as well as a Banner ID if possible. Should you need to cancel an appointment, please email us at Financial_Aid@brown.edu.

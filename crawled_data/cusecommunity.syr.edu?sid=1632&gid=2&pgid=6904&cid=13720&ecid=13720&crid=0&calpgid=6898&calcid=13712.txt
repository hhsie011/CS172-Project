friends of Syracuse University. More than 1,000 Syracuse University alumni call San Diego, Imperial and Southern Riverside Counties home, and we help connect these alumni to each other while staying connected to the University. We hold a number of events throughout the year, including game watches, a new student send-off, professional development opportunities, and family friendly activities. We look forward to seeing you soon. GO ORANGE!

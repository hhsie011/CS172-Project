Our nationally accredited academic programs address a growing demand for dedicated, well-trained health, education and service professionals. The college seeks to expand services and opportunities for learning at the CSUF Center for Healthy Neighborhoods and the Center for Sport Performance. In addition, we plan to establish a health simulation center and a mental health and behavioral wellness clinic. These objectives will improve health, development and well-being in our communities.

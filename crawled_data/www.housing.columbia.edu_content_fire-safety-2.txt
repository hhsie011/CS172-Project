General Fire Safety procedures are also outlined in the Guide to Living. Sprinkler Systems All sprinklers are inspected and maintained as per NYC Fire Code and NFPA 25. For information on the latest inspections, please contact the Fire Safety Office at [email protected]. Fire Drills New York City and State Fire Codes require that 3 fire drills be conducted each year. All fire drills and fire alarms require that all occupants must evacuate from the building. Fire Alarm Maintenance At times maintenance and testing must be performed on the fire alarm system, advance notification will be given prior to such testing. This is the only time when evacuation is not required. In An Emergency You can reach Fire Safety by phone at 212-854-8518 or by email at [email protected].

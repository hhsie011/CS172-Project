Specialized Master's Degrees and Army-Baylor Programs Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) The Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) degree is a STEM-designated program, cross-disciplinary degree, encompassing multiple fields of study within the Business School and the Department of Statistical Science in the College of Arts and Sciences. Master of Information Systems (MSIS) The Master of Information Systems program is a STEM-designated program that aims to equip you with the technical and management knowledge to become a leader in the field of information systems management and technology. Master of Accountancy (MAcc) and Master of Taxation (MTax) Baylor's accounting and tax programs are accredited, nationally-recognized programs whose students consistently score among the top in the nation on the CPA exam. Whether you are looking to be an analyst, an auditor, or even a consultant, getting a Baylor MAcc or MTax will prepare you for success in your field. Master of Science in Economics (MSEco) Our Master of Science in Economics is designed for those who are aspiring to work in consulting firms, forecasting groups, financial institutions, NGOs, and public agencies, or those who are preparing for doctoral training in economics, finance, or other related disciplines. Army-Baylor MHA and MHA/MBA Programs The Army-Baylor Graduate Program in Health and Business Administration offers CAHME, AACSB, and AUPHA recognized MHA and joint MHA/MBA options for officers (active duty and reservists) from the Army, Navy, Air Force, Coast Guard, and civilian personnel from the Department of the Army and the Department of Veterans Affairs. Doctoral Programs Entrepreneurship The PhD in Entrepreneurship leverages Baylor’s reputation as a leader in the field to train the next generation of entrepreneurship scholars. Our selective, rigorous, and comprehensive program covers the theory, history, impact, and practice of entrepreneurship as it prepares students for successful careers as researchers and teachers. Information Systems The PhD in Information Systems is a research-based program that seeks to train future researchers, scholars and teachers to analyze and understand the impact of information and communications technologies on individuals, organizations and society. Health Services The PhD in Health Services Research was created in response to the growing need and importance of data analytics in all facets of healthcare. This program prepares professionals who provide solutions to the many challenges facing leaders and health policy makers by studying and researching economics, sociology, statistics, epidemiology, and ethics.

Join us to find out Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 11, 2020 8:00 PM - 9:30 PM Online Columbia Global Centers | Beijing cordially invites you to join an online webinar to learn about how Columbia University School of Professional Studies will help you achieve your academic, personal, and professional goals. During the session, you can expect to: Learn about Career Design Lab Discover Columbia Global Centers resources Meet our world-leading faculty to learn about SPS scholar-practitioner model Connect with current students and alumni, and start to build your social support network This webinar is co-hosted by Columbia School of Professional Studies and Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. About SPS At Columbia University School of Professional Studies, our mission is to advance and accelerate careers. Through specialized programs taught by leading educators and leading-edge practitioners, SPS students gain the skills and support they need to move their careers, communities, and industries forward. With 17 master’s degrees offered online, on-campus, and in hybrid study options, our rigorous programs meet students where they are and take them where they want to go — on their terms and throughout their lives. Speakers Vicky Yang, Director of International Recruitment Onika Richards, Associate Director of Industry Relations, Career Design Lab Dave Romoff, Associate Director, Enterprise Risk Management; Lecturer in Discipline; Consultant, Cassandra Analytics Helena Xiao, Associate Director, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Katherine Li, M.S.

Whatever fascinates you about the world, you can shape that subject into a fulfilling career. At the College of Arts and Sciences, you learn from Creighton professors who stand among the brightest minds in higher education. We also provide many opportunities for you to work closely beside these faculty members, creating new knowledge through undergraduate research. The natural result of a liberal arts education is the set of skills you acquire. Critical thinking, problem solving, clear communication — these strengths remain valuable wherever you go, actively recruited for by employers in a wide range of industries. Whether your career plans revolve around landing a job, launching a venture, or going on to graduate school, a successful life begins with an education from the College of Arts and Sciences.

Search Class Offerings Order Transcripts Current and former University of Cincinnati students who completed undergraduate or graduate level coursework can find instructions about ordering University of Cincinnati transcripts on this page, and visit the Office the Registrar website for detailed instructions and important information about transcripts, such as cost and estimated delivery timeframes. Current Students & Recent Alumni Students who attended UC since 1999 should have UC login credentials to order transcripts in Catalyst. If you have login credentials, this is the fastest way to order and receive your official transcript. To order: Recover your username, if needed, by using the Get My Username tool. Visit Password Self Service and select Forgotten Password, or call IT@UC at (513)556-4357 to reset your password or regain access to your account. Log into Catalyst and choose the My Academics tile, then choose Grades/Transcripts/Enroll Cert. Select Request Official Transcript and follow the instructions to complete your transcript request. Other Transcript Ordering Options If you attended UC prior to 1999, you may not have a UC username. Create an account with Parchment to easily order electronic and mailed transcripts online. If you attended the College of Law or the College of Medicine, please visit their transcript ordering webpages for more information.

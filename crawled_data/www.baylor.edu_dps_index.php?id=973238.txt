Transportation>Zipcar Zipcar wheels with no strings attached Need a car? Borrow a Zipcar! Baylor has partnered with Zipcar to bring self-service, on-demand car sharing to the area. To use Zipcars, simply register as a member, reserve a car online or by phone, use your Zipcard to enter the car, and drive away. When you’re done, return the car to the same location where you picked it up. As a member you get: • Access to Zipcars 24/7. • Gas, insurance and maintenance are included for free! • Join for a small annual fee! Learn more and get started at zipcar.com/universities/baylor-university! Need more information? Find out more about Zipcar What is car sharing? Is car sharing for me?

Assistant to the Dean Dr. Dell is serving in an interim role to support the development of the Graduate School. In addition to this role, she serves as the Associate Dean for Academic Affairs in the College of Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services, and Informational Technology and as a faculty member in the School of Education. As a professor, Dr. Dell regularly teaches both online and on campus. She has developed multiple honors courses and led study aboard trips across the globe including to Iraq, Botswana, and Ireland. Her research interests include museum education, nature preschools, and informal science and environmental education settings. As an administrator, she aims to improve access for all students while helping them to achieve their academic and career goals.

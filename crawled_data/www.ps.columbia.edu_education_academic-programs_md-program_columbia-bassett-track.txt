These students are with their classmates in NYC for the first 18 months of medical school and then enter a curriculum caring longitudinally for their patients in an innovative rural healthcare system in Central New York State. Students receive special training in performance improvement and trauma-informed care, and have been remarkably successful in the residency match. Read more on the Columbia-Bassett Track website.

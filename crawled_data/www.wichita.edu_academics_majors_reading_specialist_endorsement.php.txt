Learn how the Reading Specialist endorsement is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The Reading Specialist endorsement requires 18 credit hours of coursework, including a practicum. It also requires two years of teaching experience in an accredited K-12 school and a passing score on the reading content exam. Once completed, you will be eligible to apply for a reading specialist initial license.

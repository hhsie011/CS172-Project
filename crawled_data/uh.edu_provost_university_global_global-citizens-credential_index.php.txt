Suite 101 cawandmacher@uh.edu Global Citizens Credential Watch the video to learn more about the program and how to earn points The Global Citizens Credential (GCC) is an undergraduate program offered at the University of Houston that recognizes students for activities that build global knowledge and skills and awards its graduates with a transcript credential, certificate, and honor cord. The GCC is a good resume-builder and helps students stand-out when applying to jobs, graduate school, and competitive international scholarships. The program also encourages students to be more active global citizens and make a greater impact in their community. Eligibility The GCC is open to any UH undergraduate student in good academic standing with a GPA of 2.00 or above Important Announcements: COVID-19 Program Update Please click here to read more about our COVID-19 Program Accommodations: Global Citizens Credential COVID-19 Program Accommodations Steps to Complete Register for the GCC Program: Complete this form to Register Join the GCC Listserv Below: Listserv: UH-GCC Your full name: Your e-mail address: action: subscribe unsubscribe Earn 10 Points in any of the Five Categories: Global Courses Language Proficiency Learning Abroad Global Scholarly Work Intercultural Activities Download the GCC Point Worksheet to Calculate Your Points Here Submit a Verification of Completion Form and Required Documentation After completing 10 points, students must submit the form below to register points: Verification of Completion Form Then email any supporting documentation to uhglobal@uh.edu Please refer to each category in Step 2 for more information on what documentation is required. Attend a Capstone Symposium You can register for a Capstone during your senior year and after earning 10 points. UH Global will offer a few Capstones each semester with the last one about a month before graduation. Sign up for a Capstone Symposium Here Reflect upon global learning experiences Listen and learn from your peers Discuss cultural theories Explore ways to engage and become a more active global citizen ADDITIONAL RESOURCES Advising Support Available Students may schedule an advising appointment by emailing us or scheduling via Navigate (under Support Services and UHGlobal). For questions, please contact us at uhglobal@uh.edu . Awards GCC graduates receive: 'Global Citizens Credential' Transcript Notation Signed Certificate Honor Cord for Graduation Graduates will be invited to a certificate ceremony to celebrate their accomplishments. Students who complete the GCC will have a $25 'Graduation Application Fee' placed on their account by the Registrar to cover the processing of the transcript and certificate.

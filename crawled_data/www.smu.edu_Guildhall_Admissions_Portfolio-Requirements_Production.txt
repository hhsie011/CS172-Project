Now Guildhall Portfolio Requirements: Production The Production track is only available to master's (MIT) program candidates. Your portfolio will consist of examples that showcase your aptitude, problem solving skills, communication skills and leadership ability. Production applicants must complete the following: Assignment 1: During the admission process, applicants to the production specialization must choose one of the other three specializations (Art, Design or Programming) as a sub-specialization. You must complete the portfolio assignment for your chosen sub-specialization as detailed on this page. Assignment 2: Submit a solution for this Case Study. Assignment 3: Submit your resume. *Note that the production track is only offered as a specialization to our Master’s degree students. It is not offered as an option to our graduate certification students.

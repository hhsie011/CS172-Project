They played a central role in state-building and economic development by gathering international information and generating capital. On the special occasion of our first in-person signature event in two years, please join us as Professor Hannah Farber looks behind the legendary wooden ships and iron men into the offices and practices of maritime insurers to bring alive a colorful community of characters and reveal a startling story of risk, money, and power in the founding era.

Graduate student mail is directed to their home department where final distribution will be made to an assigned mailbox (if the student has one). All personal mail, including letters or packages, should be sent to the graduate student's home address. Package Address & Delivery Options: Directly to the Department: Departments have direct accounts with FedEx, UPS and DHL. The mailing address for direct delivery is: Name Brown University/Department Name Street Address Providence, RI 02912 Delivery to Mail Services: Packages arriving to Mail Services for a graduate student should be addressed as: Name Brown University/Department 69 Brown Street, Mail # (previously called Box #) Providence, RI 02912 Graduate students receiving mail or packages requiring signature to complete delivery will receive an email from Mail Services with collection instructions.

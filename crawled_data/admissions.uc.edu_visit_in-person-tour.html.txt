Representatives right arrow down arrow Out of State Representatives Social Media Admissions » Visit Campus » In-Person Campus Tour In-Person Campus Tour We are always excited to welcome students to the University of Cincinnati (UC). Come see UC through the eyes of a current student on a one-hour, guided walking tour of campus and select to attend a College Close-Up session to hear from the academic college that interests you most before or after your campus tour. Register using the link below. Prior to your visit, please review your visitor guidelines. Be sure to check for availability and register for a campus tour before confirming your travel plans. Pre-registration is required. We will not be able to accommodate walk-in visitors and guests. Availability of in-person offerings is subject to change based on area health and safety trends. We’ll notify you if your visit is canceled.

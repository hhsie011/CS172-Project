Student Support Activities & Events Diversity and Inclusion Wellness Why Kansas? Be a Part of the Health Care Story Learn by Doing Receive hands-on, interprofessional learning in the new state-of-the-art Health Education Building (HEB) complete with learning studios and simulation labs. Train with the best for clinical training as each school offers a wide range of training experiences, including the opportunity to treat patients in the student-operated JayDoc Free Health Clinic. When it’s time to study, escape to dedicated group and individual study rooms in the HEB and in the A.R. Dykes Library. Play Hard Take a break from studies and get to know your classmates through social activities, like Popcorn Thursday, intramural sports, pancake breakfasts, and yoga and exercise. Join a current student-led organization or academic society or create your own. There are also opportunities to affect change and improvements through student leadership groups. Office of Student Life Join a club, attend an event and find friendships: the Office of Student Life is your No. 1 resource to see what our campuses have to offer.

Regardless of meal plan status, you can also have food delivered to your residence hall from local restaurants (via GrubHub, etc.) Remember, you should not leave isolation more than 3 times per day to pick up meals. Please note: meal pick-up ends on Friday, May 13 at 6:00 p.m. Columbia Dining will be closed for the summer and students in isolation after this date should make arrangements to have food/groceries delivered or ask friends to help by dropping off food and supplies.

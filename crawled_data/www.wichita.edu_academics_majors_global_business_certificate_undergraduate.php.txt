Frank Barton School of Business Management The undergraduate certificate in global business from Wichita State's AACSB-accredited Barton School of Business will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in leading today's organizations. The certificate is a valuable asset to any business major or non-business majors who wish to pair their major with an international business credential. Learn how Global Business is the right fit for you. Contact coordinator at john.perry@wichita.edu Curriculum This nine-credit-hour certificate consists of one required zero-credit certificate completion course and three additional courses chosen from the following: International Business Negotiating Across Cultures International Management International Marketing View the Curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

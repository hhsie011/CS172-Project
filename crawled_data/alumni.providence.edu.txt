Stay connected to PC through events, giving opportunities, and more. more events event photos 50 Years of Women at Providence College In 1971, the first “coeds” (as women students were then called) moved into Aquinas Hall. Fifty years later, the women of Providence College have shaped the character and trajectory of PC through their abilities to enlighten minds, open hearts, and transform lives. To mark this historic occasion, Friartown will celebrate Then, Now, Next: 50 Years of Women at Providence College in honor of the achievements and impact of our women students, alumni, faculty, and friends.

As a Global Scholar, you will travel and study abroad, undertake research with a faculty mentor, and serve in local and global communities. Through coursework, research, internships, experiential learning, and interaction with leaders worldwide, Baylor Global Scholars will develop as global citizens with the skills and knowledge base necessary to thrive in an increasingly global workforce. Applications for study abroad programs are currently open for the summer and fall terms of 2022. Study abroad director Bo White, Ed.D., suggested that students start preparing early in the year. In the long run, he said it is more beneficial for students to get on these tasks, given they will have more options and availability for their intended courses.

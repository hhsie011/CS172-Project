The Office is responsible for providing student guidance to all prospective and currently enrolled undergraduate and graduate students interested in all programs offered at the School of Architecture. Assisting students, parents, faculty and other University departments with all affairs related to advising, scheduling, registration, graduation and post-graduation career opportunities is the fundamental role of the office. The Assosiate Dean and Director of Academic Services, Associate Director of Undergraduate Programs, Assistant Director of Graduate Programs and the Senior Program Coordinator are all committed to ensure that every student and visitor to the School receive the upmost professional and valuable experience possible.

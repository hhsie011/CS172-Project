Wilson received a BFA from Washington University in 1998 and a MFA from Columbia University in 2005. She has had one-person exhibitions at 516 Arts, Albuquerque, NM; Smack Mellon, Brooklyn, NY; and Galleria Suzy Shammah in Milan, Italy. Her work has been included in group exhibitions at Zacheta National Gallery of Art in Warsaw, Poland; Sikkema Jenkins & Co., NY; Fred Snitzer Gallery, Miami; and the Studio Museum in Harlem, NY, among others. Wilson has been awarded residencies at the Vermont Studio Center and the Giverny Residency from the Art Production Fund in Giverny, France.

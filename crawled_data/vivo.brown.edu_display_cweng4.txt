He received his MD from National Yang-Ming University in Taiwan and his MPH at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, focusing on epidemiology and biostatistics. After medical school, he served in the Taiwanese Navy as a medical officer. Dr. Weng completed his residency at the NH Dartmouth Family Medicine Residency and was a postdoctoral research fellow in cancer epidemiology and surgical oncology at Johns Hopkins. His research interests include clinical epidemiology, thyroid disorders, cancer epidemiology - especially breast cancer, emerging diseases, addiction medicine and outcome analysis. The focus of Dr. Weng's research has been epidemiological studies in thyroid disorders and emerging diseases, breast cancer, gastro-intestinal cancers, and chronic disease epidemiology, addiction medicine and outcome analysis. Other major interests include population health research related to primary care.

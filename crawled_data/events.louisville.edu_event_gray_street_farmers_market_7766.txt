This year’s season will vary from years past. From June to October, the GSFM will open the first Thursday of each month with extended hours from 10 a.m. – 2 p.m. So please mark your calendars for June 2, July 7, Aug. 4, Sept. 1, and Oct. 6. We are proud to serve those who live and work in and around the UofL Health Sciences Center and the surrounding neighborhoods. GSFM farmers and vendors will sell locally grown produce, bread, granola, jams, desserts, and more. A rotating group of lunch vendors and food trucks will be back to serve you again this year. It’s been a long two years, and we are thrilled to welcome our customers and vendors back to Gray Street.

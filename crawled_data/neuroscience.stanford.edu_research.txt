Connecting our community. Training future neuroscientists. Wu Tsai Neurosciences Institute programs advance neuroscience research through interdisciplinary research grants. We are deeply committed to the training of future generations of neuroscientists through our graduate fellowships and interdisciplinary scholar awards for postdocs. Our community gathers at our symposia, weekly seminars, and retreat to learn and disseminate the latest neuroscience research. Through everything we do, we are bringing together brilliant people from diverse disciplines to tackle the unanswered questions in neuroscience.

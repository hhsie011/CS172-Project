One component of this project is to produce a series of reports that address a variety of important topics that impact Black Rhode Islanders. Dr. Akilah Dulin and her team have written a report about homeownership amongst Black Rhode Islanders. In this brief report are statistics showing recent data about Black homeownership, barriers and facilitators to homeownership, and policy recommendations.

Professional Studies Programs – Information Session Events Past Event Columbia University School of Professional Studies Programs – Information Session Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo October 24, 2016 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Columbia University School of Professional Studies will hold an information session. David Madigan, Ph.D., Executive Vice-President for Arts & Sciences, Dean of the Faculty, and Professor of Statistics, and Jason Wingard, Ph.D., Dean and Professor of the School of Professional Studies, will join and deliver addresses. The Information Session will cover most programs that School of Professional Studies offer, including the Masters Programs, Certification Programs, Visiting Student Program, Summer Sessions, 4+1 Programs and more. The participants will also have chances to ask questions and talk with Columbia University faculty and staff after the presentations and during a reception. The event is co-organized by Columbia University School of Professional Studies, and Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. Registration is required and space for walk-ins is not guaranteed. Registration deadline is Oct 19th, 2016. Late registration will not guarantee entrance. Agenda: 17:45-18:00 Guest Arrival/Check-in 18:00-18:30 Opening Keynote - Executive Vice President David Madigan The University in the Global Age 18:30-19:00 School of Professional Studies Address – Dean Jason Wingard Emerging Trends and Market-Driven Professional Education 19:00-20:00 Reception & Information Sessions with Faculty and Administrators Sponsored by Columbia University The Faculty of Arts and Sciences Columbia University School of Professional Studies Columbia Global Centers | Beijing The event is free and open to the public. Registration is required.

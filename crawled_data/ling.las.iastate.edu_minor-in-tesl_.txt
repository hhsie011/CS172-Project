The Minor in TESL provides concentrated coursework that prepares students to teach English to nonnative speakers of English, either in community programs in the United States or in an overseas location. Students from any program may complete the minor without further studies in linguistics. For more information, please contact Taylor Anne Barriuso, Linguistics Undergraduate Advisor.

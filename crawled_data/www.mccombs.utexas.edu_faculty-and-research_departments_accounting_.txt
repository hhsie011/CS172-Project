The mission of the Department of Accounting is to expand and create knowledge through scholarship of theoretical and practical impact and to communicate an understanding of the accounting discipline through teaching undergraduate and graduate students, training accounting scholars and educators, and collaborating with business and policy-making innovators. BBA AND MPA PROGRAMS BBA AND MPA PROGRAMS LIFE’S SHORT – START FAST Launch your career arc from the highest-ranked undergraduate and graduate accounting program in the country. Choose the four-year BBA program or the MPA option that combines your undergraduate degree with a one-year master’s program to supercharge your career potential in this robust job market. Learn More on Accounting Research AFFILIATED CENTERS & INITIATIVES Center for Risk Management & Insurance Providing an unbiased analysis of public policy issues, the Center conducts and disseminates research relevant to the operational concerns and policy implications of risk analysis and risk management. Explore the Center Texas Tax Readings Group This active group of tax scholars in accounting and finance meets regularly to read national colleagues’ new working papers. Doctoral students provide feedback and help vet early research of promise.

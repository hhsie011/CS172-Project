With a focus on serving underperforming schools and underserved communities, the College has award-winning programs that train teachers, counselors and school leaders for P-12 schools; prepare counselors for community practice; train higher education student affairs leaders; and prepare training and development specialists for business and industry. There are 13 undergraduate teacher education programs leading to certification, from early childhood, elementary and secondary to agricultural and special education.

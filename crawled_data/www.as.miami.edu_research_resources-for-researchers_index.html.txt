Research News About Us Academics Research & Outreach Resources News & Events Giving Home Research & Outreach Resources for Researchers AAA Resources for Researchers The College of Arts & Sciences (A&S) boasts some of the most productive and influential researchers and scholars at the University of Miami, and we are proud to assist faculty efforts in obtaining and managing external funding. Research Support Services acts as a liaison between A&S faculty and the Office of Research Administration. If you have any questions about funding sources and procedures, we are your best resource. Before You Submit a Research Proposal We encourage all A&S faculty to contact Research Support Services early in the process of developing your research proposal. We can help ensure that your budget projections are accurate and identify any compliance issues with College and University policies, particularly in the area of cost-sharing. By addressing any issues before submitting your proposal, the whole process will go more smoothly.

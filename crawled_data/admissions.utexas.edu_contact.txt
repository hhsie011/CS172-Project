Admissions Counselors Admissions Counselors are available via phone between 8:30 a.m. and 4 p.m. CT Monday through Friday at 512-475-7399 or via email at admissions@austin.utexas.edu. You can also make an appointment with an Admissions Counselor. International students or students applying for graduate programs should contact the Graduate and International Admissions Center. You can also connect with counselors from our admissions centers or use the Counselor Finder tool to connect directly with the Admissions Counselor who works with your school. We look forward to connecting with you via phone or email.

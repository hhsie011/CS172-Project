July 16, 2020 Foreign National Information System (FNIS) live to all new hires In January 2019, the CU HRPC team implemented the Foreign National Information System (FNIS), a secure, online international tax compliance software. The first phase—allowing foreign nationals to receive their 1042-S tax form electronically—was a success. We are now ready for the second phase: launching FNIS to all new foreign national hires.

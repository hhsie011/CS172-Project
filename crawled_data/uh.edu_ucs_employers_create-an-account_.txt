Employers and Faculty and Staff can use Cougar Pathway to: Post open internships, part-time and full-time jobs Schedule on-campus interviews and meet and greets Register for career fairs How to create a Cougar Pathway Account Go to Cougar Pathway and click [Sign up] under Faculty/Staff or Employers Complete the form with all required information and click [Submit] When we receive your account registration, we will review this information before approving your access to Cougar Pathway. The review can take 2-3 business days. Once your account has been approved, you will receive an email message with your approval and instructions for logging in. TIP: Check out our recruiting policies to understand what is required in each section.

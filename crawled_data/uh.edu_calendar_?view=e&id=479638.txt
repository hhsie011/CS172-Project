Submit an Event French Research Wednesday, May 25, 2022 12:00 pm - Research participants are needed for a study about language. The study lasts 30 minutes and consists of listening to recordings and performing an intuitive judgment task. Participants will be paid $12 for their time. We are looking for interested individuals who fit the following description: Native speakers of European French, between the ages of 18 and 40, with normal hearing.

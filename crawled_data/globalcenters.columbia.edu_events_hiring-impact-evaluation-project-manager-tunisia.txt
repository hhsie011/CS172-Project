Manager, Tunisia Events Past Event Hiring for Impact Evaluation, Project Manager, Tunisia Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo April 6, 2020 - June 1, 2020 Tunisia Organization: Country: Tunisia Education: Bachelor's Master's Other advanced degree Start Date (Earliest): 1 July 2020 Start Date (Latest): 3 August 2020 Language Requirement: English French Length of Commitment: One year Professors Eric Verhoogen (Columbia University) and Giacomo De Giorgi (University of Geneva) and Aminur Rahman of the World Bank are looking for a project manager for a randomized trial of export promotion programs in Tunisia. The position will involve working closely with the Tunisian government export-promotion agency and the researchers on all aspects of the project, including supervision of data collection. French and English language ability required, Arabic preferred. Position requires residence in Tunis. Desired Qualifications Strong quantitative background Strong computer skills including programming Ability to work independently Long-term interest in pursuing a PhD in economics Strong written and verbal communication skills Background in economics and randomized evaluations a plus, but not necessary. Prior research assistant experience strongly preferred How to Apply Complete the J-PAL/IPA Common Application. After completing a brief registration, choose the Research job category, then select “Project Manager - Tunisia”. Include resume, cover letter, transcripts, and two recommendation letters. Note that applications with incomplete submissions will not be considered. Note that you are able to submit your application before your references complete their recommendation letters; however, you must include their contact information before submitting your application. Given the volume of applications received, only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview. If you are intrested please use the link to know more about the offer and to apply. If you would like to know more about the context of the position and Pr.

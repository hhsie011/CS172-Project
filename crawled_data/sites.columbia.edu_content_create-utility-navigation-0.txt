These pages are generally not part of the main topic hierarchy of the site. For example, a link to email or donate pages aren't part of the main navigation. You can create three independent links for your utility navigation. These links appear in the upper right corner of every page. From the top navigation menu click Shortcuts > Site Settings. Select Menus tab. Expand UTLIITY NAV. Enter the Links and corresponding Titles. Click Save Configuration.

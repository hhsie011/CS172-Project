Communications Strategic Research Communications is responsible for raising the visibility of UH research and innovation programs nationally, among peer institutions and throughout Houston’s regional industry. In addition, the team manages programs to engage UH researchers and inventors, and supports Division of Research business communications. Major strategic efforts supported by UH Strategic Research Communications Inside Research: Weekly newsletter to the UH community UH Research News: Monthly newsletter to key external stakeholders FYI: Monthly newsletter to division staff Research Reaching Houston: Collection of stories documenting the impact of UH research on the city of Houston The Big Idea Magazine: Biannual publication to peer institutional leadership The Big Idea Blog: Blog dedicated to topics faced by university research and innovation enterprises UH Research Gateway: External gateway showcasing the UH research and innovation ecosystem UH Innovation: Major initiative to support and promote UH intellectual property, startups and industry partnerships. TECH Map: Geographic mapping of innovation ecosystems DOR Website: The division’s research and tech transfer operations website for the UH community Research Centers: HEALTH Research Institute, HPE Data Science Institute, Drug Discovery Institute, Hurricane Resilience Research Center, Advanced Manufacturing Institute Social Media: Facebook, Twitter Staff Tim Holt is the Interim Executive Director of Strategic Research Communications for the UH Division of Research.

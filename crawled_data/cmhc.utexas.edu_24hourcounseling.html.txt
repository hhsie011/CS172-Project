A mental health professional is available every day of the year, including holidays. You can call us when you want, at your convenience. Our mental health professionals have specific training to responding to crisis situations and will spend time addressing your immediate concerns. Common Reasons Students Call You want help with an immediate personal crisis. You are thinking about suicide. You are feeling distressed. You are in therapy or waiting to see a therapist, but need to talk with someone now. Concerns About Others If you have concerns about a student, faculty, or staff member in the UT-Austin community, contact the 24-hour Behavior Concerns and COVID-19 Advice Line (BCCAL) at 512-232-5050. The BCCAL line was developed by UT-Austin in order to provide a central resource for those who are concerned about an individual and are not sure how to help them.

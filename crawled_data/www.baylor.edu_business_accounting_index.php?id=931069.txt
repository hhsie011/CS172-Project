Whether you are looking to be an analyst, an auditor, or even a consultant, getting your graduate degree in accounting is crucial to improving your advancement opportunities in the work world. Why Baylor? Baylor's accounting program is an accredited, nationally-recognized program whose students consistently score among the top in the nation on the CPA exam, the leading professional accreditation. We offer different routes to getting your graduate degree in accounting or tax.

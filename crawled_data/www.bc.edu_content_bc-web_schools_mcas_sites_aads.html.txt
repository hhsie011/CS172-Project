Giving to AADS Program News and Notes All Faculty Publications Awards Presentations Service Students Opportunities Traveling Fellowship Named for the founding director of African & African Diaspora Studies at Boston College, the Amanda V. Houston Traveling Fellowship enables program majors and minors to enhance their education through summers or semesters of international study and travel. Dissertation Fellowship The AADS Program offers a dissertation fellowship opportunity for applicants pursuing innovative, preferably interdisciplinary, projects in dialogue with critical issues and trends within the field. Martin Luther King Scholarship This scholarship recognizes a Boston College junior who has demonstrated superior academic achievement, extracurricular leadership, community service, and involvement with the African American community and African American issues both on and off campus.

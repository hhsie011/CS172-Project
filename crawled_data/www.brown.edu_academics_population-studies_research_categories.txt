discover in one community can translate to many others. Our researchers have been at the forefront of producing relevant science on global population challenges for more than 50 years, with research and fieldwork currently being conducted in more than 20 nations, focusing on issues from health and development to environment and spatial structures. Through this type of global social science, we aim to influence the trajectory of population studies and policies to improve health, well-being, and equality around the world.

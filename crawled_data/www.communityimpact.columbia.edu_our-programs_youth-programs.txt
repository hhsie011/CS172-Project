Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more Our programs reach a wide range of youth from our neighborhood, from pre-K to high school. Community Impact oversees six out-of-school programs; one is a homework help and literacy program at a local shelter, another is an early childhood center and there are four highly successful mentoring programs. CI partners with local public schools, PS 145 and PS129, to recruit mentees. In addition, 400 CI volunteers serve as tutors to 1,400 youth in a variety of school based programs at local elementary schools, PS76, PS125 and PS192. There are two college prep programs, a health education program, an arts enrichment program and a conflict resolution program based in four local elementary and high schools. There are many ways for you to get involved!

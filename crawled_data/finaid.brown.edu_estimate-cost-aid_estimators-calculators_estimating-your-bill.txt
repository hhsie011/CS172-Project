The Brown University Bill Estimator is for informational purposes and the accuracy of the information you enter will affect the accuracy of the resulting estimate. The costs of books, travel and personal expenses are not included in this estimate. The Bill Estimator is not an actual bill, and University charges are subject to change. Using the Bill Estimator The Bill Estimator is intended for first year students but current students can use it also. Log in to Banner Self-Service to find your most recent financial aid award. Indicate your anticipated participation in Brown Student Health Insurance. Enter the amount of your Brown University Scholarship and/or federal grants, and any student loans you have been offered and plan to accept. Do not include outside scholarships in the Bill Estimator, but keep in mind that outside scholarships may be used to reduce your student loans or Estimated Annual Amount Due.

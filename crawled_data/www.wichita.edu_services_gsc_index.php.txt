The Graduate School What is the Graduate Student Council? The Graduate Student Council was founded in October 2019 and officially kicked-off in January 2020. The Council is representative of all Colleges in which hold graduate programs recognized by the Graduate School. The Council represents all graduate students at Wichita State University regarding the interests and concerns at the University; to improve the academic, social, and professional welfare of graduate students.

Aquatic CenterLeisure PoolRentalsMembership & Passes Aquatic Center The Aquatic Center at Texas Tech University Recreation allows the department to maintain an open pool facility year-round. The facility is now entirely indoors and is covered by one of the largest air-supported structures in the world, newly replaced in the summer of 2012. The facility boasts a 50-meter end-to-end length, as well as standard 25-yard lap lanes at varying depths. The large shallow end of the pool contains a basketball hoop, climbing wall and is a popular spot for group fitness classes. Summer Operations During the summer, the Aquatic Center is not available for open swim. This space is used exclusively for instruction-based classes. Facilities & Features Lazy River Wet Deck Spa Sunbathing on the deck Deep Well Splash Pad - Operational during family hours Innertubes and mats for the Lazy River are available. Reminder, lifejackets and user inflatables must be US Coast Guard approved Food & Beverage Policy On a restricted basis, Texas Tech University Recreation allows patrons to bring their own food and beverages into our facilities. As our guest, you have three options for consuming food and beverage at our aquatic facilities. You may: 1. Bring your own food and beverages within the restrictions listed below. 2. While the outdoor Leisure Pool is open, utilize the on-site concessionaire, Sam's Poolside. 3. Use the vending machines, which are located inside the Rec Center on the 2nd floor. NOTE: This policy covers public swim hours only. Private rentals will not have access to Sam's Poolside after regular hours, will still need to follow the policies outlined below. No alcohol or glass containers are allowed. Beverages include: Non-flavored/non-carbonated water in sealed, plastic bottles is preferred. However, other liquids (ice tea, coffee, soda) will be allowed. Sealed, single-serving juice boxes or pouches. Formula/baby food. Items necessary for medical purposes. Bags and coolers are permitted in the facility and are subject to inspection by UREC staff. You Can Rent the Leisure Pool for Your Special Occasion!

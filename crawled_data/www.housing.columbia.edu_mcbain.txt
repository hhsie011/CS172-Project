Exterior façade repairs are ongoing. Data from Room Selection 2021 shows the make-up of McBain as 95% sophomores, 3% juniors, and 2% mixed-point groups (average point value of mixed-point groups was 17). Please keep in mind that class make-up in each building varies year to year based on student preferences. This should not be used as predictive data for the upcoming academic year. Key Features: Large doubles Multiple, gender inclusive, single-use bathrooms are available on every floor Shared kitchen and lounge space is available on every floor Located close to neighborhood eateries and coffee shops Building Details: Housing Rate: Upperclass Rate Entrance Location: 562 West 113th Street Number of Residential Floors: 8 Single/Double Rooms: 41 singles / 175 doubles Air Conditioning: No Bathroom Style: Multiple gender inclusive, single-use bathrooms on each floor Cleaning Responsibility & Schedule: Bathrooms: Daily, Monday - Friday, by Facilities Kitchens: Daily, Monday - Friday, by Facilities Trash & Recycling: Residents are responsible for taking their trash and recycling to designated building area Flooring: Hardwood Furnishings: All rooms are furnished Building Amenities: Bike Storage: Not available.

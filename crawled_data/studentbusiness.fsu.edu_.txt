For more information, click here. A message from the Director John Bembry jbembry@fsu.edu / 850.644.9452 Welcome students, parents, and all visitors to the Office of Student Business Services. We are proud to serve as your chief financial resource during your time at Florida State University. Our office works with over 40,000 student, departmental, and non-student accounts to ensure accurate and transparent financial transaction processing and reporting. A few of our responsibilities include the calculation of tuition and fees, invoicing and collection on accounts, debt management counseling, Florida Prepaid servicing, and customer service.

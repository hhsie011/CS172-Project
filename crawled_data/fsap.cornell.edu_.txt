If you're struggling with work or life concerns, we can help. Services are free, confidential, and provided by credentialed counselors. Learn more about us, our services, and how to schedule an appointment. Current services FSAP is currently providing counseling and support services by Zoom or phone appointment, available to clients residing in NY State. Please visit our Services page for more details. Confidentiality FSAP's services are completely confidential. Client records are not shared with other university offices. Learn more about our privacy practices.

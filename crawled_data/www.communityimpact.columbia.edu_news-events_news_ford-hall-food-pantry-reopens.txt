Learn More News & Events News The Ford Hall Food Pantry Reopens Community Impact is proud to announce that the Ford Hall Food Pantry (616 W 114th Street) is open, now providing pre-packed grocery bags at the door following New York City Health guidelines of physical distancing and minimal contact. Dry goods and fresh produce are distributed Monday and Thursday from 12 pm to 2 pm. The Ford Hall Food Pantry provides on average 156 meals per day to members of the community. We are happy to be of service during this great time of need.

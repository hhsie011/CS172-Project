Notice of Non Discrimination Columbia University is committed to providing a working, learning and living environment free from discrimination and harassment and to fostering a nurturing and vibrant community founded upon the fundamental dignity and worth of all of its members. For more information, please contact the office of Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action (EOAA).

As a working professional MBA student, your classroom experience will fuel your career growth, while your job responsibilities will provide deeper insights into your education. You'll learn from some of the best professors in the country and study alongside dynamic classmates. You'll leverage all that UT Austin has to offer. And you'll do it all from Houston, a global hub of industry and culture. MBA at Houston MBA at Houston Your Journey Starts Here. Earn your MBA from the McCombs School of Business in 21 months without career interruption. The program enrolls in August, and you'll take classes on alternating weekends (Friday evening and all-day Saturday) at our MBA campus at the UT Health Cizik School of Nursing in the Texas Medical Center. The cohort-based curriculum will provide you with broad business management knowledge and skills.

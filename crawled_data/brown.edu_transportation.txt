Transportation Office visitors may utilize the fifteen-minute parking space located in Lot 17 directly behind the Page-Robinson Building. Hours of Operation Academic Year: Monday - Friday, 8:30 am - 5:00 pm Summer: Monday - Friday, 8:00 am - 4:00 pm Transportation Management Plan In order to better meet the needs of everyone who visits, studies or works on campus, Brown is conducting a comprehensive analysis of all means of travel to, from and within campus. In fall 2019, Brown hired nationally-recognized sustainable campus transportation experts Nelson\Nygaard to develop a comprehensive transportation plan. Spring 2020 shifted priorities with the COVID-19 pandemic. The project was temporarily placed on hold and began anew in late summer 2021.

This position oversees a divisional operating budget of $78 million dollars with a focus on ensuring operational and financial resources advance the division’s priorities. Provides leadership and strategic planning for the Division of Campus Life with a focus on ensuring critical staffing, operational and financial resources and programs advance the division’s priorities. Proactively identifies and manages critical financial and operational issues within the division. Establishes and implements initiatives to ensure the departments and centers within the division are effectively using their financial resources.

Hullabaloo Hall is a state of the art facility focused on the integration of the social and academic experience. This facility offers a variety of living options, with convenient access to resources available on the first floor of the building. 2021 – 2022 Rates $4,607semester Double Room $6,158semester Single Room $4,607semester Double Suite $5,724semester Single Suite Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Room Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same room are the same gender. Only residents of the same gender share a restroom.

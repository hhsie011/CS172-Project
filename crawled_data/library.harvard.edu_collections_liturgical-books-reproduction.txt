The Isham Memorial Library supports scholarship in medieval and Renaissance chant and liturgy with its collection of pontificals, breviaries, graduals and tropers. The collection is particularly strong in materials which originated in France, Italy, Spain and Germany. The liturgical books in reproduction collection also contains reproductions of volumes produced in Guatemala, the Netherlands and the United Kingdom. Accessing These Materials Each liturgical book at Isham Memorial Library is cataloged in HOLLIS. This guide includes a complete list of the books by holding library.

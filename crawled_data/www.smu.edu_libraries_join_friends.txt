Guides Find Your Librarian Attend Workshops Writing Support ☰ Libraries Join Us Friends of SMU Libraries Friends of SMU Libraries Friends of SMU Libraries, originally founded in 1970 as Colophon, is dedicated to promoting and enriching the resources, services and operations of the seven SMU Libraries. Friends hosts a series of programs each year for book lovers to become acquainted with each other and the many resources of SMU Libraries. Friends of SMU Libraries is committed to helping the libraries maintain their excellence and has funded over $1,000,000 in materials and library services.

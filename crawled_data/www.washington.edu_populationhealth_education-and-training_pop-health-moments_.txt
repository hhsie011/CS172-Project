These spots realized nearly three million household views in markets covering much of the West Coast of the United States. Each “Pop Health Moment” is animated and takes a problem-solution focus to support individuals in formulating responses to pressing population health challenges. These spots drew from faculty expertise in the UW’s College of Arts & Sciences, College of Education, College of the Environment, Information School, School of Medicine and School of Public Health.

Event Kind: Training Organizer: Department of Environmental Health Sciences SHARP Invitation Limited to: Open to the Public Website: Learn More Learn More Join us for the next live-stream Climate Change and Health Boot Camp on June 16-17, 2022. The Climate Change and Health Boot Camp is a two-day intensive course that will prepare clinicians, scientists, and other members of the research, health, and public health communities for informed, effective engagement with climate change issues in their professional lives. Professionals at all career stages are welcome to attend, and we particularly encourage trainees and early-stage investigators to participate.

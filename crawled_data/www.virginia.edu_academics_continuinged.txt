We offer a variety of continuing and executive education programs, tailored to blend academic scholarship with workplace relevance. Offered throughout the University – including the School of Continuing & Professional Studies, Darden School of Business, McIntire School of Commerce, Curry School of Education and Human Development, School of Medicine, School of Nursing, School of Engineering and Applied Science, and School of Data Science – these programs meet students where they are and equip them with the tools and guidance to succeed.

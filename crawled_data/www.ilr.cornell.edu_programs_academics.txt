It contains notations of grades for all courses completed, giving course titles and numbers, semester and cumulative averages, semesters completed, etc. Graduate schools and academic programs usually require an official transcript. Current students can request a copy of their official transcript by clicking the transcript link found in student center. More information on official transcripts can be found on the Office of the University Registrar website. Academic Records Students may view their academic record by logging into their Student Center.

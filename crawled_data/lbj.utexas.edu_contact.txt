The LBJ School is in Sid Richardson Hall (SRH), a three-story white building between Red River Street and the large, white marble LBJ Library. The LBJ School is located in the northern half of Sid Richardson Hall. There are entrances at the middle of the school on the parking lot side of the building and on the southern end of the building, which corresponds to a breezeway that connects the parking lot to the plaza surrounding the LBJ Library. View a campus map. Elevators and a stairwell are located in the middle of the LBJ School. The Office of Student Affairs and Admissions is located on the first floor. The Dean's Office and the school's main receptionist are on the third floor.

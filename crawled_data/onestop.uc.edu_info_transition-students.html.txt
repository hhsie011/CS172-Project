Set yourself up for success, as limited aid sources run out quickly. Ensure University of Cincinnati (003125) is added. Search for scholarships using the Scholarship Search tile in Catalyst about 6 months in advance. There are Transition Scholarships that may be available. Review your student loan borrowing history. Compare this to the Lifetime Aggregate Federal Loan Limits to ensure you have enough aid to finish your degree. Review the cost differences in tuition and fees between your current program and the program you plan to transition to. Check your To Do List in Catalyst and complete any listed tasks. Create your financial aid portal account, if requested. You may be required in order to submit financial aid documentation to complete financial aid verification. Accept your financial aid in Catalyst using the Accept/Decline Financial Aid link in the My Finances tile. Review options for covering remaining or out-of-pocket costs. Check your student bill in Catalyst regularly and review due dates and payment options Waive university health insurance via Catalyst if you have other coverage. Review your residency status via the My Profile tile in Catalyst and submit residency application if necessary. Check your UC student email account regularly. Plan Ahead: Pay attention to important dates and deadlines each semester. Contact One Stop with billing, financial aid and registration questions.

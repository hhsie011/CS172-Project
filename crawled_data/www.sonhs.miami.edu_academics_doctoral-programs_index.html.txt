RELATED LINKS Simulation Hospital for Advancing Research and Education (SHARE™) Student Handbooks The School of Nursing and Health Studies offers two separate doctoral programs. Graduates of the DNP program are prepared to create innovation for nursing practice and health care. The PhD (Doctor of Philosophy) is a research-focused degree. Graduates of the PhD program are prepared to assume faculty roles in research and teaching.

Sexual Assault Safety Tips Digital Citizenship Weather Safety Rave Guardian Contact Us Campus Safety Our mission is to ensure that OSU Institute of Technology is a safe environment for students to learn, employees to work and guests to visit without concern for their personal safety. Oklahoma State University Institute of Technology understands students have the right to a safe campus community. OSUIT Campus Safety protects the persons and property of students, faculty, staff and visitors of OSUIT, assisting law enforcement and other emergency service providers as needed, by being the first responder to emergencies. OSUIT Campus Safety is made up of the Campus Police Department, and various groups including the Behavioral Consultation Team, Crisis Management Team & Emergency Management, and Inclement Weather Team.

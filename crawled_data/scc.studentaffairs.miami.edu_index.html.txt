Learn more > COVID-19 RESPONSE LEARN MORE ABOUT OUR RESPONSE TO THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC COVID-19 RESPONSE LEARN MORE ABOUT OUR RESPONSE TO THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC The Heart of the U The Student Center Complex (SCC) is the place on campus for events, meetings, retail, and dining. The SCC is the community center of the University as it provides space for students to study, socialize, and engage in student life and for department offices. The SCC’s double-Olympic sized pool provides an environment for conversation, study or sunbathing. The wide range and versatility of the SCC’s facilities allow for a variety of student involvement, programs, and services.

By creating programs through which faculty and students can join forces with scientists, scholars, and artists in the region and far beyond, Brown has created a whole series of new and innovative research opportunities and degree and exchange programs. Partners range from the Rhode Island School of Design and Trinity Repertory Theatre to National University of Singapore, University of Capetown, and University of Porto. Partnerships & Exchanges Links Exchange Scholar Program Brown participates in an Exchange Scholar Program that enables advanced graduate students to study for one or two semesters in the graduate school of participating institutions. CUHK Teaching and Research Opportunities A PhD Student Exchange Program between Brown University and The Chinese University of Hong Kong offers advanced doctoral students opportunities for short-term research visits at the partner university. Neuroscience-NIH Program An opportunity for collaborative research in neuroscience with scientists at the National Institutes of Health. Mathematics-TFA Partnership The Department of Mathematics offers a special deferment option for Teach for America applicants. International Partnerships Brown has various international partnerships in a number of different countries.

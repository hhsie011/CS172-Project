This year’s summit will once again be held virtually and is FREE to attend. Rahul Gupta, M.D., MPH, MBA, FACP, director of the Office of National Drug Control Policy, will be delivering the keynote address. In addition to Gupta’s address, the summit programming will feature sessions on the addiction stigma related to harm reduction, the intersectionality of drug policy and racial justice, and substance use treatment featuring exciting and innovative speakers who are leading the work to reduce and dismantle the stigma of addiction.

TUC is a flexible option for small-medium sized events and conferences. Room Set-up Descriptions Meeting rooms may be set up in several standard configurations, based on placement of tables and chairs. Lecture style - chairs in rows with a speaker table at the front of the room Classroom style - tables with chairs in rows facing the speaker table at the front of the room Conference style - tables in a square with chairs on the outside of the square Rounds - round tables that seat 7-9 people as specified U-Shape - tables placed in a U, with the opening at the front of the room, where a speaker generally stands Tables for career or job fair - tables placed in various configurations to allow people to walk and stand between rows Tables around perimeter - tables placed along the outer edges of the room Rooms 400 A, B and C Room 400 is one very large room that is made up of three sections, A, B and C. Room dividers allow flexible use of the space as three individual rooms, or one large and one medium-sized room.

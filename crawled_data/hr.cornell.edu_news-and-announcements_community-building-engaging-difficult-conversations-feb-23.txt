Community Building: Engaging in Difficult Conversations Tuesday, February 23, 2021 at 12:00pm to 1:00pm Virtual Event Join the Employee Assembly and key leaders from across campus on Tuesday, February 23, 2021 for Community Building: Engaging in Difficult Conversations. Staff want to find continued ways to support the diverse population and engage in community building. How do we engage in difficult conversations with those around us, in a respectful and productive manner, especially when we share differing and perhaps emotionally charged perspectives? Panelists include: Mary Opperman, Vice President and Chief Human Resources Officer Angela C. Winfield, J.D., Associate Vice President for Inclusion and Workforce Diversity Reginald H. White, Senior Human Resources Director, Research Division and Employee-Elected Trustee Dr. Nelson Tebbe, Ph.D., J.D., Jane M.G.

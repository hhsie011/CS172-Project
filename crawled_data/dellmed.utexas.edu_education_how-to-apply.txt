Information for Applicants Admission Criteria To identify future physician leaders, we look for evidence of excellence in four areas: mission contribution, personal attributes, life experiences and academic ability. Application Details Apply using the Texas Medical & Dental Schools Application Service. The complete process includes a secondary application and interviews. Timeline Dell Med accepts applications from May 2 to Nov. 1 and reviews them on an ongoing basis. Interviews begin in early September and conclude in February.

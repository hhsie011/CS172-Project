Submit an Event Start Smart Salary Negotiation Wednesday, May 11, 2022 10:00 am - 12:00 pm Over the course of her working life, a woman earns roughly $1 million less than a man. (Statistics vary when race is being factored.) Women, Gender & Sexuality Studies is hosting a $tart $mart workshop that prepares college women who are approaching the job market with the confidence, knowledge, and skills they need to negotiate salaries and benefits. Workshop is free for UH students. Content is focused on those who identify or have been socialized as women; seniors and graduates students are strongly encouraged to attend.

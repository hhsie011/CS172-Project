Frosh/Transfer 101 is a discussion-style course designed to help first-year students transition to Stanford's dynamic campus. In Frosh/Transfer 101, approximately 10 first year students will come together each week to learn more about your peers, yourself, and who you will be in this new environment. Upperclass students will lead you through activities and discussions that will serve to build the foundation for a strong community that is inclusive of diversity and promotes emotional and social wellbeing. This course will also create space for you to reflect on your values and goals as you make important decisions about how you will navigate all that Stanford has to offer.

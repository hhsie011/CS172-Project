You’ll gain essential knowledge and techniques—from computer modeling to coding—and build qualifications for career success in a variety of game-related settings. Learn how the Game Design certificate is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Game design students learn how to work with a team to design characters, levels and storylines. Curriculum To complete the 15-credit-hour undergraduate certificate, you’ll take the following courses: Computer Modeling Game Design Concepts Game Design I Coding One of the following: Game Design II, Animation I, Coding II, or ARTS 240 Life Drawing. View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Time: Postponed until further notice Location: Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Join us for a reception at Columbia Global Centers | Beijing to discover Columbia Engineering! This information session is offered to help prepare students to submit and complete their application to a graduate degree program at Columbia University's Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science. We invite you to learn more about the Columbia Engineering application process, our exciting MS and Ph.D. programs, and meet alumni and representatives from our Graduate Admissions team. As this event will be in-person, you must adhere to the COVID-19 protocols listed below: Complete temperature checking before entering the Beijing Center. Present proof of health status and only enter if a green code is shown on a local color-coded health system on your smartphone. If you are from a foreign country, provide a “Health Kit” before entering. Remain at least 1 meter from others when removing your face covering to eat or drink.

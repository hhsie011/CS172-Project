Aerospace Studies Det 770, Home of the Flyin' Tigers "Commission High Performing Air Force Officers" Detachment 770 is an Air Force ROTC (AFROTC) educational/training program located at Clemson University in Clemson, S.C. The goal of DET 770 is to prepare students to become officers in the United States Air Force with a possible opportunity for entry into the emerging United States Space Force. This Detachment is partnered with several cross-town universities and colleges that work closely to support students from Anderson University, Bob Jones University, Southern Wesleyan University and Tri-County Technical College.

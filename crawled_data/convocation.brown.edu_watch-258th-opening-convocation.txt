Brown University celebrated the start of the 2021-22 academic year with the 258th Opening Convocation on Monday, September 6, 2021. President Christina H. Paxson welcomed the newest Brunonians to campus, and Chair and Professor of Africana Studies Noliwe Rooks delivered the event’s keynote address. Brown’s 258th Opening Convocation President Christina H. Paxson, Professor of Africana Studies Noliwe Rooks and other Brown leaders welcomed incoming undergraduate, graduate and medical students on Monday, September 6.

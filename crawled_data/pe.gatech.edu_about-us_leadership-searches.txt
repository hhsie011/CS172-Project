Open Searches Associate Dean, Academic Affairs Georgia Tech Professional Education is seeking qualified candidates for an Associate Dean of Academic Affairs. This is an internal search open to active faculty employees at Georgia Tech. This position reports to the Dean of Professional Education. View Job Description About the Position Interested candidates may view the job posting with the following steps: Log into OneUSG Connect Select the "Recruiting Self Service" tile Select the "Apply for Jobs" button Select "View All Jobs" Search for Job ID 237373 Additionally, this USG Knowledge Article provides step-by-step instructions for applying for internal jobs as an employee of an institution. How to Apply Please use the OneUSG process described above, or Application, CV, and three references may be emailed to Dr. Steve Harmon, GTPE Associate Dean for Research at swharmon@gatech.edu with the subject line: Associate Dean for Academic Affairs Search. Review of applications will begin after February 18, 2022 and continue until the position is filled.

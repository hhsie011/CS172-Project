Frequently Asked Questions OSRHE Policy Initial In-State/Out-of-State Classification If you feel that your initial classification is incorrect, you can complete the Petition for In-State Status and return it to the Registrar's Office. While we recommend that you complete the petition before the beginning of your first semester, the final deadlines are as follows: Fall Semester - October 31 Spring Semester - March 31 Summer Semester - June 30 Please note that in-state reclassification (and associated in-state tuition) is not granted on a retroactive basis.

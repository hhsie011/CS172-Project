Current Crime Information & Trends from the N.Y.P.D. One of the ways in which the N.Y.P.D. provides updates regarding crime trends and other relevant information is through Twitter. Every N.Y.P.D precinct has a twitter feed containing important information. You can access this information for the various Columbia areas as follows: @NYPD24Pct Covers south of West 110 Street @NYPD26Pct Covers the Morningside and Manhattanville Campuses @NYPD33Pct Covers the CUIMC Campus @NYPD34Pct Covers the Baker Field Sports Complex The N.Y.P.D. also maintains current crime data on its website. The data is updated weekly and is presented for all the 77 precincts in New York City.

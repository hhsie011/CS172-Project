Hospitality Management – Online Overview | Curriculum | International Online | Careers | Tuition & Fees | Contact Us A degree built for the future of hospitality With a track record of producing professionals capable of leading the hospitality industry into the future, Oregon State’s hospitality management online bachelor’s degree program will equip you with the innovative tools and real-world experience you need to accomplish your entrepreneurial goals. Oregon State online hospitality management instructor Todd Montgomery shares his passion and process for creating engaging experiences from a distance. Watch and read » The hospitality industry is seeking new college graduates who can go directly into management and thrive in their jobs on their first day of work. To meet that demand, this program focuses on developing skills in service, operations management, hospitality technology, food and beverage operations, concerts and sporting event management, as well as basic business fundamentals in marketing and accounting. Offered by Oregon State’s College of Business and initially developed at OSU-Cascades, the hospitality management program is connected to numerous industry partners including world-class resorts, restaurants, brew pubs and hospitality technology organizations. Oregon State Ecampus delivers a variety of degree programs online that are focused on business and management. Explore similar programs. Join the nation’s #4 ranked program Oregon State is a national leader in online education, and here’s one piece of evidence: Based on academic quality, this program is ranked No. 4 in the country by U.S. News & World Report in the category of Best Online Bachelor’s in Business Programs. A program rooted in innovation Hospitality is the world’s largest employment sector, generating nearly 200 million jobs globally. But the industry is changing. Many hospitality jobs of today won’t exist in 10 years. Through innovative approaches and coursework that is designed to meet workforce development needs nationwide, Oregon State’s hospitality management program offers the skills and knowledge you need to take on a leadership role in the industry.

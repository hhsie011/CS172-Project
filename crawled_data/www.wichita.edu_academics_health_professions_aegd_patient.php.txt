Our patients receive comprehensive dental care from licensed resident dentists and faculty members in our state-of-the-art facility. COVID-19 update: Safety and health are our top priority and we have put extra safety measures in place. When scheduling an appointment, patients will be advised on updated protocols and what to expect during your next appointment. If you or a family member have a fever, cough, or are not feeling well, please call to reschedule your appointment.

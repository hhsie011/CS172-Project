Science – Andrew Connolly Joe Giffels Sr. Associate Vice Provost for Research Administration and Integrity jgiffels@uw.edu 206 616-0804 Gerberding Hall G80 Box 351202 As Sr. Associate Vice Provost for Research Compliance, Joe Giffels serves as the University’s point person for Research Compliance, working to coordinate the efforts of the many compliance operations campus-wide, encouraging increased communication and integration of activities intended to promote research integrity. He is also the Institutional Official responsible for financial conflict of interest and manages the outside professional work request process. Joe graduated from Olivet College in Michigan with degrees in Chemistry and Biology. He spent the first years of his career at the lab bench in the Neurovirology Laboratories at the Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine before moving into research administration and compliance. Mr. Giffels has experience on both sides of sponsored research administration, serving as Director of Research and Professional Education for the Epilepsy Foundation of America in Washington, DC and as Assistant Administrator for University Affairs for the Departments of Neurology and Neurosurgery at Johns Hopkins. With expertise in clinical trial management, research data management, and the peer review process, he has published papers on several Responsible Conduct of Research topics and is a reviewer for the journal of Science and Engineering Ethics. As an administrator, he has concentrated in the areas of research involving humans, research involving animals, conflict of interest in research, research misconduct, and training in the Responsible Conduct of Research. He came to the UW following several years as Assistant Vice President for Academic Affairs at the University of Maryland Baltimore and Director of that University’s Research Integrity Office.

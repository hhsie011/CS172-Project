Small Group Admission Non-profit small groups (5 -20 participants) may request general admission into Campus Recreation. Requests should be made 5 business days in advance. Groups will be sharing facility areas with others on a first-come, first-serve basis. Group size in any one area may be limited. Campus Recreation Center staffing for supervisory needs or requested instruction will be arranged at an additional cost.

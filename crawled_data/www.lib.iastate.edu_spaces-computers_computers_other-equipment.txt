Other Equipment Audiovisual equipment The Media Center has audiovisual equipment. Pencil sharpeners Pencil sharpeners and staplers are located at the Main Desk and in the Media Center. A pencil sharpener is located next to the elevator on the Lower Level. There are pencil sharpeners, a 3-hole punch and staplers in the Learning Connections Center near the Laser Printers. Change machine A change machine is not available at this time due to COVID-19. To obtain change for bills of larger denominations, ask at the Main Desk. Telephone A free public phone is located on Lower Level of the Parks Library, at the bottom of the main, circular stairwell. This phone can be used to make free local calls, as well as card-based long distance calls.

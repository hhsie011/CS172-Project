It employs more than 300 undergradutes each semester, and every one of these students is given the chance to effect change, working closely with the professor, other UTAs, and other students. UTAs and non-UTAs alike can use this page to better understand the UTA program, what it has to offer, and what a UTA has the opportunity to do. As of Spring 2022, the Meta TAs are Ashley Chon, Rachel Ma, and Shenandoah Duraideivamani.

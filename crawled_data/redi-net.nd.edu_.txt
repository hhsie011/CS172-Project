Remote Emerging Disease Intelligence-NETwork A system-of-systems to address emerging infectious disease surveillance needs. The overarching aim of the Remote Emerging Disease Intelligence-NETwork (REDI-NET) is to develop a collaborative network among domestic and international partnering institutions to address surveillance needs to effectively DETECT, PREDICT and CONTAIN potentially emergent zoonosis of human relevance and improve the accuracy and timeliness of the ‘data-to decision’ pipeline.

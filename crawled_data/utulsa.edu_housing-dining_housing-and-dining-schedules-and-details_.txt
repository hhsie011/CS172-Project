All rates are based on the dates published in these schedules, which may be amended from time to time pursuant to the housing and dining agreement. Those approved to arrive earlier than or stay after published dates will be charged on a daily basis. Please review the FIRST AND SECOND YEAR HOUSING & DINING REQUIREMENT Cancelation Requests All cancelation requests may be submitted through housing self-service. Please be prepared to upload supporting documentation. DECEMBER GRADUATION – In order for the graduating resident to qualify for release at the end of the fall term without incurring liquidated damages, the resident must not be enrolled for the spring semester and must complete the cancellation request form through housing self-service by NOVEMBER 1, 2022. Residents vacating under this provision must do so by 3:00 PM on the Sunday following the fall commencement date. Housing Schedule for Residence Halls Housing Schedule for Residence Halls (Fisher South, Lottie Jane, John Mabee, Hardesty, LaFortune, & West Suites): Spring 2022 Term: Opens Jan. 7, 2022 (9:00 am) Closes May 8, 2022 (3:00 pm) Academic Year 2022-2023 Fall 2022 Term: Opens Aug. 20, 2022 (8:00am) Incoming Students may arrive according to the StartTU Arrival Date Closes December 16, 2022* (3:00pm) Closed Winter Break Spring 2023 Term: Opens Jan. 6, 2023 (9:00am) Closes May 7, 2023 (3:00pm) *Students approved to cancel their spring semester must vacate and return the room key by December 16, 2022 (3:00 pm) Housing Schedule for Greek Housing Owned by the University Housing Schedule for Greek Housing Owned by the University (Sorority Row & Sigma Nu): Spring 2022 Term: Open Jan. 7, 2022 (9:00am) Closes for Spring Break – March 12, 2022 (12:00pm) Reopens March 20, 2022 (9:00am) Closes May 8, 2022 (3:00pm) Academic Year 2022-2023 Fall 2022 Term: Opens Aug. 16, 2022 (8:00am) Tentatively Closes for Fall Break November 19, 2022 (12:00pm) Reopens November 27, 2022 (9:00am) Closes December 16, 2022* (3:00pm) Closed Winter Break Spring 2023 Term: Opens Jan. 6, 2023 (9:00am) Closes for Spring Break – March 11, 2023 (12:00pm) Reopens March 19, 2023 (9:00am) Closes May 7, 2023 (3:00pm) *Students approved to cancel their spring semester must vacate and return the room key by December 16, 2022 (3:00 pm) Housing Schedule for University Apartments Housing Schedule for University Apartments: Early move-in requests and extensions must be made electronically through the Apartment Pre-Checkin and Pre-Checkout system and are billed at the daily rate for the associated term. Early move-ins and extensions are granted based on the availability of the specific assigned unit. The schedule below notes start and end dates: Spring 2022 Term: Starts January 8, 2022 (9:00am) – Students may request to arrive early, via the Apartment pre-check-in process Ends May 8, 2022 (Deadline to vacate is 3:00pm, if not continuing for Summer 2022) Summer 2022 Term*: Summer Storage Starts for continuing Students – May 9, 2022 (3:00pm) New Apartment Residents (including storage) Start – May 21, 2022 Apartment Switchers must change apartments as agreed. *Please note: Students in storage that choose to return mid-summer, pay the daily rate to the start of the Fall term. Please ask someone in the housing office for your summer costs before splitting your summer between living and storage. Academic Year 2022-2023 Fall 2022 Term: Starts August 19, 2022 (9:00 am) – Students may request to arrive early, via the Apartment pre-check-in process Open Winter break for continuing residents* Spring 2023 Term: Starts January 6, 2023 (9:00am) – Students may request to arrive early, via the Apartment pre-check-in process Ends May 9, 2023 (Deadline to vacate is 3:00pm, if not continuing for Summer 2023) *Students approved to cancel their spring semester must vacate and return the room key by December 16, 2022 (3:00 pm) Housing Schedule for Summer School Residence Hall Housing Schedule for Summer School Residence Hall Summer 2022 Term: Starts May 8, 2022 (9:00 am) – Residents must confirm their move-in date/time with housing as spaces are based on availability. Ends August 6, 2022 (3:00 pm) – Fall spaces may be available for immediate move-in, please check with housing for questions.

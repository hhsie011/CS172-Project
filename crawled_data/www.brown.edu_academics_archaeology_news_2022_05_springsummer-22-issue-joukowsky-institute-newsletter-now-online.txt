The newsletter is attached and is also now available online. It features stories on two recent webinar series organized by Institute faculty members, two exhibits installed in Rhode Island Hall, recent doctoral dissertations and undergraduate honors theses, faculty publications, an online tour of New Bedford, and our excavation and fieldschool in Providence, RI.

Cooper Library 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM ▾ Special Collections and Archives: By Appointment Gunnin Architecture Library: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM Education Media Center: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM MENUMENU My Account Giving Help GIS Fundamentals Summer Workshop Series starts May 19 Posted on May 13, 2022 This summer, the Clemson Center for Geospatial Technologies (CCGT) will be hosting free workshops for all Clemson students, faculty, and staff! This means you! Come with us as we take you through the world of GIS and Data Visualization. All workshops will take place online on Thursdays and Fridays between 1:00-4:00 PM. If you attend seven of our eight core workshops, you will receive a Certificate of Attendance for GIS Fundamentals, and if you attend our final two Data Visualization workshops you will receive a Certificate of Attendance for our Data Viz fundamentals. To register, go here: bit.ly/ccgtworkshops . Want to know more? Go to our website here! Still have questions? Email ccgt@nullclemson.edu.

Records Benefits for Faculty and Staff Florida State University offers employees the opportunity to participate in a variety of benefit programs. It is mandatory for all employees to participate in one of three retirement plans. All insurance programs are voluntary and include pre-tax and post-tax options. People First is the Benefits Administrator for the State of Florida pre-tax insurance plans and is responsible for new hire enrollments, coverage changes, premium administration, and dependent eligibility, among many others. Insurance Benefits & Perks Retirement Benefit Summary The Benefits Summary provides an overview of available insurance, retirement, paid time off, and additional benefits to FSU employees. Benefits Summary The online New Employee Orientation (NEO) is available for employees to review all available insurance, retirement, and additional benefits. Employee Insurance Eligibility To comply with the federally-mandated provisions of the 2010 Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, Florida State University offers State of Florida Group Health Insurance coverage as an optional benefit for all qualified OPS employees. The coverage is subject to change based on state and federal legislation. Employees who meet the measuring guidelines, including graduate assistants and adjunct faculty, will be notified by People First to elect coverage.

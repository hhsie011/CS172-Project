Truett Seminary>Admissions>Visit Truett>Virtual Visit Virtual Visit At Truett Seminary, we want to ensure that you are able to receive the information you need about our seminary as you are exploring your future in theological education. Though we wish we could welcome you to our campus, we are excited to introduce a number of virtual opportunities to connect with our faculty, staff, and students and to get your admissions-related questions answered. In addition, prospective students are welcome to reach out directly to a Student Recruitment Team Associate. Check back frequently as new opportunities become available! Individual Virtual Appointments Easily set up a one-on-one virtual visit with a Truett recruitment associate to get your Truett admissions questions answered. Both prospective and accepted students are welcome to set up an appointment. Register Here Virtual Information Sessions Virtual Information Sessions are topic-specific opportunities to hear from a Truett staff member and get your questions answered. Session topics include Academics, Admissions, Scholarships and Financial Aid, and Joint Degree Programs. Learn More and Register Virtual Tour During this 5-minute virtual tour, you will get a glimpse of the beautiful Baugh-Reynolds Campus while also learning more about the Truett experience. Watch Now Virtual Resources This short video series introduces viewers to a few of the people and programs that make Truett Seminary unique. Learn about life in Waco, spiritual formation, the Truett faculty, and more.

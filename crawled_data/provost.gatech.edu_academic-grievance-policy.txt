The purpose of this site is to serve as a reference for many of the questions students have. Students should first discuss any concerns with the relevant faculty member; if it is not possible to come to resolution with the faculty member, students may then report the matter to the appropriate administrator (Chair or Associate Chair or Director of Studies) of the department of instruction or report it here: Report a Grievance In general, the Catalog governs all academic policies at Georgia Tech. You may consult this page for general regulations. For more specific academic rules and regulations, please consult this catalog page. In particular, you will find policies there about the following, as well as much more: Calendar Attendance Examinations Grades Withdrawal and Readmission Academic Honor Code Student Code of Conduct Faculty-Student Expectations Grievance Procedures If you need formal assistance, please contact Dr. Kyla Ross, Assistant Vice Provost for Advocacy and Conflict Resolution. For informal assistance or to speak to someone who can be a sounding board for you, please contact Ombuds staff member Russ Callen.

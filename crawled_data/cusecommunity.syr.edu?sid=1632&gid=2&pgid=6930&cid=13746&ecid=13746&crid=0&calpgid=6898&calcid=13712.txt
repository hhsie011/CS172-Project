students and friends of Syracuse University to connect with each other and stay connected to our University. We often hold game watches, attend local sporting events, participate in professional development and networking opportunities, host a new student send-off and participate in community service projects. If you have ideas/suggestions for an activity/event, please email the club, and please join us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter. GO ORANGE!

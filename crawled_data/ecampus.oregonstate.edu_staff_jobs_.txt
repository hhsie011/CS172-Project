Start comparison » Print Home  ›  Staff  ›  Join Our Team Join Our Team About Oregon State Ecampus Oregon State University Ecampus is a national leader in online education, backed by Oregon State's 150-plus years of institutional and research excellence. As members of the OSU Ecampus staff, we engage in innovative and creative partnerships with OSU faculty to provide access to exceptional learning experiences that transform the lives of students in Oregon and around the world. Our highly collaborative team is committed to helping Oregon State's distance learners achieve success; to making learning more accessible and affordable for everyone; and to conducting award-winning research on online teaching and learning.

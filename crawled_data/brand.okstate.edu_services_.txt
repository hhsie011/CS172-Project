Ads Advertising is more than just flyers and billboards. Our advertising team makes sure that your department can communicate your message to the people who want to hear it. Content and Editorial Our content and editorial team is available to assist in creating engaging and appealing content that captivates your target audiences. Whether you need help with writing or editing, we can make your content stand out. Design Our team of graphic designers can take your idea on paper and turn it into a masterpiece that is ready for prime time in print or digital media. Email Distribution Our team oversees a robust email marketing strategy, sending emails to the entire OSU system on a weekly basis. See how you can get placement in one of these or help with your email marketing strategy. Media Relations Our team provides a vast number of services to the university and works closely with journalists and media outlets to publicize student, faculty and staff expertise and accomplishments. Open Records OSU is subject to the provisions of the Oklahoma Open Records Act, which ensures and facilitates the public’s right of access to and review of government records. Our new system launched in 2021 to streamline service and the delivery of those records. Photography See the world through our lens. Our team of talented and experienced photographers will help capture the moment through high-quality images. Social Media Meet your audience where they already are with strategic social media placement. Our team can promote and share your content to reach specific, key audiences. Trademarks and Licensing Our office exists to ensure proper use of our logos and other marks. Find out how to correctly use our marks per federal, state, and common laws. Vendors Create high-quality and diverse print materials and promotional products by using our vendor services. With access to multiple vendors, you are sure to find the right vendor to fit your project needs. Video Inside OSU has content from the entire OSU community, all under one roof. From live events to yearly events like Homecoming and Commencement, Inside OSU is the place to find everything OSU. Web We offer help with website content creation if you are in the beginning stages of your website. Once launched, find out what resources we offer to help you keep your site up to date.

Cogut Institute Fellowships The Cogut Institute offers faculty, graduate, and undergraduate fellowships for members of the Brown University community interested in joining the institute’s research seminar. The seminar also includes visiting faculty appointed by the Cogut Institute and postdoctoral fellows appointed jointly by the Cogut Institute and a hosting department. Undergraduate Fellowship Graduate Fellowship Faculty Fellowship Postdoctoral Fellowship Program Fellows Seminar Fellows of all ranks and disciplines meet every Tuesday during the academic year for a seminar from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm, followed by lunch. In this collegial environment, fellows present and discuss work-in-progress. The composition of the seminar varies from year to year, based on application submissions, the review process, and award acceptance. Learn more Collaborative Humanities Curriculum The Collaborative Humanities Initiative fosters collaboration in teaching, research, and learning at the faculty, graduate, and undergraduate levels. The Collaborative Humanities Course Award supports undergraduate curricular innovation across disciplines, divisions, or schools. The Cogut Collaborative Humanities Fellowship supports graduate students at any stage of their pursuit of the Doctoral Certificate in Collaborative Humanities.

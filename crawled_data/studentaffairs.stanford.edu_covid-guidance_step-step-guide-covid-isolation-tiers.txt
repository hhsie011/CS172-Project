The easy step-by-step guide outlined on each COVID tier below will quickly help you navigate our current status and isolation guidance. Whether you are isolating in-place or placed in Stanford-provided isolation housing, all students should: Complete Health Check to inform the university, receive your 90-day green Health Check badge and Color testing exemption. Send a copy of your positive COVID test results (attached to a message with the subject line "secure: test results") to the "COVID Medical Care Team" through vadenpatient.stanford.edu, for medical care and future isolation and travel clearance. Varsity athletes should instead contact the Sports Medicine team. Submit a SNOW ticket to request resources for isolation and meal delivery options. Stanford is currently in: Blue Tier Please reference the step-by-step tiers guide below for further details.

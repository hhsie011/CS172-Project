The visitor rate for both garages is $3.00 per hour with a $15.00 per day maximum. Metered Parking: There are metered spaces and lots located all around campus for a rate of $2.00 per hour. Meters accept coin and major credit cards. There is no time limit. <!-- intentionally non-empty iframe; for xsl --> Metered Spaces: Lot 44, Lot 39, Lot 15, Lot 6, Lot 5, and Lot 2. Visitor Parking Permit: Visitors may purchase a parking permit online for meters or general lots by visiting the TigerPark Guest Account Login. This permit does not allow parking in the garages. PDF Tutorial for Purchasing a Visitor Permit Online One Day Visitor Permit for General Permit Parking is $3.00 Visitor permits may also be purchased in Parking and Transportation Services located at 505 Zach Curlin Parking Garage. If you have any questions, please contact PTS at 901.678.2212 or parking@memphis.edu. Emeriti Parking: Emeriti Parking is for retirees of the University of Memphis. The Emeriti Permit (hangtag) allows the retiree to park in any General Parking lot or at any metered space at no cost. Visitor's Citation Appeal Form: Visitors may appeal parking citations online by visiting the TigerPark Guest Account Login, by e-mailing parking@memphis.edu or by submitting an appeal form to PTS located at 505 Zach Curlin Parking Garage.

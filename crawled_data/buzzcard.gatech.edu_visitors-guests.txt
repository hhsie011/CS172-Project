People who need to access certain services at GT, but do not hold a traditional position that would qualify for a BuzzCard, can use the Visitor/Guest card instead! Who may need a Visitor/Guest card (when properly sponsored): Visiting professors Visiting scholars Guest lecturers Conferees Temporary employees Vendors Contractors (see guidance below) Sponsored visitors and guests will require the following to have a BuzzCard processed: GTID# Photo ID (government issued state ID/driver’s license or passport) Payment (if guest is responsible for payment; credit/debit only) In order to obtain the GTID#, a request must be made by the sponsoring Georgia Tech department. To obtain a BuzzCard for an Affiliate: 1. Affiliates are processed through GT Human Resources and undergo background screening. 2. A GTID will be generated for the affiliate. Human Resources will be notified when the requested GTID has been generated. 3. Human Resources can notify the Affiliate that they can now visit the BuzzCard center for photo session and BuzzCard production during normal BuzzCard Center hours. 4. The Building Manager will assign and maintain door access privileges to the Affiliate BuzzCard, as appropriate. To obtain a BuzzCard for a Contractor: 1. The Department that hires the contractor service must request a GTID number for each individual who will deliver services on campus. Each department has an assigned GTID Requestor that is authorized to request a GTID here. 2. The GTID Requestor will be notified when the requested GTID has been generated. 3. The GTID Requestor or the person requesting the contractor service can notify the Contractor that they can visit the BuzzCard center for photo session and BuzzCard production during normal BuzzCard Center hours. The cost of the Contractor BuzzCard is $30. 4. The Building Manager will assign and maintain door access privileges to the Contractor BuzzCard, as appropriate. Contact the BuzzCard Office with questions about obtaining a BuzzCard for your campus guests.

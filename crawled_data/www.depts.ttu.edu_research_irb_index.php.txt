Texas Tech's Institutional Review Board (IRB) is charged with reviewing all human subjects research in accordance with federal regulations, state laws, and local and University policies (TTU OP 74.09). The Human Research Protection Program (HRPP) serves as a liaison to the IRB and point of contact for participants and researchers. IRB faculty reviewers will not review expedited and limited submissions between May 17-30, 2022 while faculty are off duty.

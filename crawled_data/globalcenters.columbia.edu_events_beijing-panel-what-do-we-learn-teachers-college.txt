Events Past Event Beijing Panel: What Do We Learn at Teachers College? Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo July 22, 2018 3:30 PM - 4:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Event Brief On July 22, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to a panel discussion featuring Columbia alumni from Teachers College, Columbia University. They will be sharing their experiences with TC and their opinions on how to be a qualified educator who coordinates the educational, psychological, behavioral, technological, and health initiatives to remove barriers to learning at all ages. Speakers Cincy Lin, Ed.D in the program of Adult Learning and Leadership at Teachers College, Columbia University, Teaching Assistant at Teachers College, M.A in Adult Learning and Leadership at Columbia University, and B.A. in Adult and Organizational Development at Temple University Nancy Shi, M.A. in International Educational Development at Teachers College, Columbia University, Member of the National Association for College Admission Counseling (NACAC), Member of the Independent Education Consultant Association (IECA), Research Assistant at the Center on Chinese Education (CoCE), Teachers College Columbia University Yuan, graduated from Teachers College, Columbia University, ESL and PBL teacher at Moonshot Academy Moderator Hailong Wang, Former Officer at the Ministry of Commerce of PRC, former CEO at Dongfang Jianyi, Career Development Consultant at the High School Affiliated to Renmin University of China, Beijing No. 8 Middle School, Peking Middle School and other famous middle schools in Beijing Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code of confirmation. Please present the QR code at the entrance. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

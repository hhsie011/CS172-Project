IT governance provides strategic leadership, establishes campus-wide IT priorities and policies in accordance with the University Strategic Plan. General responsibilities of it governance committees The IT governance structure as a whole is responsible for the following: Establishing and communicating a campus-wide IT vision that supports the University Strategic Plan Establishing IT policies that support strategic, campus-wide IT priorities Establishing an overall IT budget structure for total IT spend on campus Defining technical architecture and standards Establishing best practices and tools for IT Established IT Governance Committees IT Advisory Committee Senate Information Technology Committee Information Technology Technical Architecture Committee (TAC) This site is maintained by Division of Information Technology.

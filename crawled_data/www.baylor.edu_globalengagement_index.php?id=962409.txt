International students/scholars and U.S. students and families have been connecting and building lasting relationships for many years. We hear stories from both international students and their student friends and families that participating in the GFP has been a rewarding experience. GFP Brochure Guidelines & Requirements ALL Participating Students and Scholars must... Be affiliated with Baylor University while applying for GFP Have intention to remain in program for at least one semester once matched Be open and willing to engage in new experiences, and the sharing

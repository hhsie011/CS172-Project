Advanced knowledge and strong values come together at the Creighton Graduate School. As a student, you will learn from highly respected and personally attentive faculty members. Our diverse programs are designed to enhance your skills and create professional opportunities. The Graduate School is outcomes oriented. Our enrollment specialists will work with your education budget and daily schedule to help you take tangible steps toward your career goals. Both with online or on-campus programs, the Creighton community is dedicated to preparing you for what’s next in your life — no matter how busy you are now.

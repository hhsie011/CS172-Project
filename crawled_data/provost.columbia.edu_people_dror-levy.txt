He is responsible for all aspects of office facilities, operations, and finance, and is part of the team that administers the New York Anti-Sexual Harassment Training for the University. Before joining the Office of the Provost, Dror worked at Harvard University, most recently as the Facilities and Business Operations Coordinator for Harvard University IT. Dror received his bachelor's degree in Business Administration from the University of Florida and master's degree in Policy, Planning & Administration from Boston University. In his free time, Dror enjoys baking all manner of breads and going for long walks in Riverside Park with his wife and two dogs.

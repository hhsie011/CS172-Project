Leahy, S.J., will celebrate the annual Mass of the Holy Spirit on Sept. 6 at noon in Conte Forum. Members of the Jesuit communities and other priests from the University will concelebrate. All members of the University community are invited to attend the Mass, a tradition for the opening of the school year at Jesuit institutions dating back to the Middle Ages. Classes are cancelled that day from noon to 1:15 p.m. Vice President for University Mission and Ministry Jack Butler, S.J., will be the homilist; music will be provided by BC's Liturgy Arts Group. The Mass of the Holy Spirit is organized by the Office of Campus Ministry in the Division of University Mission and Ministry. —Kathleen Sullivan | University Communications Back To Top Back To Top Accessibility Emergency Contact Notice of Nondiscrimination Copyright © 2022 Trustees of Boston College

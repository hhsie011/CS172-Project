Print Degree Planner (opens a new window) | Print-Friendly Page (opens a new window) Facebook this Page (opens a new window) Tweet this Page (opens a new window) (42 UNITS) The Master of Fine Arts in Screenwriting in Cinema and Television Arts is a two-year program consisting of 14 courses. Of these, 10 are devoted to scriptwriting and four relate to the professional industry. The program culminates with a portfolio of master-level scripts. Required Courses (36 units) CTVA 512 - American Television History (3) CTVA 515 - Film Theory and Criticism (3) CTVA 520 - Advanced Scene Study (3) CTVA 550 - Story Structure and Draft (3) CTVA 551 - Sitcom: Spec and Draft (3) CTVA 552 - One-Hour Drama: TV Spec and Pilot (3) CTVA 553T - Genres for Writers (3) CTVA 555 - First Draft Screenplay (3) CTVA 556 - Screenplay: Rewrite (3) CTVA 580 - Writing Project #1 (3) CTVA 581 - Writing Project #2 (3) CTVA 597 - Final Project (3) Electives (6 units) CTVA 435 - Directing Film and TV (3) CTVA 459 - Adaptation for Screenwriters (3) CTVA 480 - Management in CTVA (3) CTVA 562 - Media Literacy and Ethics: The Biz (3) Total (42 units) Back to Top | Print Degree Planner (opens a new window) | Print-Friendly Page (opens a new window) Facebook this Page (opens a new window) Tweet this Page (opens a new window) All catalogs © 2022 California State University Fullerton.

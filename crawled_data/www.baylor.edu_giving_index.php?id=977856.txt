While preparing our students to follow their callings, we must also immerse them in an environment that prepares them to pursue excellence in all they do. We ask you to come alongside and invest in Baylor's bright future. Your gift will enhance a broad range of initiatives to extend the mission and vision of Baylor University. Academic Units The thirteen schools and colleges at Baylor University are on the forefront of compelling research, teaching and scholarship. Your gift will support areas that reflect the highest priorities of the University. Athletics Baylor Athletics shines a light for excellence both on and off the field. Contributing to the success of Baylor's student-athletes will make an indelible impact for years to come. Parents Whether your student is attending, has graduated or is soon to become a Baylor Bear, learn how to contribute to a number of opportunities that enhance the overall student experience beyond tuition and classes. Special Interests The Baylor Family serves the University and the community in countless ways. Explore special giving opportunities to discover the many avenues to invest in the future of the University. Student Life Strengthen Baylor students’ opportunities and experiences through a gift to the Student Emergency Fund, Student Foundation, Student Missions, Baylor Line Camp and a number of other opportunities. Student Scholarships A scholarship to Baylor University opens doors to intellectual and spiritual discovery, to deeper knowledge and to greater dreams. Your gift to student scholarships is a gift that can change a life. The Torch (crowdfunding) This platform showcases initiatives across campus that are the passion of our students, faculty and staff. Through crowdfunding, you can help the Baylor Family raise funds to launch new projects.

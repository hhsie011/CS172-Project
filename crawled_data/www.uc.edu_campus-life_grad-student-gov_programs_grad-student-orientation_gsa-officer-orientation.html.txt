All GSA Presidents and Treasurers were required to attend this event. The GSA officer orientation was meant to help officers understand materials and steps to help graduate students attain funding, such as the Conference Travel Awards. GSA Officer Orientation If you were unable to attend the GSA officer orientation, please schedule a make-up session with the GSG Treasurer, Aida (gsg-treasurer@mail.uc.edu) or the GSG Vice President, Chan (gsg-vicepresident@mail.uc.edu).

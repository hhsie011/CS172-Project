You'll gain a strong understanding of software principles and their application. And you'll acquire in-depth knowledge on Unified Modeling Language, database systems, data mining, and software development, project management, design and implementation. Learn how Software Engineering is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 12-credit-hour certificate comprises the following courses: Introduction to Software Engineering Advanced Software Engineering Six hours of electives chosen from: Introduction to Database Systems Artificial Intelligence Deep Learning: Theory, Algorithms and Applications Image Analysis and Computer Vision Data Mining View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

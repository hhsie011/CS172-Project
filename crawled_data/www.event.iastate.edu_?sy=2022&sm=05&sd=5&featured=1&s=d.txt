Performance: Paulo Padilha e Bando May 05, 2022 7:00 PM Stephens Auditorium $5-$10, free for ISU students Iowa State Center Arts, performances Diversity Padilha is a master musician and educator who specializes in rhythm and song workshops. His music is born from the urban bustle of São Paulo and mixes traditional Brazilian rhythms and styles such as Samba, Forro and Maracatu with contemporary nuances. His band of master samba musicians and dancers will make you want to dance. The performance will also include a song with Lyrica, an Iowa State student treble-voice ensemble.

The University has designated different types of prohibited conduct as well as Procedures for resolution of reports under Policy 6.4 that correspond best with the University’s different constituencies. Please see the definitions of prohibited conduct and Procedures in effect for the 2019-2020 academic year. Alleged Policy 6.4 Violations During the 2019-2020 academic year, the Office of Institutional Equity and Title IX (OIETIX) received notice of 490 alleged Policy 6.4 violations. These reported allegations do not necessarily constitute violations of Policy 6.4. For example, the university may lack jurisdiction or simply not have enough information to move forward. After assessment of each allegation, it was determined that 408 of the allegations were potential Policy 6.4 violations.

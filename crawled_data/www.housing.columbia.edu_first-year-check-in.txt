Photo by Eileen Barroso Photo by Eileen Barroso Bag Drop Option on August 27 Get the heavy lifting out of the way the day before Check-In! Students and families who live in the tri-state area or who plan to be in New York the weekend of Check-In can request to participate in bag drop on Saturday, August 27. During bag drop, students and two guests will have early access to the student's room to move belongings to campus in advance of Check-In. Bag drop times will be assigned between 8:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. on August 27. Students can indicate their interest in bag drop through their Housing Application. Confirmation of early access for bag drop will be provided with the student's room assignment on June 30.

They are contributions that can be used immediately and in full for the purpose you set. Current-use gifts provide scholarships, support or launch programs, supplement existing funds, or finance capital improvements among other goals envisioned by the donor, making them the most flexible type of gift. Every gift is tax deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a gift receipt after your gift is received and processed, usually within seven days. Give Now Online Gifts can be made online at any time by credit card. Online gifts offer opportunities to designate your gift to one or more funds, give a gift as a tribute, and add your employer if matching gifts are offered.

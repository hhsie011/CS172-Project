RETAIL MANAGEMENT More Info WHAT WE DO Why Costa Rica Known for its pristine beaches and stunning landscape, Costa Rica values peace and education above all else. A world leader in renewable energy, Costa Rica is always looking for ways to promote social and economic progress while lessening the environmental impact. Here, dreams become realities and great talent is recognized. Become a Red Raider and experience Costa Rica in the best way possible- as part of a family that takes pride in the pura vida lifestyle and its rich traditions. Costa Rica is #1 in America Central for financial start-ups Costa Rica has more than 500 IT companies The tourism industry constitutes 13.1% of Costa Rica's GDP For 2 years running, Costa Rica was named the Happiest Country in the World WORK & STUDY Texas Tech University-Costa Rica's Work & Study program allows qualified students to gain experience not taught in the classroom, while earning a competitive salary that can help them pay for their tuition. Participating students blend their class schedules with up to 20 working hours per week.

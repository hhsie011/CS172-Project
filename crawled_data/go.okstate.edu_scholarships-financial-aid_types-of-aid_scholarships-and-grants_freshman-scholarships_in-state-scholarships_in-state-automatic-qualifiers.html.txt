The minimum criteria to qualify for the Academic Scholars Program as an Automatic Qualifier is scoring at or above the 99.5 percentile on the ACT or SAT. For Fall 2022, the required total ACT skill area sum score is 138 or SAT critical reading and math skill area sum score of 1550. Official ACT Superscores provided by ACT will be accepted for Automatic Qualifier scholarship consideration.

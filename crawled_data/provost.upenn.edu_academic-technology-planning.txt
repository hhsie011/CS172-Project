Administrative Affairs Academic Technology and Planning team manages integrated planning, implementation, and support of academic technologies, with a particular focus on educational and faculty affairs technology. The team manages Penn’s course evaluation system and oversees CollegeNet, Penn’s graduate admissions system, and Interfolio, the new system for faculty recruiting, tenure and promotion, which is being implemented as a companion project to the Human Capital Management (HCM) initiative. In collaboration with Provost Administrative Affairs, the Vice Provost for Education, as well as ISC, school partners, and many others, the Academic Technology and Planning team works on a strategic plan for the University’s academic technology and developing a process for reviewing and assessing academic technologies in use across campus.

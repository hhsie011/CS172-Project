The Swearer Center Community Advisory Board is inviting individuals and community-based agencies in Rhode Island to submit nominations (including self-nominations) to receive one of three $2500 awards recognizing organizational work in one or more of the following focus areas: 1) Community Joy & Resilience; 2) Diversity, Equity & Inclusion/Anti-Racism; 3) Work that aims to address the needs of the most vulnerable Rhode Islanders. The deadline to submit is May 30, 2022; submit your nominations via this form.

The collection contains an exceptional drawing by Diego Rivera, a study for the Detroit Industry mural (1932-33), given to the university by the artist in 1935. Works on paper by Providence-based, nationally acclaimed artists Richard Fleischner and Howard Ben Tre are also part of the collection. Recent acquisitions include drawings by Beth Van Hoesen, Denise Green, Kirsten Hassenfeld, and Nicaraguan artist Farley Aquilar.

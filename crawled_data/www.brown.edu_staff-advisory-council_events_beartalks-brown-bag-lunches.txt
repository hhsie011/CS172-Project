We strive to build a supportive and affirming space for HUG staff at all levels without the weight of being an educator for their community,and to strengthen our professional and social relationships between diverse employees, the university, and the outside community. BEARtalks The Brown Education and Awareness Roundtable Talks (BEARtalks) provide an educational space for all Brown staff members to develop their awareness and knowledge with diversity and equity, and to engage staff in discussions about approaches toward an inclusive work environment for the Brown community. BEARtalks hope to build a forum of discussion that combines both scholarly literature with current events, optimistically connecting theory with practice. Next BEARtalk Please check Today @ Brown for dates Previous BEARtalk topics have included : Supporting Muslim Community Members at Brown Self-Care and Walking in Others' Shoes Introduction to Conflict Management Our goal is to help bring awareness and inclusivity to our community. If you have a specific topic you would like to see BEARtalks cover, please email us.

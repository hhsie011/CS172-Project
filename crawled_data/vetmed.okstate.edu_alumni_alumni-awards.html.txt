Each year, the CVM Alumni Society Committee meets to review nominations and select deserving recipients in recognition of their outstanding contributions to society and their excellent portrayal of all that can be accomplished after receiving a degree in veterinary medicine. Nomination Nominate alumni who you believe has made exceptional contributions both within the profession of veterinary medicine and beyond through community, state or national activities. Nominations for the current year are due May 1. To nominate CVM Alumni, please complete the nomination form and mail the completed form and the nominee’s current C.V. to: Sharon Worrell OSU College of Veterinary Medicine 308 McElroy Hall Stillwater, OK 74078. These veterinarians bring pride to OSU’s College of Veterinary Medicine for their contributions to the profession and society as a whole.

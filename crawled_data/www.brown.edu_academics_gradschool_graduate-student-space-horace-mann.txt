All graduate students are welcome to use the space for meetings or study. Who can use it: Any graduate student or graduate student organization. Location: 4th Floor of Horace Mann House at 47 George Street. Enter through the main door and take the elevator or stairs to the 4th floor. The space occupies the east side of the top floor. Meeting area Hours of availability: 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday How to reserve it: Email [email protected] to request to use the space.

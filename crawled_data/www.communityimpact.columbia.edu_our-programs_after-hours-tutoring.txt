Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About After-Hours Tutoring After-Hours Tutoring provides one-on-one homework help, tutoring and mentoring to elementary and middle school students in reading and math, as well as subjects such as science and social studies. This is a great opportunity to connect with students and give them support in both their academics and study habits. The program operates in partnership with the Volunteers of America at Regent Family Residence, a transitional shelter serving over 140 families. Tutoring occurs at the Regent from 3:30pm - 5:30pm Mondays through Thursdays and serves students generally ranging from 3rd - 7th grade.

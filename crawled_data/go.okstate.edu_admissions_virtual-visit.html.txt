And if you need to experience the Cowboy culture from home, we hope you’ll still get that warm family feeling. Enjoy the different ways we’ve listed below for you to learn more about OSU, and then when the time comes…schedule your visit to campus! We can’t wait to see you! 360 Degree Virtual Tour Check out our interactive virtual campus tour. Experience a 360 degree view of the incredible resources and facilities available to our students. You can take a look at the Edmon Low Library, the most comprehensive Student Union in the nation, the brand new Business Building, Endeavor Engineering Building, the state-of-the-art McKnight Center for the Performing Arts and many others. Launch the Virtual Tour Third Thursday & Transfer Webinar Series Take part in webinars on a variety of admissions and OSU topics on the third Thursday of each month. Learn what it takes to #BeAnOSUCowboy, get your questions answered and make sure you're up-to-date on next steps and upcoming deadlines. We also offer a Transfer Webinar Series geared for students planning to transfer from another institution. Learn about campus resources, transfer credit guides and transfer student life at OSU. Watch recordings from previous webinar sessions or register to participate in future webinars. Watch or Register Here One-on-One Sessions You have an admissions counselor just for you at OSU! They would love to hear from you and answer all of your OSU and college-bound questions. Find your admissions counselor, and reach out to them by phone, text or email and they will schedule a time to talk with you (and your parents if you want). Find your Admissions Counselor Don’t forget social media Follow Be An OSU Cowboy on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter and SnapChat, but don’t just follow, give us a shout out! We want to hear more about what you’re doing with your time and the pressing questions you have on your mind. We’ll keep you updated if things change, so you don’t miss any deadlines and we'll make sure you’re in the loop with everything you need to know about the Cowboy family.

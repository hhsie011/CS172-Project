Our event management team has experience in managing virtual events, digitally bringing together members of the Brown community through meetings, webinars, virtual conferences, ceremonies, orientations, workshops, trainings and more. How Can We Help? If you are a student or department planning your own virtual event, be sure to utilize our step by step planning guide for virtual events. For larger events, our Event Management team can work alongside you to ensure your event is a success. Our virtual services include: Event consultation & experience design Virtual event platform recommendation & management, including coordinating technology training and assistance for presenters Event set-up and coordination with campus partners and 3rd party vendors Marketing and promotion assistance to help drive event attendance Registration, ticketing and guest management "On-site'" virtual support during event Budget management Post event debrief Please check back often for updates and additional resources will be added.

These can be awarded by governments, colleges, and even private organizations. Types of Scholarships Grants Grants are a form of financial aid that also do not need to be paid back. Grants can come from federal and state governments, as well as from Georgia Tech. Review Grant Options Loans Loans are borrowed money from a bank, government or lending institutions with the understanding you have to pay the money back. Loans also have added interest that has to be paid back with the loan. Types of Loans Work Study Students with financial need can participate in the Federal Work-Study program, using their earnings to pay towards tuition and other costs. More on Work Study Additional Options In some cases, students may not qualify for need-based financial aid such as grants or work study. However, there are a variety of creative ways to finance your Georgia Tech degree!

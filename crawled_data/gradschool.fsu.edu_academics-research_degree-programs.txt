Programs that Lead to Professional Licensure Wondering if an FSU program meets licensing requirements in the state where you intend to practice? Whether your academic path leads to a career in social work, speech pathology, law, teaching, or another field, you may need a license to practice. Before starting an academic program at FSU, make sure it meets licensing criteria in your state.

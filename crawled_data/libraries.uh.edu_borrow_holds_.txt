COVID-19: Request items/scans from MD Anderson Library and Architecture, Design, and Art Library directly for a limited time. To place a hold: Log-in to your account Use library search with keywords, titles, author names, and more A list of search results will load You can request physical books, DVDs, and other items we own for a hold A panel will load showing more options Go to the “Get It” section of the panel If there are multiple locations for the item, select the one you prefer Select “Request” Input your Pickup Location Select the Request button We’ll email your UH account when the item is ready. Pick-up your item(s) at a pick-up shelf or service desk at the library location. Please bring a valid Cougar Card for proper check-out.

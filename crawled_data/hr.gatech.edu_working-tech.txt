Working @Tech Working @ Tech Tool Kit Understanding Flexwork Arrangements Flexwork Environments For Employees For People Leaders Flexwork Policies Flexwork Resources Guiding Principles Contact Us Forms & Guides Go to TechWorks Search Search form Enter your keywords You are here: GT Home Home Working @Tech The following information is a guide for supervisors and employees as they establish work schedules. In determining work schedules, the primary consideration should be ensuring that the unit’s mission is fulfilled, with a particular focus on serving students. Supervisors should engage with employees to devise work arrangements that leverage flexibility and support employee well-being, while ensuring the efficient and effective completion of the unit’s mission. Whether you and your team have already determined your work arrangements, or have yet to have the initial discussions, we encourage you to review the tools provided to help guide your decision-making processes.

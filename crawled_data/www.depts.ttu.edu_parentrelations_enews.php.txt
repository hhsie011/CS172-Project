Parent & Family Relations is here to connect you to the Texas Tech campus as a parent or family member of a Red Raider. Sign up to receive our Parent & Family eNewsletter below. It will keep you informed about resources for your student, events and programs on campus, and how to get involved. Subscribe to the Parent & Family eNewsletter Your information may be shared with Texas Tech University-related groups such as Texas Tech Athletics, the Office of the Chancellor, the Office Of the President, etc. We will NOT share your information with any groups not associated with the University except where required by Texas law. If you have any questions about how we might share your information, please contact Parent & Family Relations at 806-742-3630.

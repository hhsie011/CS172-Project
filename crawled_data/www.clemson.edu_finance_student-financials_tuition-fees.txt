Based on the information provided, the calculator will generate the total estimated costs per semester. If you are a prospective student, you may want to use the Net Price Calculator to see an estimate of your family contribution and financial aid eligibility. This tool contains five separate tabs: Undergraduate, Graduate, Online, Other Fees, and Study Abroad. Please choose the correct tab and then use the drop-down menus to select the variables that describe your educational pursuits. Chrome and Firefox are the preferred browsers for using this tool.

Breadcrumb Home Volunteer Volunteer Opportunities Brown Annual Fund Volunteers Sub Navigation In This Section Brown Annual Fund Executive Committee Brown Annual Fund Leadership Council Students Young Alumni Young Leadership Council The Young Leadership Council encourages recent undergraduate alumni to support Brown annually at Young Leader and Partner giving levels. This is achieved through peer-to-peer outreach, strategic input, recruitment, and engagement efforts. Council members also support the Brown Annual Fund at the Young Leader level. YOUNG LEADERSHIP COUNCIL INFORMATION (PDF) YOUNG LEADERSHIP COUNCIL ROSTER (PDF) Young Leader giving levels vary by undergraduate graduation year. The levels are: $1,000+ for classes 2018-2021, $2,500+ for classes 2013-2017, and $5,000+ for classes 2008-2012. Interested in joining the council? Contact us for more information. The 5th and 10th Reunions have the unique opportunity to carry on the philanthropic tradition of Reunion Class Gifts and raise the bar for future classes. Through peer-to-peer outreach, Reunion Gift Committee Members share the importance of philanthropy and inspire classmates to support Brown’s students and faculty through the Class Gift to the Brown Annual Fund. Members of the Committee play a crucial role in helping their class celebrate continued connections to one another while making a direct impact on the University. 5th REUNION GIFT COMMITTEE ROSTER 10th REUNION GIFT COMMITTEE ROSTER Interested in serving on your Class Gift or Participation Committee?

Research manages a number of internal funding opportunities on behalf of the University as well as access to external funding opportunities. Internal Funding The University Research Foundation (URF) The University Research Foundation (URF) is an internal funding program offering the following grant opportunities: Research Grants, Conference Support, and Research Opportunity Development Grants (Planning and Phase 2). Learn More Grant: Discovering the Future A grant to transition exciting new ideas to the point at which they can be sustained by other modes of support. Learn More Limited Submission Opportunities: For certain funding opportunities investigators will find that the University as a whole can only submit a finite number of applications. The Office of the Vice Provost for Research (OVPR) oversees the selection process for grant competitions in instances where a limited number of applications are accepted. Learn More Grant: Accelerating from Lab to Market A pre-seed grant to transition exciting new ideas to the point at which they can be commercialized and brought to market. Learn More External Funding Sponsored Programs Information Network (SPIN) For Penn constituents interested in new sponsorship opportunities, the SPIN (Sponsored Programs Information Network) database provides info from private and government sources. Access SPIN by logging into PennERA and selecting “Find Funding” on the top right. Learn More Other Resources Grants.gov is the US government’s online portal to search and apply to federal funding opportunities. Grants Advisor Plus is an information service for research faculty in higher education. Foundation Center includes a searchable database of federal, foundation and corporate philanthropic opportunities. PennKey required--click “connect to resource”.

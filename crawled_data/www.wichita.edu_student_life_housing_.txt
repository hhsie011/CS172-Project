The Suites The Suites is the newest of our three communities. This suite-style residence hall is only open to first-year students and is located directly next to the Flats. Housing Updates The final deadline for Move-out in Spring semester is Friday, May 13th at noon. Residents should plan to move out 24 hours after their last final exam. Students will sign up for a Move Out Timeslot — information will be sent to their emails with steps to do so. Our room selection process has begun for the 2022-23 academic year. Check out the room selection tutorial videos here. The housing application for 2022-23 is live! Students can log in to their myWSU account and complete their housing application, including the $75 application fee and $200 prepayment. Your housing application can be found in the Student Housing section of your myWSU portal. Move-In will take place Sunday, August 14 through Tuesday, August 16 from 9am to 5pm. Students will sign up for Arrival Timeslots in June 2022.

The Provost Office is responsible for all academic activities at the University. Over 30 units report to the provost, including Clemson’s seven academic colleges, the Graduate School, Undergraduate Studies, and the Libraries. Prioritizing REAL aspirational targets – Research, Engagement, the Academic core and the Living environment – the Provost Office ensures academic excellence through the implementation of the ClemsonFORWARD Strategic Plan.

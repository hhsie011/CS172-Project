Forum Events Past Event The Columbia China and the World Forum Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 7, 2021 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM Online Time: 9:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m. (Beijing) We would like to inform you that the 2021 Columbia China and the World Forum is moved to a fully online format. The 2021 Columbia China and the World Forum is hosted by the Greater China Student Union at Columbia University's School of International and Public Affairs (SIPA), under the guidance of Columbia SIPA and Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. This student-run, non-partisan annual forum is dedicated to exchanging constructive insights on key issues affecting the global community in the spirit of friendship and cooperation. First initiated in 2017, the Forum has featured more than 150 guest speakers with 2,000 attendees and more than 100 media partners. Last year, the online views exceeded 1,000,000. This year, focusing on the international situation in the post-epidemic era, the Forum will include four expert panels, Economics and Finance, Technology, Energy and Environment, and Media, Communications, and Education, all of which are aimed to address our theme topic, "Embrace Uncertainty and Refine the Future: Empower New Growth Momentum in China and the World". The diverse and inclusive backgrounds of participants will provide multi-dimensional communication and learning opportunities, which also offer unlimited possibilities for personal growth.

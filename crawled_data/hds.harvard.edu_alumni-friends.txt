The award honors the late Reverend Peter J. Gomes, STB ’68, who served HDS for four decades as a distinguished faculty member and the Pusey Minister of Memorial Church. Read the Report Giving Make a World of Difference Harvard Divinity School offers an intellectual home dedicated to religious literacy, understanding, justice, and peace. By connecting, educating, and supporting the work of scholars and ethical leaders from 35+ different faiths and denominations, HDS invites those from all religious beliefs—including those unaffiliated with a faith tradition—to illuminate, to engage, and to serve. Your support of the School helps bring the HDS mission to fruition. Thank you for considering a gift to fund this vital work.

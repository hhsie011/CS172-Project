Postdoctoral Scholar Excellence Award for Teaching and Mentoring Students This award honors postdoctoral scholars who have made innovative and significant contributions to training future scientists at Iowa State. Receiving this award may reflect a postdoctoral scholar’s excellence in either classroom teaching or in supervision of undergraduate or graduate students in research. Postdoctoral Seed Grant These competitive grants provide postdoctoral scholars a valuable opportunity to apply for research funding in the role of a principal investigator. Postdoctoral scholars are expected to develop independent research, and this grant is an opportunity to gain experience doing so. For Principal Investigators Postdoctoral Mentoring Award This award recognizes principal investigators’ excellence in their mentorship of postdoctoral scholars.

Email Requests May be requested by Clemson University Colleges and Internal Departments/Units only Use the following link to request an email distribution to your college or department alumni/donors: alumni.clemson.edu/internalemailrequest May be requested by official Clemson Clubs and Affinity Groups only Use the following link to request an email distribution to your club or group members: alumni.clemson.edu/clubgroup-emailrequest Club and Group Event and Email Requests May be requested by official Clemson Clubs and Affinity Groups only Use the following link to submit an event request and send emails to your club or group members about your upcoming event: https://alumni.clemson.edu/eventrequest Direct Mail Requests May be requested by approved entities, internal (College, Department/Unit) or external (Clemson Club/Group, partners, etc) Use the following link to request a direct mail list for your alumni/donor audience: alumni.clemson.edu/directmailrequest Demographic Data Requests May be requested by any entity, internal (College, Department/Unit) or external (Clemson Club/Group, partners, etc) Use the following link to obtain alumni demographic information (graduation years, majors, geographic locations, etc): alumni.clemson.edu/demographicrequest Donor Stewardship/Board Engagement Requests Use this form if you are faculty, a staff member or volunteer requesting information for timely stewardship acknowledgement, engagement requests (e.g., board calling donors to thank them), or fund information: alumi.clemson.edu/donorsteward-request Volunteer/Board Member Confidentiality Agreement All volunteers must complete a confidentiality agreement if they are to be managing data to engage with donors.

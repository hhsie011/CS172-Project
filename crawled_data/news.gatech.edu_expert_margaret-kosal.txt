Her work focuses on two, often intersecting, areas: reducing the threat of weapons of mass destruction (WMD) and understanding the role of emerging technologies for security. Kosal researches military sciences, such as possible offensive uses of nanotechnology, as well as many other types of science that could be appropriated for military use.

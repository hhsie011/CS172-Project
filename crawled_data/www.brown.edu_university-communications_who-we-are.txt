Brown’s Office of University Communications is composed of teams of professional staff with extensive training and experience in distinct areas of expertise to identify the most effective communications solutions. True to the Brown ethos, our teams work collaboratively to identify the most effective single platform or multiple platforms to tell the best “stories” about Brown’s excellence, whether through news, media outreach, print, web, social media or other digital strategies. We also work across teams to provide cohesive and integrated communications solutions to members of our campus community for high-impact strategic projects.

Students gain practical experience, and a portfolio, by translating comics for accessibility, the digital humanities and other uses. Learn how graphic narrative coding and accessibility is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum This certificate is multidisciplinary, is open to students in every major and is comprised of 12 credit hours—including two required courses, ID 405: Seminar in Applied Innovation and ENGL 377: Graphic Novels. The remaining electives are chosen based on your interests and career goals. View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

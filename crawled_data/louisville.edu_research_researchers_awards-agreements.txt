research, you must setup and manage your award. The Office of Sponsored Programs Administration (OSPA), part of the UofL Office of Research and Innovation, handles the administration of all externally sponsored UofL activities, including research, training/instruction and service projects funded by governmental entities, private non-profit organizations and for-profit/industry sponsors. We can help you: Set up an award Manage your award Close out your award Subrecipient Monitoring To find the right OSPA representative for your need, please view our assignment matrix.

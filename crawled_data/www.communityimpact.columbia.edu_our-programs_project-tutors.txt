Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Project Tutors Project Tutors pairs volunteers in classrooms to assist students and support teachers. Volunteers support classroom teachers by offering students individual attention, serving as one-to-one and small group tutors, and providing extra support in the classroom in an assistant teacher role. Tutors are assigned to a specific teacher and are expected to tutor at least one session per week (2 hours with travel). Partner Organizations P.S. 145 P.S. 165 Interested in volunteering?

Posts occur the 1st and 16th day of each month. First-of-the-month posts include requests from the 1st to the 15th day of the previous month. Mid-month posts include requests from the 16th to the last day of the previous month. For example: Aug. 1 post: Includes July 1-15 requests Aug. 16 post: Includes July 16-31 requests See the Public Records Q&A for details on the law and requesting records from Iowa State.

Main content start Updated 5/5/22 In response to a global pandemic that has fundamentally changed our workplace, we are continually assessing what will best support employees' needs and help maintain the health and safety of our community. To date, this work has resulted in changes to existing policies and the implementation of new, interim policies, as highlighted here. As with any workplace policy, eligible employees may be entitled to certain COVID-19 related benefits under certain circumstances. Please talk with your manager or your school/unit/department HR Manager to clarify usage. SLAC employees: Policies may differ from those stated here due to our regulatory environment; please view the policies posted in the SLAC Intranet. COVID-19 Vaccination and Surveillance Testing Policy Updated Feb. 28, 2022: Stay updated on COVID-19 Vaccine and Testing protocols for faculty, staff and postdoctoral scholars. Learn More COVID Flex Hours Updated Feb. 19, 2022: This policy provides eligible employees with paid time to address COVID-related needs, including family care. Learn more Quarantine Pay Updated May 5, 2022: The revised policy covers employees who are required to isolate or quarantine due to a workplace exposure to COVID-19. Learn more University-Sponsored Travel Published Aug. 30, 2020: Effective June 15, this new policy is regarding travel that is paid for by the university. Learn More Travel Guidance Published Sep. 18: Health Alerts guidance on what to do when you return to Stanford from travel, restrictions and travel-related questions. Learn More Interim Policies FAQ Find answers to frequently asked questions regarding interim policies established in response to COVID-19.

OnSite Global Consulting (OnSite) is a second-year MBA elective course through TuckGO offering professional quality consulting services to a host of clients worldwide, and an extraordinary experiential learning opportunity for students. Due to the complexities of the COVID-19 pandemic, OnSite will not be available in academic year 2021 – 2022. Students will instead choose between Global Insight Expeditions (GIXs) and term exchanges (TEXs) in academic year 2021 – 2022. Tuck students have put their management skills and business expertise to the test in a global context on more than 247 projects for 175 clients in 64 countries, with remarkable outcomes for students and clients alike. Clients Accelerate your strategic initiatives with a Tuck MBA consulting team. Students Refine your cultural leadership, teamwork and client project management skills in a global context.

May 11, 2022 Celebrate GAAD by learning about IT accessibility Attend any or all of the free presentations and workshops on various accessibility topics offered by UW-IT Accessible Technology Services as part of Global Accessibility Awareness Day on May 19. Activities include trainings on web accessibility testing tools, writing good alt text for images, creating accessible charts, accessible tables in Word and PDF, and a half-day workshop on how to use screen readers to check website accessibility.

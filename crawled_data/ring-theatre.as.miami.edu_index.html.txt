This spring we welcome our patrons to experience the excitment of live theatre with two inspired productions: The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time, based on the best-selling novel by Mark Haddon, adapted by Simon Stephens, and Cabaret, by Joe Masteroff, with music by Joe Kander, lyrics by Fred Ebb. We’re very pleased our students will again have the opportunity to share all they’ve been learning “in the round” at the famed Jerry Herman Ring Theatre. As we finalize preparations for these special events, we encourage you to visit the University’s Coronavirus (COVID-19) Information page.

For over 75 years, the SMU Staff Association (SMUSA) has fostered a spirit of community within SMU. We are comprised of the officers of the Executive Committee, members of the Staff Advocacy Council, and YOU! Want to join us? You are already a member! There are no fees or dues to join the SMU Staff Association. All staff within the SMU community are automatically members of the Staff Association. If you want to have an active role in the Staff Association, we have volunteering opportunities, positions on the Staff Advocacy Council, and Executive Board. SMUSA Upcoming Events Get Involved!

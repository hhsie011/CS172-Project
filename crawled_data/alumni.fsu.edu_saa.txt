The Student Alumni Association (SAA) is a student organization that bridges the gap between students and alumni by creating connections, fostering tradition and building skillful leaders. SAA provides students with opportunities to gain leadership experience and build relationships for future success. Joining SAA is one of the first steps to becoming a Seminole for life!

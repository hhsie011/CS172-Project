Executive Education provides services to New York City’s community-based nonprofits, other nonprofits, government agencies, and businesses—not only in New York City, but also nationally and internationally. Nonprofit mid- and senior-level practitioners can also enroll in the four-week open enrollment Executive Level Program under the former Institute for Not-for-Profit Management (INM), which now comes under the umbrella of the Tamer Center for Social Enterprise.

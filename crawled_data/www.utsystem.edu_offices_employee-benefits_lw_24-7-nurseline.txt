When you or a family member has a health problem or a question, you can call the 24/7 Nurseline provided by Blue Cross Blue Shield of Texas (BCBSTX) for answers to health care questions, information about major medical issues, chronic illness support, lifestyle change support, and more. As part of your UT SELECT Medical coverage, registered nurses are available—at no cost to you—twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week at (888) 315-9473.

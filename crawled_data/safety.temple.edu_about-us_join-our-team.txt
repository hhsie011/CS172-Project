Temple releases job postings throughout the year, in order to be up to date on when the jobs get posted online prospective employees should create a profile on the Temple University Human Resources website. Once a profile is created the prospective employees will recieve an email everytime that there is a new job posting from Temple University for the job they wish to apply for. How to Make a Profile Click on Prospective Employees. Click on Careers at Temple. Click on Staff, this will open a new tab. Click on External, this will take you to a new tab. Click on the Sign on, which can be found on the right side of the screen. Then click on new user. After creating a sign on you will click the dropdown menu by your name and click on profile. When making your profile for Human Resources make sure to add the position you are interested in applying for. Questions? If you have any questions about the process feel free to email Janice Dietz.

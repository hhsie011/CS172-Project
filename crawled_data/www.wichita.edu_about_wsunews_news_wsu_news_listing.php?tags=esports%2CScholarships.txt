New esports director brings his vision of gaming to Wichita State March 30, 2022 - The vision for varsity esports at Wichita State encompasses much more than people gaming on Cybertron Computers and wearing a Logitech Headset. Travis Yang, hired in December as WSU’s Esports director, wants to build a program that follows that direction.

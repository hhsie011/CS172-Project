of Student Affairs Who We Are Departments & Areas Our Strategic Future By providing high-impact programs, supportive services, and first-class facilities in a caring and inclusive environment, Student Affairs provides students with a foundation for educational excellence at the University of Miami and prepares them for their personal and professional endeavors beyond the collegiate experience. The University of Miami Division of Student Affairs engages, supports, and develops University of Miami students as active citizens in a diverse and global society. Student Affairs efforts extend learning beyond the classroom to provide students with experiential opportunities to challenge ideas, collaborate with others, and apply their skills to make a difference in their lives and the community. Guided by the ’Canes Care for ’Canes philosophy and its Strategic Plan, Student Affairs fosters a supportive and inclusive environment where all ’Canes respect and support each other. With its student-centered approach, Student Affairs makes a significant and lasting impact on the student experience at the University of Miami and beyond. Mission Vision Strategy The Division of Student Affairs engages, supports, and develops students and the University community by providing exemplary services, programs, and facilities in a caring and inclusive environment. The Division of Student Affairs will develop and challenge all students to become engaged citizens in a diverse and global society. Student Affairs employs four core values to guide its strategy development and execution. The work of Student Affairs must be: intentional collaborative connected responsive Student Affairs programming emphasizes leadership education, student involvement, residential and commuter student life, service-learning, well-being, and recreation. Through innovative services that are responsive to the psychological and physical wellbeing of students, the Division supports students to successfully pursue academic and personal interests. Student Affairs’ multifaceted facilities are designed to meet the needs and expectations of the campus community and employ best practices in facility operation that ensure a safe and supportive environment for all.

Post-Baccalaureate Pre-Med Program Don't let the fear of the time it will take to accomplish something stand in the way of your doing it. The time will pass anyway; we might just as well put that passing time to the best possible use." - Earl Nightingale Have you considered becoming a physician, but thought it was too late to change careers? Our structured two year pre-med preparation program is specifically designed for career changers and offers assured admission to the University of Louisville School of Medicine! If you're a career changer with a Bachelor's degree or about to graduate and desire to become a physician, but lack the pre-med science course work for medical school admission; this program may be for you! Let our program help you become the physician you always wanted to be! For more information about our program, click here. Check out our Program Outcomes, Student Spotlight and Program Ambassadors!

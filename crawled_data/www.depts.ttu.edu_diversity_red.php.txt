Raider Education is the place to find resources for Anti-Racism training, facilitated conversations on civility & cultural intelligence, and consultation for student organizations, student staff, and others to get involved with inclusive leadership on campus! Request more information or sign up for a workshop TODAY! Purpose Raider Education seeks to increase opportunities for cultural intelligence, civility, and inclusive leadership skill development for all students at Texas Tech University. Through partnerships across campus with faculty, staff, and students, Raider Education provides individual trainings, group workshops, and classroom curriculum support in a student-focused model that emphasizes actions on campus. What We Offer Workshops for Student Organizations Trainings for Student Staff Groups Classroom Presentations Individual Leadership Training Events Inclusive Curriculum Development Support Community Presentations and Facilitated Conversations Raider Education Topics Include: Anti-Racism Inclusive Leadership Authentic/Values-based Leadership Understanding Social Identities Mitigating Bias Understanding Culture Creating Inclusive Organizational Culture Responding to Microaggressions Engaging in Controversy with Civility Leading Inter-group Dialogue Allyship Leadership for Social Change Custom Workshops Available Request a Workshop or Presentation Workshop Request Form Work with Raider Education as a Peer Educator Do you love creating and presenting engaging workshops and presentations about culture, diversity, and inclusive leadership? Looking for leadership experience and building your presenting skills? Come make a difference on campus by joining Raider Education as a Peer Educator! Peer Educators receive training on Raider Education topics and teaching methods and then have the opportunity to speak in classrooms, workshops, and events all over campus. Contact us today to become a part of the team! Peer Educator Informational Request Contact Information For additional information or questions, contact Cory Hamilton, Director.

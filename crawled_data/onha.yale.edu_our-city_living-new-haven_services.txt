Once here, it’s an easy city to navigate on foot, by bicycle or bus. Yale also provides an extensive free shuttle service covering many areas of New Haven. There are a number of environmentally friendly ways to get around town. From health care to voter registration, this section is meant to provide useful information about some of the day-to-day services you may need once you have made New Haven your new home.

About Open House During our Open House, we encourage pet owners, those interested in pursuing veterinary medicine careers and anyone who is interested in learning more about activities at the College of Veterinary Medicine to join us for a day of exploring veterinary medicine. Our students coordinate Open House and are very proud to show visitors the many different facets of their profession. From research, to teaching tomorrow’s practitioners, to treating Oklahoma’s livestock and companion animals, there is much to see and do at Open House. Event Highlights An appearance and photo opportunity with OSU's Bullet and Pistol Pete Food trucks and OSU veterinary medicine apparel for sale Self-guided tours of the Boren Veterinary Medical Hospital Hands-on, science based activities for children of all ages Face painting Teddy Bear Surgery (bring your torn stuffed animal for repair; available on a first-come/first-served basis) Veterinary Medicine Admissions Seminars Various outdoor animal demonstrations throughout the day Our Location Need directions? Please visit the How to Find Us page for directions and maps to the event. Food and Beverage With all the activities going, we know you are going to work up an appetite! We've got you covered with on-site food trucks. Notes No pets are allowed for the safety of your animals, as well as to protect veterinary hospital patients. Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times. All activities are free and no appointments are necessary. Virtual Tour If you cannot attend the Open House, you can schedule a personal tour or view our facilities via a virtual tour by visiting the Tours page.

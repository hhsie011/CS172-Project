Home /Learning in DC/Immersion Week Program Spotlight Immersion Week Program Spotlight These students and alumni participated in our DC Immersion Week program and have chosen to share an update on where their paths led them after their program. DC Immersion Week is an opportunity for 20 Syracuse University sophomores to experience living and working in Washington and Baltimore during their spring break. How was your experience during Immersion Week? We’d love to know! Tweet your photos to us at @SUinDC. If you’re a student who participated in Immersion Week and would like to share your experiences, contact Meredith Smith, Alumni Programs Coordinator, at masmit10@syr.edu.

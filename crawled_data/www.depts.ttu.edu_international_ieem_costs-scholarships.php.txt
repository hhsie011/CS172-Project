All students who are awarded a competitive scholarship of at least $1,000 per academic year also qualify to pay tuition at the resident rate. Scholarships Departmental Scholarships The ApplyTexas.org application includes supplemental scholarship application questions. International undergraduate students who complete and submit the application are automatically considered for funding awarded by academic colleges and departments. Deadlines for this application are February 1st for first year students and March 1st for transfer students. Apply Now! Working on campus As an international undergraduate student, you have the option to work on campus part-time. However, on-campus jobs are not guaranteed. Consult your ISSS advisor regarding your work eligibility. Once you are registered for classes you can apply for jobs on campus. You can begin your job search on the Student Job Portal.

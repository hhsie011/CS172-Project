All of our dentists are faculty members at Columbia University's College of Dental Medicine, with access to groundbreaking research and the latest technologies. As experienced providers, they are committed to delivering compassionate oral care to you and your family. Our ColumbiaDoctors Dentistry offices offer oral care in a modern, comfortable setting. We provide comprehensive care across all specialty services without referrals to outside offices. By having a world-class team of dentists and specialists collaborating on your care, you can be confident your treatment will be the best it can be. And with five Manhattan locations, ColumbiaDoctors Dentistry offers options that help make care more convenient for you and your family. Teaching Clinics The College Of Dental Medicine also offers affordable oral care options through our teaching clinics. Our dentists-in-training work under the supervision of our expert faculty to provide excellent, comprehensive care at reduced costs.

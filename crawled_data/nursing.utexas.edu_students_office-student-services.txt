Dedicated staff members answer inquiries from prospective students and distribute information about undergraduate and graduate nursing degree programs offered at The University of Texas at Austin. Prospective and current students in these degree programs are served in a variety of ways and are encouraged to direct questions about admission, registration, scholarships, campus services, career and job placement, and other related concerns to the Student Services staff. Contact Information The Office of Student Services is staffed from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. Students are seen primarily by appointment only, however, students may be seen on a walk-in basis for situations that can be addressed in approximately 15 minutes. Students with an emergency situation may see an advisor or the Assistant Dean for Student Services at any time. Office Location: NUR 2.400 Office Phone: 512-232-4780 Office Email: ss@nursing.utexas.edu Office Hours: Available by appointment; call 512-232-4780 to make an appointment if you have not already been assigned an advisor. You may schedule an appointment with your advisor online by visiting Undergraduate Student Advising & Registration or Graduate Students Advising & Registration. Contacts: For information on degree programs, contact Student Services.

Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Community Lunch Community Lunch volunteers work as a team every Friday to cook a meal from scratch for 75-100 homeless and low-income guests. Our Community Lunch program, located at the Broadway Presbyterian Church on 114th Street and Broadway, is unique for its family-style serving and inviting environment. Volunteers organize holiday parties for homeless and low-income children and fundraising events to support program goals and food justice issues. Partner Organizations Broadway Community Inc (BCI) Interested in volunteering in Community Lunch? Email communitylunchsoupkitchen@gmail.com. Volunteer commitment of 2-3 hours.

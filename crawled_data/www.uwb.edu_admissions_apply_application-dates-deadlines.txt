First-year student Transfer student Post-baccalaureate student Returning student Non-matriculated student Professional program student First-year students Key dates & deadlines You are a first-year student if you have not attended a college or university following the summer of your high school graduation or obtaining your GED. If you have taken Running Start, IB, AP, and/or College in the High School, please submit a first year application. You are a post-baccalaureate student if you have earned a bachelor’s degree, and are seeking admission for an additional bachelor’s degree and NOT a Master’s degree. You are a returning student if you have previously attended UW Bothell, but did not complete your degree program. If you're applying to UW Bothell as a transfer, post-baccalaureate or returning student, you must know your desired major and desired start date before you begin your application. Not all majors accept new students each quarter. Before you begin your application, please check whether your desired major is accepting applications for the quarter you would like to start at UW Bothell.

These venues include the Robsham Main Stage, the Bonn Studio Theater, the Venues at 300 Hammond Pond Parkway, and the Brighton Dance Studios. Robsham Main Stage Boston College's premier event space is equipped with professional lighting and sound systems, custom draperies and backdrops, and its own Box Office. Venue Details Bonn Studio Theater Located within the Robsham Theater Arts Center, this black box theater is ideal for smaller scale productions, student talent shows, and Theatre Department workshops. Venue Details Brighton Dance Studios This state-of-the-art dance and movement center features a large dance space equipped with mirrors, a sound system, ballet barres, and a coat room. Venue Details The Venues at 300 Hammond Pond Parkway This newly renovated property boasts multiple indoor and outdoor performance venues including an auditorium that can seat up to 748 patrons.

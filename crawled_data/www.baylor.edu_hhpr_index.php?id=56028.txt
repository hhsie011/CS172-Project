Students have also used this program to specialize in strength and conditioning as well as sport psychology. The program also provides an option for students to obtain their teaching certification concurrently with their master's degree. Note that this is possible regardless of undergraduate degree area. There is also a non-teaching certification track available. The Department of HHPR also offers a five-year joint BsEd/MS program in Sport Pedagogy.

VIEW THE GUIDE Cowboy Welcome Schedule The full Cowboy Welcome schedule will be available in the Cowboy Welcome app. As we add new events throughout the summer, you'll be the first to know! Download the Cowboy Welcome app to your phone at okla.st/cowboywelcomeapp. VIEW THE FULL SCHEDULE Rock the Block Fun in the sun! Join the Division of Student Affairs; the Department of Wellness, Housing and Residential Life, the Multicultural Greek Council, and the National Pan-Hellenic Council for an evening of games, activities, food, fun and friendship at the Colvin Recreation Center. Inside will be a myriad of intramural sport and club teams to answer your questions about their organization. Outside will be food, games and a petting zoo. Learn about how physical wellness is one of the many aspects to a positive college experience. Class Photo What shape will your class be? From year-to-year the class photo has become one of the most anticipated events to attend. Not only is every class photo different, it's also a secret. Cowboy Welcome leaders plan, map out and reveal the design. Be sure to wear orange! New Student Convocation This is your official welcome as a new student. It marks the start of your academic journey at Oklahoma State University. As a part of your welcome, President Kayse Shrum will provide you with details about your class and opportunities for success. In addition, Coach Mike Boynton will deliver an inspiring speech to welcome you as a new student. Library House Party Edmon Low is no ordinary library. The Library is about to get loud. Join us for music, games, prizes, snacks and other adventures while exploring Edmon Low Library. We promise we won't shush you. Supported by The Friends of the OSU Library, OSU Department of Wellness and the OSU Alumni Association. SUAB Night! Karaoke, movie trivia, bingo and much more! Celebrate your first night on campus exploring the opportunities Student Union Activities Board has to offer throughout the Student Union. Grab a passport and participate in one of our many activities such as karaoke, movie trivia, bingo and much more! FREE prizes and swag will be awarded throughout the night! Making Connections Student Organization Fair Student organizations from all over campus representing a diverse range of majors, interests and activities are excited to meet to you at this CAN'T MISS event! Each organization will have a booth with information about who they are, what they do and how you can join. The Student Organization Fair is the best way to get involved on campus and find “your people” while also familiarizing yourself with the all the services available in the Student Union. Visit CampusLink to view all of our student organizations. Late Night Cafe: Welcome Week Edition Enjoy FREE pancakes, drinks and coffee at Late Night Cafe with SUAB and RHA! We will be passing out pancakes for you and your friends to enjoy.

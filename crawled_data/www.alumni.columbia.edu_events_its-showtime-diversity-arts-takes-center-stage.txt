Alicia Mack ’03GS is the first woman of color to hold the long-established position of Director of Dance at the Julliard School. By contrast, Marcia Sells, Barnard ’81BC, ’84LAW, was recently appointed as the first Chief Diversity Officer for the Metropolitan Opera. This program, originally produced by CAA Arts Access and the She Opened the Door Initiative for the Columbia Alumni Leaders Experience, explores how to promote diversity and inclusion and erase racism in all aspects of the arts—from production, casting, and financing, to training.

TULANE UNIVERSITY FINANCIAL AID Tulane University Financial Aid Please click NEWS link below for COVID-19 FAQ's. The University Financial Aid Office, a unit within Enrollment Management, continuously strives to successfully honor our mission “to assist students in financing educational opportunities through excellent customer service and professional teamwork.” Our staff works closely with students and families. Whether you are a new, returning, or prospective Tulane student, we are committed to helping you understand how financial aid works and in identifying the resources necessary to make your education affordable.

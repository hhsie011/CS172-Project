Remembering where you were after a full day of classes and time with friends can be difficult. Here are tips for hunting down lost items. MainStreet Connection Center While the central location for Lost & Found is Public Safety, MainStreet Connection Center (MSCC) is the place to start for items found inside TUC and surrounding areas. If you lost something in or near TUC, check at the MSCC to see if it has been turned in. If you still haven't found your item, complete the Lost & Found form. mscc hours of operation lost & found form Public Safety Lost & Found Ultimately, found items are taken to Public Safety, the official lost & found repository for the Uptown Campuses. Complete the Lost & Found form on the Public Safety website. If your item is turned in, staff will be in contact. If it has not been turned in, the form will remain on file. Public Safety Three Edwards Center 51 West Corry Boulevard Cincinnati, OH 45221-0215 Office: 513-556-4900 Police Non-Emergency line: 513-556-1111 Keys & Badges/IDs: 513-556-4925 Email: publicsafety@uc.edu Public Safety Lost & Found Form Lost Bearcat Card / UC ID If you lose your Bearcat Card / UC ID, immediately log into your Bearcat Card Account and deactivate it if you have money on your card. If you subsequently find your card, you can reactivate it in the same way. Contact Keys & Badges to report the loss of your Bearcat Card / UC ID. If it is not found, you can replace it by submitting a request and paying the replacement fee. New cards must be picked up at the Keys & Badges office at Four Edwards Center (office shared with Parking Services).

The Latino Faculty Association is a UC organization (est. 2017) dedicated to creating a supportive environment for full-time Latino faculty. The group provides opportunities to develop a stronger campus community and provides a forum for discussion and subsequent actions related to concerns of the Latino population on campus. Grants Funding Program The Latino Faculty Association offers Small Grants Funding once a year to support research or scholarly activity or projects or programs that will make an impact on UC or the Greater Cincinnati community. The 2021 cycle has closed. Please check back next year for updated information on this program.

Events Past Event What's next for financial investment, international education, and public health? Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo October 26, 2019 3:00 PM - 8:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing The Beijing Center cordially invites you to join CAA Beijing and senior alumni speakers/CEOs from Columbia, Harvard, and Sheffield to identify new trends and opportunities in the respective industries. Agenda 2:30 p.m. - 3:00 p.m. Registration 3:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. Panel discussion on financial investment 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m. Panel discussion on international education Title: "New developments and patterns of international education under the new policy" Moderator: Elaine Lee, Board member of CAA Beijing Xiaofeng CHENG, Alumnus of Columbia Law School, Partner of Jingtian & Gongcheng Law Firm, Independent Director of Hailiang Education Group, and Adjunct Professor at PKU Law School Bo HUANG, Alumna of Columbia Business School and President of Hongcheng Education Technology Group Wei RAN, Alumnus of Harvard Kennedy School, former Xinhua News correspondent in Iraq and in Washington D.C., and currently the Vice-president of New Channel International Education Group Pei YANG, Alumnus of the University of Sheffield and Director, CFO, and Board Secretary of Advanced Testing Authority (ATA) 5:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. Panel discussion on public health Title: "Opportunities and challenges in public health under the new circumstances" Moderator: Elaine Lee, Board member of CAA Beijing Dr. Jianbo LEI, Alumnus of Columbia Engineering and Vagelos College of Physicians and Surgeons, Founding Executive Director, Associate Professor, and PH.D. Candidate Tutor of PKU Medical Informatics Center Dr. Yunfei CHAI, Alumnus of Columbia Mailman School of Public Health, Partner of Langrun Investment/Gaohe Capital of Medical Industry Funding Dr. Kun QIN, Alumnus of Harvard Medical School and Researcher of Chinese Center for Disease Control and Prevention Qiang BAI, Head of Beijingtong Elderly & Disabled Pension Card Service Center 6:00 p.m. - 8:00 p.m. Reception Notice The event opens to Columbia alumni only.

Published by the LeRoy Neiman Center for Print Studies, Columbia University Revolt, Resist, Defy! | Fall 2017 Organized by Marie Tennyson Gallery hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00am - 5:00pm Featuring: Polly Apfelbaum, Mark Dion, Barnaby Furnas, Ellen Gallagher, Brad Kahlhamer, Dinh Q. Le, Kara Walker, and Tomas Vu, Visual Arts An exhibition of prints published by the LeRoy Neiman Center for Print Studies that challenge, resist, revolt and defy! Featuring work by a diverse group of artists with various backgrounds, points of view, and ideologies with at least one thing in common: a need to challenge ideas, stereotypes and social issues of today. This exhibition showcases how printmaking continues to be an exceptional vehicle to share, protest, and critique contemporary culture.

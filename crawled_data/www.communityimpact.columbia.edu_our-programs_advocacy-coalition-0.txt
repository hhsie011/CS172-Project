Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Advocacy Coalition Advocacy Coalition volunteers work with homeless and low-income clients at TASC and ESOL classes and other community venues. Volunteers act as advocates by providing clients with referrals to shelters, pantries, treatment facilities, and job training programs and by helping clients to become aware of government benefit systems.

Coda Clash is part educational, part entertainment, and entirety fun. Dan and Chris present their analysis of a composer and musical form around the evening’s theme. Through the use of argumentation and audio and video clips, they try to persuade audience members to vote for their composition and presentation as the winner of the evening’s Coda Clash. Song Cycles: Twelve Poems of Emily Dickinson, Aaron Copeland and and all the days were purple, by Alex Weiser. Theme: Poetry. Coda Clash: Which song cycle most persuasively encapsulates poetry as an expressive term?

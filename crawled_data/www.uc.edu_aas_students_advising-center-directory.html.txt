While it is not always clear which questions or concerns your advisor can help you with, know that your advisor is able to refer you to the right place. Advisors are typically available via email or by appointment. You can find your advisor's email address in Catalyst. Most advisors are available for appointment scheduling in My Bearcat Network. Find your advisor If you are not sure who your advisor is, you can find this in Catalyst. Once you’re logged in, simply click the "My Academics" tile from the student homepage and the advisor will be listed under your "Academic Profile." If you don’t have an advisor listed, please contact your college advising center, using the list below, for more information. You are also welcome to send an email to the Office of Advising & Academic Services for help. How to schedule an appointment Online Most advising centers offer online scheduling through My Bearcat Network. Online scheduling lets you quickly find a time that works for you and book it in real-time. Advising centers using My Bearcat Network are marked in the directory below. Call or email If your advising doesn't offer online scheduling, you can reach out to them by email or phone. Contact information is provided in the directory below.

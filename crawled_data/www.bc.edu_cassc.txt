The Center is the primary liaison with Purchasing, Accounts Payable, the Office of Sponsored Programs, and the Human Resources Service Center. The Center can help faculty and staff with: Financial Services - financial reporting, budget management, employment, payroll related matters, travel and expense reimbursement and procurement and accounts receivable assistance. Grant Services - pre- and post-award activities and Employee Change Request (ECR) forms. Operations Services handles the issues pertaining to the building, including emergency and routine work order requests, environmental health and safety concerns, security issues, renovations, and new equipment installations.

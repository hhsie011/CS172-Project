Analytics Sense, Collect and Move Data Smart Cities Columbia-IBM Center on Blockchain and Data Transparency DSI research centers and working groups serve as engines of translational research and education and a source of technology with high commercialization potential. These clusters of multidisciplinary researchers address challenges posed by our data-rich society through their respective disciplines. Chang Professor of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research and Professor of Computer Science Email: cliff@ieor.columbia.edu Explore Our Research Centers Computing Systems for Data-Driven Science We explore the design, analysis, and application of massive-scale computing systems for processing data. Learn More Cybersecurity We develop the capacity to keep data secure and private throughout its lifetime. Learn More Data, Media and Society We use data generated by people and data about people to understand human behavior. Learn More Financial and Business Analytics We develop analytical and computational tools to manage risk and to support decision-making. Learn More Foundations of Data Science We conduct core research on problems that cut across the data sciences and engineering. Learn More Health Analytics  We work to improve the health of individuals and the health care system through data-driven methods. Learn More Sense, Collect and Move Data We study the physical aspects of sensing, generating, collecting, storing, transporting, and processing large data sets. Learn More Smart Cities We develop, monitor, and improve infrastructure, buildings, transportation routes, the power supply, and everyday activities in crowded, urban environments. Learn More Columbia-IBM Center on Blockchain and Data Transparency We bring together cross-disciplinary teams to advance innovation in blockchain, data transparency, and closely associated topics.

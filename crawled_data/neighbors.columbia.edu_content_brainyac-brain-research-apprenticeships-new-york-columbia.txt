BRAINYAC: Brain Research Apprenticeships in New York at Columbia The Zuckerman Institute's Brain Research Apprenticeships in New York at Columbia (BRAINYAC) program provides an immersive science research experience in which high school students train and work in neuroscience laboratories at Columbia University.

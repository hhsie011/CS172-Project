friends of Syracuse University with each other and our University. We often hold game watches to cheer on the Orange, attend local sporting events, hold professional development and networking opportunities, host a new student send-off and participate in community service projects. If you have ideas/suggestions for an activity/event or want to get more involved with the club, please email us.

On June 7, 2021, Columbia University introduced a new academic hiring system, Academic Search and Recruiting (ASR), powered by Interfolio Faculty Search. For Applicants Search for job postings created prior to June 7, 2021 To search for job postings created on June 7, 2021 and after Applicants will be instructed to create an Interfolio Dossier account in order to apply for a position. For HR Managers To post a position, view resumes, and screen and track applicants: RAPS Log in (before 6/7/21) ASR Log in (after 6/7/21) Reminder Notice New York's Local Law 67 prohibits employers in New York City from inquiring about or relying on an applicant's salary history during the hiring process.

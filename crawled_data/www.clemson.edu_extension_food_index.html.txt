Coronavirus Resource Page FDA: Food Safety and the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) Food Systems and Safety Clemson Extension agents with primary Food Systems and Safety responsibilities work closely with state extension specialists and researchers located on campus and at the various research and education centers located across the state. They provide expertise in food systems – processing, packaging, distribution and consumption and all levels of food safety (growing, processing, wholesale, retail and consumer) to improve the safety of foods produced in South Carolina and reduce the number of foodborne illness outbreaks.

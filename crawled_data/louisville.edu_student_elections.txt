Candidacy Guide 2022 General Election FAQ SGA General Election Rules (DOCX) Election Application Students will be able to vote in the SGA General Election starting on Monday, March 7th at 12:00 am until 11:59 pm on Thursday March 10th. All students, regardless of college, will be able to vote for the “Top Four” University-wide officer positions (President, Executive Vice President, Academic Vice President, and Services Vice President). Students in the following colleges will also be able to vote for their college council officers and Senators: College of Arts and Sciences, College of Education, College of Business, School of Music, School of Nursing, School of Public Health, and Kent School of Social Work. More information is available on the Student Government Association website https://www.uoflsga.com. The ballot will be distributed to students via their University email, but students can also access their ballot via a link found on https://www.louisville.edu/student/elections. For questions concerning candidacy or General Election rules and procedures, please contact the SGA Supreme Court at sgacourt@louisville.edu.

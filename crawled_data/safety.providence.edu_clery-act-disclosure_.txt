The Report also includes information about crime statistics for the three previous calendar years concerning reports of crimes occurring on-campus, or on public property immediately adjacent to and accessible from campus (2018, 2019, 2020). Print copies of this Annual Report are available in the Office of Public Safety, the Office of Undergraduate Admissions, and the Office of Human Resources. Eric Croce Interim Chief of Public Safety Office of Public Safety Availability of Clery 60 Day Crime and Fire Log The Office of Public Safety maintains a Clery Act 60-Day Crime and Fire Log. The following information is filed in the Log: nature of call, date/time reported, date/time incident occurred, general location, and disposition of the incident, (if known). This Log is accessible in the Office of Public Safety and is open to the public for inspection, upon request, during regular business hours (Monday through Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m).

Her role as the Equity Services Manager is to assist the AVP for Equity/Title IX Coordinator in the oversight of: the office’s intake process and provision of support and resources; the Adaptable Resolution process; and the STARRSA program at Baylor and other educational sanctions. She also serves on the McLennan County Domestic Violence Response Team (DVRT) and Sexual Assault Response Team (SART). Christina received her Bachelor of Science in Psychology and Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from the University of Washington and her Master of Arts in Marriage and Family Therapy and Counseling.

We take great pride in making our visitors feel like they are part of the Cowboy family from the moment they step on campus. But don’t just take our word for it. Come find out for yourself! Self-guided tour Our self-guided walking tour will take you from the heart of campus, past our beautiful modified Georgian-style architecture, to the latest research buildings on campus. Or use our Where's Pete Discovery Trail to discover the beauty of OSU through a fun, interactive tour of America's Healthiest Campus®! Find 15 Pistol Petes hidden along a 2.15 mile trail while searching for answers to questions along the way. Alumni tours Are you in the mood to reminisce? The OSU Alumni Association's Student Alumni Board is available to host free tours of the Oklahoma State University campus during regular business hours. The tours may be customized depending on what parts of campus you are interested in seeing.

Stay Safe. The best way to stay safe in an emergency is to be prepared for one. That's why San José State University wants you to be equipped with the right tools to address a crisis. Need help right away? Call 9-1-1 or contact campus police using a Bluelight phone. Emergency Notifications Emergency notifications can be sent directly to your phone, computer or tablet.

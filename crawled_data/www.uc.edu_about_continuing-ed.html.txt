Employer Partners For Adult Learners Student Resources Home » About UC » Office of Professional and Continuing Education Welcome to Adult Continuing Education at its Best Our programs offer a continuum of learning opportunities for adults at all stages of life–from college readiness through career development and into retirement. Courses are tailored for adult learning with a rich array of topics for personal enrichment. Our courses include professional training to earn industry certificates for career advancement, and continuing education credits for professionals. Explore all the ways you can continue on your learning path! Classes for Your Personal Enrichment For 70 years, UC has provided the Cincinnati area with non-credit adult education courses to enrich and inspire your everyday lives. There is something for anyone who wants to learn something new in our two programs: Communiversity for adults of any age and the Osher Lifelong Learning Institute (OLLI) designed for adults age 50+. COMMUNIVERSITY Olli for Age 50+ Business & Career and Information Technology Certificate Training Are you looking to learn a new job skill to advance your career, or change careers completely? We offer classes to prepare you to earn your professional certificate in business, technology and professional development to help you move forward. Do you need to earn continuing education credit hours? We are a recognized provider of continuing education programs across diverse disciplines. Our coursework is tailored for busy adults needing to fulfill their career requirements. business/career/tech training CTAP - Talent Acceleration IMAP - Microcredential Program College Readiness We’re here to help you prepare to excel on the exams with convenient and affordable classes to help you do your best on your path to undergraduate admissions or to earning your advanced degree. Classes are designed utilizing resources from the university and students receive high-quality instruction and individual attention. New Year, New You: 4 reasons to become a lifelong learner for... The benefit of lifelong learning is well documented and researched. Lifelong learning is the continued educational experience that utilizes non-credit academic courses, educational travel, and community service to fully engage the brain, heighten physical activity, and maintain healthy social relationships.

logo KU Information Technology Menu link to menu page Search this unit Start search Submit Search Home IT Security IT Security Training & Consulting We all play an important role in protecting the University's systems, data and workstations. One of the best ways to combat ever-increasing technology security threats is through education and awareness. The KU IT Security Office provides the following training services for the KU community. Online Training All KU faculty and staff are required annually to complete the IT Security Awareness Training Course in the MyTalent system. The training period is announced each year in conjunction with National Cybersecurity Awareness Month. On-Campus Trainings & Presentations The KU IT Security Office provides in-person security awareness trainings and presentations for KU groups and departments, including: Official KU groups Units/departments Administrators and management Staff at all levels Unit/department technology support staff To schedule a security awareness training and/or presentation, please contact the IT Security Office (ITSO) at itsec@ku.edu or 785-864-9003. IT Security Consulting IT security experts in the IT Security Office are available to consult with anyone in the KU community who has questions or concerns about IT security, including department leaders, faculty, staff, researchers and students. Contact the IT Security Office at at itsec@ku.edu or 785-864-9003 to request a consultation.

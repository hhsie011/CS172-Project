We've gathered everything you'll need to plan the best event you possibly can. Visit the Office of College Events The Office of College Events is here to advise you as you organize your event. If you have any questions about how to navigate through the policies and services provided by internal TC offices, or would like suggestions on how to structure your event including venue selection, TC event policies, and more. Please do not hesitate to contact us. Find a space The Office of Room Assignments has everything from room descriptions, to the request form, to help you find a space that works for your event. Need specific technology? The Media and Video Services offers event support, including providing projectors, etc. Order food CulinArt provides dining services throughout TC. On their website, you can access, menus, catering forms, and more! Want to use the Gottesman Libraries for your event? Library Services can help you with anything from booking a room in Russell Hall, to loaning out dry-erase markers. Whatever your event needs, they'll know how to get it! Create Promotional Materials External Affairs can provide guidance and expertise to create print and digital promotional materials for your event.

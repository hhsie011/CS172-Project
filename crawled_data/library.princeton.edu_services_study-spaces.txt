Eligibility requirements for assigned study carrels may apply at some locations. Some group spaces are available on a first-come, first-served basis, while others may be reserved in advance. To reserve a study space you must have an active Net ID an be authorized to be on campus. If you have questions about reserving any of the spaces, please contact refdesk@princeton.edu. Wireless connectivity and/or data and power outlets are available in most locations.

Contact UW-IT About UW-IT UW-IT Design Guide Design guide for voice, data and wireless communications The UW-IT Design Guide is a subset of the UW Facilities Services Design Guide. The primary content of the overall design guide is maintained by UW Campus Engineering. To link to the Facilities Engineering Design Guide go to Facilities Services Design Guide The documents below address nearly all voice, data, and wireless requirements for projects that will connect to the University-wide network. Projects involving these requirements are coordinated by UW-IT Communications Infrastructure office. Boilerplate specifications and design instructions Standard communications drawings Excel spreadsheets Boilerplate Specifications and Design Instructions These documents are made available in two formats: MS Word and PDF. Consultants and designers developing project specifications should work with the MS Word files. PDFs are provided to make it easier to view the documents (requires Adobe Acrobat).

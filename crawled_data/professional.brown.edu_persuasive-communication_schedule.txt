Request More Info Upcoming Info Sessions Schedule a Call Connect with an Advisor Program Schedule The four-week course includes interactive lectures, application activities with individualized feedback in small groups, and 1:1 coaching. Interactive lectures are delivered by Dr. Tannenbaum and will include the principles of effective communication distilled from best practices and academic research. You will also have the opportunity to present in front of a small group of your peers in the program and receive live feedback from Dr. Tannenbaum. Individualized coaching sessions and video analyses provide specific observations about your communication strengths and opportunities for improvement.

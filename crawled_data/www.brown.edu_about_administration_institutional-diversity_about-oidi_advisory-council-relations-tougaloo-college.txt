The Council also provides assistance to the Brown-Tougaloo Cooperative Program. The Advisory Council on Relations with Tougaloo College shall be staffed by the Vice President for Institutional Equity and Diversity. Advisory Council Members are appointed by the President for a term of three academic years, beginning July 1 and ending June 30. Terms may be renewed once at the invitation of the President.

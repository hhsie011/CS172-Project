Make a career switch. Start a new business venture. Wherever you're headed, the Jones MBA Career Center helps you get there. Translate your MBA education into career success Evening MBA students have a broad range of experience, skill sets, and career aspirations. Career Services support is integrated as an important part of your Evening MBA experience, with designated career advisors coordinating services for Evening MBA students. We offer individual career coaching to help you clarify career paths and establish targeted goals to meet your Evening MBA career objectives. The Jones MBA Career Center supports a variety of career changes: transitioning to new functional areas, changing companies, and shifting industries. You'll have access to a range of resources to help you refine your career strategy, develop your action plan, and position your skills for promotion within your current organization, or to start a career with a new employer. Download Employment Report The Jones MBA Career Center advisors work with MBA students to turn their dreams into reality. The MBA career team is keenly aware of the changing interests of job-seeking MBA candidates and the evolving needs of MBA employers. In addition to college recruiting expertise, Georgia Tech's MBA Career Services team has a wide range of corporate experience and team members have served in various roles at industry-leading companies. We are consistently ranked as a top 10 MBA program in the nation for MBA career services as a result of the hard work of our MBAs on their job search and the coaching experience of our career services staff. Evening MBA Career Resources Planning your career path is a process of continual discovery. We provide a variety of resources to help you position your skills for post-graduate employment. Career Advancement Workshop Our Career Advancement Workshop is designed to support you in meeting your individual career goals. Students typically participate in this intensive workshop halfway through their MBA career and it is delivered every spring semester. The workshop covers these and other topics: Developing and applying an effective job search strategy Crafting and communicating your story Developing a high-impact resume Personal branding and social media Preparing for competitive interviews and case interviews Networking at national job fairs Negotiating offers Scheller and GT On-Campus Recruiting options Additional Resources Take advantage of the many resources and programming opportunities we provide, such as case interview and product management interview workshops, alumni and guest speakers, Career Leader—a self-assessment tool used by more than 260 top business schools to identify student career interests with job function, areas of strength, and helpful information on organizational fit. You also may practice your interviewing skills and receive immediate feedback from your career advisor as you prepare for employer engagement. Alumni Career Resources Career support continues long after you graduate. Career services as an alum include: JacketNet Jobs, a job search database exclusively for Georgia Tech alumni Career advice Georgia Tech Alumni Association LinkedIn group with 37K+ members and growing Numerous networks and clubs around the world Scheller MBA Career Outcomes The proof is in the outcomes and Scheller MBA graduates enjoy tremendous success. Our MBAs achieve some of the highest job employment figures in the nation for post-graduation, full-time positions. Read more about our students’ success and learn about our employer relationships in our MBA Employment Report.

Enter the gates to campus at 116th Street. We are located in Dodge Hall, which is the large building directly on your left when you enter campus. To get to the main entrance to Dodge, walk down the path to the middle of campus, and when you reach the large flight of stairs, turn left and go up the stairs to the left. When you get to the top, Dodge will be straight ahead. When you enter the building lobby, go left into the LeRoy Neiman Gallery. The printshop is through the double doors.

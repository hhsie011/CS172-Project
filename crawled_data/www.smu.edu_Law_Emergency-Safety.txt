The SMU Police Department is on duty 24-7, and senior command-level officers are experience former military officers with emergency training. All officers are licensed and certified by the state of Texas and have full police authority. The SMU Police can be reached at 214-768-3333. The designated person to deal with emergencies for the law school is Betty Alexander (bettya@smu.edu) 214-768-2620 whose office is in the Dean’s Suite on the main floor of Storey Hall.

Health Promotion Counseling and Psychological Services Disability Services Gay Health Advocacy Project (GHAP) Insurance Office Medical Services Sexual Violence Response Columbia Health You are here: Home Time Management in a Virtual Learning Environment Events Past Event Time Management in a Virtual Learning Environment Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo April 13, 2020 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM America/New_York Online Managing time effectively while learning and working remotely can be a significant change for those accustomed to in-person spaces. Join Alice! in a conversation about how to manage time effectively in a virtual setting while providing space for students to learn from each other and share what strategies are working for them to meet their goals and priorities given a new (for many) remote learning experience. More specifically, we hope participants will be able to: · Identify useful time management strategies, even in a virtual setting · Select tools for addressing procrastination · Describe the process for building a balanced schedule · Identify resources that can provide support for time management **If you are interested in participating in this open workshop, please register to receive the Zoom invitation.

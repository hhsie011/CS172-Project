Students use their university username and password to log into MyHealth-e. Use MyHealth-e to: Schedule and cancel appointments Upload insurance cards and immunization records Sign up for text-message appointment reminders (must update when you change your mobile carrier) Communicate with certain providers/staff through secure messages Complete online questionnaires, forms and surveys View medical and billing records Update your profile Additional Notes Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) appointments are NOT available online. To make a CAPS appointment, call 864-656-2451. Initial ADHD appointments are NOT available online. Upon arrival to Redfern Health Center, please go to the registration area for CAPS and medical appointments. MyHealth-e cannot determine whether or not you have paid a health fee. If you have any questions about your fee-paid status, call 864-656-1541. If you have any questions, suggestions or concerns about MyHealth-e, please contact the director of administrative services at 864-656-2233.

Alumni leaders work with HKS staff to connect their classmates with each other and with the School. Their efforts strengthen the Kennedy School community while garnering the financial support HKS needs to make a difference in people’s lives. Karen Bonadio Director of Alumni Relations Caitlin Santacroce Director of the HKS Fund Alumni Leadership Alumni Board Members enhance HKS alumni engagement through various initiatives, build connections with current and future alumni and promote alumni achievements by overseeing the Alumni Awards.

St. Augustine Villanova's intellectual climate, research and scholarship activity, and integrated approach to teaching and learning foster an academic experience that creates pathways for Villanovans to ignite change wherever they go. CLOSELY CONNECTED ACADEMIC COMMUNITY Villanova is a closely connected academic community, grounded in the Augustinian Catholic intellectual tradition. With renowned faculty and a personalized, supportive learning environment, Villanova provides exceptional opportunities for students seeking a wide range of academic programs—bachelor's, master's, doctoral, law and certificate. COLLABORATIVE PURSUIT OF KNOWLEDGE At Villanova, the collaborative pursuit of new knowledge drives partnerships between our academic departments and colleges, and with global organizations. These kinds of interactions provide opportunities for faculty and students not only to innovate but to see how the applications of their discoveries help better our communities. ACCLAIMED FACULTY Our acclaimed faculty are at the core of Villanova's vibrant academic community. They are innovative and engaged classroom teachers, and among the most respected academic leaders in their fields. Students work directly with professors—building strong relationships, engaging in collaborative research, and pursuing both independent and team projects. FORGING WORLD CHANGERS Villanova attracts and forges world changers. At Villanova, we approach the pursuit of knowledge as a way to serve our global society—to deepen interactions, improve lives and ignite change. No matter where you are in your academic journey, Villanova will challenge you to use your knowledge, skills and compassion to better the world around you.

You are here: Home / Elevator Emergencies Elevator Emergencies If you find yourself in a stalled elevator: Do not try to exit the elevator car. Use the emergency telephone in the elevator to call for help or bang on the doors and shout for assistance. On the Ithaca campus call 607 255-1111 from a cell phone At Cornell AgriTech call 315-787-2215 from a cell phone Do not try to force the doors open or exit the car. Wait for trained personnel to assist you from the elevator. If you hear someone calling for help from a stalled elevator car: Call for assistance. On the Ithaca campus call Cornell Police at 607 255-1111 or use a building emergency phone At Cornell AgriTech call 315-787-2215 Reassure the persons in the car that you have reported the problem and help is on the way. Do NOT attempt to open the elevator doors.

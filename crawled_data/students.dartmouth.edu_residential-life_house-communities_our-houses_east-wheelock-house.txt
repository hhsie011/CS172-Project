Wheelock House Coming up at East Wheelock - Official House events blog East Wheelock News Share East Wheelock House PreviousNext About East Wheelock House East Wheelock House is co-led by a live-in Residential Education professional staff member and a House Professor who lives nearby. In addition, the East Wheelock House leadership team consists of four Resident Fellows and a team of Undergraduate Advisors (UGAs). An administrative assistant is also a part of the House team and student leadership opportunities exist through the House Executive Council. Assistant Director of East Wheelock House - Josiah Proietti House Professor of East Wheelock House - Sergi Elizalde Housing Information Andres, McCulloch, Morton and Zimmerman, the residence halls that make up East Wheelock House, have singles, two room doubles, one room doubles, a few two person and three person suites and four person suites. *NEW for 2022-2023 - While Andres is under renovation Ripley, Woodward and Smith halls will be used to house East Wheelock students. First-year students who are East Wheelock House members will live on a first-year floors within East Wheelock House. What Type of Room Can I Expect?: Sophomores are mostly in doubles and some suites & singles, juniors and seniors mostly in singles and suites. First-year students are typically in one room doubles, some two room doubles and some singles. Gender Inclusive Housing/Single Sex Housing: All multiple occupancy rooms in East Wheelock can be assigned to multi-gender roommate groups. Bathrooms in Morton & McCulloch are all single-use, in Zimmerman all bathrooms are within the room and in Ripley, Woodward & Smith bathrooms are gender specific, except for Woodward 3rd, which is gender inclusive.

Moderated by Jack Snyder, Robert and Renee Belfer Professor of International Relations, Columbia University. Welcoming remarks from David Freedberg, Director of the Italian Academy and Pierre Matisse Professor of the History of Art, Columbia University. Click here to RSVP or join waiting list. Please join us even if you cannot register; walk-ins are very likely to get a seat. "European Institute Lecture Series." This event is sponsored by the European Institute, the Italian Academy for Advanced Studies in America, and the Alliance program (all at Columbia), and by the Italian Cultural Institute and the EU’s "Getting To Know Europe" Program.

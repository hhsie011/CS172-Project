Abroad Programs In today’s interconnected world, students who enter the workforce having had a strong global experience hold a significant advantage over their competition. Rutgers helps expand students’ global education through more than 150 study abroad and service-learning abroad programs. With scholarship offerings and opportunities in every region of the world regardless of major, studying abroad is an option for every Rutgers student, not just a select few. Learn More about Study Abroad Search the Study Abroad Program Finder Break Boundaries, Not the Bank Every Rutgers student has access to opportunities for global learning that are academically rigorous, degree relevant, culturally transformative, and empower students to make an impact in local and global communities. The Access the World initiative puts 30 global destinations on the table that are comparable in cost or even more affordable than staying at Rutgers for a semester. Learn more about Access the World Global Health Rutgers Global Health Institute fosters collaboration across the university and with partners beyond Rutgers to improve the health of vulnerable populations. Locally and around the world, the institute promotes health equity by working with communities to create and implement comprehensive, long-term solutions to pressing global health challenges. Learn more about Global Health at Rutgers International Admissions Rutgers is home to more than 9,000 international students and scholars from more than 130 countries. International students and scholars contribute significantly to advancements in education, research, and service. The Rutgers community benefits from these rich perspectives that help to globalize our classrooms, labs, and clinics. Go to International Student and Scholar Services (ISSS) Helping International Students Succeed Rutgers First-Year International Transition (RU-FIT) is a 1-credit course designed to aid international students at Rutgers–New Brunswick in a successful transition to Rutgers. The course helps students develop a foundation for a well-rounded college experience, connect with Rutgers resources, get engaged on campus, and understand university expectations. The ultimate goal of the course is to assure preparedness by ensuring students understand what it means to academically succeed at Rutgers.

We are continually developing new and exciting programming, communications, and networking opportunities to meet the needs and fuel the interests of our alumni. As a Columbia Business School graduate, you belong to both the CBS alumni community, and the wider community of all graduates from Columbia Institutions. This larger organization is known as the Columbia Alumni Association (CAA), a collective of all University alumni. The CAA is another way to strengthen your bond with fellow Columbians of all University schools, worldwide, through online resources, intellectual opportunities, and discounts. There are also additional benefits that you can take advantage of as a graduate of Columbia Business School.

About Clickers The Turning Technologies Clicker is an easy-to-use immediate response system that obtains immediate feedback from every student in a classroom. Faculty will use Clickers in a variety of ways in class. Some ways that your instructor might use Clickers are: To give in-class surveys or quizzes with immediate feedback To create an environment for group activities in class To take attendance To create interactivity among students in large classes Students: Not all classes require a Clicker. You will be notified by your professor if a Clicker is required for a particular class. One clicker can be used for multiple classes in a semester.

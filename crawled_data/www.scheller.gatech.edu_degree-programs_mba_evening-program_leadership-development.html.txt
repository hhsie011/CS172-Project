What is CHARGE? All Scheller MBA students are automatically enrolled in CHARGE. By attending Scheller events and engaging in experiential learning activities, students build four core leadership competencies: Social intelligence, communication, team efficacy, and impact and influence. Students have the opportunity to earn points for each engagement they complete, allowing them they progress from white to navy to gold badge status. Students who achieve GOLD status in all four competencies earn a leadership certificate that will be presented at their MBA graduation awards ceremony. Goals of CHARGE: Help you build your personal leadership brand Prepare you to make an immediate and positive impact at Scheller and in theworkplace Complement academic coursework with experiential opportunities Teach how and when to leverage hard and soft skills Help you develop talking points from leadership experiences for interviews Establish a forum for peer and program recognition Four Core Leadership Competencies Social Intelligence The capacity to know one’s self and others and the ability to successfully build relationships and navigate social environments. Experiential learning activities: Service projects Unconscious bias workshops EQ awareness exercises Reflection Communication The ability to effectively share and receive information formally and informally through interpersonal, nonverbal, verbal, visual and written exchanges. Experiential learning activities: Improv training Storytelling workshop Business communications High-impact consulting workshop Team Efficacy The ability to produce a desired or intended result through collaboration in ideaformation, process, and execution. Experiential learning activities: Team charters Team-building activities Club and committee involvement Impact and Influence The ability to persuade or convince others strategically and authentically to support an idea, agenda, or direction.

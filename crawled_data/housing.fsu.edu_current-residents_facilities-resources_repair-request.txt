Repair Request Through this process you will receive three (3) emails: Confirmation of repair request submission. Work order issuance (including the work order number). Repair request completion. By submitting a repair request, you give FSU Facilities staff permission to enter your room/apartment to correct the problem. Staff will knock and announce themselves before entering, and they will hang a maintenance tag on your door knob explaining the work. If you have difficulty submitting a repair request or wish to check on a status, please contact your Facilities Supervisor.

Students Your Global University Work for Us - Student Employment Global Engagement Nights Join the Global Ambassadors for a night full of culture sharing and fun! A group of students making Chinese lanterns in celebration of the Moon Festival in September 2021. What are Global Engagement Nights? Organized and run by our undergraduate student Global Ambassadors, Global Engagement Nights are casual events to facilitate cultural sharing and global learning. Each theme is picked by a Global Ambassador. These events are ideal for any student who is interested in learning about other cultures, meeting international students or other students interested in global engagement, and having fun. For more information, contact globalambassadors@ncsu.edu Global Winter Celebration 2021 Suggestions If you have any suggestions about future Global Engagement Nights, drop them below!

The Global Engagement Committee consists of faculty and administrators from across the University. The group has met since virtually since summer 2020, but met in person for the first time in August 2021 to prepare for a new year of progress. The committee hopes to engage the entire BC community in its efforts to increase Boston College's impact around the globe and to highlight and welcome our amazing international community on campus.

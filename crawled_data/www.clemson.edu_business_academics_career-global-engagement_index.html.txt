Resources Contact Us Clemson Home Wilbur O. and Ann Powers College of Business Academics Current: Office of Career and Global Engagement Connect with us! Facebook Instagram Twitter LinkedIn The Office of Career and Global Engagement exists to cultivate and engage students within the college to become effective leaders, successful graduates and globally competitive professionals. We understand the significance of preparing students for the professional workforce and life after graduation; thus, the Office of Career and Global Engagement provides principal resources for undergraduates in the areas of career preparation, employment opportunities, mentoring and international program participation. The office also collaborates with faculty and staff to serve as a conduit for connecting with corporate partners, Clemson alumni and other constituents to enhance the Clemson Experience. Find ongoing events in the Business Student Events Calendar. Commitment to Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belonging The Wilbur O. and Ann Powers College of Business holds a Core Value of Inclusiveness. The Office of Career and Global Engagement aims to uphold this value by looking at all our work through the lens of Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belonging. Our culture is built and upheld by our behaviors, our personal commitment, our curiosity and the ways that we courageously share our perspectives and encourage others to do the same. By helping to create an inclusive culture, the Office of Career and Global Engagement will work to inspire you to try new things, speak openly and be bold. Doing this brings us together in ways that help us stand out. Our inclusive culture empowers all of us to build, develop and grow. Connect with us!

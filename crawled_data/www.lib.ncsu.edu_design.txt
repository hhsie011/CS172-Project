Lyons Design Library About the Design Library The Harrye B. Lyons Design Library supports the College of Design and has collection emphases in architecture, landscape architecture, graphic design, industrial design, and art and design. The Design Library Image Collection is now available through Luna Insight 6.3. The collection contains about 150,000 digital images covering architecture, art and design, which can be searched via Luna.

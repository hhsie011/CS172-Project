Event Kind: Training Organizer: Department of Environmental Health Sciences SHARP Invitation Limited to: Open to the Public Website: Learn More Learn More Join us for the next live-stream PI Crash Course on June 9-10, 2022. The Principal Investigator (PI) Crash Course is a two-day intensive boot camp of seminars, discussions, and hands-on activity sessions to provide exposure to fundamental leadership and management skills and tools needed for success in your lab. Senior post-doctoral students and associate research scientists about to start their independent position search are particularly welcome to attend, and we strongly encourage new and recent PIs to participate as it is never too late to get an overview of the survival skills that you will need throughout your career.

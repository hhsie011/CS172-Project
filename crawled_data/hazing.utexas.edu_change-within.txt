Each action you take to stop hazing can support a healthy group culture. In addition to workshops, consider these ideas: Review your new member education program with Student Activities staff. Adopt alternatives to hazing to build unity in your team or organization. Get discounts and ideas for healthy activities you can do as a group by participating in the Healthy Student Organization Program. Learn ways to improve wellbeing in your club by applying to be a Mental Health Ambassador. Find opportunities for all members to share ideas and opinions at meetings, events, and throughout the year. Earn recognition and win student organization awards by completing Pillars of the Forty Acres.

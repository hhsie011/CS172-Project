Contact UW-IT About UW-IT LinkedIn Learning LinkedIn Learning is an on-demand video learning platform for enhancing academic, professional, & personal skills. Free access to LinkedIn Learning is provided to all UW students, staff, and faculty by the Student Technology Fee. Content 16,000+ courses (1-3 hours) organized into brief videos called chapters (2-10 minutes) Taught by accomplished instructors and recognized industry experts Beginner, intermediate and advanced level videos Topics include cloud architecture, pivot tables, presenting, data visualization, graphic design, teamwork, and thousands more. Access UW students, staff, and faculty can log in with their UW credentials Unlimited access on desktop, mobile, and tablet devices anywhere with internet Convenient mobile app makes it easy to download videos for offline viewing Integration with Canvas Faculty can assign full courses or short videos to students and then view their viewing activity directly in Canvas.

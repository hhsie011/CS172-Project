The Professional Sales Major in the College of Business Housed in the college’s Department of Marketing, the Professional Sales major at FSU is one of the largest in the nation with more than 400 students taking one or more sales courses each semester. Graduates from the program are consistently recognized as top performers in their organizations in record time.

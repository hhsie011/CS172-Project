All Tutoring is First-Come, First-Served Tutors can work with NO MORE THAN 4 students at one time Tutoring will take place in Drane Hall, 164 OUR MISSION: The ultimate goal of the Learning Center is to provide a student with the resources needed to obtain academic independence and success. Through positive interaction and effective communication, a peer tutor is able to create an educational environment that inspires a student to develop autonomous learning strategies. The Learning Center offers a variety of FREE services to all currently enrolled undergraduate Texas Tech students to promote Academic Success: Drop-in peer tutoring in subjects such as Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Accounting, and Engineering (and many others).

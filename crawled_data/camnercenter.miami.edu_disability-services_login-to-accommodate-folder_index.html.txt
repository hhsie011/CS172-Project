Submit Accommodations Request Form View Approved Accommodations Request Professor Memos Schedule Exams with the Office of Disability Services (ODS) Student Test Booking Students are encouraged to book exams well in advance because seating at ODS is limited and reserved based on availability showing in Accommodate. Exams must be booked at least 7 days in advance through Accommodate and are based on availability. Arrangements for online exam accommodations are coordinated with the professor and not proctored through ODS. Student Responsibilities Schedule exams at least 7 days in advance Contact instructors to approve the exam request within 72 hours of the request or it will be canceled by the Office of Disability Services (ODS) Please review ODS Testing Roles and Responsibilities Exam Start Times Semester Exams 9 a.m. 11 .am. 1 p.m. 3 p.m. Final Exams 8 a.m. 1 p.m. Instructor Responsibilities: Approve/deny exam requests AND complete exam proctoring questions in Accommodate within 72 hours. Upload exam in Accommodate at least 24 hours in advance of exam date. ODS will attempt to contact a professor by email when exams are not submitted 24 hours in advance. However, ODS will cancel requests when exams are not submitted by an instructor with adequate time. Students are directed back to the instructor.

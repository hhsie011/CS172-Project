Board members are elected to a three-year term and may only serve two consecutive terms. This limitation may be waived and is not applicable to those assuming or holding positions of Committee Chair and/or Officer. Board meetings are held quarterly, during which the board reviews, discusses and votes on business and other matters relating to the Foundation. The board is vested with full control of all of the assets, affairs and business of the organization.

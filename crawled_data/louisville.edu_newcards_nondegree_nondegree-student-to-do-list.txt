Use this list as a guide so that you are sure to take care of important business before the start of school. 1. Activate your student accounts: Follow this link to set up your university email account and other online services. Please note: many university offices will only send information to your UofL email account. You will use ULINK to register for classes 2. For students visiting UofL and returning to another institution, contact your academic advisor at your home institution to be sure that courses taken at UofL will transfer back to meet the appropriate degree requirements. 3. Register for classes via ULINK. Registration process Schedule of Courses 4. Get your student ID (Cardinal Card). You must be registered for classes at least one day before getting your Cardinal Card. 5. Follow us on facebook.com/UofLAdmissions, Twitter @UofLadm and Instagram @uofladmissions.

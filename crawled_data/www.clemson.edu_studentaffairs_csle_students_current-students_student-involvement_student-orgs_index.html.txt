Learn, Grow, & Celebrate Together Each year, student organizations come together through multiple events that incorporate recruitment, organization growth, and celebration of our student organization community. These events include the Student Organization Lab, which runs all year, fall Tiger Prowl, spring Tiger Prowl, the President's Banquet, trainings and educational programs from CSLE, Clemson Student Leader Awards Banquet, and much more. If you ever have questions or want to talk through an idea for your student organization, please reach out to our staff! We would love to learn more about your student organization and help connect you and your fellow leaders to resources that can help your student organization reach its goals. Connect with us and expand your reach through: Our student organization resource library Current CSLE events Nominating a member for Member of the Week Meet with a student organization consultant Volunteer with your student org We wish you all the best, and we look forward to connecting and working with you for your student organization's growth!

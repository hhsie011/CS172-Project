Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management is located within two colleges - the SC Johnson College of Business and the college of Agriculture and Life Sciences at Cornell University. Its internationally renowned areas of expertise in food and agricultural economics, management, environmental and resource economics, and international and development economics work in concert to fulfill the School’s mission to inform and foster the public stewardship and private management of businesses, organizations, livelihoods, and natural resources. The Dyson School offers BS, MS, MPS, and PhD degrees, and conducts frontier research and delivers innovative extension/outreach programs in all four of the School’s areas of expertise. Its undergraduate degree is accredited by AACSB International—the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business.

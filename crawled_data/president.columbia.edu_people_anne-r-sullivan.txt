Sullivan Anne R. Sullivan Anne Sullivan is Columbia University’s executive vice president for finance and information technology. In this role, she oversees Columbia’s operating and capital budget, the Controller’s Office operations, Student Financial Services, the Office of the University Registrar, Treasury operations, procurement and internal audit activities at the University, and Columbia University Information Technology. Ms. Sullivan joined Columbia in 2007 from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, where she served as senior associate dean for finance and administration.

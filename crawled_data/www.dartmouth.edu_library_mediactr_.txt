Zoom sessions and in-person workshops cover online resources, media equipment instruction, introduction to editing software and more. If you're interested but unsure of how to go about assigning a media project through remote teaching and learning or in person, we can help! We are also offering 'Workshops on Demand'. We will conduct media workshops for 5 or more people on a variety of software such as iMovie, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premiere and more!

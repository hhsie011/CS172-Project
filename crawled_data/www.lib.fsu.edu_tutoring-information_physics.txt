Strozier Library We are now open for summer! Summer Hours: Monday - Wednesday, 8pm to 11pm We are available both in person and online this semester. Enter the Zoom room for Strozier tutoring. Tutoring services are supported by the generosity of the Fogg Trust. Please note: Tutors are only available during our posted hours, and you will be asked to log in with your my.fsu credentials to reach the direct Zoom link. This How to Access Online Tutoring with Zoom video will walk you through downloading and signing into Zoom, finding the link to the active tutoring session, and what happens when you log in for the first time. If you need assistance figuring out Zoom, start with this page of FSU's Information Technology Services site or Zoom's official help center. If you are a TCC or FAMU student and would like to access tutoring for the current semester, please email lib-tutoring@fsu.edu or use the Submit A Question page to register and receive the link.

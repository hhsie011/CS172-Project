The Office is responsible for administering the Provost’s initiatives in teaching and learning, leading efforts in online education, providing support for schools to offer new and extending existing courses and programs to audiences around the world, reviewing new and existing online and hybrid degree and non-degree programs, and proposing, reviewing and disseminating policies on teaching and online education.

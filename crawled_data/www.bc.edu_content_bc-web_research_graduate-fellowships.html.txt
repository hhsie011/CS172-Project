Boston College graduate student, you’ll work with renowned faculty on research with practical applications—and have access to some of the most prestigious funding opportunities in the country. You will acquire financial support and mentorship that enables project leadership from design through publication. No matter your interests, more than 40 types of fellowships help you pursue new lines of intellectual inquiry.

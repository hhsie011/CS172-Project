Senior Lecturer Office: Strode 416 Phone: 864-656-1567 Email: SGCROCK@clemson.edu Educational Background MA Communication, Technology, and Society Clemson University 2014 MATS Theological Studies Central Baptist Theological Seminary 2019 - present BA Communication - Mass Media Anderson University 2009 Courses Taught HON 3320: Communication and Ethics COMM 2500: Public Speaking COMM 2500: Public Speaking Honors COMM 2500: Special Topics: Communication & Civil Engineering COMM 1500: Introduction to Human Communication Profile Sara is a senior lecturer in the Department of Communication. She first joined the department in 2012 as a graduate student and teaching assistant. She then joined the faculty full time in 2015. Sara has served the department, college, and university in various ways including: Undergraduate Curriculum Committee member, Communication Basic Courses Committee member, a member of various faculty search committees, as a summer reading facilitator, as a judge for the Rutland Institute for Ethics' High School Ethics Case Competition, as co-director of Tiger SpeakOut, and as a faculty advisor for the Youth Movement Against Alzheimer's. Sara continues to pursue professional development opportunities at Clemson designed to make her a more effective educator. Trainings completed include: Tigers Advance (Women Advancing Together), Suicide Prevention Advocacy training, Green Zone training for teaching student veterans, and various mentor/mentee programs offered at the college and university level.

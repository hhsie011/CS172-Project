We aim to offer a prep course each semester that prepares individuals for their respective certification exam. American Council on Exercise Certifications Group Fitness Instructor | Personal Trainer American Council on Exercise offers NCCA-accredited certifications for group fitness instructors and personal trainers. This course through Campus Recreation will prepare participants to take the ACE exam and provide practical, hands-on skills for leading fitness classes or working with clients. Upon successful completion of the course, participants will have the chance to interview and audition for a position on the fitness team with Campus Recreation. Overview All individuals interested in taking this course will be required to submit an application before registering for the course. Please note, only tweleve (12) applicants will be accepted into the course. Completion of this course does not guarantee employment with Campus Recreation. You will be required to interview and audition for a position.

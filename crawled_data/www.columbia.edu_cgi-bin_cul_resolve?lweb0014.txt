ILL Services Interlibrary Loan (ILL) enables eligible Columbia University affiliates to borrow material from other libraries or obtain copies of articles or book chapters from titles that are not owned or available at Columbia University Libraries. Columbia University Libraries also lends materials to patrons of other libraries through Institutional Borrowing and reciprocal lending arrangements. Having trouble deciding between Borrow Direct and ILL? Compare services. Eligibility for ILL services Students, faculty and staff affiliated with the Morningside or Barnard campus with borrowing privileges in good standing are eligible to use Interlibrary Loan services provided by Butler Library. Provost-appointed visiting scholars with borrowing privileges in good standing are also eligible for ILL. Alumni, seminar associates and other individuals who have purchased library reading or borrowing privileges from the Library Information Office are not eligible. Please consult the Libraries' Access Privileges for a full list of permissions by affiliation. Policies Eligibility: Please check Columbia University Libraries' Access Privileges for eligibility. Loan Period: Both loan period and the ability to renew are designated by the owning institution. Please check either the paperwork or your ILL account to verify the due date and request a renewal as needed. Recall: Please note all Interlibrary Loan materials are subject to recall by the lending institution. Most recalls are for books intended for Reserves. Please return all recalled items as soon as possible. Return: New! Return items at *any* Columbia University Library circulation desk! Turnaround Time: Items usually arrive within one to two weeks. An email notice will be sent to your Columbia email account when item is ready for pick-up. If you need a book, CD or DVD faster please check availability in Borrow Direct. Renewals You can request a renewal for your ILL item provided it is not marked "No Renewals." Renewals are granted at the discretion of the lending library. Log in to your ILL account. Click on Checked Out under the ILL Request Summary section. Click on Renew for the item(s) you wish to renew. You will receive an automated e-mail within a few days notifying you that your renewal was granted or denied. If a renewal was granted, the e-mail will include your new due date. If it was denied, you must return the book by the original due date.

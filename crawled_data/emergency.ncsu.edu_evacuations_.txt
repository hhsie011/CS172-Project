Evacuation procedures for specific buildings are prepared with assistance from the NC State University Fire Marshal’s Office and disseminated by building liaisons to building occupants. Evacuation diagrams are posted near all elevators and in common areas. WHEN DIRECTED TO EVACUATE YOUR BUILDING: Assist any person in immediate danger to safety, if it can be accomplished without risk to yourself Use prescribed evacuation routes Do not use elevators Do not re-enter the building until directed by first responders Please contact your building liaison or the University Fire Marshal’s Office at 919-515-2568 for more information.

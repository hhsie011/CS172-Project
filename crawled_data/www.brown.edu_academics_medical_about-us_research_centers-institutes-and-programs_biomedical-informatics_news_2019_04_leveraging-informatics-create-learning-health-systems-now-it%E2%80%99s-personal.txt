On April 26, 2019 (4:30pm) at The Warren Alpert Medical School, Dr. Peter Embi will describe how evidence generating medicine completes the evidence cycle in the research-practice relationship, advancing us closer to realizing the vision of a learning health system. Dr. Embi is the President and CEO of the Regenstrief Institute. He is also the Associate Dean for Informatics and Health Services Research at the Indiana University School of Medicine, the Associate Director at the Indiana CTSI, and Vice President for Learning Health Systems at IU Health. Dr. Embi will describe how evidence generating medicine completes the evidence cycle in the research-practice relationship, advancing us closer to realizing the vision of a learning health system.

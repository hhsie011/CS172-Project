Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Commencement Menu Search TTU Office of the Provost Commencement Frequently Asked Questions Where is commencement being held? United Supermarkets Arena When will the Graduate RSVP be available? It will be available the beginning of March. The Graduates will be emailed. When is the deadline to RSVP? Graduates must RSVP by April 20. How many tickets will each Graduates be given? No tickets will be required for May 2022. When will the doors open? Doors will open 1 hour prior to each ceremony. When will my graduates name be called? Graduates will be announced in the order that they sit down. The individual recognition will be by college but not alphabetical order. Which college will be announced first? See the program and map. (Information to come end of November) Will the in-person commencement be live streamed? Yes, please follow this link https://www.ttu.edu/livestream/ Can I bring a camera bag or baby bag in? All bags brought into the arena have to be clear. Will photos be taken during the ceremony? Yes, your graduate will have this information. How long will the ceremonies last? We are expecting each ceremony to last 2 hours. Can I wear a decorated cap to my ceremony? Decorated caps are not allowed during our ceremonies.

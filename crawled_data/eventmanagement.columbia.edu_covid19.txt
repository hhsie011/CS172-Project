At-a-glance COVID-19 Restrictions For additional details, please refer to the most recent University Updates. Permitted No capacity restrictions on any events. Eating/drinking onsite (indoors or outdoors) is allowed at gatherings with minimal removal of masks. Visitors are permitted and are required to follow the visitor policy. Masking indoors at Columbia is optional after March 14. We strongly recommend wearing masks when in crowded settings, both indoors and outdoors. Continued Restrictions Red passes will be applied to all those who are not compliant with the Gateway Testing requirement. All non-CU attendees must have proof of vaccination and a booster as well as complete daily attestation prior to accessing a Columbia facility. Individual schools or departments are expected to ensure vaccine compliance when such visitors are invited, and prior to visiting a Columbia facility.

Image credit: Greenhealth Magazine In the summer of 2015, the UW Environmental Stewardship Committee (ESC) chose four projects to focus on as a committee during the 2015-2016 academic year. One of the projects is the Greening Health Sciences Initiative that will research current efforts in the area of environmental stewardship in UW Health Sciences, as well as identify opportunities and coordinate efforts going forward. The initiative will look at both academics and medical center operations. For inquiries or more information on the UW Greening Health Sciences Initiative, contact Sean Schmidt at sgs1@uw.edu.

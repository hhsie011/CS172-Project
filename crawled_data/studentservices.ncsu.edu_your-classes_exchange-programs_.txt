Programs Cooperating Raleigh Colleges (CRC) Inter-Institutional Program Study Abroad Exchange Programs As an NC State University student*, you also have access to the resources of other local colleges and universities through the Inter-Institutional program, the Cooperating Raleigh Colleges program and UNC Online. Cooperating Raleigh Colleges – This program allows you to take on-campus fall/spring courses at Meredith College, Peace College, Shaw University and St. Augustine’s College. Inter-Institutional Program – This program allows you to take on-campus, year-round courses at Duke University, NC Central University, UNC-Chapel Hill, UNC-Charlotte and UNC-Greensboro. Study Abroad – NC State University partners with many international institutions to provide students with enriching experiences on semester- or year-long exchanges. Visit the Study Abroad website to learn more about the various exchange opportunities and how to plan for your experience abroad. *Please note that Non-Degree Studies (NDS) students at NC State are not eligible to participate in exchange programs.

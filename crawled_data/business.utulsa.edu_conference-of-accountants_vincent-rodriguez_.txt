His primary responsibilities include architecting custom business solutions to deliver value through reliable and consistent data transformation, analysis, and prediction. Vincent performs these duties for multiple stakeholders across diverse business domains and tool sets. Prior to working at ONEOK, Vincent served in the US Air Force as a training instructor before completing his BSBA at The University of Tulsa. Vincent is the founder of TU’s Student Veterans Association as well as ONEOK’s Veteran Resource Group. He serves as a member of the IT Advisory Committee for the Tulsa Area United Way and is currently completing his Master of Business Analytics and Data Science at the Oklahoma State University. Vincent is married to Kristin and has the privilege of helping raise their two children: Roman and Bree. Sadie, a Pitbull, and Bruno, a Bea-Bull, round out the list of their dependents. 800 S.

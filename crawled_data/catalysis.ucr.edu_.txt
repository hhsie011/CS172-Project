We look forward to the development of a variety of research projects involving members of the UCR Center as well as collaborators from other institutions in academia, national laboratories, and industry, both in the US and abroad. READ MORE Our Mission Research under the umbrella of our UCR Center for Catalysis is expected to provide a unique experience for undergraduate and graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and visiting researchers, who will work on highly interdisciplinary projects, interact with multiple research groups, and be exposed to a variety of scientific skills, including the synthesis and characterization of complex molecular and solid catalysts and the use of novel nanotechnologies.

The General Bulletin is available in PDF and HTML formats at http://registrar.fsu.edu/bulletin/. The PDF version is easily searchable and allows the reader to print any or all pages of the document. The following information is intended for those individuals involved in reviewing content for the General Bulletin. To update your area reviewer's contact information, please email ar-regpub@fsu.edu and specify the name and contact information for the person who should receive specific Bulletin files.

If you are planning to pursue professional licensure in a state other than Texas, it is strongly recommended that you contact the appropriate licensing entity in that state to seek information and guidance regarding licensure requirements before beginning an academic program. For more information on professional licensure requirements in the field, please contact Ulrich Dangel or visit UT Austin’s professional licensure disclosure page.

Lunch and Learn / Webinar Series The program organizes Lunch and Learn sessions that are led by an executive in residence with groups of 10 to 12 students. These sessions provide an intimate forum for students with similar interests to get to know a particular executive in residence in an informal setting. Participants submit their résumés in advance and receive individual attention and recommendations. To learn more or suggest a Lunch and Learn session, please contact Svetlana McCarroll Swan at [email protected]. Career Management Center (CMC) The CMC is the School's primary resource for helping students build their careers with comprehensive support in assessing career options, finding internships and full time employment. The Executives in Residence Program contributes student career development by providing confidential senior level perspective and advice. Student Clubs and Associations Executives in residence frequently advise student-run clubs and participate in annual club conferences.

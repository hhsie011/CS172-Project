Concerns about potential threats may be referred to the TAMT through our reporting to the TAMT page. The TAMT meets regularly to review all evidence of threatening behavior and to develop strategies for mitigating identified threats. The TAMT will monitor potential threats until they are resolved. Additionally, the TAMT participates in regular training to ensure they are current with best practices in the area of threat identification and mitigation.

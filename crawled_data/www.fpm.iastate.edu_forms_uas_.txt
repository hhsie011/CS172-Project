All uses of UAS for other than ISU research, instruction, or extension must be pre-approved through Facilities Planning and Management (FPM). This applies to non-ISU uses of UAS such as recreation, hobbyist, or commercial. All of main campus is within 5 miles of the local airport, so permission to operate a UAS will include a stipulation that the pilot must obtain airspace authorization through an FAA Approved UAS Service Supplier. Requests will be processed within 2 business days of receipt. Note: All fields are required except comments and file attachments.

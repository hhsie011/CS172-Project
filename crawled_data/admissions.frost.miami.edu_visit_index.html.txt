All visits must be scheduled in advance and follow specific protocols. Contact us for more information. Come and discover for yourself what makes the University of Miami Frost School of Music so special. Friday is the best day for visiting during the school year when you will be able to attend music forums and meet our students and faculty. We offer tours of the Frost School of Music facilities by appointment at the following times (subject to change): Monday - Thursday: 2:30 pm Friday: 10:00 am Please call 305-284-6168 to schedule a visit/tour/appointment or for more information. We also recommend a personal appointment with the Director of Admission and Recruitment. During certain times of the year, including Spring Break, we will be holding group information sessions due to a higher volume of daily visitors. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit, as same-day requests are hard to accommodate and not always possible. The University of Miami Frost School of Music Admission Office is located in the Music Administration Center (MAC). Visitors to campus can find helpful information about parking, maps, and directions on the Parking and Transportation website. Lessons If you would like to take a private lesson or arrange a meeting with any of the Frost School faculty, please contact them directly by visiting the Faculty Profiles section of our website. The FSOM Office of Music Admission does not schedule lessons or meetings on behalf of Frost School faculty. Visit Classes Currently class visits are not available. Browse our website for more detailed information, or call us at any time. We are here to help!

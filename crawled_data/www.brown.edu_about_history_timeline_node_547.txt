Today, the LGBTQ Center, serving lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer and questioning students, staff, faculty, their family and friends and the campus community, is co-located with the Sarah Doyle Women’s Center and offers educational programming, training, support, and advocacy services to the entire Brown community.

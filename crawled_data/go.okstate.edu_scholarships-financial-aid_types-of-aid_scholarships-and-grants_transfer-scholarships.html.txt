Must have 24+ transferable credit hours AND a 3.25 college GPA.Alumni Legacy Certification Form (PDF) Renewable for up to three years with a 2.0 overall GPA. $7,000 each of the first two years and $5,000 if year three is necessary ($19,000 three-year total) *Students eligible for both the Transfer Out-of-State Achievement Scholarship and the Transfer Out-of-State Legacy Scholarship will only receive the scholarship that provides the greatest benefit to the student. You must maintain a minimum GPA and number of credit hours per semester to renew scholarship opportunities each year. Renewable qualifications may vary. Scholarship deadlines Fall-entering students Feb. 1 for Priority Scholarship Consideration; May 1 is College Decision Date. Some scholarships must be accepted by this date or they may be cancelled. August 19 is the last day to submit application materials, such as updated test scores or transcripts, for final scholarship consideration. Spring-entering students Oct. 15 for priority scholarship consideration; the Friday before spring classes begin is the final scholarship deadline.

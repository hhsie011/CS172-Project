We urge you to consult your languages teachers and other department members for advice on choosing the program that is best for you. Brown has a summer program in the Russian Federation (St. Petersburg). Brown offers other pre-approved semester programs in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Irkutsk and Prague. Information about these programs is available at the Office of International Programs. Brown in St. Petersburg As you explore this beautiful Russian city, you will study Russian history and culture with department faculty as well as the Russian language at a level appropriate to you. There are numerous cultural field trips in and around St. Petersburg, lectures conducted in the Russian Museum, and an optional weekend in Moscow. Note: This program is not available in Summer 2022.

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index AVID First Year Experience Menu Search TTU Division of Diversity, Equity & Inclusion AVID First Year Experience AVID First Year Experience (AFYE) seeks to support incoming first-year students as they transition into Texas Tech University by offering a one (1) credit hour course that will enhance students academic success skills for in and out of the classroom experiences. Through a partnership with RaiderReady Programs students will receive all the same benefits & resources offered through the RRP 1100 course in addition to being educated on the AVID Success System. RaiderReady (RRP1100) special section: AVID First Year Experience In addition to participating in the one (1) credit hour course, students will have the opportunity to engage is a wide variety of student success activities sponsored by programs within the Division of Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion.

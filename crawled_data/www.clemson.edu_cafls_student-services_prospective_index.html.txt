Alumni Connection Clemson Home CAFLS CAFLS Student Services Current: Prospective Students Welcome Future Tigers The College of Agriculture, Forestry & Life Sciences is passionate about our degree programs and the industries that we represent. The college represents 12 different academic majors with even more concentrations, emphasis areas, and minor options. Our graduates go onto careers as scientists, teachers, communicators, leaders, policymakers, technicians, managers, conservationists, and so much more. Come learn more about what the College of Agriculture, Forestry & Life Sciences has to offer through a campus tour or request more information by mail. Come for a visit The College of Agriculture, Forestry & Life Sciences offers a number of opportunities for future students to come to campus to learn more about our majors and programs! You can also request more information about any of our majors and programs. Visit us >> Transfer Information Each student's path can greatly differ. We want to help provide you with a plan that fits your needs to transfer and complete your degree successfully.

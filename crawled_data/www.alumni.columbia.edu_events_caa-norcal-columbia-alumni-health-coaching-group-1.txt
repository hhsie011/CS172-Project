Join our alumni Health Coaching Group! Each session will encourage you to reflect on your current health habits, set goals, address challenges, have accountability, and a shared community. There will be 4, 45-minute sessions with homework. To have time for sharing and updates, the group is limited to 12 participants. Speaker Bio: Megan Aronson, founder of Take Care Coaching, LLC, has been in the health and wellness field for over 10 years. She helps organizations run their health coaching programs and also offers individual and group health coaching services. She has a Master's in Health Education from Teachers College at Columbia University. She is also a Master Certified Health Education Specialist, National Board Certified Health & Wellness Coach, and Certified Professional Health & Wellness Coach.

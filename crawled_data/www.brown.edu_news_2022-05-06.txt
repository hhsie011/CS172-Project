For These Residents, It’s All Sound and Fury. Some Miami Gardens locals sued over noise levels ahead of new Grand Prix Hard Rock Stadium in Miami Gardens, Fla., will host a Formula One Grand Prix race this weekend. Vanessa Charlot for The Wall Street Journal By Omar Abdel-Baqui Updated May 6, 2022 10:59 am ET Share Text Listen to article (2 minutes) The thunderous shrills of Formula One racing cars are coming to sunny Miami Gardens, Fla., this weekend. Some locals are less than thrilled. A consortium of residents in the metropolitan Miami community are pushing officials to pump the brakes on the event. They sued the parties helping hold the event, including the City of Miami Gardens, a Formula One subsidiary and the stadium owners, in an effort to block it.

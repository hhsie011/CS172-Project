Sometimes external stressors, brain chemistry, or a combination of both can lead to depression. People can become depressed and feel overwhelmed when under a great deal of stress (like moving away to college, a parent's illness, divorce or loss of job, keeping up with grades, paying for college, etc.). Sometimes it feels like you are all alone and you just don’t know what to do. Even when things seem the darkest, there is help available, you are not alone! Please contact us at the Baylor Counseling Center and talk to a counselor today Walk-In or Call us: (254) 710-2467 Monday – Friday 8:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m. After hours and weekends call the crisis line and you will talk to a counselor.

All classes are held on-line this year! The Office of Equal Opportunity & Access is pleased to have the second cohort participate in the Equal Opportunity & Access ACADEMY (EO ACADEMY). The EO ACADEMY is a 1 year, 10 class in-house certificate program. The EO ACADEMY provides continuous learning from subject matter experts throughout the year which involves CORE foundational courses (LEVEL 1), ADVANCE courses (LEVEL 2) and elective coursework. The goal of the EO ACADEMY is to increase participants’ knowledge and skills in the field of affirmative action and equal opportunity. In addition, the EO ACADEMY provide participants the opportunity to network, build relationships with campus partners and other human resource professionals; and affords participants the experience of mentorship. Hurry and register by Friday, September 10, 2021 at oeohelp@uc.edu for the upcoming EO ACADEMY experience. The first class is scheduled Wednesday, September 22, 2021. If you have questions, please contact the Office of Equal Opportunity & Access via email at oeohelp@uc.edu or by phone 513.556-5503.

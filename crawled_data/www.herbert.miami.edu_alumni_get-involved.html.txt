As part of one of the nation’s top research universities, whose extraordinary momentum makes it one of the great success stories in higher education, the Miami Herbert Business School is on a clear path to become a world leader in global business education and research. We rely on the support of our alumni and other friends to continue the momentum and invite you to get involved! Get Involved Make a Gift There are many ways to show your support for the Miami Herbert Business School. The School's development staff will work with you to ensure that your gift meets your goals and financial needs while also benefiting the School. Ways to Give Hire an Intern or Graduate Miami Herbert Business School alumni are extremely valuable to the career placement process. Post internship and job opportunities for current students or alumni. Miami Herbert Graduate Career Advancement: Call 305-284-6905 or email Miami Herbert Career Advancement to connect and collaborate with graduate business students including MBAs and specialized master's students in Business Analytics, Finance, Sustainable Business, International Business, Health Administration, and Leadership. Toppel Career Center: Call 305-284-5451 or visit the Toppel Career Center to connect and collaborate with undergraduate students. Attend an Event The Miami Herbert Business School hosts alumni events throughout the year, in Miami and across the country, ranging from networking receptions to business impact conferences that draw hundreds annually. View our upcoming events schedule for an event near you or learn more about our annual industry impact conferences.

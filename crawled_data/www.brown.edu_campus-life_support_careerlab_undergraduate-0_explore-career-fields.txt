This page is designed to aid you in your career search and exploration process. Whether you are just setting out and learning about different possible job opportunities in the areas of your interest, or ready to apply to specific positions within your field there are resources that live here to aid you in that process. Below are fourteen parent categories of potential career fields. Many of the categories contain a combination of non-profit, for profit and government opportunities. By clicking on the parent category, you will be directed to job or field specific pages compiled with a variety of resources to facilitate the pursuit of your future career. Enjoy and good luck. Pre-Professional School Advising Health Careers Advising Health Careers Advising at Brown supports students and recent alumni interested in all major pathways into healthcare, including human and veterinary medicine, dentistry, nursing, optometry and much more. Visit the Health Careers Advising website to learn about advising resources, application processes, and office hours. You can also contact Health Careers Advising by phone at (401) 863-2781.

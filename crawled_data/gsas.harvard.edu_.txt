The Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS) at Harvard University is where scholarship and innovation meet, where ideas are challenged and theories developed, where new knowledge is created, and where scholars emerge. We actively recruit and cultivate a diverse community of students across 58 fields of study. We also guarantee 5 years of funding to all PhD candidates.

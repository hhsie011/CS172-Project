Research at the iSchool explores the interactions of people and information; the processes of managing and organizing information for meaning and use; and the impact of new technologies and behaviors on individuals, organizations and society. Our Research Grants & Awards Labs Human-Centered Data Science We critically analyze and use data in an ethically responsible manner in artificial intelligence, machine learning, information retrieval, data curation, and data analysis. Human-AI Interaction Lab Information Retrieval and Crowdsourcing Lab User Experience (UX) Research & Design We study and design cutting-edge information and technology systems that are rooted in the user experience and consider broader societal impacts. Information eXperience Lab (IX Lab) Immersive Human Development Lab Health Informatics We study the role of information and technology in health care and health decision-making in clinical, patient, and medical research settings. AI Health Lab Social Justice Informatics We leverage information and technology for societal good, helping to empower individuals and eliminate inequalities to ensure a level playing field for everyone in the Information Age. Cultural Heritage Informatics We manage, describe, organize, preserve, and provide access to information and materials in a wide range of technological forms from over the decades and centuries. Kilgarlin Information Preservation Lab Social Informatics We leverage data, information, and technology to improve organizational efficiency while also providing a more ethical and humane environment for workers and society. Critical Data Studies Lab The Bowden Folio iSchool Research At the iSchool, research transcends boundaries and crosses disciplines in order to understand the ways information and technology change our behavior, our organizations, and our society.

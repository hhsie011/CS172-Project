Appointments and Drop-in Schedules Cancellation and No-Show Policy for Scheduled Appointments: If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please notify our office 24 hours in advance. This allows our staff to offer your appointment time to another student, who is in need of our services. Failure to cancel your appointment within 24 hours, more than once, will result in losing your appointment privileges for the semester; however, you may still utilize our drop-in services. Spring Schedule: Appointments: Students can call (864) 656-0440 to schedule an individual in-person or virtual appointment with a counselor for questions or assistance. Appointments are available 10 am to 11:30 am and 1:30 pm to 4 pm Monday through Friday. You can also email our staff at cucareercenter@gmail.com to schedule an appointment. Drop-in Counseling Hours: Students may drop-in for quick, 15 minute one-on-one counseling sessions from 1:30 pm to 3:45 pm, Monday - Friday. Virtual Drop-in Counseling Hours: Students may request a quick, 15 minute one-on-one virtual counseling session from 1:30 pm to 3:45 pm, Monday through Friday by starting a chat session with a member of our staff. The on-call staff member will provide the Zoom link for the day through the chat wiindow. The chat feature is located to the bottom right of our home page. You can also email our staff at cucareercenter@gmail.com Virtual Services: We are here to assist you! Visit our virtual services page to see all of our virtual services. INT 8010 CPT Advising: If you have a 130 form and need CPT approval from the career center, email your 130 form and a copy of your offer letter to bnewsom@clemson.edu. The offer letter must include: start/end dates, pay, hours per week, and job description. Questions about which INT course to take? Email bnewsom@clemson.edu. Co-op CPT Advising: Students enrolled in Co-op with a Co-op offer should call (864) 656-3150 to schedule a time for advising. Monday - Friday, 8 am to 4:30 pm.

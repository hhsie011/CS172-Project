Each course builds upon the other. The three required journalism courses provide a core of information for beginning journalism students. Required Courses COMM 233 - Mass Communication in Modern Society (3) COMM 101 - Writing for Mass Media (3) COMM 201 - Digital Reporting and Writing (3) COMM 202 - Digital Reporting and Production (3) Electives (3 units) COMM 310 - Mass Media Ethics (3) COMM 370 - Principles and History of American Mass Communication (3) COMM 407 - Communications Law (3) Broadcast Journalism Track Required Course COMM 372 - TV News Production (3) Electives (3 units) COMM 331 - News Literacy (3) COMM 334 - Feature Article Writing (3) COMM 335 - Public Affairs Reporting (3) COMM 377 - Radio-Audio News (3) COMM 380 - Interactive Media Design (3) COMM 435 - Opinion Writing (3) COMM 436 - Reporting on the Entertainment Industry (3) COMM 438T - Specialized Reporting (3) COMM 472 - Capstone - TV News (3) Print Journalism Track Required Course COMM 471 - Capstone - Daily Titan News (3) Electives (3 units) COMM 331 - News Literacy (3) COMM 332 - Editing and Design (3) COMM 334 - Feature Article Writing (3) COMM 335 - Public Affairs Reporting (3) COMM 380 - Interactive Media Design (3) COMM 434 - Magazine Editing and Production (3) COMM 435 - Opinion Writing (3) COMM 436 - Reporting on the Entertainment Industry (3) COMM 437 - Advanced Magazine Writing (3) COMM 438T - Specialized Reporting (3) Additional Information Students majoring in communications cannot minor in communications.

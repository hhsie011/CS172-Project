By default, the following roles are available on Columbia Sites: Content Manager Available on all models Has all content privileges: creating, editing, and publishing Profile Editor Available on the Academic Model Profile editors can only modify their personal profile Columbia Sites allows you to see which users are assigned to these roles. Select People from the administrative menu Select the role you want to filter your listing NOTE: You can select to filter my multiple roles by holding down SHIFT and clicking on the roles Click the Filter button That's it! The people listing will filter on whatever role was selected.

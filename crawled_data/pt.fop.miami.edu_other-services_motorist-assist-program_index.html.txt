Program News and Updates Event Coordinators Charter Bus Services Cane Card Safety Escort Motorist Assist Program Lyft and Taxi Airport Transportation The Motorist Assist Program is designed to assist those who experience minor vehicle problems while on the Coral Gables campus. The Motorist Assistance Program can provide jump-starts for dead batteries, access to your locked vehicle, and temporary fixes for flat tires. For assistance, contact UM Police at 305-284-6666. The service is available 24/7.

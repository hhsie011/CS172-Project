Learn more Research The Warren Alpert Medical School is making a profound impact in advancing human health through research. In partnership with our affiliated teaching hospitals, The Warren Alpert Medicine School is training the next generation of physician-scientists and bringing together researchers and clinicians from across the life sciences to translate scientific discoveries into groundbreaking medical treatments. Decoding Covid-19 Seminar Series The Division of Biology and Medicine hosted a series of virtual seminars related to the novel coronavirus and the present pandemic. Those seminars are available on The Warren Alpert Medical School YouTube channel.

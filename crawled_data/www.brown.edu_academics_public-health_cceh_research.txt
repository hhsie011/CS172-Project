Health Effects of Environmental Exposures - We study if and when exposure to environmental chemicals affects childhood health. Modifiable Determinants of Environmental Exposures - Members of our center study the sources and determinants of chemical exposures in pregnant women, infants, children, and adolescents. Interventions to Reduce Chemical Exposures - We are developing effective, sustainable, and scalable interventions that reduce exposure to multiple chemicals. Molecular Epidemiology of Environmental Exposures - We study the mechanisms that environmental chemicals affect human health.

His primary goal is to continually improve every aspect of the education here and to maintain and enhance IU Bloomington’s status as one of the world’s leading research universities. The Office of the Provost oversees the academic programs, research, and policies of the schools on the Bloomington campus. All deans, vice provosts, and leaders of campus administrative units report to the provost.Get to know our provost Our vice provosts Our eight vice provosts oversee specific priorities of the university, such as recruiting a diverse student body and securing top research opportunities for faculty.

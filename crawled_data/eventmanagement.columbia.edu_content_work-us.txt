Want to work with our team? The Event Management staff is committed to developing and retaining a qualified student workforce that embraces the spirit of the University. Student staff must be able to anticipate, understand and meet the needs of customers; work as part of a team; represent the integrity of Columbia University and, most importantly, have fun. In return, a job with Event Management provides student staff with many valuable skills that will transfer in work beyond Columbia University. Positions here offer a behind the scenes look at event management, customer service training and interaction with highly skilled and diverse members of the Event Management team. Not a student? Non-Columbia students or affiliates interested in working with our team should visit careers.columbia.edu to browse available positions and apply.

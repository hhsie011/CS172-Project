Recruiting and Diversity Jane Seibel Share Recruitment Jane Seibel, Assistant Dean for Recruitment, Diversity, and Communications has an active recruitment program including visits to several conferences throughout the year, as well as a summer research program. This information will be posted on the Recruitment page with dates as we have them, but if you would like to view a comprehensive but not exhaustive list of diversity recruiting initiatives, you can view them HERE (link opens in pdf). Conferences and Events The Guarini School of Graduate and Advanced Studies at Dartmouth will send admissions staff, faculty members, students and alumni representatives to recruitment events over the course of the 2021-2022 year. Stay tuned for announcements about our recruiting calendar. Keep informed by joining our mailing list.

A vibrant community of students, scholars, and visitors, the Watson Institute strives to enhance our understanding of the world's greatest challenges and offer comparative and innovative ideas and solutions. Our faculty and students work across ten regional- and area-focused centers and initiatives, three undergraduate concentrations, and two graduate programs to move forward the Watson Institute's mission of promoting a more just and peaceful world.

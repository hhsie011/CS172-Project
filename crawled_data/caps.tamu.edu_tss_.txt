REGISTER HERE Registration deadline is Friday, June 11th In-person/ Online FRIDAY 6.24.22 Texas A&M University and Baylor University have collaborated to create a space for education, networking, and support around clinical supervision and training within a university counseling center context. The conference will occur in person on the campus of Texas A&M University in College Station, TX on Friday, June 24th. For those who are unable to attend in person, a virtual option will be available. The online option will be live streamed as opposed to recorded. Registration is $40. This year’s symposium will provide 5 CEU's and sessions by multiple presenters. Have a question? Send us an email. Conference Agenda: 9:30 am—9:45 am Welcome and Introduction 9:45 am—11:45 am Keynote: Enedalia Sauceda, Ph.D.--- “Connecting is hard right now, y’all…Practicing mindful authenticity in supervision.” 12:00 pm—1:00 pm Lunch Break 1:00 pm—2:30 pm Workshop 1: TBD 2:40 pm-3:00 pm Afternoon Break 3:00 pm—4:30 pm Workshop 2: TBD 4:30 pm—4:45 pm Evaluations and closing remarks Past Symposium Presenters and Foci Dr. Archandria Owens, Ph.D., “Supervising While Black: Racial Trauma and the Supervisory Relationship in the time of George Floyd, Breonna Taylor, and Jacob Blake" Dr. Robert Carter, Ph.D.,"Exciting the Human Potential: Connecting with Ourselves and with the People that we Supervise." Dr. Carmen Cruz, Psy.D., "Working on my Multicultural Competence as a Supervisor in a Divisive Cultural Climate." Dr. Cecilia Sun, Ph.D., “When Supervision is Hard: Strategies for Working Through Difficulties in Supervision.” CO-HOSTED BY TEXAS A&M UNIVERSITY & BAYLOR UNIVERSITY TSS Planning Committee: Esther Hooley, Ph.D. Ambika Kasbekar, M.S., LPC, NCC Ed Rogers, Psy.D.

School Mirror Awards #SUGrad22 SUccess Week Home Attend Forever Orange Week Forever Orange Week Forever Orange Week highlights the lifelong connection to Syracuse University that begins the moment a student first steps on campus—highlighting ways students can be campus leaders, fostering Orange pride, underscoring the strength of the Syracuse alumni network and emphasizing the importance of giving back. The week is designed to engage both students and alumni, honor what it means to be Forever Orange and commemorate the University's anniversary.

Updates for 2021-22 The piano in the Wallach Lounge was moved to Lerner Hall. The Wallach Lounge is closed to undergo renovations to turn the space into a dedicated e-gaming lounge. Key Features: John Jay Dining Hall, JJ's Place, and Columbia Health Medical Services are located in close proximity Expansive views of Morningside Campus, the Hudson River, and the historic Harlem neighborhood Located in the heart of campus Easy access to the Columbia Housing Hospitality Desk Building Details: Housing Rate: Either First-Year or Upperclass Rates depending on class of student Entrance location: Inside campus near 115th Street Number of residential floors: 9 Single/Double rooms within semi-suites: 141 singles / 51 doubles Elevators: 2 Flooring: Hardwood Building Amenities: Bathroom Style: Single-use, gender inclusive bathrooms are available on each floor Laundry: Free laundry facility available in both Hartley and John Jay Halls, which connect to Wallach on either side Kitchen: One full kitchen per floor Lounge: A building lounge is available on the first floor (currently closed for renovation) A study lounge is located on the 10th floor Large floor lounges are available on floors 3-8 Each suite has a common area equipped with cable TV service Cleaning Responsibility & Schedule: Bathrooms: Twice weekly by Facilities Lounges: Daily, Monday - Friday, by Facilities Trash & Recycling Residents are responsible for taking their trash and recycling to designated building area Additional Photos: Wallach student room Columbia Housing Portal Submit a Service Request Housing Web Forms Contact Us: 212-854-2946 (Monday - Friday 9:00 - 5:00) 212-854-2779 (After hours and weekends) [email protected] Questions?

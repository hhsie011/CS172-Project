Health Promotion Counseling and Psychological Services Disability Services Gay Health Advocacy Project (GHAP) Insurance Office Medical Services Sexual Violence Response Columbia Health You are here: Home Forms and Guidelines MMR Medical Exemption Instructions and Form MMR Medical Exemption Instructions and Form January 31, 2022 Instructions: Morningside and Manhattanville Students: Please complete this form and submit, with all required documentation, via the Columbia Health Patient Portal. Go to secure.health.columbia.edu and log in with your UNI. Select "Medical Clearances" from the left-hand menu. Select "Update" next to Upload Immunization Files. Select "Upload" and attach the form and required documentation. Accepted formats are gif, jpg, png, and pdf. There is a 4 MB file size limit If the item is legible, click the "Looks Good" button; then click the "Save" button. After you upload and submit your entry, the University will review your request. Allow at least 15 business days for processing. CUIMC Students: Please complete this form and submit, with all required documentation, via the Student Health on Haven Patient Portal. Read the pre-registration instructions requirements. Go to portal.studenthealth.cuimc.columbia.edu and log in with your UNI. Follow the instructions on the portal. Note that this immunization documentation is not listed on the current pre-registration forms.

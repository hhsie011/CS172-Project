closely with Provost center leadership, FRES, and the Provost's office, Provost Administrative Affairs Planning, Design, and Construction provides leadership and support for capital projects in the Provost’s centers. Planning, Design, and Construction assists with master planning, space planning, and all phases of capital projects including project initiation. All planning, design, and construction projects throughout all Provost Centers are tracked by the team. They work collaboratively with FRES, design consultants, and contractors on the implementation and delivery of projects. Important Links: To keep the Planning, Design, and Construction team aware of all capital projects and small projects requests so that they can represent the Provost Center in the process, please submit this project request form, before completing one of the below: Initiate a Capital Project (renovations and new construction with estimated total costs of $100,000 or more) Small Projects Request (renovations or improvements that total less than $100,000) For any questions, please contact the Planning, Design, and Construction team. 122 College Hall University of Pennsylvania Philadelphia, PA 19104 215-898-5000 provost@upenn.edu ©2022 University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA 19104 Footer Menu University of Pennsylvania Penn Directory Site Credits

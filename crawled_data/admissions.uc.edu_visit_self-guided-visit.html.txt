Representatives right arrow down arrow Out of State Representatives Social Media Admissions » Visit Campus » Self-Guided Visit Self-Guided Visit Explore the University of Cincinnati on your own! While guided tour options may be limited, we invite you to come to campus and get to know UC on your own time and at your own pace. Pick and choose the areas you want to see most and spend as much time as you'd like getting to know your future home. Self-Guided Tour Resources To assist you in planning for your visit and to help you navigate campus, we encourage you to familiarize yourself with the resources below. Visit Booklet Take a look at our visit booklet and learn more about our world-renowned campus and vibrant city. Hear straight from the students about why they chose UC and their experiences so far. Also, be sure to utilize the provided maps and resources which will help you navigate campus during your self-guided visit. Please note that a self-guided tour route is available on page 10 of the booklet. If you'd like a printed version of the route, stop by the Admissions Welcome Desk on the 3rd floor of University Pavilion. Welcome to UC, Admissions Visit booklet 2021 Download the visit booklet (PDF) Virtual Campus Tour Before visiting, familiarize yourself with campus by watching our virtual campus tour. Visitor Guidelines To ensure the health and safety of our university community, please review our visitor guidelines prior to your arrival on campus. Campus Parking Get more information on campus parking and walking directions to University Pavilion. Please note that as federal, state, local, and university guidelines related to COVID-19 continue to shift, opportunities and visitor expectations may shift. Always refer to the most recent communication from the University of Cincinnati for updated guidelines and information.

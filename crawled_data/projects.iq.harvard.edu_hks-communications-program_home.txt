About Leadership & Administration Academic Deans' Office Communications Program The Harvard Kennedy School (HKS) Communications Program helps students improve, develop, and execute written, spoken, and digital policy communications skills. Program offerings include for-credit communications courses, workshops throughout the academic year, as well as writing consultants who offer one-on-one consultations for HKS students. Communications Courses & Workshops The HKS Communications Program consists of both for-credit and not-for-credit offerings. A series of for-credit communications courses focusing on public speaking and writing are taught by communications faculty. In addition, more than 60 workshops are available throughout the academic year. Communications Courses & Workshops Writing Consultants & Resources We offer free private sessions with writing consultants, who can help with writing assignments, or polishing a draft for publication. Resources are also available for Writing, Public Speaking and Digital Communications.

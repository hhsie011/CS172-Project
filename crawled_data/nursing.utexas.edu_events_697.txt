Avoid Close Contact and interaction between students Face masks are recommended but not required Wash hands and cover sneezes/coughs – use hand sanitizer regularly Adhere to AHA Covid19 classroom recommendations – No pocket masks and 1-1 checks If you are sick – don’t come to class If you have any questions pertaining to our certification courses, please email BLSClasses@nursing.utexas.edu. American Heart Association (AHA) BLS Provider (CPR) is an instructor/lecture led, video-based course that teaches both single-rescuer and team basic life support skills for application in both prehospital and in-facility environments, with a focus on High-Quality CPR for all ages, how to use an AED, and choking relief in adults and infants. 100% classroom training means students are with an AHA instructor for their entire learning experience.

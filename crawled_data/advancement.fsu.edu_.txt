In support of these efforts, University Advancement works to increase Florida State University's capacity for generating private philanthropy and volunteer support. The mission of University Advancement is to enhance the University's leadership, reputation and private financial support in academics, research, service and athletics. Under the leadership of Interim Vice President Michael Hartline, the division oversees the FSU Alumni Association, FSU Foundation, and Real Estate Foundation.

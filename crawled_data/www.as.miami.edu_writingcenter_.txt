Welcome to the Writing Center Writing Center About Us Support for Writers Support for Teachers News Advising Give Events Publications The Writing Center at the University of Miami offers free, one-on-one assistance with all types of writing concerns. The Writing Center can help you at any stage of the writing process, from brainstorming to final revisions. The Writing Center serves all of the University of Miami community, including undergraduate and graduate students, faculty, and staff. For help with your writing projects, schedule a consultation today. Appointments last 50 minutes. To get the most from your Writing Center session, please be ready to share both your paper and the assignment prompt (when applicable). Click below to schedule a consultation.

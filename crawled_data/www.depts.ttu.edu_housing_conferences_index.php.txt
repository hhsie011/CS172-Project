We are excited to work with you in making your conference the best it can be and to show you some of our famous West Texas hospitality. From booking to billing, we want every summer conference to be a success. Conference Services include assistance with overnight accommodations and group meal plans. Please feel free to contact the Manager for Conference Services for any questions regarding this process. Conferences Season Summer Conferences run from June 1st – August 1st. Reservations open on Monday, November 1st, at 8:00 a.m. CST. Contact Conference Services For additional information, please contact the Camps and Conferences Team at housing.conferences@ttu.edu or Luke Ellison, Manager for Conference Services at

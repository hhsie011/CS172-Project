Cornell’s travel, health, and safety program is a critical university-wide service provided by Global Operations at no charge to the Cornell community. We provide: predeparture education and advising, a coordinated response to incidents and emergencies, and insurance coverage for emergency evacuation and urgent medical needs. We also monitor impactful world events 24/7 and offer country-specific risk analysis if enhanced intelligence is required. Site visit guidance for program evaluations is also available. As you pursue opportunities abroad, you can count on us to provide vital resources, professional expertise, and support. We encourage you to review all the services we offer on our travel pages.

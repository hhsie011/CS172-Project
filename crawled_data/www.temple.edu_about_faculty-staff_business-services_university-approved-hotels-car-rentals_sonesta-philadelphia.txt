Sonesta Philadelphia 1800 Market St. Philadelphia, PA 19103 Sonesta Philadelphia is located in the heart of Center City and is a very convenient drive to Temple University. Sonesta is easily accessible to SEPTA’s Broad Street Line, Regional Rail and bus routes. Walk to restaurants, shops and entertainment. Temple University Preferred Rates Sonesta is direct-bill approved and offers the following preferred rates to any faculty, students, alumni or affiliate of Temple University. Room rates for the Sonesta hotel Month Standard Room Deluxe Rooms Jan.1, 2022–March 31, 2022 $137.00 $147.00 April 1, 2022–Nov. 20, 2022 $167.00 $177.00 Nov. 21, 2022– March 13, 2023 $137.00 $147.00 March 14, 2023–Nov. 19, 2023 $167.00 $177.00 Nov. 20, 2023–Dec. 31, 2023 $137.00 $147.00 Direct Bill Reservations To check the availability of your dates, please contact Kim Cook at kcook@sonesta.com, Alison White at awhite1@sonesta.com and Michele Simpson at msimpson@sonesta.com. The reservation form should be filled out for each guest and emailed to the hotel for confirmation. This is only for reservations that are being paid for by the university. Reservations Not Paid for by the University For reservations that are not being paid for by the university, please instruct the individual to use this promo code to make their reservation directly online. The promo code for Temple University (TEM) is already embedded to generate the preferred rate. You can also call 1-800-SONESTA to request the Temple University preferred rate. Group Reservation and Meeting Space Requests For reservation requests of 10 or more rooms per night, or to request a meeting space, please contact Michele Simpson directly at msimpson@sonesta.com or 215-561-7500 to check availability. Parking The valet-only parking facility located at the hotel is not owned or operated by Sonesta and rates are subject to change without prior notice. The current rates for registered hotel guests include tax and allow for unlimited in-and-out privileges.

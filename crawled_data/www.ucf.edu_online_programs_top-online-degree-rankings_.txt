See all top online bachelor degrees HOW THE BEST ONLINE BACHELOR’S RANKINGS ARE CHOSEN How are the Best Online Bachelor’s Rankings chosen? U.S. News & World Report is a multi-platform publisher of news and information that includes usnews.com and annual print and e-book versions of its authoritative rankings of Best Colleges, Best Graduate Schools and Best Hospitals. U.S. News evaluates several factors to rank the best online bachelor’s degree programs, including graduation rates, faculty credentials and support services available for fully online students. These are assessed in four categories used to determine a school’s standing: engagement, services and technologies, faculty credentials and training, and expert opinion. For the Best Online Programs for Veterans ranking, all schools included in these rankings first have to be ranked among the top half of schools in U.S. News & World Report’s 2021 Best Online Programs rankings. These rankings measure these factors in consideration to financial benefits available specifically to people with military experience, such as program reputation, faculty credentials, retention rates, graduate debt loads, GI Bill status, and population of students with a military background. How the Most Innovative Rank is Chosen How Are Most Innovative Rankings chosen? A Most Innovative school, as determined by U.S. News & World Report, is one making promising innovations in technology, curriculum and other areas. To help identify these schools, U.S. News asks university presidents, provosts and admissions deans at some of the top academic colleges and universities across the country to name the institutions they think develop and deliver innovative ideas, opportunities and solutions. In a peer assessment survey, each university leader who contributed to the survey rankings were tasked to nominate up to 15 institutions in their U.S. News Best Colleges ranking category that are making the most innovative improvements in terms of curriculum, faculty, students, campus life, technology or facilities.

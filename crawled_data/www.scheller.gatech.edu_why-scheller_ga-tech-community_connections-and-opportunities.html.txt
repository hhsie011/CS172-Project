Learning is not strictly a classroom activity here. Our strong connections to global, non-profit and startup companies will broaden your mind and strengthen your resume. The many paths open to you here will enable you to hone your interests and develiop a strong record of accomplishment before you graduate. The experiences and relationships you develop here will last a lifetime and can even provide access to some of the top leaders in your field throughout your career. Involvement with the Advanced Technology Development Center (ATDC) and the Georgia Centers for Advanced Telecommunications Technology will help develop your understanding of developing technology businesses outside the classroom. Internships, co-ops, work and study abroad programs will provide platforms from which to gain experience and accelerate your "real-world" savvy. At Scheller, these opportunties might take the form of leadership roles in student clubs and organizations; networking with faculty, fellow students and successful alumni; and participation in College acitivies such as the IMPACT Speaker Series, MBA practicums, and organized social events.

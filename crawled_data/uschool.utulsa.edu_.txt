University School has been a leader in the field of gifted education since its inception in 1982. We provide a challenging yet nurturing environment for our students and look forward to the opportunity to introduce you to our program. These tours offer an excellent opportunity to see our facility, visit classrooms and meet with our director. University School University School is a full school program for gifted children from age three through eighth grade. The school provides an emotionally supportive and intellectually challenging learning environment that rewards both creativity and socially responsible behavior.

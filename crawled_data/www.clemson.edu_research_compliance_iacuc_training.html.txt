All Clemson University personnel (including students) that will be providing direct care or supervision of animals or working in research or teaching protocols that use vertebrate animals must be listed on the AUP and complete the following: Online animal welfare training through the Collaborative Institutional Training Initiative (CITI) Program Complete BOTH the “Basic Course for Research Investigators, Students and Animal Care Staff” AND the “General Zoonotic Training” courses. Enrollment in the Clemson University Medical Surveillance Program (MSP) Personnel Training Policy All non-Clemson personnel who are working in research or teaching protocols that use vertebrate animals must complete the following: Non-Clemson Personnel Form Complete the Clemson University animal welfare training (CITI) or provide evidence of other animal welfare training (approved by the IACUC) Provide a curriculum vitae (CV) or suitable alternate document Review Clemson University Policy 2.3 concerning Non-Clemson Personnel participating in activities involving Clemson University owned animals Home Welcome InfoEd Institutional Review Board Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee Training Resources Forms Contact Us Reporting Concerns Institutional Biosafety Committee Responsible Conduct of Research Research Misconduct Office of the Vice President for Research Home Admissions Campus Life Academics Visit Outreach Research About Apply To Clemson Undergraduate Graduate Professional Online Education Employment Opportunities Policy/Terms of Use Text Version Web Accessibility Ethics/Safety Hotline Privacy Policy Site Information Copyright © 2022 Clemson University Clemson, South Carolina 29634 864-656-3311 Contact Clemson Stories for the Clemson Family Clemson University on Facebook Clemson University on Twitter Clemson University on LinkedIn Clemson University on Flickr Clemson University on Instagram

Parking Locations Parking regulations are enforced in all lots, including 30 minute zones (Green curbs), as follows: Monday through Thursday from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. and Friday from 7:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Parking regulations are enforced 24 hours daily for red curb/fire lanes, 15 minute time zones, state and service/maintenance stalls, disabled stalls, special permit parking spaces, loading zones, and white and yellow curb. Parking Lot Locations include: Student Semester Parking: ( on map below) Resident Semester Parking: ( on map below) Faculty/Staff Parking: See ( on map below) Electric Vehicle (EV) Parking: Charge an electric vehicle on campus for $0.40 kilowatts/hour. Students and Faculty/Staff can receive free charging with an EV Permit. Park and Pay Spaces: Visitors and guests can purchase a Park and Pay permit for 1-2 hours at $4/hour. Spaces are not compatible with a daily, semester, or annual parking permit. ParkMobile Zones: Visitors can complete a contactless parking transaction for $4/hour in marked ParkMobile zones on campus.

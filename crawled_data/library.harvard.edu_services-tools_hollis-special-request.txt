HOLLIS Special Request is where you can manage two types of requests: Reading room requests: Requests to view special collection items in a specific library reading room Copy requests: Requests for digital or print reproductions of library materials Launch HOLLIS Special Request Things to know The first time you use the system you'll be prompted to create an account. You do not need a Harvard ID to create an account. To make a request, locate the item you'd like in HOLLIS and click "Request to Copy or Visit" You can also sign into your account and fill out the reading room request or copy request form. Your account allows you to track your current and past requests. In your account you can create draft requests and save them until you're ready to visit the library to view the materials. Need Help?

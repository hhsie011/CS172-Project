A medical permit costs $23 per week and allows parking in Gold zones, regardless of what type of parking permit you have now. To obtain a medical parking permit: Have your doctor fax the following information on a prescription pad or letterhead to 785-864-5220: Your Name KUID (if a student) How many weeks you'll need to have closer parking Come into the KU Parking office with a photo ID to complete paperwork and pay Temporary Accessible Placards If you need special parking for more than 1-3 weeks, talk to your doctor about obtaining a temporary accessible placard from the state.

We thank you for your interest in the mission and vision of Baylor University and invite you to make a gift to support student scholarships, faculty research and needs and priorities within the School of Music today. Center for Christian Music Studies This fund helps strengthen efforts by the Center for Christian Music Studies to help students excel as Christian musicians and worship leaders by providing much-needed resources for students' growth. Daniel Sternberg Society Gifts to the Daniel Sternberg Society provide scholarship support for deserving students in the School of Music, while also remembering the life of Dean Emeritus Daniel Sternberg and his many years of service at Baylor. Golden Wave Band The Golden Wave Band is the centerpiece of many Baylor traditions. Your support helps the band update instruments and uniforms, travel with athletics teams, compete in prestigious competitions and provide scholarships for deserving students. Music Education Excellence Fund Music Teachers and Choir Directors bring so much more than the gift of music into their students' lives. They teach children about other cultures, times and points of view through music. Join us in preparing tomorrow’s teachers today! School of Music Endowed Scholarship Your support helps the School of Music stand in the gap for outstanding musicians who need financial support to continue their pursuit of a music education in Baylor's program. School of Music Excellence Fund Gifts help students in Baylor's School of Music hone their skills by providing updated instruments and sheet music, performance expenses and opportunities for ensembles and conferences. Contact Us To learn more about opportunities to give to the School of Music, contact Brett Evans, Assistant Vice President for Development.

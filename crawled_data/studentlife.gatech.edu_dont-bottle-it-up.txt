GT CARE: CARE is the initial access point for mental health support at Georgia Tech. Students seeking assistance from the Counseling Center or Stamps Psychiatry need to visit CARE first for a primary assessment and referral to on and off campus mental health and well-being resources. Collegiate Recovery Program – The CRP provides support from substance use disorders and is offering virtual recovery seminars and individual consultations during this time. For more information please contact Christina Owens at christina.owens@studentlife.gatech.edu. Visit the website here.

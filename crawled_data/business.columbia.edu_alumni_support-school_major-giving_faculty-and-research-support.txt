Supporting the work of the School’s exceptional faculty—both in and outside of the classroom—is essential to the creation and maintenance of the living, breathing bridge between theory and practice that is the foundation of a Columbia education. Endowed chairs provide the School with a powerful recruiting tool in an intensely competitive marketplace. By encouraging outstanding faculty to continue their careers at this world-class institution, these chairs enhance not only the student experience, but also the intellectual capital of the School. Gifts toward research provide faculty with vital in-house funding, foster a dynamic academic environment, and encourage important connections between the School and distinguished professionals.

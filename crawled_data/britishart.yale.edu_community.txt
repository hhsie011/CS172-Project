Read more Gateway to British Art Prizewinners 2021 Making Sense of Art Adults from senior care facilities, retirement communities, and independent learning centers are welcome to join theme-based art lessons for a multisensory experience. Led by docents, this program actively engages participants through thoughtful observation of an artwork, sharing ideas, and relating narratives to their own life experiences. Contact us Docent Program Docents facilitate teaching from the Center’s collections, special exhibitions, and architecture. They lead interactive tours for visitors of all ages and abilities as well as teach specially designed programs. Becoming a docent requires a three-year commitment: one year of training followed by two years of service. Become a Docent Access Programs The Center welcomes people of all abilities to visit and participate in educational programming and tours.

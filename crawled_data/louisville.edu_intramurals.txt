Summer memberships to the SRC will go on sale Monday, May 2 for $50. Summer validations will be good until the start of the fall semester. Your current UofL student ID will be good through Sunday, May 15. Starting May 16 all IDs must be valid to gain entry into the SRC. Summer validations may be purchased in the Campus Recreation Office with credit card, cash or check during normal business hours. They may be purchased at the Service Counter/Check In station after office hours but only by cash or check. Are you a student looking for an on-campus job? The Department Of Campus Recreation has positions available. We are looking for SRC Fitness Floor Staff, SRC Desk Staff, group fitness instructors, personal trainers and sports officials. Training is provided. If you are interested please fill out an application and bring it to the IM Office in the lobby of the SRC. Application available here. You are now able to renew your membership to the SRC online. This will save you the time and necessity of renewing in-person in our office. New members may take the same link to fill out an application. At that time someone from our office will contact you and set up an appointment to have your ID made. Go to our membership page for further information. Our group fitness class program is very unique. The classes are FREE and no registration is required. Just attend any class, at any time, that you want. There are a limited number of spin bikes available for spin classes. We recommend you arrive early for those. For a complete list of classes please take the Fitness link above. Our Instagram and YouTube accounts have lots of ‘Workouts of the Day’ and pre-recorded workouts that you can access anytime. Follow us on Instagram @ulsrc and subscribe to our Youtube Channel. These will be 1-2 minute videos that will have demonstrations of the exercises and instruction on how many sets and reps to complete for the workout as well as longer, full workout classes. You may also join our online classes on the Instagram account. Information on how to plan jogging/walking/running and biking routes here.

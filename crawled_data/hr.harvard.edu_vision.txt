TOTAL REWARDS / HEALTH & WELFARE BENEFITS / Vision Harvard offers a comprehensive vision care plan from EyeMed, a leading national vision care provider. Plan benefits Coverage at greatly reduced and/or discounted rates. Vision exams, eyewear, and contact lenses, as well as discounts on services like laser surgery, all from EyeMed. Nationwide network of providers. To find a provider or see if your preferred provider is in network, call EyeMed at 866-800-5457 or visit eyemed.com. Remember: You’ll save money by using in-network providers. If you are enrolled in EyeMed, please visit the member website for additional information on claims and more. Harvard’s medical plans also offer some vision care coverage and discounts. Other union groups have individual and family coverage levels only.

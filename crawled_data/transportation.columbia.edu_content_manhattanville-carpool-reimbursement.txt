Sign up using this form Registered groups must submit a claim here Eligibility criteria for carpool members: Manhattanville faculty and staff only, full-time, at any pay grade, including union employees, are eligible Students are not eligible at this time. Columbia University Manhattan residents are not eligible. Reimbursements for Columbia's Morningside garages are not permitted at this time. How it works: Faculty and staff need to first form their carpool group. If you need help finding a carpool group, you can find a carpool or list your ride here Carpool groups need to designate a primary contact who will receive the 25% discount parking incentive Using this form, the primary contact will need to prequalify the group, and provide the names, UNIs and home ZIP codes of the people in the carpool group Legitimate carpool groups will be pre-qualified by Environmental Stewardship Columbia will reimburse the primary designated individual per each qualified carpool group for 25% of the monthly parking expense The primary carpool group member must submit a claim and uploaded and image of their receipt to Environmental Stewardship via the claims form, within 30 days of the qualified parking expense Reimbursements will be made for Morningside and Manhattanville area garages only Faculty or staff member will be reimbursed to bank account or via check If a car/vanpool group's number of Manhattanville faculty and staff falls below three people, the primary contact must immediately notify the Environmental Stewardship office. Car/vanpool groups will have 8 weeks to find a replacement member to qualify for the incentive Periodic meetings will take place to ensure the carpool group is functioning Contact Us Have questions about our shuttles, schedules, car service, or other services?

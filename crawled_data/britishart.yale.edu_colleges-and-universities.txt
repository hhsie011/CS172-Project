Our collections can become a valuable resource for teaching. Teaching with the Collections The Center's curators and staff will collaborate with you to ensure a rewarding learning experience for your students and provide resources to prepare for your visit. While the collections reflect the development of British art and culture from the early modern period to the present, objects also speak to issues of immigration, colonization, and empire. You will learn how to engage students with works of art and ensure you meet your teaching goals.

Please use the links on this page for getting your event listed on the TC site and other communication channels. Display your event on the online calendar If you have a T4 account, you can add events to the calendar through your site following our instructions and guidelines. You may request an account that you can use to post an event to the TC event calendar. Post your event to the TC Eboard The eBoards are located throughout main level hallways and the cafeteria and they can help to advertise upcoming events. You may request posting an event on the eBoards by logging in to the eboard submissions system using your Columbia ID and password. Post to the MyTC Message Board The MyTC Message Center is a general communication hub for the Teachers College community and posting is open to all members, although a few categories are restricted to particular offices. Log into the portal for more information. Social Media TC has an active presence on many social media accounts with many different voices. Your department, program or center may already have a social media account, check here for more info.

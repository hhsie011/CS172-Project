His books include CARGO CULT: STRANGE STORIES FROM MELANESIA ANED BEYOND, ISLAND ENCOUNTERS: BLACK AND WHITE MEMORIES OF THE PACIFIC WAR, CHIEFS TODAY: TRADITIONAL PACIFIC LEADERSHIP AND THE POSTCOLONIAL STATE, and ACROSS THE WORLD WITH THE JOHNSONS: VISUAL CULTURE AND EMPIRE IN THE TWENTIETH CENTURY. He has had visiting fellowships at the East-West Center (Honolulu), the Center for Pacific Islands Studies (University of Hawaii), the MacMillan Brown Centre for Pacific Studies of Canterbury University, Christchurch, New Zealand, the Kagoshima University Research Center for the South Pacific in Kyushu, Japan, Tsing Hua University in Hsinchu, Taiwan, and Centre de Recherche et de Documentation sur l’Océanie in Marseille, France.

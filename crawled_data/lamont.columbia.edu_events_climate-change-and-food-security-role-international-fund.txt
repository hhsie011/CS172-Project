7:30 PM America/New_York International Affairs Building, 420 W. 118 St., New York, NY 10027 1501 Show Map Climate Change and Food Security: The Role of the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) With Dr. Jyotsna Puri, Associate VP Strategy and Knowledge Department at IFAD IFAD is an international financial institution and specialized UN agency based in Rome. Its mission is to transform rural economies and food systems by making them more inclusive, productive, resilient, and sustainable. Since 1978 IFAD has provided US$23.2 billion in grants and low-interest loans to projects that have reached an estimated 518 million people.

Hike the Heights is an annual community hike+celebration speonsored by the CLIMB Project to promote phhysical actvity in Northern Manhattan parks. The hike will take place on the Giraffe Path, a six mile trail that connects the Cloisters to Central Park through the cliffside parks in Northern Manhattan.

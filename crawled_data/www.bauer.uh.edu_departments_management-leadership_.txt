Leadership Overview The Management & Leadership Department in the C.T. Bauer College of Business aspires to be the top choice for students seeking undergraduate, MBA and doctoral degrees in management & leadership. Our programs cover both micro areas of management – organizational behavior, leadership, and human resources – and macro areas of management – strategy, strategic leadership, and organizational theory. The goals of our management curriculum are to develop strategic thinking, leadership skills, ethical decision-making capabilities, and critical reasoning and problem-solving skills in the context of real-world business. Our graduates are prepared to lead change and manage innovation in an environment characterized by growing technological sophistication and increasing employee diversity. Students at all levels gain relevant knowledge of management & leadership theory and practice from excellent scholars. The management & leadership department faculty members are recognized as outstanding scholars, award winning teachers, and renowned experts in the field of management & leadership. Update Please note that the Department of Management & Leadership is working remotely. Please contact the following individuals via email Monday-Friday from 8 a.m.- 5 p.m., or call 713-743-4670. We will respond to emails or phone request the same business day if your request is sent prior to 5 p.m. Calyon Lewis - Program Manager: cmlewis@bauer.uh.edu Sonnie Curry - Office Coordinator: slcurry@bauer.uh.edu Steve Werner - Department Chair: Werner@bauer.uh.edu Learn More About Bauer's Management & Leadership Degrees BBA in Management & Leadership Graduate: Bauer MBA Ph.D. in Management & Leadership Online MS in Management & Leadership Learning Abroad Executive Education Strengths A particular strength of our department is its focus on leadership at all levels (individual, group and organization). We offer a certificate in Leadership as a component of the MBA program and a leadership track for undergraduates. The department is one of the leaders of the University in online education, experiential learning and learning abroad. Our students have the opportunity to study abroad in locations such as Berlin, Beijing, Croatia, and Chile. Faculty The faculty publishes in the top journals in their fields such as The Academy of Management Journal, Journal of Applied Psychology, Strategic Management Journal, and Academy of Management Review. They serve in editorial roles and on editorial boards of the leading journals in their fields. The management & leadership faculty members have been selected as Fellows of the Academy of Management and the Society for Industrial and Organizational Psychology. Most of our faculty have won University level teaching awards.

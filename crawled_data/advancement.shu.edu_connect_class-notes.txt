Assistance Program Class Notes New jobs, weddings, children, honors and awards are things to celebrate. Let your fellow alumni know what's going on in your life. Provide as much information as possible about your happy occasion at the bottom of this form and we will print it in an upcoming issue of Seton Hall magazine within the news and notes section. If you are trying to update your contact information (address, phone, e-mail) and are not sharing an announcement, go to the Update My Information page. To share news, simply complete the form below with your new information and we'll update our records to make sure you stay up to date on what's happening at The Hall. Want to send a photo? We accept wedding photos, photos of kids in Seton Hall gear and new babies. All photos will be featured on line. If you would like your photo considered for print it should be at least 300 dpi. Also, please name your photo something meaningful, i.e. Joe Smith, son of Jenny ’04 and John Smith ’04. E-mail your photo to alumni@shu.edu.

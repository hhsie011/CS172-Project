Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Office of Communications & Marketing Menu Search TTU Office of Communications & Marketing Emergency Communications Center Coronavirus Information Coronavirus Update: March 13, 2020 Texas Tech University Community, As announced yesterday, beginning March 30, all classes are to be delivered online or via other remote modalities until further notice. This approach will continue until it is determined that resuming on-campus face-to-face instruction is safe. We recognize that this is a fluid situation, and we will continue to provide regular updates of decisions with regard to the duration of online delivery or a return to face-to face instruction. At the present time, Auxiliary Services, including the residence halls, dining facilities, recreation center, student union and student health services will remain open and operational. Therefore, it is recommended that you do not make the decision to move out of your residence hall. We ask that you continue to monitor the website and FAQ at https://www.depts.ttu.edu/communications/emergency/coronavirus/. Additional updates will continue to be provided as they become available. Thank you for your patience and cooperation.

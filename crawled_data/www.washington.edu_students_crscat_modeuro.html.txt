View course details in MyPlan: HSTEU 251 HSTEU 252 The Bloodlands: East Central Europe under Hitler and Stalin (5) I&S J. Felak From 1933-1945, 14 million people were killed, in cold blood, by Nazi Germany and Soviet Union regimes between central Poland and western Russia, including those starved in the Soviet Ukraine famine, victims of Stalin's Great Terror and Hitler's Holocaust, Soviet citizens starved by Nazis during World War II & Poles murdered under joint Nazi-Soviet occupation. Course investigates these fateful events through study of history, literature & film. Offered: jointly with JSIS A 252.

Dr. O’Toole is the author of, The Faithful: A History of Catholics in America; Passing for White: Race, Religion, and the Healy Family; Militant and Triumphant: William Henry O'Connell and the Catholic Church in Boston, 1859-1944. He is the co-editor of Boston's Histories: Essays in Honor of Thomas H. O'Connor. Dr. O’Toole is studying the history of the practice of confession in America and is currently writing a new history of Boston College.

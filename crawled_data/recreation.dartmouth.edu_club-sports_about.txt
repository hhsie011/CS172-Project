During the COVID-19 pandemic a majority of our clubs have shifted to virtual opportunities, while some clubs may have limited in-person practice opportunities for those students approved to be on campus. The Dartmouth Club Cycling Team competes in races around the country. The Fencing Club competed at the USACFC Nationals the weekend of April 8th in Lansing, Michigan. Dartmouth earned silver in the combined men’s and women’s 6-Weapon team awards, placing the whole team second in the nation. Video of Club Sports Highlights: Cheer Team Watch the Club Cheerleading Squad as they cheer on Dartmouth's athletes. Seeded second in the twenty team tournament, in May 2017 the Dartmouth Women's Ultimate team won their first ever national championship by defeating the University of Texas 15-9.

ISS also provides immigration support for employees and visiting scholars. Office Hours Monday through Friday, 9:00 am - 12:00pm and 1:00 pm - 4:00 pm. We will be closed from noon to 1:00pm. Email General questions at ISS@ku.edu Insurance questions at issinsurance@ku.edu Undergraduate admissions at world@ku.edu Immigration Services Employees and Visiting Scholars Many immigration services at KU were combined into one office, International Support Services. For services related to visa sponsorship for KU employees and KU visiting scholars please go to visa.ku.edu. If you are a department who needs support in this area and wish to talk with an advisor please call us at 785-864-3617 or email iss@ku.edu. Students Our international advisors are well-versed in the regulations governing F-1 and J-1 students. Since 95% of our students are either in F-1 or J-1 status, we only provide detailed rules and procedures for these two categories online. Rules are similar, but different for F-1 and J-1 so be certain to reference the appropriate category. Immigration Advising Hours Limited Walk-in Hours for Advising Tuesday and Thurday 1:30pm to 4:00pm Remote Virtual Hours Monday, Wednesday, Friday 1:30 p.m. to 4 p.m These virtual drop-in sessions are only for: General and specific questions for currently enrolled students and active scholars Processing of reduced course load requests (electronically signed I-20s). Inquires NOT handled during drop-in advising: Insurance-related inquiries: instead please email issinsurance@ku.edu Newly-admitted student inquires: instead please email issorientation@ku.edu All other processes require a virtual appointment. To request a virtual drop-in, click the button below and provide your full name and the purpose of your “drop-in” request. Virtual Drop-in Request Virtual Appointments Call 785-864-3617 to set up a virtual appointment For most processes needing 15 minutes or more Activities and Involvement In order to develop a holistic environment for all KU students, we believe that involvement and interactions with other members of the KU community are valuable and beneficial experiences. Therefore, we provide many opportunities to get involved on campus, in the greater community, and beyond. For more information regarding the events and opportunities provided specifically for international students, visit the ISS Get Involved webpage. Student Resources Our Student Resource Coordinator is available if you are struggling with your classes or time management. Faculty & Staff Development International Support Services can provide information sessions and trainings for interested departments, offices, or organizations on campus. For more information and/or to request a training, please visit our Faculty & Staff Development webpage.

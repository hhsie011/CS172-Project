Employer Servicesteburns2022-04-07T16:39:23-05:00 Toggle Navigation Student Services Career Services Undergraduate Career Services Graduate Career Services Employer Services Employer Services Are you on the hunt for great talent? We can help! At the Ivy College of Business, we’re preparing students to be the business leaders of tomorrow. We are here to help you connect with our students, whether it’s for an internship, co-op, or full-time employment. We have the talent you are seeking. Our world-renowned educators teach our students in a state-of-the-art facility, where they are challenged with real-world scenarios and case studies. They are immersed in a diverse environment, allowing them to leave with the experience to work in an ever-changing, fast-paced business world. Our high-achieving graduates are innovative, forward-thinking leaders who turn ideas into action and set their sights on where your company wants to grow. Employers – CyHire Sign-in Services we provide to employers: We help you develop your company recruiting strategy for hiring Iowa State University students. Reach out to Iowa State students and alumni by posting your open jobs and internships on CyHire, our online system for interfacing with Iowa State students. You are welcome to create a free account and post your open positions, whether they are internships, part-time, or full-time opportunities. On-campus and virtual interviewing opportunities – There are many opportunities for you to connect with our students. We’re here to help. Career fairs – Employers have an opportunity each semester to meet with students who are seeking internships and full-time employment. Our Annual Report provides information from recent graduates regarding employers, salaries, etc. that can be helpful as you develop your plans. We also provide data from prior annual reports. At Ivy, 96 percent of business graduates are engaged in employment, graduate school, or additional education within six months after graduation. Employer resources: Policies for employers – Including Professional Employment Practices Agreement FAQs for employers Internships and co-ops – Information about the differences, benefits, and more! Business Career Services Annual Reports – Business Career Services collects data throughout the year to understand trends in hiring and employment.

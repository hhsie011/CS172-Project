Bridging the Two Cultures: Creativity and Reasoning Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo October 26, 2020 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM David Freedberg in the “Artfull Brain II” (research workshop & lecture series) Speakers for this online event: David Freedberg (Columbia) “On the Biology of Mind. Bridging the Two Cultures: Creativity and Reasoning” Danny Reible (Texas Tech University) “Why We Need Reflective Engineers and How Art Can Contribute to Their Education” Panel Discussion: “Reflectivity and Relational Reasoning in Arts and Sciences” Chair: Elize Bisanz, Director, Institute for Studies in Pragmaticism, Texas Tech University With the participation of invited guests and Institute members Kenneth Ketner, Michael O'Boyle, Elize Bisanz. David Freedberg is Pierre Matisse Professor of the History of Art, and Director of The Italian Academy for Advanced Studies in America, at Columbia University. Organized by the Institute for Studies in Pragmaticism, Texas Tech University. See the flyer for all details.

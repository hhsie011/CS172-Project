Leach Center and FMC Ready to hit the gym on your own for a self-directed workout? From free weights to selectorized equipment, ellipticals to treadmills, training spaces, indoor track and pool… our two fitness facilities offer a wide range of options FREE for current students. Learn more about the Dr. Bobby E. Leach Student Recreation Center and the Fitness & Movement Clinic online and download our app to reserve your workout time at the Leach Center. Leach & FMC Info Download the FSU Rec App Group Fitness From yoga to cycling, total body sculpt to TRX circuit training, our Group Fitness program offers many FREE class sessions each week! Focus on cardio, strength, mind & body, or any combination in one of our nearly 2 dozen different class formats. Sign-up in advance for a class in our FSU Rec mobile app or through the Rec Connect web portal and get in a great workout with 11 to 50 people in about an hour. Group Fitness Schedule Class Descriptions Registration Info Download the FSU Rec App F45 F45 Training is a global fitness community specializing in innovative, high-intensity group workouts that keep you motivated, engaged, and always reaching for the next level. Sign-up in advance for a class in our FSU Rec mobile app or through the Rec Connect web portal and get in a great workout with up to 20 people in just 45 minutes.

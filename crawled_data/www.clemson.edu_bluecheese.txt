Produced right here on Clemson’s campus, this artisanal cheese has been making its way into the hearts of cheese connoisseurs since 1941. We are pleased to announce that along with our FEDEX Standard Overnight delivery option at check out, we are able to offer South Carolina shipping destinations FEDEX HomeDelivery. In planning a gift of Clemson Blue Cheese, ensure you’ve completed your messaging during your ordering. We will pass along your special message to the recipient. If you have any concerns and/or questions about ordering and would like to speak with a Clemson Blue Cheese representative, please call us at 1-800-599-0181 and we will be happy to assist you. If you have any questions or concerns please contact Clemson Blue Cheese.

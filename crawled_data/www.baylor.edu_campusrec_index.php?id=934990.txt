Baylor>Campus Recreation>About>Our Team>Kim Balmer Kim Balmer Licensed Massage Therapist Email Kim_Balmer@baylor.edu Phone (254) 710-7542 High Res Photo CV Kim is a Lubbock native, where she attended Texas Tech University and received a Bachelor's degree in Physical Education. Growing up, Kim was active in her church and Explorer Scouts (a division of the Boy Scouts of America) where she discovered her love of high adventure outdoor sports. Kim became a registered Massage Therapist in 1995. Her massage practice focuses on medicinal and fitness areas, enabling Kim to work with patients of all ages, including those dealing with chronic pain, Parkinson, professional athletes, and stress-related conditions.

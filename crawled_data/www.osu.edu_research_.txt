Buckeyes give Make a gift Skip to main content Page content Six Reasons to Study at Ohio State's Byrd Polar Research Center Research Strengths The breadth, scope and excellence of our research and creative expression community make The Ohio State University a leading force of knowledge creation, innovation and change – locally, nationally and globally. Ohio State is one of only a few universities in the United States that, in a single location, house 15 different colleges, including seven health sciences colleges and a college of agriculture, along with a world-class medical center. At Ohio State we have the tools necessary—the creative minds, expertise, experience and world-class facilities—to discover innovative solutions to society’s most complex, pressing challenges. Learn more about some of the areas where Ohio State researchers are converging across diverse disciplines to define and address these challenges and champion human potential.

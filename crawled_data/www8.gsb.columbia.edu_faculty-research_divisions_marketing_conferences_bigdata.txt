Conferences Don Lehmann’s 50th Anniversary Marketing Analytics and Big Data Conference Frontiers of Applied Statistics in Marketing On Sep 15-16, 2017, the Marketing Division of Columbia Business School and the Data Science Institute at Columbia University hosted the Marketing Analytics and Big Data Conference. This conference is part of a Chicago-Columbia-Stanford rotating Big Data conference. The event is geared toward academics using state-of-the-art quantitative methods to study marketing problems. Conference Program: FRIDAY, September 15 William and June Warren Hall, Room 310 1125 Amsterdam Ave 13:00 – 16:30: Tutorial "Variational Inference: Foundations and Innovations" David Blei, Professor of Computer Science and Statistics, Columbia University A video of Prof Blei's tutorial can be found here and the slides can be viewed here.

Past Event Uncover the Life of Dean Lung Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo June 13, 2019 2:30 PM - 5:30 PM Gong Xiang Ji, 1 Changxiang 3rd Alley, Dongcheng Qu, Beijing On June 13, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to a panel discussion on the documentary Searching for Dean Lung and a public lecture entitled "Uncover the Life of Dean Lung". This event is also part of the 2019 Columbia Beijing Summer Arts and Humanities Series. Open the scroll of history, you will find many names connecting China and Columbia: HU Shih, FENG Youlan, XU Dishan, JIANG Menglin, XU Zhimuo, TAO Xingzhi, WU Jianxiong, and etc. But the very person Dean Lung who, allegedly, laid the first brick of this long-lasting bridge between China and Columbia, is nowhere to be found. Who is this "Dean Lung" that contributed to the fund for Chinese studies at Columbia? How is he connected to the Dean Lung Professorship at the University? This event will help you to find out. Panelists Mia Anderer, Scholar and Musician Paul Anderer, Mack Professor of Humanities and Professor of Japanese Literature, Columbia University Helena Xiao, Assistant Director, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Jianxin CHI, General Manager, Beijing Discovery Documentary Media ltd. Yalin BU, Chief Director of Searching for Dean Lung Baocheng JIA, Video Business Director, Weibo Yaliang LIU, Toutiao Project Manager Agenda 2:00 p.m. - 2:30 p.m. Registration 2:30 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. "Uncover the Life of Dean Lung" by Mia Anderer 4:00 p.m. - 5:10 p.m. Panel Discussion + Q&A 5:10 p.m. - 5:30 p.m. Closing Remarks Notice The event will be in Chinese and English. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code confirmation. Please present your QR code at the entrance. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

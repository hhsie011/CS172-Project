Institutions + Resources Close The opportunities and resources on campus are unsurpassed. Exploration is an integral part of any performer’s education and practice. At the Yale School of Music, you’re given access to a vast array of musical, technological, and scholarly resources to help you think about, create, and perform music. There are so many places within the School of Music that I love. Ashley Hale, trumpet, ’18MM Partner Institutions + Resources Experiment with the latest recording and editing techniques or view the original manuscripts of Charles Ives or Kurt Weill at the Beinecke Rare Book & Manuscript Library.

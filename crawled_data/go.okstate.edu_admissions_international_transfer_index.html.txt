The first step is completing the online application — which covers you for admission to the university and all university-sponsored scholarships. Who counts as a transfer student? You should apply as a transfer student if you have attempted a minimum of 7 semester hours of college-level credit after graduation from high school. If you have attempted less than 7 hours, see the admission requirements for transfer students. What's required?You'll need these four things to be considered for admission: Completed admission application $90 nonrefundable application fee Copy of the identification page in your passport English proficiency test scores- iBT TOEFL 61+ (DI Code:6546) or IELTS 5.5+ if you do not have 24 or more transferable credit hours from a U.S. or English-speaking post-secondary institution Official transcripts from each institution you've attended. Please note, transcripts must be submitted in the original language and translated to English.

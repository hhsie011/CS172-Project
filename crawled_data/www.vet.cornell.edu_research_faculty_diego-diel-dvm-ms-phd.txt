Email Us People Giving Emergencies In this section: Faculty Raina Plowright CVM Research Diego Diel, DVM, MS, PhD Department of Population Medicine and Diagnostic Sciences Associate Professor Diel Research Lab Department of Population Medicine and Diagnostic Sciences Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine Ithaca, NY 14853 Office: 607.253.4359 Email: dgdiel@cornell.edu Research Interest My research interests are to understand the molecular mechanisms underlying virus-host interactions, focusing on the mechanisms underlying viral immune evasion that contribute to virus virulence and disease pathogenesis. My goal in studying virus-host interactions is to translate the basic knowledge generated with this research into improved vaccines and/or vaccine vectors for disease prevention and control. Other applied virology research interests include: (i) diagnostic assay development and (ii) livestock feed biosecurity.

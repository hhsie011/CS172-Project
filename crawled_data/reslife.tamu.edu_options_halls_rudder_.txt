Constructed in 1989, Rudder Hall is named after Margaret Rudder, who was the widow of former Texas A&M President, Major General James Earl Rudder. As the First Lady of Texas A&M, she was active in both university and community affairs, continuing her involvement after her husband’s death and up until her own in 2004. 2021 – 2022 Rates Modular Hall $3,708semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Female Description The building houses female residents only.

Our Mission The mission of the Office of Community Engagement is to build on Syracuse University's rich history of engaging with the community by supporting relationships that enhance the academic enterprise and the experiences of students, faculty and staff while also strengthening the community. Our Vision Syracuse University's Office of Community Engagement builds and fosters lasting and mutually supportive connections between the University and the community to enhance the success of the University and the vibrancy of the community.

The series was filmed in summer 2021 by filmmaker and longtime Harvard Forest collaborator, Roberto Mighty. Watch Tracking the Carbon Power of the Woods, featuring Audrey Barker Plotkin and Bill Munger. Watch Climate Change Goes Underground, featuring Serita Frey, Kristen DeAngelis, and Mallory Choudoir. Watch Investigating Land Use, from Leaves to Landscapes, featuring Lucy Hutyra and Julia Marrs. Watch Tracking Forest Pests, from Cells to Satellites, featuring David Orwig, Valerie Pasquarella, and Neil Pederson. Watch Pests, Climate, and People, featuring Jonathan Thompson. Explore the full set of videos on the Harvard Forest YouTube channel.

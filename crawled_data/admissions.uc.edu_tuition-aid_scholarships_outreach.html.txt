Award recognizes the academic merit of incoming, first-year University of Cincinnati students from select, targeted geographic areas. Requirements and Eligibility Be a resident of or graduate from a high school in California, Connecticut, the District of Columbia, Georgia, Illinois, Maryland, Massachusetts, Michigan, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania, Texas, Virginia, or Wisconsin Complete the Common App and submit all required application documents by December 1 during your senior year of high school Qualifying incoming freshmen will be reviewed based on the strength of their admission application including personal statement and high school record Weighted GPA is determined and used for award eligibility if the high school weights any GPAs Award selection and amount determination are made to the highest achieving students in each academic program All UC National Outreach Awards are renewed for the length of the undergraduate academic program providing the student maintains a 3.2 GPA and completes the annual 30-hour community service requirement Additional Information Awardees will also be considered for other University of Cincinnati first-year scholarships For more information about eligibility and renewal criteria, please visit the Student Financial Aid Office Office of Admissions University of Cincinnati PO Box 210091 Cincinnati, OH 45221 UC Tools Canopy & Canvas One Stop Email Catalyst Shuttle Tracker IT Help UC VPN Bearcats Landing About Us Maps & Directions Jobs News Diversity Governance & Policies Directory Events Calendar University of Cincinnati | 2600 Clifton Ave. | Cincinnati, OH 45221 | ph: 513-556-6000 Alerts | Clery and HEOA Notice | Notice of Non-Discrimination | eAccessibility Concern | Privacy Statement | Free Speech | Copyright Information © 2020 University of Cincinnati Office of Admissions University of Cincinnati PO Box 210091 Cincinnati, OH 45221 © 2020 University of Cincinnati scroll up to the top

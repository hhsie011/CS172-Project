It's a podcast that features the untold stories of the school we love so dearly. Fearless is produced by the Texas Tech Office of Communications & Marketing. It's hosted by Taylor Peters, with special help from Allison Hirth. Season One In season one, we'll focus on Texas Tech's comeback from COVID-19. You'll hear from those at the center of our campus community – the students, the decision-makers, the educators and more. Join us each week as we uncover how Red Raiders turned the trials of a global pandemic into triumph. Behind the Mic At her core, Taylor Peters is a storyteller. She is passionate about connecting people through shared experiences and believes there's no greater feeling than empowering others to find value in their truth. Want to talk to Taylor? Email her at taylor.peters@ttu.edu. Fearless is available anywhere you listen to podcasts.

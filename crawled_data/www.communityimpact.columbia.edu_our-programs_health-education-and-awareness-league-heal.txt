Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About HEAL Health Education and Awareness League (HEAL) volunteers promote a healthy lifestyle to middle-school children attending public school in Harlem. Volunteers may lead activities such as yoga, basketball, bodyweight exercises, running, etc. while highlighting the importance of activity on the mind. Volunteers also meet bi-weekly to discuss the best ways to keep the students engaged and to understand the importance of physical activity on the body and mind.

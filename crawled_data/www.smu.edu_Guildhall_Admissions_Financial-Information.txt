Now SMU Guildhall Financial Information SMU Guildhall Financial Aid and Student Services offers a complete array of financial support services to assist students during their time at SMU Guildhall. We’re here to answer your financial questions and make the process as seamless as possible. SMU Guildhall Tuition Financial Aid Bulletin A detailed breakdown of the current tuition and fees for SMU Guildhall can be found on the bursar's website. Please send any questions regarding tuition to our admission office. Financing Your Graduate Education at SMU Guildhall SMU Guildhall is dedicated to making a high-quality education affordable, regardless of a student's family circumstances or income level. Most students who attend SMU receive some form of financial assistance through government loans, private loans, or scholarships. SMU Guildhall Financial Aid advisors are here to help you through the process. Contact us with any questions that you may have. Useful Financial Aid Websites: https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa — Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) http://www.studentaid.gov/ — Submit your GradPLUS application for credit approval, complete your loan entrance counseling for Federal Direct Stafford and GradPLUS loans, and sign your Federal Direct Stafford and GradPLUS Master Promissory note. http://www.finaid.org/ — Information on all types of financial aid including loan repayment https://nsldsfap.ed.gov/nslds_FAP/ — Detailed information on all of your Federal student loan history For International Students — For students requiring a U.S. visa, it is mandatory that the following forms be completed and returned with your SMU Guildhall application: Financial Certification for Study at SMU (Financial statement must be notarized and converted in US Dollars) Health Care — All international students who are enrolled in one or more credit hours are required to maintain the Student Health Insurance Plan as a condition of enrollment. International students must enroll in the Student Health Insurance Plan unless they have a special waiver personally granted by the Student Health Center staff.

One of the major goals of the Classics DUG is to facilitate interactions between students and professors, and to help students learn more about the department. The Classics DUG will hold a variety of activities, ranging from department mixers to informational panels. Our ideas include: Department reception for faculty and interested students Lectures Trips to the MFA to look at Classical Art Latin tutoring Informational panels about studying abroad and applying to graduate school Thesis workshops and readings The Classics DUG welcomes your feedback and involvement!

Parent Resource Documents Camp Parent Handbook Morning Drop-Off Parking Map Session Calendar of Events (coming soon) Camp Hours Camp sessions are scheduled Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Supervised camper drop-off begins at 8:45 a.m. and goes until camp begins at 9:00 a.m. Pick-up is scheduled between 4:00 and 4:15 p.m. Requests for early dismissals must be submitted to the Administrative Office by noon on the day of the request. Early dismissals prior to 3:00 p.m. will not be accepted. About the Art Show Campers display their art creations at the art show the second Thursday of every camp session. Parents and guest are encouraged to visit the Herbert Wellness Center Atrium to see their childâ€™s art work. The art show runs from Thursday morning to Friday morning. Campers collect their art projects during art class on the last day of the camp session to take home. About Extravaganza Extravaganza begins promptly at 3:30 p.m. on the last Friday of the session in Centre Court. Make sure to arrive early as seats fill up quickly! Parking without a parking permit or failure to pay via PaytoPark app will result in being ticketed. For more information please visit the parking and transportation website. Parents and guest are welcome to take pictures and film of the extravaganza show. Dismissal will commence as usual at the end of the show. Extravaganza Videos Missed an extravaganza show or want to see it again? Check out the Mini Canes YouTube Channel to see our collection of Extravaganza videos. Photos and Videos To see more photos, please visit our Flickr gallery. To view Extravaganza videos, visit the Mini Canes Playlist on the Herbert Wellness Center's YouTube page.

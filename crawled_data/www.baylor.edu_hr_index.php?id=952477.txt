When you’re ready to begin planning the next phase of your life, we’re here to help guide you. Advise you. And be with you every step of the way. Baylor Retirement Plan - 403(b) Baylor 457(b) Plan Retiree Benefits Overview Explore Investing in Your Retirement. Baylor contributes a generous discretionary amount towards retirement, but we encourage everyone to take ownership in their retirement by making your own contribution, meeting with CAPTRUST, and checking your account often by logging into TIAA. Get started today. I'm Ready to Invest/Enroll Preparing for Your Retirement. Once you've made the decision to retire, you'll have some decisions to make, and no doubt, some questions about things like Social Security, Medicare, Insurance Plans, and the benefits you’ll take with you. We can help. I'm Ready to Retire Enjoying Your Retirement. Your relationship with Baylor doesn't end with retirement — it begins an exciting new chapter — with benefits, perks, special privileges and programs to keep you connected and involved with the University. See what's ahead.

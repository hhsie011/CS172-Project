Children's Literacy Lab Teacher Resources Our Faculty and Staff UC BIPOC Teacher Pathway Teacher Awards 2022 Excellence in Education Awards For the Greater Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky Region The Cincinnatus Association and the Schools of Education at Mount St. Joseph University, Northern Kentucky University, the University of Cincinnati, Thomas More University, and Xavier University continue their long tradition of honoring outstanding Cincinnati area educators, administrators, and volunteers. Annually, seven outstanding educator awards are determined through an open nomination process. We are pleased to request nominations for the 2022 Awards. All awardees, and those who have nominated them, will be honored at a banquet on Tuesday evening, February 8, 2022 hosted by University of Cincinnati at the Cincinnati Woman's Club. In addition to recognition at this February event, each awardee will receive a monetary award. The deadline for all submissions is November 12, 2021. Outstanding Educator Award: This nomination form is for nominating an outstanding teacher in the Greater Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky Region that is Public, Private, or Parochial.

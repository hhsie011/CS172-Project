Activity The Consortium reports to the vice president for diversity and inclusion who may appoint a convener to facilitate meetings. The Consortium meets three times each semester to share information and plan joint initiatives. The Consortium maintains an updated website and sponsors programs and events. In order to review accomplishments and plan for the following academic year, the Consortium holds a retreat every summer.

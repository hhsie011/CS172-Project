Participants will join us from around the world and from across various disciplines. Our second event will feature David Landau, Chairman of Saffron Hill Ventures Ltd and Trustee of The National Gallery Trust, in conversation with Columbia University Trustee Jonathan Rosand, MD, ’88CC, ’94PS. The conversation will focus on David Landau’s Open Letter to Candidates of the last mayoral election in Venice (September 21, 2020) and his suggested 5-Point Plan for the Future of Venice. In advance of our conversation, we invite you to read the Open Letter and Comment in The Art Newspaper. Organized by Casa Muraro, Columbia’s Center for Venetian Studies, and co-sponsored by the Department of Art History and Archaeology and the Italian Academy for Advanced Studies in America.

Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Dialing Procedures On This Page Campus Phone to Campus Phone Off-Campus Calling International Call Forwarding Campus Phone to Campus Phone To dial between campus extensions (Seattle, Bothell, or Tacoma Campuses): Dial [5-digit number] (last five (5) digits of telephone number). UW Medical Center (UWMC) is served by a different phone system. The last 5-digits of their 598 prefix numbers [8-####], and their 7 digit, 744 prefix numbers [744-####] are provisioned to dial as campus numbers. Do not dial a [9] before these numbers. These area codes and prefixes can be dialed between UW campuses and UW Medicine: Area Code Prefix 206 221 425 352 206 520 206 543 206 598 206 616 206 685 253 692 206 744 206 897 Off-Campus Calling (non-UW/UWMC number) To dial a local call from an unrestricted campus telephone: Press [9]. Dial the 10-digit phone number. Ten digit dialing is now required for all off-campus local calls. To dial a long-distance call from an unrestricted campus telephone: Press [9] then [1] for long-distance access. Dial the 10-digit phone number. International number using UWATS: Dial [9] Listen for dial tone. Dial [011]. Dial [country code]. Dial [city code] (omit 0 if first digit). Dial [local number]. Dial [#]. (NOTE: Do not dial # if international number – excluding 011 – is 12 digits). Dial [authorization code]. Call Forwarding Forwards incoming calls to another telephone number. To forward calls to another UW campus or UWMC number: Dial [*][7][2]. Wait for dial tone. Dial 5-digit number. Wait for three short tones, followed by dial tone. Hang up. To forward calls to a non-UW campus telephone number: Dial [*][7][2]. Wait for dial tone. Dial [9]. Dial the number to which you are forwarding. Enter the 10-digit number you wish to forward to. Wait for three short tones, followed by dial tone. Hang up. To cancel Call Forwarding: Dial [*][7][3]. Wait for three short tones, followed by dial tone. Hang up.

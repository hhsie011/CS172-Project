Health Promotion Counseling and Psychological Services Disability Services Gay Health Advocacy Project (GHAP) Insurance Office Medical Services Sexual Violence Response Columbia Health You are here: Home Decoding Manhood and Masculinity Events Past Event Decoding Manhood and Masculinity Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo May 21, 2020 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM America/New_York Online Please join Columbia Health Sexual Violence Response (SVR) for an interactive workshop, Decoding Manhood and Masculinity on May 21st, from 3:30-5pm (ET). In this workshop, we will screen segments from actor, Justin Baldoni's Web Series, Man Enough, followed by a facilitated discussion where participants will explore themes including societies expectations of men as well as how these expectations affect interpersonal relationships. We will explore differences in the definitions of masculinity as it spans through various generations, cultures, and professions. This will be a closed space for male identified students. This session will be facilitated by Columbia Health Sexual Violence Response (SVR) staff member Jonathan Johnson, LMHC, Prevention Coordinator.

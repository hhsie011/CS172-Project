Orientation Information for New Students Campus Visit Announcement As circumstances surrounding COVID-19 evolve, university officials are monitoring the spread of the disease and taking steps to keep our community healthy. Programs sponsoring graduate student visits must follow the institution's domestic and international travel policy for university sponsored travel, and sponsored visits are not allowed. Programs may host virtual events so that students can receive pertinent information in a timely manner. Prospective students with plans to visit campus should contact their program of interest with questions about virtual events. Contact information for each program is available here. Please continue to visit coronavirus.ku.edu for additional details about campus plans. This page is designed to assist you as you transition to graduate study at KU and life in Lawrence. We encourage you to use the information found to the left as you settle into your new role as a graduate student at KU.

The Clemson Alumni Association would like to congratulate you on your acceptance to Clemson University. We cannot wait to see you in Clemson this Fall. To help you showcase your pride for being an official Clemson Tiger, we want to offer you the opportunity to tell the world you are a Clemson Tiger with your very own Tigertown Bound Yard Sign. The Clemson Alumni Association has partnered with several pick-up locations across South Carolina and Charlotte where you can get your sign. For those not located in one of the in-person pickup locations, scroll down to learn how you can have a sign shipped to your home. Main St. Rock Hill, SC 29730 March 9 – 3:00 – 6:00 pm Order a Sign to be Delivered to Your Home If you are not located in South Carolina or near one of the pick-up areas, you can order your Tigertown Bound sign online and have it shipped directly to your home. Signs ordered online are $20.12 plus shipping. To order your sign: Go to tigertownbound.privateprintstore.com/login and sign up for an account. Once on the site, select the sign you want to purchase under the class year Tigertown Bound sign or the generic Tigertown Bound sign. Once you review the preview of the sign, click “Continue.” Click “Checkout,” and enter your information to complete your order.

OSU has more than 35 graduate certificates in a wide range of disciplinary areas, from big data analytics and human resources to teaching English as a second language. Most can be earned in 12 to 18 credit hours. Often these hours can also be counted toward a master’s or doctoral degree. Even if that’s not your goal — a graduate certificate from a Tier 1 research university can help you elevate your knowledge and skills and get noticed in the competitive job market. You’ll see the difference; they will too. We’re here to help! Take the next step and apply for graduate school admission. The Graduate College has compiled a helpful list of admission requirements and frequently asked questions.

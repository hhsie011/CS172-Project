These initiatives help students stay on the pathway to graduate within four-years. Below is a summary of the various initiatives: Under the 3+3 program, all Freshmen and certain eligible Sophomores will receive a scholarship to cover the tuition cost of the second 3 credit hour course. The direct financial support will be calculated based on your residency status for assessed tuition and applied to your account after registration for summer courses. Sophomores not eligible for the 3+3 program will be provided a $500 scholarship to assist with the cost when enrolled for two classes (six credits or more) in the summer 2022 semester. Juniors and Seniors will be provided a $500 scholarship to assist with the cost when enrolled for two classes (six credits or more) in the summer 2022 semester. PLEASE NOTE: TN eCampus (formerly RODP) courses are excluded from the free offer.

Stillwater Medical Center maintains data on their current capacity level and COVID-19 patient count. VIEW STILLWATER NUMBERS IN STATE COVID-19 DASHBOARD Payne County cases Payne County data is maintained in the State of Oklahoma COVID-19 dashboard and includes testing data for the City of Stillwater, tests conducted at UHS and all other facility testing in the county. State testing stats do not include rapid positive test cases. VIEW PAYNE COUNTY NUMBERS IN STATE COVID-19 DASHBOARD OSU-Tulsa cases OSU-Tulsa positive cases reflect results from cases reported to the OSU-Tulsa/OSU-CHS Student/Occupational Health Nurse and verified through testing at OSU Center for Health Sciences in Tulsa. All confirmed results are reported to the state health department and are counted as part of state and local COVID statistics.

With guidance and support, each week we’ll learn a distinct and accessible meditative practice and spiritual outlook to deepen our relationship with each other and ourselves. Any/all spiritualities, faith backgrounds and levels of spiritual interest are welcome. Whether you’ve been practicing meditation for years or looking to try it out for the first time, join us. Once you've submited your interest, we'll then schedule you for a 1-1 conversation to talk details of what you can expect. Questions?

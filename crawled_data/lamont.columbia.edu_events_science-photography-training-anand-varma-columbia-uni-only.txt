3:00 PM America/New_York Online We invite you to join us on Tuesday, October 5, for a Science Photography training session to help research scientists improve photography skills, storytelling abilities, and gain an understanding of basic photographic workflow and file handling while in the field. The session will be led by Anand Varma, an internationally published professional photographer specializing in environmental images. When: Tuesday, October 5th, 1:30 - 3:00 p.m. Where: Via Zoom Who: This session is open to the Columbia community, and those involved in fieldwork are especially encouraged to attend. RSVP: Registration is required. You will receive a confirmation email with instructions for joining. Click "register" below. Topics to be covered include: planning/pre-visualizing framing perspective light macro/details portrait landscapes action shots iteration/experimentation The training will be 60 minutes, followed by a 30-minute question and answer session.

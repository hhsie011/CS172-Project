Miller Fund Frost Student Scholarships Award Winning Faculty Frost Community Outreach Creative Endeavors Capital Enhancement Sue Miller Fund The Sue Miller Violin, valued at $1.1 million The Miller family has initiated a Frost School of Music Instrument Collection with a generous donation of a rare, early 18th-century Guarneri violin valued at $1.1 million. The violin will be named the Sue Miller Violin. The Frost School is one of the greatest music schools in the world. These fine and rare instruments will not only help attract brilliant musicians to the school but also help them have performance experiences at the highest level. You can help by making a gift to purchase any of the instruments below, or a contribution in any amount towards our total goal of nearly $2.7 million.

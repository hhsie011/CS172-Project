Box 750215 Dallas, TX 75275 Armstrong Area Service Desk The Armstrong Area Desk is located in Armstrong Commons at 5809 Bush Avenue. The desk is on the northeast side of the buildings, facing Arnold Dining Commons. The Armstrong Desk provides card access services to residents of Armstrong Commons, Loyd Commons, Kathy Crow Commons, Crum Commons, and Ware Commons. Desk Hours: Every day, 8:00am - 8:00pm Phone: 214.768.5062 214.768.5062 On-Call Staff In-community staff members serve on-call from 8:00 p.m. to 8:00 a.m. To contact the on-call staff member, residents will find the on-call number posted in and around their residential community. Additionally, urgent and emergency response personnel can be reached via the SMU Police Department lines at 214.768.3388 (non-emergency) and 214.768.3333 (emergency). Virginia-Snider Area Service Desk The Virginia-Snider (VS) Area Desk is located in Virginia-Snider Commons at the top of Bishop Boulevard and at the corner of Asbury Street and Hillcrest Avenue. The VS Desk provides spare key services to residents of Mary Hay/Peyton/Shuttles Commons, Morrison-McGinnis Commons, McElvaney Commons, Cockrell-McIntosh Commons, Boaz Commons, Upper Division Housing, and Viriginia-Snider Commons.

Meet our current Think Tank Members: Linda Hardin Lino Lakes, MN Paul Harris San Antonio, TX Jennie Mayes San Antonio, TX Michelle Mayo Irving, TX Tonia Mervis Melissa, TX Heather Nieporte Dallas, TX Janie Reyes Dallas, TX Former Think Tank members - Thank you for your service to Texas Tech Famlies! Sonya Battle, DeSoto, Texas Susan Beard, Meridian, Idaho Jennifer Colehower, Buda, Texas Terri Johnson, Loveland, Colorado Michael Lovejoy, Berleson, Texas Brad Mayer, The Hills, Texas Jose Smith, Denton, Texas Karen Sneed, Corpus Christi, Texas Valerie Tessitore, Rockwall, Texas Benefits, Requirements, Responsibilities, and Application Process: Benefits: Support your student's university by helping shape parent/family-specific communications and programming Make connections with Texas Tech University staff and other Red Raider families Recognition on the Parent & Family Relations website Requirements for Eligibility: Must be the parent or family member of an undergraduate student currently enrolled at Texas Tech University Must be willing to meet the responsibilities outlined below Must be available for scheduled Zoom meetings. Responsibilities: PFR Think Tank members will be asked to review and provide feedback on various electronic and printed communication pieces as well as programs offered by the department. Publications for review will be shared via Dropbox, and feedback will be submitted electronically through a survey form provided by PFR. Members may also be asked to provide contributions to department communications, such as tips for new Red Raider families. Attend Zoom Meetings once per semester (Fall, Spring, once in the Summer) – PFR Think Tank meetings will occur three times a year via Zoom. Meetings will last approximately one hour and are normally at Noon Central. Additional communications will occur via email. Term of service will be a maximum of two years, as long as your student remains enrolled at Texas Tech. For example, if your student is nearing their senior year and you cannot serve for two years, we would still appreciate your membership in the Think Tank. Application Process: PFR Think Tank applications are closed at this time.

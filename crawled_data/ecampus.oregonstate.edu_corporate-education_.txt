Through strategic collaboration we can cultivate accessible, life-changing opportunities for individuals throughout the world. Our Corporate Education Unit collaborations are intended to improve employee job satisfaction and performance alongside a currency of skills to engage in professional opportunities throughout their careers. Benefits of corporate collaborations Employees see upward career mobility and financial support, and corporate collaborators see increased employee retention and organic marketing opportunities through Ecampus.

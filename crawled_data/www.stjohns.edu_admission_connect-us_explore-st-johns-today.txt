Visit Policy Please note: NYC has discontinued the Key to NYC vaccination requirement as of March 7. As a result, St. John’s University will no longer require proof of a COVID-19 vaccination for our visitors. Further, masks are no longer required to be worn in any indoor facilities or at any on-campus events or tours, and are to be worn at the discretion of each visitor. We are happy to welcome you to campus. Accepted Students Deposit Apply ON-CAMPUS VISIT Click to Open Student Ambassadors will provide guided tours of our indoor and outdoor facilities. QUEENS CAMPUS | View Calendar for Availability and Registration When you arrive to the Queens campus, please enter through Gate 1 on Utopia Parkway and check in with Public Safety to obtain a parking pass. All visitors will be directed to visitor parking and the Welcome Center, where your tour will begin! (Drop the campus map here) STATEN ISLAND CAMPUS | View Calendar for Availability and Registration When you arrive to the Staten Island campus, please check in with Public Safety so the officer can direct you to parking. Your Student Ambassador will meet you there and begin your campus tour experience. If you are no longer able to join us for your visit as scheduled, please call 718-990-3081 to let us know. SELF-GUIDED TOUR Click to Open We are pleased to now offer self-guided tours for guests who have the sense of adventure! The starting point is the Welcome Center on the Queens campus, where guests scan the available QR code to get started. Guests will follow the standard in-person tour stops, which you can see on this map. Additionally, guests will access the corresponding video that goes with each stop! The self-guided tour video chapters are easily accessible on our YouTube channel. VIDEO TOUR Click to Open Watch the Fall 2020 Virtual Queens Campus Tour video!

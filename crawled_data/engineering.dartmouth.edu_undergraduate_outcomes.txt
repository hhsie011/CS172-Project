Dartmouth Life After Dartmouth Q: What can you do with a Dartmouth engineering degree? A: Anything you want. With a foundation of communications, teamwork, and problem-solving skills, Dartmouth engineers are prepared for a wide array of industry careers and leadership positions. In addition, the BE opens the same doors for graduate study as a BS.

Read more What you need to know The COVID-19 vaccine/booster mandate continues for the 2022-23 academic year. Masks are no longer required on campus, including in classrooms and research spaces. A second booster dose also is available for those age 50 and older. Though recommended by the CDC, it is not required to fulfill MSU's vaccine/booster mandate. COVID-19 testing While the Early Detection Program and PCR testing at the Clinical Center have both ended, off-campus testing is widely available in the East Lansing area and throughout the state and country. At-home tests also are available to order for free via https://www.covidtests.gov/.

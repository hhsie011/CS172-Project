Women of UC Women of UC right arrow down arrow Women of UC Women of UC When alumnae thrive, UC prospers. Women of UC is a network of Bearcats united to advance the power of our female graduates. Together, we inspire each other to advance our personal and professional lives. We plan experiences ranging from cocktails and conversation to taking deep dives on professional issues - A mix of work and play - All with the aim of bringing women together.

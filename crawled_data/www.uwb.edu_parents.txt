The path to graduation can be just as challenging for a family as it is for their student. That’s why we’re dedicated to connecting you with the right resources to support your student’s success and ensure that the whole family can participate in a truly remarkable Husky Experience. About UW Bothell At the University of Washington Bothell, we pride ourselves on providing students with close faculty interaction and a leading-edge student experience. We strive to embody our mission statement in everything we do. Please learn more about our campus, our mission and the distinctive learning practices that make us unique.

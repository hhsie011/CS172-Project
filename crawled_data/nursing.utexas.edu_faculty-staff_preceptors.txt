Download Prospective Preceptor Information Form (PDF) Tuition Exemption Form for Clinical Nursing Preceptors and their Dependents Preceptors and/or their children must be Texas residents and must be enrolled at The University of Texas at Austin to claim exemption up to $500. Preceptor must hold RN licensure and be under a written preceptor agreement during the semester for which an exemption is sought. Dependent children must be enrolled in an undergraduate degree program. Student is no longer eligible if he/she has received this exemption for 10 semesters or summer sessions at any institution(s) or has received a baccalaureate degree.

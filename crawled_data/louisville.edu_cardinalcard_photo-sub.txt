This card confirms that you are a University of Louisville student and indicates you are entitled to the rights, privileges, and responsibilities of a student. The Cardinal Card functions as your ID card, meal card, and library card, among many other uses. The online photo submission process is only open for students or employees receiving their very first card. Our Cardinal Cards do not expire, if you have previously received a Cardinal Card from the University it is still valid. To receive a replacement Cardinal Card there is a $15 fee. Your photo must conform to the following specifications: The photo must be a recent, forward-facing, passport-style photo in color. Face must be clearly visible and facing forward - no profile shots. Background must be solid white or off-white background with no other objects showing. No textures such as doors or bricks are allowed. Please no edited backgrounds No sunglasses, hats, baseball-style caps, or novelty headgear in your photo. Headpieces worn for religious purposes are acceptable. Blurry or out-of-focus photos are not acceptable. The cardholder must be the only person in the picture. No copyrighted photos. No glamour shots, senior photos, or professional photos. Instructions: Create an acceptable digital photo using the specifications above. Photos are subject to approval by the Cardinal Card Office STUDENTS/EMPLOYEES: Upload both your photo and photo identification. Select your appropriate status type: Faculty/Staff - Faculty or Staff that are employed by the University. Housestaff - Residents or Fellows that are employed by the University. Student - Students that are currently enrolled in classes at the University. Online Academic Program - Students that are enrolled in an online academic program at the University. Fall 2022 Freshman Orientation students - You MUST register for orientation first, then select your orientation session in the drop down menu. 4. Pick up your Cardinal Card at the Cardinal Card Office, Houchens LL05, and turn in your agreement form. FAQs: When can you receive your Cardinal Card? Students Fall Semester: We start issuing student cards on the first business day in August for students enrolled in classes for Fall. Spring Semester: We start issuing student cards on the first business day after Fall commencement for students who are enrolled in classes for Spring. Summer Semester: We start issuing student cards on the first business day in May for students who are enrolled in classes for Summer. Employees Must be active on payroll at least one day prior to getting a Cardinal Card. My photo is sideways. Is that okay? Yes, photos can be uploaded sideways. Our office will rotate your photo before adding it to our card database. I am unable to upload my Cardinal Card photo and driver's license. What should I do? If you are having trouble uploading your cardinal card photo and/or driver's license. Try reducing the file size, if you are still having issues, you can visit our office in the Houchens Building LL05 to receive your Cardinal Card.

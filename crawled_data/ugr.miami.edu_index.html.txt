Welcome The Office of Undergraduate Research and Community Outreach supports the larger University of Miami mission to “transform lives through education, research, innovation, and service.” Research- Getting Started 2022 RCIF Resources Contact UGR Mission To promote, facilitate, and support undergraduate research in all disciplines for students and faculty across the University of Miami campuses and the broader community. Vision A university where all undergraduate students can enhance their overall education and future endeavors through impactful research experiences. These experiences provide students the freedom to transcend disciplinary boundaries, discover new knowledge, and hone their professional skills.

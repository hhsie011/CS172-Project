Breadcrumb Home About the Medical School Sub Navigation In This Section Curriculum Degree Programs Student Stories After Alpert Frequently Asked Questions Virtual Tour After Alpert The Warren Alpert Medical School graduates use their degrees to pursue clinical practice, research, and more. For the majority of our medical students, the culmination of their four years of medical school is residency placement in some of the most competitive programs in the country—in every medical specialty, from anesthesiology to urology. A few graduates follow different, nonclinical paths: as researchers, start-up executives, inventors, even writers. No matter what career path they choose, graduates of The Warren Alpert Medical School use their MDs to follow their passions. Match Day At Commencement you take the Physician’s Oath and become a doctor. But the first day of the rest of your life is two months earlier: Match Day. Families, friends, faculty, and staff gather round as you tear open your red envelope to reveal the next step in your professional life.

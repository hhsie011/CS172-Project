Students and Alumni Events Past Event Virtual China Career Fair for All SPS Students and Alumni Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 17, 2020 - August 21, 2020 8:30 AM - 11:00 AM Online Join us for the Virtual China Career Fair exclusive for Columbia School of Professional Studies' students and alumni. During this five-day event, students and alumni will have opportunities to speak directly to China-based companies recruiting for both full-time jobs, internships, and networking opportunities. This event is hosted by Columbia School of Professional Studies and is sponsored by Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, Columbia University Career Design Lab, UNESCO Creative Cities (Shanghai) Promotion Office, and Shanghai Global Talent Service Center. Date August 16 - 20, 2020 (U.S.) August 17 - 21, 2020 (China) Time 8:30 p.m. - 11:00 p.m. (Eastern Standard Time) 8:30 a.m. - 11:00 a.m. (China Standard Time) Please follow the instructions below to have your resume included in a resume book to be distributed to all employer partners: Save your resume as a PDF labeled LastName_FirstName Use this link to upload your resume using your Columbia UNI email address **If you are unable to access the drive folder using your Columbia UNI email address, please email your resume to [email protected] using the subject line, Virtual China Career Fair Resume_Name.** Please note that your registration grants you access to attend all five days of the virtual fair. After the registration, you will receive a confirmation email within two business days from the Career Design Lab with a link to Canvas. Prior to the event, Canvas will be updated with the schedule of employers, event link, and available job postings. Participating Companies *Employers are still registering. The list will be updated on a weekly basis.

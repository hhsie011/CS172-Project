Event Top MBA Recruitment Event Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo July 19, 2019 6:30 PM - 8:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Nairobi, Westcom Point, 8th Fl, Mahiga Mairu Ave, Off Waiyaki Way Director of Admissions Michael Robinson at Columbia University Business School, along with admissions representatives from several top MBA programs, are teaming up for a joint MBA recruiting effort in several African cities: Accra (July 15), Lagos (July 17), Nairobi (July 19), and Johannesburg (July 22). The format will feature a general admissions presentation, an admissions panel, an alumni panel, and there will also be an MBA Fair where admissions officers and local alumni will be available to answer individual questions.

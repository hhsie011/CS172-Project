Students from other disciplines may minor in cyber security. Required Courses (6 hours) CS 3073 Introduction to Cyber Security CS 4153 Computer Security * or CIS 4073 Information Security * Elective Courses (6 hours) Two courses selected from the following: CS 3353 System Administration CS 3861-3 Special Topics in Computer Science (3 hours) CS 4413 Secure Electronic Commerce CS 4153 Computer Security * or CIS 4073 Information Security * CS 3363 Data Communication and Networking or CS 4333 Computer Networks or CIS 3043 Telecommunications Eligible students may also choose from the following: CS 5143 Security Economics CS 5183 Information System Security Engineering CS 5193 Risk Management for Information Systems CS 5443 Information System Assurance CS 5473 Network Security CS 5493 Secure System Administration Total: 12 hours Note: *CS 4153 and CIS 4073 may be taken for either required or elective course credit, but not for both.

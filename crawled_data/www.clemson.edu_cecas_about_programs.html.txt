Magazine Outreach Ways to Give Clemson Home College of Engineering, Computing and Applied Sciences About Current: Departments Departments The College of Engineering, Computing and Applied Sciences (CECAS) is made up of 12 schools and departments, but the unique structure of combining engineering and science programs within one college has led to an uncommon ability to provide a team-based, integrated approach to teaching and research. Scientists and engineers are working together to find more efficient solutions. Detailed descriptions of curricula and program objectives can be found in the University Undergraduate Catalog and on the graduate school web page. Clemson Catalog Graduate School Automotive Engineering The Department of Automotive Engineering offers several programs that include M.S., Ph.D., Certificate. AUE Program Bioengineering The Department of Bioengineering offers several programs that include B.S., M.S., Ph.D., Certificate. BIOE Program Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering The Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering offers several programs that include B.S., M.S., Ph.D. ChBE Program Civil Engineering The Department of Civil Engineering offers several programs that include B.S., M.S., Ph.D., Certificate. CE Program School of Computing The School of Computing offers several programs that include B.S., B.A., M.S., MFA, Ph.D. SOC Program Electrical and Computer Engineering The Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering offers several programs that include B.S., M.S., Ph.D., Certificate. ECE Program Engineering and Science Education The Department of Engineering and Science Education offers several graduate-level programs that include Ph.D., Certificate. ESE Program Environmental Engineering and Earth Sciences The Department of Environmental Engineering and Earth Sciences offers several programs that include B.S., M.S., Ph.D. EEES Program General Engineering The Department of General Engineering is the starting point for all engineering students before they enter their intended program. GE Program Industrial Engineering The Department of Industrial Engineering offers several programs that include B.S., M.S., Ph.D., Certificate.

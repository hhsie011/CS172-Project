Accessibility resources at Boise State IT Accessibility Policy Statement The University is committed to supporting an information technology (IT) environment that is accessible to all, and in particular to individuals with disabilities. To this end, the University seeks to deploy information technology that is designed, developed, or procured with accessibility in mind. An Accessible IT environment generally enhances usability for everyone, and particularly for people using Assistive Technologies. By supporting IT Accessibility, the University helps ensure that everyone is able to access, benefit from, and contribute to its electronic programs and services.

Funded by the State of Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development through a one-time grant, MCAN is dedicated to development Tennessee's knowledge and innovation economy. MCAN provides reliable and cost-effective communications for its members. The organization plays an active role in development of research partnerships on behalf of its members.

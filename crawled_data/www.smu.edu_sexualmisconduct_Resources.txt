Sexual harassment includes sexual violence, sexual assault, dating violence, domestic violence, and stalking. Sexual harassment will not be tolerated in our University community. If you have experienced any form of sexual harassment, please know that you are not alone. SMU and community resources can help, including 24-hour emergency response, medical care and confidential counseling. You can choose the resources you are most comfortable contacting.

Located on the lower level of the Bill Daniel Student Center, our facilities boast 5 state-of-the-art bowling lanes, billiard tables, foosball tables, ping pong, shuffleboard, classic board games, and a TV lounge with Wii capability. PUBLIC HOURS OF OPERATION Monday-Thursday: 5pm-9pm Friday: 5pm-8pm Saturday: 2pm-8pm Sunday: 2pm-7pm PAYMENT We accept credit cards [Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express], cash, and checks made out to Baylor University.

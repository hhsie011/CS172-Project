Achieve. Succeed. College of Arts and Sciences (CAS) helps you to CONNECT and develop strengths as communicators and critical thinkers, ACHIEVE as problem solvers and lifelong learners, and SUCCEED with skills and adaptability to make the world a better place. SUCCESS STARTS HERE Discover all that CAS has to offer The College of Arts and Sciences is OSU’s largest and most diverse college. CAS is the intellectual core of the university building foundations for advanced study in every field. Home to 24 academic departments offering more than a hundred undergraduate and graduate degree options in the arts, humanities, social sciences, mathematics and natural sciences. Yet regardless of size, CAS faculty and staff strive to ensure every student finds success and fulfillment during their college career.

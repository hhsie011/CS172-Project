We use the acronym LGBT to encompass all gender identities and sexual orientations. We encourage all BC employees who are interested in supporting the mission of LGBT@BC, regardless of sexual orientation or gender identity, to get involved as a LGBT@BC member or to reach out to our steering committee if you have questions. We aspire by our presence and visibility to help create a safe and supportive professional environment for the lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender community of BC.

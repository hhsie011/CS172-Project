Academic Deans will be available to discuss questions and academic issues during business days through the spring semester (Monday, January 24 - Friday, May 20, 2022). Students have two options for meeting with an academic dean. Scheduled Appointments In most cases, you will be able to choose whether your scheduled appointment with an Academic Dean will be in-person or virtual.

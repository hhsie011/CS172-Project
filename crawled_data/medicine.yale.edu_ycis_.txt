Our population-based approach, which includes participation by all leading health systems in the area, as well as public health agencies and nonprofit organizations, will develop a model of implementation science, embedded in the community, that can serve as a model for widespread dissemination. Training in Implementation Science Yale Scholars in Implementation Science (YSIS) is the training core for the Center. YSIS is funded by a 5 year National Heart, Lung, Blood Institute K12 award and is designed to train junior faculty and postdoctoral fellows late-stage dissemination and implementation science. Visit the Scholars section of our site to learn more.

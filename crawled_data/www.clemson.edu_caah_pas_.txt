Architecture, Arts and Humanities Interdisciplinary Current: Pan African Studies Learn more about Pan African Studies The Pan African Studies Program provides a framework to encourage intellectual discourse on the African and African American experience. Utilizing a multidisciplinary approach, Pan African Studies grounds students in the histories, cultures, literatures, and traditions of Africans and how they impacted the various regions where they came to reside since the sixteenth century.

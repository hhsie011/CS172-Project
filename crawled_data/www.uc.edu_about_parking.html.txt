Welcome to the University of Cincinnati! Navigating your way around our large, dynamic, Uptown campuses can be daunting. Or maybe you need to find UC Blue Ash, UC Clermont, the 1819 Innovation Hub, UC Reading or UC Victory Parkway. The Parking Services goal is to make your parking experience as smooth as possible, so whether you’re coming to campus as a student, employee or visitor, we have developed a number of tools to help make your visit seamless. Here you will find real-time updates on current Uptown parking availability, permit information for students and faculty and staff, account management, maps, and more.

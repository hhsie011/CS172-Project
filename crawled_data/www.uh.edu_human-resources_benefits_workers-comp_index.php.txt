Except for emergency care, university employees who live within the IMO Network coverage area will be required to obtain medical care from a network provider in order to receive workers’ compensation benefits. For questions or additional information, please contact Risk Management at 713-743-0414 or via email at workerscomp@uh.edu. You can also visit the UH Workers Compensation website at https://uh.edu/risk-management/workers-compensation/.

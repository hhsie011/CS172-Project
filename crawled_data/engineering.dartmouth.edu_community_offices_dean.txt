Dean's Office Dean’s Office The Dean’s Office facilitates school-wide planning efforts, oversees the effective use of resources and the continuous improvement of operations, and helps shape, lead and implement programs and initiatives that operationalize the strategic priorities of the School. In addition, the Office helps define and uphold Dartmouth Engineering and college-wide policies. In consultation with members of engineering and College-wide communities, the Dean works with colleagues to create and realize a shared vision for the Thayer School of Engineering. The Dean is responsible for supporting and advancing the programs, teaching, and scholarship of the Faculty, enriching the learning environment for students, and setting the strategic plan. The Dean’s Office is located in room 204 of Cummings Hall (map).

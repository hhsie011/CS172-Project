That’s why 24/7 confidential support is available to you as part of your Columbia University behavioral health benefits. From parenting and relationships, to concerns about your health or caring for a loved one, you and your family have access to a wide range of resources to help you manage the challenges and help make life a little easier. Get confidential support Employee Assistance Program (EAP) You and your household family members can get six sessions with a counselor per issue or topic at no cost. Call 1-888-673-1153, TTY 711 or visit humana.com/eap (username: columbia | password: eap) Behavioral health support Meet with a mental health provider in person or online. Columbia Doctors Psychiatry: Call 1-212-305-6001 or visit columbiadoctors.org/psychiatry UnitedHealthcare members: Call 1-800-232-9357 or visit myuhc.com Sanvello app Get tips to help manage stress, anxiety and depression. Download the app on the App Store or Google Play. Talkspace Communicate with a licensed therapist via text, voice or video message online — use the EAP and get a 15% discount or use your UnitedHealthcare medical plan (subject to copay or coinsurance). Learn more at talkspace.com/connect.

Swim Lessons Texas Tech University Recreation is proud to offer the American Red Cross Learn-To-Swim program as part of our Aquatic Programming. The Learn-To-Swim Program begins with the basics of swimming and water acclimation (i.e. blowing bubbles, movement, etc..) and progresses through stroke introduction, development, and refinement, in addition to diving and other deep-water skills (i.e. treading, survival swimming). Learn More CPR Certification Classes Would you know what to do in a cardiac, breathing or first aid emergency? The right answer could help you save a life. With an emphasis on hands-on learning, our American Red Cross First Aid/CPR/AED courses give you the skills to save a life. All course options align with OSHA's Best Practices for Workplace First Aid Training Programs. Learn more Lifeguarding Classes University Recreation Aquatics is affilatied with the American Red Cross to offer lifeguard classes and certification upon successful completion of the course. This course prepares candidates to recognize and respond quickly and effectively to aquatic emergencies and prevent drowning and other incidents.

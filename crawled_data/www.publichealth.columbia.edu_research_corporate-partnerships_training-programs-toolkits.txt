Our research and teaching faculty have extensive expertise in designing customized curricula for a variety of target audience, including executive education students, clinical healthcare workers, community volunteers, and the general public. Recent collaborations have included the design and implementation of workshops on responsible ultrasound use; and the development and deployment of a toolkit for nurses and community health workers combatting the US opioid crisis.

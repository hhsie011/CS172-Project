In this system, you can: Search and apply for internships Search and apply for full-time jobs RSVP for events Sign up for interviews Upload resumes, cover letters and more How to make the most of Handshake Interstride Interstride is an interactive career platform designed to enhance the career exploration and job-search experience for international students. Interstride Firsthand Firsthand provides valuable career insights, expert advice, and connections to help you navigate your career. Firsthand Cane2Cane Projects Explore career paths and prepare for the workplace by completing short-term assignments for companies in your areas of interest. These opportunities are great for students who want to showcase their skills to employers but may not have the time to commit to a traditional internship. Cane2Cane Projects Parker Dewey Parker Dewey provides students with access to listings for micro internships. Parker Dewey MindSumo MindSumo helps students earn money, experience, and potential job opportunities by participating in online crowdsourcing projects called "Challenges." These challenges have been launched by over 100 of the Fortune 500 and can help students showcase their skills and stand out to some of the world's top employers. MindSumo Forage Forage is a platform where students can find free Virtual Experience Programs posted by top companies in order to build valuable career skills and get a taste of industry experience via short, real-world, company-backed online projects. Forage Tech Companies & Start-Ups The Toppel Career Center is committed to opening doors and resources in the world of tech. Here are some companies to get you started on your quest. Tech Companies Start-Ups Creatively Creatively is a job platform for creatives championing and connecting the world’s most valued creative community through best-in-class portfolios, collaborative features, and job opportunities around the globe. Creatively LinkedIn Use LinkedIn Jobs and the Alumni Tool to harness the power of your network and get hired. LinkedIn Jobs Linkedin Internships Linkedin Salary Tool LinkedIn Alumni Tool Headshots At Home Glassdoor Glassdoor is a database of company reviews, approval ratings, salary reports and more.

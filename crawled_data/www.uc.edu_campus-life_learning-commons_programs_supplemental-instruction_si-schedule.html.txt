SI will resume in Fall 2022. Note: there may be changes to the schedule throughout the semester. Please check back frequently for new updates. Students are encouraged to attend SI sessions for the course section in which they are enrolled. In the event students have a conflict with the times available for their assigned section, they are encouraged to attend other sessions for their course. Please keep in mind that individual instructor's teaching styles vary, and therefore content covered in sessions assigned to a different instructor may be slightly ahead or behind the pace of the student's assigned course.

Play About LLA Lynch Leadership Academy's mission is to develop a powerful and sustainable network of highly effective leaders at schools, within systems, and in communities, who will disrupt inequity and dramatically increase the opportunities and outcomes for all students. See Who We Are What We Do Lynch Leadership Academy increases the number of high performing leaders within a region and helps mobilize a critical mass to improve their schools, systems and communities. Building the capacity of principals and helping them assert their leadership—individually and collectively—enables principals to improve student outcomes. On-site support and a strong network increases the sustainability of the principalship.

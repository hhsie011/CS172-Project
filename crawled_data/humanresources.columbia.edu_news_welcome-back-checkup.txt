Benefits News Welcome Back: Time for a Checkup? August 17, 2021 Your physical and emotional well-being have never been more important, and we want to do all we can to help you be your best — starting with regular preventive care. Keeping up with annual physicals and dental exams may not have been possible or made your to-do list over the past year, but it’s more important than ever and can help find small things before they become big things. If it’s time for your checkup, call your health care provider — annual exams, routine dental cleanings and many preventive care screenings are covered 100% when you see an in-network provider. For a full list of personalized preventive care screening recommendations, visit uhc.com/preventivecare To find a network doctor, visit myuhc.com or call 1-800-232-9357 Find a Columbia Primary Care doctor Dental Care is also important Having a healthy mouth may help lower your risk of health complications, including heart disease and stroke. Find a dentist for your plan: • Columbia University Dentistry • Aetna • EmblemHealth (non-officer employees) Have Questions or need support?

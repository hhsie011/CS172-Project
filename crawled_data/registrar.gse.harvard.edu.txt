Attestation Process for all visitors: Visitors must complete their attestations using HGSE Clear. Once the visitor completes the attestation form, they will receive confirmation via email that they have permission to visit campus. This confirmation will include a digital green checkmark that must be displayed (either in digital or printed form) to the host, or at the security desk in Gutman Library if that is the guest’s initial arrival point. Visitor attestations are only valid for 7 days and must renewed each week by repeating the HGSE Clear process.

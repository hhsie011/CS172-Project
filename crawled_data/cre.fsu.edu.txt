Read more Nourishing Creativity luncheon at the Undergraduate Research Symposium The Nourishing Creativity luncheon is your time to shine and share your artistic productivity! The CRE pushes to broaden and include the creative arts into the research conversation. Your project can be a work in progress, incomplete, or finished. Read excerpts from your creative writing, choreograph an excerpt from a dance, read scenes from your play, show artwork, sing, or play your instrument. The topics are endless!

Public Sector Events Past Event Seminar: Innovation in the Brazilian Public Sector Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo November 10, 2016 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM Centro Ruth Cardoso Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro invites PhDs, Masters, Students and professionals of Public Administration to present their papers at the Innovation in Public Management Seminar, which will take place in São Paulo on November 10th. The event is supported by Comunitas, and aims to bring examples of innovative initiatives, discuss perspectives and challenges for public administrators, and also bring together professionals and leaders who want to improve the public administration scenario. To attend the event as a member of the audience, please click here.

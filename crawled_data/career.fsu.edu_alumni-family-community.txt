Scholarships Support the Career Center Partner with the Career Center Orientation Presentation Alumni, Family & Community Alumni Resources As an alumnus, you can still access many of our services, including career advising, access to career guides and library resources, and career fairs. Community Member Services As a community member, you are welcome to take advantage of many of The Career Center's resources, including drop-in career advising and our career events throughout the year. Family Member Resources The Career Center is devoted to aiding students in making a variety of career decisions, from choosing a major to searching for an internship, full-time job, or graduate program. As a family member, you are a valuable tool in this process. We encourage you to review our services and the resources we have provided to assist your student in reaching his or her goals. Career Advisor Scholarships Career Advisor scholarship funds support graduate students preparing for professional positions in career counseling and human resources. Sponsors will be given name recognition on a plaque located in The Career Center. Support The Career Center Give back to services that benefited your career success or a family member's career success. These funds allow us to continue providing high-quality student career development and employment programs. Partner With The Career Center Explore options for supporting career development and employment programs at Florida State University.

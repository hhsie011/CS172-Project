Launch eCOI > Contact Us Search Search form Enter your keywords You are here: GT Home OFFICE OF RESEARCH INTEGRITY ASSURANCE The Office of Research Integrity Assurance works with faculty oversight committees and boards to promote the ethical conduct of research and to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements relating to research involving human subjects, vertebrate animal subjects, rDNA, synthetic nucleic acids, and dual use research of concern research. The committees supported by this office include two Institutional Review Boards, the Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee, the Institutional Biosafety Committee, and the Institutional Review Entity. The Conflict of Interest (COI) and Export Control programs have moved to the Office of General Counsel. The Export and COI pages below will remain available while OGC develop their webpages.

She has lived and taught English as a Second Language in several schools and cities in China, including Guangzhou and Qingdao. In between living in China, she worked at East Texas Baptist University as the Assistant Director in the International Education office. Upon returning from China, she taught English to adults and worked at a private school in Knoxville, Tennessee. She has a Master’s degree from The University of Texas at Arlington in TESOL. She loves to cook, travel, and send mail to her friends. She’s beyond thrilled to be back in Texas and a part of the Global Gateway Program at Baylor.

Continued demonstrated financial need. Maintenance of Standards of Progress. Good standing at the Institute. Availability of financial resources. Most institutional scholarships require a cumulative grade-point average of 3.0. Entering first-year students are limited to receiving institutional scholarships for the first eight terms of full-time enrollment. Students are limited to receiving scholarships for two semesters of enrollment during an academic year (Fall, Spring, or Summer). A transfer student's eligibility will be determined on an individual basis depending upon hours transferred. These requirements for scholarship retention are in addition to the renewal terms listed above.

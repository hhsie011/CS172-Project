Faculty/Staff Flex Dining Plans are declining balance accounts and conveniently maintained through your Eagle One Card. Flex Dollars are accepted at the Faculty Dining Room, all dining locations, concessions, GET Mobile ordering, BC Farmers Market, CSA Farm Share, and vending machines. With Flex Dining Plans, you can choose the amount of funds added to your account at any time. Plus, Flex Dollars roll over and never expire*.

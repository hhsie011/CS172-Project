Our guide, Michael H. Hodges (SIPA ’87), was the fine arts writer at The Detroit News for 30 years and is author of "Building the Modern World: Albert Kahn in Detroit." Among the buildings we'll see are Albert Kahn's Detroit Savings Bank; the Guardian Building with its over-the-top Art Deco lobby(top image); and the David Whitney Building by Chicago legend Daniel Burnham. Michael has some excellent suggestions for places to gather for a drink or bite to eat after the tour for those interested. Cost is $10 per person; kids under 16 free.

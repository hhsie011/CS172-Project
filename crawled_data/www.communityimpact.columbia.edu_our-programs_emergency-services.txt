Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more Community Lunch, Food Pantry, Habitat for Humanity and Project for the Homeless are four CI programs that provide food and shelter to local residents in need.

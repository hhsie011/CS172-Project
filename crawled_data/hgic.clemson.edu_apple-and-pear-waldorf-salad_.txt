Photo courtesy of: Silviarita, Pixaby Ingredients 1 tbsp light mayonnaise 2 tbsp reduced-fat sour cream 1 tsp fresh lemon juice ¼ tsp cinnamon 1 cup (¾-inch) coarsely chopped Bartlett pear (about 1 pear) 1 cup medium apple, chopped (about 1 apple) 1 cup small seedless red grapes, cut in half 2 T chopped walnuts Combine first 4 ingredients in a small bowl; stir until smooth. Combine pear, apple, grapes, walnuts, and mayonnaise mixture in a large bowl; toss gently. Yield: 4 servings (serving size: ¾ cup) If this document didn’t answer your questions, please contact HGIC at hgic@clemson.edu or 1-888-656-9988. Author(s) Kimberly A.

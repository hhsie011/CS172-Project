The Title IX Coordinator provides institutional leadership in ensuring the College is in compliance with federal law and state statutes; oversees the centralized review, investigation, and resolution of all complaints related to sexual and gender-based harassment, sexual assault, sexual exploitation, dating or domestic violence, provision of alcohol and/or other drugs for purposes of prohibited conduct, and stalking; and ensures a fair, equitable, and prompt process for all involved. All disclosures or reports of violations of the College's Sexual and Gender Based Misconduct Policy will be directed to the Title IX Coordinator. Kristi Clemens serves as Dartmouth's Title IX Coordinator and Acting Senior Director, Institutional Diversity and Equity. Her responsibilities include (but are not limited to): Ensuring Dartmouth's compliance with Title IX, VAWA and other applicable federal and state mandates concerning sexual and gender-based harassment, sexual assault, sexual exploitation, dating or domestic violence, provision of alcohol and/or other drugs for purposes of prohibited conduct, and stalking; Providing institutional leadership to prevent, address, and respond to sexual misconduct; Tracking and monitoring incidents of sexual misconduct; Overseeing the investigation and resolution of all reports involving students, faculty, staff, vendors, and visitors; Coordinating comprehensive training, education and prevention efforts campus-wide; and Conducting periodic reviews of campus climate and culture concerning sexual misconduct. Title IX Formal Complaint Form When To Contact the Title IX Coordinator Any student, faculty or staff member, or participant in any Dartmouth-sponsored activity who has concerns about sexual and gender-based harassment, sexual assault, sexual exploitation, relationship and interpersonal violence, provision of alcohol and/or other drugs for purposes of prohibited conduct, and stalking ("prohibited conduct"), is encouraged to seek the assistance of the Title IX Coordinator. Contact the Title IX Coordinator to: Get information about resources (private and confidential), Supportive Measures and options for investigation and resolution under the College's Sexual and Gender Based Misconduct Policy; Share a disclosure, file a complaint, or make a report of prohibited conduct; Seek assistance regarding how to respond appropriately to a disclosure of prohibited conduct; and Ask questions about the College's policy and procedures related to prohibited conduct.

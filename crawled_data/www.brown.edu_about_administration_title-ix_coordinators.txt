Our Professional Development The Title IX Coordinator and staff are responsible for coordinating the formal and informal resolution procedures and implementing support measures and interim actions. To do this work, the Title IX Coordinator and staff are trained annually on the jurisdictional scope of Title IX, managing a Title IX grievance procedure, mitigating conscious and unconscious bias, and on topics related responding sexual harasment and sexual violence reports (see related files is specific training content). AY 2021/2022 Title IX Compliance Training by Universal Education Compliance & Training (UECAT) Affiliated Staff The following OIED Staff and in some cases external investigators support the Title IX and Gender Equity Office in the investigation and adjudication of formal complaints. These individuals are trained annually on identifying and mitgating implicit and explicit forms of bias, the specifics of their role, the jurisdictional scope of Title IX and University Sexual and Gender-based Misconduct policy, as well as University definitions of consent and incapacitation (see related files for Investigator Training).

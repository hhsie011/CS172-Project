With more than 50,000 volumes, the collection is especially strong in history, literature, and the social sciences supporting the undergraduate curricula for Columbia College, General Studies, and to a limited extent, the School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS). Undergraduate students may check out Milstein books for semester loans. Books may be checked out from the Circulation Desk (3rd floor). For assistance locating items in the Milstein Collection, please consult the Milstein Call Number Guide or ask for assistance at the service desks for Butler Circulation (3rd Floor Lobby) or Reference (Butler 310).

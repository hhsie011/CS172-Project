The Office of the Registrar directs the registration and record-keeping aspects of the School of Law and provides extensive logistical support for law school academic programs and external agencies. Some of these services include the distribution of the semester course schedule, managing the online course catalog, oversight of registration, collection of final grades, supervising the examination process, maintaining official transcripts, processing requests for official records, awarding of degrees, and issuance of diplomas. Additional responsibilities include, Banner Student System support, oversight for bar certifications, serving as a central point of contact for degree requirements, and helping to ensure compliance with law school and university rules, SACSCOC, THECB, ABA standards, Order of the Coif, AALS membership requirements, and FERPA notification and compliance. We are committed to providing a courteous and professional experience to all current and past members of the Texas Tech Law School community. You are encouraged to visit the Law School Registrar's Office if you have any questions.

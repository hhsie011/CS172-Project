Course Descriptions Graduate and Professional Academic Programs Course Descriptions Faculty Minor in Portuguese The minor in Portuguese requires 6 courses (18 credits minimum) in Portuguese. Course List Code Title Credit Hours PORT 1001 Basic I 1 4 PORT 1002 Basic II 1 4 Select 4 Portuguese elective courses from 2000+ level offerings. 10-12 Total Credit Hours 18-20 1 Students may substitute PORT 1021 Portuguese for Spanish Speakers or intermediate/ advanced level courses for the beginning PORT 1001 and PORT 1002. Students must still take at least six courses. Minors are awarded only at the time of completion of the bachelor's degree and cannot be awarded either as a stand-alone program of study or after completion of the first bachelor's degree.

Provost Beth Boehm has formed committees to address three vital areas: a coordinating committee to prepare plans for campus operations, student services and other key functions; a safe return to campus committee to plan the testing and contact tracking necessary to safely return to work and classes; and an academic scenario planning committee to address delivery of academic programming.

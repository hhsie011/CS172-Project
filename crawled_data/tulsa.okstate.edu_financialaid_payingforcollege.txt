Information Scholarships and Financial Aid Scholarships and Financial Aid Home Applying for Financial Aid Scholarships Paying for College Contact Us Paying for College College is an investment in your future, and the OSU-Tulsa Office of Scholarships and Financial Aid can help you get the most value for your money. More than $250 million in scholarships, grants and low-interest loans are awarded to students each year. Whether you’re planning to attend OSU as a part-time or full-time student, our financial aid counselors can help you determine the types of aid available based on your individual situation. To start, complete the online Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA.) The school code for the OSU campuses in Tulsa and Stillwater is 003170. How much does it cost to attend OSU? OSU’s tuition is very competitive. Check out the costs for part-time and full-time enrollment on our Tulsa campus with our convenient online tuition estimator. Actual costs will vary based on the number of credit hours you take and your major. Books are not included. Students enrolled in 12-18 hours and taking Stillwater on-site or distance learning courses will be charged block rate tuition and fees. For more information, visit the OSU Block Rate Tuition web site. Check out a complete list of degree programs offered at OSU-Tulsa.

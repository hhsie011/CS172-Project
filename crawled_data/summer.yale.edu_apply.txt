Information found on these pages will help answer your questions about applying to Yale Summer Session. Yale Summer Session accepts applications on a rolling basis once the application is open. Applications are considered complete and ready to be reviewed when all supporting documents has been received by Yale Summer Session. Incomplete applications won't be reviewed until all supporting documents are received. Once an application has been submitted a decision will be released within ten business days. For the Yale x Flatiron School Web Development Bootcamp, Acting Conservatory, Directing Workshop, and Yale Writers' Workshop, please visit the program pages for application information and deadlines.

Experience Dartmouth Connect with a Student Dartmouth Comes to You College Access Programs Summer Series Regional Deeper Dive Sessions Dartmouth admissions officers generally travel around the US and abroad to meet you where you are. In the face of COVID-19, we are bringing regional information sessions to you. These 60-minute virtual sessions are led by the admissions officers who work with applicants from your area and current students who call your region home-away-from-Dartmouth. You'll take a deeper dive into what it is like to call Dartmouth home from your part of the world and the details of the holistic admissions process. Register below for the session specific to your area.

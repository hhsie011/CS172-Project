You will need to have either received at least one dose of a COVID-19 vaccine or an approved exemption by October 15, 2021, to comply with this mandate. Individuals who do not have an exemption or do not receive their first dose by October 15 or, if required, their second dose by November 15, 2021, will be prohibited from working until proof of vaccination or an exemption is provided. For more information visit the University's Vaccines webpage, or speak with your HR contact.

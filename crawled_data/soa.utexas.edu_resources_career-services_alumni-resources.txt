Advising appointments are located on the Student Resouces page. Note: Career Services is only able to assist with alumni advising following five years after graduation. Join Texas Architecture on HookedIn As alumni of the UT Austin School of Architecture, Career Services invites you to join the Texas Architecture group on HookedIn, a new platform and online community for University of Texas alumni. Similar to LinkedIn, the platform is an opportunity for alumni, recent graduates and current students to connect for professional mentorship and job opportunities. In the coming months, Texas Exes, Texas Career Engagement and UTSOA Career Services will be building out mentoring and networking platforms on the Texas Architecture group to support our recent 2020 graduates. Get HookedIn. Provide Opportunities for UTSOA Students, Recent Graduates, and Alumni Full-time Positions + Internships: If you or your firm have employment opportunities, please submit the positions to the UTSOA Job Board or share the opportunity by contacting utsoa-careers@utexas.edu. Learn more on our Employer Resources page. Mentoring/Career Advising: Have an interest in assisting with mentoring students, graduated during a recession, and/or willing to offer career advice? Please contact utsoa-careers@utexas.edu. Panel Discussions: Interested in participating in future panels discussing the difficulties of graduating in a recession or ways the field of architecture might change in response to COVID-19? Please contact utsoa-careers@utexas.edu. COVID-19 Employment Resources site For recent alumni still searching for job opportunities, please visit the COVID-19 Employment Resources page to learn more about tips and resources available in this unprecedented time. Seeking additional Career Coaching opportunities Texas Exes has partnered with Wolfgang Career Coaching which supports alumni with a variety of career coaching services. To learn more about this opportunity visit Coaching 4 Good. support UTSOA To learn more about supporting and donating to UT Austin School of Architecture, please visit Support UTSOA. Additional Questions About Getting Involved If you have additional questions about supporting UTSOA students, recent graduates and alumni or seek additional information, please contact utsoa-careers@utexas.edu.

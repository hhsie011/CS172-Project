More information can be found below. 2022 Camp Dates Session Dates I June 13 - June 24* II June 27 - July 8* III July 11 - July 22 IV July 25 - Aug. 5 *University will be closed Monday, June 20 and Monday, July 4 2022 Registration Deadlines Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis provided space is available. Registration deadlines are based on camper status and University of Miami affiliation. Parent/Guardian(s) who miss their initial registration deadline are eligible to register on a subsequent registration date but lose priority. Camp tuition is non-transferable to other sessions. If sessions are full, campers will be wait-listed. All groups are determined by age not grade. Returning Campers and New Siblings (from previous summer): 2/14 - 3/4 UM Affiliates (New Campers)*: 3/14 - 25 Outside Community: 4/4 - Until Filled *UM Alumni: Cane/Alumni Association card, another form of identification, and C# are required to register. Contact the alumni office to obtain your electronic alumni card here. Camp Fees $450 for each 2-week session for Herbert Wellness Center members* $500 for each 2-week session for non-members $100 for each 2-week session of After Care (Camp tuition is non-transferable.) Acceptable forms of payment: Visa or Master Card. *Must be a Herbert Wellness Center member at time of registration and throughout the summer. Online Registration Procedures Create your profile as a guest online and add dependents (this is for those who do not have a wellness center account yet.) Click here for instructions. Visit the online services page to access the registration portal. Note: Please make sure you have a unique email on file for each parent in order to log on correctly (excudes students, faculty and staff) Returners need to submit all updated vaccinations and make any changes in health history if any. 2022 Mini Canes Camp Enrollment Packet Camper's completed enrollment packet will need to be emailed to minicanescamp@miami.edu . Instructions: Download the Mini Canes Camp Enrollment Packet and type-in all required information. Save your files for your records. Contact Alumni Office here for assistance on on Alumni Card. Steps to Register Step 1. To expedite the enrollment process, create your profile as a guest online and add dependents (this is for those who do not have a wellness center account yet.) Click here for instructions. Step 2. Email completed enrollment packet to minicanescamp@miami.edu Step 3. We will confirm and notify you that you can register online. Details on how to do so will be communicated to you during confirmation. Please allow us one full business day to review and confirm. You will have until the end of your registration period to enroll Please do not send your enrollment packet before the registration period as it will not be accepted Failure to register during that period will require for you to resubmit all required paperwork during your next enrollment period If an incomplete enrollment packet is submitted, you will be notified and will have to re-submit all required information again All spots are first come, first serve Cancellation Policy All requests to cancel a camper from a session MUST be submitted in writing to the office prior to the session starting. Tuition is non-transferable to other sessions. More than 6 weeks: $100 cancellation fee 6 weeks - 5 business days: $200 cancellation fee Less than 5 days: no refund Camp staff reserves the right to cancel any session or modify activities or groups due to enrollment, facility problems, staffing concerns or circumstances beyond its control (i.e. rain, pool problems or electrical outages).

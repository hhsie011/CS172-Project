One class will be offered as a Live Zoom Session(s), while the other will be offered as a Classroom Session(s). Description: The key to effective supervision is developing performance expectations and standards, clearly communicating those expectations, and providing frequent and timely feedback to employees. Participants learn to apply performance expectations and standards, assist employees in creating goals and objectives, maintain ongoing feedback and communication, implement corrective action, and evaluate employees through the formal performance evaluation process.

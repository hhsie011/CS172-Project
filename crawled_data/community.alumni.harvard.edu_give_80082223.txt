Harvard Online Giving Form HMSC Membership: special discounts and price adjustments Join or renew today! HMSC membership offers unlimited museum admissions and access to exclusive opportunities all year long. For details, take a look at the HMSC membership page. This form allows you to enter a custom amount. It is used for upgrades, special discounts, and applying the cost of admission. If these do not apply, please see our standard membership form. If you are responding to a special discount offer, enter the amount listed. We will be in touch if we have questions. For admission receipts, deduct the amount you paid for admissions tickets from the regular cost of a membership. The number of tickets deducted must be equal to or less than the number of people served by the membership level you choose. Send a scan or image of the receipt to members@hmsc.harvard.edu. For upgrades, please enter the difference between the membership you have and the one you wish to purchase. If you have any questions, please contact the HMSC Membership office at members@hmsc.harvard.edu or call 617-496-6972. Please note, membership materials typically arrive within 4 weeks.

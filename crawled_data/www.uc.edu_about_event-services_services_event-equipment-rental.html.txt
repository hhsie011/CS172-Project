CES has folding chairs, tables, pipe and drape, and more available to rent for your event.   Rental Fees Rental fees for available equipment by unit Item Description (Stock) Unit Price White Plastic Chairs (200) $2.50 5' Round Tables (30) $8.00 6' Rectangle Folding Tables (30) $8.00 High Top Tables (20) $8.00 Floor Podium (1) $25.00 Easel (10) $10.00 Coat Rack (2) $50.00 4' x 4’ Staging (4) $50.00 Stanchions (each) $7.00 Pipe and Drape - priced by the foot $2.50 LED color up-lights (9) [staff stays on site] $25/hour Portable Sound System w/ Microphone $50.00 Minimum Order Fee if Rental is less than $25 Service Fee - 15% of Final Invoice is added to final total Rental Terms Rental fee is inclusive of delivery to/from event, set-up, and take-down. Rental program is limited to events on Main West Campus. Availability of equipment is based on event reservations in our system. If we determine we can support your request, we will work directly with you to schedule the service. Signed rental agreement should be returned no later than 10 business days prior to the event. Customers are responsible for proper care of equipment. Equipment is to be returned in the same condition as it was before usage. If equipment, is lost, damaged or stolen, the customer will be responsible for cost of repairs/replacement.

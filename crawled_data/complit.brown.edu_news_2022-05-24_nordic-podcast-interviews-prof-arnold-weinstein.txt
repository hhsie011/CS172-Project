The Nordic Podcast is a podcast about Nordic literature and culture, so Xander discussed Prof. Weinstein’s 2010 book, Northern Arts: The Breakthrough of Scandinavian Literature and Art, from Ibsen to Bergman, a “magnificent and provocative exploration of Scandinavian literature and art.” You can find the podcase episode on the Fika Online blog (the interview starts at 9:32).

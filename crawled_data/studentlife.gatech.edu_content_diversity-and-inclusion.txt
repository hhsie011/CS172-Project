The Division of Student Life works to foster unique communities on campus that help make Georgia Tech a place that engages and includes everyone. You can find a list of diversity related events on the Student Diversity Programs website. There are many student groups to help you find your unique community. You'll find a complete list on the Center for Student Engagement website. The Division of Student Life is also home to the LGBTQIA Resource Center, Veterans Resource Center, and the Women's Resource Center. Learn about these programs and the services these departments offer. There are plenty of resources available if you need them.

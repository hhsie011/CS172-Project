October 22, 2017 1:30 PM - 2:00 PM New York, USA On October 20-21, Manhattan will welcome Chileans all across the United States to debate on public policies that could help to think Chile's future development. The first day will be hosted by Columbia University, located on Manhattan's Upper West Side, where conferences and panels will be taking place, along with a reception to follow. The next day will be hosted by New York University to share experiences on policymaking. With only a month away from the 2017 Chilean presidential election, the forum will deal with current topics such as immigration, education, social welfare and others, brought to you by keynote speakers and think tanks both from the domestic and international contexts. If you're in the U.S. and you have an interest in promoting Chile's development, don't miss the opportunity to participate in this insightful event in New York City.

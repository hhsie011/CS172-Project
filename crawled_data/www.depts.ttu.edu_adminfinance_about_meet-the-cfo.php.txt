Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Administration & Finance Menu Search TTU Administration & Finance About A&F Noel Sloan Chief Financial Officer Staff Informationphone 806.742.4250email noel.a.sloan@ttu.edu Address 115 Administration Building, MS 2006 Lubbock, Texas 79409 As the Chief Financial Officer and Senior Vice President of Administration and Finance, Noel Sloan partners with university leaders to support growth and strategic initiatives while ensuring accountability for financial operations is maintained and risk is minimized. In addition to managing the university's $1.1 billion operating budget, Sloan oversees the Finance division, the Business Services division, the Auxiliary Services division, the Operations division, and the Safety and Security division. Sloan is also a member of the President's Cabinet. Sloan came to Texas Tech University in 2006. Prior to being selected as the Chief Financial Officer in January 2014, Sloan served in the role of Managing Director for Financial Services & Tax. She has also served as an Adjunct Faculty member in the Rawls College of Business.

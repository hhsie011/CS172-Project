Our Certified Personal Trainers will work 1 on 1 with you, or in a small group if you prefer, in order to help you reach your fitness goals. To schedule a free consultation with a Personal Trainer, please complete the linked form below. A trainer will contact you to set up your free consultation so you may decide if you would like to purchase a Personal Training package. Click Here> Personal Training Consultation Form <to fill out your free consultation request. Notes: You have no obligation to purchase sessions prior to your consultation. You are not required to complete a physical exam prior to meeting with a trainer. Scheduling appointments is done independently with your trainer. BECOME A PERSONAL TRAINER The ACE Personal Trainer Course prepares you to pass the ACE certification exam so you can be hired as a Personal Trainer at TU or the outside community. This course meets on Wednesdays 4-6pm from September 29th – November 17th. The cost of the course is $45; however, the manual and exam do incur additional costs. For more information, complete this <form> or contact Andrew Jones at andrew-jones@utulsa.edu For more info on the American Council on Exercise (ACE) <click here> If you are already certified, please contact Andrew Jones if you are interested in applying to be a trainer. 800 S.

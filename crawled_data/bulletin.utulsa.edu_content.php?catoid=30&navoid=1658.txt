Sorem, Jr., Dean Mission The mission of the College of Engineering and Natural Sciences is to provide a modern, high-quality educational experience for all of our students. We provide the knowledge and principles on which engineering and science are founded in order to graduate engineers and scientists who are technically competent, creative, literate, ethically informed, and socially aware. Graduate Academic Programs The Graduate School of The University of Tulsa, through the College of Engineering and Natural Sciences offers the following programs: Master of Science (M.S.) degrees in biological science, computer science, cyber security, geosciences, and applied mathematics. Master of Science in Engineering (M.S.E.) and Master of Engineering (M.E.) degrees in chemical engineering, computer engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and petroleum engineering. Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degrees in biological science, chemical engineering, computer engineering, computer science, cyber studies, mechanical engineering, and petroleum engineering.

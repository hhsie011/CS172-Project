Appointment ketaxe01@louisville.edu Directions to Houchens The exits for the University of Louisville are directly off I-65 North or South. If coming from I-265 (Gene Snyder) or I-264 (Watterson Expressway), you will want to head North on I-65. If coming from I-71 South or I-64 East or West, you will want to head south on I-65. Directions, if coming from the North Take I-65 South (Toward Nashville) to Arthur St, exit 133. Turn right at the first traffic light onto Warnock Street. At the McDonalds, turn right onto Floyd Street. Continue past the light at the east entrance to the Parking Garage. At the yellow flashing light, turn left into the Student Activities Center. On your left, you will see the Visitor Parking entrance into the garage. This area will be attended by a parking attendant who can assist you with directions to the Houchens Building. The Houchens Building is located approximately one block from the parking area. Directions, if coming from the South Take I-65 North (Toward Indianapolis) to the second Eastern Parkway exit (west 133B). Turn left at the traffic light at the bottom of the ramp. Continue straight, at the McDonald's turn right onto Floyd Street. Continue past the light at the east entrance to the Parking Garage. At the yellow flashing light, turn left into the Student Activities Center. On your left, you will see the Visitor Parking entrance into the garage. This area will be attended by a parking attendant who can assist you with directions to the Houchens Building. The Houchens Building is located approximately one block from the parking area.

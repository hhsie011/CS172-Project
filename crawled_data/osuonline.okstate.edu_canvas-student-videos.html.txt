Canvas is a cloud-based system that can house course materials, facilitate instructional delivery, learning activities, and assessment processes. You can access Canvas through the OSU portal at my.okstate.edu. There you will see your courses listed on the dashboard. The videos below provide you with an overview of Canvas and its commonly used features. If you have difficulty using Canvas, you can contact the OSU Helpdesk at 744-HELP or helpdesk@okstate.edu OR you can use the 24/7 Canvas Support options on the left navigation panel of your Canvas site. Support options include Live Chat or the Canvas Support Hotline (833) 742-0661.

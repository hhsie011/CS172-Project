We in Raiderland recognize our APIDA leaders who shine and illuminate our campus and world beyond through their unique gifts and powerful voices. Through this stellar celebration and its events, Texas Tech uplifts and amplifies the voices and cultures of our APIDA students, faculty, and staff who, with generations of tradition and by hopeful ambitions, go boldly forward every single day. 2022 EVENTS APIDA Heritage Month Opening Ceremony April 11 5-8:00 pm SUB Escondido Theater Hosted by APIDA Heritage Month Committee (TTU Student Intersectional Leadership Council, One World, Students for Global Connection, TTU Division of Diversity, Equity & Inclusion, and TTU Honors College) Being APIDA & First-Gen April 13 TBD Holden Hall Hosted by TTU First-Generation Transition and Mentoring Programs Ramadan Interfaith Charity Banquet April 14 7:00 pm SUB Ballroom Hosted by Muslim Student Association Queer Reels, Real Topics Film Screening – Leitis in Waiting (2018) April 14 7:00 pm SUB Escondido Theater Hosted by TTU Office of LGBTQIA Education & Engagement APIDA Convocation May 11 5:00 pm SUB Matador Room For Spring, Summer & Fall 2022 Graduates REGISTER HERE Hosted by APIDA Heritage Month Committee Student Intersectional Leadership Council Asian, Pacific Islander, Desi & Arab (APIDA) Heritage Month The Student Intersectional Leadership Council (SILC) strives to foster an inclusive environment to ensure Raiderland is a place where all are welcome. SILC advocates for the marginalized communities on campus such as the APIDA population. If you would like to help prepare the celebration of APIDA Heritage Month or learn more ways to advocate for the APIDA and/or other diverse communities, please contact us at silc@ttu.edu We look forward to hearing from you!

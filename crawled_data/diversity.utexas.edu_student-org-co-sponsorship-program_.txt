Thank you for your interest and please let us know if we can answer any questions you may have via email to DDCE Student Co-Sponsorship. Deadline is January 31st, 2022, 5PM. Please note the following procedures that guide our co-sponsorship process: Applications are only accepted online Applications must meet the deadline established for consideration for each semester Only one application will be considered per student organization per semester After submitting your application, you will be assigned an interview time with the Division of Diversity and Community Engagement Co-Sponsorship Coordinator.

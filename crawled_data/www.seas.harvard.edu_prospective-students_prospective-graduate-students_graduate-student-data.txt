For enrolled students across all PhD and masters programs, the average undergraduate GPA was over 3.8 (on a 4.0 scale). Due to the interdisciplinary nature of SEAS, students come with a variety of undergraduate majors. Common undergraduate majors include computer science, engineering (examples: mechanical, bio, electrical, and chemical), mathematics, physics, and chemistry.

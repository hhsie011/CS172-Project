It joined the statue of Caesar Augustus, which had been placed in front of Rhode Island Hall in 1906 (it would be moved to Wriston Quad in 1952). The iconic statues were presented by brothers Moses Brown Ives Goddard and Colonel Robert Hale Ives Goddard.

Brown Alumni Card Brown Alumni may purchase an Alumni Card, at a one-time cost of $15, to access the Rockefeller, Sciences, and Orwig Music Libraries. Alumni may utilize this card to check out most circulating library materials. To purchase an Alumni Card, visit the Brown Card Office which is located in Page-Robinson Hall, 69 Brown Street, Room 511, Providence, RI, 02912.

Services Tutoring Services Learning Specialist Policies & Procedures Home About Us Workshop Presentation Form AAA Workshop Presentation Form Workshop/Presentation Request The Camner Center for Academic Resources provides workshops/presentations to University of Miami community that coordinates programming opportunities which support learning and academic success for University of Miami students. Approvals for requests are based on the discretion of the Camner Center staff and availability. Approved requests will be notified in writing. Please keep the following in consideration when you request a workshop and/or presentation: Make the request at least 2 weeks before the workshop/presentation date. Have a minimum of 10 participants to attend the workshop/presentation. Communicate any changes in needs, logistics, or preferences for the workshop/presentation with the Camner Center. Meet the facilitator(s) at the location at least 15 minutes prior to the workshop/presentation.

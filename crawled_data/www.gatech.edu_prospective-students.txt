You are here: GT Home Prospective Students Live, Learn, and Connect at Tech If you’re like most college-bound students, you have an image for what you want in a college experience: excellent reputation, challenging academics, innovative programs, first-class facilities, stellar faculty, and an exciting campus environment. Georgia Tech combines these assets with a supportive, collaborative community, where you’ll find people who are unbound by stereotypes or limitations. We embrace one another's talent and intelligence, and put it all to work to define what’s next. What's next for you? At Tech, the knowledge we share, the tools we provide, and the culture we’ve cultivated will help you find out.

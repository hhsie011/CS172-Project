What We Do Build simple, decent, affordable housing for families living in unacceptable living conditions in the Greater Boston area. Advocate on behalf of those who are homeless or lack access to affordable housing. Educate the Boston College community about Habitat for Humanity's local, national, and international ministries. Raise funds to support our building activities with Habitat for Humanity. Interested in Habitat for Humanity? Join us, and together we will bring positive change to our communities at BC and in Boston.

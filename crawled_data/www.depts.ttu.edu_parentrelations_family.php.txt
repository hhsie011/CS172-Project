Save the date for Family Weekend 2022 - October 28th & 29th, 2022! Bookmark this page and make sure you are registered for our Parent & Family eNews - we'll add updates about events for the weekend as they become available. Tickets for the football game are on sale now! Texas Tech Football vs. Baylor - Saturday, October 29th Discounted tickets to the Family Weekend football game are available at the Texas Tech Athletic Ticket Office Website. Enter the Promo Code FAMILY22 for a $40 ticket. Please note: If you would like your Texas Tech student to sit with you at the game, you will need to purchase them a ticket. TTU Students have free admission to TTU athletic events with their Student ID, but must sit in the student section. From a cell phone, please use this link instead - the promo code will already be applied. For assistance with ADA seating, please call the Athletic Ticket office at 806-742-8324 and mention the Promo Code FAMILY22. Gameday Guide - view this helpful guide on the week of the game for updates on weather, parking, what to bring (and not to bring) to the stadium, the clear bag policy, and more. General visitor parking information (NOT for Gameday parking - some lots not available Friday nights prior to football games, during football and basketball games, etc.) Family Days - All year long! We also encourage you to visit your Red Raider whenever it's convenient for your family. We promote activities and events for families both in-person and virtual, year-round through our Family Days Calendar. Explore the Family Days event tracks to learn about the different types of events you can attend!

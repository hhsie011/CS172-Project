Learn the skills you need to lead the way forward in your organization, your city, and your world. Complete the certificate part-time and online over the course of one calendar year. Credit earned for the courses may be applied to an MHA or MPH degree. Certificate Curriculum & Requirements Students enrolled in the certificate program must complete a total of 12 credit hours. This includes: HADM 7140 Population Health Management (fall) (online only) PUBH 7180 Foundations of Public Health (fall) HADM 7105 Health Policy and the Organization of Health Services (Spring) HADM 7110 Leadership and Organizational Change in Healthcare (Summer) (online only) For detailed information about this certificate program, including policies, course names and descriptions, visit the Graduate Catalog. Graduate students must maintain a 3.0 GPA ("B"). Grades of "D" and "F" will not apply toward any graduate degree, but will be computed in the GPA. No more than 7 hours of "C-", "C," or "C+" will be applied towards meeting degree requirements. All requirements for the certificate must be completed in 8 calendar years.

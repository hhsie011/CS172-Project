Visitor Daytime Parking Map The map below shows options for free, timed, and paid visitor parking. If visiting a specific department, you may also ask your contact if they are able to make special parking arrangements for your visit. (Click the map for a larger image) Visitor Evening Parking Map Areas not labeled in the map below are either free parking after 5:30 p.m. (like commuter lots) or are reserved 24 hours a day (like residence hall lots). Watch for signs when entering a lot after 5:30 p.m. (Click the map for a larger image) Visitor Weekday Night and Weekend Parking Weekdays from 8 p.m. to 7:30 a.m. and on weekends, all parking is free with a few exceptions: ADA spaces still require a placard or plate Avoid 24-hour spaces, such as residence hall lots and service vehicle spaces, which are marked with signs at the entrance to lots.

Through the Office of Hispanic Outreach, programs, services, events, and activities are developed & implemented to promote understanding & appreciation for the Hispanic/Latinx culture on campus and in the surrounding community. The Office of Hispanic Outreach advises, mentors, and guides Hispanic/Latinx students on academic & social issues & serves as the University’s representative on Hispanic/Latinx affairs.

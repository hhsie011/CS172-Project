She conducts investigations about reports of discrimination, harassment, and sexual misconduct when the responding party is a staff member, faculty member, or third-party affiliate with the University. Before joining the Office of the Provost, Sarah was a Sexual Misconduct Investigator at Indiana University. While there, she investigated reports about sexual misconduct when the responding party was a student. Additionally, Sarah oversaw the University's mediation and restorative justice program for students and provided support and training to campus partners in the areas of conflict resolution and restorative practices. Sarah received her bachelor’s degree in Journalism and Politics from New York University and her Juris Doctor from Wake Forest University School of Law.

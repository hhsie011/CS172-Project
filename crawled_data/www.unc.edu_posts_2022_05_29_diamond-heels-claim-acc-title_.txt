tournament win in program history. Posted by University Communications, Sunday, May 29th, 2022 (Photo by Helen McGinnis) Share on Facebook Share on Twitter Share on Pinterest Share via Email Print Friendly Carolina baseball won its eighth ACC title in program history with a 9-5 win over NC State on Sunday afternoon. Scott Honeycutt, a first-year at UNC-Chapel Hill, hit two home runs in the win and earned tournament MVP. It is the team’s first ACC title since 2019 and the first under head coach Scott Forbes.

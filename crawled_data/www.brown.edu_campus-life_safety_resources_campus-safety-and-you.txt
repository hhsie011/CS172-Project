The Department of Public Safety is accessible to members of the Brown community throughout the academic year to provide information, videos, personal and workplace safety awareness training sessions and outreach designed to make the Brown community aware of tools, services and actions they can take to keep themselves and their fellow members of the Brown community safe. We believe everyone at Brown can play a role in campus safety! Whether it is reporting suspicious circumstances to DPS or practicing the recommended safe behaviors displayed in the Bear Tips videos, you can contribute to campus safety.

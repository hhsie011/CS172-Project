With support from the Office of the President, his team works to move Clemson academics forward. Moving Clemson Academics Forward ClemsonForward is the strategic plan designed to bolster Clemson’s strengths, focus on the future growth of the University, overcome obstacles facing our state and create opportunities to serve the global community through tier-one research.

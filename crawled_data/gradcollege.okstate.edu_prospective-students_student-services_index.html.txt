This campus is located an hour from the two urban centers in the state (Tulsa and Oklahoma City) and provides a vibrant, yet quintessential college town atmosphere. Student and Academic Services at OSU Stillwater OSU Center for Health Sciences Educating from Tulsa, serving all Oklahomans The OSU Center for Health Sciences (OSU-CHS) prepares biomedical research scientists, physician assistants, osteopathic physicians, and other health care professionals. Graduate programs offered through OSU-CHS include Biomedical Sciences, Health Care Administration and Forensic Sciences. Student and Academic Services at OSU-CHS OSU-Tulsa Earn your graduate degree in Tulsa Located in a metropolitan setting, the mission of OSU-Tulsa is to advance knowledge, enrich lives, and enhance economic development through innovative instruction, research and outreach. You will find graduate degrees in business, education, engineering and technology, human sciences and liberal arts offered on this campus.

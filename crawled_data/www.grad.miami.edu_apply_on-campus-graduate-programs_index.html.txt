a graduate program at the University of Miami is selective and determined by each individual department/program. The Graduate School facilitates the application process and serves as a liaison between you and the program you're applying for. The Graduate School is not involved in admissions decisions. International Applicants If you are an international applicant and/or an applicant with international academic credentials, please visit the following links for important information regarding the application process. International credentialing evaluation process English proficiency requirement Department of International Student & Scholar Services General Application Procedures Online Application Click here to apply to on-campus graduate programs in the School of Architecture, College of Arts & Sciences, School of Communication, School of Education and Human Development, College of Engineering, Frost School of Music, Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science, and the Graduate School (Abess Center) at the University of Miami through our online application. Our online application is quick, secure and convenient. If you experience any difficulties with the online application, please direct all issues to CollegeNET at help@applyweb.com. NOTE: *Graduate programs that use other application methods are noted below. After you submit your application, you will receive an e-mail with instructions for setting up a CaneLink web account. You may also visit the account page to learn how to set up your account. You will use this to check the status of your application credentials and view your admission decision. Exam Score Reports If your program of interest has an entrance exam requirement, please submit official exam score reports (GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, IELTS, etc.) directly to the program you have applied to. The University of Miami's institution code for GRE and TOEFL is 5815. The University of Miami's institution code for IELTS is 4862.

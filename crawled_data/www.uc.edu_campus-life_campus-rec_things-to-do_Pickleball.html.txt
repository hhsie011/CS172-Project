at the following days and times on gym courts 5 & 6. Pickleball paddles and balls may be checked out for free at the Member Services desk. Mondays 4 p.m. - 6 p.m. Wednesdays 11 a.m. - 1 p.m. Saturdays 12 p.m. - 3 p.m. Members can also request a court set up on either court 5 or 6 provided the court is not in use by other open recreation participants or reservations. Inquire at the Member Services desk. Intramural Pickleball Current UC students, faculty, and staff with active Campus Recreation memberships may participate in intramural pickleball for an additional fee. Intramural pickleball is offered in the Fall and Spring semester. For more information, please visit the intramural website. If pickleball is not listed, the season registration period has passed.

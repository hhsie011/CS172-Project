Some of his best-known film scores are The Godfather, 8½, Fellini’s Roma, Amarcord, The Leopard, La Strada, and La Dolce Vita. In 1974 Rota won the Academy Award for Best Film Score for The Godfather II. Rota was Director of the conservatory Liceo Musicale in Bari, Italy, from 1950 until 1978. He composed ten operas, five ballets, and dozens of other orchestral, choral and chamber works.

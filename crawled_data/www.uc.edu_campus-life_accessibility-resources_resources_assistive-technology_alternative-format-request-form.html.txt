Please review the Alternative Format Request Policy and Procedure before completing this form. To complete this form, it will be helpful to have the following information ready: course schedule proof of purchase for book course syllabus If you have not purchased your book yet, please consider purchasing an ebook or other accessible digital format. If you no longer have your proof of purchase, please submit your request anyway and provide more information in the message box. If you do not have access to your syllabus at the time you submit your request, we can reach out to your instructor to request this information. Your name and identity will not be disclosed in the process. If you are requesting course materials other than textbooks, would like assistance completing this form, or have any questions about this process, please contact the Digital Access Team at digitalaccess@uc.edu.

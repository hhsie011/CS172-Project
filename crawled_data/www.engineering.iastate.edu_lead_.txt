The Leadership through Engineering Academic Diversity (LEAD) Program provides programs and services to assist in the academic, professional, and social success and retention of self-identifying multicultural, international, and women students in the College of Engineering and at Iowa State University. The LEAD Program is coordinated by the Assistant Director of Student Services and Director of Multicultural Student Success, Dr. LeQuetia Ancar, and is housed in the Engineering Student Services Office in 1300 Marston Hall.

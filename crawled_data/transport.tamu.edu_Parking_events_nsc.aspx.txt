Parking locations are listed on the permit and a map is available at NSC map. To access and print your NSC Parking Permit: Visit transport.tamu.edu Click "My Account" in the upper right hand corner. Use your NetID and Password to log in. Select "My Permits" Select your permit under "My Active Permits" Scan the barcode on your permit via phone or printed permit to gain access to the garage. To access West Campus Garage, simply access the permit and scan the barcode to enter and exit the garage. If you are bringing more than one vehicle to campus, hourly visitor parking is available for your additional vehicle(s). Additionally, if you have already purchased an annual parking permit, you can use it in valid Break & Summer Parking areas. Additional Option (summer NSCs only): Upgrade to New Student Conference Parking in Gene Stallings Blvd. Garage – 3-day permit with in-and-out access This permit is ideal for customers staying at the White Creek Apartments during the New Student Conference. Attendees can purchase a 3-day permit to Gene Stallings Blvd. Garage for $30, by logging into My Account. Gene Stallings Blvd. Garage is located near the Memorial Student Center and offers in-and-out access throughout the 3-day permit period. Available for purchase May 1 for summer NSCs and December 3 for January NSCs.

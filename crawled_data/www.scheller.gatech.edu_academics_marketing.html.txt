Marketing is about the creation of value for customers, partners and society at large. Successful marketing requires the ability to understand customer needs, identify attractive market segments, communicate effectively with those segments, and deliver superior products, services and solutions. The Marketing area at Scheller prepares students to thrive in today’s marketing landscape by providing them with knowledge that is both timely and timeless. Our curriculum combines rigorous exposure to marketing fundamentals with cutting-edge application of emerging trends and innovations. Two unique emphases of our program are marketing of technology goods and services, and marketing applications of modern technologies. Our faculty include thought leaders, researchers, and industry veterans with expertise spanning the marketing discipline. Marketing faculty members publish cutting-edge research in top journals such as the Journal of Marketing, Journal of Marketing Research, Journal of Consumer Research, and Management Science.

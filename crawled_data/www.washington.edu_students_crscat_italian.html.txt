View course details in MyPlan: ITAL 354 ITAL 355 Culture, Politics, and Media in Italy (5) VLPA/I&S Beatrice Arduini Role of media in shaping Italian history, culture, and politics: from medieval scriptoria, to Venice as Renaissance capital of printing, to Berlusconi's media empire.

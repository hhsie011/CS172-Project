experience Support our Concert Series Close Take your concert experience to the next level. Become a Yale School of Music patron and support our performance programs, including the Horowitz Piano Series, Oneppo Chamber Music Series, Yale Philharmonia, the Ellington Jazz Series, and Yale Opera. We are grateful for the gifts that help sustain these concert series. Enjoy benefits that include preferred seating or invitations to special events. The names of all donors who give $25 or more are listed in our printed concert programs. To learn more, contact us at 203 432-4158 or email concerts@yale.edu.

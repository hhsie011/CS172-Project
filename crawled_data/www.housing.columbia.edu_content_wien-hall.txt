The building offers singles and doubles. Data from Room Selection 2021 shows the make-up of Wien as 49% juniors, 32% sophomores, 10% seniors and 7% mixed-point groups (average point value of mixed-point groups was 16). Please keep in mind that class make-up in each building varies year to year based on student preferences. This should not be used as predictive data for the upcoming academic year.

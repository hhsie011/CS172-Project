predocs External Funding Alumni Students graduating from Brown and completing the PSTC training program are hired into both academic and private sector positions. In recent years, our graduates and postdocs have secured tenure-track placements at excellent institutions that include Columbia, Harvard, Johns Hopkins, NYU, Ohio State, Stanford, Tufts, UCLA, University of Chicago, University of Toronto, and Yale, and postdoctoral placement at Princeton’s Office of Population Research, Indiana University School of Law, the National Socio-Environmental Synthesis Center (SESYNC), University of Maryland, UC San Diego, and Yale.

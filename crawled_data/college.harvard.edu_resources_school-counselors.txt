Thank you for all that you do on behalf of your students. Counselor Resources Direct students to this site as they explore their college options in order to help them determine if Harvard is the right fit for their academic and extracurricular interests. Share information about our generous financial aid programs and affordability efforts, which make the cost of attending Harvard comparable to flagship state universities for 90 percent of American families. Help your students navigate the application process and application requirements (including information about high school curricular choices and standardized testing), and meet application and financial aid deadlines. If you have specific questions, need additional information, or would like to speak with the Admissions Officer who covers your area, we hope you will contact us.

SUMMER SCHOOL Spring Scholars Get ahead this summer with the Newcomb-Tulane College Summer School! Program Details Learn from anywhere! The 2022 Newcomb-Tulane College Summer School will have courses both in-person and online. Challenge yourself with one of our curated undergraduate courses and taught by Tulane faculty. Take a course in one or both of our 5-week sessions: NTC Early Summer Session: May 31 - July 1, 2022 Registration Deadline: May 26, 2022 NTC Late Summer Session: July 5 - August 5, 2022 Registration Deadline: June 30, 2022 Registration opens May 1, 2022 Steps to Register Reach out to the First Year Programs Manager, Rosie Yates! Let her know you are interested in taking a summer course, and she will help guide you through the process. Select your class (or classes!) Complete our Application Form Indicate you are a Spring Scholar Let us know your first and second choice course(s) Submit!

Each of these hubs maintain directories of affiliated experts, while the Population Health Initiative maintains directories of climate change and disaster and humanitarian experts. Healthy Aging The Healthy Aging and Longevity Research Institute maintains a list of affiliated faculty. Home School/College: School of Medicine Equity The Center for Communication, Difference, and Equity – a hub for the promotion of greater equity – maintains a list of affiliated faculty. Home School/College: College of Arts & Sciences Food Systems The Nutrition Sciences program maintains a directory of experts working in food systems across the university. Home School/College: School of Public Health Future of Cities Urban@UW fellows and affiliate share a collaborative vision for addressing urban challenges. Home School/College: College of Built Environments Homelessness Urban@UW’s Homelesness Research Initiative maintains a list of faculty experts and relevant projects. Home School/College: College of Built Environments Poverty The West Coast Poverty Center offers a home for poverty scholars at the University of Washington. Home School/College: School of Social Work Suicide Prevention Forefront Suicide Prevention maintains a roster of affiliated faculty. Home School/College: School of Social Work Violence and Injury Prevention The Harborview Injury Prevention & Research Center is a collaborative research hub for more than 30 UW departments and units. Home School/College: School of Medicine Additional Experts UW News maintains expert lists on a range of topics, many of which are relevant to population health.

The Office of Technology and Digital Innovation (formerly the Office of Distance Education and eLearning) is a member of the Classroom Readiness Committee—a group responsible for the service, management, maintenance, and renovation of all pooled learning spaces on The Ohio State University’s Columbus campus. We support over 360 learning spaces at the university, from lecture halls, to seminar rooms, computer labs, Active Learning Classrooms, and informal study areas. We provide on-demand support services to our classrooms at no cost, including: Classroom technology support (request onsite assistance) Technology equipment loan Routing and addressing all classroom related issues, including HVAC, lighting, and furniture concerns We do not reserve classrooms, but you can view reservation contact information.

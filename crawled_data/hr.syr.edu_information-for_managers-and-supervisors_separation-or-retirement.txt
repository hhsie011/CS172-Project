Contact HR Search Search Home Information for… Managers and Supervisors Separation or Retirement Separation or Retirement When it is time for an employee to end their service with Syracuse University, managers and supervisors play a critical role to ensure a smooth transition for the employee and for the University. Some reminders: Remind the employee to review the important information about what happens to their benefits upon separation [PDF]. If the employee is retiring from service, encourage the employee to schedule a meeting with HR Shared Services to review retirement benefits and get help completing important paperwork. Request that the employee reviews their home address and contact information in MySlice and make any updates to ensure receipt of year-end tax forms and other communications (i.e., COBRA enrollment notice) (Note: all University departments use the permanent address on file for mailings). Any changes after separation must be called into HR Shared Services at 315.443.4042. Collect any University property from the employee, including: Equipment (e.g., laptop, phone, two-way radio, etc.) University credit cards Service passes Keys or access cards Department will need to process the retirement or separation via Peoplesoft. Contact your IT representative to ensure system access is disabled on the appropriate date. Review information from ITS regarding Exchange email accounts for former employees. Review and submit any outstanding travel reimbursements. Notify any external vendors or partners to disable access to purchase or use systems, supplies, or equipment associated with the University’s account.

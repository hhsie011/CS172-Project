Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index CMLL Spanish Program Menu Search Do you want to connect with the 41 million people in the U.S. and the 450 million people world-wide who speak Spanish? Are you eager to experience diverse cultures, from Guatemala to Argentina, Spain to Equatorial New Guinea? A Spanish Major or Minor will connect you with this vibrant array of cultures and peoples. A Spanish degree from CMLL will also expand your career opportunities and provide you with study and working abroad opportunities. What's more, studying Spanish helps you develop your abilities in interpersonal communication, presentational skills, writing, and intercultural competence. Open doors both internationally and locally and develop the skills to advance in a variety of professions—business, teaching, interpretation & translation, community advocacy, bilingual medical professionals, international diplomacy, and many more.

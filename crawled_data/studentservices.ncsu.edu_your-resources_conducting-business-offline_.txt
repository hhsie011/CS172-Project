Business Offline Most of your administrative business to support your academic career is conducted electronically in MyPack. All NC State students must provide Voluntary Consent for Electronic Transactions during their initial login to MyPack starting Fall 2018. If you choose to opt out of the Voluntary Consent for Electronic Transactions you will not have access to conduct business transactions in MyPack. It will be your responsibility to complete transactions in person at the appropriate locations.

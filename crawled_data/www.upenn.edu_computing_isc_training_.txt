Course offerings include software products widely used on campus such as the Microsoft Office suite, Google Analytics, and Drupal, as well as topics to enhance key business skills such as project management, marketing, and collaboration. To facilitate learning, many tutorials include exercise files so that you can follow along step-by-step, and work with the same assets as the instructors. New and improved courses are added weekly.

Opportunities The Graduate Student Affairs Team Graduate Students Resources Graduate Student Affairs (GSA) provides programming and services to Graduate Engineering students that support and enhance the student experience and success. GSA offers holistic guidance from orientation to graduation, including academic advising, class registration, and events that promote student life and wellness. Our office seeks to be your go-to resource for navigating Columbia University and assisting with any questions or challenges that arise during your time here. We look forward to meeting you! Welcome to Columbia Engineering! My colleagues and I are here to support your academic, professional and personal growth by connecting you to the wealth of resources available. From Welcome Day to Commencement - we are here every step of the way. Jenny Mak, Ed.D.

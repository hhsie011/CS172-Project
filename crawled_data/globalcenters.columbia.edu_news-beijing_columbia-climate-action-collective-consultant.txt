Collective is looking for a consultant based in China Beijing Columbia Climate Action Collective is looking for a consultant based in China March 18, 2022 Columbia Climate Action Collective is a global network of stakeholders who will work together to identify key climate challenges that Columbia can help tackle through its teaching, research, and global programming. Mapping current experiences and anticipated effects of climate change over the next decade, combined with soliciting inputs into gaps in knowledge as well as desired interventions, will be fundamental to shaping the way forward. Such insights from a diversity of individuals and groups are fundamental to shaping future activities and directions in climate response and will help the University target its engagement with actions to address climate change. The purpose of this effort is to convene a global climate-focused network of actors to generate regional insights and perspectives on key climate challenges, gaps in knowledge, and desired interventions. Services Objective The work will involve two components – Project management and Workshop facilitation. This covers the two phases of the project – data collection and analysis as well as conducting interviews, surveys, and discussions. Program Management During the engagement period, the Consultant will be taking the lead in implementing the project's goals locally. They will outline the action point and determine the timeline that will ensure that the goals and objectives of this project are achieved. The Consultant will also be mandated to ensure that engagement of stakeholders will be inclusive. Workshop Facilitation The Consultant will identify a set of regional and country actors that should be tapped for this project. Stakeholder engagement will be in the form of workshops comprising diverse groups within the communities who are directly impacted by the effects of climate change or whose work and expertise revolves around this subject. Stakeholders should be smaller groups of 10-12 people to react and respond to the inputs gathered and guide deeper discussion about specific ideas on addressing the concerns and issues. The Consultant should be able and willing to organize face-to-face meetings with key informants (government or private sector) to discuss these issues. Key Deliverables The Consultant should be able and willing to organize face-to-face meetings with key informants (government or private sector) to discuss these issues. Identifying stakeholders Workshop planning Facilitating the workshop Conducting interviews Compiling a report of the findings Final Product for Services Rendered Workshop and interview reports Timeline April-June, 2022 Salary We offer a competitive remuneration package with competitive pay and benefits, in accordance with Columbia-wide salary scales, policies, and practices.

Miller School residents and fellows receive the most innovative and progressive curriculum innovations that will help them acquire the knowledge, skills, and behavioral attributes necessary for their chosen profession. They learn to become compassionate and professional caregivers in all potential health care settings including the acute care hospital, the ambulatory setting, long term facilities, and even the home arena. Our faculty includes world-renowned educators, clinicians, and scientists whose goal it is to fully prepare the next generation of physicians for fulfilling careers. We invite highly qualified residents and fellows to train with us at the U, where we change lives through teaching, research, and service.

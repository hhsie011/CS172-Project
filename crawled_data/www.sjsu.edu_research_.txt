Our office offers faculty and students a wide variety of services related to their research, scholarship and creative activity (RSCA). The Office of Research staff assist with proposal development, proposal submission to external funding agencies, and guidance to assure that RSCA is conducted in accordance with the highest ethical and regulatory standards. Whatever your research area of need, the Office of Research can help. Resources Highlights Research Development Ready to get your research off the ground? The Research Development team is here to support your RSCA efforts. We are available to help with internal and extramural funding opportunities. Research Compliance The key responsibility of the Office of Research is to ensure that research, scholarship and creative activities at SJSU are conducted safely, ethically and legally. IBM Collaboration San José State University and IBM have developed a strategic collaboration designed to provide SJSU's researchers with state of the art technology. SJSU's RSCA Adapt Plan The Research, Scholarship, and Creative Activity (RSCA) operation is currently in Phase 4 of SJSU’s RSCA Adapt Plan.

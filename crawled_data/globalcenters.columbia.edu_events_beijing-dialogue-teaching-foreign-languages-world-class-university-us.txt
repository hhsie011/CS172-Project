Languages at the World-Class University in the U.S. Events Past Event Beijing Dialogue: Teaching Foreign Languages at the World-Class University in the U.S. Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 16, 2018 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing On August 16, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to a dialogue on teaching foreign languages at the world-class university in the U.S. Professor Lening Liu from Columbia University and Professor Chih-p’ing Chou from Princeton University will be sharing their insights and experiences. Speakers Lening Liu is the Professor of East Asian Languages and Cultures and Confucius Institute Director of Chinese Language Pedagogy. He also directs the Chinese Language Program and serves as the co-director of the Certificate Program of Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages. Chih-p'ing Chou is the Professor of East Asian Studies, specializes in modern Chinese intellectual history and late Ming literature, and director of Chinese Language Program at Princeton University. His book, Hu Shi’s Thought and Modern China (Beijing, 2012), has been selected as one of the best 100 publications of the year by several major media organizations in China. Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code of confirmation. Please present the QR code at the entrance. Registration will begin 30 minutes before the program. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

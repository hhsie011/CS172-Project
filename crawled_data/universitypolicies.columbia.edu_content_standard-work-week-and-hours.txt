Regular business hours are 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. Work schedules may vary among schools and departments including differing full-time, part-time, flexible and seasonal schedules to accommodate the operational needs of the school or department. Support staff covered under a collective bargaining unit follow the work schedule as stipulated by the provisions of their respective bargaining agreement.

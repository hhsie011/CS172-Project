These non-profit corporations are responsible for the extensive redevelopment in the neighborhoods surrounding the University of Cincinnati. Projects will add new housing, retail, parking and public spaces that will serve these communities. As the university and its partners realize the revitalization of the Uptown district, it will become a truly unique, diverse, creative, and vibrant place to live, work and play.

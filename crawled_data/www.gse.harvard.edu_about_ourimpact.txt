Instagram About HGSE Our Impact HGSE Alumni are Changing the World Through Education. Through their work across all areas of the education sector, our alumni work to ensure that every child has the opportunity to fulfill his or her potential. Here are just a few of the ways in which HGSE alumni shape the lives of learners through their teaching, leadership, policymaking, advocacy, entrepreneurship, and research. Please check back occasionally for new stories that illustrate how HGSE alumni are making a difference.

Part of KU's Information Technology unit, the Media Production Studio aims to support KU Faculty, Staff & Departments. We provide audio and video recording spaces videography and post-production services support a number of video and communication applications. We're here to help KU instructional and departmental staff inform their students and promote their programs. Please connect with us, to find out how we can help.

Students must submit an updated application for admission. A student who has enrolled in another university since last attending OSU must also submit an updated transcript from each institution attended. Who counts as an international readmission applicant? If you have attended OSU but not enrolled in the immediate past semester (not including the summer sessions). What's required?You'll need these four things to be considered for admission: Completed an updated application $90 nonrefundable application fee Copy of the identification page in your passport If you attended another college since you were last enrolled at OSU, you must submit official transcripts from each institution. Transcripts must be submitted in original language and translated to English. Financial Guarantee documentation Submit all documents by email to international@okstate.edu.

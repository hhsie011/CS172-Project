DIVISION OF VETERINARY RESOURCES The Division of Veterinary Resources (DVR) is the unit responsible for the oversight and implementation of the animal care and use program. The University is accredited by the Association for Assessment and Accreditation of Laboratory Animal Care, International (AAALAC) and licensed by the USDA. This team ensures the welfare of tens of thousands of laboratory animals across the three University campuses.

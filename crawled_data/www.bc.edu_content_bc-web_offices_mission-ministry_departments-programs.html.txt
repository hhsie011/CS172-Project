Center for Ignatian Spirituality Learn more The Center for Ignatian Spirituality serves as a resource to explore and enhance the ethical and religious environment of the University. Center for Student Formation Learn more The work of student formation includes mentorship programs, retreats, leadership, and service opportunities that encourage students to reflect on their lives and deepest desires. First Year Experience Learn more The Office of First Year Experience helps our newest undergraduates transition into the intellectual, social, and cultural community of BC. Intersections Learn more The Intersections Office promotes conversation among faculty and staff about the Jesuit and Catholic mission of the University. Ministry to Priests Learn more The hope of the office is to offer pastoral care for those who do pastoral care for the people of God. Montserrat Learn more The Montserrat Office connects students at the highest level of financial need with mentoring and religious, intellectual, and social resources. Pastoral Support Learn more Pastoral Support offers spiritual guidance and direction to BC staff and alumni, including seminars, Masses, and bereavement counseling. Service Center Learn more The Mission and Ministry Service Center offers human resources support as well as budget management, web services, event planning, grant administration, and more.

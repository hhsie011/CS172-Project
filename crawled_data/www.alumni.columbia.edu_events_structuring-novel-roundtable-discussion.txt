Do you have tips, tricks or resources to share? Join Columbia Fiction Foundry for a roundtable discussion. Writers will meet on Zoom to share techniques on how to structure a novel from an initial concept to a completed first draft. We’ll discuss how to ensure the key turning points and moments of conflict in your story happen in the right place. Discussion topics will include the three-act structure and creating your character’s inner and outer journey in the story arc of your novel. Whether you’re an aspiring fiction writer or a published novelist, join Columbia Fiction Foundry as we discuss resources and share experiences in a collaborative, inclusive forum.

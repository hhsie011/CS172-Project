Emergencies Texas Tech Police Department 806-742-3931 Emergency Communication Information TechAlert! - Emergency alert notification system to communication important alerts and emergency response information (must have eRaider login) Update emergency contact information - student eRaider login required Residence Hall Safety Personal Safety in the Residence Halls Emergency and Crisis Response in the Residence Halls Campus Safety Blue Phones - blue light emergency phones are direct lines to the Texas Tech Police Department Raider Ride - a safe ride home program for students; operates on- and off campus Weather Safety Lightning Tornadoes Winter Weather Sexual Violence Sexual Assault Prevention Tips - Provided by the Risk Intervention and Safety Education department If your student needs to report sexual misconduct - Provided by the Title IX office, this page describes options for reporting sexual misconduct including sexual harassment, non-consensual sexual contact, non-consensual sexual intercourse, sexual exploitation, etc. Discuss sexual violence and consent with your student. This article, provided by RAINN, discusses ways to have this important conversation. Crime Prevention General Crime Prevention Tips - From theft prevention to pedestrian and driving safety, these tips are provided by the Texas Tech Police Department. Includes active shooter protocol information. Personal Wellness and Safety Texas Tech Crisis HelpLine - 806-742-5555. 24-hour assistance for students experiencing mental health crisis/suicidal thoughts/sexual assault/interpersonal violence Student Health Services - full service clinic with licensed physicians and pharmacy Student Counseling Center - licensed psychologists and counselors who can assist students or refer to services. Includes resources for parents and family members as well as online self-help resources Students of Concern - this section is to assist those in their efforts to enhance the education and personal achievements of students by providing information on effectively assisting TTU students. Raider Assistance Program - provides a safe and confidential place for students to address questions and concerns regarding alcohol and other drugs Risk Intervention and Safety (RISE) - focused on

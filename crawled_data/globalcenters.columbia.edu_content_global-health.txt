The Rio Center believes health equity is the responsibility of the global community, and discussions are not the domain of a single discipline – they require collaboration across organizations working at the crossroads of health and government, technology, policy, and business. Columbia’s groundbreaking work and involvement with the Global Health Security Agenda is of common interest to all our schools and their partners. Our partnerships are crucial to detecting and preventing global heath threats, and developing effective policy that guarantee access to healthcare worldwide.

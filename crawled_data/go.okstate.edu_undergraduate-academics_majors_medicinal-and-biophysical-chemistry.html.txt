Medicinal and Biophysical Chemistry provides you with topics ranging from investigations of water surrounding pharmaceutical agents and chemical components in biological systems, to new methodologies for drug syntheses. This program is designed as a formal and direct pathway to medical school and other health professions programs. Why medicinal and biophysical chemistry at OSU? At OSU, we maintain an excellent faculty-to-student ratio which means personalized attention in the advanced courses. This also allows access to state-of-the-art instrumentation and the ability to perform cutting edge undergraduate research. This will in turn provide you with practical, hands-on experience for future employers or applications to medical school. Financial Aid The Department of Chemistry offers departmental level scholarships for eligible students. The OK-LSAMP scholarship program on campus provides scholars with increased opportunities and connections, including undergraduate research experiences, graduate school preparation, international experiences, and internships/externships with companies in the STEM fields.

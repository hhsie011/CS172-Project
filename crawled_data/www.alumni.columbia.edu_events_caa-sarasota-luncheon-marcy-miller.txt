Our speaker will be one of our own local alumni, Marcy Miller, Executive Director of the Artist Series Concerts of Sarasota. Marcy will focus on this unique arts organization, which was established in 1996 by Jerry and Lee Dougherty Ross, as well as on the state of the arts community in Sarasota, as we begin to emerge from the current pandemic. Cost $25 for members and $30 for guests.

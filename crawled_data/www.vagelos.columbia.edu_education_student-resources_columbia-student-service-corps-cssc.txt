CSSC seeks to: Support health systems and their patients, workforce, and communities facing the COVID-19 pandemic through interprofessional student service-learning projects Understand the needs of local health systems and utilize the diverse skillsets of volunteer health care professional trainees and their faculty mentors Submit a Project Proposal If you have an idea for a project or have a project that aligns with our mission and need volunteers, please complete our proposal form. The CSSC leadership team will review all requests and be in touch!

Contact State Gym 2642 Union Dr. Ames, IA 50011-2029 Service Desk phone: (515) 294-6640 Parking Prepay Parking meter spaces are available outside of State Gym on the south side of the building. Entrance All users, omitting guests, must present a valid ISUCard encrypted with the current version number in order to enter State Gym. See Membership for detailed information on who can use the facility and costs for memberships, passes, and locker rental. Guests must show a current photo ID to enter. Equipment Checkout Equipment Checkout is closed until further notice. Family Hours Current Recreation Services members that are 18 years of age or older may bring up to four (4) guests (under the age of 18) and one other adult dependent/guest during Family Hours. Family Hours at State Gym, Beyer, and Lied Recreation Center are Saturday and Sunday from 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM. Hours may vary. Please confirm facility hours prior to arrival. Facility Reservations Space is available to reserve for tournaments, student groups, and special programs. A Facility Request Form must be submitted two weeks prior to the event.

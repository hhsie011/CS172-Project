The research projects combine observational, laboratory, and theoretical approaches in four themes: Carbonate Systems and Reservoir Characterization Petrophysics and Near-Surface Geophysicss Unconventional Reservoirs Geochemistry and Geobiology of Carbonates The CSL transfers the research results to industry partners through an annual meeting, webinars and publications and our website www.cslmiami.info . It offers continuing education seminars and short courses for professional geoscientists. A Certificate Program in “Applied Carbonate Geology” offers geoscientists the opportunity to become experts in carbonates.

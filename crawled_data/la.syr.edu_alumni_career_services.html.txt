The Syracuse University Alumni Association (SUAA) is ready to help you navigate the many career starts, stops, and transitions that can occur during your lifetime. Learn more about how we can help you advance your career. That includes meeting with a career development specialist for help strategizing your job search, polishing your résumé, brushing up on your interview skills, etc. One-on-one appointments are held over the phone, through video conference or in-person for those that are near SU’s campus. Additionally, online group workshops are available with topics covering resumes, job search strategies and LinkedIn. Connect with Syracuse’s Alumni Network When you graduate from Syracuse University, you join a nation of alumni that’s 250,000-plus strong—more than 8,200 of whom call the Los Angeles metro area home. Whether you’re looking for a job out of college, contemplating a career change, or seeking insight into an unfamiliar market, the alumni network can be a tremendous resource. Contact the SUinLA staff to learn about LA-based events or email Roshni Lal to learn about University-wide career programs and resources. Support Current Students Interested in helping current students advance their career? The Central Career Services team offers a variety of programs and events that will allow you to share your career story, offer advice, share jobs or internships, and more. Contact Adam Capozzi, assistant director of the alumni career network in Career Services to get involved. We look forward to helping you achieve career success and build connections with your Orange Network!

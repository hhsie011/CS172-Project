They can be applied to glass, walls, or virtually any other type of clean surface. Whether it’s one or 5,000, our staff can assist you to select the best type for your project. Helpful Tip: When designing your cling, consider the size of the surface to which you'll be applying it; you can have the cling sized to fit a window, for example.

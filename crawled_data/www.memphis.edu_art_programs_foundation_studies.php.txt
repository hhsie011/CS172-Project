A comprehensive and mixed media course of study ensures investigative exposure to the widest variety of methods. The foundation program is the prerequisite for all upper-level studio courses. This studio-based coursework provides the vocabulary, as well as a broad range of information and experience, fundamental to all areas of art and design. The teaching staff for the foundation program is drawn from the department's studio disciplines.

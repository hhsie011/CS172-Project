Wichita GEAR UP is a federally funded program through Wichita State University, designed to increase the number of students who are prepared to enter and succeed in education from middle school throughout high school. WGU staff works diligently in establishing a school culture that ensures each student has access to college readiness opportunities. The program strives to increase the number of students who are prepared to enter and succeed in post-secondary education by addressing academic rigor, family and community engagement, financial literacy, and by providing information about applying for and paying for college. Wichita GEAR UP provides many free activities and services to help students and their families plan, apply and pay for education and training beyond high school. Examples include: college campus visits and tours cultural activities tutoring and mentoring credit recovery classes ACT/SAT test preparation workshops financial aid workshops parent workshops Summer programing college exploration and preparation workshops career workshops and shadowing leadership camps Who can participate in the program? All students who graduated in the Class of 2020, and were enrolled in our participating high schools, are eligible to receive services from either the North Wichita GEAR UP or South Wichita GEAR UP grants. All current 10th-grade students, 2020-2021 school year, enrolled in our participating high schools, are eligible to receive services from either the Southeast Wichita GEAR UP or West Wichita GEAR UP grants.

Are you a UC alumnus living in the Milwaukee area? Stay up-to-date on all of the wonderful developments happening at your alma mater and with your fellow Milwaukee-based Bearcats. There are more than 1,000 alumni living in the Milwaukee area and we all share one thing in common – we’ll always be Bearcats. Get Involved There are many ways to get involved with the UC Alumni Association via the Milwaukee Network. Here are a few things you can do to get started: Join fellow Bearcats at one of our local events Follow UCAA on Facebook and share news from our alma mater Help recruit students from your area to attend UC Join the Loyal Bearcat Society Network Leader: Tony Marksteiner Milwaukee Alumni Events View more alumni programs on our event calendar.

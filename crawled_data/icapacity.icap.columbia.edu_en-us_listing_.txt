With projects in more than 30 countries, ICAP works side-by-side with ministries of health and local governmental, non-governmental, academic, and community partners to confront some of the world’s greatest health challenges. Through evidence-informed programs, meaningful research, tailored technical assistance, effective training and education programs, and rigorous surveillance to measure and evaluate the impact of public health interventions, ICAP aims to realize a global vision of healthy people, empowered communities, and thriving societies. ICAP is seeking a highly qualified candidate, Myanmar national, to serve as Program Implementation Director, by July 2022. Reporting to the Country Director, and in close collaboration with other technical directors, the incumbent will lead the implementation of project activities through provision of support to and working with the operation team and managing a team of technical and program staff responsible for State/Regional level technical assistance and ensure coordination and effectiveness of the activities. The incumbent will support the Finance and Administration Manager in the monitoring of the program budget and enduring progress towards the achievement of project goals and objectives. The successful candidate will hold a Master’s degree in public health, or related field and a minimum of 8+ years of senior-level experience designing, implementing, and managing health-related projects and 3+ years of experience working in a Myanmar. The incumbent must have strong supervisory and excellent management, leadership and interpersonal skills. The position is contingent upon availability of grant funding.

We are excited you have chosen to build your career here. All new hires attend the new hire orientation called Essential BU. This is a critical first step in your success at Baylor. Essential BU is hosted in a virtual format and covers important topics such as Baylor's history and culture, compliance requirements, and a review of our outstanding benefits package. This page is designed to help you connect with the many resources available to you as a new faculty or staff member. You can explore a campus map, take a virtual tour, begin to prepare for your first day with helpful links to information about onboarding and orientation, and much, much more!

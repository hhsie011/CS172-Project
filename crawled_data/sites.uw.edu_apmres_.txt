People Residents Administrative Staff Residency Program Leadership Faculty Places Harborview Medical Center UWMC Montlake Seattle Children’s Hospital Veteran’s Affairs Puget Sound Health Care System Research Research Opportunities Current Research Bonica Scholars QI Projects Home Anesthesiology Education Program Residency Training Program at UW Anesthesiology & Pain Medicine The educational programs at the Department of Anesthesiology and Pain Medicine teach trainee physicians the knowledge, skills, and professionalism required for clinical excellence; promote compassionate patient care; inspire a sense of inquiry and life-long learning; and develop leaders in anesthesiology, pain medicine, and critical care for the 21st century. The Department of Anesthesiology & Pain Medicine at the University of Washington serves the educational needs for the five “WWAMI” states: Washington, Wyoming, Alaska, Montana, and Idaho. We offer five different residency training opportunities: 4-year Categorical residency training in anesthesiology – 22 positions available for June 2023 3-year Advanced anesthesiology training – entry requires prior internship training – 1 position available for July 2023 (“R” position) 3-year Advanced anesthesiology training – entry requires prior internship training – 1 position available for July 2024 5-year combined Critical Care and Anesthesiology program (Includes 4 years of anesthesiology and 1 year of critical care training resulting in board certification in Anesthesiology and Critical Care) – 2 positions available for June 2023 The Bonica Scholars Program incorporates research training months during residency that optimally continues into a T32 research fellowship – 2 positions available for June 2023 Our training sites include: University of Washington Medical Center – Montlake (UWMC) Harborview Medical Center (HMC) Veterans Affairs Puget Sound Health Care System (VA) Seattle Children’s Hospital (SCH) Virginia Mason Hospital (Regional anesthesia) Northwest Hospital (NWH) Swedish Hospital (Obstetric Anesthesia) Evergreen Hospital (Obstetric anesthesia) University of Washington Be boundless Connect with us: Facebook Twitter Instagram YouTube LinkedIn Pinterest Google+ Accessibility Contact Us Jobs Campus Safety My UW Rules Docket Privacy Terms © 2022 University of Washington | Seattle, WA Skip to toolbar Log In About WordPress About WordPress WordPress.org Documentation Support Feedback Search

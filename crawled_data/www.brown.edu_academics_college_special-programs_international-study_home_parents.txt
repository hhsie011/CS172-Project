Thank you for supporting your student leading up to, during, and after their study abroad experience. Brown's Office of International Programs (OIP) provides undergraduate study abroad opportunities to approximately 500 students every year. As part of the College, and in close partnership with Brown faculty, we advise and support students interested in study abroad, manage Brown-sponsored programs worldwide, process credit transfer for study abroad students, and sponsor international scholars visiting Brown.

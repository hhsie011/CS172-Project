The GPD seeks to promote social science research on processes of social, political and economic transformation in the developing world with a special focus on the problem of inequality. The program supports training and research for PhD candidates in anthropology, economics, history, political science, and sociology by offering specialized courses, funding field-based research, providing fellowships, hosting visiting faculty, and promoting collaborative research initiatives with partner institutions in the global south.

COVID-19 Cases Dashboard SAFER Contact Assessment State University System of Florida Health Policies for 2021-2022 Get Vaccinated We expect everyone to be vaccinated against COVID-19. Find a vaccination location at vaccine.gov. Self-quarantine We expect you to stay home when sick, self-quarantine and get tested for COVID-19 no sooner than 24 hours after your symptoms begin. Get Tested Find a COVID-19 test site at https://uhs.fsu.edu/coronavirus. Self-Report Self-report all positive COVID-19 test results to the SAFER contact assessment team to protect the health of the campus community.

Student-Alumnae Gathering Events Past Event Barnard Beijing Convocation 2020 & Student-Alumnae Gathering Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo September 26, 2020 2:30 PM - 5:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Beyond Barnard Office cordially invite you to Barnard Convocation 2020 & Student-Alumnae Networking Event as we welcome the new members of the student body to Barnard's rich academic tradition. This will be a great opportunity for you to network with your fellows and alumnae, learn about their academic and professional experiences, and find out how to become a Bold Beautiful Barnard woman. We look forward to meeting all new students, current students, transfer students, exchange students, and alumnae at the Beijing Center! Alumnae Speakers Julia Qian '15, Head of CEO Office at Spark Education Yaqiao Wang '15, Incoming Associate at McKinsey & Company Zhiquan Davya Zeng '19, Equity Investor at China's National Council for Social Security Fund Iris Cai '22, junior student at Barnard College and intern at Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Agenda 2:00 p.m. - 2:30 p.m. Registration 2:30 p.m. - 3:30 p.m. Video of Barnard Convocation 2020 3:30 p.m. - 4:30 p.m. Alumnae Panel: Life after Barnard 4:30 p.m. - 5:30 p.m. Informal Discussion Reminders This event will be hosted on Sept. 26 at 2:30 p.m. in Beijing Time.

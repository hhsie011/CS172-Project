Now what? Transfer guidelines for special admission & appeal University of Washington Equivalency Guide Welcome to the UW Equivalency Guide for Washington state community and technical colleges. To find out how courses taken at a Washington state community or technical college will transfer to the University of Washington, click the college name below. For courses offered by any other institution, please see the UW’s Transfer Credit Policies. For more information about the Guide itself, please see the Equivalency Guide Manual. If you are from a UW department, or represent a Washington State community or technical college, and have questions about the equivalency review process, see the Equivalency Review Process Guidelines for more information. Note: The contents of this guide are subject to change at any time, and course equivalencies are contingent upon the curriculae of both the college and the University of Washington.

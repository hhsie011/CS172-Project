Thank you for your interest in the Cultural and Equity Centers Social Justice Youth Summer Camp! We will not host a camp in 2022, but please look out for more information about 2023! Our Social Justice Youth Camp operates for one week of exploring truth as participants develop into social justice warriors for self and society! This year’s summer camp will explore topics of diversity, inclusion, equity, and justice all while engaging with community peers and staff from the University of Louisville. We hope to build a community of youth who are students for activism, striving for advocacy, screaming for action. General Application Information Completed applications require submission of a Family Project. Upon completion of the application including all needed documents, applicants will be notified of acceptance into camp within the week after deadline. For more information, please email diveduc@louisville.edu Registration Requirements: 1. Completed Application 2. Family Project Submission 3. Completed Release Form 4.

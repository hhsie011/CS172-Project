Take the Federal Poverty level at this link and double it: https://aspe.hhs.gov/2020-poverty-guidelines/ If you are under age 24 and have been claimed as a dependent in the last 3 years, you must use your parent/caregiver's income First generation college student Neither your mother or your father attended college. Attended a Title 1 High School Two Preliminary Steps Fill out your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA), https://studentaid.gov/ If you have no FAFSA on file with the Office of Financial Aid, your application will be disqualified. Register for a National Provider Identifier (NPI) https://nppes.cms.hhs.gov/#/ You will be asked to provide that number on your application to the program. We will be obligated to provide it to HRSA because they will follow up with you.

A Park Pass allows you and the passengers in your vehicle entry into all of South Carolina’s 47 state parks. Park Passes can be checked out for up to 3 days at time with no renewals. You may only check out 1 pass at a time. A fine of $1.00/day will be assessed for passes that are returned late, and lost passes will result in a $100 replacement fee. Use the form below to submit a request for a Park Pass for your next adventure or stop by the Services Desk in Cooper Library to check for availability! If you submit a request online, our Library Services team will be in touch to confirm availability for the date selected. Anticipated park(s) visited(Required) Let us know what parks you plan on visiting on this trip. If you don't know, that's okay too.

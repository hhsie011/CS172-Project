University alumni who live and/or work in the Central New Jersey and the Jersey Shore areas. During the year, our programming includes: attending Syracuse University football, basketball and lacrosse games, cheering on the Orange at game watches, cultural activities, professional development offerings, guest speakers and our annual new student send-off.

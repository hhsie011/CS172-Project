Your group can still schedule a tour using the form on the group tours page. Join us for a special event designed just for GEAR UP! Students will take a walking tour of campus, hear from an admissions counselor and have the opportunity to visit with representatives across campus. We are offer GEAR UP events on select Wednesdays during the spring semester, and it is open to juniors and seniors in high school.

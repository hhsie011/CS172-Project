It was designed to showcase the myriad ways in which the Tech community is working to change how people think about and use the planet’s raw materials, and to inform the campus community about how they can learn more about sustainable efforts. If you know of something that should be shared on Green Buzz, contact greenbuzz@gatech.edu with information about research, academic programs, events, clubs, classes, lectures, internships, or other sustainability initiatives. Green Buzz can also be found on Twitter @GTgreenbuzz.

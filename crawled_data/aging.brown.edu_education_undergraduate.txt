The Center on the Biology of Aging provides many opportunities for undergraduate students to pursue independent research in the aging field. Interested students should contact the participating faculty. Biology of Aging Faculty The Center mounts a 200-level course in the Graduate School, BIOL2350 The Biology of Aging. The course is taught every year in the Spring Semester. BIOL2350 is a required course for graduate students in the MBoA Track. The course is also open to advanced undergraduates with appropriate background. Visit Brown Courses Catalog for additional information.

New and prospective applicants may access Banner Self-Service through their Brown Applicant Portal by selecting the Financial Aid Status link. The Banner Self-Service Financial Aid tab houses application requirements, current award information, financial aid messages, and upload financial aid documents. Helpful hints for uploading documents Fill out forms, save on personal computer as pdf; then upload saved form via Banner Self-Service. Do not include extra periods in the file name; myFile.pdf, for example. Scan in black and white (on some scanners this is monochrome) at a low resolution (200-300 dpi). Select "Other" if you are not sure of the correct label to assign to your document. Upload one file at time.

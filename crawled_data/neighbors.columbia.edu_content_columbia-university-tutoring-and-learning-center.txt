COVID-19 Vaccine Explore the Campus History Community Commitments Campus Construction and Opportunities Columbia | Neighbors You are here: Home Columbia University Tutoring and Learning Center Columbia University Tutoring and Learning Center The TLC provides high-quality face-to-face and online tutoring to local high school students. The tutoring is provided by the students of Columbia University and Barnard College. In order to facilitate the online tutoring components, students receive a loan of a laptop and a wifi hotspot. In addition to the tutoring component, high school students and their families also participate in enrichment activities designed to encourage a growth mindset and college readiness.

University of Miami School of Communication students create, design, write, photograph, film and research beginning their first semester. Take a look at our programs and you’ll see why the SoC offers an education like no other. The SoC is committed to developing forward-thinking, analytical, and ethical communication professionals for success in a technological and global society. Students are provided with quality undergraduate and graduate programs in communication that emphasize the relationship between theory and practice. Our faculty believes in freedom of expression and creativity, and encourages both collaboration and independent thinking as we prepare future scholars, professionals, and leaders for a lifetime of service and learning. At the University of Miami School of Communication, you are free to discover your passion and explore your interests. Students are immersed in hands-on education from their first semester and are involved in professional activities in a diverse city full of possibilities. How we communicate and connect with each other across the globe continues to change rapidly. University of Miami School of Communication educates tomorrow’s communicators through experiential education, award-winning faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities and equipment. The SoC provides the tools and knowledge to impact global audiences with a curriculum encouraging students to study along the spectrum of communication. As a student at University of Miami School of Communication, you will be immersed in a global mix of cultures, media, technology, and entertainment. The communication industry is evolving and the SoC is positioned in a culturally diverse city with a thriving media hub at the forefront of the global communication revolution.

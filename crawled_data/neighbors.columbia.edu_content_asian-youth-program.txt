COVID-19 Vaccine Explore the Campus History Community Commitments Campus Construction and Opportunities Columbia | Neighbors You are here: Home Asian Youth Program Asian Youth Program Asian Youth Program (AYP), in partnership with New York Asian Women’s Center, offers mentoring to children impacted by domestic violence. AYP mentors become positive role models engaging in one-to-one relationships through enrichment activities and recreational trips to cultural sites around New York City. All activities are designed to build self-confidence, foster a sense of family and community, and inspire a commitment to service among the children served in a fun and nurturing environment.

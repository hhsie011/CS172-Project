Faculty and staff are engaged and motivated to equip students with the knowledge and professional competencies that will make them competitive for medical physics residencies across the country. Career Development: Our curriculum imparts clinical and research skills essential to a successful career. Training Environment: Brown’s collaborative learning environment allows for extensive, one-on-one faculty-student interactions within a variety of clinical settings. Research and Scholarship: Protected research time enables students to develop as scholars and make important contributions to scientific discovery. Mentorship: Students learn from experts across traditional fields of study — engaging faculty in radiation oncology, biomedical engineering, radiology, physics and public health.

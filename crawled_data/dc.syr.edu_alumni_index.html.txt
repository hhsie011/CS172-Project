Home /Alumni Alumni We Have 15,000 Reasons to Love Washington, DC There are nearly 15,000 people in the DC metro area who got their start at Syracuse University. Armed with their degrees and shaped by their experiences on and off the Syracuse campus, our alums are making a difference, finding success, and supporting Syracuse University in DC and beyond. Take a look at some of the members of our DC “community of experts” and read more about how the Syracuse University Alumni Association can help you stay connected. Join One of Syracuse University’s DC-Area Alumni Clubs If you’re looking for Orange in the Washington, DC metro area, you can find it! The Syracuse University Alumni Club of DC, the Alumni Club of Greater Baltimore, and the Maxwell School DC Alumni Association have hundreds of active members and host events throughout the year. Syracuse University alumni clubs around the world draw thousands of grads together to enjoy a wide variety of social and educational programming. Events range from faculty speakers and career networking opportunities to game-watching events and evenings at the theater. Learn more about our local alumni clubs.

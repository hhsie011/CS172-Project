Facilities Email, Calendar and Contacts Supported with Microsoft Outlook < Back to Accounts and Authentication As part of your Microsoft Office365 account, Outlook provides email and calendaring services on your computer, phone, or tablet. Using this service, you have access to the University-wide address book, a scheduling assistant for organizing meetings, Zoom integration, and more. Key features and benefits Secure email storage starting at 50GB per mailbox Improved meeting scheduling with access to free/busy information for users at multiple Harvard schools via integrated full University directory All email filtered for spam, viruses, and other unwanted content Outlook app available for both iOS and Android devices Available to Faculty Students Staff Service cost Core Service - Provided at no cost There is no cost for an email account for the school’s faculty, staff, and degree-seeking students.

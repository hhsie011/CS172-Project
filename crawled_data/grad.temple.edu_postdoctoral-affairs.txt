The office processes appointments and reappointments; provides orientation and onboarding for new fellows and scholars; and trains business managers, associate deans and other staff on the processes used to appoint postdoctoral and visiting scholars. Temple University engages approximately 400 postdoctoral trainees, research associates and visiting scholars annually. Learn more about the types of postdoctoral positions available, annual stipends and orientation. Review Temple University's official policy regarding postdoctoral fellows. Temple University is a proud member of the National Postdoctoral Association. For any questions related to postdoctoral affairs at Temple University, contact Nina Marie Campellone, project manager.

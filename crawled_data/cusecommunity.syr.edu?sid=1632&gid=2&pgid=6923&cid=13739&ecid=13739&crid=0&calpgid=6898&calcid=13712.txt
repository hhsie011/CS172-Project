parents, students and friends of Syracuse University to connect with each other and stay connected to our University. We often hold game watches, attend local sporting events, plan professional development and networking opportunities, host a new student send-off and participate in community service on behalf of Syracuse. If you have ideas/suggestions for an activity/event or want to get more involved with the club, please email us. GO ORANGE!

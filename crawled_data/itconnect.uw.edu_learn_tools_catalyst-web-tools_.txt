Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Catalyst Web Tools UW-IT Service Catalog Catalyst WebQ & Gradebook Available for Available for : Instructors Students Staff Log in to Catalyst ABOUT CATALYST Service notice: Catalyst WebQ Survey and GradeBook will be retired effective June 16, 2022. Catalyst Web Tools are GradeBook, which allows instructors to track scores and calculate grades online, and WebQ Survey, designed for use in teaching, learning, research and everyday work. No service fee for eligible users. Catalyst tools help you to Track and submit grades online with GradeBook Create and distribute surveys with WebQ Catalyst Support Online Help Center Create a survey (step-by-step instructions) Create a gradebook (step-by-step instructions) Help Desk Contact the Learning Technologies Help Desk Data Retention Policy A single data retention policy does not apply to the Catalyst WebQ service due to the broad scope of administrative, academic, and personal information created and stored via this service. Consequently, users of this service are responsible for managing and deleting their Catalyst WebQ documents in accordance with University of Washington retention guidelines. For more information, please refer to the UW Records Management Services web site. Last reviewed January 19, 2022 See a problem on this page? Let us know.

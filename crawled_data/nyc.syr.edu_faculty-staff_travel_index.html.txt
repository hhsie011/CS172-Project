Located just off Central Park and near the subway connecting you to all of Manhattan and the Boroughs, Lubin House offers a safe, convienent overnight stay close to it all! Book a Room Guest Accommodations/Rates Standard rooms $175 per night Suite $225 per night Apartment $260 per night Check-In/Out Times Check in time: 2 PM Check out time: 10 AM During your stay, an active SU ID is required to enter the building. Your ID will give you access until 11:59 p.m. of your check-out date. Reservations The health and safety of our guests and staff is our top priority. Lubin House follows all University guidelines related to health and safety, and reserves the right to cancel reservations should the coronavirus situation change. For questions or to check availability, contact Andrew Levy at 212.826.0324 or anlevy@syr.edu. Guest rooms There are three categories of guest rooms at Lubin House: standard rooms, a suite, and an apartment. There are eight standard rooms, seven with one bed and one with two double beds. The suite and the apartment each contain a queen bed and a sofa bed. Room types can be booked by request, depending on availability.

They must be set when: Creating Section Content Creating Image and Info cards Section Content grid widths The entire width of the webpage is comprised of 12 sections in total. Section Content can span the entire width of the webpage (Grid Width = 12) or a portion of the page. Once the Section Content grid is set, everything added to this specific Section Content will live within the selected grid size. However, they are rendered in the context of the Section Content grid size. NOTE: Image and Info card widths apply to each card in the group.

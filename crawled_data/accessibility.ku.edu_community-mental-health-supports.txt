National Suicide Prevention Lifeline Online Chat Community Mental Health Supports Announcement Welcome, from the ADA Resource Center for Equity and Accessibility - Welcome to the KU ADA Resource Center for Equity and Accessibility’s Community Mental Health Supports. The ADA RCEA serves as a campus-wide resource on disability and accessibility. We welcome all to call, email or stop by our Resource Center located in Room 117 Carruth O’Leary, (785) 864-4946, accessibility@ku.edu. We are happy to answer questions, provide assistance and resources. The ADA RCEA’s Mental Health Resource Website seeks to organize campus, local, state, and national resources in a user friendly manner. Our Guide is not intended to serve as professional or legal advice. Individuals must decide which resources best serve them and whether or not to utilize them.

Affairs Boston College welcomes postdoctoral researchers to our intellectual community. New researchers have the opportunity to participate in local, national, and international research, and to benefit from our facilities and resources. Resources Research and Scholarship Integrity Program Office of International Students and Scholars Benefits: Human Resources Policy and Forms Postdoctoral Research Fellow Policy Postdoctoral Offer Letter Template Postdoctoral letter flow chart The Boston College Postdoctoral Association The Boston College Postdoctoral Association (BCPDA) promotes the professional, social, and family well-being of postdoctoral fellows from all BC departments. Learn More about BCPDA Contact Guillermo Nuñez Executive Director of Research Administration Office of the Vice Provost for Research 617-552-3541 Erin Sibley Director, Office for Research Protections Office of the Vice Provost for Research 617-552-1758 Postdoc Perks As one of the world's highest concentrations of academic centers, the Boston region offers significant opportunities to postdoctoral researchers. Faculty at BC often collaborate with researchers at the many nearby institutions through joint research projects, associations and conferences, and informal gatherings. BC's postdoctoral researchers are also welcome to join graduate student events and organizations and to attend the many open events offered by nearby institutions such as Harvard University, MIT, Boston University, Brandeis University, and Tufts University.

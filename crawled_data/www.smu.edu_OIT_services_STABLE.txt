This solution facilitates processes such as space management, event management, key requests, work orders, and so much more. The service will be available to faculty, staff, and students, plus anyone else with an SMU ID and password. Currently, the SMU community is able to submit work orders for Facilities using the new platform. Requests for other departments, including OIT, will be added to the solution by summer 2022.

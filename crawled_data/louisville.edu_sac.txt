The Swain Student Activities Center, affectionately known as the SAC, is the main hub for student life on the University of Louisville Belknap campus. With a multitude of dining options, amazing event spaces, cozy lounges, and vibrant student activities, there's always something to do at the SAC. The building is also home to several UofL departments including the Dean of Students, Student Affairs, Office of Student Involvement, Health and Sport Sciences, UofL Athletics, Dining Services, and the Campus Bookstore.

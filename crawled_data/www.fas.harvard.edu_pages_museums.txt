Together, these six museums (Collection of Historical Scientific Instruments; Mineralogical and Geological Museum; Harvard Museum of the Ancient Near East; Harvard University Herbaria; Museum of Comparative Zoology; and Peabody Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology) look after more than 28,500,000 objects that represent Earth’s history, life, and cultures.

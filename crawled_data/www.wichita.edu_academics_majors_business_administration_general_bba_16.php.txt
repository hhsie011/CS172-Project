Frank Barton School of Business The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in general business from Wichita State’s Barton School of Business combines its AACSB-accredited program with the unmatched opportunities for applied learning and internships found in Kansas’ largest city. You’ll be eligible to compete for some of the state’s largest business scholarships. And you can build a professional resume and make the connections that lead to your dream career. General Business is also offered as part of the WSU at McConnell program. Learn how general business is the right fit for you. Admission requirements Inside the Program State-of-the-art building planned for business school Plans to build a new, state-of-the-art building for the Barton School of Business got a boost with a $10 million donation. Once built, the 136,000-square-foot complex will replace Clinton Hall, constructed in 1970. It will be designed to foster collaboration among students, faculty and partners in the business community, as well as provide students with more internship opportunities. Curriculum This program will increase your knowledge in all basic business disciplines and functions—preparing you to perform various tasks within a firm. You are also encouraged to minor in one or two specific business disciplines. The program is ideal for you if you want to pursue a career in general management, open a business or work for a small or medium-sized corporation.

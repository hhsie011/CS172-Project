extraordinary apartment community in the heart of the Aggie experience. Our fully furnished apartment community is on west campus near the Agriculture and Life Sciences Buildings and White Creek. White Creek Apartments provides full-time undergraduate students with the opportunity to live in a fully furnished apartment. Eligibility & Assignments Texas A&M University students who are incoming freshmen classification and above. Priority is given to undergraduates when making assignments. Contracts Residents can select either a 9 1/2 month or 12 month contract. Summer only contracts are also available. The dates of the academic year contract are approximately August 15 to May 30. Exact dates are announced each year approximately 6 months prior to occupancy and are determined by the TAMU Academic Calendar. Rental Terms Rent is billed to student accounts each semester and includes all utilities. All apartments are single gender. All bedrooms are single occupancy and individually leased. Rental Styles Rent by bedroom – A student rents an individual bedroom. Roommates have individual liability for rent. Student can request a roommate or University Apartments will assign a roommate to the additional bedroom(s); must be same gender. Bedroom capacity is 1 student per bedroom. Gallery See your future apartment home in photos, including our common areas, exterior views, and coveted amenities. If you prefer, come see it for yourself and join us for a tour.

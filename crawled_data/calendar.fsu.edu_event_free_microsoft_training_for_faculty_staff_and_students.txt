Every Tuesday at either 11:00 AM or 3:00 PM ET, there will be a live online training session taught by Microsoft professionals which will be available for anyone to attend via Microsoft Teams. These live courses will cover a multitude of topics such as Microsoft Teams, Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint, SharePoint, OneNote, Microsoft Accessibility and Teaching with Minecraft, just to name a few. Excitedly, there are 40 different programs being offered, so no matter what program you are hoping to learn, there will be something for everyone. For schedule and Teams links to Microsoft Trainings, visit: its.fsu.edu/training/microsoft-trainings.

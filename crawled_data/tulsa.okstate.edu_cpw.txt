Mission The Center for Poets and Writers at OSU-Tulsa promotes the craft of writing and cultivates a thriving, diverse literary arts community. Vision The Center enriches Oklahoma through the art of writing and the power of literature. Our Core Goals Education: Provide opportunities for experienced and beginning writers to advance their craft and develop professionally Collaboration: Support and partner with organizations in the arts and humanities to maximize scale and impact of programs Community Engagement: In a spirit of reciprocity, engage local, national, and global individuals and communities to enhance the quality of life in Oklahoma #OKWrites Use our hashtag on Twitter, Instagram and Facebook to support the Center for Poets and Writers and its activities. Contact Us Contact the Center for Poets and Writers at OSU-Tulsa at cpwtulsa@okstate.edu, or visit our Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

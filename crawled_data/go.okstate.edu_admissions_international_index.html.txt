We want you to be part of the vibrant international undergraduate student population at OSU. Who counts as an international applicant? A student seeking a bachelor’s degree who is a citizen of any country other than the U.S., and who is not in the U.S. as an immigrant should apply as an international student. International students can enter OSU as a freshman or a transfer student. Apply Oklahoma State University What is it like to be an OSU student? OSU has a close-knit global community, representing more than 100 countries. The moment you arrive on campus, you will feel right at home.

Please know that college advisors and admissions staff are here to support you as you explore different options at Cornell. You can reach out to the admissions office in your target college, as well as your current (home college) staff advisor or student services office, to speak about different options available to you. Before you apply, please review the requirements and processes for the college you are interested in internally transferring to. Fall 2022 Internal Transfer Information The deadline for Internal Transfer for the Fall 2022 semester was Sunday, May 1, 2022. If you have questions about IT, please contact the individual college.

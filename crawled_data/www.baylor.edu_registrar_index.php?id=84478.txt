VETS Center To view the Veteran Educational and Transition Services available through the VETS Center Baylor VETS Center Steps to follow to use Chapter 31 benefits: Meet with a VA Counselor to apply for Chapter 31 benefits or apply online at https://www.va.gov/careers-employment/vocational-rehabilitation/how-to-apply/. Submit a valid VA form 28-1905 and the Request for Certification Form to the VA Benefits Office. The VA Form 28-1905 may be valid for more than one semester, but the Request for Certification Form needs to be submitted every semester you use Chapter 31. Provide other documents to the VA Benefits Office: - Copies of all DD-214 forms may be requested at http://www.archives.gov/veterans/military-service-records - Military Transcripts For more information found on the VA website click the following link: http://www.vba.va.gov/bln/vre/index.htm To view Baylor University policy regarding Ch. 31 Educational Benefits and Institutional Financial Aid, please click here. To view information about obtaining your Baylor student account credit balance refund (if applicable) click here.

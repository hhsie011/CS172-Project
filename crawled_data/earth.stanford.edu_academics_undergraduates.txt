Undergraduates may take just one course, register for a field trip, or go on to major. Many of the most pressing problems facing humanity today involve Earth and its resources. At Stanford Earth, you can learn about processes and issues surrounding energy, freshwater and food, the impacts of climate change and natural hazards, the distribution of natural resources, and the history of our planet. You will also gain valuable data analytic skills along the way. Meet Stanford EarthM Our students make up a welcoming, diverse, and collaborative community.

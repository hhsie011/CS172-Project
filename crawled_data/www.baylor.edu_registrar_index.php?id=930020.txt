Skip to main content Skip to main navigation Office of the Registrar Menu Baylor>Registrar>Registering for Classes>Summer/Fall 2022 BearWeb Early Registration Schedule Summer/Fall 2022 BearWeb Early Registration Registration activities are scheduled to begin Wednesday, April 6th. The Office of the Registrar must receive transcripts for any transfer coursework by Wednesday, February 23rd, for those credit hours to be included in the completed credit hour totals for assigning times for Early Registration. Beyond the order listed below, students are assigned registration times based on the total of their earned (completed) and in-progress (currently enrolled) hours. Once a student reaches their registration time, BearWeb access will remain open 24/7 through Midnight on May 15th (Minimester), June 10th (Full Summer), June 7th (Summer I) and July 13th (Summer II) for Summer 2022. For Fall 2022, BearWeb access to add/register will remain open through midnight on August 26th. Drop deadlines in BearWeb may be viewed on the University Calendar. Individual assigned dates/times will be viewable in BearWeb beginning Wednesday, March 16th.

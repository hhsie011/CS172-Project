As one of the more than 300,000 alumni across the world, you make UC proud. This Alumni page contains tips and resources to help with your specific alumni needs. One Stop Catalyst Self-Help Guide Need help navigating Catalyst? Use this One Stop Catalyst Self-Help Guide for instructions on how to do your UC related business online. Electronic/Paper Transcripts UC partners with Parchment for ordering and processing all official transcripts. Notifications are sent to your email directly from Parchment indicating the progress of your transcript request. Make sure to check your spam/junk email folders. Unofficial Transcript Recent alumni should be able to obtain an unofficial transcript via the My Academics tile in Catalyst. Verify Enrollment/Degree Free, official degree enrollment and verification documents may be printed directly from Catalyst. If you do not have access to Catalyst, please email the Registrar directly at reginfo@uc.edu. Please note: enrollment certifications for each term are available on the first day of classes for that term. Graduation and Diploma Information For more information on graduation and diplomas, please review these Graduation and Diploma Frequently Asked Questions. Loan Exit Counseling Whenever a student graduates or discontinues their education for any period other than summer term, federal student loan borrowers are required to complete loan exit counseling. You should stay knowledgeable about loans that you have borrowed and make sure to manage your loans effectively. Alumni Association Connect with other Bearcats, learn about alumni events as well as resources available to alumni, including career services, through the UC Alumni Association.

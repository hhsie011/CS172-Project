It offers a suite of features including free on-campus (5‑digit) dialing as well as local, long distance, and international calling. PennNet Phone will be gradually replaced by PennFlex Phone during FY21 and FY22. Please note: Clients of the PennNet Phone service are being migrated to the more modern and robust PennFlex Phone service. The PennNet Phone service is slated for retirement on June 30th, 2023. If your organization has not yet transitioned to PennFlex Phone, please contact ISC Client Care to learn more about this process. Benefits A variety of features including integrated voicemail, Call Forward from anywhere, Anonymous Call Rejection, and unified communications services (voicemail conversion to email) Flexibility in relocating phones Self-provision of features via web interface Ongoing maintenance and support Who Can Benefit from This Service University faculty and staff Additional Information and Resources PennNet Phone Voice Services (Overview, Ordering, Dialing, User Guides, Voice Mail FAQ etc.) Self Service Tool for Phone Settings Sign up to Join the lsp-voip Special Interests List Next Generation Unified Communications Project PennFlex Phone Service and Migration Resources PennFlex eFax Service Cellular Services Share: Facebook Twitter Print Get Service Questions & Support ISC Client Care PennNet Phone SLA Rates Receives funding through PTIC Related Services PennFlex Phone Services A-Z Computing Policies Tech Jobs @ Penn System Status © 2022 THE UNIVERSITY OF PENNSYLVANIA — 3401 Walnut Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104 — Report accessibility issues and get help — For ISC Staff

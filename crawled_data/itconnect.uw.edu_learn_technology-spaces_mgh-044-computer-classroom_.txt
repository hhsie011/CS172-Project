Mary Gates Hall computer classrooms 030 and 044 feature computers available for official UW courses and special events. Special Events: Submit a Special Event Reservation Inquiry Capacity: 45 Seating: Movable seats at 2’x5′ tables Facilities/Fixtures: Dual screen, whiteboards Wheelchair Accessible: Yes Electrical Outlets: Grounded outlets available along the perimeter walls Wi-Fi: Yes (UW NetID required) Media Equipment: Installed equipment includes podium with fixed gooseneck microphone, podium desktop computer, and video/data projector.

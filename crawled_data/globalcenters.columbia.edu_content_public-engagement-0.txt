Public Engagement We value being actively involved in finding solutions for the world’s most pressing issues. At the Center, we regularly host conferences, lectures, and forums involving a diverse panel of experts. We bring together academics from local institutions and Columbia University to promote a collaborative and mutually beneficial relationship. Public programs at the Tunis Center are essential in developing strong bonds and partnerships between the University and the local community.

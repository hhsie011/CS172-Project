Sample postcards We offer special postcard pricing for quantities up to 2500 for full color postcards. Sizes range from standard 4" x 6" to jumbo 6" x 9". Can be printed on 110# glossy or uncoated stock. Custom-sized postcards on other paper stocks are also available. With complete printing solutions offered by Print Services, we can assist in all aspects of the postcard production process including design, print, and mail distribution. Helpful Tip: When creating a postcard to be sent through U.S. mail, be sure to leave room for the postage and recipient address. Remember too that campus mail is an option for reaching faculty and staff right at their desks.

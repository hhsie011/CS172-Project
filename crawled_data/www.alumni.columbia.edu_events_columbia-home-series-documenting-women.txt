They will join Columbia at Home to discuss their films, including the two most recent: Julia, examining the life of cook Julia Child, and My Name is Pauli Murray, profiling activist Pauli Murray. West and Cohen will also share insights on the making of RBG, their 2018 Oscar-nominated film about Associate Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg. The session will be moderated by Alece Oxendine '11SOA, director, Industry & Festival Outreach, Columbia University School of the Arts. In advance of the event, viewers are encouraged to view the films: Julia and My Name is Pauli Murray are available to stream on Amazon Prime Video. This event is co-sponsored by CAA Arts Access and the She Opened the Door Initiative RBG is available to stream on Netflix.

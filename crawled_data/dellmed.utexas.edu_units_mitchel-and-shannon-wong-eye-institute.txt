The institute opens its UT Health Austin clinic in 2020. Hear more from the Wongs » Focus Areas No. 1: Delivering Outstanding Ophthalmology Training The institute is preparing to offer a residency program and clinical fellowship that align with Dell Med’s mission to train the next generation of physician leaders. Residents will participate in rotations with community ophthalmologists, caring for patients in the private practice setting. These experiences will add unique value to the institute’s educational program. No. 2: Providing the Best Clinical Care As part of Dell Med’s work to deliver value-based, patient-centered care, the team is developing an ophthalmology service that will provide the best eye care to patients throughout the Austin community. No. 3: Improving & Preserving Vision Through Research By forging collaborations with University of Texas at Austin scientists and local industry partners, the institute seeks to spearhead research initiatives targeting the preservation and improvement of vision.

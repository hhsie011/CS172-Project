Career Day Post a Job Recruit With Us Recruiting Policy Facts & Figures Career Day Post a Job Recruit With Us Recruiting Policy Facts & Figures Recruit With Us Career Services at the Mailman School is here to help your organization find high-quality job or internship candidates. To help you identify talent that meets your hiring objectives, please explore: Mailman CareerLink On-campus recruiting (e.g., information sessions and interviewing) Fall and Spring Career Day events Fall and Spring industry career panels Creating a structured practicum opportunity You can also search for prospective employees in our online resume books, which are available on a complimentary basis to prospective employers through Mailman CareerLink. To access resume books of students, please log in to CareerLink. Then click "Resume Books" on the left. You can search by resume keywords by typing into the "Resume Text Search" box. You can also search by academic department by searching under "Major." To create a resume book, click the small box at the top of the search results to select all results. Then click "Generate Book" under batch options. The services, resources, and facilities of the Career Services are available, free of charge, to employers who adhere to policies against discrimination based on race, color, national origin, religion, age, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected groups. We ask all employers to follow our Employer Policy, detailed here. Additionally, we support the NACE Principles for Professional Conduct for career services and employment professionals. Contact Us For detailed information on the many options available to assist you in recruiting Mailman School of Public Health students and alumni, please email msphocs [at] cumc.columbia.edu (Career Services).

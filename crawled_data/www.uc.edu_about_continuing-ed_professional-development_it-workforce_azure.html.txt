Free voucher for certificate exam An Azure Data Engineer also helps ensure that data pipelines and data stores are high-performing, efficient, organized, and reliable, given a specific set of business requirements and constraints. This professional deals with unanticipated issues swiftly and minimizes data loss. An Azure Data Engineer also designs, implements, monitors, and optimizes data platforms to meet the data pipeline needs. This course will prepare you for the DP-203 exam (which released June 30, 2021). PREREQUISITES There is no specific prerequisite exam for candidates to become eligible for the DP-203 exam. It is advised to have successfully passed the Microsoft Azure Data Fundamentals (DP-900) Certification Exam for a better understanding of the modules. Experience in data processing languages such as SQL or Python would be preferred. Tuition Funding Through Your Employer right arrow down arrow UC is an approved provider of tech training eligible for the Ohio TechCred. Employers who are approved for this funding will be reimbursed for their employees training. Prospective Students: Ask your employer to apply for the Ohio TechCred. Once they are approved, you may enroll and your employer will pay for your tuition. Once you complete the program and earn your certificate, your employer will be reimbursed. TechCred.Ohio.gov.

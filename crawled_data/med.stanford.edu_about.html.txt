Our close proximity to the resources of the university — including the Schools of Business, Law, Humanities and Sciences, and Engineering, our seamless relationship with our affiliated adult and children’s hospitals, and our ongoing associations with the entrepreneurial endeavors of Silicon Valley, make us uniquely positioned to accelerate the pace at which new knowledge is translated into tangible health benefits. Stanford Medicine — made up of Stanford Children’s Health, Stanford Health Care, and Stanford School of Medicine — is bringing its collective strength to the fight against COVID-19.

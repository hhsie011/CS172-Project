University Find your academic home. No matter where you’re heading—boardroom or classroom, lab, art studio, or TV studio—our six academic colleges are the gateway to everything you’ve imagined for your future (and then some). And no matter which of our 65+ majors you choose, they’re each backed by the broad underpinnings of a liberal arts education. So at the same time you prepare for a career, you’re also building skills for life. COPHS gives you a head start on your career as a pharmacist, physician assistant, or healthcare business manager. Two internships, ongoing career mentoring, and relentless exposure to real-world scenarios add up to our 99 percent placement rate.

Climbing Wall: 840-square-foot climbing wall located in Outdoor Adventures at Lakeside Village Gear Rental and Sales: your hub for learning about and getting the gear you need for the adventures you want, located in Outdoor Adventures at Lakeside Village Spruance Outdoor Gym: get fit while enjoying the outdoors Sand Volleyball Courts: two sand volleyball courts located at Outdoor Adventures Outdoor Adventures General Policies apply to all spaces listed above and are subject to change. Regarding Herbert Wellness Center Membership Outdoor Adventures is a partnership between the Department of Housing and Residential Life and the Department of Wellness and Recreation. Outdoor Adventures is managed by DWR but funded by HRL. Herbert Wellness Center membership does not include Outdoor Adventures because the fees paid for membership do not contribute to Outdoor Adventures funding. Therefore, Outdoor Adventures and Herbert Wellness Center memberships are separate fees.

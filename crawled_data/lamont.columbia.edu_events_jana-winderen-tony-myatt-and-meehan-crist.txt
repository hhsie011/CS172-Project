Please register on the Columbia School of the Arts website here: https://arts.columbia.edu/events/jana-winderen-tony-myatt-and-meehan-crist Jana Winderen and Tony Myatt discuss The Art of Listening: Under Water, their immersive, site-specific, 360° spatial audio installation on display in The Lenfest Center for the Arts. Followed by a conversation between Winderen, Myatt, and Meehan Crist '08 (Barnard '00), Writer in Residence, Biological Sciences. Introduction and welcome by Carol Becker, Dean of Columbia University School of the Arts; and by Denis Pernet, Curator, Audemars Piguet Contemporary. If you would like to experience The Art of Listening: Under Water, please register here. Registration will be open through Sunday, February 13. Co-presented by Arts Administration, Teachers College; Arts Initiative; Audemars Piguet Contemporary; Biological Sciences; Center for Science and Society; Columbia Narrative Medicine; Columbia Religious Life; Columbia Water Center; Computer Music Center; and Lamont Doherty Earth Observatory.

Construction and Evolution of Union Theological Seminary Campus Home History and Design Foundation Cornerstone Administration Building Dormitory Library Professor's Suites Conclusion This digital exhibit features images from a small collection of photographs documenting the construction of Union Theological Seminary located in the Morningside Heights neighborhood of New York City. The third location for the Seminary, the buildings were constructed from 1908-1910. The collection is housed at the Burke Library at Union Theological Seminary, Columbia University in the City of New York. Exhibit Curator Brigette C.

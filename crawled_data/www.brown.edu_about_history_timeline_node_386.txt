Robinson oversaw a number of building restorations, as well as the construction of Robinson, Slater and Sayles Halls. He established professorships in scientific fields ranging from astronomy to zoology and instituted graduate study at the University, overseeing the awarding of the first A.M.s and Ph.D.s.

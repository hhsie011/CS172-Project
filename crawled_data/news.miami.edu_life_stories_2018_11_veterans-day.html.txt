Lievonen, Michael Montero, and Diego Meza-Valdes Saluting the heroes among us By Jennifer Palma Sanchez The 'Canes family unites to recognize and pay tribute to the faculty, staff, and students who have served. While stories of their sacrifices may not be shared during team meetings or over lunch, the University of Miami is home to students, faculty, and staff who have served, and continue to serve, in the U.S. military. With over 350 self-identified veteran employees across every campus, and talented students ranging from freshmen to Ph.Dâ€™s, veterans are a part of the 'Canes family in varied roles and areas of study. Today, and each day, we salute the heroes among us and proudly share our gratitude for their continued service.

Below is a map indicating the parking area for our student's family and guests. WELCOME TO TEXAS TECH UNIVERSITY! Accessible Parking and Seating Accessible parking is located on the west side of the United Supermarkets Arena entrance for those with a handicapped parking pass. Guests needing assistance to the Arena may have their families drop them off at the southwest entrance, and their families should proceed to the Family/Guest parking area. Accessible seating is available on the concourse level of the Arena. Each guest is allowed one person to sit with them in the accessible area; other members of the family should be seated in the audience seating. Faculty/Staff Parking Texas Tech University Faculty/Staff parking for commencement is located in the Student Rec. parking lot. Faculty and staff may enter this parking area if they have a reserved or area reserved pass on their vehicle. See map above for parking area.

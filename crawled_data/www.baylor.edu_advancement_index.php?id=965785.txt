The purpose of University Advancement is to provide opportunities for the Baylor family to support and advance Baylor’s mission – to connect the generosity of Baylor’s alumni, parents and friends with areas of need and growth. Since 1845, the gifts of grateful alumni, proud parents and like-minded friends allowed Baylor to become the largest Baptist university in the world. Thank you for your interest in being part of our future.

Skip to main content Diversity Undergraduate Study Graduate Study Faculty & Research Interdisciplinary Communities News About Security The Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science has a responsibility to protect from disclosure to unauthorized parties the personally identifiable information of its website users. Therefore, the Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science has adopted and implemented a website security policy to protect account information of its website users. Notice and Disclosures The Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science will not sell, trade, nor disclose the personally identifiable information of its website users to any unauthorized third parties. Data Quality and Access The Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science takes all steps possible to ensure that the data on the website is accurate. If something is found to be inaccurate, the Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science will make every effort to correct said information as quickly as possible. While using the Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science website, certain information such as your IP Address and time spent on pages may be collected. This non-personal information is collected in order to monitor any unauthorized use or access to the Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science site. Anyone caught attempting to harm, steal information from, or otherwise damage the Yale School of Engineering & Applied Science website will be prosecuted to the full extent of the law.

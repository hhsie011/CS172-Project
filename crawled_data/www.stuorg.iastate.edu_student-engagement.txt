Iowa State University Student Organizations Menu Toggle Search Toggle Search Sign In Sign In Home Student Engagement Organizations Help Student Engagement Resources and opportunities are available for officers and members of all recognized student and campus organizations can be found at www.studentengagement.iastate.edu/resources. The Student Organization Recognition Policy governs the process for student organizations to obtain official recognition with Iowa State University. Once a student organization is recognized they are eligible to utilize many campus resources such as meeting space, financial account and access to trademarks. Student Engagement also provides students with opportunities to get involved on campus in addition to student organizations. These include leadership development and community service opportunities, arts and entertainment offerings on campus and more. For more information check out the Student Engagement website or visit our office in 1560 (ESOS) Memorial Union.

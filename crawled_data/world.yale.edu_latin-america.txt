Learn More The Americas 3 Callout Boxes Council on Latin American & Iberian Studies This MacMillan Center council, known as CLAIS, seeks to enhance the understanding of Latin America, Spain and Portugal. The council draws on the strengths of Yale’s pioneering departments of History, Political Science, Anthropology, and Spanish and Portuguese, among others. Find out more Yale Law School Latin America Linkage Program Each summer the Law School sponsors a student exchange with several universities in Argentina, Brazil and Chile. Student participants focus on a wide range of areas including human rights, constitutional law, international law, and criminal law. Find out more School of Management and FGV EAESP M2M is a suite of dual-degree opportunities for early career students in which participants spend a year abroad and another year at Yale’s MMS in Global Business and Society. Partner schools include Fundação Getúlio Vargas’s São Paulo School of Business Administration.

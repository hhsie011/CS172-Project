WORK/LIFE / Connected & Caring Community Although Harvard can seem big, it’s a dynamic and engaging community that provides many opportunities to pursue personal, social and professional interests outside your daily work. Harvard is also a caring community, connected to the larger world around us, with ways to help you donate your time, meet people with shared interests and concerns, or make contributions to causes that are important to you. Whether you want to volunteer to tutor employees learning English as a Second Language, connect with others who like to tango, or network with other IT professionals, chances are, you can find what you’re looking for right here.

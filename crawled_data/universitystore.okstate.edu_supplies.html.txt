We are the whole package. The University Store offers great prices on a huge variety of school and office supplies, including our wide selection of pens, notebooks, calculators, backpacks, art supplies and much, much more. Can't find what you are looking for? Ask one of our friendly, knowledgeable staff members and we can order it for you. In many cases we can have what you need delivered the very next day. You can also browse our online supplies at shopokstate.com. Not only does the University Store make sure it has everything that students need to be successful in the classroom, we also focus on getting faculty and staff members the tools they need to help students be successful. We know there are a lot of options when it comes to places to buy paper, folders, staples and everything else it takes to make an office run. But we also know what faculty and staff need to be able do their jobs better than anyone else in town and are dedicated to servicing the university community above all else. Here are the University Store's top three reasons why faculty and staff members should shop on campus: SAVE TIME. The University Store offers free on-campus delivery. Purchases are often delivered the next day saving unnecessary, unscheduled trips off campus. Let us do the work for you! SAVE ENERGY. When you shop at the University Store you are able to easily charge to your campus vendor account and avoid the pesky paperwork associated with typical purchase card purchases. SAVE MONEY. Ordering in bulk? We do volume discounts for large quantity orders. The University Store is even often times able to match competitor’s pricing. Call us at (405) 744-5366 for a quote.

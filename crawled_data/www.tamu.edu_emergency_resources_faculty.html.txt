To be an effective part of any emergency situation solution means knowing where, when, and how the university will respond when an emergency occurs. This is where we can help. We can facilitate the creating of emergency plans and continuing operation plans for facilities and for departments that compliment the university-wide plans, already in use. For more information, please contact the Office of Safety & Security. Click here for further information on Emergency Procedures.

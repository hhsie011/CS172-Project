School Mirror Awards #SUGrad22 SUccess Week Home Attend National Orange Day National Orange Day March 24, the date of the University's founding in 1870, is always a special day for Syracuse University students, alumni, faculty and staff. National Orange Day is the highlight of Forever Orange Week, a week of on-campus events for students designed to underscore the power of the Orange network and emphasize the importance of staying connected. For alumni, Forever Orange Week is features opportunities to give back and mentor students and participate in community service projects, both on campus and in cities across the country. Check back as we get closer to March 24, 2023 to see how the alumni club in your area will be supporting the community and demonstrating what it means to be Orange.

California State University, San Bernardino is located on 430 acres at the foothills of the San Bernardino Mountains. CSUSB is one of the 23 amazing CSU campuses across the state featuring, 5 academic colleges that award 58 undergraduate degrees, and 46 graduate degrees. Our current faculty to student ratio is 27.5:1 with ample opportunity to connect with and interact with outstanding faculty in the major of your choice. With 55% of Fall 2020 undergraduates being First-Generation college students, we are here to answer any questions about the admission process and provide the support needed for your successful transition to CSUSB. COVID-19 Update The Office of Admissions has returned to in-person services under the safety guidelines established by the Center for Disease Control (CDC) to combat COVID-19. Our staff are eager to assist new and returning students, their families, and our community. Circumstances may change rapidly. For the latest information, resources and requirements related to repopulation of both the CSUSB and PDC campuses, including face covering, vaccination requirements, and instruction methods, please visit Return of the Pack. Please provide your Coyote ID number in your email. We thank you for your continued understanding and patience and we look forward to hearing from you.

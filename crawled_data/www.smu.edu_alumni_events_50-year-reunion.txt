Each May a new class of Golden Mustangs is welcomed during Commencement. The once-in-a-lifetime tradition is called Golden Reunion Weekend. Designed to be an epic celebration with memorable experience, it includes special campus tours, participation in the commencement ceremony and the Golden Mustangs Pinning Ceremony.

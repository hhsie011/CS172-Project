Wondering where to start in your research journey? Join us for our Survival Skills workshop! By the end of this workshop, students will be able to: Utilize the wide variety of resources available for a literature review within the Columbia system Organize, prioritize, and plan ahead for their upcoming thesis/capstone/dissertation efficiently Recognize the benefits of using citation management software Use Informationist services appropriately You will receive a reminder email with a Zoom link 24 hours before the workshop. Please check your Spam folder if you do not see the email in your main inbox. If for some reason you do not receive this email or are missing the link, please contact us via hs-library@columbia.edu.

Permits Can Be Returned At Any Time A valid UH parking permit can be returned to Parking and Transportation Services at any time for a prorated refund. There are two ways the permit can be returned: Set an appointment or join a virtual line to bring it in person to the Parking and Transportation Services customer service location in University Lofts.

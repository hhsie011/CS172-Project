We've improved the home screen of the BrownU app so you have faster access to the information that matters. Along with shortcuts to app features, you can now view: Dining location bookmarks Laundry room bookmarks (students) Card balances like BearBucks (students) Today@Brown announcement summary To bookmark a dining or laundry location, look for the bookmark icon on the top right after selecting a location. To customize what displays on your home screen, select Settings from the app menu. What would you like to see in the BrownU app? We're always adding features to our app, and we want to hear from you.

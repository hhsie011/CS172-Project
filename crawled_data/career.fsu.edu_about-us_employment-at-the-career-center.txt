at The Career Center Full-time Positions Position vacancies for full-time opportunities at The Career Center are posted through the FSU Human Resources. CLICK HERE to learn more about available job opportunities at Florida State University. Career Advisor Graduate Assistants (Applications for 2022-2023 have closed.) Career Advisor Graduate Assistants provide career advising to students and community individuals, assist clients in locating career and educational planning information, interpret print and computer career assessment tools, develop skills in planning and conducting outreach presentations on career development topics, and create materials and resources related to career planning and job search topics. Assistantships are only offered to individuals who have completed the career advisor training program on a volunteer basis (usually done in late July and early August, but may have the option of completing this at other times during the year). Career advisors are required to attend a group supervision session once per week. Interested applicants should complete and submit a career advisor application, which includes a resume and letter of intent. Applications are typically accepted August 1st - April 15th. Additionally, there are Career Counseling scholarships and fellowships available to qualified students. Classification: Master's or Doctoral Majors Preferred: Career Counseling MS/EdS program, Combine Counseling Psychology and School Psychology PhD program or a related program within the Department of Educational Psychology & Learning Systems. Hours per week: 10 Length of appointment: semester-by-semester basis. For more information about career advisor graduate assistantships, contact: V. Casey Dozier, Ph.D., NCC - Program Director, Career Advising, and Counseling 1212 Dunlap Success Center, 100 S. Woodward Avenue, Tallahassee, FL 32306-4162 Office: (850) 644-8315 E-mail: casey.dozier@fsu.edu Graduate Assistant (GA) Positions (Applications for 2022-2023 have closed.) The Career Center offers a number of graduate assistantships, each one associated with various aspects of The Career Center's range of services and programs. Each graduate assistant (GA) will have "unit" specific responsibilities, but are also expected to help with a variety of Career Center activities including career fair coverage, developing resource materials, outreach programs, career advising, teaching the Career Planning Class (SDS: 3340) and more. The Career Center offers several GA Positions including: Administration Team Graduate Assistant (Position filled through Spring 2023) Employer Relations Graduate Assistant (Position filled through Spring 2024) Experiential Learning Graduate Assistant (Position filled through Spring 2024) To inquire about open graduate assistantships, contact Leslie Mille, Associate Director. Your inquiry will be forwarded to the hiring supervisor(s) with anticipated open positions. Please include your resume. Work Study Positions The Federal Work-Study is a need-based program that must be applied for by completion of the Free Application for Federal Student Aid. Contact the FSU Office of Financial Aid for more information. Student Assistant Opportunities If you are looking for part-time employment at FSU, the Career Center can offer you a great work environment, flexible scheduling and work-related experience. These opportunities as posted periodically in NoleNetwork.

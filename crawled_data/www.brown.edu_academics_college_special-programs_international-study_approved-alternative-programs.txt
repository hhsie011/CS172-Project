Programs with APPLY NOW status in ViaTRM are offered to Brown students for Fall 2022/Full Year 2022-23. Please contact the OIP at [email protected] with questions. Brown has approved over 70 study abroad programs run by other institutions, which Brown faculty have vetted for academic quality and approved for credit transfer. Brown students attend these programs regularly, and the OIP reviews them frequently to ensure compatibility with Brown's curriculum. Students are encouraged to search and review details on each of the programs and apply through our application portal.

Applications may be downloaded here (Word document). To be awarded departmental honors at graduation with the designation "With Honors in Communication," a student must fulfill the following requirements: (1) maintain a 3.5 GPA in communication coursework (2) maintain a 3.5 GPA overall (3) complete all Department of Communication major requirements (4) complete an independent thesis or an independent film/video project sponsored by a Department of Communication faculty member consisting of six hours over two semesters: three hours in research preparation (COMM 3330, 4381, or 4891), and three hours in completing the thesis project (COMM 4999), and (5) successful defense of the thesis. An honors seminar (or equivalent) will be offered at least once per academic year. Any honors student can have a communication course designated as honors with permission of the instructor and the honors program director. For more information on the honors program contact Ms. Lori Stallings.

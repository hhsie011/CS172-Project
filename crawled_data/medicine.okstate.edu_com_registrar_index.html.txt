We can assist students and alumni with the following: Medical student record requests including transcripts, diplomas, letters of good standing and more Graduate student record requests including transcripts and certification Information regarding in-state residency Information about military veteran benefits for students We strive to be helpful while also ensuring that we operate in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act of 1974 (FERPA), protecting individual rights and privacy.

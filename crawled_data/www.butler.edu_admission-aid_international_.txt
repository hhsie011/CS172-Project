International Graduate & Professional Admitted Non-Degree Financial Aid & Scholarships Tuition & Costs Come far. Go farther. You want to live in a great city. You want to surround yourself with doers who are as driven as you. You want hands-on experience—on the job, in the lab, behind the stage, wherever. You want to be in small classes with faculty who are devoted to teaching, in a place that values diversity of all kinds. And where you know you’ll feel at home. (At Butler, you’ll be with students from 30 countries!) You want to come to Butler, the #1-ranked Midwest university in the US. And we want to see you here. You can get to Indianapolis from just about anywhere—we’re just a half-hour from Indianapolis International Airport—but you can see plenty with a virtual tour or visit, too. Virtual Tour Schedule a Virtual Visit How to Apply International applicants at Butler follow our standard application processes for First-Year, Transfer, or Graduate & Professional admission, with a few additional requirements. And that starts even before you arrive. Have questions? You’ll work with the same admission counselor through the whole process. Meet Your Admission Counselor Scholarships & Grants While international, undocumented, and DACA students are not eligible for federal financial aid via the FAFSA, they can be considered for Butler gift aid—scholarships and grants. International Scholarships International Student Services The Office of International Student Services serves all international students studying at Butler, offering support navigating immigration regulations and help getting acclimated to campus and to Indianapolis. International Student Services Diversity Programs & Services To make Butler a place where people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences are included, we provide a variety of resources and opportunities to get you connected and serve your needs. Diversity at Butler Everything’s Butler in Indy Your city, your classroom We’d call Indianapolis a hidden gem, but way too many people have noticed us to say that. Home to big-time sports, world-class culture, and rich professional opportunities, the city—at 850,000 people, the 17th largest in the U.S.—is an integral part of your experience.

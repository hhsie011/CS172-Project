This five year fellowship recognizes and supports outstanding graduate students in STEM disciplines. During the fellowship, Esteban will investigate the causal spillover effect of policy interventions using synthetic control methods. He plans to develop a novel Bayesian structural time-series method for multiple synthetic controls and apply the proposed method to the marijuana legalization study.

We are ready to assist you, your spouses, or your dependents in taking advantage of your educational benefits while at the University of Memphis (UofM). Students utilizing their GI Bill® benefits are required each semester to complete the VA Certification & Deferment request. (GI Bill® is a registered trademark of the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). More information about education benefits offered by VA is available at the official U.S. government Web site at https://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill.)

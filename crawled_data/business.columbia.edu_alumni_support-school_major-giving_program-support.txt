International Giving Events News Columbia Business School’s outstanding centers, programs, and institutes facilitate groundbreaking research, dialogue with renowned practitioners, and innovative curricula that fulfill the School’s mission to provide an exceptional education that reflects the needs of today’s business world. From real estate, value investing, and entrepreneurship to social enterprise, healthcare, media, and more, the centers, programs, and institutes provide a bridge between the School and key areas of industry. Support for these extraordinary initiatives enhances the student experience and enables the School to maintain its position at the very center of business.

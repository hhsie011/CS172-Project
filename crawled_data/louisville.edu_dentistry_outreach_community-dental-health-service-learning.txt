UofL Dental Associates Outreach Community Outreach Information Community Dental Health Rotation Sites International Service Learning Contact Giving Give Now Why Giving Matters Dean's Circle Meet Your Development Representatives Home/ Outreach/ Community Dental Health/Service Learning Navigation Outreach Community Dental Health/Service Learning Student Community Enrichment Rotation Sites International Service Learning Contact Community Dental Health/Service Learning Service to the community is an essential component built into the Dental and Dental Hygiene curricula at the University of Louisville School of Dentistry (ULSD). The students are provided with community- based experiences to enhance awareness of diverse, underrepresented and underserved populations outside the university setting. The Director of Community Dental Health pre-approves student community based experiences for participation, support and course credit. This service includes three components. Community Service - Approved Community Engagement, Volunteerism, etc. for any "worthy" cause. Students are encouraged to develop and execute their own projects. Service Learning - Approved Clinical Enrichment or Non-clinical community service with a dental career focus. Students are encouraged to develop and execute non-clinical events. Service Learning Community-Based Externships – Formal clinical enrichments and career focused extramural proctorships.

Faculty who wish to partake in the procession and view the University Ceremony are asked to report to Brown Street, near Fones Alley, no later than 9:15 a.m. on Sunday. Please approach the lineup from Angell St. Faculty will line up behind the senior administration. Faculty will process through the Van Wickle Gates, down College and Benefit streets, and return to reserved seating on the College Green via Waterman Street to view the College and University ceremonies. Faculty who wish to process with the Graduate School and attend the doctoral or master’s ceremonies are asked to report to Waterman Street in front of Page-Robinson Hall no later than 9:15 a.m. WHAT TIME DOES COMMENCEMENT START? The procession steps off promptly at 9:30 a.m. Seniors must line up on Waterman St. no later than 9:15 a.m. Graduate students should line up on under the tent on Simmons Quadrangle by 9:15 a.m. Seniors, medical students, and graduate students process through the Van Wickle Gates and split off into their separate ceremony locations. Seniors will proceed down the hill to the First Baptist Church in America. WHERE AND WHAT TIME DOES THE GRADUATE SCHOOL CEREMONY BEGIN? After the doctoral and master’s students pass through the Van Wickle Gates, master’s students will then head toward Meehan Auditorium for their ceremony, which will begin around 10:30 a.m. Doctoral students will head towards Pembroke Field for their ceremony at around 10:30 a.m. WHERE AND WHAT TIME DOES THE UNIVERSITY CEREMONY BEGIN? The University Ceremony begins around 1:15 p.m. on the Main Green. The honorary and symbolic degrees are awarded here. WHERE AND WHAT TIME DOES THE DEPARTMENT OF PHYSICS DIPLOMA CEREMONY BEGIN? The Department of Physics Undergraduate Commencement Ceremony begins at 2:30 p.m. in Barus & Holley 166.

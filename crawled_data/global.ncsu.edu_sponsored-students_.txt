Students Your Global University Work for Us - Student Employment Sponsored Students The Sponsored Students team serves as the single point of contact at NC State for all questions from sponsor representatives ranging from admissions to graduation on topics including student billing, academic progress, and much more. The Sponsored Students supports the needs of nearly 200 students representing 40 countries, helping them thrive on campus and in the wider community. Our team provides one-on-one advising support to students and leads workshops on topics such as resume writing, academic success, and interview skills. Connect with us to learn how NC State can support your students and how we can customize our support for your students. NC State includes a $375 per semester fee on student invoices sent to the sponsoring organization. This fee is included for every semester the student is enrolled at NC State. For students choosing to enroll in summer sessions, this fee will only be assessed once.

Research Guides Our Staff Find your Subject Librarian The McKinney Engineering Library supports engineering education from undergraduate through doctoral, and engineering research at all levels. The Engineering Library’s collection focus is on academic materials, including those from the major engineering societies and from other scholarly publishers.

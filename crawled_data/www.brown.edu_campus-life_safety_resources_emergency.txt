To avoid delays in service, Brown strongly recommends using the Brown Emergency Services number instead of 911 when on or near campus (including Medical School, South Main, and the Jewelry District). Brown Emergency operator will dispatch the Police, Fire or EMS personnel closest to you and will be able to route them to your location on campus faster. When calling Emergency Services, please be ready to: Provide the location of the emergency (or describe the location e.g. nearby landmarks, street signs, building numbers) Describe the emergency, including type of emergency, people involved, and injuries or life threats Stay on the line with operator for further instructions until Police, Fire or EMS personnel arrives For non-emergencies you may call the Routine Public Safety Response number: 401-863-3322 Availability Available 24x7 Available to all campus No registration required Resources A to Z Index Career Opportunities Make a Gift People Directory Related Departments Department of Public Safety Office of Student Life Counseling and Psychological Services Transportation Office Institutional Diversity Health Services Environmental Health and Safety Stay Safe About Safety at Brown Brown Guardian onCall Shuttle Safewalk Personal Safety Alarm (PAL) Brown Ready Health Education Brown University Providence, Rhode Island 02912, USA Phone: 401-863-1000 Maps & Directions / Contact Us / Accessibility Â© 2022 Brown University Connect: [email protected] Facebook Twitter YouTube Instagram iTunes U Google+ LinkedIn RSS Futurity

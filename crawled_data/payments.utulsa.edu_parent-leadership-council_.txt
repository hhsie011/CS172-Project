Skip to main content University of Tulsa Logo Payments for The University of Tulsa Parent Leadership Council The Parent Leadership Council (PLC) offers parents of TU students the opportunity for meaningful engagement with university leadership during your student’s time on campus and beyond. PLC members represent a community of parents who are passionate about supporting the student experience at The University of Tulsa. Parents may join the PLC by dedicating an annual leadership gift of $2,500 or more to the Parent Fund. Your gift will help support many areas parents care about such as student success, academic programming or any other campus experience that enhances your student’s educational and co-curricular experiences. 800 S.

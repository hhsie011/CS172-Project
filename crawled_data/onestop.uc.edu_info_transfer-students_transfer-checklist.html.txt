Admitted Students Graduate Students Graduate Student Checklist Continuing Students UC Blue Ash Students UC Clermont Students Transition Students Transfer Student Checklist Complete all items on this checklist to ensure an easy transition into your college career at the University of Cincinnati. Send your official transcript(s) to the UC Undergraduate Admissions Office. Add University of Cincinnati to your 2021-22 FAFSA. Use the FAFSA code 003125. Give parent/guest access to your Catalyst account. Create your financial aid portal account, if requested. Check your To Do List in Catalyst. Submit requested documentation as soon as possible. Accept your financial aid in Catalyst using the Accept/Decline Financial Aid link in the My Finances tile. Cancel financial aid at any previous institutions to avoid delays at UC. Complete a Master Promissory Note (MPN) and Loan Entrance Counseling, if you borrow federal student loans. Review options for covering remaining or out-of-pocket costs by using the Estimated Remaining Costs Worksheet. Check your student bill in Catalyst regularly and review payment due dates and payment options. Enroll in direct deposit for your financial aid refunds in Catalyst via the My Finances tile. Waive your university health insurance, if you have other coverage. Submit your vaccination information. Review your residency status via the My Profile tile in Catalyst and submit a residency application if necessary. File the 2022-23 FAFSA as early as October 1, 2021. Set yourself up for success, as limited aid sources run out quickly. Check your UC student email account regularly. Plan Ahead: Pay attention to important dates and deadlines each semester. Contact One Stop with billing, financial aid and registration questions.

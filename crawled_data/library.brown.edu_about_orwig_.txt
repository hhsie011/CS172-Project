Rockefeller, Jr. Library | Sciences Library | Library Collections Annex The Virginia Baldwin Orwig Music Library houses the general music collection on campus: music books, scores, periodicals, sound recordings, and video recordings. The collection supports the curriculum of the Music Department and provides material for general use by the Brown community. Material is listed in our catalog, BruKnow. The Music Library is located on the 1st floor and basement floor of the Orwig Music Building.

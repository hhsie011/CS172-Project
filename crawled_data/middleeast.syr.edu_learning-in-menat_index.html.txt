Syracuse University academic programs take place throughout the region, from summer internships in Dubai and Kuwait to study abroad in a variety of countries. Here are just a few: Advertising and PR: TBWA\RAAD internship program (Dubai) Engineering: Dubai Construction Company internship program (Dubai) All majors: Study abroad opportunities around the world Syracuse University also hosts a number of visiting scholars and professionals for lectures and workshops, on the home campus and in the MENAT region. Many of these opportunities are made possible through the generosity of alumni and their affiliated corporations. Contact us about supporting learning in MENAT.

Fieldwork in Iceland, New England, California, Axial Seamount, and Lau Basin. Textbooks in inverse theory, environmental data analysis and geophysics. Fields of Interest Structure and geodynamics of the Earth's lithosphere and asthenosphere,e specially in volcanic regions; algorithm development in inverse theory and data analysis.

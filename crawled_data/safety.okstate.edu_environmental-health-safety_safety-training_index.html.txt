Each worker must be trained to safely perform their tasks, prior to performing them. Supervisors should determine which safety training is required for their workers, and ensure that their employees receive the training they need. The requirements for portable fire extinguishers in general industry are governed by OSHA regulation 29 CFR 1910.157. Training: Where the employee has provided fire extinguishers for employee use, the employer shall provide an educational program to familiarize employees on the principles and use of the extinguishers. This educational program should be completed during the initial hiring and annually thereafter.

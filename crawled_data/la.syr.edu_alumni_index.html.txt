More than 8,200 people in the LA area can say they got their start at Syracuse University. Armed with their degrees and shaped by their experiences on and off the Syracuse campus, our alums are making a difference and finding success in LA and beyond. Meet some of our LA area alums. Learn more about how the Syracuse University Alumni Association can help you stay connected. Join One of Syracuse University’s LA-Area Alumni Clubs If you’re looking for Orange in the Los Angeles metro area, you can find it! The Syracuse University Alumni Clubs of Southern California, San Diego and Northern California, as well as Syracuse University Alumni Clubs across the West Coast, have hundreds of active members and host events throughout the year. Syracuse University alumni clubs around the world draw thousands of grads together to enjoy a wide variety of social and educational programming. Events range from faculty speakers and career networking opportunities to game-watching events and evenings at the theater. Learn more about our local alumni clubs.

Recurring donors help build a strong and reliable base of support for Columbia Business School to continue its traditions of innovation, excellence, and achievement. Make a Recurring Gift Why Make a Recurring Credit Card Gift? A quick, consistent, and budget-friendly way to show your support. Quick Save time by setting up your recurring gift just once a year! Your gift renews automatically based on your preference - monthly, quarterly, or annually. Consistent Your gift provides a reliable source of support for Columbia Business School! Consistently help expand financial aid offerings, innovate academic programming, and groundbreaking faculty research. Budget-Friendly Contribute in a way that best suits your budget! A recurring gift is a flexible way to make a leadership-level donation and join the School's Hermes Society*. FAQs How do I cancel my recurring gift? To cancel your recurring gift, please contact Charon Darris at [email protected] or 212-854-0719. How do I change the credit card associated with my recurring gift? Visit business.givenow.columbia.edu and set up a new recurring gift. This will replace your previous contribution and your prior credit card will no longer be charged. *The Hermes Society is a growing group of leadership supporters of the Columbia Business School Annual Fund. Alumni membership begins at the $2,500 gift level and $1,000 level for young alumni (five or fewer years out). Find out more at bit.ly/hermescbs.

Studies at OSU-Tulsa OSU-Tulsa offers master’s and doctoral degrees, as well as graduate certificates. Tulsa-area students looking to advance in their careers can earn an OSU graduate credential close to home. OSU graduate students work with OSU faculty conducting research in areas such as engineering, education, human sciences, and business. Affordable Courses Built Around Your Busy Schedule OSU-Tulsa offers late afternoon, evening and some weekend and online courses to help you achieve your graduate degree. You can plan course work around your schedule. As a nationally accredited comprehensive university, OSU has more than 100 years of providing academic excellence. OSU-Tulsa brings OSU’s land grant passion of education, research, and outreach to downtown Tulsa. Students receive an excellent education at affordable rates. OSU is consistently ranked one of the best values in the Big 12.

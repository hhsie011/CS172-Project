Dartmouth Professionalism Reporting Office of Student Affairs Featured Slide 8 Slide 7 Slide 6 Slide 5 Slide 4 Welcome! Our Student Affairs and Services Team is committed to providing comprehensive, integrated and accessible support services to the Geisel School of Medicine student community throughout your medical education experience. We encourage our students to lead balanced lives, working collaboratively to create a healthy community that benefits both the students and their future patients. Our mission is to create a supportive environment in which students can flourish as they transition into medical professionals who are caring, compassionate, and responsible world citizens. We strive to foster in each student a sense of social and professional responsibility that engenders the trust and confidence of the public and complements the exceptional clinical training for which graduates of Geisel are known. It is our privilege to support the personal and professional success of our students. We look forward to getting to know you!

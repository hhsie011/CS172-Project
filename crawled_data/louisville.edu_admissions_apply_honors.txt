Benefits of Louisville's Honors Program provides students with the chance to fly even higher. Some perks of the program include small class sizes (no more than 25 students), professional mentoring, study-abroad opportunities, suite-style housing communities and more. Learn more about the University Honors Program. How to Apply Step 1: Apply to UofL. For students applying to UofL for Summer/Fall 2022, you must apply to UofL before applying to the Honors Program. Step 2: Apply for the Honors Program. Once you have applied to the University of Louisville, students who are eligible to apply for the Honors Program will see that option in their New Cards Gateway. Log in to your New Cards Gateway and look for the "University Honors Program" under your Competitive checklist section. Click on the link to be taken to the form to view complete application instructions. Please note: the Honors Program link will only appear on your Gateway if you meet the minimum GPA requirements to apply. For more information on specific program requirements, please email honors@louisville.edu. Honors Early Action Deadline: October 15 (details below) Regular Honors Deadline: December 15 Honors Early Action UofL's Honors Early Action application process allows Honors-accepted students to receive their University Honors Program decision earlier than ever. Honors Early Action is non-binding – students receive an early response to their application but do not have to commit to UofL until the decision deadline of May 1. To be considered for Honors Early Action, you must submit your Honors application and materials by October 15. If you apply to Honors by the Honors Early Action deadline, you will receive your decision by the end of November.

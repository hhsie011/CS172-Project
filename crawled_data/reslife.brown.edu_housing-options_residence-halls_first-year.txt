Breadcrumb Home Housing Options Residence Halls The standard size of a room is 12 feet by 12 feet. That being said, we suggest that you log in with your Brown credentials into the Facilities Campus Map to access floor plans once you know where you will be living. Each room comes with a set of furniture provided by the university. At this time, you and your roommate can also coordinate what to bring. Elevators Elevator service is available in only a few buildings. Within the first-year region, these include: Archibald-Bronson and Jameson-Mead within the Keeney Quadrangle; and on Pembroke Campus: Andrews, Metcalf, Miller, Morriss, Champlin, Emery, and Woolley. Please refer to the Student and Employee Accessibility Services (SAS) website for a complete Campus Accessibility Map.

dentistry for better health. Since 1950, the UNC Adams School of Dentistry has been a leader, and we strive to be the global model for oral health education, in care and discovery. Together, we passionately serve our people, our community and our field. North Carolina’s first school of dentistry The Adams School of Dentistry provides integrated and interprofessional educational experiences for our students and residents with unparalleled comprehensive oral health care for our patients. Our school has a rich tradition of excellence in patient care, research, service and education. In addition, we have dedicated and hardworking staff, forward-thinking faculty, a diverse learning environment, top-notch students and residents, and an amazing network of devoted and influential alumni that make this dental school second to none.

Marlen Lebish 01-27-2022 Miami Herbert Repeats as top-ranked Poets&Quants Undergraduate Business School in Florida By Marlen Lebish 01-27-2022 Miami Herbert Business School climbs six spots in Poets&Quants for Undergrads’ latest ranking. Poets&Quants for Undergrads unveiled its sixth annual Best Undergraduate Business Schools of 2022 rankings today, which placed Miami Herbert Business School first in the state and #30 in the nation, six places higher than last year. The ranking is considered the most objective assessment of undergraduate business programs with 95 schools ranked, up from 93 last year. . “Miami Herbert’s upward trajectory in rankings is a testimony to our faculty and staff’s unyielding commitment to our students’ success,” said John Quelch, dean of Miami Herbert. “We develop innovative ideas and principled leaders that transform global business and society and work hard to provide an environment in which students thrive and grow.” Poets&Quants for Undergrads compiled the 2022 exclusive rankings based on a representative survey of over 5,400 alumni and school-reported data. Recent grads across the 95 schools were surveyed on aspects of faculty availability, extracurricular opportunities, and accessibility of the alumni network. “This ranking is a powerful indicator of the momentum Miami Herbert has been building over the last few years,” said Hari Natarajan, vice dean of business programs at Miami Herbert. “This momentum is fueled by our faculty and staff’s persistent effort in building quality programs and preparing students for success.” To learn more about the Poets&Quants 2022 rankings methodology or to locate a specific school, click here.

About Recommended Reading Projects Collaborators Faculty and Staff Contact About Recommended Reading Projects Collaborators Faculty and Staff Contact Collaborators New York State Department of Health The New York State Department of Health is the primary funder for Project STAY (Services to Assist Youth) which is a partnership between HHPC and New York and Presbyterian Hospital, Inc. Project STAY has collaborated with the New York State Department of Health on several adolescent HIV/AIDS and STI Initiatives in an effort to reduce the incidents of HIV/AIDS and STI among adolescents living in NYC. New York City Department of Health and Mental Hygiene HHPC has a longstanding relationship with the East and Central Harlem District Public Health Office as we both work to address health disparities in Harlem and Upper Manhattan. HHPC has been a strong supporter of DOHMH initiatives including community education around sugar sweetened beverages, healthy food access, advertising and marketing to young people, as well as collaborative efforts among the public health and community-based organizations promoting wellness in our neighborhood.

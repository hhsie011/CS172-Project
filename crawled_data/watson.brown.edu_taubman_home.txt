Schiller About The A. Alfred Taubman Center for American Politics and Policy brings together scholars, practitioners, and students from different perspectives to address important problems facing American politics and policy. Since 1984, the Taubman Center has influenced local, national, and global policymakers working at the intersection of research and public engagement. The Taubman Center’s programming includes: Taubman Center public opinion polls Research through workshops, conferences, and scholarship Engagement with public leaders and practitioners through several speakers series Internship and research opportunities for students Upcoming Events SEE ALL OF OUR EVENTS Anton Lippitt Lecture Series in Governance - Luis Daniel Muñoz Lecture Series in Governance ─ Joy Fox VIEW PAST TALKS ON YOUTUBE Research Research Taubman Center faculty produce influential books and public issue briefs. The Center is also home to the Study of Educational Standards. The Taubman Center also supports faculty & student research partnerships.

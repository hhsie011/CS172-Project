Learn practical information and technical knowledge on each subject. Explore a wide range of environmental engineering roles and careers. ONLINE FORMAT Learn on your own time outside of work and personal schedules. Access lectures at any time using Canvas, our online portal. Complete coursework, collaborate on projects, and analyze case studies. PREPARATION Train to work for consulting companies or as government officials. Complete a graduate research project and practice presenting the results. After the program, you will be prepared for the Professional Engineering exam. COHORT MODEL Move through the 5-semester curriculum as a group. Learn with and peers and form study groups.

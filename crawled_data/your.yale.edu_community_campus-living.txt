As a member of the Yale Community, Yale Off Campus Living is a key resource for your housing and apartment search and has been designed to help ease your move into the exciting and diverse City of New Haven and its surrounding areas. Many local landlords and home sellers advertise their properties with our service. The database information includes properties for rent or sale, landlord ratings, maps of New Haven and the Yale Campus, and links to additional resources and information. Search homes/apartments Yale-Owned Apartments Landlord Rating System If you are a current resident and found your housing through OCL’s listing service, please rate your landlord! Be sure to fill out all 5 attributes to properly complete the rating system. This information is valuable to the OCL staff as well as new students looking for housing.

Two Institutions. One Unique Approach to the MBA. IE Business School and Brown University have forged a unique alliance to offer a joint Executive MBA that goes beyond the boundaries of traditional business education. The IE Brown Executive MBA provides a platform for reflective leaders to be more global in their outlook, more innovative in their thinking and more informed in their decision-making. Over 15-months, students experience a rigorous and dynamic curriculum that prepares them with the cross-industry expertise and diverse perspectives to innovate and meet the new and evolving demands for leadership. Our EMBA curriculum is triple-certified by AACSB, AMBA and EQUIS. Program Format 15 Months that include blended online sessions and five residential weeks structured for professionals. Our program blends interactive online learning with in-person sessions so you can earn an EMBA and build the network and skills vital for advancing your business in real-time — without interrupting your career.

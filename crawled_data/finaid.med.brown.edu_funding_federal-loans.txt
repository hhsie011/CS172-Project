Students may apply to borrow two federal loans by filling out the FAFSA. These federal loans are the Direct unsubsidized and Graduate Plus loans. Please visit studentaid.gov to review the status of your federal loans and for more information on your loan servicers. Click here for guidance on how to locate your federal loan servicer. Federal Direct Unsubsidized Loan Lender: US Department of Education; promissory note/application forms are provided by the Brown Loan Office Based on Need: No, but may not exceed the cost of attendance for the year less other aid Annual Borrowing Limit: Up to $47,160 Aggregate Borrowing Limit: $224,000 (less aggregate subsidized Direct loan amount) Loan Fees: 1.059% Origination Fee on or after 10/01/19 and before 09/30/2020 Interest Rate: Fixed rate of 6.54% AY22-23 Interest Subsidized In School: No Interest Subsidized During Grace: No Interest Subsidized During Deferment: No Deferment Options: Half-time student status; graduate fellowship or rehabilitation training period; unemployment or economic hardship (up to 3 years) Grace Period: 6 months Repayment Requirements: Choice of standard (fixed), graduated, extended, income-contingent or income-based repayment, up to 25 yrs to repay. Direct loans are eligible for consolidation. Prepayment Penalties: None Federal Graduate PLUS Loan Lender: US Department of Education; promissory note/application forms at StudentLoans.gov. Available May 1. Best time to apply is after June 15. A credit check will be performed and is kept on file for 180 days. Based on Need: No, but may not exceed the cost of attendance for the year less other aid Annual Borrowing Limit: No limit Aggregate Borrowing Limit: No limit Loan Fees: 4.236% on or after 10/01/2019 and before 09/30/2020 Interest Rate: 7.5% for AY22-23 Interest Subsidized In School: No Interest Subsidized During Grace: No Interest Subsidized During Deferment: No Deferment Options: Half-time student status; graduate fellowship or rehabilitation training period; unemployment or economic hardship (up to 3 years) Grace Period: 6 month automatic deferment from time of separation if issued after 6/08 Repayment Requirements: Choice of standard (fixed), graduated, extended, income-contingent or income-based repayment, up to 25 yrs to repay. Stafford loans are eligible for consolidation.

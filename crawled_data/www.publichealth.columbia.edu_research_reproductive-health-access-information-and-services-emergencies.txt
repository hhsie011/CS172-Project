Comprehensive Reproductive Health Care Health and development organisations worldwide have recognized the critical importance of global reproductive health for all people. Emergency Obstetric and Newborn Care (EmONC) Emergency Obstetric and Newborn Care (EmONC) refers to the care of women and newborns during pregnancy, delivery and the time after delivery. Contraceptive Services Contraceptive services are essential to the ability of women and families to achieve good health and well-being. Resources Download training materials and safe abortion briefs, and view selected publications. Action RAISE gives technical support to partners to develop and expand SRH services. Partners From DR Congo to Chad to Afghanistan, RAISE works closely with its partner organisations.

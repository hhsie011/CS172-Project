Applicants to any of the ten campuses of the University of Hawaiʻi may choose to apply online. Undergraduate Application University of Hawaiʻi campuses all use the System Application Form for undergraduate enrollment, however, applications are submitted to and processed by each individual campus. Undergraduate programs/majors (PDF) International Undergraduate Requirements International applicants to the University of Hawaiʻi campuses, must submit standard application information as well as the information detailed below. Fill out the Supplementary Information Form for International Applicants (PDF). Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) score. Write to Educational Testing Service, Box 899, Princeton, New Jersey 08540, to apply for the test and to have your score sent directly to the Admissions Office of the campus to which you are applying. Applicants who are non-native speakers of English are required to submit the TOEFL score. High school or any college transcripts, translated and certified by a school or U.S. consular official. External exam score (GCE O, A, etc.), translated and certified by a school or U.S. consular official. Graduate Admissions The UH System offers graduate studies at UH Mānoa, UH Hilo and through our three University Centers. Graduate program applications are handled separately from undergraduate applications. Contact the graduate admissions office at the campus or center you are interested in applying to for full details and requirements. International Graduate Students Requirements vary for international students applying to UH graduate programs. See information for the campus you wish to attend.

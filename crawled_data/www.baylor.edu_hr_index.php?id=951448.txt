Working at Baylor Consistently ranked as a "Great College to Work For" by the Chronicle of Higher Education, we offer our employees so much more than just a job! As part of the Baylor family, employees not only receive a comprehensive benefits package that includes medical and dental insurance, generous time off, and fantastic automatic retirement contributions, they also get to experience Baylor. Not only is Baylor highly rated by the Chronicle, we are also highly rated by our faculty and staff. Take a look at our reviews on Glassdoor: From free admission to various athletic events, access to wonderful libraries, museums, and recreational facilities, and tuition remission for faculty and staff and their dependents, it is no wonder Baylor employees consistently rank the University as a great place to build a career! Benefits Summary Our Compensation Philosophy About Baylor Founded in 1845 by the Republic of Texas, Baylor is a private Christian university and a nationally ranked research institution. Our more than 3,000 mission-focused faculty and staff create a transformative educational environment and experience for our nearly 17,000 undergraduate and graduate students.

Whether you are interested in learning new etiquette skills, meeting with industry professionals, networking with Graduate and Post-Professional Universities, or you are simply interested in getting involved in professional workshops, this is the place for you. Our ‘Upcoming Events’ section will showcase events happening over the next month and include information on how to register to attend those events. How to Register for PD Events To register for any of our professional events, please visit CU Course. For information on event delays, postponements or cancellations, please be sure to check the Honors College Canvas page. Upcoming Events Watch this space for information about events this fall! Note: registration for all professional development is events is via CuCourse. Pre-Health and Pre-Law Canvas Groups If you are interested in pursuing a career in health or law, join the Honors Canvas Courses designed for each. These courses are updated regularly, ensuring you're notified about upcoming events and other information and opportunities. Follow these steps to join the course you're interested in: Login to your Canvas account. Click on the Courses tab and select All Courses, followed by Browse More Courses. You can either use the search tool to search for Pre-Health or Pre-Law, depending on the course you prefer. If that doesn't work, browse all courses until you find it. When you find the course, click Join This Course to enroll. If encounter challenges enrolling in either course, please contact Holly Williams. Questions? Contact Holly Williams, Honors Advisor and Professional Development Coordinator, if you have questions about Professional Development programming for Clemson Honors College students.

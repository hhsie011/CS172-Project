Dartmouth Engineering Research Laboratories Dartmouth Engineering is a close-knit community of scholars with a collaborative culture that extends across the hall, across campus, and beyond. Many research projects engage colleagues from other institutions such as Dartmouth-Hitchcock Medical Center, Geisel School of Medicine, Irving Energy Institute, Guarini School of Graduate and Advanced Studies, and CRREL, as well as industry.

Information Campus Life Campus Life Home New Student Orientation Student Organizations Veteran Success Center OSU-Tulsa's Veteran Success Center is a space for student veterans, active military and military-affiliated students to study, relax and foster a sense of community. The Veteran Success Center is located off the Main Hall Commons and is open to any military-affiliated student to utilize at any time the University is open. In the Center, you will find: Computer workstations Relaxation and socialization space News and resources of interest to student veterans and active military For more information about the OSU-Tulsa Veteran Success Center, contact Campus Life.

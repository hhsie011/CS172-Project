Students are encouraged to connect online to share information and resources. To learn more or to register please email gsc@bc.edu Doctoral Writing Group (DWG) Learning Outcomes: Students who attend DWG will be able to: Create a plan for writing their thesis. Describe their writing behaviors and how it is impacted by their lifestyle. Determine at least one way they can adjust their writing strategy, including but not limited to developing realistic writing goals.

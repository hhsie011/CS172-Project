Established in 1976, the Office of Diversity and Multicultural Affairs (ODMA) fosters a diverse, inclusive and culturally competent learning and training environment within the Division of Biology and Medicine. Office of Diversity and Multicultural Affairs (ODMA) Social Mission To promote social responsibility, active engagement, and the well-being of our communities in the pursuit of health equity. Partners ODMA collaborates with offices and centers across the University to offer culturally competent educational programming and related support to students, faculty, and trainees.

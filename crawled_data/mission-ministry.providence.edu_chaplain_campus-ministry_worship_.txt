Romans 12:1 As a Catholic and Dominican College, Providence College encourages its students, faculty, staff, and friends to worship God regularly and so be transformed by his grace. Prayer and the worship of God are essential if we are to fulfill our highest calling: to love God with all our mind, all our heart, and all our strength, and to love our neighbors as ourselves. Students of all faiths are most welcome to participate in the liturgical life of the College. St. Dominic Chapel St. Dominic Chapel is the spiritual heart of campus. In addition to the schedule listed below, the chapel is open daily for private prayer. Students can also use their PC IDs to access the chapel after hours. Monday, May 16 through Friday, May 20, there will only be the 11:35 a.m. Mass and 12:00 p.m. confessions, with the exception of Monday, May 16, when there will also be Eucharistic adoration with confession available 7:30–9:00 p.m. Please see the schedule below. MONDAY, MAY 16 – SATURDAY, MAY 20: Monday, May 16 Mass: 11:35 a.m. Confession: 12:00–12:30 p.m., 7:30–9:00 p.m. Eucharistic adoration: 7:30–9:00 p.m. Tuesday, May 17 – Friday, May 20 Mass: 11:35 a.m. Confession: 12:00–12:30 p.m. Saturday, May 21 Confession: 1:00–3:00 p.m. After Commencement on May 22, Providence College will be on summer break and there will be no regular Mass or confession in St. Dominic Chapel until the fall semester begins. You’re welcome to attend liturgies at St. Pius V and St. Thomas Aquinas Priory. See their websites or below for the liturgical schedules.

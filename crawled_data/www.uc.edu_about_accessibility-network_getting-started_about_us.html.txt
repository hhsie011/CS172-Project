The network provides comprehensive support for eAccessibility standards at the university, including strategic policies and direction, process development, education, and implementation. This program will drive the establishment and sustainability of eAccessibility as part of our core commitment to provide an inclusive student environment. The Network The Accessibility Network is made up of four layers to provide oversight, policy, support and implementation. Each layer has a purpose and commitment to the network.

PHIA QICIP RISE TRACE EAIPC RTSL ICAP CTUPRI Optimizing Momentum Toward Sustainable Epidemic Control (OpCon) As countries strive to reach the UNAIDS 90–90–90 targets, the focus is on achieving sustainable epidemic control. OpCon is a global initiative led by ICAP and funded by PEPFAR through the U.S. Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA). OpCon is designed to support innovative, flexible and high-impact projects that contribute to improved HIV program outcomes, equity, efficiency and sustainability.

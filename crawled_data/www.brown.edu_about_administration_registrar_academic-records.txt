Academic Records The Office of the Registrar serves as the custodian for all credit-bearing academic records for all current and former Brown University students. For specific requests for certain types of academic records and/or enrollment verifications please use the links to the left for further information. Please note that certain records beyond those found in this site may also exist in other units of the University such as the Office of the Dean of the College, the Graduate School, the Warren Alpert Medical School, and the School of Professional Studies (SPS provides all records for non-credit programs). Should you have a question regarding academic records and are unable to find the information specific to your request using the site please email us at [email protected] For further information on Brown's Academic Record and Retention Schedule please visit the University's Record Retention website (Authentication is required to access).

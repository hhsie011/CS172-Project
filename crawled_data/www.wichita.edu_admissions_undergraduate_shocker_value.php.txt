AT WICHITA STATE UNIVERSITY Tuition Dollars at Work As a highly ranked research institution, Wichita State students have access to state-of-the-art laboratories, applied learning opportunities and exceptional faculty engagement. Graduate Programs Graduate students have the option of more than 100 master’s or doctoral programs across our 10 colleges, schools and institutes. Applied Learning Opportunities Every degree we offer has opportunities for internship or research experience. This gives Shockers in every major the chance to gain practical experience, build a professional network and earn college credit. Innovation Excellence Situated on 120 acres of Wichita State University, the Innovation Campus is the home to industry and research powerhouses, including Airbus Americas, Spirit AeroSystems, Textron Aviation, NetApp and several others coming in 2021 and 2022. WSU has partnered with these companies to build community partnerships that pioneer research, invention and product development. No. 1 in the U.S. in industry-funded research and development expenditures for aerospace. $164 million in total research funding for FY2020, a new record.

Whether you are a visitor, alumnus, new student or new faculty/staff, we are glad to see you on our campus. Parking & Transportation Services (PTS), an auxiliary unit within the Department of Business Services, manages all University owned parking lots and is responsible for administering the Parking and Traffic Regulations of the University of Memphis. Parking and Transportation Services (PTS) is located on the ground floor of the Zach Curlin garage. The PTS office is currently open Monday thru Friday from 8am-4pm. We encourage you to make use of our online options first and reserve face-to-face transactions for those that need to make cash payments, to pick up a permit/sticker or do not have electronic access. Email parking@memphis.edu for more information. NOTE: Until further notice, Lot 38 (the old Information Center lot) is being used ONLY as a COVID-19 testing lot and will not be used for general parking. SPRING 2022 PARKING Spring 2022 priority parking sales begin December 14, 2021. STUDENTS TigerPark, under the student tab in myMemphis, allows students to: View their citations View their permit View their appeals Purchase parking access Appeal citations TigerPark requires the citation number and license plate number to appeal a citation. For questions, please contact Parking & Transportation Services by emailing parking@memphis.edu. Alternatively, students may appeal in person during the SGA’s scheduled hearings. Please visit https://www.memphis.edu/sga/branches/judicial.php or e-mail sga@memphis.edu for hearing times and location. EMPLOYEES Employees may purchase monthly access for a parking permit online here. Employees who wish to have their parking fee deducted automatically from their paycheck can email parking@memphis.edu to check eligibility and to authorize or cancel their deduction. TigerPark, under the employee tab in myMemphis, allows employees to only: View their citations View their permit View their appeals Appeal citations TigerPark requires the citation number and license plate number to appeal a citation. For questions, please contact Parking & Transportation Services by e-mailing parking@memphis.edu. Employees have the option of appearing before the Faculty/Staff Traffic Appeals Committee. For meeting times, please email parking@memphis.edu. **REMINDER** – Only students may purchase a parking permit/access on TigerPark. Employees must visit here in order to purchase monthly access online.

Taught by top faculty from the Yale School of Public Health and elsewhere, and employing state-of-the-art tools and technology, the program aims to train professionals who seek to acquire a strong public health education, advanced training in their area of interest, and hands-on public health and leadership training. Designed from the ground up for working health professionals, the program provides extensive training in leadership and management, a broad foundation in public health, specialized knowledge in areas critical to health promotion and disease prevention, and a year-long integrative experience that enables students to apply what they have learned to a real-world public health problem. Program Takeaways Management and Leadership: Using Evidence to Drive Decisions, Design Thinking Intensive, Strategic Management in Complex Systems Intensive, Leadership Intensive Core Public Health Knowledge: Biostatistics in Public Health, Foundations of Epidemiology for Public Health, Health Policy and Healthcare Systems, Frontiers of Public Health, Health Behavior Change, Ethics in Public Health Specializations: Health Informatics Track, Environmental Health Sciences Track, Applied Analytic Methods and Epidemiology Track, or Critical Topics in Public Health Track Integration: Capstone Course Join the program Class entering in the summer of 2022 will open on August 18 Delivery This is a two-year, part-time program with a hybrid online/in-person schedule designed for working professionals.

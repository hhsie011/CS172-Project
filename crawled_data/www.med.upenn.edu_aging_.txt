In Loving Memory of John Q. Trojanowski | 1946 - 2022 We are deeply saddened by the death of John Q. Trojanowski, MD, PhD, past Director of the Institute on Aging (IOA). Dr. Trojanowski served as an outstanding leader, mentor, friend, and inspiration to us all. His memory will leave a lasting impression on the IOA and all who knew him. The IOA Team David Wolk, Eddie Lee, Brad Johnson, Kathy Jedrziewski, Ebony Fenderson, and Nicolette Calcavecchia Read more on Dr. Trojanowski's legacy here.

Leadership Addressing the changing landscape of higher education, research, and health care demands leading-edge operational and financial systems, superior planning, strategic decision-making, efficient use of resources, and more directed change. These measures will help build the platforms for success that will enable our transformative initiatives to deliver on our core mission. Operational Efficiency Improve the effectiveness and efficiency of administrative and operational processes that are critical to our long-term success.

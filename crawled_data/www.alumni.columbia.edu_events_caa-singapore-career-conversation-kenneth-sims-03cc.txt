Join us for dinner and drinks with Kenneth Sim (CC '03), President Emeritus of CAA Singapore, and Dean of the Chandler Academy at the Chandler Institute of Governance. Kenneth is an experienced public policy practitioner with over 15 years of experience in the Singapore government. During this time, he held key leadership appointments in varied portfolios including casino regulation, higher education & skills, energy policy and environmental sustainability. He also served as Special Assistant to the Deputy Prime Minister. Outside of the public service, Kenneth was Lead Consultant with Times Higher Education, a UK-based education services company, where he provided expert advice to senior university and government leaders on university performance and strategy. He is currently Dean at the Chandler Institute of Governance, a non-profit organisation which supports governments globally to establish effective governance architecture through training, knowledge creation, advice and counsel.

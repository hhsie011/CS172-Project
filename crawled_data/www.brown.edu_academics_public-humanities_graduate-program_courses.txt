Carter Brown Architecture Lectures Performances Workshops Blog Courses The Center for Public Humanities lists courses under the PHUM rubric. Our flexible program draws on Brown University's open curriculum. Elective courses can be selected from approved 1000 and 2000 level courses offered at Brown, Rhode Island School of Design, and Harvard Graduate School of Arts and Sciences. The courses sponsored by the Center for Public Humanities in 2022-2023 academic years are listed below. Visit course catalog for a complete listing of courses at Brown University.

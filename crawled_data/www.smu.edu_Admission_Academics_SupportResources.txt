From libraries to computer centers, tutors to self-teaching materials, all kinds of support options are available to help you make the most of your education. The Altshuler Learning Enhancement Center is one of the most popular spots on campus. From “drop-in” tutoring to writing strategies and study skills workshops, you’ll learn how to maximize your study time and minimize your worries. Stop by Academic Advising for person-to-person guidance and a computerized, individual Degree Progress Report that can help map out your course.

We manage the School’s website, promote faculty research, and produce a variety of print and digital publications to keep the general public and policymakers informed about important public health news that affects everyone. The following are some of the primary activities carried out by the Communications team. Promoting Harvard Chan School news Helping journalists with their print, web, TV, and radio stories by identifying experts for interviews on a wide range of public health topics Documenting Harvard Chan faculty appearances in a variety of news media Writing press releases announcing the latest research and news from the School Informing the general public Creating content for the School’s website and other Harvard publications Producing the award-winning Harvard Public Health magazine three times a year Producing a variety of digital newsletters for the general public and the broader Harvard community Maintaining the School’s social media channels and producing the Better Off podcast Telling stories of public health that reflect people’s lives Convening global leaders in health policy, advocacy, industry, and research for events produced by Harvard Chan Studio Focusing on the School Teaching communications best practices to administrators of departments, centers, and other School entities Working with School entities to identify their communications needs and determine best ways to address them Requesting communications assistance For media inquiries, contact: Todd Datz Chief Communications Officer Telephone: 617-432-8413 Fax: 617-432-8077 Email: tdatz@hsph.harvard.edu Nicole Rura Senior Media Relations Manager/Writer Telephone: 617-221-4241 Fax: 617-432-8077 Email: nrura@hsph.harvard.edu For inquiries/questions about the website, marketing, branding, social media, or other digital content, contact digitalcomms@hsph.harvard.edu.

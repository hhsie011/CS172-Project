Texas Tech offers some options. Evening Courses Texas Tech offers a variety of evening courses to help you earn your undergraduate or graduate degree. These courses provide some flexibility to help you achieve your education goals. A wide variety of undergraduate and graduate course begin at 4 p.m. or later. Weekend Courses For those professionals seeking a master's of business administration, the Rawls College of Business offers a Weekend MBA for Working Professionals that meets one weekend per month for 27 months. A week-block MBA for working professionals is offered in five one-week block sessions over a 27-month period.

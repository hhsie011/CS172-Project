She serves as the top strategy and implementation partner to Co-Founding Dean Maureen Raymo. Prior to joining the Climate School in 2021, Janice held leadership positions within Columbia at both the Vagelos College of Physicians & Surgeons and the Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science. Janice received her MS in Health Policy and Management from The New School, and her BA in Psychology from Boston College.

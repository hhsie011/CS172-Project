Key Features: John Jay Dining Hall, JJ's Place, and Columbia Health Medical Services are located in close proximity Expansive views of Morningside Campus, the Hudson River, and the historic Harlem neighborhood Located in the heart of campus Columbia Housing office and Hospitality Desk are located within Hartley Faculty-in-Residence: Professor Kymissis, Department of Electrical Engineering Building Details: Housing Rate: Either First-Year or Upperclass Rates depending on class of student. Entrance location: Inside campus between 115th and 116th Streets Number of residential floors: 9 Single/Double rooms within suites: 158 singles / 36 doubles Elevators: 2 Flooring: Bedrooms are a mix of hardwood and carpet Common areas in suites are carpeted Building Amenities: Bathroom: Semi-private within suite Laundry: Free laundry facility in basement Kitchen: One private kitchen per suite Lounge: A large building lounge with a game room (pool and ping pong) is available on the first floor A study lounge is located on the 10th floor Each suite has common area equipped with cable TV service Computer Lab & Print Station Available on the first floor Vending Machines: Available on the first floor Cleaning Responsibility & Schedule: Bathrooms: Twice weekly by Facilities Kitchens: Resident responsibility Trash & Recycling: Residents are responsible for taking their trash and recycling to designated building area What Former Residents Say: "Hartley is not only a nice place to live, but has a convenient location. Just downstairs in the lobby is the Hartley Hospitality Desk, a CUIT computer lab, and a lounge with pool and ping pong tables! In addition, it only takes two minutes to get to classes in Hamilton."

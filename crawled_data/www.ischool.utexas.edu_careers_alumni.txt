Want to get involved with the iSchool? Follow the iSchool on LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, and Youtube to find out about our future career events and connect with alumni, students, and staff. It is always wonderful to have our alumni return to share their career experiences with current students in iSchool events such as the Alumni Career Panels. Need career help? Register online as an alum with HireUTexas! Please note that current students and graduates within the past 12 months are our primary clientele. However, we do understand that the information landscape is constantly changing and presenting new, exciting opportunities for employment! Individual appointments with alumni past 12 months of graduation will be made if time allows. Looking for an iSchool candidate? Contact the Career Development Office to post an internship or job opportunity where the iSchool community can apply to. Find iSchool talents that match your team quicker! Contact Us Stay connected with UT through Texas Exes Maintain your membership with the Texas Exes Alumni Association. The Exes have a host of networking benefits including a directory, a LinkedIn Group, and regional events. Look up alumni on the Ex-Students' Association directory. Some services with the Texas Exes require membership and fees may be associated.

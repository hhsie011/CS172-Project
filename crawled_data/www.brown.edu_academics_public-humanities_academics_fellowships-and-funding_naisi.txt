Carter Brown Architecture Lectures Performances Workshops Blog NAISI Fellowship Brown University’s Native American and Indigenous Studies Initiative (NAISI) collaborates with departments across the campus to offer fellowships to students pursuing Master’s degrees. NAISI is dedicated to increasing the understanding and maintenance of the cultural traditions and political experiences of Native American and Indigenous Peoples. Public Humanities is one of the participating departments in the NAISI fellowship program and eligible Public Humanities students will be considered for this program. Please visit the NAISI website for more information on their fellowship (to be posted in the Fall 2021).

Student Information Meet the Clinical Faculty Student Forms For Attorneys & Supervisors Supervising Students Forms Blog Clinical Newsletters Contact Us Clinical and Pro Bono Programs 3085 Wasserstein Hall (WCC)6 Everett StreetCambridge, MA02138Phone: 617-495-5202Fax: 617-496-2636Email: clinical@law.harvard.edu Student Work Student Work Clinical placements are in local District Attorneys’ offices, where students represent the Commonwealth, prosecuting non-jury criminal cases in district court. Students are likely to handle: arraignments; bail hearings; pre-trial conferences; motion hearings; and trials Students are directly responsible for assisting with prosecuting a docket of cases (under the supervision of an assistant district attorney), and will need two full days free (9 AM to 5 PM) in their schedules for this clinical placement. Placements are coordinated through the Office of Clinical and Pro Bono Programs during the summer months. You can view past clinical placements and read evaluations from past clinical students in the Helios Org Search using this link.

Artificial Intelligence Events Past Event Opportunities and Challenges of Artificial Intelligence Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 19, 2017 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing On Aug. 19, three leading experts from the industry of artificial intelligence will gather at Columbia Global Centers | Beijing to join a panel discussion on the Opportunities and Challenges of Artificial Intelligence. We warmly welcome you to attend this event. SPEAKERS Xin Wang Wang Xin（王鑫）, SVInsight co-founder, former Grape Venture partner, and Geek Academy co-founder. Zhao Huang Huang Zhao（黄钊）, Turing OS Intelligence Strategy Officer, former Tencent Product Manager. Xuchen Yao Yao Xuchen（姚旭晨）, the founder of KITT-AI, focusing on natural language processing and semantic understanding technology, is committed to make human-computer interaction more natural and smooth. MODERATOR Ruichen Liu Liu Ruichen（刘芮辰）, the founder of ThinkX Productive Thinking, Columbia University Teachers College Alumnus and a member of Tsinghua University Youth of Excellence Scheme of China.

Admissions Scholarships Transfer Scholarships What is FSU Research and Creative Activity? Florida State University provides high-achieving students an engaging community with the flexibility and funding opportunities to explore their full range of interests. Whether it is understanding how people make decisions, exploring new filmmaking techniques, pushing the boundaries of alternative energy, or producing new and inspiring creative works of art. These are just a small sampling of the different types of research and creative activities taking place at FSU.

Ntozake Shange’s For Colored Girls has been named a “landmark of American theater” (New York Times) that blazed a trail for generations to come. This celebration of the power of Black womanhood returns to Broadway for the first time, reinvented, directed, and choreographed by “a true superstar of theater and dance” (NPR), Tony Award nominee Camille A. Brown.

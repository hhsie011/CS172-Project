TECHNOLOGY CONNECTION Technology Connection To place an order or ask for additional information, please email us at: tcorders@tulane.edu. Technology Connection is the Tulane University owned and operated store that assists students, faculty, and staff with their personal and institutional hardware, software, and accessory purchases. Our partnerships and collaboration with university schools, departments, and administration allow us to provide you with tailored technology solutions and services to help support your success at Tulane. Hours of Operation 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. We are closed Saturdays, Sundays, holidays and for university closures. We are only open to Tulane faculty, staff, and students (with parents or guardians) with valid Tulane ID present. If you need something not listed on our order form (iPads, desktops, etc), please email us at: tcorders@tulane.edu.

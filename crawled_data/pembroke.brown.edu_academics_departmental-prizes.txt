Students must be nominated for Pembroke Center thesis and dissertation prizes by faculty. Joan Wallach Scott Prize TAGS : Undergraduate Each year, the Pembroke Center for Teaching and Research on Women awards the Joan Wallach Scott Prize for an outstanding honors thesis in Gender and Sexuality Studies (GNSS). Marie J. Langlois Dissertation Prize TAGS : Graduate The Pembroke Center for Teaching and Research on Women annually awards the Marie J. Langlois Dissertation Prize for an outstanding dissertation in the area of feminist studies. Ruth Simmons Prize in Gender and Women's Studies TAGS : Undergraduate The Pembroke Center is pleased and honored to offer the Ruth Simmons Prize in Gender and Women’s Studies. The prize is awarded annually for an outstanding honors thesis on questions having to do with women or gender. In the spring, the Pembroke Center invites faculty in all fields to nominate honors theses for the prize. A committee of faculty who teach and write in the area of gender studies will make the selection.

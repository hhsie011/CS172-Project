December 19, 2019 For community-minded event planners, the Event Management Blue Menu provides an opportunity for events to be both delicious and philanthropic. The menu, which offers a curated selection of local and sustainable dishes, also helps neighborhood charitable organizations. A portion of the bill ($5 per person) is donated to benefit Columbia Community Service with an additional match from Event Management. Blue Menu Learn More Since its introduction in 2018, the Blue Menu raised over $13,910.00, which was used to make grants to Columbia Community Service's Pantries this summer. Four of these include Broadway Community's Community Table, NY Common Pantry, St. Mary's Episcopal Church's Outreach Soup Kitchen, and the West Side Campaign Against Hunger. The Blue Menu is available for Lerner Hall, Low Library, and Faculty House events. Menus will be refreshed seasonally.

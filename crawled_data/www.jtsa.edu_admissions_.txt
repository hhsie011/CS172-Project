List College is open to students 16 years of age or older who have completed high school and demonstrated academic excellence. Applicants to JTS’s graduate-level programs must possess a bachelor’s degree from an accredited college or university, submit transcripts from all postsecondary studies, and present evidence of the ability to pursue studies on the graduate level. Applicants to doctoral programs are strongly encouraged to have earned a master’s degree in a related discipline.

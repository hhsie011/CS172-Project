Corporate Associates Program Alumni Miami Herbert Business School is a go-to resource for organizations seeking talented undergraduate and graduate students for hiring, cutting-edge research. and consulting services, as well as for organizations seeking executive education and knowledge-building through annual impact conferences.Partner with us to help achieve your business goals. Hire Our Students Global companies turn to Miami Herbert Business School to hire graduates from our undergraduate and graduate programs who can hit the ground running. Entrepreneurial experiences, internship placements, research-backed knowledge, and involvement in a culturally diverse learning environment make our graduates highly competitive for job placements in any country and in any industry. Learn More Watch this video Executive Education Programs Whether your organization is seeking a custom executive education program to manage uncertainty and change, or you would like to support the growth of your employees through Executive MBA and non-degree certificate programs, the Miami Herbert Business School is prepared to develop your team. We invite you to explore these executive training and development programs that can help your organization compete more successfully in the rapidly changing global economy. Partner Attend/Sponsor an Event Miami Herbert Business School organizes and hosts annual business conferences that bring together hundreds of professionals for forward-thinking discussions on the issues affecting business today. Conferences include The Business of Health Care, which draws more than 700 professionals to campus each year, the University of Miami Real Estate Impact Conference, the Business ForUM organized by the Department of Accounting, and the Miami Behavioral Finance Conference. These conferences provide opportunities for professional growth and networking. Firms can also join us as one of the dozens of corporate sponsors that partner with us on these events. Conferences Corporate Associates Program The Corporate Associates Program provides access, insight, and the competitive advantage your company needs to excel across the Americas and throughout the world. Join us and see the power of this partnership.

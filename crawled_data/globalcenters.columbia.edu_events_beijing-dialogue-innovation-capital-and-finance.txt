Capital, and Finance Events Past Event Beijing Dialogue: Innovation, Capital, and Finance Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo July 9, 2018 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing On July 9, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to a dialogue with Professor Xuan Tian to discuss how to apply creativity to finance. Professor Tian from Tsinghua University's PBC School of Finance will share his insights on innovation, capital, and finance. Speakers Xuan Tian, “Cheung Kong Scholar” Distinguished Professor, Assistant Dean and JD Capital Chair Professor of Finance at PBC School of Finance, Tsinghua University Yuhuang Zheng, Associate Professor of Marketing and Faculty Director of the Marketing Department Doctoral Program at the School of Economics and Management (SEM), Research Fellow at the China Business Research Center, Tsinghua University Agenda 18:30 - 19:00 Registration 19:00 - 20:00 Speech by Professor Xuan Tian 20:00 - 20:45 Dialogue with Professor Xuan Tian and Professor Yuhuang Zheng 20:45 - 21:00 Q&A Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code of confirmation. Please present the QR code at the entrance. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

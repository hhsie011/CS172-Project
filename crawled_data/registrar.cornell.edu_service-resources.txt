We serve as an academic student service center and custodian of academic records. We offer a wide range of services in the areas of academic transcripts, certifications, registration, course enrollment, veteran's benefits, and exam schedules. Students can contact staff in Chatter, via scheduling an appointment or chat. Everyone can email the office at univreg@cornell.edu.

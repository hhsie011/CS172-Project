Payment Dates & Procedures Billing Columbia University bills students for tuition and fees according to the schedule below. When a statement is generated, an email notification is sent directly to your Columbia email account. You can also access a copy of the bill through Student Services Online (SSOL). The University does not mail paper bills. After the first statement is generated in June, bills will be generated periodically if you make schedule changes or if you carry an account balance. Payment Instructions For payment instructions and to authorize a parent to pay your bill, see Student Financial Services. Important Billing and Payment Dates Please visit Student Financial Services' Billing Schedule for the most up-to-date dates.

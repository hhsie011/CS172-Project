Ashley Webber Kyle Key Jason Autry Tricia Yee Claire Minton Yale Miller Shawna Obregon Danny Hagedorn Vikki Kowalczyk Directions Driving Directions From the North or South via I-75 From I-75, take the Hopple Street exit (Exit 3). Turn right off the exit onto Hopple Street. Hopple Street becomes Martin Luther King Drive.  Follow Martin Luther King Drive until you reach the fourth stoplight. Turn right to enter campus via Woodside Drive.  You are welcome to park in either Woodside Garage (to your right) or Campus Green Garage (to your left). From the West via I-74 Take I-74 East to I-75 South. Take the Hopple Street exit (Exit 3). Turn right off the exit onto Hopple Street. Hopple Street becomes Martin Luther King Drive.  Follow Martin Luther King Drive until you reach the fourth stoplight. Turn right to enter campus via Woodside Drive.  You are welcome to park in either Woodside Garage (to your right) or Campus Green Garage (to your left). From the Northeast via I-71 From I-71 South, take the Taft Road exit (Exit 3). The exit places you on Taft Road, a one-way street heading west.  At the intersection of Taft Road and Jefferson Avenue, follow the right turn lane onto Jefferson Avenue.  At the first light, turn left on to Corry Boulevard.  You may park in the CCM Garage or Corry Garage: You may access Corry Garage from Corry Boulevard by turning left at the first stop sign. The CCM (College-Conservatory of Music) garage is located beneath the CCM complex toward the end of the street. The entrance to the garage is in the center of the street just past the round-about. As you drive into the garage, please note that you will want to exit via the doors by the large Electronic Media sign.

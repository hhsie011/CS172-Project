Spanning component-level through system-of-systems interactions, ACL provides our sponsors with high fidelity physics-based modeling of advanced signal processing implementations to enable evaluation of technique effectiveness in a low-cost modeling and simulation environment. ACL researchers support a wide range of efforts focused on the development of advanced signals techniques including waveform design and capabilities-based operational requirements analysis for future systems.

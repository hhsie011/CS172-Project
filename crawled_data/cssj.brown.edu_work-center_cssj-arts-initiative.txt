The Imagined New This project, The Imagined New, is an interdisciplinary platform for critical exchange and research around African and African Diasporic art practices, as they relate to questions of history, archive and an alternative imagination(s) of Black aesthetic and curatorial practices. A collaboration between CSSJ and the Visual Identities in Art and Design Research Centre (VIAD), University of Johannesburg.

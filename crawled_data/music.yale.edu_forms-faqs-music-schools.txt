The program is available for woodwind, brass, string, percussion, and choir students. Applicants for the Academy must exhibit musical accomplishment, enthusiasm, and work habits conducive to an intensive music instruction program, including at least a "B" average on their NHPS report card, full participation in their school's music program (if available), and teacher approval. Interested students meeting these criteria may apply below. Please note that the choir candidates accepted should commit to a year-long participation in Morse Chorale from July 6, 2020 to June 1, 2021. Applications will be accepted until Monday, March 16, 2020. Morse Academy will take place Monday, July 6 to Friday, July 31, 2020. If you have any questions, please email musicinschools@yale.edu.

Farha Medical Library Living in Wichita Student Life The BEAT Academic & Student Affairs Healthy Hawks The well-being of students is a top priority at KU School of Medicine-Wichita. You'll quickly find support, resources and activities that enhance the student experience in Wichita, from our George J. Farha Medical Library to the Simulation Center, the Hazel Fenske Student Center and a wellness center, all on campus. You'll also discover numerous student interest groups offering opportunities to explore your passion and give back to others at the same time. Additionally, the Wellness Program coordinated by our Department of Academic & Student Affairs promotes cultural, intellectual, spiritual, social, physical and emotional well-being among our students. From kayaking on the river in downtown Wichita to painting, climbing, horseback riding and even puppy yoga, Wellness Program activities are among the many ways students enjoy and explore life in Wichita.

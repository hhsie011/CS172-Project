Daniela Zedda, a gifted Sardinian photographer, brings an eye-opening portrait of the wild and enchanting landscapes of the Barbagia and Nuoro regions of the island. She focuses on the small towns and the age-old handcraft traditions, some of them dying out as the population declines - but every location and each tradition offers a window on an unseen world. At 6:30, during the opening reception, Anthropologist M. Gabriella Da Re (of the University of Cagliari) will give a brief talk on craftwork, culture and tradition: http://www.italianacademy.columbia.edu/events/2008_2009/Sardegna_Fa08_Da... This exhibition Part of the “Sardegna in NY” series of events.

Support - protect your investment over time and ensure that your custom development solutions continue to evolve as your department does. Consultation - Need help with your own development? Consult with our expert analysts to implement best practices, authentication and authorizations and data integration with university data sources. ADS has unparalleled experience in developing custom solutions, our expert development architects, project managers, and developers are able to quickly create secure, high-quality solutions. Enabling you to take advantage of new opportunities and dynamically respond to new business challenges. Mission ADS helps Texas Tech University improve student services, gain financial control, enhance operational visibility, improve strategic decision making, and reduce costs. Our mission is to analyze, design and provide solutions to support varied organizational processes, including eLearning, business operations, eCommerce solutions, integrated university authentication and custom development.

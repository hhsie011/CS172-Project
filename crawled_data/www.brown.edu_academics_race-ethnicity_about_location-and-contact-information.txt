CSREA is located at 96 Waterman Street in the Frederick Lippitt and Mary Ann Lippitt House. Our wheelchair accessible entrance can be found in the rear of the building, accessible via the South Walk or Thayer Street. As the lift requires a key, please contact us for assistance.

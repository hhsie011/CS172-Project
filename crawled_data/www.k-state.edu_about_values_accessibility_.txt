Report a barrier If you encounter a physical or virtual access barrier on campus, you can report a barrier to the Student Access Center. Accommodation requests For students If you are a student and require assistance or wish to report an issue to the accessibility of content on this website, please contact the Student Access Center, accesscenter@k-state.edu. For visitors If you are a visitor, contact the person, department or organization sponsoring the event. For faculty or staff If you are faculty or staff, contact Human Capital Services. Policies, standards and guidelines The following links provide information on accessibility-related policies, standards and guidelines: Course Accessibility Standards Policy Assistive technology The Student Access Center provides a wide range of accommodations and services are provided to students with documented disabilities. Consultations and learning opportunities Information Technology Services provides training and assistance to faculty members, including how to record lectures with Mediasite and adding closed-captioning. If you have questions about how to accommodate a specific student, contact the Student Access Center.

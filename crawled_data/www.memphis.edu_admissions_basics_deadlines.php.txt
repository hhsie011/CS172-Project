There are several steps for you (the applicant) and the Office of Admissions to complete before an application decision can be made. After you apply, the Office of Admissions will notify you if they need any additional information. Be sure to monitor both your regular mail (sent to the mailing address on file in the Office of Admissions) and your personal e-mail account. The priority deadline for scholarship consideration is December 1 for admitted students. Therefore, if you think you may be scholarship eligible, you are strongly encouraged to submit your application before November 1 of the year before your enrollment.

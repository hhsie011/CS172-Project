All applicants must include a contact number, valid email address and PeopleSoft I.D. number when applying for the scholarship. Please read the Staff Tuition Scholarship Program Guidelines located below. If you have questions please contact the HR Service Center at 713.743.3988. If you require time away from work to complete a course, you will need to complete and have your supervisor sign the College Program Application form.

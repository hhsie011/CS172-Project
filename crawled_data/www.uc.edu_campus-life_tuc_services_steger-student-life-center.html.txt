It opened in 2004 (Buzz Yudell and Glaserworks, architects), and is approximately 40 feet wide by 500 feet long. SSLC is home to multiple student identity centers (Ethnic Programs & Services, LGBTQ Center, Women’s Center), the Dean of Students Office, Student Activities & Leadership Development (SALD), Student Conduct & Community Standards, the Student Wellness Center, Parent & Family Programs, and Experienced-Based Learning & Career Education (ELCE).

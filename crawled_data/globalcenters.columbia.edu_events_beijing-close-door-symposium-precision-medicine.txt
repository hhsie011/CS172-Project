Precision Medicine Events Past Event Beijing Close-door Symposium on Precision Medicine Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo September 18, 2018 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing On September 18, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to a close-door symposium on precision medicine, in partnership with China AI 30 Forum. Representatives from academic institutions, industry, and investment will convene at the Beijing Center to exchange and share the frontier of medical knowledge on cancer treatment, human genetic diseases, and genomics. This symposium aims to deepen our understanding on difficulties and challenges in treatment of children's inherited diseases and to provide direction and strategy. In the meantime, this symposium is looking forward to more engagement and collaboration in research with domestic experts in neonatology, pediatric surgery, pediatric neurology, pediatric cardiology, cancer treatment, and genomics. Panelists Wendy Chung, Kennedy Family Professor of Pediatrics and Medicine, Columbia University Yufeng Shen, Assistant Professor of Systems Biology and Biomedical Informatics, Columbia University CHEN Xiaoli, Researcher at China's Capital Institute of Pediatrics XU Huji, Executive Vice Dean of Tsinghua University School of Clinical Medicine and Vice President of Beijing Tsinghua Changgung Hospital CUI Hong, Chief of Pediatrics, Beijing Friendship Hospital Guest Commentators LIU Xin, Beijing Institute of Genomics, Chinese Academy of Sciences ZHU Weiguo, Deputy Chief Physician in Internal Medicine and Chief of Information Division, Peking Union Medical College Hospital WANG Shuqiang, Deputy Director of the Consultant Department, Economic Reference, The Development Research Center of the State Council CAO Zongfu, Chief of Bioinformatics Division, Research Center at the National Health and Family Planning Commission of PRC XU Qian, Partner and General Manager of Beijing, Detong Capital LIU Xiao, Executive Director, Taikang Healthcare Investment Holdings SHI Guomin, General Manager, HG Capital Healthcare Fund JIANG Weina, Deputy General Manager, Health Work XIAO Zhi, Executive Director, SDIC Fund Management YU Yufei, Research Assistant, Institute of Genetics and Developmental Biology, Chinese Academy of Sciences LIU Xiaohua, Executive Director, Innovation Investment, Guotai Junan Securities ZENG Hui, Founder, China AI 30 Forum Agenda 12:30 -

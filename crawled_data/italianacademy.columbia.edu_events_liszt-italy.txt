The Fall 2011 concert series continues with a recital of Franz Liszt’s Années de Pèlerinage, Book Two: "Italy," in honor of Liszt’s bicentenary by pianist Kariné Poghosyan. The recital will include a reading of poems in Italian by Paolo Valesio, a poet and the Giuseppe Ungaretti Professor of Italian Literature at Columbia University.

Internal Applicants If you are a current Brown staff member, follow these steps to view and apply for an open position in Workday: Log in to Workday. From Your Home Landing Page, click the Career worklet. Click Find Jobs. Once you've submitted an application, click My Applications to view the status. To update your education or external job history, click the Education or External Job History buttons. Resources Brown staff members may access job aids and screencasts through Workday Learning for more information on applying for an internal position at Brown.

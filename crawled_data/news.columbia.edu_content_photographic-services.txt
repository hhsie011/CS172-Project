However, in some instances, she may assign an authorized freelance photographer to a project. Rates and billing procedures for freelance photographers remain the same, though in some cases, an additional charge may be incurred depending on the location of the event. Services Public Relations and Event Photography Portraits Framing Pricing Digital Photography $175 per hour, billed in one-hour increments, with a one-hour minimum $150 per photo disc; $50 per additional disc Photoshop Services (upon request) $100 per hour Framing Rates vary depending on type and material selected Rush Charges Normal turn-around is 48 hours for a disk or Dropbox services. $100 rush fee will apply for a complete shoot, delivery within 24 hours. $75 rush fee for 20 quick photos, delivery within 24 hours. Billing All services are billed directly to your departmental account. If off campus, invoice will be mailed. A cancellation fee of half the photographer's fee will be charged for any event canceled after the photographer is confirmed. All photographic material becomes the property of Columbia University and is accessible to you, at your request, at any time.

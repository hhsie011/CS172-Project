Carceral State Reading Group The Center for the Study of Slavery and Justice facilitates a year-long reading group which focuses on examining the Carceral State. Faculty Fellow Seminar This interdisciplinary seminar organized by Brown faculty is open to faculty, postdoctoral fellows, and advanced graduate students from any department. Each year, a faculty fellow is selected to lead the seminar based on a proposed theme.

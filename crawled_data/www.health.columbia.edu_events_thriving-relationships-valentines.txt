Oh no. This is an opportunity to share kind, affirming, and in some cases "punny" messages around consent and thriving relationships with the people you care about. On your way to class, the dining hall, to study, or wherever you may be going, swing by the Lerner ramps and pick up SVR's Thriving Relationships Valentines for your special person or people. And for the first 100 people to stop by, you'll also receive a sweet treat!

This approach is crucial to understanding the neuronal substrate of the emotions we experience when viewing a work of art. The sessions provided a lively day of provocative discussion on the basis of the latest research in these areas. Speakers included: Marlene Behrmann (Carnegie Mellon) James Bisley (UCLA) Anna Ipata (Columbia researcher and 2007-8 Fellow at the Academy) Earl Miller (MIT) Kevin Ochsner (Columbia) Elizabeth Phelps (NYU) John Reynolds (Salk Institute) Daniel Salzman (Columbia) Jeremy Wolfe (Harvard) and Steven Yantis (Johns Hopkins) as well as Academy Director David Freedberg.

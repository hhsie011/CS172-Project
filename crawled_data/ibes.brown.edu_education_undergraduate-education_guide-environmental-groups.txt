A collaborative platform through which engineers, designers, entrepreneurs, policy makers, academics, and students engage in the free sharing of ideas and form lasting connections that lead to real-world change. Brown Climate Action Now A coalition of members of the Brown University community committed to fighting for climate justice. Brown is Green Charts the University's progress toward greenhouse gas reduction and includes updates about related courses, initiatives, research, community projects and the work of student groups. Brown Market Shares Student-run, campus-based food distribution program connecting the Brown community with regional producers through affordable weekly shares of fresh, local, and sustainable produce, dairy, eggs and meat. Ecosystem Management Initiative Recognizing the declining health of the world’s oceans, policy makers, managers and scientists have called for expanded efforts at ecosystem-based management in marine and coastal systems (MEBM). Spatial Structures in Social Sciences Through its faculty affiliates and graduate fellows, the mission of S4 is to stimulate and support new work in this emerging interdisciplinary arena. Sustainability & Student Groups Many student groups at Brown promote energy and environmental initiatives and have spurred significant progress towards a more sustainable world on and off campus. These student groups represent efforts spanning a diverse array of environmental issues and advocacy approaches.&nbsp; From composting to climate change, poetry to policy, there is a student group at Brown that can inspire your environmental enthusiasm. Sunrise Brown & RISD The Sunrise Movement hub at Brown University and the Rhode Island School of Design is mobilizing young people and college students to fight for climate justice and create millions of good jobs.

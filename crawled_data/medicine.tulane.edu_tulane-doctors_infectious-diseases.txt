Certain faculty specialize in travel and tropical medicine, tuberculosis and solid organ transplant infections. All of our faculty participate in teaching medical students, residents and fellows, and many are involved in clinical research ranging from HIV clinical trials to tropical diseases. Tulane’s CD4 Clinic is committed to providing quality healthcare focusing on health promotion, disease management, and the educational needs of persons living with HIV. Services Include Comprehensive Medical Management Compassionate Nursing Care Case Management Laboratory Services Mental Health Services Infectious Disease Consultant Medication Support Services Referral Services Testing & Counseling Health/ Nutrition Education Dental services The faculty of the Tulane University School of Medicine Infectious Diseases Section provide inpatient and outpatient care to patients at the locations listed below.

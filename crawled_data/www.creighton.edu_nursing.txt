College of Nursing Menu About Academics Future Students Admitted Students Alumni A nursing education for a better, healthier world. The Latin phrase cura personalis, or “care of the person,” means a lot to us in the College of Nursing. We provide the diverse skills and career guidance that all nursing students need to become life-changing professionals. We offer programs at the bachelor’s (BSN), master’s (MSN) and doctoral (DNP) levels—along with a variety of post-graduate APRN certificates—to meet you wherever you are today and help you advance in your career.

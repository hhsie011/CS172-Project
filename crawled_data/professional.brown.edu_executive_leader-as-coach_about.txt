Brown and ACT are seeking to expand and build on the success of the Leadership + Performance Coaching Certification (LPCC) program and offer a virtual LAC program to leadership professionals. ACT Leadership, founded in 2012, uses a systematic approach to professional development using the Assess Coach Train methodology, targeted at individuals, teams and organizations. ACT’s exceptional team teaches critical skills and experience in coaching, training, and consulting. ACT has trained over thousands of leaders in the Leader as Coach approach, helping individuals, teams and organizations shift to a more sustainable approach to leadership. “ We chose to partner with ACT Leadership because we share a common mission, to develop effective, reflective, and inclusive leaders, to effect positive change in the world and to improve human welfare. ” Leah VanWey Dean, School of Professional Studies The Brown Difference Our commitment to educational innovation, diversity and inclusion, collaboration and making an impact in the world is at the core of what we do.

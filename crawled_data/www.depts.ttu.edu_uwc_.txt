Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Women's Club Menu Search TTU University Women's Club University Women's Club The University Women's Club is open to all women in faculty/staff positions or wives of faculty/staff members during their connection with Texas Tech University and/or other institutions within the Texas Tech University System. The University Women's Club has both social and educational objectives, as well as an overall focus that unites its members. This focus is the Paul Whitfield Horn Fellowship Endowment, which funds scholarships for women graduate students. The University Women's Club contributes to the funding of this endowment through dues and various fundraisers.

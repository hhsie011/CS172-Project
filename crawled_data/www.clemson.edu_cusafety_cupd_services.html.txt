This service allows students to register the serial numbers of their valuables for easy access in the event any of these items are lost or stolen. Lost and Found CUPD acts as a central site for lost items. To report a lost or found item call 864-656-0433 or come by CUPD headquarters at 124 Ravenel Center Place, Seneca, SC 29678 between the hours of 8:30 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday. Crime Victim Services Crime victims receive continued information and assistance as provided by law. The department employs a Victims' Advocate to serve you. For more information about victim's rights and services Call Victim Advocate Sergeant Michelle Young at 864-656-5256. Fingerprinting For members of the Clemson University community who require fingerprints for prospective employers, security clearances and bar applications, the CU Police Department offers fingerprinting services Monday-Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Please call 864-656-2222 prior to coming to CUPD headquarters at 124 Ravenel Center Place, Seneca, SC 29678 to ensure that CUPD personnel are available to assist you. You may be asked to make an appointment. The cost of fingerprinting service is free for students. Applicants must present a photo ID (driver's license or passport) and a Clemson University ID. All fingerprint cards should be printed in black ink. Fingerprint cards should be provided by the requesting authority. Tiger Transit Safe rides are provided by Clemson University Parking and Transit Services via Tiger Transit to university students, faculty, staff and visitors between 6 p.m. and 6 a.m. to and from any campus location. To request Tiger Transit call 864-656-3333 to schedule a ride. During peak traffic times surrounding home football games, this service may not be available.

Some of the results and impact of these projects can be viewed by visiting our Media Coverage page or via UW News. The initiative also supported the UW Graduate School’s quarterly livestream production, “Coexisting with COVID-19,” which sought to educate and raise public awareness of some of the key challenges posed by the pandemic. Leading experts – including Drs. Anthony S. Fauci and Moncef Slaoui – in vaccine science, health metrics, policy, regulation and communications came together to share insights and explore key issues related to planning for COVID-19 vaccination.

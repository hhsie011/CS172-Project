If you have encountered a concern and would like it addressed, please submit your report on the online form below. Reporting an Accessibility or Accommodation Concern Form or call 401-863-9588. You will receive confirmation that your request has been submitted and we will inform you as to how the concern has been addressed. For any questions about web accessibility email: [email protected] Concerns about physical accessibility can also be submitted directly to the Department of Facilities Management through their online request service or by calling 401-863-7800. Some examples of access concerns that it may be helpful to report directly to Facilities Management are: an automatic door opener that is not functioning a locked door that prevents access missing or unclear signage articles blocking the entrance to a lift Thank you for taking the time to let us know about your concern!

Agyepong Distinguished Lecture on Public Health in Africa featuring a conversation on Africa’s Public Health Agenda by Dr. Agnes Binagwaho and Dr. Muhammad Ali Pate. Dr. Agnes Binagwaho is Vice Chancellor and co-founder of the University of Global Health Equity (UGHE), former Minister of Health in Rwanda, and Senior Lecturer in the Department of Global Health and Social Medicine at Harvard Medical School. Dr. Muhammad Ali Pate is the Julio Frenk Professor of Public Health Leadership, Department of Global Health and Population, Harvard T.H Chan School of Public Health.

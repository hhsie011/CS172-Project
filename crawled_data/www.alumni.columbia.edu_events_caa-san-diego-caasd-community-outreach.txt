We will participate with the “I Love A Clean San Diego” Organization in their “20th Annual Creek to Bay Cleanup” on Saturday, April 23. CAASD will help to clean-up and remove litter, debris, and invasive plants along Lower Rose Creek at Mission Bay High School, Pacific Beach starting at 9:00 am and finishing around 11:30 am. Please bring your work gloves, buckets, and your reusable water bottles to fill up; wear long pants and sturdy, comfortable shoes. This is a family-friendly event; children welcome!

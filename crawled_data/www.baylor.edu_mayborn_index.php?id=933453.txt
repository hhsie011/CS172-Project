Memberships are non-refundable and non-transferable. Membership purchase must be made in the name of up to two adults residing in the same household. Gift membership options are also available. Benefits of Membership All Member levels enjoy: 12 months of Membership Benefits (memberships are valid for one year from the date of purchase) 10% member discount in the museum store Discounts Baylor faculty, staff and students receive a 20% discount on any level of membership they purchase for themselves. Please contact Misty Thornton at (254)710-1180 for these discount options. Military receive a 10% discount on any level of membership they purchase. Must be purchased at the Mayborn to receive discount. Payment Options For your convenience, you may purchase memberships in the following ways: Pay in person at the museum with credit card, personal check, or cash. Mail your payment to us at Attn. Misty Thornton One Bear Place #97154 Waco, TX 76798-7154. Buy Online For other membership information or to purchase by phone, call or email Misty Thornton at (254)710-1180.

513.556.6236 tricia.yee@uc.edu Education B.A. English & Journalism, Minor in Marketing, Miami University A little about me... I am the Coordinator of Special Projects and Program for the University Honors Program. It is my job to take care of everything that happens behind the scenes. I also serve as a Wellness Ambassador for the UC wellness program, Be Well UC. I am a graduate of Miami University in Oxford, Ohio with a degree in English, Journalism and a minor in Marketing. I have been with the Honors program since 2007 and with the University of Cincinnati since 2005. When I am not in the Honors office my hobbies include follwing the Cubs and the NBA, watching super hero movies with my son, and cooking. Ask me about... My beautiful family My morning walks My happy planner My new puppy Advice for students My biggest advice to Honors students is to always read the Honors Newsletter!

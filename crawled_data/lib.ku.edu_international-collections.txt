International Collections KU Libraries are a strong supporter of international and language studies. For decades, KU librarians and staff have partnered with KU language departments and area studies programs to assemble diverse and unique materials from all over the world in support of research and learning at KU. The collections consist of over 1.5 million volumes and electronic objects and is known for its regional strengths for Africa, East Asia, Latin America, Russia, Eastern Europe, the Caucasus, and Central Asia. International Collections librarians and staff provide a suite of services, including: Research consultations with specialists in each world region Instructional support for KU courses related to world regions and international topics Online guides of recommended resources to help students get started with their research Resources for K-12 teachers about world regions Library tours in selected languages Regions & Collections African East Asian European Slavic and Eurasian Spanish, Portuguese, Latin American and Caribbean Global, Middle Eastern, and South Asian Online Exhibits Celebrating East Asian Scholarship: An Interdisciplinary Showcase Nobel Laureates In Literature Around The Globe Swahili Literature Through the Centuries Onitsha Market Literature International Collections Contacts About the Collections African East Asian Slavic and Eurasian Spanish, Portuguese, Latin American & Caribbean European Global Studies Location & Hours Watson Library, 5th Floor 1425 Jayhawk Blvd, Rm. 519 Lawrence, KS 66045 Monday - Friday 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. Watson Library 1425 Jayhawk Blvd. Lawrence, KS 66045 libwebhelp@ku.edu 785-864-8983 facebook instagram twitter youtube Libraries website feedback KU Libraries Find & Access Borrow & Request Locations & Hours Research & Teaching Ask a Librarian Events & Exhibits About Give Nondiscrimination statement Accessibility| Website support| CMS login Â© 2022 The University of Kansas The University of Kansas is a public institution governed by the Kansas Board of Regents.

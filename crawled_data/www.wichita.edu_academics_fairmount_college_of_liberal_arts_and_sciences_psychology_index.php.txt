B.A in Psychology Graduate Programs Request Information Psych Awards Newsletter About Us Psychology Department The Wichita State University Psychology Department is committed to conducting applied research to enhance psychological science. At our 14 dedicated research laboratories, groups and clinics, faculty members and students work side by side on projects that advance the field of psychology in real and meaningful ways. With a talented and active faculty providing quality undergraduate and graduate teaching, WSU psychology students develop a solid research orientation and deep connections to the professional community.

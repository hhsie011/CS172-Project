It comprises a group of diverse staff from across various University offices and academic departments. What We Do In support of its mission, SAC’s guiding principles are to: Act as advisers to the president and senior administrators with the aim of creating a positive working environment for all University employees; Provide staff input into Brown University’s decision‐making processes that directly affect staff; Select staff members to serve on important institutional committees; Provide input on policies and procedures which pertain to or impact staff and the University community; Establish committees and ad hoc groups to develop activities that strive to continually improve communication and morale through special educational, recreational, informational, and community service events; Create networking opportunities for staff across the University; Encourage social responsibility and service to the University and the surrounding community; Provide a supportive network to collaborate with the University in communicating and achieving its goals. For more details, read the SAC bylaws.

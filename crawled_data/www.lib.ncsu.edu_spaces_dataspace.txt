Hunt Jr. Library Floor: Third Floor Outfitted with specialized hardware and software, and staffed by Data Science Consultants, the Dataspace provides access to the tools and expertise needed to develop and use critical data science skills. Furniture Work table and office chairs Computer pod Sound Level Conversation and chatter Technology Dataspace 4K 55" QLED Monitor Mac Data Desktop Wacom Cintiq 22HD Creative Pen Display Windows Advanced Data Desktop Windows Data Desktop How do I get there? Using Elevators Go through the library gates past Ask Us Continue straight until you reach the blue or third hallway to your right. Turn right into the hallway for the elevators. Take the elevators to the third floor. After leaving the elevator bay, turn right and continue straight until you pass the stairs to the second floor on your right. The Dataspace will be on your left opposite the Game Lab. Using Stairs Go through the library gates past Ask Us Take the yellow stairs on your right to the third floor. Turn right and Dataspace will be in on your left opposite the Game Lab. Who do I contact? Contact us to get data science help or if you have questions about the Dataspace.

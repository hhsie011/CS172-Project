Break and again on Jan. 17, 2022 for Martin Luther King Day. Classes resume on Jan. 10, 2022. All OSU-Tulsa offices including Admissions, Advising, Academic Services, Bursar, Campus Life, Career Services, Enrollment, Financial Aid, Graduate Student Services and Prospective Students will be closed during this period. Computer workstations in Main Hall are available for use during Winter Break during regular building hours except on Christmas and New Year's Day. Access to Main Hall is via swipe card access only through Commons entrances. Students, faculty and staff without an OSU ID and needing access should call OSU-Tulsa Police at 918-594-8123.

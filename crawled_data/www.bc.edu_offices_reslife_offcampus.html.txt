Contact Office: Maloney Hall 413 Phone: 617-552-3075 Email: offcampus@bc.edu Facebook: fb.com/BCOffCampus/ Twitter: @BC_OffCampus Instagram: @BC_OffCampus Blogger: bcoffcampus.blogspot.com Residential Life Main Off-Campus Housing Database No matter if you are a student, staff, faculty, or alum, the Off-Campus Housing Database will prove essential to your housing search! Want to review apartment listings in the area? Secured a rental, but in need of roommates? The Off-Campus Housing Database allows you to find both, add sublet listings, review resources, and more! Log in using your Agora Portal username and password, or register as a "Guest" if you await those credentials. If you have any questions, please contact us at 617-552-3075 or offcampus@bc.edu.

We offer more than 200 semester, academic year and short-term programs in over 60 countries worldwide to meet the diverse needs of the University of Memphis student population. The UofM study abroad staff, located in the International Center room 104, are here to advise students, parents, and community members about study abroad program options, policies, and procedures as well as scholarship opportunities. Mission University of Memphis study abroad programs provide students with the opportunity to combine a rigorous academic program with a cross-cultural learning experience that is not available in the United States.

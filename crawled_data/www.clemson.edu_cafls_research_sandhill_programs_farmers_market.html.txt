Please observe all health and safety protocols. We are looking forward to a robust and productive season. The market is home to farmers from around the region. Items available include fresh local produce, meat, fish, eggs, butter, milk, cheese, honey, plants, flowers, shrubs, and baked goods. Many products are organic. Also present are several skilled crafters and artisans with a variety of offerings. For up-to-date information, sign up for our electronic market newsletter, published weekly during the season. The sign-up link is located on the right. For information on becoming a market vendor select the vendor information and vendor application in the right navigation pane.

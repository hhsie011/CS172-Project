Admission Requirements None Degree Requirements Students who change degree programs and select this major must adopt the most current catalog. Students must earn at least a "C" (2.0) in each required course. Students should consult with a departmental advisor. Departmental Residency Requirement consists of at least 15 semester hours of regularly scheduled 3000-4000 level courses taken from the UCF English Department. Courses designated in General Education Program and Common Program Prerequisites are usually completed in the first 60 hours.

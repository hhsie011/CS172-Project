Alumni Giving Home Admissions and Education Professional Training Certifications Certifications Menu Professional Training Professional Training Certifications Continuing Medical Education Observerships Medical professionals need to evolve with the practice of medicine. In order to do that they must expand their skillset and broaden their knowledge in the field. The University of Miami Miller School of Medicine certificate programs are designed to do just that. Gaining certification will help you to advance in your career and empower you to better serve patients and advance the field. Our certificates are non-credit programs that include a series of modules packaged together to address specific professional skills.

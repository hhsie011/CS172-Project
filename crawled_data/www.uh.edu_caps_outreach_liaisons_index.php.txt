We can: provide consultation about how to address a student who may be experiencing mental health concerns provide information on how to refer to CAPS for assistance act as a direct referral source for psychotherapy provide training/presentations specifically tailored to your area; and/or attend one of your department meetings to speak with your whole team about mental health resources Please email your liaison directly from the list below or via our outreach request form. If your college, department, or student organization is not represented, contact us to establish a CAPS liaison for your area. If you have any specific updates and/or corrections regarding your information on this page, please email Turia Abdulrahman.

Committee Parking & Transportation Library Assistance Wheelchair Stairlift Library employees are ready to assist all students, guests, and visitors. Any individual with a disability may inquire about assistance at the main information desk located at the entrance of the Otto J. Richter Library. Individuals may call ahead with requests to (305) 284-3234. Staff from the first floor main circulation desk will assist with retrieving books or journals from stack shelves. On weekends and holidays, when library staffing is limited, requests for assistance may take longer. In addition, students may use the e-mail reference service of the library system: Ask a Librarian.

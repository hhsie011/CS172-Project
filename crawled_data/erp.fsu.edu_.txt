From human resources to student admissions, ERP is responsible for the development, administration and ongoing support and improvement of mission-critical, enterprise systems and applications used by the university community. In other words, we act as the liaison between campus units and their information systems. Our goal is to implement software solutions that enable more effective and efficient business processes. Day in and day out, we collaborate with campus departments to deliver the tools needed to study and work at Florida State University and maximize student, faculty and staff success. Talk with us to find out how we can help achieve your business goals.

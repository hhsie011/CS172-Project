Herbert Business School alumni. Here you can learn more about how you can stay connected and support the continued growth of the Business School. Stay connected to – or get back in touch with – fellow classmates. Share your experiences, mentor a student, compete for prize money in the School's annual Business Plan Competition, provide information on internships and job openings, or make a donation. You can also take advantage of career services, as well as other services offered to UM alumni. Miami Herbert 'Canes +45,000 Miami Herbert Alumni One-of-a-kind Celebrations 130 Countries Represented by Alumni Miami Herbert Alumni represent a global community. Alumni Association The Miami Herbert experience doesn't end at commencement. After graduating, our students become lifetime members of a global alumni community with more than 40,000 members in 130 countries, along with more than 178,000 UM alumni worldwide. No matter where you are across the US or around the world, a loyal group of Hurricanes is just around the corner. The global UM Alumni Association charters 'Canes Communities to reach out to alumni, families and friends to keep that green and orange bond alive! Learn More Opportunities for Alumni Entrepreneurs Miami Herbert alumni can participate in the School's annual Business Plan Competition in many ways, from competing in the Alumni Category to supporting the competition as judges and sponsors. Josh Fu (pictured right), who graduated from the School in 2010 as an international finance and marketing major, won third place and $4,000 in the 2017 competition for Haathi Cloth, which aims to produce modern, comfortable and machine-washable kurtas, an outfit worn at Indian weddings and dance competitions. Learn More The Launch Pad The Launch Pad at the University of Miami offers free resources for alumni entrepreneurs, along with current students. Since 2008, The Launch Pad has helped with more than 3,700 clients, more than 2,400 ideas, and more than 350 companies, all while creating more than 1,100 jobs while working with alumni and students. Learn More Support to Advance Your Career The UM Alumni Association (UMAA) offers programs, resources, and relationships for alumni to manage professional success. Whether you are currently searching for a position, considering a career change, seeking fresh ideas and talent for your business, or available to offer networking advice, you can find a wealth of resources on UMAA’s career website. Learn More Lifelong Learning Miami Herbert offers executive education programs that empower individuals and organizations to succeed on the global stage. Our customized training and development programs are designed to enhance the professional knowledge and management skills of key managers and senior executives. The School also offers open enrollment certificate programs in general business which enable professionals to add basic business courses to their portfolios.

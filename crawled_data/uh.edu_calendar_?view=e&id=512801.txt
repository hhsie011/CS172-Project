Submit an Event Instagram Takeover: Layla M. Bispo Thursday, May 12, 2022 11:00 am - 11:00 pm Trans-disciplinary visual artist Layla M. Bispo (@lambiislambiis) is taking over the Blaffer’s Instagram for 3 days—Bispo’s work explores the ways in which the elements of time, space, gender, immigration, neurodivergence, and sensory input affect perception and identity. Bispo is currently a second-year graduate student studying Interdisciplinary Practices and Emerging Forms, a docent at the Blaffer Art Museum, a member of G5 Artist Collective, and chairperson of Third Space Gallery, UH. Bispo’s solo show “Crying Room Only” will have its closing ceremony at 5:30PM on Thursday, May 26, 2022 in Gallery 2 at Elgin Street Studios.

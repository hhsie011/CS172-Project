Are you an expectant parent or do you have a child under the age of 5 years? We help guide you to the early education or child care setting that best meets the needs of your child and your family by providing information on options including: Center-based care, including Affiliated Early Learning Centers Group family day care NYC Department of Education tuition-free pre-Kindergarten programs (for 3 and 4 year-olds only) In-home care Do you have a school-age child, 5 years or older? We collaborate with you to develop an understanding of the schooling environment(s) that would be a good fit for your child and we support you as you explore your choices by providing information on the range of K-12 schooling options available to families living in NYC, including: Independent Public Charter Faith-based Special education Boarding Information Request Form Please explore the content provided on our website or, to request more information on early education, child care or K-12 schooling, please complete an information request form (for Columbia affiliates only).

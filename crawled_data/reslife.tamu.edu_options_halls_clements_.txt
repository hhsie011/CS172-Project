Clements Hall is named after Rita Crocker Clements and was constructed in 1981. Ms. Clements was the wife of former Texas governor Bill Clements. Ms. Clements was a rancher’s daughter who was extremely interested in attending Texas A&M University; however, in 1949, A&M didn’t admit women. As Texas’s First Lady, Ms. Clements was a model of attainment for young women. 2021 – 2022 Rates Modular Hall $3,708semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Room Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same room are the same gender.

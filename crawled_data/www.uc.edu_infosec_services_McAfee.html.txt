McAfee ePO software provides flexible, automated management capabilities so you identify, manage, and respond to security issues and threats. The McAfee ePO server is the central software repository for all McAfee product installations, updates, and other content. The module design of ePO allows new products to be added as extensions. This includes new or updated versions of McAfee and McAfee compatible solutions from the Security Innovation Alliance. If your area is interested in utilizing ePO please send your request to infosec@uc.edu. We currently offer the following products through our McAfee ePO server: Expand All Endpoint Security Expand Endpoint Security combines anti-virus, anti-spyware, firewall, and intrusion prevention technologies to stop and remove malicious software. It also extends coverage to new security risks and reduces the cost of responding to outbreaks with the industry's lowest impact on system performance. Expand All MOVE Expand Management for Optimized Virtual Environments (MOVE) AntiVirus brings optimized, advanced virus protection to your virtualized desktops and servers, including your virtual environment in the cloud. Choose one efficient solution across multiple vendor platforms or an agentless, tuned option for VMware vShield. Expand All File and Removable Media Protection Expand File and Removable Media Protection (FRMP) ensures specific files and folders are always encrypted, regardless of where data is edited, copied, or saved. FRMP automatically and transparently encrypts the files and folders you choose before they move through our organization. Expand All Management of Native Encryption Expand Management of Native Encryption allows customers to manage the native encryption functionality offered by Apple FileVault on Macintosh OS X and Microsoft BitLocker on Windows platforms directly from McAfee ePolicy Orchestrator (ePO) software.

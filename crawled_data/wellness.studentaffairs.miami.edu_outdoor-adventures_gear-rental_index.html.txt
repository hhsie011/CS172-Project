To ensure you get the gear you need for your adventures, we highly recommended making advanced reservations by calling us at 305-284-5116. Convenient loading and unloading are available at the Lakeside Village loading dock. Be sure to check our gear rental hours of operation. We also have videos about how to set up and use our gear. Plan Your Adventures Not sure where to go or what gear you need, our experienced team can help you plan your trip. Whether you want a few recommendations about where to stand up paddleboard or camp all the way to planning the whole trip including meals and activities, we are here to help you have a successful and fun adventure. Get started by checking out curated maps of adventure locations in and around Miami. Refunds are not given for gear rentals once rented. Tents must be set up by the renter upon return. If not, a $5 fee is charged. Renters may use the Outdoor Adventure's hose upon drop off to clean any dirty or sandy gear. The late fee is twice the daily rate per item per day late. Lakeside Village Residents must pay for rentals if there is a duplicate of an item. For instance, if an LSV resident rents two paddleboards, the first one is free while the second is $18 per day. Membership is not required for faculty or staff to rent, however, membership offers discounted rental prices. Visit our membership page for details.

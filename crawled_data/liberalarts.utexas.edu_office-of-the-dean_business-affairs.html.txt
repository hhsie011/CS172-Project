If you have questions please contact one of our staff members. Business Affairs performs a wide variety of administrative functions. The office's main responsibilities include: Processing budgetary and financial information for the College of Liberal Arts Serving as a liaison between the College and UT administrative units Auditing all financial activity on dean's accounts and college endowments Monitoring compliance with UT and College of Liberal Arts procedures Facilitating faculty and staff understanding of University of Texas administrative procedures Overseeing the process of faculty recruitment and retention for all units/departments within the college Serving as an official steward for contracts involving the college Assisting departments/units with purchasing procedures Staff Business Affairs Org Chart Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Button Text Goes Here Centralized Business Services The College of Liberal Arts - Centralized Business Services (CBS) assists numerous centers and special projects with centralized, administrative support.

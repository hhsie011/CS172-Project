Students may choose to suppress their in-session telephone number and/or Iowa State e-mail address, or they may choose to suppress all of their information, which means they will no longer appear in the ISU Directory Information or have their information released to outside organizations or persons. Once a request to suppress student information has been submitted via AccessPlus, the ISU Directory Information will update overnight, Monday-Friday only. Changes made on Saturday or Sunday will update Monday overnight. If you have any questions about student information, contact the Office of the Registrar at registrar@iastate.edu.

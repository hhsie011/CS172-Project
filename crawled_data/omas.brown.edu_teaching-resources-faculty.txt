Veterans make up 0.3% of the undergraduate student body, compared with 3% at other area schools at 1% at private universities. Represent 22 concentrations, most popular are: International Relations BEO Computer Science Middle Eastern Studies Eighty percent of student veterans are older than age 25. Many student vets are married and several have children.

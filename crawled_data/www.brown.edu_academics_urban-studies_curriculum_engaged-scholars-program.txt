The ESC combines preparation, experience, and reflection to offer students opportunities to enhance the integration of academic learning and social engagement. Students interested in pursuing an ESC should review the Swearer Center’s ESC page and Engaged Scholarship Certificate Pamphlet for full details on requirements and timelines. Any questions about the ESC should directed to engaged-s[email protected]. Students can also schedule an office hours appointment with Lindsey "Coco" Co, the Engaged Scholarship Program Manager, or one of the Engaged Scholars Peer Advisors.

SHUTTLES & TRANSPORTATION Shuttles & Transportation The Tulane University Shuttles and Transportation system helps you travel safely and easily to, from, and around our campuses. Providing over 260,000 rides annually to the Tulane University community, we offer commuter connections to faculty, staff, and students between the uptown, downtown, Elmwood and University Square campuses. Tulane TapRide, an on-demand, point-to-point van service, is available 7 nights a week.

FGTMP includes a First-Generation Peer Mentorship Program that provides peer mentorship to undergraduate students. The Peer Mentor position is a paid position that is filled by currently enrolled TTU Undergraduate students. Apply here for Undergraduate Work-Study Apply here for Undergraduate Non-Work-Study Apply here for Graduate Work-Study Apply here for Graduate Non-Work Study First-Gen Peer Mentors: Are sophomore, junior, or senior students. Are assigned a caseload of first-generation students. Meet with their assigned students in one-on-one meetings to build a connection, help them navigate campus, and share resources for their success. Participate in FGTMP and Division of Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion training, activities, and events. Minimum Requirements: Applicant must: Be a First Generation Student (TTU defines a first-generation student as a student who did not have either parent(s) or guardian(s) earn a Bachelor's degree in the United States). Be an undergraduate or a graduate student who has completed at least 2 semesters at Texas Tech. Be able to dedicate 10-15 hours per week to the Peer Mentor position. Have a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.75. Preferred Requirements Have been an active participant in FGTMP Peer Mentorship Program in a previous semester. Will be enrolled for both Fall 2022 and Spring 2023 semesters and can commit to the position for 2 semesters. Compensation $10.00 hourly. 15 hours maximum per week. Work-Study and Non-Work-Study positions available. Mandatory Fall and Spring Peer Mentor Training All Peer Mentors are required to attend mandatory fall and spring Peer Mentor Training. This typically happens the week before the first week of class. Peer Mentor Training provides a space for the FGTMP Leadership Team to share updates on department/university goals, roles & responsibilities as well as an opportunity for the peer mentors to connect as a team. Application & Interviews FGTMP recruits for new Peer Mentors each fall and spring semester. Applicants will be considered if they have submitted all application materials by the deadline. Applicants that are being considered will receive an email invitation to move forward for an interview. Interviews will take place from April 8-20 and offers will be made the week of April 25-29.

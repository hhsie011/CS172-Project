The group hosts lectures from LGBTQ STEM professionals (e.g. Microsoft, BP, Chevron), monthly study nights with snacks, and year-round social events. Contact: utexas@chapters.ostem.org Queer Graduate Student Association (QGSA) The Queer Grad student Alliance is an agency of the UT’s Graduate Student Assembly. QGSA seeks to promote community across the entire UT campus for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer and questioning graduate students and their allies. They seek to provide social events and other relevant programming to give graduate students the opportunity to connect outside of their individual colleges. Contact: utqgsa@gmail.com Black Voices: A Supportive Group for Black Students This weekly discussion group is open to Black-identified students, regardless of nationality or racial/ethnic background. From race relations to career development and from cultural appropriation to sexuality and dating in the Black community, students are welcome to discuss any topic that is relevant to their experiences. Topics may include, but are not limited to, the current sociopolitical climate, the psychosocial impact of racial injustice, systemic issues that historically and currently marginalize and oppress the Black community, religious and spiritual development and identity development in the Black community. Contact: christina.alexander@austin.utexas.edu Latinx Voices: A Supportive Space for Latinx Students This weekly discussion group is open to all Latin- identified students, regardless of identities, nationality, or racial/ethnic background and/or immigration status. The group provides a safe and supportive atmosphere in which participants can engage in inviting and authentic discussion around students’ experiences as Latinx students on the UT campus. Any and all topics that are relevant to students’ experiences can be discussed such as sociopolitical issues, immigration status or conditions, intersecting identities, academics, and more. Students’ privacy will be respected. Contact: Joanna.menendez@austin.utexas.edu Black Graduate Student Association The Black Graduate Student Association (BGSA) is an interdisciplinary organization that aims to empower and provide support to Black graduate students at the University of Texas at Austin. Through academic programming, professional development, community engagement, and social events, BGSA addresses the needs of Black graduate students while also serving as a safe space for learning and discussion. We recognize the richness and diversity of African and African American cultures, and celebrate the varying expressions of all graduate students on UT’s campus that descend from Africa and the African diaspora. Our goal is to inspire community and collaboration. Students of all majors and backgrounds are welcome.

Guides Find Your Librarian Attend Workshops Writing Support ☰ Libraries Borrow & Request Alumni Services Alumni Services Welcome back to the SMU Libraries! We are pleased to support your lifelong learning and educational development. Request an Alumni Card Benefits Borrowing privileges at all SMU Libraries Access to select research databases Cost Free for Cox School of Business, Perkins School of Theology, and Graduate Program in Religious Studies (GPRS) Alumni $35 per year for all other Alumni Policies Borrowed materials may be recalled, resulting in a shorter loan period. Items will be automatically renewed unless there is a hold or recall on the item. Alumni borrowers are unable to place holds on books checked out by another patron.

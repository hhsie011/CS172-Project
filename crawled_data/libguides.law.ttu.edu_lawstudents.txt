Texas Tech University School of Law Library LibGuides Student Services About the Law Library Search this Guide Search Student Services: About the Law Library A guide to Library Services for TTU Law students About the Law Library Law Library Collections Instructional Opportunities Reference Services Circulation Services Carrels Group Study Rooms Information Technology Support Contact Us About TTU Law Library The Texas Tech University School of Law Library (“Law Library”) is the largest legal information center in the area, covering western Texas, eastern New Mexico, and southern Oklahoma. The Law Library and its faculty and staff serve and support the educational, instructional, and research needs of the School of Law. The Law Library’s website offers information about library hours, staff, services, programs, and access to electronic databases. Law Library and Reference Hours Law School ID cards provide law student and law faculty access to the Law Library 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The Library is open to the public and members of the legal community during regular operating hours. Please Note: Special hours are set for holidays and inter-session periods. For updates, visit the Library’s website or the Library Hours calendar.

The former fellows exhibiting are Juan David Hernandez '21, Bicheng Liang '21 & Yixuan Shao '21, Cara Lynch '20, Erica Mao '20, and Farah Mohammad '21. They each have their work on display alongside selected artists, Gregory Amenoff, Dana Hoey, Michael Joo, Shahzia Sikander, and Kiki Smith. Each former fellow's work is juxtaposed with editioned works by their selected artists to simulate conversation. Catlin asks the viewer to consider, mentorship, influence and medium.

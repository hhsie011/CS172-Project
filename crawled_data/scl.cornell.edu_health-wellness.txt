Student & Campus Life inspires and supports students towards the development of their best selves—in mind, body and spirit – and promotes their resilience and continued growth. Student & Campus Life fosters students’ readiness to learn and participate fully in the Cornell experience by creating an environment where they can thrive, flourish, and live life with vitality and meaning. Our ability to be role models and support each other’s health and well-being is a critical component in creating success. Cornell has offices, services and organizations in every corner of the campus that offer health and wellness assistance, support, intervention, reporting, and advocacy. Cornell Health is a nationally recognized leader in health care with an integrated approach to well-being. Services provide at Cornell Health include (but are not limited to): Counseling & Mental Health, Primary Care, Nutrition & Healthy Eating Services, Lab Testing & X-Rays, Sexual Health Care, Student Disability Services, Gender Services, Sports Medicine and other areas of integrated medical and mental health care. Cornell Health is just one element in our efforts to care for our community. Simply put: Cornellians are committed to taking care of ourselves and looking out for one another. Visit Cornell Health for more information and helpful links. Cornell is a caring community because of the efforts we all make — faculty, staff, and students — to seek help for ourselves and to offer support to others.

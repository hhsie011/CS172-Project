+86-10-82483997 / 3992 Fax: : +86-10-62680598 Email: [email protected] From Beijing Capital International Airport By Airport Express Train and Subway: From Beijing Capital International Airport, take the Airport express Train to Sanyuanqiao（三元桥） Station. Transfer to subway line 10 and head west to Suzhoujie（苏州街） Station. Exit at Exit B and walk along Haidian South road for about 200 meters until reach Caihefang（彩和坊） Road. Turn left and walk north on Caihefang Road for about 600 meters. You will see Core Plaza（立方庭） on the right side of the street and the entrance to Columbia Global Centers | Beijing is on the south of the main Core Plaza entrance. The walk is approximately 10 minutes. By Taxi: From Beijing Capital International Airport, follow the exit sign for a taxi. Fares start at 13 RMB for the first 3 kilometers and 2.3 RMB is added for each additional kilometer. Taxi fare from the airport to Columbia Global Centers | Beijing is about 130 RMB. From City Center By Subway Line 10: Take line 10 to Suzhoujie（苏州街） station and exit from exit B. Walk along Haidian South road（海淀南路） for about 200 meters until reach Caihefang（彩和坊） Road and turn left. Walk north on Caihefang Road for about 600 meters. You will see Core Plaza（立方庭） on the right side of the street and the entrance to Columbia Global Centers | Beijing is on the south of the main Core Plaza entrance. The walk is approximately 10 minutes. By Subway Line 4: Take the subway line 4 heading north to Zhongguancun（中关村） station. Exit from Exit D and walk south on Zhongguancun Street for 100 meters and turn right at Haidian Street（海淀路）. Walk along Haidian Street for about 200 meters until reach Shanyuan Street（善缘街）. Turn left at Shanyuan Street and walk for about 600 meters until you reach Caihefang（彩和坊）Road. Turn right at the crossroad and Core Plaza（立方庭） is on the north east corner. The entrance to Columbia Global Centers | Beijing is on the south of the main Core Plaza entrance. The walk is approximately 10 minutes. Note: subway rides cost 2-6 RMB depending on the distance.

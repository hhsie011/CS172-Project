Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index CMLL International Teaching Assistant Menu Search International Teaching Assistant Program The CMLL International Teaching Assistant (ITA) Program evaluates all Texas Tech University nonnative speakers of English for approval to teach in accordance with OP 64.03. TTU departments must arrange for all nonnative speakers of English who will teach in their classrooms to be evaluated for proficiency in English for teaching by the TTU ITA Program. Candidates may be evaluated via the ITA Summer Workshop (virtual), Special Interview (virtual), ESL 5310, or ESL 5312. Information for Faculty/Advisors Information for Students July 1 - July 31, 2021 Students: for information regarding ITA Workshop registration, please contact the academic advisor in your department at Texas Tech University. You cannot register yourself for the workshop; only your TTU graduate advisor may do so. Advisors: For information regarding ITA Workshop student registration, please contact Carla Burrus.

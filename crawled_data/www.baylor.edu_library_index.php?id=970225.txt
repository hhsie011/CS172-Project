The University Libraries are excited to welcome you to the Baylor family. Our mission is to connect you with the resources you need to succeed academically during your time at Baylor, and this site is a handy place to find links and information on some of our most popular and useful services for students. So take a look around, explore the resources listed below, and if you have questions, don't hesitate to ask! And be sure to follow @BaylorLibraries on Instagram, Twitter and Facebook - they're the best way to stay in touch and get the latest info from your libraries!

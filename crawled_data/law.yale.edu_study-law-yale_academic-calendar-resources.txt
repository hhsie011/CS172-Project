Visit the Library website Library Services Scholarship Repository Office of Student Affairs The Office of Student Affairs at Yale Law School helps law students navigate their legal education. The office provides academic advising, supports student groups and journals, promotes student life and community building activities, and acts as the primary resource for students who are seeking to find balance in their law school experience.

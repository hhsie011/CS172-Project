IMNI serves as an umbrella organization with over 80 participating faculty members representing nine departments across campus. IMNI supports research in materials, molecular, and nanoscale sciences and technologies. IMNI administers a state-of-the-art equipment infrastructure that consists of four facilities: Electron Microscopy Facility (EMF), Nanofabrication Central Facility (NCF), NanoTools Facility (NTF), and the Joint Engineering & Physics Instrument Shop (JEPIS). These facilities provide access to advanced instrumentation and specialized services that allow researchers to engage in matters of a broad scope and complexity. The IMNI core research facilities are available to researchers from all Brown University departments as well as external researchers from local industry and academic institutions.

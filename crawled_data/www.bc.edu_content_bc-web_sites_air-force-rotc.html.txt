The Aerospace Studies curriculum develops leadership skills while familiarizing the student with U.S. Air Force functions, organization, equipment, management, and its role in national defense. The student is commissioned upon successful completion of both the Aerospace Studies curriculum and their respective program degree requirements. Students at Boston College may enroll in Air Force ROTC at Boston University while simultaneously earning a degree in their chosen field of study.

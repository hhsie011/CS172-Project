Student Affairs offices include: Admissions Academic Advising Career Design Lab Student Life Student Academic Support Wellness The information provided on this website will be a constant point of reference and will ensure that your experience at SPS is a positive one. The links on the left point toward information on student involvement, career services, advising, registration, policies and procedures, and University Resources. Policies, procedures, requirements, and guidelines frequently vary from program to program. In many instances, the links provided here lead to a program’s site or to pages within the University site, where the most relevant information can be found. The Division of Student Affairs strives to be your primary source for answers to your academic and extracurricular questions, including those about the School’s policies, procedures, and requirements. In addition, we serve as a connector to various campus offices including but not limited to: University Registrar Student Financial Services International Students and Scholars Office Health Services Office of Disability Services Counseling and Psychological Services Columbia Residential We look forward to helping you make the most of your education at Columbia University.

Visit the UnitedHealthCare Website to read the Summary of Benefits and Coverage in a different language. How to print your card online? Your Student Health Insurance ID Card is available online 24/7 via My Account on United Healthcare website. Please keep in mind insurance ID cards are only available after you have received notification from ISS Insurance that your insurance for the semester has been activated. You CANNOT print out a card before you receive an email from ISS Insurance notifying you that your insurance has been activated. If you already have a user ID and password, you can print your card, or even request a new card be mailed to you. Go to UHC's website and click on "ID CARD". If you don't have a user ID and password yet, you can create your account by providing your name, date of birth and email address. You may also call United Healthcare toll-free at 1-800-767-0700 for assistance in setting up your My Account. You may also access your UHC My Account via your smartphone or by downloading UHC's new Mobile App from your App Store.

AAMC Pre-health Collection A free collection of instructional resources recommended for pre-health competencies. What's on the MCAT Exam? Find information about the MCAT, including how to register for it, and how to prepare, on the AAMC website. Free MCAT Tutorials The first in a collection of free educational tutorials to help students prepare for the revised 2015 MCAT are now available. National MD-PhD Program Interview/Revisit Day Calendar Includes more than 70 MD-PhD programs around the country, and is an especially useful tool for applicants with multiple interview offers. National Health Service Corps Loan Repayment Programs The National Health Service Corps offers loan repayment assistance to support qualified health care providers who choose to take their skills where they’re most needed. State and Federal Repayment, Loan and Scholarship Database This searchable database provides detailed information about many state and federal programs available to medical and other health professions students.

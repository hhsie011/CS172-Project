Students New Student Checklist Information for Admitted Students Continuing Students UC Blue Ash Students UC Clermont Students Transition Students Graduate Student Checklist Complete all items on this checklist to ensure an easy transition into your graduate career at the University of Cincinnati. Send your final transcripts to the Graduate School, located in Van Wormer Hall, or electronically to grad.info@uc.edu. Complete your 2021-22 FAFSA. Check your To Do List in Catalyst and submit requested documentation, especially for financial aid purposes, as soon as possible. Create your financial aid portal account, if requested. Accept your financial aid in Catalyst using the Accept/Decline Financial Aid link in the My Finances tile. Complete a Master Promissory Note (MPN) and Loan Entrance Counseling if you plan to borrow Federal Unsubsidized Student Loans. Accept your Graduate PLUS Loan in Catalyst and complete the Graduate PLUS Loan Master Promissory Note (MPN) if you plan to borrow it. Please note a credit check is required and this requirement is separate from the previously mentioned unsubsidized loan requirements. Review options for covering remaining or out-of-pocket costs by using the Estimated Remaining Costs Tool. Check your student bill in Catalyst regularly and review due dates and payment options. Enroll in direct deposit for your financial aid refunds using the My Finances tile in Catalyst. Waive your university health insurance, if you have other coverage. Submit your vaccination information. Review your residency status via the My Profile tile in Catalyst and submit a residency application if necessary. File the 2022-23 FAFSA as early as October 1, 2021. Set yourself up for success, as limited aid sources run out quickly. Check your UC student email account regularly. Plan Ahead: Pay attention to important dates and deadlines each semester. Contact One Stop with billing, financial aid and registration questions.

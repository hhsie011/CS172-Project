STARS are Student Admissions Representatives who volunteer to be your host on our daily visits and Experience Iowa State programs. This group of students represent all six undergraduate colleges and are involved in many other student organizations. They are trained to help you with any questions you might have. Be sure to ask them about their adventure. Want to be a STAR? Becoming a STAR is a lot more than just showing prospective students and their families around campus. Your experience will help you with public speaking, thinking on your feet, decision making, communication, and much more. With all of these skills you learn, we hear time and time again that this experience will have you shining like a star in the interview process. Iowa State students interested in applying to be a STAR should contact kchapman@iastate.edu or call 515 294-9288.

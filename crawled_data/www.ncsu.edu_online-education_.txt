College Comes to You At NC State, we want to make learning and discovery available to everyone. That means providing an online education that’s equal to the one you’d get on campus. Our wealth of online and distance programs receive the same accreditation — and are taught by the same excellent faculty — as our in-person degrees. Our programs also offer maximum flexibility. If you can’t attend classes on campus because of geography, career demands or other life circumstances, we’ve got you covered. In short, if you can’t make it to NC State, we’ll come to you. 1,000+ Online and Distance Courses 120+ Online and Distance Academic Programs Online and Distance Education Wherever you are, whatever your schedule, you can access the cutting-edge education offered by NC State. We provide dedicated resources that describe our online and distance programs, explain the admissions process and discuss potential careers once you graduate.

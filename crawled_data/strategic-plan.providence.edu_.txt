Menu Apply Visit Give Coronavirus Information & Updates Parents & Families Alumni Media MY-PC Events About Providence College History & Mission Strategic Plan 2011-2018 Close menu PC200: The Providence College Strategic Plan Ratified by the Providence College Board of Trustees in November 2018, PC200 will guide the College’s further pursuit of its destiny as a leading Catholic, liberal arts college with a national reputation. Created through an inclusive, community-based process of more than two years, PC200 outlines high aspirations over the coming ten years, while forging ever-stronger connections to the College’s core values and proud history. The Plan comprises a preamble, three goals, specific metrics, and a description of the timeline and process for its creation. The entire Plan is accessible on one page here.

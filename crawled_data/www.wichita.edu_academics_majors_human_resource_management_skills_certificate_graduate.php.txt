Frank Barton School of Business Department of Management The online graduate certificate in HR Management Skills is designed for working professionals in need of training in human resource selection, talent development and employment law. Learn how the HR Management Skills certificate is the right fit for you. Contact coordinator at gretchen.holthaus@wichita.edu Curriculum To complete the 15-credit-hour certificate in Human Resource Management. Twelve of the hours are composed of four required courses. The remaining three hours are elective courses based on your interests and career goals. Contact the coordinator for more information. View the curriculum Admission to the Program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission. An applicant must meet the minimum TOEFL and IELTS requirements set by the Graduate School.

Learn more about the Chancellor's Council. Since 1923, the universities of the Texas Tech University System have prepared generations of leaders, addressed challenges facing Texas and the world, and proved that great things happen here. Our success is possible thanks to the continued support from donors, alumni and friends. Together, we are investing in our students by creating scholarships that reward academic accomplishment and encourage diversity. We are investing in our faculty by establishing endowments that attract world-class scholars who are as accomplished in the classroom as they are in their research. And, we are investing in our institutions by building spaces where our students and faculty can learn, discover and compete. Every gift to the Texas Tech University System is shaping the future of our universities and leaving a lasting legacy. Explore ways to give to the Texas Tech University System.

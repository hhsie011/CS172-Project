Parking is available in the field along the right side of Via di Vincigliata 43, please ring at the gate. Once those spaces are filled, the alternative is to park near Ponte a Mensola or along Via di Vincigliata or on Via di S. Martino a Mensola. I Tatti cannot be held responsible for parked cars. Gates close at 8:30 PM. The only vehicles allowed to enter at gate 22 are taxis bringing or collecting guests. MAPS & DIRECTIONS How to reach I Tatti by bus From Piazza S. Marco take the ATAF Bus 10 to Ponte a Mensola. Once off the bus, use the crosswalk to cross the street and continue along Via Gabriele D'Annunzio. Take the first left (just after Trattoria Osvaldo) onto Via di Vincigliata and continue up the hill approximately 800m, past gate 43 (on your right) and up to gate 22 (on your left). This walk will take about 10/15 minutes. From the Florence railway station, Santa Maria Novella, take ATAF Bus 1, 6, 17, or 23 to Piazza San Marco. If you are arriving from the Florence airport, take the Airport Shuttle Bus, ATAF Volainbus, to the central railway station, Santa Maria Novella. How to reach I Tatti by car Click here for custom driving directions.

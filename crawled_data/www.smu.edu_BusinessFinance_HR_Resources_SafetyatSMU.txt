SMU strives to provide a safe educational and working environment for all of our staff, faculty, students, and visitors. The University has a variety of policies, programs, and services to support our goal of providing a safe campus experience. It is everyone's responsibility to be aware of these resources and utilize them as needed to ensure our campus remains free from violence, accidents, or other risks.

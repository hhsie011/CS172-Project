This office assists with advising, scheduling, and registration for courses for students across all UM departments. The Assistant Dean and Director work with several student assistants to coordinate all students services. Their services include Admissions, Financial Assistance and Student Employment, Student Affairs, Ombudsperson, the Toppel Career Center, and Housing. Admission Requirements With our competitive admission process, our Admissions page provides clarification on the policies and procedures for application and admission. Visit U-SoA U-SoA offers several visit options for prospective students, as well as shadowed tours of a U-SoA student for a day of exploration! Track Applications We provide the necessary steps to seamlessly track applications in order to find out which documents have been received and processed for the admission file. Financial Aid The University of Miami attracts highly-qualified students with recognized academic achievement by offering a substantial financial assistance program. Registration The Director of Academic Services and the Undergraduate Advisors work within the Registration division to assist students with the selection of professional and elective courses. Tuition and Fees U-SoA offers a variety of information to help students better understand their financial status and how to effectively maintain their student account. Graduate Assistantships Graduate Assistantships are available to a limited number of students based on several requirements keen towards ensuring professional success.

Harvard Kennedy School launched SLATE (Strengthening Learning and Teaching Excellence) in the fall of 2007 to reaffirm its commitment to top performance in professional education, ensuring that HKS faculty match their intellectual distinction with a mastery of classroom skills. SLATE offers professional development, consultation services, and a growing toolkit of tested materials to energize and enable teaching and learning for public service through innovation and evidence-based continuous improvement. Active Learning at HKS No other professional school of public policy has mounted a comparably ambitious effort to promote pedagogical excellence. Our Mission Preparing and distributing curricular materials such as classic cases, teaching plans, multimedia cases, digital modules, cross-disciplinary exercises, and simulations Developing, disseminating, and recognizing teaching capacities required for excellence in professional-school education Forging assessment tools that will help faculty gauge student learning Teaching Resources SLATE is committed to supporting Harvard Kennedy School's role as a global leader in public policy education by sharing its work with the larger academic community. In support of this goal, we have made a collection of guides, tip sheets and other teaching resources available for the public to browse and download. Curriculum and Innovation SLATE is proud to share the innovative curricular materials and pedagogical practices being developed at HKS. We invite faculty across the globe to browse the HKS Case Collection and the Research and Innovation section of our website to learn more.

Story Samuel's Story Selena's Story Judi's Story Shreyas' Story Student Success Program IT Learning Center The IT Learning Center provides support to students taking School of Information Technology courses. The learning center is operating in a hybrid format for the Spring semester. You will find some Teaching Assistants are in person only, some are hybrid and some are virtual only. The corresponding information can be found below. The IT Learning Center is staffed by Teaching Assistants from the Phd in Information Technology, Masters in Information Technology and Bachelors in Information Technology programs. Though the Teaching Assistants are only assigned to specific IT courses, the IT Learning Center supports all IT courses on Main Campus. Navigation Tips For help with your School of Information Technology classes, please contact the TA directly via their email listed at the bottom of this page. For the best experience at the Learning Center, please contact us prior to your visit. Looking for help with a specific course? Use your device's find/search on page command to search for your course number. The Center will be closed during UC holidays.

Wichita State University's Office of Disability Services provides students with learning, mental or physical disabilities an equal opportunity to attain their academic and personal goals to the fullest of their abilities. Our office hours are Monday – Friday 8 a.m. – 5 p.m. You can call 316-978-3309 or email us at disability.services@wichita.edu . Please follow all WSU COVID-19 guidelines while on campus. If you are not currently registered with ODS, please go to the following page to start the intake process. Once we have your completed intake form, we will email you to set up a Zoom meeting to discuss accommodations. If you are registered with ODS and want to continue using current accommodations, please submit the Continued Services Form. This form should be completed at the beginning of each semester.

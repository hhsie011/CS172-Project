the City Guides Notable Alumni Regions Special Interest Groups Travel with SU Home Connect Alumni Awards Chancellor’s Citation Chancellor’s Citation The Chancellor’s Citation is awarded to alumni from diverse backgrounds in recognition of significant civic or career achievements. It was introduced in 1983 and has been presented since then at the triennial Coming Back Together reunion. Meet our 2017 recipients. Recipients receive the Chancellor’s Citation commemorative plate, designed and made by David MacDonald, emeritus professor of studio arts. Read more about the award and artist.

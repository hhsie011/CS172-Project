Add The University of Kansas to your GrubHub app and open up endless dining possibilities; both on, and off campus! Your account can be directly linked to your meal plan giving you the ultimate flexibility in dining using your Dining Dollars. Why use your GrubHub App? Simple It's a quick & easy way to order and pay for your on/off-campus food from any mobile device. Time Instead of waiting in long lines at on-campus restaurants, order ahead through the app. Pay Easy payment with your Dining Dollars! Skip the Line! GrubHub campus pick-up is offered widely across our campus locations! Download the App! How to use Grubhub Campus Dining 1. Download the App on your mobile device. 2. Set-up your Grubhub account 3. In the settings menu, select Campus Dining and choose The University of Kansas 4. Add your KU ID number to directly link your meal plan to your Grubhub orders. 5. You're all set! Kick your feet back, and await your order. Enjoy! How does it work? $500 is automatically loaded onto your GrubHub account each semester through your KU ID. You can use these dollars both on, and off campus!

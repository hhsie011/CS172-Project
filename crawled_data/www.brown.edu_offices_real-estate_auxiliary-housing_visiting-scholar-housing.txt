This section of the website has further details and reservation information. There are a limited number of fully-furnished visiting scholar apartments located on campus. These apartments range in size from efficiency to two-bedroom units. Monthly rental rates, which include the cost of utilities and wireless internet, range from $1,800 to $2,500 per month (ICERM rates may differ from rates quoted below). Nightly rates are also available. In order to reserve space, the sponsoring department should complete and submit the Visiting Scholar Housing Request Form located in the Forms section of the Visiting Scholar Housing section of the website. For additional information about visiting scholar housing, please contact Ronni Edmonds at the Auxiliary Housing office. For information about short-term (maximum 6-months) faculty/staff housing, please contact the Assistant Director of Auxiliary Housing. Housing Options All accommodations subject to availability.

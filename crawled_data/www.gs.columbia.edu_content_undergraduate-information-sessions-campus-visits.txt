here: Home Admissions Undergraduate Admissions Undergraduate Information Sessions Undergraduate Information Sessions Led by an admissions representative information sessions cover the full range of topics that are of concern to prospective students including the application process, financial planning, student life, and more. Participants also have the opportunity to ask questions and hear from current students or graduates of GS. In addition to attending an online information session, prospective students and guests are welcome to visit the Morningside Heights campus during an outdoor-only campus tour. Please note, all visitors are expected to comply with the University's health and safety policies and will receive additional details about these policies while registering for a campus visit.

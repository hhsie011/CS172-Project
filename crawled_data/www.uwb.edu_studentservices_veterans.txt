Sexual Harassment and Discrimination Awards and Recognition Student Affairs Veterans Services Welcome to Veterans Services! Veterans Services provides assistance to students who are entitled to receive educational benefits from the Department of Veterans Affairs. Whether a student is a new applicant for benefits or has used them at previous institutions, UW Bothell Veterans Services is committed to working in partnership to ensure that the process is simple and well-organized. Here to help UW Bothell is a Veteran Supportive Campus by the Washington State Department of Veterans Affairs. Veterans Services is committed to assisting out veterans as they transition from military service to academic and civilian life. Veterans Resource Center Located in UW 1, Room 011 on the Lower Level (UW1-011) Your partner with the VA As soon as you have registered for classes, Veterans Services will certify your enrollment to the VA on a quarterly basis. Faculty & staff support Visit our faculty and staff resources page if you wish to provide support to and have better understanding of the student veteran experience. Contact Information Rosa J.

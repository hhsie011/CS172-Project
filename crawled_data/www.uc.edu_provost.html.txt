With more than 46,000 students and over 4,000 faculty, our offerings range across 13 colleges, the Graduate School and a consortium of state-of-the-art libraries. The Office of the Provost oversees all of their operations, and extends beyond that to manage academic and student services. We are committed to excellence, diversity and developing educated and enganged citizens. Learn more about the University of Cincinnati mission.

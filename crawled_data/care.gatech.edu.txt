Students experiencing a crisis that requires immediate attention may speak with a counselor at any time 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. During regular business hours, students who are not actively in counseling may call CARE at 404-894-3498 or walk-in to our office. After business hours, please call either 404-894-2575 or 404-894-3498 and select the option to speak to the after-hours counselor. Students who are experiencing an immediate life-threatening emergency on campus, call the Georgia Tech Campus Police at 404-894-2500. If off campus, please immediately call 911.

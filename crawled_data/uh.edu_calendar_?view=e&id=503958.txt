Submit an Event It Was 50 Years Ago Today: A Tribute to the Beatles Wednesday, May 25, 2022 7:00 pm - 9:30 pm IT WAS 50 YEARS AGO TODAY: A TRIBUTE TO THE BEATLES featuring an incredible lineup of artists performing their hits and a selection of songs from RUBBER SOUL and REVOLVER! TODD RUNDGREN (“I Saw The Light”, “Hello, It’s Me”), CHRISTOPHER CROSS (“Sailing”, “Ride Like The Wind”), JASON SCHEFF (lead singer from CHICAGO 1985-2016), BADFINGER featuring JOEY MOLLAND (“Day After Day”, “No Matter What”), and DENNY LAINE (founding member of THE MOODY BLUES and WINGS)— All of these artists will join forces here in Houston to perform their greatest hits plus selected songs from The Beatles’ iconic albums Rubber Soul and Revolver.

The vast majority of Pennsylvania's practicing veterinarians are Penn Vet grads, and their most vital tasks include ensuring food safety and providing critical research and care to our animal agriculture industry. Penn Vet is a world leader in educating future veterinarians and researchers, setting the standard in curricular excellence. We help to define tomorrow's veterinary leaders through the diversity and breadth of their educational experience here.

Current information about Harvard’s response to COVID-19 can be found on the main Harvard COVID-19 page; information on Harvard’s efforts to ensure a safe working, learning, and residential environment during the COVID-19 pandemic is available in the Harvard Covid-19 Safe Return to Campus Resources document. During this time, University Disability Resources continues to provide guidance and resources to Harvard faculty, staff, students, visitors and guests regarding disability-related inquiries. Our staff is currently working remotely and are available to provide assistance during regular business hours.

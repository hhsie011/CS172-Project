Thank you for choosing our facilities to host your upcoming event. We offer an array of resources and services to help assist with your event planning needs - including space allocation, audiovisual needs, and catering. With numerous venues across campus, including the Student Union, Wes Watkins Center, Bennett Memorial Chapel, as well as many beautiful outdoor spaces, we offer flexible options to meet every need. Whether you are looking for a large, elegant ballroom or a cozy meeting room, Meeting & Conference Services has a space that is sure to suit your needs.

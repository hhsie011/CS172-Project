Neeley Hall is named after M.J. Neeley ’22 and was constructed in 1980. Mr. Neeley graduated with a degree in textile engineering. He built his wealth from the ground up, starting in the transportation industry and expanding into the financial sector. Mr. Neeley was a firm believer in Texas A&M students developing their oral and written communication skills and devoted much of his free time to mentoring students in these areas. 2021 – 2022 Rates Modular Hall $3,708semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Room Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but on different floors. All residents on the same floor are the same gender. Close Gallery Get a feel for all that Neeley Hall has to offer by scrolling through the photo gallery.

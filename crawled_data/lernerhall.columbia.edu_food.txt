Jump to Navigation Lerner Hall Food Where to Eat Whether it's morning coffee, lunch on the run, or a sit down meal with friends, Lerner Hall offers a variety of choices for good food and delicious drinks. Floor 2E see floorplan Blue Java Café Blue Java Café is a quick-stop for fresh food centrally located on the ground floor of Lerner Hall, serving everything from gourmet coffee and espressos drinks, to made-to-order deli sandwiches, salads and quick serve grab-n-go items. Operated by Columbia University Dining. Phone: 212-854-0192 Online: dining.columbia.edu Blue Java Lerner Hall Hours: MONDAY - FRIDAY: 9:00 am - 5:00 pm Please see the Columbia University Dining website for more information. Café East Café East offers traditional Taiwanese teas with a twist, including tapioca pearl tea drinks, fruit smoothies, milkshakes, a selection of gourmet hot teas, dumplings, and fresh sushi. Managed by Columbia University Dining. Phone: 212-854-5355 Online: dining.columbia.edu Cafe East Spring 2022 hours: Monday - Friday: 10:30 am - 8:00 pm Saturday: 10:30 am - 7:00 pm Please see the Columbia University Dining website for any changes. Floor 3E see floorplan Ferris Booth Commons Ferris Booth Commons boasts an "All You Care to Eat" international fusion dining experience, featuring a range of fresh, organic, vegetarian and kosher products, gourmet-style food, and made-to-order specialties for breakfast, lunch or dinner. Operated by Columbia University Dining.

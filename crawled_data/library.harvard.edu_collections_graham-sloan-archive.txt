The Graham-Sloan Archive contains photocopies of stenographic reports, legal and other documents, interview transcripts, and press clippings about the transformation of the USSR Academy of Sciences into the Russian Academy of Sciences. Accessing These Materials The collection is available for in-library use. A detailed list of materials in the archive is available. Please contact Fung Library staff for assistance.

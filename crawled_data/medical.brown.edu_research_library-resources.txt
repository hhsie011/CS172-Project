The Health and Biomedical Sciences Library offers a variety of resources and services to help you with your research. Their knowledgeable librarians can assist with: Research planning Finding funding Writing a data management plan Finding data Conducting research Locating/identifying critical data sets Providing rapid evidence searching and synthesis services Conducting a comprehensive literature review / identifying appropriate review methodologies Organizing citations Analyzing and visualizing data Publishing and sharing research Compliance with funders' public access policies Depositing research products into a repository Getting a Digital Object Identifier (DOI) Measuring the impact of your research To schedule a meeting or consultation with our team, please contact HealthSciLibrarians@brown.edu.

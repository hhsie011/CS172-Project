From the lower level of Interstate 35, take the exit marked "26th Street - LBJ Library" and proceed west into campus on Dean Keaton/26th Street. Alternately, from Loop 1 (Mopac) take the Windsor Road exit and proceed east. Windsor Road becomes 24th Street which enters campus as you cross Guadalupe Street. Visitor Parking Visitor parking is available by the hour in any of our seven parking garages. The closest garages to our buildings are the 27th Street Garage (TSG), Speedway Garage (SWG), San Antonio Garage (SAG), and San Jacinto Garage (SJG).

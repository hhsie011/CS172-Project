The School of Nursing and Health Studies is committed to excellence in nursing and health science, and is committed to ensuring its students exemplify that academic excellence and service to society. The school's undergraduate and graduate curricula are community-based with a strong emphasis on cultural competency and evidence-based practice. Our school prepares you to adapt to specific health needs through coursework and outreach programs, enabling you to address the health needs of our increasingly diverse and rapidly changing society. Freshmen, Transfer, and International applicants can find detailed information on the Undergraduate Admissions website.

The second event of the program will focus on how entrepreneurs in the consumer goods industry create stronger brands and establish contact and identity with consumers through digital channels. During this event, entrepreneurs from Columbia University, University of Toronto, Tsinghua University, and the National University of Singapore will share their entrepreneurial projects, stories, and visions. Representatives from the Beijing Center, Zhongguancun Collaborative Innovation Center, and presidents of Columbia alumni clubs will also attend the event and give remarks. Speakers Naizeng Fang, Founder of Coco 100 Fang graduated with a masters degree from Columbia University, and was an investor and Vice President of Xinzhongli Capital. Coco 100 is a food and beverages company centered around coconut-created products. His company is dedicated to serving millennial consumers with a healthier lifestyle, starting with beverages made out of coconuts and expanding into various other product lines.

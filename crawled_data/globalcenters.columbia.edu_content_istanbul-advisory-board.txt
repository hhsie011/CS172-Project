Board Our Advisory Board "Columbia Global Centers | Istanbul is like an open window for the Turkish academia and civil society that lets in fresh air in an era when nation states appear to close all doors and windows and withdraw into their inner core." Prof. Üstün Ergüder The Center’s Advisory Board provides oversight and is composed of prominent academics, public figures, and business leaders. The Board meets annually and provides ongoing leadership and counsel regarding the Center’s activities.

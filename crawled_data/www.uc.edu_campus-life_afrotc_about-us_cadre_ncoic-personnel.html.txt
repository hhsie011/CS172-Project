Love Technical Sergeant Ginca R. Love is the Non-Commissioned Officer in Charge of Personnel, Air Education and Training Command, University of Cincinnati AFROTC Detachment 665, Cincinnati, Ohio. In this capacity Technical Sergeant Love is responsible for managing cadet physical exams, duty status, individual medical readiness, unit training and the personnel reliability program. In October 2005, Sergeant Love enlisted in the United States Air Force as a Personnelist. She has performed duty at both squadron and base level assignments to include the Command Support Staff of the 89th Operations Squadron, and the 11th Force Support Squadron. Her specialty is Human Resource Management. Prior to assuming her current assignment, she served as the Non-Commissioned Officer in Charge of the Administration Section for the Defense Medical Readiness Training Institute which is located on Joint Base San Antonio Fort Sam Houston, Texas.

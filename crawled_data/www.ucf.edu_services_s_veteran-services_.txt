More Events › Free Labs and Study Spaces Resources Service Social Justice and Advocacy Support Work Experience The Veterans Academic Resource Center recognizes and supports student veterans as they transition from military to student life. Located on the first floor of the CFE Arena, VARC is a welcoming environment with services to help student veterans make a smooth transition to college, access their benefits and succeed at UCF. With a dedicated lounge and private study rooms, as well as access to university services like advising, counseling and tutoring, VARC features an array of student veteran resources in one central location. The goals of VARC are simple. We help you understand and access all resources that are available to you. We provide pathways to success through convenient study areas and tutoring assistance. We guide UCF faculty and staff to a better understanding of the unique needs of student veterans. And we provide you with the tools needed to stay on track and complete your degree.

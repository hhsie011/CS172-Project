Take your education and career to the next level with a graduate degree from OSU. Earning your graduate degree takes hard work and commitment. We are here to help you cultivate your passion and expand your knowledge and skills with renowned faculty instruction and state-of-the-art facilities. Professional medical, doctoral, master’s and graduate certificate programs are available online, on the main OSU campus in Stillwater, the branch campus in Tulsa (OSU-Tulsa) and at the Center for Health Sciences (CHS) in Tulsa. We’re here to help! Take the next step and apply for graduate school admission. The Graduate College has compiled a helpful list of admission requirements and frequently asked questions.

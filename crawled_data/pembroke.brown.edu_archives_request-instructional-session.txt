The Pembroke Center Archives invite classes and community groups to request an instructional session. Each session is up to one hour in length where the archivists customize tours, displays, and guest lectures based on your course topics, objectives, and needs. Availability is limited so we ask that you complete this form to request an instructional session at least three weeks in advance of your preferred date of visit.

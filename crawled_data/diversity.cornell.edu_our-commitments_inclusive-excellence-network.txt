Guidelines Supplier Diversity Home Our Commitments Inclusive Excellence Network Inclusive Excellence Network The Inclusive Excellence Network (IEN) is a collection of programs designed to engage Cornell staff in action-oriented discussions, self-reflection, and productive discourse around topics that impact the workplace. Our goal is to continue building Cornell's culture of belonging by providing space for participants, at various levels of understanding of diversity and inclusion topics, to have meaningful dialogue.

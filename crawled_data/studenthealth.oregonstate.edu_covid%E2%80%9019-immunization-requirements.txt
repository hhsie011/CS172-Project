with Private Insurance Affordable Care Act Insurance Resources Forms You are here Home COVID‐19 Immunization Requirements COVID-19 Vaccine Information COVID Testing Information To Comply with the vaccination policy, begin here: OSU Students OSU Student Employees OSU Faculty and Staff OSU requires all employees and students who work, learn or engage with others in-person as part of their job duties to show proof of initial COVID-19 vaccinations, though exceptions are allowed when based on medical or disability reasons or sincerely held religious beliefs. Please upload your proof of vaccination to Student Health Services’ secure Patient Portal. Vaccine access: OSU will continue to support the regional distribution of COVID-19 vaccines in Corvallis, Bend and other locations throughout the state. Vaccination Program Requirements Vaccination Opportunities: Vaccines available in Corvallis Vaccines around the US OSU Students students.jpg Confirming Vaccination Confirm With Vaccine Card Once you have your vaccine card, please click here to access the Patient Portal, look for the following button on the front page and enter your information. portal_buttons.jpg PLEASE NOTE You will need to upload your COVID vaccination card. To do this, please follow the following steps. PLEASE NOTE: Your vaccination will be verified by the immunizations staff manually once both the card upload and vaccination history sections are complete. This is a manual process, please be patient. STEP 1: Upload a copy of your COVID vaccination card. To upload a copy of your card, click the “Enter my COVID-19 Vaccination Information” button, then click the green “Upload” button. Once completed, you will see an icon-sized version of your card in that section. STEP 2: Under the “COVID-19 Vaccination History” section, click “Add Immunization”, and add an event with the information from each COVID-19 vaccination dose. Please note: if you get a booster shot you will need to return to this section to add that event to the list. Completed COVID-19 Vaccination Information should look similar to this: vaccine_verify.jpg OPTIONAL: Upload an updated copy of your COVID vaccination card with your booster shot information. Confirming With Attestation To confirm with attestation, please fill out and submit this form Claiming Exemption from Primary Vaccination Claiming an Exemption IF YOU ARE FACULTY/STAFF AND YOU SUBMIT THIS EXEMPTION FORM, YOUR RESPONSE WILL NOT BE RECORDED. Please navigate here for Staff/Faculty response. For students seeking Medical or Non-Medical Exemption to the primary series of the vaccine (for a religious or philosophical belief that does not agree with the COVID-19 vaccination policy) please complete the following: Please click here to complete the educational module regarding the COVID-19 virus and vaccine. At the completion of the module you will be directed to complete a exemption form which will be sent to the immunization office. Reasons for Medical Exemption: Allergy to a component of a vaccine Anaphylactic reaction from the COVID-19 vaccine Other medical condition where your care provider advises you to not be vaccinated NOTE: If you receive an error message after completing your waiver, please check your patient portal within the next week to ensure compliance has been met.

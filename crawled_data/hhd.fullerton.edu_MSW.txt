The CSUF MSW program is committed to preparing students to work with vulnerable, marginalized, and underrepresented populations, with an emphasis on direct practice with clients. If you are passionate about addressing social problems and advocating for social justice on behalf of people in need on a micro and/or macro level, please explore our website and then reach out to us for more information. Together we can discover whether our program is a good fit for you.

EEO IS THE LAW Princeton University's Annual Security Report and Fire Safety Report outlines our campus safety and security policies and contains important tips to help every member of our community be safe and avoid becoming a victim of crime. The Report includes sections relating to crime prevention, the reporting of crimes, alcohol and drug use, sexual misconduct, and other matters. The Report also contain fire-safety policies and prevention efforts for on-campus student housing. In addition, it includes statistics for the previous three years concerning reported crimes that occurred: on campus; in certain off-campus buildings owned or controlled by Princeton University or its students; and on public property within or immediately adjacent to and accessible from Princeton University. A paper copy of the report is available from Public Safety by request.

The School of Engineering uses Airmedia for wireless connections to displays in certain meeting rooms. For an overview of Airmedia, please refer to the CIS help page on Wireless Projection. If you need to connect to an Airmedia system now, click here to download installers and instructions. The School of Engineering has Airmedia systems installed in the following rooms: Hazeltine Commons ERC 101 aka Undergraduate Teaching Lab ERC 125 ERC206 ERC 225 ERC 250 ERC 306 ERC 325 ERC 350 If you will be using various conference rooms with Airmedia, you may wish to keep a folder with shortcuts for each room on the desktop of your computer, or another convenient place. You may download individual room apps, or a folder with all of them here. Alternatively, you may also connect to each room via their web page and download installers or connect from there. Webpages are listed below, but follow the naming convention http://eng-ercXXX.brown.edu (where XXX represents the room you are in.) http://eng-erc100.brown.edu aka Hazeltine Commons http://eng-erc101.brown.edu aka Undergraduate Teaching Lab http://eng-erc125.brown.edu http://eng-erc206.brown.edu http://eng-erc225.brown.edu http://eng-erc250.brown.edu http://eng-erc306.brown.edu http://eng-erc325.brown.edu http://eng-erc350.brown.edu If you already have the Airmedia application installed, you may simply open it and enter the room number to which you wish to connect, e.g. eng-erc125.

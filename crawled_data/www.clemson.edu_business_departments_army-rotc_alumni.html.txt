Department of Military Leadership Current: Alumni Alumni If you are alumni of the Fightin' Tigers at Clemson University, please let us know! We would like to maintain a comprehensive history of our alumni, as well as provide a communication tool that will bring together a community of past, present and future Clemson University Army Officers. For the cadets, it serves as an excellent chance to see how many Clemson alumni are currently serving in the Army and allows for possible mentorship for certain career fields. For the alumni, it serves as a chance to see how your fellow Fightin' Tiger alumni are doing, what career paths they have chosen and where they are currently assigned. Please follow the step below to register as an alumnus in the Clemson database: Registering as alumni within Clemson's database allows for fellow classmates and other alumni to view your present-day status. This database also offers you the opportunity to search for long-lost friends by many different search methods. If not registered... Click the following link: https://iamatiger.clemson.edu/giving-to-clemson?tab=1 Fill out and submit the New Registration form. It may take up to two business days for confirmation. Once confirmed, follow the link to update your profile: https://iamatiger.clemson.edu/home Be sure to add Army ROTC as a Student Activity. If already registered... Follow the link to update your profile and search for friends: https://iamatiger.clemson.edu/home Become Active We offer several opportunities throughout the year, for past and current officers, to talk and share their experience and what they do in the Army. If you would like to get involved with our detachment and talk to our cadets during labs and/or special events such as Military Ball, Dining In and Awards Ceremony, please contact us at 864-656-3107.

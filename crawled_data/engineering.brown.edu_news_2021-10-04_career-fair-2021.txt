More than 250 students and over 25 companies are expected to attend. A wide range of technical companies will be present to recruit students for both summer internships and full time jobs. These companies are looking to hire both undergraduate and graduate students. Among the companies planning to recruit at this year's fair are: Amgen, Analog Devices, Cognex, Epic, Helikon Consulting, Hitachi Cable America, Hyannis Port Research (HPR), Interplex Industries, Key Tech, KVH Industries, Langan Engineering and Environmental Services, Loureiro Engineering Associates, MTX Group, Naval Undersea Warfare Center, PTC, U.S. Patent and Trademark Office, Veryst, Ximedica, and several more. If you would like to attend, please register through Handshake. You must register in advance to attend. Attendees must comply with all Brown University COVID-19 protocols, including wearing a mask while inside. Students wishing to attend must register at: https://app.joinhandshake.com/career_fairs/24417/student_preview by 12 noon on Wednesday, November 3.

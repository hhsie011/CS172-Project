We support recruitment and retention efforts to diversify the faculty, and we support staff recruitment and retention at all levels. We work with schools and departments to support the development of recruitment pipelines to broaden the participation of diverse students, scholars and staff in all fields and disciplines. And we offer initiatives, trainings and other opportunities to provide professional development. Resources In addition to the resources and initiatives offered by OIED in connection with its mission, many other University offices and programs provide resources and support.

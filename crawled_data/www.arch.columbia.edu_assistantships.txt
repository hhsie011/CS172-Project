By continuing to use this website, you consent to Columbia University's usage of cookies and similar technologies, in accordance with the Columbia University Website Cookie Notice Group 6 Assistantships Columbia GSAPP offers a limited number of teaching and research assistantships to students enrolled in full-time degree programs and to continuing non-enrolled students. Remuneration is based on the expected workload and is disbursed via semimonthly paychecks. An enrolled student receiving an assistantship will also be granted a tuition award, which is applied directly to the student’s account. SUMMER 2022 RESEARCH AND TEACHING ASSISTANTSHIPS We are pleased to announce a number of GSAPP Research Assistant and Teaching Assistant positions available for Summer 2022 for continuing and incoming students. Please click on the application link below for a detailed list of available positions. Students will be selected and placed in positions based on their skills and experience. Assistantship applications are due Monday, May 2, 2022, 8am (EDT). The assignment of assistantships will be made on the basis of qualifications for each position. They are merit-based positions; consequently, preference is given to those who have experience in the subject matter. The number of hours an assistantship holder will be asked to work is, at a minimum, 10 hours per week. Summer 2022 remuneration for the semester-long appointment period varies based on the position and falls within three ranges: 10 hours per week: $2,436 salary plus $8,344 tuition award for enrolled students 15 hours per week: $3,654 salary plus $12,516 tuition award for enrolled students 19 hours per week: $4,631 salary plus $15,645 tuition award for enrolled students Please be reminded that students can only hold one appointed position (i.e. Teaching or Research Assistantship) at a time and may not work more than 20 hours per week total for all jobs at Columbia. APPLY NOW Questions/inquiries should be directed to the Dean’s Office: assistantships@arch.columbia.edu. SUMMER 2022 INTRODUCTION TO ARCHITECTURE SECTION LEADERS Positions within the Introduction to Architecture program are available for current M.Arch and MSAAD students expecting to graduate at the end of the Spring 2022 semester. APPLY NOW Section Leader applications are due Monday, May 2, 2022, 8am (EDT). OTHER OPPORTUNITIES Visit the Career Services at GSAPP page to explore other employment opportunities.

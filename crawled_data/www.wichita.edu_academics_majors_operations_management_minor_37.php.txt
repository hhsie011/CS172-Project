Frank Barton School of Business Management A minor in operations management from Wichita State's AACSB-accredited Barton School of Business will help you build a background in strategic decision-making. You'll gain knowledge and skills in the efficient use of resources to produce goods and services. The minor is a valuable addition to many majors—and is useful in a wide range of careers in the public and private sectors. Learn how operations management is the right fit for you. Braden McCall, a business management minor, took advantage of WSU's Study Abroad Program, spending a semester studying at the ESCE International Business School in Paris, France. Taking management classes while fully immersed in another culture will give Braden the experience he'll need as he one day pursues a career in mechanical engineering. Curriculum The 15-credit-hour minor is available to business majors and non-business majors. To complete it, you'll take three required courses and choose from six additional hours of upper-division operation management courses based on your interests and career goals.

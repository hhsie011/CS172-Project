Get approval from ISS if taking classes at another school concurrently or online as part of full-time enrollment requirement Tell ISS if you plan to pursue a degree at or transfer to another school General Report change of U.S. address or phone number within 10 days (required in iHawk and highly recommended in Enroll & Pay) Leave the U.S. within 60 days of finishing your program, or, if eligible, apply for change of status or be admitted to another academic program Always read e-mails from ISS Additional Resource Department of Homeland Security's Maintaining Status webpage International Student Handbook Events Calendar Immigration Advising Hours Virtual Remote Advising Monday, Wednesday, Friday 1:30pm to 4:00pm.

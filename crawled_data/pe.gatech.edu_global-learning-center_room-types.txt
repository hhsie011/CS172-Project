More View Floor Plans The Atrium Perfect for both small and large gatherings, the atrium gives the ambiance of an outdoor event with the amenities of a modern indoor ballroom. The two-story space can accommodate 50 to 200 for seated dining, and up to 300 people for a cocktail style standing reception. In addition, our built-in Premium Technology A/V Package gives you the state-of-the-art technology and in-house expertise to host a working meal, thank your sponsors, or present awards. Learn More About The Atrium The Courtyard Ideal for receptions, networking meetings, dinners, luncheons, and professional social events, the courtyard is the perfect setting for an outdoor component to your meeting. It can accommodate up to 96 people for seated dining, and up to 200 people for a cocktail-style standing reception. It's also a comfortable spot for attendees to sit and enjoy the weather during meeting breaks.

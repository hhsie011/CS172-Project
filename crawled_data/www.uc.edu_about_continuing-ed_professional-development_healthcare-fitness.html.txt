Certification Project Management Certification (CAPM®) Healthcare & Fitness The health and fitness field has many roles, but a shared goal: helping people live their best lives possible. The purpose of health education is to equip you to pursue that goal, too. With courses in medical coding, personal fitness training, activity direction, and more, you'll have the knowledge and experience to pursue a meaningful career. Our list of health courses includes straightforward descriptions and career-focused materials. Pick and choose based on the skills you want to develop and the direction your passion for health and fitness is taking you. Certified Personal Trainer right arrow down arrow If you're thinking about studying for your personal trainer certification online, this course is an excellent way to do so. It includes exam preparation and all study materials, as well as the certification exam fee, and you will gain the knowledge, skills, and abilities you need to become a successful NASM personal trainer. Read more Certified Nutrition Coach right arrow down arrow Completing this nutrition course will make you a NASM Certified Nutrition Coach. This industry-recognized certification proves that you understand nutritional science and behavior change strategies and can effectively coach clients. Read more Pharmacy Technician right arrow down arrow Like most of Ohio, Cincinnati needs skilled pharmacy technicians. This online course will prepare you for the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board (PTCB) National Certification exam. Read more Certified Physical Therapy Aide right arrow down arrow There's a growing need for physical therapy aides. In fact, PT aide jobs will grow over 29 percent through 2029. This online course will help you master the skills you need to work as a physical therapy aide. Read more Other Career Training Programs University of Cincinnati, in partnership with ed2go, offers online open enrollment career training programs designed to provide the skills necessary to acquire professional level positions for many in-demand occupations.

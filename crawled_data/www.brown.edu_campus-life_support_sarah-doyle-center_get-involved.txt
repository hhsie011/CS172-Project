You can visit the SDC during our regular business hours and find a cozy place to study or meet with friends while enjoying a cup of tea. Follow the SDC on social media and subscribe to our e-newsletter to stay informed of our upcoming events. Every year, we hire 6-8 student coordinators to help us cultivate community within the center and plan our yearly events and programs. Check "Student Recruitment” on Workday for any open positions (we generally post open positions during the Spring semester for the following academic year). Additionally, the SDC maintains a list of Brown student organizations and groups centered on gender and feminism. You can access this list here.

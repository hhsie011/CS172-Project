Information collected during the registration process is entered into a database and used to identify the bicycle in case of theft. How to secure your bike with a U-Lock Bicycle Laws In Ohio, most motor vehicle traffic laws also apply to cyclists. In addition, the city of Cincinnati has specific ordinances pertaining to bicycles on city sidewalks. Ohio Department of Transportation Cycling Guide City of Cincinnati Municipal Code UC Bike Kitchen The Bike Kitchen is a free service provided to the community by the Office of Sustainability at the University of Cincinnati. The Bike Kitchen seeks to promote bicycle culture, safety, and accessibility as a sustainable form of transportation that builds community and fosters healthy lifestyles. The Bike Kitchen offers repair services, tune-ups, and workshops for free to the community. Register your bicycle: Please fill out the form below to register your bicycle.

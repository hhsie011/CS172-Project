Use the previous and next buttons to change the displayed slide Slide 1: Photo by: Maria Eduarda Vaz Slide 2: photo by Mariana Espíndola Slide 3: Photo by: Maria Eduarda Vaz Previous Next The Columbia Women’s Leadership Network in Brazil Program selects annual groups of professionals with the goal of creating a growing network of women who will contribute to the transformation of Brazil. Taking advantage of the talents and academic excellence of Columbia University, each cohort will consist of women working in different areas of public management, third and private sector from different parts of Brazil. These professionals will participate in workshops in Brazil and at the Columbia University campus in New York City. The program consists of different modules that include strategic training and networking activities, roundtables, a seminar and mentorship sessions. All the activities will be closely supervised/facilitated/supported by Columbia Global Centers | Rio de Janeiro, including academic coordination and local management, in close collaboration with faculty from Columbia University.

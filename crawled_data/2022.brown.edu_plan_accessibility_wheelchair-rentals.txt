Please contact individual vendors to confirm prices and availability. This information may change at any time without notice. Brown University provides this information as a service and is not responsible for vendor performance. Independence HomeHealthWares 35 Agnes St. East Providence, RI 02914 401-273-8888 Rents transport chairs for $10 per day and can deliver for $9.95. Closed weekends. Kent Home Medical 1 Knight St. Warwick, RI 02886 401-732-0022 Rents wheelchairs and other assistive equipment (no power scooters). Wheelchairs are rented by the month for $89/month (manual) and $55/month (transport). Liberty Mobility 5 Church Street West Warwick, RI 02893 401-827-8800 Rents scooters and wheelchairs with a 2-day minimum. Delivery is available for a fee depending on location. Mobility Equipment Recyclers 6802 Post Road North Kingstown, RI 02852 Phone: 401-294-4111 Fax: 401-884-0270 www.MobilityEquipmentForLess.com They will deliver for a fee with advance notice and have a rental form online. They offer wheelchairs, scooters, knee scooters and are open on Saturdays.

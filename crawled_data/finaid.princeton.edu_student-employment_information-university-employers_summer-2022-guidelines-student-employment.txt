The 2021-2022 Federal Work-Study (FWS) program also ends on May 15, 2022. If you wish to extend FWS student hires from spring into summer, you will need to rehire them for the summer period, using departmental funds. If you wish to extend non-FWS students from spring into summer, please contact student employment to request an extension. Graduating seniors (Class of 2022) are prohibited from working after May 23, 2022. Work from abroad: Undergraduates residing outside the United States during summer 2022, including students studying abroad, are prohibited from working for the University.

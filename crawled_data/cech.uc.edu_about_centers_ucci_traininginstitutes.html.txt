Training Institutes UCCI hosts Training Institutes to provide open registration training opportunities to all fit a range of need! From smaller agencies seeking to adopt a new program with few staff to train, to large agencies who have previously adopted programs and need options for training new staff, we provide both online events as well as in person training events right here at the University of Cincinnati, Ohio. Virtual Training Institute (online) UCCI’s Virtual Training Institute (VTI) is a series of ongoing training opportunities comprised of synchronous online courses each that rotate monthly through UC assessment, interventions and CBI curricula. Costs range from $1,500 - $3,500 per person depending on the course, and attendance and participate is required for all days of the event. Mon-Thurs, Nov 7-10 and Mon-Thurs, Nov 14-17 from 12-5p EST daily Annual Training Institutes (at UC) Each year, UCCI hosts a week-long Training Institute on campus at the University of Cincinnati. Registrants can choose from a variety of training options, and take advantage of networking opportunities with staff from other agencies across the US. These institutes allow agencies who have previously been trained and implemented UCCI programs to get new hire staff up to speed, while also allowing individuals from smaller agencies a cost-effective way to implement evidence-based practices.

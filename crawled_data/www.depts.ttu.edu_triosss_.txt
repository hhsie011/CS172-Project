Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index TRIO Student Support Services Menu Search TTU TRIOSSS APPLY NOW TRIO Student Support Services (SSS) is an academic support program for students who are first-generation* and/or demonstrate a financial need. Students registered with TTU Student Disability Services are also eligible for the program. TRIO SSS provides academic advising, career advising, financial aid assistance and graduate school preparation. The program also offers leadership development and a supportive community focused on empowering students to succeed. *First-generation means the student's parents did not receive a 4-year degree. Join TRIO SSS TRIO SSS Events and Workshops TRIO SSS Scholarship Application TRIO SSS Staff & Advising Current TRIO SSS participants are able to schedule appointments by emailing their advisor or calling 806-742-3629. Meetings will be conducted in person, on the phone or through Zoom. TRIO SSS Tutoring & Computer Lab Tutoring with TRIO SSS tutors is currently available in Doak Hall 108. Contact your tutor to schedule an appointment or walk-in during their scheduled hours. Additionally, TRIO SSS participants have access to 24/7 online tutoring via www.tutor.com/texastechsss.

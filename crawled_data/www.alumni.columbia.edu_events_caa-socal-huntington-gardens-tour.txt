On this 90-minute outdoor easy walking tour, participants have the opportunity to explore some of the magnificent gardens and learn how Henry and Arabella Huntington’s San Marino Ranch was transformed into the world-famous cultural landmark that we know today. Participants will be guided by Huntington Gardens docents through the grounds that include 16 themed gardens, three art galleries, the library, and current special exhibitions.

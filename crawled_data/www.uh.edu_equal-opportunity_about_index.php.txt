The Office of Equal Opportunity Services (EOS) supports the University's goal of fostering a diverse and inclusive environment by engaging in community dialogue, providing prevention education and training, and enforcing the Anti-Discrimination and Sexual Misconduct/Title IX Policies. In addition, EOS administers the University's Affirmative Action Plan, facilitates reasonable workplace accommodations for employees, and provides tools and resources for Electronic Information Resource (EIR) accessibility. Please explore our website and reach out to our office to learn more about how EOS can support your journey here at UH.

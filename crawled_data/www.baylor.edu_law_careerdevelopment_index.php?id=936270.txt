Your Career as a Baylor Lawyer Begins Here. With the number of jobs limited, competition among law school graduates is high. Legal employers are more selective on hires, it’s important to start early—and have a plan. We can help. The Career Development Office (CDO) provides students with a wealth of programs and resources to help you begin your search, build your credentials, connect with potential employers, and launch your career. Let’s get started.

HR Records Attendance & Leave The Attendance & Leave section within Human Resources is responsible for time reporting compliance, administration of various leave plans, HR information system security, and processing salaried employee separations and applicable leave payouts. The Office of Human Resources offers training and provides guidance for the administration of Florida State University's Attendance & Leave policies. This is coupled with continued centralized monitoring of time reporting for the entire University.

Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Subscribe an Academic Calendar Feed to Your Personal Calendar The UW has several academic calendars; each one shows different information: Application Deadlines Commencement Dates of Instruction Grade Deadlines Holidays Registration Deadlines Tuition and Fee Deadlines It’s easy to subscribe an academic calendar to a personal calendar. From the tables further down this page, you will choose the URL corresponding to the calendar you want to subscribe to, and paste it in the field indicated in your personal calendar. When you enter the URL, the calendar will appear in your personal calendar. You can add all of the academic calendars to your personal calendar at once. Or, if you want only grade deadlines or holidays, for example, you can add individual calendars. Complete Academic Calendars Individual Academic Calendars, Seattle Individual Academic Calendars, Bothell Individual Academic Calendars, Tacoma Tip: When you copy the URL, make sure you don’t include any extra spaces at the end of the URL. Note: The subscription to an academic calendar feed includes current and future events, but not past events. To subscribe a calendar feed to your Office 365 calendar, follow these steps: StepsActions In Outlook Web Access, open the calendar. Find OTHER CALENDARS. Right-click and then select Open Calendar. Under Internet Calendar, paste the URL of the chosen calendar feed. To subscribe a calendar feed to your Outlook calendar, follow these steps: StepsActions In Outlook, open the calendar. Find Other Calendars, and then right-click. Select Add Calendar, From internet. In the space provided, enter the URL for the chosen calendar feed. To subscribe a calendar feed to your Google calendar, follow these steps: StepsAction Open your Google Calendar. Next to Other Calendars, click the drop-down menu and select Add by URL. In the URL field, enter the URL for the chosen calendar feed. Complete Academic Calendars UW Seattle Academic Calendar Feed webcal://www.trumba.com/calendars/sea_acad-cal.ics.

Research shows you’ll do better academically and are more likely to achieve your goal of earning your degree. You’ll enjoy the convenience of being close to your classes and dining halls—and you’ll be more likely to get involved in student organizations, service learning and leadership opportunities. Hall Breakdown Creighton’s eight residence halls serve students from freshman to senior year. Learn about our halls below. Apartment Style Apartment style residence hall floors feature shared or private bedrooms and bathrooms accessible from within the apartment.

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Innovation Hub at Research Park Menu Search TTU Research Home Research Park Innovation Hub Tech Hub Entrepreneur Alumni Network The Texas Tech Alumni Association (TTAA) is excited to present the Tech Hub Entrepreneur Alumni Network. Texas Tech University has committed to developing invaluable entrepreneur, inventor, investor, mentor relationships within the student body, alumni network, and West Texas. This alumni network will encourage an ongoing Red Raider connection for our graduates as they go on to future success. The purpose of Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network is to create entrepreneurial scholarships for students and to Texas Tech alumni and connect them with the university in a meaningful way to our entrepreneurship and innovation initiatives. The Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network provides opportunities to recognize alumni contributions to the entrepreneurial ecosystem and assist entrepreneur transitions from the classroom to the marketplace through networking, mentoring, and activities supporting our Red Raider entrepreneurs. How to Join Get the benefits of being in both organizations by visiting www.texastechalumni.org/join. Select your TTAA annual membership level, then make your network contribution ($50 minimum) to the Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network on the Alumni Network step. Your network gift will go directly to the Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network at the Innovation Hub. If you are already a TTAA member and would like to join the Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network, contact TTAA at ttaa@ttu.edu. You can also contact the Texas Tech Alumni Association during standard business hours at 806.742.3641 for immediate assistance. In addition to your TTAA Member benefits, you will receive the following: Exclusive access to Conference Room 211 at the Innovation Hub Access to host one free event at the Hub per academic year Invitation to all Hub Programs and Events Contact Taysha Williams at Taysha.williams@ttu.edu if you are already a member of the TTU Alumni Association and would like to be a part of the Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network. Stay Connected Find out more about the Tech Hub Entrepreneur Network and stay connected though network events and news at www.texastechalumni.org/iHUB. THANK YOU FOR YOUR SUPPORT!

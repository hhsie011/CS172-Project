He received his B.A. from the University of Wisconsin (1968) and his Ph.D. from the University of Pennsylvania (1991). He is the author of Economy and Nature in the Fourteenth Century: Money, Market Exchange, and the Emergence of Scientific Thought (1998). He is currently researching changes in the concept of balance and equilibrium c. 1225–1375.

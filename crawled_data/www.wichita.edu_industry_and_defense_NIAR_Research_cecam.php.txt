CECAM is headquartered at Wichita State University’s National Institute for Aviation Research. CECAM Mission: To provide the nation with a center for the validation and quality assurance of composites and advanced materials to be applied in the construction of aircraft through: research, testing, certification, and technology transfer coordination and cooperation with the FAA, aircraft manufacturers, materials suppliers, and airline companies education of the aircraft manufacturing and maintenance workforces Core Members: Auburn University University of California University of Delaware Mississippi State University Northwestern University Purdue University Tuskegee University Wichita State University FAA Sponsor: William J.

You’ll gain essential knowledge and techniques to bring your artwork to life—from drawing to digital animations—and build qualifications for career success in a variety of creative settings. Learn how the Animation certificate is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Animation students develop the skills necessary to bring their artwork to life. Curriculum To complete the 15-credit-hour undergraduate certificate, you’ll take the following courses: Life Drawing Computer Modeling Digital Animation I Digital Animation II One of the following: Digital Animation III, Prop and Character, Rigging, Storyboarding. View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

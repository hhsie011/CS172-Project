Your First Legal Steps Events Past Event How to Start a Business in China: Your First Legal Steps Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo May 29, 2022 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM Online Calling all founders! Do not miss this unique opportunity to gain practical legal knowledge. Time: May 29, 2022, 8:00PM-10:00PM (Beijing) Event language: Chinese Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to attend a virtual lecture that every startup in China should pay attention to. Points to be covered: Overview of the key legal issues in establishing a business in China Do's and don'ts of financing your business Tips to avoid legal pitfalls in business This event is part of the Beijing Center's business incubation program. Our business incubation program provides young entrepreneurs of Columbia University and other Ivy League schools with access to life-changing opportunities to empower their talents and passion for innovation. Learn more about the program and apply HERE. Speaker Feifei Yao is a Partner of Corporate Legal Affairs in Yingke Law Firm, Director of Corporate Legal Affairs External Communication Center, and an investment and financing lawyer at the Securities and Capital Markets Department in Yingke Law Firm. Yao is also a coach of corporate innovation and an international second-level Human Resources Manager. She has innovative and capital-oriented thinking, and she is committed to combining law and business to empower innovators in this era.

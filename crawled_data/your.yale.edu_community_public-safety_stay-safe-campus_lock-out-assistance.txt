If you are locked out, please call our Central Alarm Station at 203-785-5555. A uniformed security officer will be dispatched to your location to provide this service. Students, please be prepared to provide: your name, the college in which you reside, entryway letter, room number, and floor for verification purposes when calling the Central Alarm Station. You will then be instructed to wait for the security officer in front of the locked door. Should you be able to open your door prior to the officer’s arrival, please call the Central Alarm Station and request to cancel lockout services. On weekdays, between the hours of 9:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m., students should contact their Head of College Office if locked out of their room. To be issued a key for your office or department door, please contact Facilities at 203-432-6888.

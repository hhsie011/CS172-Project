If your department or program is in need of new stationery, our experienced project coordinators will assist you to ensure that the ordering process goes as smoothly as it should. We can help in all aspects including designing the materials and determining the appropriate paper stock to use. You can also supply us with your print ready files, if the files have already been created. We are also able to consult on the proper use of University branding on all of your stationery. All stationery is printed on 100% recycled paper. Letterhead and envelopes will feature a Sustainable Columbia mark. Helpful Tip: For departmental stationery, speak to a Columbia Print representative about accepted University standards for envelopes and letterhead.

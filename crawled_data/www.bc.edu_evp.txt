Working with the President and Board of Trustees, the Executive Vice President also co-directs the University’s strategic planning efforts with the Provost and Dean of Faculties and directs the University’s campus master planning efforts. University Strategic Plan Ever to Excel: Advancing Boston College’s Mission In 2017, the Boston College Board of Trustees approved it’s current University Strategic Plan which identified four strategic directions that shape the University’s priorities over the next decade. Campus Master Plan Presenting a vision for the University's physical growth that would support its strategic directions, a comprehensive Campus Master Plan was created in 2009. This continues to provide guidance as the University considers future needs. Reopening Boston College A centralized repository of COVID-19 information for the BC community that includes updates on resources, policies, procedures, FAQ reponses, and communications. Veteran's Advisory Group Created in 2018, the Boston College Veteran's Advisory Group (BCVAG) was established as a resources for the University's leadership on veterans' issues and activities. Ethical Conduct / Ethicspoint Reporting The Professional Standards and Business Conduct Policy outlines the ethical standards of behavior for all Boston College employees and how to report any suspected fraud or unethical practices. Inclement Weather Policy Emergency closing, late openings, or early dismissal due to inclement weather is outlined in the Emergency Closing policy.

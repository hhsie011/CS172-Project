In the event of an immediate threat, the Police Department shall follow the emergency notification procedure described above. In other cases, if the Chief believes a serious or ongoing danger or threat exists, the Chief, working with other University officials as appropriate, shall provide a timely warning on the “BC Info” and “BC Emergency” web pages. Depending on the particular circumstances of the crime, the Chief may also use a variety of other means to communicate the timely warning, including emails, text messages, and use of campus media.

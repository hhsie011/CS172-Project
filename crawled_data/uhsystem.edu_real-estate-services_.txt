The Office of Real Estate Services: Advises UHS campuses on real estate matters including performing market research and strategically planning campus development; Leads the sale and purchase of properties for UHS; Oversees auxiliary properties and assets (such as the University of Houston Technology Bridge) including fiscal oversight, leasing management, maintenance, construction projects, capital planning and daily operations; Interfaces with public agencies and third parties to prepare and/or negotiate licenses, leases, easements, interlocal agreements and encroachments; Maintains and catalogs real estate documents and records for UHS; Manages mineral interests.

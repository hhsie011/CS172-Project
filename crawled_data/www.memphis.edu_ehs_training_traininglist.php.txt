Health and Safety Refresher – Any employee with a "non-hazardous" job status who has not attended a refresher in the last 12 months should visit Learning Curve to register for a Health and Safety Refresher course. Specialized Training – Employees with specialized job tasks may require specialized health and safety training. Many of our Specialized Training are offered in class or online. To sign up for a classroom specialized training, contact us at ehs@memphis.edu.

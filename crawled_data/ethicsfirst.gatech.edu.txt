At Georgia Tech, our mission is to develop leaders who advance technology and improve the human condition. We can accomplish our mission by putting Ethics First. The Office of Ethics and Compliance sits within the Office of the General Counsel. The Office works with the Georgia Tech community to manage compliance risk and put our ethical values into practice. Ethics First Join the Mission We Act Ethically at Georgia Tech Code of Ethics Launch Event! To help launch the new Code of Ethics, the Office of Ethics and Compliance is hosting a Code of Ethics Employee Celebration on Tech Green, Wednesday, April 13 from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. Attendees that download or receive a hard copy of the Code of Ethics will receive a free voucher for either food trucks, a free t-shirt, or a free King of Pops popsicle (first come, first serve as supplies last!). Food truck vouchers are limited to the first 100 attendees. Add the event to your calendar here! *If inclement weather occurs, the program will be cancelled.

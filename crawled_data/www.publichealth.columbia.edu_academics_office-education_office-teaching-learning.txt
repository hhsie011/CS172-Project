OTL offers a variety of consultation services for faculty, teaching assistants, and doctoral students. In addition to hosting a robust series of workshops throughout the year, OTL staff are available to meet one-on-one with instructors to develop customized support services. Our programs and resources encourage use of educational best practice to achieve our School’s vision of a truly exceptional, inclusive public health learning environment.

Go >> Student Organizations Chi Sigma Alpha – Chi Sigma Iota – Council for Exceptional Children – Council of Teachers of English – Council of Teachers of Mathematics – International Literacy Association Student Council – Kappa Delta Pi – National Science Teachers Association – South Carolina Association for the Education of Young Children Go >> Study Abroad Reggio Emilia Collaborative Study Abroad is a cultural immersion experience in Italy where students take classes while living with a host family. Go >> Student Advisory Boards The Undergraduate Student Advisory Board and Graduate Student Advisory Board work on behalf of the student body, recruit and mentor students, and serve as ambassadors of the College. Go >> Departmental Honors Students pursuing departmental honors enroll in a series of courses in their major department that prepares them to conduct and present their research.

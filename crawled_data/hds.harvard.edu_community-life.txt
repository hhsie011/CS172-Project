The HDS student body, faculty, and wider Harvard community offer ways to advance social justice, provide spiritual enrichment, and support the development of leaders in their fields who will work for a more equitable world. With more than 35 student organizations and 500+ recurring worship services, workshops, and group meetings, the HDS community is diverse and vibrant. Religious and Spiritual Life The world's many religions and spiritual traditions are practiced as much as they are studied at HDS, with astonishing variety, texture, criticism, love, and devotion.

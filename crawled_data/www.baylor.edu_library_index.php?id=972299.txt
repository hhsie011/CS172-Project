This form is designed to build a list of reserves for a single course. Follow these instructions in using this form: Complete the top part of the form which provides course information. Complete the information for an item to be put on the reserve list (book or journal article). Click on the "Add Another" button to add that item to the list. Repeat steps 2 and 3 for each item to be put on the list. When done, click on the "Send Request" button. A copy of the list that was sent to Reserves will display. You can print this page for your records if you wish to. Use the "Back" button on your browser to go back to the form page. If you want to send another Reserve List, click on the "Reset Form" button to clear all the information from the form.

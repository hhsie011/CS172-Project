Haidian District, Beijing Dear Columbia Lions, congratulations and welcome to Columbia! Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Global China Connection - Columbia, a student-run organization for Chinese undergraduate students, invite you to a welcome reception for the Class of 2025 and families. Please join us to meet with your fellow students and to learn more about the University and the Beijing Center. Current student, alumni, and parent representatives will also be in attendance to share with you their stories and experiences and to guide you on how to make the best of your time at Columbia and in New York City. We will also have a special session for Chinese-student-led clubs to present themselves. In addition, we will host breakout sessions and a Social Mixer for Q&A and open discussions. We look forward to seeing you and your parents, and welcome again to the Columbia community! Following the event, there will be a piano recital by Columbia College alumnus Nansong Huang '16 at the Beijing Center from 6 p.m. to 8:40 p.m. We encourage students and parents who love music and art to join. Agenda 2:00 p.m. - 2:30 p.m. Registration 2:30p.m. - 2:45 p.m. Opening Remarks 2:45 p.m. - 3:30 p.m. Student Representatives Panel 3:30 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. Presentations by Alumni Representatives 4:00 p.m. - 4:15 p.m. Intermission 4:15 p.m. - 4:45 p.m. Presentations by Parent Representatives 4:45 p.m. - 5:15 p.m. Club Presentations 5:15 p.m. - 5:45 p.m. Social Mixer 5:45 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. Closing Remarks 6:00 p.m. - 7:30 p.m. Dinner for Students (optional) 6:00 p.m. - 8:40 p.m. Nansong Huang's Piano Recital (optional) Reminders The event is open to Columbia undergraduates and their parents only; Please register for this event by July 4, 11:59 p.m. (Beijing); Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time.

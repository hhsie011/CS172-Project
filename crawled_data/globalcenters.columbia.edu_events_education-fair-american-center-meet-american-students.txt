Events Past Event Education Fair at the American Center: Meet American Students! Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo November 19, 2019 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM American Center, US Embassy in Tunis Show Map Want to learn more about studying at Columbia University and the Rambourg Foundation Fellowship ? Want to meet American students and learn more about their colleges and universities in the U.S.? Join us at the American Center, Tunis,for an education fair and get a better understanding of study in the U.S. and the diversity of American campuses. Students from the following institutions will be present as well: Brown University Hamilton College Colgate University University of North Carolina Stonehill College Skidmore College The American Center, the exchange programs and the Embassy’s EducationUSA program have many resources for Tunisians interested in studying at American universities. Come find out more and achieve your dream of studying in the U.S.!

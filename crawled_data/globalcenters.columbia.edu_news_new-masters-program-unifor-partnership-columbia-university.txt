partnership with Columbia University Rio de Janeiro New Masters program at UNIFOR in partnership with Columbia University June 22, 2015 Columbia University and its partnership with the Edson Queiroz Foundation - University of Fortaleza is rendering more fruits. The partnership that began with the Professional Master in Law, offered by UNIFOR in partnership with Columbia Law School. The program is on its first class and has Judges, Prosecutors, Supreme Court Judges and Police Officers among its students. In the coming months, UNIFOR is starting its new program: the Professional Master in Business Administration. This program to be offered, also in partnership with Columbia is in the process of selecting its first class. For more information on the program, please visit the website.

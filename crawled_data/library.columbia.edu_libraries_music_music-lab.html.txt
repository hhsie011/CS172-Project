The Digital Music Lab (DML), located in the Music & Arts Library in 701 Dodge, is equipped with 5 Mac workstations, with specialized music software and hardware, 2 Yamaha digital pianos, allowing users to to play, compose, record, scan, notate, edit, program, and analyze digital audio and music scores. Expert help is available from library staff. We welcome your thoughts and suggestions! Please contact us at musiclibrary@columbia.edu with any questions or comments.

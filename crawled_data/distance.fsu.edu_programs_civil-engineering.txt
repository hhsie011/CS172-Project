You can complete the program in a year and a half as a full-time student, or you can opt to go part-time at your own pace. With a curriculum totaling 30 credit hours, or 10, three-hour courses, the MEng offers areas of specialization in Structural Engineering and Construction & Infrastructure Engineering. Scholarships for non-Florida residents are available to reduce the cost of tuition.

the Career Center Plan Your Travel On-Campus Recruiting Information Sessions Employers The Florida State University is dedicated to helping students and employers connect! One of the nation's elite research universities, Florida State University is located on the oldest continuous site of higher education in Florida, in a community that fosters free inquiry and embraces diversity, located in the heart of the state capital. We strive to preserve, expand, and disseminate knowledge in the sciences, technology, arts, humanities, and professions while embracing a philosophy of learning strongly rooted in the traditions of the liberal arts and critical thinking. With an enrollment of over 45,000 students from 17 colleges within the university, women account for 57.7% of the enrollment and minorities 36.4%. Founded in 1851, we have had over 150 years of experience preparing students for professional careers and offer employers a diverse and savvy talent pool. The Career Center serves as a corporate gateway to the University to help employers understand the curriculum, meet key faculty and staff, and successfully recruit and hire students and alumni. For the ninth year in a row, the #1-way FSU students found employment was through Career Center services. Through a variety of services, programs, and resources we can help you connect and recruit FSU students. Attend a Career Fair Build relationships with FSU students and enhance your organization's brand on campus by participating in a career fair or networking session. The Career Center has multiple opportunities for you to meet with targeted groups of students and fill your hiring needs. Hire A Nole Recruit our talented students at the FSU campus or through one of our many career fairs by joining our on-campus recruiting program. Post jobs and internships, receive resume referrals, and participate in mock interviewing and other on-campus networking events. We offer many ways to meet students and help you increase your visibility at FSU. Build Your Brand Creating a personal brand can help set you apart from the crowd. Learn how to build your personal brand with help from the Career Center. Partner with the Career Center Being a Career Center Partner offers many perks and advantages. Find out more about becoming a Partner with the Career Center Plan Your Travel Ready to visit The Career Center? Access directions to our location, find places to stay with special discount rates for FSU career fair participants, and research things to do in Tallahassee.

2019-2020 Demonstration Day Events Past Event Urban Works Innovation Challenge 2019-2020 Demonstration Day Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo September 29, 2020 5:30 PM - 7:30 PM The Urban Works Innovation Challenge 2019-2020 Demonstration Day will feature startups solving the most pressing urban issues that India faces today: clean water, waste recycling, mental wellness, and gender security at the workplace, among others. Thus, through these novel innovations, they are advancing sustainability and safety in India's cities. It will be an evening of innovation, entrepreneurship, and networking amidst a gathering of investors, industry leaders, and faculty members from Columbia University. This event is by invitation only.

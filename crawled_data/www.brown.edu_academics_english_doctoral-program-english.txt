We promote the analysis of imaginative forms, cultural logics and literary and visual rhetorics across the Anglophone world. Our students are encouraged to think outside traditional conceptions of the discipline of literary studies, and often work with a diverse range of faculty, departments, and intellectual centers at Brown.

Planning Final Exam Information Final exam schedules are published about a month before the end of the semester. Final Exam Schedule for a Class My Final Exam Schedule Final exam times are scheduled based on the index of default final exam times, but your final exam may not be scheduled during its default slot, and not all classes hold final exams. Always consult your class syllabus, your instructor and/or your department to confirm your specific class information. Summer 2022 Final Exams These are the final exam dates for the Summer 2022 semester: Friday, July 8 – Saturday, July 9 (First Session) Wednesday, July 27 – Thursday, July 28 (Nine-Week Session) Saturday, August 13 and Monday, August 15 (Second and Whole Session) Dates will be updated for next semester as soon as they are available. “No-class” days and final exam days are listed in the official university academic calendar.

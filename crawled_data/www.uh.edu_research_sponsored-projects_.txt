Administration dschultz@central.uh.edu 713-743-8383 Training RACP Sponsored Projects The Office of Contracts and Grants (OCG) is established under the Division of Research to assist faculty in obtaining external funding to support their creative and scholarly activities. As such, OCG plays a role in helping the University fulfill its research, instruction and public service missions. In conjunction with college staff, OCG is tasked with the administrative and financial management of awards. Visit the Research Teams page to find pre-award, post-award, and financial reporting contacts for your department. Find specific proposal development, pre-award, and post-award activities on the Responsibilities Matrix page.

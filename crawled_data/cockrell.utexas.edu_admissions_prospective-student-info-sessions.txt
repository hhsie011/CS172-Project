Tuition Waivers Prospective Student Info Sessions Prospective Undergraduate Info Sessions Virtual Visit Prospective Student Info Sessions For Prospective Undergraduate and Transfer Students Information sessions are geared toward prospective freshmen, with the content focused on those who have not already been admitted. Sessions are typically hosted on Fridays or Mondays, 2-3 PM. An academic advisor from Engineering Student Services leads the sessions, and current students discuss their personal experiences in Texas Engineering. Students and families receive information about the different engineering majors offered as well as information about student services and programs. Learn More For Prospective Graduate Students We encourage you to connect with the graduate advisor and coordinator in your program of interest to learn more about visiting opportunities.

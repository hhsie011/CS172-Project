Street, Philadelphia, PA 19103 (Corner of 17th & Chestnut Streets, opposite Liberty Place) Reservations 2019 Temple University Member Rates Direct billing for Club Quarters Philadelphia location only. For employees not using departmental direct billing, you will be required to use your personal credit card for reservations at any of the other Club Quarters locations. Call 215-282-5100 and indicate that you want the Temple University room rate, which is billed directly to Temple, and provide the appropriate Club Quarters direct bill account number. After the reservation has been made, fax the "Temple University Room Reservation Request" to the number provided on the Room Reservation Request Form to confirm your reservation. This form is to be used only when charges are to be paid by Temple University. Invoice for payment will be forwarded directly to Accounts Payable. Please note: If Member Services does not receive this form, your reservation may be canceled. If your department does not have an account number, email directbilling@clubquarters.com. To establish an account, you will be required to send a memo on departmental letterhead to include the following information: the signature of the budget unit head responsible for charges to be billed to your account a list of the services that are to be charged the department budget account number to be charged Fax this memo to 203-487-4488 - attention: Direct Billing.

and Apostille Personal Information & Privacy Graduation Requirements Commencement Diplomas All diplomas are 11 inches by 14 inches except for those students graduating from the Agricultural Institute. Agricultural Institute diplomas earned prior to Fall 2020 may select diploma size of 8.5×11 or 11×14. Frames are available for purchase at the NC State Bookstore. Diploma Distribution December graduates: Final clearance for candidates other than Doctoral is early January. All diplomas will be mailed to the address the student listed as their “Diploma or Home/Mailing address” in their MyPack Portal by early February. May graduates: Final clearance for candidates other than Doctoral is early June. All diplomas will be mailed to the address the student listed as their “Diploma or Home/Mailing address” in their MyPack Portal by early July. Summer Session I graduates: Final clearance for candidates is late July, and diplomas will be mailed to the address the student listed as their “Diploma or Home/Mailing address” in their MyPack Portal by late August. Summer Session II graduates: Final clearance for candidates is early September, and diplomas will be mailed to the address the student listed as their “Diploma or Home/Mailing address” in their MyPack Portal by early October. NOTE: Students with a Judicial Hold set by the Office of Student Conduct or a Financial Hold set by the Cashier’s Office will not receive their diploma until the hold is released. It is the student’s responsibility to alert graduation@ncsu.edu of account clearance.

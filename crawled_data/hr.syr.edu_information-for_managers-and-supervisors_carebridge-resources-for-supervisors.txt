Contact HR Search Search Home Information for… Managers and Supervisors Carebridge Resources for Supervisors Carebridge Resources for Supervisors Carebridge, the provider for the Faculty and Staff Assistance Program (FSAP), is always available to support SU employees with all types of personal support. But did you know that they also play a special role to support you as a manager or supervisor? The Carebridge Management Consultation Team is available toll-free, 24 hours a day at 800.437.0911 to help with issues facing supervisors, such as: Absence Management Teambuilding Aggression in the Workplace Setting and Keeping Goals Supervisory Coaching Time Management Difficult Employees Employee Motivation Executive Communication Developing Talent Management/Supervisor telephone consultations are available through Carebridge on an unlimited basis to assist you in addressing these and other workplace issues. If you would like to request on-site crisis management/trauma support including grief/loss support from Carebridge for your department, contact your Senior HR Business Partner or the Wellness team to discuss your needs. Review other on-campus wellness resources to support you and your employees and stay current at wellness.syr.edu.

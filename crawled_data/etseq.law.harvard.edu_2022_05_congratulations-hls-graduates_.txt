Leave a Comment / Announcements, Historical & Special Collections / By KB Beck Congratulations to law school graduates at HLS and elsewhere! You have accomplished great things during a difficult few years, and we are so excited to celebrate your day with you. While you are making your own memories this weekend, enjoy this HLS Commencement image from fifty years ago, featuring Karen J. Orlin and others from the class of 1972: Black and white photo of Karen J. Orlin, HLS 1972, receiving her diploma in front of a large crowd. Credit: Bradford F. Herzog, 1972. Historical & Special Collections, HLS Library Your friends at the HLS Library wish you a celebratory day, and a wonderful summer. We can’t wait to see what you will do next!

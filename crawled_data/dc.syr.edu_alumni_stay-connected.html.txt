Home /Alumni/Stay Connected Stay Connected Social Networking Makes it Easy! Every Syracuse grad takes a different path in life, but it’s easy to stay connected to friends and former classmates through a number of social networking sites that keep alumni in touch with the University and each other. The ’Cuse Community is Syracuse’s original social network. Need to update your address, e-mail, or current employment? Want to search for college friends, submit a class note, or read about your fellow classmates? Join today—membership is free and exclusive to Syracuse alumni! You’re probably familiar with the Facebook pages for Syracuse University and Syracuse University Alumni, but did you know that there’s a special page for the DC region? Check out Syracuse University in DC. The same goes for Twitter. Follow @suindc to stay up to date with Syracuse University in the DC, Maryland, and Virginia areas. Syracuse University in DC is also on Instagram. Follow @syracuseuniversityindc to check out pictures from SU in DC alumni events, plus follow Otto on his adventures through the DC area. Of course, we can’t leave out LinkedIn. There are groups for B’more Orange, the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Baltimore, as well as for the Syracuse University alumni network and ’Cuse Connect, the career advice group sponsored by the Center for Career Services. Find more ways to connect. See a list of social networking and media platforms for Syracuse schools, colleges, and organizations in the University’s Social Media Directory.

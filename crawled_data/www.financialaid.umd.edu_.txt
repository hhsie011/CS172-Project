We are committed to providing you with helpful information and resources that can assist in making your investment in a UMD education affordable. Types of Aid Types of aid can be classified as either need-based, meaning you and your family are not able to cover educational expenses, or non-need based, which generally refers to aid you receive based on merit or qualification. In response to the coronavirus pandemic, the Higher Education Emergency Relief Fund was created to provide universities with funds for emergency financial aid grants. Learn more about the UMD Emergency Financial Aid Grant, the UMD Student COVID Relief Grant or the UMD Student COVID Relief Grant II. Federal loan repayment will restart* effective August 31, 2022. If you have a federal loan and expect to restart your repayment on August 31, 2022, please review the Returning to Repayment Fact Sheet and our Repaying Your Loans page. *Federal loan repayment has been suspended for the last two years due to the pandemic.

This elective certificate can be used toward completing the Applied Computing bachelor’s degree program. Learn how cybersecurity essentials is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum This 13-credit-hour certificate is comprised of four courses: Digital Forensics Cyber Physical Systems Cyber Risk Management Secure Web Development View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor."

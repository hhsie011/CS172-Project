In addition to the standard aid award, the University will provide additional grant aid of up to $495 to reimburse students for employment authorization and biometrics fees associated with DACA status renewal. In order to work on campus, DACA students must complete an I-9 form and provide a valid Employment Authorization Card to the Student Employment Office. For additional details and to request DACA reimbursement for employment authorization, please contact Benjamin Eley (beley@princeton.edu).

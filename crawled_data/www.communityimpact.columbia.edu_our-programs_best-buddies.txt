Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Best Buddies Best Buddies promotes the social inclusion and acceptance of individuals with intellectual or developmental disabilities (IDD) by matching CU students with community members who have IDD. Together, our members with and without disabilities launch advocacy efforts, volunteer nearby, and attend bonding events such as picnics, museum outings, baking events, and sports games.

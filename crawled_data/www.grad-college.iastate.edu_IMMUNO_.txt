The Immunobiology graduate program welcomes you to explore the opportunities for integrative graduate research and study toward an MS or PhD degree from Iowa State University. Our unique program includes diverse faculty expertise and flexible choices for individual specializations within the dynamic field of Immunobiology. We encourage all students to visit Iowa State University to see first hand the benefits of choosing ISU for your graduate education. If you would like to schedule a campus visit to learn more about what the ISU Immunobiology program can offer you, please contact us at the address below. Please notify us at least one to two weeks prior to your visit so that we may plan an excellent visit experience for you.

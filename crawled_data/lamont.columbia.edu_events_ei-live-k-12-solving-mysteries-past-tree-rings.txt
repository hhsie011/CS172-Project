Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo February 9, 2022 4:00 PM - 4:45 PM America/New_York Online K-12 Education Solving Mysteries of the Past-Tree Rings, Archaeology, and History Did you know that we can learn about the history of wooden material and artifacts through studying their tree rings? In this talk, students will explore how scientists study tree rings from historical structures to uncover mysteries of the past. Presenter: Cari Leland, Lecturer, Department of Earth and Environmental Sciences Target Audience: Grades 3-5 This event is free but registration is required.

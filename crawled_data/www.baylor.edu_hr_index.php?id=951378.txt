To equip you for success in your role, Baylor's purposefully-designed benefits package demonstrates our Christian commitment and caring community. The benefit chart below gives an overview of what Baylor University has to offer employees. These and other benefit advantages provide convenient opportunities for the wellbeing of Baylor faculty/staff, their families and retirees. U.S. Veterans Resources Educational Assistance NOTE: If you are a post-doc or post-doc fellow, please email askHR@baylor.edu with any benefit questions you may have. One of the ways Baylor achieves this is by offering generous time off available day 1 of employment. The chart below gives an overview of various leave options at Baylor. Eligible employees also enjoy 10 University recognized holidays. List of Time Off Categories & Leave Policies Time Off Faculty Staff Bereavement Leave [7] Sick Time [7] Vacation Time [7] Paid Parental Leave Paid Military/Emergency Responder Leave Maternity Leave Primary Caregiver Leave Military Leave Full Time (faculty, staff, and post-doc fellows (.75 FTE or more per semester) and Full time temp staff (30 or more hours a week) Part-time Faculty (less than .75FTE per semester) or Part Time Staff (29 or less hours per week) Not eligible to participate: Students (including graduate students), event workers (camp workers, concession workers, and any other individual classified as an event worker in the payroll records of the Employer), Employees whose employment contract provides the Employee shall not receive contributions, Employees of Brazos Valley Public Broadcasting Foundation, Post-Doctoral Fellows and Post-Doctoral Research Associates. Part-time faculty and staff are eligible for employer contributions if they have met eligibility requirements set forth in the plan document. Health Savings Accounts (HSAs) are available for eligible employees who enroll in the Baylor HDHP medical plan. Plans include Accident, Cancer/Specified Diseases, Short-Term Disability, Specified Health Event, Accidental Death & Dismemberment, and Long Term Care. Temporary or seasonal staff excluded.

branch of the Office of Postdoctoral Affairs is dedicated to providing mental health and wellness education and outreach that focuses on multiple areas of wellness, such as social, physical, emotional, and occupational wellness, among others. Join this month's Wellness Mini-Challenge to win a prize! Through these wellness initiatives, postdocs can develop tools and knowledge to care for their mental health and well-being during their postdoctoral training and beyond. With enhanced knowledge and resources, postdocs are empowered to create meaningful adjustments to support their well-being.

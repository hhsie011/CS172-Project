The requirements of the Latin American and Caribbean Studies major are designed to allow students to pursue a variety of distinct interests that align with a student’s personal and professional goals. This program helps prepare students for international careers in the public and private sectors, such as positions in government, business, nongovernmental and non-profit organizations, journalism, research, and teaching. Additionally, this major may serve as a vehicle into graduate school in a variety of fields.

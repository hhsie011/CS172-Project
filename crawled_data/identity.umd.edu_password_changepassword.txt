1.32.6 Passphrase Management Update Passphrase Username Passphrase New Passphrase Confirm New Passphrase Forgot Passphrase Display your Directory Entry Update Security Questions Associate Passphrase Reset Associate Profile Page Passphrase Requirements A passphrase must be at least 12 characters in length. A passphrase should contain at least one character from each of the following sets: Uppercase alphabet (A-Z) Lowercase alphabet (a-z) Number (0-9) and special characters (such as # @ $ & among others) A passphrase may not begin or end with the space character. A passphrase may not contain more than two consecutive identical characters. Passphrases should not contain: carriage return, linefeed, /, \, or a trailing * symbol.

Her primary research interests are the linkages between atmospheric carbon dioxide and climate in the past, including the validation and application of paleoproxies in marine archives. Fields of Interest (paleo)climate, ocean chemistry, plankton biology Education 2002 Dr. rer. nat., Alfred Wegener Institute for Polar and Marine Research, Bremerhaven 1999 Diplom (masters) in Marine Biology, University of Bremen, Germany. 1995 Vordiplom (BA) in Biology, University of Bielefeld, Germany.

Applications are generally available approximately one year before the term you wish to enter. Choose the application that best fits your intentions. If you have ever applied to the UofM and paid the application fee, but did not enroll at that time, complete the Readmit Application. If you have ever attended the UofM and been absent at least one complete Spring or Fall semester, please apply using the Readmit Application. If you are currently enrolled at another post-secondary institution and plan to transfer your UofM course(s) back to that institution upon completion, please apply using the Transfer Application.

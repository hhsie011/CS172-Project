Our Professional Development Certificate Program Put your summer to work and have it benefit your professional future! Join our “Career Ready Tiger: CCPD Professional Development Certificate” program this summer that will provide presentations and programming on a variety of career related topics. You will be able to choose from sessions on Communication and Personal Brand, Polishing your Professional Image, Entrepreneurial Endeavors and more. This Professional Development Certificate is an excellent addition to your resume to show employers that you effectively prepared yourself for your future workplace. If you are interested, and want additional information, please email Alex Newsome at arinz@clemson.edu. You can also directly apply to the program now at https://forms.gle/FfxmGRj9Jy4jLEk89.

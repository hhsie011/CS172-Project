Once you earn a management bachelor’s degree online from Oregon State University, you’ll be uniquely qualified to create business solutions to global problems and fill a need for strategic communicators in the job market. Hone your leadership and strategic communication skills with the program's emphasis on team-building and collaboration. Discover how to create a shared vision for teams and companies, manage organizational change and communication, and implement strategies that maximize a company’s value. This management program is offered by Oregon State's AACSB-accredited College of Business and it is delivered by OSU Ecampus, a national leader in online education. Oregon State Ecampus delivers a variety of degree programs online that are focused on business and management. Explore similar programs.

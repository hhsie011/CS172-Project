You'll take courses from a diverse range of departments and develop a work portfolio you can present to potential employers. Learn how Museum Studies is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 15-credit-hour certificate comprises management-based courses in anthropology, history and public administration, giving you an overview of nonprofit management, collections, exhibits, presentation and education. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

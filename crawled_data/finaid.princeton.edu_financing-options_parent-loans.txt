A review of creditworthiness is required, and both aid and non-aid families may apply. Parent applicants may choose between a fixed or variable rate on the Princeton Parent Loan. Additional details may be found online here. Direct PLUS Loan for Parents The Direct PLUS program is another option for both aid and non-aid families to finance the parental share of Princeton's costs. The PLUS loan is credit-based. Additional details may be found online at www.studentloans.gov.

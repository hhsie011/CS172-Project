For major advising, please go to the “A” tab on your CSUF Portal. Once you have clicked on that you will see a tab that reads “Meet Your Advisors.” Click on that tab and you will see both information for General Education Advising as well as a drop down menu to choose your major for advising. Once you activate this drop down menu, it will populate the information needed in order to contact your specific major advisor(s).

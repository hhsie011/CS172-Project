Wood, BSN, MHA, FACHE, NE-BC Chief Clinical Instructor LTC, United States Army Email Dan_Wood@baylor.edu Phone 210.808.9569 High Res Photo CV Download CV Professional Summary: Dan Wood is the Chief Clinical Instructor at the MHA/MBA Army Baylor Graduate Program since 2018. He holds a BS in Nursing, MS in Health Administration, and is currently working on his EdD in Interdisciplinary Leadership. LTC Wood has served in the Army Nurse Corps since 2001 in various clinical, management, and administrative roles. LTC Wood is a Fellow in the American College of Healthcare Executives, a Board-Certified Nurse Executive, and a Certified Professional in Healthcare Quality. LTC Wood’s proposed dissertation research for his EdD Program is centered on value-based purchasing and its effects hospital-acquired infections.

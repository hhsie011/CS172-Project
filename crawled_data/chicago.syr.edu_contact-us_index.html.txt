Contact our regional advancement staff: Keri Lowder '95, G'96 Director of Development, Regional Advancement khlowder@syr.edu 315.440.9499 About Keri Keri Lowder joined the Regional Team in July 2021 and covers the Midwest Region and Mountain States with a primary emphasis on Chicago, Cleveland, Cincinnati, and Denver. She also facilitates the work of the Chicago Regional Council. Keri comes to Syracuse with a strong background in development work including All Chicago Making Homelessness History, City Year Chicago, and Year Up Chicago. She is an alumna of Syracuse and former track team member. Keri is based out of Chicago, IL.

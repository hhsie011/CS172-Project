Students residing in Texas who would like to initiate on-line services, please call the Student Counseling Center (806-742-3674; M-F 8:00-5:00) or email Debra Ashley (debra.ashley@ttu.edu) for further information. The Student Counseling Center provides in-person individual and couples services effective July 6, 2021. For students residing in Lubbock who would like to initiate in-person SCC services, please come to our Walk-In Clinic hours, 12:30 to 3:30 Monday through Friday. You will complete some initial electronic paperwork and meet with a counselor for a same day, 20-30 minute triage session. Holiday Hours The Student Counseling Center will be closing at 3:30 pm on Monday, April 11th. The SCC will also be closed on Monday, May 30th for the Memorial Day holiday. The Texas Tech Crisis HelpLine (806-742-5555) is available 24/7 for any student in immediate mental health crisis during the holidays. Contact Us If you have any questions please call 806.742.3674. To initiate services, please come to our Walk-In Clinic hours.

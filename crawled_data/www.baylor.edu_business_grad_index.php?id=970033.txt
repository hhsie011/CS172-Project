The MBA is accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) The MHA program is tied with programs such as Johns Hopkins and Cornell University Complete U.S. News & World Report rankings Residency Year at a Top Hospital The Army-Baylor Program consists of one didactic year hosted at the U.S. Medical Center of Excellence (MEDCoE), Fort Sam Houston, Texas and one residency year at large federal or civilian healthcare system Civilian residency site options include the Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, Emory Health, UCLA Health, and Massachusetts General Hospital Military residency site options include Brooke, Tripler, Madigan, Landstuhl, and Walter Reed National Military Medical Center Peer Network of Professionals Join a robust inter-service network of healthcare professionals in the federal health sector Classmates have diverse backgrounds and may have experience working as physicians, nurses, administrators, social work, and medical evacuation pilots, among many others.

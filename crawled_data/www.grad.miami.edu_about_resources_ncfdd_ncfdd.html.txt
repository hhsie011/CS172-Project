Center for Faculty Development & Diversity (NCFDD). The NCFDD is an independent faculty development center dedicated to supporting academics in making successful transitions throughout their careers, and the institutional membership provides all faculty, postdocs, and graduate students with free access to the NCFDDâ€™s member resources. Activate your free personal membership account To claim your member benefits, you will need to activate your free personal membership account. Visit FacultyDiversity.org/Join and follow the steps below or click here to view a step-by-step guide with screenshots. We encourage you to take advantage of the resources available to you through the NCFDD. If you have any technical questions or would like to request reimbursement for an individual membership you purchased within the past three months, please email the NCFDD at Membership@FacultyDiversity.org. For other inquiries, please email graduateschool@miami.edu.

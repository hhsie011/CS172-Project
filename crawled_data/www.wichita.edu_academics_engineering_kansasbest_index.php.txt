The Kansas BEST competition, hosted by the Wichita State University College of Engineering, challenges students to build a remote-controlled robot using only specific competition kit materials within a six-week period. Teams must design, build, and test a robot that can outperform their competitors. Professional engineers and teachers volunteer to coach middle and high school teams through the engineering design process. 2022 Game Info & Event Dates Volunteer Sign Up Newsletters Sponsors Sponsors allow us to offer this program to participating teams free of charge.

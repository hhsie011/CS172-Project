Istanbul Turkey through its Cinema April 12, 2022 A Film Series Organized by Zeynep Çelik, Richard Peña, and A. Tunç Şen Sakıp Sabancı Center for Turkish Studies and the Istanbul Center offer a series of conversations on Turkish Cinema during the academic year 2021-2022. Bringing eminent film critics, writers, scholars, and filmmakers together, the conversations will focus on critically acclaimed films, highlighting their global significance and their power to portray Turkey's realities. The six films discussed reflect the socio-cultural and political predicaments of Turkey since the 1960s and represent its rich cinematographic tradition.

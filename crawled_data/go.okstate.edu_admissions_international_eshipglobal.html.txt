Select “Receive documents” under the “Universities” tab. Then, choose “Oklahoma State University Main Campus” and please select “Undergraduate Admissions” as the sender address. When prompted on their page, be sure to enter your name and OSU Banner ID exactly as it appears on your OSU admission documents. Select the shipment service you wish to use. We recommend choosing FedEx for the quickest and most reliable shipment. Once you make the payment, an email confirmation will be sent to you about your order and the Undergraduate Admissions staff will be notified that you are ready to receive your documents. International undergraduate students admitted to Oklahoma State University must use eShipGlobal to receive their admissions welcome package and Certificate of Eligibility Form I-20. If you experience any difficulty in registering and processing the shipment, please use the “Help” link in the site for step by step instructions. If you have additional questions about how to use this service, please email support@eshipglobal.com.

Your training. New heights. Your world changes when you achieve your professional goals. As you explore new perspectives, pursue aerospace continuing education and master new competencies, your confidence builds. Your network expands and your earning potential increases. When new opportunities arise, you’re ready for them Sign up for KU Aerospace Short Course news and offers Get the right training: The KU Aerospace Short Course Program offers 50+ industry-specific topics, allowing you to choose the exact training you need. Take it back and apply it: Industry-expert instructors translate theory into practical application, so you can return to work and solve real-world problems immediately. Reach your goals faster: KU’s Aerospace Short Courses range from 1–5 days in length, and are offered online, on-site and at designated locations in the U.S. Combine a prescribed set of four courses to earn your Certificate of Specialization.

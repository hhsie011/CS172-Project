We offer continuing education to keep people across multiple sectors – public, private, nonprofit – abreast of the latest ideas and strategies relevant to creating a sustainable future. Stanford Earth faculty offer a variety of educational opportunities for working professionals who are leading strategies, teams, or technical projects involving energy, the environment, and natural resources. Educational offerings include online courses, in-person open enrollment and custom executive education, and Stanford certificate programs. Due to COVID-19, offerings are currently available only online.

View all street closures Entering Campus Guests are encouraged to enter campus at 10th Street and Fowler Street by McCamish Pavilion. Traveling southbound on 75/85: Take exit 84 and follow signs for 10th Street West. Turn right onto 10th street and then left at the light onto Fowler Street. Take a left onto Ferst Drive. You may proceed down Ferst Drive to park in Tech Square or turn right onto Techwood Drive for guest drop off. Traveling northbound on 75/85: Take exit 250 and merge onto Wiliams Street. Turn left onto 10th Street and then left onto Fowler Street. Take a left onto Ferst Drive. You may proceed down Ferst Drive to park in Tech Square or turn right onto Techwood Drive for guest drop off. View Commencement Parking Map Campus Street Closures Bobby Dodd Way is closed. Techwood Drive will temporarily close for increased pedestrian traffic at the end of each ceremony. Faculty Parking All faculty and staff volunteers will receive a reserved parking pass and instructions prior to the event. Parking is limited and on a first-come, first-served basis. Faculty and staff assisting at the graduate ceremony are encouraged to walk to the ceremony. Public Transportation and Campus Map Public Transportation Georgia Tech is served by two MARTA transit stations. Riding Marta to Georgia Tech. North Avenue Station This is the closest station to the Commencement ceremonies. If walking, go west for two blocks on North Avenue. The campus is located just past the interstate bridge. Continue on North Avenue until you get to Bobby Dodd Stadium. Midtown Station The Gold Route makes continuous pick-ups from the Midtown Station to campus.

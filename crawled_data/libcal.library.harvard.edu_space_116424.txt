Special Collections Reading Room (3rd Floor) (Harvard-Yenching Library: Special Collections Reading Room) Capacity: 5 Description Harvard-Yenching Library Special Collections, including rare books, archival materials, personal papers, historical photographs, manuscript facsimiles, etc., are open to all researchers regardless of academic affiliation. Patrons must fill out a registration form to create an account for HOLLIS Special Request. Access • Researchers outside of Harvard who do not hold Borrowing Cards or Reading Room Cards will need to register for a Special Collections Card with the Widener Privileges Office. • Special Collections should be viewed in Harvard-Yenching Library's 3rd floor Reading Room, opening on Tuesdays and Thursdays, 10:00 AM-12:00 PM; 2:00 PM-4:00 PM. • Please review How to Use Harvard Library's Special Collections and Archives and make appointments online in advance. • If you need assistance, please contact Annie Wang or Sharon Li-shiuan Yang for more information.

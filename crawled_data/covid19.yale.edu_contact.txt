Available 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. (EST), 7 days a week. COVID-19 Compliance Questions About Your Own Compliance Contact your Health and Safety Leader (HSL). For units without a designated HSL, contact your Lead Administrator. Reporting a Compliance Concern You may report a concern about compliance with COVID-19 health and safety policies or regulations directly to your staff supervisor, your human resources representative, or a supervising faculty member. You may also make an anonymous or identified report through Yale’s hotline at 877-360-9253, or online at your.yale.edu/hotline. Available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. For compliance concerns in a research laboratory, you may contact your COVID-19 Safety Officer. Website Feedback Report a technical issue or let us know about a content update.

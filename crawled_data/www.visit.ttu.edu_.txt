Parking booths are open Mon – Fri from 7:30 am to 5:30 pm and closed on weekends. For weekend visits to campus, please follow all posted parking signage. Please be aware that specially-marked spaces, including handicap spaces, are enforced 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

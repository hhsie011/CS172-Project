The Harvard Map Collection offers Harvard affiliates support for and access to a number of mapping and geographic information systems tools, as well as cartographic design support. How to get help Register for our GIS in the Libraries Canvas page for helpful information and videos Explore our map and data tutorials. Make an appointment to speak to a librarian Systems We Support Software Librarians in the The Harvard Map Collection have expertise working with many geographic and cartographic software packages, including: ArcGIS (available for Harvard affiliates to download) QGIS Google Earth These programs are installed on workstations in the Map Collection Reading Room as well as the Media Lab on level B in Lamont Library, just down the hall. Online Systems Support for the following online systems is also offered: Carto Social Explorer SimplyAnalytics (formerly SimplyMap) PolicyMap China Geoexplorer Google MyMaps ESRI Story Maps Get Help We can answer questions via email or set up a time for one-on-one consultations.

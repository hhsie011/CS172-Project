The FSU College of Education offers a number of undergraduate programs for you to choose from. Upon graduation from one of our BS/MS teacher education programs, you will be eligible for a Florida Professional Educator’s Certificate and will be well-prepared for your career in the classroom and beyond. Graduates from our Sport Management program are equipped with the knowledge and skills to pursue an exciting career in the sports industry.

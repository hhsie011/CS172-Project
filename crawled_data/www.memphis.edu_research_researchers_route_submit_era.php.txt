The National Science Foundation (NSF) uses Fastlane; the National Institutes of Health (NIH) uses the eRA Commons, and most other federal agencies use Grants.gov. To request an eRA Commons and/or a Fastlane account, please send an email from your university account to cayusesupport@memphis.edu. Sponsored Programs Research Suite As the system of record for your entire research project, Cayuse SP simplifies sponsored project lifecycle management by electronically routing for approval, monitoring proposal and award status, and electronic file management. To access Cayuse, visit https://memphis.cayuse424.com and access using your myMemphis single sign on. System to System Most submission to federal entities are submitted via Cayuse 424. This system-to-system submission portal offers full support for federal or grants posted by NIH, NSF, AHRQ, CDC, NIFA, ONR, CDMRP, DoD with full support for over 99% of Grants.gov and Reseach.gov opportunities. For a step-by-step 424 guide, please click here PART 1 and PART 2. Account Set-Up Please complete the following information and send the request from your university email account to cayusesupport@memphis.edu. First Name: (Middle Name): Last Name: UID: Department: Title: Phone: FAX: Email: Please allow 48 hours for your account to be created. Once your account has been created, someone from the Cayuse Support Team will e-mail you a confirmation.

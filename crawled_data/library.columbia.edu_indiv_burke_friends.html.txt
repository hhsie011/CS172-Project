Fall 2016 Academic Year 2015-2016 Winter/Spring 2015 Spring/Summer 2014 Winter 2014 Spring/Summer 2013 Winter 2012/2013 Spring/Summer 2012 Membership The Friends of The Burke Library's purposes are to stimulate greater awareness of the Library's importance, to sponsor lectures and other programs, exhibits, and publications, and to encourage support of the Library's future growth. As a Friend of The Burke Library, you will: receive the Friends semi-annual newsletter enjoy invitations to lectures and special events at the Library have the opportunity to volunteer at the annual booksale, in the preservation lab, and in other areas of the Library Are you interested in joining the Friends of The Burke Library? Please print, fill out, and mail our Friends Membership Form, email us at burkefriends@library.columbia.edu, or call Lauren Wright at (212) 851-5610 for more information.

Strode Tower, Clemson, SC 29634 Hours Monday - Friday: 8:00am - 4:30pm Explore Degrees Let's begin Welcome to Clemson University's College of Architecture, Arts and Humanities! We are thrilled that you are considering us for your undergraduate career. As part of a top-ranked public research university, we offer a 14:1 student-to-faculty ratio and extensive opportunities for creative expression, paired with critical research strategies. In the College of Architecture, Arts and Humanities, you will find a home that is perfectly suited to exploring your creativity and critical thinking - essential skills for every career path. Our students graduate with the skills necessary to anticipate and meet the needs of rapidly changing local, regional, national and global environments across many different industries. You have a variety of interests, and so do we. Learn more about the programs and opportunities available in the College of Architecture, Arts and Humanities by scheduling a visit with us. Hear from our award-winning faculty and students to learn more about CAAH. Imagine. Create. Connect.

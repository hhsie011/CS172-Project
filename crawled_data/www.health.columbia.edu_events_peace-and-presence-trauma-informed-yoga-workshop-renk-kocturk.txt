Creating space to stay in the present moment while moving into a state of rest & restore. By incorporating mindfulness, and yoga through a trauma informed lens, participants will learn tools to support the body in choice making, embodiment, and trauma release. The language of this yoga class will be different from a normal studio class as the instructor will only be using invitational language. This class is open to all levels of movement.

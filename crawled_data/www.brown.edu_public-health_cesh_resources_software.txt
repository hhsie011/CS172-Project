We've developed two free, open-source programs: Abstrackr helps you upload and organize the results of a literature search for a systematic review. It also makes it possible for your team to screen, organize, and manipulate all of your abstracts in one place. SRDR Plus (The Systematic Review Data Repository: Plus) is a tool for extracting, managing, and archiving data. It is an open and searchable archive of systematic reviews and their data. Click here to learn more about SRDR Plus. OpenMeta[Analyst] is an open-source, cross-platform, point and click tool to perform meta-analysis. Click below to start using these tools.

Frank Barton School of Business Management The undergraduate certificate in insurance from Wichita State's AACSB-accredited Barton School of Business will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in managing groups of workers who are located in different locations. Learn how Leading and Managing a Remote Workforce is the right fit for you. Contact coordinator at john.perry@wichita.edu Curriculum This nine-credit-hour certificate consists of a required zero-credit certificate completion course, one required course and two additional courses chosen from the following: How to Manage a Remote (Distributed) Workforce (Required) Designing Effective Organizations Building Effective Work Teams Communicating Effectively in Organizations Fundamentals of Human Resource Management View the Curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

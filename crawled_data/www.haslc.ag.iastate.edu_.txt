Walter & Alpha Gamma Rho Arena Learn more Welcome to the Jeff and Deb Hansen Agriculture Student Learning Center The Jeff and Deb Hansen Agriculture Student Learning Center is a multi-faceted space used to help support Iowa State University’s mission of creating, sharing, and applying knowledge to make Iowa and the world a better place. The Hansen Center offers 58,000 sq ft of multifunctional space. The facility is capable of hosting classes and labs, banquets and conferences, livestock shows and rodeos as well as anything else in between.

We are committed to expanding our work at this intersection, while providing support to students and amplifying the voices of communities of color as we work together to find solutions. Academics Barnard’s faculty brings expertise in environmental sciences, sustainability, and social justice, and are working to provide students the tools to create a more equitable future. The Consortium of Critical Interdisciplinary Studies recently launched an Environmental Humanities minor, the Environmental Science department offers a major and minor, and the Anthropology department offers the Political Ecology Track. For additional information click here. Courses: Barnard offers a variety of courses that explore environmental justice.

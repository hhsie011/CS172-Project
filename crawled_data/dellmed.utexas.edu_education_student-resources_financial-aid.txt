That’s why Dell Medical School provides resources for loan management, budgeting and more, in addition to scholarship support for students who are eligible. Student Affairs is dedicated to helping physician leaders graduate with financial stability and stewardship skills that can be applied to their work in advancing value-based health care. See information on Dell Med tuition and explore the resources below. Financial Resources AAMC Financial Wellness Program The AAMC offers services and tools to help undergraduate and graduate medical students make informed financial decisions. The Financial Wellness Program is a free resource with a variety of online financial education courses. AAMC FIRST Videos & Webinars The AAMC’s Financial Information, Resources, Services and Tools (FIRST) program provides videos and webinars on topics like paying for medical school, the cost of residency interviews, managing loans and more. UT Austin Financial Aid Students can participate in UT Austin’s financial literacy program, which includes online learning modules, a resource guide and recommended applications for budgeting. Federal Student Aid The U.S. Department of Education equips medical students with information about how to obtain aid and repay loans. Maintaining Financial Aid Eligibility To remain eligible for federal, state and some institutional financial aid, students must comply with Dell Med’s standards for Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP). All medical students are evaluated for SAP at the end of each spring semester. Email Me! Want updates about Dell Med and the larger health ecosystem?

Our Mission The Controller’s Office supports the University’s mission by providing effective accounting systems and processes to monitor and assess the financial position and internal controls of Clemson University and its related organizations. We provide decision support to campus leaders through accurate and timely financial information, facilitate policy formulation to enhance financial integrity and develop strategies for implementing the University’s initiatives ensuring quality, cost effectiveness and simplicity. Our Vision The Controller’s Office will strive to sustain institutional financial integrity with effective internal controls and useful financial information for the University’s stakeholders.

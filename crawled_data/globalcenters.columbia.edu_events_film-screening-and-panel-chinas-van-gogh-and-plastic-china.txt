Van Gogh" and "Plastic China" Events Past Event Film Screening and Panel: "China's Van Gogh" and "Plastic China" Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo July 2, 2017 11:00 AM - 6:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing EVENT BRIEF On July 2, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and the Weatherhead East Asian Institute will cohost a panel discussion on documentary movies China's Van Gogh and Plastic China. Panelists will include YU Haibo and YU Tianqi, directors of China's Van Gogh; WANG Jiuliang, director of Plastic China; Lydia H. Liu, W.T. Tam Professor in the Humanities at Columbia University; LI Tuo, writer; and BAO Kun, critic of photography. QIAN Ying, Assistant Professor at Department of East Asian Languages and Cultures at Columbia University, will serve as the moderator. We sincerely welcome everyone who is interested in movie and documentary to come join us.

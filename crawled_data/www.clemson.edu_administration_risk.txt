riskmanagement@clemson.edu Campus Location 391 College Avenue, Suite 202 Mission The mission of the Office of Risk Services & Insurance is to support Clemson University's research and public service mission by providing superior service to students, faculty, staff and University guests in the areas of risk management, workers' compensation, property and liability insurance and related claims. The staff of Risk Services & Insurance also strives to be the "go to" person whenever anyone on campus has questions about their department's insurance needs, accidents occurring on campus or accidents occurring on or off campus that pertain to University-owned vehicles.

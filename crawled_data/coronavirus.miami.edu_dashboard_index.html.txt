The University enrolls more than 17,000 students and employs more than 16,000 people across three campuses: the Coral Gables Campus, the Medical Campus in downtown Miami, and the Marine Campus on Virginia Key. The dashboard pulls University, county, state, and national data from multiple sources and may take a few moments to load. A mobile-friendly version of this dashboard can be viewed in the UMiami app, which is available on the Apple App Store and on Google Play.

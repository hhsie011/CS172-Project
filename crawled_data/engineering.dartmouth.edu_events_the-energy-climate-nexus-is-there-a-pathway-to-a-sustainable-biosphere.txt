Nexus: Is There a Pathway to a Sustainable Biosphere? Jun 11 Saturday 2:30pm - 3:30pm EST Filene Auditorium, Moore Building Join us for the inaugural Class of 1972 Speaker Series lecture. The current human economic system is having a critical impact on the Earth's climate, and thus the sustainability of the planet to support human endeavors and the natural biosphere. In the talk, Dr. Schlesinger, former Dean of the Nicholas School of the Environment, will review the major anticipated impacts of climate change on the Earth's system and outline some potential avenues that can guide us to the future. Introductory remarks by Irving Institute Interim Executive Director April Salas.

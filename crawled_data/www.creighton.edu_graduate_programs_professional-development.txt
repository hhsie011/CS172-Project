Creighton University’s professional development opportunities are designed to keep you at the forefront no matter your industry. All of our career development courses were developed with a busy professional like you in mind. Many are offered online, and the majority can be completed in 8 weeks or fewer, with some taking just hours or days. Courses of all lengths are led by our expert faculty, and whether you opt for an online or on-campus course, you’ll have the chance to expand your professional network and get to know peers in your industry. If you’re looking to boost your resume, you can earn or work toward credentials with some of Creighton’s professional development programs.

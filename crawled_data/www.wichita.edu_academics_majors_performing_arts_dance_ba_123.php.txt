Admission requirements Inside the Program Student performance takes a collaborative approach Students pursuing a degree in dance have the opportunity to work alongside fellow fine arts students to express their creativity. In a gala concert, students from all three fine arts schools performed together to celebrate the 30th anniversary of Wiedemann Hall and the Marcussen Organ. Alongside ceramics and music students, six dancers performed "Trois Danses: Joies, Deuils and Luttes." Curriculum As a BA dance major, you'll take 78 credit hours within the dance program, including courses in acting, dance technique, academics/theory and more. You'll also choose from a number of dance electives, based on your interests and career goals. View the curriculum Careers From local productions to Broadway, a degree in dance from Wichita State's College of Fine Arts equips students with the skills, connections and confidence for careers as performers, choreographers and educators.

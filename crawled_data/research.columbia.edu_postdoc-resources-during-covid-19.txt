COVID-19 There is plenty to do and learn while in quarantine. Check out the Office of Postdoctoral Affairs Calendar and the CUPS Calendar for ongoing postdoc wellness, social and professional development events. See below for areas to explore and learn in a variety of fields. The Columbia COVID Research page highlights Columbia's ongoing and proposed research, reported and described by our faculty. It contains the COVID Hub, a searchable database of on-going projects and people who are currently working on COVID-19. Please visit Columbia's COVID-19 Resource Guide for all University communications regarding the current pandemic. Postdocs looking to volunteer can join the new initiative CRAC: Columbia Researchers Against COVID-19. The group was formed to help connect available volunteers with labs and research efforts that can use their help. They are actively coordinating with a number of research groups, and matching volunteers with projects that suit their interests and abilities.

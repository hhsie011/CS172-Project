Profiles Meet our Alumni Nabil Habayeb ’80, G’82 Nabil Habayeb is president and CEO of GE Middle East, North Africa and Turkey. Iman (Al Bahar) Qatami, ’79 Iman (Al Bahar) Qatami graduated from SU in 1979 with a degree from the College of Engineering and Computer Science. While at Syracuse, she was a member of the women’s basketball team. Reda Raad, ’95 Reda Raad serves as chair of the regional council, the University's first regional council outside of the U.S. Mike Venutolo ’84 Mike Venutolo is a graduate of the College of Engineering and Computer Science. He lives in Bahrain where he serves as CEO of Raymond International Pipeline Services Group. Abdallah Yabroudi ’78, G’79 Abdallah Yabroudi, a Syracuse University trustee, is an LCS alumnus (1978 and 1979) and serves as CEO and managing director of Dubai Contracting Company.

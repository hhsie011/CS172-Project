Programs at a Glance Yale Summer Session offers two courses as part of our Summer Drama Program, the Yale Summer Conservatory for Actors and A Practical Approach to Directing. Both are taught by faculty trained at the Yale School of Drama. The Conservatory for Actors The Yale Summer Conservatory for Actors is an intensive introduction to the basic techniques of acting over a five week period. Learn More 10-Day Directing Workshop A Practical Approach to Directing is designed to introduce essential tools for directing.

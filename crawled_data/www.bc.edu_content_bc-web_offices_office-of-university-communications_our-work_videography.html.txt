Educate. Entertain. Compelling, inspiring Boston College videos have become our hallmark. The Office of University Communications regularly breaks through a crowded media space to tell stories of BC's people, events, and ideas. Video is playing an increasingly important role in brand development. Our team of video creators partner with marketing, social media and web experts to best integrate our videos into your website, multimedia news story or social media campaign. Boston College on YouTube Boston College Vimeo Our Work Skills & Expertise Creative Strategy Our video team develops creative concepts, visual approaches, and compelling scripts to captivate your target audience. Cinematic Storytelling We combine visual creativity with a people-centric approach. Our cinematic expertise brings the essence of our subject's story to life. Artistic & Technical Expertise Compelling short-form digital video content is reliant on technical skills. Ours include: cinematography, editing, sound engineering, and color grading. Captions & Subtitles We take accessibility seriously, with all our video projects properly captioned. We also provide assistance for subtitles and translations.

CU Grow provides a framework to support the personal and professional growth of Clemson University staff in full-time positions. Through workshops and training, skills development, experiential learning and volunteer service, CU Grow makes transformational change possible. Meet the 2021-2022 Cohort! Eligibility criteria: Full-time employees, from any discipline, pay band or educational level, who have been at Clemson for at least three years Performance evaluation ratings of Successful or higher Interest in professional development, career progression and personal growth Supervisor approval and support Program Overview Brand YOU! Participate in reflective training and activities to enhance unique skills. Competencies Development Focus on job-specific development to build upon strengths and put them into practice. University Framework Extension Experience operational and governance-related sessions. Give Back! Work collaboratively on an approved community-service project. Apply in Fall 2022! The acceptance process is competitive, so thoughtfully answer questions regarding growth, development and plans. Participants will need supervisor approval to complete the application process. It is due before the application deadline. Check back here in Fall 2022 for more application details.

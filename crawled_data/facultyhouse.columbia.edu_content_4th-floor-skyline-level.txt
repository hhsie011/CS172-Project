The room serves as many as 150 people, and receptions on the terrace overlooking Morningside Park are a highlight of Faculty House. The terrace can accommodate up to 46 for private dining or cocktails. Monday through Friday, the Skyline Dining Room serves as a gathering point for the Columbia Community, providing a space for faculty and staff to meet with students and colleagues over a great meal.

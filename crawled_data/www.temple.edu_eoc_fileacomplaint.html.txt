Pause or play video The Office of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (ODEI) is a portfolio made up of three offices: Institutional diversity, TITLE IX and Equal Opportunity Compliance. As ODEI, these offices provide a comprehensive body of resources, training, programming and services that foster a safe, accepting and respectful campus experience for every student, staff and faculty member. - Dr. Valerie Harrison, Vice President for Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Institutional Diversity Institutional Diversity Our office creates brave spaces where the diverse perspectives of our students, faculty, staff and Temple’s neighbors are encouraged, engaged and empowered.

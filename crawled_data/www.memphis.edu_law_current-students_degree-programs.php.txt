Humphreys School of Law Degree Programs DEGREE PROGRAMS Memphis Law offers students two optional dual-degree programs, one joint-degree program and a part-time program. Dual- and Joint-Degrees: JD/MBA JD/MA in Political Science JD/MPH Fast Track MPH Dual- and Joint-Degree Comparison Chart 3+3 Programs: These programs and partnerships allow eligible UofM students to earn a bachelor's degree and a law degree in six years, saving a full year of time and tuition. Affiliate University program partners and other undergraduate institution partners are listed below: 3+3 Philosophy, Accelerated BA/JD program 3+3 Accelerated Legal Studies program 3+3 program with Austin Peay University Part-time Program Students interested in pursuing these options should review the information provided on the website and can contact the Law Admissions Office by email or at (901) 678-5403.

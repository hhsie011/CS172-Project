As such, the University of Pittsburgh is committed to ensuring equal access by making its websites and all other electronic and information technology accessible to all users. We are constantly striving to be better and welcome comments or suggestions at accessibility@pitt.edu, or you can report any digital accessibility barriers. More information on EIT accessibility can be found on the digital accessibility website.

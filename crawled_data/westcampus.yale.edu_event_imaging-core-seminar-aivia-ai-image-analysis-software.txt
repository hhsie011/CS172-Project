Quick and easy! WHO IS THIS FOR? You have imaging date? Any imaging data – light microscopy, EM, TEM, cryo-ET and more? You analyze that imaging data? You spend a lot of time with repetitive tasks for that analysis? You want this to be quicker, more reliable, less laborious? Then you should take a look at the AIVIA. WHY SHOULD YOU COME? An hour at the seminar seeing examples of what AIVIVA can do, and an hour with Javier looking at your data set in a hands-on session and seeing how fast and reliable the process is will transform your research workflow!

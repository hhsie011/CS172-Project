The Harvard University Archives' collections include historical records of Harvard offices, departments, and other administrative units. We provide research advice and help in the use of these collections to University staff working on projects such as departmental histories, policy reviews, anniversaries, exhibitions, and other special events. We also assist University registrars and authorized departmental administrators with access to archived student records. For more information, contact us at archives_reference@harvard.edu or 617-495-2461. For access to administrative records stored in Records Center accounts, contact Records Management Services at archives_rms@harvard.edu.

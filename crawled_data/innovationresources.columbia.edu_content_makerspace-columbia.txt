This allows entry for all current undergraduates, master’s students, university sponsored researchers, PhD candidates, university faculty, and university staff; as well as visitors that are appointed at the University and hold a valid Columbia ID. In order to use the Makerspace, you must first go through an Onboarding session, which is held once (or twice) a week. Afterwards, you can request machine specific training from any of the Makerspace staff or superusers (student volunteers) during regular office hours.

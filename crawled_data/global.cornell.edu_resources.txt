International Off-Campus Activity Toolkit Mapping Our Global Community Statements Teaching International Students Breadcrumb Home Resources Resources Global Cornell is a campus ethos that promotes a global perspective in our identity, thinking, and actions at home and abroad. At Cornell, our international outlook profoundly shapes how we teach, learn, conduct research, and interact with the world. If you're a Cornellian—faculty, staff, undergrad, grad student, or alum—these campus resources are for you. Support for Cornell's International Community Leadership and attentiveness in global matters have never been more critical for Cornell as we continue to confront the COVID-19 pandemic. The Office of the Vice Provost for International Affairs has been at the center of managing the coronavirus's impacts on international students, faculty, and staff on campus and those in the Cornell community who are studying, conducting research, and performing activities abroad.

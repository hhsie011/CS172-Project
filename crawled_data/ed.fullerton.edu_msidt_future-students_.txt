MSIDT Info Session Recording - Part 1 MSIDT Info Session Recording - Part 2 The first fully online Master of Science program in the California State University System, the MSIDT program has been providing a high quality degree program for over 15 years. MSIDT provides an exceptional education, offering a graduate program designed to integrate instructional design theory and principles and featuring emerging technologies in each course. Program Information The MSIDT Program is designed to meet the needs of working professionals. Professionals who are working in the field of technology in kindergarten through higher education, the military, and corporate settings, or for those who wish to change careers. Individuals who wish to further their skills and education in direct applications of technology for teaching, learning and curriculum development used by professionals, including: Instructional technology trainers in business, industry, military, or corporations and universities Instructional designers Web developers Software consultants or designers Anyone working with curriculum development for training in e-learning and multimedia environments for an educational (K-12, community college, higher education, or extended education), corporate or business or military settings.

Preference will be given to start-up ventures that have the potential to be financially self-sustaining in the longer term. Ventures must be led by Columbia University students, alumni, faculty or researchers, or be advised and have significant involvement by Columbia University faculty or researchers. There is no time limit after graduation for alumni to apply. The entire team need not be Columbia University affiliated; one founding member or significantly involved faculty or research advisor is sufficient. Appliction deadlines are August 15th and March 1st every year.

Submit an Event Family Hiking at Hines Lake Saturday, May 21, 2022 9:00 am - 11:00 am Join us on May 21st, 2022 from 9AM-11AM on this exciting family adventure where we’ll explore 1-2 miles (1 hour) of the 100 acres of previously inaccessible parkland at the Clay Family Eastern Glades. On this trip, you’ll enjoy the peaceful vista of Hines Lake and learn about the local wildlife & plant life. The hike will be followed by a scavenger hunt where you will need all of your outdoor skills to thrive. Ages: 6 years old & up Transportation Option: $3.00 per person (booster seats will not be provided) (Maximum adult to child ratio 2 children: 1 guardian) ***Paid parking is available at the Eastern Glades Parking Lot on East Memorial Loop Drive. Free parking can be found further north at the park and playground area.

About Teaching Assistants FAQ Contact Us About Teaching Assistants FAQ Contact Us Office of Educational Programs The Office of Educational Programs is here to provide logistical support as you navigate your academics at Mailman. For all students, OEP is a point of contact for the MPH shared curriculum and Certificate programming as well as other degree programs. OEP serves as the central hub for new school-wide academic initiatives. Additionally, OEP establishes and manages policies that support academic programs. OEP also distributes vouchers to qualified students for the Certified Public Health exam offered through the National Board of Public Health Examiners. Do you have a question about your Core schedule? Are you interested in being a Core, ISP, or Leadership Teaching Assistant? Do you want to talk to someone about changing your certificate? Do you have a question about your degree requirements? Are you interested in taking the CPH exam? We are here to answer your questions.

Reserve books are at the Library Service Desk. Complete list of our DVD collection Special Collections I want to check something out? You can check out up to 100 books at a time. Most books can be kept for 28 days, but browsing books can be kept for 14 days. You can renew online 3 times as long as nobody else wants the book. (See Holds below.) You can check out up to 10 CDs/DVDs at a time. You can keep them for 14 days. Fines & Fees I want this book, but it's already checked out? If a book is checked out to someone else, you can ask the staff at the checkout desk to either place a hold or recall the book for you. Putting it on hold means you'll get the book next as long as nobody else is in line. You'll get an email when the book is ready to be checked out. Books & DVDs on hold are at the Main Level Service Desk near the coffee shop. You can't put holds on reserve books.

University College Advising Welcome to University College Advising, the advising home for undecided students. UCA provides academic advising and assistance to a variety of diverse student populations. Our advisors assist undergraduate students with academic needs, including planning semester schedules, teaching First-Year Seminar classes, considering degree options, and developing skills to bridge the gap between student life and academic success.

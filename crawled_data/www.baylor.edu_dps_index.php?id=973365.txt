Anonymous Tips Form Step 1Anonymous Tip Information Step 2Confirm Report Step 3Finished Anonymous Tip Information If you believe you have seen a crime or suspicious incident occur on campus and would like to report it anonymously, you may call (254) 710-2222 and tell the police dispatcher you would like to anonymously report a crime. You may also complete this form. When you submit this form, your identity will not be presented to the Baylor Police Department. If you have any questions about this program, please contact the BUPD Criminal Investigations Division at (254) 710-7640. ALL INFORMATION PROVIDED WILL BE KEPT STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL.

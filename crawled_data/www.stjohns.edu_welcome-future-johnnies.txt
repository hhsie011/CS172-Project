You are about to join a legacy rich with history and tradition that has been proud to uphold its core values and continues to make our students the focus for 150 years strong. As an accepted student, you will find many resources on this page that will be helpful to you as you navigate your journey to St. John's. Please take your time looking through all of our wonderful Accepted Student Events, such as Accepted Student Day, our Spotlight and Johnnies on the Spot events, and our Guided Department Tours - and of course, be sure to register as soon as possible. We are always adding new events, so be sure to revisit this page frequently! Once again, congratulations on your acceptance - we are excited to meet you at our events and see you on campus soon!

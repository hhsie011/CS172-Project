Hall is named after Captain Andrew Moses and was constructed in 1942. Captain Moses graduated from West Point in 1897. On September 1, 1907 Captain Moses was ordered to report to Texas A&M University as Commandant where he served until 1911. The 1908 Texas A&M yearbook was dedicated to Captain Moses giving “recognition to the kindly nature, the generous spirit, the lofty soul, the sincere single-minded, clear-headed, honest man……….we take Captain Moses to be”. 2021 – 2022 Rates Corridor Hall $2,520semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Floor Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but on different floors.

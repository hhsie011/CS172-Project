Wichita State boasts a large, full-time university police force and a campus-wide phone system that includes widespread emergency call boxes. The University Police Department works closely with nearby Wichita Police Department and Wichita Fire Department stations. Should a crisis occur, the university is prepared with an emergency operations plan and an integrated communication network utilizing the newest technology to keep students, parents, faculty and staff informed. It's an unpredictable world -- but we're constantly working to make Wichita State University's campuses as safe as possible.

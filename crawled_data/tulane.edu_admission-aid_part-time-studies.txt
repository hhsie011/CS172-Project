Close Stronger TUgether: COVID-19 RESOURCES Part-Time Studies Looking for a part-time education to earn an undergraduate degree, special certificates or even a master's degree? Through the Tulane School of Professional Advancement, a stellar education is available to people of all backgrounds and who may already be holding full-time jobs. Classes are offered at several campuses in the New Orleans area and online. There are afternoon, evening and weekend classes available, making a Tulane education truly attainable. The School of Professional Advancement is also affordable, with competitively priced tuition, financial aid options, scholarships and discounts available. Applying is easy – simply fill out this online application. Advisors are available to guide you every step of the way.

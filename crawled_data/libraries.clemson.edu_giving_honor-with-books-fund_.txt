With a gift of $100, you will support the purchase of additional materials for the Libraries while commemorating the meaningful events and people from your life. Recognition A name plate like the example below will be added to a newly purchased book. Donors and honorees of the tribute will be notified periodically with the name and searchable catalogue entry of each book.

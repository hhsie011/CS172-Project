Happiness describes the experience of positive emotions, such as joy, contentment and satisfaction. Scientific studies have been finding that happiness can make our hearts healthier, our immune systems stronger, and our lives longer. Join us to review pathways that lead to happiness and health. This seminar will be facilitated by a UM FSAP consultant. FSAP Health and Wellness seminars are offered as part of the University of Miami’s Well ’Canes Program. Attend to receive 100 Pure Wellness points (to a maximum total of 300 points for FSAP 1-hour webinar attendance per calendar year).

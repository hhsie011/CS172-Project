Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Columbia Youth Adventurers Columbia Youth Adventurers (CYA) is a social excursion and mentorship program that provides low-income children an opportunity to engage in learning and enrichment activities outside of the classroom. Barnard and Columbia volunteers lead cultural and recreational trips to sites around the city. The program seeks to foster self-esteem, an appreciation of cultural activities and traditions, and a passion for education and learning. Weekly sessions are from 10am-2pm on Saturdays at various locations. Interested in volunteering? Please email your year, school, and a brief statement of interest to [email protected].

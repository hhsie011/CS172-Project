Sessions Mitchell @ UC Official Mitchell Website NCA Learning Series 2021-2022 Intro to the UK Awards Tuesday, February 22nd @ 12:30PM via Zoom Wednesday, February 23rd @ 12:30PM via Zoom UK Awards: Preparing to Apply Wednesday, March 2nd @ 12:30PM via Zoom These info sessions will cover the basics and application preparation for the Marshall, Mitchell, Rhodes, and Gates Cambridge scholarships. These four UK Awards provide fully funded graduate degrees in the United Kingdom to students who have a strong record of achievement both in and beyond the classroom. For more specific information regarding each awards' parameters and eligibility, please click their individual page links to read more. Intro to the UK Awards - In these sessions NCA Director Dr. Jenny Hyest will discuss differences among these awards, eligibility criteria, what it takes to be a competitive applicant, and the benefits of applying. UK Awards: Preparing to Apply - In this session Dr. Hyest will discuss how to identify graduate programs in the UK and review the application process for each award (including how to complete the Spring campus nomination process for Marshall, Mitchell, and Rhodes). Email nca@uc.edu with questions.

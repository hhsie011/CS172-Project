Katherine Banks is the 26th president of Texas A&M University, which is one of the largest universities in the United States with more than 71,000 students and 10,000-plus faculty and staff members. She also serves as Vice Chancellor of National Laboratories and National Security Strategic Initiatives for the Texas A&M University System through her involvement with Los Alamos National Laboratory and the George H.W.

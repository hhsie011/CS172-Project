Hall is named after J. Harold Dunn ’25 and was constructed in 1972. During his collegiate years, he served as a student leader undertaking a tough curriculum and working day-and-night. After leaving Texas A&M, Mr. Dunn went into the business industry where he made his way to the top. He transformed Shamrock Oil & Gas Company from a company running in the red to a distinguished corporation known as the Diamond-Shamrock Corporation. 2021 – 2022 Rates Commons Hall $3,708semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Suite Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same multi-room suite are the same gender.

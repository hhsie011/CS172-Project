Then chances are, SU is there, too! Through our regional events, regional offices, and alumni clubs, we give you opportunities to network with other alumni, serve as mentors to students and young alums, and connect with SU’s “community of experts,” so you can continue to stay connected and experience the breadth and depth of Syracuse University. To stay up to date on activities where you live—or plan to visit—check out our regional websites: Atlanta Boston Chicago Los Angeles New York City San Francisco Washington, D.C.

The Lindseth Climbing Center, located in Bartels is 8,000 sf of Bouldering, Top Rope, and Sport Lead Climbing opportunities, catering to beginners and experts alike. We offer a safe and welcoming environment for all who wish to climb, within our current guidelines. Rock climbing offers both physical and mental challenges, in a relaxed social atmosphere, and we are here to help you take advantage of its benefits. We also maintain the Noyes K2 Bouldering Wall, a modest bouldering wall located in the Noyes Community Recreation Center.

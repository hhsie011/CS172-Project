The entire University community also comes together for University Commencement to celebrate all of the graduates from Columbia's nineteen schools, colleges, and affiliate institutions including Barnard College and Teachers College. Ceremonies & Events View the Schedule of Events The President of the University gives remarks and officially confers degrees on the graduates. This is a long-standing tradition and a memorable and celebratory event. Ticket Information Tickets are required for most ceremonies, including the University Commencement ceremony. All graduating students who wish to participate in the University Commencement ceremony are required to register and to obtain guest tickets, if desired. Only graduating students are able to request tickets and they will receive regular emails from their individual school with information on how to do this. Disability Services does not require special tickets to receive support. Columbia University is committed to making the events and activities of Commencement Week accessible to all graduates and guests. Learn more about available accommodations and support.

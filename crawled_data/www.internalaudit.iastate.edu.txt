It helps an organization accomplish its objectives by bringing a systematic, disciplined approach to evaluate and improve the effectiveness of risk management, control, and governance processes. Mission Statement Internal Audit partners with management to enhance and protect organizational value by providing an independent and objective assessment of operational efficiencies and controls. Benefits of An Audit Presents a catalyst for positive change. Identifies and communicates risk exposure. Offers solutions to improve efficiency. Provides cost effective management advisory services.

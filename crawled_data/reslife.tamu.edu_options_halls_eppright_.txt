Eppright Hall is named after George J. Eppright and was constructed in 1989. Mr. Eppright was a rancher in Manor, Texas, and is a member of the Class of ’26. Mr. Eppright has provided Texas A&M University with major gifts of land that have been used to fund endowed academic positions & scholarships. 2021 – 2022 Rates Modular Hall $3,708semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Room Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same room are the same gender. Only residents of the same gender share a restroom. Close Gallery Get a feel for all that Eppright Hall has to offer by scrolling through the photo gallery. If you prefer, check it for yourself and join us for a tour. Showers have doors.

Watching intently are (left to right) Athletic Director Fredrick W. “Doc” Marvel, Class of 1884; Theodore Francis Green, Class of 1887; and Robert B. Jones, Class of 1907. The statue was moved to the College Green in May 1992, after the closing of Marvel Gym.

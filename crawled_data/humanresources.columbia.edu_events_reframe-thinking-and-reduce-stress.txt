It allows the immune system to function more efficiently. It isn’t surprising that people with optimistic attitudes tend to be healthier all around. What may be surprising is that optimism can be learned. This webinar demonstrates how to reframe thinking to have a more positive attitude, reduce stress, alter self-defeating patterns of thought, and utilize constructive self-feedback to reach personal goals.

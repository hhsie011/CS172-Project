Orange is what we do. The University Store has the best, largest selection of OSU apparel and spirit merchandise around. Whether you are a lil' Poke, a current Cowboy, or a proud and immortal alumni the University Store has everything an OSU fan could ever want in all the best brands, including Nike, JanSport, Cutter & Buck, Antigua, Colosseum, Columbia, 47, Gameday Couture and much, much more. Think the University Store is just T-shirts and textbooks? Not anymore. The University Store is taking orange to a whole different level with our new boutique chic selection of apparel, jewelry, shoes and handbags. The University Store also makes it easy to keep your closetfull of America’s Brightest Orange no matter where you are. Shop online 24/7 at shopokstate.com In addition to the Student Union location, Cowboy fans can also get their favorite OSU gear at the University Store's other location at Boone Pickens Stadium on Game Days. You can also find the University Store setup at many home athletic events, including Cowboy and Cowgirl basketball, Cowboy baseball, Cowgirl softball, Cowboy wrestling and Cowgirl soccer. Did you know OSU Alumni Association members can save 10% on all regularly priced OSU clothing & gift purchases at the University Store and online? Just show your membership card at checkout. To use online, click on the ‘Account’ button in the top right hand corner and select ‘My Account.” If you already have an account simply log in with your email address and password, or you can create a new account if you are a first-time user. Once you are logged in you can add your Alumni Association Member ID to your Account Information to keep on saving. The University Store is owned and operated by Oklahoma State University, and every purchase made in the University Store directly benefits the university.

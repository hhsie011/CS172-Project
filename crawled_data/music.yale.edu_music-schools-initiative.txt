Initiative Close Welcome to the Music in Schools Initiative The Music in Schools Initiative (MISI) shares, with School of Music students, New Haven Public Schools students, and individuals involved in arts education throughout the country, the School’s commitment to fostering cultural leadership and ensuring an active music life for students nationwide. The Music in Schools Initiative is supported by a gift from the Yale College Class of 1957. something for everyone The Music in Schools Initiative in New Haven is a partnership, a community, and a living laboratory. New Haven Community The Music in Schools Initiative partners with the New Haven Public Schools to support music educators and provides opportunities for students to participate in music-making. join the community join the community A Living Laboratory The Music in Schools Initiative provides School of Music graduate students the opportunity to develop their teaching style and explore music as a tool for inclusion and social transformation. learn more learn more Symposium on Music in Schools The biannual Symposium on Music in Schools gathers leaders in the field for a discussion about how music education might best become an integral and important part of any public school curriculum. learn more learn more Music in Schools in the News Music in Schools Initiative U.S. Deputy Secretary of Education Cindy Marten to Participate in Symposium on Music in Schools U.S. Deputy Secretary of Education Cindy Marten will participate in the Yale School of Music’s 2021 Symposium on Music in Schools.

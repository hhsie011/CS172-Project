Manning Hall Gallery Visit the Haffenreffer Museum's exhibits and CultureLab in the Manning Hall Gallery, located on Brown University's campus in Providence, Rhode Island. Admission: Free! Open: Tuesday - Sunday, 10 AM - 4 PM Closed Mondays and Brown University Holidays. Address: Manning Hall, First Floor Brown University Main Green 21 Prospect Street Providence, RI 02912 Contact Us: 401-863-2065 Accessibility: The building is wheelchair accessible from the Prospect Street entry. Please call ahead if you require assistance. Parking: There are limited metered two-hour parking near the Museum, and though it is usually possible to find parking within a few blocks, visitors are encouraged to allow enough time to find parking. There is also a Visitor Parking Lot located at the corner of Power and Thayer Streets. Campus maps, accessibility maps, parking maps, and a welcome map (which helpfully includes the orientation of nearby one-way streets) are available from Brown's Department of Facilities Management.

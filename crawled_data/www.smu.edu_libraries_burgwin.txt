Guides Find Your Librarian Attend Workshops Writing Support ☰ SMU Libraries Fort Burgwin Library Fort Burgwin Library The Fort Burgwin Library is located in Taos, New Mexico and serves the information needs of students and faculty in the SMU-in-Taos program. The collection, closely tailored to the Taos curriculum, focuses on the history, literature, cultures, and environment of New Mexico and neighboring states in the Southwest. The library is open only when SMU-in-Taos classes are in session. All non-SMU guests must register with the main office before visiting the library.

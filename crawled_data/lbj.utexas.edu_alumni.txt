HookedIn Upcoming Events See All 2022 PPIA Public Service Weekend: Emerging Public Policy Issues in Texas & the American South Friday Jul 22 9:00 am to Sunday Jul 24 1:30 pm LBJ School The application for the LBJ School's inaugural PPIA Public Service Weekend is now open until June 14, 2022. The event is a three-day conference empowering undergraduate students from historically underrepresented backgrounds to consider graduate degree programs and careers in public service. The primary goal of the event is to introduce students to topics in the field of public policy through the lens of justice, equity, diversity and inclusion.

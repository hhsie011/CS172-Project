We have two research units: The Applied Physics Laboratory and the Washington National Primate Research Center. Assisting the Vice Provost for Research in providing leadership in supporting UW’s research community, is a strong team of Associate Vice Provosts as well as a liaison for OR and the Pacific Northwest National Laboratories (PNNL).

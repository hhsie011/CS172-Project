The president is aided by a cabinet of leaders who are in charge of the university’s major divisions and individuals who represent other constituencies, such as faculty and alumni. The president of the University of Houston also holds the title of chancellor of the University of Houston System. The vice presidents and other administrators may also hold dual titles at the system level.

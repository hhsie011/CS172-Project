These publications are written by Extension personnel and university scientists and are developed for industry professionals, scholars, lay enthusiasts and homeowners. Search publications: The Home & Garden Information Center Research-based information for lay enthusiasts and homeowners on topics such as: landscaping, gardening, plant health, household pests, food safety, preservation, nutrition, physical activity and health. Land-Grant Press by Clemson Extension Science-based, peer-reviewed publications written by Extension personnel and university scientists for professional and academic audiences, as well as owners and managers of livestock, horses, timber and land. Extension Publications for Sale Purchase printed Extension publications such as 4-H record books; flora, fauna, wildlife & insect identification books; forest stewardship, lawn care & gardening guides; various training manuals and more. Clemson Extension Publishing Clemson University Press imprint publishes books, practical guides, manuals, and journals to advance Clemson University’s land-grant mission to disseminate quality, trusted information and enrich lives through education.

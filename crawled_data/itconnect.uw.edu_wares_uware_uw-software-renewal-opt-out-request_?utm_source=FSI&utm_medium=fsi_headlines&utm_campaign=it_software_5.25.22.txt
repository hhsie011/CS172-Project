Contact UW-IT About UW-IT UW annual software renewal opt-out request To stop automatic billing for the next license cycle , please submit an Opt-Out Form by the following dates below: There are no refunds on the licenses listed below: Zoom opt-out deadline: June 10, 2022 Adobe, Matlab, SAS, and SPSS opt-out deadline: June 10th of every year. All Microsoft Products including Dynamics 365, PowerApps, Project Online 3 and 5, Visio Plan 2, Visual Studio opt-out deadline is May 17th of every year. The exception is Crashplan which doesn’t renew until August and will not be billed until September. Crashplan opt-outs will be accepted through July 31st of every year. During current license period an individual license can be transferred to a different person upon request. Send an email to: help@uw.edu. Note: No transfer is possible for any of the licenses after the opt-out form is processed. Opt-Out Request Form. Last reviewed January 28, 2022 See a problem on this page? Let us know.

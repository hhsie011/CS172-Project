FSU’s reputation for excellence is rooted in the quality of our academic programs and research, and raising our profile in this regard takes concerted, significant investment on many fronts at once: attracting and retaining top faculty talent; becoming a destination for the brightest graduate students; encouraging high-impact interdisciplinary inquiry; and developing innovative teaching strategies. To serve the needs of our students, our scholars, and our state, we will ensure that FSU is synonymous with breakthrough thinking in every respect.

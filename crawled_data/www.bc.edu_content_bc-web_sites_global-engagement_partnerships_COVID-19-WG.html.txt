Since COVID-19 impacts every area of life—public health, economics, race, gender, politics, migration, education, health care (including mental health), sustainability, law, etc.—this seminar/working group aims to be a faculty resource on COVID-19 for BC faculty and the rest of the BC community. How is the BC faculty responding to COVID-19? Visit our COVID-19 archive to explore their work.

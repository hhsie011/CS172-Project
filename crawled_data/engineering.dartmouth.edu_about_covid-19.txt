Together: COVID-19 Information Dartmouth is committed to supporting the continued health of our community and closely monitors local, national, and global COVID-19 information and guidance to develop university-wide policies to keep our students, faculty, and staff safe. Dartmouth Engineering's COVID-19 operations team collaborates closely with Dartmouth to implement policies and provide support specific to our engineering community.

University College is comprised of seven different offices whose main goal is to support the success of each current and future OSU student. UC programs and services are located in various locations; please select the service you need from the departments listed below. Services Concurrent Programming and Advisement LASSO Center Pre-Professional Health & Law Support Services Scholar Development & Undergraduate Research Student Veteran Success Transfer Student Success University College Advising Tutoring assistance The LASSO Center offers one-on-one tutoring to any OSU student needing assistance. Qualified tutors meet GPA & course content standards and complete LASSO's nationally certified tutor training. Learn more Advising needs University College Advising provides academic advising and assistance to a variety of diverse student populations. Our advisors assist undergraduate students with academic needs, including planning semester schedules, teaching First-Year Seminar classes, considering degree options, and developing skills to bridge the gap between student life and academic success. Learn more Research opportunities The Henry Bellmon Office of Scholar Development and Undergraduate Research enhances undergraduate opportunity, encourages student-faculty mentoring, and promotes retention through scholarship/fellowship success, undergraduate research, international experience, and individual/institutional recognition.

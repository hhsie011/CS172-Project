Technology Transfer (Tech Transfer) is the process by which new innovations created are turned into products and commercialized. The key role of the Technology Transfer Office is to protect the intellectual property associated with these valuable innovations so that they can be licensed and commercialized, and brought to the marketplace for society’s benefit.

There's always more you can do to develop your skills, improve your on-the-job performance, or take the next step in your career. Luckily, as a Columbia Business School alumnus, you have unique access to further education. Audit Classes Columbia Business School alumni may audit New York–based MBA and EMBA elective classes free of charge pending available space and with permission from the professor and Alumni Relations. For more information about the courses available for auditing, please visit the Course Selection page. For more information on the auditing approval process or to request a space as an auditor for an upcoming course, please contact the alumni office at [email protected]. Alumni Tuition Benefit If you're looking to upgrade your career with one of our Executive Education courses, Columbia Business School alumni and up to four colleagues receive a 25 percent tuition benefit for programs lasting up to seven days. A 15 percent tuition benefit is available for graduates of other schools within Columbia University Alumni Edge A new lifelong learning initiative to support the career development of alumni, this series of programs represents some of the School's most popular courses and mirrors the lessons that our current students are taught in the classroom today. Explore current offerings and learn more about the initiative here. Executive Education Any leftover bidding points can be put towards the cost of an Executive Education course.

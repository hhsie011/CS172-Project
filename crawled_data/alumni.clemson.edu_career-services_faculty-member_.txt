TigerLink Gateway The TigerLink Gateway is Clemson’s campus-wide mentoring initiative and the entryway to career connections. The Gateway helps facilitate career-related conversations between students, Clemson alumni, faculty & staff, friends of Clemson, and industry leaders around the globe. Community members can seek knowledge and advice, explore career fields, become a mentor, join an official mentoring group or program, and build professional networks.

The University of Memphis is home to many world-class research centers & institutes that serve as hubs of collaboration covering a range of disciplines. The Division oversees a number of shared core research facilities, but these are only a portion of the capacity of campus. We encourage faculty to reach out and create interdisciplinary collaborations The capacity of campus provides faculty, scientists and students with access to not only to the latest instrumentation but also to experienced staff with expertise in designing and conducting experiments and analyzing data.

ACADEMICSADMISSIONSSTUDENT LIFEATHLETICSABOUTAPPLY NOW DUAL Advising Form Dual Advising is for students currently enrolled at a Kansas community college and plan to transfer to Wichita State University. Dual advising allows students planning to transfer to WSU to develop a plan of study, avoid unnecessary enrollment duplication and receive up-to-date information on admission deadlines, scholarships and curriculum changes. Benefits Develop a relationship with your Wichita State University advisor who can answer program-specific questions. Take classes you need and avoid classes you do not need which ultimately saves time and money. Receive up-to-date information about deadlines for admission, available scholarships and financial assistance, curriculum changes and other important matters. Loading...

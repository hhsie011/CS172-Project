Guidance Concur Guest Account Login Travel Resources The University of Cincinnati (UC) Central Travel Resources department is here to help you through a worry-free trip. We have guides, videos, and team members readily available to assist with creating travel requests, booking travel, completing expense reports, and anything else travel and expense-related. Concur Concur is the university's travel and expense system. Concur serves as a tool for all employees and guests of UC who participate in university-sponsored travel and/or use of purchasing cards. Concur SSO Login Concur Guest Login Concur Use Cases Booking UC-related travel Reporting transactions on a university purchasing card Seeking reimbursement for out-of-pocket expenses Approving any of the above Announcements and Reminders There are no restrictions on domestic travel at this time. All international travel requires approval at the unit level by the chair or supervisor, and at the dean or vice president level, and by UC International and Vice Provost Raj Mehta. Please reference the Travel section of the Public Health site for further travel requirements and guidance. Recent Updates We are pleased to announce our new duty of care partnership with Terra Dotta. Terra Dotta is a long-time trusted partner for our Study Abroad program, and they recently created the option to expand our use of their services. Going forward, travelers will see communications from Terra Dotta before their trips, including health and safety information for their destination. For those interested, there will also be instructions to download their AlertTraveler app. Terra Dotta will send travelers notifications of any emergencies at their location and provide tools for our Travel and Risk Management teams to communicate as needed with travelers during any events. Travelers can also use the AlertTraveler app to report any incidents they need assistance with. Please contact concurquestions@uc.edu with any questions. Federally Compliant ID Cards As of May 3, 2023, TSA will require a federally compliant driver license, identification card or another acceptable form of identification (such as a US passport or military ID) to fly within the U.S. Visit the Ohio BMV site for more info.

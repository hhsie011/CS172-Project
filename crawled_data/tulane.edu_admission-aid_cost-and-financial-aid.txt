Close Stronger TUgether: COVID-19 RESOURCES Home /Admission & Aid /Cost and Financial Aid Cost and Financial Aid So, you’ve decided you want to be a member of the Tulane family. We know that finding a university that fits all of your needs can be a stressful experience—and we’re here to help you every step of the way. Our staff will work closely with you and your family to figure out what works best for you. We’ll help you figure out everything from how the financial aid system works, to what additional resources are available to you during your time at Tulane. What you need to know: Undergraduate Student Cost of Attendance (Newcomb-Tulane College, School of Architecture, A. B. Freeman School of Business, School of Science and Engineering, School of Liberal Arts, School of Public Health and Tropical Medicine) Graduate/Professional Student Cost of Attendance School of Professional Advancement Tuition and Fees If you are an incoming freshman, the application deadline for financial aid is February 15 of your enrollment year. If you are a returning undergraduate student, your deadline is April 15 of each year. For additional information on financial aid, please visit our Office of Financial Aid.

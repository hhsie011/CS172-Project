Success 2022-23 Expense Estimator The Iowa State University Expense Estimator is intended to help new and continuing students estimate their university bill (U-Bill) and the outstanding balance owed or potential financial aid refund after financial aid is applied. The information provided is intended to assist students in creating a personal budget and encourage responsible loan borrowing. Please Note: Tuition and Fees have not been finalized for the 2022-23 year. Estimates are based on expenses directly billed by the university including: Tuition Mandatory Fees Housing if living in a University Residence Hall or University Apartment University Meal Plan Other expenses that can be included in the tool (Additional Expenses section) are: Housing if NOT living in a University Residence Hall or University Apartment Meals other than University Meal Plans Books and Supplies Personal Expenses such as Travel, Toiletries, and Laundry This tool is for planning purposes only. Your actual U-Bill will be available in AccessPlus prior to the start of each semester.

WAYS TO GET YOUR PLATE Online Complete the adjacent form and our partners at the Indian River County Tax Collector’s office* will process tag requests for any Florida resident. A representative will contact you to complete your purchase. Online orders will be processed within 1–2 business days. *Under F.S. 668.6076, email addresses are public records. If you do not want your email address released in response to a public-records request, do not send electronic mail to this entity. Instead, contact them by phone or in writing. In-Person Visit your local Department of Motor Vehicles office, tax collector, or private license plate agency. First-time U alumni plate applicants, claim your $20 rebate today!

Register directly from course description page: register from course pages We're here to help! If you would prefer to register with our student services representative, please call (513)-556-6932 and leave us a message to receive a call back. Your call will be returned within 24 hours during our office hours between 9:30 am - 6:30 pm, Monday - Friday.  Communiversity Calendar Fall Term: early September to mid-December Winter Term: early January to mid-April Spring Term: early May to late July 100% Satisfaction Guarantee We want you to love your Communiversity class which is why your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed. If you are ever less than completely satisfied with a Communiversity class, we will issue a credit for the registration fee toward a future Communiversity class. Please contact us at ce@uc.edu or (513) 556-6932 within 7 days of completing your class. Refund Policy We understand that, sometimes, life gets in the way. This is why we will give you a full refund if you must cancel your registration, at least, 7 days prior to the class start date. Simply call us at (513) 556-6932 to process your refund. Wait Lists Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis. If a class you want is full, you may place yourself on a waitlist. If a spot opens in that class, everyone on the wait list will receive an email. We will make every effort to accommodate you! Share the love for lifelong learning! Purchase a specific class as a gift, or select $50, $100, or $150 gift certificates. Our Gift Certificates may be used toward the purchase of any Communiversity class within one year.

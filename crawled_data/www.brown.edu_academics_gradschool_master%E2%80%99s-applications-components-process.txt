Please refer to the instructions below as you prepare your application in Slate. If you have any questions regarding the application process or admission to one of the master's programs below, please email us at [email protected]. Terminal Master's Programs American studies Computer Science Data Science Data-Enabled Computational Engineering and Science Design Engineering Education Engineering Innovation Management and Entrepreneurship Integrative Studies Physics Public Affairs Public Humanities Social Analysis and Research Urban Education Policy Complete an Admission Inquiry Form. A complete application to one of the above programs requires the following: Completed online application Personal statement Letters of recommendation Resume GRE General Exam, if required English Language Exam (TOEFL, TOEFL ITP Plus for China or IELTS), if required $100 application fee Please note, an application fee waiver can be requested for those who demonstrate high financial need. Instructions on how to request a waiver are found within the application. Some programs require additional materials: American Studies: 10-20 page writing sample Design Engineering: portfolio Public Humanities: up to 20 page writing sample Unless noted otherwise, the application deadline is February 1, 2021. The following programs have different deadlines: Computer Science - March 15, 2021 Education - January 18, 2021 Urban Education Policy - January 18, 2021 for financial aid consideration Public Humanities - January 15, 2021 Fifth-Year Master’s Programs Applied Mathematics Cognitive Science Comparative Literature Computer Science Data Science Data-Enabled Computational Engineering and Science Education Engineering German Studies History History of Art and Architecture Innovation Management and Entrepreneurship Linguistics Mathematics Modern Culture and Media Philosophy Physics Political Science Public Affairs Public Humanities Psychology Religious Studies Slavic Studies Social Analysis and Research Urban Education Policy Current Brown University undergraduate students are eligible to apply as a fith-year master’s candidate to any of the programs above. A complete application includes the following: Completed online application Personal statement Letters of recommendation Resume Some programs require additional materials: American Studies: 10-20 page writing sample Design Engineering: portfolio Public Humanities: up to 20 page writing sample The application deadline for fifth-year master’s students is May 1, 2021.

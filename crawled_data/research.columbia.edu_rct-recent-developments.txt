Developments RCT - Recent Developments Recent Developments in Research Compliance and Training The Office of Research Compliance and Training is closely monitoring activities by Congress and Federal agencies relating to various compliance issues. Our Recent Developments webpages will be updated as new rules, guidance or clarifications to existing compliance requirements are issued. These webpages will also update the Columbia community on any new developments within the Office of Research Compliance and Training as policies, tools and procedures evolve to ensure and maintain compliance with relevant federal and state regulations.

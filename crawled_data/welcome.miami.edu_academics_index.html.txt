International Student and Scholar Services With an average student-faculty ratio of 12:1, our classes are more focused and personal than classes at larger universities. As one of the top research universities in the country, the University of Miami brings together esteemed faculty and talented students from throughout the United States and the world. Challenging courses, projects that forge together knowledge and practice, and a stimulating, diverse environment – find it all at the U. Schools And Colleges The University of Miami’s 12 schools and colleges offer a wide variety of experiences to students. There are more than 180 undergraduate majors and programs; graduate students have access to a wide range of fields with an emphasis on research.

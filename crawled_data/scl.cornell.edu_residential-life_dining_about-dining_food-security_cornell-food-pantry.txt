Free, confidential access to food and personal care items is available to Cornell undergraduate and graduate students, as well as Cornell staff and faculty. The Cornell Food Pantry's current hours are Tuesdays and Thursdays 4-7pm, Wednesdays and Fridays 10am-1pm, and Sundays 12-3pm. Beginning Saturday, May 21st, our summer hours are Tuesdays and Thursdays 4-7pm, and Wednesdays and Fridays 10am-1pm. We won't be open Sundays for the summer. Enroll to participate: Visit mealplan.scl.cornell.edu/foodpantry to enroll in the Food Pantry program. (Note: If you enrolled prior to June 1, 2021, please re-enroll for the 2021-2022 academic year.) Make a gift in direct support of the Cornell Food Pantry.

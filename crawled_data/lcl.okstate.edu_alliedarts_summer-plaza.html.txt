This event is held weekly at the OSU Student Union plaza every Thursday from May 26 - June 23. All the outdoor fun, including live performing artists and water slides begin at 6:00 p.m. and wrap up at 8:00 p.m. There's truly some fun for everyone at Summer on the Plaza, and we want you to be a part of it!

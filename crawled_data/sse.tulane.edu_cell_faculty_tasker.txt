SCHOOL OF SCIENCE & ENGINEERING >> CELL AND MOLECULAR BIOLOGY Cell and Molecular Biology Home / Faculty & Staff / Faculty / Jeffrey G. Tasker Jeffrey G. Tasker Catherine and Hunter Pierson Chair in Neuroscience tasker@tulane.edu School of Science & Engineering Education Ph.D., University of Bordeaux, France, 1986 Biography 2015: Outstanding Researcher Award, Tulane School of Science and Engineering 2009: Professor of the Year, Honor’s Program, Tulane University Research in the Tasker laboratory uses patch clamp electrophysiology, molecular biology, and genetic manipulations to study the circuit, electrical and molecular signaling of neurons and glia in the hypothalamus and amygdala, with the goal of understanding neurophysiological, neuroendocrine, and neuropathological mechanisms involved in stress, depression, anxiety, and posttraumatic stress disorder.

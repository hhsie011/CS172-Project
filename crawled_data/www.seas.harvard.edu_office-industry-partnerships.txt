Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS) partners with industry in a number of ways. Whether your organization is interested in research, direct interaction with students and faculty, or partnership on multiple levels, we will work with you to craft an engagement strategy that reflects your company’s interests. Our primary goal is to build long-term partnerships that support our students and faculty, while advancing SEAS’ mission, vision, and values. For more information on how your organization can partner with SEAS, please contact Ellie Carlough, Director of Industry Partnerships. For students interested in opportunities to engage with industry partners and recruiters, please contact Keith Karasek, Director of Experiential and Career Development, or visit the Office of Student Career Development. We look forward to working with you.

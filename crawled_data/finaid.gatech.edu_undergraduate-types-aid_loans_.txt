Federal Loans Loans borrowed from the Federal government through the Direct Loan Program offer students a low-interest option compared to alternative loans through banks and other lending institutions. View Details Student Access Loans (Georgia residents only) The Student Access Loan (SAL) is a need-based, low interest loan for undergraduate students who are Georgia residents. These loans are administered by the Georgia Student Finance Commission (GSFC).

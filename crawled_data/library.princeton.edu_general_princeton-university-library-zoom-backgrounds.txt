Delve back into your favorite Princeton University Library collections with a new and growing series of virtual backgrounds for Zoom. This new initiative includes several downloadable images of items from our digital collections that are in the public domain, selected from our digital repository (Figgy). The series includes brief descriptions of the works and the collections from which our selections originate, with discovery links to them and to our online exhibits platform, Digital PUL (DPUL).

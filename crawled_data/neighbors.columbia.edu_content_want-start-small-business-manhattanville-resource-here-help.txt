This Manhattanville Resource Is Here to Help Want to Start a Small Business? This Manhattanville Resource Is Here to Help Topic: Manhattanville Welcome to Columbia-Harlem Small Business Development Center (SBDC), which has a new home on the second floor of one of the Columbia Business School buildings on the Manhattanville campus. The SBDC is an essential resource to the Harlem and Upper Manhattan communities, serving primarily local entrepreneurs who are interested in opening a business of their own or looking for help with an existing business. SBDC Managing Director Kaaryn Nailor Simmons gives us a tour of their new space.

Scheduling Advising Meetings via My Bearcat Network Presidential Leadership Medal of Excellence How to Apply The application and matching process occurs in the fall semester (October/November) and research placements occur in the spring semester (January - April). The 2021-22 application will open on October 22, 2021 and will close by 11:59pm on October 31, 2021. All University Honors students are eligible to apply; first- and second-year students are especially encouraged to apply. Students may apply to no more than five projects. A separate application must be submitted for each. Access the Biomedical RaMP application beginning October 22 at 6:15pm. Early applications will be deleted. Information Sessions Students who would like to learn more before deciding to apply can attend a virtual information session, held on October 14th from 5:15p-6:00p EST via WebEx. This session is not mandatory, but attendance is strongly encouraged. Meet and Greet Sessions Please find the recording for the Fall 2021 Meet and Greet Mentor Presentations here. Learn about each research project and meet the research mentors. Student applicants should plan to attend the Meet and Greet, as Research mentors will share information on their projects and what they are looking for in a mentee. Applications will open immediately following these presentations. Application Assistance If you require assistance with your application, please email Kyle Key at kyle.key@uc.edu in order to address your questions and/or set up a time to meet.

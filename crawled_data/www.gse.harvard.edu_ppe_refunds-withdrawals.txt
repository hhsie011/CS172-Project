Refunds and Withdrawals Program Refunds, Credits, and Deferrals As an HGSE Professional Education learner, you may request: A 100% refund of your tuition, A credit toward another program, or A deferral to a different iteration of the same program. In order to consider and grant your request, we ask that you submit it no later than: The final payment deadline (for online programs), or 30 days before the program start date (for on-campus programs). Once the deadline has passed, we cannot guarantee that you will be eligible for a refund or withdrawal. Submitting a Request To refund, credit, or deferral request, please contact our team directly by submitting a case in the Professional Education Community under the Question Topic “Program Cancelation or Withdrawal.” Alternatively, you may email ppe@gse.harvard.edu.

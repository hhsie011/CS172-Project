The advantages of holding an assistantship include stipends, tuition reductions and waivers, reduced-rate health insurance and an applied educational experience. Financial Aid and Funding Opportunities The Wichita advantage Our location in Wichita elevates Wichita State above traditional college towns. Because of the city's size—and WSU's focus on applied learning, research and innovation—you'll have access to industry connections and valuable professional development experiences. We prepare you for immediate success in your chosen discipline by providing ample opportunity to take what you've learned in the classroom and apply it to solving important societal and industrial problems.

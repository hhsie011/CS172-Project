Past Event 2020 Ivy League China Forum Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo October 11, 2020 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM Shunhua Hall, Shanghai Kempinski Hotel On October 11, join us for the 2020 Ivy League China Forum co-hosted by Columbia China Forum and co-sponsored by the Beijing Global Center. We hope this great opportunity will help Columbia students and alumni foster stronger connection with their fellow students from Ivy League universities, and facilitate constructive dialogues on the global political and economic paradigms that have been permanently changed by the ravages of COVID-19.

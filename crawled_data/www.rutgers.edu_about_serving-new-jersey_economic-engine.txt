About Rutgers Serving New Jersey and the World An Economic Engine New Jersey's Economic Engine Rutgers is Vital to the New Jersey Economy Rutgers is vital to the New Jersey economy—preparing the workforce, providing thousands of jobs, purchasing goods and services from New Jersey companies, building important new buildings, growing the health care field, guiding new businesses, and bringing new products and ideas to market. And we take many of our best ideas and share them across the globe. #4 Rutgers is the fourth-largest non-governmental employer in New Jersey 58,000+ Jobs supported–directly and indirectly $1 into $7 Rutgers returns almost $7 to the New Jersey economy for every $1 invested by the state 1,300+ Clinical providers in 50+ towns and 16 counties Rutgers' investment in world-class facilities Partnerships Grow the New Jersey Economy Rutgers and its partners serve a wide range of small businesses and nonprofit enterprises across New Jersey. Advancing New Jersey Small Business Success Rutgers spearheads New Jersey Small Business Development Centers located statewide. Public-Private Partnership for Performing Arts New Brunswick Performing Arts Center is a win-win for Rutgers and the community. Growing Businesses in Newark Rutgers partners in 'Hire.Buy.Live.Newark' Art Spaces for Camden Rutgers University–Camden partners with city, Cooper’s Ferry on $1 million Bloomberg Philanthropies grant to transform illegal dumping sites into art spaces for Camden neighborhoods. Food Innovation Center As a business incubator and accelerator for the food and beverage industry, Rutgers’ Food Innovation Center helps entrepreneurs make ideas into edible (or drinkable) products and bring them to market.

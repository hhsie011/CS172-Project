One of the first centers of its kind in the nation, it connected students to community partners in support of the University’s mission “to serve the community, the nation, and the world.” In 1991, the center was named the Howard R. Swearer Center for Public Service, in recognition of the late Brown president’s advocacy of public service for undergraduates.

The Division of Student Affairs at California State University, Fullerton is dedicated to understanding and improving the student experience and students' development in the co-curriculum. Student Affairs at CSUF is focused on the improvement of programs and services though student centered, intentional, and ongoing direct and indirect assessment. With student success as the guiding principle, Student Affairs advances holistic student learning and development by creating meaningful connections through educationally purposefully activities.

Members of the Coalition network with all students in the arts within Boston College and take on leadership roles across campus to ensure their interests are heard. This will be accomplished by partnering with different organizations, departments, and offices on campus. In addition, the Coalition acts as a companion advisory board to the Arts Council’s Board of Chairs and Directors to discuss ideas for collaboration as well as challenges that face Boston College student artists.

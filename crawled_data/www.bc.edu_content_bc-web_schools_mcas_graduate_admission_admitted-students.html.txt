As a Morrissey College graduate student, you join an inclusive community of leading scholars, supportive staff, successful peers, and esteemed alumni. We’re excited you’ve chosen to continue your academic journey here. Important information will be sent to you from the University and your academic department prior to the start of classes as well as a New Student email from the MCGS Admissions Office after you acknowledge attendance (beginning Mid-April). Please reference the information on this page to become familiar with some of the steps and services that will help you along the way. Your academic department can assist in matters of course selection, advising and departmental awards/appointments (if applicable). The Office of Student Services is the point of contact regarding enrollment, federal financial aid, loans, payment plans, student accounts, tuition and fees.

Mechanisms Events Past Event Investment-Related Dispute Resolution Mechanisms Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo July 15, 2021 10:30 AM - 1:15 PM Panel I: Dispute Resolution Mechanisms in Bilateral Investment Treaties (BITs) and Free Trade Agreements (FTAs) Chair: Francisco Aninat, partner at Bofill Escobar Silva Abogados Main provisions (historical evolution, trends, difference between BITs and Investment Chapters in FTAs). Investors’ rights covered by BITs and FTAs: MFN, fair & equitable treatment, protection from expropriation, protection and security, among others. Panel II: The International Centre for Settlement of Investment Disputes (ICSID) Chair: Sebastian Yanine, partner at Bofill Escobar Silva Abogados The Convention; Role of Member States; Institution Rules; Arbitration Rules; Administrative and Financial Regulations; Fees, Costs and Other Expenses. Main cases (historical evolution) and jurisprudence on the topics addressed in Panel I.

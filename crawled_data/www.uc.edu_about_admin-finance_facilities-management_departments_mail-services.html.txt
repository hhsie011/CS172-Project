Services The University of Cincinnati Mail Services strives to provide the university community with timely and accurate handling, distribution and processing of all mail (both interdepartmental and USPS mail pieces), utilizing staff and resources effectively and efficiently with auditable accountability. If you receive mail without your mail location, contact the sender to update your address with your mail location. Notice to Students and their Parents: Mail and Package Delivery and Pickup for all Occupants of Residence Halls is managed by Residence Education & Development.

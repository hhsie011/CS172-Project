Apartments are offered as space allows in Hasbrouck Apartments, a graduate and professional student community. To apply for academic-year or 12-month housing, visit Apply for Housing and follow the instructions for graduate and professional students. To apply for temporary housing, contact the Housing & Dining Contracts Office to speak with a housing coordinator. On-campus housing is not guaranteed for visiting faculty members and special guests, but every effort is made to meet your request.

Veterans Services Menu Benefits Certification Process Contacts Forms Frequently Asked Questions News Veterans Resource Center Veterans Services Veterans Services Veterans Services Veterans Services at Georgia Tech is not a division of the Department of Veterans Affairs but part of the Registrar's Office, located in Tech Tower. We serve as a liaison between Georgia Tech veteran students and Veterans Affairs. Our primary purposes are to assist veteran students with the processing of forms for educational benefits, advising veteran students about procedural requirements, and certifying enrollment of veteran students with Veterans Affairs. Benefits To start using GI Bill® education benefits, you must already be formally admitted to Georgia Tech.

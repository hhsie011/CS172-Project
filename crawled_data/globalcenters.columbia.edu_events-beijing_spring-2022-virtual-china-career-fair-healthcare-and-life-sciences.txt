and Life Sciences - Spring 2022 Events Past Event Virtual China Career Fair: Healthcare and Life Sciences - Spring 2022 Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo February 23, 2022 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM Online If you are Columbia Mailman's graduate students or alumni, please register through Mailman CareerLink. If you are GSAS' MA of Statistics, Columbia Engineering, and CBS' graduate students or alumni, please register HERE. Time: February 23, 9:00 AM-11:30 PM (Beijing) | February 22, 8:00 PM-10:30 PM (New York) Please join us for the virtual China career fair for Columbia Mailman, GSAS' MA of Statistics, Columbia Engineering, and CBS' graduate students and alumni in the fields of healthcare and life sciences. During this event, recruiters from prominent local, national, and multinational companies in China will conduct company presentations and introduce internship, part-time, and full-time positions and requirements. During Q&A sessions, you will also have real-time engagement and networking opportunities and gain in-depth information about a particular field or industry. List of Participating Companies (in alphabetical order) BFC Group Danen Capital Everpower International Holdings Ltd JD.com Kelun Philips (China) Sansure Biotech Shanghai Concord Cancer Center Tencent TK Healthcare Investment Holdings Wonders Information Reminders This event is open to Columbia Mailman, GSAS' MA of Statistics, Columbia Engineering, and CBS' graduate students and alumni only. Make sure to use your Columbia ID to sign up.

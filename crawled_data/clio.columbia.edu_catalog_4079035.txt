Title Literary and Philosophical Society of New-York Records, 1816-1830 Description 39 items (1 box) Finding Aid PDF finding aid reviewed fall 2018 and deemed duplicative. Summary Letters, documents, and manuscripts relating to the Society. The letters, most of which are addressed to Clinton and Mitchill, are from scientists in New York as well as England and France. The manuscripts include minutes of various meetings, laws and regulations of the Society, and lists of resident and honorary members. Biographical / Historical Note Scientific society founded by DeWitt Clinton and Samuel Latham Mitchill in 1816. They were the president and vice-president. Access and Use This collection is located on-site. This collection has no restrictions. Notes Source of acquisition--Columbiana. Accession number--M-59. Materials may have been added to the collection since this finding aid was prepared. Contact rbml@columbia.edu for more information.

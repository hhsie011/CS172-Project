Before scheduling a meeting with an advisor, students should take the following steps: Review the OIP website and familiarize yourself with the application process. Consider when and where you want to study abroad – you don’t need to know exactly, but give it some thought. Attend any relevant information sessions and the annual Study Abroad Fair. Meet with a peer advisor during their office hours to discuss general study abroad questions and to your explore options.

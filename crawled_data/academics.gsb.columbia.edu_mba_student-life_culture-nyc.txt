When your classes are done, take in a show, hit a museum, or get inspired just observing the city's architecture and people. Whether you come to us from south of 14th Street or South America, New York and its wonders can never be exhausted. And because so many businesses, business leaders, and alumni are based here, Columbia has a constant stream of visitors who want to share their time and their knowledge, and they're only a subway ride away. In no time at all, you'll see that Columbia Business School is a gateway to the best of New York and the very best of the business world.

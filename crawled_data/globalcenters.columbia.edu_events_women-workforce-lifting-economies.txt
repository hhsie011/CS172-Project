Economies Events Past Event Women in the Workforce Lifting Economies Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo March 24, 2021 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM Wednesday, March 24 7:00 - 8:00 pm Amman 1:00 - 2:00 pm New York UNICEF's Middle East and North Africa Region COVID-19 2020 report estimates that approximately 11 million full-time jobs were lost in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic. According to the OECD, the socio-economic fallout of the crisis are particularly severe for women in the MENA region. As existing gender inequalities in economic growth are exacerbated, women are experiencing greater regression in employment and instability in income generation. This webinar seeks to highlight women’s current experiences in the Jordanian economy and promote a rethinking of women’s roles. Panelists will discuss how to maximize women’s impact and resilience in Jordan’s long-term recovery. Speakers: Fida Taher, Managing Partner, Amam Ventures Muna Sukhtian, Managing Director, MicroFund for Women Salma Nims, Secretary-General of the Jordanian National Commission for Women’s Affairs Reem Aslan, Technical Gender Specialist at International Labour Organization, Regional Office for Arab States Welcome remarks by Hanya Salah, Deputy Director, Columbia Global Centers | Amman Moderated by Mary Nazzal, Chairperson of 17 Ventures and Founder of Landmark Hotels Upcoming Events Columbia Global CentersAmman / Beijing / Istanbul / Mumbai / Nairobi / Paris / Rio / Santiago / Tunis Follow Us Facebook Twitter Instagram Youtube Columbia University ©2022 Columbia University Accessibility Nondiscrimination Careers Built using Columbia Sites Back to Top Close

This certificate is designed to support the educational needs of varsity student-athletes in the Name-Image-Likeness (NIL) marketplace, but is available to all students. Learn how sport leadership and branding is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 15-credit-hour certificate consists of three required courses: Principles of Leadership Seminar in Personal Branding Sport and Entertainment Agencies Students then choose two electives from the following: Technology in Sport Management Applied Leadership Experience in Sport and Entertainment Sport Marketing and Promotion View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click on "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

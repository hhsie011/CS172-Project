Women Events Past Event The Power of Authentic Leadership for Women Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo Jennifer Goez May 9, 2018 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM Flacso Auditorium, Dag Hammarskjold 3269, Vitacura* A workshop led by Jennifer Goez, who runs the Columbia Business School´sWomen in Leadership Executive Education Program, which will focus on promoting female representation in executive and board positions and on methods for maximizing women’s professional development. Date: Wednesday, May 9 Time: 8:30 – 10:30 am Venue: Flacso Auditorium, Dag Hammarskjold 3269, Vitacura* At the Executive Education Division of Columbia Business School, Jennifer Goez is also responsible for the overall management and performance of the flagship the Advanced Management Program. She has a rich and varied set of experiences gained from law administration, higher education administration and teaching from San Francisco and New York. Currently, Jennifer is a doctoral student in Adult Learning and Leadership at Columbia University with a focus on research for improving adult learning practices that bridge theory to practice in executive education programming. Her research focuses on measuring the effectiveness of leadership programs, developing authenticity in women's leadership. Jennifer has worked extensively with international organizations, including MetLife, Boston Consulting Group, Sony Music, PwC, Nordea, Otsuka Holdings, Scientific Games, Kimberly Clark, Boeing, and the Hong Kong Government, among others. A proponent of lifelong learning, Jennifer has an MBA, a Certified Professional in Human Resources and an expected doctoral degree (2019) from Teachers College. *Parking at Parque Bicentenario or at the Atton Hotel (Vitacura 3201) Upcoming Events Columbia Global CentersAmman / Beijing / Istanbul / Mumbai / Nairobi / Paris / Rio / Santiago / Tunis Follow Us Facebook Twitter Instagram Youtube Columbia University ©2022 Columbia University Accessibility Nondiscrimination Careers Built using Columbia Sites Back to Top Close

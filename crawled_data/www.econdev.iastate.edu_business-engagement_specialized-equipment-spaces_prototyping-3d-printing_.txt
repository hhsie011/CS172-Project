CIRAS offers a board range of services to help businesses prosper and grow, including Prototyping or 3D Printing support. CIRAS has polymer (plastic), TPU (rubber like), and metal printing capabilities. The EDIR staff works closely with CIRAS to ensure industry leaders can find the specific knowledge and expertise needed to take their product, process, and business to the next level.

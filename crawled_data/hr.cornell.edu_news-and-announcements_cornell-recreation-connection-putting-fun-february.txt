Cornell Recreation Connection is putting the FUN into February! Get Up, Get Out, Get Moving! Cornell employees are invited to get up and take a break for some fun with Cornell Recreation Connection! FEBRUARY 24: CORNELL EMPLOYEES LUNCHTIME ICE SKATING PARTY 11:30 AM - 1:00 pm Lynah Rink All Cornell employees are invited to take a lunch-time break with the Cornell Recreation Connection at Lynah Rink. The $4 admissions fee will cover skate rental and a $1 donation to the Employee CARE Fund. Please RSVP here so we will know how much hot chocolate to make. FEBRUARY 24: INDOOR CORNHOLE PARTY 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM Human Ecology Building Commons Can't wait until summer? Come to Cornell Recreation Connection's Indoor Cornhole Party. Pizza and beverages will be served. Your Admission fee of $5 will go to the Employee CARE Fund. The CARE Fund offers assistion to faculty and staff who have experienced a sudden financial hardship. RSVP here so we will know who to expect. FEBRUARY 25: GET THE BALL ROLLING - EMPLOYEE BOWLING PARTY 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Helen Newman Bowling Center All Cornell employees are invited to this lunch-time bowling event at the Helen Newman Bowling Center. Pizza will be served. Admission is $6 and includes shoe rental. Pre-registration is suggested. Team entry forms are available from Cheryl McGraw, crm54@cornell.edu. Walk-ins will be welcome as long as there is room available.

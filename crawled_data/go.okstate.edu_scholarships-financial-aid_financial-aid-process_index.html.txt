Follow these steps to receive scholarships and other forms of aid at Oklahoma State University. We're here to help Have questions? We have the answers at the Office of Scholarships and Financial Aid. CONTACT US 1. Apply for aid OSU admission/scholarships and FAFSA Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) to find out how much funding you're eligible to receive from Federal and state sources, and see information on additional scholarships for current and incoming students. How to apply 2. Receive and review your award Stay informed You'll receive award notifications for the upcoming academic year through your official OSU email address. Learn what steps to take to review your award offers online. Award notifications 3. Accept aid Confirm your aid package To receive the funding you've been offered, log in to accept your awards before the deadlines. Most can be accepted through our online system, but follow the instructions for each award. Awards are made on a rolling timeline, so check back regularly. Accepting aid 4. Receive aid "Disbursement" of aid OSU scholarships, grants and loans are credited (disbursed) to your University account with the OSU Bursar’s Office. These payments will go directly towards your tuition. Receive aid Changes to aid Adjustments, allowances and refunds There's a chance your estimated costs could change based on special classifications, military benefits or other applicable refunds. Your aid eligibility can also change if you withdraw from courses before the semester ends.

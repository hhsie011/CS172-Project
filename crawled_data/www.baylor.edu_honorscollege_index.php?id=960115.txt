CLICK HERE to learn more about being an international student at Baylor. Step 1 - Find out if you're eligible: To be considered for any Honors College scholarships, an applicant must have and maintain at least a 3.5 GPA. You can learn more about how financial aid works for international students by visiting undergraduate admissions. Step 2 - Demonstrate financial need: International students should submit the online CSS / Financial Aid Profile to establish financial need for Honors College scholarships. Enter Baylor University’s code – 6032 – to ensure your profile is shared with the University’s Financial Aid office and subsequently, with the Honors College Scholarship Committee. Create a CSS profile Step 3 – Apply for Honors College Scholarships: We encourage international students who are eligible to apply for Scholarships in the Honors College. Explore scholarships Financial Aid: To learn more about financial aid opportunities, please visit the Office of Academic Scholarships & Financial Aid. Contact: If you have questions about your Honors College scholarship application, the best way to reach us is to email ApplyHC@baylor.edu.

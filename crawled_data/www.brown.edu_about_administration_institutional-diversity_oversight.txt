These include polices and procedures relating to: Discrimination and Harassment Sexual and gender-based harassment and violence (Title IX) Equal employment opportunity (EEO) (Title VII) Disabilities resources and accommodations (ADA/504) Non-Retaliation OIED also oversees Brown's approach to taking affirmative action to create and implement a set of positive steps to promote equal employment opportunity, and to eliminate unlawful discrimination.

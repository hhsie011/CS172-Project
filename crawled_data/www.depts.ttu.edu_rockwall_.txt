Texas Tech University's partnership with Rockwall ISD and Collin College at the College & Career Academy provides local students the opportunity to earn a high-quality degree from a reputable university without relocation. Did you know? Students can earn a high school diploma, an associate's degree and a bachelor's degree all in one location. With a focus on STEM fields at the College & Career Academy, students can also explore career options and professional certifications while pursuing their degree.

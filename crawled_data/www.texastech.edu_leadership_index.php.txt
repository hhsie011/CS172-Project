Organizational Chart Meeting the global challenges of today and the future, the administration of the Texas Tech University System directs, governs, and manages the policies and services needed to lead the way for our component institutions—Texas Tech University, Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center, Angelo State University, Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center El Paso and Midwestern State University. Board of Regents A ten-member committee composed of distinguished and dedicated Texans who seek to further higher education, health care, research, and outreach in our communities. Office of the Chancellor The chancellor of the Texas Tech University System serves as the chief executive officer of all campuses and academic sites for our five component institutions. Executive Leadership Made up of dedicated leaders, this group assists the chancellor on continuing quality enrollment growth, enhancing research, and increasing programs and opportunities that prepare students for personal and professional success. System Offices Encompassing numerous departments across the Texas Tech University System, these offices provide a variety of services to all five component institutions.

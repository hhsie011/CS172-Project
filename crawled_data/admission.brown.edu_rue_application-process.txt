RUE applicants should not complete the regular Common Application. The RUE Application link is available below. The annual application deadline for RUE applicants is March 1 for entrance in September of the same year. RUE applicants may not also apply as regular freshmen or transfer applicants in the same admission cycle. Applicants are informed of admission decisions in early to mid-May. Financial aid award letters for admitted students who applied for aid will accompany admission letters. Start Your Application RUE applicants complete the RUE application, not the Common Application. The application is available October 1 for prospective entrance in the fall of the following year. The application deadline is March 1.

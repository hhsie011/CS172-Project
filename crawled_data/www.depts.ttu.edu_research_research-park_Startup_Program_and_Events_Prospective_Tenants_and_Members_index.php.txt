Whether you are a new start-up in need of temporary lab space or an established company that is looking to expand and partner with Texas Tech, the services, and amenities we offer to provide for a vibrant and dynamic environment where opportunities for collaboration and innovation are nurtured. Facilities What's Here? Interested in becoming a member? Membership provides a shared workspace for entrepreneurs, with the help and support of established businesses, directly contributing to the entrepreneurship ecosystem facilitated by the Hub. Our collaboration spaces include community desks, whiteboards, seating areas, High-Speed Internet access, 24/7 free coffee, kitchen, vending machines, and much more. Versatile lounge areas allow for informal meetings and comfortable collaboration space that include floor to ceiling whiteboards. For more information please click the form below. Become a Member Interested in leasing our space? The Hub is for launching or growing companies based in West Texas. Today, we have approximately 40,000 square feet of state of the art flexible wet lab and office space. The Hub has several options for leasing space to startups. The facility offers 230 parking spaces, a large open Atrium, and customizable meeting rooms to accommodate almost any need. Please contact the Innovation Hub to see what space is available and fill out the form below.

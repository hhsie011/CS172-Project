Lulu Chow Wang ’83 Alumni Suite David Geffen Hall The Lulu Chow Wang ’83 Alumni Suite will attract and welcome many visitors to the School, be they prospective students interested in learning more about Columbia’s renowned MBA and EMBA programs, alumni returning to campus, or guest speakers and practitioners. Located on the second floor behind Cooperman Commons, this flexible space will also support a variety of uses, such as a private event venue for special occasions.

A significant part of this course involves solving Capture-The-Flag (CTF) and discussing strategies for solving such problems. This course covers a variety of topics including (but not limited to) reverse engineering, exploitation, binary analysis, and web. More information is available on the CS 6265 course website. This course does not count toward any specific specialization, and does not count as a foundational course.

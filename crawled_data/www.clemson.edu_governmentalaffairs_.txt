By working with the South Carolina General Assembly, United States Congress, public agencies and community decision-makers, Governmental Affairs advocates on matters of importance and further enhance the mission and operations of Clemson University. Not only does this office develop the university’s legislative agenda, it is responsible for advancing the important work being done at Clemson to encourage investment in programs and initiatives positively impacting our state and nation. 2021-2022 E&G Budget Priorities 2021-2022 PSA Budget Priorities More News...

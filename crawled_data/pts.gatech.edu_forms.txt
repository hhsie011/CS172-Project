Guest Appeals: Submit written appeals for parking violations. All appeals must be submitted within ten (10) calendar days from the date the citation was issued. This form is the official means for submitting appeals; emailed appeals are not accepted. Maintenance Request: Report maintenance issues such as potholes, broken gates, etc. Out of Area Parking Request: Customers whose assigned parking areas are full should submit this form immediately after parking in an adjacent ungated non-residential parking area (excludes E44-Lyman Hall lot, visitor lots, metered parking spaces, and reserved spaces). Customers will receive an "Out of Area Reference Number" confirming this request. This form may also be used to report gate access issues. GT login is required to submit form. This service is only available for current GT permit holders (excludes SmartPark). If login is not available, contact PTS at 404-385-7275. Permit Return Request: Return a permit for the upcoming academic year to inventory and initiate any refund (pending no outstanding balance due on account). Special Event Parking Request: Submit requests for campus event parking, staffing and equipment. Specialty Parking Request: Request Official Business Permits, Fleet Vehicle Permits or Reserved Spaces (individual or departmental). Stinger Rental Request: Request the use of a Stinger bus. Transit Advertising Request: *The transit advertising program is currently suspended for the 2021-2022 academic year.* Submit transit advertising requests and artwork changes. Validation Requests: Request validations for departmental visitors or special guests. Vendor/Contractor Permit Request: Submit permit requests for vehicles that visit the Institute frequently for business purposes. Need Help?

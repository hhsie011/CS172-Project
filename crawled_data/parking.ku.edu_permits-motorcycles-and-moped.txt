Cost for students or faculty/staff is $87/year. Motorcycle/moped permit may be purchased either by itself or together with a regular vehicle permit. Motorcycles and mopeds may not park in garages, automobile parking spots or at bicycle racks. Read signs carefully before parking, as some areas are designated as shared moped/motorcycle parking while others are moped only or motorcycle only. Students may not park in motorcycle/moped parking Lot 3 by Spencer Research Library, which is reserved for faculty and staff.

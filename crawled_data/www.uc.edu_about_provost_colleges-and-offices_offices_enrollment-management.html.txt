Enrollment Management Enrollment Management includes university offices and areas primarily concerned with providing services for the recruitment and retention of students, including: Admissions Advising and Academic Resources College Credit Plus New Student Orientation Office One-Stop Center Registrar Research and Assessment Student Financial Aid Office University Advising Association (UCUAADA) The University of Cincinnati administers the Federal Student Aid Code of Conduct in compliance with the university's Program Participation Agreement with the U.S. Department of Education and with Title IV of the Higher Education Act. The code places restrictions and duties upon both the business practices of the university and the personal conduct of individuals. Primarily, the code is administered by the Office of Enrollment Management.

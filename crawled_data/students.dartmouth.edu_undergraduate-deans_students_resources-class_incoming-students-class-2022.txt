Stay Informed: Continue to Check this Page for Communications from the Undergraduate Deans Office Dear Class of 2026! Welcome to Dartmouth College! We look forward to getting to know you once you arrive on campus and working closely with you during your time at Dartmouth. Watch for our emails, beginning in the summer of 2022, and please make sure to check this page often for updates. Each undergraduate student is assigned an undergraduate dean from our office; you will be made aware of your assigned undergraduate dean before you arrive at Dartmouth and will have several opportunities to meet them during New Student Orientation. The Undergraduate Deans Office provides support (academic, personal, career and major advising) for all undergraduate students during their entire career at Dartmouth, from matriculation through graduation. We can't wait to work with you one-on-one during your time here!

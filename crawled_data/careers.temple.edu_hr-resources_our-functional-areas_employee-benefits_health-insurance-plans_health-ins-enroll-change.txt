Submit the completed form to Benefits Administration via: HR Documents Upload For Employees channel in TUportal. Log in and navigate to TUportal > Staff Tools tab > HR Documents Upload For Employees channel, Email to benefits@temple.edu, or Fax to 215-926-2288. Open Enrollment Form (doc) Add or Remove Dependents (doc) Adding or Removing Dependents Employees may add or remove a dependent during the university's annual open enrollment period in June. Also, employees are permitted to add or remove dependents within 31 days of the date of a qualifying life event. The following are considered qualifying life events. A summary of the qualifying events and required proof documents. Qualifying Events Effective Date (as appropriate) Documentation Required You have a newborn child. July 1, or date of birth, or first of the month following life event. Hospital Announcement (Provide Social Security Card and Birth Certificate when received). You legally adopt a child. July 1, or date of birth, or first of the month following life event. Social Security Card, Birth Certificate and Court Document. Your legal marital status changes (marriage, divorce, separation or the death of a spouse). July 1, or first of the month following marriage date, or first of the month following life event. Social Security Card and Marriage Certificate, or Certified Domestic Partner form. The employment status of your spouse, certified domestic partner or eligible dependent(s) changes. July 1, or first of the month following marriage date, or first of the month following life event. Social Security Card and Verification Letter from insurer regarding coverage status. Open Enrollment Schedule Enrollment or changes to health plans, retirement savings plans and flexible spending accounts may be made during the annual open enrollment periods below. A quick summary of the benefits open enrollment periods Open Enrollment Month Effective Date Health Insurance June July 1 Dental Insurance June July 1 Employee Retirement Savings Plans June & December July 1 & Jan. 1 Flexible Spending Account: Dependent Care (Must reenroll annually) November & December January 1 Flexible Spending Account: Healthcare (Must reenroll annually) November & December Jan. 1 Commuter Benefits Program Ongoing First of the month following enrollment Contact Us For questions regarding open enrollment, contact us at 215-926-2270 or email benefits@temple.edu.

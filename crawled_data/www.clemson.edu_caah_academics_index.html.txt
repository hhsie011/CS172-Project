YouTube Join the CAAH Family The College of Architecture, Arts and Humanities offers a wide range of academic and professional programs from the visual and performing arts to the design and building disciplines to the humanities. In addition to our degree programs, every student at Clemson receives at least part of their education in the College of Architecture, Arts and Humanities, making CAAH the academic core of the University. We place a premium on creativity, deep thinking, reflection, and developing sensitivity and empathy toward fellow humans and the world we inhabit. The College goes beyond preparing our students for a specific job—we help launch them into a fulfilling life of work, creativity, inquiry, play, collaboration, and respect. We create lifelong learners capable of adapting to the technological, social, economic and cultural changes shaping our local, regional, national and international communities.

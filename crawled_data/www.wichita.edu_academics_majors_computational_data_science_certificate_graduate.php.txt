You'll gain a strong understanding of fundamental data science principles and their application. And you'll acquire in-depth knowledge on data science tools, analytical workflows, machine learning algorithms and predictive modeling. Learn how Computational Data Science is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 12-credit-hour certificate comprises the following courses: Introduction to Data Science Machine Learning Six hours of electives chosen from: Introduction to Database Systems Artificial Intelligence Deep Learning: Theory, Algorithms and Applications Data Mining Artificial Intelligence for Robotics View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

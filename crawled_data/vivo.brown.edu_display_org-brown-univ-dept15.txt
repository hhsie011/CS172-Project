The Center for Latin American and Caribbean Studies (CLACS) at Brown University serves as the hub for research and teaching related to Latin America and the Caribbean. Through a full schedule of lectures, seminars, conferences, films, concerts, and other events, CLACS promotes dialogue on academic, socio-political, economic, and cultural matters related to Latin America at all levels of the university and beyond.

Sessions Make an Appointment Apply FAQs Planning your education abroad can feel overwhelming, but we are sure that the benefits of studying abroad are endless and will only enrich your experience at the University of Miami. Stand out from the crowd once you graduate by adding study abroad to your resume and demonstrate you can thrive in a global environment. Participate in one of our 80 programs in over 40 different countries that are as short as a week and as long as a year (and anything in between). There's a program that will fit with your degree plan and goals that'll even keep you on track for graduation, too.

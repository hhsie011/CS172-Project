To get started, review the current Course Offerings on this site. Then meet with your adviser to discuss your plans, secure approval, and enroll using TigerHub. Academic Advising Academic advising occurs in your residential college and through your academic department. Plan to meet with your adviser each semester prior to adding, swapping, or dropping classes. Begin here to learn more about the process and how it relates to course selection. If you are studying abroad during course selection, email proposed courses to your director of undergraduate studies and/or adviser, as well as the instructor, if permission is required. The Office of International Programs handles enrollment for courses taken abroad.

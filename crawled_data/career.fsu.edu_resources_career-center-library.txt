To Vendors: The Career Center Library regularly evaluates its online resources for usefulness and usability for our clients. If you would like for your organization's link to be considered for inclusion on our website, please review our online resource page here and complete the online questionnaire . Your questionnaire will be reviewed by a committee that meets quarterly for the purpose of evaluating online resources. You will be notified of our decision at that time.

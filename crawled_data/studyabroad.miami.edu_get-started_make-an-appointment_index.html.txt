Apply FAQs In accordance with the University of Miami administration's efforts to slow the spread of COVID-19, the Study Abroad Office is temporarily closed to all walk-ins, however we are open virtually for appointments. Please use the contact information below to reach a Study Abroad Advisor in order to schedule a virtual appointment. You can also get in touch with our office by phone (305-284-3434) or email us (studyabroad@miami.edu) with your contact information and availability for a staff member to respond to your questions via phone or video call.

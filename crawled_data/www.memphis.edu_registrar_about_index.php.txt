Among its many services, it: drives the registration process at the University; schedules class offerings on behalf of academic departments; assigns general purpose classrooms; supplies degree and enrollment verifications to the National Student Clearinghouse; processes grades, grade changes, and enrollment changes; produces official University transcripts; assists veterans and their dependents in using their VA benefits; assists students and faculty in using online Self Service applications; provides testing and maintenance support for the Banner Student system; educates the campus with regard to FERPA compliance.

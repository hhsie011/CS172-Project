This common information covers the technical aspects of your research proposal: qualifications of the research team members and information on other their other research support; access to facilities and resources that are needed to conduct the proposed work; and an explanation of the budgetary resources you will require, if funded. Each sponsor has its own variations and specific requirements for these documents; it is also important that you read the solicitation or program information carefully to identify any non-standard requirements. If you are unsure what documents you need to prepare or need help interpreting a solicitation, use our staff directory to locate your sponsored projects officer or research development team liaison, schedule a one-on-one meeting, or make plans to drop in for a visit during help hours held in various locations across campus.

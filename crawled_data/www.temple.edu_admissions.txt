Compliance With the Four Pillars Who We Are Libraries Charles Library Applying to college shouldn’t be hard. That’s the approach we take at Temple University. Regardless of your age, nationality or background, our goal is to make the admissions process as easy and convenient as possible. Find all the information you need to navigate the application process and the contact information for people who can help answer any questions you might have. Undergraduate At Temple University, your undergraduate experience can be as rich and diverse as you choose. We boast more than 100 undergraduate programs, eight campuses—including locations in Rome and Japan—unique learning experiences, and direct access to the artistic and cultural venues of Philadelphia. What’s more, we offer a world-class education at a public university cost. Apply online or learn more about becoming an undergraduate at Temple. Graduate and Professional A graduate or professional degree from Temple prepares you not only to succeed, but to lead. Our advanced degree programs blend real-world experience with a dynamic classroom environment. Brought together, these two elements create a stimulating educational environment filled with fresh ideas and new perspectives. Apply online or learn more about Temple’s graduate and professional programs. International Each year, students from around the world choose to further their education and expand their horizons at Temple. We offer a wide range of programs for both graduate and undergraduate students, and services that help ease your transition into student life at an American university. Learn more about Temple’s international programs. Tuition and Fees Temple offers you the best of both worlds—an internationally recognized research university paired with reasonable tuition and fees of a public university. Learn more about how our commitment to making a high-quality, accessible education benefits you. Financial Aid For most Temple students, financial aid plays a crucial role in their ability to attain a college degree. At Temple, we offer a number of financial-aid and assistance options for eligible students, including scholarships, grants, loans and work-study programs. Learn more about Temple’s financial-aid programs. Campus Visits and Virtual Tour Schedule an in-person visit to our Main Campus any time. We offer weekday visits and select weekend and evening tours, and we host special events such as Open Houses throughout the fall. If you are interested in visiting our suburban campus in Ambler, Pennsylvania, please visit the Ambler Campus website to view the various visit options. Explore our Main Campus in Philadelphia now by taking a virtual tour. You’ll be able to see residence halls, learning and research facilities, and green spaces, as well as learn more about student life and our academic offerings.

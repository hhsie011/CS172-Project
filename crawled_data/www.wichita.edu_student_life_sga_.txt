The Student Senate holds meetings every Wednesday during the academic year at 6:30 p.m. in the Sante Fe Room of the Rhatigan Student Center unless notified otherwise. These meetings are open to the public. Due to current Senate rules relating to COVID-19, the gallery is not open. To view the Senate meeting virutally, please visit the SGA YouTube.

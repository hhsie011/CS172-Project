Events/Workshops CSArts CSArtists Frequently Asked Questions Meet the Staff Center for Student Advising The James H. and Christine Turk Berick Center for Student Advising (CSA) guides and supports undergraduates at Columbia College and Columbia Engineering as they navigate their educations and lives at Columbia University. Students are assigned a CSA adviser who will reach out to them the summer before they matriculate and work with them throughout their undergraduate career.

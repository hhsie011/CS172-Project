Brown was here for you when your education began, and it’s here for you still. Learn a new language, access library resources and online journals, plan the trip of a lifetime with other Brunonians, and more. Rosetta Stone With free access to Rosetta Stone software, Brown alumni can learn any of 30 languages at their own pace, with expert guidance and immediate feedback. Brown Travelers Go around the world on educational adventures with fellow alumni and Brown faculty. Library Eresources Thanks to the Brown University Library, alumni get free access to online journals, books, newspapers, and more. Executive Education Get help meeting your career goals with courses led by world-class Brown faculty from the School of Professional Studies. Brown alumni get 10% off with code ALUM10. New! 100% Online Master of Public Health Designed for working professionals and international learners from the School of Public Health. Applications for the fall 2022 inaugural cohort are due June 1, with a priority deadline of May 15.

With our lending system and multiple educational resources, the FRC provides exciting programs for all members of our community. Located at 719 North Walnut, across from the West Apartments. Operating Hours Monday – Friday: 8:00 am – 8:00 pm Saturday – Sunday: 2:00 pm – 8:00 pm Contact the FRC Need to schedule an appointment with the FRC? The Family Resource Center provides a number of scheduling services to make your time at Oklahoma State the best it can be! Notice to VacateContract Extension Request FormSchedule a Summer Storage InspectionSchedule Appointment: Primary Lessee ChangeSchedule Appointment: Apartment Transfer Adult Programs All FGSH residents are welcome to attend these class at no cost. You must complete the enrollment form before you attend these sessions. Once registered you can attend as many of the classes that you wish to attend. A limited number of spots are available for adults that do not live in FGSH (these classes are for adults only). Adult Programs Youth & Family Programs The FRC provides Children’s Programs during the school year. Your child must complete the Youth and Family Programs enrollment form before you attend these sessions.

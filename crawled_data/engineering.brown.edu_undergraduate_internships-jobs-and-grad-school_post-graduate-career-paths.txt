Post-graduate career paths Brown engineering undergraduates pursue varied and successful paths after graduation. Brown engineering students pursue many different paths after graduation. Approximately 40% work in industry, 30% go to graduate school in engineering, and 30% pursue careers in entrepreneurship/startups, finance/consulting, or public service. Brown engineers have been hired by a variety of prominent companies and been admitted to many of the top schools around the country. Take a look at where the most recent graduates are now.

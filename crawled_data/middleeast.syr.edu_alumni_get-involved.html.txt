Involved Your Syracuse University family misses you! Whether you graduated last year or 50 years ago, you’re forever Orange! Here are some of the ways you can stay involved with other alumni and your Syracuse University family. Keep in touch with campus The next time you're in the U.S., consider visiting campus. There’s always something happening, including sporting events, lectures, and of course, Orange Central—the combination homecoming and reunion celebration held every fall. Visit Cuse Community to keep up with the latest happenings! When you can't make the trip, stay up to date on Syracuse University news by subscribing to SU News and connecting with us on social media. Attend local events Syracuse University in Dubai sponsors faculty speakers, career networking events, admitted student events, and many other activities. Find out about the latest events. Build your personal contacts New to the region? Looking to establish some contacts? Join the Syracuse University MENAT Alumni Group on LinkedIn. Join our community of experts Syracuse University alumni and friends can provide today’s students with invaluable support in the form of expertise and real-world experiences. Many alumni return to campus as guest lecturers, serve on advisory boards, provide career advice, host immersion programs in the MENAT region, or offer internship or employment opportunities. Recruit new students Syracuse University alumni are some of the University’s strongest advocates. If you loved your Orange experience, share it with friends, neighbors, colleagues, and the kid who lives down the street. These people may one day become prospective students and parents. Encourage anyone who’s considering college to attend a local Syracuse University event. As you can see, there are myriad ways to stay connected and involved. Interested? Contact us!

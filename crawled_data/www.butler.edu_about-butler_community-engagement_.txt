Big. Happy. (Indy.) As proud as we are to be a vibrant, vital part of Indianapolis, we know Indianapolis is just as important to Butler. Every day, we take steps to draw our partners in the community even closer. For our students, it means the four walls of their classrooms are just a starting point for something much bigger. Pause/Play Economic Leaders A Community of Economic Leaders Since 1855, Butler has recognized the importance of connecting to the community through partnerships, philanthropy, and providing access to our incredibly skilled pool of talent. About Us Educators A Community of Educators Butler invests in our neighborhood’s public education, providing innovative project-based learning for local P-12 students through partnerships with the Indianapolis Public Schools district. Explore the College of Education Creators A Community of Creators Partnering with Indianapolis arts organizations, and producing and hosting performances and events each year, Butler, like Indy, is a community of creators working to create a more vibrant local arts scene.

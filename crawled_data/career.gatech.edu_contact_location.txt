The Success Center is located between the football stadium and the Tech Tower. Parking & Building Location: Recruiters should park in the new North Avenue visitor parking lot (labeled with a yellow sign that reads "Area #1 Parking") located across the street from the Tech Tower. As you head west on North Avenue, you will pass the football stadium on your right and the Alumni House on the left. Continue on to the next parking lot entrance on your left. After driving underneath the overhead foot bridge, turn left into the Area #1 Parking lot. After entering the driveway for the parking lot, you will go through the gate located to your left to enter visitor parking. You can park in the surface lot or drive up a ramp on the far side of the surface lot to park on the top level of the Burge parking deck. The cost of parking is $1.50 per hour and is payable by credit card upon exiting. To get to our office from the parking area, cross North Avenue on the footbridge. After crossing the street, continue straight and up the first flight of stairs. Turn right to enter the Student Success Center. Career Center Building Location: Our offices are located in the Bill Moore Student Success Center (1st Floor) and the Employer Check-in area for on campus interviews is located in the Basement area of the same building. The building is located across the street from the North Avenue Parking Area. To get to our building you can cross the street underneath the crosswalk above the street or you can use the crosswalk at the top of the parking deck. Once you walk across North Avenue from the parking area please follow the sidewalk to the right near the football stadium to the first building on the right (Building #31 - Bill Moore Student Success Center). After entering the building take the elevators to the basement level if you are conducting interviews or to the 1st floor if you are meeting with one of our staff members. Career Center Parking Procedures: The Visitor Parking Area has an automated parking attendant. You will be issued an automated ticket when you enter the parking area. The parking rate is $2.00 per hour (maximum of $15/day). When you exit the parking area you will insert your ticket into the automated attendant and then insert the payment amount indicated. The parking attendant accepts cash and credit/debit cards as payment and will provide a receipt. If the North Avenue Visitor Parking area is full, the next most convenient lot will be adjacent to the Student Center/Student Services Buildings. That lot is located on Ferst Drive. To get there turn left out of the parking area onto North Avenue. Take the first right onto Cherry Street, and follow it around the curve onto Ferst Drive. Proceed on Ferst Drive until you see the Student Center Parking banner (Parking Area 2) on the right side of the street. Ticketing and/or "booting" of illegally parked vehicles is enforced. Ticket/towing fees must be borne by the recipient. Please park in one of these visitor lots.

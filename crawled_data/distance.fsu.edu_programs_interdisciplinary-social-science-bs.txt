You'll have an opportunity to draw on several social science disciplines — such as Economics, Geography, Political Science, Public Administration, Sociology and Urban and Regional Planning — as you seek an understanding of social problems, public policy issues and questions about society. With its flexible format, the program enables you to pursue your own individual, specialized, and pre-professional interests. Visit coss.fsu.edu and coss.fsu.edu/iss/Distance-Learning for more information. For advising, please email coss-socialsciences-dl@fsu.edu.

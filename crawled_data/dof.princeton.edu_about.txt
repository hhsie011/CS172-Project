Our office is charged with ensuring the success and well-being of the faculty, professional researchers, professional specialists, and professional librarians who work at Princeton. We aim to respond to the various interests, concerns, and needs of all of our scholars, teachers, and researchers appointed through our office. We also seek to partner with the many academic and administrative offices at Princeton in order to support the life and work of our colleagues. This website provides helpful information about our policies and procedures. Here you will also find a link to the directory of our staff in the Office of the Dean of the Faculty. Please reach out to us directly if you need assistance. Gene A. Jarrett Dean of the Faculty and William S.

SUAPP Follow Us Online Facebooktwitter Home Planning Give to Gene Pearson Memorial Giving Memphis Gene Pearson Memorial Giving Gene Pearson, founding member of the Department of City and Regional Planning, and longtime director, passed away on May 23, 2017. Gene's career at the University of Memphis spanned forty years, during which he helped prepare generations of planners and had an indelible impact on the Memphis region. Gene's family has asked that, in lieu of flowers, donations be made in Gene's honor to the Department of City and Regional Planning, so that we can continue to support students in his legacy and grow the program that Gene built. To donate online: visit www.memphis.edu/giving Click "College of Arts and Sciences" Click "All CAS Funds" Select "City and Regional Planning Discretionary Fund" To donate by check: Make the check out to "University of Memphis Foundation, FUND 20729" And mail it to Department of City & Regional Planning 208 McCord Hall, University of Memphis Memphis, TN 38152 Or contact us at 901-678-2161 and we'll help you arrange a donation.

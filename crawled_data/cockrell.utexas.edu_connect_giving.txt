Virtual Visit Contact Us Giving When you make a gift to the Cockrell School of Engineering, you are playing an active role in improving the experiences for those in the Texas Engineering community. Support from our generous alumni and friends helps us engage the best and brightest students in hands-on projects and empower our award-winning researchers to shape the future of innovation. Through your investment, you will play a vital role in advancing the mission of the Cockrell School.

FAQ Contact Post-Bacc Pre-Med Program The University of Louisville School of Medicine and the College of Arts and Sciences established the Post-Bacc Pre-Med program for career changers interested in going to medical school. This program is designed specifically for individual's with a bachelor's degree who want to go to medical school but haven't fulfilled the pre-med course requirements for medical school admission. To learn more about the program, including program structure, support services, and application requirements, visit the program Post-Bacc Pre-Med webpage. Please note, the Post-Bacc Pre-Med program is not designed to be a record enhancing program for individuals who have previously completed some or most of the pre-medical curriculum. For students interested in record enhancement, you may consider the non-thesis M.S. in Physiology Pre-Health Care Track program.

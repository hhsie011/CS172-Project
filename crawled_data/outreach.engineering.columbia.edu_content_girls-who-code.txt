Photo Credit: Girls who code Girls Who Code has a Columbia club chapter that runs during the school year. This national organization is dedicated to closing the gender gap in the technology and engineering sectors. The program meets on campus and recruits high school girls from local high-need partner schools. The club runs classes on Saturdays and has two levels, (10am – 12pm for introductory and 12pm-2pm for experienced). The chapter accepts a new applicants in the beginning of each semester.

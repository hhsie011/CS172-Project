Admissions Representatives right arrow down arrow Out of State Representatives Social Media COVID-19 Updates In-Person Campus Tour Come learn about the University of Cincinnati through an admissions presentation, a one-hour student-guided walking tour of campus, as well as add the option to visit with one of our academic colleges. We host visits Monday- Friday. Accessible tour routes are available. Admissions Presentation and Campus Tour Only If you would like to register for an admissions presentation and campus tour only, please select any of the dates/times listed in the calendar below. Admissions Presentation, Campus Tour, and College Close-Up If you would like to register for an admissions presentation, campus tour, and college close-up, please review the academic college offerings below. Not sure what academic college your major of interest is in? Visit our majors and degree programs page to learn more! College of Allied Health Sciences (CAHS) If you would like to meet with the College of Allied Health Sciences following your admissions preview and campus tour, please register for a visit on Fridays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will follow at 11:30 am. College of Arts and Sciences (A/S) If you would like to meet with the College of Arts and Sciences following your admissions preview and campus tour, please register for a visit on Mondays or Fridays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will follow at 11:00 am. College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning (DAAP) If you would like to meet with the College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning, please register for a visit on Mondays at 9:00 am or Fridays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will begin at 11:00 am. College of Education, Criminal Justice, & Human Services (CECH) If you would like to meet with the College of Education, Criminal Justice, & Human Services, please register for a visit on Mondays at 9:00 am, Wednesdays at 11:00 am, or Fridays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will begin either at 11:00 am or at 1:00 pm and will be reflected when you select your session. Sessions are broken down by school within CECH: School of Education School of Criminal Justice School of Human Services School of Information Technology College of Engineering & Applied Science (CEAS) If you would like to meet with the College of Engineering and Applied Science, please register for a visit on Mondays at 11:00 am or Fridays at 11:00 am. Your college close-up will begin at 1:00 pm. Lindner College of Business (LCB) If you would like to meet with the Lindner College of Business following your admissions preview and campus tour, please register for a visit on Mondays at 9:00 am or Fridays at 11:00 am. Your college close-up will begin after your preview and tour. College of Medicine – Medical Sciences If you would like to meet with the College of Medicine (Medical Sciences) following your admissions preview and campus tour, please register for a visit on Fridays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will begin at 11:30 am. College of Medicine - Public Health If you would like to meet with the College of Medicine (Public Health) following your admissions preview and campus tour, please register for a visit on Mondays or Fridays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will begin at 11:00 am. College of Nursing (CON) If you would like to meet with the College of Nursing following your admissions preview and campus tour, please register for a visit on Thursdays at 9:00 am. Your college close-up will begin at 11:30 am. How to Register Use the calendar below to view and select your preferred campus tour date and time. If the campus tour date and time you select is grayed out, it means that tour time has reached registration capacity. On the registration form you can also select to attend an academic college close-up after your campus tour. Join the waitlist If the campus tour date & time you select is grayed out, it means that tour time has reached registration capacity. You will also have the option to join the waitlist for a full campus tour date/time. The waitlist is automated and you will be notified via email if you are officially taken off the waitlist. Please note, the waitlist form will not populate if both the campus tour date/time registration and waitlist slots have both met full registration capacity.

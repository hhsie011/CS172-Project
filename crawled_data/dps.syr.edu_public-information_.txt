With this in mind, we strive to be timely and accurate in posting public information to our website, including a comprehensive list of reported incidents for the past 60 days, digital copies of the public safety notices disseminated to the University community, a map of Blue Light locations throughout the campus area, and an annual report detailing our programming and summary crime statistics for the past 3 years. To learn more about the department’s mission and code of conduct, please review the Department of Public Safety Duty Manual. To send a media interview request to the Public Information Officer for scheduling, please submit our Media Request Form with at least 48 hours before your deadline. For general inquiries, please email dpsadmin@syr.edu.

In the plan, Brown University articulated their commitment to building on the diversity and inclusion, the concrete actions and pathways of which were articulated in 2016's University-wide Pathways to Diversity and Inclusion: An Action Plan for Brown University. In conjunction with the University-wide initiative, departments across Brown detailed their own Departmental Diversity and Inclusion Action Plans (DDIAPs), submitted and approved by the Office of Institutional Diversity & Inclusion, detailing steps forward for creating a campus-wide environment where all students are able to achieve their full potential as members of the University and future scholars and leaders. MCM's Departmental Diversity and Inclusion Action Plan can be viewed here (Brown login required).

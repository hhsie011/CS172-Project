He will also speak about the Italian Task Force's work in the rescue of monuments in Syria and across the Middle East. This will be followed by a brief introduction by the Minister and by Dario Disegni to the newly-created Museum of Italian Judaism, directed by Simonetta Della Seta. This event forms part of the 2017-18 program of the Academy’s International Observatory for Cultural Heritage.

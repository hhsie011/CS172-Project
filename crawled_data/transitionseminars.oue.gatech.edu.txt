GT 1000 is for incoming first-year students, and GT 2000 is for incoming transfer students. These courses are committed to supporting the successful transition and experience of new Georgia Tech students. Each section meets one hour per week and is taught by either an academic faculty member or an administrator with an advanced degree. All sections utilize upper-class peer leaders, referred to as “Team Leaders,” who volunteer to work closely with the instructor to facilitate small group interaction and offer advice and support to first-year and transfer students.

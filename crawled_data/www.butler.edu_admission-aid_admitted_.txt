Recently admitted to Butler? You’re in—be proud! We received more than 15,000 applications for this year’s incoming class, so it says something to have made it this far. Whether you’ve decided to join us or you’re still making up your mind, you’ll find resources on this page for admitted students and their families. Find out more about your next steps in the enrollment process, register to attend an admitted student visit, and prepare to have an incredible college experience. Don’t hesitate to contact your admission counselor if you have questions that aren’t answered here. We’re here to help! Now, the ball’s in your court. Even if you’ve been to campus, you may find that a second visit tells you what you need to know. Our Admitted Student Visits will give you an up-close look at Bulldog life. Schedule an Admitted Student Visit Take our Virtual Tour Financial Aid Next Steps Make sure you’ve filed the FAFSA—the Free Application for Federal Student Aid. We’ll need that to determine your eligibility for gifts and other assistance. We begin sending Financial Aid Notifications to incoming first-year students in mid-February. Filing the FAFSA Financial Aid Notifications Net Price Calculator I’m so there. If your decision is made, congrats, Bulldog! Let’s make it official. New students must submit a $500 deposit to secure a spot in the incoming class. After you’ve paid the deposit, take a deep breath. The hardest part is over—you’ve decided! But we’ve got a lot to do. Get an email account. Start the Virtual Registration process. Complete your housing application. Let’s get started.

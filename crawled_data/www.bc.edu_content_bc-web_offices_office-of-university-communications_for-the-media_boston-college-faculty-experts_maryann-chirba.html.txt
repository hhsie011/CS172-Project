Ford, S.J., Distinguished Scholar Mary Ann Chirba Contact Boston College Law School 885 Centre Street Newton Centre, MA 02459 Telephone: 617-552-4381 Email: maryann.chirba@bc.edu Expertise Healthcare Law Profile Mary Ann Chirba is the lead author of Health Care Reform: Law and Practice - A Comprehensive Guide to the Affordable Care Act and its Implementing Regulations, a 2 volume treatise, co-authored by Alice A. Noble and Michael M. Maddigan. With Prof. Alice A. Noble, Dr. Chirba has published and spoken extensively on the Affordable Care Act. She has also published numerous articles and been invited to present at the World Stem Cell Summit and other domestic and international conferences on FDA and international regulation of adult stem cell therapies and regenerative medicine.

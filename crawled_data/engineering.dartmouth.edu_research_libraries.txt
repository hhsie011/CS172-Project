Dartmouth Research Libraries Dartmouth libraries partner with faculty, students, and staff in research activities locally, nationally and internationally. Our library experts are embedded at critical points in the research lifecycle providing collections, information resources, innovative tools and services to support scholarly inquiry. The Library is committed to partnering with faculty and students to enhance and showcase Dartmouth scholarly output and entrepreneurial endeavors. On This Page Engineering Library Dartmouth Libraries Engineering Library Feldberg Library provides access to a wide range of information resources that support teaching, research, and learning in management and engineering. Feldberg Business and Engineering Library A home for collaboration and research, Feldberg Library brings together the engineering, business, and interdisciplinary programs of Dartmouth's West End. Feldberg librarians help faculty and students gain access to a wide range of online information resources. Feldberg Library Website Engineering Research Guide The Feldberg Library engineering research guide offers quick access to selected information research tools and resources for starting your research in engineering. Engineering Research Guide Dartmouth Libraries Dartmouth College Library The Dartmouth College Library provides gateway access to all campus libraries, their services, and information resources, including electronic journals, electronic books, research databases, and reference tools across all academic disciplines.

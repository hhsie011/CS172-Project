Read More Police Available 24 hours a day, seven days a week, the Baylor University Police Department operates marked patrol vehicles on campus, a bicycle unit, a Criminal Investigation unit, and a Crime Prevention unit. Read More Emergency Management & Fire Safety Emergency Management leads the campus toward preparation for, and efficient response to, emergencies and disasters to save lives, reduce human suffering and minimize property loss and the disruption to academic services. Read More Parking & Transportation Parking & Transportation manages all aspects of campus parking for faculty, staff, students and visitors. Read More Technical Security Technical Security manages and administers call boxes and emergency telephones, card access systems, security cameras and intrusion/alarm systems.

Career Library Monday - Friday (8 am - 5 pm) 100 South Woodward Avenue Dunlap Student Success Center, Suite 1200 Career Advising The Career Center offers both in-person drop-in career advising hours, and virtual career advising with career liaisons through NoleNetwork appointments. In-Person Drop-in Career advising hours are: Monday - Friday 9 a.m. - 4:30 p.m. (Closed for weekly meeting and training session on Friday from 1:30-2:30) If you would like to meet with a Career Liaison, please see their hours here, and to book a virtual career advising session: Login to NoleNetwork Click appointments on the menu bar options. Click request a new appointment. Select Virtual Career Advising Adjust date, time, and any other filters (optional) Click search at the bottom Select your preferred appointment time to complete the booking process by following any additional prompts. You will get a confirmation email and can always log back into your NoleNetwork account to update, change, or cancel your virtual career advising booking. The Career Center is closed on all University Holidays. CONFIDENTIALITY Garnet and Gold Scholar Society Advising Garnet & Gold Scholar Society advising is offered virtually via Zoom. Email garnetandgoldscholar@fsu.edu or click here to request a virtual advising appointment. Many questions can also be answered by visiting the GGSS website. Career Liaisons Industry Specific Career Advising available in most Colleges. *Hours are subject to change. View Career Liaison Drop-In hours here Career Center staff also serve as liaisons for all academic Colleges and specific FSU departments and programs.

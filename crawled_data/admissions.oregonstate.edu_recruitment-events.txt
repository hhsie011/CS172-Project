Events are held on-campus, across Oregon, and in several western states. We want to meet you, give you the chance to speak with our team, and ask about life at Oregon State. Application Workshops Meet Us in the Fall Beaver Open Houses are programs that offer an in-depth look at Oregon State. Take a student-led tour, check out the residence halls, and learn about our 200+ academic programs. These programs typically occur in October and November. Open House Information There are also special family night programs offered across Oregon where you can learn about OSU and its resources. Black Family Nights Latino Family Nights Meet Us in the Spring For high school students beginning their college search, Spring Previews are programs to learn the basics about Oregon State. These programs typically occur in February, March or April.

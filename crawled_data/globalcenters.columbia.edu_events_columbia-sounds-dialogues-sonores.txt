Events Past Event Columbia Sounds: Dialogues sonores Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo April 15, 2019 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM Columbia Global Centers l Paris, 4 rue de Chevreuse, 75006 Celebrating the musical connections between Columbia University and IRCAM, this concert showcases recent works by composers taking part in the 2018-19 IRCAM Cursus Program, in collaboration with performers currently in residence at Cité Internationale des Arts. Two of the featured composers, Louis Goldford and William Dougherty, are currently composition graduate students in the Music Department at Columbia University. Composers of 2018-19 IRCAM Cursus: Mathieu Corajod, Florent Caron Darras, William Dougherty, Louis Goldford, Konstantin Heuer, William Kuo, Jialin Liu, Sergio Nuñez Meneses, Adrien Trybucki, Francisco Uberto Performers and Residents at Cité Internationale des Arts: Helianna Herkkola, soprano, Samuel Casale, flute, Rina Maezawa, clarinet, Paul Hübner, trumpet, Maxime Sauze, piano, Ayano Kamei, piano, Pei-Ying Hsieh, percussion, Hsin-Chun Chou, percussion, Léo Marillier, violin, Stéphanie Huang, cello, Polina Streltsova, cello, Yi Zhou, cello; Marcel Cara, harp; Yui Nanasawa, soprano Free Entry Célébrant les liens musicaux entre Columbia University et IRCAM, ce concert présente des œuvres récentes de compositeurs participant au programme Cursus 2018-19, en collaboration avec des musiciens en résidence à la Cité Internationale des Arts. Deux des compositeurs en vedette, Louis Goldford et William Dougherty, sont actuellement des étudiants doctorants en composition du Department of Music à Columbia University. Compositeurs du Cursus : Mathieu Corajod, Florent Caron Darras, William Dougherty, Louis Goldford, Konstantin Heuer, William Kuo, Jialin Liu, Sergio Nuñez Meneses, Adrien Trybucki, Francisco Uberto Musiciens et Résidents de la Cité Internationale des Arts: Helianna Herkkola, soprano, Samuel Casale, flute, Rina Maezawa, clarinet, Paul Hübner, trumpet, Maxime Sauze, piano, Ayano Kamei, piano, Pei-Ying Hsieh, percussion, Hsin-Chun Chou, percussion, Léo Marillier, violin, Stéphanie Huang, cello, Polina Streltsova, cello, Yi Zhou, cello; Marcel Cara, harp; Yui Nanasawa, soprano Entrée libre dans la limites des places disponibles.

Analysis (MVPA) Proposals Documents and Forms Principal Investigators Publications For Volunteers Studies Seeking Volunteers FAQ for Volunteers MRI Research Facility The Brown University MRI Research Facility (MRF), provides infrastructure and support to facilitate research and educational activities using magnetic resonance imaging technology. The centerpiece of the facility is a state-of-the-art Siemens 3 Tesla PRISMA scanner, equipped with 64 receiver channels for significant gains in signal-to-noise ratio and acquisition speed.

The Testing Services Office is part of the University of Cincinnati Academic Excellent and Support Services, located in the Division of Student Affairs. Testing Services coordinates the administration of classroom tests for students with disabilities at University of Cincinnati. Students must be registered with UC's Accessibility Resources to receive testing accommodations and to take tests in our center. Announcements Testing Services has moved to French Hall West (see below for directions). Accommodated Testing services is now at French Hall West. Currently enrolled UC students that require Testing Accommodations can make an appointment online to come test with us. UC Testing Services is now offering the TEAS exam in partnership with Proctorio. The exam will be offered online so you don't have to leave your home during these uncertain times. You can register for the Online TEAS here. If you have any questions, please email us at uctesting@ucmail.uc.edu. Current and former students can register year-round for discounted Princeton Review or Kaplan Test Preparation courses. Go to Test Preparation Courses and select one of our many online offerings.

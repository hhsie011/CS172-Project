Entering in the spring? Click here for your next steps. YOU'RE ACCEPTED! Now what? ASAP: File the FAFSA and CSS Profile (required for need-based aid). February 1 – Priority deadline for summer/fall transfers If you miss this priority date, please submit the applications as soon as possible and you will be reviewed for eligibility on any funds that are available. If interested, apply to apply to the Elite Academic Opportunities like the Honors College. View your transfer credits in goBAYLOR under the "Application" tab. Next, pay your deposit! YOU'VE PAID YOUR DEPOSIT! Now what? It's official: you're a Baylor Bear, and your spot is secure. These next steps are crucial to get you enrolled. ASAP: Make sure you’ve done all the checklist items in the Accepted section above. ASAP: Enroll with Baylor’s DUO 2-Factor Authentication platform (required on your student phone). ASAP: Access your Baylor email at mail.baylor.edu. Your advisor will contact you through this email address! Be sure to check your email regularly. ASAP: Review your financial options at baylor.edu/sfs. ASAP: Submit your Health Form. Optional: Submit your Housing Application. Transfer students are not required to live on campus. April: Register for Baylor Line Camp and sign up for Welcome Week! April - May: Sign up to register for classes with an Advisor. (The invitation to schedule an appointment will be sent to your Baylor email.) Required before registering for classes with your advisor: Submit the New Student Advising Questionnaire (available in goBAYLOR). Testing requirements for: Math ALEKS, Chemistry ALEKS, and/or English language (check goBAYLOR to see if this is required of you) By May 12 (for Summer semester): Pay your first semester bill. May - June: Send your updated college transcripts with spring grades. By July 15: Submit your proof of Bacterial Meningitis Vaccination (allow 1-2 weeks for processing). By July 31 (for Fall semester): Pay your first semester bill. By August (for Fall semester): Prepare for Move-In Day at baylor.edu/move2bu and check out the recommended equipment list.

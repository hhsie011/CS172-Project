Hunt Jr. Library, Room 4105 View map Add to calendar 1070 Partners Way, Raleigh, NC 27695 View map Add to calendar Please join the Department of Agriculture and Human Sciences for a two-day training session to raise awareness and compassion for Native American students in higher education while putting Native American representation in higher education in a historical, contemporary and local context. Each day of the training will include a delicious lunch highlighting Native American-produced foods. While participation in both sessions is encouraged, it is not required.

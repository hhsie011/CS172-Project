We are responsible for transactions relating to your records (i.e., grade changes, advanced placement credits, transfer credits, academic progress, disciplinary actions, grade point computations) and managing the safe retention and appropriate use of your academic records. Additionally, it is responsible for confirming completion of degree requirements, ordering of diplomas and the awarding of degrees, Honor Roll recognition and the compiling of data for the completion of internal and external reports.

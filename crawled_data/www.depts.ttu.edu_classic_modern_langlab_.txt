Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Language Lab & Research Center Menu Search The Language Lab & Research Center (LLRC) is a specialized language learning center and library that serves CMLL faculty and students. Our services evolve with the ever-changing departmental needs. We identify solutions that meet logistical and technological demands in all possible aspects of testing, instruction, research and training. We support CMLL through a full array of services.

to graduate school or finding a job, you can find the tools and resources you need to be successful at The Career Center. Plan Your Career Drop in for career advising to discuss your career development, or enroll in SDS 3340 and engage in life and career planning. Resumes and Cover Letters Have you perfected these important professional documents? Find resources, both at The Career Center and online. Gain Experience Try on various work environments and strengthen your skills through a part-time job, an internship or co-op, service learning, leadership, or even research. Prepare for the Next Step Find resources for finding a job or internship, prepping for graduate school, and creating an employment strategy. Events See the full list of career events and networking opportunities that The Career Center has to offer. Workshops See the full list of workshops and information sessions that The Career Center has to offer. Get Involved With The Career Center Learn more about opportunities The Career Center offers for students to connect campus involvement with career preparation. Consider Graduate School Thinking about furthering your education? Discover resources for researching programs and schools, as wells as financing the cost of higher education. Graduate Students Prepare for your future by taking advantage of Career Center services and resources tailored specifically for graduate students. Distance Learners Explore career opportunities and search for jobs using a variety of online resources designed to help you plan your career.

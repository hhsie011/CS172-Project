May 06, 2022 Commencement Events on May 19 May Impact Your Check-Out As you know, this year we are also welcoming back graduates from the classes of 2021 and 2020 on Thursday, May 19. There will be several ceremonies taking place on the South Lawns from 9:00 a.m. through 6:30 p.m. This will require campus and street closures to welcome back thousands of graduates and guests. While this is a unique moment for your fellow students to return to celebrate their accomplishments, campus will be busier than usual during your move out. If your plans allow for any flexibility, we strongly encourage you to depart on Wednesday, May 18. Important Logistics: Check-Out Times and Street Closures Thank you for your help in ensuring a smooth move out and happy celebrations for all. Seniors Living in EC Check-Out is by noon on Thursday, May 19 You previously received a message that indicated that Check-Out was until 6 p.m. The correct departure time is noon. 116th Street between Amsterdam Avenue and Morningside Drive is closed to cars Tuesday, May 17 at 10 p.m. through Thursday 5/19 at 6 p.m. Access to East Campus will be by car on Morningside Drive from points north or south of 116th Street. Exit to a car on Morningside Drive from the building will be through the B3 level. Exit from Wien Courtyard will only be on foot during this time. Exit from East Campus via Ancell Plaza is not permitted on Thursday. There will be Commencement receptions taking place. Please use the B3 level to exit. If you remain until May 19, we ask you to please be respectful of your fellow graduates and guests celebrating at these events. Seniors Living in Broadway, Hogan, Ruggles and West 114th Street brownstones Check-Out is by 6 p.m. on Thursday, May 19 114th Street is closed to cars Tuesday, May 17 at 10 p.m. through Thursday, May 19 at 10 a.m. Cars will be permitted on Thursday starting at 10 a.m. Please plan accordingly.

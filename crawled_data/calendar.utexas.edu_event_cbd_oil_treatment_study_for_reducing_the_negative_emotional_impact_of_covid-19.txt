The University of Texas at Austin Texas Today Main Menu Login Search our events Search Texas Today: UT Events & Announcements Calendar › Event Details CBD Oil Treatment Study for Reducing the Negative Emotional Impact of COVID-19 Are you currently experiencing elevated stress reactions such as anxiety, depression, anger, sleep problems, or substance problems as a result of COVID-19? If so, you may be eligible to take part in a University of Texas study testing the effects of CBD oil in coping with COVID-related stress reactions. If you qualify for the study, you may receive 4 weeks of free CBD oil. To determine if your eligibility, you must first complete a web-based evaluation which is available at COVID-19 CBD Project - Subject Screening Form For more information about this study, visit our ClinicalTrials.gov listing at: https://clinicaltrials.gov/ct2/show/NCT04603781?id=NCT04603781&draw=2&rank=1&load=cart This study has been approved by the Institutional Review Board at the University of Texas (2020-07-0138).

Jim was a beloved faculty member in our department and also in the Department of Psychology. He was an extraordinary scholar, an excellent teacher, and a cherished colleague. The death of this genuinely kind and thoughtful man is a profound loss to his family, friends, students, and our community. He will be greatly missed.

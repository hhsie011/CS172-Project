Cleary Chair in Finance Jeffrey Pontiff Contact Fulton Hall 324C Telephone: 617-552-6786 Email: pontiff@bc.edu Expertise Economics & Finance Personal Website Profile Jeffrey Pontiff is an expert on Wall Street with teaching and research interests in the area of corporate finance and capital markets. His analysis has been cited in the Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Forbes, Financial Times, Baron’s Money, The Economist, Marketwatch, and ABCNews.com while his research has been published in the Journal of Finance, Journal of Financial Economics, American Economic Review, Quarterly Journal of Economics, Review of Financial Studies, and the Rand Journal. He is currently an editor at the Review of Asset Pricing Studies, an associate editor at the Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, the Journal of Wine Economics, and a past associate editor at the Review of Financial Studies and Management Science.

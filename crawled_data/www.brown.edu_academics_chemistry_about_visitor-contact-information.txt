The Department of Chemistry main office is located in Room 201 of the GeoChemistry Building ("GeoChem") at 156 George Street, Providence. The office is open Monday - Friday from 8:30 AM - 5 PM (Summer: 8 AM - 4 PM). Please stop by the main office upon your arrival. Please note you may also enter through MacMillan Hall at 167 Thayer Street, Providence.

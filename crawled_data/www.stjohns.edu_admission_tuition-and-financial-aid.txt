We hope the information provided here will prove useful and convenient to access. File for Financial Aid The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) is now available for the 2022-2023 Academic Year. New Students for the 2022–23 Academic Year should file as soon as possible. To apply for financial aid for the 2022–23 academic year, students are encouraged to complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form as soon as possible after October 1. Go to studentaid.gov or call 1-800-4FEDAID for more information. Priority FAFSA Deadline for Continuing Students for the 2022-2023 Academic Year is March 1, 2022.

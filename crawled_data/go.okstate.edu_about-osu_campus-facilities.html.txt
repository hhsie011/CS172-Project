MEETINGS, CONFERENCES & EVENTS Getting to and around campus Any Cowboy you meet will be happy to help you find your way. But for those who like to plan, check out our: Campus Maps Explore campus Whether you’re a student, part of the faculty or staff, or alumni coming home, here are the places you need to see. Student Union The one-stop-shop for student needs. The OSU Student Union is the most comprehensive union in the world. Edmon Low Library Easy to use, easy to access, we strive to provide the best library services possible. Check out our research and learning resources. Colvin Recreation Center Need a workout? Visit our recently renovated rec center for 240,000 square feet of gyms, weight rooms, jogging tracks and pools. ConocoPhillips OSU Alumni Center A home for alumni on campus. The perfect destination for any event, from small gatherings to conferences, banquets and weddings. Join the crowd Boone Pickens Stadium With a capacity of 56,790, a central location and powerful traditions, this is football done The Cowboy Way. Gallagher-Iba Arena Known as the "Madison Square Garden of the Plains," our basketball and wrestling teams still dominate this historic arena. Athletic Village The OSU Athletic Village is home to a world-class baseball and softball stadiums and state-of-the art track and field complex and training center. See the sights McKnight Center for the Performing Arts The McKnight Center is a world-class cultural attraction for renowned musicians, actors and artists from around the world — and for their fans as well. OSU Museum of Art Murals, sculptures and more are always on view at the OSU Museum of Art in downtown Stillwater, with special curated exhibits joining the displays throughout the year. National Wrestling Hall of Fame The National Wrestling Hall of Fame, located in the home of college wrestling powerhouse OSU, honors the sport by preserving its history, recognizing exceptional achievements and inspiring future generations.

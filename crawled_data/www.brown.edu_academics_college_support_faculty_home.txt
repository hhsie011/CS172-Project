In addition to the information available on this site, Besenia Rodriguez, Senior Associate Dean of the College for Curriculum, is available to discuss issues related to curricular matters and concentrations. Instructors of all ranks are encouraged to consult with the Sheridan Center for Teaching and Learning for resources around teaching and learning. Carol Cohen, Senior Associate Dean of the College for Class Advising and Health and Personal Issues, and the class advising deans, Yolanda Rome (first-years and sophomores) and Lindsay Garcia (juniors and seniors), are available to discuss questions related to academic advising.

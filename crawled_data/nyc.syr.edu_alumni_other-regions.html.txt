Although the New York metro area has Syracuse University’s highest concentration of alumni, there are large contingents of Syracuse University alumni, students, and parents in cities across the United States. That means Syracuse University maintains a presence in these areas as well. If you’re traveling to any of the following locations, visit the regional website for information about events and local alumni club activities.

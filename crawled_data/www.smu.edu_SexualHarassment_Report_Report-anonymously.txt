The form may be submitted anonymously or with a name and contact information. Learn more about reporting to police and pursuing criminal charges. To report anonymously to SMU's Title IX Coordinator Those who wish to anonymously file a complaint or provide information regarding Title IX sexual harassment to SMU's Title IX Coordinator can complete the Title IX Sexual Harassment Complaint Form. To make a third-party report of Title IX sexual harassment, please complete the Title IX Sexual Harassment Third-Party Report Form. The form may be submitted anonymously, with a name and contact information, or by a third party. The form can be emailed to accessequity@smu.edu; submitted in Perkins Administration Building, Room 204; or mailed to the Title IX Coordinator, P.O. Box 750200, Dallas, TX 75275-0200. Learn more about reporting to SMU's Title IX Coordinator and pursuing an internal grievance process.

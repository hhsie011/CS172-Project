The facility offers protein/peptide sequencing, large and small scale peptide synthesis (Fmoc), matrix-assisted laser desorption/ionization (MALDI) mass spectrometry, SDS-PAGE/electroblotting, 2-D gel electrophoresis, isoelectric focusing (IEF), in-gel and solution digestion, tandem mass spectrometry (LC-MS/MS), ion mobility mass spectrometry (IM-MS), digital image acquisition and analysis using the Typhoon imaging system and the 2D gel documentation/analysis system, and semi-preparative, analytical and micro-analytical high performance liquid chromatography (HPLC). MALDI, HPLC, SDS-PAGE/blotting, IEF, 2D gel electrophoresis, 2D gel documentation/analysis and the Typhoon imaging system are all available for user operation after appropriate training. 1D and 2D gel documentation and analysis 2-D gel electrophoresis High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) Hybridoma services In-gel and solution digestion Isoelectric focusing (IEF) MALDI Mass Analysis Peptide Synthesis Protein/Peptide Sequencing Q Exactive Tandem Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS/MS) SDS-PAGE and Electroblotting Synapt G2-Si HDMS Typhoon imaging system The Protein Facility is located on the ground floor (Room 1178) of the Molecular Biology Building on Iowa State University's central campus.

The Council reviews programs and practices that promote diversity, inclusion, and fair treatment of all members of the community. The Advisory Council on Diversity consists of ex-officio members from other relevant councils, and no fewer than fifteen other members. Members are appointed by the President for staggered terms of three years each, beginning July 1 and ending June 30. The Chair is designated biennially by the President. The Advisory Council on Diversity shall be staffed by the Vice President for Institutional Equity and Diversity.

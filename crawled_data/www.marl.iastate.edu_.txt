The laboratory houses equipment for scanning electron microscopy (SEM), energy-dispersive x-ray spectroscopy (EDS), x-ray diffraction (XRD), x-ray fluorescence (XRF), x-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), thermal analysis (SDT, TGA) and light microscopy. The Materials Analysis and Research Laboratory is located in rooms 23 through 68 in the basement level of Town Engineering Building at the northwest corner of the Iowa State University campus in Ames, Iowa.

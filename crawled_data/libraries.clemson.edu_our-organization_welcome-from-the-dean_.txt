Cooper Library 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM ▾ Special Collections and Archives: By Appointment Gunnin Architecture Library: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM Education Media Center: 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM MENUMENU My Account Giving Help Welcome from the Dean Welcome to Clemson Libraries! We are so pleased to have you back on campus in person this year. Our five libraries – Cooper, Gunnin, the Education Media Center, Special Collections and Archives, and our location at the Charleston Design Center – are open and waiting for you to visit them. We are at full capacity and no longer require seat reservations.As you are aware, the rise of the COVID-19 delta variant has proven a challenge as we return to in-person education and research. Your safety, as well as the safety of our employees, are of the utmost importance. As a result, you will need to do things when you visit us: Scan your TigerOne ID to enter our facilities. Wear a mask when inside our facilities Use the provided hand sanitizer and wipes at our locations Doing these things will help ensure you remain in good health to tackle the challenges that await on your Tiger journey. Many of you may not have visited the Libraries last year, either physically and virtually, or may be new to us entirely. What makes Clemson Libraries special? Here’s a short list: Over 800,000 books and other print materials available in the library, and over 1 million eBooks, journals, and streaming media titles available 24/7 through our Google-like search tool Access to research materials at libraries through PASCAL Delivers and across the world with interlibrary loan Unique research collections in our Special Collections and Archives, including rare books, manuscripts and papers from prominent alumni, and university records in areas of emphasis such as agriculture, architecture, athletics, textiles, politics, and the history of the university and community Librarians and other information experts who can help you with your research assignments and other projects Individual and group study spaces Technology – video cameras, mics, lighting kits, VR headsets, gaming consoles – that you can use to create high quality media projects Tailored digital media creation and making support at the Adobe Digital Studio and Cooper Makerspace Coffee and conversation at our Starbucks! And check out these new innovations for fall: Self-checkout app allows you to check out materials by scanning them with your phone at the shelf New carpeting and paint on Cooper fourth floor Coming Soon: The Digital Scholarship Lab, aiding in the use of research data and visualization for projects and presentations We encourage to explore all we have to offer as you discover, connect, and create. Have a great academic year and remember – we’re here to help. Go Tigers! Clemson University Libraries 116 Sigma Drive, Clemson, SC 29634 864-656-3027 Directory Employment Contact Us

HMNH.Public.Retail.1 Inspired by the vast collections in the museum, The Shop at HMNH offers a thoughtfully curated selection of world-class products designed to enhance your visit to the museum. From one-of-a-kind minerals and handcrafted jewelry to fun kits for the budding young scientist, you will find the perfect gift suited to any age and budget. Best of all, your purchase directly supports the mission and programs of the Harvard Museum of Natural History. Below is a sampling of products available. Want to see the full selection? Take a virtual tour of the shop. If you need more information on a specific item, please email shop@hmnh.harvard.edu. Purchases can be completed over the phone and will be shipped via UPS (domestic shipping only at this time; shipping charges are determined by weight and size). Please note: if buying more than one title or international shipping, please contact our shop at shop@hmnh.harvard.edu before making your purchase. Thank you for your continued support through this time!

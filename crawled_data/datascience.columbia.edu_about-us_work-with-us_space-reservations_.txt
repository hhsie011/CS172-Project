limited to academic activities, including meetings and discussion groups. As a courtesy to our community, users agree to return the room to its original condition, including cleaning up when necessary. Users should immediately report any room problems, concerns or questions to datascience@columbia.edu or (212) 854-5660. Mudd Building Conference rooms, which are equipped with laptop hookup for computer projection, whiteboard and conference call polycom, are available to DSI faculty, researchers, administration, and students. 417 Mudd (seating for 10) Reservations must be submitted to Sharnice Neale-Ottley at so2506@columbia.edu and are made on a first-come, first-served basis. Requests should include contact information, meeting purpose and expected number of attendees. Preference for these spaces is given to DSI faculty, researchers, and administration.

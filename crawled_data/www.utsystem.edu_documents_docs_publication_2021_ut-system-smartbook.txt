A PDF version of the SmartBook will be made available shortly. Produced by UT System's Office of Institutional Research and Analysis, the SmartBook provides a handy, by-the-numbers overview of the mission areas of UT System and all of its institutions. It also includes valuable data annually for UT students, faculty, and employees, as well as research/technology transfer and budget/finance data. SmartBook is used not only as an internal resource but by the public, as well. SmartBook provides insight into the following areas: Enrollment Student Demographic Profiles Cost of Attendance Financial Aid Undergraduate Retention and Graduation Rates Degrees and Certificates Awarded STEM Degrees Post-Graduation Earnings Faculty and Personnel Headcounts Research and Technology Transfer Budget Annual Giving / Endowment Patient Care UT System SmartBook Archive Cover Image Details Release Date Friday, April 22, 2022 Responsible Office(s) Institutional Research and Analysis Document Type Facts, Figures and Data Contact Website More Information?

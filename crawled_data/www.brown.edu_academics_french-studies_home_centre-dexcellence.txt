These centers receive recurring financial support and yearly project-based support to fund a wide variety of activities and research. Their mission is to integrate, support, and promote research and teaching connected with France. More information on the Brown French Center of Excellence, housed at the Cogut Institute for the Humanities can be found here: https://www.brown.edu/academics/humanities/center-of-excellence For a list of universities currently housing a Center of Excellence and other information about the structure of the Centers and their partnership with the Embassy, see: http://highereducation.frenchculture.org/centers-of-excellence Home Contact Information Office Hours News and Announcements Centre d'Excellence Rochambeau House Le kiosque d'informations Rochambeau (Le KIR) Current Events 2022-2023 Events 2021-2022 Events 2020-2021 Events 2019-2020 Events 2018-2019 Events 2017-2018 Events 2016-2017 Events 2015-2016 Events 2014-2015 Events 2013-2014 Events 2012-2013 Events Faculty and Staff Faculty Bookshelf Brown in France Language Placement Undergraduate Graduate Courses Resources Print this Page Share this Page Stay Connected with French and Francophone Studies Department of French and Francophone Studies Brown University Box Box 1961 84 Prospect Street Providence, RI 02912 Phone 401-863-3517 Fax 401-863-1669 french_francophone_studies @brown.edu Calendar Jun 2 All Day Registration period for Summer courses for Brown undergraduates re-opens at 9:00 a.m.EDT and remains open until Wednesday June 22 at 5:00 p.m. EDT Jun 19 All Day Residence halls open.

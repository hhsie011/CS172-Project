UH is committed to maintaining and strengthening an educational, working, and living environment where students, faculty, staff, and visitors are free from discrimination and harassment of any kind. Discrimination and harassment are antithetical to the standards and ideals of the University. The University will take appropriate action to eliminate discrimination and harassment from occurring, prevent its recurrence, and address its effects.

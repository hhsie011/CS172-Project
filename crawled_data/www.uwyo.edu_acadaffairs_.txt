Here is a list of the colleges, schools, and programs that report to the Office of Academic Affairs. The Office of Academic Affairs has the lead responsibility for academic planning and academic budget administration, academic personnel decisions and career development, university-wide curricular requirements and degree program development, assessment of student learning, and academic accreditation. The Provost and Associate Provosts work closely with academic deans and directors, faculty committees, and other divisions of the University on a broad array of policy and strategic planning issues.

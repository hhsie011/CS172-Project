BELKNAP #94 CARDINAL SHUTTLE (service provided by Transit Authority of River City (Tarc) and University Parking and Transportation. Monday - Thursday Service Begins at 6:40am at Cardinal Stadium and operates approximately every 10 minutes with the last trip leaving the SAC at 9:30pm. Friday Service Begins at 6:40am at Cardinal Stadium and operates approximately every 10 minutes with the last trip leaving the SAC at 7:30pm. With a current UofL Cardinal Card you may ride anywhere, anytime on TARC free of charge To locate TARC Routes and Times please click visit www.ridetarc.org Route 94 services the Papa Johns Cardinal Stadium bus shelter Persons parking at the stadium must have a valid UofL parking permit and park in the designated area All shuttles are wheelchair accessible and all TARC buses are bike rack-equipped Route 94 operates only when UofL classes are in session A fare applies to all non-student passengers Trains sometimes disrupt the schedule of TARC buses on the UofL campus Call University Police at 852-6111 for Escort Service to the stadium after 9:30pm Monday-Thursday and 7:30pm Friday Stops Papa John's Cardinal Stadium 2800 S Floyd Street Patterson Field 3015 S 3rd Street Engineering Graphics 2531 S 3rd Street Third Street and Eastern Parkway Ekstrom Library 2215 S 3rd Street Business School 110 W Brandeis Avenue Brook Street and Cardinal Blvd Floyd (SAC) Bus Stop 2126 S Floyd Street Thrust Theatre 2314 S Floyd Street Tarc Routes Serving University of Louisville (pdf) TARC Route #94 (pdf) © University of Louisville Contact Privacy Statement Apply Undergraduate Transfer Graduate Dentistry Law Medicine MBA International Online Learning Jobs Donate Emergency Login Email ULink PeopleSoft HR PeopleSoft Campus Solutions PeopleSoft Financials Blackboard Business Ops CardSmart CardBox Cards Career Connection Plone Contact Privacy Statement Apply Undergraduate Transfer Graduate Dentistry Law Medicine MBA International Online Learning Campuses Belknap HSC Online Jobs Donate Emergency Login Email ULink PeopleSoft HR PeopleSoft Campus Solutions PeopleSoft Financials Blackboard Business Ops CardSmart CardBox Cards Career Connection Plone © University of Louisville

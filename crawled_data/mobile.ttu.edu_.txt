Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index TTU Mobile Menu Search Texas Tech Mobile App TTU Mobile You can install Texas Tech Mobile by visiting either the iTunes or Google Play store on your mobile device and searching for Texas Tech Mobile. Once installed, you can select Texas Tech University from the "My Institution" link on the actions menu. Native Applications The Texas Tech University Mobile application can be used with the iPhone, iPad, and Android. iPhone: Android: Feedback Find a bug? Want to recommend a new feature? Your ideas and usage will help inform future development. Please send your feedback to ttumobile@ttu.edu. Features Athletics Never miss a home game – even when you're away. With Athletics, you can check schedules, scores, and keep up to date with your favorite sports in an instant. MyTech Search for open classes or check your registration status with the mobile version of MyTech! Directory Search by first and last name for phone numbers, email addresses, and office location for Texas Tech University students, faculty and staff. Dining Looking for a place to eat on campus? The Dining map will show you which places are open and where they are located. Campus Map Navigate around Texas Tech University's campus by searching for classroom buildings, houses, and offices. The map will display the location, and you can zoom in, zoom out or scroll in any direction. You can also browse locations by type, such as libraries or museums. Events Find out what's going on today at Texas Tech University or coming up soon. Events from the TTU calendar are available by category with the date and time, and location. Where available, you can click on the event's location to see it on the map. News Browse stories from TechAnnounce, the Daily Toreador newspaper, and Texas Tech Today to find the latest news about the Texas Tech community, arts and culture, and science and research. You can share articles using email, Facebook, or Twitter. Photos Browse or search your institution's photos. Save images to your iPhone or iPod Touch albums. Videos View YouTube videos posted by Texas Tech University including special events, athletics and student organizations. Library Browse the library catalog and check item availability on-the-go. Red Raider Orientation Have access to an event schedule and campus resources while visiting for orientation sessions. Get Help Be informed of critical information on the TTU campus and get easy access to important emergency contact information.

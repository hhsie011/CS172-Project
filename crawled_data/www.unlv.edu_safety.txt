Like any community, we are not immune to natural and man-made disasters. Faculty, staff, students and families must collaborate to plan and prepare for these types of emergencies. There are 4 steps we can take to #BeRebelSAFE. This event is over. Please check back soon for another special event. Be Informed Step 1: Be Informed UNLV has several tools to provide the campus community with important information during an emergency. Develop an Emergency Plan Step 2: Develop an Emergency Plan Planning in advance and knowing what you will do in different emergency situations is key to your safety. Build an Emergency Supply Kit Step 3: Build an Emergency Supply Kit Consider having a kit for your home, car, and/or residence hall, which contains basic survival supplies for at last 72 hours time. Get Involved Step 4: Get Involved There are several ways that you can be an emergency response leader on campus and at home in a time of crisis.

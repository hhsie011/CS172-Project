Recipients of the 2014 CMA/ASCAP Award for Adventurous Programming, Talea has brought to life more than 30 commissions of major new works since it was founded in 2008. www.taleaensemble.org We invite the audience to arrive at 7 pm for this 7:30 pm performance to allow time to read a new essay about the work by the critic Paul Griffiths (formerly of The New Yorker and The New York Times).

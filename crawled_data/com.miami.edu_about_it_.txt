for: About Information Technology About Information Technologybrackman2021-08-24T11:24:41-04:00 University of Miami School of Communication stays at the forefront of cutting-edge technology in its world-class facilities. Students have access to a wide array of technological resources, ranging from state-of-the-art computer labs to high-end professional equipment. The school prides itself in its ability to transform the lives of students through technology that prepares them for jobs in the industry upon graduation. The technology in the school is supported by the SoC Support group, comprised of the Office of Information Technology and the Office of Facility Operations and Engineering. SoC Support provides professional help to students, faculty, and staff at the School of Communication. Our goal is to provide effective and efficient IT services to assist the School of Communication in achieving its strategic goals. Services include: Classroom and computer lab support Software installation and troubleshooting Hardware installation and troubleshooting A/V support Website support Server administration Hardware and

Engineering Sciences Minors Students can add engineering sciences to their liberal arts studies in a variety of ways—gaining the analytical, creative, and communications skills needed to succeed and lead in any field. On This Page Options Request Info Visit Apply Options Engineering Sciences Minor Students can choose to complement a major in another discipline with a minor in engineering sciences. Learn More Human-Centered Design Minor The minor in human-centered design is an interdisciplinary program focused on the process of innovation for addressing human needs. The six-course minor combines engineering and design-thinking courses with a wide range of cultural, behavioral, and artistic offerings. Learn More Materials Science Minor The departments of Chemistry, Physics, and Engineering Sciences offer the minor in materials science, which can be combined with majors in any of the three areas.

Continue straight on Memorial Boulevard. At the fourth set of lights, turn left onto College Street. At the top of the hill, turn left onto Prospect Street. Take your first right onto Waterman Street. At the third light, turn left onto Brook Street. A visitor parking lot will be two blocks up on your left. This will be within walking distance to the Granoff Center. From the East on Interstate 195 Take Exit 2 to South Main Street and continue for half a mile until the first set of traffic lights. Turn right onto College Street and then left at the top of the hill onto Prospect Street. Take your first right onto Waterman Street. At the third light, turn left onto Brook Street. A visitor parking lot will be two blocks up on your left. This will be in close walking distance to the Granoff Center. From the Northwest Follow Route 146 to I-95 South then follow directions above (from north and south). Visitor Parking There is on-street metered parking in the area, ranging from two- to four-hour limits. There is unmetered parking in the surrounding neighborhood off of Brown Street (View Map).

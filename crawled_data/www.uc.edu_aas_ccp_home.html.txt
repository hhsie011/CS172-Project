Updates Recent legislative updates (ORC33.65.035) require that familes be informed of the following: The subject matter of a course enrolled in under the College Credit Plus program may include mature subject matter or materials, including those of a graphic, explicit, violent, or sexual nature, that will not be modified based upon College Credit Plus enrollee participation regardless of where course instruction occurs. More information will be discussed in CCP orientation and advising meetings. Please email ccp@uc.edu with questions.

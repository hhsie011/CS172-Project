Skip to main content Skip to primary site menu Google Tag Manager Dr. Hamish Fraser is Elected as an IAHSI 2018/2019 Fellow Dr. Hamish Fraser was recently elected as a 2018/2019 Fellow for IAHSI, International Academy of Health Sciences Informatics (The Academy)! The Academy serves as an honor society that recognizes expertise in biomedical and health informatics internationally. Moreover, The Academy plays an important role in exchanging knowledge, providing education and training, and producing policy documents. Membership is considered one of the highest honors in the international field of biomedical and health informatics, and BCBI is proud to congratulate Dr. Fraser on this prestigious honor.

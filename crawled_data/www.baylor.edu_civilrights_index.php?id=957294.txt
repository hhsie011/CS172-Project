The Diversity Council will support the University as it recruits, mentors, and retains diverse and thriving faculty, staff, and students. The President’s Diversity Council will continue to enhance and cultivate the Baylor experience where faculty, staff, and students are safe, welcomed, supported, and valued by the University and each other. Key Objectives: Campus Climate Cultural awareness and appreciation Safe space for intergroup dialogue Education and training on cultural engagement Hiring Recruitment, training, and retention of faculty and staff Support from Senior Administration Onboard and transition support Support Networking opportunities Resources Community Mentoring Personal and professional development Mentoring opportunities Collaboration and integration The goals of the President's Diversity Council are to: Advise the University administration on issues affecting diverse faculty, staff, and students Represent the interests and concerns of all diverse faculty, staff, and students in the university community Assess and monitor the institution’s performance in regard to representation and advancement of diverse faculty, staff, and students Assist and advocate in the development of policies and practices that promote the equitable participation and treatment of all employees and students President's Diversity Council Mission and Goals Executive Committee Members Equity Office Robinson Tower, Suite 285 One Bear Place #97011 Waco, TX 76798 (254) 710-7100 Climate Survey Student Live Diversity & Inclusion Faculty Development and Diversity General Information Colleges, Schools, Libraries & Research Admissions Administration Gateways For...

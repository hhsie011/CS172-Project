Baylor Parent Fund Whether your student is attending, has graduated or is soon to become a Baylor Bear, the Baylor Parent Fund supports those areas that enhance the overall student experience. Library Excellence Fund Gifts help enrich the student experience by providing technology updates, securing research materials and improving library study areas. Parents Endowed Scholarship Your support helps provide scholarship support for deserving students who need financial assistance to continue their pursuit of a Baylor education. Student Emergency Fund This unique fund assists students who may experience an unexpected expense, such as a car repair, medical expense or natural disasters that would prevent them from completing the current semester. Student Life Excellence Fund Gifts support students traveling to conferences and help student organizations create innovative campus programs.

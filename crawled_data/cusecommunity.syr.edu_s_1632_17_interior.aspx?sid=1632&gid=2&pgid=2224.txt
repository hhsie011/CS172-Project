From New Student Send-Offs and Syracuse Welcome when you’re first-year students to professional development events once you've graduated, the Office of Alumni Engagement is with you throughout your Syracuse experience. Interested in getting involved? The Forever Orange Student Alumni Council (FOSAC) is a student organization that celebrates the Orange spirit that is unique to Syracuse University and its students. FOSAC educates students about the importance of philanthropy and stewardship, fosters student-alumni connections and encourages service to the University and surrounding community. FOSAC hosts events throughout the year to celebrate what it means to be forever Orange. It’s never too soon to start making connections with an Orange network that is 240,000 members strong, and we partner with Career Services to help you to do just that. Soon-to-be graduates, as you prepare to join the Orange Alumni Family and Generation Orange, find more information on our alumni clubs across the country—they're our partners in making sure you remain forever Orange.

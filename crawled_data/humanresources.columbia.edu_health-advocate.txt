Personal Health Advocates are nurses with more than 10 years of experience in clinical care and case management; they are benefits experts with more than five years of experience in benefits administration and claims management; and they are trained clinical professionals (e.g., social workers, behavior change counselors, nutritionists, dieticians, dental care and pharmacy management). Phone: 866-799-2725 Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 11:00 p.m. (You can also contact Health Advocate 24 hours a day, seven days a week to get started.) Fight the Flu Early fall is the time when flu activity generally starts.

http://a.cms.omniupdate.com/servlet/OX/csufullerton/ob.gif?user=CBE&site=MihayloPublic&date=1639693373628 Master of Science in Information Systems Change to or advance in your information systems career through the Master of Science (MS) in Information Systems. Offered on the Fullerton campus, this STEM-designated program is designed for students who have an undergraduate degree in business administration. Students with a bachelor’s degree in another field may also apply but will need to satisfy additional requirements. Fullerton campus Fall and spring admission Faculty Support Key Features: Choose from a variety of concentrations: generalist, business analytics or decision sciences. Gain the knowledge and skills you need to assist organizations with their computer-based systems. Enhance your written and oral communication skills. Curriculum As an MS in information systems student, you will be required to complete 30 units of coursework with a minimum GPA of 3.0. At least 21 of the 30 units must be at the 500 level. Additional prerequisite coursework may be required. Please visit the University Catalog for course descriptions and prerequisites for the business analytics concentration and the IS management concentration.

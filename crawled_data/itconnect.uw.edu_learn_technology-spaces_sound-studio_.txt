Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Odegaard Library Sound Studio Note: Many on-campus study spaces are currently closed due to COVID-19. For information about the status of spaces, check with departmental websites or contact the building’s facilities staff. The Odegaard Library Sound Studio is an on-campus media lab that contains high-end, professional tools for recording and editing audio. The Sound Studio’s professional, industry-standard hardware and software can take any project, no matter the scale. The Sound Studio is free to use for all students, faculty, and staff of the University of Washington and can be reserved on a first come, first serve basis. If you are unfamiliar with the equipment or would like to learn more about the Sound Studio, please attend one of the offered workshops.

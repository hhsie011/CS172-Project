Meet Our Faculty Rob Krier named 2022 Richard H. Driehaus Prize laureate Rob Krier, an architect, urbanist, scholar, painter, sculptor and educator, has been named the recipient of the 2022 Richard H. Driehaus Prize at the University of Notre Dame. In conjunction with the Driehaus Prize, the Henry Hope Reed Award will be presented to Wendell Berry, an American poet, novelist, cultural critic, environmentalist and farmer, for his contributions to the discourse about nature and the city.

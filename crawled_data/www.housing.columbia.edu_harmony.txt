Located on 110th Street, the residence hall is a short walk to campus and surrounded by neighborhood restaurants and shops. Data from Room Selection 2021 shows the make-up of this building as 44% juniors, 38% sophomores, 16% seniors and 2% mixed-point groups (average point value of mixed-point groups was 19). Please keep in mind that class make-up in each building varies year to year based on student preferences. This should not be used as predictive data for the upcoming academic year.

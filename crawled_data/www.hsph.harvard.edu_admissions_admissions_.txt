Chan School of Public Health > Admissions > Apply Apply As part of Harvard University—the top-ranked Global University for Social Science and Public Health—and one of the top schools of public health in the nation, the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health actively recruits bright, motivated, and collaborative students like you, whose talent is matched by a commitment to building healthier communities around the world. We encourage you to apply whether you are a recent college graduate just beginning your immersion in the field or an experienced health care professional, researcher, or administrator who wants to deepen your impact and pursue new levels of leadership. Our rigorous programs, world-renowned faculty, and collaborative community will prepare you for whatever public health challenges you wish to tackle. Take the Next Step We look forward to learning more about your goals and helping you select the Harvard Chan degree best suited to your needs.

Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Global China Connection - Columbia University, a student-run organization for Chinese undergraduate students, will host an in-person Q&A and information session for new Columbia undergraduates. Current undergraduate students will join the event and share their campus experiences, life in New York City, Columbia resources, etc. There will be breakout sessions in groups and a social mixer to answer any questions you may have. The Beijing Center and GCC Columbia will also host a welcome reception for the Columbia Class of 2025 in July. Please stay tuned for further updates!

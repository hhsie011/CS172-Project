Jun Ke Doctorate Candidate Jun Ke, PhD candidate in biostatistics, recently successfully defended his dissertation, titled “Spatial and Temporal Correlation Analysis with an Application to fMRI Data.” Understanding that brain regions can show similar patterns of activation event when they are spatially seperated, Ke's dissertation focuses on characterizing the temporal similarity between the brain regions.

California Syracuse University Alumni Club of Southern California (SoCal Orange) SoCal Orange is one place in the Los Angeles area where Syracuse University spirit really rings strong. Visit the events page for more information about what’s coming up, then follow the club on Twitter and Facebook for the latest news and updates. The club is always looking for more volunteers too! We welcome any and all who want to join the board—whether in a leadership position or as an at-large member. Contact us at pres@socalorange.org to learn about our committee and board needs, coming events, and other ways to get involved with Syracuse University in the Southern California area. We hope to see you soon!

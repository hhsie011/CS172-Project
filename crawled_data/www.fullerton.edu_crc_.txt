We foster a sense of belonging while supporting the intellectual, personal, and social development of our students. Who We Are The LCRC serves as a home away from home. We provide a welcoming space to study, relax and build community. The space serves as a place to connect with fellow students, student organizations, staff, faculty, and resources on campus.

The Lenfest Center for the Arts is located at 615 West 129th Street, between Broadway and 12th Avenue, on Columbia University’s Manhattanville campus. The Neighborhood The Lenfest Center for the Arts is proud to join the community of vibrant arts and cultural institutions in West Harlem. Directions The Lenfest Center for the Arts is accessible by the 125th Street stop on the 1 line, the 125th Street stop on the A, C B, and D lines, as well as the M4 and M5 buses. Trip planning information from the MTA is also available online: MTA Trip Planner Get Directions Accessibility Columbia University makes every effort to accommodate individuals with disabilities.

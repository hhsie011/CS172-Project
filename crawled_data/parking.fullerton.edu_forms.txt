Online forms available to employees and students Form Description Contractors, Maintenance/Service Vendors Permit Request To be used for contractors, maintenance/service vendors needing a parking permit for work. CSU Reciprocity Application Apply for a CSU Reciprocity Parking Permit as a CSUEU-represented employee employed at two or more campuses. Declaration of Lost or Stolen Permit To be used if the permit holder loses their permit or it has been stolen. Employee Bus Pass (E-Pass) Faculty and Staff members who do not own a Parking permit may fill out an Employee Bus Pass (E-Pass) application to receive a free bus pass Employee Carpool Permit Application To be used by two or more employees commuting together. Event Permits Requesting campus department may order and pay through OPR Faculty/Staff EV Permit Application Employees may fill out an Electric Vehicle (EV) Permit Application to receive a supplemental EV Permit. Online Permit Request (OPR) Form (login) Faculty/Staff may request daily parking permits for visitors and guests. Requests should be made 24 hours in advance for paid permits, and 72 business hours in advance for complimentary permits. Parking Permit Refund Semester permit holders can request a prorated refund according to the employee proration schedule and student proration schedule . Refunds will be processed once the permit is received by P&TS staff. Payroll Deduction Cancellation Form To be used by Faculty/Staff to cancel their current Payroll Deduction Parking Permit. Cancellations will be processed once the permit is received by P&TS staff. Replacement of Lost Permit To be used if the permit purchased online is not received in the mail and the temporary permit has expired. Special Permit Application Valid in 30 minutes zones, special permit zones and parking structures. Division Head approval is required. Student Carpool Permit Application To be used by two or more students commuting together. Student EV Permit Application Students may fill out an Electric Vehicle (EV) Permit Application to receive a supplemental EV Permit. Travel Direct Deposit Form To be used for Faculty/Staff in the Commuter Rewards Program.

Brock Memorial Library: Home Official Website Databases and Full Text Journals PubMed Has more than 23 million citations for biomedical literature and life science journals. CAB Direct A comprehensive applied life science database that incorporates CAB Abstracts and Global Health. CAB Direct indexes literature in veterinary sciences, including journal articles, proceedings, book chapters, and other grey literature. Embase Find biomedical articles from 1947 to the present covering topics such as pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and clinical medicine. Science Direct A large bibliographic database of scientific and medical publications of the publisher Elsevier. Scopus Elsevier's Scopus, a large abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed literature; multi-disciplinary. AGRICOLA The Agricola database is produced by the National Agricultural Library that indexes literature of animal and veterinary sciences, plant sciences, among other agricultural-related subjects. TOXLINE in PubMed Collection of bibliographic information covering the biochemical, pharmacological, physiological, and toxicological effects of drugs and other chemicals. Animal Welfare Act History Digital Collection A full-text, searchable collection that is focused on the history of the Animal Welfare Act (AWA) TRIP (Turning Research Into Practice) Database A free medical search engine with a focus on evidence-based medicine (EBM) content. The Merck Veterinary Manual The Merck Veterinary Manual is a well-established conventional textbook on general animal health.

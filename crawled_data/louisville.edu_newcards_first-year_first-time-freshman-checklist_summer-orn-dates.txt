Please refer to this chart when planning your orientation attendance dates, and be prepared with up to three session choices (in preference order) before logging in to complete your registration. Information in this chart is subject to change, and does not guarantee placement in a specific session. Do not make travel arrangements to campus until you receive confirmation of your orientation dates. If an academic unit has reached capacity for a specific session, the word "FULL" will appear next to the date. Note: Non-honors students in the following majors: Undecided Arts and Sciences, Pre-business, Pre-dental hygiene, Pre-music, Pre-nursing, Pre-social work, Pre-engineering should select "Arts and Sciences Exploratory."

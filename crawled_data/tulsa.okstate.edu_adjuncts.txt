The administration at OSU-Tulsa has created this website for adjunct and part-time faculty. In it, you will find information about administrative details, as well as links to academic resources available to all faculty at OSU. This website is intended to be a collaborative effort. Please contact the Office of Academic Affairs in Tulsa if you have suggestions or comments regarding the site or information provided.

Continuing Education programs offer professionals an opportunity to stay apprised of the latest diagnostic and therapeutic techniques and management recommendations available to the profession. The College of Veterinary Medicine has a long history of offering face-to-face conferences and seminars, and recently launched an online continuing education platform that will allow greater access to continuing education for professionals. The online platform allowed veterinary professionals access to continuing education throughout the recent Covid-19 pandemic.

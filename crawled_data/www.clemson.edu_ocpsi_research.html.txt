University faculty members operate self-sustaining centers and perform cutting-edge research. The Office of Corporate Partnerships and Strategic Initiatives is committed to maintaining a strong relationship with corporate partners to further create research opportunities for our premier staff. This collaborative approach allows corporate partners to engage at multiple levels within the University. Centers and Institutes Office of Research Technology Licensing Whether your vision is still an idea or a finished product, the Clemson University Research Foundation (CURF) provides the expertise and machinery needed to propel your innovative technology from the laboratory into commercial markets. CURF works with external stakeholders on everything from patent protection to license negotiation. Clemson University Research Foundation Integrated Research and Teaching Creative Inquiry is Clemson's premier program to engage with students in undergraduate research across the seven colleges. Students are welcome to join projects that pique their academic curiosity while interacting with external partners in South Carolina and beyond.

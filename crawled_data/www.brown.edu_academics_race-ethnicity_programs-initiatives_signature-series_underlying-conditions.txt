conversation series that engages various experts on the dynamic and multifaceted impact of COVID-19 on already vulnerable communities of color. Although the phrase “underlying conditions” usually refers specifically to chronic, health-related risks, we are using it more broadly to capture elevated and underlying risks of many kinds.

Health Promotion Rest & Restore: Using Purpose to Create Spring Break That Really Feels Like A Break Have you had difficulty getting space from your academics and the semester? Does it ever feel hard to turn off or slow your mind down from your days or even your weeks? While these experiences may be common all the time, they may be especially amplified by the pandemic and virtual environments. Join Alice! Health Promotion and your peers for a conversation and some strategies to help set you up for a Spring Break that is authentically restful and restorative.

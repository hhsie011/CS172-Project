We look forward to hearing from you and connecting! 1,546 Alumni Parents & Friends Social Leaders Contact Us Events Follow Us Phoenix Chapter Facebook Page Meet Your Chapter Leaders Jim and Adriana Gruber '98 Lives in: Scottsdale, AZ Major: Jim, Economics and Marketing ; Adriana, Marketing Occupation: Jim, Lawyer; Adriana, CEO of Three Kids Age 10 and Under Favorite BC Memory: Rushing the field at the 1994 Notre Dame Football Game and our wedding proposal at BC outside of Bapst Library. Why did you get involved in your local chapter?: We had great experiences at BC and thought it would be fun to reconnect with fellow Eagles from all different classes who are here in Phoenix. We also wanted to continue the great lagacy of the BC Phoenix Chapter that began under the found of the chapter, Marty Ridge. What are your local favorites in Phoenix?: Hiking Papago Park, Pizza at Cibo, Breakfast at Matt's Big Breakfast and cheering on the original pro sports team in Phoenix, the Phoenix Suns. Fun Fact: We are both Arizon natives! Adriana is a second generation AZ native. This is pretty unusual for a state that is filled with transplants.

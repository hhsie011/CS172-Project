invite you to join us for our spring Beyond the Page public programming series. Whether you’re an undergraduate or graduate student, researcher, local Philadelphian, faculty or staff member, we have something for you! How do we imagine and shape the future? This semester, we explore the question What Comes Next? Whether it be new horizons for academia and our university, research trends in specific disciplines, or the ongoing fight against COVID-19, we’ll come together—both virtually and in person—to look toward the future. The semester will also feature the other events, concerts, and workshops you’ve come to expect from Beyond the Page. Visit our events page for details about how individual programs will be presented. Sign up for our mailing list to receive the latest library news and updates. Have ideas for future programs? Let us know! Most library events are online this semester.

Buffett and William B. Eimicke In Social Value Investing, Howard W. Buffett and William B. Eimicke offer practical insights for any private sector CEO, public sector administrator, or nonprofit manager hoping to build successful cross-sector partnerships. Buffett, adjunct associate professor, and Eimicke, professor of professional practice, both at the School of International and Public Affairs, tell the stories of partnerships, including those that transformed Central Park and created the High Line in New York City, fostered community-led economic development in Afghanistan, and improved public services in cities across Brazil.

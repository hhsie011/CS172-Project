Holidays in Workday Integrated Service Center Your Benefits Changing Your Benefits Changing Your Benefits Last updated Tuesday, January 25, 2022 Most benefit elections* can be changed under a particular set of circumstances, or at a given point in time. If you are currently enrolled in PEBB benefits, select a link below to learn more. (If you are new to the UW or are starting in a benefits-eligible position, please visit the New/Newly-Eligible Employees: Enrolling in Benefits – Start Here! page instead.) Life Events You can change your medical and/or dental coverage following a child’s birth or adoption, a marriage or domestic partnership, moving to a different zip code, and more. tell me more Open Enrollment Open Enrollment (November 1-30) is a once-a-year opportunity for PEBB benefits-eligible employees to enroll in or make changes to their medical and/or dental plan elections in Workday. tell me more Changes You Can Make Year-Round Some changes can be made year-round, such as changing your Tobacco Use attestation, starting/stopping/changing HSA contributions, or enrolling in optional insurance plans. tell me more *Your choice of primary retirement plan cannot be changed. If you meet the eligibility criteria, you must participate in a retirement plan as a condition of employment. Other Benefits Many benefits you receive as a UW employee - such as paid holidays and your UPASS - do not involve enrollment via Workday. For details, refer to the "Other Benefits" listed in UWHR's Benefit Summary PDFs.

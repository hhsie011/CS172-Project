The DPM curriculum fully prepares students for post-graduate residency training. DPM/MPH In collaboration with the College of Health Professions and Social Work at Temple University, the School of Podiatric offers a joint program that leads to the Master of Public Health and Doctor of Podiatric Medicine degrees. These affiliated-degree programs allow students to earn two advanced degrees in five years. Summer and Winter Internships Undergraduate students can learn about a future career in podiatry by participating in a TUSPM internship program.

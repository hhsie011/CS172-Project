Student Line Up Grad Plaza | Walk to the left once inside the check-in area. Staff will be on hand to direct you and your guests. Ceremony Location Louis Armstrong Stadium in the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center Ceremony Timing There will be one or two ceremonies based on graduate registration. We will fill the afternoon ceremony first. If/when we move past the threshold for one ceremony we will add a morning ceremony. Tickets Guests are required to have a ticket. Graduates may register for up to 8 guest tickets. Guests cannot print tickets for scanning at the stadium. They must be scanned on a smartphone. TICKET REGISTRATION IS NOW CLOSED Schedule Afternoon Ceremony - Convocation 2020-2021 (all departments) 2:45 p.m.: Arrive at Louis Armstrong Stadium wearing academic attire. Pick up name card Assemble with department and line up. Grads will not line up alphabetically or by program so plan to arrive with your friends. 3:30 p.m.: The student procession will move to the stadium. 3:45 p.m.: Process to student seating. 4:00 p.m.: Program begins. During the program, each masters grad will walk across the stage as their name is read and doctoral grads will be hooded on stage as their names are read. A professional photographer will capture all these moments.

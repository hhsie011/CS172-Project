Bike Storage in the Residence Halls Broadway/Hogan: 1st floor hallway Carlton Arms: 1st floor East Campus: Courtyard Furnald Hall: Basement Harmony: Basement Ruggles: Basement (exterior) Woodbridge: Basement Campus Bike Racks Bike racks are available to the Columbia community outside various residence halls and academic buildings. However, outside bike racks may not be used for long term storage. Bicycles secured with locks to railings not intended for bike storage, stairwells, etc. will be secured by Public Safety with an additional lock requiring the owner to report to Public Safety to have the bike unlocked. Please review the University Policy on Bike Storage for full details. Additional Resources Please also check out the resources on Columbia Transportation's website, which include bike sharing, outdoor bike rack maps, commuter bike enclosure information, bike security engraving services, and more.

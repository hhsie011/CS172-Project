SACSCOC accreditation is essential to the university. In addition to approval for the receipt of federal funding, accreditation demonstrates our commitment to providing excellent higher education and our desire to continuously improve the quality of the institution. Florida State University affirms its commitment to student success, research and creative achievement, and the quality of services that support our mission. Jim Clark, Provost Sidebar SACSCOC Reaffirmation The Commission on Colleges reaffirmed the university’s accreditation in December 2014. This is the most recent reaffirmation of Florida State University’s accreditation by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges.

Johnson Auditorium We look forward to celebrating our graduates on Saturday, May 21. REGISTRATION The online RSVP closed on April 25. LOCATION The LBJ Auditorium is located on the lower level of the LBJ complex at 2313 Red River St. Access to the Auditorium will be through the lobby of the LBJ School of Public Affairs. The ceremony will also be livestreamed on the Texas Architecture YouTube channel. TICKETS Seating is limited. All guests must have a ticket to enter the LBJ Auditorium. Ticket pick-up will be available May 9 – 20 from 8:00 am – 4:00 pm at the Office of the Dean reception desk. We will distribute five free tickets to each student participating in the ceremony. Ticket pick-up will be available at the Office of the Dean reception desk. Each student must bring their ID to pick up their tickets. If you do not need all of your tickets, you may share them with other students or return them to the reception desk. If you need more than five tickets, feel free ask your fellow students. Unclaimed tickets will be available on a first-come, first-serve basis at Will Call on the day of the ceremony. REGALIA Regalia is required to participate and may be purchased from the University Co-op. The color for the School of Architecture is lilac. ACCESSIBILITY Please contact Maria Carlsen if a graduate or guest requires special accommodations. While every effort will be made to accommodate, LBJ Auditorium may be unable to meet last-minute requests. RECEPTION A reception honoring graduates and their guests will immediately follow the ceremony on the Lyndon Baines Johnson Library and Museum Plaza. EVENING COMMENCEMENT Please join Dean Michelle Addington at the 139th Spring University-wide Commencement for the ceremony and fireworks celebration. PARKING Free parking will be available in all university parking garages and on most surface lots. First come, first serve parking is available in Lots 37-39. If surface parking becomes full, guests are encouraged to park in the Manor Garage (MAG), San Jacinto Garage (SJG), or any of the other seven university parking garages. SECURITY INFORMATION Food, drinks, and large purses and bags are not permitted in the LBJ Auditorium. THE GREAT TEXAS EXIT Graduates and their guests are invited to take part in The Great Texas Exit at the Etter-Harbin Alumni Center. DIPLOMAS Texas One Stop mails diplomas to the student's permanent address approximately six weeks after degrees are certified. ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS? Contact the School of Architecture's reception desk at (512) 471-1922.

These campuses and centers provide specialized facilities and services that create an environment where Clemson's faculty, staff and students can interact with researchers from our private-industry partners to create new knowledge and new ways of using knowledge. Research is also conducted at the Public Service Activities' five Research and Education Centers (RECs) strategically located in the state's distinct soil and climate regions.

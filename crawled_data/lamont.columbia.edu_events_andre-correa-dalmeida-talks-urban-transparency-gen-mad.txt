Events Past Event Andre Correa d’Almeida Talks Urban Transparency With Gen M.A.D. Share Add to Calendar: Calendar / Outlook Google Yahoo February 16, 2022 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM America/New_York Online Sustain What? Andre Correa d’Almeida Talks Urban Transparency With Generation M.A.D. Generation M.A.D. takes over the Columbia Climate School’s Sustain What webcast for a conversation with Andre Correa d’Almeida, an expert in urban innovation and municipal transparency. D’Almeida is an adjunct associate professor at both the School of International and Public Affairs (SIPA) and the Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation (GSAPP), Columbia University. Additionally, D’Almeida is the founding President of All4Integrity, a non-profit organization created in response to the #FreeMyCountryFromCorruption movement that he launched in 2020. Join us for a discussion on bringing transparency, innovation, and innovation to the cities of the present and future.

The BCGM features a wide variety of geological and paleontological exhibits and is an invaluable resource for science students, teachers, researchers, and anybody interested in exploring Earth history. The Campbell Geology Museum is open Monday - Sunday from 10am-5pm (closed on Clemson University official holidays). The museum will be closed on Monday May 30th. Admission is FREE - Donations are encouraged and greatly appreciated. Parking is FREE and plentiful (CU parking permit NOT required).

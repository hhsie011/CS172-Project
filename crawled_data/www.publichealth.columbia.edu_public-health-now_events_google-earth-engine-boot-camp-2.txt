Event Kind: Training Organizer: Department of Environmental Health Sciences SHARP Invitation Limited to: Open to the Public Website: Learn More Learn More Join us for the next live-stream Google Earth Engine Boot Camp on June 6-7, 2022. The Google Earth Engine Booth Camp is a two-day intensive training workshop that includes seminars and hands-on case studies to provide an overview of concepts, techniques, applications, and data analysis methods for using the Google Earth Engine to estimate environmental exposures for health research. Investigators at all career stages are welcome to attend, and we particularly encourage trainees and early-stage investigators to participate.

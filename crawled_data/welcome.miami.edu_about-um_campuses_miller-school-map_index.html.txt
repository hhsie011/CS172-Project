From The South Exit I-95 onto S.R. 836 West. Stay right to exit onto N.W. 12th Avenue. Proceed north on N.W. 12th Avenue, entering the medical center complex at N.W. 16th Street. From the North Exit I-95 onto S.R. 836 West. Stay right and take the first exit onto N.W. 14th Street. Proceed to your selected parking site. Driving from S.R. 836, and Points West (Including Miami International Airport) Take S.R. 836 East to exit at N.W. 17th Avenue North (Civic Center/Hospitals). On exit ramp, bear left to N.W. 17th Avenue, turn right, cross the bridge, and turn right at N.W. North River Drive. Make the first left turn at N.W. 13th Terrace, then bear right onto N.W. 14th Street and proceed to N.W. 12th Avenue and to your selected parking site.

We identify and respond to changing needs and help implement creative solutions that enable the University to attract and retain an exceptional workforce. While many schools and departments have their own HR offices, Central HR serves the entire Columbia community. We provide a full range of services, including recruiting, interviewing, placing and guiding employees; maintaining benefits and payroll; mediating conflict; hosting trainings; and, offering learning and development programs. Working collaboratively, we provide expertise and strategic solutions in all areas of human resource management in support of the University’s mission.

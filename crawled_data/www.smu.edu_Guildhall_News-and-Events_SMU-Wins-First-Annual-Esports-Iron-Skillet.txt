The teams competed in League of Legends, Overwatch, Valorant, CS:GO, and Rocket League — all streamed live at http://twitch.tv/smu_esports. SMU came out victorious and took home the year one iron skillet trophy. Results were as follows: League of Legends: SMU 2 - TCU 0 Overwatch: SMU 3 - TCU 0 Valorant: SMU 0 - TCU 2 CS:GO: SMU 2 - TCU 0 Rocket League: SMU 5 - TCU 4 A live audience on site was able to watch the competition from Ford Hall's Visualization Lab and atrium, and game casters simultaneously streamed from the Ford Hall game steaming booth.

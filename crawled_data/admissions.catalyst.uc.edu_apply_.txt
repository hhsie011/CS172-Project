This is an Undergraduate Application. If you are trying to apply to Graduate School, please visit their Admissions website. If you already have a prior application on file for a future or previous term, please contact the Office of Admissions to verify if a new application is necessary. First, log in or create an account below. Once you are logged in, select the relevant application: If you're applying as a first-year student at a regional campus or a transfer student at any campus, choose "Application". If you’re currently an Ohio student who is eligible and interested in taking courses through College Credit Plus, either at your school or at a UC campus, choose “College Credit Plus". Returning users:Log in to continue an application. First-time users:Create an account to start a new application.

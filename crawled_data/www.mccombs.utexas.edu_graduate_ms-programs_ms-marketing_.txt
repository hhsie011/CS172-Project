Science in Marketing The MSM is a 10-month, STEM-eligible degree that combines a traditional marketing education with cutting-edge data analytics training. Sign Up for More Information Learn More Speak with Admissions Attend an Info Session View MSM Factsheet Earn an In-Demand Degree in 10 Months The MSM is a pivotal degree that teaches applied analytical training. As a program grounded in marketing analytics, we’ll prepare you for a marketing career in a new economy – a world where data, decisions, and action are intertwined. You’ll graduate with the qualitative and quantitative skills necessary to transform data, gather insights, and develop meaningful business solutions. Learn Practical Skills from Top Academics The MSM program offers small, cohort-style classes taught by leading faculty. Our curriculum was developed by practicing marketing managers, and all MSM students get hands-on experience in a course where you’ll work with real businesses dealing with current issues.

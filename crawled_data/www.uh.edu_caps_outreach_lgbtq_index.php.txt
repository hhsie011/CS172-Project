All discussion groups are free and open to current students. The group will start this summer. Fill out the interest form to receive information. This space is dedicated to promote students' well-being, receive/offer support, and discuss topics with other LGBTQIA+ students relevant to their identities and experiences. We advocate for a respectful and free exchange of ideas and feelings, recognizing that each of us has our own story and that there is value in all of our experiences. To learn more, please fill out the interest form or contact Staci Ouch directly at souch@central.uh.edu.

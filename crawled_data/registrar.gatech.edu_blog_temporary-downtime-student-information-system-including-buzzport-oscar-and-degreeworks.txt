Temporary Downtime for Student Information System, including BuzzPort, OSCAR, and DegreeWorks Submitted by David Barbero on Thursday, March 12, 2020 Scheduled Service Maintenance for Banner Production WHAT IS HAPPENING? All of Banner Production (i.e.: Banner 9 Administrative Pages, SSB, Job Submission) and ancillary system interfaces (Global ECS interface to Banner, TouchNet, GradesFirst, Canvas, TSquare, Grade Adapter, DegreeWorks, etc.) will be unavailable on Thursday, March 12th, between 8:00 PM and 10:00 PM so that Financial Aid and Georgia Enhancement modules may be applied to the system. All jobs scheduled to be run via Global ECS will be held starting at 4:00 PM. Jobs will be released to run after the upgrade unless departments notify EIS to reschedule them. WHEN IS IT HAPPENING? The maintenance will occur between 8:00 PM and approximately 10:00 PM on Thursday, March 12th WHO IS AFFECTED BY THIS INTERRUPTION? All of Banner and ancillary systems that interface to Banner production are affected by this downtime. WHAT DO YOU NEED TO DO? Nothing. EIS will restart all applications, interfaces and services upon conclusion of the maintenance.

Learn More News & Events 04 Feb Events George Van Amson Fellowship Information Session February 4, 2021 The George Van Amson (GVA) Fellowship provides support for Columbia University and Barnard College undergraduates to pursue an unpaid nonprofit or service learning fellowship during the summer. Fellows become part of a larger community committed to social justice, civic engagement, and service learning. Funded by Community Impact, Fellows receive a $3,000 stipend and summer housing. For more information about the Fellowship, click here. Information Session: Thursday, February 4, 5-5:45pm ET over Zoom.

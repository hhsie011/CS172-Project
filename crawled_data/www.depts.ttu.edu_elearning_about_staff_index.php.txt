Her responsibilities include the oversight of the regional teaching sites and the Center at Junction, online program compliance and quality, Osher Lifelong Learning Institute (OLLI), continuing education, Academic Partnerships, and Texas Tech University K-12 (TTU K-12). More » Justin R. Louder, Ed.D. Associate Vice Provost & Interim Superintendent Justin R. Louder, Ed.D., is Associate Vice Provost for Texas Tech University eLearning & Academic Partnerships. Dr. Louder previously served as the institution's Assistant Vice Provost for eLearning, where he was the director of the Office of Online Compliance & Regulation. More » Clay Taylor, Ed.D. Assistant Vice Provost Clay Taylor, Ed.D. serves as the Assistant Vice Provost for Texas Tech eLearning & Academic Partnerships. In this role, he has direct oversight and responsibilities for the Office of Continuing & Professional Education, the Office of Academic Agreements, TTU OnRamps, and the eLearning institutional effectiveness & compliance unit.

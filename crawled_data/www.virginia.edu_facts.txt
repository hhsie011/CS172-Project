Arts Athletics Health System Libraries Research You are here Home About Us Facts & Figures X Facts & Figures A Leader From Day One The University of Virginia encompasses twelve schools in Charlottesville, as well as the College at Wise in Southwest Virginia. In 2022, the University was ranked fourth best public university by U.S. News & World Report. In the 22 years since U.S. News began ranking public universities as a separate category, UVA has ranked in the top four and continues to rank in the Top 30 among the best of all national universities, public and private.

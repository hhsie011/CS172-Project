Series: The Practice of VR/AR Application in Education Events Past Event Columbia Beijing Education Panel Series: The Practice of VR/AR Application in Education Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo December 22, 2018 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, No. 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing On December 22, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to the second panel discussion of Columbia Beijing Education Panel Series, discussing the practice of VR/AR application in education. New technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Virtual Reality (VR) are increasingly applied to education. Augmented Reality (AR) is an extension of VR technology. It sets up virtual information in the real environment, allowing learners to interact with learning content in a virtual-real combined environment using a computer or mobile device. This lecture introduces the status quo of the AR learning environment and the future development prospects in K-12 education, and shares the AR educational application cases from VR/AR+Education Lab in Beijing Normal University. Speakers CAI Su Associate Professor at Faculty of Education in Beijing Normal University, China, Director of VR/AR+Education Lab, Vice Director of the Joint Lab for Mobile Learning of Ministry of Education-China Mobile Communications Corporation, Visiting Scholar at Teachers College, Columbia University GUO Shanshan English Teacher at Tsinghua University Primary school, Visiting Scholar at Teachers College, Columbia University Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code confirmation. Please present your QR code at the entrance. Registration begins 30 minutes prior to the program. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance. If you have any questions, please leave us a message below or email [email protected] Columbia Beijing Education Panel Series is initiated by Columbia Global Centers | Beijing in 2018, aiming at providing an international education platform to discover and develop individual's competence, to share and exchange wisdom, and to explore solutions for the new development of China's education.

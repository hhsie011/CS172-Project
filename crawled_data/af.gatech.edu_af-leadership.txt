More than 1,200 staff report through the Division. Prior to joining the Georgia Institute of Technology, Kelly led the Strategic Resources and Support team at the University of Colorado Boulder, guiding the financial management and planning activities of the $1.8 billion university budget. Kelly has spent nearly 20 years in higher education at the University of Colorado System Office, Colorado School of Mines, and the University of Colorado Boulder.

To ensure your computer and telephone moves are completed properly, you must complete a move packet. Read this helpful packet that will help you complete the online move packet. When you are ready, click here to complete the move packet. A single move packet can be used for multiple people and equipment. Please submit your completed move packet or edits by Tuesday at 5:00 p.m. to ensure that your packet is included for consideration at the weekly Wednesday Move Committee meeting. If you need assistance completing your move packet, please contact HelpDesk+ at (254) 710-4357 or helpdesk@baylor.edu for assistance.

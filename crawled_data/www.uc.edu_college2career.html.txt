Opportunities for Ohioans with Disabilities (OOD) office, is proud to be able to offer college-to-career services for our students with disabilities. What is the Opportunities for Ohioans with Disabilities (OOD) office? Opportunities for Ohioans with Disabilities (OOD) provides counseling and guidance to individuals with disabilities who are seeking employment to jointly choose an employment goal based on strengths, resources, priorities, concerns, capabilities and interests. A thorough, objective understanding of your assets and liabilities, and of employment opportunities, is a prerequisite in planning.

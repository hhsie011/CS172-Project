Sustainability Report In 2019, University of Miami earned a STARS Gold rating in recognition of its sustainability performance from the Association for the Advancement of Sustainability in Higher Education. The Sustainability Report 2020 offers you a glimpse at the diversity of sustainable achievements the university has made in the last three years. We encourage any member of the UM community to consult the STARS 2019 Gold report , the Sustainability Report 2020, and send your comments and suggestions to greenu@miami.edu.

You are here: GT Home Music Technology (BS) Degree Level: Bachelor's The undergraduate program in the School of Music leads to a Bachelor of Science in Music Technology. In the program, students understand the role of technology in enabling new ways to access, consume, and create music. They master existing technologies and develop the skills necessary to innovate and create the ideas that will drive the music industry in the future.

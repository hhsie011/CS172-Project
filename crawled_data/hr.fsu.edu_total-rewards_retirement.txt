Options include a defined benefit plan and two defined contribution plans sponsored by the State. We also offer voluntary pre-tax and roth (post-tax) 403(b) plans and the Florida Deferred Compensation Plan (a 457). Coverage options are available through the Florida Retirement System (FRS), the Division of Retirement and through local and national vendors.

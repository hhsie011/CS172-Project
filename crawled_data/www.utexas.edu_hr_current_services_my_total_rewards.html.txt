These additional perks range from wellness programs to professional development opportunities. The university also promotes programs and services that support work/life balance, employee recognition for years of service and other achievements, and offers a variety of free and discounted services. Employees can login to Workday for detailed information about their paid leave balances, core benefit elections and compensation.

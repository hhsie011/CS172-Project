Past events and webinars Get Involved Give to the Institute Hassenfeld Child Health Innovation Awards Past events and webinars "Supporting K-12 Students' Learning during COVID-19: Resources for Parents and Caregivers," featuring Nancy E. Hill, Ph.D., of the Harvard Graduate School of Education, and Nathaniel Schwartz of Brown's Annenberg Institute for School Reform. In this webinar from October 21, 2020, education experts talk about supporting children and teens who are adjusting to changes in their school routines due to COVID-19. "Questions about Autism during COVID-19 and Beyond," featuring Stephen Sheinkopf, Ph.D., and Joanne Quinn In this webinar from July 23, 2020, experts from the Hassenfeld Institute talk about how to find support during challenging times. Here's a list of resources they recommend during the webinar. "Sleeping, Eating and Being Active: Healthy Routines for Kids during COVID-19 and Beyond," featuring Elissa Jelalian, Ph.D., and Whitney Evans, Ph.D. In this webinar from June 25, 2020, experts from The Miriam Hospital's Weight Control and Diabetes Research Center in Rhode Island answer questions from the community about healthy routines during the pandemic and the transition to summer. Here's a list of the resources Dr. Jelalian and Dr. Evans recommend in the webinar. "Pregnancy and COVID-19," featuring Erika Werner, M.D., M.S., and Erica Hardy, M.D., M.A., MSSc In this webinar from May 14, 2020, medical experts from Women & Infants Hospital in Providence, Rhode Island, answer questions from the community about staying safe and healthy while pregnant during the coronavirus pandemic. "Children and COVID-19," featuring Penelope Dennehy, M.D., and Adam Pallant, M.D. In this webinar from May 19, 2020, registered pediatricians answer questions from community members about how to keep their children healthy and happy during the COVID-19 crisis.

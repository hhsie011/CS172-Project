At the recommendation of CIO Kristen Constant, ITS formed a departmental Inclusion, Diversity and Equity Advisory (IDEA) working group to address the role ITS fills in fostering an inclusive and diverse departmental and campus environment. Led by ITS team members Jeff Sorensen and Cyndi Wiley, the group will identify issues within scope; identify metrics to measure progress; plan activities, discussions and/or trainings; and work to mobilize others for a thriving campus community.

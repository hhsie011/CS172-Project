Culture Lab grants, programs, and initiatives aim to catalyze a cultural transformation to achieve full belonging and empowerment for all members of the Harvard community. Funding Opportunities Innovation Fund An innovation fund that awards competitive grants of up to $15,000 or more to project teams to pilot and scale ideas that seek to strengthen Harvard’s capacity to advance a culture of belonging. Express Grants Grants available for $200 to $1,000 for supplementing costs associated with hosting conferences, meetings, events, and trainings and supporting projects and programs on campus.

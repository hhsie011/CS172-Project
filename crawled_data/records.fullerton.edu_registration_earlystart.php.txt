The goal is to increase the opportunity to complete their college degree. Based on the results of Multiple Measures, students may be required or recommended to participate in the CSU Early Start Program. The following students are exempt from participating in the Early Start Program: Students who met the multiple measures criteria for English and Math; International students (F-1 and J-1 visas only); Non-resident students who reside outside of California; Students in the Educational Opportunity Program (EOP) Summer Bridge Program.

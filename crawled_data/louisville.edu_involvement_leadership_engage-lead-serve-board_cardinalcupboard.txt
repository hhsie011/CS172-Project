Union Cardinal Cupboard Through fundraising and food drives, the Commonwealth Credit Union Cardinal Cupboard’s increases accessibility to food and health products to members of the Cardinal community in an effort to reduce their financial burden. GIVE A DONATION Hours of Operation Monday to Friday, 9:00am to 6:00PM Closed Thanksgiving Break (Nov. 24 to 26) Location Swain Student Activities Center, W312 The Commonwealth Credit Union Cardinal Cupboard is open to all members of the Cardinal community (students, staff, and faculty). Please bring your Cardinal Card ID with you during your visit. Need assistance outside of normal business hours? Try our Grab & Go food packages. Current COVID Guidelines Patrons must maintain a 6 foot distance from others in the pantry. Do not enter if you are ill or feeling sick. Use the hand sanitizer provided when entering and exiting the pantry. Do not bring your own bags. Do not enter if there are more than 3 individuals (including the staff member) in the pantry. Patrons must wear masks, when possible.

Home /Alumni/Alumni Clubs Alumni Clubs Stay in the Loop Syracuse University alumni clubs in the Washington, DC, metro area strengthen the connections between Syracuse University alumni and their alma mater through social, philanthropic, and professional events, as well as educational activities. Two of the most active clubs in the region are listed below. Syracuse University Alumni Club of Washington, DC The Syracuse University Alumni Club of Washington, DC, offers Syracuse University alums in the greater Washington metropolitan area the ability to maintain ties with the university, network, volunteer, and attend educational, social, and sporting events, centered around Syracuse University in the nation’s capital. To learn more, visit the DC Alumni Club page on Cuse Community or contact: Co-Chairs: Lottie Watts '09 & Andrew Regalado '20 suindc@gmail.com Alumni Club of Greater Baltimore B’More Orange—the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Greater Baltimore, Maryland, is continually planning events all around Charm City, and welcomes family and friends of Syracuse University alumni to join in all events.

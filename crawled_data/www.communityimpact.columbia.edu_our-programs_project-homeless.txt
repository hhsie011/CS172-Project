Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Project for the Homeless Project for the Homeless (PFH) is a student organization that staffs two small homeless shelters in the Upper West Side of Manhattan, the men's shelter at Stephen Wise Free Synagogue and the women's shelter at the New York Society for Ethical Culture. By having Columbia and Barnard students as nightly supervisors, PFH ensures that the two shelters remain open for 120 nights for the men and women who are in need of a place to stay in the City of New York. More information is on our web page http://www.columbia.edu/cu/pfh/.

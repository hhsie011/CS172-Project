The University of Louisville will hold in-person commencement exercises Saturday, May 14, 2022 at the KFC Yum! Center. These ceremonies will celebrate May 2022 graduates. The ceremony will also be live streamed. Students being recognized with a bachelor or master degree attend the University-wide ceremony for the school/college where they take their classes. Doctoral level students will receive ceremony information and instruction from the school/college. University-wide Ceremony Schedule *Note: Master level graduates participate in the ceremony for the school/college where they take their classes, not the Graduate School. Example: MBA graduates participate with the College of Business.* Saturday, May 14, 2022 10:00 a.m. University-wide Commencement Ceremony #1 Honoring graduates from the College of Arts & Sciences, Brandeis School of Law, Graduate School, Kent School of Social Work & Family Science, School of Dentistry, School of Medicine, School of Nursing, and School of Public Health & Information Sciences 3:00 p.m. University-wide Commencement Ceremony #2 Honoring graduates from the College of Business, College of Education & Human Development, JB Speed School of Engineering, and School of Music Calendar of Events Frequently Asked Questions Late Apparel Information Student Communications Student RSVP Form Please check this website regularly for updates.

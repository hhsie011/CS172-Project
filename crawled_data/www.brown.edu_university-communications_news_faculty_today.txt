[email protected] contains many of the important community news messages that students, faculty and staff traditionally have received from various offices via email — University budget information, funding and fellowship opportunities, new initiatives, important deadlines, compliance and policy guidance, etc. Upon its launch, it replaced Morning Mail as a daily digest for announcements and events around the campus community. [email protected] greets recipients each morning of the year on which there are items scheduled for publication. Published announcements are also available to members of the Brown community for online browsing at http://today.brown.edu. [email protected] complements other print and electronic communication methods on campus, such as department communiqués, official all-community email messages and the Brown online event calendar. A detailed set of guidelines and FAQs outlines the criteria and process for Morning Mail submissions.

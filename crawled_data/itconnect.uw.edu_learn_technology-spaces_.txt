Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Technology Spaces Note: Many on-campus study spaces are currently closed due to COVID-19. For information about the status of spaces, check with departmental websites, contact the building’s facilities staff, or look up the space in Scout. Odegaard Learning Commons is co-located with other services to provide students, staff, and faculty with a rich set of resources that enhance teaching and learning. Sound Studio The Odegaard Library Sound Studio is an on-campus media lab that contains high-end, professional tools for recording and editing audio. Odegaard Collaboration Pods Collaboration Pods facilitate in-person, interactive, small group projects or meetings. Group members can collaborate with up to 4 laptops on a large 42″ plasma screen, visible to all group members. Video Studio The remodeled Video Studio in Odegaard 320 offers HD recording and live web conferencing. Odegaard Learning Studio A UW-IT and UW Libraries supported classroom and workshop space. Active Learning Classrooms The Odegaard Active Learning classrooms are built for courses that focus on active collaborative learning, and include technologies that enable these courses. Mary Gates Hall Computer Classrooms Mary Gates Hall computer classrooms feature computers available for official UW courses and special events.

OSU strongly encourages the prompt reporting of incidents of discrimination of the basis of sex, gender identity, gender expression, and sexual orientation, sexual harassment, sexual assault, sexual misconduct, dating or domestic violence, and stalking. LEARN MORE AFFIRMATIVE ACTION OSU is obligated to achieve and maintain a workforce that is representative of those in the recruiting area with requisite skills. In order to achieve a representative workforce, employment and hiring goals are established for each job category based on statistical data in regards to availability pools of applicants. LEARN MORE AMERICANS WITH DISABILITIES (ADA) The university is dedicated to providing reasonable accommodation to qualified faculty and staff and all those with disabilities participating in its programs and services.

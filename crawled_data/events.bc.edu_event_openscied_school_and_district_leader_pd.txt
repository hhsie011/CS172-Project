This session runs concurrently with a 4-day Launch, so that some learning may be done alongside teachers adopting OpenSciEd. In this session, leaders will: Experience immersion as a student in the OpenSciEd curriculum Consider how OpenSciEd supports standards-aligned and equitable science learning Utilize tools and consider how to support the OpenSciEd adoption OEI Cohort school leaders, or school leaders who are registered for the full 4-day event, may attend at no additional cost. The cost of the event for non-OEI Cohort schools is $250 plus the cost of overnight lodging (~$139 per night plus tax), if requested. In the event of cancellation within two weeks of the event date, districts will be responsible for the registration fee plus lodging, if requested. If overnight lodging is requested, consider the following. All rooms are single occupancy. Please indicate on the registration form if any special accommodations are needed. Please note that COVID protocols may include testing upon arrival, proof of vaccinations, and masks depending on the COVID data at the time of the event. Please register by June 1, 2022 Email Jana Brinkhaus at kilduffj@bc.edu for more information.

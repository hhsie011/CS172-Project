Tuition and fees Tuition and fees can be found on the Bursar’s Office website. Calculate your estimated cost with the Transfer Student Cost Calculator. Bursar's Office Suite 101 Houchens Building University of Louisville Louisville KY 40292 Phone: (502)852-6503 Email: bursar@louisville.edu Textbooks The cost of textbooks and materials are dependent upon individual student programs. The Campus Store is located in the Swain Student Activities Center. Campus Store #1543 Phone: (502)852-6679 Fax: (502)852-5913 Email: uofl@bkstr.com Housing Information regarding available on campus housing options is available on the Campus Housing website. CAMPUS HOUSING Stevenson Hall University of Louisville 2301 South Third Street Louisville KY 40292 Phone: (502)852-6636 Email: oncampus@louisville.edu Mandatory Meal Plan All full-time undergraduate students are automatically assigned the standard meal plan according to where you live. As a commuter student, you are required to obtain the Base Flex Plan, a mandatory meal plan that is $275.00. Information regarding the meal plan can be found on the Campus Dining website.

You can view all submissions at any time and create a download them as needed. FOR SECURITY The CAPTCHA element MUST be added to every form on your site. From any page entry you can add a webform Enter the title for the form The title appears in capital letters centered on top of the form. Select the type of form template to create The Newsletter Sign Up will render as an email field with a signup button. The field will only accept a properly formatted email address An incorrectly formatted email address will give the user an error The Contact Us form will render as four fields for Name, Email, Subject and Message The email field will only accept a properly formatted email address All fields are mandatory and will give the user an error if not filled in Select if the form is Open or Closed Select Open to display the form fields and accept submissions Select Closed to display the form title, if entered, along with a message that the form is closed to submissions A confirmation message on the webpage is displayed upon submission.

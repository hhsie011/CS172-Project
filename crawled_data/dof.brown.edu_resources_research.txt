The Dean's office manages several restricted submission awards in conjunction with other Brown offices. While research accounts retain their maximum value when used to support research, they can also be converted into summer salary. Faculty Development Fund Funds for research and travel support are available to all voting members of the campus-based faculty. Proposals are typically due by April 15th, and the funds may be used for the following academic year. Learn more Vice President for Research The Dean's office works closely with the office of the Vice President for Research to support faculty scholarship. Faculty Research Travel Fund Faculty can apply for reimbursement for up to three conference- or research-related trips per academic year. Lectureship Funds Each spring, the Dean's office puts out a call for applications for Lectureship Funds, which can be used to bring speakers to Brown for lectures and events in the following academic year. Sabbatical & Leave Giving faculty the chance to step away from their normal duties to focus on research projects, fieldwork, artistic projects, and other professional opportunities. Funding for Emeritus Faculty Professors emeritus and emerita may be eligible to apply for up to $3,000 in research funds. ADVANCE Program The Dean's office helps support the ADVANCE program, aimed at increasing retention and advancement of women faculty in sciences and engineering.

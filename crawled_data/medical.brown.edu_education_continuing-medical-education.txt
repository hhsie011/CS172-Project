The Warren Alpert Medical School is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Continuing Medical Education to provide continuing medical education to physicians. For more than 20 years, The Warren Alpert Medical School has been dedicated to sponsoring medical education activities that enable physicians and other health care professionals to better treat, manage, and educate their patients. The Office of Continuing Medical Education sponsors live and enduring medical education activities on a wide range of clinical disciplines including, but not limited to: Anesthesiology Cardiology

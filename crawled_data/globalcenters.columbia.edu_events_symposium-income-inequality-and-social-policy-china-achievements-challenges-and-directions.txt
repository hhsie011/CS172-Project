Calendar: Google Yahoo October 22, 2016 8:30 AM - 6:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Columbia University School of Social Work will host the "Symposium on Income Inequality and Social Policy in China: Achievements, Challenges, and Directions" jointly with the China Institute for Income Distribution on October 22, 2016 Saturday at Jingshi Hotel, Beijing Normal University. The symposium includes some of the top experts on these topics in China and around the world from academia, the government, and international organizations such as the Asian Development Bank, UNICEF, and the World Bank. Topics discussed include income distribution, poverty reduction, child development, pension reform, and elder care, among others. The symposium is free and open to the public. The symposium is supported by Beijing Normal University Business School, Columbia Weatherhead East Asian Institute, and Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. Agenda: Confirmed Speakers (in speech order) : Song Xiaowu, Director, China Institute for Income Distribution, Beijing Normal University Jeanette Takamura, Dean and Professor, School of Social Work, Columbia University Neeraj Kaushal, Professor of Social Policy, Columbia University Lu Mai, Secretary-General, China Development Research Foundation Wan Guanghua, Director of Research, Asian Development Bank Institute Wang feng, Professor of Sociology, University of California-Irvine and Fudan University Gao Qin, Associate Professor of Social Policy and Social Work, Columbia University Wang Dewen, Senior Social Protection Specialist, Human Development Sector Unit, The World Bank Beijing Office Luo Chuliang, Associate Dean, China Institute of Income Distribution;

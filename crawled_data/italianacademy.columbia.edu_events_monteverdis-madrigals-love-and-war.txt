For this performance, TENET will be joined by acclaimed violinist, conductor, and music director of Blue Heron, Scott Metcalfe, along with musicians from TENET’s roster including artistic director Jolle Greenleaf and Molly Quinn, sopranos; Jason McStoots, Aaron Sheehan, Sumner Thompson, tenors; Mischa Bouvier, baritone; Hank Heijink and Daniel Swenberg, theorbos; Avi Stein, harpsichord; and special guest Robert Mealy, violin. Brilliant color, high drama, breathtaking melodies and some of the most daring vocal writing of the early Baroque make Claudio Monteverdi’s Book 8 an undisputed masterpiece.

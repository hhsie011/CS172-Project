Transfer guides are provided to assist transfer students in planning coursework efficiently and understand their future curriculum. Every effort is made to keep these guides up to date, but curriculum is always evolving. Students transferring to OSU will be required to complete the degree requirements outlined in the catalog for the year they begin at OSU.

Test Information Form COVID-19 and Testing Services Wichita State University Testing Services is open for in-person testing. We are using social distancing guidelines to establish our current testing capacity. Because of this, appointments are limited. Walk-in appointments are LIMITED. Exams available to students include make-up exams, accommodated testing, TEAS, CLEP, distance proctoring, and certification exams. To register for in-person proctoring please use RegisterBlast. If you would prefer a Zoom session (you must be registered for testing accommodations with ODS and have your instructors prior approval for remote proctoring) please send an email to testingcenter@wichita.edu PLEASE NOTE: Staff needs at least a 72 hour notice to accommodate Zoom requests.

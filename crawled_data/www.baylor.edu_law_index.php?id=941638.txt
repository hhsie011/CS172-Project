Baylor Law Veterans Clinic “Boots on the Ground” Award An annual award of $250, shared by the students who have made the most significant contributions to the Veterans Clinic program. Given by Professor Bridget Fuselier and her husband Paul. Past Recipients 2022 - Noelle L. Burke & Rose Vandenbrand 2021 - Allison Jane Taff & Bailey Elizabeth VanNatta 2020 - Drew A. Beglau & Sarah E. Van Sciver 2019 - Robert "Drew" A. Beatty & Taylor A. McConnell 2018 - Kyle J. Dennis 2017 - Katherine Grace Broussard & Javier E. Cabanillas Bill Kimble Service Award An annual award of $250 shared by two students who have demonstrated an exceptional commitment to serving the community in the past year. This award is made possible by an annual gift from Josh & Jennifer Borderud in memory of Bill Kimble, a Baylor Lawyer who dedicated his life to serving those in need. Past Recipients 2022 - Jono Cooper & Natalia Hamilton 2021 - Tamer T. Abouras & Skyler Savanna Schoolfield 2020 - Victoria L. Rhodes & Taylor A. Volesky 2019 - Sarah E. Van Sciver & Tiffany M. Wimberly 2018 - Tychanika "Tycha" J. Kimbrough & Hailee B. Zeichner 2017 - Emilia G. “Gracie” Wood 2016 - Amanda R. Sanchez 2015 - Jessica S. Freud & Ashley Nicole Morgan 2014 - Amber R. Shanafelt & Skylar Renae Simons 2013 - Siobhan Ray & Amanda Sherek 2012 - Huma Patel 2011 - Tope Tokan-Lawal & Jessica Edwards 2010 - Shamaila Malik & Tope Tokan-Lawal 2009 - Berkley Scroggins Knas & Patricia Dixon 2008 - Abigail Toth & Justin Schneider For more information about Bill Kimble, see page 603 of this Texas Bar Journal article. BARBRI Public Servant Award An annual award of a BARBRI Bar Review course, valued at approximately $2,500, to the student who has completed the most community service and pro bono hours through the Pro Bono and Public Service Program during his or her first year at Baylor Law. Given by BARBRI Bar Review. Past Recipients 2022 - Harrison Q. Watson 2021 - Skyler Savanna Schoolfield 2020 - Lanie Nichole Bennett 2019 - Taylor A. Volesky 2018 - Morgan K.

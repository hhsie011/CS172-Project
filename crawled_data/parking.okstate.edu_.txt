Parking Portal (purchase a permit) BOB Big Orange Bus BOB is a shuttle service that runs between OSU Stillwater and OSU Tulsa nine times a day. With comfortable reclining seats and on-board Wi-Fi, commuting from the two campuses has never been nicer. Follow the link and book your trip on the BOB today. Book BOB Explore Stillwater today! Hop on The Bus and let us be your guide. Orange, Black, and Gold routes are free and open to everyone. There is no need to swipe your card, and you can bring anyone with you, even if they do not attend OSU. All other routes are fare free with a student ID, just swipe your Student ID and you have access to all of Stillwater without buying a single drop of gas.

PreviousNext MES Minor Requirements The minor will consist of six courses. Students are required to take the Introduction to the Middle East Seminar (MES 1) and two core courses (choose from MES 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, or 9) or others, as approved by the Chair. Only language courses beyond the first-year sequence may count for the minor. A maximum of three language courses may count for the minor.

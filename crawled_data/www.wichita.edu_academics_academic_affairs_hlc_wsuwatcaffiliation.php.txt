Wichita State, WSU Tech affiliation in Full Effect WSU/WATC Affiliation Documents Wichita State University (WSU) and Wichita Area Technical College (WATC) are proposing to affiliate where WATC will become the WSU Campus of Applied Sciences and Technology. The documents below (starting with the HLC Change of Control Application) describe the proposed affiliation. The attachments referred to in the Change of Control document are linked below. Please review and send any feedback, comments, or questions to Dr. Rick Muma, Provost (richard.muma@wichita.edu).

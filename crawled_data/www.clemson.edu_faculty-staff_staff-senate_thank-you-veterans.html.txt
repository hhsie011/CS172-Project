Coach Swinney transcript: "I just want to give a quick shout out to all of our veterans out there. You know, we got a lot of students, and faculty, and staff veterans and I know you guys usually get to come to practice, and sorry that can't happen this year. Nonetheless want to tell you how much we appreciate all you've done and continue to do. So God bless all of you, go Tigers and thank you for your service." President Clements transcript: "Thank you to all of our students, faculty and staff who are currently serving or who have served in the military. Thank you for your service to our great country and for choosing to be a part of the Clemson family. Clemson is a better place because of you. Thank you for everything you do for Clemson and happy Veterans Day to each and every one of you. May God bless you all and go Tigers!"

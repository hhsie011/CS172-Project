I love how the UofL community is like a big family." - Eimireth Albaez, Panama, Communications The University of Louisville is devoted to welcoming students from all areas of the world. We have been catering to the needs of our international students since 1944 and are determined to keep our doors open. The University of Louisville is a diverse institution that supports, encourages, and nurtures our international students to reach new heights in their professional and personal growth. Louisville is the perfect location to study if you enjoy the big city or small town, and if you are interested in a place that fosters education and cross-cultural exchange, we highly recommend considering the University of Louisville as your next location! Explore UofL There are many ways you can experience UofL, from an in-person visit to a virtual presentation to a live campus tour on Instagram. Explore all of your visit options. Questions? Learn more about joining the Cardinal Family as an international student by visiting the Discover Global website. For more information about international admissions, please send an email to Ramonia Brents, Admissions Counselor, Sr., International Admissions or contact her at 502.852.7538.

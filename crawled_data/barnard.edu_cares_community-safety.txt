Community Safety responds to serious, unexpected, and often dangerous situations requiring immediate action; such matters may pose an immediate threat to health, life, property, environment with a high probability of escalating quickly/dangerously without quick intervention. Need immediate help on campus? Please call 212-854-6666 or 4-6666 from a campus phone Our Services Transportation Need a safe and free alternative to walking alone at night? In partnership with Columbia University, we now offer an on-demand ride share option through VIA Ride Share. We also provide a Mobile Escort Service available to the entire college community. This phone service is available anytime you are uncomfortable walking from one point to another by calling 212-854-3362. Safety Tips Learn more about how you can stay safe on and around campus. To ensure a safe community, the CARES Department provides emergency notifications via a text alert system when there are security-related reports throughout our community. You may learn more about our Campus Alert System here. Access to Campus Learn more about COVID-related protocols, campus card reader access locations, and after-hour access. We are here to help.

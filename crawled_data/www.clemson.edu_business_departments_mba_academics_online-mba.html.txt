Related Links Apply to the MBA Program Contact Admissions Graduate School Financial Aid MBA Online A Flexible Online MBA from a Reputable South Carolina Institution Accelerate your career with Clemson University's part-time online MBA. This program is specifically tailored for professionals with 2+ years of work experience who are looking to earn more in their current role or change careers. As academic leaders, we have adapted our same Corporate MBA curriculum and faculty to an online platform so professionals seeking a leap forward can take flexible online MBA courses on their own time and remotely. This is where your path to management and executive leadership begins. Real-Time or Real Flexible. Unlike traditional programs, our flexible online MBA program allows you to select the class structure that best fits your schedule and learning style - and change it anytime during the semester. That means you can join a live class through video conferencing, allowing you to participate fully and ask questions at any time. If you want to view the lecture but can't join live, then you will have the option of a recorded version. Each lecture is built by our instructional design team and has been constructed with both learning models in mind. In addition to our specially-developed remote MBA Online courses, you may also choose from any online class offered in our existing MBA Corporate catalog. This creates maximum flexibility to support your schedule and needs. Only with Clemson's online MBA program can you select a different format each week. View the detailed MBA Online Curriculum (PDF). A Degree Plus There is much more to a part-time MBA online program than just coursework, and taking online courses doesn't mean a lack of opportunities to make real-life connections. Like students in all of our MBA programs, we offer extensive resources in career planning, networking, seminars, conferences, and even short-term study abroad opportunities to help you leverage the full Clemson MBA experience.

Students may also live off campus. Housing for students studying abroad varies by program. For some programs, students are housed in dormitories; other programs place students with local families. Details are available on the individual program websites. Financial Assistance Yale Summer Session offers financial assistance to Yale College students who receive financial aid during the regular academic year. Assistance can cover up to one-half of the tuition for a course. Students may request financial assistance for a maximum of two credits. Financial assistance is awarded on the basis of the program course(s) for which applicants have applied.

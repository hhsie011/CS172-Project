Leadership Careers Careers Find Jobs and Internships Career Fair For Employers University Career Services Placement Reports Careers Our Career Development Office is dedicated to helping Hilton College students achieve their goals. We can provide general career advice, resume help, job placement services, interview tips and more. Our office also organizes twice-yearly career fairs every spring and fall, which are attended by dozens of recruiters with some of the top hospitality companies in the world, and hosts other recruitment events with individual companies throughout the semester. All students are encouraged to make an appointment with our Career Development Office during their first semester at the College, so they can discuss their career paths. To assist with post-graduation job placement, seniors are asked to register with our office at the beginning of their final semester. Appointments can be made by email or phone. For information on upcoming recruitment events, check the Hilton College calendar.

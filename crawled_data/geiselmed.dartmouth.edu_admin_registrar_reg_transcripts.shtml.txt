student's academic history at the Geisel School of Medicine at Dartmouth. Official and Unofficial Transcripts Official transcripts are printed on security paper and include the Registrar's official seal and date. Unofficial transcripts are not printed on security paper and do not include the Registrar's official seal. Current students can access their unofficial transcript (web and PDF versions) through DartHub. Requesting an Official Transcript For Current Students Current MD and MPH students may request Official Transcripts by going to DartHub and selecting the "Official Transcript" tile. There is no fee for ordering transcripts. Official transcripts may be delivered in either certified PDF ("eTranscript") or paper formats. Requests for eTranscripts are generally processed and delivered within 1 business day of the request being placed. Official paper transcripts are generally processed within 2-5 business days of receipt of your request. Please order an official transcript at least one week in advance of the required delivery date. For Alumni There is no fee for ordering transcripts. Alumni (MD and MPH) may request transcripts by clicking Alumni Transcript Request. For more information please see the following resources: Transcript Ordering Guide - Includes instructions for requesting an eTranscript for VSAS (Visiting Student Application Service) Alumni Transcript Ordering Guide Student ERAS Transcript Ordering Guide How to Receive and Validate an eTranscript Official Transcripts - Frequently Asked Questions Geisel Transcript Key If you have questions contact the Geisel Registrar's Office.

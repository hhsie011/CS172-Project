Successful applicants will receive between $500 and $1000 to help them pay for project expenses, engineering mentorship from Professors Barrett Hazeltine and Chris Bull, and entrepreneurship support from the Nelson Center for Entrepreneurship. Grants can be renewed on a case-by-case basis.

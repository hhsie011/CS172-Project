Through our three summer sessions, students can enroll in intellectually stimulating courses with some of the world’s finest scholars in Jewish studies. Summer Session I is for undergraduates only. Both graduate and undergraduate students can take courses in Summer Session II and Summer Session III. You can also learn in the JTS Beit Midrash in our summer Nishma program, designed to provide an encompassing experience of Jewish learning and encounter over the summer.

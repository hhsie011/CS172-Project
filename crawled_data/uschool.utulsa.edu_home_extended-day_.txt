The building is open from 7:30 a.m. to 5:15 p.m. Hours and costs vary by grade level. Several clubs are offered as part of our extended day program for grades I2 – IO8 (4th – 8th grades). The clubs offered are often determined by student interests and may vary from year to year. Current club offerings include: Academic Bowl (2 groups – one competition team and one practice club) Debate Club Jr. Robotics Robotics Club MATHCOUNTS Odyssey of the Mind Podcast Club Yearbook Club USchool Ambassadors Questions about the EDP program may be directed to Leslie Gore at leslie-gore@utulsa.edu. 800 S.

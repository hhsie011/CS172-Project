The year-long workshop begins the weekend of January 23, 24, 2021 and is led by Theater Alaska Writer-in-Residence Frank Henry Kaash Katasse and former Theater Communications Group Artist-in-Residence Maurice Decaul '18 MFA. All meetings will be held over Zoom. Full Press Release from Theatre Alaska available here.

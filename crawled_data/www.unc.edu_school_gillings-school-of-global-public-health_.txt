Gillings School of Global Public Health improves public health, promotes individual well-being and eliminates health inequities across North Carolina and around the world. Tackling the toughest public health challenges Our school is home to dedicated, innovative students who are passionate about improving public health. They work closely with faculty, peers and practitioners, forming bonds that help them grow as students and future public health professionals. The Gillings School’s curriculum balances classroom education with real-world experience, preparing our students to tackle the toughest public health challenges across North Carolina and around the world.

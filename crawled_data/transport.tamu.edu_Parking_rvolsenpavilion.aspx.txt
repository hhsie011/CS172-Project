Olsen Pavilion Location Located between Olsen Field at Blue Bell Park and George Bush Drive with entry from Olsen Blvd. Price Football (Olsen RV season permit holders only) $55/day (home game weekends Friday thru Sunday and Thanksgiving) Reservations available August 1st Daily $55/day Amenities Outdoor covered space with 6 large picnic tables and adjacent concrete pad with permanent grills. The pavilion will accommodate gatherings of 50 people or more and is equipped with lighting for evening events. No restroom facilities are available at this location. Check in/out Guest parking is not free and is available in nearby parking lots for a fee. Please ask about guest parking when making your reservation.

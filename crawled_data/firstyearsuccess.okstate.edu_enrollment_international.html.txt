Your success begins with connections. Whether you're a new freshman or a transfer student, we want to help you transition to OSU. All new students are required to attend orientation and enrollment to learn how to use OSU systems and enroll in your courses. International sessions take place each semester just before classes begin. You will receive an email with more information about your pre- and post-arrival steps. If you have any questions related to your orientation and enrollment, please call us at +1 405-744-3636 or email us at newstudents@okstate.edu. For Summer 2022-entering students, please contact newstudents@okstate.edu to register. Fall 2022-entering students can register below. Fall orientation and enrollment Enrollment for fall-entering students occurs in August. Registration for orientation and advising opens February 1st. Upon arrival, you will complete health screenings with University Health Services and complete ISS immigration registration. Once this is completed, you will schedule advising and enrollment. More information will be provided closer to your arrival. International Students and Scholars OSU has a global reputation for academic excellence, and we're known for our welcoming environment for students from all around the world. The International Students and Scholars Office is here to help you transition to the United States and OSU by providing assistance with academics, immigration regulations, housing, meals, transportation, health care, banking, legal issues and American customs.

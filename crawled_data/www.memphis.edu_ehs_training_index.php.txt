All UofM employees, from student workers to executives, are legally required to complete certain types of safety-related training, and supervisors are obligated to ensure that their personnel complete the training. To assist you with meeting training and compliance obligations, Environmental Health and Safety provides education and training options that cover a wide range of topics for employees at all levels. To learn what training is required for your job description, consult with your supervisor and/or Environmental Health and Safety staff.

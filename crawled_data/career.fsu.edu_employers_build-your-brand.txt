Information Sessions Build Your Brand Increase your brand’s visibility at FSU, and make your time in Tallahassee as productive as possible. Connect with our students by presenting to a student organization, or meet with Career Center staff to discover other ways you can make an impact. Crunched for time? Recruiting from a distance? Support our students remotely through the ProfessioNole program. ProfessioNole Mentors Support students by sharing your knowledge, experience, and career insight! Registering takes just a few minutes, but your help could last a lifetime. Mock Interview Program Help students practice their interview skills by offering critical and educational feedback through our mock interview program. Your organization will connect with targeted and relevant student populations and create greater brand recognition and awareness at Florida State University. The Mock Interview Program runs both fall and spring semesters with the exceptions of career fair weeks and other University breaks. Employers can elect to participate in half-day or full-day session depending on room availability. Contact Heather Lazar at Heather Lazar for more information. FSUshadow Program Looking for a no-cost engagement opportunity that allows you to connect with talented FSU students? FSUshadow connects students with employers, community partners, alumni and friends of the university for one-day job shadowing experiences to provide career exposure in a wide variety of fields and industries. Offered at locations nationwide, FSUshadow is offered during academic breaks so students can travel to you. More information can found through the FSUshadow Program link above. Class or Student Organization Presentations Promote your visit by linking to professors and student organizations. Contact the staff liaison for the college you are interested in networking with. Employer-In-Residence Program The Employer-in-Residence program provides an opportunity for employers to meet with students and provide professional advice and feedback. Garnet and Gold Partners get automatic membership is this program. Advertise in the Career Guide Convey your unique recruitment message to over 6,000 students seeking internships or full-time employment at FSU career fairs and events by advertising in the Career Guide, the official publication of The Career Center. For more information, contact Shereada Harrell, Program Director, Employer Relations at 850.644.9771 or sharrell3@fsu.edu. Meet with Staff The Employer Relations and the Experiential Learning teams are committed to providing you a seamless recruiting experience. Whether you are new to FSU or one of our long-standing partners, we will connect you with services and programs and to faculty, staff, and administrators in order to help you identify talent.

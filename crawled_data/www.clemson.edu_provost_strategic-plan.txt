The plan aims to ensure that Clemson fulfills its core mission and consistently ranks among the nation’s top 20 public universities and Carnegie tier-one research institutions. ClemsonFORWARD is built on four strategic priorities: Research, Engagement, the Academic Core and the Living Environment — easily remembered by the acronym REAL. Due to COVID, we are going through our strategies in detail for review and alignments. Stay tuned.

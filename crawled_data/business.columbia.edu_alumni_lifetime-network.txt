And for members of this community, there are a lot of benefits. Graduating students are invited to a number of alumni events throughout the year, and throughout the world, and you will automatically be added to a new alumni discussion group on the alumni website. Young alumni can participate in more than 80 alumni clubs and even help provide current students with invaluable insight on navigating campus life. As an EMBA alumnus you can attend the yearly Family Day in New York City, while PhD alumni are invited to the yearly American Economics Association conference each January.

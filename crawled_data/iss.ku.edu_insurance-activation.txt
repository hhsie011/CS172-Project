All other F-1, F-2 or J-1 international students can activate their KU UnitedHealthcare StudentResources (UHCSR) International Basic (2021-471-04) health insurance plan by taking the following steps: Click on the “Opt-in” tab in the initial insurance waiver email from ECI Services. Log into your ECI Services account and click on the “Enroll Now” icon. Insurance Activation for Dependents Open enrollment to add a dependent to your summer 2022 KU UHCSR Insurance plan is June 1, 2022 – June 30, 2022. The student's KU UHCSR health insurance plan must be activated prior to adding dependents. UHCSR must receive payment for the dependent insurance before activating the dependent insurance. F-1, F-2 or J-1 International Students with at least 50% GTA, GRA, and/or GA appointment click here for information about adding dependents. All other F-1, F-2 or J-2 international students should take the following steps: Step 1: Request a control number from UHCSR at www.uhcsr.com/control by clicking on “NOTIFY ME”. Step 2: Submit request to ECI Services to activate your KU UHCSR health insurance plan. (See info above) Step 3: Login or Create your UHCSR “My Account” to add dependents.

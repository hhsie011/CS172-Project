here: Home Student Lounge Student Lounge The GS Lounge, on the 3rd floor of Lewisohn Hall, serves as a gathering place for students and is the venue for many GS Student Life and GSSC events. The Lounge contains computer terminals with printers and provides GS students with a space to study and socialize any time of day. The Lounge is open to all GS students with a Columbia ID 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Please make sure to follow the University guidelines to access Columbia Buildings which applies to all buildings on campus including Lewisohn Hall. Please note: wearing a face covering to cover the mouth and nose is required, consistent with CDC, New York State, and New York City guidelines, while on University property, including outdoor spaces, at all times. Lounge Safety The GS Lounge may be accessed using a Columbia ID. When entering, always be mindful of who is coming in after you. For your safety and the safety of your belongings and others, remain vigilant. Cleaning Schedule Please be mindful of the GS Lounge custodial staff. The following schedule has been selected for weekly cleaning. Your patience during these times is greatly appreciated: Sunday through Thursday: Anytime between 10 p.m. – 6 a.m. Friday: Trash pickup anytime between 4 – 6 p.m. Lounge Closings On occasion, the Lounge will be closed for maintenance and private events. Closing dates are announced in advance via LCD screens located in both the Lounge and the Lewisohn Hall lobby. Closings are also announced via the bi-weekly newsletter and the News and Announcements sections of the website. Lounge Policies All GS students must follow the policies below for usage of the GS Lounge: Items & Space No items are to be left or stored in the GS Lounge—any unattended items will be brought to Public Safety Please clean up after yourself, and use appropriate garbage and recycle bins as needed The AC and heating units in the GS Lounge are operated by a remote control;

The DUG aims to highlight the undergraduate-oriented nature of the department, and encourage the expansion of the concentration within related fields. In addition to helping fellow students prepare for capstone projects and study abroad programs, they organize a number of events, including information sessions on the concentration and the Undergraduate Paper Series. All students are welcome to attend and participate in the DUG regardless of concentration. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact the DUG at mesdug@brown.edu.

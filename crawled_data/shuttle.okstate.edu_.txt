Bus boards 15 minutes prior to departure time and leaves PROMPTLY at time of departure. For more detailed information on rules on Riding the BOB, please refer to TIPS FOR RIDING THE BOB Reservations Reservations can be made be online here, or in person in Tulsa at the North Hall Information Center or in Stillwater at the Multi-Modal Transportation Terminal (MMTT) located at the corner of Monroe and Hall of Fame in Stillwater The OSU-Tulsa Information Center will be open to accept shuttle reservations Monday through Thursday from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. and Friday from 7:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Bus Fare The shuttle runs 9 trips daily from each campus Monday to Thursday,and runs 7 trips on Friday. It is open to OSU students,staff, faculty, and general public. One Way Fare: $7.50 For OSU Students $13.00 For OSU Staff, Faculty and Guests. All passengers must have a valid ticket or pass. Passengers under 2 may ride free on a parent's lap. Passengers under 15 must be accompanied by an adult.

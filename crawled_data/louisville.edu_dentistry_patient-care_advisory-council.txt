This patient group regularly meets with dental school leaders and students to discuss important topics related to improving the patient experience. These meetings benefit patients, as well as dental school students, faculty and staff. Benefits of a Patient Advisory Board Learn about priority concerns for patients, which may be different than that of the organization. Receive direct feedback from patients. Grow the School of Dentistry’s patient-centered culture. Strengthen community relations. Appreciate the barriers and opportunities for patients that were previously not understood. Identify system issues that need to be addressed in order to provide better patient-centered care. Achieve higher satisfaction ratings through collaboration in a patient-centered care model.

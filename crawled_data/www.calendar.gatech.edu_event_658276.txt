Wednesday, June 1 2022 4:00pm - 5:00pm Kendeda Building for Innovative Sustainable Design Add To My Calendar Future U. Live Podcast Event On the third stop of its nationwide campus tour, the Future U. podcast will visit Atlanta to meet with the presidents and communities of both Georgia Tech and Emory University. Georgia Tech President Ángel Cabrera and Emory University President Gregory Fenves will share their insights on the future of higher education in a post-pandemic world, followed by a panel discussion with faculty and staff from both institutions. Future U co-hosts Michael Horn and Jeff Selingo will be joined by guest host Aimée Eubanks Davis, founder and CEO of Braven, a nonprofit dedicated to bridging the education-to-employment gap faced by some of the nation’s most promising young leaders.

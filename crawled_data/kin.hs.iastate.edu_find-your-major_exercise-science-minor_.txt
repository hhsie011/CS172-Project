This minor develops a background in fitness assessment, prescription, and exercise leadership. Required courses (12 credits) KIN 258 – Physical Fitness and Conditioning KIN 358 – Physiology of Exercise KIN 366 – Exercise Psychology KIN 458 – Principles of Fitness Assessment and Exercise Prescription Five credits from the following courses A TR 220 – Basic Athletic Training KIN 259 – Leadership Techniques for Fitness Programs KIN 266 – Advanced Strength Training and Conditioning KIN 345 – Management of Health-Fitness Programs & Facilities KIN 360 – Sociology of Sport and Exercise KIN 462 – Medical Aspects of Exercise KIN 467 – Exercise and Health Behavior Change Ready to Get Moving?

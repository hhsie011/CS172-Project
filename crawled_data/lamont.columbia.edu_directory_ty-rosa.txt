Reporting to the Associate Dean for Research, Ty partners with research faculty to develop innovative and high-impact grant proposals that support the School's long-term research vision. Prior to joining the Climate School, Ty was the Assistant Director of Research Proposal Development within the Office of the Executive Vice President for Research. Within that role, she led the submission of half a dozen center grant proposals (>$10M) across all University schools and various scientific disciplines. Prior to joining Columbia, Ty was the Director of Product Marketing and Customer Experience for The Real Estate Board of New York’s Residential Listing Service. Within that role, Ty worked closely with 16,000 residential agents and brokers on initiatives to advance New York City’s residential real estate market. Prior to her time at The Real Estate Board, Ty was a Senior Associate at Landmark Ventures, a boutique venture capital and venture development firm, where she worked with clients in the marketing technology space.

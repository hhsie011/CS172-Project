Recent posts of interest View RSS feed HARVARD UNIVERSITY SCHOLARSHIP AND DISCUSSION ON UKRAINE Harvard Ukrainian Research Institute Current and historical information on Ukraine, including a page dedicated to covering the Russia-Ukraine War, with helpful links to resources. Davis Center for Russian and Eurasian Studies Insights on Russia and Ukraine from Harvard Faculty of Arts & Sciences Davis Center for Russian and Eurasian Studies. Harvard Kennedy School Insights & Analysis on Russia-Ukraine War From geopolitics to sanctions to the role of misinformation and cyberattacks, Harvard Kennedy School scholars analyze the Russian invasion of Ukraine and what it means for the world. CrisisReady Map Analyses: Tracking Population Density Changes Near Western Ukraine Data map analyses showing the evolving changes of population densities along the western border of the country. This includes population data from Poland, Hungary, Slovakia, and Romania. Other variables, such as data from Meta’s “Social Connectedness Index,” provide additional context to the patterns shaping population density and movement flows. These data maps provide important insights on the displacement dynamics and the changing dispersal patterns of Ukrainian refugees. Harvard Library: Ukraine in the World Explore the history of Ukraine and Ukrainians throughout the world drawing from Harvard's collections in Ukrainian literature, politics, history, and more BACKGROUND ON UKRAINE AND RUSSIA EMIS (Emerging Markets Information Systems) Includes country profiles; macroeconomic statistics, forecasts, and analysis; reports on financial markets, companies, and industries; exchange rates; analyst reports; and business news. Economist Intelligence Unit In-depth country analysis and data, including descriptions of the politics, economy, risk, regulation, and business environment of a country. Congressional Research Service Reports In-depth research reports and briefings prepared for Congress by Congressional Research Services. Search for Ukraine, limit by date if desired. Foreign Affairs In-depth analysis and debate of foreign policy, geopolitics and global affairs. Council on Foreign Relations Think tank and publisher focusing on US foreign policy and international affairs. NEWS SOURCES Ukrainecast BBC podcast monitoring the situation in Ukraine. Internet Archive: Ukraine Conflict This collection seeks to document conflict in Ukraine. Contributions were made by Archive-It and subject matter experts from Stanford University, Library of Congress, Global Investigative News Network, and Harvard Ukrainian Research Institute. Content includes news outlets, social media, blogs, and government websites. Russia Ukraine Monitor Map Crowdsourced effort by Centre for Information Resilience and other partners in the wider open source community to map, document and verify significant incidents during the conflict in Ukraine.

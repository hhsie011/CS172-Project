Student Organizations in CEPR Department News Contact Us Home Counseling, Educational Psychology & Research Counseling Post-bac Disability Studies Certificate Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Disability Studies The Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Disability Studies provides diverse training for professionals who work with people with disabilities. It provides students with a comprehensive background on the physical, psychosocial, vocation and legal issues that people with disabilities face on a day to day basis. Concentration Coordinator Chrisann Schiro-Geist, Ph.D., CRC Bldg. 29, Room 119 901.678.4303 cschrgst@memphis.edu Certificate Requirements The Post-baccalaureate Certificate in Disability Studies requires successful completion of 12-hours of study. Three courses, Counseling 6913, 6901, and UNIV 7400 are required. Students may select one elective course from the list below to complete the Certificate Requirements.

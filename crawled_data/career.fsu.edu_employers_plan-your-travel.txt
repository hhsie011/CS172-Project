Brand Partner with the Career Center Plan Your Travel On-Campus Recruiting Information Sessions Plan Your Travel Visiting The Career Center at Florida State University Attending a Career Fair or visiting the FSU campus is one of the best ways to reach and recruit FSU students. Here are some tips and resources for planning your trip: Directions to The Career Center The Career Center is located in the Dunlap Student Success Center (DSC) at: 100 South Woodward Avenue Tallahassee, FL 32306-4162 Driving Directions from the Tallahassee Regional Airport to Parking for the Dunlap Success Center Turn right out of the airport onto Capital Circle. Turn left on Springhill Road. Springhill Road becomes Lake Bradford Road. Turn right onto W Gaines St. Take the left exit around the roundabout onto Woodward Avenue and continue until it ends (DSC will be on the left at the corner of Woodward and Traditions). Curve right on Traditions Way. Continue past the parking garage and turn left into the gated parking area. Please park under a yellow Career Center sign. Place the permit on your dash, where it must remain visible for the entirety of your visit. Walking Directions from Parking to Dunlap Success Center Take the walkway in between the Central Utilities Plant and the Student Services Building. Turn left when you come to a large brick walkway. Walk past the Student Services Building on your left. Turn right when you reach Traditions Way. Cross Traditions Way to the Dunlap Success Center on the corner. Enter through the front door, and take the elevator to the third floor. Career Fair Travel Information Seminole Futures is located at the Donald L. Tucker Civic Center, 505 West Pensacola Street, Tallahassee, Florida 32301. STEM Career & Internship Fair is located at the FAMU-FSU College of Engineering, 2525 Pottsdamer Street, Tallahassee, Florida 32310. Our other career fairs and events are held on campus. For specific location information, please view the Employer Handbook . If you have any questions about career fair locations, directions, or parking, please call 850.644.4023. During your visit, consider experiencing one or more of the following: Dining, Nightlife, and the Outdoors World-Class Performances by FSU Music and Theatre Information and Tickets for FSU Athletics We want your trip to Tallahassee to be as successful as possible.

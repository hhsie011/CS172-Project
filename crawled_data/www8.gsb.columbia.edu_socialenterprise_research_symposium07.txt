Topics included the existing state of borrowing rates, default rates, and the methods used for financing and risk management of micro-loans, which is a specific method for lending to poor. In addition to informing the participants about the existing state-of-the art practices in microfinance, the symposium also identified major research questions facing this area in the years to come, and resulted in a publication: Microfinance: Emerging Trends and Challenges, edited by Suresh Sundaresan, the Chase Manhattan Bank Professor of Finance and Economics at Columbia Business School. Ray Rahman, Co-founder of MF Analytics and the Founding Partner of RSA Capital with Saif Shah Mohammed, Co-founder of MF Analytics: Securitization of BRAC – “Micro-Credit Backed Security (MCBS)”; lessons for managing risks using capital markets (download PDF).

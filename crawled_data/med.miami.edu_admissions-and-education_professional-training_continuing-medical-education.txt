Alumni Giving Home Admissions and Education Professional Training Continuing Medical Education Continuing Medical Education Menu Professional Training Professional Training Certifications Continuing Medical Education Observerships As a medical professional, you never stop learning. In order to best serve your patients, you must keep up with new medical advancements and discoveries. It is the goal of the University of Miami Miller School of Medicine to help you enhance the quality care you already provide through cutting-edge educational formats and the latest in emerging health care needs. Our continuing medical education provides physicians with relevant, effective, practice-based learning that supports US health care quality improvement. It is offered through the following formats: CME Managed Courses (conferences and symposia) Department Managed Courses (conferences and symposia) Regularly Scheduled Series (Grand Rounds, etc.) Enduring Materials (internet online courses) We were surveyed by the Accreditation Council for Continuing Medical Education (ACCME) in 2018 and awarded four years as an accredited provider of continuing medical education for physicians.

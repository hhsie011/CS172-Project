Start Over Polychrome pot with yellow birds separated by double red lines Creator Unknown, artist. Title Polychrome pot with yellow birds separated by double red lines. Produced New Mexico, United States, ca. 1920. Physical Properties 1 Pot ; H. 10 x W. 12 x D. 12 in. (22.5x 30.5 x 30.5 cm) Clay, pigment. Description Hand coiled pottery jar with matte painted polychrome designs. Wide mouth, short sloping neck, high shoulders and barrel body, medium base. Design consists of two yellow Zia birds and two yellow parrots separated by undulating double red band against cream slip. Various foliate and feather motifs suround birds. Fire clouds on surface. Exhibitions Animalia (Avery Library, Wallach Study Center, June 12-September 13, 2019) Credit Line Gift of Stanley B. and Caroline Stein.

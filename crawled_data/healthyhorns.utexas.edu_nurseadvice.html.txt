Medical Emergencies and After-Hours Care The UHS Nurse Advice Line is available only to UT Austin students and is staffed 24 hours a day, every day of the year. Call for advice about how to care for an illness or injury at home or to receive guidance about whether and how soon you should see a healthcare provider. UHS nurses answer the Advice Line weekdays from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. When UHS is closed, St. David’s Hospital triage nurses answer the Advice Line and can provide advice as noted above. However, they cannot schedule UHS appointments, refer you to specific facilities or help you with medication refills or administrative issues.

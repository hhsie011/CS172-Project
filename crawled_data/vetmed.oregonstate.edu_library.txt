University Research Office Directory Donate Contact Us Giving Explore Giving Options Commitment to Privacy You are here Home » Academics McDowell Veterinary Library The McDowell Veterinary Library serves the faculty, students and staff of the Carlson College of Veterinary Medicine at Oregon State University in addition to the rest of the OSU community, practicing veterinarians and the general public. The library’s collection features print and electronic resources covering veterinary medicine and selected materials on human medicine, comparative medicine and animal sciences.

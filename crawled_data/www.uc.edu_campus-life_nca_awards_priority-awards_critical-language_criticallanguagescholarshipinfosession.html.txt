Language Scholarship Info Session Critical Language Scholarship @ UC Official CLS Website Register for the Info Session NCA Learning Series 2021-2022 Tuesday, October 5th @ 12:30PM via Zoom. The Critical Language Scholarship (CLS) Program is a summer study abroad opportunity for American university students to learn languages essential to America's engagement with the world. A representative from the Critical Language Scholarship Program will be joining us remotely to provide an information session that is exclusively for UC students. This is an excellent opportunity to hear directly from a scholarship rep and to get your questions answered. Eligibility: Undergraduate and Graduate students (U.S. citizens) with a 3.0+ GPA; session open to all. Email nca@uc.edu with questions.

CVN provides working professionals worldwide an opportunity to advance their knowledge in engineering and applied science disciplines, and gain industry-relevant skills from world-renown Columbia Engineering faculty. Accepted MS students have up to one year to begin their coursework and can start in the Fall, Spring, and Summer terms. Applications are reviewed on a rolling admissions basis. Course Offerings & Degree Programs Explore our online graduate degree, certificate, and non-degree programs in the fields of: Applied Mathematics, Applied Physics, Biomedical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, Earth and Environmental Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Materials Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Operations Research. Program Application Process To be eligible for admission consideration, graduate school applicants must meet specific requirements and provide documents including recommendation letters; GRE, TOEFL, or IELTS test results; transcripts and an application. Learn more about the online program application process. Fees Fees are based on the academic program and typically are equal to tuition plus a CVN fee. Tuition and fees are adjusted annually. Executive Education Take advantage of Columbia Engineering’s rich selection of non-credit courses in a number of engineering fields that will help professionals upskill, apply knowledge towards their work, and advance in their careers. Our Executive Education portfolio includes the following offerings: Boot Camps Online executive education courses MOOCs through edX and Coursera Custom programs for your organization Columbia Engineering Executive Education also offers an online Artificial Intelligence (AI) program that empowers forward-thinking team leaders and technical managers to integrate AI strategies in their organizations.

She is focused on researcher professional development and is responsible for designing programs around building and cultivating leadership skills to empower the researchers at The Climate School to take advantage of greater funding opportunities. Prior to joining The Climate School in 2021, Natalie worked at the Zuckerman Institute for Mind Brain Behavior on institute-wide scientific programming and mentoring efforts. She charted the course for the Columbia Neuroscience Seminar Series through the transition from in-person to virtual events, setting program best practices and organizational processes in place and launched the Zuckerman Institute Affiliate Members Program.

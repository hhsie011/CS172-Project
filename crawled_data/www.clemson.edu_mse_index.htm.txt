Revolutionize. Materials scientists and engineers design, develop and produce traditional and advanced materials for diverse applications in many industries. The curriculum develops skills in problem-solving, engineering analysis and design, as well as communication. The Department builds in students a broad foundation in the science & engineering of materials, preparing students for careers in industry and graduate school.

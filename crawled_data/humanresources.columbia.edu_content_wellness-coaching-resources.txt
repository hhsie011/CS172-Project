From personalized coaching to weight loss support to mental health counseling, wellness professionals and programs are available to guide you and your family in setting goals and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Expand all Collapse all Coaching & Resources Wellness Coaching A knowledgable coach can help you maintain lasting results. UHC coaches are professionals with an average of seven years experience. You can connect with coaches at your convenience. Connect one-on-one with a coach who can make recommendations and help create an action plan to guide your progress. Call a Wellness Coach: 1-800-478-1057 Coaches can help you with: Sleeping problems Nutrition and healthy meals Stress release and mediation Weight loss Exercise and fitness planning Diabetes support Quitting smoking Wellness for the whole family Live & Work Well Portal UHC's Live and Work Well This online portal offers a variety of wellness self-help services, interactive tools and action-oriented advice to help you take steps toward feeling healthier, happier and more in control of your career, finances and well-being. Locate wellness providers, discover community and work-life resources near you and quickly and confidentially connect to expert guidance on conditions and situations that concern you. Resources Include: Crisis support hotline Alcohol and substance abuse support Personalized wellness kits Go to Live and Work Well Self-care App Sanvello App This self-care app is designed to help manage and diffuse stress, anxiety, and depression though a variety of tools and coaching techniques, including progress tracking. Get the support you need to help improve your mental health. Resources Include: Meditation activities Mindfulness activities Healthy habits tracking tool Premium services are free to UHC members.

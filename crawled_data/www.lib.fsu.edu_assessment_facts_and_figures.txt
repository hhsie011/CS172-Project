Annually we report on collections and their circulation (digital and physical), expenditures, staffing, gate counts, reference, and instruction statistics. We report statistics by fiscal year (July 1 to June 30 the following year) to the Association of Research Libraries (ARL), Association for College & Research Libraries (ACRL) and to the Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System (IPEDS) American Libraries (AL) survey.

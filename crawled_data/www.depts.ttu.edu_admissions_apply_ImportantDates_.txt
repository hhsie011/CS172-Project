Spring 2022 Priority Application Deadline Freshman/Transfer – November 1, 2021 Summer 2022 Priority Application Deadline Freshman/Transfer – May 2, 2022 Fall 2022 Priority Application Deadline Freshman – December 1, 2021 Transfer – May 2, 2022 GENERAL SCHOLARSHIP DEADLINES Scholarships based on academics, financial need, academic interest and other criteria are awarded based on admissions application. No additional scholarship application is needed. Spring 2021 General Scholarship Deadline Transfer – November 1, 2021 Fall 2022 Priority Scholarship Deadline Freshmen - December 1, 2021 Transfer – January 1, 2022 IMPORTANT FAFSA DATES At Texas Tech University, financial aid eligibility is based on financial and household information that is reported on the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). The Texas Application for State Financial Aid (TASFA) is available for all non-US citizens who are Texas residents. FAFSA Available – October 1, 2021 www.fafsa.ed.gov FAFSA Priority Date – January 15, 2022 Apply for financial aid by this date to ensure maximum eligibility. TASFA Priority Date - January 15, 2022 www.collegeforalltexans.com Have questions? Our staff is here to help.

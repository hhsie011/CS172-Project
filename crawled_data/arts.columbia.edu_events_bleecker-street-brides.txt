SEE THE FULL 2022 NEW PLAYS FESTIVAL SCHEDULE About the Artist Tatiana Kouguell-Hoell is a recent MFA Playwriting graduate of Columbia University. She previously studied at Purchase College, the University of Amsterdam and Prague Film School. Her original plays have been performed at The Tank Theater, Lenfest Center for the Arts, Studio Theater, the Stella Adler Studio for Acting, and Chateau Orquevaux in France, where she was awarded the Denis Diderot (A-I-R) Grant as an Artist-In-Residence. She lives in New York City.

Frank Barton School of Business Finance, Real Estate and Decision Sciences The undergraduate certificate in insurance from Wichita State's AACSB-accredited Barton School of Business will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in various careers in the insurance industry. The certificate is a valuable asset to any business major. Learn how Insurance is the right fit for you. Contact coordinator at stan.longhofer@wichita.edu Curriculum This 12-credit-hour certificate consists of one required course and three additional courses chosen from the following: Insurance and Risk Management (required) Money and Banking Investments Fixed Income Securities and Markets Bank and Financial Investment Analysis Future and Options Markets Selling and Sales Force Management View the Curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

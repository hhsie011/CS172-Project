Click here to learn more Biostatistics at Brown Welcome to Brown University's School of Public Health Department of Biostatistics. Biostatistics is a highly dynamic and rapidly expanding field of study. By developing new quantitative methods and making innovative applications to substantive and demanding scientific problems, biostatisticians play a central role in the advancement of biomedical and biotechnology and in public health research. Demand for advanced expertise in biostatistics continues to be high in academia and in the public and private sectors. Brown University's Department of Biostatistics educates and trains our future biostatisticians.

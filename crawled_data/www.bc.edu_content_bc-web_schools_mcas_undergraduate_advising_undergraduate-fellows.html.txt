Requirements The Advising Process Major & Minor Requirements Pre-Health Program Undergraduate Fellows Academic Policies Contact Undergraduate Fellows The Undergraduate Advising Fellows are a team of peer mentors who serve as additional resources for all MCAS students throughout the academic year. Our highly trained Advising Fellows help students with a range of issues, including course registration, EagleApps, extracurricular involvement opportunities, academic planning, and transitioning to college. You can schedule a 30 minute appointment with an Advising Fellow. These appointments are great if you want to discuss how to explore majors/minors, how to read your degree audit, and/or learn how to start building a four-year plan. For the Spring 2022 semester, Advising Fellows will begin taking appointments starting February 1, 2022. Schedule an Appointment: Click on the "Schedule 30-Min Appointment" button to schedule your meeting. All appointments take place in-person in Stokes S132 or if you choose a virtual meeting, they will take place over Google Meet. Drop-In Hours for Course Registration: The Fellows will be hosting drop-in hours during course registration for the Fall 2022 semester. Drop-in dates and times are available on the AAC front page.

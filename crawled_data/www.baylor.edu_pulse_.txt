Skip to main content Skip to main navigation The Pulse Menu Baylor>The Pulse The Pulse Honors College BIC Great Texts Honors Program University Scholars Welcome to The Pulse, Baylor University's undergraduate scholarly publication. We promote academic writing of the highest caliber from Baylor undergraduate students of all disciplines. The Pulse is fully edited and managed by Honors College students who are passionate about encouraging undergraduate scholarship and expanding the university’s academic network. Interested in learning more? We invite you to read our past editions and enjoy some of the best undergraduate scholarship at Baylor. Motivated by our mission? Consider submitting a paper or applying for editorship. We look forward to collaborating with you!

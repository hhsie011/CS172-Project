Master of Science in IT & Management The MSITM is a 10-month, STEM-designated program that teaches you to lead disruptive innovations using emerging technologies, such as deep learning and blockchain. Sign Up for More Information Learn More Speak with Admissions Attend an Info Session View MSITM Factsheet Earn an In-Demand Degree in 10 Months Information technology now continually alters the experience of business, social, and even physical interactions worldwide. Big Data and the Internet of Things are no longer catch-phrases but real-world trends that impact multi-trillion-dollar transactions. Unemployment rates in the IT industry are at historic lows, and many high-paying jobs are found within the IT sector. Learn Practical Skills from Top Academics The MSITM degree is taught by industry-leading faculty in small classes.

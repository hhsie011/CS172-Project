Exclusion to Opportunity Events Past Event Women, Work, and the Web: From Exclusion to Opportunity Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo June 28, 2019 12:00 PM - 5:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Mumbai, 12-13, Maker Chambers VI, First floor, Jamnalal Bajaj Road, Nariman Point, Mumbai 400 021 Columbia Global Centers | Mumbai is organizing a one-day workshop on June 28, 2019 from 12 p.m. to 5 p.m., addressing digital technology and its impact on the participation of women in the workforce in India. The workshop will address key research findings and engage with initiatives that use digital technologies to strengthen women’s participation in the workforce. Presenters will identify barriers to women’s participation in the digital space, discuss digital platforms that provide information and resources for women to create safe and inclusive spaces, and probe initiatives that foster opportunities for professional women in India’s urban workplaces. Featured Participants include: Anu Madgavkar, Partner, McKinsey Global Institute Anjali Shenoi, Program Coordinator, Centre for Communication and Development Studies Karla Bookman, Founder, The Swaddle Antony Alex, Co-founder, Rainmaker Gunjan Khandelwal, General Manager, ThoughtWorks India Sairee Chahal, Founder and CEO, Sheroes This workshop is by invitation only. If you are working substantially at the interface of gender, work, and technology, and would like to attend the workshop, please write to us at [email protected] for an invite.

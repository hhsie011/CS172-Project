We are a student-focused and research-intense department, offering a range of multidisciplinary coursework and academic programs. Plant & Soil Science faculty conduct research from the cellular level to large-scale ecosystems and post-production processes, and they are committed to helping you reach your educational goals. If you have any questions about our department, are a prospective student, or want to visit with our faculty, we would be delighted to hear from you.

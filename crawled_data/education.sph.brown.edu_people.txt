The Office of Academic and Student Affairs administers academic programs, curriculum, and student affairs & services at the School. The team is led by Associate Dean for Education, Melissa Clark. The staff work with faculty, SPH & University deans and staff, and students toward delivery of accurate information, efficient operations, and exemplary service. Who We Are Melissa A.

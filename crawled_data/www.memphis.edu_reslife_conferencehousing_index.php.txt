InstagramFacebooktwitter Home Housing and Residence Life Conference Housing Summer Conference Housing Conference housing accommodations are available from early June through early August for on-campus groups, individuals & interns and off-campus groups seeking affordable housing. Accommodations Extra long twin-sized beds, individual desks with chairs, and spacious closets Air-conditioned facilities with in-room thermostats in some halls Single occupancy rooms and apartments for groups Conveniently located vending machines and spacious kitchenettes Common areas and lounges with comfortable couches and lounge chairs Classrooms and learning labs are available Front desk is staffed 24 hours a day, seven days a week, to assist you during your stay. For more information: Interns, individuals OR University of Memphis groups looking for summer housing options can find registration and information in the submenu to the left or above (mobile).

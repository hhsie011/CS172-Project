The certificate was developed for both emerging and seasoned leaders in professional organizations to help boost their careers through education and self-discovery. With most of the courses taking place on the Georgia Tech-Savannah campus, enrollees participate in group learning within small, diverse groups of other executives from a range of industries. The program is comprised of eight courses, including “Relating to Others,” “Meeting Goals through Focused Performance Management,” and “Financial Decision Making.” Upon successful completion of all courses at the end of the program, participants will be better equipped to facilitate business operations and face unexpected challenges as leaders in the modern economy.

Please note: To simplify our customer experience, the Heights Express name has been discontinued. All services remain unchanged and are offered through Heights Catering. Full Service Heights Catering's award-winning staff offers waited meals, buffet dinner options, seasonal packages, cocktail reception menus, chef's stations, and much more to suit any type of gathering. View Menus Drop-Off Breakfast & Lunch Heights Catering provides free direct drop-off service for your next meeting or event. Available only to Boston College faculty and staff. Log in to Agora Portal and select "Heights Catering - Drop Off Service" under Food Options to place your order.

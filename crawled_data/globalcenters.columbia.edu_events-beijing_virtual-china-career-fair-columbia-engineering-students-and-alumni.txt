Columbia Engineering Students and Alumni Events Past Event Virtual China Career Fair for Columbia Engineering Students and Alumni Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo January 6, 2021 - January 7, 2021 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM Online Time: 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM, January 6-7 (Beijing) | 7:30 - 11:00 PM, January 5-6 (New York) Join us for the virtual career fair exclusive for Columbia Engineering's graduate students and alumni. During this two-day event, you will have real-time engagement with recruiters from reputable China-based companies, inquire about positions and requirements, and gain in-depth information about a particular field or industry. Event Highlights: Participating employers will recruit for full-time, part-time positions, internship, and summer positions More than 20 companies from seven+ industries will join the event, including financial engineering, investment, bioengineering, artificial intelligence, internet technology, business analytics, and consulting. Employers and industry leaders will go live to provide important information about the company and positions, requirements, and application process.

Skip To Main Content School of Education Search the website Open Search Box College of Human Sciences Open Mobile Menu Search the website Open Search Box Annual Reports Home> Current Students> Educator Preparation Program> Resources> Annual Reports Iowa State University Educator Preparation Annual Reports As a state-approved educator preparation program in Iowa, Iowa State University is required to complete two annual reports each spring. The annual reports summarize unit-wide data from September 1–August 31 of the previous academic year. One report is the state-level report: State of Iowa Department of Education Annual Description of Data and Results Report of Educator Preparation. The other report is a federal report: Title II Higher Education Act Institutional Report Card.

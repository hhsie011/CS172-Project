Commencement All Commencement ceremonies will take place at the Watsco Center located on the University's Coral Gables campus. Each ceremony will last approximately 2.5 - 3 hours, depending on the total number of graduates attending. Welcome Commencement is a special time at the University of Miami. Our graduates have worked hard, and commencement is our time to celebrate their achievements. Here you will find almost everything you may need to know about the Commencement ceremonies, and where to learn more.

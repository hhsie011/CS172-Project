D-CARES is a student organization that acts as a support and activism group for disabled students at TU. Anyone and everyone is free to use this group as a resource for support from fellow students. Our mission is to promote inclusion, offering a safe space for connections, advocacy, resources, education, and support to the TU community. The group is open to all disabled students and allies. If you are interested in participating and receiving information, please contact President Hannah Phillips at hlp1933@utulsa.edu. Want to join the conversation?

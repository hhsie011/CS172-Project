Share files up to 3GB with the Noles File Transfer (NiFTy) Adobe Creative Cloud Complimentary access to leading creative apps, including Photoshop, Illustrator and Dreamweaver Computer Backup Unlimited, cloud-based storage for important files at work and at home Cybersecurity Tips Helpful tips to protect yourself from phishing attacks Microsoft Office 365* Free access to Microsoft programs and services such as OneDrive for Business, Office Online, Skype for Business and more Stay Secure Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) If you regularly use 2-factor authentication (2FA) to access FSU applications such as myFSU HR or Office 365 as part of your job, make sure you have authorized a device other than your desk phone for 2FA. ITS suggests adding your mobile device and installing the Duo app to ensure you have 2FA access anywhere, anytime.

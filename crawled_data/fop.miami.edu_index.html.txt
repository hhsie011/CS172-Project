We support the University's mission by providing a wide array of services and solutions. You will find the entry page for each of our departments below. Each department is comprised of talented, knowledgeable individuals, all of whom are committed to our values and mission. Campus Planning & Development Campus Planning and Development provides expertise in master planning, land use, development regulations, mobility initiatives, historic preservation, and campus urban design. The department provides strategic analysis and direction for future development in compliance with regulations throughout our campuses. Learn more Design & Construction Design and Construction provides a variety of services and expertise in the management of capital improvement projects ranging from minor renovations to major new construction projects for all University campuses. Learn more Environmental Health & Safety Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) provides regulatory compliance guidance in the areas of occupational and environmental health and safety, through disseminating pertinent information, offering training opportunities and making available technical and professional assistance on related fields in support of, and to enhance the University's commitment to provide a safe and healthy workplace for its faculty, staff, and students. Learn more Facilities & Operations Facilities and Operations is responsible for the stewardship of University facilities on the Coral Gables and RSMAS campuses. This department is responsible for building maintenance, access control, energy management, campus infrastructure, fire and security systems, utilities distribution, grounds, and custodial services. Learn more Office of Emergency Management The Office of Emergency Management is responsible for emergency preparedness, disaster recovery, and business continuity planning for all campuses. Learn more Parking & Transportation Parking and Transportation provides parking services to customers in a safe, effective manner. Their services include parking and transportation operations, customer service, mobility strategies, and special events. Learn more Real Estate Real Estate is responsible for the University's overall real estate strategy, including acquisition and disposition of real estate assets, enterprise-wide leasing strategies, and management of University owned real estate property. Learn more Space Planning & Analysis Space Planning and Analysis strives to be a professional, customer focused, responsive, and effective provider of space management services in a cooperative framework. Our objective is to provide support to the strategic planning processes by supplying accurate and timely information, analysis, and advice to inform decision-making and re-allocation processes.

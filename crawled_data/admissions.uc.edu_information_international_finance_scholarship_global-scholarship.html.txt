If a students seeks to increase their total scholarship award up to $5,000, now is the time to take action. Scholarship Application Scholarship Requirements Completed math test requirement with SAT, ACT, or Math Placement Test (MPT) Student is not a U.S. citizen or permanent resident Deadline to Apply Submit the the UC Global Scholarship Application before March 31, 2022 at 11:59 p.m. ET. Decision Process The Scholarship Committee will complete a holistic review of your Common App application and materials. A decision will be emailed to applicants between April 1 and April 8.

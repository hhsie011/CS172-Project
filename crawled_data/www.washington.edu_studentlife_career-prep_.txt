How can you leverage your part-time work experience to land an internship or job? As you consider your next steps, we can help you map out pathways and make the most of your Husky Experience. You’ll find plenty of resources and guidance at the Career & Internship Center and within your academic department. Explore & prepare Get coaching and counseling, attend workshops and career fairs, search the Handshake database and access a variety of online resources through the Career & Internship Center. Check out the Career & Internship Center Work on campus Campus is a vibrant place with a variety of job opportunities to match almost any interest. Learn how to apply for jobs and articulate the skills you gain from student employment. Finding & maximizing campus jobs Find departmental resources Many academic departments have dedicated staff, career resources and job fairs that are specific to their fields.

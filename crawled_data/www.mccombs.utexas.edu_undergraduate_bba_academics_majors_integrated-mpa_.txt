The integrated Master in Professional Accounting program is a five-year undergraduate/graduate program that allows you to earn both a Bachelor in Business Administration and a Master in Professional Accounting. Explore iMPA Join the MPA Family Apply to the iMPA program at the end of your sophomore year and earn your graduate degree from the top-ranked accounting program in the nation. Students planning on pursuing the iMPA will follow the Accounting four-year plan during their first two years before admission into the program. View the Degree Plan View iMPA Admissions See What You’ll Learn iMPA students learn from leading faculty and gain in-demand skills, including: How to prepare and examine financial records, financial analysis techniques, profound knowledge of business transactions, how to present financial statements to assist in decision making, how to ensure that financial statements comply with laws/regulations, tax return preparation, and much more. View MPA Curriculum See the Jobs You Can Get iMPA students excel across many industries and often pursue these careers: Accountant, Associate Consultant, Investment Banking Analyst, Financial Analyst, Staff Auditor, and Tax Consultant Discover Accounting Careers See What Students Say I love the challenging nature of the coursework in the MPA program. Most classes are based around cases, which allows students to apply their academic knowledge to real-world simulations.

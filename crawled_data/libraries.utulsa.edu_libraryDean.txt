McFarlin Library exists to enrich the academic experience of TU students and faculty by providing access to our wide-ranging collection of electronic and print materials. Our dedicated and knowledgeable staff are here to help you with your research needs, including navigation of the rapidly expanding universe of information resources available on the Internet. We encourage you to stop by McFarlin Library soon. Adrian W. Alexander, MLS, CAS R.

HKS Magazine Smarter Cities An array of initiatives across Harvard Kennedy School has advanced research, teaching, and field studies to help strengthen all facets of local governance. These projects are now spread through many of the School’s research centers, and they stretch far beyond Greater Boston, across the country, and indeed around the world.

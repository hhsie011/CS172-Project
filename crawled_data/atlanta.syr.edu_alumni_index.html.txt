Join the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Atlanta If you’re looking for Orange in the Atlanta region, you can find it! The Syracuse University Alumni Club of Atlanta has hundreds of active members and hosts dozens of events throughout the year. Syracuse University alumni clubs around the world draw thousands of grads together to enjoy a wide variety of social and educational programming. Events range from faculty speakers to career networking opportunities, to game-watching events and evenings at the theater. Learn more about the Syracuse University Alumni Club of Atlanta. The Office of Alumni Engagement, schools and colleges, and campus partners all plan a full calendar of events in the area. Be sure to check out our Career and Professional Development events! Young Alumni Engagement Are you an Alum who graduated within the last 10 years? Then you're part of Generation Orange! Being GenO means that you get special discounts on Office of Alumni Engagement events and unique opportunities for special programs designed to help you on your emerging life and career post 'Cuse! Learn more about Generation Orange programs. Career Services Just because you're not a student doesn't mean you can't utilize Syracuse University's vast Career Services network! Learn all the ways we can help you advance your career. Be sure to check out our Career and Professional Development events! Update Your Info We love to hear from you and all you're doing in the area as we look to connect students and alumni within this Orange network. The best way to make sure you can connect for mentoring or hiring opportunities. Update your information on Cuse Community! Have a Cuse Community Account or want to get one? Go to cusecommunity.syr.edu, and click "Sign In." If you want to set up an account, click First Time Login. Don't want a Cuse Community Account but still want to update? Go to alumniupdate.syr.edu and update your address so we know you're in the area! We'll start sending you information on regional programs!

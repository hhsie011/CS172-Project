COVID-19 Vaccine Explore the Campus History Community Commitments Campus Construction and Opportunities Columbia | Neighbors You are here: Home News Sixth Annual Back-2-School Event Distributes Over 750 Backpacks to Harlem Students Education Sixth Annual Back-2-School Event Distributes Over 750 Backpacks to Harlem Students By Columbia Neighbors September 02, 2021 Photo Credit: Pat Lilly (The Forum @ Columbia) As part of the Columbia Community Service (CCS) Back to School Supply Drive, CCS and the Office of Public Safety teamed up to collect school supplies for students in the local community. The supplies were distributed as part of the Sixth Annual Back-2-School community event, which was hosted by 26th Precinct Community Affairs and Manhattan Community Board 9. More than 700 students from Upper Manhattan received books, pens, pencils, rulers, crayons, notebooks, backpacks, calculators, binders, and folders to help them prepare for the 2021-22 school year. The event also featured family programming and other community resources to safely celebrate a new school year.

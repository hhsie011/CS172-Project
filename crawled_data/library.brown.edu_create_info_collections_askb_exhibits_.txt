Brown Military Collection: Exhibitions The military collection periodically mounts exhibitions highlighting various materials in the collection, primarily graphics. These exhibits range from small scale displays to large exhibitions accompanied by events and catalogues. The following is a list of some exhibits mounted since 1995: Artists in Uniform: The World War II Experience (1995) Draner’s Caricatures (1998) A Splendid Little War, 1898: The Artists’ Perspective (1998) Women in War (1999) German Artists in Uniform: The World War II Experience (2000) POW: The Prisoner of War Experience (2002) Peepshows, Panoramas & other fun things: A Potpourri of Unusual Items from the Anne S.

Start Over APA PsycNET Title APA PsycNET. Published Washington, DC : American Psychyological Association. System Details Mode of access: World Wide Web. Subjects Psychology > Databases. Psychology > Periodicals > Databases. Psychology > Indexes > Databases. Psychology > Abstracts > Databases. Psychological tests > Databases. Subjects (Genre) Abstracts. Databases. Indexes. Periodicals. Electronic reference sources. Also Listed Under American Psychological Association, issuing body. Summary Database of streaming psychotherapy demonstrations featuring some of the most renowned therapists in North America working with participants on a host of therapeutic topics. Allows viewers to go straight to the heart of clinical practice with demonstrations of psychotherapy as it is done by today's leading practitioners. Resource for teaching and training in psychotherapy practice and for education about psychology. Consists of PsycINFO, PsycARTICLES, PsycBOOKS, PsycEXTRA, and PsycTESTS: the PsycINFO database is a collection of electronically stored bibliographic references, most with abstracts or content summaries; PsycARTICLES contains the full text of articles from APA journals and EPF (Educational Publishing Foundation) journals; PsycBOOKS provides full-text access to current APA boook publications; PsycEXTRA features gray literature; and PsycTESTS is a database of psychological measurement and instrumentation tools. Access and Use Restricted to subscribing institutions. Language English Language Note Content chiefly in English; search and discovery interface offered in English, German, Spanish, French, and Portuguese.

As an RN-to-BSN student, you'll: Earn your BSN in as little as three semesters Take all courses online Gain leadership skills Get up to 30 credit hours for prior coursework Pay affordable in-state tuition Build a foundation for advanced nursing degrees Learn how nursing - RN to BSN - is the right fit for you. Request Info Start Your Application Applied learning at Wichita State As part of their degree, RN to BSN students complete an applied learning or research experience to graduate from the program. The requirement can be met by completing all required coursework culminating with the Nursing Leadership Practicum for the Practicing RN course. Learn about our innovative approach Admission to the program Registered nurses must apply to WSU and submit official transcripts of courses and records verifying completion of an accredited RN program Admission requirements Curriculum The professional curriculum consists of 31 credit hours, with courses offered in eight-week blocks. Registered nurses receive 30 hours of upper division credit hours for life experience toward the BSN degree by demonstrating successful completion of the NCLEX-RN national exam.

Postdoctoral Fellows Watson Institute Postdoctoral Fellows Program The Watson Institute offers a range of postdoctoral opportunities for outstanding young scholars in the social sciences. The aims of the program are to support participants in their own research, enhance their interdisciplinary literacy and contribute to meaningful and enduring collaborative exchange. We are currently accepting applications via Interfolio http://apply.interfolio.com/44383 Review of applications will begin on October 20, 2017; to receive full consideration applications should be received by that date. Awards will be announced in January 2018. For additional employment opportunities, please visit Jobs @ Brown.

What you need to know Announcements OSU PD assists SPD with arrest May 20, 2022 The OSU PD assisted the SPD with an arrest on May 19 around after 1:30 a.m. The subject ran from police but was apprehended. OSU PD issues Apple AirTag warning March 4, 2022 Concerns are rising that a popular tracking device for valuable items could be used for stalking. Apple AirTags — which weigh less than an ounce and are an inch wide — can track a person’s keys, wallets, purse and more. OSUPD partners with Department of Sociology for 2021 class November 17, 2021 The Oklahoma State University Police Department is offering “Bridging Partnerships through Policing” (SOC 4950) for the spring and fall semesters 2021, introducing students to the realities faced by modern law enforcement. Tips for parking on campus October 30, 2021 Parking on campus can take some getting use to. Spaces close to residential life buildings can be hard to find, or sometimes nonexistent. But, there are options, and enough spaces for everyone to park on campus.

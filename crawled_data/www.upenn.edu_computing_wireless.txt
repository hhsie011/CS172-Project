Wireless service is provided though AirPennNet, AirPennNet-Guest, AirPennNet-Device and eduroam. AirPennNet provides secure authenticated wireless access using PennKey for students, staff, and faculty. The Connect to AirPennNet network assists users in configuring their devices to access AirPennNet.

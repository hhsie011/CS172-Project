Columbia SoCal is pleased to welcome Prof. Omid Malekan, who currently teaches the MBA course “An Introduction to Blockchain and Cryptocurrencies," in advance of his forthcoming book Re-Architecting Trust: the curse of history and the crypto cure for money, markets and platforms. In this virtual talk, Prof. Malekan will provide us a high-level overview of the burgeoning blockchain and cryptocurrency space.

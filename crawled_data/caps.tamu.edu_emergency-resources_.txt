When Texas A&M University classes are in session, HelpLine is available 4:00 p.m. - 8:00 a.m. on weekdays and 24 hours a day on weekends. HelpLine can be reached at 979-845-2700 (V/TTY). HelpLine can provide information, support, and referrals over the phone. If you need to speak to someone face-to-face, go to the nearest hospital emergency room.

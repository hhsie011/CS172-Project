We offer limited weekday tours at 9:30 a.m., with an additional tour on Fridays at 1:30 p.m. In the event of inclement weather, daily campus tours may be delayed or canceled. We will notify you of any changes to your tour. SCHEDULE YOUR TOUR Tailor your #okstate campus tour to your interests! Connect with a representative from your academic area of interest through a majors session, schedule an appointment with the Honors College or meet with the Office of Multicultural Affairs. You can also participate in an optional tour of our athletic facilities, which includes an up-close look of historic Gallagher-Iba Arena, Boone Pickens Stadium and our athletic trophies in Heritage Hall. Length of tours and attire Campus tours last approximately two hours and are led by current OSU students. We encourage you to wear good walking shoes and clothing appropriate for the ever-changing Oklahoma weather. Transfer tours Future transfer students are welcome to participate in on-campus visits. We also offer virtual transfer visit experiences available online on Tuesdays. Visit accommodations Oklahoma State University is dedicated to providing equal access to our guests. If you, or someone in your party, require disability-related accommodations while attending our campus visit program we will make every reasonable effort to provide appropriate accommodations and resources during your visit. Please let us know your needs as soon as possible. Advance notice provides the greatest opportunity to ensure effective accommodations are provided to meet the needs of our guests.

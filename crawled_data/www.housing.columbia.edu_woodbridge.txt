Students either share a single bedroom and use the other room as a common space or use both living spaces as single bedrooms. Data from Room Selection 2021 shows the make-up of this building as 83% seniors, 10% juniors and 7% mixed-point groups (average point value of mixed-point groups was 24). Please keep in mind that class make-up in each building varies year to year based on student preferences. This should not be used as predictive data for the upcoming academic year. Key Features: Close to Riverside Park Versatile living options that allow residents to choose the layout that works best for them: A true one bedroom double, where residents share the bedroom A flex two bedroom, where the living space is used as a bedroom so that each resident has a private room Each apartment has a full kitchen Building Details: Housing Rate: Upperclass Rate Entrance Location: 431 Riverside Drive Number of Residential Floors: 7 Single/Double Rooms within Apartments*: 6 singles / 78 doubles Air Conditioning: No Bathroom Style: One private bathroom per apartment Cleaning Responsibility & Schedule: Bathrooms: Resident responsibility Kitchens: Resident responsibility Trash & Recycling: Residents are responsible for taking their trash and recycling to designated building area Flooring: Mix of hardwood and carpet Furnishings: All bedrooms are furnished Living spaces within apartments are furnished with two dining chairs, a table, and two lounge chairs *One bedroom apartments with double occupancy can be converted to two singles depending on living preferences Building Amenities: Bike Storage: Not available.

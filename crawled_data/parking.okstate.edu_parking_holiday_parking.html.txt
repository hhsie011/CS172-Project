Reserved parking lots, reserved spaces, and all garages are enforced by OSU Police. Please check the official Registrar calendar for specific dates: Independence Day Labor Day Thanksgiving & Black Friday Christmas Eve & Day Winter University Closure New Year's Day Martin Luther King Day Memorial Day Juneteenth Student Holidays Students receive a holiday from class, but the university remains open, and faculty and staff remain at work. During Student breaks, all student surface lots (RH, SZ, GZ, OZ) are enforced as “Overflow” lots. Overflow means a current year parking permit is still required, but all student surface lots are available to permit holders; Park and Ride permit holders are still restricted to lot 120, and all single parking lot (Visual Arts Annex, Fuel Station, etc.) permit holders are restricted to their normal parking lots. All faculty, staff, and family housing surface lots, as well as all garages, meters, reserved spaces, and reserved/restricted lots are enforced as normal. Only student surface parking lots change.

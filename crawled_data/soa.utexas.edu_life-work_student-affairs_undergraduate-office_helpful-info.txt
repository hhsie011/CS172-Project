For students graduating under the 2020-22 catalog Back to Top How will I fulfill Flag Requirements? The majority, if not all, flags a UTSOA student would need are provided by courses required for the degree (B.Arch. students will complete all flag requirements with the courses required for the degree). The courses in the School of Architecture that carry flags are as follows: WRITING [3 FLAGS REQUIRED] ARC 318K World Architecture: Origins to 1750 (required for B.Arch. and BSAS) ARC 318L World Architecture: The Industrial Revolution to Present (required for all SOA students) ARI 368R Interior Design History II (required for BSID) ARC 369J City Architecture (required for B.Arch.) NOTE: BSAS and BSID students may need to obtain the third writing flag through the core curriculum or electives. CULTURAL DIVERSITY IN THE US [1 FLAG REQUIRED] ARC 369J City Architecture (required for B.Arch.) NOTE: BSAS and BSID students may need to obtain the cultural diversity flag through the core curriculum or electives. ETHICS AND LEADERSHIP [1 FLAG REQUIRED] ARC 362 Professional Practice (required for B.Arch.) ARI 362 Interior Design Practice (required for BSID) NOTE: BSAS students may need to obtain the ethics and leadership flag through the core curriculum or electives. GLOBAL CULTURES [1 FLAG REQUIRED] ARC 308 Architecture & Society (required for B.Arch. and BSAS) ARC 318K World Architecture: Origins to 1750 (required for B.Arch. and BSAS) ARC 318L World Architecture: The Industrial Revolution to Present (required for all SOA students) INDEPENDENT INQUIRY [1 FLAG REQUIRED] ARC 323D Intermediate I (required for B.Arch and BSAS) ARC 323E Intermediate Studio II and/or ARC 320G Intermediate Studio IV (required for B.Arch and BSAS) ARC 323F Intermediate Studio III (required for B.Arch and BSAS) ARI 320K Design III (required for BSID) ARI 520L Design IV (required for BSID) ARI 520M Design V (required for BSID) ARI 530T Design VI (required for BSID) QUANTITATIVE REASONING [1 FLAG REQUIRED] ARC 415L Construction II (required for B.Arch. and BSAS) ARC 435R Structures I (required for B.Arch. and BSAS) ARC 435S Structures II (required for B.Arch.) NOTE: BSID students may need to obtain the quantitative reasoning flag through the core curriculum or electives. Back to Top Courses through UT Extension UT offers certain core courses online through UT Extension. These courses are self-paced online courses but will require two exams to be taken at approved testing sites. The cost is the same for all students regardless of Texas residency status.

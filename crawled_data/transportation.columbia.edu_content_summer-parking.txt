All active Columbia University staff may apply; the restriction to administrative officers of grade 14 and higher is lifted during the summer, but the other policy criteria, including geographic eligibility qualifications, still apply. Current students are eligible for 24-hour parking at the Arbor garage in Riverdale. Summer parking is not available for affiliates of Barnard College (except Barnard College faculty residing in Columbia University housing), Teachers College, and other affiliated institutions.

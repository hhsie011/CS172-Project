Young Endowment for Intercollegiate Debate Receives Gift of Real Estate FSU Real Estate Foundation The Florida State University Real Estate Foundation provides expertise and support for all of Florida State University’s real estate needs. We create value for the University by working with it to accept and manage gifts of real estate, facilitate strategic acquisitions of real estate, create and execute long-term strategic plans and develop properties for the benefit of Florida State, its students and the state of Florida. Your gift of real estate to FSU can transform lives—at the University and beyond. Email or call us today to discuss your gift of real estate. The Florida State University Real Estate Foundation is registered as a separate corporation in the state of Florida. The FSU Real Estate Foundation is a tax-exempt public charity, and contributions to the Real Estate Foundation are deductible under section 170 of the Internal Revenue Code.

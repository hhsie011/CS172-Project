Friends, corporate groups, students, clubs, senior groups - all are welcome to enjoy a fun and memorable arts experience at a discount. Groups of 10 or more receive a 10% discount off Georgia Tech Arts Series programs. Groups of 50 or more receive a 15% discount off Georgia Tech Arts Series programs. Plus, the $3 per ticket fee is waived! Pre- and post-show receptions can also be held in the newly renovated lobby of the Ferst Center for your group. For details on bringing a group to an event, please call 404-894-2864.

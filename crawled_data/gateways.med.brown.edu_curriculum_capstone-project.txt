You will be able to choose from a variety of projects offered by your community site. Projects must be approved by both the ScM program director and the site coordinator at your community healthcare site. Goals & Objectives To integrate knowledge from different disciplines and from fieldwork experience To independently explore a focused healthcare topic To "give back" to community by collaborating with a Federally Qualifies Health Center (FQHC) to provide assistance on a quality improvement project of the FQHC's choice and of relevance to patients students have been seeing To develop multiple transferable skills (eg. flexibility, collaboration/teamwork, scientific abstract writing, poster and oral presentation skills, teaching skills) Recent Projects 2018 Capstone Projects 2019 Capstone Projects 2020 Capstone Projects 2022 Capstone Projects View previous capstone projects at the Brown Digital Repository (BDR).

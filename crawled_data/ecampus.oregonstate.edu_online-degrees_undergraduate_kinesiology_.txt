Kinesiology is the study of human movement, and this program’s broad focus examines physical activity across the lifespan, from children through older adults. As a student in the “helping major” of kinesiology, you’ll gain the skills needed to start a career in the health and wellness industry or prepare for professional programs for advanced training. Now is a good time to earn a degree in kinesiology. That's one of six highlights that assistant professor Sean Newsom, Ph.D., an assistant professor shares in a recent interview. Learn from a leading authority on public health This program is offered by Oregon State’s prestigious College of Public Health and Human Sciences, which is accredited by the Council on Education for Public Health. The CPHHS will introduce you to well-rounded public health perspectives that can impact your ability to reach people at all ages and activity levels. Benefits of learning online with Oregon State All classes are developed by OSU's world-class faculty, who are known for their research, expertise and innovation. Oregon State is regionally accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities. Oregon State Ecampus is consistently ranked among the nation’s 10 best providers of online education according to U.S. News & World Report. OSU Ecampus students receive the same diploma as on-campus students. Get the support you need Our goal is to help you succeed.

STEP 02 Submit required documents: $50 reactivation fee If coursework was completed at another institution since leaving Texas Tech: Official transcripts Minimum 2.0 GPA on completed work since leaving Texas Tech If the transcript service you use requests an email address for delivery, please use admissions.transcripts@ttu.edu. SPEEDE and Parchment are preferred digital transcript services. Official mailed transcripts may be sent to: Box 45005, Lubbock, TX 79409. STEP 03 Check your application status! Log into your RaiderConnect account to get an update on your application status, upload documents or register for events hosted by Undergraduate Admissions. RaiderConnect More Information Former Tech students pursuing a second undergraduate degree must submit an application on ApplyTexas and pay the application fee. Any returning student who has completed an undergraduate degree and is returning to take pre-requisite or other non-degree seeking coursework must submit the transient (non-degree seeking) application on ApplyTexas. For complete information on the updated Academic Standing Policy, click here. Academic Suspension Conditions Students may seek readmission after completing their suspension term of one full academic term (fall, spring, summer). If student took coursework elsewhere, a minimum GPA of 2.0 on the transfer work is required. Dean's approval will be required for reinstatement. A withdraw during the term you are readmitted for could result in Academic Dismissal. More information is listed here. Academic Dismissal Conditions Students may seek readmission after completing their dismissal term of one full calendar year. If students took coursework elsewhere, a minimum GPA of 2.0 on the transfer work is required. Dean's approval will be required for reinstatement. A withdraw during the term you are readmitted for could result in Permanent Academic Dismissal. More information is listed here. Permanent Academic Dismissal Conditions Students may seek readmission through Academic Fresh Start after 10 years.

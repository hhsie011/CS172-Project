About Yale School of Medicine Yale School of Medicine educates and nurtures creative leaders in medicine and science, promoting curiosity and critical inquiry in an inclusive environment enriched by diversity. We advance discovery and innovation fostered by partnerships across the university, our local community, and the world. We care for patients with compassion, and commit to improving the health of all people. Yale School of Medicine Mission YSM History, Facts & Figures Yale School of Medicine is one of the world’s leading centers for biomedical research, advanced clinical care, and medical education.

Manual Faculty Senate Hurricane Outreach Accreditation Contact Us Welcome The Executive Vice President for Academic Affairs and Provost is the chief academic officer. The provost oversees and coordinates academic programs, including both teaching and research, and works closely with the president to provide leadership for institutional planning and advancing the educational mission and core values of the University of Miami. The University’s schools and colleges, select centers and institutes, and key offices and administrative units report directly to the provost. Schools and Colleges The University of Miami’s 11 Schools and Colleges, located on three campuses in Coral Gables, Virginia Key, and downtown Miami, offer a myriad of exploration, learning, and challenges to our students.

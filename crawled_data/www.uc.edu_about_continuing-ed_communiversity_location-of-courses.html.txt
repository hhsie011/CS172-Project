We also offer programs at various locations throughout the city, including Sharon Woods Golf Course, throughout our historical neighborhoods, the UC Center for Field Studies, local parks, and the 1819 Innovation Hub First Floor Makerspace. BROWSE COURSE CATALOG Classes at Victory Parkway Campus UC Victory Parkway Campus: 2220 Victory Parkway, Administration Building, Cincinnati OH 45206. The Communiversity office is located on the 2nd floor in Suite 208 with office hours from 9:30 am - 6:30 pm. Located in the historic Edgecliff area, this small campus is centrally located but has a suburban feel and overlooks the Ohio River just east of Eden Park. For classes at off campus locations, you will find the address on the individual course page. Browse our Current Courses for details on your specific course. Parking on Campus Classes at the UC Victory Parkway Campus are held after 5 pm and on weekends. During these times, parking is free in the West Lot at the Administration Building. You may park in the lot facing Victory Parkway or the one on the south side of the building. Please reference the Parking Map for additional details. Parking lots are well lighted. Classes Online In addition to our in-person classes at Communiversity, we offer non-credit online classes available through our partnerhip with ed2go, one of the nation's top online education providers. You can choose from an instructor-led course for six weeks, or a self-paced format with three month access.

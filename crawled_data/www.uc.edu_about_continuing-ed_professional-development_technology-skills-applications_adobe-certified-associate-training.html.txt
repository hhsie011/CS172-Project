Bootcamp Networking Bootcamp Microsoft Excel Certification Project Management Certification (CAPM®) Adobe Certification Courses The Adobe Certified Associate (ACA) is an industry-recognized credential that demonstrates proficiency in Adobe digital skills. Become an Adobe Certified Associate to stand apart from your peers, boost your creative confidence, and expand your career opportunities. Our classes will meet live, online, and biweekly with instructor-led tutorials and demonstrations, complemented with weekly interactive critiques of projects that demonstrate the application of knowledge. Emphasis is placed on each design application modality, terminology and technical skills as they impact visual aesthetics. Project work includes creating scalable graphics for web and print. Join our email list About the Courses Adobe Certified Associate Training for InDesign right arrow down arrow In this intermediate level course, students prepare for the Print & Digital Media Publication Using Adobe InDesign CC certification exam. Classes will meet online and biweekly, with instructor led tutorials and demonstrations, complemented with weekly interactive critiques of projects that demonstrate application of knowledge. Emphasis is placed on publication design, terminology and technical skills as they impact visual aesthetics. Project work includes single and multi-page layouts for digital media and print. This class will build on student’s previous knowledge of Adobe InDesign. Your course fee includes an exam voucher for the Adobe Certified Associate (ACA): Print & Digital Media Publication Using Adobe InDesign 2018. Career Development The University of Cincinnati provides all students as part of our certification programs access to career development tools and services through UC's NEXT Apprenticeship Program, including 1 on 1 career counseling and support. Students who complete our programs gain access to exclusive job boards, resume review tools, personal branding tools, and career guidance including resume reviews, networking, and interview skills.

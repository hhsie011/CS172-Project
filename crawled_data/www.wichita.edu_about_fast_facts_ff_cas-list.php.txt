Education students gain real-world experience with USD 259 and surrounding school districts, with major- and minor-league organizations across the country, and with companies like Walt Disney World. Students have access to more than half a million dollars in scholarships each year, including the Klose Scholarship for majors in math or science education. The Corbin Education Center, designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, is a masterpiece of modern design. The Human Performance Lab gives students hands-on training with the latest technology including metabolic carts, strength dynamometers, DEXA for body composition analysis, postural stability platforms and more. The Kansas Mentor and Induction Center provides training for mentors of early career teachers. WSU’s Play Therapy Center lets students work with, and observe, children playing and interacting with others.

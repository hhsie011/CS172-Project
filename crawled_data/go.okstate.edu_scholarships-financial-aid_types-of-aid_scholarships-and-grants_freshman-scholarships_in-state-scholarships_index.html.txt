Partnered Award Description Value Eligibility Cowboy Covenant- An Oklahoma's Promise Partnership Oklahoma's Promise is a state scholarship program for registered participants that covers tuition at OSU. Cowboy Covenant is a four-year cash award funded by OSU. Full tuition (Oklahoma's Promise) AND $1,000 ($4,000 four-year total) Available to Oklahoma's Promise recipients who are fully admitted or alternatively admitted to OSU and demonstrate financial need (as determined by the FAFSA). See okhighered.org/okpromise/ for details. Cowboy Covenant information. Oklahoma State Regents' Institutional Nominee (OSRHE) A five-year full tuition waiver and four-year cash award funded by the state. Limited number available. Up to $39,900 Qualifying criteria set annually by the State Regents in late fall. See OSU Institutional Nominee information and okhighered.org/academic-scholars for details. Oklahoma State Regents' Automatic Qualifier (OSRHE) A five-year full tuition waiver and four-year cash award funded by the state. Up to $49,100 Based on ACT/SAT scores at or above the 99.5 percentile for OK residents. Additional criteria set annually by the State Regents. See OSU Automatic Qualifier information and okhighered.org/academic-scholars for details. National Merit Scholarship A five-year full tuition waiver and a combination of state, university, and National Merit funding. Up to $68,100 The National Merit Scholarship Corporation determines requirements and eligibility. National Merit finalists must select OSU as their first choice. See nationalmerit.org and OSU National Merit information for details. Students may only receive the award with the highest value they are eligible for from the University Assured Scholarship or Partnered Scholarship categories. Exceptions include Oklahoma's Promise and Cowboy Covenant, which can be combined. University Competitive Award Description Value Eligibility Academic College Scholarships Value and terms vary by academic college and department. Varied Based on the major you indicate on your application for admission. Selection may be based on a number of factors, including test scores and GPA, leadership and community service, essays, interviews and/or auditions. President's Distinguished Scholarship A four-year cash award. Up to $2,500 per year (Up to $10,000 four-year total) Highly competitive selection. Requires a minimum 3.75 GPA, a 27 ACT or 1260 SAT score and a strong record of leadership as indicated on the application for admission. President's Leadership Council (PLC) Scholarship A one-year cash award. Students serve on the President's Leadership Council. $1,500 total Highly competitive selection. Requires a strong record of leadership and community service as indicated on the application for admission. If selected, students may receive either the President's Distinguished Scholarship OR the President's Leadership Council Scholarship. Either award may be paired with other university scholarships.

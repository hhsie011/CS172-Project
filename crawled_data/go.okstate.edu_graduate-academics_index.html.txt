With OSU you have access to competitive fellowships, assistantships, tuition waivers and more. We are committed to developing the person, the scholar and the professional. Advance your education Broaden your horizons through academics and research OSU is a leading contributor to graduate and professional education in Oklahoma with nearly 100 graduate certificates, over 1,250 master's degrees and almost 250 doctoral degrees awarded each year. We also offer the state's only veterinary medicine and osteopathic medicine doctoral degree programs. VIEW ALL PROGRAMS 84Master's programs OSU offers master’s programs in arts, business, education, health, humanities and much more. 48Doctoral programs Find PhD programs in agriculture, education, engineering, sciences and so many more. First steps: how to apply Interested in furthering your education? OSU’s Graduate College oversees the application process for all certificate, master’s and doctoral programs. Admissions deadlines vary by program. Deadlines or contact information can be found on the degree program pages. Many programs have rolling deadlines and accept students year-round. Start your application today!

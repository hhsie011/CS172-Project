Depending upon the time of withdrawal, many may only receive a partial refund or no refund at all. The GradGuardTM Tuition Protection Insurance Plan enhances the schools existing refund policy if a student needs to withdraw any time during the semester. Learn more www.gradguard.com/tuition/uc or call (866) 724-4384 Participation in the Tuition Protection Insurance Plan is offered through Next Generation Insurance, LLC. The University of Cincinnati does not endorse this plan, or any other tuition insurance program. But we encourage you to review the details of this optional program. Should you then decide to participate in any of the programs offered all business is between you and the provider.

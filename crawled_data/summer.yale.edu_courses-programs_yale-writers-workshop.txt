Learn More From First to Final Draft, Find Your Voice at Yale The Yale Writers’ Workshop brings together the experience and expertise of leading teachers, authors, editors, agents and publishers in a series of panels and workshops, for the benefit of writers the world over. We are offering three sessions (one on campus and two remote) that will enhance the writing skills of any serious writer. Our faculty provides unique, tailored, and transformative experiences to all participants. The Yale Writers' Workshop experience offers: An immersive creative environment An experienced faculty of writers and editors Small workshops limited to 10-12 participants Access to industry professionals Applications for Summer 2022 are closed. We hope you will join us in Summer 2023.

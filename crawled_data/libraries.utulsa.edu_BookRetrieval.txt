Book and Material Check Out Request Request McFarlin books for pick up at the library The McFarlin Library is currently operating a book and material pick up service for our physical materials. TU students, faculty, and staff can request materials in our collection to be picked up at the McFarlin Library during operational hours. Patrons can find materials in our collection by searching the library’s catalog: https://utulsa.summon.serialssolutions.com/#!/. Patrons will be notified when materials are available for pick up. Materials can be picked up during library operational hours at the Plaza Level Circulation Desk. Materials not picked up within one week will be reshelved. Requests are limited to materials located in the McFarlin Library. Contact mcfarlinlibrary@utulsa.edu for any questions regarding book retrieval. Note: Book delivery is unavailable at this time.

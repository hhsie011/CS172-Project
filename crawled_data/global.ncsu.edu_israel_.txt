Scholar Program This fund supports travel for NC State faculty going to Israel for academic or research purposes and Israeli scholars who plan to come to NC State. It is designed to promote scientific research and to share technological capabilities for the enhancement of economic and social progress in both countries. Up to $2,500 is available per proposal. Proposals are accepted on an ongoing basis. To apply for funding, send the following materials to ncstateglobal@ncsu.edu as a combined PDF attachment.

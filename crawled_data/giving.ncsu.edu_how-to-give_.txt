Your investment in NC State drives us from good to extraordinary. Ways to Give By giving to NC State, you can have an immediate impact on a world-class institution or leave an enduring legacy. Our university offers a variety of opportunities for you to support an area of your choice, in a way that works for you. Give Today Support NC State by making an immediate impact with a gift online, by phone or by mail. Planned Gifts Planned gifts offer creative, flexible strategies for your estate and charitable planning. Matching Gifts Many employers match gifts to higher education. Find out if your employer will match your gift today. Endowments Endowments provide donors with opportunities to create living legacies that touch the lives of future generations for years to come. Annual Giving Each year, gifts from alumni, parents, faculty, staff, friends and students like you allow NC State to increase scholarships, strengthen curricula, fund community service projects, enrich the libraries and sponsor the arts. Learn More Meet our Development Officers Connect with a member of our development staff to learn more about how you can get involved and help support the mission of NC State. Our development officers are standing by to help you fulfill your philanthropic interests and goals by identifying causes, programs or colleges at NC State that are meaningful to you. They can help you plan a gift that suits your needs and answer any questions you may have.

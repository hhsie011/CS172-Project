The plan details the actions and procedures to be followed in an emergency, and assigns responsibility for certain actions to designated individuals. The Emergency Action Plan provides procedures for building evacuation, reporting emergencies, locating emergency evacuation locations, personnel accountability, fire extinguisher types and instructions for use, shelter- in- place procedures, emergency communications and building specific information.

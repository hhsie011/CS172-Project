Is your job monotonous or chaotic? Do you lack satisfaction from your achievements? If the answer is yes, you might be experiencing job burnout. Job burnout can affect your physical and mental health. If you feel isolated at work and in your personal life, you might feel more stressed. This webinar will teach participants how to recognize signs of burnout, and offer tools to help avoid burnout by developing a support system, setting goals, and maintaining a positive attitude. This webinar is audio only; video will be off for participants.

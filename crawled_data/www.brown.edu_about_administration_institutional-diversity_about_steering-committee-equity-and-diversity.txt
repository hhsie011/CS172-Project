A major role of the SCED will be to expand upon the work of the former Title IX Oversight and Advisory Board by reviewing Title IX and gender equity matters that intersect with other forms of protected classes. 2020 - 2021 Committee Members The Steering Committee for Equity and Diversity will be formed by both the Administrative Equity and Diversity Council (AEDC) and the Student Equity and Diversity Council (SEDC).

Columbia Lamont–Doherty Earth Observatory and Center for International Earth Science Information Network Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 5, 2018 11:30 AM - 5:30 PM Shanghai Gallery of Art, Three on the Bund 3F, No. 3 Zhong Shan Dong Yi Road, Shanghai On August 5, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to an earth science panel discussion in Shanghai, featuring Columbia Lamont–Doherty Earth Observatory (LDEO) and the Center for International Earth Science Information Network (CIESIN). In the morning close-door session, students from New Frontiers in Earth Science Program of Columbia University will have a dialogue with Columbia professors on key issues in climate change. In the afternoon session, six panelists, all leading experts in their respective fields, will offer their perspectives on risk management in government and private sectors, climate change, urban growth and implications for agriculture in China, air pollution and public health, and sustainable urban land use and population change. The panel will also introduce LDEO and CIESIN collaborations with groups in China. Ron Cai '97 Law, Co-President of CAA Shanghai, will deliver a welcoming speech. There will be a reception afterward. We cordially invite all the alumni to join. Panelists Arthur Lerner-Lam, Deputy Director and Lamont Research Professor of Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, Columbia University Robert S. Chen, Director and Senior Research Scientist, Center for International Earth Science Information Network (CIESIN), Earth Institute, Columbia University Christopher Small, Lamont Research Professor of Marine Geology and Geophysics, Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, Columbia University Mingfang Ting, Associate Director and Lamont Research Professor of Ocean and Climate Physics, Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, Columbia University Beizhan Yan, Lamont Associate Research Professor of Geochemistry, Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, Columbia University Xiaoshi Xing, Information Scientist, Center for International Earth Sciences Information Network (CIESIN), Earth Institute, Columbia University Agenda Morning Session (NFES only) 11:00 a.m. - 11:30 a.m. Registration 11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. Dialogue Afternoon Session 1:30 p.m. - 2:00 p.m. Registration 2:00 p.m. - 3:00 p.m. Presentations 3:00 p.m. - 3:45 p.m. Panel Discussion 3:45 p.m. - 4:10 p.m. Q&A 4:30 p.m. - 5:30 p.m. Reception (Columbia community only) Upcoming Events Columbia Global CentersAmman / Beijing / Istanbul / Mumbai / Nairobi / Paris / Rio / Santiago / Tunis Follow Us Facebook Twitter Instagram Youtube Columbia University ©2022 Columbia University Accessibility Nondiscrimination Careers Built using Columbia Sites Back to Top Close

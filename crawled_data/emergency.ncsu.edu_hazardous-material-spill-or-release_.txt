When the fire alarm sounds: Evacuate the building via the nearest exit Go to the predetermined meeting area for your building The fire alarm should be pulled and 911 called from a safe phone for additional assistance, for any of the following: Visible smoke, heavy odor of smoke or evidence of fire Uncontained release of hazardous gas Uncontained spill of hazardous liquids Hazardous materials either released or spilled into the environment require a targeted response. Please consult the appropriate resources before taking action.

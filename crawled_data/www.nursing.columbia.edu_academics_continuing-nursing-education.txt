Get More Information You are here Home > Academics > Continuing Nursing Education A Columbia Nursing Education Admissions at a Glance Academic Programs Courses Financial Aid Continuing Nursing Education Helene Fuld Health Trust Simulation Center Continuing Nursing Education The Center for Continuing Nursing Education at Columbia University School of Nursing provides continuing education to nurses, nurse practitioners, and other health care providers. We combine creative teaching strategies with relevant and current content. Accredited as a provider of continuing nursing education by the American Nurses Credentialing Center, our continuing nursing education programs are designed to meet your needs and accommodate your schedule. If you are unable to access Continuing Education courses for which you previously registered, please contact Judy Honig at jch3@cumc.columbia.edu.

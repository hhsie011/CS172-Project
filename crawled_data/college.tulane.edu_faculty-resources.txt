Home / The College / Faculty Resources Faculty Resources Faculty are the key to the success of our students. You educate and create connections with students. As an undergraduate faculty member, you are part of Newcomb-Tulane College which allows you to create even stronger academic connections. We offer opportunities to engage with students through programs like Residential Faculty and faculty-led study abroad as well as ways you can advocate and support students by referring them to our support and enrichment programs and services. NTC administers the core curriculum and creates and enforces academic policies, ensuring the integrity of our community.

Our welcoming spirit is rooted in our Oklahoma history and the diversity forged by OSU. Things to do Stillwater offers a unique combination of activities and celebrations — perfect for families, students or visitors. Find out more! Events With events such as America’s Greatest Homecoming Celebration, Remember the Ten run and the Stillwater Arts Festival — there is always something happening in Stillwater. Nightlife Whether you’re looking for sophisticated ambiance or a two-stepping honky-tonk scene, Stillwater has the nightlife hotspot to suit your style. Live music Musicians have been gathering in Stillwater, the birthplace of Red Dirt music, for over 30 years to share their songs and stories. Places to go While visiting Stillwater, you can find the perfect pair of cowboy boots, savor local flavors or indulge in iconic favorites. Check us out! Downtown Stillwater Historic Downtown Stillwater offers the latest foodie hotspots, live music venues, one-of-a-kind shopping and special holiday celebrations Outdoor adventures Grab a fishing pole, pack a picnic, take a bike ride and take in the fresh Stillwater air! Our trails, parks and lakes are the perfect place to take in the Oklahoma landscape. Cultural district Located in the heart of Downtown Stillwater, the Downtown Stillwater Cultural District is where the visual and performing arts inspire, history lives, and creativity and learning thrive! Local dining You can choose to grab a heaping plate of delicious cheese fries, experience pizza perfection or start your morning with a local coffee brew! Hotel & travel Daily flights and a hotel on campus make it easier than ever before to experience Stillwater with comfort and convenience. The Atherton Hotel This historic hotel is conveniently located in the heart of Stillwater’s campus. This full-service, freshly renovated landmark mixes rich tradition with modern-day services. Stillwater Regional Airport Fly directly into and out of Stillwater on American Airlines with #FlySWO! Skip the parking fees, long lines and drive time, and book your flight today. Hotels & lodging Find a hotel property that fits your needs! Browse amenities, hotel photos, and quick links to property websites for convenient booking.

Place your gameday order below. Luxury Boxes Luxury box holders can select from such items as lobster rolls, Carolina pulled pork, Italian subs, and more. Please be advised that "order time" will default to noon for all luxury box orders. Delivery of services will be adjusted appropriately based on the game time. Start Order Lower Lawn Tent Village Hospitality tent hosts can preorder food for gameday pickup on location. Tailgate packages include Pulled Pork BBQ, Charcuterie, Taco Bar, Continental Breakfast, Hot Breakfast, Chili Bar, and Snack Attack. Your food order will be ready for pick up at the Food Tent located in the center of Lower Lawn Tent Village. Start Order Quick Links What's Open?

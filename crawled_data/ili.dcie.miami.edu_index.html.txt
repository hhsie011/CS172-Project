Program Online Programs Graduate Business English Certificate Contact Us Home AAA The University of Miami’s Intensive Language Institute is part of the Division for Continuing & International Education. We offer language programs for professionals, organizations and corporations, and international students. You can choose from a full-time academic preparation with our Intensive English Program, you can create your own custom language program for your team’s professional needs, or you can immerse yourself in a dynamic Intensive Language Program. Helping you reach your language goals is our business. From high school through retirement, our innovative courses and programs will prepare you for whatever is next, giving you the skills you need to live the life you want.

Session Boren Awards @ UC Official Boren Website NCA Learning Series 2021-2022 Wednesday, September 29th @ 12:30PM via Zoom. Are you interested in funding to go abroad for a semester or longer to study languages that are less commonly taught in the U.S.? Boren Awards representative Kyle Cox joined us remotely to offer an information session that was exclusively for UC students. This was a wonderful opportunity to hear directly from a Boren rep about the award opportunities that are available and to pose your questions to him. This session was held remotely. Email nca@uc.edu to access the recording. Click here to read about UC’s four 2021 Boren Scholars. Eligibility: Undergraduate and Graduate students (U.S. citizens) with a 3.0+ GPA; session open to all. Email nca@uc.edu with questions.

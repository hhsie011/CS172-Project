We are here to capture those high-impact events and make sure they are available for the world to see. The world is consuming more and more video through online platforms and we are ready to help distribute your video or stream directly to your key audiences. How can we help? Live Streams We provide live streaming for events or special occasions so anyone, anywhere can tune in using InsideOSU or Facebook Live. We have years of experience with regular streaming events like commencement and homecoming. We can help you connect with your audience by planning your next event live stream. Captioning Does your video need captions that are easy to understand? With the help of ITLE, we can provide captioning services for short and high-impact videos. Inside OSU Our video team hosts and manages the Inside OSU website to ensure that your video can be hosted using a secure and easy-to-access platform for everyone.

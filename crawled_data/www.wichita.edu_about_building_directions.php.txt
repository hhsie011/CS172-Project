Watch the adjacent video to learn how to get walking or driving directions on your smartphone. Go to the WSU Interactive Map Locate and click the Search icon near the top of the page. Enter the name of the building or facility or the building code. Select the desired building from the search results. Click on the diamond-shaped Directions icon. Select either driving, biking or walking directions. Select "My Location" if using a GPS-enable device, or enter an address with city and state. Hit "return" (desktop) or click "Get Directions" (mobile).

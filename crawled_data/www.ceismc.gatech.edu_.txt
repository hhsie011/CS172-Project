Measuring the Effectiveness of Middle School STEM-Innovation and Engineering Design The Georgia Institute of Technology received a $2.7 million grant from the National Science Foundation (NSF), Discovery Research Prek-12 (DRK-12) program. This 4-year DRK-12 Impact Study, Measuring the Effectiveness of Middle School STEM-Innovation and Engineering Design, led by Principal Research Scientist Meltem Alemdar, will begin on September 1, 2021.

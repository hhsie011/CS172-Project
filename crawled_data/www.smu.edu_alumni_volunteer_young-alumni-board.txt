They work in concert with the Annual Giving and Alumni Relations staff as a conduit for the alumni community to the SMU Board of Trustees and other internal and external University constituents. The goal of the SMU Young Alumni Board is to create a thriving alumni community that meaningfully engages alumni and parents today and for years to come.

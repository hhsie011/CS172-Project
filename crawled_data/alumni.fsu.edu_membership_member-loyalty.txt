Join, Renew or Upgrade Make a Payment on a Life Membership Pledge Member Loyalty Member Perks Reward Zone Give the Gift of Membership Member Loyalty FSU Alumni Association Member Loyalty We appreciate the continued support of our members. On-going participation and contribution is the foundation of our success. Without them we would not be able to deliver the many programs, scholarships and networks available to students and alumni. To recognize past contributions and reward continued support of FSU Alumni Association members, each membership is now associated with a loyalty level*. These levels will appear on membership cards, certain emails from the FSU Alumni Association, and will soon be displayed on oneFSU profiles. Learn more about the impact of membership Garnet Up to two years of continuous membership Gold Three to four years of continuous membership Spirit Five to nine years of continuous membership Heritage Ten or more years of continuous membership Unconquered Life members *Member loyalty levels are based on continuous years of FSU Alumni Association members as noted in our records. Should you feel that it is incorrect, please contact us via email at alumni@fsu.edu.

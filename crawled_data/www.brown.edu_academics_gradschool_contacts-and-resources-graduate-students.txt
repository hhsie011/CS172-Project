At Brown, we care about the whole you and work with you to support your success. The Graduate Student Resources page lists contacts for academics, health & wellness, and the student experience. If you are not sure where to go or are not sure what your question is, you can start with one of the Graduate School deans (below) or one of the Support Deans in the Office of Student Support Services. This list of deans indicates roles, office hours and means of scheduling. Additional Graduate School contacts are available on the Deans & Staff page.

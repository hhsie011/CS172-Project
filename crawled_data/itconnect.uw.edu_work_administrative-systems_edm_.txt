Contact UW-IT About UW-IT Enterprise Document Management (EDM) Related Information Document Management System (DMS) eSignaturesUW-IT Service Catalog Document Management System (DMS) eSignaturesForms & Support Document Management System Interest Form eSignatures Interest Form Enterprise Document Management (EDM) is a business unit in UW-IT ‘s Information Management division with the focus on providing the University with modernized technology to support the creation and management of electronic records and documents. We support two services: an enterprise Document Management System (eDMS) and eSignatures. In partnering with EDM, customers will: Transform business processes that currently depend on the routing of paper documents to be more efficient and paperless. Enable cross-department collaboration around key enterprise content. Easily search, identify, and hold records responsive to public records requests, litigation, and audit. Modernize management of information. Achieve compliance with data regulations such as HIPAA and FERPA. To help your project succeed, the Enterprise Document Management program provides consultation, coordination of partner (UW-IT, Privacy Office, Records Management Services) resources, and implementation support. With every engagement, our goal is to empower you to continue to improve your processes independently.

Using correspondence and published accounts, Hart explores the role Jane Gray played in presenting her husband’s work and activities. It provides a literary, historical, and cultural context for their partnership. The article can be accessed through HOLLIS. Hart, Jonathan Locke. "Jane Gray's Framing of Asa Gray through Autobiography, Biography, and Correspondence." Canadian Review of American Studies, vol. 52 no. 1, 2022, p. 66-82.

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Office of Communications & Marketing Menu Search TTU Office of Communications & Marketing Emergency Communications Center Coronavirus Information May 20, 2020 Dear Texas Tech University Family: As this semester comes to a close, we hope that you will take a moment on Saturday, May 23rd, and join us for a celebration of the graduating class of May 2020. That morning, the Commencement Office will send a special message out to all students, faculty, and staff with a link to the May 2020 Virtual Commencement Ceremonies. In addition, May 2020 graduates (both undergraduate and graduate) can have their photos taken with President Schovanec and Raider Red on Saturday immediately following the Virtual Commencement Ceremony. Dr. Schovanec and Raider Red will pose for photos in the front yard of the President's home beginning at 11 a.m. We will be practicing social distancing in the photos and ask that you do the same when you arrive to take your photo. Details When: Saturday, May 23 Time: 11 a.m. - 3 p.m. Location: 3218 20th Street Congratulations to the graduating class of May 2020 and, as always, GO TECH!

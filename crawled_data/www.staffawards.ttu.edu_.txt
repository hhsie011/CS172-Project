In recognition of outstanding employee efforts, Texas Tech initiated three system-wide employee recognition programs. Distinguished Staff Awards Non-Faculty employees will be recognized based on the the hard work, enthusiasm, and dedication to Texas Tech. There are five levels included in this program. Award Recipients: 2021 2020 2019 2018 2017 2016 2015 2014 2013 TTU OP 70.30 ("Distinguished Staff Awards for Non-Faculty Employees") Award Guidelines Award Descriptions Length of Service Awards Employees will be recognized based on the years of service to the Texas Tech University System in 5 year increments. Award recipients: 2022 2021 2020 2019 2018 2017 2016 2015 2014 2013 2012 2011 TTU OP 70.26 ("Length of Service Awards") Service Excellence Award Employees will be awarded paid time off without reduction of other leave balances for exemplary service. Benefits eligible staff who have exhibited outstanding performance, as documented by a current performance appraisal, may be awarded service excellence leave. Outstanding performance is defined as an overall rating of at least 4.5 on a scale of 7.

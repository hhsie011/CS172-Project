Staff Staff Office of Student Affairs Sandra Bernal Garcia Associate Dean for Student Affairs and Dean of Students OSA staff are available to meet one-on-one to discuss your questions and concerns, whether it’s assistance navigating University procedures, support to help you succeed academically, or advice on how to enrich your time here through student activities, groups, and leadership opportunities. Do you have a question about available support resources? Are you interested in getting involved in extracurricular activities?

Baylor>Arts & Sciences>Students>Student Life and Activities Resources for Baylor Student Life and Activities Calendar The All-University Calendar provides a chronological list of upcoming events, including academic, athletic and student life activities on campus. Campus Activities The Baylor Department of Student Activities provides a variety of opportunities for students to enhance their university experience, developing community through campus programs. Campus Dining Baylor's campus dining locations offers wonderful options to students. You can stay current with hours of operation and events via our Baylor Dining social media channels. Campus Map Use this updated map of the Baylor University campus to find where you want to go. Counseling Center The Baylor University Counseling Center seeks to facilitate the educational experience and total development of students by reducing mental illness and distress and by enhancing mental health, well-being, quality of life and optimal human functioning. Lariat Newspaper The Baylor Lariat, established in 1900, is the award-winning student newspaper of Baylor University. McLane Student Life Center (SLC) The Student Life Center is a 156,000 square foot recreation center for student, faculty and staff. The SLC includes a fitness center, a climbing rock, leisure pool, outdoor basketball and sand volleyball courts, four-court gymnasium, conference rooms, racquetball/squash courts, indoor jogging track, aerobics studio and student lounge. Title IX Office Baylor University is committed to providing a safe and non-discriminatory learning, living, and working environment for all members of the University community.

The school offers course work in core business disciplines to provide potential managers with an understanding of the complexities of the professional world and organizations that they will join. Students in other graduate programs and undergraduate students registered with the university are welcome in Johnson courses as space allows. There are several courses designed specifically for non-Johnson students that replicate the core courses taught to MBA students. Those are found in our course offerings under “Courses for Non-Johnson students”.

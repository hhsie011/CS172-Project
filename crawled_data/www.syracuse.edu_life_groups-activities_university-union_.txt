Run entirely by students and one of the University's largest student organizations, University Union (UU) is the official programming board of Syracuse University. UU hosts a wide variety of entertainment and events for students, but it’s best known for its annual fall music festival, Juice Jam, and its full-day music event in the spring, Block Party/Mayfest.

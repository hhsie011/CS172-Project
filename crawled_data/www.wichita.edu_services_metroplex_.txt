Hughes Metropolitan Complex The Hughes Metropolitan Complex is a modern, 75,000-square-foot facility located at 5015 E. 29th Street N., approximately one mile north of the University's main campus. The facility is home to University offices and conference/meeting rooms. The Metropolitan Complex conference/meeting facilities are available for use and for rent by the University or by the community. The facility provides a wide array of conveniences for your next event, including at-door parking, on-site catering and audiovisual equipment rental. » For reservation information, please call (316) 978-3258 or email metropolitan.complex@wichita.edu.

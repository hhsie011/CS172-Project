Visit 3 Apply Below, you’ll find the various ways you can apply for admission as a first-year or transfer applicant, along with deadlines for special programs. Admission and financial aid applications, for both first-year and transfer applicants, that remain incomplete by the stated deadlines will be at a distinct disadvantage and likely will not be admitted. First-Year Applicants First-year students can choose from four admission plans: Early Decision I (EDI), Early Action (EA), Early Decision II (EDII), or Regular Decision (RD). ADMISSION PLAN APPLICATION DEADLINE FINANCIAL AID DEADLINE DECISION NOTIFICATION EARLY DECISION I November 1 November 15 Late December EARLY ACTION November 1 November 15 Late January - Early February EARLY DECISION II January 1 January 1 Late February REGULAR DECISION January 1 January 1 April 1 EARLY DECISION I (EDI) is our early, binding application option for applicants who are committed to attending UM if admitted.* EARLY ACTION (EA) is our early, non-binding application option. EARLY DECISION II (EDI) is an additional binding application option for applicants who are committed to attending UM if admitted but need more time to apply.* REGULAR DECISION (RD) is our final, non-binding application option.** Final Credentials Deadline: All supporting documents on the application checklist must be on file for consideration by November 19 for EDI applicants, December 3 for EA applicants, and January 21 for applicants who initially applied for EDII (February 11 for deferred EA applicants who opted into EDII). Applications that remain incomplete by the aforementioned Final Credentials Deadlines will be rolled over into the Regular Decision plan. Regular Decision applications that remain incomplete by February 18, will be withdrawn. *EDI and EDII applicants must complete and submit the Early Decision Agreement form, available through the Common Application. **Frost School of Music Regular Decision applicants must apply by December 1. Please follow the instructions for applying to the Frost School of Music. Transfer Applicants Transfer students have two entry term options: fall or spring. ENTRY TERM OPTION PRIORITY APPLICATION DEADLINES FINANCIAL AID DEADLINE FALL SEMESTER April 1* April 15 SPRING SEMESTER November 1* November 1 *Transfer applications and/or supporting documents received after the stated priority deadlines will be reviewed on a space available basis. Special Program Deadlines Supplemental applications are available in the applicant portal. Access to the portal will be granted once your admission application is received. Please submit any additional documentation as soon as possible. We will accept documents up to seven days after the admission plan deadline.

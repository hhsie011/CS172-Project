For Huskers, that place is Husker Hub. Husker Hub provides guidance and recommendations for students with questions about scholarships, financial aid, bills, loans, registration and more so you can get back to focusing on your studies. Student Affairs connects Huskers with programs and resources to help them succeed inside and outside the classroom. From money management to resources for well-being, campus involvement to connecting with employers, Student Affairs provides the inclusive living and learning experiences that inspire students. Student Involvement With 500+ active recognized student organizations (RSOs), recreational facilities, sporting events, concerts and more, as a University of Nebraska–Lincoln student you'll have plenty of opportunities to make new friends, experience new cultures and live your best life at Nebraska. RSOs are a great way for students to connect with Huskers that share their passions and interests. With everything from major-specific clubs to Greek life, sports clubs, cultural organizations, philanthropic endeavors and more — there's something for every student.

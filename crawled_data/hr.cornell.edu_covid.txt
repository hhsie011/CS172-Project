Vaccination Recent Articles March 23, 2022 CornellAlert: Moving to COVID-19 Alert Level Yellow COVID-19 cases on the Ithaca campus are increasing beyond the university’s predictions, indicating a substantial prevalence of the virus on campus. As a result, the Ithaca campus will move to COVID-19 Alert Level Yellow, effective immediately. March 11, 2022 Easing Mask Requirements, Effective March 14 Beginning Monday, March 14, face mask requirements will be lifted for the majority of on-campus locations. High-quality masks will, however, continue to be required in the following spaces and circumstances: January 31, 2022 Daily Check All Cornell faculty and staff will continue to get a daily email reminding them to conduct a symptom assessment; however, you will only need to complete the Daily Check health assessment if you are experiencing COVID-19 symptoms. January 28, 2022 Testing for individuals experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 Beginning Monday, January 31, Cornell will provide Symptomatic Test Kits to students, faculty and staff who are experiencing mild to moderate symptoms suggestive of COVID-19. Continue reading for pick-up locations and detailed instructions. January 14, 2022 OTC Test Coverage Effective January 15, 2022, group health coverage plans are required to allow for coverage for Over the Counter (OTC) testing to detect SARS-CoV-2 and/or to diagnose COVID-19.

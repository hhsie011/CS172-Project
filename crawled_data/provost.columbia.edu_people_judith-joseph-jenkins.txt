She is responsible for investigating claims of discrimination, discriminatory harassment and sexual misconduct filed against Columbia University staff, faculty, and third-parties affiliated with the University. Before joining the Office of the Provost, Judith was an Agency Attorney for the Law Department of the New York City Housing Authority (NYCHA). While at NYCHA, she defended the Authority against claims of housing discrimination and employment discrimination filed by tenants, employees, and applicants for public housing. Judith received her bachelor’s degree in Political Science from Columbia University and her Juris Doctor from Boston University School of Law.

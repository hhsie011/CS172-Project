This scholarship opportunity is to highlight and recognize first-generation students for engaging in various program activities, including: connecting with their Peer Mentor utilizing their expanded network of support accessing university resources and programs participating in Student Success & Professional Development Activities Minimum Eligibility Requirements Institutional GPA 2.75 (TTU GPA) Enrolled Full Time at TTU (12+ credit hours) Active FGTMP Program Participant How to Apply Requirements Completed FGTMP Scholarship Application Optional (Strongly Recommended) Peer Mentor Letter of Recommendation Faculty/Staff Letter of Recommendation For more information contact: Laura Flores, Program Coordinator Laura.Flores@ttu.edu or fgtmp@ttu.edu First Generation Transition & Mentoring

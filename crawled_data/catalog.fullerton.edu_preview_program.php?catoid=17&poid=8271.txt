These include five CTVA core and seven CTVA elective courses. Four additional courses (12 units) are required as collateral electives. These may be CTVA courses, approved courses from other departments, or courses in a minor or second major. Required Core Courses (15 units) CTVA 100 - Introduction to Cinema and Television (3) CTVA 300 - Language of Film (3) CTVA 301 - Critical Studies: Cinema (3) or CTVA 302 - Critical Studies: Television (3) CTVA 349T - Writing about the Moving Image (3) * CTVA 495 - Mass Media Internship (3) Electives (21 units minimum) Course options for electives are listed on the department website at communications.fullerton.edu//ctva. Please verify course choices with faculty. Additional Information * Meets upper-division writing requirement for Cinema and Television Arts majors.

Submit an Event UH Ensemble Dance Works Sunday, May 1, 2022 1:30 pm - 3:30 pm The School of Theatre & Dance presents UH Ensemble Dance Works. This event will be performed live and in person. The annual spring concert features original dance works by faculty and guest artists as performed by our pre-professional dance company, the UH Dance Ensemble. UH Dance students shine in new works created especially for them. Performances: April 29-30 at 7:30 PM and May 1 at 1:30 PM. Location Lyndall Finley Wortham Theatre Cost KGMCA Box Office Tickets: General Admission - $20.00 Senior Citizen (65+) - $15.00 UH Student, Faculty, Staff and Alumni - $15.00 Purchase tickets to the event here!

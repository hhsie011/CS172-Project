Most of the residence halls include spaces that foster community, whether it's in-suite common areas, floor lounges, shared kitchens or larger building lounges that can be reserved for events. The residence hall directory has details all of the lounges within the residence halls. The following is a list of building lounges that provide ideal spaces for gathering with friends, conducting group study sessions or just relaxing. Where indicated, some of the lounges can be formally booked for student events through Event Management.

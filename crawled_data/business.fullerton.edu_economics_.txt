Center for Economic Education The Center for Economic Education promotes economic and financial literacy in schools and in the community with a view to achieve upward economic mobility of low-income communities and assesses program effectiveness with high-quality research. Learn more about the Center for Economic Education. Woods Center for Economic Analysis and Forecasting Get the latest outlooks and analysis on the national, regional and local economies from the Woods Center for Economic Analysis and Forecasting. Led by expert economists, the center provides regular updates, forecasts and reports that benefit the Southern California business community. Learn more about the Woods Center.

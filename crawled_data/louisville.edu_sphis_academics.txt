The University of Louisville School of Public Health and Information Sciences was established in response to the university’s focus on excellence in research and education. The school offers several academic programs, including a BS/BA in Public Health, Master of Public Health (MPH), Masters of Science (MS and MSc), and Doctors of Philosophy (PhD). Most programs have specific concentrations that allow students to direct their studies to individual areas of interest. Undergraduate Programs BS/BA in Public Health Tracks available in Social Justice and Health Equity and Professional Health Studies. Accelerated Bachelor's-Master of Public Health (MPH) Minors available in Applied Statistics and Data Science, Epidemiology, Healthcare Management, Public Health and Social Justice and Health Equity (Fall 2022) .

Link Syracuse University has a well-earned reputation for excellence that spans 150 years of classroom teaching on a diverse, inclusive campus. That commitment to high-quality learning now infuses our online programs, providing pathways to a Syracuse University credential for part-time students anywhere in the world. Experience the benefits of a part-time Syracuse University degree program that you can earn from anywhere: Enroll in engaging courses that blend asynchronous (self-paced) and synchronous (interactive classes at scheduled times) learning, so you can connect and collaborate with your peers and professors while still enjoying the flexibility to balance your education with other life commitments. Experience the same quality instruction and academic rigor as your peers on campus, and graduate with a Syracuse University degree or credential. Enjoy having the peace of mind that dedicated support for academic advising, financial aid, career services is just a phone call, email or live chat away. Join a powerful network of alumni who are leaders in their fields. Engage in a dynamic learning environment and be fully supported by our Online Student Success team With multiple starts per year, you can apply now and start soon.

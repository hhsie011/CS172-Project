4:45 PM America/New_York Online K-12 Education Natural History of the Hudson River During this session, we will explore how nature has influenced human use of the estuary and how human beings have shaped nature in the context of the Hudson River. Learn about the development of the Hudson River since the ice ages, how its location and nature drove European settlements along the river, and how human beings have made changes to the river to further development. Presenter: Frank Nitsche, Research Scientist, Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory Target Audience: Grades 9-12 This event is free but registration is required.

Find Your Next Great Lawyer. With fewer law firms willing and financially able to shoulder the burden of training young lawyers, there’s more pressure on legal employers to identify the right graduates—with the right collection of practice-ready skills—that best match the specific needs of the firm. We can help. The Career Development Office (CDO) provides employers with a wealth of programs and resources to make the process easy—from free on-campus interviews and resume collection based on your firm’s needs—to ways to connect with prospective hires that align with your schedule and timetable. Let’s get started.

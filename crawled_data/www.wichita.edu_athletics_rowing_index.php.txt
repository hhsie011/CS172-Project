Rowing Team During the past 45 years, Shocker Rowing has been making waves in Kansas and across the nation. Each year the team builds on the tradition of the past while striving for new heights. Our program offers great opportunities to new students at Wichita State University on recreational, developmental, and championship levels. Whether you are an experienced high school rower or a new-to-the-sport walk-on, there is a chance for you to be part of our successful program.

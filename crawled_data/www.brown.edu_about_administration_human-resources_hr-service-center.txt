Located in Page-Robinson Hall, a six-story academic and administrative facility, the HRSC can be found in the Brown Business Center on the second floor. Select services, such as I-9 form processing, assistance with onboarding, foreign national processing, and guidance with employee self-service Workday functions are offered to the Brown community.

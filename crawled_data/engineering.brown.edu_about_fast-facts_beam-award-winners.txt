In 1997 as part of our celebration of 150 years of teaching engineering at Brown, we introduced Brown Engineering Alumni Medal as a means of recognizing distinguished Engineering alumni. These medals are awarded to Brown Engineering graduates who have established exceptional records of accomplishment in their engineering careers. BEAM Committee and Nominations A committee, composed of faculty members and previously chosen medalists, selects the new medalists. All faculty and alumni are invited to make nominations.

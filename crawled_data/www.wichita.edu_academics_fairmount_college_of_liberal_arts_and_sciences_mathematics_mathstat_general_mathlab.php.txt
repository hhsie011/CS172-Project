The Lab is staffed by graduate students and exceptional undergraduate students studying mathematics and/or mathematics related disciplines. In addition, numerous mathematics faculty members volunteer time in the Lab. Courses formally tutored include: Math 011 Beginning Algebra Math 012 Intermediate Algebra Math 111 College Algebra Math 112 Precalculus Math Math 123 College Trigonometry Math 131 Contemporary Mathematics Math 144 Business Calculus Math 242 Calculus I Math 370 Elementary Statistics Math Lab Hours Current hours for the in-person (371JB) and online Math Lab are: Monday - Thursday: 8:00 a.m. - 7:00 p.m. Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. No appointment is necessary. Students are encouraged to stop in at their convenience. Get free math tutoring online with the WSU Math Lab! Our Online Math Lab has "drop in" first-come-first-served tutoring on Zoom or if you just want someone to look at your paper we can do that, too.

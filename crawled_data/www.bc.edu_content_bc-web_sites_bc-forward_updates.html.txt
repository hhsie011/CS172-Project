Leahy S.J. March 30, 2022 Dear Members of the Boston College Community: Boston College is extending its walk-in asymptomatic COVID-19 testing for students, faculty, and staff in the Margot Connell Recreation Center through May 17. BC community members who wish to be tested are welcome to do so once a week at the following times Connell Testing Clinic Hours Monday: 9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. Tuesday: 9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. Wednesday: 6:30 a.m. – 2:30 p.m. Thursday: 9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. Friday: 9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. As a reminder, BC community members who are symptomatic should not enter the Connell testing site. Students should contact UHS, and faculty and staff should contact their primary care provider. Thank you.

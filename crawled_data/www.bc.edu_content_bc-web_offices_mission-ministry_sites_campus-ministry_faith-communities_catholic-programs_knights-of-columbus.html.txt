As an international organization of over 1.9 million Knights, we are committed to the principles of charity, fraternity, unity, and patriotism. Through work in diverse fields, from Special Olympics to Catholic relief programs, we forge Catholic men for lives of distinguished service for and with others. Our ranks include President John F. Kennedy, Vince Lombardi, and Babe Ruth.

As a leader, you want to set your team members up for success by helping them create goals that align with the needs of your organization and the employee’s current skills and those to be developed. In this interactive 90-minute webinar, we go deep into the leader’s role in managing performance, helping you master both goal setting and expectations. Learn how to create an environment that ensures support and accountability, as well as help your direct reports define and align goals.

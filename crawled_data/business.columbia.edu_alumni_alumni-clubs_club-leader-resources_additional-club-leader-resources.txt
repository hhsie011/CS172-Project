Some of the resources available to club leaders include: LinkedIn The official Columbia Business School Alumni LinkedIn group is an online platform for alumni to foster professional relationships and exchange industry information. Alumni are approved for group membership by the Alumni Relations team. Leadership Summits Columbia Business School’s Alumni Club Leadership Summit is held annual in New York City. Due to COVID-19, this year’s Summit, in conjunction with Columbia Alumni Leaders Weekend will take place virtually. Additional information about the weekend to follow. Experienced Professionals Career Management Materials The Career Management Center (CMC) at Columbia Business School supports all segments of the MBA talent pool—MBA students, EMBAs, and more than 47,000 global alumni. CBS Alumni can access a range of excellent career development tools through the CMC’s Experienced Professionals Career Management team. Information about the Experienced Professionals Career Management team offerings can be found on their website.

Tours Educational tours of the Herbaria facilities may be given to small groups. To request a tour, please contact us with the following information. Requests should be submitted at least two months in advance of the proposed date. Preferred date and time Duration Number of attendees Focus and purpose of visit Interests/background knowledge/age group Is handicapped access needed? Is the group also planning to visit to the glass models at the Harvard Museum of Natural History? Requests will be reviewed and responded to in a timely fashion. If the tour is approved, the applicant will be contacted to plan the details of the event. Parking Permits Visitors requiring parking may purchase a visitor day permit from Harvard Transportation and Parking. The closest parking lot is at 52 Oxford Street, behind HUH. Lots can fill up quickly, so try to obtain a permit as early as possible.

From teaching classes to conducting research, the following list provides a snapshot of valuable instructional technology tools and research resources to support your work at FSU. For a complete list of services, please search the ITS Service Catalog. Share files up to 3GB with NiFTy Computer Backup Unlimited, cloud-based storage for important files at work and at home WCMS WCMS makes it easier than ever to create and maintain mobile-first, brand-approved websites Zoom Seamlessly connect with the world around you with Zoom web conferencing LinkedIn Learning* Free access to online learning community with 13000+ videos and training courses Qualtrics* Create and distribute online surveys for your department or research myFSUVLab* Log on to FSU’s 24/7 virtual computer lab to access campus software from anywhere Microsoft Office 365* Free access to Microsoft programs and services such as OneDrive for Business, Office Online, Skype for Business and more Stay Secure Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) If you regularly use 2-factor authentication (2FA) to access FSU applications such as myFSU HR or Office 365 as part of your job, make sure you have authorized a device other than your desk phone for 2FA. ITS suggests adding your mobile device and installing the Duo app to ensure you have 2FA access anywhere, anytime.

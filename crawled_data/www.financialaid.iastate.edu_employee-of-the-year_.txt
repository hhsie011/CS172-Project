Success Student Employee of the Year Every year, one outstanding student is honored as ISU’s Student Employee of the Year. Selection is based on reliability, quality of work, initiative, professionalism, interpersonal skills, and unique contributions to the employer. 2022 Iowa State University Student Employee of the Year Bahar Hashemi Nominated by ITS Solution Center Congratulations to all of our 2022 nominees! Nominations for the 2022 Student Employee of the Year have now closed. Nominees must meet the following criteria: Undergraduate students who are paid employees either on-campus or at an off-campus community service partner Students must have been employed in a paid position part-time for a minimum of 6 months between June 1, 2021 and May 31, 2022 (anticipated) The winner from Iowa State University will be entered in state and regional competition with the Midwest Association of Student Employment Administrators. For More Information and Nomination Guidelines, view the PDF document here.

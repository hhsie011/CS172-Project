These highly-selective tracks benefit students by offering them individualized, in-depth advising from faculty, immersing them in research and giving them better opportunities for flexibility within their major while simultaneously preparing them for graduate studies or a successful career. Baylor Business Fellows Opportunity for high-ability students to study business along with other disciplines around the university through a degree plan tailored to each individual. Complete multiple majors and minors in business, math, sciences and liberal arts. Receive individualized, in-depth advising that helps you succeed in professional and academic goals. Progress more rapidly to courses in your majors and minors. Prepare yourself for internships, jobs, and graduate studies in medicine, law, business and more. Read more about Baylor Business Fellows. Computer Science Fellows Highly motivated students within the Department of Computer Science with a wide range of interests can create a tailored degree plan across disciplines. Create an individualized course of study with the advice of a program director who will mentor you throughout the entire undergraduate experience. Prepare for graduate studies or a successful career while broadening your background in your chosen area of study. Read more about the Computer Science Fellows. Hankamer Scholars This application-based program allows for more creativity and flexibility for high-ability students within the traditional BBA degree plan. Take upper-level business core and specific major classes earlier to accelerate the pace of coursework. Study alongside other Scholars who also display an excellent record of academic achievement, active extra-curricular involvement and strong leadership skills. Receive specialized guidance provided by the Hankamer School of Business advising team for their entire academic journey. Read more about Hankamer Scholars. Honors College Programs Multi-faceted community devoted to the love of learning, cultivation of wonder, nourishing of leadership, whether your goal is medicine, law, education, ministry, graduate school etc. University Scholars (major) allows high-ability students to chart a personalized educational journey and fosters interdisciplinary conversation in the liberal arts and sciences. Great Texts (major) covers foundational works of literature, theology and philosophy, while exploring connections between what we know, how we live and what we enjoy. Honors Program students explore foundational texts within small courses; the experience culminates in a senior thesis written with a faculty mentor in any major offered at Baylor. Baylor Interdisciplinary Core (BIC) students fulfill general education requirements in small, interdisciplinary courses emphasizing global engagement. BIC students may pursue degrees and majors of their choice. Read more about the Honors College. Humanities Research Fellows This program for high-ability students encourages focus and flexibility in crafting an interdisciplinary curriculum that engages the big questions of human existence today. Research-oriented program led by a faculty cohort dedicated to equipping students for cutting-edge research in the humanities. Requires a core curriculum plus 38 hours in the humanities. Allows 50 hours of free electives to complement research focus. Students can easily take a second major, earn certificates or pursue a pre-professional trajectory. Read more about the Humanities Research Fellows. Science Research Fellows Personalized, interdisciplinary major that is the first of its kind in the nation. Intended for students who aim to have a career in research. Highly selective major with only 10 students accepted per year. Immerse yourself in research from the start of your sophomore year until graduation. Combine your science degree with other fields of study. Receive individualized mentorship and career guidance that will allow you to get into the best graduate schools in the nation. Read more about the Science Research Fellows.

Dr. Elizabeth Egbert Berghout, KU University Carillonist, will perform on the 53 bronze bells housed in the historic World War II Memorial Campanile. A few stone benches are available for seating on Campanile Hill; however listeners may choose to bring additional blankets or lawn chairs. The sound of the carillon is best at least 200 feet from the tower. Please follow KU guidelines found at protect.ku.edu. In the event of inclement weather the concert will be canceled.

Clemson University Walk Zones People on bicycles, skateboards, skates and scooters must dismount as they pass through the walk zone, preventing collisions that could hurt pedestrians and riders alike. Project PAW (free bike registration) Students register their bicycles with Clemson University Police Department and are marked with an identifying number and decal. This allows students to register the serial numbers of their valuables for easy access in the event any of these items is lost or stolen.

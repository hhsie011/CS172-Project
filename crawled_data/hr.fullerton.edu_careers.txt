Faculty Staff/Management Students Benefits at a Glance The Titan Community Work/Life Balance Notice of Availability of the Annual Security and Fire Safety Report California State University Fullerton is committed to assisting all members of the community in providing for their own safety and security. Pursuant to the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act, the 2021 Annual Security Report (ASR) is available on the CSUF website. The ASR contains the current security and safety-related policy statements, emergency preparedness and evacuation information, crime prevention and sexual assault prevention information, and information about drug and alcohol prevention programming. The ASR also contains statistics of Clery crimes for California State University, Fullerton for 2018-2020. A paper copy of the ASR is available upon request by contacting the Office of the Clery Director located at the Langsdorf Hall Room 805 (Office of the Vice President for Student Affairs). In addition, pursuant to the Higher Education Opportunity Act, the 2021 Annual Fire Safety Report (AFSR) is available on the CSUF website. The purpose of this report is to disclose statistics for fires that occurred within California State University, Fullerton housing facilities for 2018-2020, and to distribute fire safety policies and procedures intended to promote safety on campus. A paper copy of the AFSR is available upon request by contacting the Housing Office located at The Housing & Residential Engagement Main Office.

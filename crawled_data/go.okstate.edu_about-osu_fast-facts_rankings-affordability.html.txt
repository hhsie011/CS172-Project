That’s why Oklahoma State University continues to provide excellent value at a great price and is named to a variety of national lists for its value. Tuition and fees are 69 percent of the national average for land-grant universities; cost of attendance is 78 percent of the national average. Financial Aid Office 15Years of best value rankings OSU has been named to Kiplinger’s Personal Finance Best College Value list 15 times since 2000. No. 1most affordable university in Oklahoma According to the College Affordability Guide, OSU is the No. 1 most affordable college in Oklahoma in 2021. Graduate with less debt Nearly 50 percent of OSU students graduate with no debt. The average amount of debt for OSU graduates is $22,591, well below the national average and the lowest in the Big 12.

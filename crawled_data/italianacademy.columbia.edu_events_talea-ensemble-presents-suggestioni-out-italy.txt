Composers Stefano Gervasoni, Pierluigi Billone, Fabio Nieder, and Gabriele Vanoni will be in attendance. The Talea Ensemble presents “Suggestioni: Out of Italy” at Columbia University’s Italian Academy for Advanced Studies. The Talea Ensemble is dedicated to introducing new, groundbreaking music to the world and increasing awareness and appreciation of contemporary music. Through their concerts in New York and abroad, The Talea Ensemble gives the distinctive voices of conductors an opportunity to be heard by broad audiences, performing their innovative works alongside established contemporary repertoire. For more information please visit www.taleaensemble.org. The program is as follows: Stefano Gervasoni: Due poesie francesi d'Ungaretti (1994) *US Premiere Stefano Gervasoni: Due poesie francesi di Beckett (1995) *US Premiere Luca Francesconi: Viaggiatore Insonne (1983) Pierluigi Billone: Mani de Leonardis (2004) Fabio Nieder: Sogni 10 lunedi (2005) *US Premiere Matteo Franceschini: Set (2008) *US Premiere Federico Gardella: Armida's Garden (2010) *World Premiere Gabriele Vanoni: New Work (2010) *World Premiere Upcoming Events The Italian Academy for Advanced Studies, Columbia University1161 Amsterdam Avenue · New York, NY 10027 Phone (212) 854-2306 Contact Us [email protected] Follow Us Facebook Twitter Instagram Youtube Columbia University ©2022 Columbia University Accessibility Nondiscrimination Careers Built using Columbia Sites Back to Top Close

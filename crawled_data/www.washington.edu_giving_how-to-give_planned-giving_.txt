Charitable Gift Annuity Rates to Increase! Charitable Gift Annuity (CGA) rates to increase, effective July 1, 2022. With a CGA, you make a gift using cash or marketable securities to the UW. In return, the University pays you and/or a loved one a fixed amount annually for life. To learn more, see below Gifts Providing Income To You.

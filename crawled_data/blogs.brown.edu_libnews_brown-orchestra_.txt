The exhibit chronicles the development of orchestral involvement on Brown’s campus from 1919 onward. Highlights include programs from performances with Leonard Bernstein, Itzhak Perlman, and Steve Reich, as well as the merger of the Brown Orchestra and Pembroke Orchestra, which happened over 30 years before the two colleges formally joined together. Dates: April 22 – October 1, 2016 Time: Library Hours Location: Orwig Music Library, 1 Young Orchard Avenue, Providence This entry was posted in Exhibits & Events, Exhibits & Events, General Interest, Special Collections, University Archives, University Archives & History by Jennifer Braga.

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index International Affairs Menu Search TTU International Affairs K12geo: Exploring World Cultures K-12 GEO Mission The mission of K-12 Global Education Outreach (GEO) is to foster cultural awareness and encourage the pursuit of higher education through programs, partnerships, and outreach opportunities. Vision Statement K-12 Global Education Outreach (GEO) endeavors to build a globally - engaged community of learners through outreach opportunities that foster intercultural understanding and enrich the quality of life for our students and communities in West Texas. OIA Mission Statement The Office of International Affairs (OIA) advances the global vision of Texas Tech University by promoting international leadership, awareness, education, scholarship, and outreach for the University and the broader community. Texas Tech University Mission Statement As a public research university, Texas Tech advances knowledge through innovative and creative teaching, research, and scholarship. The university is dedicated to student success by preparing learners to be ethical leaders for a diverse and globally competitive workforce. The university is committed to enhancing the cultural and economic development of the state, nation, and world.* *Approved by the Texas Tech University Board of Regents on May 14, 2010 <!--

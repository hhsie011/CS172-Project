Certificate of Insurance Requirements W - 9 Information Gateway to Finance provides guidance about the buy-to-pay process at Temple. The team in Purchasing (buying) and Accounts Payable (paying) can help with navigating TUmarketplace, Concur and TUportal if you are an employee; or how to become a business partner with the university. Our objective is to provide the highest level of service to faculty, staff, students and external customers. The Diamond Dollars program for students, employees, and departments makes buying and paying on campus as simple as carrying your Temple ID. Temple's campus-based debit card solution eliminates the need to carry cash.

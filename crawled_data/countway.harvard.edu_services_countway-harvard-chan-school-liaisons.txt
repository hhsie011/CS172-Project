Countway Library Harvard Chan Liaisons are a specialized group of librarians from the Research & Instruction team, Publishing and Data Services, Access Services, and the Center for the History of Medicine. We support Harvard Chan School students, faculty and staff in research and publication, as well as course assignments, projects, theses and more. For announcements about events and new resources, be sure to visit our Harvard Chan Engage site! Liaison services may include: Research development and consultation Custom instruction for classes and/or small groups Mediated searching for Public Health topics and resources Database and Harvard resource tutorials Access and reserve guidance Conducting your Review Endnote and Zotero classes Center for the History of Medicine classes and seminars Presentation Skills Research data management support One-on-one consultation and instruction Project / assignment/ thesis consultation Collaboration and mediated searching for literature, scoping and systematic reviews And developing video content For questions or custom requests, contact your Countway Harvard Chan School Liaison Librarians Chan School of Public Health Liaisons Carol Mita, MLIS Research and Instruction Librarian Email 617-432-4183 Iris Jahng, MLIS, MA Digital Scholarship Librarian Email Jessica Murphy, MLIS Public Services Librarian Email 617-432-6209 Beth Carey Access and Reference Assistant Email 617-432-2136 Erika Nosike Access and Reference Assistant Email 617-432-2136 Services Find Materials Research & Instruction Team Society Librarians Countway Spaces Publishing & Data Services Harvard Chan School Liaisons Collections 10 Shattuck Street Boston, MA 02115 (617) 432-2136 Footer Privacy Policy Archived Alerts © 2022 by the President and Fellows of Harvard College Facebook twitter instagram

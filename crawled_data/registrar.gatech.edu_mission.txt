Ensure continuously satisfactory service through good management practices and responsible stewardship and utilization of resources. Establish daily business practices which reflect the most advanced technological methods available to improve customer service. Make student data available in easily accessible formats to departments for administrative purposes and research. Interpret Institute, Board of Regents, and governmental policies to faculty, staff, students, parents and the general public. Assess the effectiveness of services provided to ensure equitable and ethical treatment of all customers.

Curators Giuseppe Gerbino (Columbia University) Mauro Calcagno (University of Pennsylvania) Thomas Peattie (University of Mississippi; Academy Fellow) Sponsors Paul Sacher Foundation. The Italian Academy; Music Department; Italian Department; Heyman Center for the Humanities; European Institute; and Reiner Fund (all at Columbia University). Italian Cultural Institute of New York. The Center for Italian Studies and the Music Department (both at the University of Pennsylvania). This exhibition is part of the Monteverdi at 450: Experiments in Sound, Image, and Movement event.

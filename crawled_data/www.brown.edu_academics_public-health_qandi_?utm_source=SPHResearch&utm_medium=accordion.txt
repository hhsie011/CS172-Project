Learn more The Center for Long-Term Care Quality & Innovation, which we call Q&I, is unique among academic research centers nationwide in our focus and approach. Our mission is to catalyze interventional research that improves care for people receiving post-acute and long-term care. We partner with innovators, researchers, and healthcare providers to use real-world pragmatic methods that accelerate testing interventions and translating findings into practice. Our website includes details about what we do and how.

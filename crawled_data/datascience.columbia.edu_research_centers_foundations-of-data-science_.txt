across the data sciences and engineering. Our research focuses on formal and mathematical models for data processing, as well as on issues concerning the engineering of large-scale data processing systems. We support and amplify the study of five specific markets – Cybersecurity, Financial Analytics, Health Analytics, New Media and Smart Cities – all of which lie at the center of New York City's innovation economy.

The University recognizes and rewards individuals on the basis of qualifications and performance. The University maintains an affirmative action program in employment for minorities, women, persons with disabilities and veterans, and requires its employees to take good faith efforts to comply with the program. The University encourages those with whom it conducts business to meet the commitments of this important program. The President delegates the authority to implement this Policy Statement to the Office of Institutional Equity and Diversity.

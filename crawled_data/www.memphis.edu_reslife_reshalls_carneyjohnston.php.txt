InstagramFacebooktwitter Home Housing and Residence Life Housing Carney-Johnston at Lambuth Carney-Johnston at Lambuth This historic building, located on the quadrangle between Wilder Student Union and the Athletic Building on The University of Memphis Lambuth Campus, located in Jackson, TN, offers housing for up to 190 students in single and double rooms with suite-style baths. Carney-Johnston's recent upgrades include new fire safety systems, heating and cooling improvements, as well as new paint and carpet in every room. The building is ADA accessible. In addition to separate floors for male and female residents, study lounges on the second floor offer 24-hour access to a quiet study area. A large living room on the first floor can be used for social activities for Carney-Johnston Hall residents. Enjoy the free laundry facilities on every floor.

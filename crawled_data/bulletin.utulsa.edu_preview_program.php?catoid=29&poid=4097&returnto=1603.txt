School of Accounting and Computer Information Systems . Director Karen Cravens The minor in computer information systems is designed for students who want to develop expertise in information technology skills beyond the level required by their major field of study. Students must fulfill the prerequisites and complete the 15 hours with a minimum grade point average of 2.0. CIS Requirements (15 hours) CIS 2013 Business Programming Concepts I CIS 3023 Business Programming Concepts II CIS 3043 Telecommunications CIS 4043 Database Design and Applications CIS 4053 Systems Analysis and Design Note: Prerequisites are required for many of the courses in the computer information systems minor.

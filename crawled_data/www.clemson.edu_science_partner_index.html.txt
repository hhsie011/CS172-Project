The College seeks to strengthen and enhance its mission and vision by joining forces with private businesses to collaborating with public institutions. Our alumni and friends help to create, sustain, and enhance existing programs and new opportunities within the College by providing support for students, faculty, facilities and other critical needs. The faculty, students and staff of the College are passionate to generate new knowledge through their research and to share their knowledge and expertise with the broader public through a variety of outreach programs. It is an exciting time to be at Clemson as we collectively advance ScienceForward.

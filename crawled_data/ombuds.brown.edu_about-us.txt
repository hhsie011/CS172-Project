The Ombuds Office follows its Guiding Principles, is laser-focused on its Mission, and provides a host of conflict management related services. Additionally, the Ombuds Office is a voluntary program. No one is required to use the Ombuds Office, but if they do, they will be understood to have agreed to abide by the Guiding Principles, including not calling the University Ombuds to testify with respect to confidential communications. Moreover, as a matter of policy, Brown does not call on the University Ombuds to disclose confidential communications or to serve as a witness. The Ombuds Office offers a confidential, informal, neutral, and independent place to have a quiet conversation, work out how to navigate a situation or the organization, or manage and solve conflicts/concerns.

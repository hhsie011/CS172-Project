The College of Engineering at the University of Miami offers a range of doctoral programs. Students can pursue PhD degrees with a single faculty member, or collaboratively in interdisciplinary projects with multiple faculty investigators. The College is engaged in addressing challenging problems, and organized in six strategic thrust areas to solve some of the most pressing societal problems. If selected for admission through a competitive process, all doctoral students receive financial assistance including a monthly stipend and a full tuition waiver. The College of Engineering has six comprehensive departments, and each of them offer PhD Programs: Biomedical Engineering Civil and Architectural Engineering Chemical, Environmental and Materials Engineering Electrical and Computer Engineering Industrial Engineering Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering PhD Research Advisors Potential applicants are strongly encouraged to contact faculty experts in their field of expertise by searching through our database of faculty experts. You can also choose earn a PhD degree in Engineering with Advisors from other highly ranked programs at other departments and schools in the University – such as the Bascom Palmer School of Opthalmology, Sylvester Comprehensive Cancer Center, Miami Project, Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences, and others. Application Deadline: February 1 The University of Miami College of Engineering admits PhD students only for entry for the Fall semester. The application for admission must be completed before the submission deadline (international applicants are highly encouraged to submit their completed applications as early as possible in order to ensure ample time for the student visa process if accepted for admission).

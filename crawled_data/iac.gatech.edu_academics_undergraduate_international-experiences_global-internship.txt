The Global Internship Program includes work experiences designed to give students practical training, cross-cultural exposure and learning, and help them acquire the skills that will set them apart from their peers when they enter the job market after graduation. Opportunities are available during summer, fall, and spring semesters. The Global Internship Program is part of the International Plan, a Georgia Tech initiative offering students a challenging academic program that develops global competence within the context of their majors. The Global Internship Program is administered by Georgia Tech's Office of International Education.

Directions Walking Walking to UTA from the center of campus takes about 10 to 15 minutes. The UTA Building is two blocks south of the main UT campus. Biking Bike racks can be found on the north side of the building (facing 17th street). Bus The building is also accessible via many Capital Metro bus routes including the free UT Shuttle Service. Use the Capital Metro Trip Planner to check specific routes and times. Car Paid parking is available in the attached GUG garage or on-street via meter parking.

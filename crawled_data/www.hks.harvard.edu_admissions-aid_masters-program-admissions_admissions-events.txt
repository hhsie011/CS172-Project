Admissions & Aid Master's Program Admissions Admissions Events Due to circumstances related to the pandemic, the Harvard Kennedy School campus remains closed for in-person visits and events, but members of the HKS community are eager to interact with prospective applicants virtually. We frequently host virtual events that allow prospective applicants to hear directly from staff, faculty, alumni, and current students—and welcome you to join us. Join us for a virtual information session Register We also welcome you to look at our public events calendar for speakers, workshops, lectures, and discussions. They are open to you.

Contact HR Search Search Home WorkLife Benefits and Resources Elder Care and Caregiving Elder Care and Caregiving Managing work while caring for your loved one is challenging. Carebridge can help! Link Carebridge Eldercare Specialists are available to help you and your loved ones make informed decisions, save time, and feel less alone as you juggle many caregiving, work, and personal responsibilities. Skilled Carebridge eldercare professionals will provide you with confidential guidance and resource pathfinding for your loved ones encountering issues associate with aging. Contact Carebridge by calling 800-437-0911. Eligibility for services: All faculty, staff, retirees eligible to participate in the University’s medical plan. Dependent Care Subsidy Program Link In support of the changing needs of families, the University’s Dependent Care Subsidy Program provides subsidies to eligible faculty and staff to assist with caring for an adult dependent. The tax-free subsidy can be used to pay expenses on eligible dependent care costs provided by qualified caregivers. The subsidies are deposited into a Dependent Care Flexible Spending Account (FSA) on behalf of the eligible employee. Visit the Dependent Care Subsidy Program webpage to learn more.

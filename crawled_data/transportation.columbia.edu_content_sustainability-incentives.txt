These resources are intended to help affiliates access the campus in a sustainable, healthy, and stress-free way. Green Passes for Low-Emission Vehicles Enroll in the Thruway Green Pass Discount Plan and receive a 10% discount on all NY-NJ state-line crossings and Thruway tolls for Hybrid-specific emissions outlined on the Thruway Authority's website. All Green Passes for low-emission vehicles can be arranged by contacting E-ZPass New York online or by phone at 1-800-333-TOLL. Reduced Tolls for Carpooling Carpooling allows you to share the cost and driving responsibilities of your commute with another passenger. Learn more about incentives that support carpooling. Park & Rides Park your car outside the city and travel in on a bus, or park your car and travel in with a co-worker. Learn more about Park & Ride options here. Tips for Successful Carpooling You don't have to carpool every day. Start small and agree to parameters with your partner. Learn more about successful carpooling.

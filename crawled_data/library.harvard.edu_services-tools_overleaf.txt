Overview An online LaTeX edit, Orverleaf provides templates for many prestigious journals, and eases the process of collaborative writing. You can store your research in the cloud and update easily with a team of researchers. Overleaf can also be linked to other services such as Mendeley, Git and Plot.ly to best fit into your workflow. Get Overleaf Free Overleaf Pro+ accounts are available for all Harvard University students, faculty and staff. You'll need to verify a Harvard University email address in order to receive your free 20GB Pro+ account. Get Overleaf Get Help We can provide training and assistance with Overleaf, including its ShareLaTeX capabilities.

Welcome to Fraternity and Sorority Life! Our staff are a part of the Center for Student Engagement at Georgia Tech. Student Engagement empowers and supports registered student organizations to catalyze belonging and create an engaging campus community. Our staff have a strong history of developing and working closely with Georgia Tech’s outstanding student leaders. The fraternity and sorority community is a campus tradition that spans over 133 years.

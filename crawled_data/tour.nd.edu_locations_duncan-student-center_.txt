Dining locations include Hagerty Family Cafe featuring coffee, crepes, smoothies, gelato, and pastries. Inside door 15 are two other locations, Modern Market and Star Ginger. Modern Market’s menu includes pizza, sandwiches, breakfast burritos, soups and salads. Star Ginger serves pho noodle bowls, poke bowls, stir fry, and sushi.

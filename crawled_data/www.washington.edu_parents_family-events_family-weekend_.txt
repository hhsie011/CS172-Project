There’s plenty to celebrate this year as we reunite at the UW Family tailgate, cheer on the Dawgs at Homecoming and throw a celebration in Red Square for W Day. Bring the entire family and build your own weekend of fun! Stay Connected with us! Invitations to UW Family Weekend will be sent to our current UW families through our UW Parent Insider Enewsletter at uw.edu/parents. Make sure you’ve signed up! Stay healthy! Proof of vaccination or a negative COVID test within 72 hours will be required to attend UW Family Weekend and masks will be required at all indoor and outdoor events.

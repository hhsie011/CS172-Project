la Formation Continue de Gabès (CREFOC) – Avenue Aboulkacem Chebbi, Gabès. Columbia Global Centers ¦ Tunis, the Hedi Bouchamaoui Foundation and the Commissariat Régional de l’Education de Gabès (Ministry of Education) invite you to meet with the representatives of EducationUSA in Tunisia, and of Worldwide Studies who will speak about the Education in USA,universities, the process of admission, fields of study, etc. High school students and fresh undergraduates are encouraged to attend, but registration is NOT required. Location: Centre Régional de l’Education et de la Formation Continue de Gabès (CREFOC) – Avenue Aboulkacem Chebbi, Gabès.

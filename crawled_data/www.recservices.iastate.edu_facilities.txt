View All Facility Hours View Facility Map Summer Hours However you play… Whether it is your first time in one of our facilities or you go twice a day, Recreation Services strives to make newcomers and rookies feel right at home. Our facilities strive to accommodate all guests of varying backgrounds and abilities. However you play, it is Your Rec Your Way. State Gym State Gym is located on the west side of campus. It was recently renovated in 2012 and now offers a variety of new amenities such as the rock climbing wall and a leisure pool with a high-dive. State gym is a great place not only to exercise, but to have a great time, too! Lied Rec Lied Recreation Athletic Center was built in 1990 and serves as the recreation center on the east side of campus. Found next to the Maple, Willow, Larch dorm complex, Lied has an array of activities to suit your workout needs or to just have some fun! Beyer Beyer Hall is located on the west side of campus, immediately across the street from State Gym. Beyer offers a variety of fitness equipment, training rooms, and group fitness classes. Beyer is also home to the new Gaming and Esports Room and SHOP (the student food pantry).

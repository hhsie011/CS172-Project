Wellness Ambassadors Accreditation About the Vice Provost Contact Faculty Explore Back to main menu Faculty Handbook Research Global Policies Contact Program Overview The Wellness Ambassadors Program, begun as an initiative of the Faculty Senate, trains Penn faculty members to be wellness liaisons to other Penn faculty members. All Wellness Ambassadors receive ICARE training, along with detailed information about wellness initiatives and resources available to Penn students, and works to promote an awareness and understanding of student wellness both within their individual School and across the campus.

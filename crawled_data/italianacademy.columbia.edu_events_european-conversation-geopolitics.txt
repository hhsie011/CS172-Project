Use the previous and next buttons to change the displayed slide Slide 1: Portrait of Enrico Letta Slide 2: Portrait of Bert Koenders Previous Next November 13, 2020 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Enrico Letta and Bert Koenders on the US election and EU foreign policy A live virtual event This online conversation on geopolitics and European Union foreign affairs features former Italian Prime Minister Enrico Letta and former Dutch Minister of Foreign Affairs Bert Koenders. The speakers will discuss how the current pandemic, the US election and the new global economic challenges are dramatically reshaping geopolitical dynamics across the world. Co-moderated by Eleonora Smeriglio and Kolja Verhage, two current students at Columbia's School of International and Public Affairs. The event is part of the “Europe in Transition” series organized by CEUSA, the Columbia University European Union Students Association.

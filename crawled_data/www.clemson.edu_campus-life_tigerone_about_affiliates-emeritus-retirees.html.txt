Clemson Home Campus Life TigerOne About the TigerOne ID Current: Affiliates, Emeritus, and Retirees Affiliate, Emeritus, and Retiree Identification How to Obtain Your ID Affiliates, Emeritus, and Retirees are not eligible for the TigerOne Mobile ID. You may request a physical TigerOne card online for a $30 fee. To request your physical TigerOne Card, please visit the Online Office to get started. Lost ID Lost cards can be requested for a charge of $30. Please submit a Lost Card Request. Broken ID If you suspect your card is broken, please submit a Broken Card Request. We will troubleshoot your issue and contact you on resolution. If your card is broken through normal wear and tear, a replacement card free of charge will be provided. You will be required to turn in your old, nonfunctioning card to receive a new one at no charge. If you do not have the old card, a $30 charge will be applied.

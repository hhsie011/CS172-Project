There a two main ways to recruit at the University of Houston, through University Career Services and College Based Career Centers: University Career Services University Career Services (UCS) serves all 46,000 students as well as recent alumni and alumni subscribers. Employers interested in recruiting from all colleges or disciplines may find it best to start here. UCS serves the UH campus as well as UH at Sugar Land and UH at Katy.

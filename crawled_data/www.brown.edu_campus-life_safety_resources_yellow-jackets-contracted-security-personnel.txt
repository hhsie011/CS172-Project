They are working with DPS to help keep you and our community safe. Brown has contracted the services of APG Security to augment the services provided by the Brown Department of Public Safety (DPS). The APG security personnel can be spotted wearing yellow and tan apparrel at various locations and events across Brown campus during the year. They are strategically placed throughout the campus at locations that have heavy pedestrian traffic. APG Security personnel monitor areas selected by the DPS and serve as additional eyes and ears for the department. They are equipped with radios and are able to communicate with DPS personnel.

Our alumni community is one of the nation’s most powerful networks—starting at matriculation and lasting a lifetime. Your journey is just beginning. Dartmouth young alumni, or recent graduates of the last decade, like you, are 11,000+ strong and make up about 18 percent of total Dartmouth alumni worldwide. Our recent graduates are as unique as Dartmouth itself. No matter where life may take you, you can stay connected to your alma mater, friends, and classmates through the alumni community and alumni organizations, volunteer opportunities, special events and programs, and much more. Whether by attending an alumni reunion; joining class, club, or group events (virtually or in person); mentoring a current student; or building your personal alumni network, our goal is to help you stay connected and engaged with Dartmouth. Learn more about how you can be involved with the Dartmouth alumni community and fellow recent grads.

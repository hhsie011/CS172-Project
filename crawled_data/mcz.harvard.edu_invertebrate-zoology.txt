Squat lobster from near American Samoa, MCZ 153400, photo by J.W. Trimble History The Museum of Comparative Zoology (MCZ) was founded in 1859. Shortly after the completion of the first wing of the Museum of Comparative Zoology in 1860, nearly 100,000 specimens were already part of the Department of Invertebrate Zoology (IZ). Currently under the direction of Curators Gonzalo Giribet and Mansi Srivastrava the collections continue to grow, and are a valuable resource to scientists, students, and others.

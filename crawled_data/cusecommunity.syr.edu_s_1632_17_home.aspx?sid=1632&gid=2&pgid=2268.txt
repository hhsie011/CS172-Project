Find out what’s happening on campus and in the lives of classmates and friends through social platforms that let you stay connected with SU and other alumni. ’Cuse Community Need to update your address, email, or current employment? Want to search for college friends, submit a class note, or read about your fellow classmates? Join the original SU social network—the ‘Cuse Community. Membership is free and exclusive to SU alumni. If you’re already registered, go right to the ’Cuse Community. To Register… First, get your Alumni ID. This isn’t your student ID number, but a special verification code we’ll give you. Just call us at 315.443.3258 or 800.782.5867, or e-mail us at sualumni@syr.edu. Once you have your number, register here! Lost your ID or password? If you’re registered but can’t remember your User ID or Password, retrieve it. Alumni Social Networks Look for Syracuse University on the social and multimedia networks listed below.

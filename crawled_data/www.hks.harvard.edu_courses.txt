They include core courses that are required for earning a degree, along with electives, modules and January-term options to cater to different interests and schedules. While classroom learning is integral to an HKS education, it is elevated by the many extra-curricular activities and programs, including lectures, seminars, brown bags, conferences and experiential learning, on- and off-campus.

GT Community Inquiries Contact: Office of EVPR Team All Other Inquiries Contact: Georgia Parmelee, Director of Research Communications Breadcrumb Home Office of Research Development Office of Research Development The mission of the Office of Research Development at Georgia Tech is to support development of large-scale research funding proposals. Support We provide strategic guidance as well as practical support for development of large, complex proposals. Strategy Guidance on strengths and gaps relative to funding opportunity and sponsor goals Development of project framing Preparation for conversations with agency officials and agency visits Project Management Management of proposal checklists and timelines Facilitation of productive meetings that include decision making and task assignment Proposal Content and Review Development of templates outlining proposals responsive to the solicitation and review criteria Review and editing to strengthen key components of proposals Management of internal and external reviews Our support packages are tailored to the needs and resources of the investigator team and depend on capacity of the office at the time of request. We coordinate with other campus resources (unit and central) to help ensure timely submission of strong funding proposals. Scope We support large-scale research proposals that typically include: Engagement of teams that span institutions and disciplines Formation of a center or institute with multiple functions Proposed budgets greater than $1 million per year and $10 million total Please contact Dr. Rebecca Terns at rebecca.terns@gatech.edu as early as possible in the proposal development process to inquire about support.

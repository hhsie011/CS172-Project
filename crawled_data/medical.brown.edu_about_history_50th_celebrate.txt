Starting in 2022, Brown is celebrating 50 years of impact in medical education and research, the contributions of alumni and faculty in Rhode Island and around the world and the promising future ahead for the Warren Alpert Medical School of Brown University. Share your excitement about this milestone and use the hashtag #BrownMD50 in your posts on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn.

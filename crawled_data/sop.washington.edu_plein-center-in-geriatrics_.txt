Home Plein Center for Geriatric Pharmacy Research, Education and Outreach Who We Are History Faculty & Staff Contact Info Education & Training Plein Certificate in Geriatric Pharmacy Plein Scholars Postdoctoral Research Fellowship Research Outreach Era Living Partnership ASCP Student Chapter News Events Previous Events Giving Collaborating to Promote Healthy Aging The Plein Center for Geriatrics was established with the generous support of Joy and Elmer Plein. The Plein Center is a global leader in transformative pharmacy research, education and outreach. We are committed to optimizing older adults’ medication outcomes and promoting healthy aging. Research Plein Center faculty, students, and postdocs conduct translational science related to the health and wellbeing of older adults and their care partners. Education & Training We offer three training opportunities to gain clinical and research skills. Plein Certificate in Geriatric Pharmacy Plein Scholars Postdoctoral Research Fellowship Outreach We promote safe and effective medication use in older adults through outreach and advocacy efforts. Support the Plein Center for Geriatrics You can directly affect the future of geriatric pharmacy, by supporting the Plein Center for Geriatrics, which will promote the discovery and optimal use of medications in older adults.

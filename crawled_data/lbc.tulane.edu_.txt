THE LAVIN-BERNICK CENTER FOR UNIVERSITY LIFE & THE COMMONS The Lavin-Bernick Center for University Life & The Commons The Lavin-Bernick Center for University Life and The Commons is more than just a building. We are a hub for campus life that houses meeting space, campus programming, dining options, the Bookstore, student involvement opportunities, and lots of student resources. We strive to enhance the Tulane student experience by creating an innovative, dynamic and inclusive environment through quality facilities, programs, and services.

International Education Month Global Engagement Exposition Global Film Series Global Engagement Exposition The Global Engagement Exposition is an annual celebration of achievements in international education and research at NC State. Global Engagement Expo held in April 2021 in Hunt Library. The event features an awards ceremony, poster session, networking and lunch. Nominees for the Outstanding Global Engagement Award, Jackson Rigney International Service Award and the Distinguished Global Alumni Award will be recognized, and the award recipients will be announced at this event. The 2023 Global Engagement Exposition will take place on April 19, 2023 in the Talley Student Union Ballrooms.

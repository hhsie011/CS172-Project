Programs Academics Centers & Initiatives News & Events Georgia Tech Scheller’s Executive Education programs and courses empower professionals from all backgrounds to drive growth and lead innovation in a changing, tech-driven world. The Georgia Tech Scheller Difference Reputation Georgia Tech Scheller is known for educating at the intersection of business and technology, with a focus on championing innovation and expanding access. Expertise Our Executive Education faculty are hand-picked to teach in our programs. Their expertise, depth of knowledge, teaching skill, and most importantly, real-world experience, is what makes them great at what they do. Experiential Learning Our programs and courses blend thought leadership with practical experience. You’ll engage in meaningful interactions and exercises with professors and peers that will help you to effectively link theory with practice. Executive Education Opportunities Lean Six Sigma Programs This is your chance to see stubborn challenges in a new light. Lean Six Sigma principles will help you uncover inefficiencies and drive impact, no matter your industry or role. Our Green Belt and Black Belt certification programs will teach you to think differently, making you an invaluable asset in any organization. Learn More Custom Corporate Programs Custom corporate programs are learning experiences designed to meet your company’s unique needs. We’ll work with you each step the way to create a fully customized solution to address your most pressing challenges.

Rockefeller, Jr. Library, otherwise known as "the Rock," serves as the primary teaching and research library for the humanities, social sciences, and fine arts. The John D. Rockefeller, Jr. Library is located on the corner of Prospect and College Streets. The Sidney E. Frank Digital Studio, Patrick Ma Digital Scholarship Lab, and Hecker Training Room are located on the first floor.

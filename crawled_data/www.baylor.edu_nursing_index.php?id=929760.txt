Riley, DNP, RN, NNP-BC Cheryl A. Riley, DNP, RN, NNP-BC Clinical Professor and DNP-NNP Specialty Track Coordinator Education: DNP Creighton University, Nebraska MSN Baylor University, Texas BSN Baylor University, Texas Contact Info: Email: Cheryl_Riley@baylor.edu Office: 333 North Washington Avenue, Ste. 301.32, Dallas, Texas 75246 Phone: 972.576.9158 Cheryl Riley has been a Neonatal Nurse Practitioner for 10 years and involved in the care of neonates since 1995. She is the Coordinator of the Neonatal Nurse Practitioner program at Baylor University, Louise Herrington School of Nursing. She received both her BSN and MSN from Baylor University and her DNP at Creighton University in Omaha, Nebraska. Her interests include the late preterm infant and delayed umbilical cord clamping. Dr. Riley has presented locally, nationally and internationally in Vietnam and in India.

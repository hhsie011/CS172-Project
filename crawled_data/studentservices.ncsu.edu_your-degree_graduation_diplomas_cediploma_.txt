University now offers Certified Electronic Diplomas/Certificates! Register/Download now All Certified Electronic Diplomas/Certificates are free of cost for degrees awarded in Spring 2020 or after! Watch an Overview video to learn more! Key Benefits More than a paper credential An official, secure and verifiable PDF version of your accomplishment Different from a transcript Unlimited sharing with family, friends, employers and social media Proof of education with only the important details Simple for employers to read and understand Better than an eDiploma Each credential provides a unique 12-digit CeDiD* The CeDiD allows for instant proof of your accomplishment using Validation Services Enhances your resume Expedite the verification process by providing your CeDiD and validation URL on your resume *CeDiD- Certified Electronic Document Identifier Validation Page Example and Demo Your browser does not support the video tag. Highly Secure Each CeDiploma and/or CeCertificate can independently validated here by future employers, State Government Licensing Agencies and any other entity that wishes to validate your achievement. The document is digitally signed and encrypted, providing greater trust and acceptance. For security reasons, the electronic credential cannot be printed. Note: A credential may not be available for all conferral dates. For additional information about the Certified Electronic Credential and its features, please visit cecredentialtrust.com. Questions?

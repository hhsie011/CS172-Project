Conversation with Jeffrey Sachs and Felipe Larraín Events Past Event The Ages of Globalization: A Conversation with Jeffrey Sachs and Felipe Larraín Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 18, 2020 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM * Please note: Attendance for this webinar has been oversubscribed. You can view the live streaming of this event on the Santiago Center's YouTube channel. Jeffrey D. Sachs, Director of the Center for Sustainable Development at Columbia University, and Felipe Larraín, Professor at Universidad Católica and Chile's former Minister of Finance, will discuss how challenges and opportunities of the 21st century can be met. The discussion, which will be moderated by Karen Poniachik, will be based upon Sachs’ latest book, “The Ages of Globalization”, which examines world history to shed light on how today’s urgent, global issues require concerted, planet-wide action.

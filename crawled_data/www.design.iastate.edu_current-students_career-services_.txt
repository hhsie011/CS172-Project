Career Services Design Career Services offers programs and services to help College of Design students develop life-long skills in career development. We offers one-on-one appointments with students an alumni, presentations, club and class visits, on-demand content related to job seeking, networking, interviewing, offers, and negotiation. We are available to discuss career paths for design majors, developing resumes, job and internship seeking, communication, preparing for interviews, developing portfolios, understanding and negotiating job offers, and more. We facilitate connections with employers seeking design students and alumni with annual in-person and virtual career fairs focusing on College of Design programs, employer presentations, and job and internship postings through CyHire, an online system exclusively for ISU students and alumni. We looking forward to working with you!

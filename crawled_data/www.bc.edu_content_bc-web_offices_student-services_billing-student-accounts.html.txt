Tuition & Fees Loan Repayment Medical Insurance Eagle One Card MBTA Passes & Parking Permits Billing & Student Accounts The Billing & Student Accounts team is responsible for the day-to-day operation of student account receivables. In line with Boston College's Jesuit ideals, Billing & Student Accounts is committed to serving students and their families with courtesy and compassion in ensuring payment of University-owed debt. In addition to awards offered by Financial Aid, Billing & Student Accounts offers a number of alternative payment methods to help families pay the balance of a Boston College education. Our team also maintains an online billing system, complete with e-mail notification and authorized third-party payer privileges, for added convenience.

Current Students Full list of resources available to students Undergraduate Student Success Center (USSC) The USSC is a coordinated network of resources focused explicitly on the goal of student success. The Undergraduate Student Success Center helps transition students to a good fit major, offers tutoring and learning skills workshops, pre-professional and career advising, and supports first-generation and transfer college students. Learn More Provost's Prize for Creative Writing The Provost’s Prize for Creative Writing is granted each spring and fall semester in acknowledgement of the best prose (fall) or poetry (spring) submission from an undergraduate student.

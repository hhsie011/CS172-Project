Launched in February 2020, the state-of-the art Studio 225 in Providence’s Jewelry District serves as the University’s new broadcast interview studio and a critical tool in promoting the expertise of Brown faculty through national and global news media. Through interviews broadcast from Studio 225, faculty and other Brown scholars can participate in live and taped television interviews with domestic and international networks. For broadcast interviews, Studio 225 is equipped with: 82-inch 4k display with standard Brown backdrop images PTZ camera Television-ready lighting Interruptible foldback (IFB) and talkback systems Control booth equipped with Tricaster, LTN broadcast technology and full network redundancy Arranging an Interview Interview requests typically originate based on conversations among Brown faculty members, OUC writers / media officers and television network producers. After scheduling and confirmation, each interview is staffed by media technicians who coordinate with the network and support faculty in the studio. For broadcast interview inquiries, contact one of OUC’s writers / media officers, call (401) 863-1638 or email [email protected]. [Note: Because the COVID-19 pandemic has limited on-campus operations at Brown to essential activities, the studio is temporarily closed.

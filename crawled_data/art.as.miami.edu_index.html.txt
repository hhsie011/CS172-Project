UM Wynwood Gallery The University of Miami off-campus gallery is located in the historic Wynwood Art District. Click here for more information. Lowe Art Museum The Lowe Art Museum serves as a teaching and exhibiting resource through its permanent and borrowed collections. Online Gallery Student & Faculty Exhibitions Ceramics + Glass + Sculpture Students are able to utilize these studios as well as a large covered outdoor space. Graphic Design + Multimedia + Photography Graphic Design and Multimedia is part of the Electronic Media area and focuses on educating and encouraging emerging artists, designers, and video artists. Painting + Drawing Our painting program is based on our rigorous foundation series and is designed to train each painting student to skillfully render images in oil and acrylic paint and then use these skills to make original art. Printmaking The Printmaking Area offers a sequence of courses in Intaglio/Relief, Lithography, and Silkscreen and Computer Assisted Printmaking.

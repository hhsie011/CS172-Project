On-site access to participating BorrowDirect libraries is now available to most Brown ID holders. Visitors from other BorrowDirect libraries are now eligible for access and on-site borrowing at Brown. » Services for BROWN ID Holders » Services for BorrowDirect ID Holders On-site Services at BorrowDirect libraries for Brown ID Holders Who can use this service? Most holders of current Brown IDs, with clear records at the Brown Library, have access and borrowing privileges at participating BorrowDirect institutions. This includes: Brown faculty and visiting scholars/fellows Brown undergraduate, graduate, and medical students Brown staff How does it work? A Brown photo ID will be required for access or borrowing. When registering for borrowing privileges, visitors will be required to validate as a BorrowDirect user. Local policies apply. Check the participating library's profile. What can be borrowed? Most books in libraries' circulating collections can be checked out. Items that are requestable via BorrowDirect are generally available for local circulation. Consider checking the BorrowDirect catalog first. Local policies apply. Brown Library On-site Services for BorrowDirect ID Holders Who can use this service? Students, faculty, and staff from participating member institutions will be accorded access and borrowing privileges at the Brown Library. Where do I go? Library privileges are issued at the Rockefeller Library during building hours until 10 p.m. See a map of library locations. Brown Libraries available for on-site use: Rockefeller Library, 10 Prospect Street Sciences Library, 201 Thayer Street Orwig Music Library, 1 Young Orchard Avenue John Hay Library, 20 Prospect Street 401-863-2165, email: rock@brown.edu Library hours & locations BruKnow — the Library catalog How does it work? Institution photo IDs are required for library admittance. When registering for borrowing privileges, visitors will be required to validate as a BorrowDirect user. Email address is also required. What can be borrowed? Most books at the Rockefeller, Sciences, and Orwig Libraries may be checked out. Most other material may be used in the library building. Media does not circulate. Items listed in BruKnow as held at the Annex or in Rock storage must be ordered on-site in advance. Allow one business day for retrieval.

Close Stronger TUgether: COVID-19 RESOURCES Home Alumni Part of a prestigious tradition going back to 1834, Tulane University alumni come from around the world. 75 alumni clubs span the globe, and every year over 15,000 Tulanians participate in alumni events. Alumni Association Reconnect with Tulane! The Alumni Association offers many benefits and services, along with ways to stay in touch. Career Services Connect with fellow alumni and students to expand your professional network and find volunteer and mentoring opportunities. Green Wave Athletics Follow your favorite team or buy something from the Team Shop to show your Green Wave pride. Reunions Reconnect with your classmates, the campus and New Orleans through a range of special events for each milestone year. Homecoming Whether or not it's your reunion year, Homecoming is a great opportunity to reconnect with your alma mater. Tulane Magazine The official magazine of Tulane, this quarterly publication features stories about the impact the university has around the world. Make a Gift Support Tulane and help us continue our mission of education and research for future generations. Request a Transcript It's easy to get a copy of your transcript when you need it as your career progresses.

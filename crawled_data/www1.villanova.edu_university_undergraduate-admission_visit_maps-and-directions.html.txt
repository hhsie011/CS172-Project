We invite you to visit our suburban 260-acre campus located in the heart of the historic Main Line. We are minutes from Philadelphia, the nation's sixth largest city, and a short trip from New York City and Washington D.C. Located near the intersection of two interstate highways, I-76 and I-476, Villanova is easy to access. There are three train stops on campus that offer convenient transportation to almost anywhere on the East Coast, including nearby Philadelphia International Airport. CAMPUS MAPS PRINTABLE CAMPUS MAP INTERACTIVE CAMPUS MAP PLANNING YOUR VISIT By Air Visitors should fly into Philadelphia International Airport (PHL), which is located approximately 20 miles from the Villanova campus. Rail service, taxi, ride share, shuttle and car rental services are all available from the airport. By Train Take SEPTA's Paoli/Thorndale Regional Rail Line to Villanova Station - located directly on campus. By Trolley SEPTA's Norristown High Speed Line travels between Norristown and 69th Street Terminal. You may get on or off the trolley at the Villanova Station or Stadium Station. DRIVING TO CAMPUS Villanova's campus is located near the intersection of two major interstate highways, I-76 and I-476, and is easily accessible by car from throughout the Mid-Atlantic.

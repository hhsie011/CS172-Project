Kemeny Lectures — This lecture series, named in honor of John Kemeny, is a series of three lectures offered annually by a distinguished mathematician. The first lecture is the series is targeted at an undergraduate audience. Lahr Lectures — In 2020, the department was pleased to announce the establishment of an annual lecture series in memory and honor of Emeritus Professor of Mathematics Dwight Lahr’s contributions to the Department of Mathematics and to the College, and in recognition of his commitment to inclusivity and diversity.

Our tuition waiver programs are extensive and we offer competitive awards for full-time as well as part-time students. Scholarship Portal is Closed The scholarship portal opened to all students on Dec. 1. Additional tuition waiver opportunities are available through each Academic Division; however applicants are not allowed to stack waivers. Recipients will only be allowed one waiver source for the school year. This scholarship portal will close March 1. Applicants applying for tuition waivers must make application via the scholarship portal no later than March 1 of each year. All applicants must be admitted to Oklahoma State University – Oklahoma City by the scholarship deadline to receive tuition waiver and scholarship consideration. Applicants will be notified by regular mail of any awards. Awards will be offered on a first come, first served basis in the following awarding priority: First-time Freshman - awards will be based on the cumulative grade point average from an official 7 or 8 semester transcript, (whichever is available), ACT scores, or GED documentation. You will be required to upload copies of your transcript or ACT scores for scholarship consideration. College Transfer Students - awards will be based on the cumulative graduation/retention grade point average from all previous official college transcripts. You will be required to upload copies of your transcript history from each previously attended institution. Current OSU-Oklahoma City Students – students currently enrolled at OSU-Oklahoma City will receive priority consideration for tuition waivers and scholarships based on the cumulative graduation/retention grade point average as determined by the OSU-Oklahoma City Registrar's Office.

Visiting Faculty Formal University appointments for visiting faculty can be requested through the appropriate faculty administration office, such as the Dean of the Faculty, School of Public Health, or BioMed Faculty Affairs. These appointments will automatically receive services granted to regular faculty. Inactive Students Services for Inactive Undergraduate Students should be requested by contacting Dean_of_College@brown.edu. Services for Inactive Graduate Students should be requested by contacting Barbara_Bennett@brown.edu. Services for Inactive Medical Students should be requested by contacting Katie_Craig@brown.edu.

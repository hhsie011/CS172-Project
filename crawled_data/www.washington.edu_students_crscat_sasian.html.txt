Texts in English translation. View course details in MyPlan: S ASIA 203 S ASIA 206 Modern Literature of South Asia (5) VLPA/I&S Jennifer E Dubrow Introduction to the modern literature of South Asia (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal), from the eighteenth century to the present. Readings include a selection of novels, short stories, and poetry drawn from the diverse literary traditions of the region and placed in their cultural contexts. All texts in English translation. View course details in MyPlan: S ASIA 206 S ASIA 225 Indian Philosophical Literature (5) VLPA/I&S Prem Pahlajrai Introduction to various topics pertaining to the vast philosophical literature of India, such as its origins and contexts, dharma; karma and free will; logic and argument. A variety of systems from Hindu, Buddhist, Jain, theistic, and non-theistic schools are covered. Taught in English. Offered: WS. View course details in MyPlan: S ASIA 225 S ASIA 254 Fairies, Genies, and Monsters: The Romance Genre in India (5) VLPA Jennifer E Dubrow Introduction to the long adventure narratives (dastan) of South Asia, with reference to the Middle and Near East. Texts in English translation. View course details in MyPlan: S ASIA 254 S ASIA 316 Postcolonial Literature of South Asia (5) VLPA, DIV Surveys postcolonial literature in South Asia from 1947 to the present. View course details in MyPlan: S ASIA 316 S ASIA 585 Radical Fictions: Literary Modernisms in South Asia (5) Jennifer E Dubrow Surveys the development of literary modernism in South Asia. Introduces major thinkers, writers, and centers for literary modernism in colonial and postcolonial South Asia.

Iowa State University is a great place to prepare for law school and careers in the legal field. We are a major research university, with exceptional student experiences and rigorous academics. Our students are well-regarded when it comes to applying for law school. Recent alumni have been accepted to Duke University School of Law, Washington University School of Law, University of Minnesota Law School, Drake University Law School, University of Iowa College of Law and many others. If you are a current student and would like to meet with a pre-law advisor, please make an appointment on ISU’s Navigate App. We look forward to assisting you!

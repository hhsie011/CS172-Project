Contact Coordinator Curriculum This 15-credit-hour certificate program comprises the following courses: Fundamentals of Play Therapy Play Therapy for Young Children Child Psychopathology in Play Therapy Advanced Techniques in Child and Play Therapy Practicum Play Therapy* * The practicum requires students to conduct 100 hours of play therapy while receiving weekly supervision from an experienced play therapist.

View course details in MyPlan: SCI T 502 SCI T 503 Science Communication for Teachers (2) Students examine effective communication of scientific research. Students critically evaluate example presentation from on-campus departmental seminars, practice communicating their own scientific research to mentors and peers, and deliver a formal oral presentation.

This exhibit displays texts and images dating from the early 17th century drawn from the Library's incomparable history of science collections, historical records of the Ladd Observatory, and a range of astronomical instruments of the 18th century to the present day used for observation by Brown astronomers. Main Gallery and Lobby, John Hay Library; through October 2009. For further information, contact Holly Snyder.

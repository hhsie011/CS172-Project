She manages initiatives related to sponsored project support. Amy provides outreach to faculty and students across the disciplines in the use of funding opportunity resources, including Community of Science (COS), available through the University Library. Additionally, Amy manages the leadership development award programs, develops the program's communications strategy, and maintains the website. She utilizes social science research to create resources and collaborates with University stakeholders to implement information sessions for department chairs on issues of gender equity in academe. Amy trained and worked for many years as a women's studies librarian, most recently at the University of Michigan Graduate Library. Prior to that she was the Librarian/Archivist for the American Association of University Women.

The Lab provides academic support in small groups and individualized sessions. The staff are prepared to assist students in developing effective study skills, review materials to explore learning styles, develop self confidence in course work and reduce feelings of fear and failure. CARE Study Rooms CARE provides 25 private and group study rooms for students to check-out. Tutoring Requirements First and Second-Year CARE students have mandatory lab requirements to ensure their academic success at The Florida State University. Tutoring Schedule CARE provides tutoring support for a number of academic courses at 1000 and 2000 level. On occasion support may be available for 3000 and 4000 level courses.

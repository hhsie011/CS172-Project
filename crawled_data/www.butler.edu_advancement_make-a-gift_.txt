Partnerships The Butler Giving Circle Gift Planning Contact Advancement Make a Gift As a graduate, parent or friend of Butler University, you know that a quality education is crucial. A well-educated individual can change the world and enrich society. What you may not know, however, is that student tuition covers only a portion of Butler’s operating costs. The rest comes from loyal benefactors who choose to support higher education through their generous gifts of time, talent and resources. If you measure your own success by the contributions you make to others, then help Butler educate its students. Your success will become society’s success. Make a gift today by using the form below. Butler University values its donors and the ethical and legal obligations it has to protect their privacy.

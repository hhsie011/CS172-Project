You wowed us with your application, and we couldn’t be more excited to have you on campus. Our community is waiting to welcome you to campus (and online). We’re confident that you’re going to love it here and grow in ways you never imagined. As soon as you’re ready for an education that enriches your whole person—academically, socially, and spiritually—we encourage you to reserve your spot at Creighton in your online student portal and celebrate. Here’s to making connections, to making a positive difference in people’s lives, and to making your mark on the world. The most rewarding four years of your life are waiting for you at Creighton. At Creighton, we help you thrive through our student resources—powerful tools and knowledge centers you can use to guide your life on campus. We offer academic and career counseling, assistance with banking and technology, and more.

Health Promotion Counseling and Psychological Services Disability Services Gay Health Advocacy Project (GHAP) Insurance Office Medical Services Sexual Violence Response Columbia Health You are here: Home All Services Mental Health Outreach Mental Health Outreach Counseling and Psychological Services provides education and training to the Columbia community as part of it's Outreach Program. Services include: educating the community about CPS clinical services; working closely with student groups, promoting mental health awareness on campus; offering workshops and presentations to Columbia students, deans, faculty, teaching assistants and administrators on mental health and wellness topics; building liaison relationships with deans at undergraduate and graduate schools, residence life staff and others to provide information about student wellness and to provide consultation regarding students of concern, while maintaining student confidentiality. Contact CPS at (212) 854-2878 for more information about professional development training and workshops. Urgent Care After-hours, call 212-854-2878 for crisis support and recommendations for care. Visit our Coping Tools page for self-help and crisis resources for coping with concerns outside our regular hours of operation.

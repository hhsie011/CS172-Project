Learn more Diversity Multicultural perspectives are critical to the success of The Warren Alpert Medical School. They enrich educational understanding, foster-culturally appropriate patient care, and enhance trust in research. We are a teaching and learning community that embraces diverse perspectives, experiences, and backgrounds. Institutional diversity enhances trust and communication, facilitates development of culturally appropriate clinical and research programs, and makes us better partners to the communities that we serve.

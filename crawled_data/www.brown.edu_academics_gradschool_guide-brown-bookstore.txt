They also carry a full line of technology products, including academically discounted computers, tablets, software, peripherals, related course and back to school supplies as well as an in-store authorized Apple and Lenovo Repair and Consultation Center. A computer trade in program is also available to reduce costs. The Bookstore offers basic furnishings for on or off-campus living, a broad selection of collegiate and casual wear, general merchandise, and a full line of trade books. Services include year round used book buyback and special book order services. Students may order course books online through the Brown Bookstore. Go to the Textbook webpage. The Textbook and Digital Book Return Policy is also detailed on the Textbook Buyback webpage.

Design Thinking is adaptable to any business environment and is highly effective way to approach to problem-solving that is efficient, innovative, and reduces risks through rapid prototyping and testing. Learn how design thinking can benefit you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum To complete the design thinking undergraduate certificate, you’ll take 8 credit hours of required courses: Design Thinking and Innovation, Design Sprints, and Design Thinking Challenges. You'll also choose one elective course from the following: Agile Product Management, Design Thinking, or Service Design Thinking. View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

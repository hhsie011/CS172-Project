Come to the Kickoff Incoming business freshmen and transfer students are invited to McCombs Kickoff, a day-long event packed with fun and held the Monday before fall classes begin. Meet new and current students while exploring some of Austin’s most popular destinations. Catch some sun and take a dip at the city's most popular swimming hole, Barton Springs. Co-hosted by our Undergraduate Business Council. Gone to Business Open to new McCombs freshmen and transfer students, Gone to Business is an event the night before classes where you can meet our deans, staff, and student leaders while they tell you about opportunities available to Texas BBAs. Come hang out at one of McCombs' biggest and best annual events, and make sure to stick around for the giveaways and for the Gone to Texas celebration at the Tower!

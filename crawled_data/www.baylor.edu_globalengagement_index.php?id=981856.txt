Should a program meet its capacity before the final deadline, the application for that program will close and no further applications will be accepted. Students with questions about our application timeline or deadlines for specific programs should contact studyabroad@baylor.edu for details. All other Spring Programs have an October 1 application deadline. TIPS Students interested in applying for spring term programs that are competitive in nature should submit their applications by the May 1 deadline. If you are unsure whether your program is considered competitive, please consult with the appropriate Study Abroad Advisor. Students applying to study abroad programs who would like to be considered for scholarships should take into account scholarship deadlines and submit their applications before scholarship deadlines for early program acceptance.

The center supports and advocates for the needs of all students, challenges students to think critically about themselves and their communities, provides engaging experiential learning opportunities and empowers students to be positive change agents. Our Vision and Values To be the nation's premier educational resource that cultivates intellectually vibrant and socially just communities. We value collaboration, communication, community, inclusive excellence, leadership, social justice, supportive environments and transformative education.

The bias reporting system is one tool used for collecting information, identifying trends and themes as well as informing future campus climate initiatives. If you or someone you know has been impacted by a bias incident, please report using the online form. If you are not affiliated with Brown University but need to submit a bias report regarding an incident that includes a member of the Brown community and/or took place during university affiliated activities, you can complete the PDF form and return it to the Office of Institutional Equity and Diversity. Questions can be emailed to [email protected] If an emergency has occurred, please call 911.

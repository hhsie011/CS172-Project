We want to influence the next generation of physicians and scientists and add to the sustainable supply who are dedicated to serving the Oklahoma community. Through targeted exposure, we are cultivating an awareness of opportunities at OSU-CHS through STEM related activities and events designed for every age. This will allow our medical and graduate students the opportunity to interact with and inspire prospective students through their participation in our service-oriented program. Our events include virtual and in-person activities that expose participants to a variety of experiences.

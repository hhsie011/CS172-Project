French is the official language in 29 countries and an official language of the United Nations, NATO, the European Union and the International Red Cross - making French a key language for professionals interested in international affairs, political science, economics and global business. Oregon State Ecampus delivers a variety of degree programs online that are focused on language and liberal arts. Explore similar programs. Take a look at the online classroom and see how easy it is to earn a language degree alongside classmates from around the world. Learn more » The online French curriculum delivers a high level of proficiency in developing speaking, listening, reading, writing and cultural competency skills that prepares you to succeed in a variety of rewarding careers. Courses are developed and taught by the same renowned Oregon State faculty who teach on campus, many of whom are multilingual speakers and have received specialized training. The personalized, engaging learning environment provides you with the tools you need to be confident in your skills and advance your career. This degree program is offered through OSU's College of Liberal Arts, which is among top-ranked universities in the nation for best online liberal arts colleges. The courses are delivered online by OSU Ecampus. Benefits of learning online with Oregon State Classes are developed by OSU's world-class faculty, some of whom are known worldwide for their research, expertise and innovation. Personalized learning environment provides students with the tools and experience to be successful. Oregon State is regionally accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities. Oregon State Ecampus is consistently ranked among the nation's 10 best providers of online education according to U.S. News & World Report. OSU Ecampus students receive the same diploma as on-campus students. Get the support you need Our goal is to help you succeed.

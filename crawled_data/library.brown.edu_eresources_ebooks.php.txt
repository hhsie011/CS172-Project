Most of our ebooks are cataloged and findable in BruKnow. You'll find answers to some frequently asked questions about ebooks at the Brown Library below. Search BruKnow for ebooks Frequently asked questions about ebooks How do I search for ebooks? You can use the search field above, or visit BruKnow. How do I download/use ebooks once I find them? You can read ebooks in a web browser, or download them for offline reading. Formats, device compatibility, and usage restrictions vary by publisher. Our Ebook Collection Guide contains comprehensive information and details about using ebooks at Brown. The library doesn't have the book that I want as an ebook ~~ can you order it? We may be able to purchase an ebook that you need. Get in touch with your subject librarian for more information and to make a request. NB: If you're an instructor wishing to make a book available for a class, be sure to request multi-user access. Can I request ebooks through interlibrary loan? No, at this time, ebooks are not available for lending from other institutions.

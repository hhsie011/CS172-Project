We are partnering with a number of French companies to offer paid internships throughout France this summer (mid-May through end of July). These internships vary across disciplines (consulting, business, engineering, computer science, etc.), and are open to any undergraduate at Brown who has a level of proficiency in French that is equivalent to the completion of a 600 level French language course by end of the Spring 2018 semester.

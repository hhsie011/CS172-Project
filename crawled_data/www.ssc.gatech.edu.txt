The Bill Moore Student Success Center Menu Home Event Rental Space Reservations Photo Gallery FAQ Directions Contact Us The Bill Moore Student Success Center The Bill Moore Student Success Center The Bill Moore Student Success Center is conveniently located in the heart of Midtown Atlanta on Georgia Tech's East Campus and comprises the west side of Bobby Dodd Stadium. Our facility houses the Career Center, Enrollment Management, the Stamps President's Scholarship Program, the Office of Scholarships & Financial Aid, and the Office of Undergraduate Admission. In addition to these Georgia Tech departments, the Student Success Center contains a variety of dynamic meeting and event spaces, which are available to the Georgia Tech community and external guests. These spaces may be used to host meetings, conferences and trade shows, corporate interviews and recruitment events, receptions and parties, weddings, and other special events. Please contact the Meeting and Event Manager at 404-385-3495 to discuss your event planning needs or to schedule a tour of the building’s event spaces. The Student Success Center’s hours of normal business operation as well as event hours are listed below; site visits are only scheduled during normal business hours only. The building observes the same holiday schedule as the Georgia Institute of Technology and will not schedule events on days when campus is closed.

Expand all Collapse all Current Job Opportunities Communication Officer Columbia Global Centers | Mumbai invites applications for Communication Officer. Working closely with the Director of the CGCM, the Communication Officer is responsible for the following activities: Writing content for the various platforms subscribed to by the Center Managing press and media outreach for covering the Center’s programs Designing and editing the quarterly newsletter of the Center Designing flyers, brochures, presentations, and publicity materials for the Center’s programs Maintaining and designing updates for the Center’s website Publicizing the Center’s programs on social media Working closely with the design agency to create the Annual Report of the Center Working with external vendors including graphic designers, video editors, etc. when necessary Producing quarterly analytical reports on the Center’s communication strategies and their impact Participating in calls with other program staff across the Columbia Global Centers network Performing other duties as required to meet the goals and mission of the Center and the Global Centers network Traveling on occasion to advance the goals of the Center To apply, please send a copy of your current resume and a cover letter to [email protected] no later than May 20, 2022, with the subject line 'Application for the post of Communication Officer.' Due to the high volume of applications, only short-listed applicants will receive notification. Communication Fellow/Intern Columbia Global Centers | Mumbai (CGC|M) invites applications for Communication Fellow/Intern. Based in Mumbai, and working closely with the Director of the Columbia Global Centers | Mumbai and Communication Officer, the Communication Fellow/Intern is responsible for the following activities: Duties and Responsibilities: Crafting social media messaging for the Center’s handles including Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn Editing videos and audio materials for the Center’s programs to be disseminated via social media and YouTube Supporting the Communications Officer in designing flyers, brochures, presentations, and publicity materials for the Center’s programs Assisting the Communications Officer with photography coverage of live events at the Center Collection and compilation of data across social media for analytical reports Performing other duties as required to meet the goals and mission of the Center and the Global Centers network Competencies/Attributes: Undergraduate in Communications/Marketing/Journalism/related fields preferred Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English Experience in academic/education sector preferred Sound technical knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Canva, Mailchimp, etc Highly-motivated and creative ability to generate new ideas This position is a one-year appointment, subject to renewal. To apply, please send a copy of your current resume and a letter of interest to [email protected] by May 7, 2022, with the subject line 'Application for the post of 'Communication Fellow/Intern'. Due to the high volume of applications expected, only shortlisted candidates will receive a response.

As part of that service, and its public commitment, MSU values transparent and consistently available information about the university and its operation to any of our stakeholders. Consistent with MSU's commitment to transparency, and in response to the Public Transparency Website Requirements (PDF), the menu below provides access to comprehensive institutional information across a broad array of operational areas.. Use the links provided below to the information of interest. Financial and Operational Indicators includes information about annual operating budgets, current fund personnel expenditures, current fund expenditures, collective bargaining agreements, health care benefit plans, financial reports, campus security and safety and all funded university positions. Student and Academic Outcomes includes information regarding enrollment, student retention, graduation rate, Pell Grant recipients, geographic origination, student-faculty ratios, student-to-university employee ratios, faculty teaching load and graduation outcomes.

At the top of the exit ramp, turn right onto North Avenue. Cross Techwood Drive (the first light) and continue on North Avenue. After passing underneath the concrete pedestrian bridge, turn left into the visitor parking area. When leaving the parking lot, cross North Avenue using the crosswalk or pedestrian bridge and continue toward and around Tech Tower. The A. French Building is located just north of Tech Tower, and the Office of Institute Relations is located on the ground floor. Driving north on I-75/85 into Atlanta Take Exit #249D (Spring Street, West Peachtree Street). At the top of the exit ramp, go through the first intersection (Spring Street). At the next intersection (West Peachtree), turn left. Continue on West Peachtree for approximately one block and turn left onto North Avenue. Cross over the interstate and Techwood Drive and continue on North Avenue. After passing underneath the concrete pedestrian bridge, turn left into the visitor parking area. When leaving the parking lot, cross North Avenue using the crosswalk or pedestrian bridge and continue toward and around Tech Tower. The A. French Building is located just north of Tech Tower, and the Office of Institute Relations is located on the ground floor. MARTA (Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority) MARTA trains can take you from the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport baggage claim area to the North Avenue station, which is a .3-mile walk to the Wardlaw Building. Trains run Monday through Friday from 5 a.m. to 1 a.m., with a one-way fair of $1.75. (Be sure to check www.itsmarta.com for the latest operating information.) Follow the signs near the baggage claim area to MARTA and take a northbound train to the North Avenue station. Exit MARTA on North Avenue and head west on North Avenue (to your right). Continue on North Avenue for .4 miles, crossing over the interstate and Techwood Drive. Proceed onto campus near Tech Tower. The A. French Building is located just north of Tech Tower, and the Office of Institute Relations is located on the ground floor.

New Bearcat Cards are free, replacement Bearcat Cards are $35. Please note that Bearcat Cards are no longer being mailed, and will need to be picked up at the Technical Services office in Edwards Four. If you recently submitted a photo, and have not received a card, please pick up your card in the Technical Services office.

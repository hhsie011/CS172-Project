Learn how clinical mental health counseling to school counseling is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum This certificate program requires 12 credit hours. These 12 credit hours allow students pursuing the 60-credit-hour clinical mental health counseling degree to meet the Kansas State Department of Education (KSDE) requirement of: having a degree in counseling and completing all required KSDE assessments for school counseling licensure. View the curriculum Admission to the program This certificate is open to students currently enrolled in the counseling program’s clinical mental health counseling degree track or non-degree A status students returning to complete the school counselor licensure requirements. All Graduate School policies related to admission will apply to students admitted under non-degree A status.

of Excellence Requirements To earn the University Honor Scholar designation upon graduation, students must successfully complete the following: Five honors experiences (designated honors seminars, pre-approved experiences or self-designed experiences) Maintain an online learning portfolio, including an annual Year in Review reflection Meet with a UHP advisor for first-year and mid-point honors advising Earn a grade of C- or higher in Gateway to University Honors (HNRS1010) Have a cumulative University GPA of 3.2 or above. Student who have a cumulative University GPA of 3.75 or above will receive the designation of Distinguished University Honors Scholar. All requirements must be completed while you are an undergraduate student. Graduates receive a UHP medal to wear during UC’s Commencement ceremonies, representing completion of this signature academic program. This designation will also appear on your official UC transcript.

Livingstone, Ph.D., hosts a number of events on campus and beyond as part of the Baylor Conversation Series. Ranging from explorations of the role of civil discourse in today’s society to the importance of Christian leadership in higher education, the President’s Baylor Conversation Series brings together accomplished Baylor faculty members, nationally renowned speakers and experts from other colleges and universities for discussions that get to the heart of vital topics and reflect Baylor's commitment to serving as a marketplace of ideas within a caring, Christian community. The dates, times and participants of Baylor Conversation Series events will be added to this list and announced on an ongoing basis.

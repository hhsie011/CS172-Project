WSU's Shocker Success Center: An oasis of opportunity May 11, 2022 — Former Wichita State University students might remember Clinton Hall as a dark and cold building, but starting in September, it will begin its metamorphosis into the bright and welcoming Shocker Success Center. Centralizing access to these services will help students stay on course to graduate.

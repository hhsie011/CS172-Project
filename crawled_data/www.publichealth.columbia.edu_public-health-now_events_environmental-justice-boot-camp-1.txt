Environmental Justice Boot Camp Theory and Methods to Study Environmental Health Disparities 10:00 am 5:00 pm Monday 15 August 2022 RSVP Add to Calendar: Google Calendar Outlook RSVP Add to Calendar: Google Calendar Outlook 3032022-ej-458x324.png Online Event Kind: Training Organizer: Department of Environmental Health Sciences SHARP Invitation Limited to: Open to the Public Website: Learn More Learn More Join us for the next live-stream EJ Boot Camp on August 15-16, 2022. The Environmental Justice Boot Camp is a two-day intensive course featuring seminars and applied analytical sessions on key concepts, exposure assessment techniques, epidemiologic methods, community engagement and health policy applications, and statistical analytic approaches for conducting effective and solution-driven environmental justice research. Investigators at all career stages are welcome to attend, and we particularly encourage trainees and early-stage investigators to participate.

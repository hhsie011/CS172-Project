Learn more About the Portal The UW Sustainability portal provides information on the University of Washington's sustainability performance and efforts. Each category page includes information on what UW is doing for sustainability in that area, resources for the UW community, and data visualizations where possible. The visualizations show information on our current performance as well as sustainability targets. For more information on the dashboard or sustainability efforts at UW, contact us at sustainability@uw.edu.

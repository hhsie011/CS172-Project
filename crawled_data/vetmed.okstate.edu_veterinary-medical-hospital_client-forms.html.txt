Below you will find the required Admission Record and Financial Policy paperwork. In respect of your time, you have the option to complete this paperwork prior to your visit. Digital Method: Fill out the Admission Record on your computer or other device (see instructions below), and print it along with the Financial Policy. Sign both documents and present them to the receptionist when you check in. OR Manual Method: Print the Admission Record and Financial Policy below, fill them out completely and sign them. Present them to the receptionist when you check in. Admission Record Financial Policy Complete Admission Record Form on Your Device To fill out the Admission Record form on your computer or other device, please open the document titled 'Admission Record'. Right click the document and select 'Save Page As' or 'Save Target As' and save it somewhere convenient, such as your desktop. Open the form from where you saved it, fill it out completely, and save it once more.

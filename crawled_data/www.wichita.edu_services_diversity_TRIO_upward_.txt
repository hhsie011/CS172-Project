Application Program Elements Frequently Asked Questions Communication Upward Bound The Communication field is one of the most exciting and diverse fields and connects to multiple career paths including: Business, Medicine, TV/Film, Education and more. A few of those careers are: TV Broadcast reporter, Social Media marketer or manager, Event Management, Public Relations Specialist, Human Resources and Sales Representative among others. All services are provided to program participants completely free of charge. In fact, students receive a small stipend for their participation. CUB is federally funded and students' eligbility is determined after completion of the CUB application. *Photo on the right: CUB Students during recent summer program with special guest speaker, Dr. James Rhatigan.

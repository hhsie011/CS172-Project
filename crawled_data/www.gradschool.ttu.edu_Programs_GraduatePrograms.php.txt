Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Graduate School Menu Search TTU Graduate School Graduate Programs All applicants must provide the following application materials to the Graduate School: DOMESTIC (ONLY US Citizens and Immigrant Permanent Residents) Application Application fee Unofficial transcripts of ALL degree-awarding post-secondary institutions attended Proof of citizenship for foreign-born US citizens Resident alien card (PR only) Please visit our Domestic Prospective Students Application Procedures page for more detailed information. INTERNATIONAL (Non-US Citizens and Non-Immigrant Permanent Residents) Application Application fee Unofficial transcripts of ALL degree-awarding post-secondary institutions attended, including a copy of official English translations for each transcript Unofficial copies of all post-secondary diplomas earned* Unofficial proof of English proficiency Proof of financial support* Passport bio page* Please visit our International Prospective Students Application Procedures page for more detailed information.

Celebrating the Class of 2022 May 23, 2022 11:00 AM | Tulane Today today@tulane.edu Print to PDF 2022_Commencement_Banner_v01.jpg On Saturday, May 21, Tulane University celebrated the Class of 2022 at its Unified Commencement ceremony in Yulman Stadium. On Friday before the ceremony, Wave Goodbye took over the Berger Family Lawn. The annual party is for graduating students and their guests and features local music and food.

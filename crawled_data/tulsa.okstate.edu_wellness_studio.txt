Location North Hall 116 Monday - Friday: 6 a.m. - 10 p.m. Saturday: 7 a.m. - 3 p.m. Sunday: CLOSED Contact 918-594-8126 tulsa.wellness@okstate.edu Fitness Studio and Classes The OSU-Tulsa Fitness Studio is located across from the Wellness Center in North Hall 117. The studio offers wellness classes throughout the week and hosts lunch and learns, clinics and other wellness programs. The Fitness Studio and all classes are available free of charge to students, faculty and staff. Enroll at the Wellness Center today for access. For more information about classes or the studio, contact the Wellness Center. Fitness Class Schedule Mondays Yoga at 1 p.m. Fridays Yoga at 12 p.m. Visit the Wellness Event Calendar for the most up-to-date schedule of Fitness Classes at OSU-Tulsa.

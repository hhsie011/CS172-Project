Application to the Cox School of Business - Graduate Degree ProgramsThank you for your interest in the Cox School of Business at SMU. The SMU Cox School’s admissions team is committed to helping you navigate the admissions process. Events are held throughout the year to provide additional insight on the application process. Once you create an account, you will be able to continue to return to your application and edit your content until your application is submitted. We encourage you to reach out to the admissions team with any questions as you prepare your application. We look forward to reviewing your application and learning more about your background. If you are having difficulty creating or logging into your application, please email jmcminn@smu.edu with a detailed explanation of your issue. Please note that you can only have ONE MBA and ONE MS application open in the same application period. An application period closes when a start term passes and previous applications are archived. If you would like to apply to another MBA or MS program during the same application period, please reach out to the admissions office for the program to which you have started applying or have applied. That program admissions office will instruct you on how to move forward. The Cox School of Business Graduate Admissions Team Returning users:Log in to continue an application. First-time users:Create an account to start a new application.

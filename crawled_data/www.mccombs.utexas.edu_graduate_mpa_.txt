Sign Up for More Information THREE WAYS TO EARN YOUR MPA DEGREE Traditional MPA Our one-year traditional MPA is open to students who hold a bachelor’s degree in any discipline from most academic institutions. In as little as one year, students can earn an MPA degree from the top accounting program in the nation! No work experience is required to apply. View More on Traditional MPA Integrated MPA The integrated MPA is designed for current Texas McCombs undergraduates. iMPA students earn a BBA degree in accounting and an MPA degree in just five years, saving students both time and money by taking graduate level courses at undergraduate tuition rates. Explore Integrated MPA ECON-MPA Our ECON-MPA is developed for current UT undergraduates majoring in economics. Students get a head start on MPA courses during their senior year and have the benefit of paying undergraduate tuition on graduate level classes. ECON-MPA students earn both a BA degree in economics and an MPA degree.

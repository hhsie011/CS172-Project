Students bring their culture, beliefs, and perspectives with them allowing our community to learn about and engage the world right here in Waco, Texas. We recognize that our international students have worked very hard to become members of the Baylor community, so we want them to know they are welcome and we want to do whatever we can to help them be successful! Baylor is a very special place where international students and scholars have been welcomed for many years! For more information about Baylor University, please click this link About Baylor. This website has a wealth of information about topics such as getting forms, arriving in Waco, life at Baylor, etc. If you have already been accepted, please click one of the links on the left for either Degree-Seeking or Exchange Students to learn about next steps. If you are planning to get an undergraduate degree (Bachelors Degree) from Baylor but you have not actually applied to Baylor yet, please click Apply to Baylor to begin the process. If you want to pursue graduate studies, please contact the academic department directly about your interest. You can also click on the Baylor Graduate School webpage. If you are coming from one of our partner institutions, contact your administrator at your home institution to begin the process. There is so much waiting for you at Baylor University and we are ready to help you in any way that we can as you take steps to becoming a Baylor student. We look forward to seeing you on campus and to working with you during your time at Baylor. Please feel free to contact International Student and Scholar Services at ISSS_support@Baylor.edu if you need assistance.

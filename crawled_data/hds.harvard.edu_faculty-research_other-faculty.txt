Visiting Faculty Visiting faculty are faculty members from other schools or universities within the U.S. or abroad who come to HDS to teach one or two courses for one or two semesters, and then return full-time to their home institutions. The Women’s Studies in Religion Program (WSRP) Research Associates for that year are included in this group. Others Offering Instruction This group includes faculty members at other Harvard Schools whose courses are jointly offered by HDS, as well as lecturers and instructors who teach HDS courses on special subjects on an adjunct, part-time basis.

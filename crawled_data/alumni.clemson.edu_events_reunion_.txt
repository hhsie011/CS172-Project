During the celebration, Golden Tigers reunite with their former classmates and commemorate their induction by participating in a graduation-style ceremony. Each Golden Tiger will have their name called and will cross a stage to be presented with at Golden Tiger Society induction certificate and lapel pin. Clemson University and Alumni Association leadership will be in attendance to congratulate the new class of Golden Tigers. The Clemson Alumni Association will celebrate the Class of 1970, the Class of 1971 and the Class of 1972 on June 9-10, 2022 at the Madren Conference Center at Clemson University. Reunion for All Classes Before 1970 The Clemson Alumni Association invites all Clemson Alumni who have previously been inducted into the Golden Tiger Society to join us June 9-10, 2022 to help celebrate the newest class of Golden Tigers! Campus and building tours, speakers, university updates and class parties are just a few of the activities you can expect at Reunion.

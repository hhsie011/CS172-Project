Time: October 12-13, 9:00 AM-12:00 PM (Beijing) | October 11-12, 9:00 PM-12:00 AM (New York) Please join Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and Columbia Engineering Graduate Career Placement Center for the virtual China career fair exclusive for Columbia Engineering's graduate students and alumni. During this two-day event, you will have real-time engagement with recruiters from reputable China-based companies, inquire about positions and requirements, and gain in-depth information about a particular field or industry.

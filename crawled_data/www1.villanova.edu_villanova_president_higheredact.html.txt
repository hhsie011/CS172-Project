It reauthorized the Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended. HEA requires higher education institutions to report and disclose information from various administrative areas and make its information readily available to interested parties via this website. The following general information subject areas provide links to references, reports and additional details. If you have questions, please contact the Department responsible for the information.

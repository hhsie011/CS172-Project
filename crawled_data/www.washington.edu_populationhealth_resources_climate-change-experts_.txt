Our initial goals with this mapping are to: Increase the level of internal and external awareness of the university’s collective expertise to address the challenges of climate change. Help boost the range of interdisciplinary collaborations occurring within and beyond the university to expand the number of innovative adaptation and mitigation projects. This resource can be searched and sorted, and we have also captured additional information about university centers and institutes. We look forward to your comments on this resource. What is population health? Population health is a broad concept encompassing not only the elimination of diseases and injuries, but also the intersecting and overlapping factors that influence health.

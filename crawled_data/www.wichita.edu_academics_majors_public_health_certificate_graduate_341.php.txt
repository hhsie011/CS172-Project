Through the graduate certificate in public health program, graduates bring population-based health knowledge to their work in health and medicine. Learn how Public Health is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 15-hour online graduate certificate in public health provides documentation that a student has completed a core set of public health courses beyond the bachelor’s degree level. Students who earn the certificate and then wish to pursue WSU's Master of Arts (MA) in aging studies may transfer all 15 credit hours to the Public Health concentration of the MA program, allowing them to complete their master's degree within one year. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission. View additional admission requirements for this program.

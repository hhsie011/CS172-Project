This diversity will not only open your mind but also expand your future horizons, preparing you for the range of careers that you will have in your lifetime. The professional staff at the Center for Careers and Life After Brown (CareerLAB) is a campus resource dedicated to helping you plan for your future. If you are preparing a resume, finding an internship, interviewing for a job, applying to professional school, or if you simply want to talk over your options, stop by during our open hours or make an appointment to meet one of our career advisors. It is never too early to start!

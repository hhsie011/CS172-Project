Learn more Student Groups On campus and in greater Providence, opportunities abound for students to indulge their passions in medicine and beyond. At The Warren Alpert Medical School, we encourage students to maintain their interests — whether in medicine, music, or advocacy — that make them whole. With more than 80 student-led organizations, students have a range of venues to learn, serve, lead, create, and support each other.

Penn Libraries Research Annex. Located in West Deptford, NJ, LIBRA will preserve and ensure access to lesser-used scholarly resources acquired over centuries by the Penn Libraries, while enabling the Libraries to continue adding new titles to shelves on campus. For more information see the LIBRA FAQ. Please contact us with questions and comments.

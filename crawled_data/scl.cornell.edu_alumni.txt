Learn more and make a gift. Cornell University alumni have a steeped tradition in giving in support of the advancement of the university, and the lives of today's Cornellians. From the moment you moved into your first dorm room or other home on campus, until you marched into Schoellkopf Stadium for Commencement, the Division of Student & Campus Life has always been there for every student like you, and thousands of Cornellians before you, every step of the way. All of units within the Division of Student & Campus Life play a vital role in the transformative experiences of our students. From mental and physical health services, the Office of the Dean of Students, student organization support, residential life, health and wellbeing services, and career services we are the foundation of the Cornell student experience. Making a Gift A gift to Student & Campus Life is a gift to every student As part of the esteemed Cornell alumni community, you are a leader for our students. Your gift today to Student & Campus Life is an investment in our students' lives and directly helps them connect with programs, facilities and services to support their life outside of the classroom. SCL strives to create a consistent foundation and to inspire transformation among all Cornell students, and our wide range of support services ensure each student is equipped for success when they leave campus.

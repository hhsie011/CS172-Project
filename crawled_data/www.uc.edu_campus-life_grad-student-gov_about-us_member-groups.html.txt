The GSG Member Groups are also called the Graduate Student Associations (GSAs). GSA officers listed below for different GSA form as a liason between the GSG and the graduate students in their cohort. Aerospace Engineering right arrow down arrow To sign forms related to conference travel or conference travel reimbursement, members of the Aerospace GSA must contact their graduate program directors, who are by default the advisors for the Aerospace GSA. Student officers (GSA officers or GSG officers) cannot sign these forms. If for some reason members of the Aerospace GSA cannot reach their Aerospace graduate program director, they will need to contact their Aerospace department head.

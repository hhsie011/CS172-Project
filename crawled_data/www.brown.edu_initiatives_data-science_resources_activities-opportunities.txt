The aim is to provide students, staff, faculty, and visitors with an introduction to current research topics in the fields of data science, mathematics, statistics and computer science. Please check our events page for more information on these and other events of interest. Undergraduate Data Science Club The Brown Data Science undergraduate club creates and supports opportunities related to Data Science for the undergraduate community. It brings together students interested in Data Science and Machine Learning by hosting workshops and talks, and through competitions in which participants gain real world experience analyzing data—often with access to unique and exclusive data sets. Find out more on the BDS website. Please contact us with any ideas for joint activities, co-sponsorship opportunities, or other suggestions.

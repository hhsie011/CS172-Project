At the Providence VA Medical Center, I serve as the director of the pulmonary function laboratory and the cardio-pulmonary exercise testing laboratory, I am director of the ICU, and I am the site director for the pulmonary and critical care fellowship. At the Medical School, I serve as co-director of the pulmonary pathophysiology course for second-year students. My interests are in respiratory physiology and testing, medical education, and lung cancer screening implementation.

Few other institutions, public or private, can boast the impressive array of disciplines we support under one collegiate “roof.” This leads to a strong, multidisciplinary network of design thinking that directly benefits you. As a student in the College Design, you’ll work alongside your peers from majors including architecture, landscape architecture, biological/pre-medical illustration, studio arts, planning, and industrial, graphic, interdisciplinary and interior design to earn a well-rounded education and build valuable relationships for the future.

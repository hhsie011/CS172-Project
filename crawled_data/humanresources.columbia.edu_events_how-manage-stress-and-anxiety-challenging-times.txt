We will focus on strategies to support well-being and resilience, and reduce the negative impact of stressful events, distressing news and challenging conversations. After participating in this seminar, you will be better able to: Recognize secondary/vicarious trauma and how to reduce its effects Identify strategies for handling stressful conversations Help children, teens and other loved ones talk about their concerns Focus on your and your family’s priorities and find ways to make a difference Reach out for help from resources including the EAP Register Now Go to Columbia's Well-being website to learn about the EAP and other resources.

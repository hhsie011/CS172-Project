WiSTEM Symposiums have four main goals: Create space where intersecting identities and scientific research coexist. Equip womxn with the tools needed to navigate educational and occupational environments. Impart skills & knowledge to promote professional development and boost participants’ preparation to enter the job market. Encourage networking that can facilitate community mentorship & sponsorship.

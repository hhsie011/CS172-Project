By serving as an annual giving volunteer, you amplify the impact of your philanthropic investment by asking your friends, classmates, fellow alumni, students, and/or family to join you in supporting annual, current-use giving to the University. Brown Annual Fund Leadership Council The Leadership Council focuses on high-level philanthropy to the University through the Brown Annual Fund. Since its inception in 2001, annual leadership contributions have grown from $5 million to $34 million. Leadership Council members are nominated to serve by invitation from the University President.

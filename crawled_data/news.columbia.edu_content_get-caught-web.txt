This spring's seminar, “The Outside Project,” is led by GSAPP faculty members Laurie Hawkinson and Galia Solomonoff. The pavilion opened at the beginning of this month and will be on view through early June in the plaza by Avery, Fayerweather, and Schermerhorn halls.

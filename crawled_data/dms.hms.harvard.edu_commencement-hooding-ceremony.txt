Regalia Orders for 2022 Graduates Academic regalia is required for degree candidates in the Morning Exercises of Commencement, the GSAS Diploma Awarding Ceremony and the DMS Hooding Ceremony. Your Harvard ID will be required both when ordering your regalia online and picking it up in-store. PhD candidates can pick up their rental regalia at the Harvard Coop (Harvard Square location) between May 19 and May 26. No reservations or pre-orders are needed for RENTALS. More information is available here. To purchase a robe or full academic regalia, please use this order form with the Harvard Coop. University Cap & Gown also offers Harvard University–approved regalia for purchase. The deadline for all rental and custom purchase orders is Saturday, April 9, 2022.

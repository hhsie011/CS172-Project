The summer B.I.T.S (Building Information Technology Skills) program provides paid summer job opportunities and support to youth ages 14-18 through the WorkReady Philadelphia program. The virtual program will run from July through mid-August and offer a unique opportunity for Philadelphia high school students to acquire new digital literacy skills in the 21st century. Participants will gain experiences in designing, developing and incubating civic start-ups to transform urban challenges into sustainable products and services. The BITS program is supported by The Office of the Vice President for Research. HOW TO APPLY The 2022 application for the Summer Youth Employment Program is now open. To access the online application, visit phila.workready.org. To be connected to Temple Univ. The BITS summer program, use the referral codes: WRS53 CODE: WRS53 – SERVICE LEARNING CODE: WRS53 - INTERNSHIP Ages 14 and 15 Must reside in the City of Philadelphia No prior experience required Ages 16-18 Must reside in the City of Philadelphia Prior job experience preferred but not required After completing the online application, make sure that you have access to the necessary documents to provide. you will need to complete a paper enrollment packet once our office re-opens. Please refer to the list below for the documents you will need to prepare. • State/student ID or report card • Signed social security card • Birth certificate • Blue work permit card (if under 18 years old) PARTNER: You can reach us via email at jennifer.jenkins001@temple.edu or by phone at 215-204-4513.

Join us for a hike to the Baldwin Hills Scenic Overlook. The hike features a winding path with numerous points to stop and admire the scenery on your way to the top. The overlook features a 270-degree view stretching from the Pacific Ocean to the downtown skyline. The hike from the trailhead to the top and back down should take about an hour. Coffee to follow! Friends, co-workers, and non-Columbia guests are welcome to join.

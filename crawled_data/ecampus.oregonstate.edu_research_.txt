Oregon State Ecampus research reveals that 30 students might be the tipping point Oregon State Ecampus research reveals that 30 students might be the tipping point The Oregon State Ecampus Research Unit recently conducted a study on a rarely researched topic in online education: class size. What the researchers found is that students’ grades were higher in certain online classes that had lower enrollment, and that it may be beneficial to limit particular kinds of courses to 30 students or fewer.

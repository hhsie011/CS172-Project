The Institute annually sponsors 12-15 large-scale thematic semester programs and their affiliated workshops, as well as topical workshops, conferences, summer programs, and special events. These events bring together scholars from around the world to discuss the latest research and to form new research partnerships. Open Positions Assistant Director for Governance and Planning The Assistant Director for Governance and Planning helps the Director manage administrative responsibilities and communications with external stakeholders and the public. Responsibilities include overseeing the Director's calendar and schedule priorities; maintaining lists of incoming proposals and ICERM responses; supporting governance board and other meetings called by the Director; preparing presentations on ICERM programs and operations; overseeing development of publicity, content on ICERM webpages, and social media messaging; serving as a liaison with Advancement for fundraising efforts; assisting the Director with functional and policy aspects of special programs and community outreach activities. Interested applicants may submit their resume and cover letter using the following link: https://brown.wd5.myworkdayjobs.com/staff-careers-brown/job/121-South-Main-Street/Assistant-Director--Governance-and-Planning_REQ174663 Check mathjobs.org for any currently open Postdoc positions and how to apply.

Information Gathering We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site by identifying (1) which parts of our site are most heavily used, and (2) which portion of our audience comes from within the Harvard network. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that user sessions will be tracked, but the users will remain anonymous. Use of Information The MGMH staff uses the information gathered above to tailor site content to user needs, and to generate aggregate statistical reports. At no time do we disclose site usage by individual IP addresses. Contacting the HMGM If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, you can contact Raquel Alonso-Perez at: ralonso@fas.harvard.edu The MGMH Web site contains links to other independently run Web sites within the Harvard network and to some sites outside the "http://mgmh.fas.harvard.edu" domain.

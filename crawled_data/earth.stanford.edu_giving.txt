Navigate to State of the School, Fall 2021 Stanford Earth FundS Annual gifts from alumni to the Stanford Earth Fund are essential to the school's success. As a primary source of unrestricted funding, the Stanford Earth Fund provides the dean with the flexibility to answer the school's most pressing challenges and to pursue innovations for which no other funding exists. The school is pleased to acknowledge and thank our lead annual fund donors at the following giving levels: Leadership Circle Gifts of $1,000 and above* Dean's Circle Gifts of $10,000 and above* Make a Gift See 2020-21 Annual Summary *Alumni graduating within the last 10 years earn recognition with gifts of $500 and $5,000, respectively.

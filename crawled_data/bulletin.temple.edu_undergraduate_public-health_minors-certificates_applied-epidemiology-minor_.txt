University Studies Course Descriptions Graduate and Professional Academic Programs Course Descriptions Faculty Minor in Applied Epidemiology About Requirements The Applied Epidemiology minor provides students interested in science and health with an opportunity to enhance their skillset obtained from the undergraduate program in public health or related undergraduate programs. Specifically, the 18-credit minor will expand student's abilities in the application of epidemiologic methods, tools and frameworks focusing on evidence-based findings and best practices, which allows students to understand how to investigate, detect, and address population/community-level health issues. The minor consists of 6 undergraduate courses (2 required; 4 electives). Students wishing to declare and pursue the Applied Epidemiology Minor should contact one of the undergraduate academic advisors below: Student Last Names A-K: Contact Natasha Benoit at natasha.benoit@temple.edu. Student Last Names L-Z: Contact Gale Carlin at carlin@temple.edu.

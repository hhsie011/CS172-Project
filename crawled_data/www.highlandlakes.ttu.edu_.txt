Texas Tech University in the Hill Country The Texas Tech University Regional Teaching Sites at Fredericksburg, Highland Lakes, and Junction make a Carnegie TierOne education in Central Texas a reality. TTU at Fredericksburg » The Texas Tech University Higher Education Teaching Site at Fredericksburg is conveniently located in the Hill Country University Center. We offer face-to-face, online and hybrid classes toward a variety of bachelor's and graduate degrees. TTU at Highland Lakes » The Frank Fickett Educational Center in Marble Falls houses the Texas Tech University Higher Education Teaching Site at Highland Lakes. We offer face-to-face, online and hybrid classes toward a variety of bachelor's and graduate degrees. TTU at Junction » The Texas Tech University Center at Junction is home to the Outdoor Learning Center and the Llano River Field Station. These sites captivate visitors of all ages with their scenic beauty, glistening spring-fed river, nature trails and wildlife.

Whether you are planning to visit London, have lived here for years, or are a new expatriate, all are encouraged to participate in our chapter’s events! We have Eagles living throughout the UK with the majority living in and around London. We are always seeking new volunteers to enrich and strengthen our club and its goals. The UK Chapter hosts and participates in many events, fulfilling the BC mission of Men and Women for Others.

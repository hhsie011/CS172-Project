Events Past Event Healthcare in China: How to tap into the right business opportunities? Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo May 30, 2022 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM Online Time: May 30, 2022, 7:00PM-8:00PM (Beijing) Event language: Chinese Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to attend a virtual panel discussion on healthcare business in China and opportunities for startups in the industry, featuring two experts to share their stories of business success and failure and provide insights to help you start, grow, or transition your business. Points to be covered: How to start a healthcare business in China? How to build partnerships for startup projects in the industry? How to promote business and entrepreneurial awareness among researchers and professionals in the health field? This event is part of the Beijing Center's business incubation program. Our business incubation program provides young entrepreneurs of Columbia University and other Ivy League schools with access to life-changing opportunities to empower their talents and passion for innovation. Learn more about the program and apply HERE. Speakers Dr. Jingjing Xu, Founder and Chairperson of Board, Meridian Medical Network Corp.; Managing Partner of Global Green Development Capital Dr. Xu has 26 years in cross-border healthcare cooperation and investment in the Silicon Valley, Europe and China. She is also a Co-founder and Vice President of the Healthcare Chapter and Chair of Founding Committee of the Investors Chapter of the centennial Western Returned Scholars Association, Member of Steering Committee of Health Big Data Application Assessment and Support of the China Healthcare Informatics and Big Data Association, and Associate Director of Smart Healthcare IoT and Big-data Program, China Health Promotion Foundation. Dr. Xu is a receiver of the 2021 Global Social Entrepreneurship Pioneer Award by the UN partner- Global Sustainable Enterprises Forum, the 2021 First BRICS Women Innovation Contest Award, the 2021 She Loves Tech Global Finalist and Champion of China, and the 2021 Best Social Entrepreneur Award of China. Dr. Wei SUN is the founder of Infinite Brain Technologies (IBT), a leading high-tech startup in developing digital therapeutics based on brain sciences. Before founding IBT, he served as a Partner of two tech funds in China. He has more than 10 years of experience in technology and healthcare both as an investor and entrepreneur. He also participated in several technological startups in the US. He received his Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Rochester, and his MBA from the University of Chicago.

Research activities include: The design, deployment, and evaluation of learning environments that increase and broaden participation in both music-making and in STEM fields. The creation of experimental musical performances and experiences that redistribute the roles of composer, performer, and listener; expose the creative process to audiences in new ways; and engage the public as musical collaborators. The design and development of core technologies to enable these experiences in areas such as real-time music notation, web audio, live coding, and network music.

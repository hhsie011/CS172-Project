The Rose Family is the recipient of the 2021 Family of the Year Award. Learn about their contributions to Clemson University. The recipient of this Parent/Family of the Year award takes additional effort to support their student's experience at Clemson while participating in the greater service leadership experience to the university as a whole. The parent/family of the year actively engages in supporting the efforts for all students' successes in college and providing enthusiasm for Clemson University. We anticipate that the Parent/Family of the Year nominee takes additional effort and time to do the following: Provides consistent service and positive morale to new students and families of Clemson University Remains actively involved in promoting Clemson University in their local communities Supports both students and families in meaningful ways Nominations for this award are accepted in late spring throughout the summer.

To ensure your Primary Tuition Scholarship (PTS) benefit application for The School at Columbia University is processed in a timely manner, submit PTS application(s) for the 2022-2023 school year to EBPA beginning June 1, 2022. Please do not send earlier. The School of Columbia University kindly requests that PTS applications be submitted no later than November 1, 2022. Helpful Tools and Resources The following resources are available on the Officers - Primary Tuition Scholarship website. These will help guide you as you prepare and submit your application(s) to EBPA: PTS Checklist - The School at Columbia University PTS Policy Tuition Programs / PTS FAQs The School at Columbia Enrollment Contract Steps to complete the PTS application process are in the PTS Checklist - The School at Columbia University. As a reminder, the PTS Benefit pays 50% of your dependent child's K-8 tuition at The School at Columbia, as long as you and your child live within the five boroughs of New York City. A child with two parents eligible for the benefit hired on or before July 1, 2011, and without a break in service of more than 31 days after 07/01/2011, can receive two scholarships simultaneously, covering 100% of the tuition. EBPA will notify you by email if your PTS application is missing any required information. Please respond as quickly as possible to avoid any processing delays. If you have questions about the PTS Policy or other Tuition Program benefits, please call the Columbia Benefits Service Center at 212-851-7000 (9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m., Monday-Friday) or email [email protected].

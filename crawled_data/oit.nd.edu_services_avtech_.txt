If the equipment is used solely in the classroom or for class work (i.e., class presentations, student projects, etc.), it can be checked out at no charge for the period of time listed on the equipment package. Most loan periods are up to 5 days depending on equipment type. Semester loan options are available on some equipment for faculty only. Bring your Notre Dame ID card to 115 DeBartolo Hall to check out equipment. More information on equipment available for check out is available in this knowledge article. For any other event, the OIT AV Technologies team can assist you in renting the equipment you need for a nominal fee. See information below. Audio Video Equipment Rental The OIT can help students, faculty, staff, and Notre Dame guests develop a plan for the right equipment and support to make your personal or professional event a memorable one. Contact us for virtually any type of event on or off-campus, including: Meetings Seminars Banquets and Parties Wedding Receptions Reunions Outdoor Movies Musical Events Live Productions AV equipment that we can provide but are not limited to is: Audio Recorders Flat panel monitors Laptops Lecterns Lighting Microphones, stands, and speakers Podiums Projectors Screens (small and large) Staging Video Cameras Video Conferencing We offer services such as tech support, video recording, and setup/strike. Quotes are free, but tech support and AV equipment rental come with a fee. Email to eventrentals@nd.edu or call 574-631-6423 for additional information.

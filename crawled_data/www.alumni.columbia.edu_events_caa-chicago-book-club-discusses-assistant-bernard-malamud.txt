The Assistant, Bernard Malamud's second novel, originally published in 1957, is the story of Morris Bober, a grocer in postwar Brooklyn, who "wants better" for himself and his family Like Malamud's best stories, this novel unerringly evokes an immigrant world of cramped circumstances and great expectations.

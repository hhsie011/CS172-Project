Staff Being Well Marketing & Communications Office of the Dean The School of Medicine is led by Toni M. Ganzel, MD, MBA, dean of the UofL School of Medicine and Vice President for Academic Medical Affairs. In 2013, Ganzel became the first female to be appointed dean of the UofL School of Medicine. In 2020, she was appointed vice president for academic medical affairs. Dean Ganzel works with a leadership team comprised of six vice deans, 23 department chairs, a Chief Diversity Officer, a Chief of Staff, a Strategic Communications team as well as department chairs and center and institute directors. Associate and assistant deans also assist the dean with the oversight and management of the School’s nearly 900 clinical and basic science faculty, more than 1,600 MD, health professions and PhD students, and approximately 960 staff employees.

The Michelin Career Center, part of the Center for Career and Professional Development, serves both students and employers through a variety of services. We offer students, in all stages of their education, career counseling and assessments, resume and cover letter reviews, information workshops, job search advice and more. Students and employers can take advantage of our on-line recruiting system, ClemsonJobLink, for job postings, on-campus interviews, company information sessions and career fairs. We are here to assist students and employers in making connections!

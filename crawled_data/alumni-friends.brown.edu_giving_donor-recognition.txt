When we say we couldn't do it without our supporters, we aren't exaggerating. Brown is a truly remarkable and distinctly unique place. We owe a lot of that to the alumni, parents, friends and students who give back year after year. From financial aid for our neediest students to attracting and retaining top-flight faculty, they help make the Brown experience possible. Simply put, Brown wouldn't be Brown without their support.

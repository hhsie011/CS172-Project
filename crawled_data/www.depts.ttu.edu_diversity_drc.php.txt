Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Dream Resource Center Menu Search TTU Division of Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Dream Resource Center The Dream Resource Center (DRC) seeks to: Educate, Support, & Advocate for undocumented students with or without Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA), also known as "Dreamers" and students with mixed-family statuses. In addition, the DRC works collaboratively across Texas Tech University and the Lubbock community to support students throughout their university experience. As more information becomes available, this dedicated space will serve as a hub for information related to serving Dreamers.

Enroll through the NJ Transit website or mobile app to take advantage of this offer. New to Mobile Student Program? Getting started is easy. Login via Columbia's web portal to verify your affiliation to enroll Fill in all required fields on the registration form Verify your full-time status and student (.edu) email address Download the NJ TRANSIT App and buy your Mobile Student Monthly Pass After you sign up, the discounted student pass can be purchased directly from the NJ TRANSIT mobile app. Passes may be purchased from the 19th of the preceding month through the 10th of the month that the pass is valid. Existing Mobile Student Monthly Users If you are an existing Mobile Student Monthly customer, you need to reactivate your account at the start of each semester. Here's how: Login via Columbia's web portal to reactivate your account Login to NJ TRANSIT with your student (.edu) email and the password Verify your full-time status and student email address Buy your Mobile Student Monthly Pass Why Mobile Student Monthly Passes? Mobile passes offer the convenience of purchasing and displaying your student monthly pass on your mobile device. No need to pre-pay or wait for a paper ticket in the mail. Buy at your leisure. It's important to remember: Download the NJ TRANSIT app Open the app and tap BUY Sign in with your .edu email and password Select a Student Monthly pass and pay for it in the app Monthly passes are available for purchase from the 19th of the previous month through the 10th of the month the pass is valid On the 1st of the month your student pass will display in the app Students who participate in this program agree that the monthly pass is for their exclusive use only. Each student is eligible to receive one discounted monthly pass per month. Passes are non-transferable. Any abuse or purposeful deception will result in the cancellation of the student's monthly pass account, and (s)he will be unable to participate in the program in the future. This will also apply to any other student who is knowingly involved. If you have any questions regarding reactivating your account, contact Quik-Tik at 1-866-QUIK-TIK.

Baugh Center for Entrepreneurship & Free Enterprise Baugh Center Sign Watch Video Full Story Paul Foster Campus for Business and Innovation Building at Night Watch Video Full Story Over the past 40 years, the John F. Baugh Center for Entrepreneurship & Free Enterprise has expanded its reach and impact to become one of the leading entrepreneurship centers in the world. Notable recognitions include being identified as a National Model Entrepreneurship Program by United States Association for Small Business and Entrepreneurship and receiving the NASDAQ Center for Entrepreneurial Excellence award by the Global Consortium of Entrepreneurship Centers. As the Baugh Center continues to grow, the entrepreneurial mindset of Center faculty and staff will enable the Center to continue to be at the forefront of entrepreneurship education, research and outreach for years to come.

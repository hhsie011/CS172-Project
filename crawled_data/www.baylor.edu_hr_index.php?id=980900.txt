Enrollment for your 2022 benefits will be from October 19 - November 2, 2021 inside Benefitfocus. This is an active enrollment so you will need to take action and select your benefit elections, even if you are not making changes from last year. Great News! There are no benefits plan design changes for 2022! However, be sure to review your plan elections and premiums and select the best option for you and your family. UPDATE: The current FSA administrator is WageWorks, but will change to HealthEquity on January 1, 2022. How to Enroll If you wish to elect in the same benefits for 2022, you should review your 2021 confirmation page Collect information for all beneficiaries (you will need to add these beneficiaries into BenefitfocusTM starting 10/19) Name Address Phone Number Tax ID (if needed) Collect information for all eligible dependents (you will need to add these dependents into BenefitfocusTM starting 10/19 Name Social Security Number Date of Birth Phone Number Review the options and understand your choices Review the benefits brochure to help you determine the best plan options for your circumstances Make sure to include any other individuals who will be affected by your elections in the decision-making process Log into Benefitfocus and make your 2022 benefit elections You can also use the Benefitfocus Mobile App User Guide Benefitfocus Demo Video 2022 Benefits Open Enrollment Information Session Video 2022 Open Enrollment Vendor Session Video Contact HR If you have additional questions regarding the 2022 Benefits Open Enrollment which are not answered through this webpage, please call 254.710.2000.

Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About SHOUT Student Health Outreach Program (SHOUT) volunteers educate families about the availability of health insurance and other government benefits and provide eligibility and screenings. Volunteers serve in community-based organizations, St Mary's Church on W126th St, BCI on Broadway and 114th St. SHOUT partners with two active non-profits, SeedCo and Children’s Defense Fund for training and enrollment activities. Volunteer commitment of 3 hours/week, various days, off-campus.

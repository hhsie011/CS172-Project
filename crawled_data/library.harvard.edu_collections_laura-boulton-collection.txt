The Laura Boulton Collection of Byzantine and Orthodox Musics represents Boulton's field work throughout the Eastern Orthodox world during the 1950s and 1960s. It includes sound recordings, accompanying documentation, and numerous ancillary materials. The collection also includes related Armenian, Russian, Coptic, and Ethiopian music materials.

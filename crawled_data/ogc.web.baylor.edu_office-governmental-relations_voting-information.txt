Student Voters - If you are registered to vote at your parents' or a home address away from Baylor, you have several voting options. You may register to vote at your Baylor residence or keep your registration at your home address. You decide where you reside for voting purposes, but you cannot register in both locations. If you keep your voter registration at your home address and will not be home during the early voting period or on Election Day, you can request that a ballot by mail be sent to you at your college address. Requesting an application for ballot to be mailed to you. The deadline to apply for a Texas ballot by mail is October, 2022. Joint General Election is November 2022. State of Texas Voter Information Online Voter Registration (voter registration card will be mailed to you and you MUST sign and return in order to be registered) Voter Registration FAQs Registering to Vote in Texas/McLennan County Application must be postmarked 30 days prior to election. January 31, 2022 is the registration deadline to be able to vote in the upcoming election. Registration forms are available in the Office of Government Relations (Pat Neff Hall, Suite 306) or by contacting the Elections Administrator located in your home county.

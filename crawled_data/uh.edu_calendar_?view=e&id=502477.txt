Submit an Event BAMSA Network Disco Celebration Thursday, May 12, 2022 5:00 pm - 7:00 pm The Blaffer Art Museum Student Association (BAMSA) invites you to the final event of the school year—the BAMSA Network Disco Celebration. Past, present, and future BAMSA members are invited to the museum for food, games, disco music and decor, and more! Take a walk-through of the UH School of Art Student Exhibition, enjoy a free photo booth, and participate in activities for a chance to win prizes. BAMSA’s mission is to generate and establish resume-worthy events, opportunities, and experiences through active engagement in the museum, while building a creative community open to all students across campus. Follow @uh.bamsa on Instagram for more information.

Named after the first torch in the university seal, vires represents strength of all kinds: physical, mental and moral. Inside the magazine, you will find alumni success stories, university news, class note updates and more to showcase the strength of the Florida State community. To submit your news for Class Notes, fill out the online form or email vires@alumni.fsu.edu. To learn how you can advertise in the magazine, visit alumni.fsu.edu/sponsorship. Read the latest edition of VIRES® online for university news, alumni stories, association updates and more. Click on the highlighted areas throughout the magazine for additional stories and videos. View past issues of VIRES® Members As an exclusive member benefit, you may opt to receive a print copy of VIRES® in the mail. If you have indicated that you prefer email only, you will not receive any print materials. Please log in to oneFSU and select My Preferences to make changes for future issues of VIRES®.

University Opportunities Brown in Washington Program The Swearer Center and the Watson Institute for International and Public Affairs invites undergraduate students (2nd semester sophomore, junior and senior) from all concentrations to apply to its Brown in Washington program for both fall and spring terms. Students accepted to this academic program will spend a semester living, working, and studying in Washington, D.C. during their junior or senior year. Participants will intern with public and nonprofit sector agencies and complete coursework with Brown faculty.

Students enrolled in this program are able to take advantage of the diverse expertise of the department's faculty, which embraces specializations in literature, language, philosophy, comparative literature, history, ethnic and cross-cultural studies, race relations, human rights in literature, cinema studies, anthropology, sociology and bilingual education. Additional Resources Gavea-Brown Publications; editorial offices of Brasil/Brazil: A Journal of Brazilian Literature; Gavea-Brown: A Bilingual Journal of Portuguese-American Letters and Studies; E-journal of Portuguese History; Pessoa Plural; and cooperation agreements with several Brazilian and Portuguese universities.

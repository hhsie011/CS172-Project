Wichita State University Libraries LibGuides Library Website Local Collections Home Enter Search Words Search Local Collections: Home Shocker Open Access Repository The University Libraries collects theses, dissertations, the works of various colleges, and much more in its Institutional Repository, Shocker Open Access Repository (SOAR). SOAR has a dual function of a publication platform and a digital archive. University faculty and staff are invited to publish research works, data, or documents in SOAR. For more information contact: soar@wichita.edu. Special Collections and University Archives Special Collections and University Archives is the unit within Wichita State University Libraries that acquires, arranges, preserves, and makes available to the public rare and unique books, manuscripts, university records, photographs and other images, and Kansas maps and atlases. Journals Hosted by WSU Libraries Journals of local content are also housed in the Open Journal Systems (OJS) platform. Interested in housing a journal there? Contact the Cataloging and Institutional Repository Librarian at susan.matveyeva@wichita.edu for details.

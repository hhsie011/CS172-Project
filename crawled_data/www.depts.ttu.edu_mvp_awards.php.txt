Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Military & Veterans Programs Menu Search TTU Military & Veterans Programs Ratings, Rankings, Awards Texas Tech is proud to have been named to multiple programs that recognize the importance of the education of veterans and their families. We are honored to be given a chance to serve veterans and veteran dependents by offering guidance and assistance as well as an overall positive college experience in which these students can strive toward degree completion and a successful future. Purple Heart University Texas Tech has been recognized as the 1st Purple Heart University in Texas. Military Friendly Best Online Colleges Texas Tech University was listed in SR Education Group's "2019 Best Online Colleges" in 2019. https://www.guidetoonlineschools.com/online-schools/military-friendly?utm_source=IMAP&utm_medium=email MAE Military Friendly College & University Texas Tech University has been recognized by Military Advanced Education as a "Military Friendly College & University" since 2012. G.I. Jobs Military Friendly Schools Texas Tech University has been recognized as "Military Friendly" by G.I. Jobs since 2010. Military Times Best for Vets Colleges Texas Tech University has been recognized by Military Times as a Best For Vets 4-Year College since 2013. U.S. News & World Report http://www.usnews.com/education/online-education/engineering/rankings?int=ae4509&int=9df408 Best for Vets: Business Schools In 2016, Texas Tech University was recognized by Military Times as #23 in "Best for Vets: Business Schools 2016". Texas Tech was first recognized in this award category in 2014.The award has not been awarded by Military Times in several years now. http://www.militarytimes.com/story/veterans/best-for-vets/2016/02/08/best-vets-business-schools-2016/79453242/ Toolkit for Veteran Friendly Institutions Texas Tech University is a proud participant in the Tool Kit for Veteran Friendly Institutions. http://www.vetfriendlytoolkit.org/?badge_id=3644 Got Your 6 "Got Your 6" is a national campaign that supports veterans in their transition into civilian life. http://www.GotYour6.org Aurora Foundation Texas Tech University received 2 grants from the Aurora Foundation to provided training and education for the faculty and staff of the Texas Tech University System. http://www.aurora4vets.org Military Friendly Employer Texas Tech is one of five universities selected by the Military Officers Association of America for its contribution of former military personnel and being proactive in hiring service members and veterans as employees. Along with Texas Tech are companies such as Amazon, Dell, Bank of America, GEICO, Peace Corps, government agencies and JPMorgan Chase & Co., to name a few. http://www.moaa.org Contact TTU Contact

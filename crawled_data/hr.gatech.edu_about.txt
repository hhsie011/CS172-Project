As the people’s unit, GTHR is engaged throughout the employee’s lifetime at Georgia Tech. Our team works with sensitive employee information and assists in the management of complex employee issues. Professionalism and confidence are of the utmost importance within our team. What we do: Onboard new employees Process payroll and maintain employment data for approximately 14,000 active faculty, staff, and students Strive to attract, recruit and retain the best talent in the marketplace Provide immigration, labor, and tax support required for foreign national faculty and staff to work at Georgia Tech as well as support employees performing services overseas.

the Role of Healthcare Professionals Events Past Event Accelerated Globalization’s Impact on the Role of Healthcare Professionals Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo May 30, 2020 7:00 PM - 8:40 PM Please join us as we discuss how the roles and responsibilities of healthcare workers will change as a result of accelerated globalization, and what that will mean to you and your family. You will learn: What new healthcare challenges are posed by accelerated globalization? What new skills and capabilities are needed? How to fix the gap between clinical medicine and public health, and improve collaborations across cultures, nationalities, and geographic regions? This event is part of the Beijing Center's Health and Medicine Series Program. Cohosts: China Primary Health Care foundation, International Healthcare Leadership (IHL) Speakers Dr. Susan Michaels-Strasser Assistant Professor, Epidemiology (in ICAP) at the Columbia University Irving Medical Center Dr. David P. Roye Jr. Founder, International Healthcare Leadership (IHL); Special Lecturer in Orthopaedic Surgery at the Columbia University Irving Medical Center Dr. Henry Ting Enterprise Director of Quality & Patient Safety, Cardiology Department, Mayo Clinic Florida Dr. Xiaopeng Ma President, Shenzhen Children’s Hospital Mr. Xirong Cao Director-General, China Primary Health Care Foundation Ms. Jan Fang Director of Strategy and Innovation, China Primary Health Care foundation Reminders Webinar will be hosted at 19:00 in Beijing Time (7:00 in NY Time) and conducted in English with simultaneous translation in Chinese.

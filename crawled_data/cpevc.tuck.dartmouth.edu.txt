Together we are committed to advancing knowledge about these industries through experiential learning opportunities. CPEVC provides programming that enhances the curricular and co-curricular experiences of Tuck students and connects them with the broader community of PE and VC professionals. What are Centers at Tuck? The six Centers at Tuck forge pathways of learning and application for our MBA students. Students are encouraged to partake of any of Tuck's individual or cross-Center opportunities to truly customize their learning and experience. Program Spotlight Comprised of two cohorts, the Venture Capital and Private Equity Fellows, the CPEVC MBA Fellows Program provides Tuck students interested in private equity and venture capital with opportunities that further their knowledge of and experience in these industries.

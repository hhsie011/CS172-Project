Following his commission, Captain Bryan attended the Infantry Basic Officer Leadership Course and Ranger School at Fort Benning, GA and reported to his first assignment at Fort Bragg, NC. There he served as a Platoon Leader and an Executive Officer in 2nd Battalion, 508th, 2nd Brigade Combat Team and deployed in support of Operation Inherent Resolve 2017. Following this assignment, Captain Bryan attended the Maneuver Captain’s Career Course. Remaining on Fort Benning, GA, Captain Bryan took command of Alpha Company, 1st Battalion, 507th Parachute Infantry Regiment at the United States Army Airborne School. In his most recent assignment, Captain Bryan commanded Able Company, 4th Battalion, 9th Infantry Regiment and deployed in support of Operation Inherent Resolve 21-22. He now currently serves as an Assistant Professor of Military Science for the University of Cincinnati in Cincinnati, Ohio. His military education includes: Ranger School, Airborne School, Infantry Basic Officer Leader Course (IBOLC), the Maneuver Career Captain’s Course (MCCC), Jumpmaster School, and Pathfinder School. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in International Relations from Lehigh University in Bethlehem, PA. He has served two combat tours in Iraq and taken part in the NATO Swift Response Exercise. Captain Bryan’s awards and decorations include the Bronze Star Medal, Meritorious Service Medal (1OLC), Ranger tab, the Combat Infantryman Badge, Mater Parachutist Badge, Pathfinder Badge, Meritorious Unit Award, and Superior Unit Award. He is also a recipient of the Order of Saint Maurice. Captain Bryan is married to the former Marissa Botting of Boston, Massachusetts. They have a son, Austin (6), and a daughter, Charlotte.

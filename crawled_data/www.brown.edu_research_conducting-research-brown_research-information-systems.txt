Research Administration Information Systems The RAIS team provides services to the research community for all electronic systems and data overseen by OVPR and Workday Grants. This includes: proposal creation in Brown's electronic grants management system (COEUS) and external electronic systems such as eRA Commons, Research.gov, and Grants.gov; Conflict of Interest disclosures and IACUC protocol development in InfoEd; as well as IRB protocol management in Coeus. RAIS delivers in-depth business intelligence solutions to the Brown community through reporting, visualizations, and workflow notifications for departments and senior management. In addition, this group is leading the development and implementation of a new integrated electronic system managing grant development, submission and management and IRB protocol development and management. We currently support two systems Coeus and InfoEd, and Sponsored Reporting.

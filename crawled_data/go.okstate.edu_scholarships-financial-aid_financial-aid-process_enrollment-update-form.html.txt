If your enrollment plans for the spring 2022 semester doesn't match the assumptions mentioned above, use this form to update our office of changes to your planned enrollment. Be sure you know the possible impact of dropping, withdrawing or failing to attend classes during the semester on your eligibility for financial aid. Any change to your plans may affect your eligibility for financial aid and/or the amount for which you are eligible. You will be notified if adjustments are made. You can check my.okstate.edu for any updates. You must notify the Office of Scholarships and Financial Aid immediately if you change the number of hours in which you enroll, prior to the deadline for 100% refund of charges. All courses must count toward degree requirements to be included for financial aid eligibility.

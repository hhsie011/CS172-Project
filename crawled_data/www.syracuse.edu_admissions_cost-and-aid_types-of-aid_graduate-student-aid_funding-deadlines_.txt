If you are seeking financial support from Syracuse University, you should note the program specific deadline on your admission application instructions. To be considered for a fellowship, your application supporting documents must be received in January, or your academic program’s admission deadline, if earlier. Apply for financial aid Important Assistantship and scholarship decisions are made only after all admissions materials are received, therefore, be sure that your application is complete by your department’s deadline. Continuing Students Contact your department chair or graduate program director during the fall semester to inquire about consideration for financial support for the following year. Related Pages Speak with a Financial Aid Counselor The Office of Financial Aid and Scholarship Programs is here to assist you and make the financial aid process as easy as possible.

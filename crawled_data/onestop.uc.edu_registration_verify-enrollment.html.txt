Class Offerings Order Transcripts Verify Enrollment or Degree Need verification for car insurance, health insurance, or to defer previous student loans? Free, official degree enrollment and verification documents may be printed directly from your Catalyst account. Enrollment certifications for each semester are available on the first day of classes for that semester. For students who do not have login credentials, please email your request to mailto:reginfo@uc.edu with enough pertinent information to complete the request. Please note that our office requires three (3) business days to process requests.

Take exit 2A, Dodge/Harney Streets. Turn right onto Douglas (one way east). Turn left onto 24th Street which will take you to the center of campus. From the North Follow I-29 South. Take exit 53B to I-480 West/Highway 6 (taking you over the Missouri River and into Omaha). Take exit 4 onto Dodge Street (the cross streets will start at 10th Street and go up). Turn right onto 24th Street, which will take you to the center of campus. From the South Follow I-29 North. Merge with I-80 West (in Council Bluffs, Iowa). Follow signs for I-29 N (Sioux City, Exit 1A). Take exit 53B to I-480 West/Highway 6 (taking you over the Missouri River and into Omaha). Take exit 4 onto Dodge Street (the cross streets will start at 10th Street and go up). Turn right onto 24th Street, which will lead you to the center of campus. From southern Nebraska Follow Highway 75 North (North Omaha Freeway). Merge with I-480/Highway 75 North toward Eppley Airfield/Downtown (get into far right lanes). Take exit 2A, Dodge/Harney Streets. Turn right onto Douglas (one way east). Turn left onto 24th Street. From the Airport Exit the airport by turning left onto Abbott Drive (heading south approximately 3 miles). Abbott Drive will turn into Cuming Street (heading west). Turn left at 16th Street (for about 0.1 mile). Turn right onto Burt Street. Turn left onto 24th Street.

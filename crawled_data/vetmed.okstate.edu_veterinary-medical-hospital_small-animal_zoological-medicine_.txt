OSU offers the latest in veterinary medical care to nontraditional animals such as birds, reptiles, and small mammals. The ZM team has access to modern veterinary medicine technology including CT scanners, and an MRI. The interdisciplinary style at the Veterinary Medical Hospital allows the ZM team access to many board certified specialists in fields such as surgery, anesthesiology, ophthalmology, radiology and more.

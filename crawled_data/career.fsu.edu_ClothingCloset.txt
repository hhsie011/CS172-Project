Quick Guides Search Internships Salary and Hiring Data ProfessioNole Clothing Closet Big Interview ProfessioNole Clothing Closet The ProfessioNole Clothing Closet provides a way for Florida State University students to access FREE professional attire appropriate for career fairs, interviews, and the workplace. Inventory includes suits, blazers, skirts, button-down shirts, and more. Our goal is to help students look their best throughout their job search so they can make a positive impression as they enter the professional world! Located in the Career Center on the third floor of the Dunlap Success Center, the Clothing Closet is available by appointment to all FSU students. Student Appointments Summer 2022 – to request access to the Clothing Closet during the summer, please email HireANole@fsu.edu with your top 3 days/times you would like to come. A staff member will be in touch within 48 hours to confirm and provide additional details. Students are limited to four items per semester due to limited supply. Summer (May through mid-August), Fall (August through December), and Spring (January through April). Appointments are available between the hours of 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday. Appointments are scheduled in NoleNetwork for fall and spring semesters. Unfortunately, the Clothing Closet cannot guarantee availability of all sizes and types of items. How to make an appointment? Login to your NoleNetwork account Click Appointments on the left-hand side Click "Request New Appointment" In the "Type" drop-down, select "ProfessioNole Clothing Closet" Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Check Availability" Upcoming appointments will then appear on the right-hand side of the appointment request window. When filling out the appointment request details, including your clothing preferences to help staff selected clothes for you in advance. Once you click "Submit Request" you will receive an email from the system confirming your request was received. You will receive a follow-up confirmation email once your appointment is approved. You must request the appointment at least two business days (48 hours) in advance. Appointment requests submitted on weekends will not be received until Mondays. Review our Dressing to Impress: Appropriate Professional Attire guide for ideas on what to wear to interviews, conferences, and/or the workplace. Clothing Donations We are able to accept donations during regular business hours (M-F 8-5 except for holidays). If you have items you would like to donate, please contact Heather Lazar at heather.lazar@fsu.edu or 850.644.6493 to arrange drop off of your donation. Items we accept: Business Suits (men’s and women’s) Blazers Dress shirts Blouses Professional Slacks Skirts Ties Belts Professional shoes All donation items must be cleaned appropriately. Items with any damage and items without sizes clearly labeled will not be accepted. Note, items deemed not acceptable for the Closet will be donated to a local charity. Other Ways to Support the Closet Do you desire to support the Closet and FSU students, but no clothes to donate? We have several ways to show your support and help our students present a professional image… Financial Support The Career Center seeks monetary donations to help support the Closet. This money enables the Career Center to purchase items that are in greatest demand by FSU students. Please contact Heather Lazar, Senior Assistant Director, Employer Relations, at (850) 644-6493 or heather.lazar@fsu.edu to learn how you can give directly to the Closet. Amazon Wish List We already have selected a menu of professional clothes and other items to help augment the inventory of items available for students in the ProfessioNole Clothing Closet. On the Amazon Wish List you choose and buy the items and they will be shipped directly to the Career Center.

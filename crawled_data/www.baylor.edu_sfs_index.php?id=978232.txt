STUDENT FINANCIAL SERVICES ORIENTATION CHECKLIST Log into BEARWEB Use your BearID, Password, and Duo two-factor authentication to login. Please verify your contact information under "Personal Information" Click "Set Up Authorized Users for E-Bill" (Granting a parent/guardian access to view your statements allows individuals to access 1098-T tax statements as well as make payments to your student account online. This step is optional.) Click the "Authorized Users" tab Click “Add Authorized User” at the bottom of page—enter email address and complete agreement Close screen to return to menu Enroll in Direct Deposit, click "Refund by Direct Deposit" Complete the form with routing and account number Click "New Student Orientation Menu" at top to return If not enrolled in Direct Deposit, paper checks will automatically be mailed for any eligible credit balances which can take several weeks to be received. Click "Title IV Authorizations" Review and select “Yes” or “No”, then click “Submit” (Selecting "Yes" grants permission to the University to apply federal student aid funds to non-allowable charges, such as student health insurance.) Required Grad Student Health Insurance (Click on corresponding tile below under "Helpful Links" for more details) If you have existing health insurance, submit proof of alternate coverage by completing the waiver process during the open enrollment period. All of the resources for insurance help can be found through the AHP website.

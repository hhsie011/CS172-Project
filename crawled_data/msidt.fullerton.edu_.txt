Why Instructional Design at CSUF? The MSIDT degree is designed to meet the needs of individuals seeking to advance their knowledge of instructional design research and improve We are ranked #4 in the U.S. their skill level. The program is designed to meet the needs of those employed by businesses and corporations, the military, technology industries, instructional design companies, software consultants and designers, educators (K-12 and higher education) and those working with curriculum development and training in e-learning. Our program courses are sequentially designed, and course content reinforces prior content knowledge taught in each course. Students learn to implement research theory into practical application.

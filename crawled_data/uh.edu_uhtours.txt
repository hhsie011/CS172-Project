Let's go! UH Walking Tours is a photographic guide of significant locations at the University of Houston for your mobile device. This web application uses GPS to detect your location guiding you to discover UH history and culture as you walk through campus. The UH Walking Tours web application was deployed in April 2012 and works across a wide range of mobile devices. UH Walking Tours was built on the jQuery Mobile platform, exploits the Google Maps API and utilizes custom information specific to UH. Device support includes the Apple iPhone, iPad and Android-based smartphones and tablets. © 2016 University of Houston. All rights reserved.

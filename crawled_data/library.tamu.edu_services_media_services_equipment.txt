Phone and laptop chargers, calculators, cameras, projectors, laptops, tablets, and more. All equipment is available on a first-come, first-serve basis and loan periods may vary. Quantities of some items are limited. Equipment is available for checkout year round. Visit your library service desk or the links below to find out what types of equipment are available at your library location. Accessories Accessories In addition to the accessories listed here, some Libraries also offer tripods; various calculators (Basic 4-function, TI-30X Scientific, TI-BAII Financial, TI-83+ Graphing, TI-84+ CE Graphing, and TI-89 Titanium Graphing); headset microphones; and various computer accessories (USB Mice, 4-Port USB Hub, Multi-Card Reader, Logitech C615 Webcam). Loan periods vary by library and accessory type.

Villanova holds a special place in the hearts and minds of hundreds of thousands of students, alumni, parents and friends. Once you walk onto campus, a shared common goal unites us to further the growth and prosperity of the University—its academic and athletics programs—and to secure Villanova’s future for generations to come. Through integrated efforts, the Office of University Advancement engages and connects with the worldwide Nova Nation, creating opportunities for Villanovans to give their time, talent and support to the University. Our knowledgeable, experienced University Advancement team builds partnerships, cultivates relationships and provides personalized service in every area of giving, with a donor-centered approach to the stewardship of gifts. As a team, we stand committed to strengthening our Villanova community—one that extends well beyond our campus and stretches across the globe. Join us!

You’ll gain essential knowledge and techniques—from editing to video storytelling—and build qualifications for career success in a variety of creative settings. Learn how the Filmmaking certificate can work for you. Contact Coordinator Filmmaking students learn both on-and off-camera skills – from acting and principles of video to script writing and lighting. Curriculum To complete the 15-credit-hour undergraduate certificate, you’ll take the following courses: Editing for Film Principles of Video Production Video Storytelling Two of the following: Cinematography, Directing I, Scriptwriting I View the curriculum How to enroll To add this certificate to your plan of study: Log into the myWSU portal, find the Student Tools box (center of the page) and click "Change my Major/Minor." Click continue (this will show your current major and minor). Click continue, then click on "Add a certificate" and choose your certificate program. Click continue and submit request.

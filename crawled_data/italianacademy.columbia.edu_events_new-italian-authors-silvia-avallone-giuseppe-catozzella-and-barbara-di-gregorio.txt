Co-sponsored by the Dante Alighieri Society of NY and the Consulate General of Italy in NY. In collaboration with the Italian Cultural Institute of NY and with the Capri Awards Foundation. The Dante Alighieri Society is pleased to invite you to a special event featuring the new generation of Italian writers selected by RCS Rizzoli: Silvia Avallone, author of the novel “Acciaio”, winner of the Campiello Opera Prima Prize and finalist for the Strega Prize Giuseppe Catozzella, author of "Alveare" Barbara Di Gregorio, author of "Le giostre sono per gli scemi" Welcoming remarks by: David Freedberg, Director of the Italian Academy, Columbia University Francesco M.

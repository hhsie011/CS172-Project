Event Kind: Training Organizer: Department of Environmental Health Sciences SHARP Invitation Limited to: Open to the Public Website: Learn More Learn More Join us for the next live-stream NIH Grant Writing Boot Camp on July 11-12, 2022. The NIH Grant Writing Boot Camp is a two-day intensive boot camp combining lectures, hands-on activities, and discussions to demystify the NIH application process. This training will prepare participants to submit an NIH grant proposal that turns reviewers into advocates, positioning the applicant for success. Investigators at all career stages are welcome to attend, and we particularly encourage trainees and early-stage investigators to participate.

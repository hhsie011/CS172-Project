From The South (including the University of Miami Coral Gables campus): Take South Dixie Highway/US-1 North. Turn Right at FL-913/Rickenbacker Causeway (Toll Road). Follow the Causeway to 4600 Rickenbacker Causeway (on the right). From the North Take I-95 South. Take Exit 1A toward Key Biscayne/Rickenbacker Causeway. Turn left at SW 26th Rd. Continue on FL-913/Rickenbacker Causeway (Toll Road). Follow the Causeway to 4600 Rickenbacker Causeway (on the right).

View programs » Ask Ecampus Welcome to Ask Ecampus. Whether you're just visiting or are already an Oregon State Ecampus student, this is where you can get answers to your questions or online assistance regarding Ecampus programs and services. Our knowledge base is designed to provide you with high-quality service around the clock. Ask a question: Knowledge base: By asking a question above, you are accessing the Ecampus knowledge base 24/7, which includes answers to hundreds of questions and is regularly updated to make sure you have the very latest information. You can also browse all questions and answers by category. Online tutoring: Online tutoring is available for registered Ecampus students. Math tutoring 24/7, science, writing and more. And it's free! Technical support: The OSU Service Desk consultants are available to answer questions and provide software assistance during business hours. Library support chat: 24/7 librarian help and assistance.

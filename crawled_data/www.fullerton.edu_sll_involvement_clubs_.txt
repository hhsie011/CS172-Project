There are over 300 clubs and organizations to suit a variety of interests and needs, including social, cultural, academic, sport, faith, community service, and more. Clubs and organizations support the mission of the University by providing active involvement in co-curricular experiences. They also help to provide valuable leadership and organizational skill development opportunities. Student Life & Leadership (SLL) provides general advisement for all clubs and organizations as well as supports the student leaders of these organizations.

International Children's Tech and Art Traveling Exhibition will arrive at Today Art Museum in Beijing, featuring creative young artists from all over the world. About the Exhibition The exhibition, titled 'One World', will integrate art, technology, and education. It aims to develop children's global competence through participating as curators, artists, storytellers, and global citizens. 'One World' is a traveling exhibition providing a stage for children to influence the world with their artworks. It will take place in 5 countries on 5 continents, starting in Beijing, then to Paris, Nairobi, Rio de Janeiro, and finally New York. This exhibition is launched by ANOBO (New York) Education Research Team and is organized by BNU-ANOBO Children's Global Competence Research Committee and Columbia Global Centers | Beijing. See the exhibition trailer video below. Exhibition Highlights Section ONE - "One Planet" This immersive and interactive AR exhibition provides a platform to inspire young artists to explore, think, and create their own planets and share these imaginative worlds with the visitors. Section TWO - "Air, Land & Sea" This interactive installation of Ecology, presented by National Geographic and INDE, allows children to travel through the earth with wildlife and walk on the moon with astronauts with advanced 3D AR technology. Section THREE - "Young Artists x Overseas Artist" Featuring large art installations created by established artists from different countries, this section establishes a dialogue between the artists and the children, allowing them to be the ones to build and express their care and love for the planet artistically.

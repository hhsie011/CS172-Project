Grants Resources: video-camera icon NIH Grants Workshop Series Recordings Three recorded workshops that focus on navigating NIH grants and the essential steps needed to complete and submit an application. mouse-pointer icon Find Funding Columbia-wide source for identifying, pursuing, and stewarding project sponsorship from extramural partners. file-video icon Finding Funding Workshop: PIVOT Recorded workshop on Pivot, a searchable funding opportunity and collaborator database. listl icon Grant Starter Kit Contains templates and primer documents to help you write and assemble your grant. book icon Funding and Grantsmanship Course Spring, semester-long course at CUIMC covers topics such as funding mechanisms for junior faculty, postdocs, and grad students, grant writing basics, and the grant review process. lightbulb icon Enhancing PI Knowledge About Grant Elements The goals of the Enhancing Principal Investigator Knowledge About Grant Elements (EPIKAGE) seminar series are to provide grantsmanship tips that are applicable to all applications as well as provide an overview of NIH grant opportunities that are specific to ESI faculty and trainees.

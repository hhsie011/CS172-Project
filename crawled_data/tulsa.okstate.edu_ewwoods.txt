Information Ellis Walker Woods Memorial About Ellis Walker Woods Committee and Partners History Home Ellis Walker Woods Memorial The Ellis Walker Woods Memorial honors the first principal of Tulsa's Booker T. Washington High School. A labor of love for more than 30 years, the conception, fundraising and construction of the memorial was guided by a dedicated committee of BTW alumni and supporters. The memorial is located at the intersection of Greenwood Ave. and John Hope Franklin Blvd. on the OSU-Tulsa campus. It was dedicated on Aug. 16, 2019 and at that time was entrusted to OSU-Tulsa to care for and maintain. Also located at OSU-Tulsa is a historical marker commemorating the original location of Booker T. Washington High School. Visiting the Memorial The memorial is open to the public. Parking at OSU-Tulsa is free for visitors. Please park in Lot A or Lot B to visit the memorial; the driveway near the memorial is a delivery entrance that cannot be blocked.

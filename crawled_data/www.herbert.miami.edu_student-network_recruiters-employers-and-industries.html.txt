Connect Now Prepared to Lead Miami Herbert graduates are prepared to lead and bring new value through data-driven decision-making. Leadership training and coaching help accelerate our students’ ability to deliver on day one. Our students have multiple opportunities to gain real-world, hands-on experience through internships, competitions, and capstone projects, as well as the Mentor Program, among the largest in the Southeast, which connects students with community business leaders for practical leadership preparation. Connect with Graduate Student Talent Graduate Career Advancement at Miami Herbert connects employers with the talent from our MBA, MS in Business Analytics, MS in Finance, MS in Sustainable Business, Master of International Business, Master of Health Administration, and MS in Leadership programs. Connect With Students Internships Every year, graduate students at Miami Herbert Business School expand their education, work experiences, and professional networks through internship positions in organizations of all kinds - both inside and outside the United States. Our graduate business programs go beyond preparing students for careers in business. They emphasize leadership skills as communication, conflict negotiation and resolution, time management, and cross-cultural and interpersonal training.

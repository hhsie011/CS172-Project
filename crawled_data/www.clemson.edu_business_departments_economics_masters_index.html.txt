Contact Us Clemson Home Wilbur O. and Ann Powers College of Business Departments Department of Economics Current: Masters Master's Programs in Economics The John E. Walker Department of Economics has two master's programs: a Master of Arts in Economics and a Master of Science in Economic Analytics. M.S. students take a second required course in applied econometrics and two required courses in data analysis for economics. M.A. students instead take three elective courses in economics. The M.S. degree is designated as a Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) degree. M.A. in Economics M.S. in Economic Analytics John E.

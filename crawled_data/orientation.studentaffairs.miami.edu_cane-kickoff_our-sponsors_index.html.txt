Parents & Families Home Orientation Our Sponsors AAA Our Sponsors Throughout each academic year, the Department of Orientation & Commuter Student Involvement provides an impactful and comprehensive opportunity for local businesses and corporate brands to showcase products and services with our student/ family population. This sponsorship gives organizations the opportunity to provide generous support by marketing goods and services on the University of Miami campus at our most eventful times. And whether sponsor’s packages included digital signage, participation in our vendor fair, or simply an advertisement placed in our schedule booklet, the goods and services provided by organizations continually had thousands of impressions throughout our events.

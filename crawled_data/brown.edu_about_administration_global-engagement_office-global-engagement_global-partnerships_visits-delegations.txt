Each year, Brown University receives hundreds of visitors and delegations to the University, who are with us for anywhere from a few hours to a few days. The Office of Global Engagement plans visit itineraries according to the interests of visitors, connecting the visitors with their counterparts at the University and promoting dialogue on issues of common interest. If you are interested in a non-admission related visit to Brown, please submit the Visit Request Form to the Office of Global Engagement. For admission related visits, please visit International Admissions and the Graduate School.

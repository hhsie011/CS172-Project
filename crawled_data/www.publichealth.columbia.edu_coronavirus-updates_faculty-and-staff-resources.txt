Columbia University, the Medical Center, and our School have been working in a closely coordinated effort with the State and City to lay the groundwork for a phased return to on-campus operations and research activities in adherence with New York State’s “Moving New York Forward” plan. However, at this time the majority of faculty and staff are still required to work remotely.

Mosher Hall is named after Edward J. Mosher ’28 and was constructed in 1975. Mr. Mosher played football while attending Texas A&M. He served many years as Chairman of the Board of Mosher Steel Inc. Mr. Mosher has served as a member of the executive board of the Former Students Association. 2021 – 2022 Rates Commons Hall $3,780semester Double Room Visit our rates page to see the proposed rates for 22-23. × Single Gender by Suite Description The building houses male and female residents next door to each other, but all residents in the same multi-room suite are the same gender. Only residents of the same gender share a restroom. Close Gallery Get a feel for all that Mosher Hall has to offer by scrolling through the photo gallery. If you prefer, check it for yourself and join us for a tour. Location Physical Address: 725 Mosher Lane, College Station, TX 77840 See also: U.S.P.S., FedEx, and UPS addresses Amenities Fully furnished rooms Furniture included (2 of each per double room): desk, chair, bed, dresser, bookshelf; beds are twin-size junior lofts Room flooring is vinyl plank Suite bathrooms with ceramic tiles 80+ channels of digital TV & internet included Electricity included Free on-site washer/dryer Water, sewer, recycling & trash included Accessible rooms available Vending machines Designed for community T.V. lounge Community events and socials Resident advisors Public hallway phones on each floor Access to Commons Lobby – convenience store, multipurpose rooms, 24-hour desk Community kitchen Resident Community Council × Suite Bathroom Description Suite Bathrooms are facilities that offer four to six students shared sinks and vanity with a separate, often stall, shower and toilet. Showers have doors. Cleaned by custodial staff once/week.

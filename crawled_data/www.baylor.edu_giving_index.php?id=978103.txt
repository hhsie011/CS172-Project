The more than 5,000 courses taught in the College provide the largest portion of Baylor's nationally recognized core curriculum, the foundation upon which every student's educational experience is built. We thank you for your interest in the mission and vision of Baylor University and invite you to make a gift to support student scholarships, faculty research and needs and priorities within the College of Arts & Sciences today. College of Arts & Sciences Endowed Scholarship Your support helps the College of Arts & Sciences stand in the gap for deserving students who need financial assistance to continue their pursuit of a Baylor education. College of Arts & Sciences Excellence Fund Gifts help Baylor's College of Arts & Sciences remain on the cutting edge by providing lab equipment, research materials, relevant technology, hands-on opportunities and stipends for students to attend prestigious conferences. James Vardaman Endowed Professorship The James Vardaman Endowed Professorship Fund will help attract and retain professors in the Department of History. The funds may be used for, but are not limited to, the support of salary, research needs, scholarship and travel expenses. William and Argye Hillis Scholars in Biomedical Sciences The Hillis Scholars program provides scholarship support and research opportunities that prepare Baylor students for top medical schools and postgraduate health science programs and to become holistic healthcare professionals. Contact Us To learn more about opportunities to give to the College of Arts & Sciences, contact Kelli Edmond, Managing Director of Development.

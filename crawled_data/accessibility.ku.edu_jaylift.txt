Procedures To receive the curb-to-curb service, a student must register with The Academic Achievement and Access Center (AAAC). Documentation must be mailed, faxed 785-864-2817, emailed (achieve@ku.edu), or hand-delivered to the AAAC office in 22 Strong. Once the student has been approved for this service, AAAC staff member authorizes the student to use the Jay Lift by email. If a student has a temporary disability, they will have a service end date included in that email. All users must supply their name, phone, and schedule to Jay Lift by phone. Call 785-312-7054, select option 2, extension 101, and leave your information. All rides require 24 hours notice prior to first ride as well as 24-hour cancelation notice. Co-curricular ride requests must be first made through the AAAC office. If a student will need to use paratransit in Lawrence, it is imperative to be certified through the City of Lawrence.

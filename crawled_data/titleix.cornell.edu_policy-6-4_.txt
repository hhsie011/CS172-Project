To this end, through Cornell University Policy 6.4, the university provides means to address bias, discrimination, harassment, and sexual and related misconduct. For the current governing Cornell policy, please see Cornell University Policy 6.4, “Prohibited Bias, Discrimination, Harassment, and Sexual and Related Misconduct.” Under Policy 6.4, the university has identified certain offices and employee positions as Designated Reporters. See the List of Cornell Policy 6.4 Designated Reporters. The Designated Mandatory Reporters at Weill Cornell Medicine can be found at page 13 in their Policy 206.

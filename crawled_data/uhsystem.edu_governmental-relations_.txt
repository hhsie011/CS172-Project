Emphasizing an engaged and comprehensive approach, the office focuses on establishing collaborative partnerships within the community, implementing a proactive external affairs program and maintaining mutually productive relationships with government entities. Jason Smith, Vice Chancellor for Governmental Relations, heads the operation, which has offices in Houston and Austin.

Nomination Deadline Sept. 1, 2022 Distinguished Alumni/ae Recognition The Distinguished Alumna/us Banquet, which will recognize the 2020, 2021 and 2022 recipients, will be held Nov. 14, 2022. Details, including ticket purchase information, will be posted here when finalized. Nominate an Alumnus/a Nominations must be made in writing, with a letter detailing the nominee's career highlights and other accomplishments. Letters or statements from at least two additional persons must be submitted in support of the nomination as well. The person submitting the nomination should compile all materials and mail as one package to: Office of Public Affairs and Alumni/ae Relations Perkins School of Theology PO Box 750133 Dallas, TX 75275-0133 Alternatively, letters may be sent together as attachments via email to: Rev.

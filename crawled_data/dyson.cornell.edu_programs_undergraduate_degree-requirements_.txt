To earn the Bachelor of Science degree in applied economics and management (AEM) from the Dyson School, you must complete at least 120 credit hours from the following four categories: AEM Core Requirements AEM Concentration Requirements College Distribution Requirements Grand Challenges Requirements Free Electives Students will start with a balance of AEM Core courses and College Distribution requirements during the first and second year. During your final two years you will work towards completing your selected concentration. The curriculum is rounded out with the Grand Challenges program, second concentrations, minors or electives. Degree requirements are based on the semester in which you entered as a Dyson major for the first time. It is important for you to refer to the correct degree requirements.

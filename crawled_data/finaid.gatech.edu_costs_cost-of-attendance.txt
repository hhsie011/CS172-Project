COA includes tuition and fees; on-campus room and board (or a housing and food allowance for off-campus students); and allowances for books, supplies, transportation, loan fees, and, if applicable, dependent care. For each academic year, the standard cost of attendance at Georgia Tech depends on your program, year in school, residency, and housing selection.

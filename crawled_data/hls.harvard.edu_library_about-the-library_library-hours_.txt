Library Calendar Library Calendar Hours at other Harvard Libraries 24/7 Library Access Harvard Law School students have 24 hours access to the second floor of the Library as a study space during the academic year. Library services and access to other floors are only available during regular hours. Reading and Exam Period Restricted Access During fall and spring reading and exam periods, HUID access to the Library is limited to Harvard Law School students, faculty and staff. Other patrons who need to use the Law collection must check in at the Circulation Desk for access.

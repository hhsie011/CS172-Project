Columbia Data science has become invaluable preparation for leadership in the 21st century. Ours is one of the most highly-rated and sought-after advanced data science programs in the world. Columbia offers students in-depth training in data science and the opportunity to work closely with diverse faculty members and our industry affiliates through part-time, full-time, and online study options.

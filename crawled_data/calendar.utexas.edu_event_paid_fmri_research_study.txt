The University of Texas at Austin Texas Today Main Menu Login Search our events Search Texas Today: UT Events & Announcements Calendar › Event Details Paid fMRI research study Are you between the ages of 20 and 45 and interested in making money participating in research? The Dunsmoor lab is looking for subjects to participate in our fMRI study. This two-day study pays up to $100, and you can get a free picture of your brain too! The requirements for participating in this study are: -No history of mental illness -No metal implants or permanent top retainer on teeth (bottom is alright) -No body piercings that cannot be removed at the time of scan. We are located at the Health Discovery Building, about a 5 minute walk from Clark Field and the Recreational Sports Center. If you are interested in participating, please email affect.experiment@gmail.com for more information.

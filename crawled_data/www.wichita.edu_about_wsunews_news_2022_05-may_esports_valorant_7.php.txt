Winning the direct bid to compete allowed the team to bypass the normal qualifier system which includes live and online invitationals. Although the CECC hosts tournaments for multiple games, WSU Esports competed only in the Valorant tournament. WSU Esports placed ninth out of the 16 teams competing in the Valorant games. WSU upset University of Central Florida, lost to Texas A&M University, and won against Sam Houston State University Qualifying for the CECC marked a milestone in the advancement of the WSU Esports program.

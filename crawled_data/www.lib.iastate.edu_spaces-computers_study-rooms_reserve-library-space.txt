Reserve a Library Space Group study rooms Group study rooms have space for 2 to 6 people and provide technology purchased using student technology fees. Available for registered students only. Each room can be reserved for up to 3 hours. Reserve a group study room Individual study rooms Individual study rooms provide seating and power outlets for lower distraction, private study. Available for registered students only. Each room can be reserved for up to 3 hours. Reserve an individual study room Family-Friendly Room Iowa State University parents can reserve the Family-Friendly Room located on the lower level. It has child-sized furniture, computers with educational software, drawing surfaces, and toys. Reserve the Family-Friendly Room Research study rooms Graduate students, faculty, or P&S staff users can apply to use a research study room. Rooms are reserved for the semester. These rooms are for research that requires extensive use of library materials.

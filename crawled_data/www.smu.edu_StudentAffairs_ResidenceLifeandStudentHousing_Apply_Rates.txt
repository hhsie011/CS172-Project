SMU Housing Rates Explore common housing fees While not an exhaustive list of housing fees, below are the housing fees commonly assessed. Fee Name Fee Description Nonrefundable advanced housing payment $100 New undergraduates pay this as part of the admission deposit Cancellation penalty Varies per cancellation date as outlined in the Housing License Agreement and here Charged if a student cancels their housing contract after the posted cancellation date Mailbox service $95 Annual fee for an on-campus PO Box provided via Mail and Copy Central Lost key / lock change $150 Residents who lose their room key and/or need a lock change Lock-out service $10 Should you misplace your room key, our staff is available to assist you by issuing a temporary replacement Damage charges Varies Damages identified upon check-out that are beyond normal wear and tear and that were not identified on the Room Inventory Inspection at check-in If you believe there has been an error, you can make an appeal related to damages or charges.

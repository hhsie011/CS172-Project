Youth Summer Sports Camp University Recreation Youth Summer Camp is exciting, fast-paced and engaging for all skill levels. The program includes a wide variety of activities that focus on ensuring active participation and an overall entertaining experience. We firmly believe the more active we are, the more fun we have. Learn more Youth Learn to Swim Texas Tech University Recreation is proud to offer the American Red Cross Learn-To-Swim program as part of our Aquatic Programming. The Learn-To-Swim Program begins with the basics of swimming and water acclimation (i.e. blowing bubbles, movement, etc..) and progresses through stroke introduction, development, and refinement, in addition to diving and other deep-water skills (i.e. treading, survival swimming). The American Red Cross Learn-to-Swim program also includes valuable tips, tricks, and practices that will create a respect for and recognition of how to safely enjoy the aquatic environment.

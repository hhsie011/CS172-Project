We have set a course to become a major national research university. To achieve that, we are recruiting top-flight faculty and providing cutting-edge opportunities for bright students while pursuing research, scholarship and creative activities. Texas Tech is a growing, dynamic university serving more than 37,000 students with academic curricula ranging from superb visual and performing arts programs to world-renowned wind science and engineering programs. Texas Tech is located in Lubbock, Texas, a thriving city of more than 250,000 people. Teaching, Learning & Professional Development The Teaching, Learning, and Professional Development Center (TLPDC) supports the university's commitment to excellence in teaching and learning. Available to faculty at all stages of their careers, the TLPDC offers assistance with teaching resources, state of the art classroom technology, academic integrity and ethics questions, and a variety of symposia and workshops. Also, the TLPDC houses the university's Teaching Academy, a long-standing entity dedicated to promoting the highest values of teaching and learning and to recognizing our most prominent faculty for teaching excellence. Research The Office of the Vice President for Research (OVPR) is committed to supporting faculty research, scholarship and creative activity. The OVPR offers a variety of faculty development opportunities and internal funding programs to promote faculty success in research and creative activities, and to assist faculty develop successful external funding proposals. The Transdisciplinary Research Academy demonstrates the university's commitment to bringing faculty with diverse backgrounds and expertise together to explore questions that have a global impact and intersect with the strategic research themes of the university. Service, Outreach & Engagement Texas Tech takes its mission of service, outreach and engagement seriously. From our emphasis on formally designated service learning courses to a robust study abroad program; from outreach and engagement programs designed to better the lives of those in our community to partnerships with groups, cities and industry around the world, Texas Tech is dedicated to being a great public research university where students succeed, knowledge is advanced and global engagement is championed.

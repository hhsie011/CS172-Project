This list will give you the call numbers, locations and status (available or charged out) for materials that are on reserves, and lead to links for electronic reserves, when available, to licensed e-journals, e-books, and streaming media. Course Reserves lists are also available on your instructor's CourseWorks page. More information about searching CLIO for Course Reserves. Problems with links If you are having trouble accessing e-reserves, ask for help at any reserves circulation desk or report the problem electronically via the LibraryWeb E-Resource Problem Report Form. Please remember to include your name, uni, instructor, course number and title of the reading in your report.

Current Students Current Students must login to InsideND and use the "Transcript Request" link to order their transcript. Current Student Request Alumni and Former Students If you participated in a certificate program, seminar, or workshop at the University, please contact the department offering the program for verification of enrollment as an official transcript is not available. Official transcripts are available for students who were admitted to the University of Notre Dame for a bachelors, law, or graduate program. There is reduced processing time for alumni signing into their myNotreDame alumni account to request a transcript. You may need to create a new myNotreDame alumni account if you are not already registered. Alumni Transcript Request Designated for previous students who did not complete a degree. You will need to create an account with Parchment, our contracted provider.

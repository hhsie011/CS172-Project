Sustainable Spaces Take the Pledge Calculate Your Commute Donate Reduce paper Green Your Event Take the Sustainable Choices Survey There are many ways faculty, staff and students can get involved with sustainability at the University of Washington. You can take the sustainability pledge, find out how your office or lab can be certified for its Green practices, organize a Green Team in your building or department, find resources on how to be a Good Dawg and act sustainably, and much more. The links below provide some ways to get started, and we also have more specific resources for students on our Student Experience page. For more information about sustainability efforts on campus, contact us. Pledge Pledge to take actions to further the UW's culture of sustainability.

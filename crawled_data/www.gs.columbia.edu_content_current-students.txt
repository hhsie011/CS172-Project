Types of Financial Aid Students at the School of General Studies may be eligible to receive scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study. Financial aid may come from GS, federal and state governments, or private sources. Applying for Aid Continuing students must reapply for both institutional scholarships and federal financial aid every year. Student Financial Services Student Financial Services (SFS) manages billing, payment and refund options for all Columbia students. Educational Financing Counseling Students who seek financial aid counseling, veteran education benefits counseling, and other aid-related topics are asked to make an appointment online. Academic Resource Center The Academic Resource Center (ARC) is dedicated to supporting the diverse nontraditional student body at GS in achieving academic excellence.

In addition, our Division is responsible for the strategic planning, management, and operation of non-academic University property, and collaborating with the neighborhood to create a safe, diverse, and economically vibrant destination. Quick Facts about our Division We maintain 218 buildings on a campus of 299 acres, excluding the Health System, New Bolton Center, and Morris Arboretum. Our Division is comprised of approximately 1,000 employees in five Departments. We complete approximately 60,000 maintenance requests per year. We care for approximately 6,500 trees, not including those in Kaskey Park (BioPond). We start approximately 250 projects a year, with over 1,000 projects open at any given time.

We are excited you have found an area of study that you are enjoying. As a declared major, you will work with advisors within your academic department to enroll, have your questions answered, and access important information and resources. Based on your major, select the advising center within the College of Liberal Arts & Sciences or corresponding academic school or department, such as School of Business, TRIO, or the Honors Program.

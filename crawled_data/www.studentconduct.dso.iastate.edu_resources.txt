Disciplinary Regulations (Code of Conduct) The Student Disciplinary Regulations is a document containing expectations for student conduct, as well as procedures for addressing and resolving alleged violations of university policies. If the student's behavior occured before February 15, 2018, this set of regulations applies to the student's behavior and resolution process. Title IX Guides for Complanants and Respondants Title IX Resource Guide Iowa State University's Title IX Resource Guide was specifically created to aid and educate students who may want to file reports of alleged dating violence, domestic violence, sexual assault, and stalking incidents. For complete information please click this link to access the PDF document: Title IX Resource Guide. Title IX Guide for Respondents Iowa State University's Title IX Guide for Respondents was specifically created to aid and educate student respondents who may be part of an investigation related to reports of alleged dating violence, domestic violence, sexual assault, and stalking incidents.

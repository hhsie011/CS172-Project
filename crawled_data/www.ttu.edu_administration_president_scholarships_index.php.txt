Nominated students will be notified on March 3, 2022, regarding the application process. 2022 Presidential Leadership and Globally Engaged Scholarship Nomination and Application Criteria: Nominators must be Texas Tech University (including regional campuses) faculty or staff members. Each faculty or staff member may nominate up to two (2) students. Nominated students must be enrolled as full-time undergraduate students at Texas Tech University (including regional campuses) and have at least a 3.00 GPA at the time of their nomination. Presidential Leadership Scholarship The Leadership Scholarship is awarded to individuals who are excelling in their studies, show involvement in extracurricular activities and demonstrate leadership qualities. Requirements: Minimum GPA of 3.0, Leadership Involvement at Texas Tech University, Extracurricular Activities (can be from Academics, Campus-related, Community Service, Cultural/Artistic, Recreation, Service Learning). Previous recipients of the Presidential Leadership Scholarship are not eligible to apply. Presidential Globally Engaged Scholarship The Globally Engaged Scholarship is awarded to individuals who wish to enhance their academic experience at Texas Tech by participating in study abroad, an internship, or conduct undergraduate research. Requirements: Minimum GPA of 3.0. The student must intend to participate in a study abroad program, internship program, and/or conduct undergraduate research. Previous recipients of the Presidential Globally Engaged Scholarship are not eligible to apply. Application and Award Information The 2022 Presidential Leadership and Globally Engaged Scholarship application period has closed. Award recipients, and those who were not selected this year to receive a scholarship, will be notified by early May 2022.

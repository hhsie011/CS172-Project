for: Graduate Certificate Application Process Graduate Certificate Application ProcessNabeel Sneij2022-04-07T16:24:05-04:00 The School of Communication offers a Graduate Certificate in Media Management (GCMM). Applications are accepted every fall and spring. The deadline for 2022 fall admission is July 15, 2022. The deadline for 2023 spring admission is December 15, 2022. Note that international students are not eligible for this part-time program. STEP 1 Online application and $85 non-refundable application fee. Sign up for a CollegeNet account online at the CollegeNet website. Please note: Your application is not complete and we cannot begin your application review until we receive all required forms. Your $85 non-refundable application fee can be paid when you submit the CollegeNet application online. STEP 2 A baccalaureate degree from an accredited U.S. institution. Send all official post-secondary (college or university) transcripts that you have attended to: Graduate Studies Office, University of Miami, School of Communication 5100 Brunson Drive, Suite 4002 Attention: Kesha Wimbley Coral Gables, FL 33146 STEP 3 One letter of recommendation. The letter of recommendation should be submitted online to CollegeNET. STEP 4 Resume. Submit your resume online through CollegeNet.

International Education Events Past Event Parents Sharing: To Provide Kids International Education Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo September 3, 2017 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing Event Intro On September 3rd, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing will hold a parents sharing event. Parent representatives of Columbia University and Stanford University's undergraduate students are invited to share their stories and experiences in how to provide their kids well-rounded education. In the meantime, Dr. Li Zhang, Columbia alumna and a scholar in project-based learning, and Ms. Hongling Peng, founder of Project-Based Learning Institute, will also attend the event and share their professional insights. Speakers Parent representatives of Columbia University and Stanford University's undergraduate students Dr. Li Zhang, Columbia University alumna, Doctor at the Helsinki School of Economics, scholar in project-based learning Ms. Hongling Peng, founder of Project-Based Learning Institute, publisher of PBL: Design and Coaching Guide Presenters Jiuge Qin, Undergraduate student at Harvard University Yixuan Shen, Grade three student at Beijing City International School OCATION AND TRANSPORTATION Columbia Global Centers | Beijing 哥伦比亚大学全球中心 | 北京 北京市海淀区中关村西区善缘街1号立方庭（西门）底商26号 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Upcoming Events Columbia Global CentersAmman / Beijing / Istanbul / Mumbai / Nairobi / Paris / Rio / Santiago / Tunis Follow Us Facebook Twitter Instagram Youtube Columbia University ©2022 Columbia University Accessibility Nondiscrimination Careers Built using Columbia Sites Back to Top Close

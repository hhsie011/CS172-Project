Amy Brandau 166C Heady Hall (515) 294-6147 abrandau@iastate.edu Future Students Majors Service Learning Meet Our Students Agricultural Business Do you have a knack for numbers and a love of the land? Then agricultural business is the perfect major for you. You can receive a unique blend of training in business, economics, and agriculture while preparing for professional careers in commodity merchandising, economics analysis, farm and agricultural production management, agribusiness management, agricultural finance, agricultural sales and marketing, natural resources, agricultural accounting and international agriculture. Prepare to be a leader in the bioeconomy of the future at the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences.

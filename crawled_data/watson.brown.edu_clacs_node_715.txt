All students are welcome to attend and participate in the DUG regardless of concentration. This year’s DUG leader is Heidy Mejia-Puerta '22. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact them at heidy_mejia-puerta@brown.edu. Heidy Mejia-Puerta '22 (she/they) is a senior from Hermiston, Oregon who is studying Education and Latin American and Caribbean Studies.

Douglas and Caroline have been mixing up spectacular cocktails in the Asia-Pacific region for over a decade. In this workshop, you will learn what it takes to make a great cocktail, from the variety of ingredients to ice, skill, and even house rules: Learn and make 3 classic cocktail recipes and how to adjust them based on your ingredients. A brief introduction into the variety of gins, whiskeys, bitters and cordials, and how they are used in cocktails. A welcome drink and takeaway cocktail for you to enjoy back home! No experience or tools are needed; all ingredients and instructions will be provided. Please email Michael Novielli at [email protected] to reserve your seat ASAP. Date and time: Saturday, February, 3-5pm Address: 8 Bukit Pasoh Rd, Singapore 089822 Costs: $108+ for CAA members; $138+ for non-members.

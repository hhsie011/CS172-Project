FAQ Contact M.S. in Physiology The Master of Science in Physiology degree program, with a pre-health concentration, is designed for students who wish to enhance their credentials for admission to professional healthcare programs. Additionally, a research concentration is available for students interested in developing competence in research for advanced technical positions in industry, government, university medical research laboratories. The M.S. in Physiology program has a strong emphasis in concepts of human physiology, consisting of thirty (30) semester hours, which may be completed over a twelve-month period (3 continuous semester terms) period. For more information on admission requirements, application processes, and curriculum, visit the M.S. Physiology program webpage. M.S.

Sarah is a serial social entrepreneur and the co-founder of six ventures in K-12 education including MOUSE.org and Computer Science for All (CSforAll). She sits on the Board of multiple nonprofits and startups including Mission: Restore, Benefit Kitchen, Visit.org, Five One Labs, and CSforALL. Sarah has a Bachelor’s Degree from Bowdoin College and a Master’s in Public Management (MPA) from SIPA.

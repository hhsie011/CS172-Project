Health Promotion Counseling and Psychological Services Disability Services Gay Health Advocacy Project (GHAP) Insurance Office Medical Services Sexual Violence Response Columbia Health You are here: Home News Dr. Michael McNeil, Chief of Administration for Columbia Health, included in Inside HigherEd article Dr. Michael McNeil, Chief of Administration for Columbia Health, included in Inside HigherEd article By Columbia Health April 25, 2022 Dr. Michael McNeil, Chief of Administration for Columbia Health and Professor of Sociomedical Sciences at the Mailman School of Public Health, was interviewed for an Inside HigherEd article about rolling back recommendations to wear face coverings indoors. The article, "Reversing Course on Mask Mandates" reads in part: “If we continue to do what we know, which is to use empirical evidence to inform our decision making, if we continue to listen to and be responsive to our populations, and if we continue to act individually and collectively, we can see our campuses through to the other side,” says Michael McNeil, the Chief of Administration at Columbia Health.

The Baccalaureate Service, with roots in medieval academic tradition, honors the achievements of the candidates for the bachelor’s (“bacca”) degree by presenting them with the laurels (“lauri”) of oration. 2022 Baccalaureate Speaker The 2022 Baccalaureate address will be given by public health leader Dr. Seth Berkley '78, '81 M.D., CEO of Gavi, the Vaccine Alliance. Learn More About the Baccalaureate Service Brown University’s Baccalaureate Service embraces lively expressions of thanksgiving, harmony and rhythm and includes music, dance and spiritual readings.

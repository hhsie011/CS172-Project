Research A Partner in Research and Innovation A Partner in Research and Innovation Rutgers has the resources, talent, and research expertise to make innovation happen and bring big ideas to life. Explore what we have to offer and how your organization could benefit from a partnership with The State University of New Jersey. 1,000+ Companies engaged in research at Rutgers 2021 data 1,000+ Active issued worldwide patents 4,151 Research awards 2021 data $145 Million In industry-sponsored research 2021 data Big Resources for Big Ideas Rutgers' 300-plus research centers and institutes are the largest network of research labs and facilities in New Jersey. Rutgers is ready to work with industry, entrepreneurs, small businesses, the public sector, and nonprofits to develop new ideas and products with local and global impact. Top 25 Public Research University Rutgers offers a wide variety of services that drive and support research, discovery, and economic development. Research teams and innovators with great ideas will find many commercialization pathways to bring inventions from concept to the marketplace. Rutgers University–New Brunswick is a top 25 public university as ranked by U.S. New & World Report. Research at Rutgers University–New Brunswick Through a robust network of experts and facilities, researchers can transform ideas into new technologies, services, and products strengthened through critical partnership and commercialization opportunities. Research at Rutgers University–Camden Rutgers–Camden's status as a Doctoral University: High Research Activity institution by the Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education reflects the university’s growing national stature as a center for high-impact research and transformative graduate education. Research at Rutgers University–Newark Our centers and institutes help create new knowledge in a broad range of academic disciplines and share academic and intellectual resources with the residents of New Jersey and beyond. Research at Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences Rutgers drives education, medical breakthroughs, and economic development while improving lives at home and in the global community. Research Centers and Institutes Spotlight In clinics and centers, in labs and libraries, in the field and across the globe, our faculty and students create knowledge, advance economic progress, improve lives, and enrich our humanity. Take a look at some of the Rutgers centers and institutes making an impact. Eagleton Institute of Politics Eagleton is a leading source of scholarly research and data about politics and government, with fellowships and research opportunities available for Rutgers undergraduates and graduate students. Eagleton is also home to the Center for American Women and Politics and the Center for Public Interest Polling. Institute of Jazz Studies The largest and most comprehensive library and archive of jazz and jazz-related materials in the world, the center's archives include photographs, recordings, and memorabilia of noted jazz artists. Rutgers Cancer Institute of New Jersey As New Jersey’s only National Cancer Institute-designated Comprehensive Cancer Center, Rutgers Cancer Institute of New Jersey’s team of internationally recognized physicians and researchers is driven by a singular focus and mission, to help individuals fight cancer. Rutgers University Cell and DNA Repository (RUCDR) As the world’s largest university-based biorepository, RUCDR Infinite Biologics has been perfecting the science of biobanking, bioprocessing, and analytics since 1999. Senator Walter Rand Institute for Public Affairs From its base at Rutgers University–Camden, the institute addresses public policy issues impacting southern New Jersey through applied research, community engagement, and organizational development. Center for Computational and Integrative Biology An interdisciplinary research center for bridging experimental and computational approaches in quantitative biology.

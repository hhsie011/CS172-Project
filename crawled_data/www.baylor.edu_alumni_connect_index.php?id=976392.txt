Louise Herrington School of Nursing Clinical Professor Dr. Jessica Peck discussed promoting holistic health during the pandemic and shared advice for parents to have meaningful conversations around these topics with children and teenagers. About our Speaker Jessica Peck, DNP, APRN, CPNP-PC, CNE, CNL, FAANP, FAAN Dr. Jessica Peck is a Clinical Professor at the Louise Herrington School of Nursing. She holds active national credentials as a Pediatric Nurse Practitioner, Nurse Educator, and Clinical Nurse Leader. Prior to her role at Baylor, she served as a Tenured Professor at Texas A&M Corpus Christi College of Nursing; Associate Professor of Nursing, University of Texas Health Science Center School of Nursing and Will Dean Bivens Singleton Professor of Pediatric Nursing, University of Texas Medical Branch. Her clinical experience includes: League City Pediatrics; Pediatric Consultants, Clear Lake Regional Medical Center and UT Physicians Bay Area Pediatrics. About the Baylor Alumni Nursing Group The Baylor Alumni Nursing Group is an organization of former Louise Herrington School of Nursing students supporting each other, current LHSON students and the University they love. Regardless of when you graduated, LHSON Alumni Group invites you to become a part of this growing community. Contact Christie Harper for more information. Proceeds from this event benefit current LHSON students through a scholarship fund. If you're able, please consider making a gift today.

To submit an obituary or remembrance, please send link or text to news@yale.edu. John M. Merriman, French historian, beloved teacher A prodigiously productive scholar and popular and admired teacher at Yale, Merriman died on May 22 after a long illness. He was 75. Anton Sovetov, graphic designer, dies at 44 Anton Sovetov, a versatile graphic designer in the Office of the University Printer has died. He was 44. Steven M. Southwick, M.D., 1948–2022 Southwick, one of the world's leading experts on psychological traumatization and human resilience, died on April 20 at the age of 73. Thomas H. Troeger, 1945–2022 A beloved member of the Yale Divinity School faculty and a prolific and gifted author, hymnist, and speaker, Troger died last week at the age of 77. Sidney Altman, pathbreaking scientist Sidney Altman, who won the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1989 for discovering the catalytic properties of RNA, died April 5. He was 82.

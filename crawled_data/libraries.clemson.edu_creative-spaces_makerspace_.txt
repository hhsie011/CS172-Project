Cooper Library. Our normal semester hours are 9 a.m. – 5 p.m. Check our website for updates and hours, and subscribe to Canvas notifications for notifications. Getting Started If you’re new to the Clemson Makerspace, you’ll need to be certified before using the machinery. Sign up for the Makerspace course in Canvas in order to get started and certified. Already completed the online training? Reserve the laser engravers, button maker, or pen tablet.

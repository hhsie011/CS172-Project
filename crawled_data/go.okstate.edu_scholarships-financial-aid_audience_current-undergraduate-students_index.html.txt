File the FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid), apply for additional scholarships and check back often. We can't wait to see you cross that stage! FAFSA It's free, so why not fill it out? You will want to file the FAFSA every October 1. FAFSA Scholarships Looking for additional scholarships, let us help you out. We have listed the links to College/Departmental scholarships as well as outside scholarships. Take a look, do your research and apply by the deadlines! Current undergraduate scholarships Eligibility In order to receive the most financial aid, you must meet eligibility requirements. Take a look and make sure you are on the right path to graduate when it comes to financial aid. Eligibility requirements Update enrollment Life happens and you may need to change your enrollment, just make sure to let us know because it can change your financial aid. Update enrollment Adjustments to cost of attendance You may request a cost of attendance adjustment to reflect a certain classification or benefit you are receiving. See if any adjustments apply to you. Adjustment to cost of attendance Summer financial aid Summer semester works a little different for financial aid. You have to notify us that you are enrolling for summer courses, and we will review your financial aid eligibility. Do it early, don't wait. Study abroad Scholarship & financial aid deadlines for current students Oct 1-File your FAFSA The FAFSA application opens October 1 for the following academic year. We recommend filling it out as soon as possible. Deadlines Vary-Additional OSU scholarships Did you know your academic college offers scholarships? They do! Their deadlines vary, so make sure to check with them on when you need to get that application turned in. Check out the additional OSU scholarships page to find direct links to your college's scholarship information.

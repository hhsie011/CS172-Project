Each fall, Tulane hosts information sessions in cities throughout the country—and in several cities worldwide—for interested students and their families. Tulane representatives also host sessions throughout the United States as part of the Metropolitan Universities Tour. These programs are great opportunities to meet with your counselor, hear more about Tulane, and ask questions about the application process. Virtual Events We also host several other virtual programs, including panels, live-streamed tours, and workshops. Click here to look for more virtual events.

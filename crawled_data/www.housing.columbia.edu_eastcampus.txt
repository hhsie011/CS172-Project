East Campus provides convenient access to campus and neighborhood restaurants and is a popular choice for Seniors. Data from Room Selection 2021 shows the make-up of East Campus as 72% seniors, 24% juniors, and 4% mixed-point groups (average point value of mixed-point groups was 22). Please keep in mind that class make-up in each building varies year to year based on student preferences. This should not be used as predictive data for the upcoming academic year.

(Due to the holiday break, fall diplomas will be ready approximately eight to ten weeks.) The following includes the anticipated dates diplomas will be printed and mailed for each term: Spring 2022 Summer 2022 Fall 2022 June 17, 2022 September, 2022 February 10, 2023 It bears your name, graduation date, degree, primary major, degree honors (ex: graduation with distinction or Honors College degrees), the university's seal and official signatures. Second majors, options, and minors are not reflected on the diploma but will be shown on your official transcript. All transcript holds must be cleared before you receive your diploma. Login to Self-Service to view your holds (under "Student Profile"). Diplomas are distributed according to the mailing address you indicated on your graduation application and available via USPS or express shipment. Diplomas requiring express or international mail delivery require use of the eShipGlobal mailing service. Visit https://study.eshipglobal.com for mailing instructions. You can review your diploma delivery request by viewing your submitted graduation application in Self-Service. Students may change their diploma mailing address by submitting a completed Diploma Release Information Form to registrar@okstate.edu. Undergraduates can earn a level of distinction based upon their final cumulative graduation/retention GPA. The level of the distinction is based on the following criteria: Cumulative Graduation/Retention GPA Distinction 3.90 to 4.00 summa cum laude 3.80 to 3.89 magna cum laude 3.70 to 3.79 cum laude GPA calculations to determine these levels of distinction use two decimal places only and do not round up (ex: a GPA of 3.689 does not qualify for the cum laude distinction). Students receive one diploma per earned degree at OSU. A single degree with two majors results in one diploma which lists the primary major only. Second majors, options, and minors are reflected on the official transcript. The sizes of Oklahoma State University’s diplomas are as follows: Bachelor's Degrees 8.5 inches x 11 inches Master's Degrees 8.5 inches x 11 inches Doctoral Degrees 14 inches x 17 inches To reorder a diploma, you will need to complete a diploma reorder request. The cost of replacement diplomas is $50. To reorder, select one of the following: Current & Former Students with an Active O-Key Account Login to Self-Service Select Student Records from the Student tab Select Request Duplicate Diploma and complete the order Former Students without an Active O-Key Account Former students may order a duplicate diploma through the Alternate Reorder option. Create an account and complete the order process.

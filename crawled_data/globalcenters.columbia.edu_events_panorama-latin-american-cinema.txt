Events Past Event A Panorama of Latin American Cinema Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo March 10, 2017 - March 14, 2017 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM Mumbai 4 day film lecture series Presented by the Dr. Bhau Daji Lad Museum in collaboration with Columbia Global Centers | Mumbai and Conducted by Richard Peña, Professor of Film Studies, Columbia University and former director of the New York Film Festival. Venue: Education Centre, Dr. Bhau Daji Lad Museum, Veer Mata Jijabai Bhosale Udyan (Rani Baug), 91/A, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Road, Byculla East, Mumbai 400027 Contact: 022 - 23731234/ 23741234 10th March, Friday: 05.00 pm - 08.00 pm 11th March, Saturday: 10.30 am - 01.30 pm; 03.00 pm - 06.00 pm 12th March, Sunday: 10.30 am - 01.30 pm 13th March: Holi Holiday* 14th March, Tuesday: 05.00 pm - 08.00 pm Each session will have a lecture by Richard Peña, followed by the film screening and Q&A. All films will be screened with English subtitles. This is a free programme. Museum entry ticket is applicable. Seating will be on a first-come, first basis each day.

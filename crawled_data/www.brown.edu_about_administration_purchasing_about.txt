These agreements provide discounted pricing on the most commonly ordered goods from suppliers. Utilizing the suppliers that have agreements in place maximizes the value of the dollars spent and provides enhanced opportunities for the University to use economies of scale in pricing and services negotiations with external suppliers. SPCI can help departments in making informed purchasing decisions by: Providing information about products, services, and University-wide agreements Recommending suppliers who can best match departmental needs Negotiating contracts and pricing agreements Identifying buying cooperatives and other group purchase opportunities for cost savings Providing information about purchasing policies and procedures Managing and coordinating bid processes SPCI is also responsible for: Developing, interpreting, and communicating purchasing policies Approving purchases when central approval is required by policy Handling the dispatching of all Purchase Orders to Suppliers Maintaining the University’s supplier data including the Supplier Registration process Collecting, reviewing and maintaining certificates of insurance from Suppliers Review and approval of contracts for the purchase of goods and services Home SPCI Process Improvement Projects (BCM, BrownBuys, Supplier Onboarding) Staff About Purchasing Standards Strategic Suppliers Strategic Sourcing Uniform Guidance Overview Ordering Goods and Services Contract Review Conducting Business with Brown University Posted Bid Opportunities Brown's Property Insurance Office Forms Policies Print this Page Share this Page Strategic Purchasing, Contracts & Insurance Brown University Box 1845 Providence, RI 02912 Phone 401-863-2206 [email protected] Brown University Providence, Rhode Island 02912, USA Phone: 401-863-1000 Maps & Directions / Contact Us / Accessibility © 2022 Brown University Connect: [email protected] Facebook Twitter YouTube iTunes U LinkedIn SnapChat Resources A to Z Index Career Opportunities Make a Gift People Directory Related Offices Insurance Controller's Office

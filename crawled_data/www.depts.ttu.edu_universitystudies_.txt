Our Mission To develop diverse and highly skilled, civically minded lifelong learners that will be globally engaged to solve complex problems. Our Vision University Studies engages students in a highly recognizable, valued, and flexible learning experience in order to advance and connect their academic and community knowledge. The department is committed to developing lifelong learners who have a civic identity and operate as catalysts for societal change. About Us University Studies is a customizable and flexible interdisciplinary learning experience.

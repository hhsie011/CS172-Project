Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Graduate Center Menu Search The Graduate Center is a facility dedicated to Texas Tech Graduate students and Postdoctoral Fellows. The Graduate Center is host to numerous support services and facilities that include a computer lab, meeting space and quiet study areas. Location Weeks Hall, Room 319 2508 15th Street Fall Hours of Operation Monday - Thursday 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. Friday 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. Saturday - Sunday - Closed Statistical Consulting The mission of the Statistical Consulting Service is to help Texas Tech graduate students and postdoctoral fellows with all aspects of statistical data analysis. While our consultant will not decide how to analyze your data or conduct statistical analyses for you, we can help you select the optimal analytic strategy and locate resources to carry it out. We can help you avoid analysis issues by offering suggestions on how to collect, input, and manage your data. Library Research Assistance Assistance in conducting research, locating information, and citing sources. Help identifying the best sources and developing effective search strategies. Referrals to Library experts in various subject areas. Answers to questions about Library services and policies.

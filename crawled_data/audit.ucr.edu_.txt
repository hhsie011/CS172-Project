Charter The University of California Internal Audit Management Charter provides the foundation for the Internal Audit functions, authority, independence, scope of operations, and standards for professional responsibilities; UCR Campus Policy 250-30 includes the objectives of UCR Audit & Advisory Services (A&AS), a restatement of the authority granted to the A&AS in order to achieve its objectives, the responsibilities that accompany that authority, and the organization of the Audit & Advisory Services.

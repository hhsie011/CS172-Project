Recordings Give to CSREA Race & Series Fellows Program The purpose of the CSREA Fellows Program is to provide space and community to further research and build a stronger community and intellectual networks within and beyond Brown. Our Fellows community includes: Brown Faculty Fellows Visiting Faculty Fellows Postdoctoral Fellows Graduate Student Fellows Brown Practitioner Fellows Practitioner Fellows Brown University Faculty Fellows, Visiting Faculty Fellows, Postdoctoral Fellows, Graduate Student Fellows, Brown University Practitioner Fellows, and Practitioner Fellows meet regularly throughout the academic year as part of the CSREA Research Fellows Seminar or Practitioners Workshop, where they share research and new works-in-progress. In addition to creating intellectual synergies, these seminars aim to create deeper cross-generational/cross-rank mentorship and dialogue. Fellows are also encouraged to participate in the annual Race at the Center of the Humanities program or a similar event to present projects to a wider audience.

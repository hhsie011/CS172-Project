the Academy Conference Home / Diversity / Diversity Programming Diversity Programming Embracing Diversity, Developing Leadership, and establishing a legacy of Excellence Diversity Programming aims to promote a socially inclusive environment that nurtures excellence through equal opportunity and respect for everyone in the college. We seek to enhance student, faculty, and staff interaction, and sensitivity to dimensions of social inclusion such as race/ethnicity, nationality, gender, social class, sexual orientation, and disability.

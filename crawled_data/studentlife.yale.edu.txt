Yale’s commitments to teaching and student engagement are distinctive, as too are its activities that prepare and assist students to take full advantage of their educational programs and of the extraordinary strengths and assets of the university. University-wide support for student life includes: Belonging at Yale, fostering a more diverse, inclusive, and welcoming campus; Yale Connect, an engagement platform for student organizations; Yale Votes, voter registration and polling information; and Yale Well, resources for student wellness.

It is also an affiliate of Clemson University and the Clemson University Foundation. It has its own independent Board of Directors and president. The Clemson Real Estate Foundation, Inc., (CUREF) seeks to accomplish four goals for Clemson University: Advance the University through gifts of real estate and property Serve as the efficient conduit through which gifts of real estate and property are received and liquidated Participate in the stewardship of real estate in support of the University Assist and advise any college or department which includes a concentration in real estate as part of its academic offerings For more information: Jason Przybyla, RPA Manager of Real Estate Operations 864-656-5660 jprzyby@clemson.edu CU Real Estate Foundation Board of Directors Property for Sale Timberlands Legacy Program Commodities for Clemson Legal Documents and Financials Policies and Procedures Contact Us Clemson Forever Links Clemson Forever Ways To Give Greatest Needs Impact of Giving Donor Recognition Related Links Methods of Giving Make an Online Gift Alumni Association IPTAY CU Land Stewardship Foundation CU Real Estate Foundation Home Admissions Campus Life Academics Visit Outreach Research About Apply To Clemson Undergraduate Graduate Professional Online Education Employment Opportunities Policy/Terms of Use Text Version Web Accessibility Ethics/Safety Hotline Privacy Policy Site Information Copyright Â© 2022 Clemson University Clemson, South Carolina 29634 864-656-3311 Contact Clemson Stories for the Clemson Family Clemson University on Facebook Clemson University on Twitter Clemson University on LinkedIn Clemson University on Flickr Clemson University on Instagram

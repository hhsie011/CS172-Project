The Annual Research Meeting (ARM) is the gathering place for individuals leading the charge to transform delivery systems and health care in a rapidly changing landscape. Over the course of this three-day conference, more than 2,500 individual attendees work together to share and strengthen the evidence needed to inform the decisions that affect the health of individuals and communities. The 2018 ARM took place in Seattle's beautiful Washington State Convention Center with the annual Brown Reception - jointly hosted by the Department and the Center for Gerontology and Healthcare Research - taking place on Pier 70. HSR Award Winners The Department congratulates 2018 AcademyHealth Interest Group Award winners Shekinah Fashaw, Margot Schwartz, Amit Kumar, and Chanee Fabius.

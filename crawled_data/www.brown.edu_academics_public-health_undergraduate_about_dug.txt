The DUG provides opportunities for undergraduates to organize and/or participate in student-run events and to become involved in the SPH community. The membership consists of interested undergraduate PH concentrators. All DUG events are open to all concentrators. Follow us on Facebook! Please join us! To join the DUG or learn more about our activities/events, please contact us at [email protected]. We look forward to hearing from you or meeting you at our next event!

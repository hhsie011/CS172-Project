How to Make a Tribute Gift Give online, indicating the individual's name in the form when requested Give Now Online >> Call 806.742.2211, making sure to mention you wish to give in honor of the individual and providing their name Mail a gift by check, making the check payable to the Texas Tech Foundation, Inc., include the individual's name and/or the name of the fund to direct the gift in the memo line, and sending to: Texas Tech University System Financial Services Box 45025 Lubbock, TX 79409-5025 What Will the Gift Support? A tribute gift can be directed to any fund of your choosing while honoring the individual. Some donors choose to give to a fund or area meaningful to the honoree. Sometimes there might already be a fund established in the honoree's name, which can be found using the search function in the online giving form. Funds may also be recommended by the honoree or their family. Creating a Fund for an Honoree A number of alumni and friends create funds in recognition of an impactful individual. Funds have honored professors, parents, friends, children, and more. These may take the form of endowments or current-use funds and can support scholarships, professorships, programs, or capital projects like naming benches, rooms, and beyond. Contact us to learn more >> How to Recommend Gifts be Made to a Fund as a Tribute In lieu of gifts or flowers for a celebration or memorial service, some families instead ask for gifts to be made to Texas Tech or a certain fund in honor of an individual. Any of the contact information above may be included. For additional assistance, contact the Office of Advancement.

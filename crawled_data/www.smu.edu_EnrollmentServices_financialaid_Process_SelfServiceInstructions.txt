Select the Financial Aid year for which you wish to view awards. The most current Aid Year is selected by default. Choose the “New” tab to view offered aid. Within each award, select the action you wish to perform, Accept or Decline. You may Accept/Decline multiple awards at one time. Some awards may have the option to be reduced. If so, you will be allowed to edit the amount shown in the Accepted Amount box. To save the Accept/Decline actions, click “Submit” at the bottom of the page.

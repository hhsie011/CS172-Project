Initiative All-City Ensembles fall 2020 All-City @ Home In place of the All-City Ensembles, we are pleased to introduce All-City @ Home. learn more Close In collaboration with the New Haven Public Schools, the Music in Schools Initiative runs All-City Ensembles throughout the school year. All-City Ensembles provide tuition-free performance opportunities for students beyond the activities they have in their schools. The All-City band, choir, and string ensembles are led by a collaborative team of Teaching Artists from the Yale School of Music and music educators from the New Haven Public Schools. programs All-City Bands view schedules Toggle The All-City Bands are open to all New Haven Public Schools students in grades 4 through 12 who play a brass, woodwind, or percussion instrument. The All-City Bands rehearse weekly during the school year (October through May) at Neighborhood Music School. Family and friends are invited to the Yale School of Music for the Winter Concert in December and the Spring Concert in May. Rehearsal location: Neighborhood Music School (100 Audubon St., New Haven, CT, 06511) 2019-2020 Conductors José Lara (John S. Martinez School) Myles Ross (Worthington Hooker School) Jesús Cortés-Sanchez (Braeburn Elementary School/Charter Oak International Academy, Music in Schools Initiative alumnus) Rubén Rodríguez (director, Music in Schools Initiative-New Haven, Yale School of Music ’11MM) Aidan Zimmermann (Teaching Artist, Yale School of Music ’20MM) All-City Choirs view schedules Toggle The All-City Choirs are open to student singers in grades 3 through 12 in the New Haven Public Schools. The All-City Choirs rehearse weekly during the school year (October through May) at the Yale School of Music. Students’ family and friends are invited to the Yale School of Music for the Winter Concert in December and the Spring Concert in May. The All City Choirs also perform at the Yale Glee Club/NHPS High School Choral Festival. Rehearsal location: Hendrie Hall (165 Elm St., New Haven, CT, 06511) 2019-2020 Conductors Danielle Storey Carson (Wilbur Cross High School) Meaghan Sheehan (Betsy Ross Arts Magnet School) Jaminda Wasser (Wexler-Grant Community School) Stephanie Tubiolo (Princeton University Glee Club) All-City Orchestra view schedules Toggle The All-City Orchestra is open to New Haven Public School students in grades 4 through 12 who play violin, viola, cello, or double bass. The All-City Orchestra rehearses weekly during the school year (October through May) at the Yale School of Music. Students’ family and friends are invited to the Yale School of Music for the Winter Concert in December and the Spring Concert in May. Rehearsal location: Hendrie Hall (165 Elm St., New Haven, CT, 06511) 2019-2020 Conductor Natalie Jimenez-Lara (Ross Woodward Classical Studies Magnet School) Applications + Auditions learn more Toggle The initial application cycle ends on September 30, though students are encouraged to apply at any point throughout the year. After submitting an application, students will be invited to perform a low-pressure audition.

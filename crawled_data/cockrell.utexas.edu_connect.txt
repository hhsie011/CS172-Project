Education K-12 Outreach Giving Areas to Support Ways to Give Virtual Visit Contact Us Connect The Cockrell School thrives on connection. Our faculty and students collaborate on groundbreaking innovations, our industry partners help graduates transition into successful careers and our alumni and friends make it all possible through their dedicated support. We strive to continue to strengthen this interconnected community, and we couldn’t do it without you. By staying connected and giving back to Texas Engineering, you can help us improve quality of life and advance our society. No matter your interests or priorities, we offer a variety of opportunities to engage with the school and support your passions. Whether you are a donor making a gift, an alumnus volunteering your time or a corporate partner hiring students, we encourage you to get involved however you can. We also want to know how we can help you. Through career resources and networking events for alumni and research and recruitment opportunities for corporate supporters, we are fostering our graduates’ professional growth and connecting our partners with leading researchers and well-rounded young engineers. Together, we are pursuing bold ideas, solving complex problems and changing the world. We hope you will join us.

Resources like the Cornell Method of Note-Taking and SQ4R provide long-term reading and note taking strategies that will make test preparation a snap when it comes to finals week. The Exam Prep Countdown and will help you begin to organize your study plan and anticipate essay question responses. We hope these resources are useful, and recommend that you review our other pages to find additional time management plans and study resources!

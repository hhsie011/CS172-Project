Reflections on Afro-Asian Solidarity Events Past Event The Bandung Spirit Symposium: Reflections on Afro-Asian Solidarity Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo August 8, 2018 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Event Brief On August 8, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to the symposium, The Bandung Spirit: Reflections on Afro-Asian Solidarity. The program aims to revisit the lost legacy of Bandung and reflect on the future of our globalized world. Panelists Souleymane Bachir Diagne, Professor of French and of Philosophy, Columbia University Mamadou Diouf, Leitner Family Professor of African Studies, Director of Institute for African Studies, and Chair of the Department of Middle Eastern, South Asian, and African Studies (MESAAS), Columbia University Stathis Gourgouris, Professor of Classics, English, and Comparative Literature, Columbia University Lydia H. Liu, Wu Tsun Tam Professor in the Humanities, Director of the Institute for Comparative Literature and Society, Columbia University LI Tuo, Writer, Critic, and Adjunct Research Associate in the Department of East Asian Languages and Cultures, Columbia University QIAN Ying, Assistant Professor at Department of East Asian Languages and Cultures, Columbia University WANG Hui, Professor in the Department of Chinese Language and Literature and Department of History, Tsinghua University YIN Zhiguang, Assistant Professor, College of Humanities, and Director of the Global China Research Center, University of Exeter WEI Ran, Research Fellow at the Institute of Foreign Languages and Literatures, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences XIONG Ying, Research Fellow at the Institute of Chinese Literature, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences LIANG Zhan, Research Fellow at the Institute of Foreign Languages and Literatures, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences Agenda 9:30 - 10:00 Registration 10:00 - 10:15 Welcoming Remarks by Professor Lydia H. Liu and Professor Stathis Gourgouris 10:15 - 12:00 Session I. Bandung and Universalism Moderator: Lydia H. Liu Panelists: Souleymane Bachir Diagne, WANG Hui, Stathis Gourgouris 13:00 - 15:00 Session II. Rewriting World Literature Moderator: LI Tuo Panelists: Lydia H. Liu, XIONG Ying, YIN Zhiguang 15:30 - 17:30 Session III. Intellectuals and Cinema in the Third World Moderator: LIANG Zhan Panelists: QIAN Ying, WEI Ran, Mamadou Diouf 18:30 - 21:00 Film Screening and Discussion: Hyenas Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code of confirmation. Please present the QR code at the entrance. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of the registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

They engage in strategic community partnerships investigating customized, evidence based mindfulness-based interventions for local and global communities. Faculty in BSS-affiliated faculty conduct a diverse body of research on mindfulness interventions including: mindfulness-based stress reduction (MBSR), mindfulness-based cognitive therapy, mindfulness-based blood pressure reduction (MB-BP), acceptance and commitment therapy (ACT), and digital delivery of mindfulness training for behavior change such as smoking, overeating and anxiety.

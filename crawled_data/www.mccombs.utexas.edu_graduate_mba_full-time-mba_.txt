Full-Time MBA Full-Time MBA Your Journey Starts Here. The Full-Time MBA experience at the McCombs School of Business allows you to immerse yourself in learning, exploration, and developing meaningful relationships with your classmates. You'll take classes at Rowling Hall on The University of Texas at Austin campus, and the flexible curriculum gives you the option to specialize in one of 20+ concentrations, 14 of which are STEM certified.

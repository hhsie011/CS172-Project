and friends of Syracuse University to connect with each other and stay connected to our University. Whenever you graduated, we all share that Syracuse connection and love for Syracuse University. We hold game watches, a new student send-off and an annual community service project. If you’re willing to get involved with the club or have ideas/suggestions for upcoming club programs, please email us. Be sure to join our Facebook page, too. GO ORANGE!

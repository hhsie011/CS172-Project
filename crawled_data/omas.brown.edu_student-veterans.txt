New Students While all undergraduate student veterans arrive at Brown with the life experience of having served in the military, the application process by which you enter Brown will impact your arrival and transition process. Academics & Life at Brown The Open Curriculum will give you the right to choose and the freedom to be the architect of your own education. Financial Aid Brown's financial aid policies, combined with the Veteran Administration's Yellow Ribbon Program, makes a Brown education accessible and affordable. Peer Advisors Connects incoming student veterans and ROTC cadets with student veterans and students cadets already on campus. Brown University Student Veterans Society The Brown University Student Veterans Society (BUSVS) was formed in 2009 to help foster a community of support and awareness for student veterans and students interested in military service. Brown Veterans Network The Office of Military-Affiliated Students is working with colleagues across the university to provide an opportunity to network with student veterans at Brown.

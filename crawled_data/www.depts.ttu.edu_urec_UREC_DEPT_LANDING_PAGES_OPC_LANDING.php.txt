Trips We offer a wide ranging number of trips throughout the year; from local favorites such as Palo Duro Canyon to ski-trips up in Colorado. The Outdoor Pursiuits Program has something for you! Learn More Equipment Rental Information Planning your own adventure? Need equipment? The Outdoor Pursuits Center maintains one of the largest inventories of outdoor rental equipment in West Texas. The Outdoor Pursuits Center is located just outside the North Entrance of the Student Rec Center on the TTU campus. Learn More Bike Shop Most bike issues can be easily fixed, so don't miss out on commuting around campus because of a flat tire or other minor problems. Our student-run bike shop offers these services and more. Pricing is hard to determine without seeing what the bike needs. To get a free estimate bring your bike in and let our mechanics help you find the simplest solution. Learn more Climbing Wall Information Enjoy the natural elements inside the comfort of the Rec Center! The Climbing Center offers University Recreation members the opportunity to rock climb year round indoors while hanging high. The Climbing Center has over 4,000 sq ft of climbing, a 2015 boulder expansion, two-full length cracks, 10-foot roof section, 16 top-rope stations, 15 lead lines, and climbing routes for the beginner to the advanced climber.

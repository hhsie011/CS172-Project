DAS also maintains this website (accessibility.huit.harvard.edu). Meet the DAS team Upcoming trainings and office hours Digital Accessibility Liaisons (DAL) The network of DALs coordinates local efforts, facilitates training, and monitors progress. At least one DAL has been appointed to represent each school and administrative unit. View the list of DALs Browse the DAL Portal User Research Center (URC) The URC lab offers consultation in user-centered design and technology demonstrations, and hosts events on topics relating to user experience and accessibility. Learn more about the URC University Disability Resources (UDR) UDR provides leadership and guidance on university best practices, employee disability accommodations, planning accessible events, finding accessibility vendors, and consultation on physical access or renovations, while ensuring compliance with federal and state regulations.

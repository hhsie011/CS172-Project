Business Information and Technology (BIT) is the use of Information and Technology in an organization. As a student of BIT, you'll learn the best ways for an organization to meet its goals and business challenges. BIT students learn to analyze, design, develop, and implement information technology strategies that enable organizations to be more effective and profitable. You'll learn to manage large IT projects, analyze complex data sets, and design secure and efficient Information Systems. How Does a Degree in BIT compare?

Now more than ever, we need to be providing support to improve resiliency of those providing these essential components of care. Resiliency Based Clinical Supervision can provide a vital link to supporting staff and instilling practices that assist in retention and the overall professional wellness of those receiving this service. A new CEU Certificate in Resiliency Based Clinical Supervision from the University of Kansas provides the knowledge, skills, and practice opportunities needed to provide excellent clinical supervision for social workers and other helping professionals grounded in resiliency-based practices. This certificate program is designed for clinical social work practitioners and other behavioral health professionals. It consist of 24 CEUs and individual sessions can be taken without enrollment for a certificate. Session #2: Technology Enhanced Resiliency Based Clinical Supervision - 5 general CEUs and 3 ethics CEUs Session #3: Integrating Resiliency Based Clinical Supervision into Your Professional Practice - 5 general CEUs and 3 ethics CEUs Session #3: Integrating Resiliency Based Clinical Supervision into Your Professional Practice - 5 general CEUs and 3 ethics CEUs Cost: Individual sessions cost $120.00 for 8 CEUs - Must attend all four workshops in a session to receive 8 CEUs.

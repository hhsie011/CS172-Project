The Office of the Dean of Students is one of the primary areas within the Division of Student Affairs at SMU. This office oversees departments that provide essential services, support and programs to students, faculty, staff, and the University as a whole. Please feel free to explore our services and to learn more about what we have to offer. The Office of the Dean of Students is located on the top floor (L2) of the Hughes-Trigg Student Center, Suite 205. Select a link below, or click on About Us above for details regarding the functions of our office, and how to get in touch with us directly.

It proposes a detailed set of actions that will enable the University to achieve its greatest aspirations. Guided by its mission to transform lives through education, research, innovation, and service, the University of Miami will invest in its people—faculty, students, and staff— so they can mobilize the power of knowledge to better understand and address the most important challenges of our times. 2019 State of the University Address Our Aspirations Our Priorities Our Process ROADMAP HEADLINES 2019 State of the University Town Hall President Julio Frenk touted the University of Miami’s recent accomplishments during his second annual State of the University Town Hall address Sept. 12, 2019. “We have made great strides in becoming the institution we hope to be not only by our centennial, but for our 200th and 300th anniversaries,” Frenk said. The event also featured talks and performances by faculty and students. Watch a short video of the event or the complete archived broadcast.

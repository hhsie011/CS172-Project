If you would like to book an appointment with Fr. Edmund outside of office hours, you can email or call his cell phone to schedule. A Dominican friar, Fr. Edmund McCullough joined the OCRL & BRCC Staff in 2020. He grew up in Catonsville, Maryland and was ordained a priest in 2017. Before joining the order, he served as a FOCUS missionary at New York University and Ramapo College of New Jersey. Learn more about the Brown-RISD Catholic Community here.

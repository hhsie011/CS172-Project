The CLES family has expanded to include Special Education, Early Childhood Unified - MAT, the Graduate Certificate in Literacy, the Graduate Certificate in Mentoring and Coaching, and the Teacher Apprentice Program. With our new growth comes a new name that better reflects all of our programs - Intervention Services & Leadership in Education. Welcome to the ISLE! Intervention Services & Leadership in Education The Intervention Services & Leadership in Education (ISLE) department's vision is to prepare skilled, scholarly, and socially responsible professionals. ISLE offers several graduate degree programs in Counseling, Educational Leadership, Educational Psychology, School Psychology, Early Childhood Education/Elementary Education, and Special Education. The Department also offers non-degree graduate programs and programs for those seeking licensure as a Building Principal, District Superintendent, or Reading Specialist.

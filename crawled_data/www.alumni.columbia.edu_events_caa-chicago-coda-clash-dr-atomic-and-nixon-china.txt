Coda Clash is part educational, part entertainment, and entirety fun. Dan and Chris present their analysis of a composer and musical form around the evening’s theme. Through the use of argumentation, and audio and video clips, they try to persuade audience members to vote for their composition and presentation as the winner of the evening’s Coda Clash. Musical Compositions: Dr. Atomic and Nixon in China, John Adams Theme: Historical Events Coda Clash: Which opera most persuasively encapsulates history as an expressive term?

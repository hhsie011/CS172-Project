for: Alumni Spotlights Alumni Spotlightsbrackman2019-12-17T17:51:43-05:00 Being a ‘Cane does not end upon graduation. Our alumni form a network of leaders in their fields of study, backed by the SoC’s unique educational experience. They are employed in major TV networks, newspapers, government agencies, and many other notable national and international organizations. They are screenwriters, producers, publicists, journalists, filmmakers, advertising executives, and entrepreneurs who stay in touch with the school, participate in University events, and help connect students with career opportunities.

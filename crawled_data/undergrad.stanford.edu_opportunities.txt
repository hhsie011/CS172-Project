Here is a guide to some of the important offerings you should consider during your undergraduate career. Opportunities by Area Research and Independent Projects One of the best ways to sharpen your intellectual interests is through research or an independent project. Learn more about how you can take part in the Stanford tradition of inquiry, investigation, and discovery. Fellowships Fellowships and scholarships can provide you with financial support for undergraduate, graduate, and professional study, as well as internships, travel, and mentoring opportunities. Awards and Graduation Honors You can be awarded for your academic, research or writing excellence. Arts There are many opportunities for you to participate in arts communities during your time at Stanford, including fine arts, dance, drama, creative writing, music, media and film. Overseas & Off-Campus Stanford University offers you a variety of off-campus opportunities to enrich and diversify your undergraduate experience. Leadership Whether you believe leaders are born or made, Stanford provides numerous opportunities for you to develop leadership skills. Public & Community Service Stanford offers you a wide variety of public and community service opportunities, ranging from on-campus courses to off-campus research to community-based leadership projects. Diversity Outreach & Opportunities Diversity Outreach at Stanford offers internships, fellowships and exchange programs, as well as other unique opportunities. Graduate & Professional School Advisors and advising resources are available to you to help you understand general concepts about graduate and professional schools. Explore the opportunities available and discern which path is right for you. Stanford Undergrad is your guide to undergraduate academics and opportunities found within the Vice Provost for Undergraduate Education.

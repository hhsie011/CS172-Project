Start as a Freshman The College of Natural Sciences' award-winning Freshman Research Initiative has revolutionized the undergraduate experience. Over 1000 freshmen each year start working on independent research projects in faculty labs. Learn more about FRI » Research Opportunities There are ample opportunities to become active in research and related experiential learning on campus for students in the sciences. Learn more about getting involved by major » Summer programs allow students to work under the supervision of faculty mentors on research projects for 8 to 10 weeks. Funding is often provided. Opportunities are available both on the UT Austin campus and at host universities across the country. Learn more about summer research » Undergraduate Research Funding The College offers a number of funding opportunities for students doing research. We can also help direct you to many outside sources of funding for students engaged in research.

Gifts to scholarships, departmental excellence funds and many other areas of priority may be made in memory or tribute. Additionally, those who love Baylor have an opportunity to add their names to its landscape through a variety of campus memorial and tribute recognitions. Whether you wish to remember a loved one, honor a special individual or celebrate a momentous event in your life, campus landscape memorials and tributes offer you a way to leave your mark on Baylor. There are several options available for memorial or tribute donations or installations on campus. Click below to learn more or to purchase a campus memorial or tribute. Memorial and Tribute Funds Honor the legacy of a loved one through a gift to a memorial or tribute fund. Bricks and Pavers Make a lasting imprint through the purchase of a granite paver or an engraved brick.

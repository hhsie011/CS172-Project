Animation Orange Shorts: An animation studio dedicated to helping students to collaborate and create animated film shorts outside the classroom Comedy Zamboni Revolution: Syracuse's oldest improv comedy group performs several times a semester Dance Creations Dance Company: Founded as an African dance company 40 years ago, Creations has expanded its repertoire to include jazz, ballet, salsa/merengue, tap, modern, and hip-hop Dance Works: An audition-based dance organization that performs hip-hop, lyrical, jazz, ballet, tap, and contemporary styles of dance Kalabash Dance Troupe: A Caribbean and hip-hop group that promotes awareness of Caribbean culture through expressive dance ONEWORLD Dance: Dedicated to entertaining, educating, and inspiring the Syracuse and surrounding communities through African dance and music Otto's Empire Belly Dance Troupe: Celebrates multiple cultures associated with the art of belly dancing including Egyptian, Turkish, Greek, Lebanese, and more Outlaws Dance Troupe: Aims to bring a passionate and original hip-hop dance experience to all with a diverse collection of members of varying styles and backgrounds Raices: A Latin dance group committed to sharing the richness and diversity of Latino culture through music and dance Music Black Celestial Choir: BCC seeks to encourage, uplift, and minister to the student body and surrounding communities Black Legacy: A musical group that celebrates the classic sounds of Motown Cheon Ji In: A traditional Korean drumming group Groovestand: An award-winning a capella singing group that performs at various venues on and off campus throughout the year Holiday Ensemble at Syracuse University: A group that performs and raises money for various charitable organizations in the community Main Squeeze: A ladies' a cappella group known for its diverse musical styles and fun dance moves The Mandarins: Syracuse's premier all-female a cappella group Orange Appeal: A men's a cappella group dedicated to sharing a passion for music, regardless of major or minor Otto Tunes: A mens' a cappella group founded in 2011 Oy Cappella: A primarily Jewish, co-ed a cappella group performing contemporary music Sour Sitrus Society: Syracuse University's pep band Setnor School of Music Performing Ensembles : Instrumental and choral ensembles open to all Syracuse students Entertainment Programming University Union : The official programming board, which schedules major concerts, weekly cinema screenings, comedians, and speakers Theatre First-Year Players: The premier student-run musical theatre organization that revolves around giving non-drama major first-year and transfer students the opportunity to perform in an annual spring musical Black Box Players: Produces many different kinds of theatrical events, include fully produced plays, cabarets, festivals, workshops, play readings, and improv shows Merely Players: Seeks to create innovative and relevant performances of Shakespeare's works through the use of color-blind and gender-blind casting What Theatre: A student theatre troupe that produces and performs original and professional musicals and plays Syracuse Stage : The professional theatre of Syracuse, which presents several plays, classics, and musicals all year round Get involved Featured Stories Perfect Harmony Syracuse University’s choirs, choruses,

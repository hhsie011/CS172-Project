Preparation Proposal Preparation SPA is responsible for reviewing grant applications before they are submitted to the sponsor. SPA is the institutional authority to legally bind the institution in grants administration matters. Every department is assigned a SPA Project Officer, who will review grant applications for completeness and budget accuracy. The Project Officer will be the Signing Official. Further details and guidance for creating and assembling grant applications can be found in the Sponsored Projects Handbook.

Review student budgets Paying for college There are several sources of financial aid to help students pay for their college education. This aid can come from the federal and state governments, the University, private sources and more. Learn more about financial aid options below. Grants Grants are a form of financial aid from the federal government you typically don't have to repay, such as the Pell Grant. Learn about grants Scholarships Scholarships are a form of financial aid you don't have to repay and come from multiple sources, including the University and private organizations. Learn about scholarships Loans Student loans come from the federal government and private sources and is money you borrow and have to repay with interest. Learn about loans Work-Study Federal Work-Study is a program that allows students to earn money by working part-time on-campus and in other settings. Learn about work study Have questions?

CISI is the medical insurance provider selected by the USG for all students studying abroad. The CISI insurance coverage is mandated and included in the program costs for the duration of the program dates. CISI USG Policy Details Students planning to arrive before the program starts, or stay after the program ends, MUST purchase additional days of insurance through CISI . The current rate of $1.32 per extra day will be renegotiated and updated after August 1, 2022. This extra coverage will automatically be billed to the students via their Bursar account and will be based on the information they provided in the Arrival Questionnaire requested from them.

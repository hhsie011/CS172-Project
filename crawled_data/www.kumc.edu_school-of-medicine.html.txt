Read more Diversity The Office of Diversity and Inclusion (ODI) in the School of Medicine is dedicated to recruiting, retaining and promoting diversity and health equity among its students and faculty. We offer curricular offerings and educational programming to create and maintain a campus climate conducive to unlocking the potential of all and achieving educational excellence. Through these committed actions, KU will become a national leader in inclusive excellence and health equity by purposefully mirroring the communities that we aspire to partner with and serve.

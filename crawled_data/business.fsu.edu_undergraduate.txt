Scholarships Start your journey. While every student’s academic journey is unique and dependent on personal interests and goals, the Undergraduate Programs Office strives to provide all business students with accurate information regarding academic matters in a friendly and timely manner. College of Business academic advisors are available to assist students with developing and implementing academic planning to support them in attaining their educational and professional goals. Our advisors want to ensure students are aware of the resources and opportunities available to them at the college and Florida State University. You can schedule your appointment through Campus Connect by logging into your FSU portal (my.fsu.edu) and clicking on the CC icon. In Campus Connect, click on the blue "Get Advising" button and follow the prompts to schedule your appointment based on the type of service you need help with. We will continue using the Zoom virtual drop-in link, https://fsu.zoom.us/j/94301067670, for our drop-in hours of 9:00-11:00 a.m. You should access the link through your FSU account by 10:30 a.m. to be admitted and added to the queue to ensure you can be seen before we begin our appointments at 11:00 a.m. Remember, appointments are always best with very little or no wait time. Advising for Non-FSU Students Prospective students, who are not currently enrolled at FSU, should email the college’s Admissions Office at admissions@business.fsu.edu. Your email will be routed to an appropriate advisor, who can schedule an appointment with you. Can I be advised via email or via phone? If you have a brief question, there may not be a need to come to the office. Please send an e-mail message to ugprog@business.fsu.edu from your FSU email account or speak with an academic advisor at 850-644-3892. Due to confidentiality laws, we cannot discuss your academic records over the phone or through a non-FSU email account. Be sure to include your full name and phone number in the e-mail message. Your message will be answered promptly by an advisor. Please keep in mind, during peak times such as drop/add period and when the registration window opens, the office is very busy. To receive a prompt response it is recommended to stop by the office during advising hours.

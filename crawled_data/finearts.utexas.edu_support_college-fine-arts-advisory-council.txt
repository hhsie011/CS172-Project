APPLY GET INVOLVED Search this Website Search College of Fine Arts Advisory Council The College of Fine Arts Advisory Council is dedicated to the growth, development and excellence of the College of Fine Arts in the university and in the State of Texas. Specifically, the objectives of the College of Fine Arts Advisory Council are: To assist the college in fundraising and obtaining financial support for scholarships, faculty development, facilities and programs. To be an advocate for the College of Fine Arts and to increase interest in the Fine Arts, and particularly the work in the arts being done at the University of Texas at Austin. To advise the Dean of the College of Fine Arts. 2020-2021 Member Roster Caroline Schupbach, Chair Active Members Sissy Alcorn-Roberts Elizabeth Anders Sarah Aubrey Stephen Austin Joy Authur Bettina

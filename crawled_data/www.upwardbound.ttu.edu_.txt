Our goal is to provide the K-12 community with access points into higher education through programming and scholarship. We support the Division of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion's mission in promoting student diversity by exposing K-12 students to diverse cultural, economic, social, and academic backgrounds. Our students learn how to actively listen, communicate, and respect others; developing into upstanding leaders in their communities. College Connect programming includes: Coordinating on and off-campus visits for schools and non-affiliated Texas Tech University organizations Participating in Lubbock community events Hosting the Texas Alliance for Minorities in Engineering (TAME) area event Following up with summer participants and their interest in TTU Organizing residential summer camps News and Events Registration for our summer camps is now OPEN!

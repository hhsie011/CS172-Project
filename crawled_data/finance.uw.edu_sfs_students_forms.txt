Petitions Request waiver of tuition forfeiture fees Tuition forfeiture fees have specific guidelines or reasons where fees may be waived. Read the guidelines carefully before submitting your petition. Please complete this online tuition forfeiture petition and, if necessary for documentation, a Health Care Provider Verification Form. If you are unable to file an online petition, please contact our office at 206-543-4694 or sfshelp@uw.edu. NOTE: This form is used on the Seattle campus only. If you are a student at Bothell or Tacoma, please contact the Student Affairs Office on your campus for the proper forfeiture petition procedure. Health Care Provider Verification In order to consider a petition for a waiver of tuition forfeiture fees due to disability, the University of Washington, Seattle requires documentation from a licensed Health Care Provider verifying a condition that prevents the student from attending the University during the quarter petitioned. Please select the appropriate petition: Seattle Campus Bothell Campus Tacoma Campus Continuum College/Professional & Continuing Education Holds Removal Request Students with outstanding balances for tuition, aid repayments or housing charges may have a hold placed on their account restricting their ability to register or order transcripts. For some situations we may be able to lift the hold. Please complete the form to request your hold to be removed.

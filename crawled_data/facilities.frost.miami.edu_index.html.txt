Halls are available for student recitals, and community organizations are welcome to inquire about rental opportunities. Austin Weeks Recording Studio Recently, the studio underwent a million-dollar overhaul, making it the first of its kind to house three full-size integrated mixing consoles with digital and analog capabilities. The new control room boasts a 24-channelAvid S6 controller; a 32-channel all-analog API 1608 with Automation; and an SSL 948 Delta, a digitally controlled analog console.

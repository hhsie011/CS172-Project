Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index University Career Center Menu Search TTU University Career Center University Career Center Services What We Do: UCC Services The University Career Center (UCC) provides all services to undergraduate and graduate students as well as alumni from Texas Tech University. Students/Alumni can schedule face-to-face, phone, or video appointments. Our office is located in the Wiggins Complex. In order to take advantage of our services, login to your Hire Red Raiders account and make an individual appointment online. If you have any questions navigating the site, please call our office at (806) 742-2210. UCC services include: Document Critiques | Mock Interviews | Career Assessments | Career Exploration | Job Search | Webinars | Career Fairs & Events | Presentation Requests | Interview Room Reservations | Career Closet Document Critiques Students/alumni may bring their resumes, CVs, cover letters, personal statements, teaching philosophies, and research statements to the UCC to get professional advice on how to make it the best it can be. Mock Interviews Our staff can customize a mock interview to fit each individual's needs through one-on-one, panel, phone, and video mock interviews and will offer immediate feedback and tips for improvement. Career Assessments The UCC offers four online career-based assessments that can help students/alumni explore majors, minors, and career paths. Our staff is qualified to interpret assessment results and help students/alumni explore possible career options. Career Exploration Our professional career counselors are trained to help students/alumni from all majors and can help answer career-related questions, provide feedback, and clarify your next steps in reaching your career goal. Job Search Through the UCC, students/alumni have access to various online job board and job search tools where they can find details about job and internship postings as well as in-depth information about potential employers and industries. Webinars Each semester the UCC webinars provide information on various aspects of career development, including topics such as developing your professional documents, how to work a job fair, salary negotiation, and what to do once you have been hired. Career Fairs & Events Each semester the UCC offers a variety of free events to help students/alumni prepare for future careers. These events include career fairs, mocktail parties, etiquette dinners, resume critique days, and career exploration days. Presentation Requests Are you looking for a guest presenter to provide marketable skills training and professional development for your student organization? Request a University Career Center staff member to speak with your group about their career! Interview Room Reservation If you are needing a space for a career-related reason, please complete our Interview Room Reservation Request Form. Career Closet The Career Closet is open to current students in need of a business professional outfits for job and internship interviews and other professional development opportunities. There is no charge for this service. Login to your Hire Red Raiders account and schedule an appointment online. If you have any questions navigating the site, please call our office at (806) 742-2210.

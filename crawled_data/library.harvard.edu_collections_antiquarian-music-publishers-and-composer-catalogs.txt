The Loeb Music Library retains many catalogs we receive from antiquarian book dealers and auction houses offering musical items for sale. In addition, we preserve selected music publishers' catalogs. Accessing These Materials These materials are housed in the library's closed stacks and may be requested for use in the Isham Memorial Library. Inventories are available upon request. Contact Christina Linklater email: Email Phone number 617-496-3359 Related Collections Collection Isham Memorial Library Collection Isham Memorial Library A quiet room on the second floor of the Loeb Music Library, the Isham Memorial Library houses rare and unique music materials.

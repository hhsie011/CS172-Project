Traffic management is a proactive plan for influencing the flow of vehicles and pedestrians with the goal of minimizing delay or inconvenience. How many hours are spent on traffic management per year? Approximately 17,000 hours. Approximately half of these hours are spent on managing traffic for special events (OPAS, football games, etc.).

InclusiveVT Engagement and Outreach Research ready1 Beyond Boundaries In 2015, President Sands challenged the university community to imagine Virginia Tech’s role in higher education a generation into the future. The result — Envisioning Virginia Tech Beyond Boundaries — lays the foundation for the university’s strategic planning process, including the development of InclusiveVT, Destination Areas, VT-Shaped Learning, and the Partnership for an Incentive Based Budget.

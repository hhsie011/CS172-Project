Information Conference Center Home Meeting and Event Space Request Form Room Descriptions Rates Facility Use Policies Planning Your Event Campus Map OSU-Tulsa Conference Center The OSU-Tulsa Conference Center offers easy access, comfort and state-of-the art technology. OSU-Tulsa campus is located downtown, adjacent to the Greenwood Cultural Center and the Brady Arts District in one of Tulsa's most unique settings. Located off Interstate 244, this versatile facility is available for rental to businesses, organizations and individuals for meetings, conferences and teleconferences, all at attractive rates. Facilities include an auditorium, two banquet rooms, tiered lecture halls and break-out rooms. All rooms are enclosed to offer maximum privacy. For more information on our facilities and services, view our room descriptions and information about planning your event at the OSU-Tulsa Conference Center.

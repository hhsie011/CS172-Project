A listing of these violations is accessible via the following link: Report of Actual Findings of Violations of the Clemson University Student Code of Conduct by Student Organizations Formally Affiliated with Clemson University (PDF). Pursuant to the South Carolina Freedom of Information Act, additional information related to findings, sanctions, and organizational sanction completion may be available. Members of the public may obtain additional information that is not protected under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), 20 USC 1232g, by emailing their request to FOIA@clemson.edu.

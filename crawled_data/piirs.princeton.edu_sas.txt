students the opportunity to study the political, economic, social, cultural and religious institutions and practices of India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and Nepal. Considering South Asia as a region and not just a collection of nation-states, the Program also encompasses study of local and global circuits of connection and the South Asian diasporas.

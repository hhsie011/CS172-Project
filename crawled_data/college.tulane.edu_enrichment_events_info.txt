Home / Events / Informative and Networking / Information Sharing and Networking Information Sharing and Networking International Education Week A week of programming in celebration of the vibrant global community that has been cultivated on our campus. International Education Week is a joint initiative of the U.S. Department of State and the U.S. Department of Education and is an opportunity to celebrate the benefits of international education and exchange worldwide. Study Abroad Fair Each Fall we host a fair to showcase the opportunities that are available to students, while also highlighting the experiences of study abroad alumni, international students and visiting exchange students. Career Fair Employers from a variety of industries recruit for job and internship opportunities across all majors. Tulane Takeovers Multiple events throughout the semester across the country that connect alumni, parents and recruiters with our current students to create opportunities for networking and mentoring. Career Conversations An ongoing series with industry experts meant to spark conversation and help students identify careers of interest and learn from successful individuals.

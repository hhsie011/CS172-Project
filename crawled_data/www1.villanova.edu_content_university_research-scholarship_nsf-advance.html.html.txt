VISIBLE aims to empower academic leaders—in STEM and across all disciplines—through supporting faculty career progression, advocating for and enabling systems change for equity and building inclusive leadership practices. VISIBLE is supported by a National Science Foundation ADVANCE Institutional Transformation Grant. About VISIBLE Resources and Programming The VISIBLE Office provides resources and programming to help build academic communities where faculty are effectively hired, supported and advanced.

Related Links Apply to the MPAcc Program Graduate Course Catalog College Global Engagement Programs MPAcc Program Highlights 80% Avg. Pass Rate CPA Exam Over the last three years, our students have reported exam results, averaged across all four parts of the CPA exam, of approximately 80%, well above the reported national average of 55%. 93% Employment Rate – 3 year Avg Access to firms, companies and governments of all sizes allowing you to find the right employer to meet your career aspirations. Bachelor's-to-Master's Program Eligible Clemson undergraduates can take courses that will simultaneously count towards both your Bachelor's and MPAcc degree. Integrated CPA Review Students study for and are encouraged to take all four parts of the CPA exam prior to graduation. Concentrations Students can choose either a tax or audit concentration or mix and match elective classes to meet your educational goals. Study Abroad Opportunity During summer semester, students can participate in our study abroad program that concludes with two weeks of coursework at Magdalen College, Oxford University. Downtown Location All MPAcc classes are held in Greenville, the business hub of Upstate South Carolina. Our facility on Main Street puts you in the heart of a vibrant community surrounded by businesses, restaurants and beautiful green spaces.

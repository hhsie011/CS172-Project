See Outcomes Request Info Heider College of Business Menu Academics Undergraduate Graduate Community Connection News & Events About Alumni LessMore Links The Heider Mindset: A Higher Level of Business To us, you are much more than a business student. You’re an ethical leader in the making. Our proprietary Heider Mindset curriculum prepares you to enter the workforce in any economy: up, down, or—as is standard today—disrupted and thus primed for innovation. At Heider, we give you the knowledge and strategic frameworks you need to understand business clearly, creating intentional opportunities along the way for you to actually experience different career paths. 99% of our students are employed, pursuing an advanced degree or involved in a service program within 6 months of graduation. More than 80% of our students complete an internship (often more than one) in their time at Creighton. So whether your future plans involve landing a job, launching a venture or going on to graduate school, your success starts here.

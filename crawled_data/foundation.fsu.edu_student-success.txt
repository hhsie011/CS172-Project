It sets us apart and what drives us to do more. By giving our students the programs and resources they need to flourish, their success is our own, and we need to do everything we can to ensure these successes continue both now and long after they leave the university. Achievements like this don’t happen without help. That’s why we’ve identified five key, interconnected initiatives that—with your support—will create the hallmark of student success at Florida State University: EXPERIENTIAL LEARNING CARE PRESIDENTIAL SCHOLARS THE HONORS EXPERIENCE FACULTY SUPPORT We believe no matter a student’s chosen field of study, no matter the degree, success is available to everyone, and it all starts on the same path. Join us as we shape the future of student success.

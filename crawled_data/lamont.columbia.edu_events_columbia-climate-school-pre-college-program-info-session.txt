Our programs will expand your knowledge and prepare you to understand, analyze, and apply cutting-edge research to the complex problem of our changing climate. Our Pre-College program is for high-school students (Grades 9-12) who want to sharpen their knowledge and skills in the areas of climate change and sustainability. The programs will help participants build important skills, advance their learning outside of school, and prepare for the college experience. These workshops are excellent supplements to classroom learning and will help students develop important skills needed in the 21st century workforce. This info session is specifically for the Empowering You(th) for Climate Action workshop. Laurel Zaima of the Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, the instructor for the workshop, will lead the info session and answer questions about what learners can expect.

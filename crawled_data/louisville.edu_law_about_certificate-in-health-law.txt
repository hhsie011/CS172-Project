With input and insight from experts in the field, the curriculum for the certificate includes courses to enhance knowledge of the legal, regulatory and ethical issues related to the delivery of health care. Courses are taught by leading health law attorneys, top professionals and experienced faculty. The Certificate in Health Law: Provides the practical skills necessary to communicate effectively about the law and follow all regulations related to health care; Helps professionals better understand patient legal concerns; Is appropriate for hospital administrators, patient advocates, physicians, nurses and others looking to increase their knowledge of health law; Provides coverage of policy and regulations, health care/insurance coverage, health care providers, access to care, ethical choices and more; Touches on the broader areas of law, including contracts, administrative, employment and tax law. Designed for today’s busy professional, the certificate is offered online through an innovative distance learning format. All courses are self-paced, allowing professionals the flexibility to complete coursework and assessments as convenient. In order to earn the certificate, participants must complete nine of the offered courses. The certificate and courses are available at a 20 percent discount for UofL employees. In order to receive the discount, employees must use their university email and phone for verification. Contact healthlaw@louisville.edu for more details. The certificate is a non-credit, professional development certificate.

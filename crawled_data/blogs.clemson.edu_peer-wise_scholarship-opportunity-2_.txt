The application deadline is June 14, 2022. Please see the details in the attached flyer and application. Please distribute this information widely. Women In Defense Palmetto Chapter (WID PC) offers at least two scholarships to female students pursuing an undergraduate degree in a STEM discipline in a South Carolina college or university. One scholarship is reserved for a Service Member/Veteran/ROTC enrollee.

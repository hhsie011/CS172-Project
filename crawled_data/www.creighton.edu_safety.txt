Creighton is committed to keeping you informed and providing resources to ensure a safe and compliant community. Compliance and ethical conduct is everyone’s responsibility. Have an emergency? Omaha: 402.280.2911 Phoenix: Emergency: 911 Non-Emergency: 402.280.4400 If you’d like to report a non-emergency please fill out the “Tell Someone” online form (net ID and password required) or call the “Tell Someone” reporting line at 402.280.4400. For all other safety resources, please refer to the list below. Image Active Shooter Situations An active shooter situation occurs when an intruder enters a building or campus grounds and threatens the personal safety and welfare of our students, faculty, staff and guests. 402.280.2911 View Emergency Information Campus Security & Public Safety The Department of Public Safety is responsible for campus safety and security at Creighton. Public Safety is a private security force that operates 24 hours per day, seven days a week, with three shifts of officers who receive a variety of specialized training that includes first-aid, CPR and the use of firearms. The department is service-oriented and maintains a close working relationship with municipal, state and federal law enforcement agencies. 402.280.2104 Public Safety Website 2021 Annual Security and Fire Safety Report Chemical & Laboratory Safety The Environmental Health & Safety website provides tips for safer procedures in laboratories, including compressed gas safety, flammable material storage and waste disposal. 402.546.6400 Environmental Health & Safety website Compliance and Ethics The University has established help lines and related resources to report incidents and address questions regarding compliance responsibilities and ethical issues. An anonymous hotline is available to report activities that may involve serious misconduct, violations of laws and regulations, or University policies. View Compliance & Ethics Information Emergency Weather Information In the case of severe weather, it’s vital that all everyone in the Creighton community knows what to do and where to go. 402.280.5800 Twitter: @CreightonAlert Employee Assistance Program (EAP) Faculty, staff and their families can access Creighton’s EAP for confidential consultation on a wide variety of personal, family and/or work-related problems that may contribute to high levels of stress and interfere with health and work performance. 1.800.424.4831 Environmental Health & Safety The Environmental Health & Safety website serves as a gateway to Creighton’s Environmental Health & Safety department. Find policies and procedures, safety tips, training information and more. 402.546.6269 Environmental Health & Safety website Harassment & Discrimination The Office of Title IX and Civil Rights Compliance works to reduce obstacles and foster an environment that is welcoming and inclusive to all Creighton community members. You can report harassment and discrimination using the “Tell Someone” online form. 402.280.3189 Office of Title IX and Civil Rights Compliance On-Call Health Resources Contact Public Safety if you need a student health provider, counselor on duty, residence life, campus minister or a university responder. 402.280.2104 Public Safety Website Parking All vehicles parked on Creighton University property are required to display a valid university parking permit. For all questions related to parking, contact Public Safety. 402.280.2104 Parking Information parking@creighton.edu Personal Mobile Safety Device "POM" The personal mobile safety device or “POM” is a discreet handheld remote that enables students on and off campus immediately summon assistance from Public Safety or 911 to an exact geographic location in the event of an unsafe, uncomfortable or emergency situation. Visit the NEST to order your POM device.

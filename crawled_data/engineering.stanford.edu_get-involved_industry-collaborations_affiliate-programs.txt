Last year, hundreds of top companies reaped the benefits of membership in one of Stanford Engineering’s many affiliate programs. For more than 60 years these programs in the School of Engineering have provided an avenue for industry to get involved with Stanford departments and faculty. Corporate members can benefit from attending annual meetings and research symposia, receiving topical reports and publications, and conducting individual meeting with researchers. In some instances, member companies have opportunities to recruit students at events. From nanotechnology to AI and FinTech to photonics, these affiliate programs cover a wide variety of research topics and industry interests. Browse our list of affiliate programs to find the right one for your company.

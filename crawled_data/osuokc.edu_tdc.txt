Our mission is to improve the work-related skill levels of the workforce in our community. We have a host of partners from academia and industry who can serve as trainers or consultants for your particular needs. Our method of delivery includes contract training, seminars, non-credit classes and a variety of consulting services. We can design programs tailored to meet your company’s needs. We offer our campus as your location for training or consulting needs or will come to your organization and provide services onsite. Want to know more? Click on the links above or call 405.945.3383.

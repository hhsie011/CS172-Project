The training doesn’t just happen in Austin — medical students from around the world can access educational resources that equip them to transform health care. Opportunities Discovering Value-Based Health Care This set of interactive, online learning modules teach the foundations of value to all kinds of learners. Whether the learner is a longtime health practitioner or someone new to the concept, the free curriculum will impact their work. Choosing Wisely The Choosing Wisely STARS (Students and Trainees Advocating for Resource Stewardship) program convenes med students from around the country and activates them to lead change — starting in their own schools.

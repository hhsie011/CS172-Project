Practices in Early Childhood Education Events Past Event Beijing Panel: Theory and Best Practices in Early Childhood Education Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo July 20, 2018 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM Columbia Global Centers | Beijing, 26, 1F Core Plaza, 1 Shanyuan Street, Zhongguancun, Haidian District, Beijing Event Brief On July 20, Columbia Global Centers | Beijing invites you to a panel discussion: Theory and Best Practices in Early Childhood Education and Commemoration of Columbia Chinese Alumnus Mr. Heqin Chen, featuring Mr. Zhaohui Chu and Mr. Xiaowei Ke of China Education Improving Institute. Their focus is to provide all children in China with access to high-quality education and achieve the fullest development both physically and mentally. Speakers Zhaohui Chu, Chairman of China Education Improving Institute, Drafter of National Education Policy of China, Professor of Education Science of China National Institute Xiaowei Ke, Vice Chairman of China Education Improving Institute, Chen Heqin's grandson, member of the Chinese Writers' Association Baitong Yan, M.A. from Teachers College, Columbia University, founder of No-Boundaries International Art Exhibition, Chief Director of Children's Education Department at journey School, Long Island, former founder of an educational institution for children, former employee at Avenues: The World School Moderator Xu Zhu, Member of Board of Directors of China Education Improving Institute, Commentator of China Central Broadcasting Station, Senior Media Professional of China Agenda 3:30 p.m. – 4:00 p.m. Registration 4:00 p.m. – 4:30 p.m. Speech by Mr. Zhaohui Chu 4:30 p.m. – 5:00 p.m. Speech by Mr. Xiaowei Ke 5:00 p.m. – 5:45 p.m. Dialogue 5:45 p.m. – 6:00 p.m. Q&A Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to limited seats, registration will be verified through the QR code of confirmation. Please present the QR code at the entrance. Please plan to arrive no earlier than the registration time and no later than 20 minutes after the end of the registration period. If you are unable to attend the event, please inform us in advance.

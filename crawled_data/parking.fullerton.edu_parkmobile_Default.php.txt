ParkMobile Makes It Easy When you're on the go, the free ParkMobile app makes it easy to pay for parking at CSUF right from your phone. Once you set up your account, simply select your parking zone, vehicle, pay for the day or hour, and go. It's smart, contactless payment in the palm of your hand! Download The App ParkMobile is free to download and available for iPhones and Androids.

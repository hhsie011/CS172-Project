Global Centers | Tunis Tunis OSTX taskforce visit to Columbia Global Centers | Tunis January 16, 2020 On January 16th, OSTX had a visit to the Columbia Global Centers | Tunis. The aim of the activity was to open up the university professors and students, members of OSTX taskforce, to Columbia Global Centers Amman and Tunis. It was an opportunity to understand the activities and roles of Global Centers and brainstorm on future collaboration. The visit was concluded by a brief presentation for OSTX next steps and preparation to the TOT for mentors and the entrepreneurial bootcamp.

In alignment with University policy, El Centro's Community Room (access through Meyer Green) will be open during the academic quarter, Monday - Friday 10am - 4pm except for holidays. Masks will be strongly recommended while inside the community room unless actively eating or drinking, to promote the safety and well being of our comunidad.

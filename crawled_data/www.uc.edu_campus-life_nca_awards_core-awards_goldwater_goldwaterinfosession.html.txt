Info Session Goldwater @ UC Official Goldwater Website NCA Learning Series 2021-2022 Tuesday, September 7th @ 12:00PM via Zoom The Goldwater Scholarship recognizes students who have the potential to become tomorrow's STEM research leaders. Dr. Kevin Haworth (UC COM, Goldwater National Reviewer, former Goldwater Scholar) and Dr. Jenny Hyest (NCA Director) discussed award eligibility and benefits, application tips, and pre-application and application processes. The info session was held virtually. To access the recording, you must request to be added to the UC Goldwater Canvas Community. All important Goldwater deadlines and details are laid out on this Canvas page to help you prepare your application. Email nca@uc.edu to request to be added. Eligibility: 2nd- & 3rd-year (or 4th-year in a 5-year program) STEM students with a 3.5+ GPA who are interested in research careers can apply. 1st-year students should attend to learn more. U.S. citizens and Permanent Residents are eligible. Email nca@uc.edu with questions.

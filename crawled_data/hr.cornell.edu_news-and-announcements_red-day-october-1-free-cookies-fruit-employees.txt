High Five RED Day (Recognizing Employees Day) Tuesday, October 1, 2019 at 12:00pm to 1:30pm Cookies and fresh fruit will be available at various campus locations to say thank you to hard working Cornell employees. Tables will be hosted from 12:00 to 1:30 pm or until supplies run out. The Employee Recognition Team will be hosting tables in the following locations: Klarman Hall Atrium EHOB Atrium Mann Library Lobby Duffield Atrium Veterinary Medical College Lab of Ornithology Goodies will also be dropped off at the following locations: Seneca Place, Botanic Gardens, Humphreys Service Building, the Grounds Shop, 1501 Clara Dickson Hall, 350 Caldwell Hall, and the Breazzano Center. Watch for information in your building about times for these locations.

Learn how mathematical foundations of data analytics is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The certificate consists of 15 credit hours, including two required courses—Introduction to Data Analytics and Data Analytics Capstone—and three electives based on your academic and career goals. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Graduate Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

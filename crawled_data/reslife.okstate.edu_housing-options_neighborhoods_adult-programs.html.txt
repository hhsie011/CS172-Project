Registration for these programs must be completed before you attend. Adult Program Registration Form Once registered you can attend as many of the classes that you wish. The classes are taught by a variety of educators, including faculty, (current and retired), graduate students, and students training to teach English as a second language. Attendance and participation will be rewarded at the end of each semester, through the form of certificates. 2022 Summer Schedule Intermediate English: Monday and Wednesday 9:30 am - 10:30 am Beginners English: Monday and Wednesday 11:00 am – 12:00 pm TOEFL Prep: Monday 2:00 pm – 3:00 pm Grammar: Tuesday and Friday 10:00 am – 11:00 am Advanced English: Tuesday & Thursday 11:30 am -12:30 pm English in Cinema: Tuesday 3:00 pm – 5:00 pm Discover Stillwater: Wednesday 1:00 pm – 3:00 pm Conversations: Thursday 1:00 pm – 2:00 pm Mommy and Me: Thursday 9:00 am – 10:00 am Class Descriptions Intermediate English Come join us to improve reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills. This class will improve overall English skills by teaching language through all forms of communication. Beginners English Come join us to improve reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills. This class will improve overall English skills by teaching language through all forms of communication. TOEFL Prep Are you wanting to take the TOEFL, but feel overwhelmed or underprepared? Then this class is for you! We will be preparing for and practicing taking the TOEFL exam. This class will practice each of the four sections of the TOEFL and will have taken home practice assignments so you can continue your training during the week. Grammar Come improve your English grammar and vocabulary by learning new words and how to use them in the proper way. Advanced English Come join us to improve reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills. This class will improve overall English skills by teaching language through all forms of communication. English in Cinema The goal of this program is to provide students with a summer experience by providing them with opportunities to enjoy a meal and an educational movie. Discover Stillwater The goal of this program is to provide students with a meaningful summer experience in the form of fun activities, adventure, and exploration that they would not normally have. Conversations This course will be a combination of both structure and conversation practice. You will have a different conversation topic to discuss every week. Mommy and Me Mothers of FGSH with children will be welcomed to participate in a class that will allow you to enjoy quality time with their child while engaging in fun, craft, and developmental activities.

CLICK HERE to subscribe to our quarterly newsletter! THE DSC IS GOING VIRTUAL Educators! Looking for virtual teaching materials to give student field experiences? As a member of the Organization of Biological Field Stations , the DSC is helping to bring the field to you . The Virtual Field project provides: A suite of videos from field stations giving students experience finding evidence of ecological and evolutionary concepts Live events giving students opportunities to talk with researchers at their field sites. All resources come with teaching guides . The Virtual Field is supported by the National Science Foundation. WHAT IS ZZYZX? The Desert Studies Center is built within the grounds of the Soda Springs Historic District. Soda Springs, in Chemehuevi territory, has been inhabited for thousands of years. Precious springs have always attracted visitors in the dry Mojave Desert. Never a ghost town! The DSC is a vibrant center for desert education, research, and appreciation. Staff of the DSC are always on site to keep the Center maintained and thriving.

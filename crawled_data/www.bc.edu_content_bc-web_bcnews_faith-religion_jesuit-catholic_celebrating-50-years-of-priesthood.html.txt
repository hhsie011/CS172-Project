Donald A. MacMillan, S.J., and Rev. Walter J. Smith, S.J. (Photo by Rev. Harvey Egan, S.J.) On May 20, a Mass at St. Mary's Chapel celebrated Rev. Walter J. Smith, S.J., a faculty member at the Boston College School of Theology and Ministry and at Pope St. John XXIII National Seminary, and Rev. Donald A. MacMillan, S.J., a former BC chaplain currently in alumni relations and the chaplaincy at Boston College High School, who each mark 50 years of priesthood this year.

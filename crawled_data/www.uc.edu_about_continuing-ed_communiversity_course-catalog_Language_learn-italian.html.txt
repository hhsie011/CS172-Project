Even if you never travel to Italy, this lively class will make you feel like you have been there! Please check back for next class dates. ITALIAN: LEVEL 1 $189 / Seven Classes Instructor: Antonio lemmola In-person at VPC Campus Italy is a popular destination for American travelers and, whether you visit there or not, this class will take you on a journey through Italy's landscape and culture as you learn to speak this beautiful, musical language. You’ll acquire the basics of pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structure as you begin to develop your conversational skills. Emphasis will be on speaking and pronunciation, plus special expressions for travelers. Tips on traveling and enjoying the culture of Italy will also be included. Please bring the book: Italian: A Self-Teaching Guide, 2nd Edition by Edoardo A. Lèbano (Author). See book on Amazon. Please check back for next class dates. ITALIAN: LEVEL 2 $189 / Seven Classes Instructor: Antonio lemmola In-person at VPC Campus Increase your knowledge of Italian through conversation, culture, and camaraderie. We’ll spend the first part of each class on grammar, past tense, and vocabulary. The second part will be devoted to conversation, Italian crosswords, and interesting topics in Italian culture. You will need some knowledge of basic Italian or to have taken Italian for Beginners. Please bring the book: Italian: A Self-Teaching Guide, 2nd Edition by Edoardo A. Lèbano (Author). See book on Amazon. Antonio lemmola Language Instructor Antonio Iemmola specializes in introducing adults to the language of his native country.

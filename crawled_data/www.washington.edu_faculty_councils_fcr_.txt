Faculty councils serve as deliberative and advisory bodies for all matters of University policy, and are primary forums for faculty-administrative interaction in determining that policy. Faculty Code, Sec 42-33. In accordance with Executive Order No. 8, the Faculty Council on Research (FCR) is responsible for reviewing classified, proprietary and otherwise restricted research. FCR shall be responsible for all matters of policy relating to research and scholarship. Faculty Code, Sec 42-37.

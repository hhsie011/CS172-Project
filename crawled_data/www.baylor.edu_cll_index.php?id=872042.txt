Us Contact Us My Housing Baylor>Campus Living & Learning>Housing Options>Upper Division Communities Upper Division Communities Upper-Division Communities offer upper-division students the amenities of an apartment with the features and resources of on-campus housing. Like all on-campus communities, the Upper-Division Communities are furnished and are fully staffed, including Community Leaders, Residence Hall Directors, and Resident Chaplains. These communities feature one to four bedroom apartments with private or shared bathrooms and full kitchen and living areas.

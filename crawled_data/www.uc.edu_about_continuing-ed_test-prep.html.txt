College Essay Writing CompTIA® Certification Prep Test Prep We’re here to help you prepare to excel on the exams with convenient and affordable classes to help you do your best on your path to undergraduate admissions and on to advanced degrees. We'll help you increase your odds with test prep that includes: Unique courses that utilize resources from the university Experienced, high-quality instruction Small group setting for individual attention ACT Prep right arrow down arrow Build confidence and make the most of your potential by studying strategies for taking the ACT and by reviewing topics covered by the ACT (math, writing and reading comprehension). Read more SAT/PSAT Prep right arrow down arrow You will learn specific techniques for taking the math, writing, and reading comprehension parts of the test. Read more GRE and GMAT right arrow down arrow This live online course includes a comprehensive review of reading comprehension, sentence equivalence questions, quantitative comparison and reasoning, and data integration. Our personalized approach helps you learn test-taking techniques as you practice on actual GRE/GMAT tests from previous years, which is the best way to prepare for any standardized test. Read more LSAT right arrow down arrow In this course, you will delve into a detailed analysis of each LSAT problem type: reading comprehension, analytical reasoning, and logical reasoning. Read more LSAT with SPI right arrow down arrow This course prepares students for the LSAT and for creating a successful application. It includes Special Prelaw Intensive. Read more MCAT right arrow down arrow Receive a comprehensive math review and techniques for tackling both problem solving and data sufficiency questions. Read more Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) right arrow down arrow This course will cover all four areas of the TOEFL iBT; reading, writing, listening and speaking.

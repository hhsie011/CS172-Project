Noisy Neighbors? Come to CareerLAB; We can help. Reserve time in one of our four interview rooms for your upcoming interviews. To Reserve an Interview Room: Click on the room nuber below and grab the time you need. • Room 217 • Room 219 When selecting time for your interview add a little padding – 15 min for set up and 15 min for exiting. A confirmation will automatically be sent once you book it. All of the interview rooms are on the second floor of the CareerLAB. Upon arrival feel free to just go to the second floor and walk to the very back of the hall and you'll see Room 217 or 219. You can go in, then unmask, turn on the air purifier and set up your space. Upon leaving, please turn off the air purifier, put your mask on and you are free to leave.

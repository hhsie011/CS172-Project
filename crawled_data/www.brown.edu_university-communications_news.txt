On a daily basis, our writers and media officers work with a network of journalists from news organizations across the globe to highlight compelling stories from campus and to respond to media queries for information or perspective on topics and issues related to Brown. For Faculty and Staff. We engage with faculty and staff looking to collaborate on publicizing Brown news or to learn more about the news platforms we offer. To effectively interact with journalists and tell the Brown story to the broader community, we rely on close partnerships with our colleagues from across campus. For Journalists. We offer media resources and information on how journalists can best work with us. We welcome inquiries from anyone who wishes to contact us today.

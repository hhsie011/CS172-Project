Conference Housing Columbia University is a world-renowned institution within one of the most exciting cities in the world, making it the perfect choice for to host a conference or event. During the summer, Columbia Housing often opens its residence halls as the accommodations for conferences both large and small. Our Conference Housing team welcomes over 50 conferences, roughly 25,000 guests from countries around the world. The availability of Conference Housing varies year to year. Event organizers interested in learning more can submit a Conference Housing Request Form so that we have your request on record.

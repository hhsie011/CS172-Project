requirements can be complex, challenging, and require considerable time and attention of an Investigator and their research team. This page features curated resources for researchers, students, and administrators to learn about key subject areas and campus partners in research compliance. Animal Welfare The Office of Animal Welfare facilitates the IACUC review of research protocols and grants involving live vertebrate animals. Office of Animal Welfare Biosafety Key Biological Safety resources provided by EH&S for researchers and their teams, for all stages of research. Environmental, Health and Safety Biosafety Embryonic Stem Cells (ESCRO) The UW Embryonic Stem Cell Research Oversight Committee reviews and approves research involving human embryonic stem cells. ESCRO Environmental Health and Safety A gateway to online EH&S services as they apply to Principal Investigators, employees, forms for research grant proposals, and instructions for adding or relocating laboratory equipment. Environmental Health and Safety Export Controls Guidance to faculty, staff, officers, and administrators of their responsibilities related to U.S. export control laws and associated regulations governing University academic and research operations. Export Controls Financial Conflict of Interest An overview of policies, procedures, and systems for the disclosure of Significant Financial Interests and management of Financial Conflicts of Interest relating to University research. FCOI Fire Code Hazardous Material Compliance Information, resources, and tools to meet the requirements of the International Fire Code for proper laboratory storage, use, and disposal of hazardous materials. Fire Code Hazardous Materials (HazMat) Compliance Foreign Influence Information and guidance regarding foreign interests in sponsored programs Foreign Interests in Sponsored Programs Human Subjects / IRB The Human Subjects Division (HSD) is the University compliance office responsible for coordinating review and approval by Institutional Review Boards (IRBs) for research activities involving human subjects. Human Subjects Division Post-Award Financial Compliance (PAFC) Helps the UW community understand and fulfill the requirements of post-award fiscal regulations. Post-Award Financial Compliance (PAFC) Radiation Safety The Radiation Safety Office issues Authorizations (sub-licenses) for certain uses of radiation (x-rays, nuclear medicine, radiation therapy, etc). Environmental, Health and Safety Radiation Safety Responsible Conduct of Research Information regarding sponsor requirements, and University offerings, for Responsible Conduct of Research (RCR) Training. Responsible Conduct of Research Required Research Training A dynamic resource to assist researchers and their teams in identifying Required Research Trainings. Required Training Research Misconduct The Office of Research Misconduct Proceedings (ORMP) coordinates the University's handling of allegations of research misconduct. ORMP UW Medicine Compliance A central location for compliance information that impacts UW Medicine faculty, staff, students, and administrators. UW Medicine Compliance Outside Professional Work for Compensation (Form 1460) Outside Professional Work for Compensation (Form 1460) Reviews policy and process requirements for prior approval of professional work to be conducted outside University duties of faculty, librarians, and other academic personnel.

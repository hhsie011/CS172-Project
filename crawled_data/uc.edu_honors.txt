Resources Seminar Development Workshops Creating and Ordering Honors Seminars Home » Campus Life » University Honors Program University Honors Program Passionate People Changing the World The University Honors Program (UHP) is committed to helping students maximize their educational opportunities at UC while discovering and pursuing their passions in life and using their gifts and talents to make meaningful contributions to society. We believe experiential learning and consistent reflection can help students leave their unique imprint on campus and in their communities. The University Honors Program comprises students in the top 7% of University of Cincinnati undergraduates, nearly 1500 in total across all nine undergraduate colleges. We offer an inclusive community in which students are inspired to make an impact from their very first semester on campus. Our students focus on UC's Next Lives Here core values of inclusion, innovation, and impact to then graduate among the most competitive professionals in the world. To that end, we are invested in developing students into global citizen scholars who lead innovative efforts toward solving the world's complex problems. University Honors' commitment to an individualized, student-centered approach allows students to have a wide range of experiences to explore new passions while also investing in their gifts and talents. Current UC students who aren't yet members of the University Honors community can apply to join the program. Our application opens once per year each fall; clik below for more details. Also, our UHP Ambassadors offer just a glimpse of what's possible to our prospective students.

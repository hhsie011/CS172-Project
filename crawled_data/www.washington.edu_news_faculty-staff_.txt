Media training Find interview tips, op-ed advice and learn more about media training opportunities Data and reports Browse and download data on UW’s media mentions and more Experts Directory The UW News Experts Directory is used by journalists around the world to find people who can speak to various topics in the news. We have nearly 300 experts in the directory, but we are always looking for additional UW researchers across subject areas to add. To inquire about getting your profile added, please reach out to the appropriate Public Information Officer who covers your department. For medical experts, see UW Medicine Newsroom. Speakers Bureau The University of Washington is home to researchers who have expertise on many topics that affect people’s daily lives, from minimum wage and elections to wildfires and earthquakes. The UW Speakers Bureau is a list of researchers who have indicated they are willing to present their work in public talks to community groups. To inquire about getting your profile added, please reach out to the appropriate Public Information Officer who covers your department.

The Syracuse University Alumni Club of Chicago would like to extend a “welcome” to all alumni and friends of Syracuse University who have come to our website to learn more about us and what we do in Chicago. Our mission is to provide Syracuse University alums, students, parents, and basically anyone who loves the Orange an opportunity to keep updated on Syracuse University happenings and come together to enjoy local Chicago events, fundraising endeavors, networking, Syracuse University game watching, and more. A core group of dedicated Syracuse University alums comprises our Board and we meet monthly to determine ways in which we can bring a piece of Syracuse University to all of you in Chicago. Syracuse University Admissions in Chicago is looking to get local alumni involved with local college fairs and accepted student events. These events are held at local high schools for hundreds of high school students who are browsing all of their post-secondary options. If you loved your time at Syracuse University and you want to share your Orange passion with prospective students, email us at suchicagopresident@gmail.com for more details!

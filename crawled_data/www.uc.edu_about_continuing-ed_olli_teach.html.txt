The moderators who lead OLLI classes are volunteers who share their knowledge, passions, and hobbies with the OLLI community of lifelong learners. We are looking for moderators in all fields of knowledge from art to zoology and everything in between. FEEDBACK FORM FOR CURRENT MODERATORS Proposals for Fall 2022 are no longer being accepted. Term Dates at Most Locations: Sept. 19-Nov. 10 Mondays & Tuesdays at Adath Israel: Oct. 24-Nov. 14 Fridays at Ohio Living Llanfair: Sept. 23-Nov. 11 New moderators of multiweek courses must complete an orientation session on May 27, 1-3:30 pm, before your proposal will be considered. This also applies to those who have previously presented one-time programs for OLLI at UC and those who have taught for other OLLI programs. Completion of the session does not guarantee acceptance of your proposal. To find out if a late proposal can be considered, please use the contact information below.

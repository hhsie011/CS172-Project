VILLANOVA UNIVERSITY ALUMNI ASSOCIATION The mission of the Villanova University Alumni Association (VUAA) is to create a mutually beneficial, lifelong culture of belonging and returning to Villanova University—for education, for relationships, and for philanthropy—through the development of meaningful and transformative engagement opportunities. All Villanova graduates, regardless of graduation year, college or degree, are considered members of the VUAA. Villanova parents, families and friends are also members of the VUAA. No dues are assessed for membership. Discover the power of the Nova Nation: 130,000+ strong. Leaders across industries. Innovators and problem solvers. Dedicated mentors and volunteers.

RN-BSN Online Deadlines by Semester Start Spring Summer Fall Nov. 1 March 1 July 1 RN to BSN Application Instructions Note: If you are UC Blue Ash student, view application instructions at the bottom of this page. 1. Submit an Online Application Go to admissions.catalyst.uc.edu/apply, set up a new account with a username and password, select the Uptown Campus and complete your application. 2. Submit Additional Items Submit the $50 application fee Submit your transcripts from all post-secondary programs 3. Confirm Your Admission Submit the $100 enrollment deposit. UC Blue Ash Students UC Blue Ash Students must complete an application for re-admission. Application for Readmission If you do not have a UC M#, email nursingbearcats@uc.edu. State Regulations Because of state boards of nursing and department of education regulations, the University of Cincinnati College of Nursing is unable to accept applications for our online RN to BSN program from individuals who reside in the following states: Louisiana Massachusetts Oregon Rhode Island Tennessee Washington Questions? Contact the Office of Student Recruitment at nursingbearcats@uc.edu or 513-558-3600.

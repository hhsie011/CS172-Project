Comic artist Jeph Jacques, who makes the strip "Questionable Content," makes at least $7,000 per month from the site. PledgeMusic funds musicians working under a variety of genres, from hip hop to rock. AppsFunder is for funding app development, including games and productivity tools. Apart from Kickstarter, there are many crowdfunding sites that feature projects under a wide variety of different fields, such as Indiegogo and RocketHub.

This document is an effort to make public and centrally available the broad range of classes relating to sustainability that are available. The Sustainability Office hopes this document will help students to identify sustainability classes that fit into their studies in order to increase overall sustainability knowledge and literacy. While not an exhaustive list of all the sustainability courses at Penn, the inventory was created in an attempt to capture the majority of courses across departments and Schools at Penn. Please advise the Penn Sustainability Office about any missing courses, updates, or revisions (sustainability@upenn.edu). Use the link in the paragraph above to download the PDF.

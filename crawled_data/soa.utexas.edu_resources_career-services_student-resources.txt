Staff provide guidance through one-on-one meetings and group workshops, and seek to connect students to the professional community through networking events, professional panels, employer presentations, career fairs, and more. NOTE: CAREER SERVICES EVENTS AND MEETINGS WILL BE HYBRID FOR THE FALL. PLEASE SEE EMAILS FOR VIRTUAL LINKS AND INFORMATION REGARDING IN-PERSON ACTIVITIES. CAREER ADVISE & COUNSELING Resume, Cover Letter & Portfolio Reviews To schedule a resume, cover letter, and/or portfolio review, please select the appropriate major below. Please schedule appointments ONE day in advance to allow Career Service staff to confirm the meeting details. For alumni seeking advising appointments, please note: UTSOA Career Services is only able to provide advising within five years following graduation. ADVISING APPOINTMENTS (click on links below to view availability and schedule) Architecture, Interior Design, Urban Design, Sustainable Design: Resume, Cover Letter, Portfolio Reviews Landscape Architecture, Architecture, Interior Design: Resume, Cover Letter, Portfolio Reviews CRP, Historic Preservation, Arch History: Resume Cover Letter, Portfolio Reviews All Programs: Job Search, Networking, Interview, Negotiation Advice Career exploration & Counseling Select the link below to schedule an appointment with The University of Texas at Austin's Texas Career Engagement career counselors to discuss majors, career exploration, graduate school planning, and more. Texas Career Engagement: Career Counseling JOB BOARD The UTSOA Job Board hosts internship, full-time, and freelance opportunities. This resource is available only to UTSOA students and alumni. NOTICE: Career Services provides a free-of-charge job posting service and makes no recommendations regarding potential employers or student employees. All negotiations are handled directly between the student and employer. Employers and students are urged to perform due diligence when offering, applying for, or accepting private, off-campus employment.

She joined Columbia after working for a company in Texas that provided health screening services to agency clients, targeting everyone from migrant field workers to corporate employees. She received a Bachelor of Science degree in Kinesiology and Health, with a focus on Exercise Science, from The University of Texas at Austin in 2016. She holds a Certified Medical Administrative Assistant certificate from the National Healthcareer Association.

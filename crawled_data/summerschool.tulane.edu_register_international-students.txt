Home / International Students International Students Newcomb-Tulane Summer School welcomes International Students from other universities to take advantage of our five sessions and access to courses from our 5 undergraduate schools! NTC Summer School 2022 will be held in a completely remote format making our courses accessible from around the world. Get ahead on a credit towards your degree or try something new. Summer courses occur during 3 sessions, specially designed to fit your schedule. The 3 summer school sessions include: Maymester Intensive May 16 - May 27, 2022 Early Summer Session May 31 - July 1, 2022 Late Summer Session July 5 - August 5, 2022 Monday, May 30th: University closure in observance of Memorial Day Monday, July 4th: University closure in observance of Independence Day The 2022 Visiting Student application will open in April 2022. To apply to Newcomb-Tulane Summer School as an International Student, fill out the Visiting Student application and provide the following: Copy of your most recent transcript Transcripts are to be uploaded to your application Government issued ID Proof of English Language Proficiency Course Selection All visiting students are required to have earned at least a high school diploma, or its equivalent, by the start of the summer session. Students are expected to have completed the stated course prerequisites by the start of the session. Enrollment is for Summer only.

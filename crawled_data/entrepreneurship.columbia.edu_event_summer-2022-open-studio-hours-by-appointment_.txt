During Studio Hours, teams or individuals are invited to discuss their projects or ideas one-on-one with human-centered designers in the Studio. Summer Open Studio Hours will be made available by appointment: please email designstudio@columbia.edu. Regular Open Studio Hours will return in Fall 2023.

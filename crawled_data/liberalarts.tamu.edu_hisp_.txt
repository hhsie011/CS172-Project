Learn more about the College of Arts and Sciences. The Department of Hispanic Studies is committed to fostering a welcoming environment for all students and members of the campus community. On March 11, 2022, Texas A&M University announced its designation as a Hispanic-Serving Institution (HSI) by the U.S. Department of Education. The Department of Hispanic Studies considers HSI status an honor and upholds its responsibility to attract, retain, serve, and educate our Hispanic/Latinx students, nurturing and preparing them to make our University, State and Nation stronger, more resilient, and more equitable, thus advancing the foundational ideals of equity and justice. The Department of Hispanic Studies is fully committed to participate in this endeavor, equipping Hispanic/Latinx students with knowledge and skills to expand their educational opportunities, making them competitive in the 21st century economy.

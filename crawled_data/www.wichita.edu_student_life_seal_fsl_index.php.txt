We take pride in our academic achievement, leadership development, our commitment to bettering our communities, and our commitment to bettering our members through brotherhood and sisterhood. We are a community comprised of 26 social, Greek-letter organizations that have a variety of backgrounds, values, and missions. Together, we form the Shocker Greek Community. INTEREST FORM IFC Interfraternity Council The Interfraternity Council (IFC) is the governing body and policy-making organization of the North American Interfraternity Conference fraternities and social fraternities. IFC's chief purpose is to coordinate the effort of the individual fraternities in matters of philanthropy, scholarship, leadership and brotherhood. OUR CHAPTERS HOW TO JOIN MGC Multicultural Greek Council The Multicultural Greek Council (MGC) is the governing body and policy-making organization of National Pan-Hellenic Council, Incorporated (NPHC), National APIA Panhellenic Association and other culturally based organizations. MGC's chief purpose is to coordinate the effort of the individual fraternities and sororities in matters of service, scholarship, brotherhood/sisterhood, leadership and academic excellence. OUR CHAPTERS HOW TO JOIN SHOCK THE YARD STEP SHOW PC Panhellenic Council The Panhellenic Council is the governing body and policy-making organization of the National Panhellenic Conference Sororities. The purpose of the Panhellenic Council is to coordinate the efforts of the individual sororities in matters of philanthropy, scholarship, leadership and sisterhood. We are home to five out of the 26 sororities within the NPC: Alpha Phi, Delta Delta Delta, Delta Gamma, Gamma Phi Beta, and Kappa Kappa Gamma.

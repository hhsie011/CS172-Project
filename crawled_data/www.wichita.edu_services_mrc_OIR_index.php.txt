The Office of Instructional Resources provides WSU instructors of any rank a single point of contact for your training needs. Not only do our own experts offer training directly, we partner with experts across the campus to bring you the information you need to become the best instructor you can be. Think of us as the hub you can come to for help. To get started, click "In This Section" above to see what we have, and no matter where you go in our suite of pages, you can get back to this starting page by clicking "Return to OIR." Want to request a page, or need further help? Email us at OIR@wichita.edu.

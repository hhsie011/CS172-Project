Welcome to the homepage for the Brown University chapter of the American Academy of Environmental Engineers and Scientists (AAEES)! Environmental engineering uses engineering, mathematics, biology, and chemistry to develop and implement engineering solutions to protect human health and the environment. AAEES at Brown focuses on creating a community for students concentrating in Environmental Engineering or related fields. Leadership 2021-2022 This academic year, the AAEES chapter is led by Adrian Hernandez ’22 and Rachelle Shao ’22, with Dr. Indrek Külaots as the chapter advisor. Contact us at adrian_hernandez@brown.edu, rachelle_shao@brown.edu, and indrek_kulaots@brown.edu. Class of 2020 virtual meetup Theme: Amalie Lite designed by Anariel Design.

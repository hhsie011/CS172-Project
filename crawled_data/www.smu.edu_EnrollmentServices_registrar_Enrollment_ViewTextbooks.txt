This feature is presented to you by my.SMU and the SMU Bookstore. For Students You are now able to view and purchase the textbooks that are needed for specific courses and sections for a particular term of enrollment. Login in to my.SMU Click the Manage Classes tile Click on Class Schedule and select appropriate term Click on the View Textbooks link towards the top of the page. A list of all your Required, Recommended and Supplemental materials will load for all the courses that you have enrolled in for the selected term. In addition, you may also browse text book information for specific courses and sections for any other terms by clicking the “View Textbooks” button in the Class Search page. Please note that you may still go to the SMU Bookstore site and look up text book information one course at a time. At any time, you may purchase these textbooks on-line. * For Perkins School of Theology courses, textbook information is available through Cokesbury Bookstore cokesbury@smu.edu located in room 112 Selecman Hall or call 214-768-3226. * For Plano-in-Legacy courses, text book information is available through your department.

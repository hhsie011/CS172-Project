S.H.A.R.E.™ is a 5-story, Simulation Hospital offering on-site, virtual, and extended-reality simulation opportunities in a variety of clinical, research, and educational settings. S.H.A.R.E.™ offers fully-equipped, customizable environments ranging from an ambulance bay, emergency department, incident command center, outpatient clinics, labor and delivery suites, operating rooms, adult and pediatric intensive care, medical-surgical units, a home health care residence, and much more. The five-story, 41,000-square-foot, fully equipped facility serves as a hub for students, health care professionals, first responders, and corporate partners to design, test, and master skills and technologies to transform health care education, research and practice.

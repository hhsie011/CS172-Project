We strive to produce doctors skilled in present technologies and ready for future innovation. The Office of Admissions is proud to play a role in that mission by admitting truly remarkable and committed students whose intellectual, educational, and experiential diversity and accomplishments never cease to impress us. We are delighted by your interest in The Warren Alpert Medical School and invite you to explore the information on this website. If you feel inspired to become part of our community, please consider applying. Curriculum Our innovative curriculum is designed to impart all the skills, knowledge, attitudes, and abilities to produce competent physicians and scientists. Degree Programs The Warren Alpert Medical School offers degree programs in which you can earn a Master of Science, Master of Public Health, or doctoral degree in addition to your MD. Student Life The Warren Alpert Medical School offers both the small, tight-knit community as well as the opportunity and activity of an Ivy League School in an exciting city.

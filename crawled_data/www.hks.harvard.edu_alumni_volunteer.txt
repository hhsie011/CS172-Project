Roles and Opportunities The Harvard Kennedy School alumni community is one of the largest networks of public policy professionals in the world. From presidents to prime ministers, analysts to social entrepreneurs, alumni in the HKS network are helping people around the world live in societies that are more safe, free, just, and sustainably prosperous. Volunteers from the alumni community play a vital role in connecting their classmates with the School. Working with HKS staff members, volunteers facilitate engagement and advance giving opportunities around the world. View the various ways you can stay connected with HKS: Building Community: Volunteer to help build greater alumni-to-alumni connections. Invest in the Future: The HKS Fund plays an integral role in supporting current and future students, as well as additional programming opportunities. View the ways you can help with our fundraising efforts. Interact with Students: Alumni have a great impact on current and future HKS students. Learn how you can help guide the next generation of HKS. If you're interested in pursuing any of the above opportunities, please complete the form below.

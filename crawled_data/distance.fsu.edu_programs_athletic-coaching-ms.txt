Our goal is to provide current and future coaches and sports professionals with current knowledge and best practices that are based on experience and research. We want our students to be the most knowledgeable and successful in the profession. The Athletic Coaching program is one of seven FSU online master’s in education programs ranked No. 21 among public institutions, No. 22 among all institutions, and No. 7 in the nation for veterans by U.S. News & World Report in 2022.

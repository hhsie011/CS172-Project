You will need to have either received at least one dose of a COVID-19 vaccine or an approved exemption by October 15, 2021, to comply with this mandate. Individuals who do not have an exemption or do not receive their first dose by October 15 or, if required, their second dose by November 15, 2021, will be prohibited from working until proof of vaccination or an exemption is provided. For more information visit the University's Vaccines webpage, or speak with your HR contact. University's Vaccines Webpage University COVID-19 vaccination requirement Get Rewarded For Making Healthy Choices The university has partnered with Independence Blue Cross to provide eligible employees a variety of powerful and easy-to-use wellness tools. The Total Wellness program is designed to assist employees with taking a holistic approach to wellness by incorporating physical fitness with personalized well-being plans while guiding your wellness journey. Challenges Wellness Resources Tracking and Redeeming Rewards Any health information recorded through this program is securely stored by Independence Blue Cross. Temple University will not have access to your protected health information. The university will only know of your participation in the program and the earned dollars in order to issue your rewards. The American Heart Association recognizes Temple University’s Total Wellness program. The organization continues to assess the university’s wellness resources, tools and activities ensuring high standards of effectiveness, inclusion and innovation. Learn more about the American Heart Association. Contact Us For questions regarding the Total Wellness program, call 215-926-2285 or email TotalWellness@temple.edu.

Microsoft Excel Certification Project Management Certification (CAPM®) Pharmacy Technician Like most of Ohio, Cincinnati needs skilled pharmacy technicians. This 100% online course will prepare you for the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board (PTCB) National Certification exam and help you complete an externship with a leading pharmacy chain. This program is offered in partnership with ed2go, one of the nation's top online education providers. Enroll Now Course Objectives Learn federal and state laws that govern pharmacies, as well as the ethics of pharmacy practice. Be fully prepared to sit for the National Certification exam titled Pharmacy Technician Certification Exam (PTCE), offered by the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board (PTCB). Become eligible to apply for a 100+ hour externship at a national pharmacy chain. What is the Job Outlook for Pharmacy Technicians? right arrow down arrow According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), demand for pharmacy technicians will grow 12% through 2028 – that equates to over 30,000 new jobs. Burning Glass found that the greater Cincinnati metropolitan region has one of the fastest growing needs for pharmacy technicians in the state of Ohio. In fact, demand for jobs will grow by 11.2% over the next decade. The BLS notes that on average, pharmacy technicians earn an annual salary of $32,700. Enroll Now What Do Pharmacy Technicians Do? right arrow down arrow Pharmacy technicians order and stock medications, prepare medications for distribution and provide information to patients. Pharmacy technicians may perform many of the same duties as a pharmacist; however, their work must be approved by a pharmacist before medication can be dispensed. Enroll Now What Do Pharmacy Technicians Need a Certification? right arrow down arrow Requirements for pharmacy technicians vary by state, but most require certification, registration or licensure. Earning your certification from the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board (PTCB) provides a valuable, industry-recognized credential that meets most states’ requirements. Enroll Now How Long Does It Take to Complete This Pharmacy Technician Course? right arrow down arrow This pharmacy technician course can be completed in one year or less. This does not include the time you spend in an externship program or preparing and sitting for state licensure. Enroll Now Is a Pharmacy Technicial a Good Career? right arrow down arrow As the population ages and the prevalence of chronic diseases rises, more pharmacy technicians will be needed in pharmacies and may even see themselves taking on greater responsibilities in their role.

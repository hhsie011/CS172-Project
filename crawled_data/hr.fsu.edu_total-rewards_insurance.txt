Supplemental Insurance Retirement Insurance FSU offers comprehensive insurance options to meet employee needs. Options include health insurance, prescription drug plans, flexible spending accounts, health savings accounts, dental plans, life insurance, vision plans, and an array of supplemental insurance plans. Coverage options are available through the Division of State Group Insurances (DSGI) and through local and national vendors. Enrollment and Effective Dates Enrollment information, eligibility, Open Enrollment, Qualifying Status Change (QSC) events, and effective dates of coverage. Learn More Health Insurance Division of State Group Insurance (DSGI) HMO, PPO, and High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) options and prescription services. Learn More Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) DSGI offers 3 Flexible Spending Account options to set aside pre-tax dollars to use. Learn More Health Savings Account (HSA) DSGI offers a Health Savings Account to High Deductible Health Plan participants to offset the cost of services. Learn More Dental Insurance DSGI offers several pre-paid (HMO), PPO, PPO & Indemnity, and Indemnity dental plan options. Learn More Life Insurance DSGI offers 4 life insurance options to eligible employees. Learn More Vision Insurance DSGI offers competitive vision coverage through Humana Vision. Coverage provides discounts on an annual eye exam plus glasses or contact materials. Learn More Supplemental Insurances DSGI offers 6 optional supplemental insurance plans to employees to offset the cost of services received for accidents, cancer, hospitalization, or disability. Learn More Additional Supplemental Insurances FSU has partnered with local and national vendors to provide employees 5 additional insurance options available as a post-tax deduction.

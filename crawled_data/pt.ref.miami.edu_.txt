Welcome We are committed to providing safe and reliable parking and transportation options that provide mobility, accessibility, and enhance sustainability for the University community. Our department is responsible for the overall management of parking facilities, traffic control, and meeting the transportation needs of the Coral Gables campus. The information herein will assist you in making your transportation and parking experience at the University as pleasant as possible one.

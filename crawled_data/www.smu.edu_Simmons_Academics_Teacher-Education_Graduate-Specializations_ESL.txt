Teacher Education Graduate Specializations English as a Second Language (ESL) Apply Now Request Information Course Descriptions SMU’s Annette Caldwell Simmons School of Education and Human Development offers four courses that prepare teachers to take the TExES English as a Second Language (ESL) supplemental certification exam. These courses may also be applied toward SMU’s 36-credit-hour Master of Education (M.Ed.) with a specialization in ESL. Courses The four courses (12 credit hours) required for the ESL specialization are: EDU 6312 Applied Linguistics EDU 6317 Culture and Community in Education EDU 6320 Language Teaching Research, Theory, and Practice EDU 6390 Classroom Instruction and Assessment for Language Learners Graduate Specializations Admission Tuition and Scholarships Bilingual Education English as a Second Language (ESL) Gifted and Talented Learning Therapy Literacy Studies Mathematics Special Education STEM Urban Education Request Information Upcoming Events Request info Contact the Department of Teaching & Learning Contact Us 214-768-2346 teacher@smu.edu Request Information Visit Us Harold Clark Simmons Hall 6401 Airline Rd., Suite 301 Dallas, TX 75205 Contact Us Maps and Directions Legal Disclosures © Southern Methodist University, PO Box 750100, Dallas, TX 75275 facebook twitter

The Division of Academic and Student Affairs offers tools and resources to reach your desired destination. Academic Advising Programs and Services While you’re at NC State, Academic Advising Programs and Services helps you discover what you’re passionate about through the Exploratory Studies program, and pairs you with a personal advisor who will support and encourage you along the way. Our faculty and staff are dedicated to helping you by developing a customized academic plan, connecting you to resources and high impact experiences, and helping you achieve your academic goals to create a well-rounded learning experience both inside and outside the classroom. Inter-College Transfer Exploratory Studies Explore Majors and Minors Academic Advising Academic Success Center Formerly the University Tutorial Center, the Academic Success Center provides NC State students with tutoring services, both drop-in and scheduled, as well as peer mentoring and writing support. The Academic Success Center is now located in newly-renovated D.H. Hill, Jr. Library on Main Campus. Schedule Tutoring Today Student Success GPS Student Success GPS is a one-stop platform that helps you navigate your college courses, stay organized, and keep you connected with your advisor whenever you need them. Access GPS in two ways – either through your browser or the dedicated mobile app.Get the App Disability Resources The Division of Academic and Student Affairs is committed to ensuring that no qualified student with a disability at NC State is excluded from, denied the benefits of, or be subjected to discrimination under any program or activity. The Disability Resources Office helps to ensure that students with disabilities are provided an inclusive experience and equal opportunity to participate in courses and programs by determining eligibility for accommodations and serving as a resource to faculty in helping them understand their obligation in providing equal access.

Texas Tech University Directory Raiderlink A-Z Index Transportation & Parking Services Menu Search TTU Transportation & Parking Services Vendors and Contractors Vendors and contractors who are not Texas Tech University employees can receive parking accommodations to help make deliveries on campus or reach their temporary on-campus job site. To request vendor or contractor parking, please fill out all blanks in the form below and hit submit when finished. TPS sends a confirmation email upon submission of the request. After TPS reviews your request, a TPS representative contacts you regarding your request. If you see this don't fill out this input box. Contractor/Vendor Request Form *Name of Company: Enter the name of the company. *Distribution Method: Please Select Pick up in person By mail Select a distribution method from the drop-down list. *Request Permit Duration: Please Select Less than one year (temporary) Yearly Permit (May to May) Select the Permit's Duration from the drop-down list. *Address Line 1: Enter the primary address. Address Line 2: Enter the secondary address (leave blank if none). *City: Enter the City State: Please Select Alabama Alaska Arizona Arkansas California Colorado Connecticut District of Columbia Delaware Florida Georgia Hawaii Iowa Kansas Kentucky Louisiana Maine Maryland Massachusetts Michigan Minnesota Mississippi Missouri Montana Nebraska Nevada New Hampshire New Jersey New Mexico New York North Carolina North Dakota Ohio Oklahoma Oregon Pennsylvania Rhode Island South Carolina South Dakota Tennessee Texas Utah Vermont Virginia Washington West Virginia Wisconsin Wyoming Select a state from the drop-down list. Zip Code: Enter the Zip Code. Number of Cars: Enter the number of cars. *Vehicle Information Please enter the following information in for each vehicle that needs a dash pass: License Plate #; State; Make; Color;Style. If you have temporary plates, be sure to list your vehicle Identifation number (VIN). *Reason for Request: Example: We have a contract to do routine maintenance at the Student Union, and need to park in service parking. Primary Contact Information: This person will serve as the primary contact between University Parking Services and your company. This is the person we will contact should any issue arrive. *(Primary Contact) First Name: Enter your First Name. *(Primary Contact) Last Name: Enter Your Last Name. *Position: Enter your Job Position. *Phone Number: Enter your Phone Number. (Ex: XXX - XXX - XXXX) Phone Extension: Enter your Phone Extension. (Only if available. If not, leave blank) *Email: Enter your Email Address. Secondary Contact Information: Secondary contact information is optional. (Secondary Contact) First Name: Enter your secondary First Name. (Secondary Contact) Last Name: Enter your secondary Last Name Position: Enter your secondary Job Position. Phone Number: Enter your secondary Phone Number. (Ex: XXX - XXX - XXXX) Phone Extension: Enter your secondary phone extension. Email: Enter your secondary Email. Supporting Documentation Supporting documentation is optional. If you would like to submit supporting documentation you can email us at parking@ttu.edu, please reference the company you are submitting the document for. Or you can fax your supporting documentation to (806)742-3458 using a company letterhead to verify your affiliation. Please send to the attn. of "Contractor/Vendor Permit".

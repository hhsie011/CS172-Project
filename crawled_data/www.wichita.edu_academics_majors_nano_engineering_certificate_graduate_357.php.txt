Learn how nano engineering is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The 12-credit-hour certificate covers the following courses: Required: Introduction to Nanotechnology Select three from the following: Polymeric Composite Materials Advanced Biomaterials Synthesis and Applications of Nanomaterials Corrosion Science Engineering Advanced Laser Applications in Manufacturing View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

Belknap Campus Student Activities Center Louisville, KY 40292 (502) 852-6679 books@louisville.edu HSC Campus "K" Building - 1st Floor Louisville, KY 40202 (502) 852-5284 books@louisville.edu For Students: Students may purchase course materials in person at the campus bookstores or via the UofL Bookstore website. For Faculty/Staff - Course Material Orders: All textbook and course materials orders must be submitted to the official campus bookstore to ensure compliance with the federal government Higher Education Opportunity Act. This action allows students to see textbook and course material information as they register for classes and helps them receive more money at buy back. Adoptions must include all course materials including books, Access Codes, web-based homework codes, custom course packs, and any other materials that students have to pay for to be in your class. Deadline to submit Fall course materials: April 15th Deadline to submit Spring course materials: October 15th Deadline to submit Summer course materials: March 15th Please submit your course material orders using one of the following options: Follett Discover (This online tool provides hassle-free access for instructors to research, discover and adopt course materials. Instructors must access Follett Discover via their Blackboard homepage). Email: books@louisville.edu, and include: Course information (Department, Course #, Section #) Information about the text (ISBN, title, author, edition, etc.) Whether the book is recommended or required Any other pertinent information You will receive an email to confirm that your adoption was received, and a second email to confirm that it has been entered into our database. We request that all adoptions be submitted in a digital format so that communications can be tracked for questions or concerns about adoptions.

APPLY GET INVOLVED Search this Website Search Diversity, Equity and Inclusion The College of Fine Arts is committed to fostering a culture and climate that is inclusive and welcoming of students, faculty and staff of all backgrounds, experiences and viewpoints. Since 2014, the college has been working to implement the Fine Arts Strategic Plan for Diversity, with efforts led by Associate Dean for Diversity, Equity and Inclusion John Yancey.

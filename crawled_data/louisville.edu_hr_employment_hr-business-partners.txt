Business Partners (HRBP) What we do The HR Business Partners (HRBP) are here to support university departments in their day to day HR operations. As HR business partners, we will consult and coach departments on topics such as employment, employee relations and compensation best practices. We will also work with departments to educate, train and interpret policies and procedures to help ensure department compliance with federal guidelines. HRBP Office Hours Our HRBP team is available by appointment during the select times below: M/T/W/TH/F 8:00 am - 4:30 pm Belknap Campus: Human Resources Office 1980 Arthur Street Contact us to schedule an appointment!

You'll learn principles behind instructional content and delivery, program design and best practices. The program is a good fit for educators and corporate trainers. Learn how Instructional Design in Professional Practice is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum This 15-credit-hour certificate consists of the following courses: Change, Creativity and Innovation Learning Theory and Curriculum Design Inquiry Into Instructional Practice: Part 1 Inquiry Into Instructional Practice: Part 2 Instructional Leadership: Professionalism and Collaboration Students who earn the certificate and then wish to pursue WSU's Master of Education (M.Ed.) in learning and instructional design will have already earned 15 of the required 30 credit hours within the degree. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

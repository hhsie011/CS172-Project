Excel Alumni Supporting alumni with maintaining and growing their social networks, independent living skills, and careers. Learn More Ideas to Serve I2S promotes an in-depth discovery and mapping of local and global social issues. Our approach is centered on community-based expertise. Learn More Impact Speaker Series Impact talks are meaningful conversations focused on racial and social justice and DE&I with leaders from the corporate and the social sector. Learn More Leadership for Social Good Study Abroad The Leadership for Social Good Program provides students with insights into global civil society and an opportunity to make a positive impact by working with a nonprofit. Learn More Leadership Studies Minor Provides students with in-depth knowledge of leadership theory and skills through rigorous coursework and a capstone experience in the social sector. Learn More Pro Bono Consulting Pro Bono Consulting is a collaboration between Scheller MBAs and community partners working together to resolve key strategic challenges. Learn More Servant Leadership The mission of the Cowan-Turner Servant Leadership Program is to promote the understanding and practice of servant leadership at Georgia Tech. Learn More Social Impact Fellows The fellowships support collaboration with community partners while providing students with hands-on experience with a non-profit organization.

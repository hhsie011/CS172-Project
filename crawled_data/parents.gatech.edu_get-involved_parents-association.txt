We encourage you to stay involved during your student’s entire collegiate experience at Georgia Tech to ensure your student’s success! All Georgia Tech parents are automatically members of the Parents Association. It provides its members discounts, invitations to special events, and services designed to strengthen the relationship the Institute has with our students’ families, increase communication with parents, and actively involve them in campus life. It's free, and it's the best way for parents to stay connected to the GT! As a member of the Parents Association, you receive: Parent and Family Calendar/Parent Handbook Monthly electronic newsletters with timely announcements Invitations to events, such as Family Weekend and Sibs Day Email notifications with important information Sign up to stay informed and connected Interested in getting more involved?

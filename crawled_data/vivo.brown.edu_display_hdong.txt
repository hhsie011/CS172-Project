Full Text Research Research Overview My main research interest is in partial differential equations, including nonlinear elliptic and parabolic equations, the Navier-Stokes equations, the quasi-geostrophic equations, the reaction diffusion equations, the probability approaches of PDEs, and rates of convergence of finite difference approximations for elliptic and parabolic Bellman's equations. I am also interested in the probability theory and stochastic partial differential equations. Research Statement My main research interest is in partial differential equations, including nonlinear elliptic and parabolic equations, the Navier-Stokes equations, the quasi-geostrophic equations, the reaction diffusion equations, the probability approaches of PDEs, and rates of convergence of finite difference approximations for elliptic and parabolic Bellman's equations. I am also interested in the probability theory and stochastic partial differential equations.

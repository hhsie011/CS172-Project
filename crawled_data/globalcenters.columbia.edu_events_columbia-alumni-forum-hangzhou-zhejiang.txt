Zhejiang Events Past Event Columbia Alumni Forum in Hangzhou, Zhejiang Share Add to Calendar: Google Yahoo March 31, 2018 10:30 AM - 3:50 PM Room 520, Tin Ka Ping College, Zhejiang University Xixi Campus On March 31, the Columbia Alumni Forum in Zhejiang will be held in Hangzhou, China. It aims to enhance the exchange of ideas among Columbia alumni and serve the local community, strengthening the cooperation and introducing young talents to Zhejiang, as a result, stimulating the local economy in general. The Forum invites Columbia alumni, education specialists, and technology professionals to discuss the core of quality education in the new era of globalization, technology innovation, and investment strategy. Alumni participants will experience the breath-taking scenery of Hangzhou in a hiking tour before lunch, which serves our distinguished guests with delicate and delicious traditional Hangzhou cuisine. In the afternoon, the public Forum in education and technology welcomes everyone to join and share opinions. The event is organized by Columbia Zhejiang Alumni and is supported by Columbia Global Centers | Beijing and CAA Shanghai. Agenda 10:30 a.m. Hiking (CU Alumni only) 12:00 p.m. - 13:45 p.m. Alumni Lunch 14:00 p.m. - 14:20 p.m. Registration 14:30 p.m. - 15:50 p.m. Education Forum 16:00 p.m. - 16:50 p.m. Technology Forum Special Notice The event will be conducted in Chinese. Due to the limited seats at lunch, registration is required through scanning the QR code below.

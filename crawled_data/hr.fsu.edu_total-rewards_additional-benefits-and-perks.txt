FSU provides employees the opportunity to participate in exclusive discounts for current FSU employees. Discounts and perks are available at a variety of campus, local, and national vendors. Discounts are applied at the point of purchase and may require a valid FSU ID card presented. Arts, Entertainment, and Travel FSU has partnered with local and nationwide vendors to provide discounts to employees for art, entertainment, and travel. Learn More Education and Development FSU provides discounts and educational opportunities on campus and through distance learning. Learn More Seminole Savings Discount Program FSU offers an Employee Discount Program for products and services at businesses serving our employees' diverse needs and interests. Learn More Health and Wellness FSU has partnered with campus and local vendors to provide health and wellness discount services and opportunities to employees. Learn More Technology FSU offers several technology-related discounts to employees. Learn More Financial Planning Learn more about payroll deductions, giving back to FSU, and Truist.

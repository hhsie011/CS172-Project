Our services and benefits to alumni include: Careers: Resources and services are available for the career development and job search needs of UW alumni. UW.edu Email Forwarding: You can have your uw.edu email address forwarded to a personal email service. NetID Access: Keeping you plugged into all things digital at the UW Transcripts: Easy access to your academic records Update Your Information: The easiest way to make sure you’re connected and in the know BECU: Reinforce your commitment to the UW community with every purchase you make, thanks to a special partnership with BECU If you can’t find what you’re looking for, please contact us and we will be happy to assist you.

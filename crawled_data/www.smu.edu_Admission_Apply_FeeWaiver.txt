To use this fee waiver, select which application deadline and method are right for you and apply using any of our three applications. Follow these directions depending on your application type: The Common Application – select fee waiver code TW1300 to answer the question “Do you intend to use one of these school-specific fee waivers?” under SMU’s member questions tab. The MyCoalition Application – on the payment page use fee waiver codes TW1300 or PR17147. ApplyTexas – when prompted for payment method, select "Request for fee waiver." SMU continues to remain temporary test-optional for Fall 2022. All applicants are automatically considered for a variety of merit-based scholarships. If you are in need of assistance, please contact your admission counselor. Find yours at smu.edu/counselor or call 214-768-2058.

We provide a platform for UGS students to explore leadership while planning and executing beneficial events and activities for fellow students. UGSC belongs to the Senate of College Councils, the student governance organization engaged in academic affairs on campus. 2021-2022 Officers Johnny Klevenski, President Alyssa Titone, Vice President Andres Suniaga, Public Relations Director Andy Ma, Financial Director Membership Any of the following students are eligible for council membership Current or former School of Undergraduate Studies students FIG peer mentors Bridging Disciplines Program students Get Involved The UGS Council meets weekly during the fall and spring semesters. For information on upcoming meeting or events, interested students should follow us on Hornslink, Twitter, or join our GroupMe. Activities of the Undergraduate Studies Council include Hosting social and academic events for School of Undergraduate Studies students Presenting proposals and legislation on behalf of students to the dean of Undergraduate Studies Deliberating and voting on Senate of College Councils legislation relevant to UGS Town hall meetings with UGS administration Leadership Leadership opportunities are available, even for students in their first semester. Members may serve on committees in UGS and lead council projects in addition to being considered for one of several officer positions. All UGSC members enjoy being members of the Senate of College Councils network.

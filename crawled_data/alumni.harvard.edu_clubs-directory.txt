Harvard Clubs are geographically-based alumni communities that connect thousands of alumni around the world to each other and to the University through local programming, communications, and service opportunities. In areas where the alumni population is smaller than would support the administration of a club, we have Harvard Alumni Contacts, who are full-time residents who facilitate local alumni engagement.

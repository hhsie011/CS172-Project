Summer Special Sessions 1 & 2, 2022 March 15, 2022 Registration and enrollment begin. Online enrollment available for Cornell students via the Student Center. Online registration available for visiting students via SCE's class roster. May 1, 2022 Financial aid deadline. Variable registration deadlines for visiting students For visiting students, your registration deadline is 14 calendar days prior to the start date of your class. For example, if your class begins on June 1, your registration deadline is 11:59 p.m. on May 17. Note: All tuition and fees must be paid in full in order for registration and enrollment to be complete. Variable registration deadlines for Cornell students For students currently registered in a Cornell degree program, your enrollment deadline varies as follows: For classes up to 35 calendar days in duration: Your enrollment deadline is 2 days after the start date of your class. For example, if your class begins on June 1, your enrollment deadline is 11:59 p.m. on June 2. For classes 36 or more calendar days in duration: Your deadline is 3 days after the start date of your class. For example, if your class begins on June 1, your enrollment deadline is 11:59 p.m. on June 3. Variable drop and course change deadlines Drop and course change deadlines for your class can be found in Student Center by clicking on the "Deadlines" icon located above your class schedule. Day 1–3 of class 0% tuition liability for courses dropped during this period. Day 4–5 of class 50% tuition liability for courses dropped during this period. Day 6 of class 100% tuition liability for courses dropped on or after this date. Variable dates Last day of instruction and final exam dates vary based on class/program dates and length. August 31, 2022 Grades from Student Center and transcripts are available after this date.

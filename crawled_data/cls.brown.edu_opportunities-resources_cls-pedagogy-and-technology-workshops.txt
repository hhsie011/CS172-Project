By promoting research on language pedagogy and offering programming relating to the teaching and learning of languages, CLS helps to facilitate communication among language faculty at Brown and to encourage students to pursue the innovative language-learning opportunities Brown makes available. Many events are recorded and available on our CLS youtube page. To keep updated on CLS events, please subscribe to our newsletter.

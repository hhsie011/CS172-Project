Gift Opportunities – Make a Difference Wu Tsai Neurosciences Institute relies on the generous support of alumni, parents and friends to foster highly novel and collaborative brain research across all disciplines with the goal of better understanding and treating the human brain in health and disease. Our flagship initiatives to transform neuroscience are our Big Ideas in Neuroscience. Download the Big Ideas in Neuroscience Brochure Philanthropic support significantly helps Stanford advance groundbreaking discoveries as well as uniquely train a new breed of neuroscientist. Expendable and endowment support at all levels will have a dramatic impact on many lives far into the future.

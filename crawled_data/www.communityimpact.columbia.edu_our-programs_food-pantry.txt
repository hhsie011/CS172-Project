Your support allows us to bring high-quality services to the Morningside Heights and Washington Heights communities. Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About the Food Pantry Coordintors and volunteers staff a weekly Food Pantry on Mondays and Thursdays at Ford Hall on West 114th St, between Broadway and Riverside Drive. Fresh food and dry goods, donated by community groups is provided to neighborhood families, homeless individuals and the elderly. Ford Hall also has a closet of warm clothes for our clients. We are always looking for clothing and houseware donations. For more information contact the Senior Program Coordinator, Ricardo Bustamante, at [email protected] or call 212-854-6310.

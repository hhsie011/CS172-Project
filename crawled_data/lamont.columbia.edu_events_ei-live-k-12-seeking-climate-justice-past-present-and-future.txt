11:45 AM America/New_York Online K-12 Education Seeking Climate Justice – Past, Present, and Future This session will review and reflect on overlapping socio-economic, racial, and environmental/climate injustices faced by marginalized communities in the past and present times, as well as identify and address their vulnerability to future risks as acerbated by climate change. Presenters: Melody Braun, Senior Staff Associate and Dannie Dinh, Program Officer, International Research Institute for Climate and Society Target Audience: Grades 6-12 This event is free but registration is required.

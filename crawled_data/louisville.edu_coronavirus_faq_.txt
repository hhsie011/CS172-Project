COVID-19 Vaccine FAQ FAQ - All UofL Members FAQ - Masks FAQ - Students FAQ - Faculty FAQ - Employees FAQ - COVID-19 Vaccine Home / FAQ Question Center Ask a question or report a concern Have questions or concerns about COVID-19 protocols and operations? Contact the Business Operations COVID Support Team, open Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Any questions or concerns received after business hours, will be responded to by the next business day. If you have a serious compliance concern that is suspected to cause health or safety issues after business hours, contact ULPD at 502-852-6111. ULPD will determine if there are other appropriate staff on call to address the concern or if an officer should assist.

logo KU Information Technology Menu link to menu page Search this unit Start search Submit Search Home IT Services and Software Zoom Eligibility: Faculty Staff Students Visitors Description — Zoom combines video conferencing, online meetings and in-conference group messaging into one easy-to-use tool that is ideal for KU conference rooms and online meetings. Zoom at KU allows for video, audio and content sharing among up to 100 participants on most devices, including Windows and Mac computers, Android and iOS devices, and KU video conferencing room systems (e.g., Polycom). Zoom connects with conference room systems (e.g., Polycom), as well as computers and mobile devices. Show MoreMore description Access — Participate in Meetings Download the Zoom app on your KU-Owned workstation, personal computer, and/or mobile device at KU's Zoom Video Conferencing website. Host/Schedule Meetings A KU Pro Zoom account is required to host/schedule meetings. Only KU faculty, staff and GTA/GRAs are eligible for KU Zoom Pro accounts. Sign in at KU's Zoom Video Conferencing website to get a KU Pro Zoom account. Show MoreMore description Support — Support is available through the KU Information Technology Customer Service Center at itcsc@ku.edu or 785-864-8080. Training and Tutorials — Visit the Zoom Help Center for information, guides and support.

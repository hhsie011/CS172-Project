Find out more about the closure here. The Mallet Chemistry Library serves the chemical research needs of the University of Texas at Austin, particularly for the students, faculty and staff of the Department of Chemistry, the Biochemistry Division of the Department of Molecular Biosciences and the Department of Chemical Engineering. The Chemistry Library's print collection totals over 100,000 volumes covering all aspects of chemistry, biochemistry, chemical engineering and food technology.

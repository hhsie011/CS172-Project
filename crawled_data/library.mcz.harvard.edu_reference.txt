Research Assistance There are many ways to tap the research potential of the Ernst Mayr Library. Library orientation sessions and research consultations are available upon request. Orientation sessions typically include a tour and explanation of the major research tools available in the Ernst Mayr Library. To schedule a group or individual appointment, please call 617-495-2475 or email the reference staff. Staff are also available for research instruction in labs, offices, or other locations outside of the library. "Top Ten Things Graduate Students Should Know About Using the Libraries at Harvard" is a great research starting point for all Harvard researchers.

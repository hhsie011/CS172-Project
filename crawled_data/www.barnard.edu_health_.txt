Do wellness your way — start here! Barnard believes that every area of your health — physical, mental, sexual, and emotional — is linked. Feel Well, Do Well @ Barnard is a campus-wide initiative that integrates the core principle of your wellness, your way to set the standard of what health and wellness can look like on a 21st-century college campus. Introducing Feel Well, Do Well @ Barnard Promoting Physical and Mental Health Well-Being Health and wellness are positively tied to academic excellence. Feel Well, Do Well @ Barnard empowers our students with health and wellness knowledge through coordinated campaigns and programs. Enhancing Social Connectedness Research shows that social connectedness has positive impacts on cognition, motivation, and overall health. Through training and activities, Feel Well, Do Well @ Barnard enhances social connectedness, recognizing that each member of our community has a role to play in creating a supportive environment. Ensuring Equity of Access to Services Feel Well, Do Well @ Barnard is focused on addressing disparities across campus for students, especially those having nondominant identities. We are using a culturally sensitive approach to investigate and address how inequity of access to services influences performance inside and outside of the classroom. COVID-19 Infection Control Measures at Barnard College The health and safety of our community is our top priority as we adapt during the COVID-19 pandemic, and we must all recognize that this is a shared responsibility as members of the Barnard community. Below are the measures our community is taking to ensure a safe and healthy environment: Campus and Community Safety Measures: COVID-19 vaccine requirement Masks/face coverings COVID-19 testing Community-oriented culture: Community Pledge Daily symptom attestations More information can be found on Barnard's COVID-19 information hub.

& Seminars Association Meetings Summits JSG Graduation Menu Home Education Graduate Find a Supervisor Find a Supervisor The following faculty and research scientists serve as supervisors for graduate students. The supervisor is a member of the Graduate Studies Committee who oversees a student’s program of work, directs the student’s dissertation or master’s thesis research, and serves as chair of the student’s supervisory committee. Incoming students can choose a supervisor during their first or second semester.

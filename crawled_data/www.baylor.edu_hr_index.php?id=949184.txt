Baylor offers a comprehensive and fulfilling intentionally-designed benefits package that demonstrates our Christian commitment and caring community in order to ensure employee success in their roles. Benefitfocus LoginExplore Benefits At Baylor, you and your family are cared for personally and professionally. Explore all of Baylor's benefits and rewards to see what consistently makes Baylor a "Great College to Work For." My Benefits Medical Affordable quality medical insurance partnered with Blue Cross Blue Shield of Texas offering excellent coverage. Dental Affordable dental insurance partnered with Cigna offering quality coverage. Vision Voluntary enrollment vision plan partnered with EyeMed Vision offered with affordable monthly premiums pre-tax. Retirement Eligible employees receive a University contribution to their 403(b) retirement income account managed by TIAA, after first year of employment AND free financial advisement through CAPTRUST. Time Off Generous time off available day 1 of employment such as vacation, sick, 10 recognized holidays, maternity, caregiver, and much more. Tuition Remission Available to full-time faculty and staff and their eligible spouse/dependents based on years of service. Enrollment Forms Accommodations Additional Benefit Programs Leadership & Learning Courses Wellbeing Baylor Wellbeing is designed to support the total wellbeing of Baylor faculty, staff, and their families by providing resources to promote the five dimensions of wellbeing: Physical, Financial, Spiritual, Emotional/Social, and Professional. Learn More About Wellbeing Advantages Baylor employees enjoy a wide range of advantages, benefits, perks, and privileges not often available at most universities. Athletics Tickets Faculty, staff, and retirees are eligible for discounted or free admission to campus Athletic events. About Athletics Tickets Dining Discounts & Options There are several on-campus dining options and many offer discounts up to 50% off based on an employee's status. About Dining Options Free Parking Faculty and staff receive on-campus parking access at no charge. About Free Parking Recreation, Fitness & Nutrition Baylor has a free on-site fitness center, nutrition advisement, and recreational activities for Baylor faculty, staff, and their families. About Campus Recreation Mayborn Museum The Mayborn Museum features natural science and cultural history focusing on Central Texas while offering multiple fun discovery rooms that encourage hands-on learning. About the Mayborn Campus Events Throughout the year, Baylor hosts many exciting campus-wide events such as Dr Pepper Hour, Diadeloso, Staff Forums, and many more to enjoy. About Campus Events Adoption Assistance As part of Baylor's commitment to a caring Christian community, the Adoption Assistance Program helps families going through the adoption process. About Adoption Assistance Free & Discounted Software Baylor ITS offers some software (free or discounted) for faculty and staff home use such as anti-virus, Adobe, and certain Microsoft products.

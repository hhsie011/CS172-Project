One of the award recipients is selected from the McLennan County Bar Association membership, and one of the recipients is selected from the McLennan County Young Lawyers Association. 2018 Goal: 2,500 hours Achieved: 2,570 hours Stephen Rispoli, Assistant Dean of Student Affairs and Pro Bono Programs at Baylor Law School presents Vic Feazell and Breanne M. Daniels with a 2018 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award 2018 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award winners are Vic Feazell, of Law Office of Vic Feazell, and Breanne M. Daniels, of Witt, McGregor & Bourland, PLLC. 2017 Goal: 2,500 hours Achieved: 2,520 hours Matt Czimskey, President of the Waco-McLennan County Young Lawyers Association presents David Cherry with a 2017 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award Kristen Parker was awarded a 2017 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award 2017 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award winners are David Cherry, Of Counsel, Haley & Olson, PC, and Kristen Parker of Kristen Parker Law. 2016 Goal: 2,000 hours Achieved: 2,450 hours Josh Borderud, Immediate Past President of the Waco-McLennan County Young Lawyers Association presents Michael Bourland with a 2016 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award Josh Borderud, Immediate Past President of the Waco-McLennan County Young Lawyers Association presents Marcy Wilkins with a 2016 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award 2016 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award winners are Michael Bourland of Witt, McGregor & Bourland, PLLC, and Marcy Winter Wilkins of Beard Kultgen Brophy Bostwick & Dickson, LLP. 2015 Goal: 1,000 hours Achieved: 2,269 hours 2015 Outstanding Pro Bono Advocate Award winners are Ralph E. Cooper, a solo practitioner, and Matt Czimskey of Beard Kultgen Brophy Bostwick & Dickson, LLP.

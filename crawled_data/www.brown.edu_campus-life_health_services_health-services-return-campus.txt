Health Services at Brown University Health Services provides wellness services and medical care to Brown students. Health Services and its affiliated departments aim to provide high-quality, patient-centered primary care. All services are confidential, easily accessible and responsive to your individual needs. 401-863-3953 Health Services recognizes that physical and emotional health and well-being are necessary in order for you to pursue your educational and personal goals at Brown University. Partnering with students, Health Services staff provides holistic care that is respectful of personal, cultural and social identities. Care options are designed to provide space for education, self-discovery and intellectual growth.

You are part of a diverse and vibrant community of students. Did you know that nearly 30% of Red Raiders identify as a First-Generation College Students? That is over 9,000 student who are the first in their family to pursue and earn their degree! All First-Generation College Red Raiders have access to First-Gen Peer Mentorship! (See requirements below): Admitted and Enrolled at Texas Tech University Identify as a First-Generation College Student Mentee Check List Attend at least 3 Peer Mentor Meetings Attend at least 2 events throughout the semester What you should ask yourself: Who is my First-Gen Peer Mentor?

The shuttle bus service complies with ADA specifications to connect the Project Site to subway stations at: 96th Street and Broadway 116th Street and Broadway (Morningside campus) 125th Street and Broadway Harlem Hospital Center (135th Street and Lenox Avenue) Columbia University Medical Center (168th Street and Broadway) The shuttle bus service runs on a regular schedule throughout the day on every weekday, except on state and federal public holidays. Eligibility The shuttle bus is free to the elderly and disabled in the local community (as well as their attendants).

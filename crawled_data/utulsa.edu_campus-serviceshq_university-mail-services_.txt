University Mail Services is a full-service operation offering all postal services including insured mail, and priority, express, and parcel-post package services. FedEx shipments also are available at a discounted rate. Manager Leah Leffingwell mail.services@utulsa.edu 918-631-2211 Location, Hours, and Services East end of Allen Chapman Student Union 3135 East 5th Place Tulsa, OK 74104 United States Postal Service & Package Window 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Monday through Thursday 8:30 a.m. to Noon Friday (Summer Hours) University Mail Services is closed on weekends. Mail Services observes all TU Holidays. Payment Methods We accept cash, credit, debit, check, and Hurricane Gold dollars. Amazon Lockers Located on the east end of the Student Union patio is an Amazon Hub Locker, a secure, self-service delivery location for packages from Amazon.com. Upon checkout, select “Pick up from an Amazon Locker” and choose “Amazon Hub Locker – Pepe | at The University of Tulsa.” Once your package is delivered you will have three days to pick up your package from the Amazon locker before it will be returned. You can ensure your package is secure and can pick it up at a convenient time as this location is available 24 hours, 7 days a week. Click here for more information about Amazon Hub Lockers. Mail Pickup Outgoing mail pickup from the Postal Service is at 4:00 p.m. Monday through Friday. A drop slot is available inside the building for your stamped mail. Post Office Box Rental Post Office Box rental is available for $40 a year, $15 per semester or $10 for the summer session. No prepayment is required as this service can be billed to your university personal account. How to Address TU Mail Use this format to address student mail and packages: First and Last Name Physical street address Residence Hall or Apartment Number Tulsa, OK 74104 Mail must be addressed to a student who is registered with the campus housing as living on campus. Mail Delivery to Campus Housing Residence Halls Mail Services is also responsible for the delivery of student mail to all of the residence halls. Residence Hall students’ packages can be picked up at Mail Services located in the student union. A notice will be sent to your TU student email address the day the package is received in the University Mail Services. Student ID and signature are required to receive packages. Package pickup hours are between the hours of 8:30 a.m. and 4:00 p.m., Monday–Friday. If you are unable to come and pick up your package you can email mail services and designate another person: mail.services@utulsa.edu Campus Apartments United States Postal Service (USPS) delivers letters directly to students who reside in TU’s Campus Apartments. Packages shipped by USPS will be delivered to Mail Services in the student union and students will receive an email notification. Norman Village residents will pick up all USPS from the mail services located in the student union. If you are unable to come and pick up your package you can email mail services and designate another person: mail.services@utulsa.edu. Package delivery companies will deliver to individual apartment doors. Greek Housing United States Postal Service (USPS) delivers directly to students who reside in Sorority and Fraternity houses. Package delivery companies will also deliver to the individual greek houses.

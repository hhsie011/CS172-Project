August 27, 2021 Hours of Operation Fall Rush Hours: September 4 Saturday: 10:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. Closed: September 5 and 6 Regular Fall Hours: September 7 - December 18 Mon – Fri: 9:00 a.m. – 9:00 p.m. Saturday: 10:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m. Sunday: CLOSED How to Receive Mail   on Campus The Student Mail Center is located in the lower level of Wien Hall. Your mail should be addressed to you using the following format:   Your Full Name  [UNI] or Mailbox Number* Columbia Student Mail   70 Morningside Drive   New York, NY 10027-7236   *Students who lived on campus at any point during the 2020-21 Academic Year may have been assigned a mailbox number. Those students can continue to use their mailbox number or may transition to using the UNI in the mailing address. In-room Package Delivery Columbia Mail makes every effort to deliver packages to your room before you arrive on campus. We will accept mail and packages starting August 2. Items received before August 19 will be pre-delivered to the resident's room. Items received after August 19 can be picked up at the Student Mail Center after you arrive.

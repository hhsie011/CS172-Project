Let our knowledgeable conference service professionals help you to stay organized and ensure that your conference goals are met, ultimately providing a welcoming and positive campus experience for your event guests. Lodging Solutions Offering both single and double room layouts, all rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with a bed (extra-long twin), dresser, desk, chair, trash can, recycling container, and window coverings. Learn More & View Rates Meeting Spaces With venues for events of all sizes, the University of Miami allows conference planners to be creative in planning events at The U. Together with overnight accommodations and food and beverage services, we can provide all of your conference needs. Learn More & View Rates Food & Beverage The Office of Conference Services connects event planners with a variety of both on- and off-campus food service vendors to deliver high-quality dining and catering options for budgets both big and small. Learn More Amenities Event planners can rest assured that we offer all of the amenities your attendees expect - and even some they may not!

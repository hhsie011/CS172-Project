Three Ways You Can Get Materials FSU OneSearch Please check FSU Libraries OneSearch before requesting materials. UBorrow For Faster delivery of Print Books search the FPALN Statewide Library Catalog of all 40 public university and college university libraries. Login to request books from other libraries through Uborrow. Verify the prepopulated information and Send Request. Interlibrary Loan (ILLiad) Log in to ILLiad to request articles, books, and more from libraries around the world Library Express Delivery Service (LEDS) Current FSU faculty, staff, post-docs, and all graduate assistants may have materials delivered to their department offices. Article requests are emailed directly. Select Dept. Office in your Illiad USER INFORMATION account. Select Dept. Office as your pickup location when placing your UBorrow and ILL requests.

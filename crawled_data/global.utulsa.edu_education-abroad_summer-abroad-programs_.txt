Students with heavily structured academic programs or situations that prevent them from being away for longer than 3 to 5 weeks often enroll in summer programs. Summer programs also offer the opportunities for students to try something new or acquire new experiences. There are many summer programs across the globe including programs that offer internships and undergraduate research. TU utilizes a select group of providers to offer the quality programs thereby providing many choices for students.

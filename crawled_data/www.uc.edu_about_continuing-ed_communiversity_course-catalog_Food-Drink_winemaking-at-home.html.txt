Register SUMMER WINEMAKING WORKSHOP Thu., Jun. 30, 6-8 pm / $59 / One Class Includes ingredients for wine, a receptacle, and a wine book. Instructor: Brandon Nelson In-person at VPC Campus Back by popular demand! This term we'll teach you how to make a summer-style wine at home. Winemaking can be a fruitful and satisfying hobby. This course will cover all the essential materials you will need to start your own batch and monitor its progress from fermentation to bottling. We’ll talk about additional wines you can create, limited only by your imagination! Students will start their own gallon of wine in class to take home and complete the fermentation process. We will also enjoy a tasting of homemade wine and you'll leave with a book and recipes for future batches! About Your Instructor Brandon Nelson Brandon Nelson is an IT technician by trade with a passion for microbiology. He has become an expert homebrewer and winemaker with a fascination for the process of fermentation. Fun fact: Brandon also grows mushrooms at home!

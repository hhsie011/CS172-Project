Criminal Justice Career Expo Criminal Justice Career Expo right arrow down arrow Event Information Criminal Justice Career Expo The annual Criminal Justice Career Expo is a major event that provides networking opportunities with over 30 professionals in fields of law enforcement, asset protection, social work, human services, and psychology. This event is hosted by the School of Criminal Justice at the University of Cincinnati. Event Info Student Registration Industry Registration The School of Criminal Justice hosts this free public event, which allows attendees to explore the variety of career opportunities for individuals interested in pursuing a degree in Criminal Justice while also networking with HR professionals and hiring managers who are seeking passionate talent within the industry the industries of law enforcement, social work, corrections, youth advocacy, human services, and psychology. This event will be held virtually and will require participants to register to attend. Come and network with local, regional, and national organizations who are seeking to hire qualified candidates and interns. Feel free to stop by at any time throughout the day and stay as long as you would like.

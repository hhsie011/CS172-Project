LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT / Our Mission and Offerings CWD offers live, instructor-led workshops & other online learning options! CWD is offering live workshops led by CWD instructors via Zoom as well as on-demand online programs. See our flyer for class schedule and details. Managers and team members alike will appreciate the Working Remotely courses on the Harvard Training Portal, a curated collection of LinkedIn Learning courses with tips on how to increase productivity and stay connected while working from home. You can also explore other online learning through LinkedIn Learning and Harvard ManageMentor. Our Mission As part of Harvard Human Resources, the Center for Workplace Development (CWD) provides programs, tools, coaching, and consulting to Harvard employees and administrative and faculty leaders to achieve individual and organizational effectiveness. Our Vision To unleash the powerful potential of Harvard’s employees to fuel organizational success. Offerings The Center for Workplace Development (CWD) offers something for everyone, from communication and management skills and English for Speakers of Other Languages (ESOL) classes to leadership programs, management coaching and career development courses. Whether you’re an executive looking to enhance your leadership skills, a manager charged with driving organizational change, or an individual contributor seeking to broaden your skill set, we’ve got you covered. Check out some of our courses here. Resilience, Recovery, Renewal Initiative The Resilience, Recovery and Renewal Initiative aims to support all employees, managers, and administrative and faculty leaders through the COVID-19-related impact to our work, as we learn how to respond, re-engage, and emerge stronger as individuals and an organization. Explore training, consulting, coaching, education, toolkits and more related to the first phase of Resilience.

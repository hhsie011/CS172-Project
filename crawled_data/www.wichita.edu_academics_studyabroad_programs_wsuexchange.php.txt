These agreements allow WSU students to pay their regular tuition and fees to WSU, which makes study abroad extremely affordable for most students. In most cases, the students will pay their housing and meal costs at the host institution. The following exchange agreements are currently active: ENGLAND University of Chester FRANCE University d'Orleans GERMANY Hamburg University of Applied Sciences JAPAN Aoyama Gakuin University Kansai Gaidai University Okayama University MEXICO Tecnologico de Monterrey (26 campuses nationwide) SOUTH KOREA Dankook University SPAIN University of Deusto (Bilbao Campus) Business School Exchange Programs AUSTRIA University of Applied Sciences , Wiener Neustadt GERMANY Berlin School of Economics & Law FRANCE ESCE ESC Pau Business School SPAIN University of Deusto (San Sebastian Campus) Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB) SWEDEN Jonkoping International Business School (JIBS) Note: If you are currently a student at one of our exchange partner universities listed above and wish to apply to study as an exchange student at WSU for one semester or one year, please contact the Study Abroad Advisor at your university and complete our Exchange Student Application.

Discover how to create, revise, and implement learning environments across a wide range of contexts. Chat Now Request Info Program At A Glance 20 Months to Complete Limited Seats Applicable Course Work 1 Cumulative Project SACSCOC Accredited GRE waived for eligible applicants Explore the theory behind cognitive and non-cognitive learning processes The Master of Science in Education Applied Learning Sciences provides students with expert knowledge in the design of learning environments and curriculums that can be applied immediately within the workplace. Students will complete a cumulative project in place of a thesis. The modular structure of the program is flexible and allows students to choose electives to tailor the degree to your personal and professional interests. Earn a Degree That Will Enable a Rewarding Career Students who graduate from the Master of Science in Education Applied Learning Sciences program can pursue a career in formal education settings such as universities, K-12 schools with roles in curriculum development and instructional design. Unlike other masters of education programs, UOnline graduates can move beyond the traditional education sphere and consider roles in professional learning design, training, and organizational development roles in consultancies and training providers. Entrepreneurial graduates can look at developing learning products or technologies for education technology companies. Where do you see yourself? Study Online and Learn from Campus Faculty Learn from experienced, well-respected professionals. The Applied Learning Sciences faculty are a multilingual and multicultural group of academics and professionals who have a wealth of experience in different areas of education. All online faculty members also teach on campus. Meet your faculty What is it Like to Study in UOnline?

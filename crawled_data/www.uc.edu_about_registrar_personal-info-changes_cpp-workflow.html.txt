Undergraduate Change of College Form allows students to make changes to their current career path or apply for readmission. Accessing the Form Program Changes & Readmissions Form Login If you have forgotten your UCID, you can retrieve it using the Get My UCID online utility. To reset your password, use the UC Password Self-Service Portal. The first time you log in, you will be on the New Change screen. You will be asked "What is the goal of your visit today?" Continue reading this page to see what types of changes you are able to make. If you have a pending submission you will be on the My Forms screen upon logging in and able to view your submission there. Types of Changes Below are the five possible options you will see when accessing the form. Click on the links below for more information regarding each change and instructions for completing the form. If you are unsure which option you should select, please contact your academic advisor before submitting your request. Apply for Readmission to UC Change Existing Major to a New Major Add a Major Change Major Specialization Drop an Existing Major Checking the Status of Your Form Submissions Requiring Your Action After submitting your request, log back in to view your My Forms screen. Submissions requiring an action on your part to Confirm or Cancel will be shown. Submissions Waiting to be Processed If you do not have any available action items such as Review, Confirm, or Cancel, your submission has been sent for final processing. You will not be able to take any action until this has been completed and your student degree record has been officially updated in Catalyst. Finalized Submissions Once your request has been finalized, the Status will change to Registrar-Accepted, and you will be able to Print your submission for your records. Still have questions? If you have any questions or concerns, please contact your college or academic advisor.

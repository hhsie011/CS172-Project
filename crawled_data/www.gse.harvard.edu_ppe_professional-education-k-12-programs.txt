Professional Education K-12 Programs Professional Education is a vital part of the HGSE community, which bridges research and practice through a robust portfolio of professional development programs. Designed to provide actionable solutions for teachers, coaches, school-level leaders, system-level leaders, and other education professionals, our programs aim to transform the way educators approach and solve problems and help educators deliver on the promise of an excellent education for every student. Our programs are uniquely tailored to a variety of roles and accommodate a range of learning styles and preferences – ranging from one day to year-long engagements, both online and on campus. We also collaborate with a wide range of districts and education organizations to deliver tailored professional development plans for large groups or teams.

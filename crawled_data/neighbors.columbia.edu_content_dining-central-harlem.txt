Dining in Central Harlem Extending from the north end of Central Park at 110th Street to the Harlem River, between 5th and St. Nicholas Avenues, Central Harlem has a diverse population and rich cultural heritage. This is reflected in the variety of cuisines available at its many restaurants, from Frederick Douglass Boulevard's restaurant row to busy 125th Street and beyond.

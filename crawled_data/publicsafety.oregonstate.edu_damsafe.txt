You can alert your friends to be a virtual companion and follow your progress through the “Track Me” feature. When you safely arrive at your destination, you then stop the virtual tracking. The App’s red Alert Button allows users to alert designated guardians with their exact location in case of emergencies by tapping the app icon. It also provides one-button calling to campus Public Safety.

Nominations open October 15th and close December 31st each year. Any alum of Clemson University may nominate a person to be an Honorary Alumnus. Applicants will be judged based on an individual’s outstanding service to Clemson and/or the Alumni Association, lifelong devotion to Clemson University and demonstrated loyalty to Clemson and the Alumni Association.

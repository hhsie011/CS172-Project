These fully accredited programs are designed with asynchronous classes so you can complete course work based on deadlines, not assigned class meetings. Our online courses also provide flexibility, so wherever you can make a high speed internet connection, you can access your class and the many resources available to you. The following degree programs can be completed entirely online. Undergraduate Child Development and Family Studies Child Life Specialist Early Intervention Specialist Early Care and Learning Health Services Information Technology Legal Studies Liberal Studies Organizational Leadership Graduate Human Resources Leadership Liberal Studies (Master level) Liberal Studies (Doctor level) Strategic Leadership Training and Development Graduate Certificates Aviation Human Factors Liberal Studies Strategic Leadership Getting started in your online courses Before registering for an online course, be sure to read all course footnotes in the schedule of classes and contact the department in which the course is offered to determine additional requirements. Though they are engaging and convenient, they do require high levels of self-discipline and motivation. To ensure a successful online experience, it is essential to have regular and reliable access to appropriate computer equipment and a high speed Internet connection. You must also have an active University of Memphis email account.

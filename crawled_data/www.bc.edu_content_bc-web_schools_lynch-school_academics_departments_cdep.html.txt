Psychology (CDEP) Learn how to promote purposeful lives from the inside out in the Counseling, Developmental, and Educational Psychology program. Together, these programs focus on advancing the science of the mind and improving the well-being of children and adults, spurring positive social change in the process. prepared to lead the field at top colleges and universities, policy centers, government agencies, and non-government organizations around the world.</li> </ul> <p>Building the next generation of intellectual leaders in developmental and educational psychology who are pursuing research, teaching, and organizational leadership to improve the lives of children, families, and communities.<br />

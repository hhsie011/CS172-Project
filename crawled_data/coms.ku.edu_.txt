In Communication Studies, we explore the ways in which human communication can help build communities, bridge cultures, organize people, and strengthen relationships. Our award-winning faculty both research and teach in two major areas: Relationships & Social Interaction and Rhetoric & Political Communication. With nearly 400 undergraduate majors and 50 graduate students, we are committed to offering a welcoming educational environment and maintaining the highest standards in both undergraduate and graduate teaching.

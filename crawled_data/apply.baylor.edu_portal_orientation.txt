All experiences are open to new first-year and transfer students. During the months of June and July, Baylor offers 12 Orientation sessions, 11 of which are offered as a Combined Session with our extended orientation program called Baylor Line Camp. This allows you to attend both programs during a 5-day campus experience. Limited opportunities are also available to attend a two-day Orientation Only Session. There is no cost to attend Orientation, and guests are encouraged to attend. The Baylor Line Camp portion is just for students and requires a $125 registration fee. Skip to Registration Taking Summer Classes? SUMMER OF DISCOVERY STUDENTS: Please be sure to select program dates that do not conflict with your summer course term. Summer 1 dates are June 3 – July 8. Summer 2 dates are July 11 – August 12.

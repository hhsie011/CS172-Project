Learn how Building-Level Leadership/Principal is the right fit for you. Contact Coordinator Curriculum The certificate program requires the completion of 15-21 credit hours. Applicants who have prior coursework in multicultural or diversity training and research can have these courses evaluated and potentially waived, resulting in a program of 15 credit hours. Students are required to complete an applied learning or research experience. This requirement can be met through practicum experiences. View the curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission to the program: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

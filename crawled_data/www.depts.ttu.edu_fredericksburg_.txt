Begin your journey as a Red Raider! Contact us and let us know what you are interested in! Academic Programs Why TTU at Fredericksburg? At the Fredericksburg Regional Site, you have the convenience of being able to take a majority of courses online, but also the benefit of belonging to a community of learners. By taking hybrid classes, you'll get to know and work with local faculty and other local students. Complete with knowledgeable admissions and advising staff as well as helpful IT personnel, we're here to make sure you succeed! And with state-of-the-art facilities, wine labs and a teaching vineyard, students can also have hands-on learning experiences to prepare for a career in the wine-making industry.

Undergraduate Research and Creative Activity Forum The Undergraduate Research and Creative Activity Forum (URCAF) provides an opportunity for undergraduate students at Wichita State University to present their scholarly and creative activity to a faculty, student and community audience, while competing for cash awards. Each student's work at the forum is presented under the mentorship of a WSU faculty member. This year's URCAF will be held on April 15, 2022, at the Rhatigan Student Center. For more information (including this year's schedule of events), click on the "General Information".

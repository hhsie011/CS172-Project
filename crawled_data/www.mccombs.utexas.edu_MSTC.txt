MSTC Make It Innovative Here The MS in Technology Commercialization degree connects you with the people, knowledge, and skills to take your ideas to market. Begin Your Application Process Where Business, Tech, and Innovation Meet. Our MSTC program was the first-ever go-to-market graduate program in the U.S. Designed as a launchpad for your businesses and products, MSTC gives you everything you need to take your dreams to market either through a venture of your own or an existing organization. You can attend classes onsite at the McCombs School of Business at UT Austin, or online via video conferencing. Launch Your Dreams. Earn Your Master’s. We know that excellence isn’t tied to your undergraduate major or any specific test score, so we look at candidates holistically and evaluate your fit with the program as a whole. Read About our Admissions Process Learn Practical Skills from Top Academics Here, you’ll learn from cutting-edge research that’s helped our students launch technologies into successful ventures. You’ll start in May and finish the next May, with classes that meet on alternating weekends.

Our division embraces the vision of Seton Hall University as a home for the mind, the heart and the spirit, and our goal is student success. The Division of Student Services strives to educate the whole person and to help our students be successful in the classroom and on the playing fields, in their relationships with faculty and with friends, and in their growth and development toward a chosen career. We welcome students in crisis, students at academic crossroads, students who are questioning their personal choices, and students who are seeking involvement in the community that is Seton Hall University. For Parents The several offices of the Division provide essential services - like housing and security - but the primary mission of the Division is to support student success and enrich the experience of Seton Hall students by providing rich and varied opportunities for personal growth and development. Learn more information relevant to parents » For Students The Division of Student Services is comprised of several departments that provide programs and services that will enrich your experience and support your ability to succeed at Seton Hall and beyond.

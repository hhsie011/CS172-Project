Frank Barton School of Business Finance, Real Estate and Decision Sciences The graduate certificate in business analytics from Wichita State’s AACSB-accredited Barton School of Business will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to analyze data, visualize it, predict future performance and gain insight into developing future business strategies. The certificate is a valuable asset to any business major. Learn how Business Analytics is the right fit for you. Contact coordinator at sue.abdinnour@wichita.edu Curriculum This 12-credit-hour certificate consists of four courses chosen from the areas of business intelligence, statistical methods, forecasting, modeling, and database planning and management. View the Curriculum Admission to the program There are two paths to admission: For those already enrolled in graduate school, simply complete the Declaration of Intent to Pursue a Certificate Form. Prospective students should apply using the Graduate School application for admission.

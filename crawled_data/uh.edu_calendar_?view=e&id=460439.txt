Submit an Event Staff Council Meet & Greet Friday, May 6, 2022 11:30 am - 1:00 pm The Staff Council Meet and Greet offers UH staff members an opportunity to meet Staff Council members over lunch at Cougar Woods Dining Commons where staff can openly share and discuss any staff topic or issues, and learn more about getting involved with Staff Council in a friendly atmosphere over food. In addition, Staff Council Meet and Greet serves as an engagement opportunity for new Staff Council members to be mentored and to socialize with the rest of Staff Council. The University of Houston requires guests on campus to follow Prevention Protocols, which should be reviewed before visiting. Location Cougar Woods Dining Commons Cost All staff members will be responsible for covering their own entrance to Cougar Woods Dining Commons.

View Salaries and Statistics Data >90% Of Texas Engineering undergraduates are employed or attend graduate school upon graduation $77K Average starting salary for B.S. graduates 1500+ Employers recruit Texas Engineering students every year In the Engineering Career Assistance Center (ECAC), students are guided through the recruitment process with career counseling and workshops to help develop resumes and interviewing and job searching skills. Throughout the year, we host employers and recruiters for student interviews, career fairs and information sessions. During their academic careers, students have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through externships and co-op and internship positions to help prepare them for full-time employment after graduation. We encourage students and employers to utilize our job posting database and take advantage of our on-campus recruiting opportunities.

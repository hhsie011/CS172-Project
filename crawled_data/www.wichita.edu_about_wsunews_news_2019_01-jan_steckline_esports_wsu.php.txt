January 25, 2019Engineeringcomputer scienceCampus Lifeesports Junior grows passion for gaming through WSU's Esports program By Julian Liby, Undergraduate Admissions Meet Blane 'Deadly' Steckline, the Damage Per Second player for the Wichita State Overwatch team. In addition to being a junior computer science major, Blane is also a Grandmaster-tier Overwatch player, reached rank No. 64 on the North American server, and has competitive gaming experience at a semi-professional level. Take a listen to what Blane has to say about his experience so far with the Esports program at Wichita State and how it's allowed him to combine his passion for gaming with his college experience.

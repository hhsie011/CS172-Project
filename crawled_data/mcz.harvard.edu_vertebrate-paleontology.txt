Since its founding in 1859, the MCZ has amassed approximately 90,000 specimens of fossil fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds and mammals. Past Curators include: Charles E. Hamlin (Assistant) (1880-1886), Alpheus Hyatt (Assistant) (1886-1889), Charles Rochester Eastman (Assistant staring in 1894)(1896-1910), Alfred Sherwood Romer (1934-1963), Bryan Patterson (1963-1970) ...

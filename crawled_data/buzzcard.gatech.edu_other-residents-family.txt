Family member gtID#s are generated by the BuzzCard Center. A BuzzCard will be required to access GT facilities: Building & door access Parking Deck entry CRC Access Application Process To request a BuzzCard for your family member or other resident, you will need to complete a request form and include the following with your submission: Sponsor (enrolled student, staff or faculty) information: name, GTID #, email Family member (for whom the request is being made) information: legal name, date of birth Attach verification document for the family member: Acceptable Documents: Marriage Certificate Health insurance Card F2 Visa Photo (of family member): passport style photo. No selfies, please. Requests will be processed in 2-3 business days. This form takes advantage of the Georgia Tech Login Service. You will be required to login with your GT username.

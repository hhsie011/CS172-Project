Gateways Alumni Outcomes The overwhelming majority of Gateways students who apply to medical school on completion of the program are accepted. After Gateways Historically 87-97% of each class applies to medical school. Others pursue a variety of interesting career paths. All students and alumni receive close mentoring and advising throughout their time at Brown as well as after graduation. On average 92% of graduating students applied to medical school (4 year data) For Class of 2018 & 2019: 92% of those who applied have been accepted to medical school So far 85% of those who applied from the class of 2020 and 86% from the class of 2021 have been accepted to medical school (in progress) For Class of 2020 and 2021: applications are still in progress**; many 2020 applications were disrupted due to COVID-19 related delays in MCATs etc. (**Most students apply to medical school at the end of the program; some take an additional gap year before applying; therefore, accurate medical school admissions data lags by 1-2 years after graduation) Medical School Admissions Med.

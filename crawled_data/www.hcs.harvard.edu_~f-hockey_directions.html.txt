Harvard's athletic facilities are state-of-the-art, and feature Jordan Field. One of the premier field hockey venues in the nation, this Astroturf field is home to the Harvard Field Hockey team. Directions: Jordan Field is located near Harvard Stadium in the Harvard Athletic Complex. From West: Storrow Dr. to Harvard Square exit. Right onto North Harvard St. One-half mile, enter Gate 6 on right. From East: Storrow Dr. to Harvard Square exit. Left onto North Harvard St. One-half mile, enter Gate 6 on right. From Mass Pike: Exit 18 to Storrow Drive Westbound As above.

This award recognizes Julia's excellent academic and research record, as well as the potential of her upcoming senior thesis. Each year, the award is given to a Junior in one of the DEEPS concentrations. It was established in honor of Sarah LaMendola '04 through the generosity of her family and friends. The award supports students who demonstrate outstanding potential for undergraduate research. We are proud to recognize Julia with this prestigious award.

Academic Programs Clemson University offers both online and blended programs. Both formats are instructor-driven and grant students flexible access to course content. Online programs offer all required course content online and in ways that honor student schedules. Blended programs require students to interact via scheduled online meetings, hybrid (a mix of online and in-person) courses, independent studies, and/or traditional on-campus courses. M.Ed./Ed.S.

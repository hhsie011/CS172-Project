We offer a standalone MSIS degree as well as an MBA/MSIS joint degree option. No previous background in MIS is required in order to apply. In the MSIS program, you can pursue a degree program tailored to your specific needs and structured around your career goals. We offer specific concentrations that can be completed within your MSIS program of study, including: Business Analytics Cyber Security Entrepreneurship and Corporate Innovation For areas of interest not currently offered as concentrations, we can target your program of study to focus on areas such as: Application Development Management of the IS/IT Function Click HERE to learn more about our programs.

Undergraduate Graduate Contact Us School of Nursing Given the rapidly aging population and the increasing need for health services, the demand for all levels of nursing continues to grow. Wichita State offers several undergraduate and graduate degrees to help meet this need. Our quality programs exceed national pass rates on the Registered Nurse national exam and Advanced Practice Registered Nurse professional certification exams. We offer flexibility for students with diverse educational goals. Our programs appeal to students because of our passionate and distinguished faculty, partnering clinical facilities and the practical hands-on experience received through practicum, clinical simulation and scholarly activities relevant to practice.

Penn Computing There is no single computer center at Penn. Responsibility for computing support is shared by Penn's twelve Schools, its business units, and the Office of Information Systems & Computing (ISC). This site focuses on University-wide services, many of them provided by ISC, and offers entry points to School computing information.

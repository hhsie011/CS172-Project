for: Alumni Events Alumni Eventsbrackman2019-12-17T17:52:19-05:00 The University of Miami Alumni Association works to keep you involved and active by planning many events and programs throughout the year. Whether you’re returning for Alumni Weekend & Homecoming, joining fellow alumni across the country for alumni club events, or coming to one of our numerous special events, such as receptions with university leaders and alumni networking events, the UMAA offers something for everyone. For more information, please visit the University of Miami Alumni Association website. Alumni Weekend & Reunions Alumni Weekend and Homecoming is an annual celebration where all alumni gather on campus to celebrate being a ‘Cane! The weekend is full of campus traditions such as the Audrey R. Finkelstein UM Experience, Class Reunion Celebrations, Homecoming traditions such as the Parade, Boat Burning, Pep Rally and Fireworks, Homecoming Pregame and Game, the Annual Golden Ibis Society Celebration Brunch, and a number of gatherings and weekend extras are hosted throughout the weekend. Regardless of your graduation year, campus involvement, or area of study, there is something for everyone! Welcome to the Neighborhood Whether you are new to the area or returning to your hometown, you are invited to meet and network with fellow ‘Canes in your neighborhood. Join recent graduates and members of your local alumni club to learn more about the University of Miami alumni community in your area and how you can get involved. Summer Send-offs Alumni around the country welcome the newest members of the University of Miami through Summer Send-off events. Game Watch Parties For those ‘Canes fans who are unable to travel to Miami Hurricanes football games, be sure to join your fellow alumni and friends at University of Miami Alumni Association Game Watch Parties taking place across the country!

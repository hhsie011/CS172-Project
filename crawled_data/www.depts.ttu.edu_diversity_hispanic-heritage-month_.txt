September 14 What is servingness in Hispanic Serving Institution? Panel presentation. 4:00PM - 5:30PM Virtual - Register online Hosted by: Division of Diversity, Equity & Inclusion This informative panel discussion will provide attendees with opportunities to learn from leading scholars and administrators regarding the notion of "servingess" and the advancement of our ability to intentionally serve our students.

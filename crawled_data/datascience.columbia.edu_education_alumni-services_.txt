Columbia As one of the 600+ Columbia data science alumni worldwide, you are an integral part of an elite network of talented data scientists and data engineers. By partnering with the DSI Career Development and Alumni Services team, you make this unique alumni network even more powerful. Connect Have you joined our alumni LinkedIn group? DSI alumni connections have helped secure internships and jobs for current students and former classmates. You may also network with students, alumni, and industry leaders during our quarterly industry meetups. Mentor Are you available to support members of this community as they explore potential trajectories to solidify their career goals? You may share your own experiences during an alumni roundtable, industry panels, mock technical interviews, information sessions for targeted cohorts of students, and career fairs. Advance Would you like to change course? Your status as a Columbia DSI alum allows you to take advantage of our career development services regardless of your age or level of experience. You may request appointments with our career counselors, participate in on-campus recruiting events, attend job fairs, and utilize our employment and networking resources We’re here for you We welcome your ideas. Please contact John Hyde, jh4253@columbia.edu, to discuss your recommendations for our career and alumni programs.

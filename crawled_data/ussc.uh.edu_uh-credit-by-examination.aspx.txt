During the Spring 2022 term, our office will serve students both in-person and remotely. Testing Services provides a wide range of academic testing and evaluation services to UH students and the community. We strive to make each testing experience as successful as possible. Tests are administered by, including but not limited to Placement Exams, the DSST Program (formerly the DANTES Program), and the TSI Assessment (TSIA2). Testing Services is equipped for computerized and paper-and-pencil tests. For specific information about the exams offered visit the Examination List page.

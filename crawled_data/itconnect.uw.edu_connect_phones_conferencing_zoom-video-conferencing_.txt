Watch a quick video on how to download and sign in to UW Zoom About UW Zoom Zoom helps you to Share screens and host real-time video conversations Participate in group video chat during online class sessions Host virtual office hours Collaborate remotely on research With a paid add-on, host live web broadcasts to thousands of people worldwide Record to the cloud or computer for easy sharing (storage for 90 days) Conduct HIPAA-compliant telehealth sessions Zoom is compatible with room systems With a paid add-on, Zoom can connect with SIP- or H.323-based video conference devices such as: Polycom Lifesize Cisco Zoom is mobile friendly Connect on the go with Zoom iPhone and iPad app and Zoom Android app Zoom includes a phone conference line A free phone conference line is included with your Zoom license. Participants of your web conference can simply dial a phone number and code to enter your meeting. This is helpful if internet access is not available for participants.

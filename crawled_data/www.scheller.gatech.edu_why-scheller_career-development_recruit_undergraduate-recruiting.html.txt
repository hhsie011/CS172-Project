We are committed to providing exceptional customer service to help you find the talent you are seeking. Our undergraduate program graduates students with a broad education in business, combined with focused study in one or more of the following areas: Operations, IT Management, Marketing, Accounting, Finance, Strategy and Innovation, Leading and Managing Human Capital, and General Management. Georgia Tech students perform extremely well in internships and full-time positions after graduation, based on their strong leadership, problem-solving abilities and excellent communication skills. With 1,300 students in our undergraduate program, we have many talented prospective employees for you to meet. We make it easy for you to post roles and recruit the talent you're seeking. THE SCHELLER DIFFERENCE The Scheller Undergraduate Career Services team posts and advertises internships, full time jobs, and co-ops directly and exclusively to the business school population. Students may apply via CareerBuzz, your website, or an email resume drop. View current internship and full time pay information HOW TO POST INTERNSHIPS Scheller students complete internships throughout all four years of college, during fall, spring, and summer semesters. Students are able to deliver real value for their employers. Internships generally run for approximately 12 weeks during the course of a semester. To post your internship to the business school population, email the Career Services team including: Company name Internship duration Confirmation of pay Means of application (link to an online application or an email address for resume drop) Job description The Career Services team will post your internship immediately upon receipt. Five Term Institute Calendar View the start and end dates for each semester to help you plan your internship duration. Five Term Institute Calendar FULL TIME JOBS Scheller graduates students at the end of each semester: December, May, and August. To post your full time job opportunity to upcoming graduates, email Stan Broome or Michael Dutcher including: Company name Means of application (link to an online application or an email address for resume drop) Job description The Career Services team will post your full time job immediately upon receipt. CO-OP PROGRAM Co-ops are registered and coordinated via Georgia Tech's central career office, the Center for Career Discovery and Development. This program allows students to build experience with your organization on a predetermined schedule. Students work full-time at your company every other semester until they have worked three terms. Center for Career Discovery and Development While your co-op will be registered and advertised via the Center for Career Discovery and Development, we are also happy to advertise it directly to business students. To advertise it directly to business students email Stan Broome or Michael Dutcher including: Company name Co-op duration Confirmation of pay Means of application (link to an online application or an email address for resume drop) Job description Need help defining your recruitment strategy?

Learn More Our Programs Community Impact is made up of over 27 different groups servicing the Harlem, Morningside Heights and Washington Heights areas. Our groups partner with service organizations, social service offices, religious institutions, senior centers and schools to serve over 8,000 people each year. Learn more About Asian Youth Program Asian Youth Program (AYP), in partnership with Womankind (formerly New York Asian Women’s Center), offers mentoring to children impacted by domestic violence. AYP mentors become positive role models engaging in one-to-one relationships through enrichment activities and recreational trips to cultural sites around New York City. All activities are designed to build self-confidence, foster a sense of family and community, and inspire a commitment to service among the children served in a fun and nurturing environment.

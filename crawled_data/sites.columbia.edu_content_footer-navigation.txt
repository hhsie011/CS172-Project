This is typically used to link into popular or important pages on your site. IMPORTANT: By default, the top level menu items of your site will also appear in the footer. By following the instructions below you can override the default and create custom navigation links. From the top navigation menu click Site Settings. Select Menus tab. Expand FOOTER NAV. Enter the Links and corresponding Titles. NOTE: You can enter a maximum of five links for the footer navigation. They display vertically on the footer.

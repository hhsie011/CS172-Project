What is involved? How can it help? In this session, we’ll cover some of the practices and theory of ethnography, including online ethnography, participant-observation, surveys, interviews, and autoethnography. We’ll also discuss how to incorporate it into the classroom as well as your research with real-world examples. Please register below!

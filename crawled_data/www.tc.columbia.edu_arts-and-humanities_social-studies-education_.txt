Apply Now Welcome to the Social Studies Education program Social Studies education at Teachers College is one of the originators of the field. Today, we take advantage of our location in the New York City to guide students’ inquiries about the social world. Through your graduate education at TC, you’ll combine coursework with inquiry and hands-on experiences in NYC public schools and educational institutions to create a powerful and equity-driven pedagogies and research. Like the dynamic, global city around us, you will be a future-focused leader in social studies education. You’ll join a diverse community of learners who come from all over the world to advance their knowledge and hone their skills and abilities. You share a motivation to be highly effective educators, educational leaders, and researchers who reshape social studies education.

The Team delivers the tools and resources to ensure Engineering students have access to co-curricular and extracurricular programs and activities. It coordinates research experiences and undergraduate/graduate teaching opportunities. The Team also serves as the facilitator for prospective student inquiries around Engineering programs. We assist students of all levels and backgrounds in achieving their goals and engaging fully in their experience at Brown University School of Engineering. We welcome any questions you may have, please feel free to contact one of the following members of our team.

For questions please email Nick Lumpkin (nicholas.lumpkin@uc.edu) The Campus Recreation Team Building program is offered in a two-hour time frame and can be combined with other activities including meeting space, open recreation or climbing. The team building program focuses on bringing members of your team closer through various exercises using both the body and mind. The program is open to any group inside or outside the University of Cincinnati community. Team Building Specialists will focus on communication and unity throughout the program. The mix of activities for your event will be selected by the specialist prior to your arrival. Activity Categories Get Ready – a series of ice-breakers that will get your mind and body ready to excel. Trust Me – these activities focus heavily on communication and help teammates feel more comfortable working together, building trust. That’s Impossible! –these activities give team members an opportunity to think outside-the-box to achieve their goals. To arrange a team building program or for questions, contact nicholas.lumpkin@uc.edu, or call 513-556-0708. Team Building Rates 2-hour program $190 - up to 30 participants $250 - 30 -50 participants 2-hour program + one hour climbing $250 - up to 30 participants $310 - 30 -50 participants 2-hour program + meeting space* for up to 4 hours $325 - up to 30 participants $385 - 30 -50 participants 2 hour program + 2 hour climbing + meeting space* for up to 4 hours $450 - up to 30 participants $510 - 30 -50 participants *Meeting Space includes the use of tables, chairs, and tablecloths. Prices are subject to change.

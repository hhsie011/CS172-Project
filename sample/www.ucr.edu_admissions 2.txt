                     <h2 class="separator-line-before">Let Your Amazing Journey Begin</h2> 
                     <p>As one of the most highly ranked academic institutions in the country, we inspire our students to dream big, push boundaries, defy the odds, and blaze new trails. &nbsp;Our undergraduate and graduate programs are led by dedicated, award-winning faculty who engage students in experience-based learning and research. Our welcoming, innovative, entrepreneurial campus community embodies the very best of California.</p> 
                     <p>UC Riverside is one of the most ethnically diverse and inclusive research universities in the nation. We&nbsp;have been named the third-best college for Hispanics in the country (BestColleges.com, 2016). We are also an Asian-American, Native American, and Pacific Islander Serving Institution (AANAPISI).&nbsp;</p> 
                     <p>At UCR, 86% of undergraduate students receive financial aid and 70% of those who receive aid have their full fees covered by grants and/or scholarships. Learn more about&nbsp;<a href="http://financialaid.ucr.edu/">UCR Financial Aid</a>.</p> 
                     <p>Whether you’re aiming for graduate or professional school or are looking ahead to succeed in your career, UCR is the right place to help you achieve your goals.</p>

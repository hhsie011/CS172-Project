                        <h2>Unique. Committed. Real.</h2> 
                        <h2>Just Like You!</h2> 
                        <p>A unique student like you is the heart of UC Riverside. We invite — we need — your individuality, your passion, and your voice. Your kind of commitment keeps us on our toes.&nbsp;(And, we love that about you.) We welcome your kind of real energy — and we'll never let it fade.</p> 
                        <p>At UCR, we’ll challenge you with our world-class faculty, including two Nobel laureates, a Pulitzer Prize winner, countless social activists, and world influencers. You’ll find a kaleidoscope of faces, narratives, and cultures that celebrate the differences that define and the journey that unites us. We’re glad you're here — now let’s see what special experiences we can add to your story.&nbsp;</p>

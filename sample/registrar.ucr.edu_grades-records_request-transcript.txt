                  <h2 id="-menu">Grades &amp; Records</h2> 
                  <nav role="navigation" aria-labelledby="general-navigation"> 
                   <ul class="vertical menu"> 
                    <li><a href="/grades-records" title="Grades &amp; Records" data-drupal-link-system-path="node/176">Grades &amp; Records</a> </li> 
                    <li><a href="/grades-records/request-transcript" data-drupal-link-system-path="node/181" class="is-active">Request a Transcript </a> </li>

                <div class="layout__region layout__region--column-2"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:8db74fd8-4b73-4bd4-9615-afec1cd18b46" class="ucr-custom-block"> 
                  <div class="grid-container full"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h2 class="separator-line-maroon-after">How To Order Transcripts</h2> 
                    </div> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <ul class="accordion ucr-accordion" data-accordion data-allow-all-closed="true" data-deep-link="true" data-update-history="true" id="accordion-8e987fa433d7708fdf2491e3d9b05443"> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#current_students_alumni" class="accordion-title"> Current Students &amp; Alumni </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="current_students_alumni"> 
                        <p>Current students and alumni can order and pay for official transcripts online through <a href="http://rweb.ucr.edu/" target="_blank" title="R'Web">R’Web</a>. You will need your UCR NetID and password to login and place your order. Both electronic and paper transcripts are available.</p> 
                        <p>If you need an attachment to be sent with your transcript, you will have the opportunity to upload a PDF, Word, or image file when placing your order.</p> 
                        <p>Need a UCR NetID or forget your NetID/password? When you go to R’Web, select "Password Reset." Answer the security questions and you will be provided with your NetID and password.&nbsp;If you need additional assistance with your NetID or password, contact the Registrar’s Office at (951) 827-7284. If you need assistance with Multifactor Authentication, please contact <a href="https://its.ucr.edu/" target="_blank" title="Contact Technical Support">BearHelp</a>.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#third_parties" class="accordion-title"> Third Parties </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="third_parties"> 
                        <p>Third parties ordering transcripts on behalf of a student may do so by filling out an <a href="https://exchange.parchment.com/u/registration/33599/institution#/tpo " target="_blank" title="Request a Transcript">Online Request Form</a>. Please note that you will be required to complete and submit a provided release form, which includes the signature of the student before your order will be processed.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                     </ul> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:d39e5a92-f077-4fa4-bd92-d3f6313784a0" class="ucr-custom-block"> 
                  <div class="grid-container full"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h3 class="separator-line-maroon-after">Transcript Processing Times and Fees</h3> 
                     <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="width: 100%;">
                      <thead>
                       <tr>
                        <th scope="col" width="125"><strong>Transcript Type</strong></th> 
                        <th scope="col" width="300"><strong>Processing Time</strong></th> 
                        <th scope="col" width="100"><strong>Cost</strong></th> 
                       </tr>
                      </thead>
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td><strong>eTranscripts (PDF)</strong><br><em>Fast, electronic delivery</em></td> 
                        <td>Generally available within 2 hours<sup>1</sup></td> 
                        <td>Free (Covered by Single Document Fee)&nbsp;</td> 
                       </tr>
                       <tr style="background-color:#DCDCDC">
                        <td><strong>Paper Transcripts (Rush)</strong><br><em>USPS First Class Mail</em></td> 
                        <td> <p>Orders placed by 11:00 am generally ship the same day<br> Orders placed after 11:00 am will ship the next business day<sup>2</sup></p> </td> 
                        <td>$10</td> 
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td><strong>Paper Transcripts (Rush)</strong><br><em>FedEx Overnight</em></td> 
                        <td>Orders placed by 11:00 am generally ship the same day<br> Orders placed after 11:00 am will ship the next business day<sup>2</sup></td> 
                        <td>$40<sup>4</sup></td> 
                       </tr>
                       <tr style="background-color:#DCDCDC">
                        <td><strong>Paper Transcripts (Rush)</strong><br><em>FedEx International</em></td> 
                        <td>Orders placed by 11:00 am generally ship the same day<br> Orders placed after 11:00 am will ship the next business day<sup>2</sup></td> 
                        <td>$65<sup>4</sup></td> 
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td><strong>Paper Transcripts</strong><br><em>Pick up at HOSS</em></td> 
                        <td>Available for pickup 2 business days after placing your order<sup>3</sup></td> 
                        <td>$20</td> 
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <p><strong>Please Note:</strong> Processing time is the amount of time it takes for your transcript order to ship or be available for pickup. The delivery speed of your transcripts depends on the options you select and the location of the recipient.</p> 
                     <p><sup>1</sup><small><i>During peak processing times, please allow up to 24 hours for delivery.</i></small><br><sup>2</sup><small><i>Pacific Time. Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding holidays.</i></small><br><sup>3</sup><small><i>Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm, excluding university holidays.</i></small><br><sup>4</sup><small><i>Prices may fluctuate occasionally as shipping costs change.</i></small></p> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:33bc35c9-af0d-48ce-9159-52c96e840eb3" class="ucr-custom-block"> 
                  <div class="grid-container full"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h4 class="separator-line-maroon-after">Other Important Information:</h4> 
                     <ul>
                      <li>Requests for transcripts must be made through <a href="https://rweb.ucr.edu/" target="_blank" title="R'Web">R’Web</a>. You may not make a transcript request in person or by phone, email, or fax.</li> 
                      <li>UCR does not provide a faxing service for transcripts.</li> 
                      <li>Most universities want transcripts delivered electronically or mailed directly to them. Please ensure that you know their requirements prior to ordering your transcript.</li> 
                      <li>Transcripts ordered for pick up at the <a href="https://hoss.ucr.edu" target="_blank">HOSS</a> are held for 30 calendar days. If you do not pick up your transcripts within this time, a new order must be placed.</li> 
                     </ul>
                     <p>&nbsp;</p> 
                     <h4 class="separator-line-maroon-after">Questions?</h4> 
                     <ul>
                      <li>If you have questions, please call the Transcript Helpline for assistance at (844) 728-2927. Phone support is available from 8:00 am to 4:00 pm.</li> 
                     </ul>

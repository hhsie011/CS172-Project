                     <h2 class="separator-line-before">Explore our Dynamic Campus Community</h2> 
                     <p>As a member of America’s most prestigious public university system, UC Riverside is one of the most dynamic campuses in the nation. We invite you to explore everything we have to offer — in person or online.&nbsp;</p> 
                     <p>Start your journey with UCR: View our&nbsp;<a href="https://campusmap.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">campus map</a>,&nbsp;<a href="https://www.ucr.edu/academics" target="_blank">explore our degree programs</a>, or&nbsp;<a href="https://admissions.ucr.edu/visit" target="_blank">schedule your campus visit today</a>.</p> 
                     <h4>The Riverside Experience</h4> 
                     <p>The vibrant city we call home boasts arts, culture, entertainment, family fun, outdoor life, festivals, sports, nightlife, and more – including the top ranked holiday lights display in the United States. You can even explore Riverside’s rich history through a&nbsp;<a href="http://www.riversidedowntown.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/rdp_walking_map.pdf" target="_blank">self-guided walking tour of downtown</a>or&nbsp;a guided walking food tour from alumna, Jennifer Kolb with her company <a href="https://rivsitesandbites.com/">Riverside Sites and Bites</a>.</p> 
                     <p>If you want to venture outside our city limits, all of Southern California is within easy reach — by plane or car. Ontario International Airport (ONT) is a quick 20-minute drive from campus.&nbsp;</p>

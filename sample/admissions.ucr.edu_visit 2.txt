           <article role="article" about="/visit"> 
            <div> 
             <div class="layout layout-one-col grid-container"> 
              <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
               <div class="cell"> 
                <div class="layout__region layout__region--main"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:27fe4f21-e98c-4507-a69d-d158b3ee49ec" class="ucr-custom-block">

                     <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x grid-margin-y"> 
                      <div class="cell large-12"> 
                       <h3 class="separator-line-maroon-after"><span style="color:#1d3a61;">Get to Know UCR</span></h3> 
                       <p>There's no better way to get a feel for life at UC Riverside than with a campus tour! Explore UCR's colleges, get to know our campus history and culture, and discover why UCR is home to UNIQUE, COMMITTED, REAL students just like you!</p> 
                      </div> 
                     </div> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:9a823876-9088-4060-a024-1585120e126a" class="ucr-custom-block"> 
                  <div class="grid-container full"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x grid-margin-y"> 
                      <div class="cell large-4 small-12 first"> 
                       <div alt="campus-tour-individual.jpg" data-embed-button="media_browser" data-entity-embed-display="media_image" data-entity-type="media" data-entity-uuid="ec7b4788-2ed4-493e-84cb-de745876bd31" data-langcode="en" title="campus-tour-individual.jpg" class="embedded-entity"> 
                        <img src="/sites/g/files/rcwecm2006/files/2021-09/Visit%20Campus%20Family%20Tour.jpg" alt="campus-tour-individual.jpg" title="campus-tour-individual.jpg" typeof="foaf:Image">
                       </div> 
                       <h4><br><span style="color:#1d3a61;">Family Tours</span></h4> 
                       <p><span style="color:#000000;">Come to campus and bring up to three (3) guests! Register for a general campus tour or an information session.</span></p> 
                       <p><a class="btn-ucr-outline-md-blue" href="https://connect.ucr.edu/portal/campus_tours" target="_blank" title="Book a reservation"><span style="color:#000000;">Book a reservation</span></a></p> 
                       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
                      </div> 
                      <div class="cell large-4 small-12 first"> 
                       <div alt="campus-tour-group" data-embed-button="media_browser" data-entity-embed-display="media_image" data-entity-type="media" data-entity-uuid="a84a3661-1fad-4ff6-b80f-850002a1ede9" data-langcode="en" title="campus-tour-group" class="embedded-entity"> 
                        <img src="/sites/g/files/rcwecm2006/files/2021-09/Visit%20Campus%20Self-guided%20Tour.jpg" alt="campus-tour-group" title="campus-tour-group" typeof="foaf:Image">
                       </div> 
                       <h4><br><span style="color:#1d3a61;">Self-Guided Tours</span></h4> 
                       <p><span style="color:#000000;">Explore UCR at your own pace and bring up to four (4) guests!&nbsp;Register to tell us when you plan to come and then download a map of must-see campus landmarks and fun facts.</span></p> 
                       <p><a class="btn-ucr-outline-md-blue" href="https://connect.ucr.edu/register/?id=6bf3836e-efbc-428f-8a81-bc6457977e6e" target="_blank" title="Register today"><span style="color:#000000;">Register today</span></a></p> 
                       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
                      </div> 
                      <div class="cell large-4 small-12 first"> 
                       <div alt="campus-tour-connect.jpg" data-embed-button="media_browser" data-entity-embed-display="media_image" data-entity-type="media" data-entity-uuid="7e715f8f-cf9d-4e3a-9910-d1c618941ada" data-langcode="en" title="campus-tour-connect.jpg" class="embedded-entity"> 
                        <img src="/sites/g/files/rcwecm2006/files/2021-09/Visit%20Campus%20Group%20Tour.jpg" alt="campus-tour-connect.jpg" title="campus-tour-connect.jpg" typeof="foaf:Image">
                       </div>

                        <h4><span style="color:#1d3a61;">Air</span></h4> 
                        <p><a href="http://www.flyontario.com/" target="_blank" title="Ontario Airport">Ontario International Airport (ONT)</a>&nbsp;is the most convenient air travel choice, located about 20 miles (32 km) northwest of UCR. By car, it takes 20 minutes to get to UCR from ONT in light traffic and up to an hour at rush hour. Bus and shuttle transportation options are also available.</p> 
                        <p><a href="http://www.ocair.com/" target="_blank" title="John Wayne Airport">John Wayne Orange County Airport (SNA)</a> to is 45 miles&nbsp;(72 km)&nbsp;southwest of UCR. Shuttle options are also available.</p>

                        <p><a href="http://www.metrolinktrains.com/" target="_blank" title="Metrolink">Metrolink</a>&nbsp;is the regional rail system linking Riverside County to Los Angeles, Orange, San Bernardino, San Diego, and Ventura counties. UCR is located near the&nbsp;<a href=" https://www.metrolinktrains.com/rider-info/general-info/stations/riverside---downtown/" target="_blank" title="Riverside Downtown">Riverside – Downtown</a> <a href="https://www.metrolinktrains.com/rider-info/general-info/stations/riverside---hunter-parkucr/" target="_blank" title="Hunter Park">Riverside – Hunter Park/UCR</a>&nbsp;stations, and&nbsp;<a href="https://www.riversidetransit.com/" target="_blank" title="RTA">Riverside Transit Agency (RTA)</a>&nbsp;bus transportation is available from both stations to campus.</p> 
                        <p><a href=" https://www.amtrak.com/home" target="_blank" title="Amtrak">Amtrak</a> is a national rail system that operates across the United States. The Riverside – Downtown station is accessible to campus via RTA bus.</p> 
                        <div alt="bus" data-embed-button="media_browser" data-entity-embed-display="media_image" data-entity-embed-display-settings="galleria_thumbnail" data-entity-type="media" data-entity-uuid="707799ca-df32-47a3-89c5-a04fa1f612c9" data-langcode="en" title="tarvel-tours-bus.jpg" class="embedded-entity align-left"> 
                         <img src="/sites/g/files/rcwecm2006/files/styles/galleria_thumbnail/public/2020-01/tarvel-tours-bus.jpg?h=038a9462&amp;itok=1qkLAP_u" width="100" height="100" alt="bus" title="tarvel-tours-bus.jpg" loading="lazy" typeof="foaf:Image">
                        </div> 
                        <h4><span style="color:#1d3a61;">Bus</span></h4> 
                        <p><a href="https://www.riversidetransit.com/" target="_blank" title="Bus">Riverside Transit Agency (RTA)</a> buses connect UCR to the surrounding communities as well as the Riverside — Downtown and Riverside — Hunter Park/UCR train stations.</p> 
                        <p><a href="http://www.omnitrans.org/" target="_blank" title="Omnitrans">OmniTrans</a> provides bus service from Ontario International Airport (ONT) to campus.</p> 
                        <div alt="car" data-embed-button="media_browser" data-entity-embed-display="media_image" data-entity-embed-display-settings="galleria_thumbnail" data-entity-type="media" data-entity-uuid="90259ded-e42c-4152-a664-3eb5a13e15b3" data-langcode="en" title="tarvel-tours-car.jpg" class="embedded-entity align-left">

If needed, car rental facilities are available at all major airports.</p>

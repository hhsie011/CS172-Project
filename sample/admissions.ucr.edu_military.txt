                     <div class="grid-container"> 
                      <h2>Find a Network of Support at UCR.</h2> 
                      <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x grid-padding-y"> 
                       <div class="cell large-auto"> 
                        <p>As a student veteran or service member, you'll receive dedicated support from the moment you apply to UCR. Get assistance from an <a href="/contact" title="Contact your counselor">admissions counselor</a>&nbsp;during the application process. Connect with our <a href="https://financialaid.ucr.edu/veterans/veterans-educational-benefits" target="_blank" title="Financial Aid: Veterans Educational Benefits">Financial Aid</a> office to maximize your education benefits. Discover opportunities and build community at the <a href="https://veterans.ucr.edu/community" target="_blank" title="Student Life: Veterans Resource Center">Veterans Resource Center (VRC)</a>. &nbsp;</p> 
                        <p>UCR is proud to be recognized as one of the best universities in the United States for student veterans. UCR is a <a href="https://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill/yellow_ribbon/2018/states/ca.asp" target="_blank" title="Yellow Ribbon Program">Yellow Ribbon Program</a>&nbsp;campus, which means that the university&nbsp;and the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs provide funds to cover all nonresident supplemental tuition and fees for eligible students in all majors, with no limit.</p> 
                        <p>Attend a university committed to your success. <a href="https://apply.universityofcalifornia.edu" title="UCR Admissions">Apply to UCR today!&nbsp;</a></p>

                       <h2 class="separator-line-before">Financial Aid</h2> 
                       <p><a href="/transfer#cost" title="Transfer: Cost"><strong>Learn how affordable a UCR degree can be!</strong></a></p> 
                       <p>UCR's <a href="https://financialaid.ucr.edu/veterans/veterans-educational-benefits" target="_blank" title="UCR Veterans Educational Benefits">Financial Aid</a> office is here to help you maximize the educational benefits you earned through your military service.</p> 
                       <h4>Follow these steps to access your benefits:</h4> 
                       <div class="dateblock"> 
                        <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x"> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-3 large-1"> 
                          <h5>1.</h5> 
                         </div> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-auto large-auto"> 
                          <p><a href="http://go.ucr.edu/apply" target="_blank">Apply to</a> and be admitted to UCR.&nbsp;</p> 
                         </div> 
                        </div> 
                       </div> 
                       <div class="daterow"> 
                        <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x"> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-3 large-1"> 
                          <h5>2.</h5> 
                         </div> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-auto large-auto"> 
                          <p>Apply for Financial Aid by completing the <a href="https://studentaid.ed.gov/sa/fafsa" target="_blank" title="Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)">Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)</a> by the March 2 deadline.</p> 
                         </div> 
                        </div> 
                       </div> 
                       <div class="daterow"> 
                        <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x"> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-3 large-1"> 
                          <h5>3.</h5> 
                         </div> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-auto large-auto"> 
                          <p>Establish your educational benefits eligibility through the <a href="https://va.gov/" target="_blank" title="U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs">U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs</a>.</p> 
                         </div> 
                        </div> 
                       </div> 
                       <div class="daterow"> 
                        <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x"> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-3 large-1"> 
                          <h5>4.</h5> 
                         </div> 
                         <div class="cell small-auto medium-auto large-auto"> 
                          <p>Once you've enrolled in classes at UCR, certify your enrollment with the <a href="https://financialaid.ucr.edu/help" target="_blank" title="UCR Financial Aid">Financial Aid</a> office.</p> 
                         </div> 
                        </div> 
                       </div> 
                       <p><a href="https://financialaid.ucr.edu/veterans/veterans-educational-benefits" target="_blank" title="UCR Veterans Educational Benefits">Learn more about how Financial Aid works with student veterans and servicemembers.</a></p> 
                       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
                       <div class="infoblock"> 
                        <h5><strong>UCR Federal School Code</strong>: 001316</h5> 
                        <p>To qualify for financial aid, you need to complete the <a href="https://studentaid.ed.gov/sa/fafsa" target="_blank">Free Application for Financial Aid (FAFSA)</a>&nbsp;each year by March 2.</p> 
                       </div> 
                       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
                       <p>As a student veteran or servicemember, you may be eligible for exemption from the <a href="https://sbs.ucr.edu/student-services#payment_options" target="_blank" title="Deferred Payment Plan (DPP) ">Deferred Payment Plan (DPP)</a>&nbsp; application fee and the first late fee each quarter. Through DPP, you can pay your tuition and fees in three installments each quarter with no interest.</p> 
                       <p>If you were disabled while on active duty after Sept. 11, 2001, you could qualify for the <a href="http://operationeducation.ucr.edu/" target="_blank" title="Operation Education Scholarship">Operation Education Scholarship</a>&nbsp;program at UCR. Get financial assistance for tuition, fees, books and living expenses, as well as academic, home and social support. The <a href="http://operationeducation.ucr.edu/applying/" target="_blank" title="Application Deadline">application deadline</a>&nbsp;is May 15.</p>

                       <h2 class="separator-line-before" id="cost">Resources &amp; Support</h2> 
                       <p>UCR offers dedicated programs and resources for student veterans and servicemembers. Get the support you need to earn your degree and start a new career.</p> 
                       <div class="grid-x grid-margin-x grid-padding-y"> 
                        <div class="cell large-auto"> 
                         <h6>Housing</h6> 
                         <p>Our <a href=" http://housing.ucr.edu/" target="_blank" title="UCR Housing Office">Housing office</a> can help you find a military roommate (if desired) and will work with you if your GI Bill payment is late.</p> 
                         <h6>Operation Education Scholarship</h6> 
                         <p>In addition to financial assistance, students who receive the <a href="http://operationeducation.ucr.edu/" target="_blank" title="Operation Education Scholarship">Operation Education Scholarship</a> are also provided with academic, home and social support.</p> 
                         <h6>Operation VETS</h6> 
                         <p><a href="https://careers.ucr.edu/resources/career-planning/career-development-programs" target="_blank" title="Operation VETS">Operation VETS</a> (Veteran, Employment, Transition, Success)&nbsp;is a career-development program designed for student veterans and servicemembers of all majors and class levels.</p> 
                         <h6>Priority Enrollment</h6> 
                         <p>Enjoy <a href="https://veterans.ucr.edu" target="_blank" title="Priority Enrollment">priority enrollment</a> beginning your second quarter at UCR so that you can get the classes you need.</p> 
                         <h6>Student Organizations</h6> 
                         <p>UCR is home to more than 500 student organizations, including the <a href="https://highlanderlink.ucr.edu/organization/svo" target="_blank" title="Student Veterans Organization">Student Veterans Organization</a>, a group offering community and support to student veterans and service members on campus and through <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/UCRstudentvets/" title="Join us on Facebook">social activities</a>.</p> 
                         <h6>Veterans Resource Center (VRC)</h6> 
                         <p>The <a href="https://veterans.ucr.edu" target="_blank" title="Veterans Resource Center">VRC</a> is the go-to support center for you and your dependents at UCR. Located in <a href="https://campusmap.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">Bannockburn H-108</a>, the center is where you can find resources, get referrals, use computers and printers for classwork, get free Scantrons and blue books at exam time, connect with other student veterans and servicemembers&nbsp;and just hang out between classes.</p>

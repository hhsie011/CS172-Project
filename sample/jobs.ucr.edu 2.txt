                     <h3 class="text-align-justify">Why Work At UCR?</h3> 
                     <p>US News ranked UCR as the number 35 public university in the U.S.&nbsp;– it is the premier research university in Inland Southern California and one of its largest employers. UCR is part of the world renowned and prestigious University of California (UC) system. UC provides exceptional benefits in addition to the special intellectual and cultural advantages of being a member of the UC Community.</p> 
                     <p>UCR recognizes the importance of enabling employees to explore engaging career opportunities through education, development, leadership programs and promotional opportunities; enjoy a variety of <a href="https://events.ucr.edu/" title="UCR Events Calendar"><span style="color:#234674;">campus events</span></a>; live a healthy lifestyle with <a href="https://hr.ucr.edu/about-us/workplace-health-wellness/wellness"><span style="color:#234674;">wellness resources</span></a> and an on-site <a href="https://recreation.ucr.edu/facilities" title="UCR STudent Recreation Center (SRC)"><span style="color:#234674;">Recreation Center</span></a>; strive for <a href="/employee-resources/alternative-flexible-work-schedules" title="work life balance"><span style="color:#234674;">work-life balance</span></a> through policy, programs and an on-site <a href="https://ecs.ucr.edu/" title="UCR Early Childhood Services - Child Development Center"><span style="color:#234674;">Child Development Center</span></a>; and provide ample opportunity to network, engage and connect with fellow employees. Faculty and staff can keep up to date on the UCR news and happenings via the online news outlet, <a href="https://insideucr.ucr.edu/" title="Inside UCR - UCR news"><span style="color:#234674;">Inside UCR</span></a> as well as the interesting cultural opportunities that exist in <a href="https://rivco.org/sites/default/files/2020/10/Final x3 Cut Mixed 1080p.mp4" title="Riverside County video - Who we are and Where did we come from"><span style="color:#234674;">Riverside County</span></a>.</p> 
                     <h3 class="text-align-justify">College to Career Program</h3> 
                     <p>Are you graduating soon and wondering what to do next – look no further than UCR! The Talent Acquisition and Diversity Outreach team has launched their College to Careers program. This program helps incoming and recent graduates learn about, and prepare for career opportunities available at UCR. Graduate students are encouraged to apply for open jobs on campus and begin their UCR careers. For more program information, please visit the <a href="https://hr.ucr.edu/ucr-college-career-program" title="College to Career Program webpage"><span style="color:#234674;">College to Career Program webpage</span></a>.</p>

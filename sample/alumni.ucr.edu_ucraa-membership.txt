                <div class="layout__region layout__region--main"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="field_block:node:page:body"> 
                  <div class="basic-body"> <span><h3><span style="font-size:40px;">Forever Highlanders</span></h3> <p>&nbsp;</p> <h5><span style="color:#329af0;"><strong>UCR has a growing network of alumni across the globe, united by UCR’s world-class education. </strong></span></h5> <p>We invite you to join this powerful community of support, pride, and unity.</p> <p>Become a UCR Alumni Association member – it’s the next best thing to being back on campus.</p> <h5><span style="color:#329af0;"><strong>New membership program details coming soon.</strong></span></h5> <p>For additional questions contact,&nbsp;<a data-auth="NotApplicable" data-linkindex="0" href="mailto:ucralum@ucr.edu" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">ucralum@ucr.edu</a>.</p> </span> 
                  </div>

                </div>

             <div class="layout layout-one-col grid-container full"> 
              <div class="grid-x"> 
               <div class="cell"> 
                <div class="layout__region layout__region--main"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:98b0af97-c313-418a-a1f8-106e251c1c12" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins ucr-custom-block-custom-paddings" style="background: linear-gradient(rgba(29,54,97,0.65),rgba(29,54,97,0.65)), url(/sites/g/files/rcwecm771/files/HUB-color-bg.jpg); background-size: cover;"> 
                  <div class="grid-container ucr-custom-block-font-white margin-vertical-2 padding-top-5 padding-bottom-4 padding-horizontal-4"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h2 class="text-align-center"><strong><span style="font-size:1.8em;">Why Join UCRAA?</span></strong></h2> 
                     <h3 class="text-align-center"><br><strong>Your membership strengthens the programs we pride ourselves on at UCR, keeps alumni connected through common ground, and enhances the student experience.</strong></h3> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div> 
              </div> 
             </div> 
             <div class="layout layout-one-col grid-container"> 
              <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
               <div class="cell"> 
                <div class="layout__region layout__region--main"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="inline_block:basic" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins ucr-custom-block-custom-paddings"> 
                  <div class="grid-container margin-top-1 margin-bottom-0 medium-margin-horizontal-0 padding-0"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h3 class="separator-line-before">Membership&nbsp;Benefits</h3> 
                     <ul>
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Access to extended career services.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Book borrowing privileges at all 10 UC Libraries.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Discounts up to 30% off&nbsp;Dell Products.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Theme park and&nbsp;concert/events discounts.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Hotel and car rental discounts.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Free or discounted admission to select UCR Alumni Association events.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Discounted insurance through Geico.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">10% off UC Riverside Extension Courses and discounts at many UC Extensions.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Discounted travel packages through AHI Travel and AESU Young Alumni Journeys.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Exclusive invitations to networking events and campus activities.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">10% discount at the UCR Bookstore.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Discounted tickets to UCR Athletics home events.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Discounted Meyer and Associates Insurance Related Products.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">Spotlight News Premium Subscription Discount.</li> 
                      <li style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;">First-time homebuying workshops through American Pacific Mortgage and up to $750 credit towards closing costs and other savings on your purchase or refinance.</li> 
                     </ul>
                     <p style="margin:0in 0in 0.0001pt 32px;"><a class="btn-ucr" href="https://alumni.ucr.edu/annual-membership" title="Annual Membership">Annual Members</a>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<a class="btn-ucr" href="https://alumni.ucr.edu/lifetime-membership-benefits" title="Lifetime Members">Lifetime Members</a></p>

                 <div data-block-plugin-id="block_content:a55dca50-cc57-4ad4-b17d-b39714a9d674" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins margin-top-1 margin-bottom-5 medium-margin-left-1 medium-margin-right-4"> 
                  <div class="grid-container full"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h3 class="separator-line-before">Membership FAQs</h3> 
                    </div> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <ul class="accordion ucr-accordion" data-accordion data-allow-all-closed="true" data-deep-link="true" data-update-history="true" id="accordion-bf1d6e5f28ea62ef379b03bed70f7755"> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#how_do_i_access_a_specific_membership_benefit" class="accordion-title"> How do I access a specific membership benefit? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="how_do_i_access_a_specific_membership_benefit"> 
                        <p>Members can access their exclusive benefits by contacting the <strong>Alumni Association at 951.827.2586</strong> or <strong>email <a href="mailto:ucralum@ucr.edu" target="_blank">ucralum@ucr.edu</a></strong>. For Extension Center discounts, please contact <strong>a member of the UCR Extension team at</strong> <strong>951-827-4105</strong> and have a copy of your membership card accessible for proof of membership. For Bookstore online discount, please contact<strong>&nbsp;<a data-auth="NotApplicable" data-linkindex="0" href="mailto:sm8106@bncollege.com" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">sm8106@bncollege.com</a></strong>&nbsp;and have a copy of your card.&nbsp;If you are already a member, login below.</p> 
                        <p><a href="/annual-membership">Annual Members</a></p> 
                        <p><a href="/lifetime-membership-benefits">Lifetime Members</a></p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#i_lost_my_membership_card_how_do_i_get_a_replacement" class="accordion-title"> I lost my membership card. How do I get a replacement? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="i_lost_my_membership_card_how_do_i_get_a_replacement"> 
                        <p>Membership replacement cards are available at any time. <strong>Email&nbsp;<a data-auth="NotApplicable" data-linkindex="1" href="mailto:ucralum@ucr.edu" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">ucralum@ucr.edu</a>&nbsp;or call our office at 951.827.2586</strong> to obtain a new card.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#who_can_join_the_ucr_alumni_association" class="accordion-title"> Who can join the UCR Alumni Association? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="who_can_join_the_ucr_alumni_association"> 
                        <p>Membership is open to all graduates, former students, current students, parents, faculty, staff, and friends of UCR.</p> 
                        <p><em>Member benefits are subject to change.</em></p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#do_i_qualify_for_dual_membership" class="accordion-title"> Do I qualify for dual membership? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="do_i_qualify_for_dual_membership"> 
                        <p>Any two members residing in the same residence qualify for dual membership rates. Dual members can join online anytime.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#how_long_does_it_take_to_process_membership" class="accordion-title"> How long does it take to process membership? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="how_long_does_it_take_to_process_membership"> 
                        <p>It can take up to 4 weeks to receive your personalized membership card in the mail. However, you'll receive a digital version of your membership card instantly if you <a href="https://membership.myadv.ucr.edu/join" target="_blank" title="Join Online">join online</a>.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#do_i_have_to_be_a_member_to_keep_my_ucr_student_email_address" class="accordion-title"> Do I have to be a member to keep my UCR student email address? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="do_i_have_to_be_a_member_to_keep_my_ucr_student_email_address"> 
                        <p>UCR Alumni who graduated after 2010 have access to their UCR email address for life, regardless of your UCRAA membership status. If you graduated before 2010, you may have to set up an email forwarding address. For assistance, please call the <strong>Alumni Association office at 951.827.2586</strong>.&nbsp;For assistance, <strong>email&nbsp;<a data-auth="NotApplicable" data-linkindex="2" href="mailto:ucralum@ucr.edu" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">ucralum@ucr.edu</a>&nbsp;or call the Alumni Association office at 951.827.2586</strong>.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#does_my_membership_provide_online_library_journal_access" class="accordion-title"> Does my membership provide online library journal access? </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="does_my_membership_provide_online_library_journal_access"> 
                        <p>No, however, members can access journal databases by visiting any UC Campus Library.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                     </ul>

                     <h2 class="separator-line-before">UCR: Get Involved and Inspired</h2> 
                     <p>Located in the 10th most populous county in the United States and California’s fastest-growing region, UC Riverside has much to offer. Its diverse, inclusive, and globally focused community reflects California’s many cultures and perspectives.</p> 
                     <p>As a member of the world’s most prestigious public university system, we offer our students a premier educational experience. UC Riverside ranked as a Top 10 public university in the U.S. — and America’s most transformative public university — by Money Magazine in 2020.&nbsp;</p> 
                     <p>But what makes us great goes far beyond our academic rigor and rankings. There are countless ways to get involved and inspired at UC Riverside. We’re home to a thriving arts community, one-of-a-kind&nbsp;<a href="https://events.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">campus events</a>, inspiring lecture series, and more than 450&nbsp;<a href="https://studentlife.ucr.edu/student-organization-list" target="_blank">student organizations</a>.&nbsp;</p> 
                     <p>More reasons to be inspired on the UC Riverside campus:</p> 
                     <ul>
                      <li>Our 40-acre&nbsp;<a href="https://gardens.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">Botanic Gardens</a>&nbsp;and famed 22-acre&nbsp;<a href="https://citrusvariety.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">Givaudan Citrus Variety Collection</a>;</li> 
                      <li>The&nbsp;<a href="https://library.ucr.edu/" target="_blank">UCR Library</a>, home to more than 2 million books and one of the world’s largest cataloged collections of science fiction and fantasy;</li> 
                      <li>UCR’s&nbsp;<a href="https://artsblock.ucr.edu/Page/california-museum-of-photography" target="_blank">California Museum of Photography</a>, the largest photographic center in the western United States; and</li> 
                      <li><a href="https://kucr.org/" target="_blank">KUCR</a>, a non-commercial campus radio station that broadcasts indie rock, jazz, and classical music, as well as public affairs and news programs.</li> 
                     </ul>

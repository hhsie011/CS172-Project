             <div class="layout layout-one-col grid-container"> 
              <div class="grid-x"> 
               <div class="cell"> 
                <div class="layout__region layout__region--main"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="field_block:node:page:body"> 
                  <div class="basic-body"> <span></span> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="inline_block:basic" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins ucr-custom-block-custom-paddings"> 
                  <div class="grid-container margin-0 padding-0"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h2 class="separator-line-before">See What’s Possible</h2> 
                     <p>At UC Riverside students of every background and ambition, from first-generation college-goers to dedicated graduate researchers, are encouraged to dream big — and achieve their dreams.&nbsp;</p> 
                     <p>Our students make our campus community a great place to live, learn, and grow — sharing their ideas, identities, and sense of what’s possible to help propel us forward in the dynamic digital and cultural space of an increasingly interconnected world.</p> 
                     <p>UC Riverside offers its undergraduate students more than 80 majors, providing plenty of options to pursue the subject matter about which they are most passionate. And our drop-in tutoring and study lounges designed to accommodate a variety of learning styles, and impressive research library — one of the largest in the nation — ensure our students have everything they need to achieve their academic goals.</p> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="inline_block:accordions" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins margin-top-0 margin-bottom-1 medium-margin-horizontal-0"> 
                  <div class="grid-container"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <ul class="accordion ucr-accordion" data-accordion data-multi-expand="true" data-allow-all-closed="true" data-deep-link="true" data-update-history="true" id="accordion-83e026e71a08475aa0e46b153279852b"> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#academic_resource_center" class="accordion-title"> Academic Resource Center </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="academic_resource_center"> 
                        <p>All of the tools you need to succeed in your college classes can be found right here on campus. The&nbsp;<a href="http://arc.ucr.edu/">Academic Resource Center</a>&nbsp;is comprised of educators, counselors and advanced students who are here to help you grow, learn and discover.</p> 
                        <p><strong>Services available include:</strong></p> 
                        <ul>
                         <li>Drop in tutoring.</li> 
                         <li>Study skills.</li> 
                         <li>Supplemental Instruction.</li> 
                         <li>Fully equipped study lounges.</li> 
                        </ul> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#libraries" class="accordion-title"> Libraries </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="libraries"> 
                        <p><a href="https://library.ucr.edu/">UC Riverside libraries</a>&nbsp;are among the largest academic research libraries in the United States, with one of the UC systems’ most complete collections. With over 2 million volumes, 28,000 print and electronic serial subscriptions, 1.7 million microforms, 300,000 government information sources, and access to 165,000 e-books, we’re ready to connect you with the resources you need, right when you need them.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#majors_minors_and_programs" class="accordion-title"> Majors, Minors, and Programs </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="majors_minors_and_programs"> 
                        <p>Within our three schools (<a href="http://www.engr.ucr.edu/">Bourns College of Engineering</a>,&nbsp;<a href="http://chass.ucr.edu/">College of Humanities, Arts and Sciences</a>, and&nbsp;<a href="http://cnas.ucr.edu/">College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences</a>), the university offers majors ranging from art to anthropology, botany to business administration, English to environmental science, and mechanical engineering to music. UC Riverside isn't just a world-class university, it's your college of choice. Find out more about our&nbsp;<a href="http://admissions.ucr.edu/academics/index.html">academic programs</a>.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                     </ul> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div> 
              </div> 
             </div> 
             <div class="layout layout-one-col grid-container"> 
              <div class="grid-x"> 
               <div class="cell"> 
                <div class="layout__region layout__region--main"> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="inline_block:basic" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins ucr-custom-block-custom-paddings"> 
                  <div class="grid-container margin-0 padding-0"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <h3 class="separator-line-before">Taking Education to the Next Level</h3> 
                     <p>As the top-ranked university in the nation for social mobility (U.S. News 2020), UC Riverside is committed to helping students take their education to the next level. Across the board at UCR, challenging courses, interdisciplinary research opportunities, and inspiring faculty — including two Nobel Prize winners, and 13 members of the National Academies of Science and Medicine — take students on a journey of innovation and growth.&nbsp;</p> 
                     <p>With opportunities to participate in our&nbsp;<a href="http://ue.ucr.edu/units/honors.html" target="_blank">University Honors</a>&nbsp;program, take immersive summer sessions, or study across the globe, Highlanders connect with like-minded individuals who help to shape one another’s futures through shared curiosity, creativity, and community impact. The experience they gain benefits them as students and beyond — CNBC ranked UCR in the Top 10 colleges that pay off the most in big cities.</p> 
                    </div> 
                   </div> 
                  </div> 
                 </div> 
                 <div data-block-plugin-id="inline_block:accordions" class="ucr-custom-block ucr-custom-block-custom-margins margin-0"> 
                  <div class="grid-container full"> 
                   <div class="grid-x grid-padding-x"> 
                    <div class="cell"> 
                     <ul class="accordion ucr-accordion" data-accordion data-multi-expand="true" data-allow-all-closed="true" data-deep-link="true" data-update-history="true" id="accordion-56fcbf827bf08e55a58d65bf1ee67980"> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#summer_study_abroad_program" class="accordion-title"> Summer Study Abroad Program </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="summer_study_abroad_program"> 
                        <p>Experience spending the summer abroad while staying on track to graduate! UCR’s Summer&nbsp;<a href="http://studyabroad.ucr.edu/">Study Abroad</a>&nbsp;Program offers UCR general catalog courses taught by UCR faculty in an international location. Including 5 weeks of cultural immersion while you deepen your learning, a global adventure awaits. Learn more about this unique opportunity to spend your summer studying abroad.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#education_abroad_program_and_opportunities_abroad_program" class="accordion-title"> Education Abroad Program and Opportunities Abroad Program </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="education_abroad_program_and_opportunities_abroad_program"> 
                        <p>Join more than 4,500 University of California students who live and learn across the globe. Offered in partnership with more than 140 universities in 35 countries, UCR’s unparalleled&nbsp;<a href="http://studyabroad.ucr.edu/">Education Abroad</a>&nbsp;Program and Opportunities Abroad Programs offer exciting opportunities to deepen your studies, expand your perspective, and become immersed in cultural experiences you’ll never forget.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                      <li class="paragraph paragraph--type--accordion paragraph--view-mode--default accordion-item" data-accordion-item> <a href="#uc_center_at_sacramento" class="accordion-title"> UC Center at Sacramento </a> 
                       <div class="accordion-content" data-tab-content id="uc_center_at_sacramento"> 
                        <p>Take advantage of hands-on learning and research opportunities in California's public policy arena through the&nbsp;<a href="http://uccs.ucdavis.edu/your-campus/riverside">UC Center at Sacramento</a>. There are two integrated programs offered, academic policy and journalism, to help enrich your professional success. Chosen applicants will live and learn in Sacramento through an internship within the Capitol's policy community. Learn more about this resume-building, life-changing opportunity.</p> 
                       </div> </li> 
                     </ul>
